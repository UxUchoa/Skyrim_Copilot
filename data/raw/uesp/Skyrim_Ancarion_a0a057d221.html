Ancarion

Ancarion, a High Elf wizard, is the leader of the Thalmor on Solstheim. He is attempting to locate a source of stalhrim for the Thalmor. The Stalhrim Source map can be pickpocketed from him, taken from his dead body, or given to you by him in order to complete the quest A New Source of Stalhrim.

Quest-Related Events[edit]

A New Source of Stalhrim[edit]

His greetings:

"You are interfering with official Thalmor business. You will leave immediately if you value your well-being."
"Meddle in our affairs, and your end will be swift and painful."
"Get out of here, before I lose my patience."
"Leave at once! Do not test me, or I promise that you will regret it."

Speaking to him:

I'm here about the Stalhrim map.
"So you know my purpose here, then? I suppose you must have found that dullard of a blacksmith. Unfortunately for you, my mission here is also a secret. To protect it, I have no choice but to silence you. Now, give me one good reason that I should not kill you where you stand."

Three options are available. Note that successfully completing the third option and starting the hidden quest requires a Speech skill of at least 75.

Whatever result you gain from negotiation with Ancarion you will always end up with the Stalhrim Source map in hand.

Sell Stalhrim Armor and Weapons to Ancarion[edit]

His greetings:

"Back with more Stalhrim items, I trust?"
"Unless you have more Stalhrim weapons and armor for me, we have nothing to talk about."
"Ready to do more business?"

Speaking to him:

Shall we do some business?
"What do you have for me?"

His goodbyes:

"You have kept up your end of the bargain nicely. I admit, I wasn't sure that you would."
"I'll be here when you have more Stalhrim weapons and armor."
"There is more coin if you wish to claim it. You know what I want."
"Do not think we are now friends. I have tolerated you, nothing more."
"You are alive only at my pleasure. Do not forget that."
"I am done with you. Go on, get out of my sight."
"Nothing more to sell me?"
"I was hoping you'd have more."
"Is that all?"

Notes[edit]

  • Ancarion does not have unique facial data. Instead, he uses the default male High Elf face.

Bugs[edit]

  • Ancarion is unaffected by the Master Trader perk.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
  • If you kill Ancarion after agreeing to sell Stalhrim items to him, the quest objective is never removed.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
