Deor Woodcutter's House

Deor Woodcutter's House is the home of Deor Woodcutter and Yrsa in Skaal Village.

It is on the south side of the village, between Oslaf's House to the northwest and Baldor Iron-Shaper's House to the east.

Residents[edit]

Residents
Deor Woodcutter
Yrsa

Walkthrough[edit]

Exterior[edit]

To the left of the entrance is a wood chopping block. Along the east wall is a woodcutter's axe, two pieces of firewood, a wood pile, and a blue mountain flower. Along the west wall are two more wood piles. The entrance has a novice lock.

Deor Woodcutter's House[edit]

Deor Woodcutter's House interior

The entrance leads into a large main room with an end table on your left with a pheasant and iron dagger on top. here are many sacksof food throughout. Against the south wall is a wood pile with six pieces of firewood and a woodcutter's axe leaning against the pile. Above is a wall shelf holding a sprig of snowberries. In the southwest corner is a doorway to a double bedroom. In the middle of the room is a small square table and two chairs. On the table is a salmon steak and eidar cheese wedge. Against the west wall is a crate, a barrel with a mead barrel on top and above it all is a wall shelf holding an ice wolf pelt. To the right of the entrance are three barrels, with a cabbage and potato on top of one of them. To the left is a set of shelves containing a copy of Deathbrand. Around the corner is a fire pit with a cooking pot and a cast iron pot containing a cabbage beside it. Above the cooking pot is a hanging rack containing three whole salmon and a garlic braid. To the west along the north wall the room continues alongside the bedroom with a narrow table against the north wall with a bottle of ale and a copy of The Woodcutter's Wife on top. At the rear against the west wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of ale, a cast iron pot containing three pieces of clam meat, an open crate containing two loaves of bread, and two eidar cheese wheels.

Entering the bedroom there is a cupboard on your left with a deer hide, a medium coin purse and copy of Final Lesson. Against the west wall is a dresser with a bottle and wine and a wooden bowl with a piece of clam meat inside. Coming out from the north wall is a double bed with an end table on either side. On the one on the left is a loaf of bread and bottle of alto wine, while on the one on the right is a goat cheese wedge.
