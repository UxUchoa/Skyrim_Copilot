This page lists all the caves in Skyrim.

 Ashfall's TearCC — A small cave north of Raven Rock containing a hidden Tribunal Temple shrine to the goddess Almalexia. (map)
 BenkongerikeDB — A medium-sized cave southeast of Saering's Watch containing bristlebacks and rieklings. (map)
Blackbone Isle GrottoCC — A small cave connected to the sea, situated on Blackbone Isle. (map)
 Bleakcoast Cave — A small cave east-southeast of Winterhold containing frost trolls. (map)
 Blind Cliff Cave — A medium-sized cave southwest of Karthwasten along the banks of the Karth River containing Forsworn. (map)
 Bloated Man's Grotto — A small cave north of Falkreath containing animals and spriggans. (map)
 Bloodchill CavernCC — A small cave southwest of Winterhold containing a subterranean vampiric player home. (map)
 Bonechill Passage — A small cave east of Falkreath containing leveled animals. (map)
 Boulderfall Cave — A small cave northwest of Riften containing necromancers. (map)
 Brinewater Grotto — A small cave north of Solitude containing horkers or bandits. (map)
 Bristleback CaveDB — A small cave in the northwest of Solstheim island containing bristlebacks and rieklings. (map)
 Brittleshin Pass — A small passage through the mountains between Whiterun Hold and Falkreath Hold containing a necromancer and skeletons. (map)
 Broken Fang Cave — A small cave southeast of Rorikstead containing skeletons and vampires. (map)
 Broken Helm Hollow — A small cave in the southeast corner of The Rift containing bandits. (map)
 Broken Oar Grotto — A small cave north of Solitude containing Blackblood Marauders and their leader Captain Hargar. (map)
 Bronze Water Cave — A small cave west of Windhelm containing leveled animals. (map)
 Brood Cavern — A small cave southwest of Morthal containing leveled animals. (map)
 Bruca's Leap Redoubt — A small cave southwest of Dragon Bridge containing Forsworn. (map)
 Castle Karstaag CavernsDB — A small cave on the north coast of Solstheim containing bristlebacks and rieklings. (map)
 Castle Karstaag RuinsDB — An icy clearing outside Castle Karstaag Caverns. (map)
 Chillwind Depths — A small cave south of Dragon Bridge containing Falmer, chaurus, and frostbite spiders. (map)
 Clearspring Cave — A small cave west of Shor's Stone containing a troll. (map)
 Cold Rock Pass — A small passage through a mountain south-southwest of Morthal containing a frost troll. (map)
 Coldcinder CaveDB — A small cave southeast of Raven Rock containing ash spawn and netches. (map)
 Cragslane Cavern — A small cave north of Shor's Stone containing bandits and pit wolves. (map)
 Cragwallow Slope — A medium-sized cave southeast of Windhelm home to conjurers and atronachs. (map)
 Cronvangr Cave — A medium-sized cave southwest of Kynesgrove containing vampires and frostbite spiders. (map)
 Crystaldrift Cave — A small cave southwest of Riften containing leveled animals. (map)
 Darkfall CaveDG — A small cave south of Mor Khazgur containing frostbite spiders and trolls. (map)
Darkfall PassageDG — A small cave only accessible through Darkfall Cave serving as the only access to the Forgotten Vale.
 Darkshade — A small cave east of Whiterun containing trolls. (map)
 Darkwater Pass — A small cave south-southwest of Darkwater Crossing containing Falmer and chaurus. (map)
 Dimhollow CryptDG — A medium-sized cave east of Stonehills and southwest of Frostmere Crypt where Serana is trapped. (map)
 Duskglow Crevice — A small cave south of Dawnstar containing Falmer, chaurus, bandits, and skeevers. (map)
 Eldergleam Sanctuary — A small underground grove and worship site of the followers of Kynareth south-southwest of Windhelm. (map)
 Fallowstone Cave — A small cave northeast of Riften containing leveled animals, frostbite spiders, and giants. (map)
 Forebears' HoldoutDG — A small cave southeast of Dragon Bridge. (map)
 Forsaken Cave — A medium-sized cave west of Windhelm containing draugr and leveled animals. (map)
 FrosselDB — A small cave on a peninsula north of Skaal Village containing bristlebacks and rieklings. (map)
 Frostmoon CragDB — A small camp under a rock overhang south of Mount Moesring and northeast of the Abandoned Lodge inhabited by werewolves. (map)
 Frostroot CaveCC — A cave in Eastmarch, north of Kagrenzel. (map)
 Glacial CaveDB — A small cave north of Benkongerike containing rieklings. (map)
 Glenmoril Coven — A small cave northwest of Falkreath containing hags or Glenmoril Witches. (map)
 Gloomreach — A medium-sized cave in the Reach containing chaurus and Falmer. (map)
 Graywinter Watch — A small cave east of Whiterun containing trolls. (map)
 Greywater Grotto — A small cave east of Falkreath containing leveled animals. (map)
 Gromm's PassCC — A small cave located in the Rift, south of Forelhost. (map)
 Haemar's Shame — A medium-sized cave east of Helgen containing vampires. (map)
 Halldir's Cairn — A small cave southwest of Falkreath containing draugr, ghosts, and the powerful undead mage Halldir. (map)
 Hob's Fall Cave — A small cave west of Winterhold containing necromancers and skeletons. (map)
 Honeystrand Cave — A small cave south of Ivarstead containing bears. (map)
 Hrothmund's BarrowDB — A small cave southwest of Benkongerike. (map)
Iron Tusk CaveCC — A small cave on the island of Giant's Tooth. (map)
 Liar's Retreat — A small cave and connected underground cellar northeast of Karthwasten containing bandits and invading Falmer. (map)
 Lost Echo Cave — A small cave west of Solitude containing Falmer and chaurus. (map)
 Lost Knife Hideout — A medium-sized cave northeast of Ivarstead containing bandits. (map)
Mara's Eye Den — A small cave southwest of Windhelm containing vampires. (map)
 Moss Mother Cavern — A small, open-roofed cavernous grove lush with many kinds of flora and fungi, located due north of Hunter's Rest and northwest of Half-Moon Mill which is inhabited by bears and spriggans. (map)
 Movarth's Lair — A small cave north-northeast of Morthal containing vampires led by Movarth Piquine. (map)
 Nightingale Hall — The home of the Nightingales between Riften and the Shadow Stone. (map)
 Orotheim — A small cave that is home to bandits intent on poaching mammoths. (map)
 Pinemoon Cave — A small cave northwest of Dragon Bridge containing vampires and wolves. (map)
 Pinepeak Cavern — A small cave north of Ivarstead containing bears. (map)
 Purewater Run — A small cave south-southeast of Markarth containing slaughterfish. (map)
 Ravenscar Hollow — A small cave inhabited by hagravens west of the Thalmor Embassy and due east of the Steed Stone. (map)
 Reachcliff Cave — A small cave east-southeast of Markarth containing draugr. (map)
 Reachwater Rock — A small cave east-southeast of Markarth containing draugr. (map)
 Rebel's Cairn — A small cave northwest of Rorikstead. (map)
 Red Scar CavernCC — A cave located east of Shor's Stone, occupied by the Mythic Dawn cult. (map)
 Red Eagle Redoubt — A small cave and two exterior camps east of Markarth containing Forsworn. (map)
 Redoran's Retreat — A small cave west-northwest of Whiterun containing bandits and a dog. (map)
RielleCC — A medium-sized Ayleid ruin located beneath the Jerall Mountains, just east of the Pale Pass. It straddles the border between the Rift in Skyrim and County Bruma in Cyrodiil. In Skyrim, it is accessed from the north by way of Varlais Cavern, a cave entrance located to the south of Arcwind Point. (map)
 Rimerock Burrow — A small cave northwest of Dragon Bridge containing an atronach and the powerful conjurer Sebastian Lort. (map)
 Runoff CavernsCC — A large cave west of Lost Valley Redoubt containing mercenaries, Dwarven automatons, atronachs, ice wraiths, spriggans, and animals. (map)
 Ruunvald ExcavationDG — A medium-sized cave east of Shor's Stone containing Vigilants charmed by the conjurer Minorne. (map)
 Septimus Signus's Outpost — A small cave north of the College of Winterhold inhabited by Septimus Signus. (map)
 Shadowgreen Cavern — A small cave northwest of Solitude containing leveled spriggans and animals. (map)
 Shimmermist Cave — A medium-sized cave northeast of Whiterun containing Falmer and chaurus. (map)
 Sightless Pit — A medium-sized cave south-southwest of Winterhold containing Falmer and chaurus. (map)
 Snapleg Cave — A medium-sized cave northwest of Rift Watchtower occupied by hags and monsters. (map)
 Soljund's Sinkhole — A small moonstone mine and settlement east of Markarth initially containing draugr. (map)
 Southfringe Sanctum — A small cave southeast of Helgen containing the conjurer Bashnag and his coven. (map)
 Steepfall Burrow — A small cave northwest of Dragon Bridge containing frost trolls and ice wolves. (map)
 Stillborn Cave — A small cave south-southeast of Winterhold containing Falmer and chaurus. (map)
 Stony Creek Cave — A small cave southeast of Windhelm containing bandits. (map)
 Sunderstone Gorge — A small cave north-northwest of Falkreath containing warlocks and skeletons. (map)
 Swindler's Den — A small cave east of Rorikstead containing bandits. (map)
 Tolvald's Cave — A large cave northeast of Shor's Stone containing Falmer, chaurus, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. (map)
 Uttering Hills Cave — A small cave southwest of Windhelm containing bandits. (map)
 White River Watch — A small cave east-southeast of Whiterun containing the White River bandits. (map)
 Wolfskull Cave — A medium-sized cave west of Solitude containing bandits or necromancers and draugr. (map)
 Yngvild — A medium-sized cave east-northeast of Dawnstar containing draugr and Yngvild ghosts. (map)

Notes[edit]

  • Version 2.0.9 of the Unofficial Skyrim Patch replaces Yngvild's cave map icon with the one used for Nordic ruins.
  • Version 3.0.6 of the Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch replaces the cave map icon for Soljund's Sinkhole with the one used for mines.
