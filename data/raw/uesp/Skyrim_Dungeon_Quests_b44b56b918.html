The following side quests and miscellaneous quests generally start when you approach or explore the listed dungeons and progress to completion within each dungeon. Each quest is categorized as a dungeon quest in the game data and the Prima Official Game Guide and usually provides a backstory about the associated dungeon. For information on the many other quests involving dungeons, see the Quests page.

Dungeon Side Quests[edit]

  •  Darklight Tower — A medium-sized fort southwest of Riften containing hagravens, hags, and witches. (map)
    • Repentance: Help Illia prevent her mother Silvia from transforming into a hagraven.
  •  Forelhost — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Riften containing Dragon Cultists, draugr, skeevers, and the dragon priest Rahgot. (map)
    • Siege on the Dragon Cult: Enter the ruins of Forelhost and defeat Rahgot to recover his mask for Captain Valmir.
  •  Frostmere Crypt — A medium-sized Nordic ruin south-southwest of Dawnstar containing bandits and the unique wispmother the Pale Lady. (map)
    • The Pale Lady: Find out what happened in Frostmere Crypt.
  •  High Gate Ruins — A medium-sized Nordic ruin west of Dawnstar containing draugr and the dragon priest Vokun. (map)
    • A Scroll For Anska: Help Anska trace her roots in High Gate Ruins.
  •  Hillgrund's Tomb — A small Nordic ruin southwest of Windhelm containing draugr. (map)
    • Ancestral Worship: Help Golldir save his aunt and stop a necromancer from defiling his ancestral barrow.
  • Nchuand-Zel — A large Dwarven ruin accessed from inside Understone Keep. (map)
    • The Lost Expedition: Recover the journals from the expedition in Nchuand-Zel.
  •  Treva's Watch — A medium-sized fort southeast of Ivarstead containing bandits. (map)
    • Infiltration: Help Stalleo take back Treva's Watch.
  •  Valthume — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Markarth containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the dragon priest Hevnoraak. (map)
    • Evil in Waiting: Seal away an ancient evil.
  •  Volunruud — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Morthal containing draugr and the ghost of Kvenel the Tongue. (map)
    • Silenced Tongues: Unlock the burial chamber of the Tongue Chieftain Kvenel.

Dungeon Miscellaneous Quests[edit]

  •  Angarvunde — A medium-sized Nordic ruin west of Riften containing draugr. (map)
    • Angarvunde: Discover the treasure of Angarvunde.
  •  Ansilvund — A medium-sized Nordic ruin north of Riften containing draugr and necromancers led by Lu'ah Al-Skaven. (map)
    • Ansilvund: Kill Lu'ah Al-Skaven in Ansilvund.
  •  Blind Cliff Cave — A medium-sized cave southwest of Karthwasten along the banks of the Karth River containing Forsworn. (map)
    • The Affairs of Hagravens: After discovering an imprisoned hagraven, you must help her reclaim her lost tower.
  •  Frostflow Lighthouse — A lighthouse west-southwest of Winterhold containing Falmer, chaurus, and frostbite spiders. (map)
    • Frostflow Abyss: Uncover the tragic events inside Frostflow Lighthouse.
  •  Mistwatch — A medium-sized fort east of Darkwater Crossing containing the Mistwatch Bandits and their leader. (map)
    • Mistwatch: Help Christer locate his kidnapped wife Fjola.
  •  Moss Mother Cavern — A small, open-roofed cavernous grove lush with many kinds of flora and fungi, located due north of Hunter's Rest and northwest of Half-Moon Mill which is inhabited by bears and spriggans. (map)
    • Moss Mother Cavern: Help an injured hunter avenge his fallen companions.
  •  Nilheim — A small ruined Nordic tower east-northeast of Ivarstead containing bandits disguised as guards. (map)
    • Telrav's Request: Escort Telrav back to his camp in Nilheim.
  •  Ragnvald — A medium-sized Nordic ruin north of Markarth containing draugr and the dragon priest Otar the Mad. (map)
    • Ragnvald: Discover the secrets of Ragnvald.
  •  Rebel's Cairn — A small cave northwest of Rorikstead. (map)
    • Rebel's Cairn: Find Red Eagle's sword.
  •  Sleeping Tree Cave — A giant camp west of Whiterun. (map)
    • Sleeping Tree Cave: Question Ysolda about an interesting note you found on a dead Orc.
  •  Southfringe Sanctum — A small cave southeast of Helgen containing the conjurer Bashnag and his coven. (map)
    • The Savior of Selveni Nethri: Help a necromancer escape from Southfringe Sanctum.
  •  Yngol Barrow — A small Nordic ruin northeast of Windhelm containing the shade of an ancient Nord warrior. (map)
    • Yngol's Barrow: Find the treasure unlocked by the Coral Dragon Claw.
