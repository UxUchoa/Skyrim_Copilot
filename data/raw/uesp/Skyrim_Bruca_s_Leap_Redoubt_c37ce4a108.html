Bruca's Leap Redoubt

Bruca's Leap Redoubt is a small cave southwest of Dragon Bridge containing Forsworn. It contains only one zone, Bruca's Leap Redoubt.

Related Quests[edit]

  • Bounty: Forsworn: Kill a Forsworn leader for a bounty reward. (radiant)
  • The Ghost of Old Hroldan: Put an ancient ghost to rest. (radiant)
  • The House of Horrors: Help Molag Bal take revenge upon a servant of Boethiah. (radiant)
  • Kill the Forsworn Leader: Do a favor for Jarl Igmund by killing a Forsworn leader. (radiant)
  • Lisbet's Missing Shipment: Retrieve a missing Dibella Statue for a struggling store. (radiant)
  • This location is one of many potential targets for one or more of the Radiant quests.

Walkthrough[edit]

Exterior[edit]

The exterior campsite

Two Forsworn, one a melee fighter and the other an archer, have made camp outside the cave entrance. The campsite features a chest, five bedrolls, and a tanning rack. Also in the area are two Namira's rot fungi, a chickens' nest, and a chicken.

Bruca's Leap Redoubt[edit]

The main chamber

The cave interior is quite small, consisting of a curving passage leading down to a chamber with wooden scaffolding. The Forsworn Briarheart shaman and its melee fighter bodyguard will likely be immediately alerted to your presence. However, by sneaking, you may snipe at them from the window-like opening straight down from the entrance. An iron dagger is on the ledge nearby. At the bottom of the passage is a small coin purse and a Dwarven arrow.

Lying on a table in the chamber is the corpse of a Forsworn fighter who was in the process of being transformed into a Briarheart, with his heart and an iron dagger placed nearby. A bear pelt, a cave bear pelt, and two cow hides are scattered around, and an ingredient barrel of uncommon quality is near the table. A bookshelf holds a copy of the Enchanting skill book A Tragedy in Black, a few ingredients (including one sample each of juniper berries, sabre cat tooth, troll fat, and white cap), and an apothecary's satchel of uncommon quality. A random potion is in a bucket nearby. The boss chest is behind the bookshelf, but beware the bear trap in front of it.

A few fungi grow in the cave, including one blisterwort, three Namira's rot, and one white cap.

Bugs[edit]

  • Bruca's Leap Redoubt isn't marked as a cave, so cave-based radiant quests won't target it.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
  • The Forsworn members here may attack their own chickens.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch, version 4.1.1, addresses this issue. Both factions have been set to be friendly towards one another to prevent this from happening.
  • The cave contains a goat hide you cannot interact with.
