Asta, living a life of tranquility

Asta is a Nord worshipper of Kynareth who walks around in Eldergleam Sanctuary. She wears clothes and a pair of boots, and is equipped with a leveled dagger and a leveled bow with leveled arrows, all of which may be up to elven in quality. She also carries at least eight gold. Her only company during her worship of Eldergleam is fellow worshipper Sond.

Quest-Related Events[edit]

The Blessings of Nature[edit]

When first spoken to:

What is this place?
"You are in Eldergleam Sanctuary - just a small glimpse of what beauty Kynareth has to offer. Wonderful place, isn't it?"
And that tree? Is that Eldergleam?
"Yes, my friend. Though no one has been able to get close to her for as long as I can remember."
Why not?
"As you can see, Eldergleam's roots are large and stretch far, blocking any path to her trunk. Though, believe it or not, there are rumors of a weapon that even Eldergleam herself would lift her roots for, more out of fear than respect."
Where can I find such a weapon?
"Oh, I don't believe those rumors, my friend. Besides, who would ever want to harm such beauty?
Now, if you'll excuse me, I have nothing more to discuss. I encourage you to take your time and enjoy yourself. You look like you could use the rest."

If you have Nettlebane:

"Oh, I don't believe those rumors, my friend. Besides, who would ever want to harm such... Wait, it can't be."
What is it?
"The rumors were true! You carry the one weapon Eldergleam fears - Nettlebane. I beg of you, don't harm the tree! Nothing good can come of it."
You mean this weapon? (Show Nettlebane)
"The rumors were true... I beg of you, don't harm the tree! Nothing good can come of it."

If spoken to again if you have Nettlebane:

"You don't know what you're dealing with. There will be consequences if you harm that tree. Leave this place - you're not welcome here anymore."

If you ignore her warnings, then Asta may be killed by spriggans after you pierce Eldergleam with Nettlebane.

Dialogue[edit]

Outside of the quest, her greetings can be:

"Amazing that such beauty could exist in a place as desolate as the volcanic tundra."
"Praise Kynareth for allowing such beauty in the world."
"A truly remarkable place, isn't it?"

Notes[edit]

  • For some reason Asta's line "Oh, I don't believe those rumors, my friend. Besides, who would ever want to harm such beauty?" was recorded in almost every single voice type in the game, including unique voice types. While only Asta's variant is set up to work in the game, all other recordings can still be found in the voice files.

Bugs[edit]

  • If she is killed during The Blessings of Nature, her body will persist indefinitely due to missing stages in the quest script to track her death.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
  • Despite being a unique NPC, she's incorrectly flagged to respawn in the game data.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
