Bjormund Wind-Strider's corpse

Bjormund Wind-Strider is a dead Nord and former Crimson Dirk who is encountered during the quest Bones for a Crow. His body is found at the very top of the tower at Arcwind Point, at the bottom of the stairs which lead to the book Withershins, after the player has battled past the enemies that occupy the tower. When he chased the same bounty as you, Crowstooth killed him. As part of the reward for the quest (any spoils of Crowstooth's), you may take his unique armor.

Bjormund wears the Dragonbone Mail, Dragonplate Insulated Boots, Dragonplate Insulated Gauntlets and a Dragonplate Insulated Helmet, and carries Nord Mead, an Amulet of Akatosh, and a random selection of loot.

  • Bones for a CrowCC: Hunt down the Forsworn Crowstooth.

Bugs[edit]

  • Bjormund's body may not appear.
  • Can appear in High Hrothgar.
