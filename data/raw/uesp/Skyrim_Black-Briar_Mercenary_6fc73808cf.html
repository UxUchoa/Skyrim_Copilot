Four Black-Briar Mercenaries are present inside the Black-Briar Lodge. They wear steel plate armor, leveled light boots, and have a 50% chance of wearing leveled light bracers. These four carry 25-125 gold, a torch, a key to the lodge, and a leveled weapon.
