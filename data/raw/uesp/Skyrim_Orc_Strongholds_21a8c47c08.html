Dushnikh Yal

This page lists all the Orc strongholds in Skyrim.

 Dushnikh Yal — A prosperous Orc stronghold in the southwest of Skyrim, southeast of Markarth. (map)
 Largashbur — An accursed Orc stronghold beset by giants in the southeast of Skyrim, west-southwest of Riften. (map)
 Mor Khazgur — An Orc stronghold in the far northwest of Skyrim, west of Solitude and north of Markarth. (map)
 Narzulbur — An Orc stronghold in the east of Skyrim, southeast of Windhelm. (map)

Blood-Kin[edit]

Only Orc characters have immediate access to the strongholds unchallenged. Characters of any other race must first become Blood-Kin of the Orcs before being welcome in the strongholds. You can enter a stronghold without being Blood-Kin, but the residents will continuously tell you that you are not welcome. If the stronghold includes merchants, trainers, or potential followers, their services will be unavailable until you comply.

Several quests fulfill this requirement. Members of any stronghold you approach may give you the quest The Forgemaster's Fingers if you have not become Blood-Kin already. Alternatively, completing any quest listed below will make you Blood-Kin. Upon first completing one of these quests, the grateful Orc will say, "For your help, I'm sending word to the Orc strongholds that you're to be trusted. You should be welcome there now.", signifying that you are now Blood-Kin. In addition, each associated Orc's disposition towards you will increase by 1 upon completing the quests below, with two possible exceptions.

Notes[edit]

  • If you start "Unbound" as an Orc, Hadvar will say: "You from one of the strongholds, Orc? How did you end up here?"
  • If you become Blood-Kin and later bring your follower to the Orc strongholds, the residents will treat your follower as an extra ally as long as you and your follower are together.
  • Another Orc who would've made you Blood-Kin, named Makhel Abbas, was meant to play a role in a Civil War quest, but this quest didn't make it into the final game.

Bugs[edit]

  • Atub and Garakh at Largashbur were apparently intended to be merchants (apothecary and blacksmith respectively), but do not function as merchants themselves.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. They were assigned the wrong settings. Once fixed, they will be affected by Master Trader, but not Investor.
  • Even after becoming Blood-Kin to the Orcs, you may still be warned about trespassing in the longhouses at the Orc strongholds.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch, version 4.1.5, fixes this bug.
