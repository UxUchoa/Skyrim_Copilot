Frostmoon Crag

Frostmoon Crag is a small camp under a rock overhang south of Mount Moesring and northeast of the Abandoned Lodge inhabited by werewolves.

The Frostmoon pack is the last true werewolf pack in Solstheim according to its leader Majni, whose father and grandfather were the previous alphas. Rakel's parents were also pack members. Majni's brother Akar isn't very sociable. Hjordis's pack was destroyed by werebears; she remains with Majni's pack until they can help her reclaim her lands.

The pack members prefer to remain free in the wilderness. They warn non-werewolves away from their camp, but will welcome other werewolves who respect their law. They fully embrace their lycanthropy, and worship "Lord" Hircine. Majni will offer to sell four rings blessed by their Daedric patron to a werewolf who has Hircine's favor.

Frostmoon Crag[edit]

The normal approach to the crag is from the south. Partway up the slope, on the right are two silver ore veins. At the top of the slope near the camp, you will be addressed by Rakel, who sits on a rock with a lit lantern. If you are a werewolf, regardless of whether you are currently in werewolf form, she will welcome you. You may then speak with Majni, who will offer to sell you the four rings blessed by Hircine noted below. If you aren't a werewolf, the pack members will treat you as a trespasser, and will become hostile if you loiter too long or draw your weapon.

The camp

The camp is under a rock overhang facing southeast. In the middle is a campfire with four wooden plates, two of which hold a piece of venison, a pheasant breast, and two raw rabbit legs. Southwest of the fire is an unusable mead barrel, with three bottles of Nord mead, two bottles of ale, and an elixir of enhanced stamina on the ground next to the barrel. Farther southwest are two tanning racks and a hay pile serving as bedding, with a battleaxe and a wooden plate holding a piece of venison and a raw rabbit leg on the hay pile. Southeast of the mead barrel is a knapsack leaning against a wood pile. West of the fire are two more hay piles and an adept-locked chest next to a large coin purse. Northeast of the fire is the final hay pile and a large rock splattered with blood, with a dagger on the rock.

Four snowberry plants grow under and near the overhang. The four hay piles and all low-value items will be free to use and take after you speak with Majni if you are welcomed by the pack.

Notes[edit]

  • A Shrine of Zenithar is northwest of the cave, along with a corundum ore vein, an East Empire Pendant, two silver ore veins, and two sapphire geodes.
  • The four rings available for purchase from Majni are the Ring of Bloodlust, Ring of Instinct, Ring of the Hunt, and Ring of the Moon.
  • If your lycanthropy is cured after you first speak with Majni, he will no longer talk with you or sell you the rings.
