Dayspring Canyon

Dayspring Canyon is the wilderness area surrounding Fort Dawnguard, accessed through a cave entrance southeast of Riften.

The canyon's narrow path

The entrance to the canyon is through a narrow fissure in the rock wall a short distance east of the path that leads up to Black-Briar Lodge, along the road to Morrowind. The fissure leads into a narrow crevice that eventually opens into the isolated canyon. It is the only available route to Fort Dawnguard the first time you go there.

Walkthrough[edit]

Initial state[edit]

Waterfalls in the canyon

Once through the fissure, there is a snowy path heading east with a few snowberry bushes along it. As the path turns to the southeast, it widens to offer a view of a pool which is fed by melting ice falling as waterfalls from the surrounding peaks. The fertile canyon contains many fauna and flora, including blue, purple, and red mountain flowers, spiky grass and thistles, several types of fungi including bleeding crown and fly amanita, and many butterflies and blue butterflies during the day, with torchbugs and luna moths appearing in the evening and at night. The pool is full of salmon, and there are mudcrabs nearby. Rabbits, foxes, and elk abound in the canyon.

The path leads down past the shore of the pool, where you'll find a rowboat and two racks, one holding seven whole salmon, and the other drying a net with two food barrels nearby. Next to the rowboat and the salmon rack is a wooden table with an iron war axe on your left. Past this point, the path turns to the north. Ahead, you get your first view of a fort to the northeast. Farther uphill to the north is a snowberry bush and an entrance to Mossy Glen Cave. As you near, the fort is identified as Fort Dawnguard. It appears to be a vast, sprawling structure. The path continues past the start of a wooden palisade wall, with some barrels of food behind. A hammer and a saw are nearby, implying the palisade has been recently worked on. On your right is a wooden lookout platform. The path passes under an arch formed by a walkway far above. Beyond the arch is a campfire, with Durak standing nearby practicing with a crossbow, and a wood chopping block to the left. You can recover many steel bolts from the tree stump Durak has been using for target practice. You can ask Durak what he's shooting with, and he'll give you a crossbow and 45 steel bolts. (See here for a way to be given another crossbow and 45 more steel bolts).

Beyond this area is a fenced vegetable patch containing eight gourd plants, seven cabbage plants, and five potato plants. The path then turns to the south and approaches the entrance to the fort. On your left is another wooden palisade wall, with a gap in the middle. On the other side of the wall, most of the trees have been cut down for the construction. There are two snowberry bushes in this area, and in the northern corner, another entrance to Mossy Glen Cave. To the right of the gap in the wall is another wood chopping block and a woodcutter's axe. The first time you approach the fort, you will meet Agmaer, who has also heard of the vampire hunters and is looking to join. At the entrance, Celann will instruct you to enter to meet Isran, their leader.

There are three balconies on the roof of Fort Dawnguard, accessible from the fort itself. One of them has two crossbows, 21 steel bolts, three Dawnguard war axes, a Dawnguard warhammer, three steel arrows, two bed rolls, a small coin purse, a bottle of ale, an Amulet of Talos, and copies of The Talos Mistake and 2920, Morning Star, v1. Another one has two more crossbows, 14 steel bolts, another Dawnguard war axe, a Dawnguard helmet, a pair of Dawnguard gauntlets, a Scroll of Banish Daedra, two magicka potions, a bed roll, a leather strip, a green apple, a bottle of Honningbrew mead, and a copy of The Lusty Argonian Maid, v1. The third has three more crossbows, 16 steel bolts, three more Dawnguard war axes, another Dawnguard warhammer, a pair of Dawnguard boots, a Dawnguard shield, a grindstone, a tanning rack, three Dawnguard banners around the tower, 13 steel arrows, a bottle of Nord mead, a small coin purse, a silver ring, a bed roll, a magicka potion, small antlers, cooked beef, a bowl of beef stew, and two bottles of Black-Briar mead.

Later changes[edit]

At the end of the quest Bloodline, a small group of vampires attack Fort Dawnguard. Their bodies remain on the ground until after the quest Prophet, when Dayspring Canyon undergoes a change.

The Dawnguard builds two palisade walls along the narrow path leading to the fort, complemented with chevaux de frise. The inner wall has a heavy wooden gate. Tilde and Vori stand sentry on elevated watch posts. Ollrod patrols between the palisade walls. A safe distance from the palisades, two Vampire Scouts scurry back and forth among the bushes near the road. During Beyond Death, four civilian refugees (Alvide, Barknar, Jayri, and Sern) build a small makeshift camp behind the palisade walls. The camp has an Imperial tent, a wood chopping block, and a cooking pot.

Notes[edit]

  • If you use the snow on the east side of the mountain behind the highest balcony, you can potentially use it to walk along the ledge and drop down to the two lower balconies, and scale the mountain to get to the rest of Dayspring Canyon without backtracking through the fort. Don't drop down too far, though, or you'll instantly die once you land.
  • The grindstone and tanning rack are on the roof of Fort Dawnguard, reachable by climbing the west stairway inside and exiting at the top. While these are technically in Dayspring Canyon, as opposed to part of Fort Dawnguard, they can only be accessed via the fort.
  • Using the Clear Skies shout here will clear away fog and rain, but not cloud cover.
  • You can manage to escape the collision box placed around the map from the roof of Fort Dawnguard by hugging the mountain to the west. From there, if you explore the flat world around it, you can eventually find a strange map square, which is between Froki's Shack and the Ruins of Bthalft according to the map. It is modeled and will load its LOD and a rock tower; when approaching, it emits grotto sounds.
  • Only two of the three Skyrim Atmospheres tracks that play in areas like Riverwood and Ivarstead play in Dayspring Canyon; specifically, one of the two tracks that play during the day, and the one track that plays at night.
