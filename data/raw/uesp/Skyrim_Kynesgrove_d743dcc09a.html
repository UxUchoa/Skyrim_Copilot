Kynesgrove

Kynesgrove is a small settlement south of Windhelm.

Steamscorch Mine Camp

Ganna and Gemma Uriel came to Kynesgrove to start a lumber mill without realizing the nearby grove's trees were sacred to Kyne. The settlement contains remnants of the grove they initially cut. They now mine malachite for a living, unable to afford starting a lumber mill elsewhere. They can be heard constantly arguing about the life they left behind in Cyrodiil. Kjeld runs the settlement and will buy malachite ore from you. Iddra runs the Braidwood Inn. Dravynea the Stoneweaver keeps the mine from collapsing and getting too warm with a potion she makes from frost salts.

The settlement consists of Braidwood Inn, Steamscorch Mine (a malachite mine), and a camp containing three tents used by the miners. There is a smelter outside the mine, a grindstone beside the inn, and a stone quarry just east of the inn. Next to the camp is a vegetable patch containing six leeks, four gourds, eight potato plants, and ten cabbages. A wood chopping block and a tanning rack are also found near the vegetable patch. A short distance downhill from the inn is a chicken coop consisting of two chicken nests for the two chickens that wander the settlement. There are a number of dragon's tongue plants around the settlement as well as a nightshade plant and a snowberry bush around the camp. Uphill to the south are some beehives and red mountain flowers among the trees. To the east is a dragon mound where the dragon Sahloknir is resurrected by Alduin during the quest A Blade in the Dark.

Notes[edit]

  • Despite Ganna Uriel mentioning that she and her sister built a lumbermill at Kynesgrove, it is nowhere to be found. The Creation Kit identifies the bank of the river just down the hill from Kynesgrove as "Frostbark Lumber Mill", which suggests the location she describes was cut from the game.
  • Kynesgrove is one of the most common places to encounter dragons, as they often fly overhead.
  • Kynesgrove also appears in ESO.

Bugs[edit]

  • If Klija Wolf-Claw appears at Kynesgrove but you then side with the Legion and kill her during the quest Battle of the Champions CC, this version of her will not be disabled, and she will continue to live at Steamscorch Mine Camp. ?
  • One of the pieces of malachite ore outside the mine may be buried under the ground, making it irretrievable.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
  • There is a broken navmesh in the mine, making the back 1/3 of the mine largely inaccessible to the NPCs when they work there.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
  • After A Blade in the Dark is finished, the town guards may comment on a dragon attack here as though it took place somewhere else.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch, version 4.3.8, fixes this bug.
