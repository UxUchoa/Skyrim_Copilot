Goldenhills Plantation

Goldenhills Plantation is an abandoned farm east of Rorikstead. The farm is initially haunted, but you will be able to claim it as your own once you've put the spirits to rest as part of the quest The Unquiet DeadCC. The farm has various exterior construction options, and you can hire a personal steward to manage the farm's production and buy upgrades for the farmhouse interior. You are also able to set any pet capable of combat (with the exceptions of Meeko and Gogh) to guard the farm. Like the steward, these can be swapped out at any time.

The interior of the farmhouse consists of 5 rooms across three floors, including a two-room basement and a large master bedroom/loft. There's a skill book Song of the Alchemists on the shelf in the basement.

Income[edit]

One farmhand makes 12.5% more money than no farmhand, two farmhands make 25% more money than no farmhand. Bunkhouse makes 15% more money, bunkhouse's benefit is always available even if there's no farmhand. Bunkhouse can stack with farmhands, makes 1.25*1.15=1.4375 times comparing to the base income.
There's definitely a fractional part that doesn't show directly in the game when calculate the income.
Though you cannot collect gold while you have no steward, profits will still accumulate over time despite not having a steward assigned. Simply hire a steward to collect all the money.
The plantation has a total of 38 plots of Fertile Soil (27 in front of the house, 11 on the side of the house). By building the Exterior Decoration you gain an additional 6 plots of Fertile Soil, bringing the total of available plots of Fertile Soil to 44.

Notes[edit]

  • There's a Nord War Horn in Rin's room, which is unobtainable otherwise, a unique Alembic in Jonquil's secret lab, a Broken Iron War Axe Head and a Broken Iron War Axe Handle in the box on the second floor. They will disappear once the rooms are renovated.
  • After completing A Farmer's Life For Me, the steward can be fired and rehired as much as you like. An active steward cannot be asked to follow you (as opposed to Hearthfire stewards), so they must be fired to do so. The same is true with the guard pet.
  • Unlike at your player-built homesHF, you can create butter by placing milk into the butter churn here, and you don't need to wait for it to replenish.
  • Not all containers can be used as safe storage. The two barrels at left side when enter the cellar and the Fish Barrels behind the workbench are all respawning containers.
    • This issue has been addressed by the Unofficial Skyrim Patch; barrels in the house don't respawn anymore.
  • Armored Trolls that are told to guard the farm cannot be taken as followers again. Once dismissed as a guard, you cannot ask it to follow and you will have to buy a new one.
  • You can use console commands to swap the children's room for the library. Select the addition you currently have using prid <RefID>, then type disable to remove it. Then type prid <RefID> to select the addition you want, and type enable to enable it instead. The RefID for the children’s room is FExxx945 and the library is FExxx94A.
  • Changing the shrine in your library via console commands is possible.[verification needed — what's the default?] First, remove your current shrine by typing prid <RefID> then disable, and place the new one by typing prid <RefID> then enable. The RefIDs for the shrines are Akatosh: FExxxD96, Arkay: FExxxD7E, Azura: FExxx8FC, Boethiah: FExxxD94, Dibella: FExxx90C, Julianos: FExxxBFC, Kynareth: FExxxC86, Mara: FExxxC54, Mephala: FExxxBD6, Stendarr: FExxxC51, Talos: FExxxD8F, and Zenithar: FExxxD75.

Bugs[edit]

  • Purchasing the alchemy and enchanting lab and entering the house may crash the game.
    • Purchasing this upgrade last seems to prevent this. If this happens, try to purchase the upgrade, then fast travel away and back before entering the house for the first time.
  • Children and pets may not recognize the farm as a home. However, Hilda (the pet goat) goes there if the player commands it.
  • Children may freeze in place at the spot they are adopted.
  • If children are adopted before Grelod the Kind is killed, your child might get stuck at the orphanage. Even if you tell them "it's time for bed". They still have all of the regular dialogue they would at your home, however they will not leave the orphanage. ?
  • Once the Dawnguard quest line is started random groups of three vampires will attack the farm. Farm animals including horses will protect the farm. ?
  • The cupboard at the bottom of the stairs will be replaced by a Pantry Cupboard later, leaving any stored items in the original cupboard permanently inaccessible. ?
    • To recover the items, use the following console command: "FExxxDB9".removeallitems player.
