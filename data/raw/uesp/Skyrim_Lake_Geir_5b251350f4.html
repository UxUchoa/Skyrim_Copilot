Lake Geir

Lake Geir is a lake in the western Rift.

Ivarstead is located on the shores of Lake Geir. In the center of the lake there is an island which is home to Geirmund's Hall. CatfishCC can be found swimming in the waters here.

Geographically, the lake is fed by the Treva River and is drained by Darkwater River (see Bugs).

Related Quests[edit]

  • Caught in the RainCC: Catch rainy weather fish.
  • Forbidden Legend: Investigate the forgotten Gauldur Legend.
  • The Straw that Broke: Discover what is plaguing Narfi's mind.

Notes[edit]

  • On the eastern shore is a row boat containing two sacks beached opposite a small island with a tree stump. On the island there are two treasure hunters who will attack upon seeing you. They have dug up an unlocked chest containing minor loot. You will find two white cap and a mora tapinella that can be harvested on the island. Further inland is their camp including a small tent, two bedrolls, an open, empty chest, four sacks and three barrels, one of which has on it a bottle of Honningbrew mead, a small coin purse, and a note directing you to the treasure on the island. There are also three rabbits hanging beside the tent that can be harvested. There is also a woodcutter's axe next to where the rabbits are hanging.
  • The name "Lake Geir" is sourced from the Skyrim Prima Game Guide.

Bugs[edit]

  • The water current markers are pointing in the wrong direction, such that the lake appears to be fed by an unseen underground spring at the bottom of The Throat of the World (directly behind Vilemyr Inn) and drains towards Treva River and Lake Honrich in addition to draining into Darkwater River. ?
