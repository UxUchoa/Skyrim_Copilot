The Hall of the Elements is a large training and meeting chamber inside the College of Winterhold.

Besides training spell casting, the chamber is also used for teaching classes, lectures, and meetings.

Related Quests[edit]

  • First Lessons: Join the College of Winterhold.
  • The Eye of Magnus: Reclaim the College and contain the Eye.

Walkthrough[edit]

Inside the hall with the Eye of Magnus at the center

A pair of massive wooden doors serves as the main entrance. These open to the antechamber, which has two doors: one to the northwest that leads to the Arch-Mage's Quarters, and one to the southeast that leads to the Arcanaeum. The hall itself is a large circular room with a high ceiling supported by a ring of pillars. The hall is entered through a pair of large gates which have the College symbol worked into the design. Around the outside edge of the hall is a series of wooden benches, with small light spheres between every two benches. There is a large focus point in the center of the room, with magical energy rising from it up to the ceiling above. During most of the College of Winterhold questline, the Eye of Magnus is kept in the hall, floating above the focus point.

Notes[edit]

  • It is impossible to wait in the Hall of the Elements.
  • Lectures are regularly held here. For details, see here.
  • When the Eye of Magnus appears in the Hall of the Elements, there is an option to "Activate Eye of Magnus" when you point your crosshairs over it in close proximity, but doing so has no effect.
