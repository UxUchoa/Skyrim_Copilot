Baldor Iron-Shaper's House

Baldor Iron-Shaper's House is the home of Baldor Iron-Shaper in Skaal Village.

It is on the south side of the village, between Deor Woodcutter's House to the west and the Shaman's Hut to the east.

Residents[edit]

Residents
Baldor Iron-Shaper

Walkthrough[edit]

Exterior[edit]

The building has two entrances, both on the north side, with one to the east and one to the west. To the east is a complete blacksmithing area, complete with a forge, a grindstone, a smelter, a tanning rack, and a workbench, with an open box on a crate between the tanning rack and workbench containing two pieces of iron ore and a leather strip. Between the two entrances is another tanning rack, a fish barrel, a crate with a snow fox pelt on top, and another workbench. To the right of the west entrance is a snowberry bush. The east entrance is unlocked, and the west entrance has a novice lock.

Baldor Iron-Shaper's House[edit]

The entrance leads into a large room with a large mezzanine above. On your left is a small round table and two chair. On the table is a bottle of wine, an eidar cheese wedge and two loaves of bread. Against the south wall is the flight of stairs to the top floor and under the stairs is a barrel and four sacks. In the southwest corner of the room is a small square table and two chairs, a bottle of wine, a bowl of horker stew and a plate with a sliced eidar cheese are on the table. Above the table is a large hanging rack with three elves ears and three garlic braids hanging from it. There is a wall shelf by the table on the west wall with a shield plaque holding a banded iron shield and two crossed iron greatswords. To the right of this are two wall shelves with a sack on and a wood pile and woodcutter's axe underneath. Beside this is a bucket containing with quivers of steel arrows and then a fireplace. There is a cooking pot in front of the fireplace and a shelf above with a bottle of wine on it. There is a stool to the right of the fire. There is a crate with an long open crate to the right inside the crate are six pieces of iron ore, three pieces of corundum ore and a piece of malachite ore. Above these is a wall shelf containing a blacksmith's potion, a bottle of wine and a bottle of alto wine and beside the crate is a small open crate containing nine iron ingots. The room continues into a small bedroom area along the west wall. In the small bedroom area is a single bed along the northern wall with a pair of boots and a copy of The Lusty Argonian Maid, v2.

To the right of the entrance is a cupboard, on it's shelves are two bottles of Nord mead, a bottle of ale, a horker tusk and goat cheese wheel. To the left is a fish barrel with a cabbage on top and a sack on the floor beside it. Around the corner against the east wall is a pair of weapon racks separated by an anvil. The weapon rack on the right has a long bow and the one on the left has an iron warhammer. Next to this is an open crate with five iron and four steel ingots. Around the corner again is a small round table and chair with a bottle of ale on the table. To the left of this is a second entrance. To the left of this entrance is a dresser with a fox and deer pelt on top and in the northeast corner is a bucket with three pieces of large charcoal. Against the north wall is a second flight of stairs up to the top floor. Under these stairs are two barrels-one of which has four potatoes on, a crate, four sacks and a dresser. There is a chest on top of the dresser. Between this and the small bedroom area is a weapon rack holding a steel battleaxe.

Climbing the stairs by the south wall there is a barrel and sack at the top. Following the floor to the north you pass a narrow table on your right with a copy of Forge, Hammer and Anvil and an eidar cheese wedge. Opposite this is an open crate with two iron ore pieces and three iron daggers. Next to this are two crates with a leather strip and iron sword on top. On the right is the main mezzanine area with a crate with an iron shield and helmet and a leather strip on. To the left of this is an open crate with a leather strip and two more large pieces of charcoal. Against the west wall is a low table with a display case on containing two iron daggers with a chair to the right of it. At the end by the stairs up from downstairs is a cupboard, on the shelves is a blacksmith's apron, a pickaxe, a pair of shoes, a steel ingot and two iron.
