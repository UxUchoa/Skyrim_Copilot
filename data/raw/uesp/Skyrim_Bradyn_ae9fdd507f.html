Bradyn

Bradyn is a dead Dark Elf miner found in Kolbjorn Barrow during the quest Unearthed. He wears miner's clothes, boots, and gloves. He carries a copy of the book Ahzidal's Descent, a pickaxe, and other minor items and gold.

Related Quests[edit]

  • Unearthed: Assist Ralis Sedarys in his search for the relics of Ahzidal in Kolbjorn Barrow.
