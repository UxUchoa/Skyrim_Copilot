Dawnstar

Dawnstar is a city on the northern coast of Skyrim, and the capital of the Pale. It is initially aligned with the Stormcloaks under Jarl Skald. The city is a relatively small fishing and mining settlement with few services. The residents will report having horrible nightmares, which some initially blame on a witch's curse.

For more information on Dawnstar, see the lore article.

Notes[edit]

  • With Hearthfire installed, you can purchase a plot of land in the Pale to build a house on. Also, two stone quarries will be present.
  • Dawnstar Sanctuary is a house granted upon completion of the Dark Brotherhood quest Hail Sithis!
  • Dawnguard adds a permanently disabled map marker for Dawnstar Docks.
  • Quicksilver Mine is just south of Fruki's House, in the northwest corner of town.
  • Iron-Breaker Mine is just east of The White Hall, at the east end of town.
  • A blacksmith's forge and hide tanner are outside Rustleif's house, near the water.
  • A tent is northeast of town, past the hill on which the lighthouse is situated. Inside, someone has set up a lovers' retreat, complete with two unowned bedrolls, a few articles of clothing, wine, flowers, and an amulet of Mara.
  • Dawnstar also appears in Arena and Dawnstar.

Bugs[edit]

  • Ahkari's hidden merchant chest in Dawnstar is accessible due to a bug. See Ahkari's page for details.
