This article is about NPC beggars. For other uses, see Beggar.

There are a number of beggars in Skyrim. The majority of them will ask for a gold piece when approached; giving them one gold will reward you with the blessing The Gift of Charity.

Name Location Gift of Charity
Angrenor Once-Honored Candlehearth Hall (Windhelm) Yes
Beggar (CC) Windhelm docks No
Brenuin The Bannered Mare (Whiterun) Yes
Degaine The Warrens (Markarth) Yes
Dervenin Temple of the Divines (Solitude) Yes
Edda Riften Yes
Lucia (HF) Wind District (Whiterun) Yes
Narfi Ivarstead (The Rift) Yes
Noster Eagle-Eye The Winking Skeever (Solitude) Yes
Silda the Unseen Candlehearth Hall (Windhelm) Yes
Snilf Riften Yes
Svari Addvar's House (Solitude) Yes

Related Quests[edit]

  • Argonian Ale Extraction: Brenuin longs for the taste of Argonian Ale. Can you steal some for him?
  • Blood on the Ice: Windhelm is plagued by a shadowy killer.
  • Dungeon Delving: Do a favor by retrieving an item from a cave. (radiant)
  • The Gray Cowl of NocturnalCC: Investigate the legend of the Gray Fox.
  • Heart of CrimsonCC: Confront the murderous leader of a bandit gang.
  • The Heart of Dibella: Find a new Sybil for the Temple of Dibella.
  • Kill Narfi: Kill a hapless beggar in Ivarstead.
  • The Mind of Madness: Gain access to and solve the mystery of the Pelagius Wing.
  • Quest all Beggars Have: Take pity on a beggar. (radiant)
  • Speak to Degaine: Ask a beggar about money.
  • The Straw that Broke: Discover what is plaguing Narfi's mind.
