Quick Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Receive this quest from Matriarch Drevlan in Ashfall's Tear.
  2. Get the Propaganda Letter from Priest Drureth.
  3. Speak with Aphia Velothi and give her two hours to translate the letter.
  4. Disseminate translated Propaganda Letters to the public.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

A Task for the Goddess[edit]

This quest is offered as part of the Ghosts of the Tribunal quest if you choose to disguise yourself as Hand Kenro Hlan before entering Ashfall's Tear. Doing so will allow you to speak with the normally hostile Matriarch Drevlan. Offer to serve the Goddess Almalexia, and Drevlan will give you this quest along with Buyer Beware and Careless Curation. These quests are all optional and can be completed in any order, although you must do them if you intend to remain aligned with the Tribunal Temple.

For this quest, you will be instructed to speak with Priest Drureth. He can be found in one of the side rooms within the temple in Ashfall's Tear. He will hand you a transcribed Propaganda Letter which will need to be translated from the original Daedric text before it can be distributed.

Information Campaign[edit]

The Raven Rock Temple would be very interested in knowing about this letter

Return to Raven Rock with the untranslated letter and speak with Aphia Velothi. She is a former Temple priestess and will agree to secretly translate the text of the letter. If you have not yet encountered Aphia, she can initially be found just inside the entrance to Raven Rock Mine. It will take her two hours to translate the document.

With the translated letters in hand, you will be presented with two options on how to progress the quest:

  • Give the letters to the Reclamation Priest within the Raven Rock Temple. He will reward you with a leveled amount of gold.
  • Distribute four copies to the general public, You will need to persuade, intimidate or bribe them to read the letter or to show the letter to their patrons. Completing either of these options will complete the quest and will progress your list of tasks for Matriarch Drevlan. When all three tasks are completed, she will give you the quest Ashen Heart.

Reward[edit]

The outcome of this quest depends on whether you chose to distribute the propaganda or alert the New Temple authorities, there're 4 circumstances in total, either no upgraded Ashfall's Tear, level 1 upgraded Ashfall's Tear, level 2, level 3.

If you chose to distribute the material to the public, you will return to Ashfall's Tear to find a number of new occupants. These new converts will have cleaned the temple's living areas, replacing the destroyed furnishings previously found there, all planters are now sprouting plants. Talk to Matriarch Drevlan for gold reward, and she will give you Letter from Matriarch Drevlan, both the letter, the gold rewarded and the upgraded Ashfall's Tear have 3 versions depending on whom you disseminated the translated propaganda letters to.

  • No upgrading and large gold reward, if you give all four translated propaganda letters to the Reclamation Priest for gold reward.
  • Level 1 upgrading and small gold reward, if you give all four letters to non-business people, there will only be one new Ordinator and one new Temple Priestess, and the forge will be still obsolete without Blacksmith.
  • Level 2 upgrading and medium gold reward, if you give at least one letter to business people except Geldis Sadri, there will be 4 new Ordinators and 2 new Temple Priestesses, a blacksmith vendor will appear along with a now-functioning forge and his merchant chest under the floor by the forge.
  • Level 3 upgrading and large gold reward, if you give at least one letter to Geldis Sadri, on top of level 2 upgrading, Caretaker Ineril will appear as a new general goods vendor in the alchemical garden.

If you have ever given at least one of the translated propaganda letters to the Reclamation Priest, after you complete Ashen Heart and return to Ashfall's Tear once again, he will appear in the company of three members of the Redoran Guard and attempt to kill the heretics within, you are free to join in if you so choose, however they four are much weaker than the whole heretic, the Reclamation Priest carries Reclamation Priest's Journal.

Notes[edit]

  • You needn't disseminate all four letters, only one is enough to upgrade Ashfall's Tear, the rest three can be given to Reclamation Priest, however Geldis Sadri is the best target because of the appearance of Caretaker Ineril.

Notes
  • Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., <Alias=LocationHold>) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
  • Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
  • Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
  • If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
  • On the PC, it is possible to use the console to advance through the quest by entering setstage ccASVSSE001_QuestB stage, where stage is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using resetquest ccASVSSE001_QuestB.
