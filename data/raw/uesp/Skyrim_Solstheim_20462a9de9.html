The Dragonborn add-on adds several new places, mostly on the island of Solstheim. For an overview of all the icons used for places on the map, see Maps.

Cities[edit]

 Raven Rock — A city on the southwest coast of Solstheim. (map)
  • Abandoned Building — The home of Bralsa Drel and Rirns Llervu in Raven Rock. (map)
  • Alor House — The home of Fethis Alor and his daughter Dreyla Alor in Raven Rock. (map)
  • The Bulwark — An imposing stone wall created to protect Raven Rock from ash storms, with the city's garrison and jail both built directly into the wall. (map)
  • The Bulwark Jail — The jail in Raven Rock, built directly into the Bulwark. (map)
  • Caerellius House — The home of Crescius Caerellius and Aphia Velothi in Raven Rock. (map)
  • Glover Mallory's House — The home of Glover Mallory in Raven Rock. (map)
  • Ienth Farm — The home of Garyn Ienth and Milore Ienth in Raven Rock. (map)
  • Morvayn Manor — The home of Lleril Morvayn, Adril Arano, and Cindiri Arano in Raven Rock. (map)
  •  Raven Rock Mine — A small ebony mine in northern Raven Rock initially containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the dragon priest Zahkriisos. (map)
  • The Retching Netch — The cornerclub in Raven Rock. (map)
  • Severin Manor — Initially the home of Mirri Severin, Tilisu Severin, and Vendil Severin in Raven Rock, which can be obtained as player housing by completing the quest Served Cold. (map)
  • Temple — A Dunmer place of worship in Raven Rock. (map)
  • Morvayn Ancestral Tomb — A Dunmer ancestral tomb in Raven Rock. (map)
  • Temple Ancestral Tomb — A Dunmer ancestral tomb in Raven Rock.
  • Ulen Ancestral Tomb — A Dunmer ancestral tomb in Raven Rock. (map)

Settlements[edit]

Towns[edit]

 Skaal Village — A village on Solstheim. (map)
  • Baldor Iron-Shaper's House — The home of Baldor Iron-Shaper in Skaal Village. (map)
  • Deor Woodcutter's House — The home of Deor Woodcutter and Yrsa in Skaal Village. (map)
  • Edla's House — The home of Edla and her son Nikulas in Skaal Village. (map)
  • Greathall — The home of chief Fanari Strong-Voice and Tharstan in Skaal Village. (map)
  • Morwen's House — The home of Morwen in Skaal Village. (map)
  • Oslaf's House — The home of Oslaf, Finna, and their daughter Aeta in Skaal Village. (map)
  • Shaman's Hut — The home of shaman Storn Crag-Strider and his daughter Frea in Skaal Village. (map)
  • Wulf Wild-Blood's House — The home of Wulf Wild-Blood in Skaal Village. (map)

Minor Settlements[edit]

 Tel Mithryn — A Telvanni tower settlement east of Fort Frostmoth on Solstheim. (map)
  • Tel Mithryn — The main tower and home of Neloth and his apprentice Talvas Fathryon in Tel Mithryn. (map)
  • Tel Mithryn Apothecary — The home of the apothecary Elynea Mothren in Tel Mithryn. (map)
  • Tel Mithryn Kitchen — The home of the cook Ulves Romoran in Tel Mithryn. (map)
  • Tel Mithryn Steward's House — The home of the steward Varona Nelas or Drovas Relvi in Tel Mithryn. (map)
 Thirsk Mead Hall — A mead hall south-southwest of Skaal Village on Solstheim. (map)

Farms[edit]

 Old Attius Farm — An abandoned Imperial farm south-southeast of Raven Rock. (map)

Shacks[edit]

 Abandoned Lodge — A small shack north-northeast of Raven Rock. (map)
 Hrodulf's House — An abandoned shack southwest of Fort Frostmoth and southeast of Kolbjorn Barrow. (map)
 Ramshackle Trading Post — An old shack south of Kagrumez and west-northwest of Ashfallow Citadel. (map)

All-Maker Stones[edit]

 Beast Stone — An All-Maker Stone west of Thirsk Mead Hall. (map)
 Earth Stone — An All-Maker Stone southwest of Raven Rock. (map)
 Sun Stone — An All-Maker Stone north-northwest of Tel Mithryn. (map)
 Water Stone — An All-Maker Stone north of Raven Rock. (map)
 Wind Stone — An All-Maker Stone west-northwest of Skaal Village. (map)

Camps[edit]

 Bujold's Retreat — A camp east of Thirsk Mead Hall containing Nords exiled from the hall by rieklings. (map)
 Haknir's Shoal — A small pirate camp north-northwest of Skaal Village. (map)
 Last Vigil — A small camp northeast of Riften. (map)

Caves[edit]

 Ashfall's TearCC — A small cave north of Raven Rock containing a hidden Tribunal Temple shrine to the goddess Almalexia. (map)
 Benkongerike — A medium-sized cave southeast of Saering's Watch containing bristlebacks and rieklings. (map)
 Bristleback Cave — A small cave in the northwest of Solstheim island containing bristlebacks and rieklings. (map)
 Castle Karstaag Caverns — A small cave on the north coast of Solstheim containing bristlebacks and rieklings. (map)
 Castle Karstaag Ruins — An icy clearing outside Castle Karstaag Caverns. (map)
 Coldcinder Cave — A small cave southeast of Raven Rock containing ash spawn and netches. (map)
 Frossel — A small cave on a peninsula north of Skaal Village containing bristlebacks and rieklings. (map)
 Frostmoon Crag — A small camp under a rock overhang south of Mount Moesring and northeast of the Abandoned Lodge inhabited by werewolves. (map)
 Glacial Cave — A small cave north of Benkongerike containing rieklings. (map)
 Hrothmund's Barrow — A small cave southwest of Benkongerike. (map)

Clearings[edit]

 Headwaters of Harstrad — The source of the Harstrad River. (map)

Docks[edit]

 Northshore Landing — A small abandoned dock on the northwest coast of Solstheim inhabited by mudcrabs. (map)

Dragon Lairs[edit]

 Saering's Watch — A dragon lair in the shadow of Frykte Peak on Solstheim. (map)

Dwarven Ruins[edit]

 Fahlbtharz — A large Dwarven ruin east of the Water Stone containing rieklings, albino spiders, oil spiders, and Dwarven automatons. (map)
 Kagrumez — A small Dwarven ruin south of the Temple of Miraak. (map)
 Nchardak — A large Dwarven ruin north-northeast of Tel Mithryn. (map)

Landmarks[edit]

 Altar of Thrond — A landmark west of the Temple of Miraak next to a small cave containing hagravens. (map)
 Brodir Grove — A small circle of stones northeast of Raven Rock containing reavers. (map)
 Frykte Peak — One of the highest points on Solstheim, near Saering's Watch. (map)
 Horker Island — A large island east of Skaal Village occupied by horkers. (map)
 Hvitkald Peak‎ — One of the highest points on Solstheim, west of the Abandoned Lodge. (map)
 Mortrag Peak — One of the highest points on Solstheim, south of Castle Karstaag Ruins. (map)
 Mount Moesring — One of the highest points on Solstheim, south of Moesring Pass. (map)
 Stalhrim Source — A small outdoor Nordic ruin east of Broken Tusk Mine and southwest of Mortrag Peak. (map)

Military Forts[edit]

 Ashfallow Citadel — A small Imperial fort east of Highpoint Tower and northwest of the Sun Stone being used as a base of operations by the Morag Tong. (map)
 Fort Frostmoth — A small fort south of Highpoint Tower and northeast of Hrodulf's House occupied by ash spawn and General Falx Carius. (map)
 Highpoint Tower — A small fort east of Raven Rock containing albino spiders, ash spawn, and Neloth's former apprentice Ildari Sarothril. (map)

Mines[edit]

 Broken Tusk Mine — A small heart stone mine north of Raven Rock containing rieklings. (map)
 Damphall Mine — A small iron and silver mine north of Raven Rock containing reavers. (map)
 Raven Rock Mine — A small ebony mine in northern Raven Rock initially containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the dragon priest Zahkriisos. (map)

Nordic Ruins[edit]

 Bloodskal Barrow — A small Nordic ruin north of Raven Rock containing reavers. (map)
 Gyldenhul Barrow — A small Nordic ruin east of Skaal Village containing the spirit and treasure of Haknir Death-Brand. (map)
 Kolbjorn Barrow — A small Nordic ruin southeast of Raven Rock containing draugr, spiders, and the dragon priest Ahzidal. (map)
 Vahlok's Tomb — A small Nordic ruin south-southeast of Thirsk Mead Hall containing corrupted shades, draugr, and the dragon priest Vahlok the Jailor. (map)
 White Ridge Barrow — A medium-sized Nordic ruin in the northern part of Solstheim inhabited by bandits and spiders. (map)

Passes[edit]

 Moesring Pass — A mountain pass due northwest of the Temple of Miraak, inhabited by rieklings guarding the nearby Strange Vessel. (map)

Ruins[edit]

 Snowclad Ruins — A small outdoor Nordic ruin northwest of the Temple of Miraak containing werebears. (map)

Ships and Shipwrecks[edit]

Northern Maiden — A ship initially docked at the Windhelm docks. (map)
 Wreck of the Strident Squall — A shipwreck south-southeast of Raven Rock containing reavers. (map)

Temples[edit]

 Temple of Miraak — A Nordic temple west of Thirsk Mead Hall containing cultists, draugr, and skeletons. (map)

Notes[edit]

  • Upon arrival at the docks of Raven Rock, you will be able to fast travel freely between Skyrim and Solstheim through the use of the To Skyrim and To Solstheim map markers.
  • If you attempt to swim away from Solstheim, an invisible barrier will prevent you from proceeding further, and the message "You cannot go that way." will appear on the top left of the screen.
  • Several of the exploration musical tracks that previously played in Morrowind play on Solstheim. The Skyrim Atmospheres tracks that play in areas like Dawnstar and Winterhold also play here.
  • The clouds on the main Skyrim map seem to move east, whereas the ones on the Solstheim map seem to move west.
  • The "Show On Map" function in your journal for quests that take place on Solstheim will only work while you are on the island.
  • If you bring SeranaDG here, she may comment on the island's lack of vegetation.
  • Arngeir won't help you learn any Words of Power here.

Bugs[edit]

  • The combat taunt "You won't leave Skyrim alive!" may also play if the battle takes place on Solstheim.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
  • Some merchants on Solstheim can incorrectly prompt you with dialogue that's used by Fences. There are none on the island, unless you invest in Glover Mallory with the Fence perk active.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.

Achievements[edit]

Achievements related to Solstheim places include:

  • Outlander (20 points/Bronze) — Arrive on Solstheim
  • Solstheim Explorer (20 points/Bronze) — Discover 30 locations on the island of Solstheim
