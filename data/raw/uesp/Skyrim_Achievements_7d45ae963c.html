Achievements (specifically Xbox 360 Achievements, Steam Achievements, and PlayStation 3 Trophies) are awards given to the player for accomplishing various milestones in the game. Fifty achievements and fifty-one trophies are available in the basic game. Hearthfire adds five achievements and Dawnguard and Dragonborn add ten achievements each.

On the Xbox 360, a total of one thousand Gamer Points are available in the base game, or 1,550 points with all add-ons installed; the "Points" column in the following table provides the Gamer Points associated with each specific achievement. On the PC, Steam achievements stand on their own and don't include points. In addition, use of console commands and mods on the PC version does not prevent the awarding of achievements. On the PS3, the Trophies are sorted into types: Bronze, Silver and Gold. Earning every trophy from the base game will unlock a Platinum trophy.

The Special Edition has a separate achievement list for Steam, Xbox One, and PS4, meaning they can be earned twice by playing both versions of the game. Note that the use of mods (including Verified Creations) disables Special Edition achievement progress for both PC and console. Since the Special Edition comes with all three major add-ons, the achievement list contains all of the below add-on-related achievements by default (i.e. 75 achievements or 76 trophies). The use of Creation Club Creations does not disable achievements as they are official content, however the Creation Club content does not add any additional achievements.

Similar to the Special Edition, Skyrim VR also has a separate achievement list for Steam and PS4. The use of mods disables achievement progress on PC.

More detailed information on how to unlock each achievement can be obtained by clicking on the achievement's name.

Missable Achievements[edit]

Most of the achievements can be obtained regardless of your previous game choices. However, a handful of achievements can be missed:

  • Oblivion Walker (30 pts/Silver) — Collect 15 Daedric Artifacts
    • There are a total of sixteen available Daedric Artifacts that qualify for this achievement, five of which can be missed depending upon your choices during each associated Daedric Quest. See Oblivion Walker for more details.
  • One with the Shadows (30 pts/Silver) — Returned the Thieves Guild to its former glory
    • Can be missed if Erikur dies before completing The Dainty Sload. After starting Bound Until Death, he is no longer essential and can be killed.
  • With Friends Like These... (10 pts/Bronze) — Join the Dark Brotherhood
  • Bound Until Death (10 pts/Bronze) — Complete Bound Until Death
  • Hail Sithis! (30 pts/Silver) — Complete Hail Sithis!
    • All three Dark Brotherhood achievements become unavailable if you choose to Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!
  • War Hero (10 pts/Bronze) — Capture Fort Sungard or Fort Greenwall
    • The associated quest (The Battle for Fort Sungard or The Battle for Fort Greenwall) can be skipped depending upon the outcome of Season Unending.
  • Auriel's Bow (20 pts/Bronze) — Use the special power of Auriel's Bow
    • Auriel's Bow is not marked as a quest item after completing Kindred Judgement, so this achievement can be missed if the bow is lost or despawns.
  • Werewolf Mastered (20 pts/Bronze) — Acquire 11 werewolf perks
    • After curing lycanthropy twice (either using a Glenmoril Witch Head or by becoming a Vampire Lord), the player will never be able to become a werewolf or acquire werewolf perks again.
  • Stalhrim Crafter (20 pts/Silver) — Craft an item out of Stalhrim
    • The associated quest can be missed if Deor Woodcutter or Fanari Strong-Voice dies before it starts.

Notes[edit]

  • Achievements are absent from the Nintendo Switch version of the game.

Bugs[edit]

  • Achievements can occasionally be missed by Steam and PS4, even though they have been completed in-game.
    • To fix this, reload an earlier save and re-complete the achievement.
    • Making sure that the "Enable the Steam Overlay while in-game" is enabled (from the Steam client's properties) before starting the game prevents this issue.
