Quick Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Enter the Ratway and follow the Ghostly Apparition.
  2. Investigate the Vault.
  3. Defeat The Guardian and see if it has affected the shield.
  4. Head to the north chamber and defeat the Guardian again.
  5. Head to the east chamber and defeat the Guardian again.
  6. Return to the sword chamber.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

As soon as you install the Dawnfang and Duskfang Creation, head to Riften. Townsfolk are talking about a ghost hiding in the Ratway and guards have warned them not to approach the area. As you begin your search, a ghostly apparition appears to guide you throughout the Ratway to the Guardian Vault. She then disappears in front of the Guardian Vault, leaving you to your investigation.

In the chamber, you find a long dead skeleton and a sword. Upon inspecting the latter, the Vault Guardian tries to stop you and you have to defeat him. His death seems to have weakened the shield protecting the sword, and a door opens to the north. Head there to find once more the Vault Guardian and weaken the shield. Before leaving the northern room, be sure to open the cages to the left of the upper platform. Behind the gate there sits the skeleton of Verrick Tilwald, which will reanimate and attack you as you enter. Once the skeleton is dispatched, take Verrick's Note, which is pinned beneath a dagger on the table next to Of Crossed Daggers. Reading this will initiate the Bloodthirst quest. Return to the sword chamber and head to the east, defeat the Vault Guardian for the last time.

The Guardian and the Ghostly Apparition, in life Lorant and Emma Bouchard, will be reunited and you'll be free to take Dawnfang and Duskfang.

Notes[edit]

  • For historical information on Dawnfang & Duskfang, see the lore article.
  • It is possible to yield to Lorant's final form once he appears non-magical, at which time he simply stands in place peaceably with arms outstretched, effectively pausing the battle.

Notes
  • Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., <Alias=LocationHold>) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
  • Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
  • Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
  • If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
  • On the PC, it is possible to use the console to advance through the quest by entering setstage ccBGSSSE013_AquisitionQuest stage, where stage is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using resetquest ccBGSSSE013_AquisitionQuest.
