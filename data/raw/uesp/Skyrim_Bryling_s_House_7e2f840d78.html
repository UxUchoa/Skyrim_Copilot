Bryling's House

Bryling's House is a small house in Solitude, the home of Bryling, a Thane of Solitude.

It is located just outside the Blue Palace on the left-hand side, next door to the Bards College. Bryling spends most of her day in the palace, and her house contains lots of valuable items, making it an easy target if you are asked to steal from Solitude for The Bedlam Job.

Residents[edit]

Residents
Bryling
Irnskar Ironhand

Related Quests[edit]

  • This location is one of many potential targets for one or more of the Radiant quests.

Walkthrough[edit]

Exterior[edit]

There are a flight of stairs up to the main entrance. The front door is apprentice-locked most of the time. There is a second entrance at the side of the house via a small garden which has various harvestable plants growing in it. There are also a couple of hanging mosses growing from the walls by the stairs and around to the side of the building where there are quite a few food barrels stacked up. An accessible horizontal ledge outside contains a potion of minor healing.

Bryling's House[edit]

First Floor[edit]

First floor main room

There are a number of food sacks and barrels throughout the house and the crockery is of the finest quality including plates, bowls, goblets etc. Entering through the main entrance, the room has a narrow wooden table against the southwest wall with a platter holding two sweet rolls and two honey nut treats and a plate of green apples on top and a low table and chair in front. On the table is a plate of bread and cheese. Along the northwest wall is an open crate containing bunch of tundra cotton and a red mountain flower. To the right is a small wardrobe containing fine clothes and with a copy of Uncommon Taste on top. In the northern corner is a round wooden table with a candlestick on top. Opposite against the southeast wall is a cupboard containing clutter with a vase with a snowberry bush to the left.

To the northeast is an opening that leads into a kitchen and dining area with a small reading area to the left, where there are a pair of bookcases against the southwest wall holding eleven common books, a potion of extreme stamina and a solution of strength. There is a second vase containing a snowberry bush against the northwest wall and against the northeast wall is a set of shelves holding a common book, a bunch of tundra cotton, draught of strength, a potion of health and a potion of regeneration. In the middle of this area is a low table and chair, with three common books and a bottle of wine on the table. This part of the room is separated from the kitchen an dining area by a pair of sets of shelves and an open crate, which contains a bunch of lavender, a bunch of juniper berries, a potion of plentiful magicka, a potion of minor healing and two empty lesser soul gems. On the shelves are a weak poison, a weak stamina poison, two common books, a potion of vigorous stamina, a potion of ultimate magicka and an empty lesser soul gem, with an empty petty soul gem on a stand beside the shelves.

In the kitchen and dining area is a long wooden table set for two with two seared slaughterfish, a portion of grilled leeks, a potion of regeneration and a bunch of tundra cotton with a round wooden table beside the table is small round table with a loaf of bread and a couple of cheese wheels on top. At the other end of the table is a small unit holding a bottle of alto wine. Against the northeast wall is a bookcase holding six common books, a potion of health, a flute and a drum. To the right in the eastern corner is a small set of shelves holding a few goblets and having an open crate in front of it containing a potion of regeneration, two red apples and three potatoes. Against the southeast wall is a small cupboard containing more clutter, while to the right is a fireplace with a cooking pot in front on a stand, a piece of raw beef underneath and a cabbage in a cast iron pot on the hearth. On the mantelpiece is a bottle of frostbite venom.

To the southwest is an alcove with a small cupboard with an open crate on top containing two leeks and five tomatoes against the southeast wall. Against the southwest wall is a set of shelves holding a common book, a bunch of tundra cotton, a draught of strength, a potion of health, a potion of regeneration and a basket containing two bunches of lavender. Against the northwest wall is a narrow table with a bowl of bread and a plate holding a sliced eidar cheese and an iron dagger. To the northeast there is a stairwell with a flight of stairs leading up and down.

Second Floor[edit]

Main bedroom

At the top of the stairs is another large vase containing a snowberry bush. There is a door opposite the stairs on the southwest wall, with two single owned beds coming out from the southwest wall with end tables on the outside of both and an open crate at the foot of the left-hand bed containing a bunch of tundra cotton and a red mountain flower. Against the southwest wall is a small cupboard with a thistle branch on top. In front of the cupboard is a low table and chair with a plate of bread and cheese and a common book on top. Against the northeast wall is a narrow table with a bowl of bread, a plate holding a sliced eidar cheese and an iron dagger on top, with a wall shelf above it holding another thistle branch, while against the northwest wall is a wooden bench with a round wooden table to the left of it and two common books on top and a third underneath.

The hallway continues to the southeast where there is an alcove just past another door to the northeast. In the alcove is another low table and chair with a plate of bread and cheese and a common book on top. Against the northeast wall is a narrow wooden table with a wall shelf above, while against the southeast wall is a small cupboard holding a potion of health, a flute and a drum on top. Against the southwest wall are a pair of wall shelves holding a bunch of lavender and a potion of plentiful healing.

The bedroom to the northeast has a large owned double bed coming out from the northeast wall, with an end table on either side and a long unlocked respawning chest at the foot of the bed containing random items. In the northern corner is a small wardrobe containing fine clothes, while in the eastern corner is a small round table with a pot on top and an unlocked wooden chest containing gold beside it against the southeast wall. Against the southwest wall is a narrow wooden table holding a bowl of bread and a plate holding a sliced eidar cheese and an iron dagger. Behind the door to the northwest is a small wardrobe containing fine clothes and with a basket to the right of it containing a small coin purse and a wash basin on a stand to the right of that.

Cellar[edit]

Cellar

Descending the stairs leads to a storage area with a cupboard on the right at the bottom of the stairs holding a few empty wine bottle. To the northwest is a large stack of food barrels with more behind a large vat. In front of this against the southwest wall is a cupboard holding two loaves of bread and several cheese wheels. There are more sacks of foodstuffs to the left and a set of shelves holding several mead barrels. To the left of this is a rack hanging from the wall with a garlic braid, two bunches of elves ear and a bunch of frost mirriam all hanging from it. In the southern corner is the back entrance to the house. There is a small square wooden table in the middle of the room with a common book on top, while against the northeast wall is a cupboard holding an iron mace, a respawning knapsack containing leveled items, and a woodcutter's axe. To the right is a second cupboard holding a bunch of tundra cotton, a gourd, three garlic bulbs, a set of fine clothes and a pair of boots. To the right against the southeast wall is another cupboard. Above these cupboards are three garlic braids hanging from the ceiling.

Notes[edit]

  • Bryling's House appears as the The Antiquarian Circle's headquarters in ESO.
  • The key to the house is carried by Bryling and Irnskar Ironhand.
  • There is a Loot shadowmark on the top of the stairs wall.
  • With the exception of most barrels, sacks, and other respawning items mentioned above, all containers in the house are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
