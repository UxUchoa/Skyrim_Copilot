This overview article describes places of interest in Skyrim that are not marked on your world map upon discovery, are not represented by icons on your compass, thus cannot be fast traveled to, and in most cases are not directly related to any quests. Some of the places on this list do not have official names or titles, in which case unofficial names have been used to describe the places.

Each location listed in the table here is discussed in detail, with images, on the unmarked locations pages for the regions linked in the menu above.

This sortable table features ALL unmarked locations in the game. Only exterior, unmarked shrines are included, not those inside locations or at locations with map markers. The secondary location label is as listed in the Prima Guide, for example 8.D is A Peddler's Misfortune. Location types include Hostile (bandit/forsworn/hagraven/necromancer/etc), Creature, Camp (hunter/fisher/empty/etc), Caravan, Boat (afloat/wrecked), Shrine, House, Dragon Mound, Jail, Ruin/monument, just Loot, or Other place of interest.

Bandit Camps and Bandit Ambushes[edit]

  • FALKREATH Bandit Camp: Ilinalta Foothills ----- On the road between Helgen and Riverwood, just south of the Guardian Stones and east of a Shrine of Talos, there is a camp inhabited by three leveled bandits. It contains a chest, Treasure Map I, and a copy of the One-handed skill book Night Falls on Sentinel. (map)
  • THE PALE 2 mixed up (Dawnstar Frost Troll Den / Shoreline Bandit Camp) ----- Between Dawnstar and Pale Imperial Camp, there is a camp inhabited by three leveled bandits. It has a chest, food barrels and a copy of the Block skill book Death Blow of Abernanit. (map)
  • FALKREATH Khajiit Caravan Massacre ----- On the road between Helgen and Haemar's Shame is a wrecked Khajiit caravan, complete with two carts, seven dead Khajiit and two dead horses. In the furthest cart are some sacks and a chest, as well as a copy of the Speech skill book, The Buying Game. When you approach from Helgen, multiple bandits will ambush you, usually consisting of one or two melee fighters and two archers. If you head up the road over the hill and then turn around and come back, another three-four bandits will spawn around the wreckage and attack. Note that this ambush only spawns if you head directly there after exiting Helgen. If you went through Helgen Keep with Hadvar, you can go to Riverwood, recruit Faendal, and have him help you battle the bandits and collect the skill book for you, but only if you do not enter Alvor's house. If you went through Helgen Keep with Ralof, the bandits and caravan will disappear as soon as you enter Riverwood or another village or city. If you've already spawned the ambush previously, by the time the nearby Stormcloak camp spawns (which happens only after visiting a major city), the caravan remains will have been cleared up and all the bandits will respawn, sitting around the stump just off the road to the right. They will be non-hostile unless spoken to, or outright attacked. x=37220&y=-79480 When the Falkreath Stormcloak Camp is enabled for the first time it permanently disables an unmarked place of a Khajiit Caravan Massacre south-southwest with a skill book The Buying Game.

Caravan Remains and Shipwrecks[edit]

  • HJAALMARCH Ambushed Caravan ----- On the side of the road southeast of Dragon Bridge is a caravan loaded with crates, rugs, and a knapsack. Next to it are the corpses of a horse, a Nord man, and a Redguard woman, the latter carrying the Merchant's Journal. Falmer arrows stuck in the horse, other Falmer arrows around the location, and a Falmer ear suggest a Falmer attack, possibly related to the nearby Chillwind Depths. (map)
  • FALKREATH The Silvermoon: Lake Ilinalta ----- South of The Lady Stone is a shipwreck beneath the waters of Lake Ilinalta. The shipwreck contains a chest and a leveled weapon. (map)
  • WINTERHOLD ----- Northeast of the Wreck of the Winter War are the sparse remains of ship. Among the oars and wood are three barrels. A short distance to the north is a birds' nest with a pine thrush egg. (map)
  • FALKREATH Burning Caravan: Evergreen Grove ----- East of Knifepoint Ridge and west of the Evergreen Woods dragon mound, there are remnants of a caravan loaded with crates, rugs, and a chest. Next to it are the corpses of a horse and two burned humanoids. (map)

Creature Lairs[edit]

  • FALKREATH ----- Southeast of Evergreen Grove is a bugling elk near a stony Nordic bust. A hostile animal often wanders into the area. (map)
  • THE PALE Barnacle Boat ----- On the coast between Dawnstar and the Wreck Of The Brinehammer, there is an upturned skiff inhabited by two mudcrabs and containing a skeleton hand, a knapsack, a scimitar, two pieces of mudcrab chitin, and a copy of the Alteration skill book Daughter of the Niben. (map)
  • FALKREATH Bear Cave: Halldir's Cairn ----- One or two bears lair WNW of Halldir's Cairn under a rocky overhang. The corpses of two hunters and an elk are found on the site, and beehives can be found in the trees nearby.
  • FALKREATH Sunken Barrow: Lake Ilinalta ----- Underwater in the east end of Lake Ilinalta, southwest of Anise's Cabin, there is a sunken ruin where two slaughterfish reside. The ruins also contain a leveled mace, two soul gems in a stone bowl, and an unlocked chest under the stairs.

Houses[edit]

  • HJAALMARCH ----- Northeast of Ustengrav and north of Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp, there is a ruined, burning shack. Fire continuously burns the previous occupant and remaining furniture. Before dragons are activated, this shack is home to a single bandit. (map)
  • THE RIFT Burning Farmhouse ----- In a valley south of Forelhost there is another burning shack where fire continuously burns the previous occupant and remaining furniture. A scroll of Conjure Flame Atronach can be found here, indicating a possible cause of the fire. Some potatoes and leeks may be harvested from the untouched garden. (map)
  • THE PALE ----- With Fishing installed, there is a hut on the coast west of Dawnstar which contains a bed, various furniture, fishing supplies, and other supplies. (map)

Hunter's Camps[edit]

  • FALKREATH Hunter's Camp: The Guardian Stones ----- There is a downhill path west of the Guardian Stones which leads to a fisherman's camp. With Fishing installed, this site becomes a fishing spot. (map)
  • FALKREATH Fisherman's Island: Lake Ilinalta ----- On a small island in the eastern part of Lake Ilinalta, there is another fisherman's camp. (map)
  • FALKREATH Fisherman's Camp: Lake Ilinalta ----- On the northwestern part of Lake Ilinalta, there is another camp belonging to a fisherman. This camp contains a copy of the One-handed skill book Fire and Darkness. (map)
  • FALKREATH Hunter's Camp: Sunderstone Gorge ----- West of Sunderstone Gorge lies a hunter's camp. It consists of a chest, a sack, a small tent with a bedroll, and a hunter's bow with 12 iron arrows. One hunter lives here. (map)
  • THE RIFT Hunters' Camp: Autumnshade Hills ----- Southeast of Autumnshade Clearing is a camp occupied by a pair of hunters. One of their tents contains a copy of the Archery skill book The Gold Ribbon of Merit. (map)
  • WINTERHOLD Hunter's Overlook: Fellhammer Wastes ----- Due south of The White Hall in Dawnstar and southeast of the Shrine of Dibella is a small camp occupied by two hunters. (map)
  • HJAALMARCH Single Hunter Camp ----- 290 ft N of North Cold Rock Pass, is an unmarked hunter encampment, occupied by a single hunter. Under the tent is a copy of the Archery skill book The Gold Ribbon of Merit. (map)

Warlock Altars[edit]

  • FALKREATH Sacrificial Altar: Evergreen Woods ----- Halfway between Cracked Tusk Keep and Evergreen Grove is a Nordic altar occupied by a necromancer. The altar holds two poisons and a copy of the Conjuration skill book 2920, Frostfall, v10.
  • FALKREATH The Conjurer's Altar: Lake Ilinalta ----- North of Lakeview Manor, there is a Nordic altar occupied by a necromancer. This site contains embalming tools, a sword, a burial urn, and the Conjuration skill book 2920, Hearth Fire, v9. (map)

Smaller Places of Interest[edit]

  • FALKREATH ----- South of South Brittleshin Pass is an empty dock. With the Fishing creation, some fishing supplies can be found here. Fish from Temperate Lakes can be caught here. (map)
  • THE RIFT ----- Northwest of Fallowstone Cave, partway up the mountain slope, there is an unmarked clearing with a silver ore vein and an orichalcum ore vein. A copy of the Illusion skill book Incident at Necrom is well-hidden at the base of one of the trees on that slope. (map)
  • FALKREATH ----- West-southwest of Knifepoint Ridge, if you climb the ridge to the top, you'll find a skeleton with a blood-spattered knapsack and leveled bow lying next to it. (map)
  • THE REACH ----- Between Reachwater Rock and Kolskeggr Mine there's an abandoned rowboat at a bend in the river. In the boat are some bottles, an apothecary's satchel, an adept-locked strongbox, and one each of the following gems: an emerald, a garnet, a flawless garnet, a ruby, a flawless ruby, and a flawless diamond. In the river next to the boat is a chest and two mead barrels. (map)
  • THE REACH Brush Strongbox: Riverside ----- Southeast of Karthwasten, at a point roughly between Four Skull Lookout and Sundered Towers, there is a locked strongbox with a leveled dagger leaned up against it hidden in the center of a pile of driftwood. (map)
A tattered flag and an unfortunate soul
  • THE REACH The Bloodied Bandit ----- In the mountains almost due east of Ragnvald, south of Bthardamz, a dead Orc bandit can be found draped over a novice-locked chest containing minor loot. A random one-handed weapon is beside it, and another one is carried by the Orc. Two dead wolves next to him hint at his cause of death. (map)
  • FALKREATH / THE RIFT ----- Between Haemar's Shame and Alchemist's Shack, off the mountain pass between Helgen and the western lands of the Rift, a pile of stones marks a steep, narrow path heading east from the road up through a small notch in the rock walls of the pass. A quicksilver ore deposit and a copy of the Alteration skill book Daughter of the Niben can be found at the end of this path. (map)
  • FALKREATH ----- West-northwest of Haemar's Shame and east of Falkreath Stormcloak Camp is an overhanging rock face. Underneath lies a skeleton with a coin purse, along with a few animal bones. There are two snowberry bushes here as well. (map)
  • THE REACH ----- East of Bruca's Leap Redoubt and southwest of Dragon Bridge Overlook at the bottom of the middle waterfall is an apprentice-locked dungeon boss chest. It's near a fallen tree crossing the river. (map)
  • THE RIFT ----- Southeast of Angarvunde and northwest of Avanchnzel is a lean-to sheltering an apprentice-locked strongbox, two lockpicks, and two leveled potions (healing and invisibility). Two canis roots grow next to it. (map)
  • HJAALMARCH ----- Northwest of Orotheim on the river shore in a tiny alcove is a lootable skeleton next to a novice-locked common chest. (map)
  • FALKREATH ----- Southwest, next to Orphan Rock is a lootable skeleton, helm, and coin purse in a hollow fallen tree. Mora tapinella grows on the tree. (map)
  • WINTERHOLD ----- On an island between the Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos and Bleakcoast Cave is a helmet inside the trunk of a hollow fallen tree along with the Heavy Armor skill book Chimarvamidium. (map)
  • THE RIFT ----- Northeast of Ivarstead and southwest of Darkwater Crossing, north of Nilheim, on the southern shore of the river is a dungeon chest. That section of the river has a keyhole shape and is at the base of a waterfall. Skill book Aevar Stone-Singer is almost due north of and very close to this location (as described above). (map)
  • FALKREATH ----- Southeast from South Skybound Watch, at the base of a large tree surrounded by boulders and rocks is a skull with a red mountain flower in its mouth, perched atop an apothecary's satchel.
