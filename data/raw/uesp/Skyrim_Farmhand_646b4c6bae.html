Farmhand

Farmhands are generic workers that you can ask your Personal Steward to hire for Goldenhills Plantation. Once hired, two of them will work the farm and dwell in the Goldenhills Farm Bunkhouse. If the bunkhouse hasn't been constructed yet, they will instead set up bedrolls and camp outside.

You can also personally hire Angrenor Once-Honored, Brenuin, Embry, or Narfi instead of the two generic farmhands.
