  • Find 20 deathbell for Ingun Black-Briar

  • Deliver Mead to Wilhelm
  • Show Mead to Indaryn

  • Obtain Ore Sample from Filnjar
  • Return Ore Sample to Hafjorg

  • Locate a clue about Brand-Shei's past
  • Return to Brand-Shei

  • Obtain a Mark of Dibella from Bolli
  • Obtain a Mark of Dibella from Hofgrir
  • Obtain a Mark of Dibella from Indaryn
  • Confront Haelga with the Marks of Dibella
  • Speak to Svana
Haelga's Bunkhouse Wujeeta Helping Hand: Help Wujeeta cure her skooma addiction.
  • Give Wujeeta a healing potion
The Raid: Help the Jarl of Riften to uncover and stop the illegal skooma trade in town.
  • Identify Riften's skooma dealer
  • Report Sarthis Idren to the Jarl of Riften
  • Enter Riften Warehouse
  • Eliminate Sarthis Idren
  • Recover skooma source information
 Helping Hand
Hall of the Dead Alessandra Pilgrimage: Help Alessandra make peace with her dead father.
  • Bring Alessandra's Dagger to Andurs in Whiterun
  • Return to Alessandra
Honorhall Orphanage Constance Michel Consider Adoption: Speak with Constance Michel about Adoption (HF) This quest does not count towards Thane of the Rift.
  • Speak with Constance Michel about Adoption

Marise Aravel's House Marise Aravel Ice Cold: Find five ice wraith teeth for Marise Aravel.
  • Find 5 Ice Wraith Teeth for Marise
  • Return to Marise
Merryfair Farm Synda Llanith Gather Wheat: Cabbage, Gourd, Wheat.
Mistveil Keep Harrald Bring It!: Deliver Harrald's sword.
  • Pick up Harrald's Sword from Balimund
  • Deliver Harrald's Sword
Mistveil Keep Laila Law-Giver Supply and Demand: Clear Cragslane Cavern for the Jarl of Riften.
  • Report skooma source to Jarl of Riften
  • Find the skooma dealers in Cragslane Cavern
  • Disrupt the skooma operation
  • Report to the Jarl of Riften

I Done Got Thaned!: Help out the Jarl and citizens of the Rift, and be named Thane for your service. (radiant)

Mistveil Keep Wylandriah Hunt and Gather: Retrieve Wylandriah's spoon, ingot, and soul gem.
  • Retrieve Wylandriah's Spoon
  • Retrieve Wylandriah's Ingot
  • Retrieve Wylandriah's Soul Gem
  • Return to Wylandriah
Riften Shadr Stabilized: Help Shadr clear his debt with Sapphire.
  • Speak to Sapphire about Shadr's debt
  • Return to Shadr
Riften Jail Sibbi Black-Briar The Lover's Requital: Help Sibbi Black-Briar locate his former fiancée, so he can kill her.
  • Discover Svidi's whereabouts
  • Tell Sibbi Black-Briar about Lynly
  • Lie to Sibbi Black-Briar about Lynly

  • Defeat Hofgrir in an unarmed brawl
Snow-Shod Farm Addvild Gather Wheat: Leek, Potato, Wheat.
Temple of Mara Dinya Balu Spread the Love: Distribute religious literature to the citizens of Riften.
  • Distribute the Warmth of Mara within Riften
  • Return to Dinya
 The Book of Love
Temple of Mara Maramal The Bonds of Matrimony: Find someone in Skyrim to marry. This quest does not count towards Thane of the Rift.
  • Speak to Maramal about arranging your wedding
  • Attend your wedding ceremony
The Bee and Barb Talen-Jei Sealing the Deal: Find three flawless amethysts for Talen-Jei's engagement ring.
  • Get 3 flawless amethysts for Talen-Jei
  • Return to Talen-Jei
The Ragged Flagon Galathil (DG) SurgeryDG: Change your appearance. (DG) This quest does not count towards Thane of the Rift.
  • Locate the face sculptor in Riften
  • Talk to the face sculptor in the Ragged Flagon
