Quick Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Speak to Geldis Sadri in the Retching Netch.
  2. Distribute ten jars of Sadri's Sujamma to citizens of Raven Rock.
  3. Return to Sadri at the Retching Netch to receive your reward.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

A New Mixture[edit]

In the center of Raven Rock is the only inn in town, the Retching Netch. The proprietor is Geldis Sadri and he can be found on his spot behind the bar around the clock. Talk to him and ask about the specialty of the house and he will proudly answer, "The finest sujamma to cross your lips, friend. My own personal formula that I call "Sadri's Sujamma." In fact, I've just added some new ingredients to the mixture that I think makes it taste better than ever. All I need to do is get the word out that it's ready and they'll flock here for it." Offer to help him spread the word about the new mixture and he will hand you ten samples of Sadri's Sujamma. You are now supposed to find ten thirsty citizens that will try out the new drink in town.

Reward[edit]

Once you have distributed all ten samples of the sujamma, return to Geldis Sadri and tell him the good news. He will be happy about the publicity and say, "Perfect! All that's left now is to go ahead and wait for the patrons to come in. As promised, here's a little something for yourself." He will then hand you a leveled amount of gold as reward, finishing the quest.

Levels Reward
1-29 250
30-39 500
40-49 1000
50-59 1500
60+ 2000

Notes[edit]

  • If you wait 24 in-game hours after an NPC accepts a sample of the sujamma, you can give them another sample which adds to your total count.
  • There are 13 possible people who will accept sujamma. Two of them are from the Severin family, whom you may have to kill as part of a quest; Drovas will be unavailable after Reluctant Steward; and Bralsa Drel will be unavailable until you complete another quest, but there will still always be the other 10 possibilities unless one or more of them are killed by other means.
  • Unlike other quest items, Sadri's Sujamma is disposable. Make sure to keep them in your inventory while moving your belongings to chests.
  • If you drop all of your bottles of Sadri's Sujamma before giving them to 10 people, you can ask him for more, and he will give you 2 more bottles. You can do this as many times as you like until you give 10 people the sujamma.
