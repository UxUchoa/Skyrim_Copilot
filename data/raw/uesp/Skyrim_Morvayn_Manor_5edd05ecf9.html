Morvayn Manor

Morvayn Manor is the home of Lleril Morvayn, Adril Arano, and Cindiri Arano in Raven Rock.

It is on the waterfront between Caerellius House to the southeast and Glover Mallory's House to the northwest, opposite the entrance to the Morvayn Ancestral Tomb.

Walkthrough[edit]

Exterior[edit]

To the left of the entrance are five scathecraw plants, a food urn, two unusable barrels, and an empty cart. To the right of the entrance is a trama root, three more scathecraw plants, and five unusable barrels. Along the sides and back of the building on the waterfront are two ash yam urns, three unusable barrels, and three crates. A Redoran guard is posted outside the entrance, which is unlocked.

Morvayn Manor[edit]

Morvayn Manor throne room

The entrance leads into the main room, where First Councilor Lleril Morvayn holds court on his throne, protected by a Redoran guard posted next to the entrance. Along the west wall opposite the entrance are three chairs and a table holding a bottle each of flin, matze, shein, sujamma, and wine, three bowls of horker and ash yam stew, two ash yams, an eidar cheese wedge, a goat cheese wedge, a loaf of bread, an apple pie, and a piece of cooked boar meat. To the south of the table are two benches along the east and west walls. Along the curved south wall is a chair often occupied by Second Councilor Adril Arano, the throne, and a sideboard holding a bottle each of matze, shein, and flin, a sample of Felsaad tern feathers, and copies of Bone, Part I, Bone, Part II, and The True Noble's Code. The wall ledge holds the following items, starting from behind the throne along the south wall and ending at the doorway to the next room along the north wall: bottles of sujamma, matze, shein, sujamma, flin, and shein, copies of all three volumes of History of Raven Rock, both volumes of The Red Year, The Poison Song, Book I, and The Poison Song, Book II, two bottles of wine, a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of sujamma, and copies of The House of Troubles and Words of the Wind.

The next room is circular, and features the stairs leading up to the upper floor. Along the curved west wall is an arcane enchanter, a wood pile, a cooking pot on a stand over a fire, a braid of garlic and a dead rabbit hanging from a rack, and a food urn. Along the north wall beneath the stairs is a set of shelves holding three loaves of bread, a bottle each of wine, alto wine, matze, shein, and flin, two cabbages, four ash yams, an eidar cheese wheel, an eidar cheese wedge, two goat cheese wheels, and a sliced goat cheese. Above the set of shelves are two dried frost mirriam, two dried elves ears, and two garlic braids hanging from the ceiling, and to the right of the set of shelves is a sack, two food urns, an ingredient urn, an ash yam urn, and a crate.

The small circular room on the upper floor contains three food urns, an ash yam urn, a wood pile, three crates, one of which has a sample of Felsaad tern feathers on top, two sacks, an unusable barrel, and a straw bale. An adept-locked door on the south wall leads to the second zone, Morvayn Manor Chambers.

Morvayn Manor Chambers[edit]

The entrance leads into Adril and Cindiri Arano's bedroom. In the southwest corner is a set of shelves holding two bottles of wine, two bottles of shein, a bottle of matze, a copy of The Poison Song, Book II, a mammoth tusk, an ash yam, a sliced goat cheese, and two eidar cheese wheels. On the wall ledge near the set of shelves are three more bottles of alto wine, a bottle of shein, and a bottle of wine, and to the right of the set of shelves is a bucket containing three more ash yams. In the northwest corner is a chair and a small round table holding a piece of cooked beef, with a bottle each of shein and matze on the wall shelf above the table, and an unlocked East Empire Company strongbox on the wall ledge to the right of the table. In the southeast corner is an owned double bed, with two bottles of shein, a bottle of flin, and a bottle of matze on the wall ledge to the right of the bed. In the northeast corner is a bench. Along the north wall is a door to the next room.

The next room is circular, and serves as Lleril Morvayn's bedroom. Along the west wall is an expert-locked display case containing a chitin heavy armor cuirass and a chitin heavy helmet. Along the north wall is an owned double bed, with a sideboard at its foot holding copies of History of Raven Rock, Vol. I and II, two notes titled House Redoran's Reply and Adril's Survey Results, and a lute. Along the east wall is a chair and a set of shelves holding three bottles of flin, a bottle of matze, a bottle of sujamma, copies of The Five Far Stars and Deathbrand, a garnet, and an emerald. Although it is possible to jump onto the rafters under the domed ceiling, there are no items on the rafters.

Bugs[edit]

  • There isn't enough bed space in Morvayn Manor for the Arano couple to be able to sleep in their assigned upstairs area.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 1.0.4, addresses this issue. Their bed has been upgraded to a double, and the navmesh has been adjusted to fit the new sleeping arrangements.
  • The East Empire Company strongbox here is unlocked, rather than expert-locked like almost all other East Empire Company strongboxes.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
  • Several buildings in Raven Rock, including Morvayn Manor, were incorrectly assigned the music heard in Apocrypha rather than the default Solstheim music heard in other houses.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 1.0.1, fixes this bug. (details)
  • The music bug listed above also affects the Morvayn Manor Chambers.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 2.0.8, fixes this bug. (details)
