This article is about alchemy labs used to make potions and poisons. For the player-built home addition related to the Hearthfire add-on, see Alchemy Laboratory.

An Alchemy Lab

Alchemy Labs are places where it is possible to perform Alchemy. A category listing all places where alchemy labs can be found is available at Category:Skyrim-Places-Alchemy Labs.

Locations[edit]

Eastmarch[edit]

  •  Ansilvund Excavation — A medium-sized Nordic ruin north of Riften containing draugr and necromancers led by Lu'ah Al-Skaven. (map)
  •  Cragwallow Slope — A medium-sized cave southeast of Windhelm home to conjurers and atronachs. (map)
  •  Cronvangr Cave — A medium-sized cave southwest of Kynesgrove containing vampires and frostbite spiders. (map)
  •  Eastmarch Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in Eastmarch, located southeast of Mzulft and north of Stony Creek Cave. (map)
  •  Fort Amol — A medium-sized fort southwest of Windhelm initially occupied by warlocks. (map)
  •  Mistwatch — A medium-sized fort east of Darkwater Crossing containing the Mistwatch Bandits and their leader. (map)
  •  Mzulft — A large Dwarven ruin south-southeast of Windhelm containing Dwarven automatons, Falmer, and chaurus. (map)
  •  Narzulbur — An Orc stronghold in the east of Skyrim, southeast of Windhelm. (map)
  •  Palace of the Kings — A large castle at the far end of the Valunstrad district in Windhelm. (map)
  •  Sacellum of Boethiah — The shrine to the Daedric Prince Boethiah. (map)
  •  Snapleg Cave — A medium-sized cave northwest of Rift Watchtower occupied by hags and monsters. (map)
  •  Stony Creek Cave — A small cave southeast of Windhelm containing bandits. (map)
  •  Uttering Hills Cave — A small cave southwest of Windhelm containing bandits. (map)
  • The White Phial — The alchemical shop located on the north side of Windhelm's market district. (map)

Falkreath[edit]

  •  Anise's Cabin — A small shack west-southwest of Riverwood. (map)
  •  Bloodlet Throne — A small fort southwest of Helgen containing vampires, wolves, gargoyles, and death hounds. (map)
  •  Cracked Tusk Keep — A medium-sized fort west of Falkreath containing Orc bandits and hunters led by Ghunzul. (map)
  •  Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary — An active Dark Brotherhood sanctuary west of Falkreath. (map)
  • Dead Man's Drink — An inn in the town of Falkreath. It is owned by Valga Vinicia. (map)
  •  Falkreath Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Falkreath Hold, located east of Helgen. (map)
  • Grave Concoctions — A store in Falkreath specializing in alchemy and home for Zaria who runs the store. (map)
  •  Haemar's Shame — A medium-sized cave east of Helgen containing vampires. (map)
  •  Hag Rock Redoubt — A small Nordic ruin south of Markarth containing Forsworn. (map)
  •  Ilinalta's Deep — A medium-sized fort north of the Lady Stone containing necromancers and skeletons. (map)
  •  Knifepoint Ridge — A small bandit camp and iron and corundum mine northwest of Falkreath. (map)
  •  Southfringe Sanctum — A small cave southeast of Helgen containing the conjurer Bashnag and his coven. (map)
  •  Sunderstone Gorge — A small cave north-northwest of Falkreath containing warlocks and skeletons. (map)
  •  Twilight Sepulcher — The location of the Pilgrim's Path and main entrance to Nocturnal's Ebonmere portal, resting place for the Skeleton Key. (map)

Haafingar[edit]

  • Angeline's Aromatics — The alchemy shop in Solitude. (map)
  • Castle Dour — A large fortress in Solitude which acts as the headquarters of the Imperial Legion and residence of the Emperor in Skyrim. (map)
  • Darkfall PassageDG — A small cave only accessible through Darkfall Cave serving as the only access to the Forgotten Vale.
  •  Forgotten ValeDG — An isolated region in northwestern Skyrim once inhabited by the Snow Elves worshipping the god Auri-El. (map)
  •  Haafingar Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Haafingar, located northeast of Dragon Bridge and south of the Statue to Meridia. (map)
  •  The Katariah — The personal transport vessel of the Emperor, Titus Mede II. (map)
  • Potema's Catacombs — A series of catacombs designed to house the Wolf Queen of Solitude. (map)
  •  Volkihar KeepDG — A large fortress on an island far northwest of Solitude connected to Castle Volkihar which serves as the Volkihar vampire clan's home. (map)

Hjaalmarch[edit]

  •  Fort Snowhawk — A medium-sized fort west of Morthal initially occupied by necromancers and skeletons. (map)
  •  Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Hjaalmarch, located east of Ustengrav and west of Mzinchaleft. (map)
  •  Labyrinthian — A large Nordic ruin southeast of Morthal containing draugr, skeletons, trolls, wisps, and the dragon priest Morokei. (map)
  •  Lost Valkygg — A small Nordic ruin northeast of Labyrinthian containing draugr. (map)
  •  Movarth's Lair — A small cave north-northeast of Morthal containing vampires led by Movarth Piquine. (map)
  • Thaumaturgist's Hut — An alchemy shop in Morthal. (map)

The Pale[edit]

  •  Dawnstar Sanctuary — A disused Dark Brotherhood sanctuary on the shore of the Sea of Ghosts near Dawnstar. (map)
  •  Forsaken Cave — A medium-sized cave west of Windhelm containing draugr and leveled animals. (map)
  •  Fort Dunstad — A large fort south of Dawnstar initially occupied by bandits. (map)
  •  Hall of the Vigilant — A secluded lodge south-southwest of Dawnstar, home to the Vigilants of Stendarr. (map)
  •  Irkngthand — A large Dwarven ruin west of Windhelm containing bandits, Dwarven automatons, Falmer, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. (map)
  • The Mortar and Pestle — The alchemy shop in Dawnstar, between Brina's House and Beitild's House. (map)
  •  Nightcaller Temple — A small fort on the clifftop overlooking Dawnstar. (map)
  •  Pale Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in the Pale, located west of Dawnstar and northwest of Mzinchaleft. (map)

The Reach[edit]

  •  Blind Cliff Cave — A medium-sized cave southwest of Karthwasten along the banks of the Karth River containing Forsworn. (map)
  • Burguk's Longhouse — The longhouse of Chief Burguk, located in the Orc stronghold of Dushnikh Yal. (map)
  •  Darkfall CaveDG — A small cave south of Mor Khazgur containing frostbite spiders and trolls. (map)
  •  Deepwood Vale — A large Nordic ruin west of Dragon Bridge containing Forsworn. (map)
  •  Dushnikh Yal — A prosperous Orc stronghold in the southwest of Skyrim, southeast of Markarth. (map)
  •  Fort Sungard — A large fort east-southeast of Markarth initially occupied by Forsworn. (map)
  • The Hag's Cure — An apothecary in Markarth owned and operated by Bothela. (map)
  •  Hag's End — A small Nordic ruin west of Dragon Bridge containing a hagraven, hags, and witches. (map)
  •  Hag Rock Redoubt — A small Nordic ruin south of Markarth containing Forsworn. (map)
  •  Harmugstahl — A small fort north of Karthwasten containing the warlock Kornalus and his enchanted frostbite spiders. (map)
  •  Karthspire Camp — A large Forsworn camp and cave east of Markarth. (map)
  •  Mor Khazgur — An Orc stronghold in the far northwest of Skyrim, west of Solitude and north of Markarth. (map)
  •  Reach Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in the Reach, located west of Liar's Retreat and northeast of Karthwasten. (map)
  •  Shrine to Peryite — The shrine to the Daedric Prince Peryite. (map)
  •  Sundered Towers — A small pair of linked ruined Nordic towers southeast of Karthwasten containing Forsworn and hagravens. (map)
  •  Understone Keep — An ancient Dwarven keep built into the mountainside, used as a palace by the Jarl of Markarth. (map)
  •  Valthume — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Markarth containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the dragon priest Hevnoraak. (map)

The Rift[edit]

  •  Alchemist's Shack — A small shack south of Ivarstead. (map)
  •  Avanchnzel — A medium-sized Dwarven ruin west of Riften containing Dwarven automatons. (map)
  • Black-Briar Manor — The Black-Briars' residence in Riften. (map)
  •  Boulderfall Cave — A small cave northwest of Riften containing necromancers. (map)
  •  Darklight Tower — A medium-sized fort southwest of Riften containing hagravens, hags, and witches. (map)
  • Elgrim's Elixirs — A damp alchemist's shop located on the lower level, by the canal that flows through the center of the city of Riften. Owned and run by Elgrim and his wife Hafjorg. (map)
  •  Forelhost — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Riften containing Dragon Cultists, draugr, skeevers, and the dragon priest Rahgot. (map)
  •  Fort DawnguardDG — A large fort southeast of Riften which serves as the Dawnguard's base of operations. (map)
  •  Largashbur — An accursed Orc stronghold beset by giants in the southeast of Skyrim, west-southwest of Riften. (map)
  •  Mistveil Keep — A castle located at the southern end of Riften. (map)
  • The Ragged Flagon — A tavern that is part of the Thieves Guild's headquarters. (map)
  • The Ratway — A network of sewer tunnels running beneath Riften, and home to the Thieves Guild. (map)
  •  Redwater DenDG — A shack northwest of Riften containing bandits and vampires. (map)
  •  Rift Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in the Rift, located south of Ivarstead and west of the Ruins of Bthalft. (map)
  •  Sarethi Farm — A farm east of Ivarstead that cultivates Nirnroot. (map)
  •  Shroud Hearth Barrow — A medium-sized Nordic ruin east of Ivarstead containing draugr and skeletons. (map)
  •  Tolvald's Cave — A large cave northeast of Shor's Stone containing Falmer, chaurus, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. (map)

SolstheimDB[edit]

  •  Altar of ThrondDB — A landmark west of the Temple of Miraak next to a small cave containing hagravens. (map)
  •  Ashfall's TearCC — A small cave north of Raven Rock containing a hidden Tribunal Temple shrine to the goddess Almalexia. (map)
  •  Ashfallow CitadelDB — A small Imperial fort east of Highpoint Tower and northwest of the Sun Stone being used as a base of operations by the Morag Tong. (map)
  •  Bloodskal BarrowDB — A small Nordic ruin north of Raven Rock containing reavers. (map)
  •  Damphall MineDB — A small iron and silver mine north of Raven Rock containing reavers. (map)
  • Edla's HouseDB — The home of Edla and her son Nikulas in Skaal Village. (map)
  •  Highpoint TowerDB — A small fort east of Raven Rock containing albino spiders, ash spawn, and Neloth's former apprentice Ildari Sarothril. (map)
  • Ienth FarmDB — The home of Garyn Ienth and Milore Ienth in Raven Rock. (map)
  •  Raven Rock MineDB — A small ebony mine in northern Raven Rock initially containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the dragon priest Zahkriisos. (map)
  • Shaman's HutDB — The home of shaman Storn Crag-Strider and his daughter Frea in Skaal Village. (map)
  • Tel Mithryn ApothecaryDB — The home of the apothecary Elynea Mothren in Tel Mithryn. (map)
  •  Temple of MiraakDB — A Nordic temple west of Thirsk Mead Hall containing cultists, draugr, and skeletons. (map)

Whiterun Hold[edit]

  • Arcadia's Cauldron — An alchemy shop in Whiterun's market area. (map)
  •  Broken Fang Cave — A small cave southeast of Rorikstead containing skeletons and vampires. (map)
  •  Dragonsreach — The residence of the Jarl of Whiterun Hold, the hold's seat of political power, and the location of the hold's jail. (map)
  •  Drelas' Cottage — An isolated cottage at the foot of a mountain in northern Whiterun Hold, and south of Morthal. (map)
  •  Dustman's Cairn — A medium-sized Nordic ruin northwest of Whiterun containing Silver Hands, draugr, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. (map)
  •  Fellglow Keep — A medium-sized fort east-northeast of Whiterun containing warlocks, atronachs, and vampires, and the mages' leader, the Caller. (map)
  •  Honningbrew Meadery — A small meadery southeast of Whiterun owned by Sabjorn. (map)
  •  Rannveig's Fast — A small Nordic ruin east-northeast of Rorikstead containing subjugated ghosts and Sild the Warlock. (map)
  • Sleeping Giant Inn — A small inn and tavern in the town of Riverwood. (map)
  •  Valtheim Towers — A small pair of linked ruined Nordic towers east of Whiterun containing bandits. (map)
  •  White River Watch — A small cave east-southeast of Whiterun containing the White River bandits. (map)
  •  Whiterun Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Whiterun Hold, located south of the Ritual Stone and east of White River Watch. (map)

Winterhold[edit]

  •  Alftand — A large Dwarven ruin southwest of Winterhold containing Dwarven automatons, Falmer, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. (map)
  • Arch-Mage's Quarters — The area within the College of Winterhold in which its current Arch-Mage resides. (map)
  • Blackreach — An immense, naturally lit cavern inhabited by hordes of Falmer and containing the ruins of a Dwemer city. (map)
  • Hall of Countenance — The main living and sleeping quarters for most of the higher ranked mages of the College of Winterhold. (map)
  •  Hob's Fall Cave — A small cave west of Winterhold containing necromancers and skeletons. (map)
  •  Journeyman's Nook — A small outdoor Nordic ruin southeast of Winterhold containing a bandit. (map)
  • The Midden — A medium-sized dungeon underneath the College of Winterhold containing the Atronach Forge and home to the Augur of Dunlain, a former mage of the college. (map)
  •  Mount Anthor — A mountaintop dragon lair south-southwest of Winterhold. (map)
  •  Saarthal — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southwest of Winterhold. (map)
  •  Winterhold Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in Winterhold Hold, located southeast of Dawnstar and northwest of Frostflow Lighthouse. (map)

Player-Owned Houses[edit]

  •  Bloodchill CavernCC — A small cave southwest of Winterhold containing a subterranean vampiric player home. (map)
  • Breezehome — A house that can be purchased in Whiterun. (map)
  • Dead Man's DreadCC — An ancient ship located within the Grotto on Blackbone Isle. (map)
  • Gallows HallCC — A necromantic player home in Eastmarch, north of Mara's Eye Pond. (map)
  •  Goldenhills PlantationCC — An abandoned farm east of Rorikstead. (map)
  •  HendraheimCC — A Nordic hall player home in the Reach, north of Bilegulch Mine. (map)
  • Hjerim — The house in Windhelm you can purchase. (map)
  • Honeyside — A two-story house in the northwest corner of Riften. (map)
  •  MyrwatchCC — A mage tower player home east of Morthal. (map)
  • NchuanthumzCC — A Dwarven player home in Eastmarch, north of Kagrenzel. (map)
  • Proudspire Manor — A three-story house that can be purchased in Solitude. (map)
  • Severin ManorDB — Initially the home of Mirri Severin, Tilisu Severin, and Vendil Severin in Raven Rock, which can be obtained as player housing by completing the quest Served Cold. (map)
  •  Shadowfoot SanctumCC — A small player home located in the Ratway beneath Riften. (map)
  •  Tundra HomesteadCC — A cottage that can be purchased in Whiterun Hold, located just northeast of Battle-Born Farm. (map)
  • Vlindrel Hall — A house available for purchase in Markarth. (map)
  • With the Hearthfire add-on, you can build a lab in any of your constructed homes, either in the Main Hall, or by building an Alchemy Laboratory.HF

Bugs[edit]

  • You may clip through the alchemy lab, and appear to be standing in the middle. This has no effect on exiting or using the table. ?
  • Sometimes after making many potions the lab doesn't let you select more ingredients. This is fixed simply by leaving it and then selecting it again. ?
  • Very rarely, the alchemy lab will be invisible; if it is on a tabletop, only the bare surface of the table will appear, and if the lab is a freestanding piece of furniture such as the one in the Midden, the whole piece will be invisible and any items stacked on top will appear to be suspended in midair. The lab will still be visible to the cursor and can be interacted with as normal, although the animation will also show the lab as invisible.
