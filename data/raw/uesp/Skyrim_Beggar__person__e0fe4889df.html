Beggar

This Beggar is an elder found at the Windhelm docks as part of the related quest. Unlike most beggars, he will not give you a blessing in return for 1 gold.

Related Quests[edit]

  • Heart of CrimsonCC: Confront the murderous leader of a bandit gang.

Quest-Related Dialogue[edit]

Heart of Crimson[edit]

Here. Have a hundred gold pieces. (100 gold)
Here. Have a gold piece. (1 gold)
Instead of alms, I will pray for your prosperity.
You look hungry. Here, have some bread.
"Thank you, oh thank you."
