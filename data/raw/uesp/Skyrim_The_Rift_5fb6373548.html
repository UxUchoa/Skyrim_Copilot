The Rift is a hold in southeast Skyrim, with its capital in Riften. It is initially affiliated with the Stormcloaks, but can be captured by the Imperials after completing Compelling Tribute and The Battle for Fort Greenwall. It can also change sides as part of negotiations during Season Unending.

The Rift is the southernmost of Skyrim's holds, sitting on a massive plateau in the southeastern corner of the province. The Rift borders both Morrowind and Cyrodiil, and is separated from the rest of the world by steep cliffs and mountains, including the Jerall Mountains, the Velothi Mountains, and the legendary Throat of the World. It holds two large lakes as well as autumnal deciduous forests.

In addition to its capital in Riften, the Rift also houses the settlements of Ivarstead and Shor's Stone.

Flora and Fauna[edit]

The Rift is particularly distinctive for its high number of aspen trees, with white bark and leaves in autumnal hues. For those looking for alchemy ingredients, the Rift contains all variants of mountain flowers, as well as the native scaly pholiota and the canis root. The Rift is also the only place to find wild gourd plants.

As for the fauna the most notable is the rich population of fish and Nordic barnacle in Lake Honrich, which fuel the economy of Riften.

Hold Capital[edit]

  •  Riften — A major city in the southeast of Skyrim near the borders of Morrowind and Cyrodiil, and the capital of the Rift. (map)
  •  Mistveil Keep — A castle located at the southern end of Riften. (map)
  •  Riften DocksCC — An extensive dock located on the western side of Riften. (map)
  •  Riften Stables — A stable outside the northern entrance to Riften. (map)
  •  Thieves Guild — The headquarters of the Thieves Guild located with the sewers beneath Riften, known as The Ratway. (map)

Farms, Towns, and Settlements including Orc Strongholds and Inns[edit]

  •  Alchemist's Shack — A small shack south of Ivarstead. (map)
  •  Black-Briar Lodge — A large remote building east of Riften. (map)
  •  Froki's Shack — A small shack southeast of Ivarstead. (map)
  •  Goldenglow Estate — A honey farm west of Riften containing mercenaries, skeevers, and the farm's owner Aringoth. (map)
  •  Heartwood Mill — A struggling lumber mill on the shores of Lake Honrich, west of Goldenglow Estate. (map)
  •  Ivarstead — A milling town in the western Rift. (map)
  •  Largashbur — An accursed Orc stronghold beset by giants in the southeast of Skyrim, west-southwest of Riften. (map)
  •  Merryfair Farm — A poor, small farm northwest of Riften, owned and run by Dravin Llanith and his wife Synda Llanith. (map)
  •  Sarethi Farm — A farm east of Ivarstead that cultivates Nirnroot. (map)
  •  Shor's Stone — A mining town in the northern Rift. (map)
  •  Snow-Shod Farm — A small farm south of Riften that is owned by Vulwulf Snow-Shod. (map)

Unmarked Places[edit]

  • There are three Dragon Mounds in the Rift.
    • Northeast of Autumnwatch Tower.
    • West of Autumnshade Clearing.
    • North of Lost Tongue Overlook.
  • There are two Shrines in the Rift.
    • A Shrine of Talos southwest of Froki's Shack.
    • A Shrine of Zenithar west of Fallowstone Cave.

The Rift Quests[edit]

Quests that are started in or related to the Rift (except for radiant quests begun in other holds) are listed here:

Main Quest[edit]

  • The Way of the Voice: Meet the Greybeards.
  • A Cornered Rat: Locate Esbern.

Imperial Legion Civil War Quests[edit]

  • Reunification of Skyrim: Reunite Skyrim under the leadership of the Empire. (radiant)
  • The Battle for Fort Greenwall: Take Fort Greenwall for the Imperials. (radiant)

Daedric Quests[edit]

  • The Cursed Tribe: Help lift the curse placed on the Orcs of Largashbur by Malacath.

Dark Brotherhood[edit]

  • Innocence Lost: Help an orphaned child avoid being returned to a sadistic caretaker.
  • Kill Narfi: Kill a hapless beggar in Ivarstead.

Thieves Guild[edit]

  • All primary quests except for Hard Answers, as well as the special jobs, either originate or terminate here.

Side Quests including Divine Quests[edit]

  • The Book of Love: Help the Temple of Mara spread the cause of love throughout Skyrim.
  • Forbidden Legend: Investigate the forgotten Gauldur Legend.
  • Infiltration: Help Stalleo take back Treva's Watch.
  • Kyne's Sacred Trials: Participate in Kyne's Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of Skyrim.
  • Promises to Keep: Help Louis Letrush get Frost from Sibbi Black-Briar.
  • Repentance: Help Illia prevent her mother Silvia from transforming into a hagraven.
  • A Return To Your Roots: Go where a famous alchemist could not in order to aid ground-breaking research.
  • Siege on the Dragon Cult: Enter the ruins of Forelhost and defeat Rahgot to recover his mask for Captain Valmir.
  • Unfathomable Depths: Uncover the memories of the Dwemer.

Dawnguard Quests[edit]

  • Dawnguard Main Questline
    • DawnguardDG: Speak with the leader of the Dawnguard.
    • AwakeningDG: Find out what the vampires are looking for.
    • BloodlineDG: Choose your side!
    • ProphetDG: Find and rescue a Moth Priest from the vampires.
    • Chasing EchoesDG: Explore Castle Volkihar.
    • Unseen VisionsDG: Perform a ceremony to read the Elder Scroll.
  • Dawnguard Side Quests (all start here, several take place exclusively in the Rift)
    • Ancient TechnologyDG: Retrieve Dwemer schematics to improve technology for the Dawnguard. (radiant)
    • Bolstering the RanksDG: Find Florentius Baenius and recruit him into the Dawnguard.
    • Cleansing LightDG: Kill the boss of a vampire lair. (radiant)
    • Destroying the DawnguardDG: Kill the Dawnguard leaders.
    • Hide and SeekDG: Kill a vampire masquerading as a citizen. (radiant)
    • A New OrderDG: Recruit Isran's old allies for the Dawnguard. (radiant)
    • RescueDG: Rescue a victim from vampires. (radiant)

Creation Club Quests[edit]

  • Blue in the FaceCC: Investigate rumors of the Blue God's return.
  • Creature of LegendCC: Track down and tame the last living Unicorn.
  • The Gray Cowl of NocturnalCC: Investigate the legend of the Gray Fox.
  • The Lost PaladinCC: Find and claim the Sword of Heroes.
  • Nordic JewelryCC: Learn about Nordic jewelry.
  • Pets of SkyrimCC: Find trained pets around Skyrim.
  • Shadowfoot SanctumCC: Purchase a cozy sewer home ideal for a thief.
  • A Soul DividedCC: Seek out a cursed Tsaesci blade in the Ratway.
  • Vile WhispersCC: A malevolent Daedric entity long thought destroyed has resurfaced in the Velothi Mountains.
