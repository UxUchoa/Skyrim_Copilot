Tel Mithryn

Tel Mithryn is a Telvanni tower settlement east of Fort Frostmoth on Solstheim.

The settlement is home to House Telvanni Master Wizard Neloth. Its four mushroom structures were grown near the beginning of the Fourth Era out of special fungal spores from Morrowind. The structures include the main tower, the apothecary, the kitchen, and the steward's house. Master Neloth has a dubious reputation on the island, but is instrumental in progressing the Dragonborn questline and offers other unique services to the enterprising adventurer.

Each of the structures is accessible by a long organic ramp ascending to a round wooden door. A heart stone deposit and a convenient pickaxe are behind the kitchen, with two more deposits on the cliffs near the coast to the southeast, while a few bleeding crown and fly amanita fungi and scathecraw plants grow near the ramps. Other than the unique exterior architecture, most items of interest are inside the buildings.

Related Quests[edit]

Quests Starting Here[edit]

Dragonborn[edit]

  • The Path of Knowledge: Discover the locations of the Black Books in order to stop Miraak.

Side Quests[edit]

  • From the Ashes: Help Talvas Fathryon by killing an ash guardian.
  • Healing a House: Help Elynea Mothren repair the tower of Tel Mithryn.
  • Reluctant Steward: Find a new steward for Neloth in Raven Rock.

The following quests become available after you've spoken to Neloth during Reluctant Steward.

  • Telvanni Research: Extract a sample from an ash spawn for Neloth's research. (Also requires Conjuration 40)
  • Heart Stones: Find a heart stone for Neloth.
  • Experimental Subject (A): Help Neloth by being a test subject in his experiment.
  • Azra's Staffs: Retrieve a staff made by Azra Nightwielder for Master Neloth.

The following quests become available after completing Reluctant Steward.

  • Black Book: The Hidden Twilight: Learn the Black Book's hidden knowledge.
  • Briarheart Necropsy: Examine a Briarheart for Neloth.

The following quest becomes available after completing Experimental Subject (A) and requires Destruction 40.

  • Wind and Sand: Retrieve a copy of Wind and Sand for Neloth.

The following quest becomes available after completing Briarheart Necropsy.

  • Old Friends: Find the source of attacks on Tel Mithryn.

The following quest becomes available after completing The Gardener of Men.

  • Lost Knowledge: Retrieve the Black Books for Neloth.
