Quick Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Go to Solitude.
  2. Speak to General Tullius.
  3. Speak to Legate Rikke.
  4. Clear out bandits from Fort Hraggstad.
  5. Return to Legate Rikke.
  6. Take the Imperial Legion oath.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

Imperial Introductions[edit]

"I'm telling you, Ulfric's planning an attack on Whiterun."

Anytime after escaping Helgen during Unbound, anyone you meet with ties with the Imperial Legion (like Hadvar) will encourage you to travel to Solitude to enlist. This will start the miscellaneous quest Imperial Introductions. In particular, some of them will specify to talk to Legate Rikke at Castle Dour. When you enter, you will witness her trying to convince an ill-informed General Tullius that Nords will follow their traditions, even in a time when loyalty is tested. Apparently, rebel leader Ulfric Stormcloak is preparing to attack Whiterun so the two strategists have their hands full. After the scene, if you try to speak to Legate Rikke, General Tullius will approach you. He will recall you from Helgen and, by any of your dialogue options, point you towards Legate Rikke for putting someone as resourceful as you to good use.

Rikke will commend you for surviving the destruction of Helgen and tell you that she has arranged a small test of your skills before you're allowed to join the Legion. When asked which test she will explain: "The kind that evaluates your usefulness during... duress. I'm sending you to clear out Fort Hraagstad. If you survive, you'll pass. If you die, then I'll have no further use for your corpse.". She will then explain that the Legion will set up a garrison at Fort Hraggstad but that the place has been overrun with bandits and needs to be cleared. She will not take no for an answer and will just angrily say: "What's the matter, soldier? I gave you an order. I want that fort, and I want it now. Move it!". This will complete the miscellaneous quest Imperial Introductions and start Joining the Legion. If you have already visited Fort Hraggstad and cleared it, she will be quite surprised: "Is that so? I was right about you.". This will complete Imperial Introductions and start Joining the Legion with the first stages completed.

Joining the Legion[edit]

Prove Your Usefulness[edit]

The fort is located near the Thalmor Embassy, west of Solitude in the snowy terrain past Wolfskull Cave. Once there, a number of bandits will attack you on sight. The fort has archers, atop towers, so approach with caution. Once the exterior area is clear, locate the entrance to the fort and enter. It consists of two interior zones: the main zone and the prison, both of them accessible from separate entrances in the courtyard. In the prison, two bandits await you in the first room, with another guarding the area downstairs. In the main zone, two bandits and their chief will ambush you upon entering. You can find a copy of the Archery skill book, The Gold Ribbon of Merit upstairs. When all is said and done return to Legate Rikke in Solitude.

The Oath[edit]

"Long live the Emperor! Long live the Empire!"

Upon your next enter in the castle, Legate Rikke and Tullius will once again be debating, this time about the Jagged Crown. Tullius will once again show a lack of respect for Nordic tradition. After the scene, Rikke will approach you and will be pleased to see your success, complimenting you on a job well done: "Welcome back, soldier. I'm glad you made it in one piece. I'll send men to garrison the fort right away. You did well. I'm impressed. But before we go any further, it's time for you to officially join the Legion.". General Tullius will then approach you to take the oath binding you to the service of the emperor and the Legion. Repeat the oath after him:

"Upon my honor I do swear undying loyalty to the Emperor, Titus Mede II..."
"...and unwavering obedience to the officers of his great Empire."
"May those above judge me, and those below take me, if I fail in my duty."
"Long live the Emperor! Long live the Empire!"

Tullius will then welcome you to the Legion and send you to the castle's blacksmith, Beirand, starting the miscellaneous quest Get Outfitted, who will provide you with a set of Imperial armor. You may choose heavy, medium, or light Imperial armor. Once Tullius has sent you towards Beirand the quest ends and the next one starts, your first assignment for the Empire as an Auxiliary of the Imperial Legion, The Jagged Crown.

Achievements[edit]

One achievement is unlocked when you complete this quest:

  • Taking Sides (10 points/Bronze) — Join the Stormcloaks or the Imperial Army

Notes[edit]

  • Joining the Legion and Joining the Stormcloaks are mutually exclusive quests: completing one automatically fails the other (if it was started or the prerequisite miscellaneous quest was started).
  • You can still join the Imperial Legion after completing the Dark Brotherhood. The oath you take upon joining omits "Titus Mede II", but he'll still say "Long Live the Emperor!"
  • Even though Legate Rikke says she will send troops to garrison the fort straight away if you return to Fort Hraggstad before speaking to her Imperial soldiers will already be present. Because you have not taken the Imperial oath yet you will be trespassing if you enter the buildings and you will get attacked if you do not leave.
  • If you clear Fort Hraggstad before starting this quest, Legate Rikke will have different dialogue, surprised by your go-ahead. Nevertheless it won't affect the course of the quest.
  • After completion of this quest, random spawned groups of Stormcloak Soldiers may attack you and you will no longer be welcome in Stormcloak camps or forts.

Bugs[edit]

  • If Fort Hraggstad is not the first Civil War Fort you clear, after clearing it of bandits, even during this quest, soldiers will never garrison and enemies respawn instead. This is due to a hidden quest not ending after clearing the first Civil War Fort. See this talk section for info.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
    • The console command resetquest CWFortObserver can be used to reset the hidden quest, then clear the fort again. Repeat this for every Civil War Fort you want to be garrisoned.
  • You may encounter a bug/message "No Active Quest" or when you get closer to the fort the quest arrow may just disappear. Go ahead and clear out all the bandits around and in the prison first. Then, head to the main zone. The quest arrow will then reappear and point to the chief bandit. You can then properly clear out the fort and complete the quest.
  • One or more bandits may be in a completely different part of Skyrim.
