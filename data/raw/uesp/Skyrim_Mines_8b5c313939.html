Embershard Mine

This page lists all the mines in Skyrim. Mines generally contain several veins of ore. Ore veins in uncleared mines, including unclearable mines such as Gloombound Mine, respawn in 10 in-game days, while ore veins in cleared mines respawn in 30 in-game days. For details on how to mine ore and lists of where various ores can be found, see Mining.

 Bilegulch Mine — A small orichalcum mine northwest of Falkreath containing Orc bandits. (map)
 Broken Tusk MineDB — A small heart stone mine north of Raven Rock containing rieklings. (map)
Cidhna Mine — A medium-sized silver mine and forced-labor prison camp in southern Markarth. (map)
 Damphall MineDB — A small iron and silver mine north of Raven Rock containing reavers. (map)
Dushnikh Mine — A small orichalcum mine supporting the Orc stronghold of Dushnikh Yal. (map)
 Embershard Mine — A small iron mine southwest of Riverwood containing bandits. (map)
Fenn's Gulch Mine — A small silver mine in northwestern Karthwasten. (map)
 Fort Fellhammer — A medium-sized fort and iron mine south of Dawnstar containing bandits. (map)
 Gloombound Mine — A small ebony and iron mine supporting the Orc stronghold of Narzulbur. (map)
Goldenrock Mine — A small corundum mine in southeastern Darkwater Crossing. (map)
 Halted Stream Camp — A small bandit camp and iron mine north of Whiterun. (map)
Iron-Breaker Mine — A small iron mine in eastern Dawnstar. (map)
 Knifepoint Ridge — A small bandit camp and iron and corundum mine northwest of Falkreath. (map)
 Kolskeggr Mine — A small gold mine and settlement east-northeast of Markarth initially containing Forsworn. (map)
 Left Hand Mine — A small iron mine and settlement southeast of Markarth. (map)
 Lost Prospect Mine — A small gold mine east-northeast of Riften. (map)
Mor Khazgur Mine — A small orichalcum mine supporting the Orc stronghold of Mor Khazgur. (map)
 Northwind Mine — A small iron mine northwest of Shor's Stone containing skeletons. (map)
Quicksilver Mine — A small quicksilver mine in western Dawnstar. (map)
 Raven Rock MineDB — A small ebony mine in northern Raven Rock initially containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the dragon priest Zahkriisos. (map)
Redbelly Mine — A small ebony mine in western Shor's Stone initially containing frostbite spiders. (map)
Rockwallow Mine — A small iron mine in eastern Stonehills. (map)
Sanuarach Mine — A small silver mine in northern Karthwasten initially containing Silver-Blood mercenaries. (map)
 Soljund's Sinkhole — A small moonstone mine and settlement east of Markarth initially containing draugr. (map)
Steamscorch Mine — A small malachite mine in northeastern Kynesgrove. (map)
 Whistling Mine — A small iron mine and settlement southeast of Winterhold. (map)
