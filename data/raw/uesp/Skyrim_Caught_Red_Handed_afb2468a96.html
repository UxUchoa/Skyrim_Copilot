Quick Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Talk to Svana Far-Shield in Haelga's Bunkhouse.
  2. Obtain the Marks of Dibella from Bolli, Indaryn, and Hofgrir.
  3. Confront Haelga.
  4. Return to Svana.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

A Worshipper[edit]

If you visit Haelga's Bunkhouse in Riften, you may see Svana Far-Shield busy doing some cleaning there. The work is difficult and endless, and she prefers to call it slavery. Ever since her parents died and Haelga took her in, it's been a nightmare. She was surprised to learn that Haelga is such a wretched woman. "So now I'm stuck living here while those pigs she calls customers grope me and say the most awful things." In her opinion, Haelga is disgusting and she takes her worship of Dibella a bit too seriously. According to Svana, Haelga slept with three different men in the last month. Svana would like to see her squirm just once. She happens to know that after Haelga makes love, she gives her partner a token of her affection called a Mark of Dibella. If you collect the marks from Bolli, Indaryn, and Hofgrir (All three men are located in Riften), and confront Haelga with them, it would cause her endless embarrassment. Furthermore, Svana asks that you collect the marks without violence.

Three Lovers[edit]

In order to get the three Marks of Dibella, you can use your Speech skills to persuade or intimidate the men into giving their marks to you. Alternatively, you can just pickpocket the marks from your targets. If you kill any of the men, the quest will fail immediately.

Bolli can usually be found wandering in the market or working in Riften Fishery. Talk to him about the mark and he will say "I have no idea what you're talking about. I'd never sleep with anyone besides my good wife, Drifa [sic]." You must pass an average-level Speech check to persuade him.

Two Rewards[edit]

Once you have all three Marks of Dibella, return to Haelga's Bunkhouse and show them to Haelga. She will be surprised. "What? How... where did you get these?" Obviously, she doesn't want anyone else to know about it, especially Svana. She will give you a magic scroll to keep you quiet. "If word got out that I'm practicing my Dibellan Arts in Riften, they'll run me out of town."

Bring the news to Svana, and she will be quite happy. "Oh I know. Isn't it wonderful? I bet she squirmed like a skeever when you pulled them out of your pocket." She believes things are going to be a lot different around here with your help. Then she will give you a piece of enchanted armor in return. "I want you to have this. It was my father's but I'm certain you'll put it to good use."

Notes[edit]

  • Blessing of Dibella (+10 Speechcraft for 8 hours) from the shrine in Haelga's bedroom and Gift of Charity (+10 Speechcraft for 1 hour) from giving alms to Edda or Snilf can improve your chance to persuade targets to hand over marks of Dibella.
  • After you complete this quest, you will be allowed to take low value items from anywhere in Haelga's Bunkhouse (due to your new friendship with Svana), even items found in her boarders' containers.
  • Svana will not offer the quest if one of the three lovers was already killed before talking to her.

Bugs[edit]

  • If you have pickpocketed any of the marks of Dibella by accident before receiving the quest, make sure to find all three before attempting to speak to anyone. You will get a notice to confront Haelga in your miscellaneous quest log. You can talk to Haelga and Svana without the requisite dialogue options and then talk to Haelga again. You will get to give the stones to Haelga after all and your quest will complete and a reward given.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
  • After acquiring one of the marks before ever picking up the quest, Svana will urge you to find the rest of the marks though you won't see anything in your quest log. However after obtaining all three, a miscellaneous objective to confront Haelga will then appear and the quest can be taken through to completion from that point on with items received from both Haelga and Svana.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
  • When asking Bolli about his relationship with Haelga he will remark that he has never slept with any woman other than his wife Drifa. However, Bolli's wife is Nivenor.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
  • Hofgrir Horse-Crusher's persuade check is incorrectly flagged to check his Speech level rather than yours.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
    • Hofgrir's intimidation check can be used, instead, which always works.
