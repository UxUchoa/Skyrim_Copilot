Ienth Farm

Ienth Farm is the home of Garyn Ienth and Milore Ienth in Raven Rock.

It is in the marketplace between Glover Mallory's House to the southeast and Alor House to the northeast.

Residents[edit]

Residents
Garyn Ienth
Milore Ienth

Walkthrough[edit]

Exterior[edit]

Ienth Farm field

To the left of the entrance is where Milore Ienth generally sells her wares, with an alchemy lab, an open box containing two leveled potions, a second open box containing four torchbug thoraxes and a bowl of fire salts, two unusable barrels, one of which has three more bowls of fire salts on top, and a crate all nearby. To the right of the entrance is where Garyn Ienth generally sells his own wares, with a long open box containing fourteen ash yams, a second open box containing thirteen more ash yams, an ash yam urn, an empty open chest, three crates, and an unusable barrel all nearby. The entrance has an apprentice-level lock.

The following harvestable crops and plants can be found primarily in the field north of the house, with a few plants near the southwest corner of the building beyond more unusable barrels and an empty cart:

Crops Plants
21 Ash Yam
10 Scathecraw
1 Trama Root

Ienth Farm[edit]

Ienth Farm interior

The entrance leads into the upper floor of the house. Along the south wall are four unusable barrels, two of which have a medium coin purse and a food urn on top, respectively, two food barrels, two crates, one of which has a copy of The Poison Song, Book III on top, an open box containing four ash yams, two chairs and a table, and a bench. The table holds two goat cheese wheels, a goat cheese wedge, three loaves of bread, an apple pie, a bottle of shein, a bottle of sujamma, a bottle of Nord mead, and a basket containing three ash yams. Near the west wall is a cooking pot on a stand in front of the fire, with two chairs and a small round table holding a bottle of shein, a bottle of sujamma, and a note to Milore nearby. Along the north wall is a wood pile, a woodcutter's axe, two open boxes containing a total of ten ash yams, six sacks, five crates, two unusable barrels, a sideboard holding three loose coins, three bottles of alto wine, and two bottles of wine, a narrow table, and a food urn. In the middle of the room, stairs lead down to the lower floor, with a bottle of shein, a wood pile with two pieces of firewood, three unusable barrels, one of which has copies of The Poison Song, Book I and Book II on top, and a bench around the sides of the stairs.

Ienth Farm alchemy lab

At the bottom of the stairs, to the right of a doorway are two food urns. Beyond the doorway is an area with an alchemy area to the south, a farm area to the north, and a room beyond a door ahead to the west. In the central area is an ingredient barrel with copies of The Poison Song, Book VI and The Red Year, Vol. I on top and three unusable barrels. In the alchemy area, to the left are three ingredient barrels, two of which have an ingredient urn and a wooden bowl containing three slaughterfish eggs on top, respectively, and a sideboard holding three samples of Felsaad tern feathers, two salt piles, a falmer ear, a sample of frost mirriam, a creep cluster, a dragon's tongue flower, and a potion of light feet. In the center is an alchemy lab with a potion of illusion on top. To the right is a set of shelves holding two skeever tails, a bowl of frost salts, three taproots, a canis root, a falmer ear, two potions of minor healing, a potion of minor magicka, a potion of resist shock, two bowls of bone meal, a potion of cure disease, and four sacks. Finally, to the right of the shelves is an open box containing three deathbell flowers, a sample of fly amanita, and a sample of Namira's rot.

In the farm area are three circular ash pile plots growing a total of sixteen ash yams and ten scathecraw plants, several open boxes and baskets containing a total of thirty-two ash yams, six ash yam urns, five straw bales, and a wooden table holding two ash yams, a scathecraw sample, and a bottle of sujamma.

Ienth Farm bedroom

The room to the west is the bedroom. In the middle of the room is an owned double bed with an empty open box at its foot. Near the south wall is a chest, a small round table holding an empty petty soul gem and three lockpicks, a bench, a set of shelves, a chair, and another small round table holding two medium coin purses. The set of shelves holds two bottles of shein and copies of The Real Barenziah, v1 and v2, Ancestors and the Dunmer, and The True Noble's Code. Near the north wall are three unusable barrels, a food barrel, two chairs, and a table holding four loose coins and copies of The Axe Man and Myths of Sheogorath.

Bugs[edit]

  • Several buildings in Raven Rock, including Ienth Farm, were incorrectly assigned the music heard in Apocrypha rather than the default Solstheim music heard in other houses.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 2.0.8, fixes this bug. (details)
