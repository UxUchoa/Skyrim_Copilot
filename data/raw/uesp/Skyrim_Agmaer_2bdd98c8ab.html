Agmaer
Agmaer as a Dawnguard member

Agmaer is a Nord bandit initially found in Dayspring Canyon on his way to join the Dawnguard. He wants to become a vampire hunter but is too nervous to enter Fort Dawnguard alone and will instead accompany you to see Isran. He will move on to become one of the first recruits of the Dawnguard and will, after completing the quest Prophet, be available as a follower.

Before becoming a member of the Dawnguard, Agmaer wears a set of farm clothes with a pair of hide bracers and a pair of hide boots. He carries random leveled bandit loot and a torch, and also has a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold. He is equipped with an iron war axe which he refers to as his Pa's axe.

When you return from Dimhollow Crypt after the quest Awakening, Agmaer will be a full-fledged member of the Dawnguard and don the clothes to match the position, including brown Dawnguard heavy armor with Dawnguard boots and Dawnguard gauntlets, and is now equipped with a Dawnguard war axe and a crossbow. He will also have a new routine in Fort Dawnguard. He owns one of the cots in the northern section on the ground floor and sleeps there between 8pm and 4am. After he wakes up he walks over to the western dining area where he hangs out all day long, eating, relaxing, or wandering around.

Quest-Related Events[edit]

Dawnguard[edit]

"I'm, uh... my name is Agmaer, sir."
"Uh, crossbow? I've never..."

As you enter Dayspring Canyon, you will find the young farmer standing near the central lake, obviously undecided on whether to see Isran or not. When he notices you he will say:

"Oh, hey there! You here to join the Dawnguard, too? Truth is, I'm a little nervous. I've never done anything like this before. I hope you don't mind if I walk up with you. Hey, uh, don't tell Isran I was afraid to meet him by myself. Not the best first impression for a new vampire hunter, I guess. You've probably killed lots of vampires, huh? I'm sure Isran will sign you right up. Not sure he'll take me. I hope so."

If you talk to Agmaer on the way to Fort Dawnguard, he will say:

"I heard what's going on. The vampires, the Dawnguard, all of it. I wanted to help, so here I am."

When he arrives at the foot of the fort, he will stop and admire the view:

"That must be it. Fort Dawnguard... Wow. Bigger than I expected. Where is everybody? This place looks almost deserted."

Just before reaching the entrance, he will say:

"I guess this is it. Wish me luck."

Once inside, you will find Agmaer respectfully waiting while Isran has a discussion with Vigilant Tolan about the destruction of the Hall of the Vigilants. If you approach Agmaer he will only whisper:

"You go ahead. I'll talk to Isran in a minute."

He will then stay in the background while you talk to Isran and he sends you to Dimhollow Crypt. Isran will then turn to Agmaer:

Isran: "You there, boy. Stop skulking in the shadows and step up here. What's your name?"
Agmaer: "I'm, uh... my name is Agmaer, sir."
Isran: "Do I look like a 'sir' to you, boy? I'm not a soldier, and you're not joining the army."
Agmaer: "Yes, si... Isran."
Isran: "Didn't I tell you to step forward? Hmm... Farm boy, eh? What's your weapon?"
Agmaer: "Uh, my weapon? I mostly just use my pa's axe, when wolves are attacking the goats or something."
Isran: "'My pa's axe', Stendarr preserve us. Don't worry, I think we can make a Dawnguard out of you. Here, take this crossbow and let's see how you shoot."
Agmaer: "Uh, crossbow? I've never..."
Isran: "Yes, a crossbow. Best thing for killing vampires. Just take a few shots at those crates over there."

When speaking with Agmaer at this point, he will explain why he wants to join the Dawnguard:

Why did you join the Dawnguard?
"I've heard the rumors around Skyrim. I know something's going on. And I've seen the vampires. I felt I had two choices. I could either live in fear of the night, or I could help do something to help protect my people."

When exiting conversation he will say either:

"Keep your neck covered!"
"Take care."

Kindred Judgment[edit]

When it is time to confront Lord Harkon during the quest Kindred Judgement, Agmaer will listen to Isran's speech in the main hall along with the other Dawnguard members. When Isran gives the order, he will travel halfway across Skyrim to Castle Volkihar and will participate in the joint effort to kill all residing vampires, while shouting:

"Victory to the Dawnguard!"
"We'll secure the castle. You just get to their master!"

When all is said and done, he will sheathe his weapon and walk all the way back to Fort Dawnguard and live there indefinitely with the other members. If you interact with him at this time he will state, "Isran will never admit it, but I'm sure he's proud of you."

Notes[edit]

  • When Agmaer shoots the steel bolts at the targets during his initial training, they never disappear. Agmaer will continue to shoot bolts until you return from any Dawnguard quest. This is a good way to get a lot of steel bolts early.

Bugs[edit]

  • If the Dragonborn add-on is installed, when asking him to follow you, Agmaer may have the same dialogue as Talvas Fathryon.
    • The Unofficial Dragonborn Patch, version 1.0.2, fixes this bug.
  • Agmaer may not regenerate after entering bleedout because he inherited stats from the bandit templates where the bleedout override checkbox was enabled.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch, version 4.0.8, fixes this bug.
  • If you have killed Durak before entering Dayspring Canyon, when you first meet Agmaer, he will be hostile to you.
