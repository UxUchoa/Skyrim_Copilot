Half-Moon Mill

Half-Moon Mill is a lumber mill that supplies Helgen and Falkreath. It is operated by Hert and Hern, who live in a small cabin across from the mill itself. It is located on the shore of Lake Ilinalta, west of The Lady Stone, east of Hunter's Rest, beside the road that leads north from Falkreath towards Rorikstead.

Walkthrough[edit]

Exterior[edit]

The cabin and chicken coop

The property is adjacent to the road, beside a stone bridge that crosses a stream that is filled with tree trunks and branches as it flows from Lake Ilinalta. The stream splits the property and is rich in salmon. The wood mill and a butcher shack are on the east side of the stream, with a wooden bridge leading to the main cabin to the west. The butcher shack contains a table holding three haunches of raw venison and two joints of raw beef. Hanging from the ceiling is a rack holding two rabbits. Under the table are gory human remains, a possible clue as to the true nature of the mill's inhabitants. A troll skull is found on the ground as well.

The cabin is a single-story building with a small henhouse containing two chicken nests between the road and the cabin. In front of the cabin, near the stream, is a tanning rack, and opposite the henhouse are a wood chopping block and a grindstone. There are several tree stumps around the property, harvested by the mill. At the rear of the property is a large iron ore vein with a pickaxe stuck in the ground beside it, and not far to the north is another iron ore vein. Near this is a tree stump containing a dead rabbit, a fork, and a bottle of Nord mead. Numerous plants and flowers may be found around the property. If the Fishing creation is installed, some Fishing Supplies can be found near the shore, along with a salmon rack and two baskets. Fish from Temperate Lakes can be caught here.

When speaking with Hert, she will instruct you to grab an axe, as she will pay you for chopping wood.

Half-Moon Mill[edit]

The interior of the cabin

The cabin itself is a single-room dwelling. Upon entry there is a chair and barrels on the left, against the southeast wall. Against the southwest wall is a cupboard holding only clutter, with more food barrels next to it, mostly containing raw meats. A long wooden table and bench along the northwest wall holds a haunch of raw venison, a joint of raw beef, a bottle of Nord mead, and a bottle of alto wine, with two pine thrush eggs under the table. Hanging above the table are three garlic braids, two bunches of elves ear, and a bunch of frost mirriam. To the right is a hanging rack holding three rabbits and three pheasants. To the right of the table is a kettle containing three chicken's eggs and another food barrel. In the middle of the northwest wall is a fireplace with a cooking pot in front and a kettle on the hearth containing two leeks. In front of the fireplace is a round wooden table and two chairs. On this table is a bottle of wine, a bottle of alto wine, and two pheasant breasts. To the right of the fireplace, against the northwest wall, is a large owned double bed with an unlocked chest at the foot of it. Under the bed on the right-hand side is a copy of Physicalities of Werewolves. In the northern corner is an end table holding a bottle of Nord mead. Against the southeast wall is a wardrobe and dresser, both containing common clothes, with a food barrel between them.

Notes[edit]

  • You can buy sawn logsHF here after installing Hearthfire by speaking to Hert. You can also cut your own for free if she has given permission to do so.
  • When building a home with the Hearthfire add-on, your map marker may send you here to buy sawn logs. If Hert is dead, you may simply go to another wood mill to purchase lumber to clear the objective.
  • To the northwest is a memorial pillar with a flag and the corpse of a Dark Elf lying beside it.
  • In the northwest corner of Lake Ilinalta, north from Half-Moon Mill, is an unmarked fisherman's camp with a copy of the one-handed skill book Fire and Darkness.
  • Although the owners here are vampires themselves, they will still become hostile towards you if you are a blood-starved vampire.

Bugs[edit]

  • The ownership on the double bed here is incorrectly assigned to RiverwoodSvensHouseFaction instead of HalfMoonMillFaction, preventing Hert and Hern from using it.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
