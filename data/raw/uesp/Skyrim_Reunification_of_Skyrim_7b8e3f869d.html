Quick Walkthrough[edit]

Regain each of Skyrim's Holds (depending upon your choices during Season Unending):

  1. Regain The Pale.
  2. Regain The Rift.
  3. Regain Winterhold.
  4. Regain Hjaalmarch.
  5. Regain Falkreath.
  6. Regain The Reach.
  7. Regain Eastmarch.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

After defending Whiterun from the Stormcloaks during the previous quest, Battle for Whiterun, the Whiterun Hold will finally side with the Imperial Legion. Go to Castle Dour to report your success to General Tullius. He will award you your first promotion, the rank of Quaestor, along with a leveled enchanted weapon and order you to meet Legate Rikke at one of many Imperial Military Camp for your next assignment; depending on your progress through the Main Quest the Camp you will be sent to will change as follows.

Quest Options[edit]

This quest governs the overall progress of the Civil War for the Imperial Legion. It serves as an "umbrella quest", taking you through a series of individual quests that you receive as it progresses.

The overall goal of the quest is to conquer, for the Empire, each hold of Skyrim that is currently under Stormcloak control. You will accomplish this one hold at a time, by taking control of the military fort in each, ending with one final siege on the enemy's capital, Windhelm, in the order of the following table. The holds to be conquered can be altered by negotiation during the optional quest Season Unending and therefore only a subset of the following quests needs to be completed.

  • Without doing Season Unending, only the Stormcloak-controlled holds in the following table need to be conquered.
  • By doing Season Unending:
    • The initially allied-controlled holds only need to be reconquered if given to the enemy during negotiation.
    • The initially enemy-controlled holds will not need to be conquered if taken from the enemy during negotiation.
    • The initially enemy-controlled holds will need to be conquered if not taken during negotiation.
    • If you progress it to the point of a truce, any active quest in the series will auto-complete and cannot be restarted. If that quest involves a Military Fort, the related hold is transferred to allied control.
    • This quest will be put on hold, until you complete Dragonslayer.

Reporting for duty[edit]

Between each hold conquest, you will be prompted to report to your faction leader for a reward and the next hold assignment. Then, to get your orders, you need to speak to Legate Rikke at the Military Camp you are assigned to with the "Reporting for duty." option.

The Pale[edit]

The Pale does not need to be regained if it was given to the Imperials during Season Unending

Report to Legate Rikke at the Pale Imperial Camp.

  • A False Front: Find and deliver some forged documents to the Stormcloaks.
  • The Battle for Fort Dunstad: Take Fort Dunstad for the Imperials. (radiant)

Reward: Praefect rank with leveled sword (see Bugs).

The Rift[edit]

The Rift does not need to be regained if it was given to the Imperials during Season Unending

Report to Legate Rikke at the Rift Imperial Camp.

  • Compelling Tribute: Use a corrupt steward to gain intelligence on the Stormcloaks. (radiant)
  • The Battle for Fort Greenwall: Take Fort Greenwall for the Imperials. (radiant) ( 10 pts/Bronze)

Reward: Tribune rank with leveled shield (see Bugs).

Winterhold[edit]

Winterhold does not need to be regained if it was given to the Imperials during Season Unending

Report to Legate Rikke at the Winterhold Imperial Camp.

  • Rescue from Fort Kastav: Take Fort Kastav for the Imperials and rescue the prisoners within. (radiant)

Reward: Legate rank with leveled armor.

Hjaalmarch[edit]

Hjaalmarch only needs to be regained if it was given to the Stormcloaks during Season Unending

Report to Legate Rikke at the Hjaalmarch Imperial Camp.

  • The Battle for Fort Snowhawk: Retake Fort Snowhawk for the Imperials. (radiant)

Reward: leveled sword.

Falkreath Hold[edit]

Falkreath Hold only needs to be regained if it was given to the Stormcloaks during Season Unending

Report to Legate Rikke at the Falkreath Imperial Camp.

  • The Battle for Fort Neugrad: Retake Fort Neugrad for the Imperials. (radiant)

Reward: leveled sword.

The Reach[edit]

The Reach only needs to be regained if it was given to the Stormcloaks during Season Unending

Report to Legate Rikke at the Reach Imperial Camp.

  • The Battle for Fort Sungard: Retake Fort Sungard for the Imperials. (radiant) ( 10 pts/Bronze — only if The Battle for Fort Greenwall did not need to be completed)

Reward: leveled sword.

Eastmarch[edit]

Eastmarch always needs to be regained as it hosts the enemy's capital

Report to Legate Rikke at the Eastmarch Imperial Camp.

  • The Battle for Fort Amol: Prepare for the final assault on Windhelm by taking Fort Amol for the Imperials. (radiant)
  • Battle for Windhelm: Conquer the Stormcloak capital and bring Ulfric to justice! ( 30 pts/Silver)

Reward: leveled enchanted sword.

After completing The Battle for Fort Amol, return to Rikke who will tell you "We're taking the city. Meet the soldiers gathering for the attack. Move it!". This will automatically start Battle for Windhelm. As part of that quest, General Tullius will have a short dialogue with Ulfric Stormcloak, who will request the Dragonborn to be the one who kills him, as it will "make for a better song." General Tullius won't care whether you kill Ulfric or not, just as long as he's dead. If you choose to kill Ulfric, General Tullius will tell you to use his sword (which is a leveled enchanteed sword) to do the deed (this is not required though) and Battle for Windhelm will be complete once you deliver the final blow. If you choose to allow Tullius to do it, Battle for Windhelm will complete after that, when General will reward you with his leveled enchanted sword. Either way, Reunification of Skyrim will now be complete and you can join Rikke and Tullius outside to hear him delivering the final speech of the Civil War, promising increased pay and freedom for them to return to their families while giving compensation to families who have lost loved ones. Then, you're allowed to talk to Legate Rikke and General Tullius on what will happen next. Congratulations, Skyrim is now part of the Empire once again and peace is restored.

Notes[edit]

  • This quest is mainly used to move you between the other Imperial quests.
  • This quest and Liberation of Skyrim are different radiant versions of the same quest.
  • Season Unending can be started anytime from prior to starting the Civil War Quest-line to when there are at least 2 holds left for you to conquer for your allied side.
  • If, after Dragonslayer, depending on when the truce halted this quest, there is no quest mark, to resume it, you may need to speak to your faction leader.
  • It is not possible to receive and complete all the potential quests in the series, as negotiation during Season Unending is limited.
  • Trading the wrong holds during Season Unending can prevent obtaining some unique radiant quests from the series, ranks and War Hero achievement.
  • Ranks are not automatic, they are rewarded if the conditions are met: successful attack (not fleeing) during the related quest and not obtaining the hold by negotiation during Season Unending.
  • Ranks are progressive but not required: if you miss one, you can obtain the following one by completing the relative quest. So it is actually necessary to complete only Rescue from Fort Kastav to obtain the final rank of Legate.
  • Each rank comes with its leveled item reward.
  • To check your rank you can spam-speak to any allied soldier until he greets you by your current rank.
  • When a hold switches ownership, deposed Jarls and court/family are exiled, usually to their allegiance side's capital, and replaced by opposing ones, while related Civil War Forts are re-garrisoned by the new owner's soldiers.
  • At any point during this quest, enemy-controlled Civil War Forts are always garrisoned by enemy soldiers and will be garrisoned by allied ones when conquered. However initially allied-controlled ones remain inhabited by generic enemies, needing individual clearing.
  • Any allied military camp located within an enemy hold disappears upon conquest of that specific hold.
  • Legate Rikke will replace the Officer at any military camp for the duration of the hold assignment. If she is not there but the quest marker is upon an empty spot wait a few hours for her to arrive.
  • After every quest of this series (except for Battle for Windhelm), unmarked by in-game references, you can approach Legate Rikke for a medium leveled gold reward; this is not always recommended though, as this is a cause for the missing "Reporting for duty." dialogue option (see Bugs).

Bugs[edit]

  • Legate Rikke may not offer the "Reporting for duty." dialogue option.
    • This is not a proper bug, more like a soft-lock, sometimes deterministically caused by player's actions, and may or may not be solvable. Try the following fixes, listed from the most to least reliable. If you still can't fix it, you may need to identify when you triggered the bug and reload a save file previous to that point, then progress the quests in the series in a different order. The safest way to prevent the hardest version of this bug to happen at all is to do all the quests in the series straight away, skip Season Unending, do it before the Civil War Quest-line or between completed quests from this series, avoiding as much as possible Legate Rikke, as loading her cell can improperly set up the next quest, even if you don't speak to her.
    • If you progressed Season Unending to the point of a truce, Legate Rikke will offer dialogue only after you complete Dragonslayer.
    • Go to any indoor location and wait/rest for 30+ days. This allows all location cells to respawn.
    • If the fort that was supposed to be the target of the next mission is occupied by generic enemies, kill them. Then travel away and spend some time (e.g. wait for 24h).
    • Ask General Tullius "How goes the war?" until the phrase is no longer white (the indicator that he has something new to say).
    • Repeatedly talk to Rikke then run into her to interrupt her dialogue.
    • Go to jail and serve time.
    • Activate the Beast Form and wait until it wears out.
    • The console command movetoqt CWObj can be used to teleport you to the current objective of this quest, aka Legate Rikke.
    • If you manage to move Legate Rikke to another location (for example by making her follow you, casting a frenzy spell and pacifying afterwards), then letting her walk back to the current Imperial Camp can fix it. You can also attack her until she becomes hostile.
      • The console commands prid 198BB to target her then moveto player can be used to teleport her to your current location.
    • Triggering her hostility (for example by random enemies) and going outside the camp also resets Rikke.
    • Head to General Tullius. Talk to him, then exit out and open up the console command getstage cwobj to verify the variable is set to 1.00. If it is so, enter resetquest cwobj. Use getstage cwobj to confirm the quest stage is 0.00. Enter setstage cwobj 1.
    • The console command coc followed by location code of the imperial camp (for example coc MilitaryCampReachImperial) can be used to teleport to the chosen Military Camp.
    • It is possible the last fort you captured has not properly registered as Imperial. You can manually set ownership of the fort through console command. First enter an interior location, then use help 'Fort Dunstad' (replace with the fort you just tried to capture) to obtain its LCTN: variable (something like 00019182). Then, use GetKeywordDataForLocation %LCTN% CWOwner to check the owner of this location; if it shows 0 (nobody) or 2 (Stormcloaks), then use SetKeywordDataForLocation %LCTN% CWOwner 1. After that, the 'Reporting for duty' prompt should appear.
    • At the regain Eastmarch stage, if the "Reporting for duty." dialogue is not offered by Rikke to start Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Amol, travel to Fort Amol and kill every necromancer. After that, sleep in one of the beds and Stormcloaks should occupy the Fort. Use movetoqt CWObj to directly teleport to Rikke. If the option doesn't show, talk to Tullius in Castle Dour by entering the following command in the console coc SolitudeCastleDour, and then go to Rikke with the previous command.
    • If nothing else works, you may need complete the entire Reunification of Skyrim quest via the console command setstage cwobj 255. You cannot use the console to start the next quest within the series, as the bug applies to the over-arching quest.
    • This may be cleared by saving and resetting the entire system.
  • After The Battle for Fort Dunstad, if General Tullius will promote you to Praefect rank you are actually promoted to Tribune with related leveled shield. It is not possible to get the actual Praefect rank. Since you already are, this will also "skip" the actual promotion to Tribune after The Battle for Fort Greenwall.
    Mod Notes: mistake in the script TIF__000CE0E8: there is "3", should be "2".
  • The war strategy maps located at military bases and command headquarters do not update as intended.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 1.2.1, addresses this issue. Each map needs to be visited twice for the correction to take effect in saved games.
  • After retaking Winterhold and upon talking to General Tullius, you will receive a piece of leveled armor. However, General Tullius may give you no further instructions and your journal has the text "Report to General Tullius". This prevents the quest line from continuing.
    • There is a way to force Tullius "What's next" option after he gave you the armor by setting ownership of Winterhold and Fort Kastav to the Empire manually through console command. First, use help 'Fort Kastav' to obtain LCTN: variable (something like 00019182) and help 'Winterhold' (there may be two of them). Then, use GetKeywordDataForLocation %LCTN% CWOwner for Fort Kastav and Winterhold LCTNs to check the owner of this locations; if any of them shows 2 (Stormcloaks), then use SetKeywordDataForLocation %LCTN% CWOwner 1. After that 'What's next' option should appear.
    • There may still be dialogue issues when you reach Winterhold. To prevent this, work through "Reunification of Skyrim" until you finish regaining Fort Greenwall. Before you report to General Tullius, finish the main questline. During "Season Unending", you will be given the option to put Winterhold on the negotiating table. It will most likely be the only option given, since you have control of the other holds thus far. If you choose that option, then all of the Imperial quests regarding Winterhold will be skipped and will automatically fall under Imperial control, without requiring you to attempt bugged conversations.
    • If there is still no dialogue option from Tullius to continue on in the quest after Winterhold, simply travel to the Eastmarch Imperial Camp and talk to Legate Rikke, that will proceed with the questline and she will give a reward as if you just completed a quest for her, even if it's the first time talking to her.

Notes
  • Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., <Alias=LocationHold>) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
  • Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
  • Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
  • If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
  • On the PC, it is possible to use the console to advance through the quest by entering setstage CWObj stage, where stage is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using resetquest CWObj.
