Corsairs are hostile NPCs unique to the Dainty Sload, a pirate ship docked near Solitude. They are under the command of the elusive Captain Volf, who is never encountered in-game.

The corsair patrolling the decks

These two Corsairs are male Nords on the Dainty Sload. They each carry a scimitar and a small amount of gold. They wear ragged trousers, Redguard boots, and fur bracers. One sits at a table in the downstairs kitchen-like room with the corsair in the section above. The other is at the top, guarding the entrance to the interior of the ship.
