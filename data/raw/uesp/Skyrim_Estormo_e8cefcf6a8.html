Estormo

Estormo is a Thalmor agent reporting to Ancano. He will confront you at the end of Labyrinthian during the related quest. When approached, he will converse with you briefly. Shortly afterward he will attack regardless of your response.

Estormo wears hooded Thalmor robes, Thalmor gloves, and Thalmor boots. He knows a number of spells, namely Stoneflesh, Conjure Flame Atronach, Chain Lightning, Flame Cloak, Lightning Bolt, Fast Healing, and Steadfast Ward.

Related Quests[edit]

  • The Staff of Magnus: Retrieve the Staff of Magnus.

Quest-Related Events[edit]

The Staff of Magnus[edit]

He will barge through the metal doors to confront you and block the only exit, saying:

"So, you made it out of there alive. Ancano was right... you are dangerous. I'm afraid I'll have to take that Staff from you now. Ancano wants it kept safe... oh, and he wants you dead. Nothing personal."
Get out of my way.
"You fool, you don't stand a chance."
I have no quarrel with you.
"I am afraid you do."

After this he will become hostile.

Bugs[edit]

  • Due to his low agression, if you sheathe your weapon, he will accept your yield.
