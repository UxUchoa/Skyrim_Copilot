Quick Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Speak to Maven Black-Briar.
  2. Meet Mallus Maccius at The Bannered Mare.
  3. Speak to Sabjorn in the Honningbrew Meadery.
  4. Engage in some industrial sabotage and witness the mead tasting failure.
  5. Acquire the promissory note and return to Maven.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

Preparation[edit]

As you will be battling Ataxia-bearing skeevers, resist disease or cure disease potions or enchantments are useful. Furthermore, given the vermin's speed, a fast, light melee weapon is advised. Given Hamelyn's magical ability, resist magic, shock, fire, and frost spells or enchantments are useful.

Black-Briar Business[edit]

Now that you're a full member of the Thieves Guild, you've been told that Maven Black-Briar wants to see you. You are likely to find her on the second floor of The Bee and Barb. After a condescending greeting, Maven will solicit your cooperation in putting her competitor, the Honningbrew Meadery, out of business. She will also ask you to perform some industrial espionage to understand how Honningbrew could have started up their operation and achieved success so quickly. She will direct you to meet Mallus Maccius at The Bannered Mare in Whiterun for further instructions.

Meet Mallus Maccius[edit]

Mallus will tell you that Sabjorn, Honningbrew's owner, is planning to hold a special tasting for an important personage, and that you must poison the mead for the occasion. He suggests that you approach Sabjorn and offer to help him with a pest infestation, then use the poison he will give you to accomplish the deed. He instructs you to access the mead vats through a tunnel leading there from the meadery's basement. Although he claims you could alternatively pick the lock on the brewery door, you can't, as it requires a key which is only found inside the boilery. His own motives are quite simple: he'll be given control of the meadery from Maven once Sabjorn is out of the way.

Infestation Elimination[edit]

Sabjorn will quickly accept your offer to help and provide you with the required poison as well as the key to the Meadery. If you successfully persuade him, he'll even give you half your pay upfront (500 gold) - although the way things turn out, this is all you'll get from him.

Sabjorn will also, if asked, talk about how he gets free labor from Maccius by trapping him into a debt he can't afford to repay. In the basement, you'll easily find the tunnel Maccius pointed as the infestation's source. Fight your way past the tough Venomfang skeevers as well as a few frostbite spiders, taking care to avoid bear traps and tripwire-activated swinging maces along the way.

You'll emerge in a large chamber where a mad bandit wizard named Hamelyn is guarded by several vermin. Try to use sneak to avoid fighting them all the same time, as Hamelyn can cast powerful destruction spells and is especially lethal if he catches you in a weakened condition. Poison the skeever nest next to Hamelyn's alchemy lab and don't miss the Sneak skill book Three Thieves lying atop a chest nearby. Press on, avoiding two bear traps, and you'll arrive at the door leading to the boilery.

Mellow Mead[edit]

Head up the stairs to a balcony overlooking the room and head towards the lone brewing vat on the far side. Poison it and exit using the Honningbrew Brewhouse key hanging on a hook on the right side of the door. Head back inside the meadery and you'll meet either Commander Caius or Sinmir (if the Stormcloaks have taken Whiterun) tasting the mead - Sabjorn will not pay you what he owes you yet (citing the need to go through with the mead tasting first), so if you got your 500 gold earlier, that's as good as it gets. The taster will sense something wrong with the drink and blame it on poor sanitary conditions. For this perceived offense, he will place Mallus Maccius in charge of the meadery and haul Sabjorn to jail at swordpoint - so you never DO get the rest of the pay (or any of it, if you didn't talk him into giving half in advance).

Once Sabjorn has departed, speak to Mallus about gathering business intelligence for Maven and he'll hand over the key to Sabjorn's dresser. Head upstairs and steal the promissory note from the dresser. An expert-locked door nearby will open to reveal a room with some silver ingots, a silver necklace, some coin purses, and the Honningbrew Decanter, a miscellaneous unique item that can be sold to Delvin Mallory for a fair price. Be sure to read the Alchemy skill book A Game at Dinner, which is found on the table closest to the door.

Mysterious Missive[edit]

Head back to the Bee and Barb in Riften and report your success to Maven, who is frustrated by her inability to identify the note's author. When you point out the author's symbol is the same as that on the bill of sale previously found in the Goldenglow Estate safe she suggests taking it to Brynjolf. Provided you didn't kill anyone but Hamelyn, she will then pay you with a leveled enchanted sword or dagger. Once you're back in the Cistern, Brynjolf will muse that the identical symbol points to someone attempting to drive a wedge between Maven and the guild, and will send you off to Mercer to continue your search. Mallus will now also be available as a fence.

Notes[edit]

  • If you report back to Sabjorn while having a bounty of at least 1000 gold in Whiterun, the captain of the guard will try to arrest you on sight. Remaining out of sight and talking to Sabjorn is virtually impossible, so it's advised to bring the captain to his knees and quickly talk to Sabjorn, then remain undetected until the winetasting scene starts. It is then recommended to stay hidden until the captain leaves with Sabjorn. Alternatively, just pay off your bounty before you head back to the Meadery.
  • If you sneak past Hamelyn and don't kill him, Mallus will still talk as if you have killed him. Even if you return to Honningbrew Meadery Basement, after the quest is over, Hamelyn will be there.
  • Despite Mallus suggesting it as an alternate point of entry, you can't enter the brewhouse until after you make your way through the tunnels for the first time. The front door is unpickable and can only be unlocked using the key that you find inside the boilery.
  • Right after the persuasion check, you can exit out of the conversation, allowing you to restart the conversation and it offers a chance to repeat the check indefinitely and train Speech.

Bugs[edit]

  • If you break into Honningbrew Meadery before receiving this quest, it will not become available at the end of Loud and Clear.
    • The Official Skyrim Patch, version 1.4, addresses this issue. However, if this bug has already been triggered on a prior version to 1.4, then the quest will remain unavailable on that save file even with the patch installed.
    • If you cannot obtain the quest, you can force it to start using the console with startquest TG03, followed by setstage TG03 10.
  • If you approach the nest in the first person view, the "activate nest" button needed to poison the nest may fail to appear.
    • Changing to the third person view can sometimes resolve this.
    • You can also advance this manually using the console command setstage TG03 45.
  • After completing the quest Loud and Clear, if you do not ask Brynjolf why Maven Black-Briar is requesting a meeting, the quest will not become available. Also Brynjolf will not speak to you, saying only that he has "important tasks to be doing".
    • The Official Skyrim Patch, version 1.4, fixes this bug.
    • If you cannot obtain the quest, you can force it to start using the console with startquest TG03, followed by setstage TG03 10.
  • If speaking to Maven outside of the Bee and Barb, while she is headed to the inn, she will simply stand in the same spot day and night until the quest is over.
  • Eimar has some lines at the Bannered Mare that don't make sense for him to use while he's there. One wasn't conditioned to the location he's in, and the other was using an incorrect function to check if this quest is finished or not.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch, version 4.2.7, fixes this bug.
  • If Whiterun Hold has a Stormcloak government at the time you do this quest, then Sinmir is supposed to be present for the tasting rather than Commander Caius. However, there are multiple bugs that can interfere with this.
    • Activating this quest but not finishing it before activating Liberation of Skyrim may cause Caius to be present for the tasting instead of Sinmir, where he will fail to drink the mead, causing the quest to stall.
      • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
      • You can also transport Sinmir to the Meadery using the console commands prid 813ba and moveto player.
      • You can also skip this scene using the console command setstage TG03 80. This will progress the quest to where you have to talk to Mallus Maccius again.
    • Another variation of this bug can sometimes place Caius at the tasting even if the Stormcloaks have already gained Whiterun before you activate this quest. Fortunately, this variation does not prevent the quest from progressing as intended.
  • If you successfully destroy three beehives and complete the Loud and Clear quest, but go back to Goldenglow Estate and destroy more beehives before you speak to Maven Black-Briar, Maven will speak to you as if you failed the earlier quest.
  • After you finish the quest, the Honningbrew Mead barrels outside of the meadery may not be changed to Black-Briar Mead barrels.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 2.1.1, addresses this issue. Proper enable parents have been added to the existing barrels as well as the created replacement barrels. The barrels were also missing their ownership settings.

  • Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., <Alias=LocationHold>) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
