Karthwasten Miner's Barracks

Karthwasten Miner's Barracks is the collective housing area for local miners in Karthwasten, located between Enmon's House and Fenn's Gulch Mine in the northwestern part of town.

Local miners Belchimac, Lash gra-Dushnikh, and Ragnar reside here. It consists of one zone, Miner's Barracks.

Miner's Barracks[edit]

Miner's Barracks interior

This housing area is well stocked and set up for the local miners, with all the required supplies and amenities in the small space. On the south side of the building is a single bed along the east wall, owned by Lash gra-Dushnikh. A respawning food barrel sits in the southeast corner, next to a non-respawning cupboard holding various kitchenwares. The west wall hosts a dining table which holds two pine thrush eggs, a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of Nord mead, and a bucket containing three potatoes. Above the table are some hanging ingredients, including some dried frost mirriam, two portions of dried elves ear, and three garlic braids. Next to the hanging ingredients is a hanging rack with three freshly caught rabbits and three pheasants. A respawning food barrel sits on the floor to the right, next to a set of shelves holding one bottle of wine and two bottles of alto wine on top, a copy of the book Songs of the Return, Vol 19 on the middle shelf, and two bottles of alto wine and three bottles of wine on the bottom shelf.

The fireplace is situated farther to the right on the western wall, with a copy of Olaf and the Dragon sitting on the right side of its base. A small table in front of the fireplace holds one bottle each of wine and alto wine, along with one loaf and one piece of bread. On the north side of the room, a non-respawning end table sits at the center, between Ragnar's single bed on the left, and Belchimac's single bed on the right. At the foot of each bed is a small non-respawning chest, and the end table holds a single bottle of Nord mead. Finally, on the eastern wall, just north of the front door, a respawning food barrel sits next to a non-respawning dresser, which holds three bottles of Nord mead and two pickaxes. Above the dresser are three hanging garlic braids, some dried elves ear, and some dried frost mirriam.

Notes[edit]

  • The front door has a novice lock, the key to which is carried by Belchimac, Lash gra-Dushnikh, and Ragnar.
  • A stone quarryHF can be found behind the building, and a grindstone can be found on the front porch, with a woodcutter's axe lying nearby.
