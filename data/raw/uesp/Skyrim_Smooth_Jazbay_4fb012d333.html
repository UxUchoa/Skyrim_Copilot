Quick Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Speak to Avrusa Sarethi.
  2. Collect 20 Jazbay Grapes.
  3. Return to Avrusa Sarethi.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

Avrusa Sarethi needs a large amount of jazbay grapes in order to cultivate nirnroots. She's running low and unable to search out the ingredient herself, as she is preoccupied with her energetic sister and farm chores. She asks you to hunt down twenty grapes for her current nirnroot crop. As Avrusa notes, jazbay grapes are somewhat hard to come by. The plants are indigenous to the volcanic tundra region of Eastmarch, and while desolate, the tundra is fairly easy to traverse with a few lesser animals and non-hostile giants.

There are a few locations where it can be found in greater quantities than usual. Along with the quantities available, they are:

  • 49 around Steamcrag Camp (Eastmarch)
  • 47 around Witchmist Grove (Eastmarch)
  • 35 around Eldergleam Sanctuary (Eastmarch)
  • 33 around the Atronach Stone (Eastmarch)
  • 31 around Bonestrewn Crest (Eastmarch)
  • 26 around Kynesgrove (Eastmarch), especially along the road heading southeast
  • 25 around Mistwatch (Eastmarch)
  • 24 around Broken Limb Camp (Eastmarch)
  • 23 around Cronvangr Cave (Eastmarch)
  • 22 around Riverside Shack (Eastmarch)

Once you have collected twenty samples, head back to Avrusa who will thank you for your aid and reward you with five random potions. You can return to Sarethi Farm periodically to harvest Nirnroot and Crimson Nirnroot.

Bugs[edit]

  • This quest may leave behind the objective to collect the jazbay grapes due to calling the objective completion before the final item swap is done.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch, version 3.0.1, fixes this bug.
