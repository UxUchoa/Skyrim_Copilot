Nord Dockworker

The East Empire Dockworkers are employees found at the East Empire Company Warehouse. The Breton dockworker wears farm clothes and boots. The Nord dockworker wears a belted tunic and boots. Neither of the dockworkers carry anything in their inventory.

Notes[edit]

  • The dockworkers are replaced by East Empire wardens when the quest Scoundrel's Folly is started.
  • Both of the dockworkers are members of the warrior class, which determines the level of their skills.
  • The Breton dockworker does not have a voice type.
  • According to their respective refs, the Nord dockworker's name is Slading and the Breton's is Jonne.
  • Neither of the dockworkers have any AI packages, resulting in them just standing in place unless provoked.
