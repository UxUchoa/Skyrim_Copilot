Radiant Options[edit]

This is a radiant quest which you can receive from any of the following people.

Walkthrough[edit]

If you talk to one of the quest givers while having alcohol in your inventory, you will have the option to share a drink with them. This then makes them your friend, meaning that you can take low value items that belong to them and that they may occasionally give you a gift.

Notes[edit]

  • This quest does not have any stages or objectives.
  • This quest can be repeated indefinitely.
  • Certain expensive drinks will not give you the option to share a drink.
    • Version 1.3.3 of the Unofficial Skyrim Patch partially fixes this bug.
  • Because this is a favor quest which affects the quest giver's disposition, it will count towards the "Help the People" part of the thane quest for the quest giver's hold.
  • Because this quest is unmarked, completing it won't help with progress towards the "Hero of the People" Achievement.
  • Only drunks with the "MaleDrunk" voicetype can be quest givers for this quest.
