Other Information

Morality No Crime Aggression Very Aggressive
Voice Type CrDraugrVoice
Faction(s) DraugrFaction
A Frost Spirit

The Frost Spirit will be encountered in Labyrinthian during the quest The Staff of Magnus. It is found in a room with a blue-glowing ice crystal. Approaching the crystal will spawn the spirit.

Related Quests[edit]

  • The Staff of Magnus: Retrieve the Staff of Magnus.
