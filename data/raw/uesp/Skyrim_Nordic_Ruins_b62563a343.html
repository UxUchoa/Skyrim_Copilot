Concept art of Nordic Ruins

This page lists all the Nordic ruins in Skyrim.

 Angarvunde — A medium-sized Nordic ruin west of Riften containing draugr. (map)
 Ansilvund — A medium-sized Nordic ruin north of Riften containing draugr and necromancers led by Lu'ah Al-Skaven. (map)
 Arcwind Point — A small outdoor Nordic ruin south of Ivarstead containing draugr, skeletons and, after Dragon Rising, a dragon. (map)
 Bleak Falls Barrow — A medium-sized Nordic ruin west of Riverwood containing bandits, draugr, and skeevers. (map)
 Blind Cliff Bastion — A medium-sized cave southwest of Karthwasten along the banks of the Karth River containing Forsworn. (map)
 Bloodskal BarrowDB — A small Nordic ruin north of Raven Rock containing reavers. (map)
 Bromjunaar Sanctuary — A hidden location reached by equipping the wooden mask while inside a building in Labyrinthian.
 Champion's RestCC — A medium-sized Nordic ruin located in the Rift, east of Shor's Stone. (map)
 Dead Men's Respite — A small Nordic ruin southwest of Morthal containing draugr and frostbite spiders. (map)
 Deepwood Redoubt — A large Nordic ruin west of Dragon Bridge containing Forsworn. (map)
 Dustman's Cairn — A medium-sized Nordic ruin northwest of Whiterun containing Silver Hands, draugr, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. (map)
 Folgunthur — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Solitude containing draugr, frostbite spiders, and the draugr Mikrul Gauldurson. (map)
 Forelhost — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Riften containing Dragon Cultists, draugr, skeevers, and the dragon priest Rahgot. (map)
 Frostmere Crypt — A medium-sized Nordic ruin south-southwest of Dawnstar containing bandits and the unique wispmother the Pale Lady. (map)
 Geirmund's Hall — A small Nordic ruin east of Ivarstead containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the draugr Sigdis Gauldurson. (map)
 Gyldenhul BarrowDB — A small Nordic ruin east of Skaal Village containing the spirit and treasure of Haknir Death-Brand. (map)
 Hag Rock Redoubt — A small Nordic ruin south of Markarth containing Forsworn. (map)
 Hag's End — A small Nordic ruin west of Dragon Bridge containing a hagraven, hags, and witches. (map)
 High Gate Ruins — A medium-sized Nordic ruin west of Dawnstar containing draugr and the dragon priest Vokun. (map)
 Hillgrund's Tomb — A small Nordic ruin southwest of Windhelm containing draugr. (map)
 Ironbind Barrow — A small Nordic ruin southwest of Winterhold containing draugr and the tomb of Warlord Gathrik. (map)
 Kilkreath Ruins — A large ruined temple west of Solitude containing corrupted shades created by the necromancer Malkoran. (map)
 Kolbjorn BarrowDB — A small Nordic ruin southeast of Raven Rock containing draugr, spiders, and the dragon priest Ahzidal. (map)
 Korvanjund — A medium-sized Nordic ruin northeast of Whiterun containing draugr. (map)
 Labyrinthian — A large Nordic ruin southeast of Morthal containing draugr, skeletons, trolls, wisps, and the dragon priest Morokei. (map)
 Lost Valley Redoubt — A massive Nordic ruin inhabited by Forsworn and hagravens. (map)
 Ragnvald — A medium-sized Nordic ruin north of Markarth containing draugr and the dragon priest Otar the Mad. (map)
 Rannveig's Fast — A small Nordic ruin east-northeast of Rorikstead containing subjugated ghosts and Sild the Warlock. (map)
 Saarthal — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southwest of Winterhold. (map)
Shalidor's Maze — A small Nordic ruin east of Labyrinthian containing atronachs and a dremora. (map)
 Shriekwind Bastion — A small Nordic ruin north of Falkreath containing vampires and skeletons. (map)
 Shroud Hearth Barrow — A medium-sized Nordic ruin east of Ivarstead containing draugr and skeletons. (map)
 Silent Moons Camp — A small Nordic ruin northwest of Whiterun containing bandits. (map)
 Silverdrift Lair — A small Nordic ruin south-southeast of Dawnstar containing draugr. (map)
 Sivdur's RespiteCC — A Nordic ruin located north of Windhelm. (map)
Skuldafn — A large Nordic ruin with a portal to Sovngarde containing draugr, dragons, and a dragon priest. (map)
 Snow Veil Sanctum — A medium-sized Nordic ruin north-northeast of Windhelm containing draugr. (map)
 Twilight Sepulcher — The location of the Pilgrim's Path and main entrance to Nocturnal's Ebonmere portal, resting place for the Skeleton Key. (map)
 Ustengrav — A medium-sized Nordic ruin northeast of Morthal containing bandits, warlocks, draugr, skeletons, and frostbite spiders. (map)
 Vahlok's TombDB — A small Nordic ruin south-southeast of Thirsk Mead Hall containing corrupted shades, draugr, and the dragon priest Vahlok the Jailor. (map)
 Valthume — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Markarth containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the dragon priest Hevnoraak. (map)
 Volskygge — A medium-sized Nordic ruin west of Solitude containing bandits, draugr, frostbite spiders, and the dragon priest Volsung. (map)
 Volunruud — A medium-sized Nordic ruin southeast of Morthal containing draugr and the ghost of Kvenel the Tongue. (map)
 White Ridge BarrowDB — A medium-sized Nordic ruin in the northern part of Solstheim inhabited by bandits and spiders. (map)
 Yngol Barrow — A small Nordic ruin northeast of Windhelm containing the shade of an ancient Nord warrior. (map)
 Ysgramor's Tomb — A small Nordic ruin northwest of Winterhold containing Companion ghosts, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. (map)

Notes[edit]

  • Version 2.0.9 of the Unofficial Skyrim Patch replaces Yngvild's cave map icon with the one used for Nordic ruins.
