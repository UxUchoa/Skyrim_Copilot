Cairine

Cairine is a Breton citizen living in The Warrens, the hub for all the poor and sick of Markarth. She used to work at the Smelter in Markarth, but is now too sick to do so. Instead, she spends her whole day sitting on the ground of the Warrens holding her stomach in pain.

She wears a set of ragged robes and a matching pair of ragged boots. She only carries an iron dagger.

Dialogue[edit]

When approached she can say:

"You don't want to be here. No one wants to."
"Don't know anyone here doesn't have Rattles or Ataxia."

Quest-Related Events[edit]

New Allegiances[edit]

If you are part of the Volkihar vampiresDG, you will eventually get tasked by Vingalmo to turn a citizen into a vampire. Cairine is one of these possible victims. He will describe her as:

"A pathetic creature in her present condition, but the gift will embolden her. And her lowly state suits her to being our eyes and ears in Markarth."

You can transform her:

"Something's happened, I can't quite remember... I don't feel... right... I'm going to lay down... for awhile..."

Once you have turned her, she will be very grateful and greet you with:

"Great gift of blood you've given me. Blood that boils in my veins. Gives me strength. You can use my coffin whenever you like."
"Blood of the sick. Blood of the weak. Blood of the unsuspecting noble lord. Savor each flavor. Unsuspecting, delicious prey."
"I keep an eye on their comings and goings. No one suspects poor Cairine. Poor, poor Cairine. Ha!"
"The darkness hums and we sing its praises. Sweet melodies. Sweet like the water that flows through their veins."

Rumors[edit]

Smelter worker Omluag will mention her when asked who works alongside him:

"A few people in the Warrens used to work here. Degaine broke some bones a while back. Eltrys quit. Cairine is too sick."

Notes[edit]

  • Despite what Cairine says, no one in the Warrens has Rattles or Ataxia. These diseases are only present on wild animals in Skyrim, unlike Oblivion, where beggars also carried such diseases.
  • Cairine herself does not have any kind of disease you can catch either. Her behavior is caused by her AI package MarkarthCairineWarrenIdlePackage which is linked to a marker, resulting in her sitting on the ground with the "wounded" animation. She does this around the clock because her package has no set duration, despite the fact she has other packages set up in her schedule, which all get overridden.

Bugs[edit]

  • When "killed" for the first time, Cairine will lie still on the floor, yet she is still able to blink, detect you and engage in conversation.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Patch, version 1.0, fixes this bug.
  • You might not be able to turn Cairine into a vampire, due to her never standing and her sitting animation preventing you from feeding on her. ?
    • If you select her in the console and type moveto player, she will be standing and you can proceed normally.
