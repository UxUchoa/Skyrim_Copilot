This page lists all of the Miscellaneous Quests for the town of Ivarstead.

Giver Location Quest Giver Quest Objective(s) Requirement
Fellstar Farm Boti Gather Wheat: Cabbage, Potato, Wheat
Ivarstead Narfi Quest all Beggars Have: Take pity on a beggar. (radiant)
Ivarstead Narfi The Straw that Broke: Discover what is plaguing Narfi's mind.
  • Ask Wilhelm about Reyda
  • Locate Reyda's remains
  • Bring Reyda's Necklace to Narfi
Klimmek's House Klimmek Climb the Steps: Deliver a load of goods up to High Hrothgar for Klimmek.
  • Bring the supplies to High Hrothgar
  • Return to Klimmek
Vilemyr Inn Temba Wide-Arm Chop Wood: Get paid to chop firewood. (radiant)
Vilemyr Inn Temba Wide-Arm Grin and Bear It: Give ten bear pelts to Temba Wide-Arm.
  • Gather 10 bear pelts for Temba Wide-Arms [sic]
  • Return to Temba Wide-Arms [sic]
Vilemyr Inn Wilhelm Lifting the Shroud: Investigate the haunted barrow outside of Ivarstead.
  • Investigate Shroud Hearth Barrow
  • Bring Wyndelius's Journal to Wilhelm
