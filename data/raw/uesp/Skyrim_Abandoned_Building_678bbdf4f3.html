Abandoned Building

The Abandoned Building is the home of Bralsa Drel and Rirns Llervu in Raven Rock.

It is on the waterfront west of Glover Mallory's House, being the last building on the waterfront along the path to the Earth Stone to the west. Until the stone is cleansed during the quest Cleansing the Stones, the building is uninhabited. After the stone is cleansed, Bralsa Drel and Rirns Llervu will take residence.

Walkthrough[edit]

Exterior[edit]

To the left of the entrance are two unusable barrels. To the right of the entrance is a lit brazier and three scathecraw plants. Along the back of the building on the waterfront are three crates and more unusable barrels. The entrance is unlocked.

Abandoned Building[edit]

First room
Second room
Upper floor

The entrance leads into a squalid building littered with ash piles, cobwebs, and tankards and other minor items messily strewn about. In the middle of the room is a bottle of Nord mead on the floor. In the southeast corner is another bottle of Nord mead on an ash pile. Along the west wall is a sideboard holding a bottle of Nord mead, a bottle of sujamma, a copy of Confessions of a Dunmer Skooma Eater, and a bottle of matze, with a copy of The Poison Song, Book IV on the wall ledge behind the dresser. Along the north wall is another bottle of matze on the wall ledge, a doorway to the next room, and two more bottles of Nord mead on the wall ledge and a crate, respectively.

The next room is circular, and features the stairs leading up to the upper floor. Along the curved west wall are two owned bed rolls, with a bottle of flin, a bottle of sujamma, a bottle of shein, a loaf of bread, and a wedge of goat cheese between them, and a crate holding another loaf of bread and a copy of The Five Far Stars. Along the north wall beneath the stairs are two ingredient urns and an unusable barrel. The small circular room on the upper floor contains two sacks, a meat barrel, three unusable barrels, and four crates.
