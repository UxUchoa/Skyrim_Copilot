Radiant Options[edit]

This is a radiant quest which you can receive from any of the following people.

Eastmarch Windhelm, Candlehearth Hall Angrenor Once-Honored
Eastmarch Windhelm, Candlehearth Hall Silda the Unseen
Haafingar Solitude Dervenin†

†Dervenin will no longer be available for this quest once The Mind of Madness is finished.

Walkthrough[edit]

If you talk to one of the quest givers, they will ask you for some gold. Giving them one gold piece completes the quest and rewards you with the blessing The Gift of Charity.

Notes[edit]

  • This quest does not have any stages or objectives.
  • This quest can be repeated indefinitely.
  • You will not have the option to give a beggar gold for four in-game hours after you last gave a beggar gold, or if you have no gold.
  • Because this is a favor quest which affects the quest giver's disposition, it will count toward the "Help the People" part of the thane quest for the quest giver's hold.
