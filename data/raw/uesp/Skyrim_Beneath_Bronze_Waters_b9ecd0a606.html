Quick Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Travel to Bronze Water Cave.
  2. Read the fisherman's journal.
  3. Find the Dwarven fishing rod schematic in Alftand Ruined Tower.
  4. Bring the Dwarven fishing rod schematic to Calcelmo.
  5. Use the dwarven fishing rod to catch two aetherium dynamo cores near Bronze Water Cave.
  6. Insert the aetherium dynamo cores into the dynamo actuators.

Detailed Walkthrough[edit]

The schematics

This quest can be started after The Ultimate Feast has been completed by talking to Viriya:

Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?
"Of course. It's all in this note."

She will then hand you a bounty that asks you to investigate the outside of Bronze Water Cave. As you arrive at the cave, you will have to pick up and read the journal that is on a plank of wood. The journal mentions a fishing rod of Dwarven make that might be found in Alftand Ruined Tower.

Travel to Alftand Ruined Tower and after a short while inside you will find the dead body of Jorid, the fisherman mentioned in the journal. Press the button on the desk near the body, but be careful as it will activate flamethrowers ahead of you while opening the door.

Head through the now open door, but be wary of the pressure plates on the floor as they will activate spears. You will find the Dwarven Fishing Rod Schematic on a desk. Once obtained you will need to take the schematics to Calcelmo and speak to him:

I investigated Bronze Water Cave and discovered a dormant Dwarven mechanism.
"Yes..."
Fishermen saw glowing objects that may activate it, but couldn't retrieve them.
"Hmmm... No, that's probably not of any use."
They believed using a Dwarven fishing rod might help. Here are the schematics.
"Ah, yes! There it is! Thank you so much."
In exchange for the schematic, could you build a Dwarven fishing rod for me?
"Of course."

You will then need to wait 24 hours and return to Calcelmo:

I've returned. Is the Dwarven fishing rod complete?
"I've been holding onto this for you. Here! I hope you like it."
Consider the bounty paid in full. I'll try using this near Bronze Water Cave.
"Oh, now, do be careful..."

At this point you will not only acquire a Dwarven Fishing Rod but unlock the ability to create Dwarven Fishing Rods yourself, just in case you misplace the first one.

The chest rises.

Head back to Bronze Water Cave where you will have to fish in two spots for the Aetherium Dynamo Cores. Once you have fished them up, place them into the dynamo actuators. This will cause a chest to rise from the water, but will also activate some dwarven spider workers by the cave entrance. Once the dwarven spider workers have been dealt with, open the chest to find the pair of enchanted axes Irkngthand War Axe of Fire and Irkngthand War Axe of Freezing, as well as other typical random chest treasure.

It will also unlock the ability to craft the Irkngthand War Axe, a slightly superior brand of one-handed Dwarven War Axe, which appears to be basically as strong as a Glass War Axe but only requires the Dwarven Smithing perk. This is the base item of which you just received the two enchanted examples.

Bugs[edit]

  • The Dwemer spider ambush outside of Bronze Water Cave can be set off prematurely due to the spiders not actually being disabled prior to Beneath Bronze Waters. If set off prematurely, the quest will then be impossible to start because the spiders are required for the quest.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch, version 4.2.9a, fixes this bug.
  • The trigger box for Kinthal's Saints bandit camp at Bronze Water Cave may attempt to disable itself even though it's controlled by an enable parent.
    • The Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch, version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The DisableWhenDone flag has been removed as it's also flagged DoOnce and won't trigger again anyway.

Notes
  • Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., <Alias=LocationHold>) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
