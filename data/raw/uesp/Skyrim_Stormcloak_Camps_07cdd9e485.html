Winterhold Imperial Camp

This page lists all the military camps that are established for the Skyrim Civil War. Military forts are listed on a separate page.

Imperial Camps[edit]

 Eastmarch Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in Eastmarch, located southeast of Mzulft and north of Stony Creek Cave. (map)
 Falkreath Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in Falkreath Hold, located east-northeast of Glenmoril Coven and north of Knifepoint Ridge. (map)
 Hjaalmarch Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in Hjaalmarch, located south of Ustengrav and northwest of Stonehills. (map)
 Pale Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in the Pale, located west of Dawnstar and northwest of Mzinchaleft. (map)
 Reach Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in the Reach, located south of Bthardamz and west of Karthwasten. (map)
 Rift Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in the Rift, located south of Ivarstead and west of the Ruins of Bthalft. (map)
 Whiterun Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in Whiterun Hold, located east of Rorikstead and far west of Whiterun. (map)
 Windhelm Military Camp — An Imperial camp set up east of Brandy-Mug Farm for the attack on Windhelm. (map)
 Winterhold Imperial Camp — An Imperial camp in Winterhold Hold, located southeast of Dawnstar and northwest of Frostflow Lighthouse. (map)

Stormcloak Camps[edit]

 Falkreath Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Falkreath Hold, located east of Helgen. (map)
 Haafingar Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Haafingar, located northeast of Dragon Bridge and south of the Statue to Meridia. (map)
 Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Hjaalmarch, located east of Ustengrav and west of Mzinchaleft. (map)
 Pale Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in the Pale, located southeast of Bronze Water Cave on the south shore of Lake Yorgrim. (map)
 Reach Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in the Reach, located west of Liar's Retreat and northeast of Karthwasten. (map)
 Rift Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in the Rift, located northeast of Sarethi Farm and west of Rift Watchtower. (map)
 Solitude Military Camp — A Stormcloak camp set up west of Katla's Farm for the attack on Solitude. (map)
 Whiterun Military Camp — A Stormcloak camp set up east of Pelagia Farm for the attack on Whiterun. (map)
 Whiterun Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Whiterun Hold, located south of the Ritual Stone and east of White River Watch. (map)
 Winterhold Stormcloak Camp — A Stormcloak camp in Winterhold Hold, located northeast of Windhelm and south of Snow Veil Sanctum. (map)

Notes[edit]

  • The three camps that include "Military Camp" in their titles are temporary, established only when your side is the attacker in a siege (Stormcloaks on Whiterun and Solitude; Imperials on Windhelm only, since their role in the Battle of Whiterun is defensive). The "Imperial Camps" and "Stormcloak Camps" are more permanent locations for the side not in control of the hold in question.
  • Neither the Imperials nor the Stormcloaks will set up camp in their own former capital's hold: there is no Haafingar Imperial Camp even if the Stormcloaks take Solitude, or Eastmarch Stormcloak Camp even if the Imperials take Windhelm.
  • After the Civil War questline is completed, the military camps for the side that prevailed will be disbanded, and their markers will be removed from the world and local maps. However, the local map will still show your location's title as "Imperial Military Camp" or "Stormcloak Military Camp", as appropriate. If one side takes over a hold before the end of the war, the newly controlling faction may disband its camp in that hold and move the soldiers who were at that camp to that hold's capital.
  • Although Balgruuf is initially neutral, Whiterun Imperial Camp won't be set up unless you capture Whiterun for the Stormcloaks.
  • Both Winterhold camps are closer to other hold capitals than they are to the city. Winterhold Imperial Camp is closer to Dawnstar, and Winterhold Stormcloak Camp is closer to Windhelm.
  • Unlike regular blacksmiths, simply talking to the quartermasters at these camps is enough to see what they have for sale.
  • Falkreath Stormcloak Camp and Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp both feature a roasted skeever on a campfire that you can harvest for charred skeever hide. Every other camp has a cooking station you can use.
  • Calcelmo may ask for Dwemer items bought from these camps, sending you a note by courier. The note will state only "military camp" instead of the camp's actual name. For example, Reach Stormcloak Camp and Winterhold Imperial Camp will simply be recognized in the note as "Stormcloak military camp" and "Imperial military camp", respectively.
  • Version 1.3.3 of the Unofficial Skyrim Patch has all of the wounded soldiers at each camp check to see if the field CO at their camp is alive or dead before enabling themselves. But this may be moot even then, since the CO's are always essential, anyway.
  • Version 2.1.2 of the Unofficial Skyrim Patch marks all items and containers at each camp as owned by the camp's respective military faction, regardless whether they were owned beforehand or not.

Bugs[edit]

  • When a military camp is disbanded and its war map removed, the map's city and fort markers may remain floating in the air.
  • The Imperial legates and Stormcloak officers in their respective camps remain essential once the Civil War questline is finished. ?
