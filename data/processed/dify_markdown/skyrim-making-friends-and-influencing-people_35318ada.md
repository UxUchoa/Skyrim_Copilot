## Radiant Options

This is a radiant quest which you can receive in any of the nine [holds](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") of Skyrim. The name of the objective appearing in your journal will be one of the following, depending upon the hold:

- Assist the people of [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_Eastmarch "Skyrim:Thane of Eastmarch")
- Assist the people of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_Falkreath "Skyrim:Thane of Falkreath")
- Assist the people of [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_Haafingar "Skyrim:Thane of Haafingar")
- Assist the people of [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Thane of Hjaalmarch")
- Assist the people of [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_the_Pale "Skyrim:Thane of the Pale")
- Assist the people of [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_the_Reach "Skyrim:Thane of the Reach")
- Assist the people of [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_the_Rift "Skyrim:Thane of the Rift") (see [notes](#Notes))
- Assist the people of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_Whiterun "Skyrim:Thane of Whiterun")
- Assist the people of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_Winterhold "Skyrim:Thane of Winterhold")

## Quick Walkthrough

Before starting this quest, you must first befriend the [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of the hold.

1. Speak to the jarl to receive the quest.
2. Improve the [disposition](/wiki/Skyrim:Disposition "Skyrim:Disposition") of three or five people of that hold, usually by doing [favor quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Favors "Skyrim:Favors") for them.
3. Return to the jarl.

## Detailed Walkthrough

You will need to improve the disposition of three people for the minor holds Falkreath, Hjaalmarch, the Pale, and Winterhold. For the major holds Eastmarch, Haafingar, the Reach, the Rift, and Whiterun, you will need to help five people. The people you help must be ordinary citizens of the hold; members of [Khajiit caravans](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_Traders "Skyrim:Khajiit Traders") and Orcs living in [strongholds](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") do not count, and neither does befriending the hold's [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") (as that is a prerequisite to being given the objective).

Disposition can be improved in any of the following ways:

- by performing [favor quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Favors "Skyrim:Favors") on their behalf
- by completing certain [side quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Side_Quests "Skyrim:Side Quests")
- investing 500 gold in stores, if you have the [investor perk](/wiki/Skyrim:Investor "Skyrim:Investor")
- dropping some armor pieces in front of citizens and [allowing them to keep it](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Reactions "Skyrim:World Interactions")
- [rescuing](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_Mission "Skyrim:Rescue Mission") kidnapped citizens

Citizens that you befriend before starting this quest also count towards your total, and as such, it is possible to complete this objective for a particular hold immediately after starting the Thane quest. Also, citizens that you befriended which then died still count towards your total. (Usually in those cases, you will have received an [Inheritance](/wiki/Skyrim:Inheritance "Skyrim:Inheritance").) Note that if you befriend the same citizen twice, it only counts once for this quest.

## Notes

- While [the Rift thane quest](/wiki/Skyrim:I_Done_Got_Thaned! "Skyrim:I Done Got Thaned!") is active, most of its objectives do not show up in your journal. The Rift thane quest uses its own code to replace the functions of this quest, so Objective 20 is never assigned. The requirement to assist citizens of the Rift is replaced by an invisible requirement to complete [miscellaneous objectives in Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften#Miscellaneous "Skyrim:Riften"), which starts automatically after completing [Supply and Demand](/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand "Skyrim:Supply and Demand"). The game will track the number of Riften quests you complete, unlike in other holds where you are required to ask the jarl if there is something you can do to help first. Once you have completed enough miscellaneous quests, you will be given an objective to return to the Jarl of Riften.
- If Balgruuf is Jarl of Whiterun, you cannot receive this quest from him. He will make you Thane as a quest reward for completing [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising"), which is early in the main quest. Only if you side with the Stormcloaks and remove him from power is this quest available in Whiterun.

## Bugs

- "Assist the people of Haafingar" may not be cleared from your quest log by Solitude's jarl once completed, though you are able to become a thane and gain a housecarl in your home (once purchased). The concern may be complicated by having already helped five people before talking to the jarl for the first time.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Entering the console command `setobjectivecompleted FavorJarlsMakeFriends 30 1` after becoming a thane may resolve this.
  - Continuing to assist the people of Haafingar can cause the counter to update to 6/5, at which point the objective is completed and cleared from the quest log.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage FavorJarlsMakeFriends stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest FavorJarlsMakeFriends`.
