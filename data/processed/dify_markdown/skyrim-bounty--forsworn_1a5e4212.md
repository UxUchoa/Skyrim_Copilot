## Radiant Options

Any innkeeper in the Reach can give you this quest, as can [Raerek](/wiki/Skyrim:Raerek "Skyrim:Raerek"). You will be directed to one of the possible [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") camps in the hold, and you will then need to obtain your reward from Jarl [Igmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Igmund "Skyrim:Igmund").

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Get the [bounty letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty_(forsworn) "Skyrim:Bounty (forsworn)").
2. Go to the Forsworn camp and kill the leader (usually a [Briarheart](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn#Forsworn_Briarhearts "Skyrim:Forsworn")).
3. Return to Igmund for the reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Speaking with any innkeeper or Igmund about work may lead them to give you a letter of bounty requiring you to kill a Forsworn leader. Head to the camp specified in the letter and kill the marked Forsworn leader. Then return to Igmund for your reward of 100 gold.

## Notes

- This quest is contingent on Igmund being the jarl; it will not be given if the city has changed hands.
