# Skyrim:Temples

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-place-Temple_of_Kynareth.jpg/200px-SR-place-Temple_of_Kynareth.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Temple_of_Kynareth.jpg)

Temple of Kynareth

This page lists all the named **temples**, including those to the [Nine Divines](/wiki/Lore:Nine_Divines "Lore:Nine Divines"), in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").

:   **[Temple of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Temple of Dibella")** — A small Aedric temple dedicated to Dibella, the Aedric goddess of love, beauty, and artistry. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+Dibella)
:   **[Temple of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Temple of the Divines")** — The main temple of Solitude and Skyrim, housing shrines to the Eight Divines. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+the+Divines)
:   **[Temple of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Temple of Kynareth")** — A small temple in Whiterun devoted to the goddess Kynareth. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+Kynareth)
:   **[Temple of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Mara "Skyrim:Temple of Mara")** — A temple in Riften devoted to the Aedric goddess Mara, one of the Eight Divines and goddess of mortal understanding, love and compassion. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+Mara)
:   **[Temple of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Talos "Skyrim:Temple of Talos")** — Located just northwest of Candlehearth Hall in Windhelm. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+Talos)
:   [![SR-mapicon-Cave.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-mapicon-Cave.png/19px-SR-mapicon-Cave.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Temple of Xrib](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Xrib "Skyrim:Temple of Xrib")** — A medium-sized cave south-southwest of Winterhold containing Falmer and chaurus. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Sightless+Pit)

A Snow Elf temple, added by the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") official add-on, is found in the [Forgotten Vale](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale "Skyrim:Forgotten Vale").

:   **[Inner Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Inner_Sanctum_(Vale) "Skyrim:Inner Sanctum (Vale)")**[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") — A relatively well-preserved portion of the Chantry of Auri-El in the Forgotten Vale.

Other temples, added by the [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") official add-on, are found on [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim").

:   **[Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple "Skyrim:Temple")**[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") — A Dunmer place of worship in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Temple)
:   [![SR-mapicon-Temple of Miraak.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-mapicon-Temple_of_Miraak.png/20px-SR-mapicon-Temple_of_Miraak.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Temple_of_Miraak.png) **[Temple of Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Miraak "Skyrim:Temple of Miraak")**[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") — A Nordic temple west of Thirsk Mead Hall containing cultists, draugr, and skeletons. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Temple+of+Miraak)
