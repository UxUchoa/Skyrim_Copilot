Dremora Sehtkynaz

**Dremora Sehtkynaz** is a [Dremora](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora "Skyrim:Dremora") mage whom you must summon during the quest [The Arms of Chaos](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:The Arms of Chaos") as part of the [Arms of Chaos](/wiki/Skyrim:Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:Arms of Chaos") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). Once you have obtained the [Sovereign Band](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovereign_Band "Skyrim:Sovereign Band"), you will be directed to use it at a Daedric altar east of [Skytemple Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins "Skyrim:Skytemple Ruins") in order to summon three Guardians of the Sigil Stones. Activating the altar will summon three Daedra in short succession: Dremora Sehtkynaz, [Dremora Lyrkynaz](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Lyrkynaz "Skyrim:Dremora Lyrkynaz"), and [Dremora Sovereign](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Sovereign "Skyrim:Dremora Sovereign"). Dremora Sehtkynaz is the first Dremora to appear.

Dremora Sehtkynaz utilizes powerful [frost](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage "Skyrim:Frost Damage") magic in combat and can summon a [frost atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Atronach "Skyrim:Frost Atronach"). He carries a dagger and mace along with a [Blue Sigil Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Sigil_Stone "Skyrim:Blue Sigil Stone"). He will drop a [Daedra Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra_Heart "Skyrim:Daedra Heart") when killed.
