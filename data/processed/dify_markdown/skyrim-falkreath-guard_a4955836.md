Falkreath Guard

**Falkreath Guards** are [guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Guards "Skyrim:Guards") who act as the military and police force of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). These guards serve under [Jarl Siddgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Siddgeir "Skyrim:Siddgeir"). Should the [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") forces gain control of Falkreath, they will be replaced by [Stormcloak guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Guard "Skyrim:Stormcloak Guard") under the service of [Jarl Dengeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Dengeir_of_Stuhn "Skyrim:Dengeir of Stuhn").

Falkreath guards are clad in a set of Falkreath guard's equipment. This includes a [Falkreath guard's armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Guard%27s_Armor "Skyrim:Falkreath Guard's Armor"), a [Falkreath guard's helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Guard%27s_Helmet "Skyrim:Falkreath Guard's Helmet"), and a [Falkreath guard's shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Guard%27s_Shield "Skyrim:Falkreath Guard's Shield"). They also wear a pair of [fur boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Boots "Skyrim:Fur Boots"). They are equipped with an [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword") and an [Imperial bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bow "Skyrim:Imperial Bow") supplemented with 20 [steel arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow "Skyrim:Steel Arrow"). They also carry a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), a 50% chance of a [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch"), and a variety of [leveled loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").
