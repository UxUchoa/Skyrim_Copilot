## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Milore Ienth](/wiki/Skyrim:Milore_Ienth "Skyrim:Milore Ienth").
2. Obtain five samples of [netch jelly](/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Jelly "Skyrim:Netch Jelly").
3. Return to Milore Ienth for your reward.

### Hunt for Jelly

The residing apothecary and [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") trainer in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock"), [Milore Ienth](/wiki/Skyrim:Milore_Ienth "Skyrim:Milore Ienth"), sells a wide variety of exotic ingredients, all of which she grows and harvests herself. However, due to the harsh terrain and the dangers outside the [Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark "Skyrim:The Bulwark"), she has problems gathering [netch jelly](/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Jelly "Skyrim:Netch Jelly"). Tell her you are willing to help and she will immediately promise you a reward if you bring back five samples.

The netch jelly samples are dropped by the peaceful, but potentially dangerous, [netch](/wiki/Skyrim:Netch "Skyrim:Netch") that can be found all over [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim"). If you have started (or completed) the quest [The Final Descent](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Final_Descent "Skyrim:The Final Descent") you can gather the only two harvested samples of jelly in Solstheim, specifically deep within [Raven Rock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Mine "Skyrim:Raven Rock Mine"), on an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"). However, you will either need to visit an apothecary or go netch-hunting for the rest of the samples. The table below shows all fixed locations of the netch, which often reside in familial groups:

Once you have gathered five samples of netch jelly, return to Milore and hand them over. She will be impressed and say, *"Goodness, it must have been quite difficult tracking those creatures down out there. Here, take these. They should help you during your journeys."* She will then hand you two [Potions of Ultimate Well-Being](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Ultimate_Well-being "Skyrim:Potion of Ultimate Well-being"), ending the quest.

## Notes

- If you already have five samples of netch jelly in your inventory when starting the quest, Milore will say, *"My goodness, you're quite resourceful aren't you? This is all I can offer in return for this. I hope it's enough."* This ends the quest right away.
