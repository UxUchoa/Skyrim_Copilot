Orphan's Tear

**Orphan's Tear** is a [shipwreck](/wiki/Skyrim:Shipwrecks "Skyrim:Shipwrecks") west of [Ravenscar Hollow](/wiki/Skyrim:Ravenscar_Hollow "Skyrim:Ravenscar Hollow") on the northern coast.

The ship has broken into two sections; one section is partially submerged and holds the cabin, while the other is almost fully submerged. It is occupied by three [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear_02.jpg)

Orphan's Tear bandit camp

There are three bandits based here, who will become hostile and attack as soon as you approach. They are a melee fighter, an archer, and a magic user. Once they are taken care of, you can proceed with exploring the shipwreck. The bandits' campsite on the shore consists of a campfire and a large tent with two bedrolls and a table holding some cheese wedges and a wheel, and an adept-locked chest containing random [leveled loot](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists "Skyrim:Leveled Lists") just outside the tent. Near the tent, there are four [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") hanging from a rack and a bucket containing three more salmon behind a small boat, all of which can be harvested. Beside the bucket of salmon are some crates with two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") creation installed, there is also a fishing pole here, with some [Fishing Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Supplies "Skyrim:Fishing Supplies") found a bit farther up the shore. Fish that can be found in freezing waters can be found here. There is also a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") lying on a wooden block in front of the large tent.

Inside the shipwreck's cabin, you will see a table propped up against the wall with an unlocked chest hidden behind it. Also, if you look hard enough, you can find two bottles of [skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma") and a [potion of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka") beside the bedroll. There are a couple of barrels on this level containing foodstuffs. Proceed down the stairs into the water to find several barrels containing common [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") and also a pair of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots") near a bed. If you proceed farther down one level you will see a skeleton trapped underwater to the left of the stairs, along with another unlocked chest surrounded by four barrels. To the right of the stairs is a [potion of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Magicka").

There are two harvestable clams between the two parts of the boat and two [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Grass Pod") plants next to the large tent. The other submerged section of the shipwreck does not have any notable loot, however, other than a few barrels.

## Notes

- There is a total of 45 [Nordic barnacle clusters](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle_Cluster "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle Cluster") in the area, where you can harvest [Nordic barnacles](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle").
- There is an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") southeast of the campsite.
