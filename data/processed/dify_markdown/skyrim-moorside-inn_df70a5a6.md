Moorside Inn

**Moorside Inn** is the only [inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Inns "Skyrim:Inns") in [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal"). It is owned and run by [Jonna](/wiki/Skyrim:Jonna "Skyrim:Jonna"), from whom you may rent a room for 10 gold per night. Food can also be purchased and sold here. [Lurbuk](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurbuk "Skyrim:Lurbuk") is the inn's bard, although his performances are [not popular](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Lurbuk "Skyrim:Kill Lurbuk"). Business seems slow, if not nonexistent, as Jonna will tell you that the front door doesn't get much use. One of the few regular visitors is [Gorm](/wiki/Skyrim:Gorm "Skyrim:Gorm"), who comes in every evening for his dinner.

[Hroggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hroggar "Skyrim:Hroggar")'s burned-down house is found next to Moorside Inn.

### Exterior

There are two separate flights of stairs leading up to the porch on the front of the inn. Behind the inn against the wall are two patches of [giant lichen](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Lichen "Skyrim:Giant Lichen") that can be harvested.

### Moorside Inn

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-interior-Moorside_Inn_Common_Room.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Moorside_Inn_Common_Room.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Moorside_Inn_Common_Room.jpg)

The common room inside the Moorside Inn.

Through the door, you enter the main common room of the inn, with the counter at the far end; [Jonna](/wiki/Skyrim:Jonna "Skyrim:Jonna") can usually be found behind it. There are five long wooden tables and benches against the walls of the room. Four of the tables hold a couple loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and several bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). The table on your right as you enter also holds two [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), and pieces of cheese. In the center of the room is a fire pit. There are several barrels of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") found around the room, mainly in the corners. There are three chairs at the northeast end of the room, which is normally where [Lurbuk](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurbuk "Skyrim:Lurbuk") plies his trade, except late at night when he retires to the first room on the left to sleep.

There are three doorways leading to three bedrooms, two to the southeast and a larger room to the northwest. There are more pieces of cheese and bottles of wine and alto wine on the counter and under it. Also under the counter are three small [coin purses](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a [steel battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Steel Battleaxe"), plenty of bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), and a copy of *[The Windhelm Letters](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Windhelm_Letters "Skyrim:The Windhelm Letters")*. Against the northwest wall, on either side of the doorway to the larger bedroom, are two sets of shelves holding three [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), four loaves of bread, seven bottles of Nord mead, six bottles of wine, and two bottles of alto wine. Behind the counter are three hanging racks with six [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") and ten [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") hanging from them, and between them are two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, an elves ear, and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging from a beam. Behind all of this is a row of vats.

The first bedroom on the left that Lurbuk generally uses consists of a single owned bed in the eastern corner, next to which is a round table and chair holding a common book and a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead"). At the foot of the bed is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and to the left of the doorway is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). The second bedroom consists of a single unowned bed in the southern corner, a round wooden table and chair next to it, and a dresser against the southwest wall holding four common books, while inside a basket is a copy of the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Racial Phylogeny](/wiki/Skyrim:Racial_Phylogeny "Skyrim:Racial Phylogeny")*.

The larger bedroom consists of a large double bed in the middle of the northwest wall, with dressers on either side holding a bottle of wine, a bottle of alto wine, two bottles of Nord mead, a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), and six common books. On the floor, beside the dresser on the right, is a large coin purse. Along the northeast wall are a pile of food sacks and a long unlocked chest. To the right of the doorway is a wooden table and chair holding a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") and a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"). This is the room you will be given if you decide to pay to stay the night.
