Drennen's ghost

**Drennen** is the [ghost](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost "Skyrim:Ghost") of a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") who met his death in [Avanchnzel](/wiki/Skyrim:Avanchnzel "Skyrim:Avanchnzel"). He now wanders the halls of his tomb with his similarly ghostly companions [Breya](/wiki/Skyrim:Breya "Skyrim:Breya"), [Watches-The-Roots](/wiki/Skyrim:Watches-The-Roots "Skyrim:Watches-The-Roots"), and [From-Deepest-Fathoms](/wiki/Skyrim:From-Deepest-Fathoms "Skyrim:From-Deepest-Fathoms").

His corpse can be found in [Avanchnzel Animoncultory](/wiki/Skyrim:Avanchnzel_Animoncultory "Skyrim:Avanchnzel Animoncultory") in a sloping corridor after the factory-like room. On his body is his [studded armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor "Skyrim:Studded Armor") and a pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"), as well as an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") and a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Related Quests

- **[Unfathomable Depths](/wiki/Skyrim:Unfathomable_Depths "Skyrim:Unfathomable Depths")**: Uncover the [memories](/wiki/Skyrim:Lexicon "Skyrim:Lexicon") of the Dwemer.

### Unfathomable Depths

As you first enter Avanchnzel you will see the ghost of Drennen speaking to the other ghosts:

:   **Drennen**: *"I don't like this place. It feels like we're being... watched."*
:   **Breya**: *"It's a simple job, Drennen. We get in, we steal the Lexicon, we leave. Don't get jumpy."*
:   **Fathoms**: *"If you two cannot handle this, we can find others to hire."*
:   **Roots**: *"Enough. Their services will be more than adequate. Let us continue."*

Further in the ruins you will see him having a conversation with Watches-The-Roots:

:   **Drennen**: *"This place is unbelievable."*
:   **Roots**: *"Indeed. Avanchnzel is as much a library as it is a city. Built to hold the vast memories of the Dwemer."*

In Avanchnzel Animoncultory at the campsite:

:   **Drennen**: *"This place is huge."*
:   **Roots**: *"It is large, indeed. I did not anticipate Avanchnzel's size or the time needed to uncover the entrance. A few hours sleep and we should be ready to continue through."*
:   **Drennen**: *"Maybe we should turn back. I don't want to sleep here."*
:   **Roots**: *"Drennen, you do understand that the Lexicon at the bottom of this place holds the accumulated memories of centuries of Dwemer."*
:   **Breya**: *"Not to mention our pay."*
:   **Drennen**: *"So?"*
:   **Fathoms**: *"So we're not turning back, you fool."*
:   **Breya**: *"Besides, you're not afraid of a few sleeping metal men. Are you, Drennen?"*

Just before the doors to the Avanchnzel Boilery:

:   **Drennen**: *"I can't do this. We shouldn't be here. I'm sorry."*
:   **Breya**: *"Drennen!"*
:   **Fathoms**: *"Let the field mouse run. Your pay will be double."*
:   **Breya**: *"My pay will be nothing if we die in here!"*
:   **Roots**: *"Calm yourself, Breya. We are closing in on the Lexicon. I can feel it calling. We'll continue without Drennen."*
