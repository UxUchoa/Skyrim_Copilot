Blizzard Rest

**Blizzard Rest** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") south of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

## Walkthrough

The camp is a short distance from the road that runs between Dawnstar and [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"); if *[Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")* is installed, it is just east of [Heljarchen Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Heljarchen_Hall "Skyrim:Heljarchen Hall"). Two [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and two [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") occupy the camp. The camp is on a ledge with a rock overhang above, with the mammoths normally found below the ledge. The typical painted stones and mammoth bones surround the camp. The fire has a ring of rocks circling it. Two [charred skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") are on stakes cooking over the fire. A few paces to the southwest of the fire is an expert-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") under the overhang, and to the south are three large [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl"). The first giant is near the fire, while the second giant is either a short distance east next to a dead mammoth or roaming the tundra south of the camp.
