Alfarinn

**Alfarinn** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H"), and the [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") [carriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Carriage "Skyrim:Carriage") driver.

Alfarinn wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes"), a pair of [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1 "Skyrim:Gloves"), and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots"). He is equipped with an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe").
