The Winterhold region in the northeast corner of Skyrim

**Winterhold** is a [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in northeast [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), with its capital in the city of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"). It is initially affiliated with the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks"), but can be captured by the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") after completing [Rescue from Fort Kastav](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav "Skyrim:Rescue from Fort Kastav").

Winterhold is the northernmost hold in Skyrim, and has weather to match. The [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts") is off the northern coast, [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch") is to the southeast, and [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale") is to the south and west.

Perhaps the most notable feature of Winterhold is the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)"), a fortress-like school for mages which was spared on a rocky outcropping even though the [Great Collapse](/wiki/Lore:Great_Collapse "Lore:Great Collapse") tore down most of the capital around it. All that remains of the once-great city are four still-standing buildings (and the collapsed remains of several others) near the entrance to the College.

## Topographical Overview

Winterhold consists of snowy coasts and even snowier mountains. To the west and southwest is the Winterhold mountain range, which marks the border between Winterhold and [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale"), and some of the northern border with [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch"). Between the mountains and the sea to the east lies a tundra, where the elements have rendered the landscape barren and lifeless. Rock and ice shelves, and occasionally shingle beaches, form the barrier between Winterhold and the sea to the north and east. To the north the tundra stretches out into the Sea of Ghosts, forming one of the most inhospitable environments in Skyrim.

### Flora and Fauna

Not many plants or creatures can survive Winterhold's harsh continental climate, and the hold is generally devoid of life as a result. The only plants that make their home in the mountains are the [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry "Skyrim:Snowberry") bush and some [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"). By the coast, [Nordic barnacles](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle"), [clams](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam "Skyrim:Clam") and [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Spiky_Grass "Skyrim:Spiky Grass") thrive. The area around the [Wreck of the Winter War](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Winter_War "Skyrim:Wreck of the Winter War") is known to be rich in [fish](/wiki/Skyrim:Fish "Skyrim:Fish") and [dragonflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonfly "Skyrim:Dragonfly") and a large, isolated population of [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(creature) "Skyrim:Salmon (creature)") can be found by the coast.

As for large fauna, only the hardiest of creatures make their home in Winterhold, such as [wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf"), [ice wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf "Skyrim:Ice Wolf"), [snow bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear "Skyrim:Snow Bear"), [snowy sabre cats](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowy_Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Snowy Sabre Cat"), and [horkers](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker "Skyrim:Horker"). Occasionally, an unlucky traveler might also stumble upon an [ice wraith](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith"), or worse, a [wispmother](/wiki/Skyrim:Wispmother "Skyrim:Wispmother").

### Routes and Pathways

There is only one main road running through Winterhold. It originates as a branch of the [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun")-[Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") road. It runs northward through the eastern side of the hold right to the city of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"). Another smaller and less used road runs from Winterhold to the ancient ruins of [Saarthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Saarthal "Skyrim:Saarthal").

## Notes

- Winterhold is the only Hold that doesn't have a plot of land for sale if *Hearthfire* is installed, making [building a house](/wiki/Skyrim:Construction "Skyrim:Construction") there impossible. As a result, it is also the only Hold where no [housecarls](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl") are available. It doesn't matter if you helped [Korir](/wiki/Skyrim:Korir "Skyrim:Korir") or not.

## Bugs

- Due to the fact that [Ramati](/wiki/Skyrim:Ramati "Skyrim:Ramati"), [Mani](/wiki/Skyrim:Mani "Skyrim:Mani"), and [Sudi](/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi "Skyrim:Sudi") at [Frostflow Lighthouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostflow_Lighthouse "Skyrim:Frostflow Lighthouse") are part of [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill them, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
- [Kjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjar "Skyrim:Kjar") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") is in the Winterhold crime faction, rather than the [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch") one.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
- Oddly, since the [Jarl's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Winterhold)") is marked as being owned by the Winterhold crime faction, if you help anyone in the hold (including [Kjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjar "Skyrim:Kjar") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") and ironically enough, anyone from the [College](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)")), you will be able to take things from the longhouse without it being considered theft.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19319)) A new faction was created specifically for the residents of the longhouse in order to set ownership correctly.
