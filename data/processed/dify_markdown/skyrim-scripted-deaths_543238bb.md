# Skyrim:Scripted Deaths

## Overview

This article provides a list of all Skyrim [NPCs](/wiki/Skyrim:NPCs "Skyrim:NPCs") who die because of a **scripted death** at some point in the game. A scripted death is one where a game script forcibly kills the character, no matter what choice the player makes. Scripted deaths are usually caused by a quest. [Essential NPCs](/wiki/Skyrim:Essential_NPCs "Skyrim:Essential NPCs") are not immune to scripted deaths, though their essential status must be removed before the death script takes place.
