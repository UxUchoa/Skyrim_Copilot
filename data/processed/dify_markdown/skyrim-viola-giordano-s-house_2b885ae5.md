Viola Giordano's house

**Viola Giordano's House** is the home of [Viola Giordano](/wiki/Skyrim:Viola_Giordano "Skyrim:Viola Giordano") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm")'s Valunstrad district.

Viola Giordano is the sole occupant of the large two-story Nordic-style house in one of the better neighborhoods of the city directly opposite the [house of Clan Shatter-Shield](/wiki/Skyrim:House_of_Clan_Shatter-Shield "Skyrim:House of Clan Shatter-Shield") and to the north of the graveyard and [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Windhelm) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Windhelm)"), but she rarely spends much time there.

## Viola Giordano's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e1/SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg)

The interior of Viola Giordano's House

The house is completely open-plan downstairs with a single room extending the length of the house and support pillars along the right-hand third of the house. The house has many food sacks around the place. Upon entering there is a chair in the northwestern corner, with some crates beside it and a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") on the floor. There is a wooden table in an alcove on the north wall, with a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") and [plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") on top. At the other end of the room is a matching alcove with another table in it, which has a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [skeever tail](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever_Tail "Skyrim:Skeever Tail"), two [Nordic barnacles](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle"), three [rock warbler eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a [pheasant breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"), a rack of [dog meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat "Skyrim:Dog Meat") and a fillet of [horker meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat "Skyrim:Horker Meat") on the table and a bowl of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal") on the floor under it. To the left of the alcove is a set of shelves holing a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and three [goblets](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet").

On the rear wall is a large fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") hanging in front of it and tables on either side. The table to the left has a salt pile on top and a basket in front containing a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), while the table to the right has a pile of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") including two loose pieces on top. There is a long wooden table and benches in front of the fireplace with two bottles of alto wine, a bowl of [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), a plate of three [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), another with a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes") and [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a salt pile and two goblets. In the southeastern corner is a square wooden table with a mead barrel on top, with another alcove to the right with a long unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") in it and a set of shelves to the right holding various pieces of cheese and a bottle of Nord mead.

The stairs leading up to the top floor are against the south wall with a wall screening them from downstairs. On this south wall is a set of shelves holding a bottle of alto wine and three common books, next to this is a chair. There are a couple of large empty vases next to the pillars and to the right when entering is a dresser which is used as the drop off point for returning the ring without Viola's notice which has a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute") on top of it. There is a fourth alcove to the left of the dresser with a few barrels in it and some more crates against the south wall, to the left of the alcove.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House_02.jpg)

The upstairs of Viola Giordano's house

At the top of the stairs there is an alcove on the right with three [cow hides](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow_Hide "Skyrim:Cow Hide") piled on the floor. In front is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). Along the edge of the stairwell are two more large empty vases and a low table and chair. On this table is a [solution of extra magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Solution of Extra Magicka"), a copy of *[The Book of Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra "Skyrim:The Book of Daedra")* and some [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and cheese. Against the south wall is a tall wardrobe with a square wooden table to the right of it and a copy of *[Spirit of Nirn](/wiki/Skyrim:Spirit_of_Nirn "Skyrim:Spirit of Nirn")* on top. Against the west wall, to the left of a doorway into [Viola](/wiki/Skyrim:Viola_Giordano "Skyrim:Viola Giordano")'s bedroom are a couple of barrels and a long end table with a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") on top. To the right, against the north wall, is a set of shelves holding three common books and a bottle of each, alto wine, [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and Nord mead. To the right of this is a chair and farther along an empty open crate and then an end table which has a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") and a fancy [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") and [candlestick](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlestick "Skyrim:Candlestick") on top, with a drum on the floor beside it. Farther to the right, next to another alcove is a set of shelves holding seven more common books.

The L-shaped bedroom through the doorway at the western end of the floor contains a double bed coming out from the west wall with a long unlocked chest at the foot of the bed. To the left in the southwestern corner is an end table with a wash basin on a stand around the corner, with a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") on the floor beside it. To the right of another alcove to the south is a set of shelves holding a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"). In the alcove are two corner shelves with a bottle of wine on one and a solution of extra magicka on the other. In the middle of this space is a large round wooden table with a bottle of alto wine, a jug and goblet and bread and cheese on top. Beside the doorway is a bookcase holding nine common books, while on the other side of it is an open crate containing two pairs of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"). There is another long unlocked chest in yet another alcove to the north with a tall wardrobe to the left of the alcove with a small wardrobe on the west wall to the left of the larger one.

## Notes

- There is a [![Loot](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Loot") Loot [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Shadowmarks "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") to the bottom right of the entrance door and another one to the right before the arch in front of her house.
- [Vignar Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Vignar_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Vignar Gray-Mane") and his court (i.e., [Brill](/wiki/Skyrim:Brill "Skyrim:Brill") and [Olfina Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Olfina_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Olfina Gray-Mane")), as well as [Avulstein Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Avulstein_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Avulstein Gray-Mane"), were supposed to go here if exiled, but it is impossible for this to happen, so they will never be seen here.
