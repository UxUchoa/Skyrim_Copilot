## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to the quest giver.
2. Steal the requested item.
3. Return to the quest giver.

### Dengeir of Stuhn

[Dengeir of Stuhn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dengeir_of_Stuhn "Skyrim:Dengeir of Stuhn") was once the Jarl of Falkreath but has since stepped down and is now a Thane. He is a paranoid and patriotic supporter of the Stormcloaks. He believes that [Lod](/wiki/Skyrim:Lod "Skyrim:Lod") the blacksmith is writing a treasonous letter and wants you to steal it for him.

The letter is on Lod's bedside table. It is simply a note requesting extra iron. Steal it and return it to Dengeir. Dengeir will at first be confused, and then realize Lod is probably not up to treason, even if he is making extra weapons for the Empire. He will then give you a reward of leveled gold.

Completion of this quest opens up another radiant quest, [Dark Ancestor](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Ancestor "Skyrim:Dark Ancestor").

### Malur Seloth

Talking to [Malur Seloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Malur_Seloth "Skyrim:Malur Seloth") and asking him what he does prompts him to reveal that he doesn't really do anything. He wants to appear more mage-like when in the jarl's presence, and wishes to acquire [a staff](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Arcane_Authority "Skyrim:Staff of Arcane Authority").

The one he wants belongs to [Nelacar](/wiki/Skyrim:Nelacar "Skyrim:Nelacar") who resides in the [inn](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth "Skyrim:The Frozen Hearth"). The staff is located in the shop counter in Nelacar's room. Steal it and return to Malur. He will reward you with one skill point in [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech").

### Stands-In-Shallows

When speaking to [Stands-In-Shallows](/wiki/Skyrim:Stands-In-Shallows "Skyrim:Stands-In-Shallows"), he mentions that he overheard sailors talking about their stashed [extra powerful Skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Double-Distilled_Skooma "Skyrim:Double-Distilled Skooma"), and he would like to get it.

The bottle is located in the [New Gnisis Cornerclub](/wiki/Skyrim:New_Gnisis_Cornerclub "Skyrim:New Gnisis Cornerclub") in Windhelm, underneath the second set of stairs. Steal it and return to Stands-in-Shallows, who will reward you with a skill point in [Sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak").

## Notes

- Only one radiant quest of a similar type can be assigned at any given time. You must clear the radiant quest from your miscellaneous quests in order to be eligible to receive another of the same type.
- Because this is a favor quest which affects the quest giver's disposition, it will count towards the "Help the People" part of the [thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") quest for the quest giver's hold.
- This quest requires you to commit a [crime](/wiki/Skyrim:Crime "Skyrim:Crime"); see that page for details about criminal activity.
- Malur's and Shallows' items are available to retrieve from the start of the game, while the letter Dengeir requests only gets enabled once he has mentioned it.

## Bugs

- The quest might not trigger if you have already stolen the quest item before talking to the related NPC. Since the quest item is marked as such, you are left with no way of getting rid of it.
