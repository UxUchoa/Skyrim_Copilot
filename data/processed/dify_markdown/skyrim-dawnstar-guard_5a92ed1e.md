Dawnstar Guard

**Dawnstar Guards** are [guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Guards "Skyrim:Guards") who act as the military and police force of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"). These guards serve under [Jarl Skald](/wiki/Skyrim:Skald "Skyrim:Skald"). Should the [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") forces gain control of Dawnstar, they will be replaced by [Imperial guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Guard "Skyrim:Imperial Guard") under the service of [Jarl Brina](/wiki/Skyrim:Brina_Merilis "Skyrim:Brina Merilis").

Dawnstar guards are clad in a set of Pale guard's equipment. This includes a [Pale guard's armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Guard%27s_Armor "Skyrim:Pale Guard's Armor"), a [Pale guard's helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Guard%27s_Helmet "Skyrim:Pale Guard's Helmet"), and a [Pale guard's shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Guard%27s_Shield "Skyrim:Pale Guard's Shield"). They also wear a pair of [fur gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Fur Gauntlets") and a pair of [fur boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Boots "Skyrim:Fur Boots"). They are equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:One-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:One-Handed Weapons") and a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow") supplemented with [steel arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow "Skyrim:Steel Arrow"). They also carry a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") and a [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch").
