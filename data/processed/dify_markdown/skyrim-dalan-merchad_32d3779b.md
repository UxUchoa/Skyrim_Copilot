Dalan Merchad

**Dalan Merchad** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") who works at the [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") docks. He serves as a sailor aboard the ship [The North Wind](/wiki/Skyrim:The_North_Wind "Skyrim:The North Wind"), under the ship's captain, [Kjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjar "Skyrim:Kjar").

He despises Windhelm and with the ship permanently docked, Dalan has no choice but to wander around on deck all day and night, without getting any sleep, at times pausing to sit down and eat.

Dalan wears merchant [clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0004223C "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d "Skyrim:Boots"). He wields an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") in combat, and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").
