## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read *[Bounty: Lost Wares](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Lost_Wares "Skyrim:Bounty: Lost Wares")*
2. Travel to [Old Hroldan Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Hroldan_Inn "Skyrim:Old Hroldan Inn")
3. Fish for the [Aged Flin](/wiki/Skyrim:Aged_Flin "Skyrim:Aged Flin")
4. Return the bottles to [Ri'saad](/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad "Skyrim:Ri'saad")

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-quest-Spirits_Away_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Spirits_Away_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Spirits_Away_02.jpg)

The fishing spot

This quest can be started after [Private Lessons](/wiki/Skyrim:Private_Lessons "Skyrim:Private Lessons") has been completed by talking to Viriya:

:   **Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?**
:   *"Of course. It's all in this note."*

She will then hand you a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Lost_Wares "Skyrim:Bounty: Lost Wares"), which explains that a Khajiit merchant named [Ri'saad](/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad "Skyrim:Ri'saad") lost some bottles of [Aged Flin](/wiki/Skyrim:Aged_Flin "Skyrim:Aged Flin") in the river west of [Old Hroldan Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Hroldan_Inn "Skyrim:Old Hroldan Inn") and needs help retrieving them.

You will need to travel to Old Hroldan Inn and head west toward the river. When you arrive at the overturned cart, you will have to fish for three bottles of the Aged Flin.

Once you have all three bottles, travel to Ri'saad and then speak to him:

:   **Here's all of the bottles of aged flin you lost in the river.**
:   *"Excellent. And now, please accept this token of appreciation."*

He will then reward you with the [Ring of Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Khajiit "Skyrim:Ring of Khajiit").

## Notes

- There is a [hide helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Helmet "Skyrim:Hide Helmet") directly to the left of the fishing spot among some crates.
