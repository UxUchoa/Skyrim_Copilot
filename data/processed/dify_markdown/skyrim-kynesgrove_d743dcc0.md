Kynesgrove

**Kynesgrove** is a small [settlement](/wiki/Skyrim:Settlements "Skyrim:Settlements") south of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-place-Kynesgrove_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Kynesgrove_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Kynesgrove_02.jpg)

Steamscorch Mine Camp

[Ganna](/wiki/Skyrim:Ganna_Uriel "Skyrim:Ganna Uriel") and [Gemma Uriel](/wiki/Skyrim:Gemma_Uriel "Skyrim:Gemma Uriel") came to Kynesgrove to start a lumber mill without realizing the nearby grove's trees were sacred to [Kyne](/wiki/Lore:Kyne "Lore:Kyne"). The settlement contains remnants of the grove they initially cut. They now mine [malachite](/wiki/Skyrim:Malachite "Skyrim:Malachite") for a living, unable to afford starting a lumber mill elsewhere. They can be heard constantly arguing about the life they left behind in [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil"). [Kjeld](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjeld "Skyrim:Kjeld") runs the settlement and will [buy malachite ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Mine_Ore "Skyrim:Mine Ore") from you. [Iddra](/wiki/Skyrim:Iddra "Skyrim:Iddra") runs the [Braidwood Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Braidwood_Inn "Skyrim:Braidwood Inn"). [Dravynea the Stoneweaver](/wiki/Skyrim:Dravynea_the_Stoneweaver "Skyrim:Dravynea the Stoneweaver") keeps the mine from collapsing and getting too warm with a potion she makes from [frost salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Salts "Skyrim:Frost Salts").

The settlement consists of [Braidwood Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Braidwood_Inn "Skyrim:Braidwood Inn"), [Steamscorch Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine "Skyrim:Steamscorch Mine") (a malachite mine), and a [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine#Steamscorch_Mine_Camp "Skyrim:Steamscorch Mine") containing three tents used by the miners. There is a [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") outside the mine, a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") beside the inn, and a [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry "Skyrim:Stone Quarry") just east of the inn. Next to the camp is a vegetable patch containing six [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), four [gourds](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), eight [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") plants, and ten [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"). A [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") and a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") are also found near the vegetable patch. A short distance downhill from the inn is a chicken coop consisting of two [chicken nests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") for the two [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken") that wander the settlement. There are a number of [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") plants around the settlement as well as a [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") plant and a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush around the camp. Uphill to the south are some [beehives](/wiki/Skyrim:Beehive "Skyrim:Beehive") and [red mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") among the trees. To the east is a [dragon mound](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Mounds#Kynesgrove_Resurrection "Skyrim:Dragon Mounds") where the [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") [Sahloknir](/wiki/Skyrim:Sahloknir "Skyrim:Sahloknir") is resurrected by [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin") during the quest [A Blade in the Dark](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Blade_in_the_Dark "Skyrim:A Blade in the Dark").

## Notes

- Despite Ganna Uriel mentioning that she and her sister built a lumbermill at Kynesgrove, it is nowhere to be found. The [Creation Kit](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit "Skyrim Mod:Creation Kit") identifies the bank of the river just down the hill from Kynesgrove as "Frostbark Lumber Mill", which suggests the location she describes was cut from the game.
- Kynesgrove is one of the most common places to encounter dragons, as they often fly overhead.
- Kynesgrove also [appears](/wiki/Online:Kynesgrove "Online:Kynesgrove") in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online").

## Bugs

- If [Klija Wolf-Claw](/wiki/Skyrim:Klija_Wolf-Claw "Skyrim:Klija Wolf-Claw") appears at Kynesgrove but you then side with the [Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") and kill her during the quest [Battle of the Champions](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_of_the_Champions "Skyrim:Battle of the Champions") [CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War_Champions "Skyrim:Civil War Champions"), this version of her will not be disabled, and she will continue to live at Steamscorch Mine Camp. **?**
- One of the pieces of malachite ore outside the mine may be buried under the ground, making it irretrievable.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
- There is a broken navmesh in the mine, making the back 1/3 of the mine largely inaccessible to the NPCs when they work there.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
- After [A Blade in the Dark](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Blade_in_the_Dark "Skyrim:A Blade in the Dark") is finished, the town guards may comment on a dragon attack here as though it took place somewhere else.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.8, fixes this bug.
