Lake Fjalding

**Lake Fjalding** is a frozen lake near [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall").

It is the largest lake on [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim"), although it is covered by ice year-round. It is commonly used as a mating ground for the [horkers](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker "Skyrim:Horker") of the island. The [Iggnir River](/wiki/Skyrim:Iggnir_River "Skyrim:Iggnir River") to the south and the [Isild River](/wiki/Skyrim:Isild_River "Skyrim:Isild River") to the north meet here. The [Isinfier Plains](/wiki/Lore:Isinfier_Plains "Lore:Isinfier Plains") lie to the south.
