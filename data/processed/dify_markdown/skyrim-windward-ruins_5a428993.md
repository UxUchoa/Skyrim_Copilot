Windward Ruins

**Windward Ruins** is a small [outdoor Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings "Skyrim:Nordic Dwellings") southwest of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") containing [skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever").

It is one of the three ruins visited for the quest [Destruction Ritual Spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction_Ritual_Spell "Skyrim:Destruction Ritual Spell"). With the [Vigil Enforcer Armor Set](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_Enforcer_Armor_Set "Skyrim:Vigil Enforcer Armor Set") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, [Azarain](/wiki/Skyrim:Azarain "Skyrim:Azarain")'s corpse can be found here.

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-place-Windward_Ruins_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Windward_Ruins_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Windward_Ruins_02.jpg)

Windward Ruins interior

There is a central circular area surrounded by an enclosed outer passage. It is guarded by two [skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever") and contains an adept-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") behind a tilted pillar by the outer wall of the passage. During the [Kyne's Sacred Trials](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials") quest, you will find a larger-than-life, ghostly skeever within the ruin. During the quest [Destruction Ritual Spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction_Ritual_Spell "Skyrim:Destruction Ritual Spell"), you will need to activate a plinth in the central area to place the book *[Power of the Elements](/wiki/Skyrim:Power_of_the_Elements "Skyrim:Power of the Elements")* received from [Faralda](/wiki/Skyrim:Faralda "Skyrim:Faralda"), then cast a [flame spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage#Spells "Skyrim:Fire Damage") at the plinth; you will then advance the quest and receive the clue to the second location. There is a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry "Skyrim:Snowberry") bush in the central area.

## Notes

- Three leveled [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") have set up a camp 580 feet to the northwest below a rock ledge. The only items of note in the camp are an unlocked treasure chest and a copy of the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Hypothetical Treachery](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Hypothetical_Treachery "Skyrim:A Hypothetical Treachery")*.
- [Agrane Peryval](/wiki/Skyrim:Agrane_Peryval "Skyrim:Agrane Peryval")'s camp can be found north of Windward Ruins if you have the [Saturalia Holiday Pack](/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Holiday_Pack "Skyrim:Saturalia Holiday Pack") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed.
