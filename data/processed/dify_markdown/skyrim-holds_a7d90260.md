# Skyrim:Holds

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-map-All_Holds.jpg/300px-SR-map-All_Holds.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-map-All_Holds.jpg)

Map showing the different holds

This page lists the nine **holds**, or regions, of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"), each governed by a [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") who lives in the region's capital [city](/wiki/Skyrim:Cities "Skyrim:Cities").

In the absence of a High King, the holds have effectively split from one another, with some even at war with each other. As a consequence, [guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Guards "Skyrim:Guards") in each hold only care about [crimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Crime "Skyrim:Crime") you have committed within that hold, with any bounties you acquire tied to a specific hold. In addition, the [Tribal Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Tribal_Orcs "Skyrim:Tribal Orcs") faction and [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") track their crime separately from any of the Nord holds.

Many of the holds have distinct climates, such as the Rift being an aspen forest, Falkreath Hold being a pine forest, and Eastmarch being tundra. Snowy mountain environments are also found in most holds.

:   **[Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch")** — A hold in eastern Skyrim, with its capital in Windhelm.
:   **[Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold")** — A hold in southern Skyrim, with its capital in Falkreath.
:   **[Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar")** — A relatively small hold in northwest Skyrim, containing the provincial capital Solitude.
:   **[Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch")** — A hold in northern Skyrim, with its capital in Morthal.
:   **[The Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale")** — A hold in northern Skyrim, with its capital in Dawnstar.
:   **[The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach")** — A hold in western Skyrim, with Markarth as its capital.
:   **[The Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift")** — A hold in southeast Skyrim, with its capital in Riften.
:   **[Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold")** — A hold in central Skyrim, with its capital in Whiterun.
:   **[Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)")** — A hold in northeast Skyrim, with its capital in the city of Winterhold.
