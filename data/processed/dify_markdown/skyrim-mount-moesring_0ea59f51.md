Mount Moesring, seen from the south

**Mount Moesring** is one of the highest points on Solstheim, south of [Moesring Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Moesring_Pass "Skyrim:Moesring Pass"). The marker for this peak does not appear on the compass until you have [climbed](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing "Skyrim:Mountain Climbing") nearly to the top.

On foot, it is best to start your climb just north of the [shrine of Zenithar](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Zenithar "Skyrim:Shrine of Zenithar"). Once you reach a lower peak, it's advisable to save, as falls from this height can kill you. Leap across the gap from the lower peak, keeping as much height as possible, then continue climbing while circling clockwise around the peak to reach the top, where you can stand without slipping. See [this article](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing "Skyrim:Mountain Climbing") for tips on mountain climbing.

From the top, you can see most of Solstheim, even at night. To the immediate north is Moesring Pass with [White Ridge Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Ridge_Barrow "Skyrim:White Ridge Barrow") beyond, and both [Mortrag Peak](/wiki/Skyrim:Mortrag_Peak "Skyrim:Mortrag Peak") and [Frykte Peak](/wiki/Skyrim:Frykte_Peak "Skyrim:Frykte Peak") etched against the sky in the distance. To the west you can see [Fahlbtharz](/wiki/Skyrim:Fahlbtharz "Skyrim:Fahlbtharz"), while to the southwest is [Hvitkald Peak](/wiki/Skyrim:Hvitkald_Peak "Skyrim:Hvitkald Peak"), with [the Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark "Skyrim:The Bulwark") protecting Raven Rock clearly visible to the left of the peak. Far to the south is the [Wreck of the Strident Squall](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Strident_Squall "Skyrim:Wreck of the Strident Squall"), with the circular path leading up to [Brodir Grove](/wiki/Skyrim:Brodir_Grove "Skyrim:Brodir Grove") carved into the landscape. To the south-southeast is [Highpoint Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Highpoint_Tower "Skyrim:Highpoint Tower"), and farther to the southeast is the settlement of [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(settlement) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (settlement)"), with the Red Mountain belching ash and rock into the sky beyond. To the east is the massive stonework surrounding the [Tree Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Tree_Stone "Skyrim:Tree Stone") at the [Temple of Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Miraak "Skyrim:Temple of Miraak"). The view between the Temple of Miraak to the east and Moesring Pass to the north is blocked by another mountain range.
