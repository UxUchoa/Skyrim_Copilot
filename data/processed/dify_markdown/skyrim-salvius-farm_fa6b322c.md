Salvius Farm

**Salvius Farm** is a small farm east of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). [Vigdis Salvius](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigdis_Salvius "Skyrim:Vigdis Salvius") and [Rogatus Salvius](/wiki/Skyrim:Rogatus_Salvius "Skyrim:Rogatus Salvius") tend the farm and live in the Farmhouse.

The farm itself contains 17 [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") plants and three [chicken nests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"). Three [rock warbler eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") can be found around the walls of the farm and many [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") can be harvested nearby.

Rogatus will ask you to deliver a [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Rogatus%27s_Letter "Skyrim:Rogatus's Letter") for him, while [Vigdis](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigdis_Salvius "Skyrim:Vigdis Salvius") runs the farm and will pay for any harvested potatoes.

- Just to the east of the farm by the signpost adjacent to the bridge is the camping site for [Ri'saad](/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad "Skyrim:Ri'saad")'s [caravan](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_Traders "Skyrim:Khajiit Traders").

## Bugs

- The chicken may not be marked as owned by the farm.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.1, fixes this bug.
- The Imperial guard who patrols near the farm is called "Markarth City Guard", even though he isn't in the city.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The guard is now called "Reach Hold Guard".
