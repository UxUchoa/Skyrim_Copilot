### A Student of the Craft

On top of his duties as court wizard, [Farengar Secret-Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Farengar_Secret-Fire "Skyrim:Farengar Secret-Fire") of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") also provides tips on the basics of [enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") to prospective mages. Find him at his research station in [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach") and ask him how to enchant weapons and armor. Upon doing so, he will introduce you the basics of enchanting in Skyrim:

*"Ah, a student of the craft. You'll need to start by learning an [enchantment](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting_Effects "Skyrim:Enchanting Effects"). Take an enchanted weapon or piece of armor, then use the Arcane Enchanter to learn its secrets. The item is destroyed in the process, so be wary. Once you know an enchantment, you'll need a filled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), and something to enchant. You'll use the Arcane Enchanter for that, too. Of course I have everything you need for sale, if you have the coin."'*

### Master in the Making

Using this advice, you may walk over to his arcane enchanter and begin enchanting. The quest will complete upon activating the enchanter. It is not necessary to enchant or even disenchant an item to complete this quest.

## Notes

- This quest will end with no failure message if you stray too far from Dragonsreach, either by fast travel or by simply walking down the steps outside. The quest remains active throughout nearly all of Dragonsreach including both the Great Porch and the castle grounds, but can disappear if you get too close to the secret escape to the Guard Barracks in the dungeons.[*verification needed*]
- This quest is always available, even if you already have 100 Enchanting levels.
