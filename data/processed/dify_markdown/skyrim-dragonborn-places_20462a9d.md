# Skyrim:Dragonborn Places

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-place-Solstheim.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solstheim.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Solstheim.jpg)

The island of Solstheim

The *[Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")* add-on adds several new **places**, mostly on the island of [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim"). For an overview of all the icons used for places on the map, see [Maps](/wiki/Skyrim:Maps#Icons "Skyrim:Maps").

## Cities

:   [![SR-mapicon-Raven Rock.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-mapicon-Raven_Rock.png/20px-SR-mapicon-Raven_Rock.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Raven_Rock.png) **[Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock")** — A city on the southwest coast of Solstheim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Raven+Rock)

    :   - **[Abandoned Building](/wiki/Skyrim:Abandoned_Building "Skyrim:Abandoned Building")** — The home of Bralsa Drel and Rirns Llervu in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Abandoned+Building)
        - **[Alor House](/wiki/Skyrim:Alor_House "Skyrim:Alor House")** — The home of Fethis Alor and his daughter Dreyla Alor in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Alor+House)
        - **[The Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark "Skyrim:The Bulwark")** — An imposing stone wall created to protect Raven Rock from ash storms, with the city's garrison and jail both built directly into the wall. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=The+Bulwark)

        :   - **[The Bulwark Jail](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark_Jail "Skyrim:The Bulwark Jail")** — The jail in Raven Rock, built directly into the Bulwark. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=The+Bulwark+Jail)

        - **[Caerellius House](/wiki/Skyrim:Caerellius_House "Skyrim:Caerellius House")** — The home of Crescius Caerellius and Aphia Velothi in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Caerellius+House)
        - **[Glover Mallory's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory%27s_House "Skyrim:Glover Mallory's House")** — The home of Glover Mallory in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Glover+Mallory%27s+House)
        - **[Ienth Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Ienth_Farm "Skyrim:Ienth Farm")** — The home of Garyn Ienth and Milore Ienth in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Ienth+Farm)
        - **[Morvayn Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Morvayn_Manor "Skyrim:Morvayn Manor")** — The home of Lleril Morvayn, Adril Arano, and Cindiri Arano in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Morvayn+Manor)
        - [![SR-mapicon-Mine.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-mapicon-Mine.png/20px-SR-mapicon-Mine.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Mine.png) **[Raven Rock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Mine "Skyrim:Raven Rock Mine")** — A small ebony mine in northern Raven Rock initially containing draugr, frostbite spiders, skeevers, and the dragon priest Zahkriisos. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Raven+Rock+Mine)
        - **[The Retching Netch](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Retching_Netch "Skyrim:The Retching Netch")** — The cornerclub in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=The+Retching+Netch)
        - **[Severin Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Severin_Manor "Skyrim:Severin Manor")** — Initially the home of Mirri Severin, Tilisu Severin, and Vendil Severin in Raven Rock, which can be obtained as player housing by completing the quest Served Cold. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Severin+Manor)
        - **[Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple "Skyrim:Temple")** — A Dunmer place of worship in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Temple)

        :   - **[Morvayn Ancestral Tomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Morvayn_Ancestral_Tomb "Skyrim:Morvayn Ancestral Tomb")** — A Dunmer ancestral tomb in Raven Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Morvayn+Ancestral+Tomb)
            - **[Temple Ancestral Tomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_Ancestral_Tomb "Skyrim:Temple Ancestral Tomb")** — A Dunmer ancestral tomb in Raven Rock.

## Notes

- Upon arrival at the docks of Raven Rock, you will be able to fast travel freely between Skyrim and Solstheim through the use of the [![SR-mapicon-To Skyrim.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-mapicon-To_Skyrim.png/18px-SR-mapicon-To_Skyrim.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-To_Skyrim.png) **To Skyrim** and [![SR-mapicon-To Solstheim.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-mapicon-To_Solstheim.png/18px-SR-mapicon-To_Solstheim.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-To_Solstheim.png) **To Solstheim** map markers.
- If you attempt to swim away from Solstheim, an invisible barrier will prevent you from proceeding further, and the message *"You cannot go that way."* will appear on the top left of the screen.
- Several of the exploration musical tracks that previously played in *[Morrowind](/wiki/Morrowind:Music "Morrowind:Music")* play on Solstheim. The Skyrim Atmospheres tracks that play in areas like [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") and [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") also play here.
- The clouds on the main Skyrim map seem to move east, whereas the ones on the Solstheim map seem to move west.
- The "Show On Map" function in your journal for quests that take place on Solstheim will only work while you are on the island.
- If you bring [Serana](/wiki/Skyrim:Serana "Skyrim:Serana")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") here, she may comment on the island's lack of vegetation.
- [Arngeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Arngeir "Skyrim:Arngeir") won't help you learn any [Words of Power](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Words_of_Power "Skyrim:The Words of Power") here.

## Bugs

- The combat taunt "You won't leave Skyrim alive!" may also play if the battle takes place on Solstheim.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
- Some merchants on Solstheim can incorrectly prompt you with dialogue that's used by [Fences](/wiki/Skyrim:Fence_(merchant) "Skyrim:Fence (merchant)"). There are none on the island, unless you invest in Glover Mallory with the Fence perk active.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
