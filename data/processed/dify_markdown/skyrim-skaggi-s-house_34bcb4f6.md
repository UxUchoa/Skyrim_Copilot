Skaggi's House

**Skaggi's House** is the home of [Skaggi Scar-Face](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaggi_Scar-Face "Skyrim:Skaggi Scar-Face") and [Sosia Tremellia](/wiki/Skyrim:Sosia_Tremellia "Skyrim:Sosia Tremellia").

It is the last house on the left on the approach to [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine"), next door to [Daighre's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Daighre%27s_House "Skyrim:Daighre's House").

### Exterior

It is a single story Dwemer style dwelling, with a novice-locked door. It is opposite the smelter outside of the mine. A [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") can be found wandering around outside.

### Skaggi's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/SR-interior-Skaggi%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Skaggi%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Skaggi%27s_House.jpg)

Skaggi's house interior

The house consists of a single room with a large fireplace on the east wall with two [cooking pots](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a third pot containing a [rabbit haunch](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_Haunch "Skyrim:Rabbit Haunch") and [carrot](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"). To the left of the fireplace, on the floor is a mead barrel and basket containing two sheaves of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), while to the right is a pile of firewood. Opposite the fireplace, coming out from the west wall is a large double bed with an end table to the right holding a bunch of [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") and two bunches of [jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes"), one in a wooden bowl. To the left of the bed, on a ledge is a copy of *[Killing - Before You're Killed](/wiki/Skyrim:Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed "Skyrim:Killing - Before You're Killed")* and on the floor, in a bucket are copies of *[The Holds of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Holds_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:The Holds of Skyrim")* and *[The City of Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_City_of_Stone "Skyrim:The City of Stone")*. Against the south wall is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), while against the north wall is square wooden table and two chairs, with a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") on the table.
