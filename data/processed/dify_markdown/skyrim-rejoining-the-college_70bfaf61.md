## Quick Walkthrough

1. Find [Tolfdir](/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir "Skyrim:Tolfdir").
2. Pay the fine.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This quest will automatically begin after killing another College member, provided you have already joined the College yourself. The quest description will inform you that you have been suspended from the College. It is a very short quest, requiring you only to find [Tolfdir](/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir "Skyrim:Tolfdir") in the College and speak to him. Tolfdir lectures you about behaviors that the College finds inappropriate, and states that he will have your suspension lifted if you pay a fine. Paying the fine results in immediate reinstatement into the College's ranks and completion of the quest.

## Notes

- This quest may begin even if you have completed the College of Winterhold questline and became Arch-Mage.
- Murder is the only crime that will trigger this quest; lesser crimes, such as pickpocketing and theft, will not.
- The fine for the first incident is 250 gold. If you commit another crime and receive the quest again, the fine climbs to 500 gold. It increases to 750 gold for the third incident, and 1,000 gold for each incident thereafter.
- Tolfdir increases the amount of the fine according to the number of past incidents, not their severity. Therefore, as long as it's your first such incident, you can murder a non-essential member of the College and only have to pay 250 gold, one quarter of the bounty that would be charged in any hold for murder.
- Tolfdir may instead be found in [Saarthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Saarthal "Skyrim:Saarthal") if you are between starting [Under Saarthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Under_Saarthal "Skyrim:Under Saarthal") and finishing [Hitting the Books](/wiki/Skyrim:Hitting_the_Books "Skyrim:Hitting the Books").
- The [Wizard Duel](/wiki/Skyrim:Wizard_Duel "Skyrim:Wizard Duel") random encounter may lead to your expulsion from the College, triggering this quest.

- Sometimes killing yourself will trigger this quest. On PC, it can be accomplished, by typing the console command `player.placeatme 00000007` (Dovahkiin's base ID), then killing the newly created NPC.
