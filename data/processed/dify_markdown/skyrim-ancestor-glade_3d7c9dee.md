Ancestor Glade

The **Ancestor Glade** is a small glade in a cavern east-southeast of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). It contains only one zone, *[Ancestor Glade](#Ancestor_Glade)*.

The glade is one of several on [Tamriel](/wiki/Lore:Tamriel "Lore:Tamriel") and the only one in [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"). It is so named for being home to swarms of [ancestor moths](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestor_Moth "Skyrim:Ancestor Moth"), and is the only location where such moths can be found. It also features the rare [yellow mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Yellow_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Yellow Mountain Flower"). With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, the waters of the glade are home to the rare [angelfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Angelfish "Skyrim:Angelfish")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing"), [pygmy sunfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Pygmy_Sunfish "Skyrim:Pygmy Sunfish")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing"), and [spadefish](/wiki/Skyrim:Spadefish "Skyrim:Spadefish")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing"). The cavern is central to the quest [Unseen Visions](/wiki/Skyrim:Unseen_Visions "Skyrim:Unseen Visions"), although it can be visited at any time.

## Related Quests

- **[Unseen Visions](/wiki/Skyrim:Unseen_Visions "Skyrim:Unseen Visions")**: Perform a ceremony to read the Elder Scroll.

### Exterior

The entrance is high in the mountains, above the snowline east of Falkreath, south of [Pinewatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Pinewatch "Skyrim:Pinewatch"). A few paces to the northeast of the entrance, behind a large rock, is an old campsite complete with an extinguished campfire, with two pots hanging over it. One contains a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") and the other a [venison chop](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison_Chop "Skyrim:Venison Chop"). Beside the campfire are an [ice wolf pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf_Pelt "Skyrim:Ice Wolf Pelt"), a [snow bear pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Bear Pelt"), a dead [ice wolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf "Skyrim:Ice Wolf"), an unowned bedroll, and a novice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). Everything in and around the campsite is marked as owned and, as such, taking anything is considered stealing, although you can skin the dead ice wolf without committing a [crime](/wiki/Skyrim:Crime "Skyrim:Crime").

### Ancestor Glade

The entrance descends into an overgrown passage that leads to a dead end. Another path on the left follows the wall, leads you across a fallen tree, and then into another short tunnel opening up to the main glade. If you have not yet started the quest Unseen Visions, you'll be ambushed by three [spriggan earth mothers](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan_Earth_Mother "Skyrim:Spriggan Earth Mother") in the first area of the cavern, regardless of your level. During the quest, there is a wooden gate that will close behind you as you enter the main glade, to prevent you leaving prematurely. There are many piles of rocks, all found against the outer walls of the main glade, which have items of treasure on top. The items can easily fall off, so it's worth scouring the ground nearby and downhill from them if an item is missing.

The following harvestable plants can be found in the area. [Hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss") is entirely within the initial cavern, while most other plants are found within the glade itself. Ancestor Glade interior

There are five Canticle trees found within the glade, which appear as trees covered with white and pink blossoms. During the quest, they can be harvested for their [bark](/wiki/Skyrim:Canticle_Bark "Skyrim:Canticle Bark"). Before the bark can be harvested, you will require the [draw knife](/wiki/Skyrim:Draw_Knife "Skyrim:Draw Knife"), which is found by following the path from the entrance to the main glade as it descends several flights of stairs. At the bottom, a path passes under several square arches formed by a flat stone being placed atop two upright stones a few paces apart. In the center of an island surrounded by hot springs and shallow water is a strange stone shrine, with the knife in the center. It is only obtainable during the quest. One of the Canticle trees and several yellow mountain flowers are nearby.

Another Canticle tree can be found by following the outer wall to the left of the entrance to the main glade. Continuing past this, you will find a large pile of rocks against the outer wall with a random [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") and leveled [potion of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka") on top. Back from the entrance, following the outer wall to the right, you will come to another pile of rocks with a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") on top. Continue following the wall down to the western corner of the glade and on yet another pile of rocks is a random [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") or random [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet") and random enchanted [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"). Follow the stream toward the springs until you reach the shrine. From there, the stairs to the southwest lead up, curving to the southeast before coming to a flat, stone landing. Turn southwest just before the landing, passing across several more heated springs, to the third Canticle tree. To the west of it is a pile of rocks with a random enchanted [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword") on top.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-interior-Ancestor_Glade_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ancestor_Glade_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ancestor_Glade_02.jpg)

Ancestor Glade interior

Return to the landing and follow the stairs and path to the east as they curve northeast, bringing you to another Canticle tree. Following the outer wall beyond this tree, you will find a pile of rocks with a random bow on top. From the fourth tree, there is a path to the southwest just left of the one you got here by. This one leads upwards past a pile of rocks topped with a filled random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") in a holder. On a lower rock are four loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), an [amethyst](/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst "Skyrim:Amethyst") and a [silver jeweled necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Jeweled_Necklace "Skyrim:Silver Jeweled Necklace"). Beyond these is a spring coming out from under the outer wall before dropping over the edge and forming the largest waterfall in the glade. Across the stream is the fifth and final Canticle tree. Just past this, concealed by a fir tree, is a final pile of rocks where you will find a random enchanted helmet and a [ruby](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby "Skyrim:Ruby"). You will be above the first turning of the stairs, with a short drop from here.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-interior-Ancestor_Glade_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ancestor_Glade_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ancestor_Glade_03.jpg)

Treasure found within

There are multiple swarms of ancestor moths all around the area, as well as a few gatherings that land on the flora around the glade. The former can only be interacted with during the quest, while the latter you are able to harvest like other insects for their [wings](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestor_Moth_Wing "Skyrim:Ancestor Moth Wing") for use in [alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy"). Near the first tree, to the left of the entrance, are the most that can be caught individually, either landing on flora or flying on their own between patches of flowers.

Once you've collected the knife, harvested the bark and attracted seven swarms of ancient moths, you will need to enter the beam of light near the shrine in order to read the [Elder Scroll of Blood](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder_Scroll_(Blood) "Skyrim:Elder Scroll (Blood)"). After reading the Elder Scroll, you will be attacked by a leveled group of either [Dawnguard soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)") including an [armored troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Armored_Troll "Skyrim:Armored Troll"), or [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") including [vampire's thralls](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Thrall "Skyrim:Vampire's Thrall"), depending on your chosen side. These enemies will continue to respawn every few days, like any other dungeon inhabitant. They spawn in a group of five to six at the foot of the stairs leading into the glade.

## Notes

- It is highly recommended that you save before entering the glade, especially during Unseen Visions, due to the number of reported [bugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Unseen_Visions#Bugs "Skyrim:Unseen Visions") relating to the quest.
- During Unseen Visions, the glade is unoccupied. However, enemies from the opposing faction arrive in the glade as soon as Unseen Visions ends.
- This location contains the most (and only non-quest-locked) yellow mountain flowers, and the only place in the game for gathering ancestor moth wings.
- The Draw Knife does not appear in its holder if the *Dawnguard* quest has not been initiated. Attempting to harvest bark from the trees will have the game telling you that "you lack the proper item".
- If you use [Become Ethereal](/wiki/Skyrim:Become_Ethereal "Skyrim:Become Ethereal") while the moths are following you, the moths will become ethereal as well.
- The music that plays inside is environmental music instead of cave music.

## Bugs

- You may only find the ancestor moth swarms but no ancestor moths that you can catch, leaving you unable to collect any ancestor moth wings.
- During Unseen Visions, the vampire or Dawnguard attackers who get enabled are never disabled again after the quest is over. This leads to some of them respawning here later when they aren't supposed to.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.3, fixes this bug.
- The location name, parent location, and worldspace map marker settings for Ancestor Glade may be missing.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.3, fixes this bug.
