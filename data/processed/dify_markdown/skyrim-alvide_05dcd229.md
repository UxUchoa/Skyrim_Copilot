Alvide

**Alvide** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") camped out in [Dayspring Canyon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dayspring_Canyon "Skyrim:Dayspring Canyon"). She came to [Fort Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dawnguard "Skyrim:Fort Dawnguard") for protection from the [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") but the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)") won't let her in. She came with her husband [Barknar](/wiki/Skyrim:Barknar_(Dawnguard) "Skyrim:Barknar (Dawnguard)") who also lives in the camp.

Like the other refugees, Alvide will show up in Dayspring Canyon during the quest [Beyond Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Beyond_Death "Skyrim:Beyond Death"). With no access to the fort, she will do very little but wander around, at times chopping some firewood or use the [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"). She never sleeps. She wears a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic#0001be1a "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b "Skyrim:Boots").

## Dialogue

She is worried about her situation, potentially saying:

:   *"What if the vampires come here, after the Dawnguard? We'll be stuck right in the middle."*
:   *"Barknar insisted we come here, said we'd be safe. He also said we'd be inside the walls."*
:   *"At least Barknar and I are here together."*
:   *"I hope we can go home soon. I don't like it here."*

## Notes

- Strangely, Alvide lacks any kind of schedule. She will simply idle where she was put.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dawnguard Patch"), version 1.2.3, addresses this issue. She has been given a default sandbox package.
