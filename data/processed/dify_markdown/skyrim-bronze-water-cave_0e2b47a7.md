Bronze Water Cave

**Bronze Water Cave** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") west of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") containing leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals"). It contains only one zone, *[Bronze Water Cave](#Bronze_Water_Cave)*.

The entrance to the cave is on the north shore of the semi-frozen [Lake Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Yorgrim "Skyrim:Lake Yorgrim"). Several Dwemer bronze pipes on either side and in front of the entrance seem to be distributing hot water or steam into the lake.

### Bronze Water Cave

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-interior-Bronze_Water_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bronze_Water_Cave.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bronze_Water_Cave.jpg)

Bronze Water Cave interior

Upon entering, there is a short tunnel that opens into a small chamber containing more pipes along the walls. Two leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") have made this cave their home. On the ground are various bones, both human and animal, as well as blood and gore. Among the debris is a random [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"). Against the east wall is an unlocked Dwemer [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and farther to the south (toward the exit), beside the same pipe, is an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel").

The cave's main purpose is to serve as an escape route from the flooded *[Irkngthand Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand#Irkngthand_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Irkngthand")* near the end of the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") quest [Blindsighted](/wiki/Skyrim:Blindsighted "Skyrim:Blindsighted"), with the exit from *Irkngthand Sanctuary* in the back of the cave. During the quest, this cave will not contain any animals. After completion of the quest, Bronze Water Cave can be used to access *Irkngthand Sanctuary*.

## Bugs

- The [Dwarven spider](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Spider "Skyrim:Dwarven Spider") ambush outside Bronze Water Cave can be triggered prematurely due to the spiders not being disabled before the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") quest [Beneath Bronze Waters](/wiki/Skyrim:Beneath_Bronze_Waters "Skyrim:Beneath Bronze Waters"). If the ambush is triggered prematurely, the quest will then be impossible to start, as the spiders are required for the quest.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.9a, fixes this bug.
- The trigger box for Kinthal's Saints bandit camp at Bronze Water Cave may attempt to disable itself even though it's controlled by an enable parent.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The DisableWhenDone flag has been removed as it's also flagged DoOnce and won't trigger again anyway.
