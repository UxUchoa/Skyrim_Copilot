The Water Stone

The **Water Stone** is an [All-Maker Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones") north of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

Once [cleansed](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones"), this stone gives you the power to [heal](/wiki/Skyrim:Waters_of_Life "Skyrim:Waters of Life") everyone around you for 200 points. Once you use this power, it disappears from the Powers menu, and you must return to the Water Stone to obtain it again.

## Related Quests

- **[Cleansing the Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones")**: Use the [Bend Will](/wiki/Skyrim:Bend_Will "Skyrim:Bend Will") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") to cleanse each of the remaining [All-Maker Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-place-Water_Stone_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Water_Stone_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Water_Stone_02.jpg)

The cleansed Water Stone

The stone shows the usual signs of construction, with wooden scaffolding on the southeast side, and [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), [shovels](/wiki/Skyrim:Shovel "Skyrim:Shovel"), hammers, and carts in the vicinity. Several [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") plants and [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") grow around the stone. A path leads south to [Damphall Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Damphall_Mine "Skyrim:Damphall Mine").

Before you cleanse the stone, a leveled [cultist](/wiki/Skyrim:Cultist "Skyrim:Cultist") will attack as you approach. [Benkum](/wiki/Skyrim:Benkum "Skyrim:Benkum"), [Hjalfar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjalfar "Skyrim:Hjalfar"), [Liesl](/wiki/Skyrim:Liesl "Skyrim:Liesl"), and [Palevius Lex](/wiki/Skyrim:Palevius_Lex "Skyrim:Palevius Lex") will be working on and bowing to the stone. When you cleanse the stone, a leveled [lurker](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker") will rise from the water around the base of the stone and attack you and the workers. After the lurker is defeated, the sailors and their ship just offshore will be removed from the game once you leave the area.

## Notes

- Typically, a low-level [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") will also fly in from the west, although there are scattered reports of this not happening. If you haven't already triggered the rieklings hidden in barrels to the southeast, they'll also arrive shortly, which can make for a chaotic battle. Fortunately, you don't need to guarantee the safety of the sailors.
- The Water Stone is not included in the list of stones you can wake up at when [sleeping](/wiki/Skyrim:Sleeping "Skyrim:Sleeping") on Solstheim if [Cleansing the Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones") isn't finished yet.
- The Water Stone also appears in *[Bloodmoon](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Water_Stone "Bloodmoon:Water Stone")*.

## Bugs

- The sailors are sometimes not removed along with their ship after the stone is cleansed, and are left stranded by the beach west of the stone.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.2, fixes this bug.
