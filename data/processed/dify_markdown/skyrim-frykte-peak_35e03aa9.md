Frykte Peak, seen from Saering's Watch

**Frykte Peak** is one of the highest points on Solstheim, near [Saering's Watch](/wiki/Skyrim:Saering%27s_Watch "Skyrim:Saering's Watch"). The marker for this peak does not appear on the compass until you have [climbed](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing "Skyrim:Mountain Climbing") nearly to the top.

Frykte Peak is easiest to reach with a [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse")—using [Summon Arvak](/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arvak "Skyrim:Summon Arvak")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") is recommended. It is reachable on foot with enough persistence, but requires some creative jumping. See [this article](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing "Skyrim:Mountain Climbing") for tips on mountain climbing.
