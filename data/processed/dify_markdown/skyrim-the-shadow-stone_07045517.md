**The Shadow Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones") scattered across Skyrim, and is located south of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), east of [Snow-Shod Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow-Shod_Farm "Skyrim:Snow-Shod Farm"), near [Nightingale Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Hall "Skyrim:Nightingale Hall").

## The Shadow Stone

***Once a day, those under the sign of The Shadow can become [invisible](/wiki/Skyrim:Invisibility_(effect) "Skyrim:Invisibility (effect)") for an extended period.***

When nearing the stone care should be taken, as there is a [respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") high leveled [conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjurer "Skyrim:Conjurer") or [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer") lurking by the stone. As soon as you are detected, they will warn you off, if you don't immediately leave they will attack you. They may be accompanied by multiple hostile [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") who will all attack as soon as you are detected. The stone itself is surrounded by a further nine standing stones that were carved, but show no markings. A few paces to the east, by a fallen piece of masonry is a lootable skeleton with a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") laying on the ground beside it.

The power the stone confers is unique in that the [invisibility](/wiki/Skyrim:Invisibility_(effect) "Skyrim:Invisibility (effect)") effect takes place instantly, instead of having to cast it like you would with an [Invisibility](/wiki/Skyrim:Invisibility_(spell) "Skyrim:Invisibility (spell)") spell, this lack of delay could save you in a tight spot.

## Notes

- The power granted by the Shadow Stone lasts 60 seconds or until canceled.
