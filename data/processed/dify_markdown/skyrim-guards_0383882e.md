**Guards** are NPCs who are usually found in towns and cities in Skyrim. If you commit a [crime](/wiki/Skyrim:Crime "Skyrim:Crime"), they can arrest you and will attack if the arrest is resisted. Under some conditions, guards can be bribed; see the crime page for details.

## Guards by Region

All of the guards below will pay attention to your bounty in the hold that they are patrolling for, and only attack you if you resist arrest when they try to arrest you. Note that it is possible for a guard in the wilderness to wander into a different hold, usually because of combat, but you will still only be arrested if you have a bounty in the hold that the guard is from.

In some quests, there are NPCs called guards who do not obey these rules, and may attack you despite not having tried to arrest you; most of those NPCs are in the Reach.

## Notes

- Guards will attack you if they see you wearing the [Gray Cowl of Nocturnal](/wiki/Skyrim:Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal "Skyrim:Gray Cowl of Nocturnal")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gray_Cowl_Returns! "Skyrim:The Gray Cowl Returns!"). [Followers](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") who wear the cowl are not affected by this.
- For unknown reasons, female guards only appear in [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks")-controlled cities and holds, and are completely absent from [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion")-controlled cities as well as [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn").

## Bugs

- Guards may have access to double-damage perks which can cause them to deliver one-shot deathblows, even to high-level players.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
- A number of city, town, and hold guards fail to respawn due to missing respawn flags in the game data. This can potentially lead to depopulation of the entire guard force in the affected areas over time.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
- CWSoldierExtraDamageToPlayer and CWGuardExtraDamageToPlayer don't take into account the NPCs with those perks using two-handed weapons, as other extra damage perks do.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
- Guards will sometimes have their usual routines glitch so that instead of patrolling their areas, they will engage in common civilian behavior such as sitting on benches or chopping wood. **?**
