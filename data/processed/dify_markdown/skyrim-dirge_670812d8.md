Dirge

**Dirge** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [food vendor](/wiki/Skyrim:Food_Vendor "Skyrim:Food Vendor") and a member of the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") who lives and works at the [Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon") as their bouncer. He is brother to [Maul](/wiki/Skyrim:Maul "Skyrim:Maul"), a former member of the Guild. Initially he will be found standing imposingly on the wooden runway leading to the Flagon from the [Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway"), but will then start patrolling the entire span of the tavern.

Befitting his role, he is clad in [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor"), [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers"), and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots"), and wields a leveled [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe") (up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") quality). He also carries a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and has a random chance of carrying some gold, lockpicks, food, drink, gems, or jewelry.

## Dialogue

Although he is in good relations with fellow lookout [Tonilia](/wiki/Skyrim:Tonilia "Skyrim:Tonilia") and bartender [Vekel](/wiki/Skyrim:Vekel_the_Man "Skyrim:Vekel the Man"), he will be coarse with you and always square you up with a selection of random threats:

:   *"Stay outta trouble, or there's gonna be trouble."*
:   *"Last person who made trouble for Vekel ended up with a smashed head."*
:   *"Vekel doesn't like strangers snooping around the Flagon."*
:   *"There's two things to do at the Flagon: spend coin and then get out."*

When you join the guild, his attitude won't improve one bit, even if you eventually become Guild Master.

:   "*Even if you're one of us, you better not make trouble.*"
:   "*I don't care if you're best buddies with the Guild Master. I'll still smash in your skull if you try anything.*"
:   "*The last person that made trouble for Vekel ended up floating in the canal... catch my drift?*"

If you're suspended from the guild, he'll warn you:

:   "*You better straighten things with the Guild or you won't be welcome here anymore.*"

Speaking to him:

:   **Why do they call you Dirge?**
:   *"They call me Dirge, 'cause I'm the last thing you hear before they put you in the ground. Why? You think it's funny or something?"*

:   :   **No way. That name would scare anyone. (Persuade)**
    :   *"Oh yeah? I think so too. You're pretty smart. Hey, here. Have one on the house.*"

:   :   **I think it's hilarious. You got a problem? (Intimidate)**
    :   *"No, no. Sorry if I sounded so mean there. My brother always said I have a big mouth. Here, have one on me."*

If you pass either speech option, Dirge will give you a bottle of [Black-Briar Mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") for free.

### A Cornered Rat

During the quest [A Cornered Rat](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Cornered_Rat "Skyrim:A Cornered Rat"), Dirge may help with gathering information on [Esbern](/wiki/Skyrim:Esbern "Skyrim:Esbern"). If you ask him about an old man he'll reply in four different ways, depending on whether you're part of the guild, whether you're friends with him and whether you've already learned he's in the Warrens:

:   **I'm looking for an old guy, hiding out somewhere in Riften.**

:   "*Don't know his name, but there is a guy like that hiding out down here. Paid plenty for nobody to know about it. You're not the first person to come in here asking about him, either, so be careful.*"
:   "*Yeah, I think I know who you mean. He's paid good money for nobody to know he's down here, but I guess I can tell you.*"
:   "*That's interesting. Why do I care? You want a drink, or maybe just a few broken bones?*"
:   "*Down in the Ratway Warrens. Nice place, if you don't mind the stench, rats, and assorted lowlifes as neighbors.*"

If you don't manage to get the information straight from him you can try to bribe, persuade or intimidate him:

| Option | Dialogue | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| I need to talk to him. I'm a friend. His life's in danger.  **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: A friend, huh? Well... then it's probably okay if I tell you. **Failed**: I guess you don't hear too well. I never heard of this "friend" of yours. | | | |
| Maybe this will help your memory.  **(Bribe)** | **Passed**: You know what? I think I do know the guy you're talking about. **Failed**: Maybe it would. If you had it. | | | |
| Are you going to tell me, or am I going to have to beat it out of you?  **(Intimidate)** | **Passed**: Easy there. I don't want that kind of trouble. Guy ain't worth it. **Failed**: Yeah. That's what it's going to take. | | | |

If you press him to tell you who else is looking for Esbern he'll confide:

:   "*Various shady types I've never seen before. I'd watch my back if I was you.*"

## Notes

- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide")*, the summary for him reads *"Dirge is a hulking behemoth of an Imperial who serves as the bouncer and general crowd control for the Ragged Flagon. Dirge earned his nickname because his voice is the last thing people hear before they die. He has been good friends with Vekel the Man for years."*
- If [Vekel the Man](/wiki/Skyrim:Vekel_the_Man "Skyrim:Vekel the Man") were to die, Dirge would take over running the bar in the Ragged Flagon. However, since Vekel is essential, this will not happen during standard gameplay.
- As Maul is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord"), it must be assumed the two had the same father but different mothers.

## Bugs

- After talking to Maul about him, Dirge was supposed to tell you:
  **I met your brother.**
  *"Maul? Yeah. That jerk works for Maven Black-Briar now. Left me down here watching this garbage heap. I guess he's better at all that talking stuff anyway. I'd just mess everything up."*
  However, the wrong dialogue variable is checked, so you'll never hear it.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.1, fixes this bug.
- Dirge's confidence level is so high, you cannot intimate him no matter how high your [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill is.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
