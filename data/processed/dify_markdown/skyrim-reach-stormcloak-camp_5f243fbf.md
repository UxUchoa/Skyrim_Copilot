Reach Stormcloak Camp

**Reach Stormcloak Camp** is a [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Camps "Skyrim:Stormcloak Camps") in [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"), located west of [Liar's Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Liar%27s_Retreat "Skyrim:Liar's Retreat") and northeast of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten").

The camp is normally commanded by [Kottir Red-Shoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Kottir_Red-Shoal "Skyrim:Kottir Red-Shoal"), except during the related quests, at which point [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist") will assume command.

## Reach Stormcloak Camp

It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. To the north and east of the campfire are three small tents containing unowned hay piles. Between the small tents is the command tent, containing a lit brazier, a round table, and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of their time. Southwest of the fire is the hospital tent, which contains three hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers and an unlocked chest containing various items to treat the sick, including potions of [cure poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison "Skyrim:Cure Poison"), potions of [cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Cure Disease"), and [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). Beside the tent is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and a low table holding a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). Down the hill behind the hospital tent is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). Beside the block are two picketed [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses"). Unlike other camps, taking them is not considered stealing; however, if the camp no longer exists, the horses will still be present, and taking them will then be considered stealing. West of the campfire is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and an end table. To the right of the commander's tent are some barrels. Farther west is the workstation for the [Stormcloak quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Stormcloak Quartermaster"), consisting of an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"). A long wooden table and a crate hold various [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") and [steel weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") and [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor").

Any time [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Legion "Skyrim:Joining the Legion"), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.
