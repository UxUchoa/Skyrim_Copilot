Broken Tower Redoubt

**Broken Tower Redoubt** is a medium-sized [fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Military_Forts "Skyrim:Military Forts") east of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten") containing [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"). It contains two zones, both named *Broken Tower Redoubt*: the [ground level](#Broken_Tower_Redoubt:_Ground_Level) and the [upper tower](#Broken_Tower_Redoubt:_Upper_Tower).

It is a dangerous place, making travel between Karthwasten and [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead") hazardous. The guards of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") will warn you of the danger, stating, *"Beware the region northeast, across the river. Forsworn have taken refuge in Broken Tower Redoubt and Red Eagle Redoubt."*

### Exterior: Ground Level

The fort lies along the road between Karthwasten and Rorikstead. One [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") patrols the road, and another two Forsworn on the middle level exterior will attack with bows. If they lose sight of you, they will descend through the ground floor and try to find you. A [blue mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") grows by the door, and two [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") grow near the western end of the fort. Just past the western edge of the fort, two blue mountain flowers, a purple mountain flower, and two [juniper](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") trees grow by a dirt path leading to [Liar's Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Liar%27s_Retreat "Skyrim:Liar's Retreat").

### Broken Tower Redoubt: Ground Level

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt.jpg)

The main room of the fort

The ground floor door opens to a short twisting tunnel. After two turns it opens to a large room, with two Forsworn, one on the lower level and one on the higher level. There is a table to the left with two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and straight ahead is a small room with a random [potion of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka") on a barrel and fifteen bottles of Nord mead on a set of shelves. Opposite the first table is another with a lute on it. To the right of that table, in the darkness, is an adept-locked door leading to a storage room with thirteen bottles of Nord mead and a random [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina") on some shelves, another two bottles of Nord mead on a barrel, and a chest. Above the chest are seven hanging [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), with another two pheasants on the floor behind the chest.

From the main part of the room there are two sets of stairs. The one to the north has collapsed, but the one to the south is still intact. At the far end of the left-hand side on some fallen masonry are two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and hiding between two barrels is a [Dibella statue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dibella_Statue "Skyrim:Dibella Statue"), while to the right-hand side is a door leading deeper into the fort. In the next corridor, there is a [spiked wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Spike_Wall "Skyrim:Spike Wall") trap with two [pressure plates](/wiki/Skyrim:Pressure_Plate "Skyrim:Pressure Plate"), one on either side of the trap. The first is hidden in some hay by two barrels on the approach and has a table next to it holding a bottle of Alto wine, a wooden plate with a salmon steak and raw salmon meat on it, and a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). The second is beside another two barrels around the corner, just before the end of the spiked wall located to the right.

After this is a corridor running in a semi-circle around a circular room. There is a Forsworn patrolling the corridor, which has two doors; one to the north that leads outside to the exterior middle level, and one to the east leading back to the main room. There are two single beds and four bedrolls, all unowned, in the circular room. On the shelves to the left from the first entry are a copy of the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Mirror](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mirror "Skyrim:The Mirror")*, a [frenzy poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Frenzy_Poison "Skyrim:Frenzy Poison"), a [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel "Skyrim:Satchel"), an open [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"), and bottles of alto wine and wine. On the cupboard shelf after that are a [solution of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Solution of Enhanced Stamina") (worth over 1,000 gold), several bottles of alto wine and wine, and a bowl on top. By the end of the bed beside the cupboard is a crate with three loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), a large coin purse, a [silver ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ring "Skyrim:Silver Ring"), and sometimes a random potion on top, and by the other bed are a novice-locked chest and a table with a mead barrel and a bowl containing bread. There are also a wardrobe and a second table in the room.

The corridor to the east leads back to main room, above the collapsed staircase. There is a table with a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and a bottle of alto wine. The corridor leads to the base of the east tower, passing another exit to the middle exterior level on the left which has a barrel with a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") on top next to it. By the base of the stairs are a table with a salt pile and a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") on top, as well as some shelves with three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), three loose septims, other food, a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), and another drum. Halfway up the stairs is a [tripwire](/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire "Skyrim:Tripwire") that activates some [falling boulders](/wiki/Skyrim:Falling_Boulders "Skyrim:Falling Boulders"). A Forsworn patrols this part of the stairs, and can be killed by triggering the tripwire at the appropriate point. This may alert two other Forsworn in the area at the top of the stairs.

At the top of stairs is a room where another Forsworn waits. There are two unowned single beds in the room, and a table with a random potion, a bunch of [jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes"), a haunch of [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"), a bowl of [tomato soup](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato_Soup "Skyrim:Tomato Soup"), and a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat"). There are another bowl of tomato soup and a fillet of salmon meat on some cupboard shelves, a set of shelves holding two flutes and drums, and an unlocked chest by one of the beds. There are more stairs on the other side of the room, with a pickaxe on the ground by their base, leading up to a third exit, past a final Forsworn.

The camp on the middle level

### Exterior: Middle Level

The middle level of the exterior is guarded by two Forsworn, one to the east and one to the west. On the eastern side are an empty table and a simple tent with a bedroll. On the western side is a small camp, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), two hay piles, and a table with three [iron swords](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"), and a random [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe") on top and a pair of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots") underneath. There are two entrances to the interior from this level, one at each end.

### Exterior: Top Level

The top level exterior is the only way to get to the top of the western tower. It is guarded by a single leveled Forsworn, and there are two tables: one with a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow") and [forsworn arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Arrow "Skyrim:Forsworn Arrow") (which may have fallen through the table and be underneath), and the other with a random leveled potion.

### Broken Tower Redoubt: Upper Tower

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt_02.jpg)

The Briarheart and the statue covered in blood in the sacrificial chamber

The top level of the tower contains a statue and shrine to [Dibella](/wiki/Lore:Dibella "Lore:Dibella"), guarded by a [Forsworn Briarheart](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Briarheart "Skyrim:Forsworn Briarheart"); there is also a [lightning rune](/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Rune "Skyrim:Lightning Rune") in the middle of the room. The top level is split into the main chamber and a side bedroom. There is an expert-locked cell off the main room that is empty (unless you are here to rescue someone for a quest, in which case they will occupy this cell); the Briarheart carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Prison_Key "Skyrim:Broken Tower Prison Key") that unlocks the cell.

There is a table to the left of the entrance that holds a decapitated skeever, a copy of the [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Liminal Bridges](/wiki/Skyrim:Liminal_Bridges "Skyrim:Liminal Bridges")*, a random healing potion, two pieces of [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), and a copy of *[Ancestors and the Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestors_and_the_Dunmer "Skyrim:Ancestors and the Dunmer")*. There are two random frost weakness poisons, a bottle of wine, and two bottles of alto wine on the shelves beside that. On the floor there are seven sprigs of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry "Skyrim:Snowberry") and a bunch of [jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes") leading to the statue and [shrine of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Shrine of Dibella"). Beside the entrance to the bedroom is another set of shelves containing three flutes and two drums, with a lute next to it.

The bedroom to the north has an unowned double bed, an unlocked chest, a table, a cupboard, and an end table with some cheese on top. On the table are a bowl of [beef stew](/wiki/Skyrim:Beef_Stew "Skyrim:Beef Stew"), a [silver emerald necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Emerald_Necklace "Skyrim:Silver Emerald Necklace"), two bottles of alto wine, a copy of *[The Firmament](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firmament "Skyrim:The Firmament")*, a bowl of [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a plate holding a [grilled chicken breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Chicken_Breast "Skyrim:Grilled Chicken Breast"), and a [troll skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull "Skyrim:Troll Skull"). On the cupboard shelves are a random magicka potion, two [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), a canis root, a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), and some [horse meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Meat "Skyrim:Horse Meat").

## Notes

- It is possible to gain access to the highest level of Broken Tower Redoubt without going through all the levels by climbing down the mountain behind the fort.
- If you have started both [The Heart of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Heart_of_Dibella "Skyrim:The Heart of Dibella") and [The House of Horrors](/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Horrors "Skyrim:The House of Horrors"), and the [Radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant") system has chosen Broken Tower Redoubt as the location for [Logrolf the Willful](/wiki/Skyrim:Logrolf_the_Willful "Skyrim:Logrolf the Willful"), both he and [Fjotra](/wiki/Skyrim:Fjotra "Skyrim:Fjotra") will occupy the same cell in the tower.
- Despite being occupied by Forsworn, there is a large amount of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") to be found in the lower interior, 34 in total.
- There are a couple ore veins nearby: an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") on the road to the east, and a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") to the northeast with a skeleton and pickaxe. There is another corundum vein outside Liar's Retreat to the northwest.
- Aside from the Briarheart, all Forsworn enemies encountered here are female.
