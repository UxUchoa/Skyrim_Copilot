Reachwind Eyrie

**Reachwind Eyrie** is a small [Dwarven](/wiki/Lore:Dwemer "Lore:Dwemer") tower east-southeast of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). It contains only one zone, *[Reachwind Eyrie](#Reachwind_Eyrie)*.

The tower is perched atop a large cliff overlooking the [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River"), surrounded by some of Skyrim's greener lands. The building is remarkably well-preserved, considering the thousands of years it has stood unlocked.

### Reachwind Eyrie

Upon entering, there is a stone bench on either side of the door. Continuing up the winding stairway, you will come to a small room strewn with various [Dwemer scrap](/wiki/Skyrim:Ores_and_Ingots#Dwemer_Metal_.28Smeltable.29 "Skyrim:Ores and Ingots") and [Dwemer dishes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items#Dwemer_Dishes "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Items"). There is also a Dwemer dresser that has a 25% chance of containing 1-9 gold, as well as up to three random Dwemer scrap (75% chance each). If you accumulate all the scrap metals found in the room, you can smelt a total of 23 [Dwarven metal ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Metal_Ingot "Skyrim:Dwarven Metal Ingot") (more if the random items found in the dresser are smeltable).

Apart from Dwemer clutter, there are a few items of interest resting on the desk with the Dwemer banner hanging above it. These include a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Chimarvamidium](/wiki/Skyrim:Chimarvamidium "Skyrim:Chimarvamidium")*, a random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), a random [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), and a random [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet"), which can be either light or heavy. Also, there is an apprentice-locked chest containing random [loot](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). There are also two [wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") lying around the room.

### Exterior: Reachwind Eyrie Overlook

Reachwind Eyrie Overlook is an outdoor balcony that is accessible through a door to the left of the desk. Looking out of the balcony, you will see a spectacular view of western Skyrim. You will be able to see [Pavo's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Pavo%27s_House "Skyrim:Pavo's House") toward the northwest, and [Kolskeggr Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine") and [Sky Haven Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Sky_Haven_Temple "Skyrim:Sky Haven Temple") toward the north. Looking out to the northeast, you can see the ruined [Sundered Towers](/wiki/Skyrim:Sundered_Towers "Skyrim:Sundered Towers") and the tower at [Bleakwind Bluff](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleakwind_Bluff "Skyrim:Bleakwind Bluff") slightly east of it. Finally, if you look toward the east, you should be able to see [Old Hroldan Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Hroldan_Inn "Skyrim:Old Hroldan Inn").

To the left of the balcony door lies a birds' nest containing a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). There is a random [one-handed sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") resting on the parapet of the balcony. To the right of the balcony rests an expert-locked chest containing random loot.

## Notes

- This may be the tower of [Gauldur](/wiki/Lore:Gauldur "Lore:Gauldur"), as *[Daynas Valen's Notes](/wiki/Skyrim:Daynas_Valen%27s_Notes "Skyrim:Daynas Valen's Notes")* reveals that he was buried near it, and [Reachwater Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwater_Rock "Skyrim:Reachwater Rock") isn't too far away.
- The guards at [Dushnikh Yal](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnikh_Yal "Skyrim:Dushnikh Yal") may add this location to your map if you talk to them.
- [Dwarven Spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Spider "Skyrim:Dwarven Spider") may spawn here at higher levels.
