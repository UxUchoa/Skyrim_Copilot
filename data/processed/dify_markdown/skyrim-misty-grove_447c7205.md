**Misty Grove** is one of the [Myriad Realms of Revelry](/wiki/Lore:Revelry "Lore:Revelry") created by [Sanguine](/wiki/Skyrim:Sanguine "Skyrim:Sanguine"), where he is holding a feast with some of his followers. It is only accessible through a magic portal found in [Morvunskar](/wiki/Skyrim:Morvunskar "Skyrim:Morvunskar") during the related quest.

### Misty Grove

The grove can only be reached once you have followed the clues to locate [Sam Guevenne](/wiki/Skyrim:Sam_Guevenne "Skyrim:Sam Guevenne") during the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Night_To_Remember "Skyrim:A Night To Remember"). You travel here by entering a portal inside [Morvunskar](/wiki/Skyrim:Morvunskar "Skyrim:Morvunskar"). Upon being transported to the grove, you arrive at the beginning of a sunken path lit with lanterns spaced evenly along its length, with raised banks on both sides preventing you from straying too far from the fixed pathway. The path crosses and recrosses a burbling stream flowing from north to south, which also has raised banks. It always appears to be evening when you arrive. As you travel along the path, it is possible to catch a few [torchbugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug "Skyrim:Torchbug"), but the grove appears to contain no other creatures and no harvestable plants, flowers, or fungi. The path leads to a clearing prominently featuring a long table laid with a feast of four [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat"), a [long taffy treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Taffy_Treat "Skyrim:Long Taffy Treat"), four bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), and a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"). Around the table sit eight [commoners](/wiki/Skyrim:Commoner "Skyrim:Commoner") who are carousing and seem fairly deep in their cups; they wear a collection of common clothing, but don't carry any other items. There are two smaller tables beside the main table with small mead barrels on top, and strings of lanterns hanging overhead light the whole clearing. Finally, there is Sam Guevenne, who after conversation reveals himself to be the Daedric Prince [Sanguine](/wiki/Skyrim:Sanguine "Skyrim:Sanguine"). He gives you his [staff](/wiki/Skyrim:Sanguine_Rose "Skyrim:Sanguine Rose"), and returns you to the inn where you first met and agreed to the competition. Any items you picked up while in the grove remain in your inventory when you return to the inn.

## Notes

- Sam Guevenne describes this place as one "where the wine flows like water".
- None of the items here are owned, thus making them free to take.

## Bugs

- If you return to Morvunskar after completing A Night To Remember, sometimes the portal to Misty Grove will still be accessible. Passing through it will end with you being stuck in Misty Grove, as Sanguine is no longer there to teleport you out.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
  - Reload a save from before you entered.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") On the PC, you can open the console and type `tcl` to toggle collision, then fly under the map just below where you enter Misty Grove to find a door that will take you back to Morvunskar.
