# Skyrim:Side Quests

## Overview

[![Side Quests Knotwork](//images.uesp.net/9/96/SR-qico-Miscellaneous.png)](/wiki/File:SR-qico-Miscellaneous.png "Side Quests Knotwork")

**Side quests** are standard-length quests that are not part of a questline and are not [Daedric quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Quests "Skyrim:Daedric Quests"). Completing side quests generally increases the "Side Quests Completed" statistic in your journal and counts toward the [Sideways](#Achievements) [achievement](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements").

Side quests are listed separately in your journal. Unlike [miscellaneous quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests"), they are not subcategorized, and are logged in your journal after completion. Side quests can be identified by the side quest knotwork (pictured above left) displayed next to the quest title in your journal and on each quest's page on this wiki.

Quests are sorted below by their usual starting location.

## Achievements

One [achievement](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements") is obtained by completing side quests:

- [![SR-achievement-Sideways.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-achievement-Sideways.png/30px-SR-achievement-Sideways.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Sideways.png) **Sideways** (20 points/Bronze) — Complete 10 side quests
  - Completing any 10 qualifying side quests will unlock this achievement. Any quest that uses the side quest knotwork in your journal generally counts toward the achievement, but there are a few unusual cases:
    - The Black Book side quests, [Blood on the Ice](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood_on_the_Ice "Skyrim:Blood on the Ice"), and [Lost to the Ages](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages "Skyrim:Lost to the Ages") do not count toward the achievement, but are listed on this page and are treated as side quests in your journal.
    - [The Affairs of Hagravens](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Affairs_of_Hagravens "Skyrim:The Affairs of Hagravens") counts toward the achievement, but is not listed on this page and is treated as a [miscellaneous quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests") in your journal.
    - [Delayed Burial](/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial "Skyrim:Delayed Burial") is listed on this page and counts toward the achievement, but is otherwise categorized as a [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") quest and is marked in your journal with the Dark Brotherhood knotwork.
    - [Alteration Ritual Spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration_Ritual_Spell "Skyrim:Alteration Ritual Spell") is not listed on this page and does not count toward the achievement, but is mistakenly marked in your journal with the side quest knotwork instead of the correct [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)") knotwork.
  - In most cases where a quest has two possible conclusions, either ending will qualify toward the achievement. Exceptions include [A Scroll For Anska](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Scroll_For_Anska "Skyrim:A Scroll For Anska") and [The Heart of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Heart_of_Dibella "Skyrim:The Heart of Dibella"). See the individual quest pages for details.
  - You can track your progress toward the achievement using the "Side Quests Completed" statistic on the "Quest" page of your journal's "General Stats".
