## Walkthrough

If you talk to one of the quest givers while having alcohol in your inventory, you will have the option to share a drink with them. This then makes them your friend, meaning that you can take low value items that belong to them and that they may occasionally give you a gift.

## Notes

- This quest does not have any stages or objectives.
- This quest can be repeated indefinitely.
- Certain expensive drinks will not give you the option to share a drink.
  - Version 1.3.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") partially fixes this bug.
- Because this is a favor quest which affects the quest giver's disposition, it will count towards the "Help the People" part of the [thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") quest for the quest giver's hold.
- Because this quest is unmarked, completing it won't help with progress towards the "[Hero of the People](/wiki/Skyrim:Hero_of_the_People "Skyrim:Hero of the People")" [Achievement](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements").
- Only drunks with the "[MaleDrunk](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Voice-MaleDrunk "Category:Skyrim-Voice-MaleDrunk")" voicetype can be quest givers for this quest.
