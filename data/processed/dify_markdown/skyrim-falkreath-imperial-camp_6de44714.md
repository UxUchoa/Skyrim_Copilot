Falkreath Imperial Camp

**Falkreath Imperial Camp** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Camps "Skyrim:Imperial Camps") in [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold"), located east-northeast of [Glenmoril Coven](/wiki/Skyrim:Glenmoril_Coven "Skyrim:Glenmoril Coven") and north of [Knifepoint Ridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Knifepoint_Ridge "Skyrim:Knifepoint Ridge").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg)

Falkreath Imperial Camp

This camp will only be present if the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") hold [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") and the rest of Falkreath Hold, whether through martial might or via the negotiation during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"). [Imperial Soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Soldier "Skyrim:Imperial Soldier") are camped here under the command of [Legate Skulnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Skulnar "Skyrim:Legate Skulnar"), except during the related quest, when [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") takes command. Like similar Imperial camps that only exist when the hold has changed hands, it has neither a [quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Imperial Quartermaster") or any [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse"). Unlike most military camps, it also has no [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block").

## Falkreath Imperial Camp

It is a typical Imperial camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. There is an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil") and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") beside the fire. To the west of the campfire is the command tent, containing a round table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. South of the campfire is the Legate's tent, containing an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. To the right of this tent is a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"). To the southeast is the hospital tent, containing three bed rolls, two of which are occupied by wounded soldiers, and a low table holding a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). To the left is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs"). Northeast of the campfire is a row of three small tents containing unowned bed rolls. To the right of the first of these tents is an owned [steel helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Helmet "Skyrim:Steel Helmet"). There are many [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") and [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch") in and around the camp.

Any time [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold") is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Joining the Stormcloaks"), you are free to sleep in the small tents.
