## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Nazir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazir "Skyrim:Nazir").
2. Kill [Agnis](/wiki/Skyrim:Agnis "Skyrim:Agnis").
3. Return to Nazir.

## Detailed Walkthrough

After speaking to [Nazir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazir "Skyrim:Nazir"), you are told to locate and assassinate a target known as [Agnis](/wiki/Skyrim:Agnis "Skyrim:Agnis"). She can be found inside the keep of [Fort Greymoor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greymoor "Skyrim:Fort Greymoor") which is west of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). The fort is initially garrisoned by bandits, whom you can either sneak past or kill. However if you have advanced far enough in the civil war quest line (beyond the [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun")), it is garrisoned by soldiers of whichever faction controls Whiterun - *your* faction, that is, making the assassination much easier. Either way, Agnis is easily found inside the keep and, having only a dagger and some modest clothing for protection, is equally easy to kill.

If you approach Agnis before you kill her, you will receive three dialogue options; you will only be able to choose one as you can not initiate conversation with her again. Note that none of these dialogue options will cause her to become hostile.

| Topic | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| **"You've cleaned your last room, servant."** | *"But I have many more rooms to clean. Fort Greymoor is quite large, and rather filthy if I do say."* |
| **"Sleep now, old woman. Embrace the Void."** | *"Sleep? I can't sleep now! There's too much to be done. And what's this Void you're rambling on about?"* |
| **"(Remain silent)"** | *"I swear, you people are an odd bunch. Please, just step aside, that I may return to my duties!"* |

After the kill, report back to Nazir for a leveled gold as a reward.

## Notes

- When receiving payment for the finished job, [Nazir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazir "Skyrim:Nazir") will say *"Infiltrating an occupied fort and making it out in one piece is nothing to joke about. Well done."*, but his voice dialogue actually says *"Infiltrating an occupied **military** fort and making it out in one piece is nothing to joke about. Well done."*
- Certainly with the Unofficial Skyrim Patch (Legendary or Special Edition) installed, and maybe without it: If you clear the fort of bandits without harming Agnis, but without having taken sides in the Civil War, it will be re-garrisoned with Imperials, since Jarl Balgruuf opposes the Stormcloaks. These Imperials will be neutral (and thus non-hostile) to an unaligned player, just as they would to a pro-Imperial one (i.e. one who has brought the Jagged Crown to General Tullius, even if the Battle of Whiterun has not yet taken place): the only way to face a hostile garrison is if the player has cleared the fort of bandits, then subsequently sided with the Stormcloaks (by bringing the Jagged Crown to Ulfric instead) and then decides to go in and murder Agnis \*before\* the Battle of Whiterun - for instance, if they bring Ulfric's axe to Balgruuf and then visit Fort Greymoor while Balgruuf is still in charge of Whiterun, before returning to Ulfric and starting the battle quest.

## Bugs

- Agnis belonging to the Dark Brotherhood faction can cause you to get expelled from the Dark Brotherhood if she gets attacked at the wrong time.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.7, fixes this bug.
