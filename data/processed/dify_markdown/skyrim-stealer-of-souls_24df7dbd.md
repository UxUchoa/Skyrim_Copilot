The Soul Stealer Arrows

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read the [letter from Calcelmo](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Calcelmo "Skyrim:Letter from Calcelmo").
2. Retrieve the [Soul Stealer Arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Stealer_Arrow "Skyrim:Soul Stealer Arrow") from [Kagrumez](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrumez "Skyrim:Kagrumez").

## Detailed Walkthrough

**Note: This quest, along with a [Letter from Calcelmo](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Calcelmo "Skyrim:Letter from Calcelmo") and the [Dwemer Arrow Diagram](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Arrow_Diagram "Skyrim:Dwemer Arrow Diagram"), were originally added to your inventory upon installing the [Skyrim:Arcane Archer Pack](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Archer_Pack "Skyrim:Arcane Archer Pack") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition"), the letter and quest no longer appear. You can still acquire [Soul Stealer Arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Stealer_Arrow "Skyrim:Soul Stealer Arrow") and the Dwemer Arrow Diagram from [Kagrumez](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrumez "Skyrim:Kagrumez") on [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim").**

Kagrumez is a high-level dungeon with a minimum enemy level of 25. As such, it is recommended that you prepare for a tough fight if you plan to complete this quest at a lower level. The majority of the dungeon is initially inaccessible, so you will first need to complete [The Kagrumez Gauntlet](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Kagrumez_Gauntlet "Skyrim:The Kagrumez Gauntlet"), a fetch quest which requires you to gather [Kagrumez Resonance Gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrumez_Resonance_Gem "Skyrim:Kagrumez Resonance Gem") from all over Solstheim. You need to collect four gems in order to unlock the final chamber in Kagrumez.

Once you have unlocked the final chamber, you can find several Soul Stealer Arrows next to the [Dwarven Black Bow of Fate](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Black_Bow_of_Fate "Skyrim:Dwarven Black Bow of Fate"). Pick up the arrows to complete the quest. You will now be able to craft Soul Stealer Arrows at any [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge").
