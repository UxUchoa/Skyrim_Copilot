The barracks by the gate in Whiterun

**Guard Barracks** is a building near the main gate in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). It is located across the street from [Warmaiden's](/wiki/Skyrim:Warmaiden%27s "Skyrim:Warmaiden's").

It is used by [Whiterun Guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Guard "Skyrim:Whiterun Guard") as an alternative to the bigger, and similar named [barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard_Barracks "Skyrim:Guard Barracks") behind the [Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare").

## Guard Barracks

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_%28Gate%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_%28Gate%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_(Gate).jpg)

Guard Barracks interior

The barracks consists of two cramped rooms, a dining room often used by a single [guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Guard "Skyrim:Whiterun Guard"), and a sleeping area with four owned beds. The dining room contains two chairs by a table, a minor loot chest, a food barrel, and four food sacks. The small table seats two and has some food on display, two [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). A set of shelves contains a chunk of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"), the loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), some [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), and [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese").

The sleeping area contains four beds squeezed head-to-toe between the north and south walls with an end table with clutter between them. The end table to the east has a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and two [honey nut treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat") on top and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") on the floor below. A copy of *[Beggar Prince](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar_Prince "Skyrim:Beggar Prince")* is carelessly thrown on the floor in the northeastern corner. The shelves along the northern wall hold two bottles of wine, two bottles of ale, and a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"). The cupboard under the shelves holds clutter. Under the cupboard sits a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") and, placed on a stool, a [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Hat "Skyrim:Hat").

## Notes

- There is a [![Danger](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/SR-icon-shadowmark-Danger.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Danger.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Danger") Danger [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Shadowmarks "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") around the right corner from the door and double marked low down on the wall and a wooden support.
- If the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") takes over Whiterun during [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)"), nobody will live in the barracks and all items will be free to take.
- There are four food barrels on the roof of the barracks.
