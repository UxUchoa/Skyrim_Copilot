Erden Relvel

**Erden Relvel** is an antagonist in the questline of [Ghosts of the Tribunal](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal"). He aims to continue spreading the destructive influence of [Dagoth Ur](/wiki/Lore:Dagoth_Ur "Lore:Dagoth Ur") as a puppet of the [Mask of Dagoth Ur](/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Dagoth_Ur "Skyrim:Mask of Dagoth Ur"), and must be stopped.

He will appear at Tel Mithryn graveyard after the completion of [Ghosts of the Tribunal](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest) "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal (quest)") is complete, and once you have solved the [puzzle of the armory](/wiki/Skyrim:Priest_Arthamis%27_Note "Skyrim:Priest Arthamis' Note") in [Ashfall's Tear](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashfall%27s_Tear "Skyrim:Ashfall's Tear") and read Erden's [challenge to you](/wiki/Skyrim:Erden_Relvel%27s_Note "Skyrim:Erden Relvel's Note"). He summons several [Ash Zombies](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Zombie "Skyrim:Ash Zombie") to aid him in combat.

He wears the Mask of Dagoth Ur and the [Robe of the Lich](/wiki/Skyrim:Robe_of_the_Lich "Skyrim:Robe of the Lich"). In his inventory is the final Fahlbtharz Forge Gem.
