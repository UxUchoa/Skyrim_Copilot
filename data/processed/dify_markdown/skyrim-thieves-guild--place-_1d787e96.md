The **Thieves Guild** is the headquarters of [the Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") located with the sewers beneath [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), known as [The Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway").

The headquarters are based within [The Ragged Flagon - Cistern](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon_-_Cistern "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon - Cistern") and a bar named [The Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon"). For full details on these places, see their individual pages.

### Thieves Guild Headquarters

There are two entrances to the headquarters: one via [the Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway") into [The Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon"), and a second directly into [The Ragged Flagon - Cistern](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon_-_Cistern "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon - Cistern") hidden beneath a tomb that dominates the small graveyard to the east of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften")'s [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Riften) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Riften)").

You can only access the second entrance once you have joined the guild and completed [Taking Care of Business](/wiki/Skyrim:Taking_Care_of_Business "Skyrim:Taking Care of Business"). To enter the headquarters using this entrance, press a small button in the center of the tomb; this opens a secret stone staircase. Descend it and open the sewer covering. To exit directly from the Cistern, climb the ladder in the alcove to the southwest, then pull a chain to retract the tomb.

As you progress the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") [questline](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Quests "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), complete reputation quests for [Delvin Mallory](/wiki/Skyrim:Delvin_Mallory "Skyrim:Delvin Mallory"), and complete miscellaneous guild quests, the headquarters will be upgraded, and additional thieves will move in.

## Notes

- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") An extra room is added to the Flagon by the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"). This room is located to the left of the door leading to [the Ratway Vaults](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway_Vaults "Skyrim:The Ratway Vaults"). It is mainly filled with beds for those who live in the Flagon.
- The special features within the Flagon only appear as the extra merchants move in after completing the required reputation quests.
- There is a "Guild" [Shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (Thieves Guild)") to the left of the entrance door from the Ratway Vaults.
