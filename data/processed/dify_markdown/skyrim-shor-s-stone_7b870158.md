Shor's Stone

**Shor's Stone** is a mining [town](/wiki/Skyrim:Towns "Skyrim:Towns") in the northern [Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift").

It is north-northwest of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") in the pass between the Rift and [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch"). The town's name is taken from the [Nordic](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") god [Shor](/wiki/Lore:Shor "Lore:Shor"). The residents are having trouble with [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider") that have infested the local [ebony](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony "Skyrim:Ebony") mine, [Redbelly Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Redbelly_Mine "Skyrim:Redbelly Mine").

## Notes

- An [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") and a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") are due north of [Sylgja's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Sylgja%27s_House "Skyrim:Sylgja's House").
- Shor's Stone is the only town besides [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") that operates with its own [guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone_Guard "Skyrim:Shor's Stone Guard").
- Animals spawn very close to Shor's Stone, which may occasionally cause fights to break out between the villagers and animals.
- Music tends to play irregularly in the area, as you can hear one song fade and another replace it.
- Shor's Stone also [appears](/wiki/Online:Shor%27s_Stone "Online:Shor's Stone") in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online").
