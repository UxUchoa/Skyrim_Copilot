Filnjar's House

**Filnjar's House** is a house in the small mining settlement of [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone").

It is the residence of town [blacksmith](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith "Skyrim:Blacksmith") [Filnjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Filnjar "Skyrim:Filnjar"). It consists of one area only, *[Filnjar's House](#Filnjar.27s_House)*.

As is custom with the houses of blacksmiths, all tools are located on the porch outside the house.

## Filnjar's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-interior-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg)

The interior of Filnjar's House

The house consists of a ground floor and a basement, the latter used mostly for storage. The front door opens to the sight of a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"), an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield") leaning against the fireplace, a small table with two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") and a copy of *[The Cabin in the Woods](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cabin_in_the_Woods "Skyrim:The Cabin in the Woods")*. Next to the table is Filnjar's owned bed with a minor loot chest and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#Shoes "Skyrim:Clothing") on the floor. In the southwestern corner sits a clothes wardrobe with a [blacksmith's apron](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Apron") on top, along with a small table that holds five gold coins, a coin purse and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). Lastly, a food barrel stands in the adjacent corner.

To the left of the entrance stands a table with a variety of food and drinks; one bunch of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"), three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), one bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and one of regular [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and two bottles of [nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). The shelves next to the table hold even more food, two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a cluster of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), three [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), some [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") and a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"). Hanging above the shelves are three [dried elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear"), two [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid"), and one sample of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). Finishing off the area is a food barrel in the northeastern corner, right above the stairs leading down to the basement.

The basement is spacious but pretty barebones. A food barrel stands at the foot of the stairs, next to a table with a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow") and three [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"). Adjacent to this table is another table with a single loaf of bread. In the western part of the room, past the hay piles, is a food barrel, a wardrobe with clothes, and an unlocked chest with some random gold and minor loot. More loot can be gathered from an end table that has two [blacksmith's potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Potion "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Potion") on top.
