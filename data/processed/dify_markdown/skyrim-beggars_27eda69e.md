# Skyrim:Beggars

## Overview

This article is about NPC beggars. For other uses, see [Beggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar "Skyrim:Beggar").

There are a number of **beggars** in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"). The majority of them will [ask for a gold piece](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_all_Beggars_Have "Skyrim:Quest all Beggars Have") when approached; giving them one gold will reward you with the blessing [The Gift of Charity](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gift_of_Charity "Skyrim:The Gift of Charity").
