Shaman's Hut

The **Shaman's Hut** is the home of shaman [Storn Crag-Strider](/wiki/Skyrim:Storn_Crag-Strider "Skyrim:Storn Crag-Strider") and his daughter [Frea](/wiki/Skyrim:Frea "Skyrim:Frea") in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

It is on the southeast side of the village, south of the [Greathall](/wiki/Skyrim:Greathall "Skyrim:Greathall").

It contains only one zone, *[Shaman's Hut](#Shaman.27s_Hut)*.

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance is a bench. The entrance is unlocked.

### Shaman's Hut

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-interior-Shaman%27s_Hut.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Shaman%27s_Hut.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shaman%27s_Hut.jpg)

Shaman's Hut interior

The entrance leads into a room with a well-dressed dinner table to the left and three respawning barrels and a non-respawning sack to the right, all of them with food. Next to the barrels are a set of shelves with two bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), an [ash yam](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam") and one bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") along with a respawning food sack. The dinner table holds three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") in a bowl, a [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), two pieces of [salmon meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat "Skyrim:Salmon Meat"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a piece of bread, a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). On a shelf above are two [salt piles](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") on a plate and a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute").

Opposite the table is the entrance to the room with an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanters "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanters"). A non-respawning dresser holds various clothing and a bowl with a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") sits on top, next to a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000209a6 "Skyrim:Clothes"). To the left of the enchanter is a copy of the book *[Children of the All-Maker](/wiki/Skyrim:Children_of_the_All-Maker "Skyrim:Children of the All-Maker")* and in a wooden bowl are a [Nordic barnacle](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle"), a [pearl](/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl "Skyrim:Pearl"), and a rare [small pearl](/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Pearl "Skyrim:Small Pearl"). Also on the table there's a filled [lesser soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Lesser Soul Gem"), a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk"), a salt pile, and a plate with another Nordic barnacle and a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"). Above the enchanter are two non-respawning food sacks, some [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"), and a bit of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") on a shelf. On the floor to the left of the table is an open box containing a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato").

A huge fireplace covers most of the floor in the central room with two pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") and two ash yams on the side. On the roof supports above is a single bottle of ale. There is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs") in the southwestern corner, with a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") in a bowl and a freshly caught [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and some [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam") hanging above. The non-respawning cupboard to the left contains clutter and the respawning barrel contains food. The shelves above the cupboard hold a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a fillet of [horker meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat "Skyrim:Horker Meat"), and another salt pile. A bottle of alto wine and a bottle of ale can be found on the top shelves along with a respawning [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") containing uncommon ingredients.

Another respawning apothecary's satchel on a ledge, this time containing rare ingredients, can be found beneath the mudcrab trophy on the wall, along with some wheat and a bucket containing a sample of [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw"), a sample of [trama root](/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root "Skyrim:Trama Root"), and a [mudcrab chitin](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Chitin "Skyrim:Mudcrab Chitin"). Some shelves near the bedroom door hold a [deer hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide "Skyrim:Deer Hide"), a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots"), and two non-respawning food sacks. The bedroom contains two owned beds and a set of shelves with three pearls and a horker tusk.
