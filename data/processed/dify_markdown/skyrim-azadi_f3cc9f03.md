Azadi

Azadi is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") who can be found in [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"). He begins the quest [Interception](/wiki/Skyrim:Interception "Skyrim:Interception").

## Related Quests

- **[Interception](/wiki/Skyrim:Interception "Skyrim:Interception")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Elite_Armaments "Skyrim:Redguard Elite Armaments"): Aid the Hammerfell resistance.

## Quest-Related Dialogue

When first spoken to you can either brawl him or speak him down:

:   *"Well, look what we have here."*
:   **Looking for a fight?**
:   *"Yes."*

| Option | Dialogue | | | | Reaction |

Here, this is for you."* **Failed**: *"I'll teach you to talk to me that way."* | | | | **Passed**: Gives [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Azadi%27s_Note "Skyrim:Azadi's Note") and warps away **Failed**: Brawl begins |
| **I don't have time for this right now.** | *"I can smell your shame!"* | | | | Nothing |

After defeating him in a brawl:

:   *"You're tougher than you look. You got me, fair and square. Thank you. Here this is for you."*

He will then hand you a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Azadi%27s_Note "Skyrim:Azadi's Note") and disappear.
