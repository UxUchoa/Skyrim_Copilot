The Mortar and Pestle

**The Mortar and Pestle** is the alchemy shop in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"), between [Brina's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Brina%27s_House "Skyrim:Brina's House") and [Beitild's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Beitild%27s_House "Skyrim:Beitild's House").

It is run by the elderly [Frida](/wiki/Skyrim:Frida "Skyrim:Frida") who also lives above the main trading room.

It contains only one zone, *[The Mortar and Pestle](#Walkthrough)*.

**NOTE**: Like all other stores, The Mortar and Pestle has a number of fixed wares on display scattered around the main trading room. These can be bought from the proprietor Frida and will disappear from the room when purchased. For a list of these special items, see [this section](#Displayed_Merchandise).

## Walkthrough

The main trading room is dimly lit and Frida can be found behind the counter every day between 8am and 8pm. Without the displayed merchandise, a lot of beverages reveal themselves. On the shelves of a non-respawning cupboard behind the counter are three bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and four bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") along with seven bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). Atop the counter are three bottles of alto wine and two bottles of wine. Underneath the counter are four additional bottles of wine, a single bottle of alto wine, and a [potion of resist shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Shock "Skyrim:Potion of Resist Shock"). There is also a small hidden [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), two non-respawning sacks with food, and a copy of the [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Hearth Fire, v9](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Hearth_Fire,_v9 "Skyrim:2920, Hearth Fire, v9")*. Above the shop counter are two [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid"), some [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam"), and two bunches of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear"), as well as three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). Along the wall next to the cupboard sits a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum"), a non-respawning chest, and a respawning meat barrel. Underneath the stairs leading to the second floor is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and a respawning food barrel.

Frida sleeps in her owned bed on the second floor, where even more beverages sit on some shelves next to a respawning food barrel in the northeastern corner: six bottles of wine and two bottles of alto wine. A dinner table is set with two wedges of [goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge") and one [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), with two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") on the side. There is also a [chicken's egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") and two bottles of alto wine. A non-respawning dresser in the corner holds clothes and on its top sit a few spare ingredients: one [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"), one [creep cluster](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster"), one [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell"), and one sample each of [frost salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Salts "Skyrim:Frost Salts"), [fire salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts "Skyrim:Fire Salts"), [ectoplasm](/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm "Skyrim:Ectoplasm") and [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"). On the other side of the bed are five gold coins on a table and a non-respawning unlocked chest.

## Notes

## Bugs

- The store may be locked during normal business hours and open in the middle of the night when merchant services are unavailable.
  - Successful workarounds may be to gain access to 2nd floor door and wait 24 hours while inside during normal business hours. then go downstairs and attempt to make a purchase
