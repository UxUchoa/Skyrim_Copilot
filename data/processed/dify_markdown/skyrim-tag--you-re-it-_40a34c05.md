## Detailed Walkthrough

**Tag, you're it!** is a [game](/wiki/Skyrim:Activities#Interactions "Skyrim:Activities") that can be played with the children in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"), [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") and [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). With [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") installed, the game can be started with your [adopted children](/wiki/Skyrim:Adoption "Skyrim:Adoption") as well.

To play, initiate the game by offering or accepting the game from eligible children. The aim of the game is to avoid being tagged by whoever is "it". If you are it, you must try to tag someone else. The quest will update whenever "it" changes hands and quest arrows show who you can tag or who to avoid.

The game ends after 90 seconds of play and the quest is removed. This has no material benefit and is mostly just for fun.

## Notes

- Entering a new area while playing the game will usually cause the game to end and you may have to speak to the children again to play.
- Games are only playable from 8am until 9pm.
  - Adopted children can play tag at night if asked to.
- Starting a game of tag via your adopted child can cause all children in the area to play, even those who won't normally play.

## Bugs

- While [Fjotra](/wiki/Skyrim:Fjotra "Skyrim:Fjotra") is following you during the quest [The Heart of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Heart_of_Dibella "Skyrim:The Heart of Dibella") initiating a game of tag can cause her to play the game allowing you and other players to tag her. However, because she is following you, she won't run after or tag anyone when she becomes it.
- If you fast travel during this quest, the children may end up fast traveling with you. **?**
- Starting a game with more than 4 children present will get all children running, but only 4 of them can be tagged. **?**
