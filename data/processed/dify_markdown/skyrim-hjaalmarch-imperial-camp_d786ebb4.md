Hjaalmarch Imperial Camp

**Hjaalmarch Imperial Camp** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Camps "Skyrim:Imperial Camps") in [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"), located south of [Ustengrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Ustengrav "Skyrim:Ustengrav") and northwest of [Stonehills](/wiki/Skyrim:Stonehills "Skyrim:Stonehills").

This camp will only be present if the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") hold [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") and the rest of Hjaalmarch, whether through martial might or via the negotiation during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"). [Imperial Soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Soldier "Skyrim:Imperial Soldier") are camped here under the command of [Legate Taurinus Duilis](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Taurinus_Duilis "Skyrim:Legate Taurinus Duilis"), except during the related quest, when [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") takes command. Like similar Imperial camps that only exist when the hold has changed hands, it has neither a [quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Imperial Quartermaster") or any [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse").

## Hjaalmarch Imperial Camp

It is a typical Imperial camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. There is an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil") and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") beside the fire. To the northwest is the Legate's tent, with the command tent to the right and the hospital tent to the left. The Legate's tent contains an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed and a dresser beside it. The command tent contains an end table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. The hospital tent contains four bed rolls, three of which are occupied by wounded soldiers, a low table holding a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), and an unlocked chest containing various items to treat the sick, including potions of [cure poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison "Skyrim:Cure Poison"), potions of [cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Cure Disease"), and [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). To the left of the hospital tent is a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), while to the right is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and some barrels. Northeast of the campfire are three small tents containing unowned bed rolls. To the south of the campfire is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block").

Any time [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch") is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Joining the Stormcloaks"), you are free to sleep in the small tents.
