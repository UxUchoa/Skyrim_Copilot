Brunwulf Free-Winter's House

**Brunwulf Free-Winter's House** is the home of [Brunwulf Free-Winter](/wiki/Skyrim:Brunwulf_Free-Winter "Skyrim:Brunwulf Free-Winter") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

It is a one story house, with a partial loft area located in Windhelm near the main entrance. Next door to [Aretino Residence](/wiki/Skyrim:Aretino_Residence "Skyrim:Aretino Residence"), almost directly opposite [Calixto's House of Curiosities](/wiki/Skyrim:Calixto%27s_House_of_Curiosities "Skyrim:Calixto's House of Curiosities"). It consists of only one zone, *[Brunwulf Free-Winter's House](#Brunwulf_Free-Winter.27s_House)*.

## Brunwulf Free-Winter's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/SR-interior-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg)

The interior of the Free-Winter household

The entrance has a novice level lock. The entry leads straight into the main living area, with a practice dummy and a pile of firewood against the south wall. There are quite a few sacks of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") around the whole house. To the west of the entrance is a flight of wooden stairs up to a small loft area and behind the stairs is a small square wooden table with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), two [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and a couple of wedges of cheese on top. Against the west wall is a small set of shelves holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a bottle of Nord mead. Against the north wall is a dresser containing [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") with two [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot") and a [potion of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka") on top. Further to the right still against the north wall is a long wooden table holding a [mammoth snout](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Snout "Skyrim:Mammoth Snout"), three [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), three bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), a bunch of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), a salt pile, a loaf of bread, a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") and three [slaughterfish eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg"). Right of this is a fire pit with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") over it and a chair in front. By the chair is a copy of *[Immortal Blood](/wiki/Skyrim:Immortal_Blood "Skyrim:Immortal Blood")*. Beside the fire pit is a narrow wooden table holding a haunch of raw [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"), a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and a loaf of bread, with three wall shelves holding various skulls beside the table and hanging from the wall beside the shelves is a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow"). There is a doorway through into a bedroom to the east, with a bookcase to the left holding four common books. To the right of the doorway is a wall shelf holding a set of [hide armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor"), [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Shield "Skyrim:Hide Shield") and [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet"), a [potion of the berserker](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Berserker "Skyrim:Potion of the Berserker") and an [iron greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Greatsword "Skyrim:Iron Greatsword") leaning against the wall beside it. Under the shelf is a long unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing gold. To the right, in an alcove on the south wall is a large round wooden table holding an [iron battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Iron Battleaxe") and [troll skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull "Skyrim:Troll Skull").

The bedroom is very small consisting of a single owned bed opposite the doorway in the northeastern corner, with a respawning [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") on the floor by the head of the bed, which contains leveled items. Against the east wall is a small unit holding a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). In the southeast corner is a corner shelf holding a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), with a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") on the floor beside it. To the right, against the south wall is a small cupboard with an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") and eight loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") on top. The loft area is very small and only contains a couple of barrels, one of which contains food.
