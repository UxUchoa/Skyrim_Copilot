An Afflicted Refugee uses Vile Vapor

**Afflicted Refugees** can be [encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Hook.2C_Afflicted_Refugee_Headed_to_High_Rock "Skyrim:World Interactions") wandering [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"). They have unusually red skin and the ability to [vomit poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Vile_Vapor "Skyrim:Vile Vapor") at close range.

Their inventories are fully leveled, but they usually wear [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes").

## Related Quests

- **[The Only Cure](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Only_Cure "Skyrim:The Only Cure")**: Slay an apostate [worshipper](/wiki/Skyrim:Orchendor "Skyrim:Orchendor") for the Daedra Lord [Peryite](/wiki/Lore:Peryite "Lore:Peryite").

## Dialogue

When approached they will say:

:   *"Are you finished ogling the grotesque? I suppose I should be grateful you didn't simply attack me."*
:   **Your skin... Are you ill?**
:   *"I am one of the Afflicted. I'd have been dead from this plague a year ago if not for [Peryite](/wiki/Lore:Peryite "Lore:Peryite")'s protection."*
:   **Where are you going?**
:   *"Returning to [High Rock](/wiki/Lore:High_Rock "Lore:High Rock"). Our shepherd lost his way, and I fear Peryite's wrath may consume those who remain with him. Kesh could tell you more. I just want out of Skyrim as quickly as possible."*

This will start a miscellaneous objective: Find Kesh at the Peryite Shrine.

If spoken to again:

:   **I have more questions.**
:   *"And I have no more time for talking. Go speak with Kesh if you're so curious."*
