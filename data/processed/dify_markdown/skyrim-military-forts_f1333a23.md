# Skyrim:Military Forts

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-place-Fort_Greenwall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fort_Greenwall.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Greenwall.jpg)

Fort Greenwall

[![SR-mapicon-Fort.png](//images.uesp.net/0/09/SR-mapicon-Fort.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) This page lists all the **military forts** in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"). [Military camps](/wiki/Skyrim:Military_Camps "Skyrim:Military Camps") are listed on a separate page.

The locations using the fort map marker can be roughly divided into three types. Forts affected by the **Civil War** appear in the first section. The occupants of these forts are initially bandits and mages. As the [Skyrim Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") progresses, either the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") or [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") will take over and occupy the fort.

Forts can serve as **settlements** for factions: the [Greybeards](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards"), the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard"), and the [Volkihar Vampire Clan](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Vampire_Clan "Skyrim:Volkihar Vampire Clan")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard").

Other forts are set up as standard **dungeons**, with respawning enemies and a boss you must kill to clear the dungeon.
