Graywinter Watch

**Graywinter Watch** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") east of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") containing [trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll "Skyrim:Troll"). It contains only one zone, *[Graywinter Watch](#Graywinter_Watch)*.

## Related Quests

- **[Animal Extermination](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Extermination_(B) "Skyrim:Animal Extermination (B)")**: Fulfill a contract given to the [Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions") to clear out a beast den. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials")**: Participate in [Kyne](/wiki/Lore:Kyne "Lore:Kyne")'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").

### Exterior

The cave, which is beneath the [Ritual Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ritual_Stone "Skyrim:The Ritual Stone") overlooking the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River"), is easily reachable from the nearby road. Along the west side of the Ritual Stone's rock foundation is a dirt path which leads directly to the cave.

### Graywinter Watch

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg)

Froki's Bow, with a nearby dead bandit

There are two [trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll "Skyrim:Troll") in the cave; one is visible from the tunnel, and the other is hidden near the campfire. Six [blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort"), three [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap"), and four [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") fungi grow throughout the cave. To the right of the entrance is a ramp to the higher part of the cave.

At the top of the ramp is a row of nine skulls. There is a small campsite here, with a central campfire and the corpses of a [female Nord bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(dead)#Nord_Female "Skyrim:Bandit (dead)") and a [male Nord bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(dead)#Nord_Male "Skyrim:Bandit (dead)"). There are two bedrolls on one side of the fire, and a tent on the other. To the right of the tent is a copy of the [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor "Skyrim:Vernaccus and Bourlor")*, three [steel arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow "Skyrim:Steel Arrow"), a pile or quiver of random [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows "Skyrim:Arrows"), and the respawning [Froki's bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Froki%27s_Bow "Skyrim:Froki's Bow") atop a food barrel. Lastly, by the bedrolls is a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack").

## Notes

- There are fifteen humanoid skulls in the cave, though only twelve can be lifted.
- Two [corundum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") are north of the cave, across the river next to a rocky outcropping and a tree.
- With version 1.3.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), Froki's Bow will only spawn here once.
