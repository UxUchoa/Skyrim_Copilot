Alexia Vici

**Alexia Vici** is an elderly [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [citizen](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)").
She is only present during the [wedding](/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Until_Death "Skyrim:Bound Until Death") of her daughter, [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici"). She is also the aunt of [Emperor Titus Mede II](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real) "Skyrim:Emperor Titus Mede II (real)").

Alexia wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes") along with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots").

## Related Quests

- **[Bound Until Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Until_Death "Skyrim:Bound Until Death")**: Assassinate [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici") at her own wedding.

### Bound Until Death

At the wedding, Alexia can be found in a political debate with [the groom's](/wiki/Skyrim:Asgeir_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Asgeir Snow-Shod") [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") father, [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Vulwulf_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Vulwulf Snow-Shod"):

:   **Vulwulf**: *"Typical Imperial rot. You speak of Skyrim like you know the land, know its people. You're from [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil")! You know nothing about us! Nothing!"*
:   **Alexia**: *"Oh, I know enough. I know those trouble-making Stormcloaks refuse to submit to [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") authority. Such seditious behavior. Why it's treason!"*
:   **Vulwulf**: *"And when the elves marched into your beloved Cyrodiil, and everyone took up arms against them, wasn't that treason? Against the [Dominion](/wiki/Lore:Aldmeri_Dominion "Lore:Aldmeri Dominion")? Huh?"*
:   **Alexia**: *"Why that's not the same thing at all. The Dominion were invaders. Conquerors. We had to fight them, to preserve our own way of life."*
:   **Vulwulf**: *"My point exactly."*

She is clearly unhappy with her daughter's marriage, as she can be found saying:

:   *"What my daughter sees in that... barbarian, I'll never know."*
:   *"The thought of paying a dowry to that, that... Stormcloak turns my stomach."*
:   *"My nephew, the Emperor. Too busy to attend the wedding of his beloved cousin. We won't stand for this slight, I assure you."*

If spoken directly to:

:   **Having a good time?**
:   *"I most certainly am not. I've just lost my daughter to a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") beast, and my nephew Titus is too busy playing Emperor to even show up."*

When Vittoria Vici dies, Alexia can be found screaming:

:   *"My daughter! Dead! Noooooo!"*
