Falkreath Barracks Exterior

**Falkreath Barracks** is the living quarters for the [Falkreath guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Guard "Skyrim:Falkreath Guard") and also contains [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold")'s jail.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-interior-Falkreath_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Falkreath_Barracks.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Falkreath_Barracks.jpg)

Falkreath's barracks

It is on the edge of Falkreath, southeast of the [Jarl's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Falkreath) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Falkreath)") and next door to [Grave Concoctions](/wiki/Skyrim:Grave_Concoctions "Skyrim:Grave Concoctions").

### Exterior

At the rear of the building is a practice area where the guards can practice their melee and archery skills; there are two practice dummies and two archery targets. There is a flight of wooden stairs leading up to the balcony and the entrance to the sleeping quarters. There is also a well, three [thistle plants](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch") and two [stone quarries](/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry "Skyrim:Stone Quarry")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") against the rock face, past the stairs, one of which is hidden behind the trees, and the other of which is further to the right, in line with the wooden fencing on the edge of the premise.

### Falkreath Barracks

The entrance leads to a room used by the [Falkreath guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Guard "Skyrim:Falkreath Guard") to rest and relax in. It consists of a round wooden table and two chairs to the left and a flight of stairs up to the upper floor against the northeast wall. On the table are two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"). Opposite the entrance is a flight of stairs leading down to the basement which is used as the jail. To the left of these stairs is a set of shelves holding four bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), three of wine and three of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), two pieces of [leather](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather "Skyrim:Leather") and two [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). There is a barrel of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") to the right with three [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") on top and four [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") hanging from a rack above.

To the right of the stairs leading down to the jail is a square wooden table holding a copy of *[Killing - Before You're Killed](/wiki/Skyrim:Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed "Skyrim:Killing - Before You're Killed")*, two [potions of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Healing") of differing strengths, three [potions of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Stamina") of differing strengths and a [steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Steel War Axe"). The books *[Guard Dossier: Aesrael](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard_Dossier:_Aesrael "Skyrim:Guard Dossier: Aesrael")*, *[Guard's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard%27s_Note "Skyrim:Guard's Note")* and *[Guard's Bounty Letter Draft](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard%27s_Bounty_Letter_Draft "Skyrim:Guard's Bounty Letter Draft")* are also found on it. There is a [steel warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Warhammer "Skyrim:Steel Warhammer") leaning against the wall to the left of the table. Against the southwest wall are several bales of straw with two practice dummies in front. To the right of the practice dummies there are two more food barrels with a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and a [potion of plentiful stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Stamina") on top. There is a wooden bench against the northwest wall.

Upstairs is the dormitory with three single beds, two of which are owned, coming out from the southwest wall. They all have unlocked [chests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the beds and contain minor loot. A copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Legendary Sancre Tor](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legendary_Sancre_Tor "Skyrim:The Legendary Sancre Tor")* can be found on top of one of the chests. Against the southeast wall is a wooden bench with two bottles of Nord mead on the floor beside it. The northwest wall has a door that exits onto the balcony. To the right of the door is a round wooden table and two chairs. On the table are two bottles of alto wine and five loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). Above the stairs are two barrels with four bottles of Nord mead on top.

### Falkreath Jail

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/SR-interior-Falkreath_Barracks_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Falkreath_Barracks_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Falkreath_Barracks_02.jpg)

Falkreath's prison

Through the door into the jail is a room used by the guards. The main cell is opposite the entrance, against the southeast wall. Inside the adept-locked cell are two bedrolls, several sacks of foodstuffs in the far right corner of the cell and a table and chair on the northeastern wall holding two loaves of bread. Hidden behind the food sacks are several half loaves of bread and a lockpick. The guard's room has two wooden benches along the northwest wall. Against the southwest wall are several straw bales with a practice dummy in front and a pile of food sacks beside it. In the southern corner there are two food barrels, and to the left along the southeast wall are the prisoner belongings chest and the evidence chest. In the middle of the southern half of the room is a large wooden table and chair with one bottle of alto wine, one bottle of wine, and a copy of *[Rising Threat, Vol. III](/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._III "Skyrim:Rising Threat, Vol. III")* with a quill sitting next to it. To the left of the entrance are several crates and sacks, and tucked between the crates and the wall is a copy of the [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") skill book *[The Black Arts On Trial](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Arts_On_Trial "Skyrim:The Black Arts On Trial")*. At the northeast end of the room is a partially partitioned area with crates, barrels, sacks, and a table and chair. On a barrel next to the southeast wall are copies of *[Rising Threat, Vol. I](/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._I "Skyrim:Rising Threat, Vol. I")* and *[II](/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._II "Skyrim:Rising Threat, Vol. II")*. At the end of the room is a separate cell used for [Sinding](/wiki/Skyrim:Sinding "Skyrim:Sinding"). It looks like a well with knee-high water in the bottom, with spears blocking the entrance. Approaching the spears will cause Sinding to initiate conversation. If you agree to help him, the quest [Ill Met By Moonlight](/wiki/Skyrim:Ill_Met_By_Moonlight "Skyrim:Ill Met By Moonlight") will start and you will see him turn into a [werewolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf "Skyrim:Werewolf") and leap out the top of the well.
