Thalmor Headquarters

**Thalmor Headquarters** is a building in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") within the walls of [Castle Dour](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour "Skyrim:Castle Dour").

The entrance is at the top of a long straight flight of stairs leading up from the courtyard in Castle Dour, to the right of the door guarded by two [Solitude guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Guard "Skyrim:Solitude Guard") that leads to [General Tullius](/wiki/Skyrim:General_Tullius "Skyrim:General Tullius").

## Thalmor Headquarters

Despite its name, the building is bereft of [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor") and anyone else. It appears to have been abandoned suddenly: upon entering, immediately on the left is a wooden bench, with a large vase holding a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush next to it and a small cupboard opposite. Ahead, in the main part of the room, is a table with a tactical map of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") marking the current status of the [civil war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"). On the left, against the southwest wall, is a bookcase holding eight common books, a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), and a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum"). Against the northwest wall is a small square wooden table and two chairs, with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), three [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), and a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration") along with [plates](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") and [goblets](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"). Beside this table are two smaller round tables holding [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and cheese and a sample of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton").

The northeast wall has a stairwell in the middle with flights leading up and down. To the left is a small cupboard, while to the right is a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") of bread and a plate holding a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). To the right, against the southeast wall are two more bookcases holding a [potion of extreme stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extreme_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Extreme Stamina"), a [solution of strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Strength "Skyrim:Solution of Strength"), and eleven common books. Farther to the right is an unlocked display case containing a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") and [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword"). There are two more vases holding snowberry bushes, one halfway up the stairs and another at the top.

At the top of the stairs is a very large bedroom with a large double four-poster bed coming out from the southwest wall, with a basket containing a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") to the right, a small round table to the left with copies of *[Death of a Wanderer](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_of_a_Wanderer "Skyrim:Death of a Wanderer")* and *[Killing - Before You're Killed](/wiki/Skyrim:Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed "Skyrim:Killing - Before You're Killed")* on top, a long unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed, and a wall shelf above the bed on the southwest wall holding a common book and a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flower. In front of this is a narrow wooden table with a [platter](/wiki/Skyrim:Platter "Skyrim:Platter") on top holding an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), three [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), and two loaves of bread. Against the southwest wall, to the right of the bed is a washbasin on a stand next to a small wardrobe containing [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"). Against the northwest wall is a small cupboard containing clutter and three vases, two of which hold snowberry bushes with the third holding tundra cotton. Farther to the right is a narrow table with a platter holding two [sweet rolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") and two [honey nut treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat") and a plate holding a [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"). In front of the narrow table is a chair and a low table holding a plate of bread and cheese, a goblet, and a potion of regeneration.

In the eastern corner is a large planter with holding a plant. Against the southeast wall is a set of shelves holding a potion of health, a [potion of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Enhanced Stamina"), a potion of regeneration, a [potion of extra magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Extra Magicka"), two common books, a flute, and a drum. Next to this is a small set of shelves holding a bottle of alto wine and a vase with a bunch of tundra cotton in it. In front of the sets of shelves is a chair and a low table with a plate of bread and cheese, a goblet, and a potion of regeneration on top. In the southern corner is an open area with three tall wardrobes containing a collection of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and fine clothes and a set of shelves holding two samples of [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales"), a [potion of haggling](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling "Skyrim:Potion of Haggling"), a bowl of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), two bottles of [frostbite venom](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Venom "Skyrim:Frostbite Venom"), a deathbell flower, a bowl of [void salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Void_Salts "Skyrim:Void Salts"), and a basket holding two bunches of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender").

Descending the stairs leads to another room, this one covered in cobwebs and dust as if it hasn't been used in a while, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand by the bottom of the stairs on the right. There are two single beds coming out from the rear wall, with a wall separating them. Both areas are the same with a single bed and a low empty table in each. In the enter of the room is a small square wooden table and chair, with a common book and [empty wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle") on top of the table. Against the southeast wall is a small set of shelves holding a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") and a mead barrel, while against the northwest wall is a cupboard holding a few more empty bottles and other clutter. To the right, in the northern corner is a second cupboard, this one holding two sweet rolls, a bowl of tomatoes, a few loaves of bread, and several pieces of cheese, with a haunch of raw [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison") on top and [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels on either side.

## Notes

- Unless you are aligned with the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") or have completed [Search and Seizure](/wiki/Skyrim:Search_and_Seizure "Skyrim:Search and Seizure") for [Ondolemar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ondolemar "Skyrim:Ondolemar"), entering the headquarters is considered [trespassing](/wiki/Skyrim:Crime "Skyrim:Crime"), and taking anything in the building is considered stealing. However, as no NPCs are normally present in this cell, you are extremely unlikely to be caught. The building is therefore an easy place to target if the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") accepts a [bedlam job](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bedlam_Job "Skyrim:The Bedlam Job") contract in Solitude.
