Falion's House

**Falion's House** is the home of the town wizard in [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal").

The house is the residence and shop for the wizard [Falion](/wiki/Skyrim:Falion "Skyrim:Falion") and his child apprentice [Agni](/wiki/Skyrim:Agni "Skyrim:Agni").

### Exterior

The house is located on the edge of the water behind the [Thaumaturgist's Hut](/wiki/Skyrim:Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut "Skyrim:Thaumaturgist's Hut"), along the jetty from [Alva's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Alva%27s_House "Skyrim:Alva's House") in Morthal. There is a covered porch along the front of the building and continuing around to the left. There are two wooden benches and a food barrel to the left of the front door. In the area to the left are two more wooden benches, a large pile of firewood and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone").

### Falion's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-interior-Falion%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Falion%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Falion%27s_House.jpg)

Interior

This is a one-room home, from where Falion also sells his services and items. On your left upon entering is a long wooden table with a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel beside it. On the table is a sample of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") and four [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). Above the table, hanging from the ceiling are two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and two bunches of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). To the right of the table is a basket containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), with a hanging rack above holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)").

In the northwestern corner is a chair and against the north wall is a set of shelves holding copies of *[The Great War](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Great_War "Skyrim:The Great War")*, *[Azura and the Box](/wiki/Skyrim:Azura_and_the_Box "Skyrim:Azura and the Box")* and the [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Catalogue of Armor Enchantments](/wiki/Skyrim:Catalogue_of_Armor_Enchantments "Skyrim:Catalogue of Armor Enchantments")*, as well as a [Draught of Extra Magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Draught of Extra Magicka"), a [Potion of Extra Magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Extra Magicka"), an [Enchanter's Potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Potion "Skyrim:Enchanter's Potion"), an [Enchanter's Draught](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Draught "Skyrim:Enchanter's Draught"), six bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two filled [petty soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem"), two filled [common soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem") and one empty common soul gem. The [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") become available for purchase from Falion once you have the [Merchant](/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant_(perk) "Skyrim:Merchant (perk)") [skill perk](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Perks "Skyrim:Skill Perks"). There is an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter") in the northeastern corner.

Against the east wall is a single owned bed coming out from the wall, with a round wooden table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") and a copy of the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Rain's Hand, v4](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Rain%27s_Hand,_v4 "Skyrim:2920, Rain's Hand, v4")* to the left of the bed. In the middle of the east wall is a fireplace with a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") on the mantelpiece and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand in front. To the right is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). Against the south wall is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table are five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). In the southwestern corner, along the west wall is a second single owned bed with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of it.
