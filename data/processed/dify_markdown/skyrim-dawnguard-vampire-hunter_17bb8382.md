Dawnguard Vampire Hunter

The **Dawnguard Vampire Hunter** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") who can be [randomly encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions "Skyrim:World Interactions") if you have accepted [Lord Harkon's](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord_Harkon "Skyrim:Lord Harkon") gift of [vampirism](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampirism "Skyrim:Vampirism"), for commited betryal to Dawnguard and completed [The Bloodstone Chalice](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bloodstone_Chalice "Skyrim:The Bloodstone Chalice"). He carries [orders](/wiki/Skyrim:Writ_of_Dawn "Skyrim:Writ of Dawn") to "exact justice" on you for being a vampire. However, he don't appear at all if you are a member of the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)").

## Notes

- You can still encounter the Dawnguard Vampire Hunter even after finishing the Dawnguard questline for Volkihar Clan Vampires.

## Dialogue

When he attacks you, he'll use three unique lines of dialogue in addition to standard combat dialogue:

:   *"Halt, vampire!"*
:   *"Your end has come!"*
:   *"Light take you!"*
