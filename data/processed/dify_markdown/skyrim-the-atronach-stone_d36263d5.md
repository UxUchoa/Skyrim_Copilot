**The Atronach Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones") scattered across Skyrim.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-place-Atronach_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-Atronach_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Atronach_Stone_(active).jpg)

The Atronach Stone when it's activated

*"Those under the sign of The Atronach absorb a portion of incoming spell damage and have a larger pool of Magicka, but recover it more slowly."*

The stone adds a passive power that increases [magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Magicka "Skyrim:Magicka") by 50 points and adds 50% [Spell Absorption](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Absorption "Skyrim:Spell Absorption"), but decreases [magicka regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Regenerate_Magicka "Skyrim:Regenerate Magicka") by 50%.

The Atronach a useful Stone for mages thanks to its sizable [Fortify Magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Magicka "Skyrim:Fortify Magicka") effect, while the Spell Absorption can be utilized by any character wishing to reduce damage and effects from incoming magic. The reduced magicka regeneration can be overcome with [perks](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration#Skill_Perks "Skyrim:Restoration"), [potions and enchantments](/wiki/Skyrim:Regenerate_Magicka "Skyrim:Regenerate Magicka"), or by simply fortifying magicka even further (see Notes).

## The Atronach Stone

The stone is located almost due south of [Bonestrewn Crest](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonestrewn_Crest "Skyrim:Bonestrewn Crest"), east-northeast of [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing") in the southern-most part of the hot springs. It is found at the top of a mound with several other stones jutting out at an angle around it, surrounded by roiling volcanic pools. At its base is a steaming, shallow pool where a couple of [jazbay grape](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes") plants are growing. Other nearby vegetation that can be harvested include some [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") plants and [creep clusters](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster").

## Notes

- With no other modifiers, this Stone reduces your base magicka regen from 3% per second to 1.5% per second out of combat.
- Because magicka regenerates at a percent rate of your total magicka, increasing total magicka actually increases the amount of magicka recovered per second. A character with 200 magicka and 50% reduced regeneration recovers the same amount of magicka per second as a character with 100 magicka and base magicka regen.
- [High Elves](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Elf "Skyrim:High Elf") have a natural affinity for The Atronach Stone, combining their already high magicka pool with the [Highborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Highborn "Skyrim:Highborn") power to nullify the reduced magicka regen.
- The Atronach Stone is one of only a few methods of obtaining the rare Spell Absorption effect.
  - Combining it with the [Breton's](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [Dragonskin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonskin "Skyrim:Dragonskin") power grants 100% Spell Absorption, making the player completely immune to magic for 60 seconds. This can be used to trivialize some of the most powerful enemies in the game, such as [Dragon Priests](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Priest "Skyrim:Dragon Priest"), who deal damage exclusively through use of magic.
- If you choose to forge the [Aetherial Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherial_Crown "Skyrim:Aetherial Crown") during [Lost to the Ages](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages "Skyrim:Lost to the Ages"), The Atronach Stone can be combined with [The Apprentice Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Apprentice_Stone "Skyrim:The Apprentice Stone") to achieve 50% Spell Absorption, +50 magicka, and 50% magicka regeneration, with the downside of 100% [weakness to magic](/wiki/Skyrim:Weakness_to_Magic "Skyrim:Weakness to Magic").
- The Spell Absorb effect also has a chance of absorbing incoming diseases. This is because diseases are a magical effect.
- You can take advantage of otherwise mundane magic effects to recharge your magicka. Jumping up and down on a forge, for instance, will provide a small but noticeable boost to magicka regeneration.

## Bugs

- The Atronach Stone has a 50% chance of causing all [Summoning](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration_Spells "Skyrim:Conjuration Spells") spells and powers to fail. See [Spell Absorption](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Absorption#Bugs "Skyrim:Spell Absorption") for details.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
