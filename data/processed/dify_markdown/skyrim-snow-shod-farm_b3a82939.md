Snow-Shod Farm

**Snow-Shod Farm** is a small farm south of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") that is owned by [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Vulwulf_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Vulwulf Snow-Shod").

[Addvild](/wiki/Skyrim:Addvild "Skyrim:Addvild") and [Leonara Arius](/wiki/Skyrim:Leonara_Arius "Skyrim:Leonara Arius") live there and run the farm for Vulwulf.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-place-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg)

Snow-Shod Farm

There are two buildings on the lot: a farmhouse and a windmill. In the field, there are four bushels of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") , ten [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and five [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") plants, all of which [Addvild](/wiki/Skyrim:Addvild "Skyrim:Addvild") will pay you for gathering. There is a [Gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), two patches of [Scaly Pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota") mushrooms, a [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), a [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") and a [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") all growing in and around the farm. There are also four [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken"), two nests for collecting [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") and one [cow](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow "Skyrim:Cow") that produces [milk](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug_of_Milk "Skyrim:Jug of Milk") all on the farm.

### Snow-Shod Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm.jpg)

Snow-Shod Farm interior

Entering the farmhouse brings you straight into the main living room, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand against the northeast wall. Coming out from the southeast wall is a large wooden table with benches either side, with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") on top. There are [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks and barrels all around the farmhouse. On the southwest wall is a wall shelf holding clutter, with a chair against the same wall and a cupboard further to the right of the chair. The cupboard holds a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and three loaves of bread. Above the cupboard, hanging from the ceiling are two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and two bunches of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). A doorway to the northwest leads to the top of a flight of stairs leading down to the cellar. The stairs descend to the northeast.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg)

Snow-Shod Farm bedroom

At the bottom is a storage area with a pile of firewood and a couple of straw bales under the stairs and against the southwest wall is a set of shelves that hold several [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), two loaves of bread, several pieces of cheese and a copy of the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Reality & Other Falsehoods](/wiki/Skyrim:Reality_%26_Other_Falsehoods "Skyrim:Reality & Other Falsehoods")*. There is a dresser to the left, against the southeast wall with a bottle of wine atop. To the left is a doorway through into their bedroom which consists of a double bed coming out from the middle of the southeast wall, with a wall shelf above the bed holding a bottle of alto wine and a dresser at the foot of the bed with a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") on top. In the eastern corner is an end table with a copy of *[Rising Threat, Vol. IV](/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._IV "Skyrim:Rising Threat, Vol. IV")*, a chair against the southwest wall and a wardrobe in the northwest corner containing [clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing").

## Notes

- The farmyard is one of the common places you can encounter the [revelers](/wiki/Skyrim:Reveler "Skyrim:Reveler").
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the barrels outside the building as owned by the farm.

## Bugs
