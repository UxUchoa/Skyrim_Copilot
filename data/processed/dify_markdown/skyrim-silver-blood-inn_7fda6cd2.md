Silver-Blood Inn

**Silver-Blood Inn** is an [inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Inns "Skyrim:Inns") located in the city of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth").

The inn is owned by Markarth's prominent [Silver-Blood](/wiki/Skyrim:Thonar_Silver-Blood "Skyrim:Thonar Silver-Blood") clan, even though the members of the family are never seen in the building. They leave the business of running the inn to [Kleppr](/wiki/Skyrim:Kleppr "Skyrim:Kleppr"), who rents rooms and sells food and drink. His wife, [Frabbi](/wiki/Skyrim:Frabbi "Skyrim:Frabbi"), works at the inn, at least when she is not busy arguing with her husband. If Kleppr dies she takes over running the business, with nearly as many complaints about her husband as when he was alive. Their two children, [Hreinn](/wiki/Skyrim:Hreinn "Skyrim:Hreinn") and [Hroki](/wiki/Skyrim:Hroki "Skyrim:Hroki"), are also kept busy with jobs around the inn.

## Silver-Blood Inn

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-interior-Silver-Blood_Inn.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Silver-Blood_Inn.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Silver-Blood_Inn.jpg)

Inside the inn

The Silver-Blood Inn is one of the largest inns in [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), and many other townspeople can frequently be found at the inn. Entry is directly into the common room with a central bar facing you and a fireplace behind against the southwest wall. There are two corridors off this room one to the southeast and one to the northwest, with two flights of stairs up to closed doors also one to each side. [Kleppr](/wiki/Skyrim:Kleppr "Skyrim:Kleppr"), the innkeeper, is always standing behind the counter ready to welcome all-comers. There are many [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks around the establishment and all the beds and tables are made of stone. In the northern and eastern corners are sets of shelves, the first holds a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") and the second just clutter. To the right of the eastern shelves is a shelf with five [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and a cast iron pot containing a potato and [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), with a barrel containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") to the right and having two pieces of cheese on top. A matching ledge opposite to the north holds a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), with an unusable barrel beside it with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") on top.

The counter is a square U-shape, facing the entrance. On the counter there is a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge") and a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), with six wooden bar stools arranged in twos on each side. Under the counter are two small [coin purses](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a loaf of bread and a [key to Margret's room](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Margret%27s_Room "Skyrim:Key to Margret's Room"), that is useful during [The Forsworn Conspiracy](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forsworn_Conspiracy "Skyrim:The Forsworn Conspiracy") quest, however you can gain it purely by asking Kleppr. Between the corridor and stairs to the southeast is a small set of shelves with a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") and a wooden bowl containing two [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") and a [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") on top. To the left of the fireplace against the southwest wall is a basket containing a cabbage and [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"). There is nothing of interest on the right side of the fireplace. Nor is there anything of interest on a set of shelves between the corridor and stairs to the northwest.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-interior-Silver-Blood_Inn2.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Silver-Blood_Inn2.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Silver-Blood_Inn2.jpg)

Your room in the inn

The corridor to the southeast has a door a few paces along on the left, which is the room you can rent. It enters into a bedroom with a single owned bed against the northeast wall, with a table to the southeast. There is only clutter in this room. Further along this corridor is a flight of stairs leading up to Margret's room, if you managed to save her during the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forsworn_Conspiracy "Skyrim:The Forsworn Conspiracy") she will either be in here eating or in the common room sat by the fireplace. The room has a single owned bed against the southeast wall, with a wall shelf above holding a copy of *[Nightingales: Fact or Fiction?](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingales:_Fact_or_Fiction%3F "Skyrim:Nightingales: Fact or Fiction?")* and a bunch of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). To the northeast is a table and chair with a bottle of wine, a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") and a goat cheese wedge on top with a [plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") and [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"). At the other end of the room is a shelf with a few loaves of bread and three apples on top of it. In front of the shelf is an end table with a plate holding a [silver necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Necklace "Skyrim:Silver Necklace"), a medium coin purse and three loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") all on top. The end table also contains [Margret's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Margret%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Margret's Journal") (see [notes](#Notes)).

The closed door to the southeast opens directly into Kleppr and [Frabbi's](/wiki/Skyrim:Frabbi "Skyrim:Frabbi") quarters. They consist of a double owned bed against the northeast wall with an end table on either side of the bed. Between the end table and bed on the left, is a shelf with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and small coin purse on it. The end table to the right has a bottle of wine on top. To the right is a shelf on the southeast wall with an apprentice [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") next to a dresser with a [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"), a medium coin purse and a wooden bowl containing a bunch of [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") on top. Above the dresser is a shelf with a barrel containing ingredients, while to the right is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") and a bunch of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"). In the southern corner of the room is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") and pot hanging in front of it. The pot contains a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg") and portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"). To the right of the fireplace is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a table and two chairs to the right. On the table is a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, a salmon steak, a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a portion of grilled leeks, a [tomato](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), two loaves of bread and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge").

The door to the northwest opens into a twin bedroom with two single owned beds. They are both facing the same way, against the northeast wall with an end table between them with a bunch of tundra cotton and two loose septims on top. By the first bed, on the northeast wall is a shelf is a small coin purse and copy of *[The Legend of Red Eagle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle "Skyrim:The Legend of Red Eagle")*, which if you haven't already started the [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest) "Skyrim:Rebel's Cairn (quest)") reading it will cause the quest to start. Above the head of the bed, on a shelf is a bottle of alto wine and a loaf of bread. The shelf by the second bed has a loaf of bread on it. The shelf on the northwest wall, by the foot of the second bed has on it a couple of apples and a bunch of lavender in a cast iron pot. To the left of this is a tall wardrobe. There is a fireplace in the western corner with a cooking pot on a stand in front and a rack hanging from the ceiling to the right, holding a bunch of dried elves ear and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), with a seared slaughterfish on a block of wood beneath it. To the left of the fireplace is a pile of firewood with a shelf above it holding a copy of *[Herbane's Bestiary: Hagravens](/wiki/Skyrim:Herbane%27s_Bestiary:_Hagravens "Skyrim:Herbane's Bestiary: Hagravens")* and to the left of the firewood is a barrel containing ingredients with a loaf of bread and [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") on top. In the southern corner is a table and two chairs, with a bottle of wine, a baked potato, a red apple, a loaf of bread and a goat cheese wedge on top.

The corridor to the northwest has a door on the right similar to the one you rent, with an owned single bed against the southeast wall, with a pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") and a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") on the floor above the head of the bed and a loaf of bread on a shelf beside them. Above the bed is another wall shelf holding a bottle of alto wine and a basket containing a few loose [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"). Against the northwest wall is a table with a bottle of alto wine, a potato, a couple of pieces of eidar cheese and a basket containing two gourd on top. To the left of the table on a shelf is a small coin purse. Like the other corridor past this room there are stairs leading up to another bedroom, used by [Hogni Red-Arm](/wiki/Skyrim:Hogni_Red-Arm "Skyrim:Hogni Red-Arm") when he isn't working, selling raw meat from his stall on the market. It has a single owned bed against the northwest wall, with a shelf above holding a bottle of wine and a bucket containing three potatoes by the foot of the bed. On the shelf to the southwest is an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"), a [Dwemer Pan](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Pan "Skyrim:Dwemer Pan"), two bottles of alto wine, a pair of cabbages, a red apple and a leather strip. Against the northeast wall is a table and chair, with an iron dagger, a bottle of wine, a joint of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef") and a tomato on top.

There are several people who spend either all or a significant amount of their time at the inn. [Ogmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Ogmund "Skyrim:Ogmund") is the local bard and spends most of his time here, although he sleeps at his [own house](/wiki/Skyrim:Ogmund%27s_House "Skyrim:Ogmund's House"). [Cosnach](/wiki/Skyrim:Cosnach "Skyrim:Cosnach"), [Degaine](/wiki/Skyrim:Degaine "Skyrim:Degaine"), and [Muiri](/wiki/Skyrim:Muiri "Skyrim:Muiri") are some of the regulars at the bar in the evening, but they do not live in the inn.
