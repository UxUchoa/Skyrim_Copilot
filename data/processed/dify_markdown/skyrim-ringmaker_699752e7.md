## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Madesi](/wiki/Skyrim:Madesi "Skyrim:Madesi") in his stall in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften").
2. Gather one [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk"), one [gold ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore "Skyrim:Gold Ore"), and two [flawless sapphires](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Sapphire "Skyrim:Flawless Sapphire").
3. Return to Madesi.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-quest-Ringmaker_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Ringmaker_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Ringmaker_02.jpg)

All the necessary components

Speak with [Madesi](/wiki/Skyrim:Madesi "Skyrim:Madesi") and ask him about his [jewelry](/wiki/Skyrim:Jewelry "Skyrim:Jewelry"). You will learn that he is running low on supplies due to heavy [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") presence on the roads and needs help gathering some components. He requires two [flawless sapphires](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Sapphire "Skyrim:Flawless Sapphire"), one [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk"), and one piece of [gold ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore "Skyrim:Gold Ore").

Flawless sapphires, like all [gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Gems "Skyrim:Gems"), can be randomly found or [mined](/wiki/Skyrim:Mining "Skyrim:Mining"). One guaranteed sample can be stolen from the shelves in the Inner Sanctum of the [Temple of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Temple of Dibella") in Markarth.

Mammoth tusks can be looted from slain [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth"), as well as bought from [hunters](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter") and certain merchants. Three guaranteed samples can be found in the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)")'s [Arcanaeum](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum "Skyrim:The Arcanaeum"); eight guaranteed samples can be found in [Halted Stream Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Halted_Stream_Camp "Skyrim:Halted Stream Camp"). The closest one is in [Black-Briar Meadery](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Meadery "Skyrim:Black-Briar Meadery"), where it can be stolen from the shelves of a cupboard in [Indaryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Indaryn "Skyrim:Indaryn")'s bedroom.

Gold ore can be found randomly in containers or in buildings, but an easy place to find some gold veins is in [Kolskeggr Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine"). There's also [Lost Prospect Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Prospect_Mine "Skyrim:Lost Prospect Mine"); while the latter is closer, it doesn't have as many veins as Kolskeggr Mine. Alternately, one can just use [the Transmute spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Transmute_Mineral_Ore "Skyrim:Transmute Mineral Ore") to turn one Iron Ore into one Gold Ore.

You can return the items to Madesi as you find them, or all at once. He will give you 100 gold for each item returned (i.e., 200 for the two sapphires). Once you've returned them all, ask Madesi if he needs anything else and he will thank you with a leveled necklace.

## Notes

- Completing this quest counts towards your eligibility for becoming [Thane of the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_the_Rift "Skyrim:Thane of the Rift").
- On a purely money-making basis, this quest is a financial loss. Even at the lowest speech level, you can sell flawless sapphires (including to Madesi himself) for more then the quest reward of 100 per sapphire. At higher speech levels, you can sell a mammoth tusk for more than 100 gold. Purchasing the items for this quest will make the money loss even worse.
- Madesi will accept stolen goods in fulfillment of this quest, which may be useful if you are trying to avoid dealing with [fences](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Fences "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") or spending a perk point on the [fence perk](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech#Fence "Skyrim:Speech").
- You cannot toggle the objective in your journal for individual items; doing so will instead toggle all 3 items at once or the remaining two if you delivered one.
- [Halted Stream Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Halted_Stream_Camp "Skyrim:Halted Stream Camp") is the location of a [bounty quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Bandit_Boss "Skyrim:Bounty: Bandit Boss"), possible location of a [Dungeon Delving](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Bandits)") quest from [Amren](/wiki/Skyrim:Amren "Skyrim:Amren"), and possible location of the [Amulet of the Moon](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_the_Moon "Skyrim:Amulet of the Moon") quest, making it easy to complete multiple quests at once if you choose to find your mammoth tusk there.

## Bugs

- There are no quest markers for delivering the items back to Madesi.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- The quest may continue to prompt the objective to bring Madesi his items if the objective to do so is in your quest journal.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.9, fixes this bug.
