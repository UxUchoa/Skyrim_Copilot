**The Rift** is a [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in southeast [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), with its capital in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). It is initially affiliated with the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks"), but can be captured by the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") after completing [Compelling Tribute](/wiki/Skyrim:Compelling_Tribute_(Imperial) "Skyrim:Compelling Tribute (Imperial)") and [The Battle for Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Greenwall (Imperial)"). It can also change sides as part of negotiations during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending").

The Rift is the southernmost of Skyrim's holds, sitting on a massive plateau in the southeastern corner of the province. The Rift borders both [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind") and [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil"), and is separated from the rest of the world by steep cliffs and mountains, including the [Jerall Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Jerall_Mountains "Skyrim:Jerall Mountains"), the [Velothi Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Velothi_Mountains "Skyrim:Velothi Mountains"), and the legendary [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World"). It holds two large lakes as well as autumnal deciduous forests.

In addition to its capital in Riften, the Rift also houses the settlements of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") and [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone").

## Flora and Fauna

The Rift is particularly distinctive for its high number of aspen trees, with white bark and leaves in autumnal hues. For those looking for alchemy ingredients, the Rift contains all variants of [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Mountain Flower"), as well as the native [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota") and the [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"). The Rift is also the only place to find wild [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") plants.

As for the fauna the most notable is the rich population of [fish](/wiki/Skyrim:Fish "Skyrim:Fish") and [Nordic barnacle](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle") in [Lake Honrich](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Honrich "Skyrim:Lake Honrich"), which fuel the economy of Riften.

### Daedric Quests

- **[The Cursed Tribe](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe "Skyrim:The Cursed Tribe")**: Help lift the curse placed on the [Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") of [Largashbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur "Skyrim:Largashbur") by [Malacath](/wiki/Lore:Malacath "Lore:Malacath").

### Dark Brotherhood

- **[Innocence Lost](/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost "Skyrim:Innocence Lost")**: Help an orphaned [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino "Skyrim:Aventus Aretino") avoid being returned to a sadistic [caretaker](/wiki/Skyrim:Grelod_the_Kind "Skyrim:Grelod the Kind").
- **[Kill Narfi](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Narfi "Skyrim:Kill Narfi")**: Kill a hapless [beggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi "Skyrim:Narfi") in [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead").

### Thieves Guild

- All [primary quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Primary_Quests "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") except for [Hard Answers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers "Skyrim:Hard Answers"), as well as the special jobs, either originate or terminate here.

### Side Quests including Divine Quests

- **[The Book of Love](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Love "Skyrim:The Book of Love")**: Help the [Temple of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Mara "Skyrim:Temple of Mara") spread the cause of love throughout Skyrim.
- **[Forbidden Legend](/wiki/Skyrim:Forbidden_Legend "Skyrim:Forbidden Legend")**: Investigate the forgotten [Gauldur Legend](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends "Skyrim:Lost Legends").
- **[Infiltration](/wiki/Skyrim:Infiltration "Skyrim:Infiltration")**: Help [Stalleo](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalleo "Skyrim:Stalleo") take back [Treva's Watch](/wiki/Skyrim:Treva%27s_Watch "Skyrim:Treva's Watch").
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials")**: Participate in [Kyne](/wiki/Lore:Kyne "Lore:Kyne")'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").
- **[Promises to Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Promises_to_Keep "Skyrim:Promises to Keep")**: Help [Louis Letrush](/wiki/Skyrim:Louis_Letrush "Skyrim:Louis Letrush") get [Frost](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost "Skyrim:Frost") from [Sibbi Black-Briar](/wiki/Skyrim:Sibbi_Black-Briar "Skyrim:Sibbi Black-Briar").
- **[Repentance](/wiki/Skyrim:Repentance "Skyrim:Repentance")**: Help [Illia](/wiki/Skyrim:Illia "Skyrim:Illia") prevent her mother [Silvia](/wiki/Skyrim:Silvia "Skyrim:Silvia") from transforming into a [hagraven](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven "Skyrim:Hagraven").
- **[A Return To Your Roots](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Return_To_Your_Roots "Skyrim:A Return To Your Roots")**: Go where a [famous alchemist](/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion "Skyrim:Sinderion") could not in order to aid ground-breaking research.
- **[Siege on the Dragon Cult](/wiki/Skyrim:Siege_on_the_Dragon_Cult "Skyrim:Siege on the Dragon Cult")**: Enter the ruins of [Forelhost](/wiki/Skyrim:Forelhost "Skyrim:Forelhost") and defeat [Rahgot](/wiki/Skyrim:Rahgot_(creature) "Skyrim:Rahgot (creature)") to recover his mask for [Captain Valmir](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Valmir "Skyrim:Captain Valmir").
- **[Unfathomable Depths](/wiki/Skyrim:Unfathomable_Depths "Skyrim:Unfathomable Depths")**: Uncover the [memories](/wiki/Skyrim:Lexicon "Skyrim:Lexicon") of the Dwemer.
