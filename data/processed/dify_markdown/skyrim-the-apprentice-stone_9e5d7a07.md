The Apprentice Stone

**The Apprentice Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones") scattered across Skyrim.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone_(active).jpg)

The Apprentice Stone when it's activated

*Those under the sign of The Apprentice recover Magicka faster, but are more susceptible to Magicka damage.* Activating the Apprentice Stone increases [magicka regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Regenerate_Magicka "Skyrim:Regenerate Magicka") by 100% and increases [weakness to magic](/wiki/Skyrim:Weakness_to_Magic "Skyrim:Weakness to Magic") by 100%.

The stone is set in the typical arrangement of standing stones on the northeast end of a small marsh archipelago. Little else is found here other than plants typical to the marshes of [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"). A [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") plant is growing just to the west of the stone structure, at the base of a large tree. Another is at the base of the Apprentice stone itself. [Swamp fungal pods](/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod "Skyrim:Swamp Fungal Pod") are also growing in the stone's circle and more can be found at the base of a tree just to the right of the circle of stones. A [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") is found on the shoreline to the southeast of the circle.

## Notes

- Although the loading screen tip states the Apprentice Stone doubles both magicka regeneration and magicka, only magicka regeneration is doubled.
- East-southeast of the Stone, a Nordic burial pit has partially sunk into the swamp, and has been taken over by several [mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab"). A novice treasure chest, several burial urns, and a copy of the [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Locked Room](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Locked_Room "Skyrim:The Locked Room")* can be found here.
