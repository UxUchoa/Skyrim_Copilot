Verner and Annekke's House

**Verner and Annekke's House** is the only house in [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-interior-Verner_and_Annekke%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Verner_and_Annekke%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Verner_and_Annekke%27s_House.jpg)

Verner and Annekke's home

It is a one-room dwelling home to [Annekke Crag-Jumper](/wiki/Skyrim:Annekke_Crag-Jumper "Skyrim:Annekke Crag-Jumper") and her husband [Verner Rock-Chucker](/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_Rock-Chucker "Skyrim:Verner Rock-Chucker"), the owners of [Goldenrock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenrock_Mine "Skyrim:Goldenrock Mine") and the original settlers of [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing").

### Exterior

The house has a field attached, enclosed with wicker fencing and planted with seven bushels of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"). There is a lean-to being used as a chicken coop with two [nests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") in it, with two [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken") and a [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") wandering near the house. To the left of the chicken coop is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), and in front of the house by the southwestern corner are a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") and a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block").

### Verner and Annekke's House

The entrance is novice-locked at night, but will be unlocked during the day. Against the west wall there are two [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels in each corner separated by a cupboard holding three bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). There is a long wooden table against the north wall with a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and a bucket containing a few [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") on it. Above this, hanging from the ceiling, are three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, two bunches of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), and a rack that holds three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and three [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). To the right of the table are another food barrel and a set of shelves; the shelves hold four more bottles of alto wine and four more bottles of wine. To the right of the shelves is a fireplace with a round wooden table and two chairs in front. On the table are a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, and some [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and cheese. On the hearth are a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") and a kettle containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"). On the east wall is an owned double bed with an end table to the left and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed. There is a bottle of Nord mead on the end table. To the right of the bed against the south wall is a tall wardrobe with a food barrel and dresser to the right. On the dresser are six [corundum ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ingot "Skyrim:Corundum Ingot"), two pieces of [corundum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore "Skyrim:Corundum Ore"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead").
