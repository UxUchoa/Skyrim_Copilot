## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to your fellow inmates.
2. Ask [Grisvar the Unlucky](/wiki/Skyrim:Grisvar_the_Unlucky "Skyrim:Grisvar the Unlucky") for his spare [shiv](/wiki/Skyrim:Shiv_(item) "Skyrim:Shiv (item)").
3. Obtain a bottle of [Skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma").
4. Swap the Skooma for Grisvar's spare shiv.

### So what are you in for?

Before throwing you into [Cidhna Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Cidhna_Mine "Skyrim:Cidhna Mine"), the guards were careful enough to remove all of your equipment, leaving weapon-based characters at a distinct disadvantage. If you chat to your fellow inmates, however, you will learn that [Grisvar the Unlucky](/wiki/Skyrim:Grisvar_the_Unlucky "Skyrim:Grisvar the Unlucky") has a spare weapon, and might be willing to let you have it.

### If you want to get ahead, get a shiv

Grisvar admits to having an extra [shiv](/wiki/Skyrim:Shiv_(item) "Skyrim:Shiv (item)") but isn't keen to part with it. He is something of a [Skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma") addict, and has heard that [Duach](/wiki/Skyrim:Duach "Skyrim:Duach") has got a bottle of the good stuff. Grisvar claims to be "shaking just thinking about it", and wants you to obtain the bottle for him.

### Distilled Pleasure

Grisvar isn't as discerning as his request makes him seem, and will accept any bottle of Skooma. Several of the mine's inmates possess bottles of Skooma but Duach is often to be found on his own, making it easier to pick his pockets or kill him quietly. You can also try to pass a [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") check with Duach or beat him in a brawl to make him hand over his bottle. However you obtain the Skooma, take it back to Grisvar.

### Forlorn hope

The Skooma is warmly received, and Grisvar gladly hands over his spare shiv. He asks you to promise you won't ever use it on him - and it's perhaps just as well that no option to accept the promise is offered.
