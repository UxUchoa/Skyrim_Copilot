These **Bretons** are all male [citizens](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)") and wear randomly determined farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") (7 variations) or a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic"), a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") (4 variations), with a 50% chance for a [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Hat "Skyrim:Hat") (3 variations). Additionally there is a slim chance they may carry a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and a small amount of gold.

They can be found in the following locations:
