**Solitude Lighthouse** is a stone building on the northern coast of Skyrim that helps to guide ships into Solitude harbor. It is tended to by a Khajiit named [Ma'zaka](/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27zaka "Skyrim:Ma'zaka"), who lives here alone.

### Exterior

There are several [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Grass Pod") plants and a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") nearby. Directly west of the [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"), in the small cavity of the rock formation is a bottle of [Skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma") and an [Empty Skooma Bottle](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Skooma_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Skooma Bottle"). They are somewhat difficult to see due to the gravel texture of the ground. The [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") depicted in [Treasure Map III](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_III "Skyrim:Treasure Map III") is to the northeast, under the cliff edge. As you pass through the outer archway there is a door on your left that leads to the interior, which may be apprentice-locked. Continuing through the inner arch you come to a long spiral stone staircase that leads all the way up to the top and the fire that acts as the light for ships.

### Solitude Lighthouse

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-interior-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg)

Ma'zaka sitting in the residence

Through the door on the left, you enter what appears to be a normal house, with barrels of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") on either side of you, with a wooden table further to the left against the south wall. On the table is a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield"), an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet"), an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"), a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow"), and a random [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword"). Against the west wall is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). To the right is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a piece of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef") and a cabbage in a cast iron pot on the hearth. To the right of the fireplace, coming out from the west wall is an internal wall. There is a plate holding [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks") on top of it, and a stack of two open crates on the floor beside it containing a sample of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") and an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing"), a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") and two loaves of bread.

Along the east wall are two stacked open crates containing a sample of fly amanita, a gourd, a rock warbler egg and an orange dartwing, three [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and two potatoes. To the left of these is a set of shelves holding eight [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot"), a piece of [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore"), a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"). Further to the left, still against the east wall are two more stacked crates containing a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), six [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulbs, four [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), and a cabbage. A small set of shelves next to these holds two potatoes, a portion of grilled leeks, two loaves of bread, a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") breast and a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). Hanging above these are four [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), and a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant").

Behind the internal wall is a table and chairs set for two, with two portions of grilled leeks, two [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a loaf of bread, an eidar cheese wedge, and a bulb of garlic. Above the table on the west wall is a wall shelf holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). Against the north wall, next to the table, is a small set of shelves holding a copy of the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Lunar Lorkhan](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lunar_Lorkhan "Skyrim:The Lunar Lorkhan")*. To the right of this is a doorway into Ma'zaka's bedroom, which consists of a single unowned bed with an end table holding a common book and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). There is a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") beside the bed and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed. On your right as you enter the room is a small unit holding a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk"), a [cave bear pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Cave_Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Cave Bear Pelt"), a [red mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), and a basket containing two bunches of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). To the left in the northeast corner is a stack of barrels containing foodstuffs.

## Notes

- If the fire here is extinguished, the lighthouse will never be re-lit. If you place torches into the fire pit and travel to somewhere else, the torches and the lighthouse beacon will be lit once you return. However, when viewed from a distance, there will still be no visible light.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.4, fixes this bug.

## Bugs

- A seemingly defined area stretching from the ship to the south up to just northwest of the lighthouse and east of the rocks may cause the game to crash upon entry.
  - Waiting 31 days for the world cell to reset may resolve this.
