Ironback Hideout Cellar

**Ironback Hideout Cellar** is a cellar located underneath the ruined house at [Ironback Hideout](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout "Skyrim:Ironback Hideout"). It can be accessed via a trapdoor hidden within a tent, although this entrance is initially locked.

## Ironback Hideout Cellar

Once you have obtained the [Ironback Hideout Cellar Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout_Cellar_Key "Skyrim:Ironback Hideout Cellar Key") as part of the related quest, you will be directed to enter the cellar. Within, you will find an uncluttered entrance area containing an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"). The deceased Orc Kragrash used the basement to store his vampire hunting equipment, which is separated from you by a set of bars. The cellar key can be used to unlock the barred door and access these items. Behind the bars you will find a good deal of loot, including a treasure chest and a wall-mounted [silver greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Greatsword "Skyrim:Silver Greatsword"). Laid out on a table you will find an [ebony crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Crossbow "Skyrim:Ebony Crossbow"), an [Elven crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Crossbow "Skyrim:Elven Crossbow"), and several [Exploding Dwarven Bolts of Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Fire "Skyrim:Exploding Dwarven Bolt of Fire") and [Shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Shock "Skyrim:Exploding Dwarven Bolt of Shock"). These crossbow items can usually only be crafted.
