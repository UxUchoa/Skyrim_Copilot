# Skyrim:Main Quest

## Overview

[![SR-qico-Dragonborn Knotwork.png](//images.uesp.net/e/e8/SR-qico-Dragonborn_Knotwork.png)](/wiki/File:SR-qico-Dragonborn_Knotwork.png)

Skyrim's **main quest** consists of seventeen required quests and two optional quests. During these quests you discover your heritage as the [Dovahkiin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dovahkiin "Skyrim:Dovahkiin"), learn why [dragons](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") have returned to the world, and find a way to respond to the dragon threat. Completing the main quest will take approximately 15-30 hours depending on your character's level. Eight [achievements](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements") (170 points; 7 Bronze and 1 Gold) are directly tied to completing the main quest; in addition, several [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts#Achievements "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts")-related achievements are inevitably unlocked during the quests. Quests belonging to the main quest are marked in your journal by the dragon head knotwork, which is pictured above.

1. **[Unbound](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound "Skyrim:Unbound")**: Escape [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") custody and a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") attack. ([![SR-achievement-Unbound.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-achievement-Unbound.png/18px-SR-achievement-Unbound.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Unbound.png) 10 pts/Bronze)
2. **[Before the Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Before_the_Storm "Skyrim:Before the Storm")**: Inform the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater") of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") about the dragon attack on [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen").
3. **[Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (quest)")**: Retrieve the [Dragonstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonstone "Skyrim:Dragonstone"). ([![SR-achievement-Bleak Falls Barrow.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-achievement-Bleak_Falls_Barrow.png/18px-SR-achievement-Bleak_Falls_Barrow.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Bleak_Falls_Barrow.png) 10 pts/Bronze)
4. **[Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising")**: Investigate a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") sighting near [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").
5. **[The Way of the Voice](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Way_of_the_Voice "Skyrim:The Way of the Voice")**: Meet the [Greybeards](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards"). ([![SR-achievement-The Way of the Voice.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-achievement-The_Way_of_the_Voice.png/18px-SR-achievement-The_Way_of_the_Voice.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-The_Way_of_the_Voice.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
6. **[The Horn of Jurgen Windcaller](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Horn_of_Jurgen_Windcaller "Skyrim:The Horn of Jurgen Windcaller")**: Retrieve a [Greybeard](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards") artifact from [Ustengrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Ustengrav "Skyrim:Ustengrav").
7. **[A Blade in the Dark](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Blade_in_the_Dark "Skyrim:A Blade in the Dark")**: Prove you are Dragonborn to [Delphine](/wiki/Skyrim:Delphine "Skyrim:Delphine") by slaying the dragon [Sahloknir](/wiki/Skyrim:Sahloknir "Skyrim:Sahloknir").
8. **[Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity")**: Infiltrate a party at the [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy"). ([![SR-achievement-Diplomatic Immunity.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-achievement-Diplomatic_Immunity.png/18px-SR-achievement-Diplomatic_Immunity.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Diplomatic_Immunity.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
9. **[A Cornered Rat](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Cornered_Rat "Skyrim:A Cornered Rat")**: Locate [Esbern](/wiki/Skyrim:Esbern "Skyrim:Esbern").
10. **[Alduin's Wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin%27s_Wall "Skyrim:Alduin's Wall")**: Locate the prophecy within [Sky Haven Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Sky_Haven_Temple "Skyrim:Sky Haven Temple"). ([![SR-achievement-Alduin's Wall.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-achievement-Alduin%27s_Wall.png/18px-SR-achievement-Alduin%27s_Wall.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Alduin%27s_Wall.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
11. **[The Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:The Throat of the World")**: Receive a lesson from [Paarthurnax](/wiki/Skyrim:Paarthurnax_(dragon) "Skyrim:Paarthurnax (dragon)").
12. **[Elder Knowledge](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder_Knowledge "Skyrim:Elder Knowledge")**: Recover an [Elder Scroll](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder_Scroll "Skyrim:Elder Scroll") to learn an [Ancient Secret](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonrend "Skyrim:Dragonrend"). ([![SR-achievement-Elder Knowledge.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-achievement-Elder_Knowledge.png/18px-SR-achievement-Elder_Knowledge.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Elder_Knowledge.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
13. **[Alduin's Bane](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin%27s_Bane "Skyrim:Alduin's Bane")**: Exploit the Time-Wound to learn [Dragonrend](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonrend "Skyrim:Dragonrend") and defeat [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin") at the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World").
14. **[The Fallen](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Fallen "Skyrim:The Fallen")**: Capture and interrogate [an ally](/wiki/Skyrim:Odahviing "Skyrim:Odahviing") of [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin"). ([![SR-achievement-The Fallen.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/SR-achievement-The_Fallen.png/18px-SR-achievement-The_Fallen.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-The_Fallen.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
    - (optional) **[Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending")**: Enforce a ceasefire to secure [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach").
    - (optional) **[Paarthurnax](/wiki/Skyrim:Paarthurnax_(quest) "Skyrim:Paarthurnax (quest)")**: Deal with a [potential threat](/wiki/Skyrim:Paarthurnax_(dragon) "Skyrim:Paarthurnax (dragon)").
15. **[The World-Eater's Eyrie](/wiki/Skyrim:The_World-Eater%27s_Eyrie "Skyrim:The World-Eater's Eyrie")**: Battle through [Skuldafn](/wiki/Skyrim:Skuldafn "Skyrim:Skuldafn") to reach [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin")'s portal to [Sovngarde](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovngarde_(place) "Skyrim:Sovngarde (place)").
16. **[Sovngarde](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovngarde_(quest) "Skyrim:Sovngarde (quest)")**: Seek out [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin") within [Sovngarde](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovngarde_(place) "Skyrim:Sovngarde (place)").
17. **[Dragonslayer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonslayer "Skyrim:Dragonslayer")**: Put an end to the [World-Eater](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin"). ([![SR-achievement-Dragonslayer.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/SR-achievement-Dragonslayer.png/18px-SR-achievement-Dragonslayer.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Dragonslayer.png) 50 pts/Gold)
