Taarie has fashion advice and a job for you.

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Taarie](/wiki/Skyrim:Taarie "Skyrim:Taarie").
2. Put on [Radiant Raiment Fine Clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant_Raiment_Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Radiant Raiment Fine Clothes").
3. Introduce [Radiant Raiment](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant_Raiment "Skyrim:Radiant Raiment") to [Elisif the Fair](/wiki/Skyrim:Elisif_the_Fair "Skyrim:Elisif the Fair").
4. Return to Taarie.

### You're Going to Wear That ?

If you meet up with [Taarie](/wiki/Skyrim:Taarie "Skyrim:Taarie") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), she will comment on your outfit: *"If you're heading to the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace"), you might want to rethink that outfit."* You may ask her for some suggestions on what to wear to meet the jarl. Taarie's abrasive attitude toward you will change immediately. She will regard your meeting in the Blue Palace as an opportunity to introduce [Radiant Raiment](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant_Raiment "Skyrim:Radiant Raiment") to [Elisif the Fair](/wiki/Skyrim:Elisif_the_Fair "Skyrim:Elisif the Fair"). Taarie offers to pay you to wear one of Radiant Raiment's outfits when you speak to the jarl. She'll let you keep the clothes as well.
You may be skeptical of Taarie's sudden wellspring of good will; maybe she wants to make you look silly in front of the jarl. Taarie assures you that you cannot possibly look any sillier than you do right now, so go ahead and accept the offer.

### Does This Make My Rear-End Look Big?

The [Radiant Raiment Fine Clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant_Raiment_Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Radiant Raiment Fine Clothes") are added to your inventory with instructions from Taarie: *"Try to get Elisif's opinion on it and if she likes it mention it's from Radiant Raiment."* Equip the clothes and go speak with Elisif. Depending on your business at the Blue Palace, the conversation starter about your apparel can seem awkward. Yet, Elisif has an eye for fashion, even in the midst of turmoil. You may ask for her opinion on your outfit and she will tell you, *"It's quite fetching actually. The craftsmanship is excellent."* Tell her it was made by Radiant Raiment. She will be glad to put in a request for a few dresses quite soon.

### A Job Well Done

Return to Taarie and tell her that Elisif loved the outfit and that she'll be placing an order soon. Taarie will be quite happy with the result: *"That's wonderful. You've done us a great service."* You can keep the outfit as she promised and she will also give you some gold for your trouble.

## Bugs

- After you finish the quest, Taarie's disposition will not get raised as expected.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19403))
- It is possible to steal the outfit prior to actually receiving the quest, which would then leave the outfit permanently flagged as stolen even though you're supposed to be given the outfit freely.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.1, fixes this bug.
