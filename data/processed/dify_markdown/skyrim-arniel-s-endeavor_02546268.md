### Part 1

Talk to Arniel Gane and ask if there is any College business you can assist him with. He'll state that it's nothing official, but he is working on a "project". He refuses to say anything about it, but he asks you to bring him ten [Dwemer cogs](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Cog "Skyrim:Dwemer Cog"). They can be found randomly throughout any and all Dwarven ruins. If you have not already found a Dwarven ruin, [Alftand](/wiki/Skyrim:Alftand "Skyrim:Alftand") is near the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)"), and the ruin under [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") is easy to find and lower-level friendly.

There are plenty of Dwemer cogs inside [Dwemer Museum](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Museum "Skyrim:Dwemer Museum") in [Understone Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep "Skyrim:Understone Keep"), specifically in the closest right corner from the entrance; with [stealthy](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak") movement, all cogs can be obtained without any repercussions, making this the easiest and quickest method. The main College quest line will take you to [Mzulft](/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft "Skyrim:Mzulft"), which also has more than ten Dwemer cogs. Depending on the ruins and your carry capacity (cogs weigh 10 each) you may end up needing two or three trips to get all the cogs.

Once you have obtained the ten Dwemer cogs, return to Arniel and he will reward you with a [small amount of gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Gold_Rewards "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

### Part 2

Arniel needs a very specific item for his project and is having trouble getting Enthir to give it to him. He'd like you to speak to Enthir and return to him with the item. Speaking to Enthir reveals that the item Arniel wants never belonged to him and is Enthir's property. He agrees to relinquish it, though—if you'll retrieve the [Staff of Tandil](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Tandil "Skyrim:Staff of Tandil") he sold to an unscrupulous character.

The character is a boss level caster in a radiantly assigned dungeon. Depending on which one you get the quest may be increasingly more or less difficult. Some of the dungeons, such as [Boulderfall Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Boulderfall_Cave "Skyrim:Boulderfall Cave") and [Cragwallow Slope](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragwallow_Slope "Skyrim:Cragwallow Slope"), are extremely small while others might be rather involved or of a difficult nature. The staff should be either on the boss level caster, somewhere nearby, or in the boss level chest.

Once you have the staff, return to Enthir. He'll give you the [warped soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Warped_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Warped Soul Gem"), which can then be returned to Arniel to proceed with the quest.

### Part 3

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_%28C%29.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_%28C%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_(C).jpg)

A Dwemer Convector heated by Arniel's spell

Begin this quest by asking [Arniel](/wiki/Skyrim:Arniel_Gane "Skyrim:Arniel Gane") about the progress of his project. He will say that it is going terribly. He reveals that he is trying to simulate the disappearance of the [Dwemer](/wiki/Lore:Dwemer "Lore:Dwemer") but the warped soul gem that you acquired for him needs to be charged. Unfortunately, the apparatus he was using to charge it was destroyed in the process and he has no time to build another. Thus, he is only too happy to accept your generous offer to charge it for him.

Arniel tells you that the Dwemer used to use convectors to charge their soul gems and that some of these devices probably still exist among Dwarven ruins. He needs two more charges after his own initial charge to power the gem. Due to the nature of the warped soul gem, it will destroy the convector it is charged in. What you need to do is find a few Dwemer convectors and place the warped soul gem inside. He will teach you a spell called [Arniel's Convection](/wiki/Skyrim:Arniel%27s_Convection "Skyrim:Arniel's Convection") that must be channeled onto the Dwemer convector until the stone is charged. Keep channeling the convector until the quest updates. You will then have to find a new convector and repeat this process up to two times. There seems to be some variance on whether it takes one to three times to fully charge the stone and use of dual casting can complicate the matter.

There are six Dwemer convectors in all of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"):

- [Druadach Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Redoubt "Skyrim:Druadach Redoubt"): Across the river from it in an unmarked ruin, east-northeast of the map marker.
- [Deep Folk Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Deep_Folk_Crossing "Skyrim:Deep Folk Crossing"): Across the bridge from it at an unmarked ruin, just north of the map marker.
- [Dwarven Storeroom](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Storeroom "Skyrim:Dwarven Storeroom"): Simple freestanding Dwarven structure near Mzulft. No enemies.
- [Mzulft](/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft "Skyrim:Mzulft"): Near the entrance in the first rooms after the hall, just before the second incline. Must have started [Revealing the Unseen](/wiki/Skyrim:Revealing_the_Unseen "Skyrim:Revealing the Unseen").
- [Ruins of Bthalft](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Bthalft "Skyrim:Ruins of Bthalft"): Exterior only ruins, [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") here.
- [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"): Southwest of the village at an unmarked ruin with a single [warlock](/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock "Skyrim:Warlock").

Two are northward of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten"), one south of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"), one southwest of [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"), and two among the ruins of Mzulft. The two northwest of Karthwasten are probably the easiest to use since no enemies spawn at their locations (wild animals may be a nuisance however) and they are relatively close together. One is directly north of Karthwasten, northeast and across the river from Druadach Redoubt. It is at the base of the unmarked Dwemer pillar. If you cross the river and follow it upstream you will come to Deep Folk Crossing. Cross the bridge and the second Dwemer convector will be in front of you. The one south of Ivarstead is at the Ruins of Bthalft and has bandits. The one southwest of Shor's Stone is between there and [Autumnshade Clearing](/wiki/Skyrim:Autumnshade_Clearing "Skyrim:Autumnshade Clearing") and has a rogue mage. At Mzulft, one is inside the storeroom (a separate building to the right of the ruin entrance when you arrive) and the other is within the ruins proper.

### Part 4

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_03.jpg)

The dead courier

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Arniel%27s_Endeavor_02.jpg)

Arniel disappearing in a flash of light

Unlike previous parts of the quest, about two or three days must pass before continuing. Thereafter, talk to Arniel again. He is often to be found on the upper floor of the [Hall of Attainment](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Attainment "Skyrim:Hall of Attainment"), but may also be in the [Hall of Countenance](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Countenance "Skyrim:Hall of Countenance"). Once more, he's used Enthir's services, and once more, you have to pick up the pieces.

Talk to Enthir, who is almost always in his room in the Hall of Attainment. He's quite annoyed since he has no idea where the item is—or even what it is, for that matter. He was only tasked with providing transport for it from [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind") and it simply hasn't arrived. Enthir tells you to go look for the [courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Courier "Skyrim:Courier") somewhere in the east, and marks a location on your map.

The precise location of the courier varies. When you finally do locate him, it's immediately apparent why he didn't complete the delivery. Retrieve the blade "[Keening](/wiki/Skyrim:Keening "Skyrim:Keening")" from his body and return it to Arniel. He is outraged that it wasn't wrapped properly and amazed that you didn't die when you touched it. By way of explanation, he relates something of the background of [Keening](/wiki/Lore:Keening "Lore:Keening") and [Sunder](/wiki/Lore:Sunder "Lore:Sunder"), the [legendary tools](/wiki/Lore:Kagrenac%27s_Tools "Lore:Kagrenac's Tools") of the Dwemer Tonal Architect [Lord Kagrenac](/wiki/Lore:Kagrenac "Lore:Kagrenac").

Arniel now conducts his experiment in an attempt to figure out how and why the Dwemer disappeared. Striking the warped soul gem with Keening, he finds that it does nothing. He strikes again, gaining no apparent result once more. Having lost his temper, he curses, lashes out a third time... and disappears in a flash of light, never to be seen again.

The quest is then unceremoniously updated as complete. Pick up Keening from the floor if you wish (although it seems to have come down in the world somewhat since the Third Era). [Summon Arniel's Shade](/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arniel%27s_Shade "Skyrim:Summon Arniel's Shade") will be added to your spell list automatically.

## Notes

- Part three is not available until you complete the quest [The Eye of Magnus](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Eye_of_Magnus "Skyrim:The Eye of Magnus").
  - It's thus irrelevant that one cannot enter Mzulft until having started [Revealing the Unseen](/wiki/Skyrim:Revealing_the_Unseen "Skyrim:Revealing the Unseen") to find the convector, since this quest has already been completed long before The Eye of Magnus.
- The quest tracker will not track Dwemer cogs you pick up, but they still count for the quest, so you must keep count of them yourself.
- It is not necessary to carry around all ten cogs at the same time; utilize storage at the College to avoid carrying around the heavy cogs.
- Part two is almost identical in nature to [Onmund's Request](/wiki/Skyrim:Onmund%27s_Request "Skyrim:Onmund's Request"), but the radiant dungeon assignment might differ. It is possible to be sent to the same location in both quests.
- No matter how many times you use Keening to strike the warped soul gem, you will not disappear in a blinding flash.
- Enthir will have slightly different dialogue during this quest if you are the arch-mage or completed [Hard Answers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers "Skyrim:Hard Answers").
- After providing Keening to Arniel, he will comment on the absence of Sunder. Even with the [Sunder & Wraithguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Sunder_%26_Wraithguard "Skyrim:Sunder & Wraithguard") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed and [Sunder](/wiki/Skyrim:Sunder "Skyrim:Sunder") in your inventory, it will not impact the quest, and Arniel will still mention that he lacks the latter. This is due to the fact that Sunder is added via the Creation Club long after the game was released, so there were no lines recorded for him regarding it.
- Despite displaying the "Take Warped Soul Gem" command when highlighted, the soul gem cannot be removed after the experiment has ended (nor during the experiment).

## Bugs

- In some cases the warped soul gem will get duplicated after heating it. This prevents further dialogue with Arniel, thus making it impossible to complete the quest.
  - You may be able to fix this by leaving one of the duplicates of the gem in the Dwarven convector. This should allow you to be able to finish the dialogue with Arniel.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You can remove extra warped gems through the console with the command `player.removeitem 6a10a 1` (that's for the final version only, the others have different form IDs) for each extra warped gem in your inventory.
  - Assuming this does not occur on the third and final convector heating, this can be fixed by putting both soul gems in the next convector box and then heating it with the spell, this will result in one of the soul gems disappearing, thus leaving only one to collect and allowing the completion dialogue with Arniel.
  - This can be caused by adding the Destruction: Augmented Flames perk or Dual Wielding.
- The quest can fail to update, getting stuck on "Place the warped soul gem into a final Convector". Dual/single casting, alternate convectors does not remedy.

  **Mod Notes**: The objective displayed jumps ahead of the item you possess, causing the script conditions to fail.

  - This can be caused by adding the Destruction: Augmented Flames perk or Dual Wielding.
  - Load a save from before the first convector was used, and heat each convector by casting with a single hand only in short pulses, stopping immediately when each quest stage is reached.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Another option is to place the warped soul gem into a convector, type `setstage MGRArniel03 20` in the console, then heat the convector and the quest updates. If it doesn't update, make sure a warped soul gem is in the convector and type `setstage MGRArniel03 30`.
  - Make sure that you did not leave any item in any convector, they must all be empty.
- After returning the gem the quest will be completed but if you look at it under completed quests the bullet marker for the last stage is hollow (as if the stage was not completed). This does not seem to affect gameplay.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
- It is possible to get Keening from the courier before talking to Enthir about the mysterious item Arniel wanted delivered from Morrowind. When this happens, you can find the courier, but you can't advance the quest because you can't get Keening from him again.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Type `setstage MGRArniel04 30` in the console. (Note that you don't actually have to go to the courier again to do this.)
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Go to the location of the courier and type `player.dropitem 0006a13c 1` in the console (assuming you have only one Keening in your inventory), then pick up the dropped Keening on the ground in front of the courier. The objective <Find the courier> will complete and the incomplete objective <Deliver the package to Arniel> will appear in your journal.
  - You can wield Keening in front of Arniel and talk to him. This could trigger the following stage.
  - You can also wield Keening and use a weapon plaque, then remove it from the plaque.
  - You can also wield Keening and use a weapon rack, have your follower pick it up, go to the location of the courier's body, then finally take Keening from your follower. This will register as completed task of finding the courier.
  - Sometimes this doesn't work and instead the weapon is placed on the ground. Just get a follower to pick it up. When you take it from them, the quest will update and work properly from there.
- A quest journal would trigger multiple times.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
- Keening may disappear with Arniel instead of falling to the floor as it should.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
  - Keening may be forced through the floor and land in the room below the storage area where Arniel's experiment is held. If it goes missing, try looking for it on the first floor of the [Hall of Countenance](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Countenance "Skyrim:Hall of Countenance") where [Phinis Gestor](/wiki/Skyrim:Phinis_Gestor "Skyrim:Phinis Gestor") normally sleeps (the first room to the left if you're facing the stairs).
- If Arniel happens to have another weapon equipped before you give him Keening (he has a tendency to pick up stray weapons on occasion), he will attack the warped soul gem with that weapon instead of Keening. The quest will still proceed as normal however, and Keening will still drop when Arniel disappears.
- If you previously killed the boss of the radiant dungeon that contains the Staff of Tandil, the quest marker may direct you to the empty space where the boss's body lay.
  - May happen if dungeon has non-respawning boss, e.g., [Ansilvund](/wiki/Skyrim:Ansilvund "Skyrim:Ansilvund")
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")Type `setstage MGRArniel02 30` and `player.additem 0006a093 1` in the console to acquire the staff and continue the quest.

  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.8, fixes this bug.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage quest stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest quest`.
