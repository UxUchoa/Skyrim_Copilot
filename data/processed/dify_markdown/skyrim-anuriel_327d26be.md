Anuriel

**Anuriel** is a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") and the [steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Steward "Skyrim:Steward") to [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") [Laila Law-Giver](/wiki/Skyrim:Laila_Law-Giver "Skyrim:Laila Law-Giver") of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). She is usually found at the Jarl's side in [Mistveil Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Mistveil_Keep "Skyrim:Mistveil Keep") and will sleep from midnight to 8am in her bedroom just beyond the doorway to the inner part of the Keep. She is the girlfriend of [Unmid Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmid_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Unmid Snow-Shod"), and you can overhear a conversation between the two about sneaking out for a "training" session. Should the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") take control of Riften and have Jarl Laila [replaced](/wiki/Skyrim:Maven_Black-Briar "Skyrim:Maven Black-Briar"), Anuriel will be exiled to the [Palace of the Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings").

Once the Jarl grants you permission, Anuriel can sell you a house called [Honeyside](/wiki/Skyrim:Honeyside "Skyrim:Honeyside") in the city for 8,000 gold. In order to get permission you must complete the quest [Supply and Demand](/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand "Skyrim:Supply and Demand").

If you side with the Imperial Legion in the [civil war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"), you will be sent by [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") to find material you could use to attempt to blackmail her to obtain information that could assist in the Imperial takeover of [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"). In the process, it is discovered that Anuriel is actually a [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") infiltrator put in place by Maven Black-Briar to manipulate Jarl Laila into defending Maven's interests and keep her blind to the activity of the Thieves Guild.

Anuriel wears a set of [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000cee80 "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") and a pair of [fine boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#000cee82 "Skyrim:Fine Boots"). She is equipped with a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") which can be up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") quality, and carries a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

Her greetings:

:   *"Welcome to Mistveil Keep. I'm Jarl Laila's steward, Anuriel."*
:   *"As Riften's Steward I'm afraid I can provide only limited assistance, but speak your mind."*
:   *"If you're here to report any instances of corruption, be confident that we're handling these rumors accordingly."*

Greetings when exiled:

:   *"I'm afraid my days as the steward of Riften has come to an end."*
:   *"The Imperials now control Riften and have placed Maven Black-Briar on the Jarl's throne."*
:   *"Perhaps the Stormcloaks might rally to take back Riften one day. Only time will tell."*

When spoken to:

:   **What do you do around here?**
:   *"As steward, I serve as an intermediary between the Jarl and her subjects. I also handle the less important and day-to-day policies that effect daily life in Riften. Lately I've had my hands full suppressing these ridiculous accusations of corruption in the keep."*

    :   **What are the rumors of corruption you mentioned?**
    :   *"There are those that believe the Jarl does nothing for this city... that it's corrupt and run by the Black-Briar family. I can assure you that this is entirely false. We are fully in control of everything."*

:   **Tell me about the Thieves Guild.**
:   *"I'd hardly call them a Guild. More like unorganized rabble. In fact, I wouldn't say they were a threat at all. No, our resources should be spent on preparations for invasion."*

Her goodbye:

:   *"You're welcome in Riften as long as you continue to obey our laws."*

### Compelling Tribute

:   *"Not here. Come with me. Stay close and the guards will leave you alone."*

She will lead you back into her room:

:   *"I suppose you're here to extort something from me? Is that it? Well, what is it you want?"*

:   :   **You rebels are doing a fine job destroying yourselves. Soldiers running errands?**
    :   *"So. You're an Imperial spy... Interesting... I have no strong love for the Stormcloaks but I can't say I'm all that fond of the Empire, either. This war of yours makes it difficult for a resourceful woman to advance her interests. Perhaps we can find a way for both of us to prosper from this unfortunate discovery?"*

:   :   **What if this letter were made public?**
    :   *"It would ruin me. I have a good thing going here. The Jarl benefits from my more creative arrangements as well, though it'd be impossible for her to admit that. Laila is a simple and traditional woman. Which makes it easy to manipulate her, but impossible to regain her graces once offended. Look. Perhaps we could come to some kind of agreement?"*

Either option leads to:

:   **What kind of agreement did you have in mind?**
:   *"What if I told you about a large shipment of gold and weapons?"*
:   **Go on, I'm listening.**
:   *"Oh, no. I'm no fool. You'll learn what you need to know once we've come to an agreement."*
:   **How much are we talking about?**
:   *"Enough to make a significant difference in the war."*
:   **And what about something for me, right now? (Persuade)**
:   *"Well I... I suppose I'm not much left a choice in the matter, am I? Very well. I trust a hefty purse of septims will suffice?"*
:   **All right, it's a deal. Where can I find this shipment?**
:   *"They're taking it by wagon to Windhelm. If you hurry, you'll catch them before they get far. It'll be a fairly slow moving caravan. The shipment is quite heavy, and guarded by many men. Now, let's pretend we never had this discussion."*

## Conversations

Unmid may discuss the jarl's safety with Anuriel:

:   **Unmid**: *"Anuriel, would you please stop declaring that the city is safe for the Jarl to roam? You and I both know that isn't true."*
:   **Anuriel**: *"I would never knowingly place Laila in danger, you know that too. Why do you always question my judgment? You don't trust me."*
:   **Unmid**: *"I trust you, I'm just concerned about her safety. That's all. Don't be angry with me."*
:   **Anuriel**: *"I'm not angry with you. Tell you what, lover. Meet me in my chambers later and we can discuss the situation in private."*

Anuriel can also be seen talking with Unmid about a "combat training" session. However, she will sound unusually seductive:

:   **Anuriel**: *"Unmid, my sweet. Are we still meeting later for... combat training?"*
:   **Unmid**: *"Of course. Your training isn't complete yet."*
:   **Anuriel**: *"I look forward to it then."*

Laila can regularly be seen discussing court matters with her steward Anuriel:

:   **Laila**: *"Anuriel, a word please."*
:   **Anuriel**: *"Yes, my lady?"*
:   **Laila**: *"It's been brought to my attention that the poison known as [skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma") may be present in our city. What do you know of this?"*
:   **Anuriel**: *"I believe it to be a falsehood perpetrated by the Empire in order to weaken the citizen's*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *confidence in your ability to rule."*
:   **Laila**: *"Excellent, then they'll*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *be no need to devote any resources to stopping it. Thank you, Anuriel. That will be all."*

:   **Anuriel**: *"My lady, Maven Black-Briar has sent word that she requires your seal in order to expand her shipments into [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind")."*
:   **Laila**: *"But of course! Tell her she'll have it. Maven is one of the few people I can trust and I'd be delighted to give her my blessing."*
:   **Anuriel**: *"Yes, my lady. I will pass the good news on to her."*
