Endrast

**Endrast**, a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") [sorcerer](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatSorcerer "Skyrim:CombatSorcerer"), was a local explorer of some renown who accompanied [Sulla Trebatius](/wiki/Skyrim:Sulla_Trebatius "Skyrim:Sulla Trebatius") on an expedition to the [Alftand](/wiki/Skyrim:Alftand "Skyrim:Alftand") Dwemer ruins.

After they were captured by [Falmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer "Skyrim:Falmer"), he managed to pick the lock on their cell so they could escape. Despite initially being saved by [Yag gra-Gortwog](/wiki/Skyrim:Yag_gra-Gortwog "Skyrim:Yag gra-Gortwog"), he succumbed to his wounds beneath Dwarven steam pipes in the animonculory not long after writing his final thoughts in his [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Endrast%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Endrast's Journal"), found nearby his corpse along with a piece of [charcoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal "Skyrim:Charcoal"), and a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick").

Endrast wears [ragged robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes "Skyrim:Ragged Robes") with no shoes.

## Notes

- Endrast was likely created with [Enthir](/wiki/Skyrim:Enthir "Skyrim:Enthir") as a base, as he still retains some of the latter's AI packages and factions. This also results in a number of oddities, as seen below.
  - At some point, the Unofficial Patch also removed the AI packages from his package list, although this isn't noted in the changelog.

## Bugs

- He's part of the College of Winterhold faction, despite never being found there. This inclusion could lead to his death triggering the [Rejoining the College](/wiki/Skyrim:Rejoining_the_College "Skyrim:Rejoining the College") quest should he be [reanimated](/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate_(spells) "Skyrim:Reanimate (spells)") and re-killed.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=21011))
- Due to the fact that he's part of the [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)") crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill him, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
