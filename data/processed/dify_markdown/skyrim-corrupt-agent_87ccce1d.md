Corrupt Agent

The **Corrupt Agent** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") who may appear in [Penitus Oculatus Outpost](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Outpost") in [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever"). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Mother "Skyrim:Night Mother") picks him as the contract giver.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task.

The Corrupt Agent wears a set of [Penitus Oculatus armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Armor "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Armor") with a matching [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Helmet "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Helmet"), pair of [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Bracers "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Bracers"), and pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Boots "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Boots"). He wields an [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword") and carries a copy of *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother "Skyrim:A Kiss, Sweet Mother")*.

He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever#Detailed_Walkthrough "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever") for details.
