The Druadach Mountains

The **Druadach Mountains** are a mountain range in western [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") serving as a natural barrier to separate [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") from [High Rock](/wiki/Lore:High_Rock "Lore:High Rock") and [Hammerfell](/wiki/Lore:Hammerfell "Lore:Hammerfell").

The range curves from the northeast around [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") to [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold"). The misty peaks tower over [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") and [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten") in [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"), though they gradually diminish closer to [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar") and [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). The [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River") originates in the range and drops down many waterfalls as it flows to the northeast following the range to the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts"). Since the [First Era](/wiki/Lore:First_Era "Lore:First Era"), the [Reachmen](/wiki/Lore:Reachmen "Lore:Reachmen"), now known as the [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"), have been taking advantage of the cliffs, caves, and rugged terrain of the Druadach Mountains in guerrilla warfare campaigns against invading armies. The range is home to many ancient [Dwemer ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwemer Ruins"), [Nord fortresses](/wiki/Skyrim:Forts "Skyrim:Forts"), and [Orc strongholds](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds").
