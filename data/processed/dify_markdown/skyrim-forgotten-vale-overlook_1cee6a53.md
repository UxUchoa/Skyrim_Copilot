Forgotten Vale Overlook

**Forgotten Vale Overlook** is a small river valley in the [Forgotten Vale](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale "Skyrim:Forgotten Vale"), which can be accessed by using the [paragon platform](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale#Paragon_Platform "Skyrim:Forgotten Vale") with the [emerald paragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald_Paragon "Skyrim:Emerald Paragon"). It is the location of *[Unknown Book, Vol. II](/wiki/Skyrim:Unknown_Book,_Vol._II "Skyrim:Unknown Book, Vol. II")*.

### Exterior

The exterior to this river valley is the [Forgotten Vale](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale "Skyrim:Forgotten Vale"). It can be accessed by using the [paragon platform](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale#Paragon_Platform "Skyrim:Forgotten Vale") with the [emerald paragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald_Paragon "Skyrim:Emerald Paragon"), or by using the [Whirlwind Sprint](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Sprint "Skyrim:Whirlwind Sprint") [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") when standing on one of the icebergs outside [Temple Balcony](/wiki/Skyrim:Inner_Sanctum_(Vale)#Auriel.27s_Chapel "Skyrim:Inner Sanctum (Vale)").

### Forgotten Vale Overlook

The paragon portal teleports you to the eastern bank of a turbulent river in the isolated river valley above the waterfall at the southern end of the frozen lake. The broken stone arch forming the portal allows you to return directly to the paragon platform. There are no enemies here. The river is fairly wide, with rapids among the many rocks in the water, and both of the riverbanks are cluttered with broken masonry and fallen trees. Head downstream by proceeding north along the eastern bank. Just to the right of where the waterfall cascades into the lake below is a lootable [skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") lying next to an unlocked stone [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), with an enchanted [elven greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Greatsword "Skyrim:Elven Greatsword"), a quiver of [elven arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Arrow "Skyrim:Elven Arrow"), a random enchanted [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow"), and *[Unknown Book, Vol. II](/wiki/Skyrim:Unknown_Book,_Vol._II "Skyrim:Unknown Book, Vol. II")* on the ground in front of the chest. Looking up towards the northwest, you'll see the balcony of the [Inner Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Inner_Sanctum_(Vale) "Skyrim:Inner Sanctum (Vale)") on the cliffs above.
