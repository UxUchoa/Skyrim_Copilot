# Skyrim:Bandit Thief

## Overview

In the original version of the [Divine Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Divine_Crusader "Skyrim:Divine Crusader") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"), two **Bandit Thieves** could be found at [Four Skull Lookout](/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Skull_Lookout "Skyrim:Four Skull Lookout"). They had stolen the Crusader's Relics and were killed during the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Relics_of_the_Crusader "Skyrim:Relics of the Crusader"). However with the release of [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition"), the creation was reworked so that the relics are found on two dead NPCs, [Eigorn](/wiki/Skyrim:Eigorn "Skyrim:Eigorn") and [Oren](/wiki/Skyrim:Oren "Skyrim:Oren"), as it did not fit the lore of the Crusader's Relics for bandits to be able to wear them.

## Related Quests

- **[Relics of the Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Relics_of_the_Crusader "Skyrim:Relics of the Crusader")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Divine_Crusader "Skyrim:Divine Crusader"): Reclaim the holy relics of Pelinal Whitestrake.
