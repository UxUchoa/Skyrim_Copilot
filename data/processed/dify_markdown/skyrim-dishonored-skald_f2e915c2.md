Dishonored Skald

The **Dishonored Skald** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") who may appear in the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever"). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Mother "Skyrim:Night Mother") picks him as the contract giver.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task.

He wears a set of [iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor"), including the [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots"). He also wields an [iron warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer "Skyrim:Iron Warhammer") and carries a copy of [*A Kiss, Sweet Mother*](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother "Skyrim:A Kiss, Sweet Mother").

He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever#Detailed_Walkthrough "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever") for details.
