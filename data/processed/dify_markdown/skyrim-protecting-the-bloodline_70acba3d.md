Protecting the Bloodline

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Fura Bloodmouth](/wiki/Skyrim:Fura_Bloodmouth "Skyrim:Fura Bloodmouth") or [Garan Marethi](/wiki/Skyrim:Garan_Marethi "Skyrim:Garan Marethi").
2. Travel to radiant location: a [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") camp or [warlock](/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock "Skyrim:Warlock") lair.
3. Kill the feral-blooded [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") before it turns its allies into vampires.
4. Return to the quest giver.

## Detailed Walkthrough

You will be asked to kill a vampire before it turns its allies. [Fura Bloodmouth](/wiki/Skyrim:Fura_Bloodmouth "Skyrim:Fura Bloodmouth") will say:
*"A feral-blooded has holed up with a group of bandits/necromancers. Likely wants to create a nest of broodlings."*
"We don't need competition for blood, and we don't want the mortals getting up in arms making things difficult for us."
"Go destroy the feral beast before this becomes a problem."

When hunting the vampire, it will be at a random location, either a [bandit camp](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Bandit_Camps "Category:Skyrim-Places-Bandit Camps") or [warlock lair](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock_Lairs "Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock Lairs"). This can include locations added by [official add-ons](/wiki/Skyrim:Add-ons "Skyrim:Add-ons") such as [Hrodulf's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Hrodulf%27s_House "Skyrim:Hrodulf's House") in [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim").
