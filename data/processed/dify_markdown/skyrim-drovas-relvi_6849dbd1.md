Drovas Relvi

**Drovas Relvi** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") who works as a cook at [the Retching Netch](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Retching_Netch "Skyrim:The Retching Netch") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock"). He works for [Geldis Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Geldis_Sadri "Skyrim:Geldis Sadri"). He relocates to [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(settlement) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (settlement)") after completing the quest [Reluctant Steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Reluctant_Steward "Skyrim:Reluctant Steward").

When he moves to Tel Mithryn, he will live at Tel Mithryn Steward's House, and he'll have the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_Key "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Steward's Key") to the house added to his inventory, along with starting an entirely new daily routine. He will start his day in his house at 6am, and stay there for two hours. Then at 8am, he'll head over to the [apothecary](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Apothecary") to visit [Elynea](/wiki/Skyrim:Elynea_Mothren "Skyrim:Elynea Mothren") for an hour. He will then head to the [kitchen](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Kitchen") to visit [Ulves](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulves_Romoran "Skyrim:Ulves Romoran") for an hour. He will then head up to the main [tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)") to check on Neloth for an hour. He will go outside the tower to get some air for an hour. After that, at noon, he'll head back to his house to eat a one-hour lunch. When he's finished, at 1pm, he'll go outside for an hour. Then at 2pm, he'll visit Elynea again for an hour. After that, at 3pm, he'll once again visit Ulves for an hour. Then he'll go outside, once again, for an hour. He'll then, at 5pm, once again check on Neloth for an hour. Then at 6pm, he'll go home and eat dinner for an hour. At 7pm, when he's done with dinner, he'll give Elynea one last hour-long visit, then head home. Then at 10pm he'll head to work on the [Sun Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Stone "Skyrim:Sun Stone") for eight hours, or, if the stone has been [cleansed](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones"), he'll sleep in his house indefinitely (see [bugs](#Bugs)). The only exception to his routine is if you still haven't completed [From the Ashes](/wiki/Skyrim:From_the_Ashes "Skyrim:From the Ashes"); then he will stay in his house until the quest is completed.

Drovas wears farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0006c1da "Skyrim:Clothes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots"). He carries a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") and a small amount of gold.

### Raven Rock Conversations

In the Retching Netch, he can be seen conversing with his boss, Geldis:

**Geldis**: *"Drovas, I thought I told you I needed the jazbay crushed for my Emberbrand Wine. Where is it?"*
**Drovas**: *"We ran out of jazbay berries almost two weeks ago."*
**Geldis**: *"Damn it. I wish you would have told me sooner."*
**Drovas**: *"Sorry, boss."*

**Geldis**: *"Drovas! I need you to clean the rooms again. I went through there, and there's ash everywhere."*
**Drovas**: *"Sorry, boss. The ash comes in faster than I can clean it up."*
**Geldis**: *"Well, then you'll just have to clean them twice a day. Whatever it takes."*
**Drovas**: *"Yes, boss."*

### Reluctant Steward

When [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth") finds out his [old steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Varona_Nelas "Skyrim:Varona Nelas") is dead, he sends you to Raven Rock to find a new steward. When asked if he wants the job

:   **Do you want to become the new steward of Tel Mithryn?**
:   *"Uh... you mean leave Raven Rock? For good? To go work for a crazed Telvanni wizard? Sure. Just let me get my things. On second thought, nevermind. I can get new stuff."*
:   **You seem awfully eager.**
:   *"Well, I've never been one to pass up an opportunity. Sorry to run, but I'm off to my new job."*

He will then leave the Retching Netch and Raven Rock for good, running all the way to Tel Mithryn to start his new job. If you try to converse with him when he's making the trip to Tel Mithryn, he will only say:

:   *"I'm off to take up my new position with Master Neloth."*

### A New Debt

After setting Drovas up as steward, when you head back to Raven Rock, [Mogrul](/wiki/Skyrim:Mogrul_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Mogrul (Dragonborn)") will approach you saying Drovas owed him one-thousand septims, and that since you were the one to make him untouchable, the debt is now yours. You can now ask Drovas if he owed Mogrul money:

:   **Do you owe Mogrul money?**
:   *"Uh...Well, sort of. I owed him a thousand septims, but not any more. He doesn't dare try to collect now that I'm Neloth's steward."*
:   **Except now he expects me to pay him.**
:   *"Wow. Sorry about that. He's never going to stop you know. He'll keep sending thugs after you until either you pay him or kill him."*
:   **Why don't you just pay him?**
:   *"Pay him? Why? I mean, I don't have the money. Well, I might soon. Neloth does pay well. But the important thing is that I don't have it. In fact, it's not likely I'll ever have it. Expenses you know. Sorry about the thugs and Mogrul and everything."*

If you exit the conversation now and once again ask him if he owes Mogrul money, he'll say:

:   *"I already told you, Mogrul can't touch me now. Whatever is between you and Mogrul is your problem."*

You'll now only have one option to persuade. Note that you need a [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill of 75 to successfully persuade him:

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **You should pay me what you can. (Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"Well... I... Oh, fine. Here. This is all I have."* **Failed**: *"I don't see why. He's your problem now."* | **Passed**: Get 250 gold **Failed**: Nothing |

During the quest, when exiting conversation he can say:

:   *"Mogrul isn't someone you can ignore."*

After the quest, if you paid off his debt:

:   *"I heard you paid my debt to Mogrul. I don't know how I can every*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *repay you."*

If you killed Mogrul:

:   *"I heard Mogrul is dead. That is a relief!"*

## Bugs

- If he has become a steward and the [Sun Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Stone "Skyrim:Sun Stone") has been cleansed, his sleep package will always be active, and therefore he will be found sleeping indefinitely in [Tel Mithryn Steward's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Steward's House").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- If he becomes a steward and the Sun Stone has been cleansed, his sleep package may still have him trying to sleep at the Retching Netch, even though he doesn't work there anymore.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.3, fixes this bug.
