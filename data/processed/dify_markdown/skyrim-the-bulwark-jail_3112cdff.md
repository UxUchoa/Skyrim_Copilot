The Bulwark Jail

**The Bulwark Jail** is the jail in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock"), built directly into [the Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark "Skyrim:The Bulwark").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SR-interior-The_Bulwark_Jail.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Bulwark_Jail.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bulwark_Jail.jpg)

Escape tunnel leading to Coldcinder Cave

For historical information on the Bulwark, read *[History of Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock "Skyrim:History of Raven Rock")*.

### Exterior

The Bulwark stands at the east end of Raven Rock, with its exterior nearly buried in ash. A short timbered tunnel burrows through the ash to the city's main gate, with the unlocked entrances to the garrison ("the Bulwark") and the jail facing each other just inside the gate. Two [Redoran guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran_Guard "Skyrim:Redoran Guard") are posted outside these two entrances.

### The Bulwark Jail

The entrance leads into a narrow room running from west to east, with the expert-locked cell door dividing the space in two. Outside the cell, along the north wall are two unusable barrels, the prisoner belongings chest sitting on a grate showing an area below, two crates, a chair and a small table holding a bottle of [shein](/wiki/Skyrim:Shein "Skyrim:Shein"), a bottle of [sujamma](/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma "Skyrim:Sujamma"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a [cooked beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Beef "Skyrim:Cooked Beef") steak, a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), and three food barrels next to the cell door. A single inattentive Redoran guard acting as jailor usually sits at the table.

Inside the cell, along the north wall is a set of shelves holding a bottle of [matze](/wiki/Skyrim:Matze "Skyrim:Matze"), a bottle of sujamma, an [ash yam](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam"), a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), and copies of *[Bone, Part I](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone,_Part_I "Skyrim:Bone, Part I")* and *[Bone, Part II](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone,_Part_II "Skyrim:Bone, Part II")*, followed by a large unlit fireplace with five pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") covering a novice-locked grate to an escape tunnel. Along the south wall is an open box, a chair, an owned single bed, and another chair and a small round table holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two ash yams, a loaf of bread, and a [charred skeever meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Meat "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Meat"). The tunnel below the fireplace leads to the grate under the prisoner belongings chest, as well as another grate in the floor connected to a natural tunnel with a door to [Coldcinder Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Coldcinder_Cave "Skyrim:Coldcinder Cave").
