Fort Greymoor

**Fort Greymoor** is a medium-sized [fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Military_Forts "Skyrim:Military Forts") west of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") initially occupied by [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). It contains two zones: *[Fort Greymoor](#Fort_Greymoor)* and *[Fort Greymoor Prison](#Fort_Greymoor_Prison)*.

The fort will be taken over by whoever wins the [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"), or by the faction currently holding Whiterun if you first clear it of bandits (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

## Related Quests

- **[Kill Agnis](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Agnis "Skyrim:Kill Agnis")**: Kill [Agnis](/wiki/Skyrim:Agnis "Skyrim:Agnis") in Fort Greymoor.

### Exterior

The fort is crumbling and part of the missing sections of wall have been replaced by log palisades. The main entrance to the courtyard is past two rows of barricades arranged in half circles around the arch forming the entrance from the southeast. To the left of the entrance when viewed from outside of the fort is a small cage hanging from a beam; it contains a dead [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). You can loot this bandit either by standing under and jumping or by dropping from above. There are two bandits on guard on the wall over the entrance with three more spread around the walls and courtyard.

There are practice dummies set up to imitate guards on the walls around the outer wall of the fort.

There are two stables near where the eastern tower used to stand and between the first of these and the wall is an unlocked chest. To the north of the stables is a wooden hut with a blacksmith's [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") and [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench") inside and a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") on the side next to a wagon wheel. In front of the forge is a log table with a pile of firewood underneath. Opposite the arched entrance is a short flight of stairs up to a raised area with a set of wooden double doors leading to the interior of the fort. There is a fire to the left and a couple of barrels in the corner farther to the left. To the right is a boarded over well. To the left of the northern tower is a double flight of wooden stairs up to another wall connecting this tower with another to the west. There is a doorway leading inside where there is a fire with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") next to it and a crate with two loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") on top. There are barrels, crates and a sack in this area as well as an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") to the right of the second doorway. The second doorway leads to the top of the main part of the fort.

On your right is a flight of stairs up to the top of another tower. A bandit is on guard here and a small round table and chair. On the table is a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") and a couple of coins that may have fallen to the floor. A dummy is holding a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow") and quiver of [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow") that can taken.

Continuing along the top of the main part of the fort, there is a trapdoor entrance behind some rubble by a large wooden structure leading up to a lookout platform. There is a second flight of stairs back down to a barrel containing ingredients with a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") lying on the ground. Heading southeast along the wall, there are stairs down and up, past another dummy guard to the tower in the southern corner of the fort. There is another trapdoor here that leads into the prison. This completes the circuit of the outer wall of the fort.

Entering through the main double doors (**E** on map) will bring you straight to a crossroads with rooms to the southwest, northwest and northeast. To the southwest is a dining room with four long wooden tables holding three bottles of Nord mead, three [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish") steaks, a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"). There is a large fireplace to the north of the room with a copy of *[Argonian Account, Book 1](/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian_Account,_Book_1 "Skyrim:Argonian Account, Book 1")* on the floor and an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet") and [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") on the mantle over the fire. To the southeast is a short corridor leading to an unlocked wooden door to *[Fort Greymoor Prison](#Fort_Greymoor_Prison)* (**F** on map); there is an [oil slick](/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Slicks "Skyrim:Oil Slicks") on the floor here. To the left of the door are some small cages, and a couple of barrels are to the right. To the south of the dining room is an opening to a wide spiraling stairway leading up to the right. On your left is an [iron warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer "Skyrim:Iron Warhammer") on the floor, with an iron helmet nearby. At the top of the stairway there is a circular room above the dining room. A bandit wanders here, and three practice dummies are set up around the room. To the northeast is a ladder to a trapdoor in the ceiling leading outside (**G** on map), and directly opposite against the wall is an unlocked boss chest (**B** on map). To the southeast is an open door leading to a bedroom with a [corpse](/wiki/Skyrim:Corpse "Skyrim:Corpse") of an Imperial soldier, and between the door and ladder is a lectern holding a copy of the [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Rislav The Righteous](/wiki/Skyrim:Rislav_The_Righteous "Skyrim:Rislav The Righteous")* (**C** on map).

Inside the bedroom are an empty sideboard and table, an unowned double bed, and a bookcase containing a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), a random pair of boots, and copies of *[Darkest Darkness](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkest_Darkness "Skyrim:Darkest Darkness")*, *[Brief History of the Empire, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:Brief_History_of_the_Empire,_v2 "Skyrim:Brief History of the Empire, v2")*, *[The Wolf Queen, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wolf_Queen,_v3 "Skyrim:The Wolf Queen, v3")*, *[A Dance in Fire, v4](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v4 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v4")*, *[The Song of Pelinal, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v2 "Skyrim:The Song of Pelinal, v2")*, and *[The Hope of the Redoran](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Hope_of_the_Redoran "Skyrim:The Hope of the Redoran")*.

The short corridor to the northwest from the main entrance has twelve weapon racks, with six on each side holding two iron swords and two [Imperial swords](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword"). This leads to a room dominated by a large cage containing two burnt corpses; a magic-using bandit is looking at a [skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") on a table to your left as you enter. Under the table is a novice-locked chest. Behind the cage is a pair of [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots"), and on a table against the wall to the northwest are a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a random [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"), a random [regeneration potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"), a small coin purse, four loose coins, and a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick").

Through a doorway to the west are what appears to be the latrines, with three stalls with loose stone walls between each. On one of these walls is an [Imperial light helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Helmet "Skyrim:Imperial Light Helmet"). There is a wide flight of stairs spiraling down opposite. At the bottom through a doorway is a [tripwire](/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire "Skyrim:Tripwire") that activates a [swinging mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Swinging_Mace "Skyrim:Swinging Mace") trap. In the room behind are a dead [skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever") and a short corridor heading to the northwest, which leads to a corridor being used as a storage area with two more dead skeevers and three [bear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") scattered on the ground. On your left are two sets of shelves containing seven sacks, a pair of leather boots, a [potion of the defender](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Defender "Skyrim:Potion of the Defender"), a [potion of the warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Warrior "Skyrim:Potion of the Warrior"), and a [potion of strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Strength "Skyrim:Potion of Strength"), with two more sacks between the two sets of shelves. Straight ahead is a partially flooded corridor leading to a sewer pipe and exit that leads outside (**D** on map) to the northwest of the fort.

On your right is a platform with barrels and an adept-locked chest underneath. There are four more sets of shelves on top of the platform containing a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two [wolf pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf_Pelt "Skyrim:Wolf Pelt"), three sacks, a small coin purse, two [woodcutter's axes](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), ten bottles of Nord mead, a pair of leather boots, and a lockpick. There are four more bottles of Nord mead on the ground beside them. To the left of the platform is a doorway to wide spiraling stairs leading up, and to the left of this is a firewood pile with a woodcutter's axe on a wooden block.

There is a bandit patrolling these stairs and a [steel warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Warhammer "Skyrim:Steel Warhammer") found partway up. At the top are several straw bales and a doorway into the kitchen area. The corridor to the northeast of the main entrance leads to the same kitchen area, with a bandit guard and [Agnis](/wiki/Skyrim:Agnis "Skyrim:Agnis") (**A** on map) if you are here for the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Agnis "Skyrim:Kill Agnis"). On your left as you enter this room is a large fireplace with a spit hanging in front of it with three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") in a pot on the spit. To the right is a door to a storage room with three sets of shelves containing a random [illusion potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Illusion "Skyrim:Potion of Illusion"), a random [healer potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Healer "Skyrim:Potion of the Healer"), a random [enhanced stamina potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Enhanced Stamina"), and a potion of minor healing. In the center of the kitchen are two long wooden tables laid end to end with a whole [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and four pieces of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). To the southeast is a door leading to a bedroom with a single bed, a dresser with a copy of *[A Dance in Fire, v1](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v1 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v1")*, and a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") on a wooden plate on top. To the right is a table with a practice dummy and iron dagger. Against the southeast wall is a bookcase containing a random strength potion and copies of *[Brief History of the Empire, v1](/wiki/Skyrim:Brief_History_of_the_Empire,_v1 "Skyrim:Brief History of the Empire, v1")*, *[Beggar Prince](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar_Prince "Skyrim:Beggar Prince")*, *[The Song of Pelinal, v6](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v6 "Skyrim:The Song of Pelinal, v6")*, *[The Monomyth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Monomyth "Skyrim:The Monomyth")*, and *The Wolf Queen, v3*.

### Fort Greymoor Prison

[![Fort Greymoor Prison](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-map-FortGreymoor02.jpg/300px-SR-map-FortGreymoor02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-map-FortGreymoor02.jpg "Fort Greymoor Prison")

Fort Greymoor Prison

You enter into a circular room with a bear trap right by the door (**B** on map). There are several small cages and one larger apprentice-locked cage. Inside the cage are a dead [hunter](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter") and a [charred skeever hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") on a wooden plate. There is a long wooden table in the center of the room with a bottle of wine, an iron dagger, and a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") in a wooden bowl on the table. To the northwest is a door to *[Fort Greymoor](#Fort_Greymoor)* (**A** on map) with a pair of random boots beside it. On the west side of the room is a ladder to the trapdoor in the ceiling that leads to the top of the tower (**C** on map). At the rear of the room is an opening leading to a wide spiraling stone stairway with a bandit patrolling up and down. To the right of the opening is a small round wooden table with a medium coin purse, two loose coins, and an iron dagger on top.

You can hear a bandit talking to themselves at the bottom of the stairs. Through a doorway there are a set of shelves full of worthless clothing and a doorway to the northeast to the cells. There are six cells all containing bedrolls, three on each side of a central corridor. On the left, the first is unlocked with a dead hunter inside, and the second and third are empty. On the right, the first is empty, the second is novice-locked and has a dead [High Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Elf "Skyrim:High Elf") inside, and the third is master-locked, but contains nothing. The corridor then turns to the east, where there is a bandit with their back to you inspecting a dead [troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll "Skyrim:Troll") on a table. Be careful to avoid the [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes") hanging from the ceiling. On the table with the troll are an iron dagger, two lockpicks, and a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"). Under the table is a bear trap, and to the left is an unlocked chest. In the northern corner are two small empty cages hanging from the ceiling. In the middle of the northeast wall at head height is a level-locked chest on the wall containing a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and leveled poison.

## Notes

- After clearing the fort of bandits, the exterior chests will be disabled when you leave the area.
- If you have cleared the fort of bandits but have not completed the quest [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun") for the Imperials or Stormcloaks, only the external bandits will respawn. The interior will respawn, but not the bandits.
- If you have cleared the fort of bandits and have completed the quest Battle for Whiterun, the Imperial or Stormcloak soldiers will not be hostile toward you.
- Many internal names and an unused key indicate the fort was originally known as **Fort Blackmoor**, which appears in *[Arena](/wiki/Arena:Arena "Arena:Arena")* as the village [Black Moor](/wiki/Arena:Black_Moor "Arena:Black Moor").

## Bugs

- If this is not the first [Civil War Fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Military_Forts#Civil_War_Forts "Skyrim:Military Forts") you clear, after clearing it of [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"), soldiers will never garrison and enemies respawn instead. This is due to a hidden quest not ending after clearing the first Civil War Fort. See this [talk](/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Fort_Hraggstad "Skyrim talk:Fort Hraggstad") section for info.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")The console command `resetquest CWFortObserver` can be used to reset the hidden quest, then clear the fort again. Repeat this for every Civil War Fort you want to be garrisoned.
