Autumnshade Clearing

**Autumnshade Clearing** is a circular glade inhabited by a [spriggan](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan") and its attending [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals").

It is located north of [Goldenglow Estate](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenglow_Estate "Skyrim:Goldenglow Estate") and southwest of [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone").

## Autumnshade Clearing

There are up to three leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") supporting the leveled [spriggan](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan") in the clearing. Around the area are several plants and fungi that can be harvested, including several patches of [blue mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"), [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella"), [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown"), [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota") and [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"). There are also a few skeletal remains around the clearing. A shallow cave at the west end of the clearing holds the bodies of two [hunters](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter"), one of which contains a copy of the [archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Father Of The Niben](/wiki/Skyrim:Father_Of_The_Niben "Skyrim:Father Of The Niben")*.

[Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") guards will sometimes refer to "trouble" at the clearing, speculate that it's due to spriggans, and add the location to your map.

## Notes

- The [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") skill book *[Racial Phylogeny](/wiki/Skyrim:Racial_Phylogeny "Skyrim:Racial Phylogeny")* can be found to the north of the clearing in a small [unmarked location](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places#Unmarked_Ruins_and_Monuments "Skyrim:Unmarked Places").
- The [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") skill book *[The Gold Ribbon of Merit](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gold_Ribbon_of_Merit "Skyrim:The Gold Ribbon of Merit")* can be found in a hunter's tent southeast of the clearing.
- There are several [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") around the camp to the north, east-northeast, east-southeast, south, and west-northwest.
- A total of 18 [canis roots](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root") can be found in the area.
- Autumnshade Clearing also [appears](/wiki/Online:Autumnshade_Clearing "Online:Autumnshade Clearing") in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online").
