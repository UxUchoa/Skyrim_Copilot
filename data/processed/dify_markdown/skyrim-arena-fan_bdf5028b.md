The corpse of the Arena Fan

The Arena Fan is a deceased [Bosmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") found at a camp southeast of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"), southwest of [Geirmund's Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Geirmund%27s_Hall "Skyrim:Geirmund's Hall"), and northeast of [Honeystrand Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Honeystrand_Cave "Skyrim:Honeystrand Cave"). A [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Arena_Fan%27s_Note "Skyrim:Arena Fan's Note") is found on his body that details how he journeyed to Skyrim in search of a former Bloodletter of the [Imperial City](/wiki/Lore:Imperial_City "Lore:Imperial City") Arena, [Urgnok](/wiki/Skyrim:Urgnok "Skyrim:Urgnok").

He is garbed in simple [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0006C1D8 "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"). In addition to his aforementioned note, he also carries a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v8](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v8 "Skyrim:The Crimson Dirks, v8")*.

## Bugs

If the corpse despawns, it must be the case that the player unknowingly draws near enough to spawn the Arena Fan's corpse, then leaves away unknowingly too.
