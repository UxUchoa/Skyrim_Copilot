Tel Mithryn Kitchen

**Tel Mithryn Kitchen** is the home of the cook [Ulves Romoran](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulves_Romoran "Skyrim:Ulves Romoran") in [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(settlement) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (settlement)").

It is one of the four mushroom structures in the settlement, northwest of [Tel Mithryn Steward's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Steward's House").

## Tel Mithryn Kitchen

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.jpg)

Tel Mithryn Kitchen interior

The entrance leads into a small circular room comprising the entire interior. To the east is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand over a fire, four samples of [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), an empty pot, and a cooking spit over a fire. To the south is a table holding another empty pot, six more samples of canis root, a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a piece of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"), a piece of [horse meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Meat "Skyrim:Horse Meat"), and a piece of [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"). To the west are six crates, an open box holding three heads of cabbage, a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and an owned bedroll.
