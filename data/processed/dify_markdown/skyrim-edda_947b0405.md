Edda

**Edda** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [beggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggars "Skyrim:Beggars") from [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") who, during the day, can be found near the market stalls, asking for septims. She sleeps in [Beggar's Row](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar%27s_Row "Skyrim:Beggar's Row") between midnight and 7am.

Edda wears a beggar outfit consisting of [ragged robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes "Skyrim:Ragged Robes") and [ragged boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Boots "Skyrim:Ragged Boots"). She wields an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") in combat and also carries some [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").
