Largashbur

**Largashbur** is an accursed [Orc stronghold](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") beset by [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") in the southeast of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), west-southwest of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften").

The stronghold contains two zones: *[Largashbur Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur_Longhouse "Skyrim:Largashbur Longhouse")* and *[Largashbur Cellar](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur_Cellar "Skyrim:Largashbur Cellar")*.

The stronghold features a makeshift altar to [Malacath](/wiki/Skyrim:Malacath "Skyrim:Malacath"), adorned with [Orcish Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Armor "Skyrim:Orcish Armor"), [Orcish Boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Boots "Skyrim:Orcish Boots"), an [Orcish Helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Helmet "Skyrim:Orcish Helmet"), an [Orcish Shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Shield "Skyrim:Orcish Shield"), an [Orcish Battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Orcish Battleaxe"), and seven bunches of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). Largashbur is the only one of the four Orc strongholds not to have a mine, and indeed has lent two of its working-age Orcs to [Narzulbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Narzulbur "Skyrim:Narzulbur"), since Narzulbur has a mine but a shortage of workers.

As with all Orc Strongholds, you must become [Blood-Kin](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood-Kin "Skyrim:Blood-Kin") to the [Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") before you are welcome in the stronghold and able to barter or receive other services.

#### Daedric

- **[The Cursed Tribe](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe "Skyrim:The Cursed Tribe")**: Help lift the curse placed on the [Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") of Largashbur by [Malacath](/wiki/Lore:Malacath "Lore:Malacath").

### Exterior

Two [goats](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") and a [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse") are found in the exterior area.

### Buildings

- [Largashbur Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur_Longhouse "Skyrim:Largashbur Longhouse") - Primary dwelling for all residents.
- [Largashbur Cellar](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur_Cellar "Skyrim:Largashbur Cellar") - Storage cellar and makeshift bar, with a few food barrels and sacks and a level-locked chest just outside the trapdoor entrance behind the longhouse.
- Apothecary's Shack - Exterior building with an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"), and a few ingredients on a set of shelves and hanging from a pole.
- Blacksmith's Shack - Exterior building with a [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge"), a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a few pieces of Orcish equipment on a set of shelves, a table, and a barrel.
- Tanner's Shed - Exterior building with a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), a food barrel, and a few pelts on a table.

## Notes

- [Lob](/wiki/Skyrim:Lob "Skyrim:Lob"), [Ogol](/wiki/Skyrim:Ogol "Skyrim:Ogol"), [Ugor](/wiki/Skyrim:Ugor "Skyrim:Ugor"), and the unnamed Orcs outside the stronghold are not essential, and can be easily killed by invading giants, even before you arrive.
- After completing [The Cursed Tribe](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe "Skyrim:The Cursed Tribe"), Lob, Ogol, and Ugor can be recruited as followers.

## Bugs

- Triggering combat here while the quest The Cursed Tribe is active can block quest progression.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- The giant will continue to respawn and attack the stronghold after the quest The Cursed Tribe has been completed, as the disable flag is not implemented. This can eventually result in the death of everyone within other than Atub.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The giant's attacks can be stopped by opening the console and entering the code `"000A0E72".disable`.
- Atub and Garakh were apparently intended to be merchants (apothecary and blacksmith respectively), but do not function as merchants themselves.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. They were assigned the wrong settings. Once fixed, they will be affected by [Master Trader](/wiki/Skyrim:Master_Trader "Skyrim:Master Trader"), but not [Investor](/wiki/Skyrim:Investor "Skyrim:Investor").
