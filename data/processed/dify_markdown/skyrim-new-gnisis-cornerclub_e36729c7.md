New Gnisis Cornerclub

**New Gnisis Cornerclub** is a [tavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Inns "Skyrim:Inns") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). It is located in the Gray Quarter, next to [Sadri's Used Wares](/wiki/Skyrim:Sadri%27s_Used_Wares "Skyrim:Sadri's Used Wares").

The tavern is run by [Ambarys Rendar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ambarys_Rendar "Skyrim:Ambarys Rendar") and his employee, [Malthyr Elenil](/wiki/Skyrim:Malthyr_Elenil "Skyrim:Malthyr Elenil"). Ambarys tends the counter while Malthyr cleans the taproom (he was supposed to take over the club if Ambarys dies, but doesn't, due to a [bug](#Bugs)). It consists of one area only, *[New Gnisis Cornerclub](#New_Gnisis_Cornerclub)*.

**NOTE**: Like all other stores, New Gnisis Cornerclub has a number of fixed wares on display scattered around the main trading room. These can be bought from the proprietors and will disappear from the room when purchased. For a list of these special items, see [this section](#Displayed_Merchandise).

## New Gnisis Cornerclub

The ground floor consists of the taproom, which is quite messy and worn-out. The front door opens to the sight of the bar, which seats four and has a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), a wedge of [eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), and a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") on display.

Hidden behind the bar is some [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"). The shelves contain a bottle of Nord mead, two [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). The only table for customers contains a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), and a bottle of Nord mead. To the north is the staircase leading to the second floor and, hidden underneath the stairs, sits the [Sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak") skill book *[The Red Kitchen Reader](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Kitchen_Reader "Skyrim:The Red Kitchen Reader")*.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-interior-New_Gnisis_Cornerclub_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-New_Gnisis_Cornerclub_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-New_Gnisis_Cornerclub_02.jpg)

Second Floor

The second floor contains a number of items related to the [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") Legion. Banners hang from the ceiling and an [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword") with matching [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Shield "Skyrim:Imperial Shield"), [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Armor "Skyrim:Imperial Armor"), [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bracers "Skyrim:Imperial Bracers"), [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Helmet "Skyrim:Imperial Helmet"), and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Boots "Skyrim:Imperial Boots") can be found along the southern wall. The single table holds a loaf of bread, a boiled cream treat, and a bottle of alto wine.

The dresser holds lower-class [clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and has a salt pile and the [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") skill book *[A Game at Dinner](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Game_at_Dinner "Skyrim:A Game at Dinner")* on top. The wardrobe contains more clothes and the barrel contains food. The shelves hold a bottle of alto wine, a copy of *[The Firsthold Revolt](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firsthold_Revolt "Skyrim:The Firsthold Revolt")* and the tavern's [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"). Hidden underneath the stairs to the third floor is a rare bottle of [double-distilled skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Double-Distilled_Skooma "Skyrim:Double-Distilled Skooma"), related to the quest [Some Light Theft](/wiki/Skyrim:Some_Light_Theft "Skyrim:Some Light Theft").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/SR-interior-New_Gnisis_Cornerclub_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-New_Gnisis_Cornerclub_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-New_Gnisis_Cornerclub_03.jpg)

Top floor

The top floor contains the owned bed where Malthyr Elenil sleeps between midnight and 8am. There is also an empty [pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Pot "Skyrim:Pot") and two large food sacks scattered on the floor. The table along the western wall contains the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"), a copy of *[The Arcturian Heresy](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcturian_Heresy "Skyrim:The Arcturian Heresy")*, and a slice of bread. The two sets of shelves in the northwestern corner only contain a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), a bottle of Nord mead, and some [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). A pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes "Skyrim:Shoes") is on the floor behind the bed, at the bottom of a dresser with clothes, which has a set of fixed [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing") on top, along with twelve gold coins. At the foot of the table in the northeastern corner are three food sacks while the table itself holds a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and another food sack. Lastly is a table with a [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Healing") on top.

## Notes

- The New Gnisis Cornerclub is likely a reference to [Gnisis](/wiki/Morrowind:Gnisis "Morrowind:Gnisis") in *[Morrowind](/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind "Morrowind:Morrowind")*.
- This is the only inn where you cannot rent a room.

## Bugs

- Malthyr is supposed to take over running the New Gnisis Cornerclub if Ambarys dies. Although he stands behind the bar as if he is running it, he does not offer any [services](/wiki/Skyrim:Services "Skyrim:Services").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.2, fixes this bug.
- 3 of the cornerclub's regular customers ([Adelaisa Vendicci](/wiki/Skyrim:Adelaisa_Vendicci "Skyrim:Adelaisa Vendicci"), [Arivanya](/wiki/Skyrim:Arivanya "Skyrim:Arivanya"), and [Niranye](/wiki/Skyrim:Niranye "Skyrim:Niranye")) cannot use the furniture here due to the ownership of the bar stools and chairs being set to the WindhelmCornerclubPatrons faction which none of them are in, resulting in them standing around doing nothing when they're here.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
- Ambrys sometimes welcomes you in by telling you that they have beds. This doesn't make sense given that there are no beds available for rent here.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.4, addresses this issue. A condition check has been added to prevent that dialogue scene from playing in this particular location.
