Greenspring Hollow

**Greenspring Hollow** is a shallow outdoor cave west-northwest of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") containing a leveled [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals").

## Related Quests

- **[Animal Extermination](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Extermination_(B) "Skyrim:Animal Extermination (B)")**: Fulfill a contract given to the [Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions") to clear out a beast den. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Shalidor's Insights](/wiki/Skyrim:Shalidor%27s_Insights "Skyrim:Shalidor's Insights")**: Discover the writings of Shalidor and benefit from his wisdom. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

## Walkthrough

As you approach the outcropping, a single predator will likely attack you. Once it is dealt with, you can safely search the creature's den. It appears a foolish [hunter](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter") decided to camp here and paid with his life. His body lies on the blood-splattered ground next to a fallen cooking spit. Some lootable skeletal remains are farther back next to two unusable bedrolls and a level-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). A copy of the *[Argonian Account, Book 2](/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian_Account,_Book_2 "Skyrim:Argonian Account, Book 2")* rests on a boulder beside the chest, with a random [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow") next to it.

The hunter's lean-to on the right side of the outcropping shelters a total of ten [pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Hides#Unprocessed "Skyrim:Animal Hides") of various animals. An [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), and a [bear trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") are near the lean-to. On the left side of the outcropping is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning "Skyrim:Tanning"). A [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat"), a piece of [horse meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Meat "Skyrim:Horse Meat"), and some [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison") hang from the ceiling on a rope. Two clusters of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), an [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), and a [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot") grow around the camp.

Follow the nearby stream south to find a [Nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") and a [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") near the river. You may encounter a few more predators in the area as well.

## Notes

- Only the level-locked chest, skeletal remains, and apothecary's satchel respawn. All other items, including the hunter's body, only appear on your first visit. Therefore, any radiant quest that requires recovering an item from this location can be completed even after it has been cleared.

## Bugs

- The animal does not respawn, which makes it impossible to complete the quest [Animal Extermination](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Extermination_(B) "Skyrim:Animal Extermination (B)") if [Aela](/wiki/Skyrim:Aela "Skyrim:Aela") asks you to clear this location after it had been previously cleared.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
