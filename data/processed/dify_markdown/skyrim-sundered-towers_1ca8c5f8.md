Sundered Towers

**Sundered Towers** is a small pair of linked ruined [Nordic towers](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Towers "Skyrim:Nordic Towers") southeast of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten") containing [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") and [hagravens](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven "Skyrim:Hagraven").

## Walkthrough

The towers are at the top of a series of stone staircases and winding paths interspersed with landings occupied by the Forsworn camped at [Red Eagle Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle_Redoubt "Skyrim:Red Eagle Redoubt"). The entrance to the towers is a level-locked iron door which can be unlocked with the [Red Eagle Tower key](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle_Tower_Key "Skyrim:Red Eagle Tower Key") carried by the Forsworn Briarheart of the redoubt. Inside is a copy of *[The Legend of Red Eagle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle "Skyrim:The Legend of Red Eagle")* and a random pair of [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets "Skyrim:Gauntlets") on a sideboard opposite. A flight of stairs to the right ascends to a doorway outside to a wooden ramp up to the next level, where a raised wooden bridge is on the right and a doorway is to the left. Just inside on the right is a chain that lowers the bridge. At the back of the room behind a flight of wooden stairs is an [urn](/wiki/Skyrim:Urn "Skyrim:Urn"), while the stairs lead up to another level with more stairs up to the highest level; both of these levels are empty.

Once you have lowered the wooden bridge, you may cross to a second tower to the east. On the far side is a doorway ahead and a wooden ramp down to the left. Through the door, the room contains a novice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") and a flight of wooden stairs leading up, with another flight above these leading to the top; again, these levels are both empty. Descending via the ramp brings you to a doorway with a broken sideboard opposite holding a random [potion of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka") and a random empty [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"). There are also stairs leading down on the right as you enter. At the bottom is a pile of rubble with a random [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") among it, with an iron door opposite secured by a bar on this side preventing entrance from the other side.

## Notes

- This location cannot be independently [cleared](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeons#Clearing "Skyrim:Dungeons"). It will only show up as such when the boss-level creatures in Red Eagle Redoubt have been killed, as the game considers these zones one unit for the purpose of calculating clears.
  - Sundered Towers itself will never add to your count of cleared locations, and therefore does not contribute to the [Delver](/wiki/Skyrim:Delver "Skyrim:Delver") achievement.
- Although the door bar prevents easy access to Sundered Towers from the plateau, this can be circumvented by climbing a rock ramp opposite the third tree south of the barred door, then jumping up more rocks to reach the tower with the bridge release chain. This approach allows [Red Eagle Ascent](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle_Redoubt#Red_Eagle_Ascent "Skyrim:Red Eagle Redoubt") to be traversed downward instead of upward.

## Bugs

- The key might not be on the corpse of the Briarheart.
- The book may clip through the pillar on the altar, making it irretrievable. **?**
  - Cast an explosive area-of-effect spell such as [Fireball](/wiki/Skyrim:Fireball "Skyrim:Fireball"), and the book should reveal itself, allowing you to grab it.
- Killing and looting the Briarheart may fail to complete the corresponding quest objective for [Repairing the Phial](/wiki/Skyrim:Repairing_the_Phial "Skyrim:Repairing the Phial"). **?**
  - Kill and loot the Briarheart before talking to [Quintus Navale](/wiki/Skyrim:Quintus_Navale "Skyrim:Quintus Navale"), which should mark the quest objective as complete. Then, return to Quintus and proceed with the quest.
