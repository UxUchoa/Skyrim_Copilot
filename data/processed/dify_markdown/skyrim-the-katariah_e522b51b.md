The Katariah

**The Katariah** is the personal transport vessel of the Emperor, [Titus Mede II](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real) "Skyrim:Emperor Titus Mede II (real)").

The ship has an exterior (the deck) and two interior zones: *[The Katariah](#The_Katariah)* and the *[Emperor's Quarters](#Emperor.27s_Quarters)*.

It only appears after the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") quest [Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!") has been started, anchored off-shore in the mouth of the [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River") near [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude").

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-place-The_Katariah_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Katariah_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Katariah_02.jpg)

Windshear

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-place-The_Katariah_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Katariah_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Katariah_03.jpg)

The figurehead of Katariah

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-place-The_Katariah_04.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Katariah_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Katariah_04.jpg)

Throne area

The main exterior area for the ship is the deck area which can only be accessed from the interior. The initial access point to the ship is via the anchor chain at the stern of the ship leading through the hull into a storeroom in the lower hold. Access to the deck can be made through two exits, one from a ladder near the captain's quarters and one through a door opposite the emperor's quarters. The former of these opens a trapdoor under the forecastle on the main deck and gives the best concealment upon exiting, while the latter leads out onto the main deck beside the stairs leading up to the quarterdeck.

Under the forecastle beside the trapdoor is a long wooden table with a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish") steak and several pieces of cheese on top. The decks have many stacks of crates, barrels and sacks containing provisions. There is a [sailor](/wiki/Skyrim:Sailor "Skyrim:Sailor") and five [Penitus Oculatus agents](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Agent "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Agent") on deck, the sailor and one agent are on the main deck, one agent is on the forecastle, two are on the quarterdeck and one is on the poop deck. There are stairs leading up to the forecastle both to the port and starboard of the ship. On the forecastle, on the starboard side is a rowboat provisioned and containing an open crate containing a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a random [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), four bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead")—these are all owned and taking them is considered stealing. At the end of the bowsprit is a unique [scimitar](/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar "Skyrim:Scimitar") called [Windshear](/wiki/Skyrim:Windshear "Skyrim:Windshear"). (See the bugs section on that page if it's not there.) The figurehead on the front of the ship appears to be that of an elven maiden.

The main deck has a novice-locked chest beside some crates, two long wooden tables and benches and two rowboats, one provisioned and one empty. The quarter deck has another rowboat that is provisioned and also holds an owned [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel "Skyrim:Satchel"). Beside this rowboat is a table and chairs with a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine and a loaf of bread on top. The poop deck is where the ship's wheel is located and has a throne for the Emperor with a small table beside it, with a copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[King](/wiki/Skyrim:King "Skyrim:King")*, a bottle of wine and three [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") coins on top.

### The Katariah

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-interior-The_Katariah_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Katariah_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Katariah_03.jpg)

Storeroom

Entrance to the Katariah is made via the anchor chain that takes you into a large storeroom containing many crates, barrels containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"), food sacks and an apprentice-locked chest. Upon entering there is a sailor just leaving the room. Among the crates on the floor, on the port side is a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and to the starboard, is a random [potion of light feet](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Light_Feet "Skyrim:Potion of Light Feet") hidden behind a lit lantern, on a barrel. Any noise can alert several sailors and a Penitus Oculatus agent. There are crates, sacks and barrels containing provision throughout the interior. Stairs to the south lead into a wide corridor with a bedroom on the right containing a sailor, three single beds, a table and chairs, a wooden bench and a small bookcase holding five common books and a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"). On the table is another common book, a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), a small coin purse and four gold coins. Leaning against the south wall, next to the wooden bench is an [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword"). All the beds onboard are unowned. You can hear someone ahead working metal on an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil").

To the east is another open door to a bedroom containing three more single beds, a small table and chair, a wooden bench and a dresser, which has an [Imperial helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Helmet "Skyrim:Imperial Helmet") on top and an Imperial sword on the floor in front of it. The corridor is staggered to the south passing a table and a couple of chairs. Ahead is yet another open door, with a set of shelves before it on the right holding seven common books, a bottle of alto wine and two bottles of wine. As you approach the end of the corridor there is an opening on the right into a dining room containing two sailors and an agent, the room is double height with a balcony around the upper area. Any noise when dealing with these sailors and agent can bring several agents and sailors to investigate. The room at the end of the corridor is another bedroom which contains five single beds, two dressers, a wardrobe, an end table, a table built around a support pillar and a couple of chairs. To the left of the first dresser is a pair of [Imperial boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Boots "Skyrim:Imperial Boots"), with some cheese on the second. The wardrobe and dressers all contain common [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), while on the end table is a [gold ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ring "Skyrim:Gold Ring") and a [silver ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ring "Skyrim:Silver Ring") and on one of the beds in the southeastern corner is a random [battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe "Skyrim:Battleaxe").

The dining room contains many tables and to the north is a counter with a sideboard behind, these hold an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a total of six bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), many pieces of cheese, several loaves of bread, a gourd, four portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), four [honey nut treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat") and a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"). On the west wall are two shield plaques each holding an [Imperial shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Shield "Skyrim:Imperial Shield") and two crossed Imperial swords. To the south is a doorway into another storeroom with a pair of bookcases flanking it. These hold a total of sixteen common books and a random potion of healing. Inside the room are two sideboards and a cupboard. On the sideboards are many more pieces of cheese, a bulb of garlic, a portion of grilled leeks, an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat"), a fillet of [horker meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat "Skyrim:Horker Meat"), a copy of *[Uncommon Taste](/wiki/Skyrim:Uncommon_Taste "Skyrim:Uncommon Taste")* and a couple of loaves of bread. On the floor to the right of the cupboard is a medium coin purse.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-interior-The_Katariah_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Katariah_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Katariah_02.jpg)

The cells

In the dining room, stairs leading up to the balcony overhead are in the northwest corner. There is an expert-locked door near the top of the stairs, with a dresser and chair to the right and to the south is the balcony seen from below with two agents sat on the far side of it facing away from you. Along the west wall are a row of three unlocked cells each containing an unowned bedroll and little else of interest. There is an unlocked door on the north wall that leads into a bedroom containing a sailor as well as a dead sailor and agent. There are four single beds, an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"), an end table, a long wooden table and several chairs. Behind the lab, on a crate is a random [potion of invisibility](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Brief_Invisibility "Skyrim:Potion of Brief Invisibility"), while on the long table is an apothecary's satchel, a random filled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), a portion of grilled leeks, a sprig of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), a loaf of bread, some cheese and leaning against a chair against the west wall is a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute").

Another unlocked door is in the middle of the east wall, the sounds of someone hammering on an anvil appear to be coming from this room. In the room is an agent either using the anvil or a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench") with a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") beside these. On the workbench is a random [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword") and on the wall are a total of eight weapon racks holding four Imperial swords and a random [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword"). At the northern end of the room is a sideboard with a set of [Imperial armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Armor "Skyrim:Imperial Armor"), a pair of [Imperial bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bracers "Skyrim:Imperial Bracers") and an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") on top, a pair of [Imperial light boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Boots "Skyrim:Imperial Light Boots") on the floor beside it. To the right are shelves holding an [Imperial helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Helmet "Skyrim:Imperial Helmet") and a quiver of [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"), with an [Imperial shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Shield "Skyrim:Imperial Shield") and a random [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow") on a couple of crates.

On the southern side of the balcony are two tables, where the two agents were sitting, a corridor with cupboards flanking it, the one to the southeast holds a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes") and a bunch of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). The short corridor to the south leads into another storage area with a ladder leading up to the deck of the ship directly in front of you and a door to the right. Around against the north wall is a table with a satchel on top, there is nothing else of interest apart from a few more barrels of ingredients in this area. The door to the right is unlocked and opens into [Captain Avidius](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Avidius "Skyrim:Captain Avidius")' room.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-interior-The_Katariah.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Katariah.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Katariah.jpg)

Captain Avidius' room

The room contains a double bed in the southwest corner, with a small table beside it and a dresser at the foot of the bed with a pair of random gauntlets and a random [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration") on top, with a lute leaning against the side of it. In front of the dresser, against the south wall is a master-locked chest, while against the north wall is a long wooden table with flute, a small coin purse and three gold coins on top and to the right is a set of shelves holding a random [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), a random potion of healing, a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") and a flute. Further to the right of these is a wardrobe containing random clothing and a wooden bench. Finally beside the door as you leave are two hooks with a copy of [Katariah master key](/wiki/Skyrim:Katariah_Master_Key "Skyrim:Katariah Master Key"), which opens any and all doors on the ship including the door to the Emperor's quarters. Copies of this key are also carried by Captain Avidius, [Lieutenant Salvarus](/wiki/Skyrim:Lieutenant_Salvarus "Skyrim:Lieutenant Salvarus") and [Emperor Titus Mede II](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real) "Skyrim:Emperor Titus Mede II (real)") himself.

Beyond the expert-locked door is another corridor with a bedroom on the immediate left containing an agent sat in a chair, three single beds, a bookcase holding three common books, a drum and a row of mountain flowers including five [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), five [purple](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") and one [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), a wardrobe and in a small area to the northeast is a set of shelves holding a medium coin purse with a random [warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer "Skyrim:Warhammer") in front.

Opposite this room is an area with a wooden bench, a dresser and chair with an open door beside them. This leads into another bedroom containing two more agents, one of which is normally sleeping. There are four more single beds, an end table, a wardrobe, a dresser, a cupboard holding an iron mace, and a table and chairs in the room. The corridor continues to the north, where there is a door and two corridors heading east and west. Both of these lead around to stairs leading up to another room above. The door opens into a library, Lieutenant Salvarus is standing right behind the door and will spot and attack as soon as you enter. In the middle of the room is a table covered with a tactical map of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), an iron dagger and a copy of *[Mixed Unit Tactics](/wiki/Skyrim:Mixed_Unit_Tactics "Skyrim:Mixed Unit Tactics")*. There are a total of six bookcases along the rear wall containing many common books, a random [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet"), a random empty soul gem, a satchel and an iron dagger, with a pair of [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") on the floor to the right of them.

The corridor to the west has a small table with a bottle of alto wine on top beside a wooden bench. The room at the top has two doors, one master-locked leading to the Emperor's quarters and the other leading outside. There are long end tables on either side of the Emperor's door, one of which has a couple of [candlesticks](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlestick "Skyrim:Candlestick") and a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") on top, while the other has a common book and [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") on top. To the right of the door leading outside is a long wooden table with an empty random soul gem, three common books and an apothecary's satchel on top, with two bunches of dried elves ear and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging from the ceiling above. To the right is a cupboard and a set of shelves holding a [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") and three more common books and to the left of the door outside is another cupboard this one holds four bottles of alto wine, five bottles of wine, a flute and a drum.

### Emperor's Quarters

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters.jpg)

First room

These consist of two rooms, the first is the Emperor's office and dining area while the second is up a few stairs to the west and are his sleeping quarters. The office is lavishly appointed with several richly woven rugs, many banners hanging from the walls and between the stained glass rear windows and many fancy [plates](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate"), goblets, bowls, etc around the room. A desk, at which the Emperor himself can be found sat during the quest [Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!"), is opposite the door with a tactical map of Skyrim on top along with a bottle of wine, several common books and a copy of *[Brothers of Darkness](/wiki/Skyrim:Brothers_of_Darkness "Skyrim:Brothers of Darkness")*. To the left as you enter the room is a small unit holding two bottles of alto wine, two of wine and two of Nord mead. Next to this is a small table and chair with a common book on the table. Opposite is a small sideboard with a bowl containing [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and in front a low table with a honey nut treat and [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") on top. To the east is a dining table laid for one, with a [cooked beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Beef "Skyrim:Cooked Beef") steak, two portions of grilled leeks, a bowl of [tomato soup](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato_Soup "Skyrim:Tomato Soup"), a seared slaughterfish steak, a baked potato, two loaves of bread, a bulb of garlic, a sprig of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"), and a piece of cheese on top.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters_02.jpg)

Dining area

Around the dining table is a sideboard, a set of shelves holding two bottles of alto wine and one of wine, a narrow table holding two sprigs of snowberries, a bunch of lavender, a red mountain flower and an expert-locked display case containing a [steel mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Steel Mace"), with a weapon plaque hanging on the wall above holding a [steel greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Greatsword "Skyrim:Steel Greatsword"). Next to this is a bookcase with eleven common books and copies of *[The Dragon War](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dragon_War "Skyrim:The Dragon War")*, *[The Adabal-a](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Adabal-a "Skyrim:The Adabal-a")* and *[On Oblivion](/wiki/Skyrim:On_Oblivion "Skyrim:On Oblivion")* on the shelves. Behind the desk along the stern wall are a pair of small wardrobes containing fine clothes, with an adept-locked display case on top containing a random [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe") and a pair of random [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor") on the floor to the left, a sideboard with a copy of *[The Rise and Fall of the Blades](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rise_and_Fall_of_the_Blades "Skyrim:The Rise and Fall of the Blades")* on top and a small unit with several more common books and a bottle of wine on the shelves. Against the west wall to the right of the sleeping quarters is a giant map of Skyrim with a wall shelf above a sideboard with rolls of paper on top holding three common books. An adept-locked door in the port side bulkhead provides access to a narrow outdoor balcony, that makes for a quick getaway after you pay your respects to the Emperor.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Emperor%27s_Quarters_03.jpg)

Sleeping quarters

The door to the sleeping quarters is expert-locked. The room contains a four-poster double bed coming out of the south wall, with an end table with a copy of *[Lost Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends "Skyrim:Lost Legends")* on top to the left and a sideboard at the foot of the bed with a set of [Emperor's robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor%27s_Robes "Skyrim:Emperor's Robes"), a [gold ruby necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ruby_Necklace "Skyrim:Gold Ruby Necklace"), a small coin purse, five gold coins and an open crate containing two gold rings, silver ring and a [flawless ruby](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Ruby "Skyrim:Flawless Ruby") on top. To the left of the bed is a wash basin and sideboard with a pair of [fine boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots "Skyrim:Fine Boots") beside it, while to the right is a tall wardrobe containing fine clothes, and a sideboard with an apothecary's satchel, a leveled [potion of glibness](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Glibness "Skyrim:Potion of Glibness"), a leveled potion of healing, a flute and a copy of *[Glories and Laments](/wiki/Skyrim:Glories_and_Laments "Skyrim:Glories and Laments")* on top. Against the northwest wall is a small wardrobe with a random pair of [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets "Skyrim:Gauntlets") on top and a lute leaning against it, with a long unlocked chest to the right and further to the right of this is a bookcase with a satchel, a unique pair of [gilded wristguards](/wiki/Skyrim:Gilded_Wristguards "Skyrim:Gilded Wristguards"), a random helmet, a couple of candlesticks, six common books and a copy of *[Sovngarde: A Reexamination](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovngarde:_A_Reexamination "Skyrim:Sovngarde: A Reexamination")* on the shelves. Behind the door is a corner shelf with several common books and a random filled soul gem in a holder.

- The Katariah is erroneously flagged as a dungeon; radiant quests choosing it as a target location require the completion of the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") questline to retrieve the target object.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- The quest controlling scenes on board the Katariah don't start properly due to a bad story manager node condition.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.
- One of the Penitus Oculatus agents respawns despite the ship being classed as a "no reset zone".
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
- The ship has no distant LOD, meaning it will pop out of nowhere when you get close.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.4, fixes this bug.
- Some beverages and potions on the ship are mistakenly set as owned.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
- The Katariah may partially despawn as you move away from it. This results in shipboard objects apparently floating in midair, and NPCs falling into the water.
- You may find [Lisette](/wiki/Skyrim:Lisette "Skyrim:Lisette") in the ship's mess hall or dead on the deck after [Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!").
