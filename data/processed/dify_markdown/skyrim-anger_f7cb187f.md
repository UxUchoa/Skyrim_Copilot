Anger

**Anger** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [guard](/wiki/Skyrim:GuardImperial "Skyrim:GuardImperial") who is an aspect of [Pelagius the Mad's](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_the_Mad "Skyrim:Pelagius the Mad") mind. He only appears in [The Mind of a Madman](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_a_Madman "Skyrim:The Mind of a Madman") during the related quest. He will be beating up the much smaller [Confidence](/wiki/Skyrim:Confidence_(NPC) "Skyrim:Confidence (NPC)"), and you will be tasked with evening the odds with the help of the [Wabbajack](/wiki/Skyrim:Wabbajack "Skyrim:Wabbajack").

Anger is equipped with [studded Imperial armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Imperial_Armor "Skyrim:Studded Imperial Armor") along with [Imperial light boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Boots "Skyrim:Imperial Light Boots"), [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Bracers "Skyrim:Imperial Light Bracers"), and a [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Helmet "Skyrim:Imperial Light Helmet").
