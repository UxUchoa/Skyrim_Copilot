A Dawnguard Scout

**Dawnguard Scouts** are [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") of any [race](/wiki/Skyrim:Races "Skyrim:Races") that sometimes appear at the fast travel arrival point just outside the [Castle Volkihar](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Volkihar "Skyrim:Castle Volkihar") [keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Keep "Skyrim:Volkihar Keep"). They arrive in groups of three (one melee and two archers) and will attack immediately if you are allied to the [Volkihar vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Vampire_Clan "Skyrim:Volkihar Vampire Clan") and are a [vampire lord](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Lord "Skyrim:Vampire Lord"). However, they don't appear at all if you are a member of the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)").

Dawnguard scouts each carry a [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_War_Axe "Skyrim:Dawnguard War Axe") and [crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Crossbow "Skyrim:Crossbow"). They may also have a small amount of gold on them. They wear only [Dawnguard armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Specialty_Gear#Dawnguard_Armor "Skyrim:Specialty Gear"): light, heavy, or a mix of the two. Sometimes they do not have the helmet or the gauntlets.

## Notes

- If you attack them before they become hostile, you'll get a bounty in Whiterun, despite Castle Volkihar being in Haafingar. To avoid a bounty, wait until they become hostile, then attack.
- After completing quest [Destroying the Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Destroying_the_Dawnguard "Skyrim:Destroying the Dawnguard") on Vampire side, they disappear permanently from game, after [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)") lost the war against to [Volkihar Vampire Clan](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Vampire_Clan "Skyrim:Volkihar Vampire Clan").
