## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak with Klimmek about his trips to [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar").
2. Receive [Klimmek's Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Klimmek%27s_Supplies "Skyrim:Klimmek's Supplies"), and climb the steps to High Hrothgar.
3. Place supplies in indicated chest.
4. Return to Klimmek for your reward.

### Klimmek's Problem

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-quest-Climb_the_Steps_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Climb_the_Steps_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Climb_the_Steps_02.jpg)

*"I'm just not ready to make the climb to High Hrothgar."*

When you arrive in [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") for the first time, you will find [Klimmek](/wiki/Skyrim:Klimmek "Skyrim:Klimmek") and [Gwilin](/wiki/Skyrim:Gwilin "Skyrim:Gwilin") having a conversation on the bridge:

**Gwilin**: *"On your way up the 7,000 Steps again, Klimmek?"*
**Klimmek**: *"Not today. I'm just not ready to make the climb to High Hrothgar. The path isn't safe."*
**Gwilin**: *"Aren't the Greybeards expecting some supplies?"*
**Klimmek**: *"Honestly, I'm not certain. I've yet to be allowed into the monastery. Perhaps one day."*

Apparently Klimmek makes a trip every few weeks up the 7,000 steps to High Hrothgar to deliver supplies to the [Greybeards](/wiki/Lore:Greybeards "Lore:Greybeards"), though he has never actually seen them in person. Unfortunately, he is now older, and finds the climb more difficult. You can choose to help him, with the promise of a reward. He will give you a bag of supplies and ask you to place them in a chest outside the High Hrothgar monastery.

### Deliver the Goods

If you have already discovered High Hrothgar, you may simply travel there. If not, you must climb the 7,000 steps leading out of Ivarstead and up the mountainside, encountering some [wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf") along the way. Once at the top of the 7,000 steps, you will see the monastery with a chest located in the center of a pair of stairwells that lead up to the building itself. Now just open the chest and place the supplies inside. Klimmek's supplies are found in the *Misc* section of the inventory.

### There and Back Again

Once you have placed the supplies in the chest, return to Klimmek, who will compensate you for your leg work. He will give you a level-dependent amount of gold as a reward.

| Levels | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1–9 | 500 |
| 10–19 | 750 |
| 20–29 | 1,000 |
| 30–39 | 1,250 |
| 40+ | 1,500 |

## Notes

- Klimmek's supplies will remain in the chest indefinitely. They can be taken and returned at any time, however doing so will not grant you any further reward. The supplies are also always persistent; placing them in the inventory of a corpse keeps them from despawning.
- There are only about 700 steps on the way up to High Hrothgar, not 7,000.
- Gwilin can sometimes be killed by nearby bears. In that case, Klimmek will no longer be on the bridge and you will have to track him down in order to start the quest.

## Bugs

- If you drop Klimmek's supplies after completing the quest, they will hover in the air and can be used as a platform of sorts.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
