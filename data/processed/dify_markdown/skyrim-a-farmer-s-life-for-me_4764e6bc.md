Set up a farming operation

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Plant 10 crops. *(Any [plantable food or ingredient](/wiki/Skyrim:Fertile_Soil "Skyrim:Fertile Soil"))*
2. Recruit a [Personal Steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Personal_Steward "Skyrim:Personal Steward") for your plantation.
3. Recruit [farmhands](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmhand "Skyrim:Farmhand") to help you with the tasks. *You can ask your steward to hire them.*
4. Build an [animal pen](/wiki/Skyrim:Farming_Construction "Skyrim:Farming Construction").
5. Talk to your steward about purchasing livestock.
6. Wait a few days before the Goldenhills plantation starts to pay off.
7. Talk to your steward to collect the day's income.
8. Collect the products of the harvest in the pantry.

### Farming

Once you have completed [The Unquiet Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Unquiet_Dead "Skyrim:The Unquiet Dead"), [Goldenhills Plantation](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenhills_Plantation "Skyrim:Goldenhills Plantation") is now yours. You will be given this quest to walk you through the various farming activities needed to get the place up and running.

Your first step will be to plant 10 crops in any of the plots of [Fertile Soil](/wiki/Skyrim:Fertile_Soil "Skyrim:Fertile Soil") around the property. There are plenty of plots on the property, and you can change your selection of crops at a later date if you so choose. Once this is complete, you will be prompted to hire a [personal steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Personal_Steward "Skyrim:Personal Steward"). Many potential [followers](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") will be willing to work as your steward if you bring them to the farm. You can also move your [spouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Spouse "Skyrim:Spouse") to Goldenhills and ask them to take over as steward instead.

### Stewardship

With your choice of steward in place, the next step will be to hire some manual labor for the farm. You can speak to your steward about this and they will hire two generic [farmhands](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmhand "Skyrim:Farmhand"). Alternatively, if you wish to provide your own hires, you can recruit [Angrenor Once-Honored](/wiki/Skyrim:Angrenor_Once-Honored "Skyrim:Angrenor Once-Honored"), [Brenuin](/wiki/Skyrim:Brenuin "Skyrim:Brenuin"), [Embry](/wiki/Skyrim:Embry "Skyrim:Embry"), or [Narfi](/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi "Skyrim:Narfi").

Once you have hired two farmhands, you will be prompted to [construct](/wiki/Skyrim:Farming_Construction "Skyrim:Farming Construction") an animal pen. This will require you to use the [Farmer's Workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer%27s_Workbench "Skyrim:Farmer's Workbench") near the entrance to the farmhouse. There are several construction options available, but for the animal pens all you will need is three [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") and a set of [nails](/wiki/Skyrim:Nails "Skyrim:Nails"). Build it and you will be prompted to purchase some livestock via your steward. They will arrange for the purchase of [cows](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow "Skyrim:Cow"), [goats](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat"), and [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken") for the farm.

### Harvest

With all this in place, you now have a fully operational farm. You will have to wait a few days for the plantation to begin turning a profit. When your first income is available, you will be prompted to collect payment from your steward. Your farm will continue to generate revenue passively, which you can collect from your steward at your leisure.

The final step to complete this quest will be to investigate the Pantry Cupboard inside the farmhouse, down the stairs. Open it and you will receive the following tutorial message:

:   *Crops, milk, and eggs produced by the farm will be automatically stored in this cupboard.*

Within the cupboard you should find the first few samples of auto-harvested crops. This cupboard will continue to be filled passively over time, depending on what you choose to grow on the farm.

Congratulations, you have completed setting up your farm! There are plenty of other upgrades and activities to do at Goldenhills to develop it into a truly profitable enterprise.

## Notes

- If you hire Narfi, you may still have to kill him later for an early Dark Brotherhood writ. It is possible to hire another farmhand to replace him if this occurs.

## Bugs

- You might not get the dialogue options to make any of your followers a steward after planting the crops.
- Even after hiring a steward, the quest marker might be stuck on stage 10.
- It is possible that the quest will not automatically start after completing The Unquiet Dead.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage ccVSVSSE004_RestoreFarmQuest stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest ccVSVSSE004_RestoreFarmQuest`.
