First Mate

The **First Mate**, an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)"), is the second-in-command of a gang of [corsairs](/wiki/Skyrim:Corsair "Skyrim:Corsair") aboard the [Dainty Sload](/wiki/Skyrim:Dainty_Sload "Skyrim:Dainty Sload"), a ship tied up southwest of [Solitude Lighthouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Lighthouse "Skyrim:Solitude Lighthouse"). He spends all of his days aboard ship.

The first mate wears a set of [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#00086991 "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") and a matching pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993 "Skyrim:Fine Boots"), and wields a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") of the best possible quality. He also carries the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Dainty_Sload_Footlocker_Key "Skyrim:Dainty Sload Footlocker Key") to his footlocker and between 20 and 56 gold coins
