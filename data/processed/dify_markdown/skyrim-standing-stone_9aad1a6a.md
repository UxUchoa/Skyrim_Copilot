# Skyrim:Standing Stone

## Overview

[![SR-mapicon-Doomstone.png](//images.uesp.net/2/2d/SR-mapicon-Doomstone.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Doomstone.png)

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-place-Guardian_Stones.jpg/200px-SR-place-Guardian_Stones.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Guardian_Stones.jpg)

The three Guardian Stones

The **Standing Stones** are magical stones found in the wilderness which can give your character special abilities and powers. There are thirteen Standing Stones in total, each associated with one of Tamriel's thirteen [constellations](/wiki/Lore:Astronomy#Constellations "Lore:Astronomy"). Usually, only one stone's effects can be active at a time, although you can change which stone is active at any time by activating a different Standing Stone. However, by wearing the [Aetherial Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherial_Crown "Skyrim:Aetherial Crown")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard"), you may be affected by two Standing Stones concurrently.

The first three stones most players will encounter are the three [Guardian Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Guardian_Stones "Skyrim:The Guardian Stones"): the Mage Stone, the Thief Stone, and the Warrior Stone. These three stones are found together in a circle along the road to [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood") near [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta"). Selecting one of these stones allows you to effectively specialize in one group of [skills](/wiki/Skyrim:Skills "Skyrim:Skills"). All of the other stones are located on their own.

## Achievements

[Achievements](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements") related to the Standing Stones are:

- [![SR-achievement-Blessed.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-achievement-Blessed.png/30px-SR-achievement-Blessed.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Blessed.png) **Blessed** (10 points/Bronze) — Select a Standing Stone blessing
  - You must discover one standing stone, then activate its blessing. This is most easily accomplished using one of the three Guardian Stones.
- [![SR-achievement-Standing Stones.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-achievement-Standing_Stones.png/30px-SR-achievement-Standing_Stones.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Standing_Stones.png) **Standing Stones** (30 points/Silver) — Find 13 Standing Stones
  - You must discover all thirteen of the standing stones (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

## Notes

- The Standing Stones are comparable to the [Birthsign Stones](/wiki/Oblivion:Doomstones#Birthsign_Stones "Oblivion:Doomstones") from [Oblivion](/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion "Oblivion:Oblivion").
- If you are at or near one of the Standing Stones, and bring up the local map, the map title may refer to it as a "Doomstone". Nothing else in the game seems to use this term for these objects, though.
- [Followers](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") won't pray at a Standing Stone if you order them to do so.
- Reading *[An Explorer's Guide to Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:An_Explorer%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim "Skyrim:An Explorer's Guide to Skyrim")* will mark the locations of the following Stones on your map: the Guardian Stones, the Ritual Stone, the Shadow Stone, and the Lover Stone.
- Although the loading screen tip states the Apprentice Stone doubles both magicka regeneration and magicka, only magicka regeneration is doubled.
- [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") is boosted by the Thief Stone. Whether this is intentional or not is unclear:
  - Archery is displayed clearly in the Warrior section of the skill menu, and as each menu section contains a consistent six skills, the Thief Stone's governance of Archery breaks that consistency, perhaps suggesting that this is unintentional.
  - Because Archery has been a stealth skill in previous Elder Scrolls games, its playstyle differs from the Warrior's typical melee tactics and synergizes with [Sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak"), and the Thief would have no offensive skills if not for Archery, some players believe that Archery was intentionally placed under the Thief Stone.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, moves Archery under the Warrior Stone.

## Bugs

- [The Atronach Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Atronach_Stone "Skyrim:The Atronach Stone") has a 50% chance of causing all [Summoning](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration_Spells "Skyrim:Conjuration Spells") spells and powers to fail. See [Spell Absorption](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Absorption#Bugs "Skyrim:Spell Absorption") for details.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- Reading *An Explorer's Guide to Skyrim* after the Guardian, Ritual, Lover, and Shadow Stones are marked on the map will cause a "Map updated." message to appear on the top left of the screen again, though no new Stones will be marked. **?**
- It is possible to receive the "Standing Stones" achievement by visiting any of the standing stones a total of 13 times rather than finding all 13 (or visiting the Guardian Stones 5 times). This may result in being stated to have visited more than 13 stones, as repeat visits are not taken into account. You must wait for the location to respawn for repeat visits to increment the counter.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
- There is no loading screen for the Tower Stone.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
