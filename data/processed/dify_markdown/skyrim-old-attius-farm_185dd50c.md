Old Attius Farm

**Old Attius Farm** is an abandoned Imperial [farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Farms "Skyrim:Farms") south-southeast of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

The first time you approach the farm, [Captain Veleth](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Veleth "Skyrim:Captain Veleth") will be battling three [ash spawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Spawn "Skyrim:Ash Spawn"). Once the ash spawn are dead, you will receive a miscellaneous quest objective to talk to the captain. Speaking with him initiates the quest [March of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead "Skyrim:March of the Dead"). Outside the house is a dead [Redoran guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran_Guard_(dead) "Skyrim:Redoran Guard (dead)") that carries an [Elven sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Sword "Skyrim:Elven Sword"), [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Bow "Skyrim:Elven Bow"), and arrows, and a full set of [Bonemold armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold#Bonemold_Armor "Skyrim:Bonemold") (the chest armor is the [pauldron](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold#Bonemold_Pauldron_Armor "Skyrim:Bonemold") version).

The [Ghosts of the Tribunal](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") adds a trapdoor to the house leading to **[Old Attius Farm Cellar](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Attius_Farm_Cellar "Skyrim:Old Attius Farm Cellar")**. You will need to enter the cellar during the quest [Buyer Beware](/wiki/Skyrim:Buyer_Beware "Skyrim:Buyer Beware").

## Walkthrough

The farm is along the road leading south from Raven Rock. There is little left of the farmhouse, but the plants in the two gardens still grow among the weeds. There is an empty chest on the porch, and a [trama root](/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root "Skyrim:Trama Root") grows through the back wall. In the left-hand garden are ten [ash yams](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam") (including one growing outside the fence), three [scathecraws](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw"), and a trama root. In the right-hand garden are eight ash yams, two scathecraws, and a trama root. An additional seven trama roots grow in the immediate vicinity, with a large number of scathecraw plants between the farm and Raven Rock.

## Notes

- Directly west of the farm is an [unmarked location on a small island](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Solstheim#Reavers.27_Folly "Skyrim:Unmarked Places Solstheim"). Three [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Grass Pod") plants and a [heart stone deposit](/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone_Deposit "Skyrim:Heart Stone Deposit") are found on the island. In the middle is a small camp with two bloodied bedrolls, a [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"), a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), a box holding two bowls of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal") and a pair of [bonemold gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Bonemold Gauntlets"), a food barrel, and two dead [reavers](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver "Skyrim:Reaver"). There is a boat on the southern side of the island, with a sack inside and a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") on a crate nearby. Beware, as three [ash hoppers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Hopper "Skyrim:Ash Hopper") will emerge from the ground as you explore the camp.
- According to Captain Veleth, the farm was destroyed by the eruption of [Red Mountain](/wiki/Lore:Red_Mountain "Lore:Red Mountain") almost two hundred years ago, in [4E 5](/wiki/Lore:Fourth_Era#4E_5 "Lore:Fourth Era").
- The dead Redoran Guard's inventory replenishes itself when the farm resets.
