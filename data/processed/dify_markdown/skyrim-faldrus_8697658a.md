Faldrus

**Faldrus** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [elemental mage](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatMageElemental "Skyrim:CombatMageElemental") who can be [randomly encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Dark_Elf_on_Pilgrimage_to_Azura.27s_Shrine "Skyrim:World Interactions") wandering in the wilderness. He is on a pilgrimage to the [Shrine of Azura](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura "Skyrim:Shrine of Azura") and may point you to it.

When he arrives at Azura's shrine, he will join [Aranea Ienith](/wiki/Skyrim:Aranea_Ienith "Skyrim:Aranea Ienith") in front of the statue and raise his arms in praise. However, he will no longer provide further dialogue options.

## Related Quests

- **[Visit the Shrine of Azura](/wiki/Skyrim:Visit_the_Shrine_of_Azura "Skyrim:Visit the Shrine of Azura")**: Help find and repair [Azura's Star](/wiki/Skyrim:Azura%27s_Star "Skyrim:Azura's Star").

## Dialogue

When near him, he will greet you with:

:   *"I'm on a pilgrimage to the Shrine of Azura, the Goddess of Twilight. If you'll excuse me."*
:   **Where is this shrine?**
:   *"In the mountains to the northeast. I'll mark it on your map."*

## Notes

- Even when he is at the [shrine](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura "Skyrim:Shrine of Azura"), he will give the same dialogue responses as he does when originally met and has no further dialogue.
- Faldrus does not have unique facial data. Instead, he uses the default male Dark Elf face, albeit with different hair.

## Bugs

- The location for the [Shrine of Azura](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura "Skyrim:Shrine of Azura") may not get marked on your map, despite Faldrus saying that he will do so.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
