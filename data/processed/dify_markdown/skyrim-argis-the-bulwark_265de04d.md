Argis the Bulwark

**Argis the Bulwark** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") living and working at [Vlindrel Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Vlindrel_Hall "Skyrim:Vlindrel Hall") in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). Once you have purchased the home and [attained](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_the_Reach "Skyrim:Thane of the Reach") the rank of [thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") of [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") by completing quests issued by the [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl"), Argis is appointed to be your personal [housecarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl"). As such, he is not accessible until Vlindrel Hall has been purchased.

Argis wears a set of [steel armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Armor"), a pair of [steel Nordic gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Nordic Gauntlets"), and a pair of [steel cuffed boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Cuffed Boots"). He is equipped with a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") and a [steel shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield "Skyrim:Steel Shield").

Argis can be asked to accompany you as a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"), a capacity in which he is rather useful. At level 50, Argis has 100 in Block, Heavy Armor, and One-handed, as well as 98 Archery and 72 Two-handed. He is also a potential candidate for [marriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage").
