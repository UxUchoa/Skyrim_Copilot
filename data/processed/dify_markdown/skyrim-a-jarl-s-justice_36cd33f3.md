Visiting Advisor at the White Hall in Dawnstar

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak with [Isran](/wiki/Skyrim:Isran "Skyrim:Isran").
2. Warn the [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of the [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") threat.
3. Find and take [evidence](/wiki/Skyrim:Orders_(DG) "Skyrim:Orders (DG)") to the jarl.
   - *(Alternate)* Discreetly kill the [Visiting Advisor](/wiki/Skyrim:Visiting_Advisor "Skyrim:Visiting Advisor") and return to Isran.
4. Kill the Visiting Advisor.
5. Report back to Isran.

## Detailed Walkthrough

After speaking with [Isran](/wiki/Skyrim:Isran "Skyrim:Isran"), you will be sent to inform a random [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of a [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") threat within their court. The jarl will note the seriousness of your allegation, and ask for evidence. This can be obtained by pickpocketing the [Visiting Advisor](/wiki/Skyrim:Visiting_Advisor "Skyrim:Visiting Advisor"), who can usually be found in the main court room of the jarl's hall, for his [orders](/wiki/Skyrim:Orders_(DG) "Skyrim:Orders (DG)"). Note that if your pickpocket attempt fails he will attack you, often leading to confrontation with nearby NPCs and likely death. This method is particularly effective when you are a [Thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") of the relevant hold, as you will then be able to tell the guards to disregard your crime.

Return to the jarl with the note and they will grant you leave to kill the vampire with no retaliation from the hold's guards. Note that despite nominally being a vampire, the Adviser is immune to the [Sun Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Fire "Skyrim:Sun Fire") spell [*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:A_Jarl%27s_Justice)*]. Alternatively, you can skip the evidence gathering stage and instead discreetly eliminate the Visiting Advisor through either stealth or using a [Frenzy](/wiki/Skyrim:Frenzy_(spell) "Skyrim:Frenzy (spell)") spell while hidden. Once the vampire lies dead, return to Isran.

## Notes

- If the Jarl you were to report to is deposed, including via [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"), this quest will automatically be completed.
- It is impossible for the advisor to be at the court of [Maven Black-Briar](/wiki/Skyrim:Maven_Black-Briar "Skyrim:Maven Black-Briar") or [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak"), due to their unique voice types.
- Unlike other [Dawnguard radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Quests#Dawnguard_Quests "Skyrim:Dawnguard Quests"), this one can only occur every twelve days at best.

## Bugs

- Isran will only assign Dawnstar or Riften as target locations for this quest.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dawnguard Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- The advisor is supposed to stay within a certain distance of the jarl, but in locations like Understone Keep and occasionally others, this can leave the advisor inside a locked room or in other unintuitive locations.
  - Save and reload the game, or exit the building and re-enter; the advisor may show up in a different location.
  - Wait until the jarl goes to bed or wakes up; this will typically cause the advisor to follow or, if locked in a room, to teleport close to the jarl. (This may require you to leave and re-enter or save and reload.)
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Use the following console commands to move directly to the advisor: `sqv DLC1RH07`. Look for the line that starts with Ref 'Vampire' and note the ID in parentheses at the end of that line. Then use `player.moveto ID` and you should be taken directly to the advisor.
- It is not considered a crime to attack the disguised vampire despite being warned by Isran that it would be if you're caught.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.7, fixes this bug.
