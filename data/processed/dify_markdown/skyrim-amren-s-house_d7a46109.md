**Amren's House** is a residence in the Wind District of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), located next to [Uthgerd's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Uthgerd%27s_House "Skyrim:Uthgerd's House") and across from the [House of Clan Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:House_of_Clan_Battle-Born "Skyrim:House of Clan Battle-Born"). It is the home of [Amren](/wiki/Skyrim:Amren "Skyrim:Amren"), his wife [Saffir](/wiki/Skyrim:Saffir "Skyrim:Saffir"), and their daughter [Braith](/wiki/Skyrim:Braith "Skyrim:Braith").

## Amren's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/SR-interior-Armen%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Armen%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Armen%27s_House.jpg)

Saffir enjoying her reading time

Entering leads directly into a large room with a round wooden table and a couple of chairs with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and cheese on top of the table. Beside this is a flight of wooden stairs against the northeast wall leading up to a partially-floored upper floor. On your right is a cupboard, a [fish barrel](/wiki/Skyrim:Barrel "Skyrim:Barrel"), and a food sack. On the cupboard's shelves are three bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"). If you have already completed the [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Bandits)") to recover Amren's [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Amren%27s_Family_Sword "Skyrim:Amren's Family Sword"), this will also be found on a shelf. On top of the cupboard is a bottle of wine and loaf of bread, while on the fish barrel is a bowl containing a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage").

Under the stairs are more food sacks and a barrel, and in the eastern corner of the room is a small table and chairs with a bottle of wine and [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") on top. Above the table are three [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid") and four bunches of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear") hanging from a rack. In the middle of the southeast wall is a large fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand in front and a bottle of wine on the mantelpiece. Coming out from the middle of the southwest wall is a large double bed with an end table to the left holding a copy of *[Nords Arise!](/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_Arise! "Skyrim:Nords Arise!")* To the right of the bed is a dresser containing [fine](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") and common [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a bunch of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton") and a food sack on top. Climbing the stairs leads to [Braith](/wiki/Skyrim:Braith "Skyrim:Braith")'s sleeping area, with a single bed at the end, an end table to the right, and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing leveled items at the foot of the bed. On the end table is a copy of *[The Holds of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Holds_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:The Holds of Skyrim")*.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Amren%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Amren's House") to which is carried by Amren and Saffir.
- There is [![Danger](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/SR-icon-shadowmark-Danger.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Danger.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Danger") Danger [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (Thieves Guild)") to the left of the door near the ground due to Amren's high proficiency using [one-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:One-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:One-Handed Weapons").
  - Due to the plants added in front of the house in the Special Edition, this shadowmark is no longer visible.
- After [returning Amren's family sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits)#Amren.27s_Family_Sword "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Bandits)"), all low level items in the house become free to take, and both beds become usable.
