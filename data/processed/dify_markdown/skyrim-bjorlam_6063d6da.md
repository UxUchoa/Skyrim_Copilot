**Bjorlam** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H"), and the [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") [carriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Carriage "Skyrim:Carriage") driver. He can be found near the [Whiterun Stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables "Skyrim:Whiterun Stables"), and will take you to any of the [hold capitals](/wiki/Skyrim:Cities "Skyrim:Cities") on his carriage, for a fee.

Bjorlam wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes"), a pair of [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1 "Skyrim:Gloves"), and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots"). He is equipped with an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe").

## Dialogue

You can ask him what he knows about Whiterun, to which he'll reply:

:   **What do you know about Whiterun?**
:   *"Well, I know the [Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions") make their home here. Their mead hall, [Jorrvaskr](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorrvaskr "Skyrim:Jorrvaskr"), is the oldest building in the city. Oh, and there's a nasty feud between two families called the [Gray-Manes](/wiki/Skyrim:Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Gray-Mane") and [Battle-Borns](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle-Born "Skyrim:Battle-Born"). You'll want to be careful there. The rest you can find out by asking the townsfolk. I'd start with the barkeep at [the Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare"), or the castle steward."*

Apart from this, he shares his dialogue with other carriage drivers.
