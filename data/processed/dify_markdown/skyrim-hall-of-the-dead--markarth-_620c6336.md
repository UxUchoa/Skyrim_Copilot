The **Hall of the Dead** is a large building in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") where the corpses of the deceased are laid to rest.

There are two entrances to Markarth's Hall of the Dead. The first is found near the entrance to the [Hag's Cure](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Hag%27s_Cure "Skyrim:The Hag's Cure") and the [Markarth Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth_Ruins "Skyrim:Markarth Ruins"). The second is found inside [Understone Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep "Skyrim:Understone Keep"), near [Calcelmo](/wiki/Skyrim:Calcelmo "Skyrim:Calcelmo"). The Hall is home to [Brother Verulus](/wiki/Skyrim:Brother_Verulus "Skyrim:Brother Verulus"), and is the starting point for the quest [The Taste of Death](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Taste_of_Death "Skyrim:The Taste of Death"). It consists of one zone, *[Hall of the Dead](#Hall_of_the_Dead)*.

## Hall of the Dead

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).jpg)

Hall of the Dead interior

There is very little within the hall. To the west of the shrine is a food barrel, two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two [eidar cheese wedges](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), and a sample of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"). To the east of the shrine is the skill book *[2920, Rain's Hand, v4](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Rain%27s_Hand,_v4 "Skyrim:2920, Rain's Hand, v4")* above an owned bed.

## Notes

- You are required to [pick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking") either adept-locked door to enter without the key. The key is given to you by Brother Verulus during the related quest.
- If the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") capture Markarth (whether Markarth is taken by force or given to the Stormcloaks during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending")), [Ondolemar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ondolemar "Skyrim:Ondolemar") will perish, and his remains will be found here.
- There is a secret room in the Hall. It can be seen on the map to the left of the altar of [Arkay](/wiki/Lore:Arkay "Lore:Arkay"), if you stick to the wall.

## Bugs

- Several coffins and other graves in this Hall of the Dead were not initially disabled like they should be, resulting in them all being marked as owned by Brother Verulus.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.2b, addresses this issue. Unassigned coffins and urns have been changed to objects that don't display the owner's name.
