Regain your thaneship

## Quest Giver

This is a radiant quest; however, unlike most of the other thane quests, you can only receive it when [Vignar Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Vignar_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Vignar Gray-Mane") is Jarl of Whiterun. If Balgruuf is the jarl, you can only become Thane of Whiterun by completing [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising").

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Assist the people of Whiterun Hold.
2. Purchase [Breezehome](/wiki/Skyrim:Breezehome "Skyrim:Breezehome").
3. Return to the jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This is an umbrella quest for several other quests that must be completed if you wish to become Thane of Whiterun. In order for Vignar to be jarl, you have to have completed [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)") for the Stormcloaks. Completing this quest befriends him automatically.

Speak with the jarl and he will ask you to [assist the people of the hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People "Skyrim:Making Friends and Influencing People"). You must assist five people in the hold, usually by completing [favor quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Favors "Skyrim:Favors") for them. Note that many of the quest givers are non-essential NPCs, so their deaths would result in a loss of potential quests to complete. If they die after you assist them, they still count towards your Thane status. You can also befriend merchants if you have the [Investor](/wiki/Skyrim:Investor "Skyrim:Investor") perk by investing in their stores.

You must also purchase [Breezehome](/wiki/Skyrim:Breezehome "Skyrim:Breezehome"), which costs 5,000 gold, if you have not done so already. Simply purchasing it is enough, there is no requirement to decorate, although it becomes more useful if decorated.

- **[The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature")**: Restore the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") for [Danica Pure-Spring](/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring "Skyrim:Danica Pure-Spring").

- **[Missing In Action](/wiki/Skyrim:Missing_In_Action "Skyrim:Missing In Action")**: Rescue a [missing Stormcloak supporter](/wiki/Skyrim:Thorald_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Thorald Gray-Mane") from the [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor").

### Reward

After helping five inhabitants and purchasing [Breezehome](/wiki/Skyrim:Breezehome "Skyrim:Breezehome"), the journal tells you to return to the jarl. The reward, the [Blade of Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_Whiterun "Skyrim:Blade of Whiterun"), is a random, leveled, enchanted blade. There is a 50% chance that it will be a sword (one-handed), and a 50% chance it will be a greatsword (two-handed).

- - This is changed with version 2.1.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch").
