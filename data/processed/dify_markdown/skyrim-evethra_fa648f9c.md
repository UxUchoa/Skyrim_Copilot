Evethra

**Evethra** is an arcane blacksmith who researched how to make the arms and armaments from [Amber](/wiki/Skyrim:Amber "Skyrim:Amber") and [Madness Ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Madness_Ore "Skyrim:Madness Ore"), and has gone insane as a result. She is a part of the [Saints & Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"), and killing her will yield you the necessary journal that will grant you the ability to craft Amber and Madness weapons and armor.

She wears the unique [Arcane Blacksmith's Apron](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Blacksmith%27s_Apron "Skyrim:Arcane Blacksmith's Apron") with a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b69e "Skyrim:Shoes"), and carries an [Iron Dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") as well as minor loot. She knows the [Firebolt](/wiki/Skyrim:Firebolt "Skyrim:Firebolt"), [Ice Spike](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Spike "Skyrim:Ice Spike"), or [Lightning Bolt](/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Bolt "Skyrim:Lightning Bolt") spell, as well as [Stoneflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Stoneflesh "Skyrim:Stoneflesh"), [Steadfast Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Steadfast_Ward "Skyrim:Steadfast Ward"), and [Fast Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Healing "Skyrim:Fast Healing").

She has set up camp at the Nordic ruins north of [Mistwatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Mistwatch "Skyrim:Mistwatch"), using the table as a true madwoman's altar: [bloody rags](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloody_Rags "Skyrim:Bloody Rags"), tools, and chunks of ore join the two ingots of [Refined Moonstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Refined_Moonstone "Skyrim:Refined Moonstone") already present. An assortment of swords of varying materials, some enchanted, are stuck in the ground around the table.
