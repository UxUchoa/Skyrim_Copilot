Delacourt

**Delacourt** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [rogue](/wiki/Skyrim:Rogue "Skyrim:Rogue") and the resident [bard](/wiki/Skyrim:Bard "Skyrim:Bard") of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). He spends his entire day at [Dead Man's Drink](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Man%27s_Drink "Skyrim:Dead Man's Drink"), either entertaining the customers or sitting for a meal. He can [sing](/wiki/Skyrim:Bardic_Performances "Skyrim:Bardic Performances") *Ragnar the Red* or *Age of Aggression*.

He wears a set of merchant [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0006ff45 "Skyrim:Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d "Skyrim:Boots"). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and carries a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

He may cheerfully tell you:

:   *"Keep in song in your heart to ward off the gloom."*
:   *"Perhaps one day, I'll write a song about you!"*

If spoken to before triggering [Investigate the Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Investigate_the_Bards_College "Skyrim:Investigate the Bards College"):

:   **What do you do around here?**
:   *"I'm a bard, trained at the Bards College in Solitude. Wanderers like you should think about applying."*

## Bugs

- Delacourt has no sleep package, making it impossible for him to do so.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
