Four **Fishermen** can be found in various locations. They can be male or female of any race, and each one wears a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") with farm [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"), and carries an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"). With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, they will be wearing [fishing apparel](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Items#Apparel "Skyrim:Fishing Items") instead and carry fishing rods. Unlike most hunters, fishermen do not offer trades.

## Dialogue

They have the same dialogue as generic [hunters](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter") encountered in the wilderness:

:   *"I've been hunting and fishing in these parts for years."*
:   *"It's not like my poaching is hurting anyone. The Jarl can hardly eat every deer now can he?"*
:   *"There's enough for all of us if you aren't greedy."*
