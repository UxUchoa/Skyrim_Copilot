## Quick Walkthrough

1. Travel to [Castle Dour](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour "Skyrim:Castle Dour") and read the Emperor's [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_to_General_Tullius "Skyrim:Letter to General Tullius").
2. Locate the camp southeast of [Fort Greymoor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greymoor "Skyrim:Fort Greymoor") and defeat the [Alik'r mercenaries](/wiki/Skyrim:Alik%27r_Mercenary "Skyrim:Alik'r Mercenary").
3. Take the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Alik%27r_Mercenary_Chest "Skyrim:Key to Alik'r Mercenary Chest") from [Majid](/wiki/Skyrim:Majid_the_Mad "Skyrim:Majid the Mad") and recover the [cursed Lord's Mail](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail_(Cursed) "Skyrim:Lord's Mail (Cursed)").
4. Go to Kynareth's shrine at the [Temple of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Temple of the Divines") to claim the [Lord's Mail](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail_(item) "Skyrim:Lord's Mail (item)").

### Mission from the Emperor

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-quest-Gift_of_Kynareth_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Gift_of_Kynareth_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Gift_of_Kynareth_02.jpg)

The letter in Castle Dour

Head to [Castle Dour](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour "Skyrim:Castle Dour") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") to intercept a letter from the Emperor. The [*Letter to General Tullius*](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_to_General_Tullius "Skyrim:Letter to General Tullius") can be found on the ground floor of Castle Dour in the room to the north of [General Tullius's](/wiki/Skyrim:General_Tullius "Skyrim:General Tullius") map room. You are only required to read the letter to start the quest; taking it counts as theft, so be sure that you are not seen if you decide to take it with you. The letter reveals that the [Lord's Mail](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail_(item) "Skyrim:Lord's Mail (item)"), a legendary [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") artifact, has been stolen by a band of [Alik'r mercenaries](/wiki/Skyrim:Alik%27r_Mercenary "Skyrim:Alik'r Mercenary") who were last seen south of [Fort Greymoor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greymoor "Skyrim:Fort Greymoor") in [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold").

The Alik'r camp is actually located southeast of Fort Greymoor, and is home to two generic Alik'r mercenaries led by [Majid the Mad](/wiki/Skyrim:Majid_the_Mad "Skyrim:Majid the Mad"). Taking on all three at once can be a tough fight, so prepare accordingly. Once the mercenaries lie dead, recover the [Key to Alik'r Mercenary Chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Alik%27r_Mercenary_Chest "Skyrim:Key to Alik'r Mercenary Chest") from Majid's body and use it to open the nearby chest. Inside, you will find a [cursed version](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail_(Cursed) "Skyrim:Lord's Mail (Cursed)") of the Lord's Mail along with a set of instructions titled *[Majid - Please Read](/wiki/Skyrim:Majid_-_Please_Read "Skyrim:Majid - Please Read")*. The note warns that the cursed armor should not be worn; indeed, if you equip it you will be subjected to a constant 50-point reduction to your Health, Stamina, and Magicka.

### Blessing of Kynareth

In order to remove the curse from the Lord's Mail, you will need to seek the blessing of its mythic creator, [Kynareth](/wiki/Lore:Kynareth "Lore:Kynareth"). This will necessitate a return to Solitude to visit the [Shrine of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Shrine of Kynareth") in the [Temple of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Temple of the Divines"). Upon activating the shrine, you will be informed that *"The Lord's Mail has been cleansed"*. This concludes the quest and rewards you with a set of [heavy](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor_(item) "Skyrim:Heavy Armor (item)") [ebony](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony "Skyrim:Ebony") armor with [poison resistance](/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Poison "Skyrim:Resist Poison"), [magic resistance](/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Magic "Skyrim:Resist Magic"), as well as a unique area effect that inflicts 5 points of [health absorbance](/wiki/Skyrim:Absorb_Health "Skyrim:Absorb Health") per second on nearby opponents. For the purpose of the [*Matching Set*](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor#Matching_Set "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") perk, it counts as [Imperial armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Armor#Imperial_Armor "Skyrim:Imperial Armor").

## Notes

- For historical information on the Lord's Mail, see the [lore article](/wiki/Lore:Lord%27s_Mail "Lore:Lord's Mail").
- The Alik'r mercenaries wear unique Redguard clothing items.
- If playing on Survival Mode, you will need to disable Survival Mode in order to complete the quest and get the Lord's Mail. The easiest way is to disable Survival mode outside of the temple then re-enable it once you've cleansed the armor. For some reason, it won't work when you have to pay to use the shrine.
