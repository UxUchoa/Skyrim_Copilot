## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to Jarl Igmund to initiate quest.
2. Travel to the dungeon.
3. Kill the Forsworn Leader.
4. Return to Jarl Igmund in [Understone Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep "Skyrim:Understone Keep") to collect the gold reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Travel to Understone Keep in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") and speak to Jarl Igmund to initiate the quest in which you have to kill a Forsworn Leader. He will direct you to a dungeon in [The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"), which is where the Forsworn Leader is hiding. You must travel there and face the leader.

Once you travel to the dungeon you will most likely have to deal with most leveled Forsworn before you can eliminate the leader.

### Reward

Once you have dealt with him/her, return to Understone Keep to collect your reward from Jarl Igmund.

| Levels | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1–9 | 400 |
| 10–19 | 600 |
| 20–29 | 800 |
| 30–39 | 1,000 |
| 40+ | 1,200 |

## Notes

- If Raerek is in a room with an adept lock, wait until he is sitting by the Jarl to receive your reward. He is there by 10am.

## Bugs

- It is possible to accept this quest and then find that all NPCs are non-aggressive towards you at the site. If this is the case feel free to kill or pickpocket any non-named NPCs (normally simply named "Forsworn") because none of the other NPCs will attack you.
- Upon the initial meeting with Jarl Igmund, he may not have the dialogue option to start this quest available. Exiting Understone Keep and fast travelling somewhere else (e.g. Karthwasten) and back may resolve this.
- If you complete the No One Escapes Cidna Mine quest line before ever speaking to Jarl Igmund, he may not ever give the dialogue option to start the quest. No known fix.
