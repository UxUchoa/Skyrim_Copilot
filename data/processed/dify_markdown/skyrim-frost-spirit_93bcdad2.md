A Frost Spirit

The **Frost Spirit** will be encountered in [Labyrinthian](/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian "Skyrim:Labyrinthian") during the quest [The Staff of Magnus](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Staff_of_Magnus "Skyrim:The Staff of Magnus"). It is found in a room with a blue-glowing ice crystal. Approaching the crystal will spawn the spirit.
