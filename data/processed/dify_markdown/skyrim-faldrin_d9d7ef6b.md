Faldrin

**Faldrin** is a dead [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") fisherman who can be found in the first chamber up the stairs in [Alftand Ruined Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Alftand#Alftand_Ruined_Tower "Skyrim:Alftand"). His [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Fisherman%27s_Journal_(Fishing) "Skyrim:Fisherman's Journal (Fishing)") can be found outside of [Bronze Water Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Bronze_Water_Cave "Skyrim:Bronze Water Cave"). Some steps in the corridor behind him lies the body of another fisherman named [Jorid](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorid "Skyrim:Jorid").
