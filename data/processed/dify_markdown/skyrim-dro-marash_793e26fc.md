Dro'marash

**Dro'marash** is a [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit "Skyrim:Khajiit") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:TrainerSpeechcraftJourneyman "Skyrim:TrainerSpeechcraftJourneyman") who follows [Ahkari](/wiki/Skyrim:Ahkari "Skyrim:Ahkari")'s [trade caravan](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_Traders "Skyrim:Khajiit Traders"). He can be found walking the roads between [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") and [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), or camped outside either city. He is a common-level [trainer](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers") in [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech").

Dro'marash wears a full set of [steel armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Armor"), including a pair of steel gauntlets (two variants), and a pair of steel boots (two variants). He is equipped with a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") and a [steel shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield "Skyrim:Steel Shield"), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").
