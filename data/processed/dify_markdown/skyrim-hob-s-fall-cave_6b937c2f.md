Hob's Fall Cave

**Hob's Fall Cave** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") west of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") containing [necromancers](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer") and [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton"). It contains only one zone, *[Hob's Fall Cave](#Hob.27s_Fall_Cave)*.

### Exterior

Hob's Fall Cave is at the end of a path enclosed by sheer walls of ice, which starts at the shoreline to the northeast and ends at the entrance. Usually, a leveled [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") will be found by the shore where the path begins. Several [clams](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam "Skyrim:Clam") and [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Grass Pod") plants, and a few blood splatters, are along the path. Another leveled animal is on the ice above and to the west of the entrance. A [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush can also be found nearby.

### Hob's Fall Cave

Isabelle Rolaine

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave.jpg)

Wooden palisade

If you are on the quest [Drowned Sorrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Drowned_Sorrows "Skyrim:Drowned Sorrows"), upon entering, [Isabelle Rolaine](/wiki/Skyrim:Isabelle_Rolaine "Skyrim:Isabelle Rolaine") lies dead in front of you. She carries a [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Isabelle%27s_Letter "Skyrim:Isabelle's Letter"). Past her, the tunnel turns north then opens into a two-level chamber. This chamber contains several small empty cages, a cart filled with bones and a [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), and [empty wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle") scattered all over the floor. Above you to the north is a ledge with a wooden palisade along the edge. The tunnel to the west is the apparent way forward, though it is possible to climb the ledge with difficulty (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

The tunnel twists and turns before continuing to the north. There is a skeleton patrolling this section. Shortly after the tunnel turns to the west, a necromancer sits at a table on the right with their back to you. There is a copy of *[The Book of Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra "Skyrim:The Book of Daedra")* in front of them, along with an empty random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") and a [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Healing"). To the right is an unlocked chest in front of a bookcase containing copies of *[Beggar Prince](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar_Prince "Skyrim:Beggar Prince")* and *[The Bear of Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bear_of_Markarth "Skyrim:The Bear of Markarth")*, and another copy of *The Book of Daedra*. The tunnel continues on until it turns west and you see a lit brazier; take care, as around the corner to the right, a skeleton and a necromancer lurk.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave_02.jpg)

Wooden bridge

The tunnel continues up a steep incline then turns east, where a wooden bridge spans a deep cavern. There's another necromancer and skeleton pair on the far side. Sounds of fighting may draw a patrolling necromancer from the tunnel beyond the bridge. When you turn due east again and see a descending slope in front of you, beware, as there are two necromancers and a skeleton in the room at the bottom.

Just before the entrance to the room, a tunnel branches off to the left. After a few twists, you'll find two skeletons in the tunnel. At the end, it opens into the chasm you previously crossed; the bridge is above you. Below, accessible by an earthen ramp to your left, is a room with a necromancer. The cupboard here holds an [unusual gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem") needed for the quest [No Stone Unturned](/wiki/Skyrim:No_Stone_Unturned "Skyrim:No Stone Unturned"), a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), two random [potions of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), and several common books. Below the ramp is an alchemy lab, and on the table to the right of that, at the bottom of the ramp, you'll find the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Exodus](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Exodus "Skyrim:The Exodus")*. In the pit under the bridge right above the cupboard with the unusual gem, there is a chest on a ledge with an adept-level lock.

Backtrack to the deep cavern with two necromancers and a skeleton. On your left are two sets of shelves. The first contains a random magicka potion, a random [healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing "Skyrim:Healing") potion, and a copy of *[Boethiah's Proving](/wiki/Skyrim:Boethiah%27s_Proving "Skyrim:Boethiah's Proving")*. The second contains three bottles of Nord mead, three bottles of wine, and an apothecary's satchel. To the northeast, on another raised section, are an apprentice-locked chest and a bookcase containing a random magicka potion and a random healing potion, another apothecary's satchel, and copies of *[The Wispmother](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wispmother "Skyrim:The Wispmother")*, *[Aedra and Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:Aedra_and_Daedra "Skyrim:Aedra and Daedra")*, *[The Aetherium Wars](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars "Skyrim:The Aetherium Wars")*[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard"), *[The Firmament](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firmament "Skyrim:The Firmament")*, *[Frontier, Conquest](/wiki/Skyrim:Frontier,_Conquest "Skyrim:Frontier, Conquest")*, *[The Oblivion Crisis](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Oblivion_Crisis "Skyrim:The Oblivion Crisis")*, and *[Gods and Worship](/wiki/Skyrim:Gods_and_Worship "Skyrim:Gods and Worship")*. With the [Arcane Accessories](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Accessories "Skyrim:Arcane Accessories") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, an "Ancient Tome Chest" containing all 16 [new spell tomes](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Accessories_Items#Books "Skyrim:Arcane Accessories Items") can be found here. Against the wall on the other side of the room is an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter") with a copy of the [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") skill book *[Enchanter's Primer](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Primer "Skyrim:Enchanter's Primer")* on top. To the east in the middle of the wall is a tunnel barred by spears; the chain to lower them is to the right.

In the next room are three [spellcaster traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Spellcaster_Trap "Skyrim:Spellcaster Trap") that shoot [Frostbite](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite "Skyrim:Frostbite") spells at you as you pass. It's easy enough to approach from the side and grab the gems safely, as they only shoot straight ahead. Continue to the south to find another necromancer patrolling the tunnel near a rise. At the end, the tunnel turns to the west by two barrels and then immediately to the north, rising up into a large two-leveled room with three more necromancers on the upper level.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hob%27s_Fall_Cave_03.jpg)

The final room

On the bottom level is a ramp leading up to the left of a lit brazier and a wooden bench against the back wall. On the upper level is a table being used as an altar, with several benches arrayed in front. The corpse of [Larina](/wiki/Skyrim:Larina "Skyrim:Larina"), who you heard shouting when you first entered the cave, will be on the altar if she was not raised during the boss fight. The altar also holds a [black soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Black Soul Gem") and a random [potion of lasting potency](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Lasting_Potency "Skyrim:Potion of Lasting Potency"). Beside the altar is a small wooden table holding a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat"), a [pheasant breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and a random magicka potion. There is a boss chest to the left of the altar which will contain [Pantea's Flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items#Pantea.27s_Flute "Skyrim:Quest Items"), a quest item for the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College"), among other loot (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

To the west are two hanging cages and a large, expert-locked cage containing two more corpses that may be raised. Past these is the ledge with the wooden palisade seen earlier, providing a shortcut back to the exit.

## Notes

- If you are at least level 30, you can start the quest [Boethiah's Calling](/wiki/Skyrim:Boethiah%27s_Calling "Skyrim:Boethiah's Calling") by reading *Boethiah's Proving*.
- [Hobs](/wiki/Lore:Hob "Lore:Hob") are hairless monkey-like creatures sometimes used as servants.
- After Larina has been sacrificed, if you approach the altar, you can overhear the necromancers carrying out a ritual to the [God of Worms](/wiki/Lore:God_of_Worms "Lore:God of Worms") to create a black soul gem:

:   :   *"We offer this soul to The Revenant: he who watches over the opponents of Arkay and his power over life and death. Fie upon Arkay and his followers! Fie!"*
    :   *"Yeah, fie!"*
    :   *"Fie. We beseech you. Darken this gem so that we may use its power to restore The [Order of the Black Worm](/wiki/Lore:Order_of_the_Black_Worm "Lore:Order of the Black Worm"). The Order shall rise. Grant us this power so that in your name we may vanquish our enemies."*
:   Interrupting the ritual does nothing, as the soul gem is already black.

- Version 2.0.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") replaces the [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush outside with the snow-covered version of the same plant.
- In [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.

## Bugs

- If you take Pantea's Flute before you are given the quest, you may be unable to complete the quest.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
- This cave has a broken navmesh with two triangles twisted underneath the main grid which can cause NPCs to be confused and get lost while they're here.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
- It is possible to climb the ledge in the first two-level chamber, though it is difficult and requires going right and left on the stones multiple times. You can then open a cage, talk with Larina (who will only greet you), and take Pantea's Flute without a fight, as nobody will be present except Larina.
