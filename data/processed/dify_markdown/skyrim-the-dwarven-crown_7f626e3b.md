## Quick Walkthrough

1. Enter the Vardnknd Gallery section of [Runoff Caverns](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns") and retrieve the [Dwarven Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Crown "Skyrim:Dwarven Crown") from [Elberon](/wiki/Skyrim:Elberon "Skyrim:Elberon")'s body.
2. Locate the [Dwarven Autumn Visage](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Autumn_Visage "Skyrim:Dwarven Autumn Visage") in a side room the Autumn's Bells section of the dungeon.
3. Locate the [Dwarven Spring Visage](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Spring_Visage "Skyrim:Dwarven Spring Visage") down a long side passage in the Spring's Symphony section of the dungeon.
4. Locate the [Dwarven Winter Visage](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Winter_Visage "Skyrim:Dwarven Winter Visage") in a hidden area in the Winter's Chimes area of the dungeon, accessible by means of a sloping pipe.

### Crown of Legend

You must travel to [Vardnknd Gallery](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns#Vardnknd_Gallery "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns"), the second section of [Runoff Caverns](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns"), a dungeon which will already be marked on your map. Once you enter this section of the dungeon, a wizard named [Elberon](/wiki/Skyrim:Elberon "Skyrim:Elberon") will be struck by lightning, dying instantly. This quest will then begin, prompting you to loot Elberon's corpse for his strange [Dwarven Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Crown "Skyrim:Dwarven Crown").

Once you've obtained the Crown, you will be prompted to search for three masks which can be slotted into the headpiece. All three of these can be found within the dungeon in their corresponding areas, although they are designed to be well-hidden and therefore may take a bit of exploration to find.

### Mask of Autumn

The [Dwarven Autumn Visage](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Autumn_Visage "Skyrim:Dwarven Autumn Visage") is located in the [Autumn's Bells](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns#Autumn.27s_Bells "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns") section of Runoff Caverns. Head east after entering the main chamber until you find the bridge over the ravine; after crossing the bridge, go north along the brick road and it will lead you straight to the entrance to the corridor that leads to the mask.

### Mask of Spring

The [Dwarven Spring Visage](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Spring_Visage "Skyrim:Dwarven Spring Visage") is located in the [Spring's Symphony](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns#Spring.27s_Symphony "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns") section of Runoff Caverns. It is located at the end of a side hallway accessible through a door a short way into the chamber. However, the door is locked; the key is held by the [Blooming Spriggan Matron](/wiki/Skyrim:Blooming_Spriggan_Matron "Skyrim:Blooming Spriggan Matron") boss at the end of the section. The hallway is partially filled with a green miasma, so the [Dwarven Drone Fly](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Drone_Fly "Skyrim:Dwarven Drone Fly") you obtain in the room on your right just before you jump out of this section might prove useful.

### Mask of Winter

The [Dwarven Winter Visage](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Winter_Visage "Skyrim:Dwarven Winter Visage") is found in the [Winter's Chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns#Winter.27s_Chimes "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns") section of Runoff Caverns, in a hidden area that can be challenging to find. It is located in the second section, after you make the first jump. It is off the short hallway containing [Frosted Dwarven Spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frosted_Dwarven_Spider "Skyrim:Frosted Dwarven Spider"), through a Dwarven gate that is raised above ground level. The gate must be reached by climbing up a bank of snow and a sloped Dwarven pipe (as pictured above). From the gate, you must dive into the water through a short underwater passage to access the section. Once you have navigated to the chamber containing the mask, you will be attacked by a powerful [Frosted Dwarven Centurion](/wiki/Skyrim:Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion "Skyrim:Frosted Dwarven Centurion") which uses frost attacks.

### Forging the Crown

The masks can be worn on their own, but you can make their effects more powerful by affixing one to the crown. Take the crown, a mask, and two [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strip "Skyrim:Leather Strip") to any forge or anvil, and you can craft the [Dwarven Crown of Autumn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Crown_of_Autumn "Skyrim:Dwarven Crown of Autumn"), the [Dwarven Crown of Spring](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Crown_of_Spring "Skyrim:Dwarven Crown of Spring"), or the [Dwarven Crown of Winter](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Crown_of_Winter "Skyrim:Dwarven Crown of Winter"). You can only have one of these at a time, but you can swap them out with each other at a forge as often as you like, only costing you two leather strips each time.

## Notes

- There is no Dwarven Summer Visage.
