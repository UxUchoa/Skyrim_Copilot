## Quick Walkthrough

1. A [challenger](/wiki/Skyrim:Challenger "Skyrim:Challenger") appears.
2. Meet the challenger.
3. Accept or refuse the challenge.
4. Kill the challenger or flee.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Once you have a high level of skill in any [magic](/wiki/Skyrim:Magicka "Skyrim:Magicka") school, upon entering a habitable location or exiting an inn after sleeping, a [challenger](/wiki/Skyrim:Challenger "Skyrim:Challenger") may appear and challenge you to a duel. Regardless of your answer, a combat ensues.

## Notes

- If you try to back out of the duel by saying "sorry", any nearby [guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Guards "Skyrim:Guards") will help you with your duel.
- If you do not use magic to fight the mage, he will taunt you by saying: "This is a mage's duel. You know magic!"
- If any of your [followers](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") join in on the duel, he will order them to stop, explaining that it is supposed to be a one-on-one duel. This does not seem to affect the quest in any way, and companions will not abide to his demands. The same happens if other NPCs nearby are hit by his spells.
- This duel can lead to your expulsion from the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)"). There is a [quest to rejoin](/wiki/Skyrim:Rejoining_the_College "Skyrim:Rejoining the College") if this happens.

## Bugs

- If you enter another cell while fighting against the Challenger, you will lose their location and be unable to finish this mini-quest.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- If you fight him with conjured weapons, the Challenger will still taunt you as if you weren't fighting him using magic.
