Greathall

The **Greathall** is the home of chief [Fanari Strong-Voice](/wiki/Skyrim:Fanari_Strong-Voice "Skyrim:Fanari Strong-Voice") and [Tharstan](/wiki/Skyrim:Tharstan "Skyrim:Tharstan") in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

It is on the east side of the village, north of the [Shaman's Hut](/wiki/Skyrim:Shaman%27s_Hut "Skyrim:Shaman's Hut").

### Exterior

The building has two entrances, with the front entrance to the west and the back entrance to the east. To the left of the front entrance is a lit brazier and a [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"). To the right of the front entrance is another lit brazier. The entrances are unlocked.

### Greathall

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/SR-interior-Greathall.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Greathall.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Greathall.jpg)

Greathall interior

The front entrance leads into a large hall with balconies around the western half of the room. Stairs are against the northern and southern walls. There is a large fire pit in the center of the room with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") on the south side. Around the fire pit are three pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"), a pot of [honey](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey "Skyrim:Honey"), a stand containing three fillets of [horker meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat "Skyrim:Horker Meat"), an open crate containing a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a cast iron pot, which contains two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), next to a chair.

Against the eastern wall is an open display case containing two [horker tusks](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk") and a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"). In front of this, next to a pillar, is a basket containing three [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") samples. To the right of the display case, in the southeast corner, is a crate. Against the southern wall between the crate and stairs is a set of shelves containing an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), an [ash yam](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam"), a sack and a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"). Under the southern stairs are two crates, a mead barrel, three sacks and a chair. In front of this are two lit braziers with a wood pile and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"). To the right of the lit braziers, hanging under the balcony, are two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") and [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"). Also against the southern wall is a barrel with a [deer hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide "Skyrim:Deer Hide") beside it.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b5/SR-interior-Greathall_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Greathall_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Greathall_02.jpg)

Back door

In the southwest corner, against the western wall is a set of shelves containing a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"), three [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), two bottles of alto wine and a goat cheese wheel. There is a second entrance in the middle of this wall. Farther to the right is a small set of shelves containing two potatoes, a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide"), a [snow fox pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Fox Pelt") and a metal bowl, which contains three [gold ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore "Skyrim:Gold Ore") samples. In the northwestern corner is an open display case containing two animal skulls.

Against the northern wall is a desk with copies of *[Deathbrand](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(book) "Skyrim:Deathbrand (book)")*, *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor "Skyrim:Vernaccus and Bourlor")*, *[Children of the All-Maker](/wiki/Skyrim:Children_of_the_All-Maker "Skyrim:Children of the All-Maker")*, *[Fall of the Snow Prince](/wiki/Skyrim:Fall_of_the_Snow_Prince "Skyrim:Fall of the Snow Prince")*, *[Ghosts in the Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_in_the_Storm "Skyrim:Ghosts in the Storm")*, *[Last King of the Ayleids](/wiki/Skyrim:Last_King_of_the_Ayleids "Skyrim:Last King of the Ayleids")*, *[The Guardian and the Traitor](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Guardian_and_the_Traitor "Skyrim:The Guardian and the Traitor")*, *[The Song of Pelinal, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v3 "Skyrim:The Song of Pelinal, v3")*, *[The Real Barenziah, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Real_Barenziah,_v3 "Skyrim:The Real Barenziah, v3")*, *[Ahzidal's Descent](/wiki/Skyrim:Ahzidal%27s_Descent "Skyrim:Ahzidal's Descent")*, a [roll of paper](/wiki/Skyrim:Roll_of_Paper "Skyrim:Roll of Paper"), a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and two bottles, one of alto wine and the other [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine").

To the right of the desk is an end table. Farther to the right, under the northern stairs, are two barrels, a crate and a sack. In front of these are two more lit braziers with a crate and three sacks in between. To the left of the lit braziers, hanging under the balcony, are two samples of elves ear, a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid, a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and a pheasant. Still against the northern wall, between the stairs and the corner, is a small round table and two chairs. On the table is a wooden plate holding a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"). In the northeast corner is a set of shelves containing a [deer pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt "Skyrim:Deer Pelt"), an [empty bottle of wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle"), a leather strip and an open crate containing three pieces of [leather](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather "Skyrim:Leather"). To the right of this is a barrel with a large piece of [charcoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal "Skyrim:Charcoal") on the ground beside it. Next to a pillar in front of the western entrance is a crate, with a basket containing five large pieces of charcoal in, and two more around, it. Towards the middle of the room between both sets of lit braziers is a long table with benches either side, on the table is a mead barrel and a cast iron pot containing a salmon steak and some [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-interior-Greathall_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Greathall_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Greathall_03.jpg)

Boudoir

Climbing the northern stairs you come to the sleeping area for Fanari Strong-Voice. In front of you, in the northwest corner, is a barrel and a long end table with a horker tusk and sack. A crate and second sack is in front. To the left of the end table is a wardrobe, followed by a small end table with a sample of frost mirriam on top. To the left of this is a double bed and then another end table with a [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel "Skyrim:Satchel") on top. Under the left-hand side of the bed is a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"). Farther to the left is an empty chest. In the southwestern corner is a dresser with a [Sabre Cat snow pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Snow_Pelt "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Snow Pelt"). There is a set of shelves against the southern wall that contain two sacks. At the front of the balcony from left to right is a dresser with an apprentice-locked display case on top. Inside the cabinet are three [pearls](/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl "Skyrim:Pearl") and three [small pearls](/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Pearl "Skyrim:Small Pearl"). Next is a bench and finally a crate with a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") on it.
