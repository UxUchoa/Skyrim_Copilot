East Empire Warden

The **East Empire Wardens** take over the [East Empire Company Warehouse](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Warehouse "Skyrim:East Empire Company Warehouse") during [Scoundrel's Folly](/wiki/Skyrim:Scoundrel%27s_Folly "Skyrim:Scoundrel's Folly"). The wardens turn hostile if they spot you.

Each warden wears [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), [steel armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Armor"), [steel cuffed boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Cuffed Boots"), and an open-faced [steel helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Helmet "Skyrim:Steel Helmet"). They carry a leveled [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), a [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch"), two leveled [health potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potions"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and a [small amount](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists "Skyrim:Leveled Lists") of gold.
