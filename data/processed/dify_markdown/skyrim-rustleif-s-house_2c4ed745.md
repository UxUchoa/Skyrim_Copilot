Rustleif's House

**Rustleif's House** is a smithy near [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar")'s shoreline. [Rustleif](/wiki/Skyrim:Rustleif "Skyrim:Rustleif") has his smithy and home set up facing the bay. He is a renowned weaponsmith, and he is often called upon to arm and outfit the jarl's men. His Redguard wife, [Seren](/wiki/Skyrim:Seren "Skyrim:Seren"), assists him and is pregnant. Both Rustleif and Seren provide merchant services.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House_02.jpg)

Blacksmithing

There is a raised, covered porch in front and to the left of the door, with a couple of food barrels to the right and wooden benches to the left of the door. There are two sets of steps leading up to the porch and a snowberry bush beside one of them. On the side of the house the [smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") setup is rather minimal, consisting of only a [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") and a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") and where they buy and sell their goods, although two [smelters](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") can be found elsewhere in town. The front door is novice-locked during the day when they are both around the forge working and at night.

### Rustleif's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg)

Rustleif's house

The house is a single-room dwelling that is a little spartan inside. Upon entering, on your left is a long wooden table and bench holding a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a sprig of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). To the left of the table, on the floor is a basket containing four [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") and to the right is a basket containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"). Further to the right against the north wall is a set of shelves holding two leeks, a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), three potatoes, with several pieces of cheese on top. Above these shelves are two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging from the ceiling. There is a food barrel in the northeastern corner.

Against the east wall is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot, beside a large double bed with a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes "Skyrim:Shoes") by the foot of it and a basket containing two [potions of vigor](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor "Skyrim:Potion of Vigor") to the right and a round wooden table beside this holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead"). Next to this against the south wall is a tall wardrobe with a [blacksmith's apron](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Apron") on top and pile of firewood, with a hang rack holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") between them. Opposite the door, on the south wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") on the mantlepiece. In front of the hearth is an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield"). To the right of the fireplace are several barrels and against the west wall is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table is a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold").

:   *"Located overlooking the shoreline of Dawnstar, Rustleif has his smithy and home set up facing the main road. Rustleif is a renowned weaponsmith, and he is often called upon to arm and outfit the Jarl's men. His Redguard wife, Seren, assists him and is currently with child. Inside, their home is comfortable but a little spartan."*
