Half-Moon Mill

**Half-Moon Mill** is a lumber mill that supplies [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen") and [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). It is operated by [Hert](/wiki/Skyrim:Hert "Skyrim:Hert") and [Hern](/wiki/Skyrim:Hern "Skyrim:Hern"), who live in a small cabin across from the mill itself. It is located on the shore of [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta"), west of [The Lady Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lady_Stone "Skyrim:The Lady Stone"), east of [Hunter's Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter%27s_Rest "Skyrim:Hunter's Rest"), beside the road that leads north from Falkreath towards [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead").

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill_02.jpg)

The cabin and chicken coop

The property is adjacent to the road, beside a stone bridge that crosses a stream that is filled with tree trunks and branches as it flows from [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta"). The stream splits the property and is rich in [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(creature) "Skyrim:Salmon (creature)"). The [wood mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Sawn_Log "Skyrim:Sawn Log") and a butcher shack are on the east side of the stream, with a wooden bridge leading to the main cabin to the west. The butcher shack contains a table holding three haunches of raw [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison") and two joints of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"). Hanging from the ceiling is a rack holding two [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). Under the table are gory human remains, a possible clue as to the [true nature](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") of the mill's inhabitants. A [troll skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull "Skyrim:Troll Skull") is found on the ground as well.

The cabin is a single-story building with a small henhouse containing two chicken nests between the road and the cabin. In front of the cabin, near the stream, is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), and opposite the henhouse are a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"). There are several tree stumps around the property, harvested by the mill. At the rear of the property is a large [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") stuck in the ground beside it, and not far to the north is another iron ore vein. Near this is a tree stump containing a dead rabbit, a fork, and a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). Numerous plants and flowers may be found around the property. If the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") [creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") is installed, some [Fishing Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Supplies "Skyrim:Fishing Supplies") can be found near the shore, along with a salmon rack and two [baskets](/wiki/Skyrim:Basket "Skyrim:Basket"). Fish from [Temperate Lakes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_(activity)#Biomes "Skyrim:Fishing (activity)") can be caught here.

When speaking with [Hert](/wiki/Skyrim:Hert "Skyrim:Hert"), she will instruct you to grab an [axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), as she will pay you for [chopping wood](/wiki/Skyrim:Chop_Wood "Skyrim:Chop Wood").

### Half-Moon Mill

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/SR-interior-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg)

The interior of the cabin

The cabin itself is a single-room dwelling. Upon entry there is a chair and barrels on the left, against the southeast wall. Against the southwest wall is a cupboard holding only clutter, with more [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels next to it, mostly containing raw meats. A long wooden table and bench along the northwest wall holds a haunch of raw venison, a joint of raw beef, a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), with two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg") under the table. Hanging above the table are three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). To the right is a hanging rack holding three rabbits and three [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). To the right of the table is a kettle containing three [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") and another food barrel. In the middle of the northwest wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a kettle on the hearth containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"). In front of the fireplace is a round wooden table and two chairs. On this table is a bottle of wine, a bottle of alto wine, and two [pheasant breasts](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"). To the right of the fireplace, against the northwest wall, is a large owned double bed with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of it. Under the bed on the right-hand side is a copy of *[Physicalities of Werewolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Physicalities_of_Werewolves "Skyrim:Physicalities of Werewolves")*. In the northern corner is an end table holding a bottle of Nord mead. Against the southeast wall is a wardrobe and dresser, both containing common [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a food barrel between them.

## Notes

- You can buy [sawn logs](/wiki/Skyrim:Sawn_Log "Skyrim:Sawn Log")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") here after installing [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") by speaking to Hert. You can also cut your own for free if she has given permission to do so.
- When [building](/wiki/Skyrim:Construction "Skyrim:Construction") a home with the Hearthfire add-on, your map marker may send you here to buy sawn logs. If Hert is dead, you may simply go to [another wood mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Mills "Skyrim:Wood Mills") to purchase lumber to clear the objective.
- To the northwest is a memorial pillar with a flag and the corpse of a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf_(dead) "Skyrim:Dark Elf (dead)") lying beside it.
- In the northwest corner of Lake Ilinalta, north from Half-Moon Mill, is an unmarked fisherman's camp with a copy of the [one-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Fire and Darkness](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_and_Darkness "Skyrim:Fire and Darkness")*.
- Although the owners here are vampires themselves, they will still become hostile towards you if you are a [blood-starved vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampirism#Stages_of_Vampirism "Skyrim:Vampirism").
