## Detailed Walkthrough

When visiting her shop during business hours, you can hear [Ghorza gra-Bagol](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghorza_gra-Bagol "Skyrim:Ghorza gra-Bagol") berating the smithing skills of her hapless apprentice, [Tacitus Sallustius](/wiki/Skyrim:Tacitus_Sallustius "Skyrim:Tacitus Sallustius"). She mentions a skill book, *[Last Scabbard of Akrash](/wiki/Skyrim:Last_Scabbard_of_Akrash "Skyrim:Last Scabbard of Akrash")*, that might teach him something.

The marker points to [Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard "Skyrim:Fort Sungard"), which is south of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead"). The fort is well guarded by [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") and the book is in [Fort Sungard Muster](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard_Muster "Skyrim:Fort Sungard Muster"). However, you do not need to retrieve this exact copy of the book; any copy will do. The other two locations are [Gallows Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock "Skyrim:Gallows Rock") and an unmarked area south-southeast of [Black-Briar Lodge](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Lodge "Skyrim:Black-Briar Lodge"). With the [Bloodchill Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodchill_Manor "Skyrim:Bloodchill Manor") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, a copy can also be found on a shelf to the right of the [Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodchill_Cavern "Skyrim:Bloodchill Cavern")'s smithing area.

For retrieving the book, she will reward you with a free smithing lesson and will tell you that she is advising the [Orc Strongholds](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") that you can be trusted and that you should now be welcome in them.
