The Western Watchtower

The **Western Watchtower** is a small [fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Military_Forts "Skyrim:Military Forts") west of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").

## Related Quests

- **[Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising")**: Investigate a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") sighting near [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").

## Walkthrough

The tower is along the main road heading west from Whiterun, allowing the [Whiterun guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Guard "Skyrim:Whiterun Guard") in the area to watch for any potential threats approaching from that direction. It can be entered via the crumbling wall that forms a ramp upwards. Outside the tower the area between the fallen walls contains a training dummy with some Iron Arrows shot into it. Inside the tower are two unowned bedrolls and a small table with a lantern. The second floor contains an archery target and a bale of straw, while the top floor is empty. There are two [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") banners flying from the tower.

Four Whiterun guards will be stationed at the tower to keep watch for enemies (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

Once the quest [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") is complete, the area between the fallen walls contains two burnt corpses with loot and the charred remains of a wooden hut.

## Notes

- There are two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") in the rocks to the west.
- The Western Watchtower will not be marked as a location on your map until the quest Dragon Rising begins.
- The Imperial Legion banners will be replaced by the [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") banners if the [Stormcloaks capture Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)") during the [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") questline.
  - Version 1.3.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") replaces the default Imperial Legion banners with yellow Whiterun banners to better reflect that Whiterun Hold is originally neutral in the Civil War questline.
- Version 4.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch") replaces Western Watchtower's fort map icon with the one used for [Imperial towers](/wiki/Skyrim:Towers "Skyrim:Towers").
