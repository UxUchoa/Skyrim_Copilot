**Solitude** is a major city on the northwest coast of Skyrim, and the capital of both [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar") and the entire province. It is the largest city in Skyrim and seat of the province's High King, and is built on a natural arch over the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Lore:Sea_of_Ghosts "Lore:Sea of Ghosts"). The ruler of Solitude is [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") [Elisif the Fair](/wiki/Skyrim:Elisif_the_Fair "Skyrim:Elisif the Fair"), widow to the late [High King Torygg](/wiki/Skyrim:High_King_Torygg "Skyrim:High King Torygg"), with her throne in the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace").

The city houses many important buildings, including the [headquarters](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour "Skyrim:Castle Dour") of the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") in Skyrim, the [East Empire Company Warehouse](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Warehouse "Skyrim:East Empire Company Warehouse"), and the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College"). The [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy") is also near Solitude.

When you enter Solitude for the first time, [Roggvir](/wiki/Skyrim:Roggvir "Skyrim:Roggvir") is executed for treason.

For more information on Solitude, see the [lore article](/wiki/Lore:Solitude "Lore:Solitude").

- **[Lights Out!](/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out! "Skyrim:Lights Out!")**: Help run a [cargo ship](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Icerunner "Skyrim:The Icerunner") aground.

## Notes

- [Proudspire Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Proudspire_Manor "Skyrim:Proudspire Manor") is a [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Houses "Skyrim:Houses") available for purchase after completion of certain quests for the jarl. It may be purchased from the steward for 25,000 gold.
- 6 health potions (3 standard, 2 plentiful, and 1 minor) can be found at certain points around the city. You can take all 6 aforementioned potions for free, and they all respawn every 10 days.
- [Hawks](/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk "Skyrim:Hawk") can be seen circling the sky above the city. You can kill them for their [beaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk_Beak "Skyrim:Hawk Beak") and [feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk_Feathers "Skyrim:Hawk Feathers"), both of which are alchemy ingredients.
- Solitude also appears in *[Arena](/wiki/Arena:Solitude "Arena:Solitude")* and [ESO](/wiki/Online:Solitude "Online:Solitude").
- There is an initial conversation scene may be missed. [Sorex Vinius](/wiki/Skyrim:Sorex_Vinius "Skyrim:Sorex Vinius") and [Jaree-Ra](/wiki/Skyrim:Jaree-Ra "Skyrim:Jaree-Ra")'s conversation only takes place if the player never enters the related area, the time doesn't matter.

## Bugs

- With the Skyrim Special Edition a door to "Quick Test Cell" can be seen on the local map near the entrance to the city. This door is otherwise invisible and can't be interacted with. It is a placeholder door for the [Saints & Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") which changes it to an entrance to the [Solitude Sewers](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Sewers "Skyrim:Solitude Sewers"). Without the Creation it is just an exit from the [CTest](/wiki/Skyrim:Test_Cells#CTest "Skyrim:Test Cells") cell. **?**
