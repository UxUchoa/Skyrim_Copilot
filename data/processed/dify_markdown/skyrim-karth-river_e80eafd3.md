Karth River

The **Karth River** is a river flowing from [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") to [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude").

The Karth River originates in [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") at Lost Valley Redoubt from a waterfall flowing from the mountain range. It is unknown where the river that supplies that waterfall flows from without consulting with another provincial map of a region such as Hammerfell or High Rock. From there it flows north and northeast, temporarily splitting in two around the [Karthspire](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthspire "Skyrim:Karthspire"), then passing [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten") and [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") before finally reaching the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts") near Solitude. This river is fed from at least four different sources, including the waterfalls at [Lost Valley Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Valley_Redoubt "Skyrim:Lost Valley Redoubt"), the stream at [Hag Rock Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Hag_Rock_Redoubt "Skyrim:Hag Rock Redoubt"), also known as the [Reachwater River](/wiki/Lore:Reachwater_River "Lore:Reachwater River"), the stream sourced from the three waterfalls that flow through Markarth from the mountains above, and the waterfall just above [Deep Folk Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Deep_Folk_Crossing "Skyrim:Deep Folk Crossing") near the border with High Rock.
