Ari's corpse

**Ari** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") that was killed by [spriggans](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan") when her party chased a [bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear") sow into [Moss Mother Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern "Skyrim:Moss Mother Cavern"). After [Niels](/wiki/Skyrim:Niels "Skyrim:Niels") was ambushed and almost instantly killed, she took off running before being overcome. The only survivor of their party was [Valdr](/wiki/Skyrim:Valdr "Skyrim:Valdr") who requests that you help avenge his fallen comrades. If you help Valdr, he will bury Ari's corpse.

Ari wears [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor_(item) "Skyrim:Light Armor (item)") (either [fur](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor"), [hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor"), or [studded](/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor "Skyrim:Studded Armor")), with either [fur shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes") or [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"), and a 50% chance of a pair of bracers (either [fur](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Bracers "Skyrim:Fur Bracers") or [hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers")). She carries a [one-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:One-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:One-Handed Weapons") (either [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron "Skyrim:Iron") or [steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel "Skyrim:Steel")) with a 20% chance of a shield (either [hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Shield "Skyrim:Hide Shield") or [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield")), along with a small amount of gold. She may also carry other minor items and loot.
