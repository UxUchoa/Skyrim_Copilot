**Braidwood Inn** is an inn at the center of the small settlement of [Kynesgrove](/wiki/Skyrim:Kynesgrove "Skyrim:Kynesgrove").

The inn was built next to the mine and serves as dining hall, shop and everything else for those who live in Kynesgrove. It's not far due south of Windhelm, but the residents act like it's miles away from anywhere else.

[Iddra](/wiki/Skyrim:Iddra "Skyrim:Iddra") and her husband, [Kjeld](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjeld "Skyrim:Kjeld"), run the inn together. However, Kjeld is more interested in the running of the mine and will buy any malachite ore you have.

It only consists of one zone, which is the [interior](#Braidwood_Inn).

## Exterior †

The inn is set near [Steamscorch Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine "Skyrim:Steamscorch Mine"), which has a camp set up outside the inn consisting of three small tents with bed rolls in them around a central campfire. There are a couple of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and a rack with nine [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") hanging from them. There are also a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"), a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") and a small vegetable garden with ten [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), four [gourds](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), six [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and eight [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") plants. Of all the items only the salmon are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. Next to the inn itself is a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), plenty more barrels of foodstuffs and just down the road to the west is a chicken coop housing two [chicken nests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"). There is a flight of steps up to the porch on the front of the inn, with a wooden bench just to the right of the entrance.

## Braidwood Inn

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/SR-interior-Braidwood_Inn.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Braidwood_Inn.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Braidwood_Inn.jpg)

Braidwood Inn common room

The front door of the inn, as with most in [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), leads directly into the common room which consists of a central long fire pit with five long wooden tables and benches against the side walls and the counter at the far end. On the tables are a selection of bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a couple of loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), with the exception of the table in the southwestern corner which also has a couple of [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") and several pieces of cheese. The room has three doorways off of it, two to the west and one to the east. On the counter is a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, several pieces of cheese and two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), while under the counter two bottles of alto wine, six bottles of wine, fourteen bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), three small [coin purses](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a [steel warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Warhammer "Skyrim:Steel Warhammer") and five [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") coins. Behind the counter, against the north wall are five large vats and several food barrels. In front of these are racks hanging from the ceiling holding eleven [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and six [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), with two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, a bunch of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") also hanging from the ceiling. Either side of the doorway to the east are sets of shelves that hold two bottles of alto wine, six bottles of wine, seven bottles of Nord mead, four loaves of bread and three bunches of elves ear. In the northwest corner, next to one of the vats is a flower basket holding two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") and a pine thrush egg.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-interior-Braidwood_Inn_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Braidwood_Inn_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Braidwood_Inn_02.jpg)

A bedroom in Braidwood Inn

The largest of the three bedrooms is the one to the east, it is also the one you are given and shown to if you rent a room at the inn. It contains a double bed coming out of the south wall with an empty unlocked long [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") to the left, with a large coin purse hidden to the left of it, in the southeastern corner. Against the east wall are two dressers containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and having a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), two bottles of alto wine, three bottles of Nord mead, a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") and a copy of the uncommon book *[Songs of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Songs of Skyrim")* on top. To the right of the bed, in the southwestern corner is a small square wooden table and chair with a fancy [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") and [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") on the top of the table. Against the north wall are a pile of three food sacks.

Opposite this bedroom are two more, with the left-hand one containing a single owned bed in the northwestern corner and a round wooden table and chair to the right of it. On the table is a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") and three common books. At the foot of the bed is an unlocked chest containing leveled items, with a tall wardrobe against the north wall. The right-hand room contains two single owned beds against the south wall, separated by an unlocked chest containing leveled items and a round wooden table and chair in the northwestern corner, with two common books on top.

## Notes

- Once you have [helped](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Few_Words_with_You#Iddra_and_Roggi_Knot-Beard "Skyrim:A Few Words with You") Iddra by speaking to Roggi, the beds in the small bedrooms become free to use.
- [Kjeld the Younger](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjeld_the_Younger "Skyrim:Kjeld the Younger") won't take over as publican of the inn if his parents are dead.
- Other than most barrels and sacks, all containers in the inn are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") and safe to store items in.

## Bugs

- If anything happens to Iddra, Kjeld will take over room rentals, but not merchant services. In the absence of Iddra, it is impossible to buy or sell things in Kynesgrove.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue. Kjeld can now sell innkeeper merchandise as well if he takes over as the publican of Braidwood Inn.
- The music that plays inside is incorrectly set to environmental exploration music, rather than the standard tavern music.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.1, fixes this bug.
- The AI counter markers in Braidwood Inn have no ownership assigned which can cause NPCs other than the vendors to use them.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.1, fixes this bug.
