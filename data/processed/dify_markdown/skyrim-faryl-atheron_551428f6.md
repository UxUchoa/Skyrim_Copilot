**Faryl Atheron** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") who lives with his brother [Aval](/wiki/Skyrim:Aval_Atheron "Skyrim:Aval Atheron") and his sister [Suvaris](/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron "Skyrim:Suvaris Atheron") at the [Atheron Residence](/wiki/Skyrim:Atheron_Residence "Skyrim:Atheron Residence") in the Gray Quarter of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). He is employed as a farmhand by [Bolfrida Brandy-Mug](/wiki/Skyrim:Bolfrida_Brandy-Mug "Skyrim:Bolfrida Brandy-Mug") at her [farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Brandy-Mug_Farm "Skyrim:Brandy-Mug Farm") just outside the city gates.

Faryl spends every evening between 8pm and 2am wandering the streets of Windhelm, so he is usually there at the Gray Quarter during the night for [Rolff Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Rolff_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Rolff Stone-Fist")'s loud insults. From 2am till 8am he sleeps at his house. During the day, Morndas through Fredas, Faryl can be found at the steps of [New Gnisis Cornerclub](/wiki/Skyrim:New_Gnisis_Cornerclub "Skyrim:New Gnisis Cornerclub") where his brother Aval joins him with a drink around 7pm. Faryl works at Brandy-Mug Farm on Loredas and Sundas from 8am until 8pm.

He wears a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0001be1b "Skyrim:Clothing"), and carries around the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Atheron_Residence_Key "Skyrim:Atheron Residence Key") to his family house along with a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

He sometimes talks about his employer.

:   *"Bolfrida is certainly grating, but she pays well. Thankfully nobody from the city can see me out here."*

He does not share the hatred towards the [Nords](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") as many other Dunmer do and he will tell you that;

:   *"Farm work is hard work. It's good to relax when I can, but I still have to listen to my brothers*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *harping about "injustices.""*

Although he does like some aspects of his job, Faryl will explain to you that it's not the first job he would have wanted to take:

:   *"Of course working for Bolfrida isn't my first choice. But what else can a Dunmer do out here?"*

He will also tell you about his brother's dislike of how he and his sister work for Nords:

:   *"My sister and I both work for the Nords. Our brother is ashamed of us, but at least we can affort*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *to eat."*

### Conversations

When working at the farm, he can be seen having the following conversations with his employer:

:   **Faryl:** *"I don't think we're going to get those potatoes to come in."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Well of course, not with an attitude like that!"*
:   **Faryl:** *"The ground is practically frozen. We can't dig down far enough."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Come on, we just need a little elbow grease."*

They'll talk about what it would be like to try growing crops in [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind").

:   **Bolfrida:** *"I'll bet this is better than trying to grow something in Morrowind, though, isn't it?"*
:   **Faryl:** *"Firstly, I've never even seen Morrowind. Secondly, in volcanic soil the plants practically grow themselves."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Oh, my, I hadn't thought of that."*
:   **Faryl:** *"There are places in the world that aren't wrapped in ice, you know."*

They'll also talk about growing crops in permafrost.

:   **Bolfrida:** *"Now, I've been reading about the best ways to grow corn in permafrost..."*
:   **Faryl:** *"I keep telling you, without a warming enchantment, it will never grow past your ankles."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Right, but if you plow the soil with [fire salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts "Skyrim:Fire Salts")..."*
:   **Faryl:** *"Then you've salted it and nothing will grow at all. Genius."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Oh dear, I hadn't thought of that."*

## Notes

- In one of his greetings, Faryl mentions having to listen to his "brothers", despite only having a singular brother, Aval.
- Version 1.2.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") alters Faryl's schedule so that he works on weekdays and relaxes in the weekend, as opposed to the other way around, based on his dialogue implying he's working most of the time.
