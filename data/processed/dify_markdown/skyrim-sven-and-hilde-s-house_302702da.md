Sven and Hilde's House

**Sven and Hilde's House** is a small residence at the western end of [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"), located next to the [Riverwood Trader](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood_Trader "Skyrim:Riverwood Trader").

It is the home of [Hilde](/wiki/Skyrim:Hilde "Skyrim:Hilde") and her son [Sven](/wiki/Skyrim:Sven "Skyrim:Sven"), the local bard. It consists of one zone, *[Sven and Hilde's House](#Sven_and_Hilde.27s_House)*.

## Sven and Hilde's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-interior-Sven_and_Hilde%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Sven_and_Hilde%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sven_and_Hilde%27s_House.jpg)

Sven and Hilde's House, interior

It is a one-room house with no dividing lines between the areas. Straight ahead is the fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") and a kettle containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") at its base, and a respawning meat barrel to the right. To the left of the door is a chair, a few barrels, and a non-respawning cupboard in the corner with a respawning food barrel nearby. To the left of the fireplace is a table with a bucket containing two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), one bottle each of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and two leveled one-handed weapons—a [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") and a [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"). Both weapons can be up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") quality at level 23. Hanging above the table are harvestable [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and [raw food](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Food "Skyrim:Raw Food"). There is one dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), two portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), and three [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant").

To the right of the door is a non-respawning dresser with a bottle of [Black-Briar Mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead"), two samples each of elves ear and frost mirriam, and four garlic bulbs sitting on it, along with a respawning food barrel beside it. Hanging above the dresser is another set of harvestable ingredients—one dried frost mirriam, one dried elves ear, and three garlic braids. There is a small table with two chairs in the middle of the house by the fireplace, which serves as the dining table; it is set with one bottle each of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and alto wine, as well as one loaf and one piece of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). The shelves to the right of the fire contain some general household items, and four bottles each of wine and alto wine. There are two owned beds by the far right wall. The bed with a chest (non-respawning) at one end is Sven's and the one without a chest is Hilde's. Between the beds is a non-respawning end table holding one bottle of Nord mead.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice-level lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Sven%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Sven's House") to which is carried by both Hilde and Sven.
- After completing the quest [A Lovely Letter](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Lovely_Letter "Skyrim:A Lovely Letter") in Sven's favor, all low-value items become free to take, and both beds become usable.
- There is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning "Skyrim:Tanning") to the right of the front door, as well as two respawning food barrels a short distance to the left. Another two respawning food barrels can be found behind the house, in the east corner.
