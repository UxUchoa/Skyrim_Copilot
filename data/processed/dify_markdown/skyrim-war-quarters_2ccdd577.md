The **War Quarters** is a small [Dwarven building](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwarven Ruins") in [Blackreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackreach "Skyrim:Blackreach"). It is set into the southeastern wall of the cavern, northeast of the [Great Lift at Raldbthar](/wiki/Skyrim:Great_Lift_at_Raldbthar "Skyrim:Great Lift at Raldbthar"). The building is devoid of any [creatures](/wiki/Skyrim:Creatures "Skyrim:Creatures") or [automatons](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Automatons "Skyrim:Dwarven Automatons").

## War Quarters

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-interior-War_Quarters_Sleep_Chamber.jpg/200px-SR-interior-War_Quarters_Sleep_Chamber.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-War_Quarters_Sleep_Chamber.jpg)

The sleeping chamber

It is a typical deserted Dwemer building. Upon entering, the first room has a balcony at the rear with a door accessing it, and a doorway in front of the balcony to the south. The southern doorway leads into another empty room, with an unlocked door to the east that opens into a T-junction. To the south is a sleeping chamber, with seven beds arranged in a fan formation. To the north is another empty room, with a stone double bed in an alcove to the north and a door to the west. This door opens onto the balcony at the rear of the first room, which has two unlocked Dwemer [chests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") along its edge.

## Notes

- One copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, MidYear, v6](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_MidYear,_v6 "Skyrim:2920, MidYear, v6")* can be found on a table at the top of the stands overlooking the arena-like ring just west of the War Quarters.
