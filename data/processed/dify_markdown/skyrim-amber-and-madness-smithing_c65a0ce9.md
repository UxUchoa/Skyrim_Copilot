## Quick Walkthrough

1. Investigate the area north of [Mistwatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Mistwatch "Skyrim:Mistwatch").
2. Kill [Evethra](/wiki/Skyrim:Evethra "Skyrim:Evethra").
3. Read [Evethra's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Evethra%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Evethra's Journal").

## Detailed Walkthrough

After defeating [Thoron](/wiki/Skyrim:Thoron "Skyrim:Thoron") for [Restoring Order](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoring_Order "Skyrim:Restoring Order") (or by sneaking to avoid his detection while he is still alive), you can loot the items on his work table. The note *[Note on Amber and Madness Ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_Amber_and_Madness_Ore "Skyrim:Note on Amber and Madness Ore")* will give you a [miscellaneous quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests") to investigate the area north of Mistwatch.

Travel to Mistwatch, a tower just east of the Atronach Stone. In the geyser fields just north of the tower, you'll find a stone altar and the camp of the mad blacksmith [Evethra](/wiki/Skyrim:Evethra "Skyrim:Evethra"). Approach her and the objective will update to "Kill Evethra". Do so, then loot [her journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Evethra%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Evethra's Journal") along with the unique [Arcane Blacksmith's Apron](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Blacksmith%27s_Apron "Skyrim:Arcane Blacksmith's Apron").

Read the journal to complete the quest and gain the ability to craft Amber/Madness items using the [Glass Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Smithing "Skyrim:Glass Smithing") and [Ebony Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Smithing "Skyrim:Ebony Smithing") perks respectively.

## Notes

- The [Shadowrend](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowrend "Skyrim:Shadowrend") [creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") takes place to the west in close proximity to this quest's location.
- Due to the large number of hostiles in the area, Evethra may leave her camp to fight [bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear"), [mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab"), or a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon"). She is marked [essential](/wiki/Skyrim:Essential_NPCs "Skyrim:Essential NPCs") until you receive the objective to kill her, but can be difficult to locate because of this.
- Although when you read the journal there is a message "You can now create new equipment with Glass Smithing" and a similar one for Ebony Smithing, to forge the new items you need to use the "Misc" section of the menu, not the Glass or Ebony section.
