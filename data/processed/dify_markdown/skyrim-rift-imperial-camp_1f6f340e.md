Rift Imperial Camp

**Rift Imperial Camp** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Camps "Skyrim:Imperial Camps") in [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"), located south of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") and west of the [Ruins of Bthalft](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Bthalft "Skyrim:Ruins of Bthalft").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e1/SR-place-Rift_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rift_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Rift_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg)

Legate Fasendil

The camp is normally commanded by [Legate Fasendil](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Fasendil "Skyrim:Legate Fasendil"), except during the related quests, at which point [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") will assume command.

## Rift Imperial Camp

It is a very small and compact Imperial camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. Due to the proximity of the [Ruins of Bthalft](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Bthalft "Skyrim:Ruins of Bthalft"), there are regular skirmishes between the Imperials in the camp and the bandits in the ruins. To the northeast and southeast of the camp are barricades, while to the northwest and southwest the camp is butted up against rocks rising above. South of the campfire is the Legate's tent, containing an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. To the right of this tent is the command tent, containing an end table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. To the west is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"), while to the north is the hospital tent, which contains three bed rolls occupied by two wounded soldiers and a small table holding a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). Outside is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and some barrels. Behind the hospital tent are two picketed [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse"). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. To the east of the campfire is a row of three small tents containing unowned bed rolls. Behind these tents is the workstation for the [Imperial quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Imperial Quartermaster"). It consists of an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), with a wooden table covered in various [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") and [steel weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") and [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor"). More are behind, on a barrel and some crates.

Any time [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift") is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Joining the Stormcloaks"), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.

## Notes

- Two ore veins are near the camp: a [corundum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore "Skyrim:Corundum Ore") vein southeast of the camp, and an [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") vein northeast of the camp.
