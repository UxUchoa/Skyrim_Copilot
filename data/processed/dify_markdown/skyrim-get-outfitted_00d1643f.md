## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [General Tullius](/wiki/Skyrim:General_Tullius "Skyrim:General Tullius") after clearing out [Fort Hraggstad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Hraggstad "Skyrim:Fort Hraggstad").
2. Go to [Beirand](/wiki/Skyrim:Beirand "Skyrim:Beirand").
3. Pick Heavy, Medium, or Light Imperial armor.

### You're In the Army Now

After successfully clearing the [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") from [Fort Hraggstad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Hraggstad "Skyrim:Fort Hraggstad"), your usefulness has been proven to [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke"). Your oath has been sworn; you are officially an Imperial soldier and protector of the Empire. It's time you look the part. [General Tullius](/wiki/Skyrim:General_Tullius "Skyrim:General Tullius") tells you to go to [Beirand](/wiki/Skyrim:Beirand "Skyrim:Beirand"), the [smith](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Blacksmith "Skyrim:Solitude Blacksmith"), to get outfitted with your own [Imperial armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Armor "Skyrim:Imperial Armor"). Talk to Beirand and choose your preferred weight:

| Armor Type | Helmet | Boots | Shield | Bracers | Armor |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Heavy | Imperial Helmet | Imperial Boots | Imperial Shield | Imperial Bracers | Imperial Armor |
| Medium | Imperial Light Helmet | Imperial Light Boots | Imperial Shield | Imperial Light Bracers | Studded Imperial Armor |
| Light | Imperial Light Helmet | Imperial Light Boots | Imperial Light Shield | Imperial Light Bracers | Imperial Light Armor |

When you are properly outfitted, Beirand will admonish you: *"Now, that's it. One free outfit per soldier. You lose that, or get it banged up, you'll need to pay like everyone else."*

## Notes

- It is not possible to do this quest if you first join the legion by giving the [Jagged Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Jagged_Crown "Skyrim:Jagged Crown") to General Tullius.
- Note that the Medium option contains one heavy armor piece (shield) and the rest are light armor pieces.
- Although Beirand tells you that you'll need to pay if you lose or damage the Imperial armor set he gave you, you can forge a new set yourself.
  - [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch") 1.9 makes it possible to forge Imperial Light Armor.
- After the completion of this quest, Beirand is no longer [essential](/wiki/Skyrim:Essential_NPCs#Beirund "Skyrim:Essential NPCs").
- Beirand's inventory will carry less items after this quest.[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Get_Outfitted)*]

## Bugs

- If you join the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") (either [initially](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Joining the Stormcloaks") or by defecting after [The Jagged Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Jagged_Crown_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Jagged Crown (Imperial)")), this quest is never shut down which leaves Beirand in a perpetual essential alias.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
