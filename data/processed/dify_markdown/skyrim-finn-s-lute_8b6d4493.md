## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Inge Six Fingers](/wiki/Skyrim:Inge_Six_Fingers "Skyrim:Inge Six Fingers").
2. Locate [Finn's Lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Finn%27s_Lute_(item) "Skyrim:Finn's Lute (item)") in [Stony Creek Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave "Skyrim:Stony Creek Cave").
3. Return for your reward of a 1-point increase to all [Stealth](/wiki/Skyrim:Stealth "Skyrim:Stealth") skills.

### A Unique Lute

Right after the Burning of [King Olaf](/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_One-Eye "Skyrim:Olaf One-Eye") ends the quest [Tending the Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Tending_the_Flames "Skyrim:Tending the Flames"), talking to the otherwise authoritarian [Inge Six Fingers](/wiki/Skyrim:Inge_Six_Fingers "Skyrim:Inge Six Fingers") will be somewhat wistful. When asked, she will tell you that a year ago, thieves broke into the College and stole her most precious item: the very first eight course lute ever created, namely [Finn's Lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Finn%27s_Lute_(item) "Skyrim:Finn's Lute (item)"). As the Dean of Lute, Inge has been devastated ever since, but recently learned the location of the thieves, specifically [Stony Creek Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave "Skyrim:Stony Creek Cave"), a cave in southeastern [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch").

### Stony Creek Cave

A small pond hides the entrance to the cave, complete with a dock and a boat. Upon entering, the sight of a waterfall and a dead Breton hanging from a tree trunk hints at things to come. Follow the only available path upstream and fight the expected [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") trying to protect their treasure. When the path splits in two, head right and beware of the [swinging mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Swinging_Mace "Skyrim:Swinging Mace") trap right before the chamber with the bandit leader. Pick the fight and search the body for *[Treasure Map X](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_X "Skyrim:Treasure Map X")*, then look around the area for interesting items, including a copy of the [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mystery of Talara, Part 4](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_Part_4 "Skyrim:Mystery of Talara, Part 4")*, an [unusual gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem"), along with a few ingredients and an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"). Finally, open the chest and claim the lute and some valuable loot.

### An Unexpected Lesson

Before exiting the cave, it is possible to investigate the rest of the cave which will net you another fight against a bandit, along with a locked treasure chest, two [moonstone veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein") and a waterfall hiding a shortcut from [Kagrenzel](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrenzel "Skyrim:Kagrenzel"). Exit the mine the way you came and return to the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College"). Back at the College, Inge Six Fingers will be overwhelmed with joy. As she is unable to pay what the lute is worth, she will instead teach you a few tricks she has picked up over the years, and raise each of your [Stealth](/wiki/Skyrim:Stealth "Skyrim:Stealth") skills by one point. This includes [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy"), [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor"), [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking"), [Pickpocket](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket "Skyrim:Pickpocket"), [Sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak"), and [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech").

## Bugs

- After you turn in the quest, the lute will remain in your inventory, still marked as a quest item.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You can remove the quest item using the Console and the following console code: `Player.removeitem 000DABAB 1`
- Finding the lute before the quest is assigned may result in the lute being stuck your inventory as a quest item even after finding Inge Six Fingers and telling her you have found it.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.
- Finding the lute early may also result the quest objective becoming stuck in your journal, even after returning the lute and receiving your reward.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.
  - To resolve this, click on the "I've found Finn's Lute" option in dialogue when you speak to Inge, rather than the "Why are you sad?" option. It works out just as if you had accepted the quest and then found it.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Alternatively, you can use the console code `setstage BardsCollegeLute 40` after accepting the quest, which will update it to telling you to give Inge the lute, allowing the quest to proceed as normal.
