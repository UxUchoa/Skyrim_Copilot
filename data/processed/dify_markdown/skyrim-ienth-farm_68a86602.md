Ienth Farm

**Ienth Farm** is the home of [Garyn Ienth](/wiki/Skyrim:Garyn_Ienth "Skyrim:Garyn Ienth") and [Milore Ienth](/wiki/Skyrim:Milore_Ienth "Skyrim:Milore Ienth") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

It is in the marketplace between [Glover Mallory's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory%27s_House "Skyrim:Glover Mallory's House") to the southeast and [Alor House](/wiki/Skyrim:Alor_House "Skyrim:Alor House") to the northeast.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-place-Ienth_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ienth_Farm_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Ienth_Farm_02.jpg)

Ienth Farm field

To the left of the entrance is where Milore Ienth generally sells her wares, with an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs"), an open box containing two leveled [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Standard_Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), a second open box containing four [torchbug thoraxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug_Thorax "Skyrim:Torchbug Thorax") and a bowl of [fire salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts "Skyrim:Fire Salts"), two unusable barrels, one of which has three more bowls of fire salts on top, and a crate all nearby. To the right of the entrance is where Garyn Ienth generally sells his own wares, with a long open box containing fourteen [ash yams](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam"), a second open box containing thirteen more ash yams, an ash yam urn, an empty open chest, three crates, and an unusable barrel all nearby. The entrance has an apprentice-level lock.

The following harvestable crops and plants can be found primarily in the field north of the house, with a few plants near the southwest corner of the building beyond more unusable barrels and an empty cart:

| 21 Ash Yam | 10 [Scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw")  1 [Trama Root](/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root "Skyrim:Trama Root") |

### Ienth Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/SR-interior-Ienth_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ienth_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ienth_Farm.jpg)

Ienth Farm interior

The entrance leads into the upper floor of the house. Along the south wall are four unusable barrels, two of which have a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a food urn on top, respectively, two food barrels, two crates, one of which has a copy of *[The Poison Song, Book III](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song,_Book_III "Skyrim:The Poison Song, Book III")* on top, an open box containing four ash yams, two chairs and a table, and a bench. The table holds two [goat cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), a bottle of [shein](/wiki/Skyrim:Shein "Skyrim:Shein"), a bottle of [sujamma](/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma "Skyrim:Sujamma"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and a basket containing three ash yams. Near the west wall is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand in front of the fire, with two chairs and a small round table holding a bottle of shein, a bottle of sujamma, and a *[note](/wiki/Skyrim:To_Milore_from_Nilara "Skyrim:To Milore from Nilara")* to Milore nearby. Along the north wall is a wood pile, a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), two open boxes containing a total of ten ash yams, six sacks, five crates, two unusable barrels, a sideboard holding three loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), three bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a narrow table, and a food urn. In the middle of the room, stairs lead down to the lower floor, with a bottle of shein, a wood pile with two pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"), three unusable barrels, one of which has copies of *[The Poison Song, Book I](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song,_Book_II "Skyrim:The Poison Song, Book II")* and *[Book II](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song,_Book_II "Skyrim:The Poison Song, Book II")* on top, and a bench around the sides of the stairs.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/SR-interior-Ienth_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ienth_Farm_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ienth_Farm_02.jpg)

Ienth Farm alchemy lab

At the bottom of the stairs, to the right of a doorway are two food urns. Beyond the doorway is an area with an alchemy area to the south, a farm area to the north, and a room beyond a door ahead to the west. In the central area is an ingredient barrel with copies of *[The Poison Song, Book VI](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song,_Book_VI "Skyrim:The Poison Song, Book VI")* and *[The Red Year, Vol. I](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Year,_Vol._I "Skyrim:The Red Year, Vol. I")* on top and three unusable barrels. In the alchemy area, to the left are three ingredient barrels, two of which have an ingredient urn and a wooden bowl containing three [slaughterfish eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg") on top, respectively, and a sideboard holding three samples of [Felsaad tern feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Felsaad_Tern_Feathers "Skyrim:Felsaad Tern Feathers"), two [salt piles](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a [falmer ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Ear "Skyrim:Falmer Ear"), a sample of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), a [creep cluster](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster"), a [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") flower, and a [potion of light feet](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Light_Feet "Skyrim:Potion of Light Feet"). In the center is an alchemy lab with a [potion of illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Illusion "Skyrim:Potion of Illusion") on top. To the right is a set of shelves holding two [skeever tails](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever_Tail "Skyrim:Skeever Tail"), a bowl of [frost salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Salts "Skyrim:Frost Salts"), three [taproots](/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot "Skyrim:Taproot"), a [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), a falmer ear, two [potions of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a [potion of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), a [potion of resist shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Shock "Skyrim:Potion of Resist Shock"), two bowls of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"), a [potion of cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Potion of Cure Disease"), and four sacks. Finally, to the right of the shelves is an open box containing three [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flowers, a sample of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), and a sample of [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot").

In the farm area are three circular ash pile plots growing a total of sixteen ash yams and ten scathecraw plants, several open boxes and baskets containing a total of thirty-two ash yams, six ash yam urns, five straw bales, and a wooden table holding two ash yams, a scathecraw sample, and a bottle of sujamma.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-interior-Ienth_Farm_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ienth_Farm_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ienth_Farm_03.jpg)

Ienth Farm bedroom

The room to the west is the bedroom. In the middle of the room is an owned double bed with an empty open box at its foot. Near the south wall is a chest, a small round table holding an empty [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem") and three [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"), a bench, a set of shelves, a chair, and another small round table holding two medium coin purses. The set of shelves holds two bottles of shein and copies of *[The Real Barenziah, v1](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Real_Barenziah,_v1 "Skyrim:The Real Barenziah, v1")* and *[v2](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Real_Barenziah,_v2 "Skyrim:The Real Barenziah, v2")*, *[Ancestors and the Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestors_and_the_Dunmer "Skyrim:Ancestors and the Dunmer")*, and *[The True Noble's Code](/wiki/Skyrim:The_True_Noble%27s_Code "Skyrim:The True Noble's Code")*. Near the north wall are three unusable barrels, a food barrel, two chairs, and a table holding four loose coins and copies of *[The Axe Man](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Axe_Man "Skyrim:The Axe Man")* and *[Myths of Sheogorath](/wiki/Skyrim:Myths_of_Sheogorath "Skyrim:Myths of Sheogorath")*.
