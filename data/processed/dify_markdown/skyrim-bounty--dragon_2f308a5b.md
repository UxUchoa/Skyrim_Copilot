## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak with an innkeeper, jarl, or steward to receive the bounty.
2. Kill the [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") at the indicated location.
3. Speak with the jarl or steward of the hold to receive your reward.

### Here There Be Dragons

After your adventures at [Whiterun's](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") [Western Watchtower](/wiki/Skyrim:Western_Watchtower "Skyrim:Western Watchtower") during [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising"), a proliferation of dragons are about. In your travels, ask an innkeep, jarl or steward for some work. You may learn that a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") has been harassing travelers and citizens around its [lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Lairs "Skyrim:Dragon Lairs"), and the jarl of the hold has put out a bounty on the dragon. You are an enterprising sort, with newly acquired [dragon soul absorbing powers](/wiki/Skyrim:Dovahkiin "Skyrim:Dovahkiin") and [shouts](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts"), so take up the job.

### The Dragon Hunter

Seek out the offending dragon in its lair. There is a [wide variety of dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon#Abilities "Skyrim:Dragon") that you may encounter in each lair you visit. Once defeated, and the dragon soul is absorbed, be sure to remove the [dragon bones](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bone "Skyrim:Dragon Bone") and [dragon scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Scales "Skyrim:Dragon Scales"). These are quite valuable in the local shops, and can be used to make and improve [dragonbone and dragonscale armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Armor "Skyrim:Dragon Armor").

### The Bounty

Once the dragon is dead, you will be directed to the [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") or [steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Steward "Skyrim:Steward") of the hold to receive your payment.

## Notes

- If you are tasked to kill a dragon at a lair that you have already cleared, the dragon there will respawn. However, killing a respawned dragon (whether it has respawned because of this quest, or naturally after 10 or 30 days) will probably result in not getting a dragon soul.
- You need to have completed [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") first before receiving this quest.

## Bugs

- The dragon at Mount Anthor may not spawn, and the quest target will point to an incorrect location.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") command `setstage BQ04 100` to skip to the next stage, or `setstage BQ04 200` to jump directly to quest completion.
