**Eimar** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") who works at the [Honningbrew Meadery](/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Meadery "Skyrim:Honningbrew Meadery").

He wakes at 6am for a one-hour breakfast, then starts his workday. During work, he can usually be found sitting at the bar, drinking, or cleaning the place. He takes a break at noon to eat for one hour. His workday ends at 8pm, when he has a four-hour dinner before going to bed at midnight.

Eimar wears farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0006C1D8 "Skyrim:Clothes") with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452F "Skyrim:Boots"). He wields a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") in combat.

## Related Quests

- **[Dampened Spirits](/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits "Skyrim:Dampened Spirits")**: Put [Maven Black-Briar's](/wiki/Skyrim:Maven_Black-Briar "Skyrim:Maven Black-Briar") competition out of business.

## Dialogue

Eimar seems very happy with his job and has nothing but praise when speaking to him:

:   *"If you need to sample some of the finest mead in Skyrim, you're in the right place."*
:   *"If you're looking to purchase any mead, speak to the owner, [Sabjorn](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabjorn "Skyrim:Sabjorn")."*
:   *"We're a small meadery, but growing quickly. Sabjorn's only been running this place for a short time now and look at it."*
:   *"[Mallus](/wiki/Skyrim:Mallus_Maccius "Skyrim:Mallus Maccius") is teaching me everything he knows about brewing. Hopefully, I can take over his position some day."*

However, after the quest [Dampened Spirits](/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits "Skyrim:Dampened Spirits"), he will no longer be as enthusiastic:

:   *"It's a shame the [Black-Briar Meadery](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Meadery "Skyrim:Black-Briar Meadery") took this place over. I never liked it as much as our [Honningbrew](/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Mead "Skyrim:Honningbrew Mead")."*
:   *"If you're looking to purchase any mead, speak to the brewmaster, Mallus."*

## Bugs

- During Dampened Spirits, Eimar has some lines at the [Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare") that don't make sense for him to use while he's there. One wasn't conditioned to the location he's in, and the other was using an incorrect function to check if the latter is finished or not.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.7, fixes this bug.
