Whiterun

**Whiterun** is a major city in the center of Skyrim, and the capital of [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold"). Its central location makes it the province's major commercial hub, as well as a crucial strategic point in the [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") between the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") loyalists and [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") rebels, as control of Whiterun Hold grants access to all surrounding lands. Whiterun is initially nominally aligned with the Empire, but [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") [Balgruuf the Greater](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater") seems to care more about the people of Whiterun than either side of the conflict, rendering the hold effectively neutral.

Whiterun comprises three districts arranged in tiers and connected by stairways. The Plains District is the lowest and contains the city's marketplace and shops, inns, a few homes, and the city gates. The Wind District is the main residential area; it features the [Temple of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Temple of Kynareth"), [Jorrvaskr](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorrvaskr "Skyrim:Jorrvaskr"), the ancient mead hall and headquarters of the [Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions"), and the [Skyforge](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge "Skyrim:Skyforge"), worked by the greatest blacksmith in Skyrim, [Eorlund Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Eorlund_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Eorlund Gray-Mane"). The Cloud District is dominated by the government seat [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach") and its [dungeon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach_Dungeon "Skyrim:Dragonsreach Dungeon"), and is the highest point in Whiterun. Local businesses and services include the smithy and weapons shop [Warmaiden's](/wiki/Skyrim:Warmaiden%27s "Skyrim:Warmaiden's"), the tavern and hunting supply store [The Drunken Huntsman](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Drunken_Huntsman "Skyrim:The Drunken Huntsman"), [Belethor's General Goods](/wiki/Skyrim:Belethor%27s_General_Goods "Skyrim:Belethor's General Goods"), the alchemy shop [Arcadia's Cauldron](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcadia%27s_Cauldron "Skyrim:Arcadia's Cauldron"), and [The Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare") inn. The jarl's court wizard [Farengar Secret-Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Farengar_Secret-Fire "Skyrim:Farengar Secret-Fire") has spells and enchanting supplies for sale and can teach you the basics of enchanting.

For more information on Whiterun, see the [lore article](/wiki/Lore:Whiterun "Lore:Whiterun").

## Notes

- [Breezehome](/wiki/Skyrim:Breezehome "Skyrim:Breezehome") is a [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Houses "Skyrim:Houses") available for purchase after completion of the quest [Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (quest)"). It may be purchased from [Proventus Avenicci](/wiki/Skyrim:Proventus_Avenicci "Skyrim:Proventus Avenicci") for 5,000 gold, as a reward for retrieving the [Dragonstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonstone "Skyrim:Dragonstone") for [Jarl Balgruuf's](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater") court mage, [Farengar Secret-Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Farengar_Secret-Fire "Skyrim:Farengar Secret-Fire"). If it is not purchased at this point, and Whiterun is conquered by the Stormcloaks, it may be purchased from [Brill](/wiki/Skyrim:Brill "Skyrim:Brill") as a reward for [helping the Stormcloaks conquer Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)").
- Unused data in the game files indicates at least six buildings were removed from Whiterun: Maiden-Loom Manor, Pathway Guard Tower, Shrine of Talos, Ulfberth's House, Whiterun Jail, and Wintersand Manor.
- Approximately 220 alchemy [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") are freely available in Whiterun, with a respawn time of 30 days. These include 71 purple mountain flower, 41 blue mountain flower, 30 lavender, 23 tundra cotton, and 22 red mountain flower found throughout the city, as well as 14 nightshade in the graveyard outside the Hall of the Dead and 7 Nordic barnacle in the pool outside Dragonsreach.
- Whiterun also [appears](/wiki/Arena:Whiterun "Arena:Whiterun") in *[Arena](/wiki/Arena:Arena "Arena:Arena")*.
- There are 2 initial conversation scenes may be missed. [Braith](/wiki/Skyrim:Braith "Skyrim:Braith") and [Lars Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:Lars_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Lars Battle-Born")'s conversation, [Amren](/wiki/Skyrim:Amren "Skyrim:Amren") and [Saffir](/wiki/Skyrim:Saffir "Skyrim:Saffir")'s conversation only take place between 8am~8pm, and if the player never enters the related area.

## Bugs

- If you sprint as soon as you fast travel to Whiterun, the game may freeze.
- [Louis Letrush](/wiki/Skyrim:Louis_Letrush "Skyrim:Louis Letrush") may be found partially embedded into the ground just outside the city.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.8, fixes this bug.
- If you finish [In My Time Of Need](/wiki/Skyrim:In_My_Time_Of_Need "Skyrim:In My Time Of Need"), the two [Alik'r Warriors](/wiki/Skyrim:Alik%27r_Warrior "Skyrim:Alik'r Warrior") who started it will return to the city.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- If the Stormcloaks take Whiterun, two guards will spawn on the left side of the main gate instead of one on either side.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- Some of the items at the vendors in the marketplace aren't owned by them, allowing you to pick them up for free.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.1, fixes this bug.
- A dialogue line referring to the Gildergreen here can be spoken by [children](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") in any city due to a missing condition check.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Hearthfire Patch"), version 1.1.2, fixes this bug.
- You can leave the city without using the main gate by jumping on a barrel and over a wall near the gate. You can also do it by using the red plant formation on the north corner of House Gray-Mane.
