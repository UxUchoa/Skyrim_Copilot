Left Hand Mine

**Left Hand Mine** is a small [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron "Skyrim:Iron") [mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mines "Skyrim:Mines") and [settlement](/wiki/Skyrim:Settlements "Skyrim:Settlements") southeast of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). The mine contains only one zone, *[Left Hand Mine](#Left_Hand_Mine)*; the settlement's residences are detailed on separate pages.

The mine is owned by [Skaggi Scar-Face](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaggi_Scar-Face "Skyrim:Skaggi Scar-Face"), who will [buy iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Mine_Ore "Skyrim:Mine Ore") from you.

### Exterior

Before crossing the stone bridge to the mining settlement, a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") is on the ground to the left. Another is across the bridge to the right. The three buildings that make up the settlement feature the [Dwemer](/wiki/Lore:Dwemer "Lore:Dwemer") architecture of nearby Markarth. [Daighre's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Daighre%27s_House "Skyrim:Daighre's House") and [Skaggi's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaggi%27s_House "Skyrim:Skaggi's House") are on the east side. Opposite the houses to the west is the [Miner's Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Left Hand Miner's Barracks"); with *[Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")* installed, a [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry "Skyrim:Stone Quarry") is behind the barracks. A [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") and some [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken") wander the settlement, and outside the barracks are two [chicken eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"). The settlement initially shelters two refugees from [Kolskeggr Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine") who were displaced by [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"). Talking to them or Skaggi triggers the [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine_(quest) "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine (quest)") to reclaim Kolskeggr Mine.

A [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") and four pieces of [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") on a crate are near the mine entrance in the southwestern corner of the settlement.

### Left Hand Mine

The entry tunnel goes down past a small side shaft on the right. A few specimens of [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap"), [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), and [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") grow at the mouth of this small shaft. The first of five iron ore veins is found there. The table ahead holds two [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"). More white caps grow behind the empty barrels to the right of the table.

A crosscut divides the mine into east and west. The shaft to the west has three pairs of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots") next to the shelves and three more pickaxes. On the ground is a basket holding six [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") bottles and a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") containing a potion and a random piece of [jewelry](/wiki/Skyrim:Jewelry "Skyrim:Jewelry"). The end of the shaft widens, and there are two iron ore veins, one to the west and one to the east.

To continue to the other end of the crosscut, the shaft to the east has a small table with an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet"). Strangely, two [red mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") plants are growing here; one on the left side of the shaft and the other under the scaffolding ahead. When the shaft opens up, an iron ore vein is found to the east just before the scaffolding. There are several [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks in this area. The final iron ore vein is on the north wall accessed from the platform at the top of the scaffolding. A small table on the platform has some loose gold coins, with a pair of [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") by the table.
