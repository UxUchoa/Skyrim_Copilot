Become Thane of Falkreath

## Radiant Options

This is a radiant quest which you can receive from either [Siddgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Siddgeir "Skyrim:Siddgeir") or [Dengeir of Stuhn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dengeir_of_Stuhn "Skyrim:Dengeir of Stuhn"), whichever is Jarl of Falkreath. The Jarl lives at the [Jarl's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Falkreath) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Falkreath)") in [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath").

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Befriend the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath").
2. Assist three people of [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold").
3. Return to the Jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-quest-Thane_of_Falkreath.jpeg/200px-SR-quest-Thane_of_Falkreath.jpeg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Thane_of_Falkreath.jpeg)

The invitation from the Jarl([HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire"))

This is an umbrella quest for several other quests that must be completed if you wish to become Thane of Falkreath. In order to receive it, you must first aid the current Jarl of Falkreath. If Siddgeir is Jarl, the previous quest will usually be to [kill a Bandit Leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Bandit_Leader_(Jarl) "Skyrim:Kill the Bandit Leader (Jarl)"); however, if he was ousted from Falkreath during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") and reinstated following [The Battle for Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Neugrad"), that is sufficient to begin this quest. If Dengeir is Jarl, whether he was installed via [Rescue from Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Rescue from Fort Neugrad") or [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"), then the completion of that quest is sufficient to fulfill the prerequisite.

Once you have completed the prerequisite, speak with the jarl again and he will ask you to [assist the people of Falkreath hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People "Skyrim:Making Friends and Influencing People") three times in order to become Thane. This can be difficult as there are only a limited number of quests in the hold and the quest givers are non-essential, which could result in their death and irrevocable loss of potential quests. Note, however, that you can also befriend merchants if you have the [Investor](/wiki/Skyrim:Investor "Skyrim:Investor") perk by investing in their stores.

### Reward

After helping three inhabitants, the journal tells you to return to the jarl. The reward, the [Blade of Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_Falkreath "Skyrim:Blade of Falkreath"), is a random, leveled, enchanted sword. There is a 50% chance that it will be a sword ([one-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed")), and a 50% chance it will be a greatsword ([two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed")). You will also receive a pittance of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") and the title of Thane for Falkreath Hold. With the [Hearthfire add-on](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire"), you will also be rewarded a personal [housecarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl"), [Rayya](/wiki/Skyrim:Rayya "Skyrim:Rayya").

## Notes

- Favours to Falkreath citizens done as part of Daedric quests do not count toward the total.
- The quest may become nearly impossible to complete if you finish the [Rescue from Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Rescue from Fort Neugrad") quest of the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") before you enter Falkreath, because the game does not accept radiant quests as favours.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") This can be fixed with the console codes `setstage favorjarlsmakefriends 95` and `setstage favor258 20`
- Assistance to citizens done prior to accepting this quest counts towards the total.

## Bugs

- Siddgeir may become stuck in a "Foe" relationship with you if you've killed his housecarl for the Dark Brotherhood (even if you did it secretly). If so, he'll never grant you Thaneship.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. The relationship will only be harmed if you are caught killing the housecarl.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You can fix this with the console command `player.setrelationshiprank 00019822 2`
  - If Siddgeir is no longer Jarl, the new Jarl will grant you Thaneship.
- [Zaria's](/wiki/Skyrim:Zaria "Skyrim:Zaria") store cannot be invested in with the Investor perk.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. You can now befriend her by investing in her shop, which will count towards Thaneship.
