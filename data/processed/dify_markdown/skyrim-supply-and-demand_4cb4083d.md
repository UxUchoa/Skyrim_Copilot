## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read the [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Shipment%27s_Ready "Skyrim:Shipment's Ready") you were required to find to finish the [previous quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid "Skyrim:The Raid").
2. Report to the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften").
3. Visit and clear the [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern").
4. Return to the Jarl of Riften.

### Locate The Suppliers

After you have read the [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Shipment%27s_Ready "Skyrim:Shipment's Ready") you will need to return to the [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl"). When you tell her that you have discovered the location of the [skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma") traders, she will ask that they *"be destroyed once and for all"*, and entice you with the prospect of a title in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). This prompts an update of the quest to find the cave.

The cave is north of Riften, and northeast of [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"). There is a single [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") and two [pit wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Pit_Wolf "Skyrim:Pit Wolf") outside the cave. The wolves should be inside the two level-locked cages, but are sometimes found roaming the small camp.

### Disruption

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-interior-Cragslane_Cavern.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Cragslane_Cavern.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Cragslane_Cavern.jpg)

Cragslane Cavern Guard

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2f/SR-interior-Cragslane_Cavern_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Cragslane_Cavern_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Cragslane_Cavern_02.jpg)

The wolf pit of Cragslane Cavern

Enter the cave to find a bandit standing guard near a lit lantern; after you ignore the warnings, or get too close, you will be attacked. Further down the tunnel and around the corner is the main gathering room of the suppliers. There are three [gamblers](/wiki/Skyrim:Gambler "Skyrim:Gambler"), a [barkeep](/wiki/Skyrim:Barkeep "Skyrim:Barkeep"), and a pit wolf in the first part, and a bandit, and two pit wolves locked in cages, in the second part.

Loot the barkeep for the [Craglane chest key](/wiki/Skyrim:Craglane_Chest_Key "Skyrim:Craglane Chest Key"), and the second bandit for the [Craglane dog cage key](/wiki/Skyrim:Craglane_Dog_Cage_Key "Skyrim:Craglane Dog Cage Key"). Go behind the bar in the northwestern corner to find a leveled apprentice-locked chest, a bowl of [moon sugar](/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar "Skyrim:Moon Sugar"), two bottles of skooma, and a copy of the [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Wolf Queen, v1](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wolf_Queen,_v1 "Skyrim:The Wolf Queen, v1")*. The second part of the cave contains another leveled apprentice-locked chest with a [bear trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") in front of it, the two apprentice-locked wolf cages, and spread over the preparation area, a few [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"), a bowl of moon sugar and seven bottles of skooma. There is also a set of [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes"), which are rare to find lying loose.

### A Reward Well Earned

After two quests involving two fights, one of which descends into a large brawl, you have well earned a reward. Return to the jarl and she will reward you with a random enchanted weapon, and express her gratefulness, *"Your continued efforts have been of great benefit to the people of Riften."*

Additionally, if [Maven Black-Briar](/wiki/Skyrim:Maven_Black-Briar "Skyrim:Maven Black-Briar") is jarl, then following the eradication of the bandits in [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern"), she will send her people to gather any remaining merchandise because *"We can't have that substance falling into the wrong hands now, can we?"* She will also reward you with some gold, stating that *"this should suffice as your cut of the... or rather, a reward for your actions."*

## Notes

- Every person in the cave must die for the quest to progress. All the wolves, however, can be spared.
- The rewarded weapon's enchantment and type are both random.
- This is the third of three quests that are required to unlock the option to become [Thane of Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:I_Done_Got_Thaned! "Skyrim:I Done Got Thaned!") for the Jarl of Riften. There are additional requirements noted on that quest's page.

## Bugs

- If [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern") has been cleared before receiving the quest, you will need to go to more trouble to complete it.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.9, fixes this bug.
  - When this occurs, you need to clear the cavern three times in order for the quest to complete. You need to kill a total of seven bandits to "disrupt the operation", but only the three bandits respawn after you have cleared it. Travel far away and wait thirty days for the cavern to reset. Return and kill the bandits three times to get a total of seven.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You can use the console command `player.setstage FreeformRiften01 40` to advance to the next quest stage.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") An alternative workaround is to repeatedly resurrect one of the dead bandits via console until you have a total of seven dead NPCs.
  - Another workaround is to repeatedly resurrect one of the dead bandits via [Dead Thrall](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Thrall "Skyrim:Dead Thrall") until you have a total of seven dead NPCs.[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Supply_and_Demand)*]
- If you exit the cave during the fight and reenter, the quest may not advance.
  - Sometimes after you buy the house the jarl still won't have the quest finishing dialogue.[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Supply_and_Demand)*]
