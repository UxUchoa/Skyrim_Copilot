## Quick Walkthrough

1. Activate the Broken Dwarven Horse outside [Runoff Caverns](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns").
2. Enter the Autumn's Bells section of the dungeon.
3. Locate the [Horse Right Front Leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Right_Front_Leg "Skyrim:Horse Right Front Leg") next to the dead [mercenary](/wiki/Skyrim:Mercenary "Skyrim:Mercenary") in the entrance hallway.
4. Locate the [Horse Left Back Leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Left_Back_Leg "Skyrim:Horse Left Back Leg") in the main chamber, near the field of White Molded Wheat.
5. Cross the bridge in the main chamber and locate the [Horse Right Back Leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Right_Back_Leg "Skyrim:Horse Right Back Leg") between the fields of Sickly Green Wheat and Orange-Encrusted Wheat.
6. Locate the [Horse Left Front Leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Left_Front_Leg "Skyrim:Horse Left Front Leg") up on a pedestal along the southern wall of the main chamber.
7. Place harvested samples of the six special wheat varieties in the depositors to open the chamber containing the [Conduit Control Gem](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Conduit_Control_Gem&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Conduit Control Gem (page does not exist)") and grab the [Horse Head](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Head "Skyrim:Horse Head") found within.
8. Return outside and activate the broken horse to obtain your own [Dwarven Horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Horse "Skyrim:Dwarven Horse") mount.

### A Broken Horse

Unlike most quests added by [Creation Club](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"), this one does not automatically begin upon installing [Forgotten Seasons](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Seasons "Skyrim:Forgotten Seasons"). Instead, you must travel to the entrance of [Runoff Caverns](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns"), which will already be marked on your map. Outside, you will find a "Broken Dwarven Horse", a metallic Dwarven torso lying partially submerged in a stream. Activate this object to begin the quest, which will task you with assembling the pieces to reconstruct this strange equine [construct](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Automatons "Skyrim:Dwarven Automatons").

All of the parts are in the Autumn's Bells section of Runoff Caverns, which is found deep inside, within the Dwarven ruins of Vardnknd. You will need to traverse the entirety of the Runoff Caverns section of the dungeon to reach the hub area at Vardnknd Gallery. The entrance to Autumn's Bells is in this central chamber.

### Exploring the Ruins

All of the Dwarven items needed to reconstruct the horse are zero weight due to being [quest items](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items "Skyrim:Quest Items"), so you needn't worry about getting them back out of the ruins. They will not be marked on your map until you get close, meaning you may need to scour the area to find them all. You will need to locate five in total:

- The first part of the horse is easy to find: it is the [Horse Right Front Leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Right_Front_Leg "Skyrim:Horse Right Front Leg") next to the dead [mercenary](/wiki/Skyrim:Mercenary "Skyrim:Mercenary") in the entrance hallway of Autumn's Bells. You will likely come across this mercenary while completing the [Forgotten Seasons](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Seasons_(quest) "Skyrim:Forgotten Seasons (quest)") quest.
- The [Horse Left Back Leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Left_Back_Leg "Skyrim:Horse Left Back Leg") is in the main chamber, near the field of White Molded Wheat. It is on the east side of the collapsed tower (behind a chest).
- The [Horse Right Back Leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Right_Back_Leg "Skyrim:Horse Right Back Leg") is on the other side of the main chamber, which will necessitate crossing the bridge in the center. It's under a collapsed building to the south from the Sickly Green Wheat.
- The [Horse Left Front Leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Left_Front_Leg "Skyrim:Horse Left Front Leg") is up on a pedestal along the southern wall of the main chamber.
- The final piece of the horse will necessitate completing the Autumn's Bells puzzle. This can be done by harvesting samples of the six special wheat varieties found growing in the chamber and placing them in the depositors located back near the field of Glowing Red Wheat. Once all six wheat varieties have been deposited, the chamber containing the [Conduit Control Gem](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Conduit_Control_Gem&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Conduit Control Gem (page does not exist)") will be unlocked; the [Horse Head](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Head "Skyrim:Horse Head") lies on a pile of gold within.

### Reward

With all five pieces assembled, you can return to the surface and activate the broken torso once again. Doing so will create a functioning [Dwarven Horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Horse "Skyrim:Dwarven Horse"), which can be ridden as a mount. It takes no damage whatsoever; you can stay mounted on it and avoid all harm.
