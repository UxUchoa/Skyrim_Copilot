Angeline's Aromatics

**Angeline's Aromatics** is the [alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") shop in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). The proprietor, [Angeline Morrard](/wiki/Skyrim:Angeline_Morrard "Skyrim:Angeline Morrard"), trades potions, food, ingredients, and books, and has 500 base gold. If anything happens to Angeline, her niece, [Vivienne Onis](/wiki/Skyrim:Vivienne_Onis "Skyrim:Vivienne Onis"), runs the store in her place.

## Angeline's Aromatics

The entrance leads directly into the shop with the counter across in front of you. On the counter are three common books, a couple of open crates displaying various merchandise, with a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid, two bunches of dried [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging above the counter against the west wall (which is all merchandise). Above the counter at the other end are two further garlic braids and two more bunches of dried elves ears (which are also merchandise). There are also a [petty](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem") and [lesser](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Lesser Soul Gem") [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") for sale on a ledge and stand. Against the south wall, to the left of the entrance is a low table holding three more common books and some more merchandise, with a small non-respawning wardrobe containing [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") next to it with an open crate on top. There is then a narrow table with two more open crates, one on the narrow table and the other on the floor in front of it, beside two black kettles. These all contain merchandise for sale.

To the right of the entrance is a wooden bench beside a set of shelves with more displayed merchandise and a pair of boots. North of these is a set of double doors leading to the residence, with an alcove to the east containing an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and to the west access behind the counter. Against the north wall, behind the counter are four sets of shelves mainly holding various displayed merchandise, along a set of blue mage robes and an empty petty soul gem. Under the counter are more items that are sold. At the western end of the counter is another alcove with more merchandise displayed on a set of shelves and a cupboard.

The door to the north leads to a dining area with a long wooden table with a chair and a wooden bench pulled up to it. It is laid for three people with two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"), a bunch of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), some [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), some [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), and some [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"). Against the south wall are two sets of shelves holding clutter. Against the east wall is a narrow table with a bowl of bread, a plate holding an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel") and a plate of [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), with a wall shelf above holding a bottle of alto wine and two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). To the left is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand, while in the northeastern corner is a small non-respawning cupboard. Against the north wall is a bookcase holding eight common books, a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") and a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"). By the door, against the west wall is a wooden bench where you can often find [Angeline Morrard](/wiki/Skyrim:Angeline_Morrard "Skyrim:Angeline Morrard") sitting down.

Climbing the stairs there is a large vase containing a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush half way up. At the top of the stairs is a landing with a small cupboard on the left holding a bottle of alto wine and three bottles of [Honningbrew mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Mead "Skyrim:Honningbrew Mead"). Along the north wall is a wooden bench and a low table, and a door leading to [Angeline Morrard's](/wiki/Skyrim:Angeline_Morrard "Skyrim:Angeline Morrard") bedroom opposite. There are two more doors to the east, and around the corner against the south wall is a narrow table with a [chaurus egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Eggs "Skyrim:Chaurus Eggs"), a stone bowl containing a sprig of snowberries, a [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), a [blue mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), a [Hagraven feather](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Feathers "Skyrim:Hagraven Feathers"), and a [Hagraven claw](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Claw "Skyrim:Hagraven Claw") on top. There are two wall shelves above, holding a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), a [potion of resist fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Fire "Skyrim:Potion of Resist Fire"), a [weak aversion to shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Aversion_to_Shock "Skyrim:Weak Aversion to Shock"), a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a copy of *[Pension of the Ancestor Moth](/wiki/Skyrim:Pension_of_the_Ancestor_Moth "Skyrim:Pension of the Ancestor Moth")*, and a copy of *[The Firmament](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firmament "Skyrim:The Firmament")*.

Angeline's bedroom is a large bedroom with a wooden bench opposite a small non-respawning wardrobe containing fine clothes on either side as you enter. There is a four-posted double bed coming out of the south wall opposite, with a non-respawning end table on either side. On the right-hand one of these is a copy of *[The Amulet of Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Amulet_of_Kings "Skyrim:The Amulet of Kings")*. Against the west wall is a table [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a bowl of bread, and a plate with a [sliced Eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") on it. A wall shelf above holds a bottle of alto wine. At the foot of the bed is an unlocked long [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing random items. To the left of the bed are two wall shelves holding a [potion of plentiful healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Healing") and a set of [novice robes of illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Robes_of_Illusion "Skyrim:Novice Robes of Illusion"), as well as a sprig of lavender. There is a chair in the southeast corner with a non-respawning tall wardrobe to the left along the east wall. In the northeast corner are two bookcases, a wooden bench and a low table with a bunch of tundra cotton and two common books. The bookcases hold a set of [blue mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Blue Mage Robes"), a bunch of elves ears, a sprig of snowberries, a [weak poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Poison "Skyrim:Weak Poison") and three more common books.

The left-hand door on the east wall of the landing leads into [Vivienne Onis'](/wiki/Skyrim:Vivienne_Onis "Skyrim:Vivienne Onis") bedroom containing a single owned bed in the northeastern corner and a non-respawning end table to the right, as well as a small non-respawning wardrobe against the south wall holding a [solution of strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Strength "Skyrim:Solution of Strength"), a [potion of extreme stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extreme_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Extreme Stamina") and a common book. Further to the right is a chair by a narrow table which is holding an iron dagger, a bowl of bread and a plate with a sliced Eidar cheese with a wall shelf above with two bottles of Nord mead and a bottle of alto wine on it. There is a tall non-respawning wardrobe and washbasin on a stand opposite, against the north wall. The right-hand door leads into a study of sorts with a wall shelf and two bookcases against the north wall. There is a potion of plentiful healing and a sprig of lavender on the wall shelf. On the bookshelf are eleven common books as well as copies of *[2920, Sun's Dusk, v11](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Sun%27s_Dusk,_v11 "Skyrim:2920, Sun's Dusk, v11")* and *[2920, Evening Star, v12](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Evening_Star,_v12 "Skyrim:2920, Evening Star, v12")*. There is also an apprentice-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") containing random items. Against the south wall is a large round wooden table and two chairs, with the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger") on top.

Descending the stairs to the cellar leads to a storage area with several [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels around the cellar. Against the north wall is a cupboard holding a bowl of [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), several pieces of cheese, a few loaves of bread, two [sweetrolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), and a haunch of raw [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"). Further to the right is an unlocked chest containing random items with a garlic braid, two bunches of dried elves ears and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") all hanging from the ceiling above. To the east is a small unit with a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") and against the south wall is another non-respawning cupboard holding a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), more loaves of bread and many more pieces of assorted cheese. Finally in the southwest corner is a long wooden table with a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"), a bunch of tundra cotton, a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"), some [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries"), two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell"), some dried elves ears, a [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue"), some [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") a loaf of bread and a plate with a red and two green apples on top.

## Notes

- Most of the food, [beverages](/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Beverages "Skyrim:Food"), [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") out in the open (with the exception of hanging bunches of herbs) around the residence and shop are sold by Angeline, in addition to the displayed merchandise. Because of this quirk, the shop has one of the largest sets of wares for sale in the game.
- Approaching the alchemy lab will cause Angeline to follow you to it, as if you were in the private living quarters of the building, even though you are still well within the shop limits.
- In [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online"), the building was occupied by a [bank](/wiki/Online:Bankers "Online:Bankers") called [Haafingar Hoard](/wiki/Online:Haafingar_Hoard "Online:Haafingar Hoard").

## Bugs

- When investing gold in Angeline's store, the chat option does not disappear. Therefore it is possible to invest repeatedly in her shop. However, because of the way the script works, she does not retain this gold. Consequently it is impossible for her base gold to be raised by the investing perk.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
  - You can use the console command `set PerkInvestorSolitudeApothecary to 0` to invest in the store.
