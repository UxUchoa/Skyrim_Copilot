# Skyrim:Bandit (dead)

## Overview

This article is about bandit corpses. For living bandits, see [Bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). For other uses, see [Bandit (disambiguation)](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(disambiguation) "Skyrim:Bandit (disambiguation)").

These **bandits** are deceased NPCs found in a variety of locations in Skyrim, particularly in caves and in the wilderness. Their races and inventories will vary, but all are dead and are found in fixed locations.

Ilinalta's Deluge

These **bandits** are all female [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [wizards](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard "Skyrim:Bandit Wizard").

They wear [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor") while their footwear is randomly determined. There is always a 50% chance of a pair of [fur shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes") and a 50% chance of leveled footwear; from level 1-5 they can be [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"), from level 6-18 they can be [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots"), and from level 19 it can be [scaled boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots"). They carry a random [staff](/wiki/Skyrim:Staves "Skyrim:Staves"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), some random loot, some gold, and either a potion of health or magicka.

Despite their condition, they also know a variety of spells, specifically [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward"), [Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing "Skyrim:Healing"), [Oakflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Oakflesh "Skyrim:Oakflesh"), magicka recovery, and three [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") spells, specifically [Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Flames "Skyrim:Flames"), [Frostbite](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite "Skyrim:Frostbite"), and [Sparks](/wiki/Skyrim:Sparks "Skyrim:Sparks").

They can be found in the following locations:

- In the prison corridor of [Fort Greenwall Prison](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall#Fort_Greenwall_Prison "Skyrim:Fort Greenwall"), along with several [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider") nearby.[[¹]](#intnote_civilwar)
- In [Ilinalta's Deluge](/wiki/Skyrim:Ilinalta%27s_Deep#Ilinalta.27s_Deluge "Skyrim:Ilinalta's Deep"), in the final room in front of [Malyn Varen's](/wiki/Skyrim:Malyn_Varen "Skyrim:Malyn Varen") skeletal corpse.
- [Liar's Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Liar%27s_Retreat "Skyrim:Liar's Retreat") in the dining area behind a tipped over table
- In the first room of [Irkngthand Arcanex](/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand#Irkngthand_Arcanex "Skyrim:Irkngthand"), just past the second flight of stairs.[[²]](#intnote_tg)
- In the second chamber of [Shimmermist Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Shimmermist_Cave "Skyrim:Shimmermist Cave"), alongside two other dead bandits in a small alcove.
- On top of a rock southeast of [Harmugstahl](/wiki/Skyrim:Harmugstahl "Skyrim:Harmugstahl"), with a nearby [sabre cat](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Sabre Cat") and the Destruction [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books"), *[Horror of Castle Xyr](/wiki/Skyrim:Horror_of_Castle_Xyr "Skyrim:Horror of Castle Xyr")*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-131669&y=50453)

[^¹](#note_civilwar)Must not have completed either the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Greenwall (Imperial)") or [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Greenwall (Stormcloaks)") related civil war quests.

Additionally, they have a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Bracers "Skyrim:Fur Bracers") but, at level 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers"), and at level 19 it can be [scaled bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers "Skyrim:Scaled Bracers"). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of them carrying a leveled shield, up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") quality at level 12.

The carry an amount of gold and either a [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), a [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword"), or a [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"), all of them leveled to elven quality at level 22. Additionally, they will carry three potions and some random loot such as [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and [gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Gems "Skyrim:Gems").

They can be found in the following locations:

This bandit may also be [reanimated](/wiki/Skyrim:Raise_Zombie "Skyrim:Raise Zombie") by a nearby leveled [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer"). Snapleg Cave

These **bandits** are all male [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)").

They wear leveled [heavy armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"); up to level 6 they will wear [iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor") or [banded iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Banded Iron Armor"), and [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots"). Up to level 18 they wear [steel armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Armor"), and [steel cuffed boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Cuffed Boots") or [steel shin boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shin_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Shin Boots"). From level 18 onward, they wear [steel plate armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Plate Armor") and [steel plate boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Plate Boots"). Additionally, there is a 50% chance they will wear gauntlets and/or a helmet, also leveled; up to level 6 they can be [iron gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets") and/or an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet"). Up to level 18, there are two variations of each item, specifically [steel Nordic gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Nordic Gauntlets") or [steel imperial gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Imperial_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Imperial Gauntlets") for the hands, and a [steel helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Helmet "Skyrim:Steel Helmet") or a [steel horned helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Horned_Helmet "Skyrim:Steel Horned Helmet"). From level 18 onward, they can wear [steel plate gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Plate Gauntlets") and/or a [steel plate helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Helmet "Skyrim:Steel Plate Helmet").

Their weapon is random and leveled as well (up to elven quality at level 22) and can be either a [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), a [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword"), or a [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"). They always carry three [health potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and some random loot. They also have a slim chance of carrying a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and/or some gold.

They can be found in the following locations:

He wears a randomly determined set of [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Light_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"); at all levels, there is always a 50% chance he will wear [fur shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes") and a set of [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor"), while the other 50% can differ for both shoes and armor; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") and/or [studded armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor "Skyrim:Studded Armor"), at level 6-18 it can be [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") and/or [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor"), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots") and/or [scaled armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Armor") or [scaled horn armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Horn Armor"). Additionally, he has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Bracers "Skyrim:Fur Bracers") but, at level 1-5 there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers"), and at level 19 it can be [scaled bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers "Skyrim:Scaled Bracers"). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of him carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12.

His weapon is random and leveled as well (up to elven quality at level 22) and can be either a [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), a [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword"), or a [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"). He always carries some random loot and a bit of gold.

He wields a leveled bow; from level 1-3 it will be either a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow") or a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow"), from level 4, it will be an [Imperial bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bow "Skyrim:Imperial Bow"), from level 10 it will be an [Orcish bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Bow "Skyrim:Orcish Bow"), from level 15 it will be a [Dwarven bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Bow "Skyrim:Dwarven Bow"), and from level 20 onward, it will be an [elven bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Bow "Skyrim:Elven Bow"). The twenty arrows are leveled as well and follow the leveling of the bows. Additionally, he will carry six arrows of the highest possible quality and a [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") leveled to elven quality at level 20. He also carries a bit of random loot and some gold.

She carries an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and has a slim chance of carrying a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and/or some gold. Despite her condition, she also knows a few spells, specifically [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward"), [Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing "Skyrim:Healing"), [Oakflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Oakflesh "Skyrim:Oakflesh"), and the three standard [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") spells, [Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Flames "Skyrim:Flames"), [Frostbite](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite "Skyrim:Frostbite"), and [Sparks](/wiki/Skyrim:Sparks "Skyrim:Sparks").
