## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read the Black Book *[Untold Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book:_Untold_Legends_(book) "Skyrim:Black Book: Untold Legends (book)")* in [Benkongerike Great Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Benkongerike_Great_Hall "Skyrim:Benkongerike Great Hall").
2. Make your way through [Apocrypha‎](/wiki/Skyrim:Apocrypha_(Untold_Legends) "Skyrim:Apocrypha (Untold Legends)").
3. Learn the Black Book's hidden knowledge.
4. Choose your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

There are five chapters: Chapter I is basically a corridor that leads to Chapter II, containing no enemies and only three [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") in the way of loot. These can be found at the very end of the chapter, at the final bend just before the book that leads to Chapter II.

Chapter II has exits to both Chapter III and Chapter IV, enabling you to bypass Chapter III if you wish. There is a leveled [lurker](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker") to battle before reaching either exit. The lurker is standing near a Font of Stamina and a Font of Magicka. There is another Font of Magicka by the exit to Chapter IV. The exit to Chapter IV can be found by taking the corridor south and then west; whereas the exit for Chapter III is taking the corridor north.

Chapter III contains a [seeker](/wiki/Skyrim:Seeker "Skyrim:Seeker"), a [spell tome of Conjure Seeker](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Seeker "Skyrim:Conjure Seeker") if your Conjuration skill is at least 65, and a container of loot. These are all in the same enclosed starting area, so that the Seeker will attack you just as you enter the Chapter; the spell tome is in the beginning area as well. There is no direct path from Chapter III to Chapter IV, so you do have to return to Chapter II.

Chapter IV contains the most loot including four stealth based [skill books](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books"), two soul gems and four containers of loot as well as three seekers to battle. It starts off on an open platform with two Fonts of Stamina; walking down the corridor will take you past a well-lit Font of Magicka. You will head south into another open area; all the Seekers are here together. There are various landings here going in roughly the eight cardinal directions; the northeast one contains a locked treasure pod, a soul gem, and the four skill books. East is another locked pod and another Scrye which opens the middle. Southeast goes nowhere. South is initially unclimbable. Southwest contains another locked treasure pod. By the west gate is a Scrye which opens said gate. Inside is another Scrye which opens the east landing. There is a pod to the northwest. North returns you to the corridor to Chapter II. The middle Scrye opens out the southern stairs, allowing exit to Chapter V.

Chapter V contains a corridor that initially points towards the final reward, but may bend to the left or right whenever you move down the corridor. If it does, you'll be directed towards a room with a leveled lurker. Kill the lurker and a door will open behind it, allowing you to return to the central room. Once any side areas have been dealt with, the corridor should return to its original position, leading to the [Black Book](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book_(book) "Skyrim:Black Book (book)"): *[Untold Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book:_Untold_Legends_(book) "Skyrim:Black Book: Untold Legends (book)")*.

### Rewards

Your choice of one of three lesser powers:

- [Bardic Knowledge](/wiki/Skyrim:Bardic_Knowledge "Skyrim:Bardic Knowledge") — Summons a spectral drum that plays for you, increasing [stamina regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Regenerate_Stamina "Skyrim:Regenerate Stamina") for you and nearby allies for 300 secs.
- [Black Market](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Market "Skyrim:Black Market") — Summons a [Dremora merchant](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Merchant "Skyrim:Dremora Merchant") to trade with you for 15 secs.
- [Secret Servant](/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_Servant "Skyrim:Secret Servant") — Summons a [Dremora butler](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Butler "Skyrim:Dremora Butler") to carry your excess items for 15 secs. (Max carry weight 148)

## Notes

- There is a table in Chapter IV that has the four skill books [The Gold Ribbon of Merit](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gold_Ribbon_of_Merit "Skyrim:The Gold Ribbon of Merit"), [Advances in Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Advances_in_Lockpicking "Skyrim:Advances in Lockpicking"), [Beggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar_(book) "Skyrim:Beggar (book)"), and [The Rear Guard](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rear_Guard "Skyrim:The Rear Guard"), which train archery, lockpicking, pickpocket and light armor, respectively.
- You can change the reward at any time by traveling to Solstheim and re-reading the book. This will transport you to Apocrypha near the reward location and you can select a different one.

## Bugs

- Sometimes after defeating one of the lurkers in Chapter V, the nearby door will open and then shut before you have a chance to pass through.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Use the `tcl` console command to clip through the door.
- If you return to Chapter III, the hallway configuration may change, leaving any dropped items in the previous configuration permanently inaccessible. **?**
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Use the `tcl` console command to clip through the door(s) and regain your items.
- Although it appears that the corridor in Chapter V was always supposed to move to the left, right, or possibly each in sequence, it will often fail to do so.
- Sometimes after completing the book, you will have a false combat signature, preventing you from exiting. **?**
  - Reload the game.
