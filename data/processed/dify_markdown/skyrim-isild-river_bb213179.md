The Isild River

**Isild River** is a river in northern [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim") on the [Felsaad Coast](/wiki/Lore:Felsaad_Coast "Lore:Felsaad Coast").

Before the [Red Year](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Red_Year&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Red Year (page does not exist)") of [4E 5](/wiki/Lore:Fourth_Era#4E_5 "Lore:Fourth Era"), the Isild River ran northwest from the frozen [Lake Fjalding](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Fjalding "Skyrim:Lake Fjalding") until it merged with the [Harstrad River](/wiki/Skyrim:Harstrad_River "Skyrim:Harstrad River") at the foot of the [Moesring Mountains](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Moesring_Mountains&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Moesring Mountains (page does not exist)"). The river then continued to its mouth on the icy Felsaad Coast, west of [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village"). It was reportedly a beautiful part of the island, at the right time of year.

The cataclysms of the Red Year substantially changed the landscape of Solstheim. Many of the landmarks remained the same; the river is still west of Skaal Village and flows from Lake Fjalding to the coast. However, the Isild and Harstrad rivers no longer meet. Instead, each empties separately into a small bay on the northern coast.

## Notes

- The name *isild* comes from Danish and Norwegian words *is* meaning *ice* and *ild* meaning *fire*, translating it to *Icefire River*.
