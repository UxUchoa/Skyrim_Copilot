Purewater Run

**Purewater Run** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") south-southeast of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") containing [slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Slaughterfish").

## Related Quests

- **[Interception](/wiki/Skyrim:Interception "Skyrim:Interception")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Elite_Armaments "Skyrim:Redguard Elite Armaments"): Aid the Hammerfell resistance.

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-interior-Purewater_Run.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Purewater_Run.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Purewater_Run.jpg)

The first area

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-interior-Purewater_Run_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Purewater_Run_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Purewater_Run_02.jpg)

The submerged Dwarven head and two Dwarven chests

Through the entrance, the cave is full of shallow water populated by several ingredient fish, including [abecean longfin](/wiki/Skyrim:Abecean_Longfin "Skyrim:Abecean Longfin"), [silverside perch](/wiki/Skyrim:Silverside_Perch "Skyrim:Silverside Perch"), and [histcarp](/wiki/Skyrim:Histcarp "Skyrim:Histcarp"). Straight ahead of the entrance are two large [Nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") plants and a [quicksilver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore Vein").

With the [Redguard Elite Armaments](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Elite_Armaments "Skyrim:Redguard Elite Armaments") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, during the quest [Interception](/wiki/Skyrim:Interception "Skyrim:Interception"), [Fijeh](/wiki/Skyrim:Fijeh "Skyrim:Fijeh") and later [Rakeed](/wiki/Skyrim:Rakeed "Skyrim:Rakeed") and a locked chest that can only be opened with the [Remnant Supply Chest key](/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Supply_Chest_Key "Skyrim:Remnant Supply Chest Key") will be found here. The chest contains [Remnant Agent Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Agent_Armor "Skyrim:Remnant Agent Armor"), [Remnant Agent Boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Agent_Boots "Skyrim:Remnant Agent Boots"), [Remnant Agent Gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Agent_Gloves "Skyrim:Remnant Agent Gloves"), [Remnant Agent Hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Agent_Hood "Skyrim:Remnant Agent Hood"), [Remnant Shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Shield "Skyrim:Remnant Shield"), two [Remnant Scimitars of Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Scimitar_of_Fire "Skyrim:Remnant Scimitar of Fire"), two [Potions of Extended Invisibility](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extended_Invisibility "Skyrim:Potion of Extended Invisibility"), and *[The Ebon Arm](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ebon_Arm "Skyrim:The Ebon Arm")*.

To the east is a deep pool partially hidden by a waterfall. It contains two unlocked Dwarven chests near a large Dwarven head, several [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(creature) "Skyrim:Salmon (creature)"), two slaughterfish, and seven patches of [slaughterfish eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg") in and around the chests, over the Dwarven head, and on the walls. A second waterfall cascades down the cave wall between the deep pool and ore vein. A copy of the [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Cherim's Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Cherim%27s_Heart "Skyrim:Cherim's Heart")* can be found inside one of the underwater chests. There is a small air pocket directly above the chests.

Getting out of the pool is tricky; you must jump at just the right spot. It is advised to have any followers wait outside so that they don't get in your way.

## Notes

- Outside the cave, following the waterfalls north to the point just before the stream merges with another larger stream, you will find a lootable skeleton on a ledge under a tree with a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a leveled [warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer "Skyrim:Warhammer") nearby. To the east, an unlocked chest is on the south bank a short distance downstream from the skeleton.

## Bugs

- Followers may get stuck at the entrance to the dungeon due to a disconnected navmesh.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.10, fixes this bug.
- Followers may get stuck in the pool with the Dwarven head.
  - The easiest way to avoid this is to ask them to wait outside, although even if they become stuck in the pool, they will normally rejoin you when you leave the cave.
- The main water plane is filled with holes, allowing you to slip through at the borders.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
- One of the patches of slaughterfish eggs on the Dwarven head can't be interacted with.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
