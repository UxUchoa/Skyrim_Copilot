# Skyrim:Items

## Overview

Skyrim is full of **items** both magical and mundane. The articles listed here provide information on all items in the game.

## Finding Items

Depending upon whether you are looking for a specific item or just browsing for general information, there are several different ways to navigate through the many item-related pages.

### Search

If you know the exact name of the specific item you want to find, you can search for it [here](/wiki/Special:Search "Special:Search") or at the search box at the top of the page.
0x000F71CD

### Categories

Within the category **[Skyrim-Items](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Items "Category:Skyrim-Items")** are many subcategories listing nearly every item in the game by function. If you don't know the exact spelling of a specific item, browsing through one of the categories may help you to find it.

### General Articles on Items

Most of these articles provide general information on various types of items, and also include lists of the mundane (non-enchanted and widely available) varieties of the items.

- [Ammunition](/wiki/Skyrim:Ammunition "Skyrim:Ammunition"): All arrows and bolts, magical and non-magical
- [Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor"): Base unenchanted armor
- [Books](/wiki/Skyrim:Books "Skyrim:Books"): All books in the game
- [Clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing"): Base unenchanted clothing and jewelry
- [Food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"): Ingredients that can be eaten raw or [cooked](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking")
- [Ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"): A complete list of alchemical ingredients
- [Keys](/wiki/Skyrim:Keys "Skyrim:Keys"): A list of all keys in the game
- [Miscellaneous Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Items"): Includes gems, minor quest items, etc.
- [Notes](/wiki/Skyrim:Notes "Skyrim:Notes"): Random notes and letters found in the game
- [Ores and Ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Ores_and_Ingots "Skyrim:Ores and Ingots"): Includes scrap and dwarven metal
- [Permanent Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Permanent_Items "Skyrim:Permanent Items"): Items preventing corpses from being erased, useful for necromancers
- [Potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"): All potions, beverages, and poisons
- [Quest Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items "Skyrim:Quest Items"): All quest items found in-game that cannot, for at least a time, be removed from your inventory
- [Soul Gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"): All soul gems in the game and how to use them
- [Specialty Gear](/wiki/Skyrim:Specialty_Gear "Skyrim:Specialty Gear"): Faction specific armor as well as some miscellaneous apparel
- [Template Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Template_Items "Skyrim:Template Items"): Items whose sole purpose is to define other items
- [Unobtainable Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Unobtainable_Items "Skyrim:Unobtainable Items"): Items which either cannot be seen or equipped by the player or which exist only in the [Creation Kit](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit "Skyrim Mod:Creation Kit") and cannot be seen without the use of the console.
- [Weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons"): Base unenchanted weapons

- [Magic Items with Guaranteed Locations](/wiki/Skyrim:Magic_Items_with_Guaranteed_Locations "Skyrim:Magic Items with Guaranteed Locations"): A list of guaranteed generic magic items

### Creating Custom Items

- [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy"): Create your own custom potions.
- [Baking](/wiki/Skyrim:Baking "Skyrim:Baking")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire"): Create custom recipes at an oven.
- [Cooking](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking"): Create custom recipes at a cooking pot or spit.
- [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting"): Create enchantments for armor, clothes, and weapons.
- [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing"): Create and improve unenchanted armor and weapons.
