Temple Ancestral Tomb

The **Temple Ancestral Tomb** is a [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") ancestral tomb in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

It can only be accessed from within the [Raven Rock Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple "Skyrim:Temple"), the [Morvayn Ancestral Tomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Morvayn_Ancestral_Tomb "Skyrim:Morvayn Ancestral Tomb"), and the [Ulen Ancestral Tomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb "Skyrim:Ulen Ancestral Tomb"), as it doesn't have direct access outside. The [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Tomb_Key "Skyrim:Raven Rock Tomb Key") required to unlock the doors between the tomb and these three otherwise separate areas can be obtained as part of the quest [Clean Sweep](/wiki/Skyrim:Clean_Sweep "Skyrim:Clean Sweep").

## Temple Ancestral Tomb

The entrance from the Temple leads into a corridor lined with four lit braziers which proceeds north to the tomb room. In the room, to the west is a corridor leading to the key-locked door to the Morvayn Ancestral Tomb, and to the east is a matching corridor leading to the key-locked door to the Ulen Ancestral Tomb. The room and west and east corridors feature a total of nine ash piles littered with bones, with the two central piles surrounded by steps and lit candles. An [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox") is partly buried in the ash pile to the north. Also in the area are three ash yam urns to the north and west, two benches to the west, four unusable barrels to the south and east, and six more lit braziers around the central piles and in the west and east corridors.
