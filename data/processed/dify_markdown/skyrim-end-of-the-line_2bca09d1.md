Help Viriya build her dream stall

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read the letter from Viriya.
2. Obtain 18 pieces of firewood, 20 nails, 4 iron fittings and a lock.
3. Bring the building supplies to Viriya
4. Wait 24 hours.
5. Visit Viriya's stall in the Grand Plaza.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Soon after completion of [Wave Breaker](/wiki/Skyrim:Wave_Breaker "Skyrim:Wave Breaker"), a courier will deliver you yet another [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Viriya "Skyrim:Letter from Viriya"). It's from Viriya. In her hiatus, she has decided to leave the Riften Fishery and run a Mudcrab merchant stall within the Riften "Grand Plaza." The objective is to bring her building materials for the stall. She needs: 18 pieces of Firewood, 20 Nails, 4 Iron Fittings and a Lock. Bring all the gathered items to her:

:   **Here's everything you'll need to build a stall in the plaza.**
:   *"By the Gods. I don't even know what to say."*
:   **I'll stop by the plaza tomorrow once you've gotten everything set up.**

Wait for 24 hours. After that time goes by visit her new market stall in the Plaza. She'll thank you for helping her with her dream job and will commence to give you an enchanted amulet named [Viriya's Charm](/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya%27s_Charm "Skyrim:Viriya's Charm"):

:   **Look's like you're all set up.**
:   *"It would seem so. I wanted to give you this, a token of my appreciation. Thanks, good luck out there."*

This ends the Fishing questline. Fishing radiant quests can still be done for [Swims-In-Deep-Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water "Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water") afterwards. These include the quests for: [Further Study](/wiki/Skyrim:Further_Study "Skyrim:Further Study"), [Rubbish Retrieval](/wiki/Skyrim:Rubbish_Retrieval "Skyrim:Rubbish Retrieval") and [Stocking Up](/wiki/Skyrim:Stocking_Up "Skyrim:Stocking Up").

## Notes

- Once Viriya becomes a merchant, she will have 150 gold. You cannot invest with her, but her shop benefits from the [Master Trader](/wiki/Skyrim:Master_Trader "Skyrim:Master Trader") perk.
