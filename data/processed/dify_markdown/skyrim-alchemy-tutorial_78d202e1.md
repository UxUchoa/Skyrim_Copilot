## Quick Walkthrough

1. Ask for permission to use the alchemy lab.
2. Combine the ingredients at an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs") to create a potion.

### The Recipe

Ask any of the quest givers if you can use the [alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") lab located in their shop or inn, as the case may be. They will all consent with the same reply, *"Long as you clean up. You know how to use it right? Mix a couple of ingredients together, see what happens. A little [Wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") and [Blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort") makes a healing potion, if you didn't know. We have plenty of ingredients for sale, too."*
This is just a suggestion, crafting any potion will complete the quest objective, you can buy [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") directly from the quest giver.

### Crafting the Potion

Once your ingredients are collected, approach the alchemy lab and select at least two of them. Note that to succeed into crafting a potion, each ingredient must share an [alchemical effect](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Effects "Skyrim:Alchemy Effects") with another. Confirm the crafting and create a potion to complete the quest. After this, the effect will be added to the Alchemy menu if you don't have it already.
