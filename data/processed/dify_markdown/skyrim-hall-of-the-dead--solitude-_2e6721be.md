The Solitude Hall of the Dead

The **Hall of the Dead** is where [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude")'s dead are brought to be prepared for burial.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude).jpg)

The interior of Solitude's Hall of the Dead

Solitude's Hall of the Dead is tended by [Styrr](/wiki/Skyrim:Styrr "Skyrim:Styrr"), a priest of Arkay. There is a [shrine of Arkay](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Arkay "Skyrim:Shrine of Arkay") in front room, which will grant you the [Blessing of Arkay](/wiki/Skyrim:Blessings#Blessing_of_Arkay "Skyrim:Blessings") for eight hours. The dead themselves are buried in [Solitude Catacombs](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Catacombs "Skyrim:Solitude Catacombs"), which can be accessed through the door in the basement.

### Solitude's Hall of the Dead

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude)_02.jpg)

Shrine of Arkay

Upon entering you are in a small room with a small pillar, opposite is an unlocked door. In the right half of the room is a firepit with a stone wall around it. In the eastern corner there is a wooden bench and narrow table against the walls with a low table in front of them. On the tables are; a plate of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and cheese, a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"), a common book, an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and a bunch of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton") in a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl"). In the northern corner there is a small unit with an [iron warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer "Skyrim:Iron Warhammer") and the shrine to Arkay.

Around the central pillar there are several [skulls](/wiki/Skyrim:Skull "Skyrim:Skull"), including one [troll skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull "Skyrim:Troll Skull") on a table. The left half of the room is dominated by a table and chairs in the center. The table is set for two, plates containing a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish") and two [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), as well as two goblets, a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"), bread and cheese. Beside the table is an open crate containing a sample of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd")  and an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing"). Against the southeast wall is a set of shelves holding several random [weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons"), including two [bows](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow"), a [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword"), two [battleaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe "Skyrim:Battleaxe") and a [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"). To the right of this against the southwest wall is a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield") on a unit. In the western corner there is a barrel of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") and a set of shelves against the northwest wall holding a potion of regeneration, a [potion of resist fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Fire "Skyrim:Potion of Resist Fire"), two [potions of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and one of [minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), several wheels of cheese, a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and six [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). To the left of the internal door is a small set of shelves holding an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") and [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") and three [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow").

Through the door, on your right is a small wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a wall shelf above with a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flower and common book on it. In the eastern corner is a round table with a goblet on top. Opposite the door, against the northwest wall is a narrow table holding a bowl of bread, a plate of [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and a sack underneath. To the southwest are flights of stairs leading up and down. At the bottom of the stairs there are a pile of sacks containing foodstuffs and a corridor flanked by rows of lit braziers leading to a closed door that gives access to **Solitude's catacombs**.

Climbing the stairs to the top floor, you come to a landing with a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush in a planter at the top of the stairs, a wardrobe containing clothes against the northwest wall with a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk") on top. Opposite the top of the stairs on the northeast wall is the door to [Styrr's](/wiki/Skyrim:Styrr "Skyrim:Styrr") bedroom. Against the southeast wall is a large round wooden table holding three common books, a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a [cup](/wiki/Skyrim:Cup "Skyrim:Cup") and a [solution of strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Strength "Skyrim:Solution of Strength"). Next to this against the northeast wall is another narrow table holding bread and cheese in a bowl and on a plate respectively and a sack underneath. Above the narrow table is a wall shelf holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead").

Styrr's bedroom consists of a double owned bed in the northern corner and an open crate at the foot containing a potion of regeneration and [draught of strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Strength "Skyrim:Draught of Strength"). To the right against the northeast wall is a tall wardrobe containing clothes. There is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") behind the door in the western corner to the left of a cupboard. Against the southwest wall there is a long unlocked chest, with a wall shelf above it holding a [potion of plentiful healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Healing") and a bunch of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). Against the southeast wall is a set of shelves with two [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales"), two bottles of [frostbite venom](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Venom "Skyrim:Frostbite Venom"), a [potion of haggling](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling "Skyrim:Potion of Haggling"), a bowl of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), a bowl of [void salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Void_Salts "Skyrim:Void Salts") and a basket containing two bunches of lavender. To the left of the unit is a pair of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots"), with a couple of sacks to the left of these. In the eastern corner in front of another cupboard is a low table and chair. On the table is a plate of bread and cheese, a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a goblet.
