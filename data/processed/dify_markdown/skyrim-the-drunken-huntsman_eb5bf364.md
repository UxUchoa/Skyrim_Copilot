The Drunken Huntsman

**The Drunken Huntsman** is a [tavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Inns "Skyrim:Inns") and hunting supply store in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). It is located across the street from [Breezehome](/wiki/Skyrim:Breezehome "Skyrim:Breezehome"),

and is co-owned by [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") brothers [Elrindir](/wiki/Skyrim:Elrindir "Skyrim:Elrindir") and [Anoriath](/wiki/Skyrim:Anoriath "Skyrim:Anoriath"). Elrindir tends the counter, buying and selling weapons, armor, food, and miscellaneous items 24 hours a day. It consists of one area only, *[The Drunken Huntsman](#The_Drunken_Huntsman),* that includes a main taproom and two small bedrooms, one downstairs and another upstairs.

The inn owes its name to a hunting incident between the brothers. On a moonlit night after consuming too much mead, Anoriath mistook his brother for a deer, shooting at him and piercing his rump with an arrow. They knew then they had come up with the name for their business.

**NOTE**: Like all other shops, The Drunken Huntsman has a number of fixed wares on display scattered around the main room. These can be bought from Elrindir and will disappear from the room when purchased. For a list of these special items, see [this section](#Displayed_Merchandise).

## The Drunken Huntsman

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-interior-The_Drunken_Huntsman.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Drunken_Huntsman.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Drunken_Huntsman.jpg)

The Drunken Huntsman interior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_%28The_Drunken_Huntsman%29.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_%28The_Drunken_Huntsman%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_(The_Drunken_Huntsman).jpg)

The note added by [Civil War Champions](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War_Champions "Skyrim:Civil War Champions")

There are two entrances to the Drunken Huntsman, although both lead you into the taproom. Elrindir stands at his counter in the western corner, providing his services around the clock. Hidden underneath the counter is a copy of the [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") skill book *[The Black Arrow, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Arrow,_v2 "Skyrim:The Black Arrow, v2")*. Behind the counter is a storage alcove with a clothes wardrobe and a food barrel. A special [fish barrel](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Barrel "Skyrim:Containers") holds a variety of fish. If the [Civil War Champions](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War_Champions "Skyrim:Civil War Champions") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") is installed and you haven't yet joined either the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") or the [Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"), a note named *[Battle of Champions](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_of_Champions "Skyrim:Battle of Champions")* will appear on the counter, and you will be directed to read it to start the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_of_the_Champions "Skyrim:Battle of the Champions").

North of the counter is a small table for patrons, with a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat"), two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). At an alcove table on the east side sits [Jenassa](/wiki/Skyrim:Jenassa "Skyrim:Jenassa"), a [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") mercenary who can be recruited as a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") for 500 gold. Her table has a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") in a bowl. In the center of the room are two [cooking spits](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") dangling over a burning fire, and along the northwestern wall is another table for customers, with a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), a bottle of [nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and a bottle of wine. [Olfrid Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:Olfrid_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Olfrid Battle-Born") will often sit at this table to enjoy his meals. Another small table stands near the cooking spits with a bottle of ale. Underneath the ladder leading to the bedroom on the second floor, sits an [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and, on a food barrel, two bottles of ale.

The brothers' bedroom on the north side has two owned beds, although only Anoriath ever sleeps. The bed to the northwest has a food sack underneath it and a non-respawning unlocked chest containing leveled items at the bedhead. The [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger") of the Huntsman can be found atop a dresser, right next to a locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox").

The second floor consists of one bedroom reserved for regulars [Nazeem](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazeem "Skyrim:Nazeem") and his wife [Ahlam](/wiki/Skyrim:Ahlam "Skyrim:Ahlam"), who sleep there every night despite owning [Chillfurrow Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Chillfurrow_Farm "Skyrim:Chillfurrow Farm") outside of town. At the foot of the owned double bed stands a respawning unlocked chest containing gold, and the wardrobe contains cheap clothing. Two end tables with clutter stand on either side of the bed, one with a copy of *[The Third Era Timeline](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Third_Era_Timeline "Skyrim:The Third Era Timeline")* on top, the other with a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and a bottle of alto wine.

  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.6, addresses this issue. The arrow and dagger were also missing their ownership settings.
