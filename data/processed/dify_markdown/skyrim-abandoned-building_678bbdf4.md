Abandoned Building

The **Abandoned Building** is the home of [Bralsa Drel](/wiki/Skyrim:Bralsa_Drel "Skyrim:Bralsa Drel") and [Rirns Llervu](/wiki/Skyrim:Rirns_Llervu "Skyrim:Rirns Llervu") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

It is on the waterfront west of [Glover Mallory's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory%27s_House "Skyrim:Glover Mallory's House"), being the last building on the waterfront along the path to the [Earth Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Earth_Stone "Skyrim:Earth Stone") to the west. Until the stone is cleansed during the quest [Cleansing the Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones"), the building is uninhabited. After the stone is cleansed, Bralsa Drel and Rirns Llervu will take residence.

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance are two unusable barrels. To the right of the entrance is a lit brazier and three [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plants. Along the back of the building on the waterfront are three crates and more unusable barrels. The entrance is unlocked.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_03.jpg)

Upper floor

The entrance leads into a squalid building littered with ash piles, cobwebs, and [tankards](/wiki/Skyrim:Tankard "Skyrim:Tankard") and other minor items messily strewn about. In the middle of the room is a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on the floor. In the southeast corner is another bottle of Nord mead on an ash pile. Along the west wall is a sideboard holding a bottle of Nord mead, a bottle of [sujamma](/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma "Skyrim:Sujamma"), a copy of *[Confessions of a Dunmer Skooma Eater](/wiki/Skyrim:Confessions_of_a_Dunmer_Skooma_Eater "Skyrim:Confessions of a Dunmer Skooma Eater")*, and a bottle of [matze](/wiki/Skyrim:Matze "Skyrim:Matze"), with a copy of *[The Poison Song, Book IV](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song,_Book_IV "Skyrim:The Poison Song, Book IV")* on the wall ledge behind the dresser. Along the north wall is another bottle of matze on the wall ledge, a doorway to the next room, and two more bottles of Nord mead on the wall ledge and a crate, respectively.

The next room is circular, and features the stairs leading up to the upper floor. Along the curved west wall are two owned bed rolls, with a bottle of [flin](/wiki/Skyrim:Flin "Skyrim:Flin"), a bottle of sujamma, a bottle of [shein](/wiki/Skyrim:Shein "Skyrim:Shein"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and a wedge of [goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge") between them, and a crate holding another loaf of bread and a copy of *[The Five Far Stars](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Five_Far_Stars "Skyrim:The Five Far Stars")*. Along the north wall beneath the stairs are two ingredient urns and an unusable barrel. The small circular room on the upper floor contains two sacks, a meat barrel, three unusable barrels, and four crates.
