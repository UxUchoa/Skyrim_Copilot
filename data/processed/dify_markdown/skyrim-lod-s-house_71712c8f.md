**Lod's House** is the home of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath")'s [blacksmith](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith "Skyrim:Blacksmith"), [Lod](/wiki/Skyrim:Lod "Skyrim:Lod").

Lod the blacksmith

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-interior-Lod%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Lod%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lod%27s_House.jpg)

Lod's house

It is almost always novice-locked and will require [stealth](/wiki/Skyrim:Stealth "Skyrim:Stealth") for entry. It's made up of two rooms: a living area and a basement. It is found near the center of Falkreath, between *[Gray Pine Goods](/wiki/Skyrim:Gray_Pine_Goods "Skyrim:Gray Pine Goods")* and *[Grave Concoctions](/wiki/Skyrim:Grave_Concoctions "Skyrim:Grave Concoctions")*.

### Exterior

There is a raised, covered porch along the length of the front of the house with steps leading up to it. To the left of the house is a larger cover area where Lod plies his craft at his [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge"). There are also a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"). There are two more tanning racks in front, not under cover. Beside these is an open crate containing four [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). Around the house there are four [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") and one [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") and a [thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"). Unlike most merchants Lod doesn't display any of his wares.

### Lod's House

Upon entering there is a long wooden table on the left against the north wall. On the table is a sprig of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"), two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). A basket on the floor to the right contains two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), with a set of shelves to the right of that. These hold two more leeks, a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), with two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg") and several pieces of cheese on top and two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging above. There is a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel in the northeast corner, above the stairs leading down to the basement.

The stairs run along the east wall. Opposite the table on the south wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") on the mantel. There is an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield") on the hearth. To the left, hanging from the ceiling, is a rack holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), and to the right is a wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). Next to the wardrobe is a round wooden table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead"), and a [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Some_Light_Theft "Skyrim:Some Light Theft")-related [private letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Private_Letter "Skyrim:Private Letter") is lying on the table next to his bed. In the southwestern corner and running along the west wall is a single owned bed, with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot. To the right of the bed, against the west wall is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table are five [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse").

In the basement, along the north wall are two long wooden tables. The first holds a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow") and three [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"), while the second has a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of Nord mead, an iron dagger, an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), a set of [iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor") and a pair of [iron gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets"), with a pair of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots") underneath. To the left is an end table and chair. On top of the end table are two [blacksmith's potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Potion "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Potion") and a copy of the [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Light Armor Forging](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor_Forging "Skyrim:Light Armor Forging")*. Against the west wall is an unlocked chest, a wardrobe containing more clothes, and a couple of food barrels. Along the south wall is a wooden block with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") on top and a set of shelves holding nothing but clutter. To the left is a round wooden table and chair, with a loaf of bread on the table and a garlic braid, a bunch of elves ear and a bunch of frost mirriam hanging above.
