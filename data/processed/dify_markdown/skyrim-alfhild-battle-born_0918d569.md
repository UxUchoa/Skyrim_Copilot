Alfhild Battle-Born

**Alfhild Battle-Born** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") and a member of [Clan Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle-Born "Skyrim:Battle-Born") living in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). [Jon Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:Jon_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Jon Battle-Born") is her brother, and her parents are [Olfrid](/wiki/Skyrim:Olfrid_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Olfrid Battle-Born") and [Bergritte](/wiki/Skyrim:Bergritte_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Bergritte Battle-Born"). She is married to [Idolaf](/wiki/Skyrim:Idolaf_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Idolaf Battle-Born"), with whom she has a son, [Lars](/wiki/Skyrim:Lars_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Lars Battle-Born"). She maintains [Battle-Born Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle-Born_Farm "Skyrim:Battle-Born Farm") outside Whiterun and will buy [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), and [gourds](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") for a [harvesting job](/wiki/Skyrim:Gather_Wheat "Skyrim:Gather Wheat") well done.

At 6am, she goes to the [Temple of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Temple of Kynareth"), and then goes to the farm at 10am. She stays there until 8pm when she goes home, and goes to sleep at midnight. On Tirdas and Turdas, she goes to listen to [Heimskr's](/wiki/Skyrim:Heimskr "Skyrim:Heimskr") sermon at 3pm.

Alfhid wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000209a6 "Skyrim:Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5 "Skyrim:Boots"), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). She carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_House_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Key to House Battle-Born") to the [House of Clan Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:House_of_Clan_Battle-Born "Skyrim:House of Clan Battle-Born"), a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Battle-Born_Farmhouse "Skyrim:Key to Battle-Born Farmhouse") to Battle-Born Farm, and a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

When approached, she will often comment on the ongoing feud between the Battle-Born and [Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Gray-Mane") clans but takes a different view of the feud than her husband and father, saying:

:   *"This war's as stupid as our feud with Clan Gray-Mane."*
:   *"Need something? Unlike the rest of my family, I've got work to do."*

She may also reveal that her son [Lars](/wiki/Skyrim:Lars_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Lars Battle-Born") often gets bullied:

:   *"My boy Lars has been getting beat senseless, and by a [girl](/wiki/Skyrim:Braith "Skyrim:Braith"), no less!"*

She will also tell you about her brother Jon's interests:

:   *"My brother Jon's got the right of it - join the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College") and avoid this pointless war."* She will add, *"Too bad he died before he had the chance."* if Jon is dead.

She exits conversation with either:

:   *"I'll see you."*
:   *"It's back to work, then."*
