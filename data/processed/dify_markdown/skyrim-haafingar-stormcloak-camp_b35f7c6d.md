Haafingar Stormcloak Camp

**Haafingar Stormcloak Camp** is a [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Camps "Skyrim:Stormcloak Camps") in [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar"), located northeast of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") and south of the [Statue to Meridia](/wiki/Skyrim:Statue_to_Meridia "Skyrim:Statue to Meridia").

The camp is normally commanded by [Istar Cairn-Breaker](/wiki/Skyrim:Istar_Cairn-Breaker "Skyrim:Istar Cairn-Breaker"), except during the related quests, at which point [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist") will assume command.

## Haafingar Stormcloak Camp

It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. To the south of the campfire are three small tents containing unowned hay piles. To the southwest is the command tent, containing a dresser and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of their time. To the right of the command tent are some barrels. To the south is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and an end table. To the north of the campfire is another small tent containing an unowned hay pile, with a [thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch") plant behind the tent. Farther to the north is the hospital tent, which contains three hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers and a low table holding a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). To the left of the hospital tent is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs"), next to an unlocked chest containing various items to treat the sick, including potions of [cure poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison "Skyrim:Cure Poison"), potions of [cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Cure Disease"), and [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). To the right of the hospital tent is another small tent containing an unowned hay pile. Beyond the small tent are two picketed [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses"). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. In front of the hospital tent is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). To the south is the workstation for the [Stormcloak quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Stormcloak Quartermaster"), consisting of an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"). A long wooden table and a crate hold various [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") and [steel weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") and [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor"). There are many [torchbugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug_Thorax "Skyrim:Torchbug Thorax") near the camp at night, and during the day several [butterflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Passive_Creatures#Insects "Skyrim:Passive Creatures") can be found near the picketed horses.

Any time [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar") is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Legion "Skyrim:Joining the Legion"), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.
