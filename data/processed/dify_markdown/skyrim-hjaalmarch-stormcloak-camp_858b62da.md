Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp

**Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp** is a [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Camps "Skyrim:Stormcloak Camps") in [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"), located east of [Ustengrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Ustengrav "Skyrim:Ustengrav") and west of [Mzinchaleft](/wiki/Skyrim:Mzinchaleft "Skyrim:Mzinchaleft").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/SR-place-Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp_02.jpg)

A Stormcloak quartermaster working at the bench

The camp is normally commanded by [Arrald Frozen-Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrald_Frozen-Heart "Skyrim:Arrald Frozen-Heart"), except during the related quests, at which point [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist") will assume command.

## Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp

It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [charred skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") cooking over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. Around the campfire are three small tents containing unowned hay piles. West of the campfire are two picketed [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses"). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. To the north is the command tent, containing a lit brazier, a round table, and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of their time. To the northwest is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and an end table. To the left of the commander's tent is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). East of the campfire is the hospital tent, which contains three hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers and a low table holding a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). Beside the hospital tent is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs") and some barrels. To the south is the workstation for the [Stormcloak quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Stormcloak Quartermaster"), consisting of an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"). A long wooden table and a crate hold various [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") and [steel weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") and [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor").

Any time [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch") is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Legion "Skyrim:Joining the Legion"), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.
