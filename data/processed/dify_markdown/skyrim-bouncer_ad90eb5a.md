The Bouncer

The **Bouncer** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") guarding [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern"). He must be killed to complete [Supply and Demand](/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand "Skyrim:Supply and Demand").

## Related Quests

- **[Supply and Demand](/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand "Skyrim:Supply and Demand")**: Clear [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern") for the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften").

## Dialogue

Upon seeing you, he will draw his weapon and warn you away, saying:

:   *"These fights are invite only. And you're not invited. Slag off."*

He will attack if you approach too closely.

## Notes

- Before version 1.9 of the [Official Skyrim patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch#Version_1.9 "Skyrim:Patch") the bouncer was simply named 'Bandit'.
- Another bouncer can be found during a random encounter finding a dead body near two dead pit wolves. A [small note](/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Note "Skyrim:Small Note") can be found on this dead bouncer which leads to [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern").
