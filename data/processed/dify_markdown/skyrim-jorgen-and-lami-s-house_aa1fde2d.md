Jorgen and Lami's House

**Jorgen and Lami's House** is an unassuming, one-room cottage where [Jorgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorgen "Skyrim:Jorgen") and [Lami](/wiki/Skyrim:Lami "Skyrim:Lami") live. It lies in the outskirts of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") across from the lumber mill.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-place-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House_02.jpg)

The sawmill that supports Jorgen and Lami's lives

Their house is separated from the rest of the town of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") as their house is across a bridge from the rest of the buildings. There are a few steps leading up to the porch at the front of the house, the door is normally novice-locked. Opposite the house is the saw mill with two [wood chopping blocks](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"), a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") beside it. There are two [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") plants near where the sawn logs stack once cut and a [swamp fungal pod](/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod "Skyrim:Swamp Fungal Pod") slightly uphill to the north.

### Jorgen and Lami's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-interior-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.jpg)

Inside the house

They live rather simply in a one room home. To the left as you enter is a set of shelves holding two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulb and three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). Above these shelves are two garlic braids, three [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") all hanging from the ceiling. To the left of the shelves is a basket containing two leeks and to the right of the shelves is a barrel of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"), with a bucket on top hiding a copy of the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Morning Star, v1](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Morning_Star,_v1 "Skyrim:2920, Morning Star, v1")*. Behind the barrel is a unique item, a bottle of [Jessica's Wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Jessica%27s_Wine "Skyrim:Jessica's Wine"). Classed as a [beverage](/wiki/Skyrim:Beverages "Skyrim:Beverages"), it restores five points of [stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Stamina "Skyrim:Stamina").

In the middle of the south wall is a queen sized bed, at the end of the bed is an adept-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), which is identified as Jorgen's chest. To the right of the bed is a wooden table holding a copy of *[Songs of the Return, Vol 56](/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_the_Return,_Vol_56 "Skyrim:Songs of the Return, Vol 56")* and two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead"). Next to this, against the west wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). In the middle of the west wall is a fireplace, with a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") on the mantlepiece, a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front of the fire and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes "Skyrim:Shoes") in front of the hearth. Hanging from the ceiling to the left of the fireplace is a rack holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)").

In the northwestern corner, still against the west wall is a long wooden table and bench. On the table are two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). Under the table is a [potion of vigor](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor "Skyrim:Potion of Vigor"). In the northeastern corner is a square wooden table with two chairs, holding a [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Healing"), a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a [potion of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Enhanced Stamina"), a [taproot](/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot "Skyrim:Taproot"), a [creep cluster](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster"), a [torchbug thorax](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug_Thorax "Skyrim:Torchbug Thorax"), a bowl of [spriggan sap](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan_Sap "Skyrim:Spriggan Sap"), a bowl of [ectoplasm](/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm "Skyrim:Ectoplasm"), a sprig of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a [spider egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Egg "Skyrim:Spider Egg"). To the left in the middle of the north wall is a food barrel.

## Notes

- If you have started the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past "Skyrim:Pieces of the Past") to retrieve the parts of [Mehrunes' Razor](/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor "Skyrim:Mehrunes' Razor"), speaking to Jorgen can result in him giving you his [house key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Jorgen and Lami's House") and [chest key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Jorgen%27s_Chest "Skyrim:Key to Jorgen's Chest"). The [hilt of the razor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hilt_of_Mehrunes%27_Razor "Skyrim:Hilt of Mehrunes' Razor") can be found in Jorgen's chest.
