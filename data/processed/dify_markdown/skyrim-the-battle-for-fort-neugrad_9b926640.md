## Quick Walkthrough

1. Get your orders from Legate Rikke at the Falkreath Imperial Camp.
2. Rendezvous with the legionnaires north of [Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Fort Neugrad").
3. Clear the fort of Stormcloak rebels.
4. Report the news to General Tullius.

### Report for Duty

When speaking to [General Tullius](/wiki/Skyrim:General_Tullius "Skyrim:General Tullius") he will make a bitter comment about your decision to give up [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"). He will then direct you to [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") at the [Falkreath Imperial Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Imperial_Camp "Skyrim:Falkreath Imperial Camp"). She will give you orders to rendezvous with a group of Imperial soldiers preparing to attack [Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Fort Neugrad") and to assist in the assault.

### Join the Soldiers Preparing the Assault

Make your way to Fort Neugrad. The fort is located a small ways west of hold capital [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"), and the soldiers are waiting just southwest of the fort. As you approach the group, they will draw their weapons and attack the fort.

### Take the Fort

Assist your fellow soldiers in clearing the fort of Stormcloak rebels while the game updates you with the percentage of enemies remaining. The battle takes place in the exterior parts of the fort only, in the courtyard and on the walls.

Once you have killed all Stormcloak rebels, the quest will be completed. The [Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim") quest will update and prompt you to return to General Tullius to report your success and receive your reward.

## Notes

- Only if [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch") is given to the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") this quest will be given to you.
- Completing this quest will exile [Dengeir of Stuhn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dengeir_of_Stuhn "Skyrim:Dengeir of Stuhn") and [Tekla](/wiki/Skyrim:Tekla "Skyrim:Tekla") to [Candlehearth Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlehearth_Hall "Skyrim:Candlehearth Hall"), and re-install [Siddgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Siddgeir "Skyrim:Siddgeir") as [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of Falkreath.
- After completion of this quest, like any Civil War quest under [Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim"), unmarked by in-game references, you can approach Legate Rikke for a [medium leveled gold reward](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Gold_Rewards "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"); this is not always recommended, as entering her cell sets up the next quest in the series and, if triggered improperly, causes the missing *"Reporting for duty."* dialogue option (see [this](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim#Bugs "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim") bug section). Return to Legate Rikke to receive the leveled gold reward only when you want to start the next quest in the series and skip the reward when the game doesn't prompt you to return to her. Don't approach Rikke between quests of different holds.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage CWFortSiegeFort stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest CWFortSiegeFort`.
