Elberon's Apprentice

**Elberon's Apprentice** is a hostile mage who will appear in the Vardnknd Gallery section of [Runoff Caverns](/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns "Skyrim:Runoff Caverns") during the related quest, alongside several hostile mercenaries. He is the apprentice of [Elberon](/wiki/Skyrim:Elberon "Skyrim:Elberon"), a [Bosmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") wizard who is fatally struck by lightning when you first enter Vardnknd Gallery.

He is wearing [Apprentice Robes of Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Apprentice_Robes_of_Illusion "Skyrim:Apprentice Robes of Illusion"), an [apprentice hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Apprentice_Hood "Skyrim:Apprentice Hood"), and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"), and wields a [Dwarven dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Dagger "Skyrim:Dwarven Dagger"). He can use the [Lightning Bolt](/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Bolt "Skyrim:Lightning Bolt"), [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward") and [Invisibility](/wiki/Skyrim:Invisibility_(spell) "Skyrim:Invisibility (spell)") spells.

He uses generic [bandit dialogue](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit#Dialogue "Skyrim:Bandit").
