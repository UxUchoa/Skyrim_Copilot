Durian

Durian (also called **Dawnguard**) is an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [Ranger](/wiki/Skyrim:Ranger_(class) "Skyrim:Ranger (class)") who can be found inside [Bloodchill Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodchill_Cavern "Skyrim:Bloodchill Cavern"), attending the banquet during [Guests for Dinner](/wiki/Skyrim:Guests_for_Dinner "Skyrim:Guests for Dinner"). He's secretly a member of the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)").

## Related Quests

- **[Guests for Dinner](/wiki/Skyrim:Guests_for_Dinner "Skyrim:Guests for Dinner")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodchill_Manor "Skyrim:Bloodchill Manor"): Attend a feast.

### Guests for Dinner

:   **Are you here for the dinner party?**
:   *"Yep."*

:   **Are all the guests present?**
:   *"It would seem so."*

After the vampires are killed (Player as a Non-Vampire):

:   **The vampires are all dead. And the will says the house belongs to me.**
:   *"Very good. But you've created a debt between us. Here. Take this. We are even now."*

He will then hand you the [Bloodchill Manor Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodchill_Manor_Key "Skyrim:Bloodchill Manor Key").

## Notes

- He retains the changed name "Dawnguard" and can be found at [Candlehearth Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlehearth_Hall "Skyrim:Candlehearth Hall") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") after the completion of [Guests for Dinner](/wiki/Skyrim:Guests_for_Dinner "Skyrim:Guests for Dinner") as a Non-Vampire.
- Durian will not appear if you complete the quest [Guests for Dinner](/wiki/Skyrim:Guests_for_Dinner "Skyrim:Guests for Dinner") as a [Vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampirism "Skyrim:Vampirism").

## Bugs

- Leaving the cavern before finishing the quest may cause Durian to be a permanent follower that is impossible to get rid of.
