The Beast Stone

The **Beast Stone** is an [All-Maker Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones") west of [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall").

Once [cleansed](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones"), this stone gives you the power to [summon](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Werebear "Skyrim:Conjure Werebear") a [werebear](/wiki/Skyrim:Werebear "Skyrim:Werebear") to fight alongside you for 60 seconds. Once you use this power, it disappears from the Powers menu, and you must return to the Beast Stone to obtain it again.

## Related Quests

- **[Cleansing the Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones")**: Use the [Bend Will](/wiki/Skyrim:Bend_Will "Skyrim:Bend Will") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") to cleanse each of the remaining [All-Maker Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/SR-place-Beast_Stone_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Beast_Stone_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Beast_Stone_02.jpg)

The Thirsk warriors and the rieklings

The stone shows the usual signs of construction, with wooden scaffolding on the southeast side, and [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), [shovels](/wiki/Skyrim:Shovel "Skyrim:Shovel"), hammers, and carts in the vicinity. Several [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") plants and [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") grow around the stone. Paths lead southeast to Thirsk Mead Hall and west to the [Temple of Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Miraak "Skyrim:Temple of Miraak"), with a branching path leading north-northeast to the [Wind Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Wind_Stone "Skyrim:Wind Stone").

Before you cleanse the stone, a leveled [cultist](/wiki/Skyrim:Cultist "Skyrim:Cultist") will attack as you approach. During the day, two [rieklings](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling "Skyrim:Riekling") will be working on and bowing to the stone. During the night, three more rieklings and [Elmus](/wiki/Skyrim:Elmus "Skyrim:Elmus") will also be found at the stone, and after you witness the dialogue between [Bujold the Unworthy](/wiki/Skyrim:Bujold_the_Unworthy "Skyrim:Bujold the Unworthy") and [Kuvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kuvar "Skyrim:Kuvar") at [Bujold's Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Bujold%27s_Retreat "Skyrim:Bujold's Retreat"), Bujold, [Halbarn Iron-Fur](/wiki/Skyrim:Halbarn_Iron-Fur "Skyrim:Halbarn Iron-Fur"), [Hilund](/wiki/Skyrim:Hilund "Skyrim:Hilund"), and Kuvar will be found here as well. When you cleanse the stone, a leveled [lurker](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker") will rise from the water around the base of the stone (or possibly appear on the nearby hill) and attack you and the workers. After the lurker is defeated, the rieklings will remain near the stone for a short time, then leave; however, if you linger for too long, they may eventually attack.
