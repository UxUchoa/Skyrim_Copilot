Refugees' Rest

**Refugees' Rest** is a small ruined [tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Towers "Skyrim:Imperial Towers") east of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") containing leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-place-Refugees%27_Rest_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Refugees%27_Rest_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Refugees%27_Rest_02.jpg)

The *Decree of Monument*

The tower is easily found by taking the road northeast from Windhelm, passing [Brandy-Mug Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Brandy-Mug_Farm "Skyrim:Brandy-Mug Farm"), [Hlaalu Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Hlaalu_Farm "Skyrim:Hlaalu Farm"), and [Hollyfrost Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Hollyfrost_Farm "Skyrim:Hollyfrost Farm") and continuing toward the border with [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind"). It is the last building before the border is reached and was once an important meeting place for refugees from Morrowind following the [Red Year](/wiki/Lore:Red_Year "Lore:Red Year"). However, years of abandonment and neglect have taken their toll on the tower, as its foundation is beginning to collapse and it has tipped and sunken into the ground.

The doorway into the tower on the southwestern side leads into the ground floor, where bones and rubble are strewn about along with copies of *[Ancestors and the Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestors_and_the_Dunmer "Skyrim:Ancestors and the Dunmer")* and *[The Firsthold Revolt](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firsthold_Revolt "Skyrim:The Firsthold Revolt")*. A stone spiral staircase leads up to the top of the tower, which features an adept-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") but nothing else of interest.

Outside on the northeastern side of the tower is a small graveyard partially surrounded by a broken stone wall. At its base along the road is a plinth holding the *[Decree of Monument](/wiki/Skyrim:Decree_of_Monument "Skyrim:Decree of Monument")*, issued by all the [jarls](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of Skyrim to commemorate the tower's past.

Many [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes and varieties of [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") grow around the tower. On the opposite side of the road to the entrance are the remains of a wagon partially buried in the snow.
