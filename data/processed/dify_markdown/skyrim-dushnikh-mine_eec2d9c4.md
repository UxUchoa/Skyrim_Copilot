Dushnikh Mine

**Dushnikh Mine** is a small [orichalcum](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum "Skyrim:Orichalcum") [mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mines "Skyrim:Mines") supporting the [Orc stronghold](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") of [Dushnikh Yal](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnikh_Yal "Skyrim:Dushnikh Yal"). It contains only one zone, *[Dushnikh Mine](#Dushnikh_Mine)*.

It is owned and worked by [Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") from Dushnikh Yal. [Gharol](/wiki/Skyrim:Gharol "Skyrim:Gharol") will [buy orichalcum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Mine_Ore "Skyrim:Mine Ore") from you.

### Exterior

The exterior of the mine is the Orc stronghold of Dushnikh Yal. A [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") is near the covered work area beneath the mine.

### Dushnikh Mine

There are numerous [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") and [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks throughout the mine. Upon entering you are at the top of a ramp descending to the east, at the bottom is a cart containing five pieces of [orichalcum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore") (which are free to take if you are [blood-kin](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood-Kin "Skyrim:Blood-Kin") to the Orcs), with several food barrels behind. The path turns to the south, at the next junction there is a dead end to the east with a long wooden table and chair and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot. On the table is a copy of *[The Code of Malacath](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Code_of_Malacath "Skyrim:The Code of Malacath")*, a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a piece of orichalcum ore and a random pair of [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets "Skyrim:Gauntlets"). To the west is another T-junction, with a rock pillar in front of you, on a rock behind it is a random [stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Stamina "Skyrim:Stamina") [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") and [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"), to the north the path bears to the west with an orichalcum ore vein on the north wall and two [potions of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina") (see [bugs](#Bugs)) on a rock almost opposite, against the south wall. Farther to the west are three more ore veins against the south wall and on the ground in front of it.

Heading south from the T-junction the path descends before turning to the east and opening out into a large cave with a raised wooden platform against the rear wall in front of you, the ramp up to it is on your right. There are two ore veins on the east wall reached by climbing the platform. At the end of the platform is a long wooden table and bench, with two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a potion of minor healing on top the table. Under the platform against the east wall, on the ground is one final ore vein. In the northern corner of the lower area is a square wooden table with a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and mead barrel on top and a pair of [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") next to two unusable barrels beside it.

## Bugs

- You may not be able to interact with the two potions of minor stamina found at the northern end of the mine.
