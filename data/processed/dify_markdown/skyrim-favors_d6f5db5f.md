# Skyrim:Favors

## Overview

**Favors** are [miscellaneous objectives](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests") and [activities](/wiki/Skyrim:Activities "Skyrim:Activities") for citizens of Skyrim that typically involve doing a small errand for the quest giver. Completing these favors can increase their [disposition](/wiki/Skyrim:Disposition "Skyrim:Disposition") towards you, thereby progressing any objective to [assist the people of a hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People "Skyrim:Making Friends and Influencing People") as part of the quest to become a [Thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") of that hold (except in the Rift – see [notes](#Notes)).

Increasing your [relationship rank](/wiki/Skyrim:Disposition "Skyrim:Disposition") with the quest giver implies:

- You are freely able to take any low-value items in the quest giver's home.
- If the quest giver is a candidate for marriage, they will now like you enough to consider [marrying](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") you.
- If the quest giver is a potential follower, they will now like you enough to [follow](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") you.
- The quest giver may allow you to sleep for free in beds they own.
- You have provided assistance to a citizen of their [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds"), which helps you become a [thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") in that hold—even if the favor is completed before you start the hold's thane quest.

Note that favor quests for jarls usually lead to the beginning of the hold's thane quest, rather than counting as assistance to citizens, and also that people who live in [Orc strongholds](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") or travel with [Khajiit caravans](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_Traders "Skyrim:Khajiit Traders") do not count as citizens of holds, and so improving their relationship rank does not help with any thane quests.

## Quests

Several of the quests below are [radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant") quests, and some have multiple quest givers. If you have an active objective from a radiant quest, you must complete that quest before you can receive the same quest from a different quest giver; however, you can receive another quest which has the same name but is a different quest, for example if you are on the Delivery (Hillevi) quest you can begin Delivery (Sorex) without any problems.

Some factions have favor quests that you can do for members of that faction. Those quests are not listed here; see the faction pages for details.

### Activities

These favors display no information in your quest log, and with the exception of brawling per the activity [Fight! Fight!](/wiki/Skyrim:Fight!_Fight! "Skyrim:Fight! Fight!"), are repeatable and can be easily completed without any skill use.

- **[Chop Wood](/wiki/Skyrim:Chop_Wood "Skyrim:Chop Wood")**: Get paid to chop firewood. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Fight! Fight!](/wiki/Skyrim:Fight!_Fight! "Skyrim:Fight! Fight!")**: Prove your strength by wagering on a fist fight with another. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Gather Wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Gather_Wheat "Skyrim:Gather Wheat")**: Get paid to gather crops. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Mine Ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Mine_Ore "Skyrim:Mine Ore")**: Get paid to [mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mining "Skyrim:Mining") ore. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Quest all Beggars Have](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_all_Beggars_Have "Skyrim:Quest all Beggars Have")**: Take pity on a beggar. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Quest all Drunks Have](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_all_Drunks_Have "Skyrim:Quest all Drunks Have")**: Make a drunk dance for joy. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

## Notes

- Favor quests in [The Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift") do not provide progress towards becoming [Thane of the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_the_Rift "Skyrim:Thane of the Rift"), as that task uses a counter based on completion of [miscellaneous objectives in Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests#Riften "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests") instead. This includes those that are not Favors, and does not include objectives outside of Riften itself.
- Only one type of these Favor quests can be active at a time:
  - **[A Few Words with You](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Few_Words_with_You "Skyrim:A Few Words with You")**: Do a favor by talking to someone about a problem. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
  - **[Delivery](/wiki/Skyrim:Delivery "Skyrim:Delivery")**: Deliver an item to an associate. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
  - **[Dungeon Delving (Bandits)](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Bandits)")**: Do a favor by retrieving an item from a bandit hideout. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
  - **[Dungeon Delving (Caves)](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Caves) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Caves)")**: Do a favor by retrieving an item from a cave. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
  - **[Kill the Bandit Leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Bandit_Leader "Skyrim:Kill the Bandit Leader")**: Do a favor by slaying a bandit leader. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
  - **[Rare Gifts](/wiki/Skyrim:Rare_Gifts "Skyrim:Rare Gifts")**: Do a favor by finding a rare item. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
  - **[Some Light Theft](/wiki/Skyrim:Some_Light_Theft "Skyrim:Some Light Theft")**: Do a favor by stealing an item. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
