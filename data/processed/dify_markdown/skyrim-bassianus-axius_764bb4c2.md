**Bassianus Axius** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [citizen](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)") and a resident of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") where he works as a fisherman. A lover of [Fastred](/wiki/Skyrim:Fastred "Skyrim:Fastred"), Bassianus' desire is for both of them to leave for [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") against the wishes of Fastred's father, [Jofthor](/wiki/Skyrim:Jofthor "Skyrim:Jofthor"). Fastred herself is undecided on the matter and still has feelings for Bassianus' colleague and housemate, [Klimmek](/wiki/Skyrim:Klimmek "Skyrim:Klimmek"). During the quest [The Book of Love](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Love "Skyrim:The Book of Love") you will have to sort out the love triangle on behalf of [Mara](/wiki/Lore:Mara "Lore:Mara") and ensure things turn out the best for Fastred.

Ironically, Bassianus lives in the same [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Klimmek%27s_House "Skyrim:Klimmek's House") as his rival Klimmek, where he sleeps every night between 10pm and 4am. He starts the day with a one-hour breakfast with Klimmek then heads over to [Vilemyr Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Vilemyr_Inn "Skyrim:Vilemyr Inn") where he spends the entire day with the other regulars, sitting, conversing, or drinking. At 1pm and 6pm he pauses for one-hour meals. When triggering state 90 "Find Fenrig" of the related quest, he will move to Riften, though the choices you make in completing his portion of the quest will determine whether or not Fastred accompanies him. He was supposed to check in at [Haelga's Bunkhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse "Skyrim:Haelga's Bunkhouse") and sleep there every night between 8pm and 4am, but doesn't due to a [bug](#Bugs). Instead, he will wander around Riften 24 hours a day.

He wears a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b "Skyrim:Boots"). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") which can be up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") quality, and carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Klimmek%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Klimmek's House") to Klimmek's house, as well as a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-npc-Klimmek_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Klimmek_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Klimmek_04.jpg)

Bassianus and Klimmek arguing

Bassianus is not overly welcoming, and will greet you with:

:   *"You don't look like a pilgrim. Why bother visiting Ivarstead?"*
:   *"Hmm? What do you want?"*

Like the other Ivarstead citizens, he will tell you a bit about [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar"):

:   **Anything you can tell me about High Hrothgar?**
:   *"Klimmek brings food supplies up to them once every few weeks when the weather permits it. Other than that, they seem like a quiet lot. Don't really know too much about them."*

He will finish conversation with:

:   *"Hmph. What a boring conversation."*

### Conversations

The problems with Fastred have seemingly taken its toll on the relationship between the two fishermen. When they meet inside the house, they can be heard arguing:

**Bassianus**: *"If you have a moment, I've come up with a few ideas I wanted to share."*
**Klimmek**: *"I'm becoming sick to death of your ideas. Not one of them has paid off since you moved in."*
**Bassianus**: *"Look, I'm merely suggesting a few things that could expand our business."*
**Klimmek**: *"'Our' business? As I remember things, it was you that showed up at my doorstep looking for a job and a place to live. Try and remember that."*

**Klimmek**: *"We're behind again. I need you to catch another two bushels by week's end if I'm to make another delivery."*
**Bassianus**: *"Blood from a stone, Klimmek. I've been out there every morning. They just aren't biting."*
**Klimmek**: *"Come on, everyone in town knows that you spend more time with Fastred than fishing in the river."*
**Bassianus**: *"That's none of your business and I appreciate if you wouldn't mention it again."*

Bassianus' crush on Fastred is known all over town, and even local innkeeper [Wilhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Wilhelm "Skyrim:Wilhelm") will ask him about it:

**Wilhelm**: *"Have you spoken to Fastred yet?"*
**Bassianus**: *"No. I'll tell you, Wilhelm, if I could sweep her away from here tomorrow I'd do it, but Klimmek still has so much to learn."*
**Wilhelm**: *"Klimmek is doing just fine. You should follow your heart."*
**Bassianus**: *"Perhaps one day, but not today."*

### The Book of Love

If you choose to confront Bassianus at Boti's request:

:   **Boti said to talk to you about Fastred.**
:   *"Oh, my love. How I long to take her away from all of this. Why did Boti send you to me?"*

:   :   **I'm not sure. I hope things work out for you.**
    :   *"As do I. It's a puzzling situation."*

:   :   **She said that you should elope.**
    :   *"I always had a feeling she liked me. More than that brute Jofthor, at least. Jofthor... what about him? He'll come after us and... it will be unpleasant."*

        :   **Boti said she'd handle Jofthor.**
        :   *"She would? If she's willing to deal with him, then everything would be all right. I'll... I'll go speak to Fastred. Post-haste!"*

He will then run off to see Fastred, and this conversation will play:

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-quest-The_Book_of_Love_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Book_of_Love_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Book_of_Love_03.jpg)

*"Bassianus, sweetie, what is it?"*

## Notes

- Bassianus (and Fastred, if you did not side with Klimmek) will teleport to Riften at [stage 90](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Love#Quest_Stages "Skyrim:The Book of Love") (when you are told to find [Fenrig](/wiki/Skyrim:Fenrig "Skyrim:Fenrig")) of the related quest to ensure that you are nowhere near Ivarstead to witness the nonexistent journey.
- Curiously, Bassianus has an overtly Imperial name, but is considered a Nord in-game.
