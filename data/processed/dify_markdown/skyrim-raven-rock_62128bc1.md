Raven Rock

**Raven Rock** is a [city](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn_Places#Cities "Skyrim:Dragonborn Places") on the southwest coast of [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim"). It is the first place discovered in the [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") expansion. Once an [East Empire Company](/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company "Lore:East Empire Company") mining outpost, it is now a [House Redoran](/wiki/Lore:House_Redoran "Lore:House Redoran") colony. A far cry from the prosperous mining town of the past, Raven Rock has become a mostly forgotten settlement, its ebony mine depleted.

The city consists of a single thoroughfare running from the entrance through [the Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark "Skyrim:The Bulwark") in the east to the [Earth Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Earth_Stone "Skyrim:Earth Stone") in the west, following the line of the small adjacent bay. The jail and the Redoran guards' garrison are built into the Bulwark, which is the city's main defense against the ash from the [Red Mountain](/wiki/Lore:Red_Mountain "Lore:Red Mountain").

A curved wooden boardwalk runs along the bay, with a short pier near the center and a longer pier to the east. The [Northern Maiden](/wiki/Skyrim:Northern_Maiden "Skyrim:Northern Maiden"), which plies its trade between Solstheim and the city of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") in [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), is docked at the long pier, and three rowboats are docked elsewhere in the bay. The various shops and services of Raven Rock can be found in the central marketplace around the city's well north of the bay, while the [Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple "Skyrim:Temple") is near the Bulwark to the east. There are many freely available crafting stations outside in the city, including three [wood chopping blocks](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"), two [tanning racks](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), two [smelters](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter"), a [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge"), a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"). These stations are outside [Alor House](/wiki/Skyrim:Alor_House "Skyrim:Alor House"), [Glover Mallory's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory%27s_House "Skyrim:Glover Mallory's House"), [Ienth Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Ienth_Farm "Skyrim:Ienth Farm"), and [Raven Rock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Mine "Skyrim:Raven Rock Mine"), as well as on the long pier.

It is possible to acquire a home here, [Severin Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Severin_Manor "Skyrim:Severin Manor"), after completing the quest [Served Cold](/wiki/Skyrim:Served_Cold "Skyrim:Served Cold").

For historical information on Raven Rock and House Redoran, see the respective lore articles on [Raven Rock](/wiki/Lore:Raven_Rock "Lore:Raven Rock") and [House Redoran](/wiki/Lore:House_Redoran "Lore:House Redoran").

## Bugs

- The ominous music heard in [Apocrypha](/wiki/Skyrim:Apocrypha "Skyrim:Apocrypha") was incorrectly assigned to many of the buildings in Raven Rock, despite their unthreatening atmospheres.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.1, fixes this bug.
- The favor quests here don't improve the dispositions of their respective quest givers.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.1, fixes this bug.
