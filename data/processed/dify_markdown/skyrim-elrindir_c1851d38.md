Elrindir

**Elrindir** is a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") [fletcher](/wiki/Skyrim:Fletcher_(class) "Skyrim:Fletcher (class)") who owns [The Drunken Huntsman](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Drunken_Huntsman "Skyrim:The Drunken Huntsman") with his brother, [Anoriath](/wiki/Skyrim:Anoriath "Skyrim:Anoriath"). He can be found behind the counter at The Drunken Huntsman at all times. He will buy and sell items all day and night.

Elrindir wears a set of merchant [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0004223c "Skyrim:Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d "Skyrim:Boots"). He is equipped with a [steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Steel War Axe") and a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow") supplemented by twenty [steel arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow "Skyrim:Steel Arrow"), and carries a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

When spoken to:

:   **Why the name "Drunken Huntsman"?**
:   *"Yes, I hear that question often. My brother Anoriath and I came up with it one night after consuming... rather too much [mead](/wiki/Lore:Mead "Lore:Mead"). After our drinks, we went out for a moonlight hunt. We got separated, and in his drunken state my brother mistook me for a deer. He shot an arrow that pierced my... well, my rump. After such a memorable adventure, we knew we had the name for our shop."*

:   **Who should I talk to for work?**
:   *"Try [Hulda](/wiki/Skyrim:Hulda "Skyrim:Hulda"), the innkeeper at the [Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare"). It's just up the road, near the market. You should check in at [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach"), too. There's so much going on these days, the Jarl or his Steward might have need of help."*

**OR**

:   *"Try [Olfina](/wiki/Skyrim:Olfina_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Olfina Gray-Mane"), the new innkeeper at the Bannered Mare. It's just up the road, near the market. You should check in at Dragonsreach, too. ## Conversations

Elrindir can be seen having random conversations with his brother Anoriath, as outlined below:

:   **Anoriath**: *"I've been thinking of hunting out beyond the boundaries of [Whiterun hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold")."*
:   **Elrindir**: *"Again? You just had an 'extended hunt' last month. To the Pine Forest, wasn't it."*
:   **Anoriath**: *"That was three days. I'm thinking more like three weeks."*
:   **Elrindir**: *"Three weeks? You're mad! You can't keep the stand closed for that long!"*
:   **Anoriath**: *"Why not? Business has been terrible. Most folk would rather hunt their own meat than buy ours."*
:   **Elrindir**: *"Nonsense. That's just an excuse for you to avoid working, and 'commune with the greenwood like our fathers of old' and blah blah."*
:   **Anoriath**: *"Congratulations, brother. You've been living in [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") so long, you've become a Nord."*
:   **Elrindir**: *"At least the Nords don't shirk their responsibilities."*

:   **Elrindir**: *"Our stock of arrows is running a bit low."*
:   **Anoriath**: *"All right. I'll fletch some more when I get the time."*
:   **Elrindir**: *"Good. I'll do the same. We've still got plenty of shafts from that [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood") shipment."*

:   **Anoriath**: *"It's been two months, and no word from father."*
:   **Elrindir**: *"Don't worry, brother. I'm sure he's fine. We've waited longer before receiving a letter."*
:   **Anoriath**: *"Might be worth riding out to [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), see if a messenger ship's come in."*
:   **Elrindir**: *"If it'll put your mind at ease, then I agree. But not now, all right? We need the business from the stand."*

## Notes

- If you chose to [kill Anoriath](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Anoriath "Skyrim:Kill Anoriath") for the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood"), Elrindir will have a negative disposition towards you, even if you weren't detected.
- Elrindir is marked as a friend of [Olava the Feeble](/wiki/Skyrim:Olava_the_Feeble "Skyrim:Olava the Feeble"). However, the two will never meet or interact in-game, nor will they ever mention each other.
