Silent Moons Camp

**Silent Moons Camp** is a small [Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Ruins "Skyrim:Nordic Ruins") northwest of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") containing [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). It contains only one zone, *[Silent Moons Camp](#Silent_Moons_Camp)*.

## Related Quests

- **[In the Shadows](/wiki/Skyrim:In_the_Shadows "Skyrim:In the Shadows")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow_of_Shadows "Skyrim:Bow of Shadows"): Save the Jarl of Whiterun from an assassination plot.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-place-Silent_Moons_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Silent_Moons_Camp_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Silent_Moons_Camp_02.jpg)

Lunar Forge

The main part of the ruins are built on a hill and consists of a thick semi-circular wall with a walkway along the top around an open-topped dome-like barrow with an enclosed outer walkway around the central area which contains the unique [Lunar Forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Lunar_Forge "Skyrim:Lunar Forge"). The normal approach is from the southwest, where a stone hut on the left can be found as you approach, similar to several huts found at [Labyrinthian](/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian "Skyrim:Labyrinthian"). It contains two [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). Past this, by the bottom of a series of flights of wide stairs, on the left is a campfire, with a long wooden table holding two portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), four [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), and a wedge of cheese. There is also a rack with three whole [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") hanging from it and four food barrels nearby, while on the right is a wooden door leading into the interior. There is also a doorway blocked by a lowered gate in the bottom of the thick wall to the south. There are a number of leveled [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") guarding both the approach and working [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") at the top. There is a broken stone table in the outer walkway around the forge with three [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot") on top.

The Lunar Forge includes a complete blacksmith's forge, an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"). On the workbench is a copy of the [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Light Armor Forging](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor_Forging "Skyrim:Light Armor Forging")*, a book *[Notes On The Lunar Forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Notes_On_The_Lunar_Forge "Skyrim:Notes On The Lunar Forge")* which describes the forge, and a random leveled one-handed weapon enchanted with a unique enchantment which will burn the target when the moons are out. This and other enchanted "[lunar weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Items#Lunar_Weapons "Skyrim:Leveled Items")" found in the camp may take one of the following forms: [lunar iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Lunar_Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Lunar Iron Mace"), [lunar iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Lunar_Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Lunar Iron Sword"), [lunar iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Lunar_Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Lunar Iron War Axe"), [lunar steel mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Lunar_Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Lunar Steel Mace"), [lunar steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Lunar_Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Lunar Steel Sword"), or [lunar steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Lunar_Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Lunar Steel War Axe"). On top of the grindstone is a second random leveled one-handed lunar weapon. There is also an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") to the right of the workbench.

### Silent Moons Camp

Immediately through the door is an area with distinctly Nordic architecture, with a campfire surrounded by four unowned bedrolls. There is a long wooden table and chairs on your left and a set of shelves in the back corner of the room holding four bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). To the right of the fire is a tunnel, with a small table, three food barrels, and an empty cart on either side of it. The tunnel descends a short distance to the east, before turning south and opening out into a storage area, where you are likely to find the bandit leader and up to two bandits. In the storage area are four barrels of food, an unlocked chest, as well as clusters of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), and [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot") fungi. There is also a tunnel to the east, which turns immediately south and descends to a pair of adept-locked iron double doors. A bandit will be guarding these and will be alerted to any fighting above. There are two food barrels and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") to the left of the door.

Through the doors is a room with a ladder in the middle, with an unlocked chest to the right. Around the walls are three sets of shelves holding a [solution of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Health "Skyrim:Solution of Health"), a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), a [philter of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Philter_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Philter of Regeneration"), three [potions of extra magic](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Extra Magicka"), a [potion of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Enhanced Stamina"), a [potion of plentiful stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Stamina"), a random leveled one-handed lunar weapon, three loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), a bottle of [lingering stamina poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Lingering_Stamina_Poison "Skyrim:Lingering Stamina Poison"), two [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish") steaks, a bowl of [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat"), a sample of [hagraven feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Feathers "Skyrim:Hagraven Feathers"), and some bread. Hanging from the ceiling is a rack holding two bunches of [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam"), a [garlic braid](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid"), and a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). There is also a lever behind the door to the left, when entering the room, which operates the door you entered through.

The ladder exits inside a tower containing an unlocked chest and a chain to the right of the doorway, which raises the gate blocking access. There is an upper floor within the tower; however, this can only be reached by climbing partway up the hill and dropping down to it from the outer wall. On the upper level is a long wooden table holding a random leveled one-handed lunar weapon, a [draught of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Health "Skyrim:Draught of Health"), and a potion of regeneration, with a potion of enhanced stamina on the floor under the table. To the right of the table is a food barrel, and against the west wall is a set of shelves holding two random [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") of healing, magicka, or stamina and an [iron cuirass](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor").

## Notes

- Humorously, the bandits occupying the camp may inadvertently draw the ire of the [mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab") from the nearby stream; the mudcrabs will then launch an assault on the camp and may succeed in clearing the bandits from the camp exterior at lower levels. Similarly, if you are having trouble with the bandits in the camp exterior, you can lead them to the two peaceful [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") that patrol the plains near the ruin; these creatures should handily defeat the bandits. You can assist the mammoths, but beware of friendly fire, or you'll have to deal with both the bandits and the mammoths.
- You can not actually use the Lunar Forge to create lunar enchanted [weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons"), although you can disenchant one of the weapons then create your own using any [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter").
- The best possible lunar weapons will spawn here if you are at least level 8.
- The gate to the tower stays open and doesn't reset when the location respawns.
- Nothing respawns in the tower except one of the random potions on the set of shelves on the upper floor.
- Two [silver ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") are on the east side of a dry stream bed south of the camp.
- Two [moonstone ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein") are north of the camp.
- One [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") is on a small hill northeast of the camp in the direction of [Halted Stream Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Halted_Stream_Camp "Skyrim:Halted Stream Camp").
- *Notes On The Lunar Forge* incorrectly states that the Lunar weapons here give you a [vampire-like](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampirism "Skyrim:Vampirism") effect that transfers health from the victim to the user like [absorb health](/wiki/Skyrim:Absorb_Health "Skyrim:Absorb Health"), when their [real enchantment](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Damage "Skyrim:Light Damage") doesn't absorb health at all.
