Kjenstag Ruins

**Kjenstag Ruins** is a small [outdoor Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings "Skyrim:Nordic Dwellings") east-northeast of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal").

## Walkthrough

It is a circular ruin with an arched entrance to the north. Inside there is a short central wall with an empty stone table in front and an adept-locked wooden [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") behind. It is possible to climb to the top of the walls via the southeast where the structure has partially crumbled or to the west by climbing a rock formation and jumping from there. However, apart from the view, there is nothing of interest up there. There are also no harvestable plants in the immediate vicinity.

Upon approaching the ruins at night between 8pm and 5am, an unnamed [ghost](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost "Skyrim:Ghost")(reif 000725f7) will run from the entrance, leading you to an [unmarked barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Hjaalmarch#Ghost_Barrow "Skyrim:Unmarked Places Hjaalmarch") just southeast of [Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp"). The barrow is being robbed by two [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"), one of whom carries the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Dance in Fire, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v2 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v2")*. The barrow itself features a trap door covering a spiral staircase leading down to a sarcophagus containing a [mummified draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Draugr "Skyrim:Containers") and three items of leveled loot: a [weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons"), a [necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Necklace "Skyrim:Necklace"), and a [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"). As the ghost only appears once, it might be easy to miss.

## Notes

- If you happen upon the unmarked barrow between 5am and 8pm and open the trap door before approaching Kjenstag Ruins, the ghost will not appear. The sarcophagus containing the mummified draugr and loot will be open when you return to the barrow.[*verification needed*]
- If you kill the ghost before arriving at the unmarked barrow, you can get a complete set of [Ancient Nord Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord_Armor "Skyrim:Ancient Nord Armor") from its ashes.
- Down the hill from the ruin to the north, behind a rocky cliff face and between two dead trees, is an isolated [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block").
- Kjenstag Ruins also [appears](/wiki/Online:Kjenstag_Ruins "Online:Kjenstag Ruins") in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online").
