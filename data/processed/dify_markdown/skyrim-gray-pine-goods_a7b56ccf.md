**Gray Pine Goods** is a small general goods store in [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") home to the proprietor [Solaf](/wiki/Skyrim:Solaf "Skyrim:Solaf") and his brother [Bolund](/wiki/Skyrim:Bolund "Skyrim:Bolund").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-interior-Gray_Pine_Goods.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gray_Pine_Goods.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gray_Pine_Goods.jpg)

Gray Pine Goods

It is near the center of Falkreath, opposite [Dead Man's Drink](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Man%27s_Drink "Skyrim:Dead Man's Drink") and next to [Lod's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Lod%27s_House "Skyrim:Lod's House").

### Exterior

The building has a balcony at the front, which is accessible from the rear via a flight of wooden stairs or from the door on the top floor of the house. Underneath the balcony to the left of the entrance is a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel. To the right of the property is another flight of stairs ascending to the top of a wall that serves as a lookout point for the town guards.

### Gray Pine Goods

Upon entry, Bolund will be in the process of leaving (see [bugs](#Bugs)). The counter is to the right of the entrance and contains several items. Against the southwest wall is a set of shelves and a cupboard, both of which are filled with food, beverages, and [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"). A food barrel is in the west corner, and a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") is under the counter. Against the southeast wall is a dresser with a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword"), an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"), and three [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow") on top. A ceiling rack containing three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, and two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") hangs above the dresser.

Opposite the door on the southeast wall is a fireplace with four [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") hanging from a rack to the left. Further to the left is a food barrel and a set of shelves containing an iron mace. There is a flight of stairs leading up to the living quarters against the northeast wall with several barrels, a pile of firewood, and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot underneath.

At the top of the stairs is a long wooden table against the southeast wall. On the table are several pieces of cheese, two pieces of [salmon meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat "Skyrim:Salmon Meat"), three bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a couple of loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and three [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). To the right is a set of shelves holding only clutter. Coming out from the southwest wall are two owned single beds with a round table between them and an unlocked chest to the left. On the table are two bottles of alto wine and five [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). In the northern corner above the stairs are several sacks of foodstuffs and a couple of barrels. A door leading out onto the balcony is located in the middle of the northwest wall.
