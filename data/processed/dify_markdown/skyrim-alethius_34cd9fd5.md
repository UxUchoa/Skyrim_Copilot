Alethius

**Alethius** is a deceased [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") whose corpse can be found just before the entrance to [Nchuand-Zel](/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel "Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel"), within the Nchuand-Zel Excavation Site underneath [Understone Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep "Skyrim:Understone Keep") in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). He wears a full set of [Imperial armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Armor "Skyrim:Imperial Light Armor"), and carries a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Alethius%27s_Notes "Skyrim:Alethius's Notes") that begins the quest [The Lost Expedition](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition "Skyrim:The Lost Expedition"). He was assigned to guard the research expedition into Nchuand-Zel, but met his untimely death, likely by [Nimhe](/wiki/Skyrim:Nimhe "Skyrim:Nimhe"), the giant [frostbite spider](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider") guarding the entrance to Nchuand-Zel, whose lair is just above Alethius's body.

## Related Quests

- **[The Lost Expedition](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition "Skyrim:The Lost Expedition")**: Recover the journals from the expedition in [Nchuand-Zel](/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel "Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel").

## Notes

- His note is written to someone named Salonia, and [two](/wiki/Skyrim:Salonia_Carvain "Skyrim:Salonia Carvain") [NPCs](/wiki/Skyrim:Salonia_Caelia "Skyrim:Salonia Caelia") with that first name exist in Skyrim. However, neither of them appear to have any connection to him.
- It's possible that he was created with [Bassianus Axius](/wiki/Skyrim:Bassianus_Axius "Skyrim:Bassianus Axius") as a base, as they share the same voice type and it would explain Alethius' otherwise random faction inclusions.

## Bugs

- Due to the fact that he's part of the [Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift") crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill him, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.6, addresses this issue. He has been removed from the crime faction.
