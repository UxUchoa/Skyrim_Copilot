The **Boethiah Cultists** are worshippers of the Daedric Prince [Boethiah](/wiki/Lore:Boethiah "Lore:Boethiah") and are located at the [Sacellum of Boethiah](/wiki/Skyrim:Sacellum_of_Boethiah "Skyrim:Sacellum of Boethiah"). They play a part in the quest [Boethiah's Calling](/wiki/Skyrim:Boethiah%27s_Calling "Skyrim:Boethiah's Calling"), but they can also be found as a [random encounter](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Boethiah_Cultist_attack "Skyrim:World Interactions"), carrying *[Boethiah's Proving](/wiki/Skyrim:Boethiah%27s_Proving "Skyrim:Boethiah's Proving")* in their inventory and initiating the quest. They can be easily recognized by a unique sign tattooed in black on the right side of their faces.

## Bugs

- The Boethiah Cultist who attacks you in a [random encounter](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions "Skyrim:World Interactions") (WEDA02Cultist) doesn't become hostile until he's been very close to you for some time.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.9, addresses this issue. An alias property needed to be adjusted to make sure he immediately attacks on sight.
- Looting the book from the dead Boethiah Cultist may fail to close the objective to investigate the cultists once the book is read.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.0.8, fixes this bug.
- After A Matter of Pride is finished, some Boethiah Cultists may respawn in the [Arcanaeum](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum "Skyrim:The Arcanaeum") when the area resets. From there, they can follow you wherever you go, and they can appear anywhere, including inside player homes.
  - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch"), version 1.6.629, fixes this bug.
