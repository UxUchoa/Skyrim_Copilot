## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Filnjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Filnjar "Skyrim:Filnjar") about the local mine.
2. Clear [Redbelly Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Redbelly_Mine "Skyrim:Redbelly Mine") of [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider").
3. Return to Filnjar.

### Of Sulfur and Spiders

[Redbelly Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Redbelly_Mine "Skyrim:Redbelly Mine") is a small [ebony](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony "Skyrim:Ebony") [mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mining "Skyrim:Mining") located in [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"). The mine gets its name from the red mist filling the caverns and smelling like the sulfurous hot springs out in the volcanic tundra. The local blacksmith, [Filnjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Filnjar "Skyrim:Filnjar"), will tell you the mine is not producing right now. In fact, the mine is currently overrun with [spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider"). Filnjar is worried that the invading arachnids will venture out of the mine looking for food soon. What's worse, the town guards are as useless as "a fifth wheel on a wagon" as they prefer to keep an eye out for enemy soldiers rather than trouble themselves protecting the town's major commodity. If you are willing to give Filnjar a hand, he will tell you, *"Keep to your word, and I'll line your pockets with as much as I can scrape up."*

### Spider Extermination

The mine is to the west, close to [Filnjar's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Filnjar%27s_House "Skyrim:Filnjar's House"). Get into the mine and you'll find some frostbite spiders soon. There are two groups of three spiders, with medium and small spiders attacking en masse. Remove every spider to complete the quest. You can also find the three ebony ore veins near the bottom level of the mine.

### Gold for Spiders

Return to Filnjar and tell him the good news. *"Gone? Every one of them? Why, that's incredible."* Then he'll reward you with leveled gold for reopening the mine and putting Shor's Stone back on the map.

| Levels | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1–9 | 500 |
| 10–19 | 750 |
| 20–29 | 1,000 |
| 30–39 | 1,250 |
| 40+ | 1,500 |

## Notes

- There is a [Shor's Stone Guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone_Guard "Skyrim:Shor's Stone Guard") that sits outside the mine. You can utilize a common bug where they will follow you into the mine while dialogue is ongoing and they can then be used to help clear the mine, though it's not a difficult task unless attempted at a very low level.
- Killing all the frostbite spiders triggers the option to "Return to Filnjar" regardless of having talked to him or not.
- Filnjar's dialogue does not change even if the Civil War is over; he still says the guards are looking out for enemy soldiers.

## Bugs

- This quest does not display a quest pointer as intended due to checking the wrong conditions for the objective alias.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
- There are several desiccated corpses within the mine despite Filnjar stating that nobody had been harmed by the infestation.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
- After first completion, the spiders may respawn and the miners will continue to work in the newly infested mine, potentially causing harm to NPCs in the mine.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
  - If the spiders respawn and you have already completed the quest once, the quest will reset at the point where you have to return to Filnjar after killing some or all of the spiders, allowing you to repeat the quest as many times as you want.
  - The miners leave the mine at night; entering the mine then should allow you to kill the spiders again without any harm coming to the miners.
