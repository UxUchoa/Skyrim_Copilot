Cold Rock Pass

**Cold Rock Pass** is a small [passage](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") through a mountain south-southwest of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") containing a [frost troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll").

Although the interior of the ruin is named "Cold Rock Pass", the map markers for the two entrances are named "North Cold Rock Pass" [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=North+Cold+Rock+Pass&zoom=14&legacy=true) and "South Cold Rock Pass" [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=South+Cold+Rock+Pass&zoom=14&legacy=true), counting as two separate locations for the "Locations Discovered" statistic. The northern entrance is south of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal"), and the southern entrance is northeast of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead").

### Exterior: North Cold Rock Pass

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-place-North_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-North_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-North_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg)

North Cold Rock Pass

The northern entrance is found by following the path southwest of Morthal toward [Eldersblood Peak](/wiki/Skyrim:Eldersblood_Peak "Skyrim:Eldersblood Peak"); when you pass a banner attached to a post on your left, the entrance is on the next switchback to the left. An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") is along the path near the entrance, and several [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes grow in the vicinity.

### Exterior: South Cold Rock Pass

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-place-South_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-South_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-South_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg)

South Cold Rock Pass

The southern entrance is found by ascending the stone stairs leading north to the left of the entrance of [Rannveig's Fast](/wiki/Skyrim:Rannveig%27s_Fast "Skyrim:Rannveig's Fast"). A dead [skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever") is next to the entrance. An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") is down the hill to the entrance.

### Cold Rock Pass

Upon entering via the nothern entrance, you enter a natural cave heading down and to the west. Ahead is a wooden crate near a lootable skeleton and a sack of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"). To the south, to the left of an earthen ramp leading up is a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein"). At the top of the ramp the cave opens up in front of you, with a couple rock pillars supporting the ceiling and a frozen pond of water being fed by a small waterfall. Southwest of the waterfall on a higher ledge is a frost troll. The ramp leading up to it is along the northwest wall of the cave.

The ledge has a [quicksilver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore Vein") on the southwest wall of the cave. Among the bones and blood splatters on the ground are some [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") or [steel weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") and a dead skeever or two. There may be the body of a [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") by the edge, although this may have fallen off and be lying on the ice below. Near the frost troll is a dead [elk](/wiki/Skyrim:Elk "Skyrim:Elk") and an open chest holding a set of random armor, a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword"), and two [potions of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). To the south, below the ledge is a large shallow pond that has mostly frozen over, with the path through the cave beside it. Against the east wall, opposite the waterfall is a food barrel next to an unlocked chest. At the southern end of the pond is a second corundum ore vein, with a ramp up to its left leading past a cart containing a food barrel, a second barrel beside it, and a second lootable skeleton, ending at the southern entrance.

## Notes

- Although the *[Prima Official Game Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide")* places this location in [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"), the game data states it is in [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.1, addresses this issue. The pass has been set to be part of Hjaalmarch instead.
  - Technically, the pass itself is a transition between the two holds, with its southern entrance in Whiterun Hold and its northern entrance in Hjaalmarch, so either could be said to be applicable.
- Version 2.0.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") replaces all [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes outside North Cold Rock Pass with the snow-covered version of the same plant.
- In [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
