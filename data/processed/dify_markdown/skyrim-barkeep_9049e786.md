The barkeep tending his bar at Cragslane Cavern

The **Barkeep** is a hostile [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") found in [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern") serving drinks to the [gamblers](/wiki/Skyrim:Gambler "Skyrim:Gambler"). He wears [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0005b6a1 "Skyrim:Clothes") and [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0 "Skyrim:Shoes") and is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:One-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:One-Handed Weapons") (up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") quality). He carries a considerable amount of leveled gold, ranging from 25 to 125 gold, and the [Craglane chest key](/wiki/Skyrim:Craglane_Chest_Key "Skyrim:Craglane Chest Key") which opens the chest behind his bar.
