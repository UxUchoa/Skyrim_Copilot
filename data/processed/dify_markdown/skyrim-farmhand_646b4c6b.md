Farmhand

**Farmhands** are generic workers that you can ask your [Personal Steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Personal_Steward "Skyrim:Personal Steward") to hire for [Goldenhills Plantation](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenhills_Plantation "Skyrim:Goldenhills Plantation"). Once hired, two of them will work the farm and dwell in the [Goldenhills Farm Bunkhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenhills_Farm_Bunkhouse "Skyrim:Goldenhills Farm Bunkhouse"). If the bunkhouse hasn't been [constructed](/wiki/Skyrim:Farming_Construction "Skyrim:Farming Construction") yet, they will instead set up bedrolls and camp outside.

You can also personally hire [Angrenor Once-Honored](/wiki/Skyrim:Angrenor_Once-Honored "Skyrim:Angrenor Once-Honored"), [Brenuin](/wiki/Skyrim:Brenuin "Skyrim:Brenuin"), [Embry](/wiki/Skyrim:Embry "Skyrim:Embry"), or [Narfi](/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi "Skyrim:Narfi") instead of the two generic farmhands.
