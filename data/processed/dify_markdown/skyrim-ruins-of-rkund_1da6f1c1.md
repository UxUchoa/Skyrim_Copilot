Ruins of Rkund

The **Ruins of Rkund** are a small [Dwarven ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwarven Ruins") in the mountains southwest of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") containing a [wispmother](/wiki/Skyrim:Wispmother "Skyrim:Wispmother") and [wisps](/wiki/Skyrim:Wisp "Skyrim:Wisp").

## Walkthrough

The ruins are most easily accessed by passing through the [hagraven](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven "Skyrim:Hagraven")-infested [Darklight Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower "Skyrim:Darklight Tower") and heading southeast into the mountains. They consist of a few broken pillars and collapsed buildings; the one farthest to the east contains an unlocked [Dwemer chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). In front of this is a Dwemer floor lantern in a stone pit, with the bodies of two [hags](/wiki/Skyrim:Hag "Skyrim:Hag") lying next to it, along with a few pieces of [Dwemer metal](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Metal "Skyrim:Dwemer Metal") and an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"). As you approach the ruins, three [wisps](/wiki/Skyrim:Wisp "Skyrim:Wisp") will be seen roaming the area. Approaching the lantern will trigger the appearance of their [wispmother](/wiki/Skyrim:Wispmother "Skyrim:Wispmother"). The only other items of interest are a few [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes in the area.

## Bugs

- The two dead hags may appear with their clothing unequipped.
- The wisps don't seem to respawn.
- You may have to attack the lantern in the center in order to trigger the wispmother event.
