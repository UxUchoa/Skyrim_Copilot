Part of Beggar's Row

**Beggar's Row** is a small part of a sewer that has been converted into a homeless den in lower [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), by the canal that runs through the center of the city. It is by the northern stairs down from upper Riften, opposite [Elgrim's Elixirs](/wiki/Skyrim:Elgrim%27s_Elixirs "Skyrim:Elgrim's Elixirs"). It only contains one [interior zone](#Beggar.27s_Row).

## Beggar's Row

Outside the door are several barrels that are free to take from. Beggar's Row is a heavily vaulted part of the sewer, that has two owned hay piles and one owned bedroll inside it. [Madesi](/wiki/Skyrim:Madesi "Skyrim:Madesi"), [Edda](/wiki/Skyrim:Edda "Skyrim:Edda") and [Snilf](/wiki/Skyrim:Snilf "Skyrim:Snilf") all sleep in here at night. There is a campfire to the north and a couple of chairs as well as several [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks and barrels around the place. Scattered about the floor are a couple of bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and various vegetables, a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg") and several pieces of cheese. At the southwest connection there are two bottles of [skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma"). There is a copy of the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Dance in Fire, v7](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v7 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v7")* due north upon entering, under a fern. The flat sack near the tipped-over basket is non-respawning, while the other upright sack does respawn.
