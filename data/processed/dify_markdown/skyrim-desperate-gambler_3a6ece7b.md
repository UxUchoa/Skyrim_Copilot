Desperate Gambler

The **Desperate Gambler** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") who may appear in the [Windhelm Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm_Barracks "Skyrim:Windhelm Barracks") during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever"). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Mother "Skyrim:Night Mother") picks him as the contract giver.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0003452e "Skyrim:Clothes") along with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f "Skyrim:Boots"). He carries a copy of *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother "Skyrim:A Kiss, Sweet Mother")* and wields an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword").

He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever#Detailed_Walkthrough "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever") for details.
