## Quick Walkthrough

1. Wait for a [courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Courier "Skyrim:Courier") to deliver a [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya%27s_Hastily_Written_Letter "Skyrim:Viriya's Hastily Written Letter") from [Viriya](/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya "Skyrim:Viriya").
2. Travel to the shack west of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") and join the defense.
3. Kill several waves of [frenzied mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Frenzied_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Frenzied Mudcrab") and regroup with Viriya inside the shack.
4. Loot [flaming pots](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Flaming_Pot&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Flaming Pot (page does not exist)") from the mudcrabs and use the catapults to launch them at the [Emperor Crab Guardian Spirit](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor_Crab_Guardian_Spirit "Skyrim:Emperor Crab Guardian Spirit").
5. Speak with Viriya once the spirit is defeated. Visit the jarl of Dawnstar for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This quest will only be triggered after you have exhausted [Viriya](/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya "Skyrim:Viriya")'s fishing jobs. The final quest she will offer you is [Beneath Bronze Waters](/wiki/Skyrim:Beneath_Bronze_Waters "Skyrim:Beneath Bronze Waters"). Once this is completed, you will receive a [hastily written letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya%27s_Hastily_Written_Letter "Skyrim:Viriya's Hastily Written Letter") from a [courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Courier "Skyrim:Courier").

Read the letter and you will learn that the [frenzied mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Frenzied_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Frenzied Mudcrab") have emerged again and that the guards sent to deal with them were attacked by a spirit. Head to the shack on the shore west of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") where you will have to defeat the frenzied mudcrabs alongside Viriya and the guards. After a short while, another wave of frenzied mudcrabs will attack, once defeated you will need to regroup with Viriya inside the shack.

All of a sudden a loud explosion goes off and the whole ground rumbles. Head outside to see the huge "Emperor Crab Guardian Spirit" that has risen from the sea! The explosion was from a wagon full of Flaming Pots. The Frenzied mudcrabs will begin grabbing these pots and scurrying away with them. Kill them and grab these pots while avoiding the guardian crab's huge pincer slams. Head back to the catapults and load them up with flaming pots one at a time. Activate the catapult and it flings the flaming pots at the giant crab ghost. Do this about six times and it will be reduced to the ether. Viriya gives her thanks. Head to the White Hall in Dawnstar to receive a gold reward from the Jarl. At this point Viriya goes missing but after a little while sends a letter to begin [End of the Line](/wiki/Skyrim:End_of_the_Line "Skyrim:End of the Line").

## Notes

- This quest can be initiated by heading straight to the fishing shack, even without getting the letter.
- The courier has been known to arrive, meet you in Dawnstar and start this quest even if [Beneath Bronze Waters](/wiki/Skyrim:Beneath_Bronze_Waters "Skyrim:Beneath Bronze Waters") is in progress but unfinished. Whether this is intentional or a bug is unknown.
- After this quest a fisherman respawns at the fishing shack.
- About 4-5 of the Flaming Pots can be collected and kept when the quest is over.
- It was originally planned for the quest to end with you fighting a living Emperor Crab rather than a ghost. Once the crab was defeated, you would have been able to hollow out the crab's body and use it as a player home, however this was [cut for time](/wiki/General:Cartogriffi%27s_Posts#2022 "General:Cartogriffi's Posts"). There is a placeholder cell still in the [Creation Kit](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit "Skyrim Mod:Creation Kit") (`ccPlaceholder18Interior01`), as well as an unfinished location record stating it was called the "Emperor's Homestead".

## Bugs

- Sometime Viriya will not join you in the shack. Save/quitting and reloading can cause her to trigger the regroup in shack but is not guaranteed. However, fast traveling to another location, saving and reloading than fast traveling back to Viriya will fix the bug in most cases.
- If Dawnstar is under Imperial control, the objective to report to the Jarl may still direct you to [Skald](/wiki/Skyrim:Skald "Skyrim:Skald"), who is likely to be in exile in [The Palace of the Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") (in this case, [Jarl Brina Merilis](/wiki/Skyrim:Brina_Merilis "Skyrim:Brina Merilis") will not have dialogue options to complete the objective). Additionally, the guards spawned to assist in the battle may still be [Dawnstar Guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar_Guard "Skyrim:Dawnstar Guard"), some of whom seem to be Essential or Protected -- once they make their way back to Dawnstar after the fight, they'll fight with (and kill) the resident Imperial soldiers. **?**
