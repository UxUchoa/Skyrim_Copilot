## Quick Walkthrough

1. Go to [the Arcanaeum](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum "Skyrim:The Arcanaeum") and read [*Soran's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Soran%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Soran's Journal").
2. Locate and [tame](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses#Tamable_HorsesCC "Skyrim:Horses") the [Unicorn](/wiki/Skyrim:Unicorn "Skyrim:Unicorn") just west of [Lost Prospect Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Prospect_Mine "Skyrim:Lost Prospect Mine").

### First Named, Last Tamed

Upon installing the [Wild Horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Wild_Horses "Skyrim:Wild Horses") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"), you will be directed to [the Arcanaeum](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum "Skyrim:The Arcanaeum") in the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)") to learn more about reports of a recent [Unicorn](/wiki/Skyrim:Unicorn "Skyrim:Unicorn") sighting. Once there, you will find [*Soran's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Soran%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Soran's Journal") sitting on [Urag gro-Shub](/wiki/Skyrim:Urag_gro-Shub "Skyrim:Urag gro-Shub")'s desk. It details the research on Unicorns carried out by a wildlife scholar named Soran Hariksen, who sighted a Unicorn in the forests of [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"). The creature is supposedly from the distant past and was brought forward in time by the [Psijic Order](/wiki/Skyrim:Psijic_Order "Skyrim:Psijic Order") after the [last living Unicorn](/wiki/Oblivion:Unicorn "Oblivion:Unicorn") was sacrificed to [Hircine](/wiki/Lore:Hircine "Lore:Hircine") during his [quest](/wiki/Oblivion:Hircine "Oblivion:Hircine") in [*Oblivion*](/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion "Oblivion:Oblivion").

Once you have read the journal, you will be directed to an area just west of the entrance to [Lost Prospect Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Prospect_Mine "Skyrim:Lost Prospect Mine"). The Unicorn can be found right next to the nearby pond. It is a [wild horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Wild_Horse "Skyrim:Wild Horse") and will run away from you if approached. It must be [tamed](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses#Tamable_HorsesCC "Skyrim:Horses"), which is a mechanic added by the Creation. You must mount the Unicorn until it stops bucking in order to claim it as your own. You may be thrown off when it bucks, which will necessitate chasing it down again once you get back on your feet.

### Reward

Once tamed, the Unicorn will become your mount. If you decide to switch to a different mount, it will make its way to the College of Winterhold courtyard and wait for you there.

## Notes

- Like other horses, the Unicorn can be [named](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses#Horse_NamingCC "Skyrim:Horses") and [saddled](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses#SaddlesCC "Skyrim:Horses"), which are two other mechanics added by this Creation. To do this, you must visit a [stable](/wiki/Skyrim:Stables "Skyrim:Stables") with the Unicorn as your active mount.
