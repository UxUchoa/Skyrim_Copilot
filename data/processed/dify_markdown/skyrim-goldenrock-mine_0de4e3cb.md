Goldenrock Mine

**Goldenrock Mine** is a small [corundum](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum "Skyrim:Corundum") [mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mines "Skyrim:Mines") in southeastern [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing"). It contains only one zone, *[Goldenrock Mine](#Goldenrock_Mine)*.

It was discovered and established by the adventurer [Annekke Crag-Jumper](/wiki/Skyrim:Annekke_Crag-Jumper "Skyrim:Annekke Crag-Jumper"). [Verner Rock-Chucker](/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_Rock-Chucker "Skyrim:Verner Rock-Chucker") will [buy corundum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Mine_Ore "Skyrim:Mine Ore") from you.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine.jpg)

Mine treasure

Around the entrance to the mine are stacks of crates and food sacks that are marked as owned. Among these are four pieces of owned [corundum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore "Skyrim:Corundum Ore"). The [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") is a few paces away, with three owned [corundum ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ingot "Skyrim:Corundum Ingot") on the ground beside it.

The miners' camp is just outside and to the right of the mine entrance. It consists of a central campfire surrounded by four small tents, three of which contain bedrolls, while the fourth contains a hay pile. Once you are on friendly terms with the settlement, you can use these freely. Eight whole [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") hang on fishing racks behind the tents.

### Goldenrock Mine

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine_02.jpg)

Mine interior

The mine has many [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") and shovels lying around. The entrance tunnel descends to the southeast to a T-junction with a corundum ore vein on the ground at the junction and a dead end to the right with a second ore vein. The tunnel continues descending ahead. At the bottom there is a braced section with a bedroll under it along with a couple food barrels; on top of one of the barrels is a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Chimarvamidium](/wiki/Skyrim:Chimarvamidium "Skyrim:Chimarvamidium")*. The tunnel turns to the northeast and opens into a larger cavern with wooden scaffolding around a central rock pillar allowing access to two more ore veins. At the bottom on the left are several food sacks and a random [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword") on a stack of crates. Ascending the ramp leads to the two ore veins on the walls, a small round wooden table with an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet") and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") on top, and an open box holding three bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead").
