## Quick Walkthrough

Find the Fishing Supplies in the assigned city. Use them and start fishing. When the line pulls strong without water splashing, you have an item hooked. Keep doing this until you have all four.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Go to the mentioned area and fish for rubbish. It works better with the standard fishing rod.

## Bugs

- Due to an issue with how the fishing supply objects were set up, it is unlikely you'll ever get this quest in Dawnstar or Morthal.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.9, fixes this bug.
