**Solitude Sawmill** is a wood mill southwest of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). It consists of a house and a sawmill. It is owned by [Hjorunn](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjorunn "Skyrim:Hjorunn"), a Nord who employs an Orc named [Kharag gro-Shurkul](/wiki/Skyrim:Kharag_gro-Shurkul "Skyrim:Kharag gro-Shurkul"). You can sell firewood to Hjorunn, and also buy [sawn logs](/wiki/Skyrim:Sawn_Log "Skyrim:Sawn Log")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") here after installing [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") by speaking to Kharag gro-Shurkul.

### Exterior

The path leading to the mill has several [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch") along it. There are two garden plots containing a total of fifteen [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and 20 [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") plants. Alongside the house are several barrels and sacks all containing [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"), towards the rear of the house are two chicken nests containing [eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"). Beside the mill is a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") and [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). Along the shore just downstream of the mill is a second grindstone, two food barrels, a table on which are three [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") and a firepit with an empty pot over it. With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") creation installed, some [Fishing Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Supplies "Skyrim:Fishing Supplies") can be found on the shore near the firepit. The path continues past this and an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") a few feet further along, then passes a [clay deposit](/wiki/Skyrim:Clay_Deposit "Skyrim:Clay Deposit") [HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") to a small damaged jetty where a small boat has been moored. Inside the boat is a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and bucket containing three [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"). A [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") is on top of a post at the end of the jetty. Another clay deposit is on a small island southwest of the sawmill.

### Solitude Sawmill

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-interior-Solitude_Sawmill.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Sawmill.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Sawmill.jpg)

Solitude Sawmill interior

Upon entering there is a half-height internal wall on your left and two open crates along the northeast wall containing a sample of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing"), four [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and three bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"). On the other side of the internal wall is a table holding two potatoes, two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") and several pieces of cheese. Under the table are two open crates containing a sample of fly amanita, a gourd, a rock warbler egg, an orange dartwing, three leeks and two potatoes. Beside the table is a basket containing potatoes and two cabbages. Against the southwest wall is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, two loaves of bread, a cabbage, and a potato. To the right of this is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front. On the hearth is a piece of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef") and a cabbage in a cast iron pot. There is a second interior wall to the right of the fireplace with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a [plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") holding a portion of grilled leeks on top. There is a rack holding three garlic braids hanging from the ceiling.

Against the northeast wall are the stairs up to the top floor. Under the stairs are a set of shelves holding two potatoes and a cupboard holding a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"), an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") and a woodcutter's axe. To the left are two barrels and sacks of foodstuffs. In the western corner of the building is a bedroom with a single unowned bed with an end table beside it. Behind the internal half-height wall beside the fireplace is a table and chairs set for two with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), two portions of grilled leeks, two seared slaughterfish, a loaf of bread, an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and a bulb of garlic.

At the top of the stairs is a wooden bench, with a table and two chairs to the left, in the western corner. On the table is a loaf of bread and bottle of wine. Above the table is a wall shelf holding three copies of *[The Firmament](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firmament "Skyrim:The Firmament")*. Against the southwest wall is a small unit holding two [goblets](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"), a plate, a common book and an unlocked display cabinet containing an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). Beside the unit is a sack. The top floor only covers half of the building and has a railing around it. In the southern corner is a second single unowned bed with an end table beside it and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed.

## Notes

- The [key to the millhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Solitude_Sawmill "Skyrim:Key to Solitude Sawmill") is carried by Hjorunn and Kharag.
- Unlike most other houses, it is locked with a novice-level lock during the day, but unlocked at night.

## Bugs

- The keys to "Hjorunn's room" and "Kharag's room" don't unlock anything, as there are no doors within the mill.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. The keys are removed from their inventories.
