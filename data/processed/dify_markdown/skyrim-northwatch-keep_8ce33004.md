Northwatch Keep

**Northwatch Keep** is a small [fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Military_Forts "Skyrim:Military Forts") northwest of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") containing [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor"). It contains only one zone, *[Northwatch Keep](#Northwatch_Keep)*.

The fort is used as a jail by the Thalmor, and is off-limits to all others. Ignoring the warnings of the [Northwatch guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Guard "Skyrim:Northwatch Guard") at the entrance will result in the [patrolling guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor#Thalmor_Warriors "Skyrim:Thalmor") becoming hostile.

## Related Quests

- **[Missing In Action](/wiki/Skyrim:Missing_In_Action "Skyrim:Missing In Action")**: Rescue a [missing Stormcloak supporter](/wiki/Skyrim:Thorald_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Thorald Gray-Mane") from the [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor").

### Exterior

There are two entrances to the courtyard, with two entrances to the interior of the fort. The fort's main entrance is guarded by one [Northwatch guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Guard "Skyrim:Northwatch Guard") and is unlocked. There are two archery targets to the left of this entrance. Three more guards patrol the grounds, and three [Northwatch archers](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Archer "Skyrim:Northwatch Archer") patrol the walkways above; they all turn hostile when you trespass on the grounds. One guard is leaning by the door, another is in the camp to the left of the rear entrance, and the third is working the [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") near the camp. There is one archer standing on the battlements above the first guard, and the other two are patrolling the walkways. A back gate to the courtyard is near the fort's master-locked rear entrance, both near the forge. There is a campfire on the walkways above near a lookout platform. There are three lean-tos with unowned bedrolls near the forge, and a table opposite the locked entrance with a random one-handed weapon on top. Several barrels can be found within the courtyard, three [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes are around the forge and locked entrance, and a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") is to the right of the lean-tos.

### Northwatch Keep

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-interior-Northwatch_Keep.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Northwatch_Keep.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Northwatch_Keep.jpg)

Prisoners have a grim future

Through the unlocked front door is a small room with two weapon plaques on the east wall holding two [steel maces](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Steel Mace"). Ahead is a flight of stairs that descends into a circular room with a guard and a [Northwatch mage](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Mage "Skyrim:Northwatch Mage") sitting at a table. There is a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") among several [empty wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle") on the table, and two open crates stacked beside the table are full of more empty bottles. There is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") on the other side of the table from the entrance, and two cupboards against the walls holding two [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks, a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), three bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), a single loose [septim](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), a set of [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"), and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") on their shelves. On the far side of the room is an antechamber with a set of shelves on the left holding seven common [books](/wiki/Skyrim:Books "Skyrim:Books") and a wooden door ahead. Through the door is a passage that descends a further two flights of stairs, passing some barrels on the right, before entering another passage with a wooden table on your left, with a [woodcutters axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") on top and another to the right beside an unusable wooden block. The passage continues to the east, where it descends another long flight of stairs; at the bottom is an archer. Any noise made when dealing with the archers will alert a guard and a mage in the next room.

The next room is used as a bar, and contains a guard and a mage, unless they have already converged on you; one is sitting at a table, and the other is behind the bar. There are three small tables in the barroom, with a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a wedge of cheese, a joint of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"), and a bottle of wine on top of them. Against the west wall is a stack of barrels, two of which contain bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). To the right of these is a weapon plaque holding a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword"). In the northeastern corner is a bar with five bottles of Nord mead, a bottle of wine, and an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie") on the counter, and two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), five bottles of Nord mead, two bottles of wine, and a copy of the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Morning Star, v1](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Morning_Star,_v1 "Skyrim:2920, Morning Star, v1")* on the shelves behind the counter. There is a passage to the north, which passes more barrels and after a couple turns opens into another passage heading west, with a cart on your right containing a dead [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit").

The passage leads into a two-leveled room, with a guard to your left standing in front of a door, and two archers on the upper level, with one archer patrolling up and down. Any fighting will alert a mage who will emerge through the door on the left and who favors shock spells. This can be a challenging battle for the unaware. Through the door on the left is an L-shaped room with crates stacked up against the south wall, one of which has a copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") skill book *[The Legendary Sancre Tor](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legendary_Sancre_Tor "Skyrim:The Legendary Sancre Tor")* on top. Beside the crates is a table and chair with a joint of raw beef and a bottle of Nord mead on top. At the far end of the room is a large double bed with a set of shelves and a single weapon rack holding a random [one-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons") to the left. On the shelves is a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") and an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"). The mage is carrying a copy of the [Northwatch Captain's key](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Captain%27s_Key "Skyrim:Northwatch Captain's Key"). Returning to the previous chamber, the stairs to the upper level are against the north wall. At the top of the stairs is a cart containing a burnt corpse, and along the wall are weapon plaques holding a steel sword and mace. To the left are two tables, with a bottle of wine on top and empty bottles of wine on and around them. There is an unlocked chest to the left of a passage heading west from this level.

The passage takes a couple turns before leading to a T-junction in a wider passage with crates on either side. There is a guard sitting at a table to the right. On the table is a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), and a leveled dagger. Opposite this is a row of five weapon racks holding a selection of low quality weapons. There is a lit brazier at the junction with a woodcutter's axe beside an unusable wooden block. To the left from the junction is a torture room with various torture equipment, including racks and a heated clothes iron set in coals. The room is occupied by a [Northwatch interrogator](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Interrogator "Skyrim:Northwatch Interrogator"), who carries the [Northwatch Keep key](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Keep_Key "Skyrim:Northwatch Keep Key") which unlocks the fort's back entrance, and who will be alerted by any fighting nearby and converge on you. In the room is an unlocked chest, a table with a bulb of garlic on top, four common books on a small shelving unit, a set of shelves holding a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poisons "Skyrim:Poisons") and a [troll skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull "Skyrim:Troll Skull"), and two weapon racks on the wall that may hold a random weapon. [Thorald Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thorald_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Thorald Gray-Mane") can be found shackled to the north wall between a table covered with blood with two iron daggers and a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") on top and a set of shelves holding a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and one of [magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"). As you approach, he will ask, "You there, what are you doing?" You will need to speak to him in order to release him.

Beside the table to the right of the junction is a wooden door that opens into a long passage with two cells on either side and two guards patrolling up and down, who may be alerted to any fighting and converge on you in the previous passage. There are two levers on the left at the start of the passage that open the cells; the cells are each master-locked and can be [lockpicked](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking"). Three of the cells are occupied by [unnamed prisoners](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Prisoner "Skyrim:Northwatch Prisoner"); however, they do not try to escape when released (see [Bugs](#Bugs)). At the far end is a table with a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a bottle of Nord mead, and a loaf of bread on top. Around the corner is a flight of stairs leading up, with two stacked, open crates filled with empty wine bottles and a cart containing a [roughspun tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic "Skyrim:Roughspun Tunic"), [ragged trousers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers "Skyrim:Ragged Trousers"), and [footwraps](/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps "Skyrim:Footwraps") on either side of the base of the stairs. At the top of the stairs is a final room containing two sets of shelves holding two bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), an iron dagger, nine common books, and three food sacks. Opposite the stairs is the master-locked door that leads outside by the forge, which can be opened using the Northwatch Keep key.

## Notes

- If the entire fort is cleared prior to the quest Missing In Action, only the inside of the fort will be reset when the quest progresses to the objectives in which you are required to save Thorald.
- With the *[Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard")* add-on installed, you can find [Icewater Jetty](/wiki/Skyrim:Icewater_Jetty "Skyrim:Icewater Jetty") just northwest of here.

## Bugs

- None of the unnamed prisoners will attempt to escape from captivity, even if the dungeon is cleared and the cell doors are unlocked.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, addresses this issue. The prisoners now flee once rescued.
- [Atub](/wiki/Skyrim:Atub "Skyrim:Atub") erroneously also has a Northwatch Captain's Key when she has no use for it.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
