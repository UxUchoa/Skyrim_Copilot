Skyborn Altar

**Skyborn Altar** is a mountaintop [dragon lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Lairs "Skyrim:Dragon Lairs") east-southeast of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal"). Its [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") teaches part of the [Frost Breath](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Breath "Skyrim:Frost Breath") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").

## Related Quests

- **[Bounty: Dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Dragon "Skyrim:Bounty: Dragon")**: Kill a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") in its [lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Lairs "Skyrim:Dragon Lairs"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Dragon Seekers](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Seekers "Skyrim:Dragon Seekers")**: Go to a [dragon lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Lairs "Skyrim:Dragon Lairs") with [Farkas](/wiki/Skyrim:Farkas "Skyrim:Farkas") or [Vilkas](/wiki/Skyrim:Vilkas "Skyrim:Vilkas") and kill the dragon. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Walkthrough

The path to Skyborn Altar is just south of [Labyrinthian](/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian "Skyrim:Labyrinthian"); it can also be reached from the south by traveling between [Silent Moons Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Silent_Moons_Camp "Skyrim:Silent Moons Camp") and [Dustman's Cairn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dustman%27s_Cairn "Skyrim:Dustman's Cairn"). The dirt path is marked by two stone bird heads flanking the base of the stone steps heading up from the south.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_02.jpg)

Before Dragon Rising

Before the [dragons' return](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising"), the word wall will be guarded by a [wispmother](/wiki/Skyrim:Wispmother "Skyrim:Wispmother") and her [wisps](/wiki/Skyrim:Wisp "Skyrim:Wisp"). In front of the wall is a sacrificial table, and on it is a [shrine of Akatosh](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Akatosh "Skyrim:Shrine of Akatosh"), a dead [skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton"), two leveled [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), a leveled [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), and a leveled weapon.

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon"), which is resting on the wall. There is a [dead adventurer](/wiki/Skyrim:Adventurer#Adventurer12 "Skyrim:Adventurer") slumped against the right-hand side of the wall.

Regardless of whether [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") has been completed, the boss chest is behind the pillar to the left of the word wall, and there is a burial urn a few feet north of the chest, hidden in the rocks.

## Notes

- A [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") is a short distance southwest from the bottom of the steps.
- Three [orichalcum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore Vein") are 140 feet southeast of the altar [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&x=-3047&y=46305&zoom=16&legacy=true).
- A [moonstone ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein") is in a gap in the rocks to the northeast, between the altar and the [Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Mehrunes_Dagon "Skyrim:Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&x=-3047&y=46305&zoom=16&legacy=true).
- An adept-locked chest is due south of the word wall. Follow the path down two flights of stairs, past the first tree on the left with a lump of rock next to it, and turn due east at the second tree. The chest is behind the tree, against the rock face.
- The dragon's death animation may plow into the rock from the north and leave a trail but no dragon. Climb down to the north to find the dragon's body.

## Bugs

- This location might not be marked as cleared even after killing all inhabitants.
  - Reloading a save before clearing it and trying again will fix this.
- The map marker location data for Skyborn Altar points to a random XMarker rather than to the map marker itself.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.3, fixes this bug.
