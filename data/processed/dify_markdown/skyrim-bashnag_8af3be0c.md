**Bashnag**, an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatMageConjurer "Skyrim:CombatMageConjurer"), is the leader of the [spellsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Spellsword_(NPC) "Skyrim:Spellsword (NPC)") coven residing in [Southfringe Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum "Skyrim:Southfringe Sanctum"). He cast [Selveni Nethri](/wiki/Skyrim:Selveni_Nethri "Skyrim:Selveni Nethri") out of his circle of mages, cursed and chased her into a [spider](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider") web, giving you the opportunity to save her as part of a [miscellaneous quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Savior_of_Selveni_Nethri "Skyrim:The Savior of Selveni Nethri").

He spends most, if not all, of his time at the [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanters "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanters") in the back of the dungeon. He always wears [ancient Nord armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord_Armor "Skyrim:Ancient Nord Armor"), but the rest of his equipment is leveled. His boots can be [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots"), [steel cuffed](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Cuffed Boots") from level 6, and [steel plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Plate Boots") from level 18 onward. His gauntlets will always be enchanted with either [Fortify Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Apparel#Fortify_Alchemy "Skyrim:Generic Magic Apparel"), [Fortify Marksman](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Apparel#Fortify_Marksman "Skyrim:Generic Magic Apparel"), [Fortify One-Handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Apparel#Fortify_One-Handed "Skyrim:Generic Magic Apparel"), [Fortify Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Apparel#Fortify_Smithing "Skyrim:Generic Magic Apparel"), or [Fortify Two-Handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Apparel#Fortify_Two-Handed "Skyrim:Generic Magic Apparel"); and they can be [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets"), [steel Nordic](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Nordic Gauntlets") from level 7, [Dwarven](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Dwarven Gauntlets") from level 13, [steel plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Plate Gauntlets") from level 19, [Orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Orcish Gauntlets") from level 26, [ebony](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Ebony Gauntlets") from level 33, [dragonplate](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonplate_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Dragonplate Gauntlets") from level 41, and finally [Daedric](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Daedric Gauntlets") from level 49 onward. He also wears two pieces of unenchanted leveled jewelry, specifically a [ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Ring "Skyrim:Ring") and an [amulet](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet "Skyrim:Amulet"). He wields a leveled [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), which can be [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"), [steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Steel Mace"), [Orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Mace "Skyrim:Orcish Mace") from level 9, [Dwarven](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Mace "Skyrim:Dwarven Mace") from level 15, and [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Mace "Skyrim:Elven Mace") from level 22 onward. He also carries a leveled [staff](/wiki/Skyrim:Staves "Skyrim:Staves"), three random [gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Gems "Skyrim:Gems"), and some gold.

His spells are taken from the standard [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer") spell lists. Technically speaking, he is an unnamed necromancer that will respawn, but has been given the name Bashnag through the related quest. In addition to a name, he's also been given a copy of the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Art of War Magic](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Art_of_War_Magic "Skyrim:The Art of War Magic")*, and the [Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing "Skyrim:Healing"), [Fast Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Healing "Skyrim:Fast Healing"), and [Conjure Frost Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Frost_Atronach "Skyrim:Conjure Frost Atronach") spells. This simply means that when he respawns, he'll have a generic necromancer name, without the book and extra spells.

## Related Quests

- **[The Savior of Selveni Nethri](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Savior_of_Selveni_Nethri "Skyrim:The Savior of Selveni Nethri")**: Help a necromancer escape from [Southfringe Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum "Skyrim:Southfringe Sanctum").

## Notes

- With [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") installed, his equipment can also consist of [Nordic material](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic "Skyrim:Nordic").
- One internal reference names him "**Hacklecomb**".
- Bashnag almost never leaves the room he is found in under normal circumstances. This applies even to normal combat; if you are having trouble with him, run back the way you came and he very likely won't follow.

## Bugs

- He can be named generically as a necromancer instead of Bashnag when he is the target boss of a radiant quest.
