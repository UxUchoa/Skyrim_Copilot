Belyn Hlaalu's House

**Belyn Hlaalu's House** is a small home in the Gray Quarter of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). It is the first house on the left when entering the Quarter from [Candlehearth Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlehearth_Hall "Skyrim:Candlehearth Hall").

It is the home of [Belyn Hlaalu](/wiki/Skyrim:Belyn_Hlaalu "Skyrim:Belyn Hlaalu"), the owner of [Hlaalu Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Hlaalu_Farm "Skyrim:Hlaalu Farm"), who lives here alone. It consists of one area, *[Belyn Hlaalu's House](#Belyn_Hlaalu.27s_House)*.

## Belyn Hlaalu's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-interior-Belyn_Hlaalu%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Belyn_Hlaalu%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Belyn_Hlaalu%27s_House.jpg)

Inside Belyn Hlaalu's House

The front door opens to the sight of an old cart stashed underneath a ladder leading upstairs. Along the eastern wall sits an end table containing clutter, with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") on top. At the foot of the ladder is a dresser with [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing"). An entrance to the west leads to the dining area; the southeastern corner has seven food sacks, while the nearby set of shelves holds a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat"), three heads of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), a sample of [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota"), a bouquet of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"), and a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). Leaning against the shelves is another pickaxe. The obligatory fireplace can be found along the southern wall, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") above the hot embers, and a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") leaning against the wall. The table in the southwestern corner holds three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a wedge of [goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), and a wedge of [Eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). The dinner table only seats one, and has more food, including a slice of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), some [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and a bottle of regular [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). Lastly, the shelves in the northeastern corner have two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of regular wine, and a Nord mead on display.

The upstairs area is spacious, but also rather bare. At the top of the stairs is a small table with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). In the bedroom, two food barrels decorate the southeastern corner, one of them located right next to Belyn's owned bed. In the adjacent corner is an end table with clutter, a copy of *[Dunmer of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Dunmer of Skyrim")*, and a respawning [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Knapsack "Skyrim:Containers") with leveled items. The chest contains random items, while the dresser contains random [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing"). More clothes are scattered around the dresser: a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#Boots "Skyrim:Clothing"), a [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#Hats "Skyrim:Clothing"), and a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic"). Hidden behind the dresser is a copy of the [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Frostfall, v10](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Frostfall,_v10 "Skyrim:2920, Frostfall, v10")*.

## Notes

- With the exception of some barrels, sacks, and the knapsack, all containers in the area are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
