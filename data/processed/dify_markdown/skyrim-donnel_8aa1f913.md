Donnel

**Donnel** is an [elderly](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder "Skyrim:Elder") [witchblade](/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade "Skyrim:Witchblade") who lives and works at [The Treasury House](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Treasury_House "Skyrim:The Treasury House") in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). He and [Nana Ildene](/wiki/Skyrim:Nana_Ildene "Skyrim:Nana Ildene") clean the house. They both show their [true selves](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") during the related [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forsworn_Conspiracy "Skyrim:The Forsworn Conspiracy").

Donnel can always be found sweeping the floor at the foot of the northeastern set of stairs. He never sleeps or eats, and will only leave his spot and reveal his true intentions after you have questioned [Thonar](/wiki/Skyrim:Thonar_Silver-Blood "Skyrim:Thonar Silver-Blood").

He wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000209a6 "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000209a5 "Skyrim:Clothing"), and is equipped with a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"). He carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Markarth_Treasury_House "Skyrim:Key to Markarth Treasury House") to the Treasury House and a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). To aid him in combat, Donnel knows the spells [Oakflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Oakflesh "Skyrim:Oakflesh"), [Raise Zombie](/wiki/Skyrim:Raise_Zombie "Skyrim:Raise Zombie"), and [Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Flames "Skyrim:Flames").

## Related Quests

- **[The Forsworn Conspiracy](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forsworn_Conspiracy "Skyrim:The Forsworn Conspiracy")**: Investigate a murder in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") to discover a sinister plot surrounding the [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn").

### The Forsworn Conspiracy

After questioning Thonar he, along with [Nana](/wiki/Skyrim:Nana_Ildene "Skyrim:Nana Ildene"), will then proceed to attack you and anyone else in the Treasury House.

## Dialogue

When spoken to he will only say:

:   *"I work for the Silver-Bloods. Help clean."*
:   *"Just here to work."*

## Notes

- Donnel's death can be avoided by pickpocketing and reading [Thonar's](/wiki/Skyrim:Thonar%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Thonar's Journal") or [Nepos's journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Nepos%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Nepos's Journal") instead of questioning them, then going directly to [Eltrys](/wiki/Skyrim:Eltrys "Skyrim:Eltrys").
  - Alternatively, it can be avoided by going directly to Eltrys after questioning [Nepos the Nose](/wiki/Skyrim:Nepos_the_Nose "Skyrim:Nepos the Nose").
