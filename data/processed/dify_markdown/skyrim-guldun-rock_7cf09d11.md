Guldun Rock

**Guldun Rock** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") east of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). It contains only one zone, *[Guldun Rock Cave](#Guldun_Rock_Cave)*.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-place-Guldun_Rock_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Guldun_Rock_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Guldun_Rock_02.jpg)

Two mammoth cheese bowls

The camp is off the road that follows the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River") east from Whiterun to [Fort Amol](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Amol "Skyrim:Fort Amol"), directly south of [Valtheim Towers](/wiki/Skyrim:Valtheim_Towers "Skyrim:Valtheim Towers"). A path leading from the road is marked by a stone cairn surrounded by two [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton") shrubs and several [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), [purple](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"), and [red mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"). Near the first bend in the path is a leveled [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") that will normally be alerted to your passing. The path leads to a wide flight of stairs up to the plateau with the camp.

Two [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") occupy the camp. Several painted stones are on your right as you approach the camp; between two of these is a fire-hardened stake with bunches of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") and [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") hanging from it. Nine [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl") and many bones and skulls are scattered around the camp. The campfire is north of *Guldun Rock Cave* up a flight of stone stairs on top of a ruined stone building. It provides a good vantage point to view quite a distance to the north. North of the campfire, near two [orichalcum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore Vein"), is a leveled animal.

### Guldun Rock Cave

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-interior-Guldun_Rock_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Guldun_Rock_Cave.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Guldun_Rock_Cave.jpg)

Guldun Rock Cave

The cave is typically unoccupied. Two patches of [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") mushrooms grow within the cave. As you enter, there is a central rock pillar with painted stones and a mammoth cheese bowl around it. On a flat rock to the west is a dead [skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever") with three [charred skeever hides](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") under it and another mammoth cheese bowl beside it. A large campfire is on the other side of the central pillar with a dead [cow](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow "Skyrim:Cow") to the west, while to the south is another flat rock with two [coin purses](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") (one large, one medium), a random filled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), six loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), and a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Knights of the Nine](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Knights_of_the_Nine "Skyrim:The Knights of the Nine")* on top and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") behind. Northeast of the central pillar is a second fire built from carefully stacked wood of the type usually used by giants to cook larger animals like [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses") and cows.
