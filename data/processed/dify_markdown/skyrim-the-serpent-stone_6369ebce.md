The Serpent Stone

**The Serpent Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones") scattered across Skyrim.

While the stone is active, the player can use a ranged attack that will [paralyze](/wiki/Skyrim:Paralyze_(effect) "Skyrim:Paralyze (effect)") a target for 5 seconds and also do 25 points of damage. Activating the stone will present the message:

"*Once a day, those under the sign of the Serpent can use a ranged paralyzing poison on opponents.*"

## The Serpent Stone

The stone is on **Serpentstone Isle**, directly east of [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)") and northeast of [Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_The_Pride_of_Tel_Vos "Skyrim:Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos"). It is guarded by an [ice wraith](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith"). The island is fairly empty, apart from a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") on the southeastern shore, a couple of [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Grass Pod") plants on the northern shore, and the occasional [snow fox](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox "Skyrim:Snow Fox"). There are routes up from both the eastern and northern shores, to a square arch atop a short flight of stairs, with the stone standing beyond.
