Four Skull Lookout

**Four Skull Lookout** is a small [outdoor Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings "Skyrim:Nordic Dwellings") south of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten") containing [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit").

It is one of the three ruins visited for the quest [Destruction Ritual Spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction_Ritual_Spell "Skyrim:Destruction Ritual Spell"). With the [Divine Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Divine_Crusader "Skyrim:Divine Crusader") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, the named bandits [Eigorn](/wiki/Skyrim:Eigorn "Skyrim:Eigorn"), [Oren](/wiki/Skyrim:Oren "Skyrim:Oren"), [Remy](/wiki/Skyrim:Remy "Skyrim:Remy"), and [Viparth](/wiki/Skyrim:Viparth "Skyrim:Viparth") can be found here. They are in possession of the Crusader's Relics.

## Walkthrough

The easiest approach to the ruin is from the [Red Eagle Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle_Redoubt "Skyrim:Red Eagle Redoubt") lower camp (before the ascent). The ruin consists of a single room with open doorways to the northwest and southeast. It contains a single unlocked chest containing [leveled loot](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists "Skyrim:Leveled Lists") next to a campfire, with a searchable pot hanging over it and two unowned bedrolls around it. Next to one of these are an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") and [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield"). Outside, in front of the northwestern doorway, is a pedestal, with a chair off to the left. It is the home of three bandits: a warrior, archer, and wizard.

There are three [juniper trees](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") around the ruin, and a birds' nest containing a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg") lodged against the southwestern side of the ruin. One [hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss") plant can be found to the left of the southeastern doorway. Nearby is a leveled animal that may get drawn into any battle with the bandits.

## Notes

- Directly west of the ruin, on a promontory toward the cliff, a [shrine of Zenithar](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Zenithar "Skyrim:Shrine of Zenithar") can be found along with minor loot including a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), a couple of rolls of paper, several loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), and a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse").
- A [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") can be found among the rocks just in front of the pedestal.
