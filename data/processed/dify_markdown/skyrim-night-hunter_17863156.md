## Quick Walkthrough

1. Travel to [Ironback Hideout](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout "Skyrim:Ironback Hideout") and read *[Kragrash's Letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Kragrash%27s_Letter "Skyrim:Kragrash's Letter")*.
2. Locate Kragrash's burnt corpse on a cliff edge south of the [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy") and take his [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout_Cellar_Key "Skyrim:Ironback Hideout Cellar Key").
3. Return to Ironback Hideout and enter the [cellar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout_Cellar "Skyrim:Ironback Hideout Cellar") for your reward.

### Kragrash the Vampire Hunter

The quest starts when you read [Kragrash's letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Kragrash%27s_Letter "Skyrim:Kragrash's Letter") at [Ironback Hideout](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout "Skyrim:Ironback Hideout"), a remote bandit camp high in the mountains of [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar"). This location can be quite difficult to reach as it only has one approach.

Defeat the [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") camped out at the hideout and locate *[Kragrash's Letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Kragrash%27s_Letter "Skyrim:Kragrash's Letter")* on the table inside the ruined house (it may be difficult to see). Searching the hideout, you may come across a new trapdoor beneath a tent. This trapdoor has been added by the Creation and is locked for now.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-quest-Night_Hunter_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Night_Hunter_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Night_Hunter_02.jpg)

Claim Kragrash's crossbows for yourself

The letter reveals that Ironback Hideout used to be occupied by an [Orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") vampire hunter named Kragrash. He contracted [vampirism](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampirism "Skyrim:Vampirism") and subsequently became suicidal, hiking east into the mountains to watch one final sunrise before he turned. In his letter, he invites you to seek him out and claim his vampire hunting equipment.

You will need to travel to the nearby [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy") to find Kragrash's final resting place. To the south of the embassy's entrance, you will come upon a burnt corpse on the edge of a cliff; this is the remains of Kragrash, who apparently covered himself in [Dwarven oil](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Oil "Skyrim:Dwarven Oil") and ensured that he would be burnt up in the sunlight. Loot his corpse to find the [Ironback Hideout Cellar Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout_Cellar_Key "Skyrim:Ironback Hideout Cellar Key") which can be used on the trapdoor back at Ironback Hideout.

### Reward

With the key, you will have the ability to enter [Ironback Hideout Cellar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout_Cellar "Skyrim:Ironback Hideout Cellar"), a remarkably well-preserved base of operations containing a vault filled with vampire hunting equipment. The quest will end once you enter the cellar and you'll gain the abilitiy to craft [Ebony Crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Crossbow "Skyrim:Ebony Crossbow") and [Elven Crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Crossbow "Skyrim:Elven Crossbow"), then you are free to unlock the vault and loot everything within. A Silver sword and greatsword can be found here. Laid out on a table you will find an [Ebony Crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Crossbow "Skyrim:Ebony Crossbow"), an [Elven Crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Crossbow "Skyrim:Elven Crossbow"), and several [Exploding Dwarven Bolts of Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Fire "Skyrim:Exploding Dwarven Bolt of Fire") and [Shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Shock "Skyrim:Exploding Dwarven Bolt of Shock"). These two powerful crossbows are the main reward for this quest, and can be enhanced using the nearby [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil").

## Notes

- The Enhanced variants for both crossbows can be crafted only and won't appear in merchant inventories similar to the expanded crossbows. The regular Ebony and Elven crossbows will be available for purchase from the [Fletcher](/wiki/Skyrim:Fletcher_(place) "Skyrim:Fletcher (place)") upon their discovery.
- This is one of the few locations, which is not added by the Dawnguard DLC, where Silver weaponry can be found without progressing through the Companions questline.
