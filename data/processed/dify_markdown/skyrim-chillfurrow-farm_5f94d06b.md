**Chillfurrow Farm** is a small farm just outside of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), owned by [Nazeem](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazeem "Skyrim:Nazeem"). It is located south of [Battle-Born Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle-Born_Farm "Skyrim:Battle-Born Farm").

Despite technically being Nazeem's property, he is always absent. The daily maintenance of the farm is in the hands of his employee, [Wilmuth](/wiki/Skyrim:Wilmuth "Skyrim:Wilmuth"), who also lives in the farmhouse. A [Whiterun Guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Guard "Skyrim:Whiterun Guard") will look after the farm around the clock, and another guard will cross the front yard several times a day. It consists of one zone, *[Chillfurrow Farm](#Chillfurrow_Farm)*.

### Chillfurrow Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg)

Inside Chillfurrow Farm

Farm owner [Nazeem](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazeem "Skyrim:Nazeem") is reputed to be an arrogant snob among the citizens of Whiterun, but has definitely provided his employee, [Wilmuth](/wiki/Skyrim:Wilmuth "Skyrim:Wilmuth"), with some luxurious surroundings. The front door opens to the sight of the traditional huge fireplace with two [cooking spits](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") and a small table with some common books. Above the fireplace hang three samples of [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam"), three [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid"), and two fresh [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"). In the southeastern corner is a small table with two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a large dinner table with some food, including [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), three [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), and another bottle of wine. The northeastern corner has a food sack and a food barrel, and on top of the latter, an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel") and a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"). While the nearby cupboard contains clutter, the shelves above have a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a chunk of [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"), and some [horse meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Meat "Skyrim:Horse Meat") on display. A small working desk decorates the southwestern corner, with two [honey nut treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat") in a bowl and several books. Underneath the tabletop is an end table with clutter, on the floor, a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5 "Skyrim:Boots"). A food sack is leaning against a set of shelves along the western wall with even more books.

An entrance to a small storage unit can be found next to the fireplace and it contains six food sacks, a goat cheese wheel on top of a barrel, and a wardrobe with clothes. On top of the wardrobe sits a piece of [dog meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat "Skyrim:Dog Meat") and a [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). The bedroom contains Wilmuth's owned bed along with a handy keg and a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") next to his pillow. The end table underneath contains clutter, the sack underneath the bed contains food, and the [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") is non-respawning, unlocked, and contains leveled items. Adjacent to the bed, along the northern wall, is a dresser with clothes, with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a coin purse, an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), and a copy of the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") skill book *[The Importance of Where](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Importance_of_Where "Skyrim:The Importance of Where")* on top.

## Notes

- All dressers, end tables, and cupboards in the location are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") and safe to store items in.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the sacks, bucket, and pitchfork outside of the building as owned by the farm.

## Bugs

- The [grain mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Grain_Mill "Skyrim:Grain Mill") on the property was supposed to be able to produce [sacks of flour](/wiki/Skyrim:Sack_of_Flour "Skyrim:Sack of Flour") with [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") installed. However, the one at Chillfurrow Farm doesn't work.
- Nazeem was supposed to visit this farm every Loredas and Sundas at 12pm, but his patrol package is set to 'any' meaning it will always take precedence.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.2, fixes this bug.
