## Quick Walkthrough

**Note:** This quest does not appear in your journal.

1. Speak to [Tolfdir](/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir "Skyrim:Tolfdir").
2. Hunt down his missing [alembic](/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir%27s_Alembic "Skyrim:Tolfdir's Alembic") in the [College](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)").
3. Return to Tolfdir.

### Good Old Tolfdir

Tolfdir is an elderly mage and professor at the College of Winterhold and one of the first people encountered at the college. When you have completed [Under Saarthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Under_Saarthal "Skyrim:Under Saarthal") and have begun [Hitting the Books](/wiki/Skyrim:Hitting_the_Books "Skyrim:Hitting the Books") Tolfdir will be residing at the college. If you run across him looking confused, ask him if he needs help.

### Tolfdir's Alembic

Embarrassed, Tolfdir will admit to misplacing his [alembic](/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir%27s_Alembic "Skyrim:Tolfdir's Alembic"). And, while he could indeed use a different one, he has a strange sentimental attachment to this particular piece of equipment. Help the professor out by finding this lost item.
The alembic will appear in one of three randomly-selected locations in the [Hall of Countenance](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Countenance "Skyrim:Hall of Countenance"):

- On the top floor: in the northern room, on top of a barrel to the right of the central dining bench.
- On the top floor: underneath the left display case in the enchanting room.
- On the bottom floor: on a crate in the eastern room, with the big barrel in it.

However, even without the quest being activated, you will run across the alembic numerous times in your wanderings about the college. Simply return the alembic to Tolfdir when you speak to him, and earn the reward each time. Tolfdir will give you thirty gold and an empty petty, lesser or common soul gem for returning his misplaced property.

## Notes

- Finding Tolfdir's Alembic before triggering the quest will not change the quest dialogue or outcome; you can simply start the quest and give the alembic to Tolfdir to receive your reward.
- Unlike previous titles where alembics were a major part of [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy"), this is the only individual alembic that can be found in the base [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim") game. The [Farming](/wiki/Skyrim:Farming "Skyrim:Farming") creation adds a second one in the basement of [Goldenhills Plantation](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenhills_Plantation "Skyrim:Goldenhills Plantation"). All other alembics in the game are permanently attached to alchemy stations.
- Sometimes, the alembic will get dislodged from its normal location, possibly falling behind other objects or onto the ground nearby.
- Version 1.0 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") adds journal objectives to this quest.

## Bugs

- Tolfdir may offer the dialogue option that initiates the quest after it has already been completed. Selecting this option will re-add the quest to your journal, but the Alembic will not respawn, leaving the objective permanently stuck in your journal.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Entering the console command `player.additem "00026c31" 1` to give yourself a second alembic will allow you to complete the quest by speaking to Tolfdir as normal.
