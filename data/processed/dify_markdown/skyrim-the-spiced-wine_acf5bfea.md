## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Evette San](/wiki/Skyrim:Evette_San "Skyrim:Evette San").
2. Persuade [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici") to hand over the goods.
3. Return to Evette.

### The Spiced Wine

[Evette San](/wiki/Skyrim:Evette_San "Skyrim:Evette San") is a [food vendor](/wiki/Skyrim:Food_Vendor "Skyrim:Food Vendor") who is famous for her [Spiced Wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Spiced_Wine "Skyrim:Spiced Wine"). You can find her at her market stall in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") during the day. She may ask you *"You wouldn't have a few minutes to run down to the docks would you?"* If you are willing to help her, she will tell you *"I've got a shipment of Spices the [East Empire Company](/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company "Lore:East Empire Company") is holding up. I need someone to convince them to release it."*

Get out of the city and follow the road down to the harbor area. You can find [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici") near the [East Empire Company Warehouse](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Warehouse "Skyrim:East Empire Company Warehouse") during the day. Talk to her about Evette's shipment. Vittoria will say, *"Ah yes. If she wants to pay the two thousand gold tariff than we'll be all set."* At this point, you can try to persuade or bribe Vittoria. You must pass an easy [Speech check](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech#Persuasion_Options "Skyrim:Speech") to persuade her. You can also pay the 2000 gold tariff for the shipment.

Return to Evette with the good news and she will be very happy. *"Thank you so much. I swear it's a fight with them every shipment."* Then she will give you two bottles of Spiced Wine for your troubles. You can also take more drinks from her stall freely.
