The Challenger

The **Challenger** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard "Skyrim:Bandit Wizard") who is [randomly encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#WIChangeLocation03 "Skyrim:World Interactions") in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim_(place) "Skyrim:Skyrim (place)") (or [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")). He wears [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor") and a leveled pair of [light boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor"), and he carries a [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"), along with various [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), [poisons](/wiki/Skyrim:Poisons "Skyrim:Poisons"), and other items depending on his level.

### Wizard Duel

When you first encounter him, he will call out to you:

:   *"You call yourself a wizard? I challenge you to a duel!"*

:   :   **Sorry. You'll have to find some other way to stroke your ego.**
    :   *"I won't take no for answer!"*

:   :   **It's your funeral.**
    :   *"The gods will be the judge of that."*

Either way, you're in for a fight.

He expects you to use magic, and if you don't follow his assumed rules of the duel, he may react with these lines:

:   *"Stop it! This is a one-on-one duel!"*
:   *"I thought you were a wizard. Hit me with a spell!"*
:   *"You're cheating!"*
:   *"It's no fun unless you fight me with magic."*
:   *"This is a magic duel. Ma-gic. You know, with spells."*
