Old Attius Farm Cellar

**Old Attius Farm Cellar** is a cellar located underneath the ruined house at [Old Attius Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Attius_Farm "Skyrim:Old Attius Farm"). It can be accessed via a trapdoor hidden within the ruined wooden farmhouse.

You will follow a suspicious [Redoran Guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran_Guard "Skyrim:Redoran Guard") to this location during the related quest. He will enter the cellar to meet with the [Buyer](/wiki/Skyrim:Buyer "Skyrim:Buyer"), a mysterious individual seeking to purchase the [Mask of Vivec](/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Vivec "Skyrim:Mask of Vivec").
