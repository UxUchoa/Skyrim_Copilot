**Addvild** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") who works for [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Vulwulf_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Vulwulf Snow-Shod") at [Snow-Shod Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow-Shod_Farm "Skyrim:Snow-Shod Farm") with his recently married wife, [Leonara Arius](/wiki/Skyrim:Leonara_Arius "Skyrim:Leonara Arius"). He [appreciates](/wiki/Skyrim:Gather_Wheat "Skyrim:Gather Wheat") honest work and will pay a septim each for [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and five septims each for [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat").

He starts his day at 6am with a one-hour breakfast at the farmhouse. At 7am, he goes outside to tend the crops and look after the farm animals until his bedtime at midnight, taking a break at 7pm to dine at the house for one hour.

He wears farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#.28000209A6.29 "Skyrim:Clothes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5 "Skyrim:Boots"). He wields a [leveled dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") in combat and carries a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") along with [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

Regarding his position, he is extremely appreciative of Vulwulf Snow-Shod for trusting him to take care of the farm without supervision. This is evident in his oft-repeated phrases:

:   *"Vulwulf has been quite generous to let me take care of their farm considering I am not part of their family."*
:   *"The Snow-Shod's*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *are very trusting to let me run this place without their supervision."*

Also when spoken to:

:   *"I'm glad there are guards outside the farm. I don't think I could defend the place alone."*
:   *"If you're looking to purchase goods, I have none to sell. You should head into [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") instead."*
:   *"Pretty quiet out here on the farm. My wife and I kind of like the solitude."*

If you have cleared out [Aringoth](/wiki/Skyrim:Aringoth "Skyrim:Aringoth")'s safe during the quest [Loud and Clear](/wiki/Skyrim:Loud_and_Clear "Skyrim:Loud and Clear") and have not returned to [Brynjolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Brynjolf "Skyrim:Brynjolf"), Addvild will express concern about the nearby [estate](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenglow_Estate "Skyrim:Goldenglow Estate"):

:   *"Did you see the fires from Goldenglow Estate? I was afraid the whole place was going to burn to the ground!"*

## Conversations

He may converse with his Leonara:

:   **Leonara**: *"My darling, you've made me the happiest woman in all Skyrim."*
:   **Addvild**: *"It brings me joy to hear that, but what possessed you to say that so suddenly?"*
:   **Leonara**: *"I've already lost a family, and we're about to start a new one. I don't want that to happen again."*
:   **Addvild**: *"My sweet Leanora*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")]*, I will never let anything happen to us. I promise."*

:   **Leonara**: *"I'm not so sure the few guards they've assigned to the farm are enough."*
:   **Addvild**: *"It will have to do. Besides, if it came to blows, I'm sure the Snow-Shod's*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *would come out here and assist us."*
:   **Leonara**: *"I suppose so. I just fear they won't make it out here in time."*
:   **Addvild**: *"Just worry about keeping the fields tended and let the Jarl worry about the war."*
