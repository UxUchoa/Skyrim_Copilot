The Sea of Ghosts along the northern coast of Skyrim

The **Sea of Ghosts** is a vast ocean to the north of Skyrim.

The Sea of Ghosts is part of the [Padomaic Ocean](/wiki/Lore:Padomaic_Ocean "Lore:Padomaic Ocean"); it meets the [Abecean Sea](/wiki/Lore:Abecean_Sea "Lore:Abecean Sea") off the coast of northwestern [High Rock](/wiki/Lore:High_Rock "Lore:High Rock"), and the [Inner Sea](/wiki/Lore:Inner_Sea "Lore:Inner Sea") along the shores of northern [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind"). Some exploration of the sea has yet to be completed, and icebergs are a common sight in its cold, misty waters. The island of [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim") is located in the Sea of Ghosts, and the island's [Harstrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Harstrad_River "Skyrim:Harstrad River"), [Iggnir](/wiki/Skyrim:Iggnir_River "Skyrim:Iggnir River"), and [Isild](/wiki/Skyrim:Isild_River "Skyrim:Isild River") rivers flow into it. The [Atmorans](/wiki/Lore:Atmoran "Lore:Atmoran") sailed from their homeland, the northern continent of [Atmora](/wiki/Lore:Atmora "Lore:Atmora"), to [Tamriel](/wiki/Lore:Tamriel "Lore:Tamriel") across this sea.
