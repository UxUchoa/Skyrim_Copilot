Buyer Beware

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read *[Masks of the Tribunal](/wiki/Skyrim:Masks_of_the_Tribunal "Skyrim:Masks of the Tribunal")*.
2. Shoot down the bird outside the Bulwark's south gate.
3. Read the [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_the_Target "Skyrim:Note on the Target").
4. Follow the Redoran Guard to Old Attius Farm Cellar.
5. Kill the guard and the [Buyer](/wiki/Skyrim:Buyer "Skyrim:Buyer").
6. Retrieve the Mask of Vivec.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-quest-Buyer_Beware_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Buyer_Beware_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Buyer_Beware_02.jpg)

The Felsaad Tern

After reading *[Masks of the Tribunal](/wiki/Skyrim:Masks_of_the_Tribunal "Skyrim:Masks of the Tribunal")*, you will need to travel to Raven Rock, and head up the stairs near the front of the Temple to the top of the Bulwark. Face south towards [Old Attius Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Attius_Farm "Skyrim:Old Attius Farm") to see a lone [Felsaad Tern](/wiki/Skyrim:Felsaad_Tern "Skyrim:Felsaad Tern") circling close by. As long as it's not too close to midnight (any time up to 11) this bird will appear and continuously circle around. If it's knocked out of the sky an objective prompt to "retrieve the note" will appear. Find the bird's body and loot it to find a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_the_Target "Skyrim:Note on the Target").

Locate the Redoran Guard marked with a quest objective. Follow him south to the Old Attius Farm site, and from there down through the trapdoor into the [Old Attius Farm Cellar](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Attius_Farm_Cellar "Skyrim:Old Attius Farm Cellar"). Kill the guard and the [Buyer](/wiki/Skyrim:Buyer "Skyrim:Buyer"), and retrieve the [Mask of Vivec](/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Vivec "Skyrim:Mask of Vivec") and a Fahlbtharz Forge Gem. Optionally loot the Redoran Guard for a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran_Guard%27s_Note "Skyrim:Redoran Guard's Note") describing his motivations.

Finishing this quest completes one of the three tasks of the goddess in the [Ghosts of the Tribunal](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest) "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal (quest)") quest. It also provides one of the four forge gems required to complete the [Trueflame](/wiki/Skyrim:Trueflame_(quest) "Skyrim:Trueflame (quest)") quest.

## Notes

- The Redoran Guard needn't be followed or watched. Just head over near the Old Attius Farm Cellar trap door and wait past midnight, during 12AM~4AM. It'll be unlocked and the Buyer will be down there with the guard. They'll attack on sight so kill them.
