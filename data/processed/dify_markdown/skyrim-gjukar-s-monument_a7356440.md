Gjukar's Monument

**Gjukar's Monument** is a stone monument commemorating an ancient battle southeast of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead").

A sword, a shield, and a few flowers are at the base of the monument. During the quest [The Book of Love](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Love "Skyrim:The Book of Love"), a ghost named [Ruki](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruki "Skyrim:Ruki") can be found here searching for her husband [Fenrig](/wiki/Skyrim:Fenrig "Skyrim:Fenrig").

## Related Quests

- **[The Book of Love](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Love "Skyrim:The Book of Love")**: Help the [Temple of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Mara "Skyrim:Temple of Mara") spread the cause of love throughout Skyrim.

## Notes

- An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") is near the road south of the monument.
- A [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") is among the rocks north of the monument.
- Due east of the monument is an [unmarked ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Crumbling_Bastion "Skyrim:Shrines") containing a [shrine of Zenithar](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Zenithar "Skyrim:Shrine of Zenithar"), an [amulet of Zenithar](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Zenithar "Skyrim:Amulet of Zenithar"), a copy of the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Second Seed, v5](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Second_Seed,_v5 "Skyrim:2920, Second Seed, v5")*, two [silver ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot"), and a [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel "Skyrim:Satchel"), with a novice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") directly above these items on top of the ruin.
- Southeast of the monument near the road is a pool with the remains of a long-dead colossal [mudcrab](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab") surrounded by its smaller living cousins. These remains will be joined by the [Mudcrab Guardian Spirit](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Guardian_Spirit "Skyrim:Mudcrab Guardian Spirit") during the quest [Kyne's Sacred Trials](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials").
