## Detailed Walkthrough

To begin the quest you must find the [Saints Bandit's Cage Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_Bandit%27s_Cage_Key "Skyrim:Saints Bandit's Cage Key"). It is located in a chest found in the Saints Bandit camp just to the west of [North Brittleshin Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:North_Brittleshin_Pass "Skyrim:North Brittleshin Pass"). This is the first Saints bandit camp you encounter during the quest [Balance of Power](/wiki/Skyrim:Balance_of_Power "Skyrim:Balance of Power").

Once you have the key, you can open the cage and talk to the [Manic Elytra Nymph](/wiki/Skyrim:Elytra_Nymph "Skyrim:Elytra Nymph") inside. Accept it as your pet and the quest will end, granting you the spell [Teleport Pet: Manic Elytra Nymph](/wiki/Skyrim:Teleport_Pet:_Manic_Elytra_Nymph "Skyrim:Teleport Pet: Manic Elytra Nymph").

## Bugs

- At the end of the quest to get the Elytra pet for Mania, the dialogue to accept it as a follower is incorrectly checking for Stage 50 in the quest for the Dementia version - this stage doesn't exist, and isn't the right quest to check either. It should be checking for Stage 25 on the Mania quest.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.7, fixes this bug.
