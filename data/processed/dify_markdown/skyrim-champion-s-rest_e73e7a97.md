Champion's Rest

**Champion's Rest** is a medium-sized [Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Ruins "Skyrim:Nordic Ruins") located in [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"), east of [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"). It was once used as a gladiatorial arena but now serves as a crypt for fallen warriors. Once lost to time, miners from Shor's Stone have recently broken through into the ruins while mining for [silver](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver "Skyrim:Silver").

It consists of two zones: [Champion's Rest](#Champion.27s_Rest) and [Champion's Rest Amphitheater](#Champion.27s_Rest_Amphitheater).
