Nepos's House

**Nepos's House** is a luxurious private residence owned by [Nepos the Nose](/wiki/Skyrim:Nepos_the_Nose "Skyrim:Nepos the Nose"), who is a wealthy resident of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). It is located on the third terrace on the northern side of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"), next door to [Vlindrel Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Vlindrel_Hall "Skyrim:Vlindrel Hall"), above [Endon's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Endon%27s_House "Skyrim:Endon's House") and two terraces above [Arnleif and Sons Trading Company](/wiki/Skyrim:Arnleif_and_Sons_Trading_Company "Skyrim:Arnleif and Sons Trading Company").

His three [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") servants appear to be very protective of him, especially [Uaile](/wiki/Skyrim:Uaile "Skyrim:Uaile"), who questions visitors at the door and claims to be Nepos's maid.

## Nepos's House

It is a large single-story property that is lavishly furnished. There are numerous [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and sacks throughout the property, along with plenty of fancy cookware. The main room has two stone tables with wooden chairs down the middle of the room, laden with four portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), three bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two [pheasant breasts](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"), two [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), two [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), plenty of loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), five [slaughterfish eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg"), many pieces of cheese, two [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), two [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a rack of [dog meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat "Skyrim:Dog Meat"), a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulb, a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), and a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse").

There is a dresser with a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch") on top against the south wall. To the right, in the southwestern corner, is a round wooden table and two chairs with a large coin purse, a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), and a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat") on top. Against the west wall is a cupboard holding a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, a couple of loaves of bread, a sheaf of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). To the right of this is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") with a set of shelves to its right of that. These shelves hold a bottle of wine, a couple of pieces of cheese, and a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"). Beside the shelves is a small square table with clutter on top. In the northwestern corner is a door into a bedroom. Along the north wall is a stone bench, a couple of wooden chairs, and a stone shelf holding a loaf of bread. In the northeastern corner is door to another room. Against the east wall is a stone table with a joint of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"), a thistle branch, a basket containing a haunch of raw [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison") and a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), and another basket containing a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and a sample of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"). To the right of the table is a piece of loose [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") on top of a food barrel. In the southeast corner is a large fireplace with a pile of firewood to the left. To the right is a goat cheese wheel in a cast iron pot and in front is a wooden chair. Above the wood is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding two [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). Against the south wall is a kettle holding a cabbage, a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") and a potato, with another rack above it holding two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), a garlic braid, and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam").

The bedroom to the northwest contains two single owned beds both coming out from the north wall, separated by an end table with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on top. To the left of the beds is a chair and against the west wall is a tall wardrobe and a dresser, both containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). On the dresser is a sprig of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") and a green apple. There is also a small square wooden table against a wall to the southwest with another green apple and mead barrel on top. Finally against the east wall is a set of shelves holding a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute") and [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum").

Through the other door is a kitchen area in front of you and, on a raised section, another sleeping area. In the middle of the room is another stone table with a wooden chair at either end. On the table is a bottle of alto wine, a portion of grilled leeks, a raw rabbit leg, a seared slaughterfish, a carrot, two loaves of bread, a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat"), and several pieces of cheese. Behind the table, on the rear wall is a large fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front. On your right as you enter, on a stone shelf is an apprentice-locked display cabinet with an [enchanted steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Weapons "Skyrim:Generic Magic Weapons"). The display cabinet can be unlocked with the [key to Nepos's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Nepos%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Nepos's House") which is carried by all the residents. Against the east wall is a set of shelves holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a [draught of extra magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Draught of Extra Magicka"), a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), a couple of pieces of cheese, and a potato in a cast iron pot. To the right of the shelves is a wash basin and beside this on the floor is a basket containing two cabbages and several potatoes. In the northeast corner is a small square stone table with a bunch of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") and an unlocked chest, with a basket under the table containing two gourds and a potato.

At the western end of the room are stairs up between two shoulder-high walls, to the raised section. On the partition walls is a bowl containing an empty [greater soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Greater_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Greater Soul Gem"), a set of [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"), and a wooden bowl containing five [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") coins. On the raised section are two owned beds; the first coming out from the south wall is a single and the other coming out from the west wall is a large double. To the right of the single is a tall wardrobe with a pair of [fine boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots "Skyrim:Fine Boots") to the left of it and in an open crate, on top of another crate are four [silver ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot"). There are end tables on either side of the double bed. On the left-hand one is a copy of *[Of Fjori and Holgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir "Skyrim:Of Fjori and Holgeir")* with a stone shelf to the left holding three more common books and a copy of the [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mystery of Talara, Part 4](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_Part_4 "Skyrim:Mystery of Talara, Part 4")*. On the right-hand end table is a [fine hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Hat "Skyrim:Fine Hat") and two gold coins. Higher up on the north wall is an easily-missed shelf with a silver ingot and medium coin purse, and a wash basin further to the right on a stand.

## Notes

- There is a "Loot" [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (Thieves Guild)") to the top right of the entrance door.
- The two unlocked chests do not respawn.
