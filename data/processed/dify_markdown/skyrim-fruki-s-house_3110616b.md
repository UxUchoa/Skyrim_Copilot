Fruki's House

**Fruki's House** is a small house in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"), and the residence of [Fruki](/wiki/Skyrim:Fruki "Skyrim:Fruki"). It is located north of [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine").

### Exterior

It is at the farthest edge of town next to [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine"), facing the bay on the opposite side from [Silus Vesuius's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Silus_Vesuius%27s_House "Skyrim:Silus Vesuius's House"). In front is the jetty that [The Sea Squall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Sea_Squall "Skyrim:The Sea Squall") is moored at and next to the house is the [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") that the miners use. There are steps leading up to an enclosed deck in front of the house. The rocks and snow pile up quite close to the rear of the property.

### Fruki's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-interior-Fruki%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Fruki%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Fruki%27s_House.jpg)

Fruki's house

On your left upon entering there is a single owned bed in the eastern corner. In the middle of the southeast wall is a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel next to a tall wardrobe with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") atop, which contains only minor loot. Another single owned bed is in the southern corner, with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") by the foot of the bed. There is a fireplace opposite the door on the southwest wall with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") on the hearth. To the left of the fireplace is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). To the right of the fireplace is a long wooden table and bench. On the table are several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). In the northern corner of the room is a square wooden table and two chairs. On this table is another pickaxe, a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), two bottles of Nord mead, a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute") and five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold").

## Notes

- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide")* describes the building as follows:

:   *"Fruki and [Land](/wiki/Skyrim:Lond "Skyrim:Lond"), the miners working the Quicksilver veins, live here in this small structure in which Fruki was raised. They occasionally speak with [Borgny](/wiki/Skyrim:Borgny "Skyrim:Borgny") and [Edith](/wiki/Skyrim:Edith "Skyrim:Edith"), who take the other shifts to keep the mine productive— when [Leigelf](/wiki/Skyrim:Leigelf "Skyrim:Leigelf") isn't running the operation into the ground."*
