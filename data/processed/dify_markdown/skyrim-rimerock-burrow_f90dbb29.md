Rimerock Burrow

**Rimerock Burrow** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") northwest of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") containing an [atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach "Skyrim:Atronach") and the powerful conjurer [Sebastian Lort](/wiki/Skyrim:Sebastian_Lort "Skyrim:Sebastian Lort"). It contains only one zone, *[Rimerock Burrow](#Rimerock_Burrow)*.

## Related Quests

- **[A Daedra's Best Friend](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Daedra%27s_Best_Friend "Skyrim:A Daedra's Best Friend")**: Help [Barbas](/wiki/Skyrim:Barbas "Skyrim:Barbas") reunite with his [master](/wiki/Lore:Clavicus_Vile "Lore:Clavicus Vile").

### Exterior

The rough path leading to the cave takes you over a wooden bridge spanning a crevice in the cliff wall just before the entrance. The path branches off the larger road east of [Pinefrost Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Pinefrost_Tower "Skyrim:Pinefrost Tower"), with the fork marked by a pile of rocks; the fork can nevertheless be easily missed, as there is likely to be an encounter as you near the junction. It is advisable to have a light source before entering the cave.

### Rimerock Burrow

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-interior-Rimerock_Burrow.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Rimerock_Burrow.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Rimerock_Burrow.jpg)

Rimerock Burrow interior

Upon entering, there is a dark tunnel leading down to the main cavern on the lowest level. There are raised areas in front and to your right. A leveled atronach patrols from the raised area to your right down to the lower level. This area contains nothing of interest. Sebastian will be found on the raised area in front of you, normally working at the arcane enchanter. To get to Sebastian, you will have to climb a ramp on the far left of the cave against the wall. There is a large stone table on your left with an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter") near it. On the table is a [scroll of conjure familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Familiar "Skyrim:Scroll of Conjure Familiar"), a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), a goblet, and a bowl containing a sample of [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") and a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). If sent here by [Clavicus Vile](/wiki/Skyrim:Clavicus_Vile "Skyrim:Clavicus Vile"), the [Rueful Axe](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rueful_Axe "Skyrim:The Rueful Axe") will also be lying on the table. If you are here prior to accepting the related quest, the Rueful Axe will be replaced with an ordinary [steel battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Steel Battleaxe").

Between the table and arcane enchanter is a tunnel heading south then west leading to a storage and bedroom area. On your right before reaching the end of the tunnel is Sebastian's bedroom containing a single unowned bed and an end table next to it. On the table is a copy of the [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Hearth Fire, v9](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Hearth_Fire,_v9 "Skyrim:2920, Hearth Fire, v9")*, an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). Next to the end table are two barrels, one of which contains ingredients; a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and some [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear") are hanging over them.

Just after the bedroom area is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing leveled loot and any items you are sent here to recover. At the end of the tunnel there are two sets of shelves. ## Notes

- Sebastian Lort respawns along with the rest of the cave.
- Despite being considered a werewolf lair in the game data, no [werewolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf "Skyrim:Werewolf") are ever found in the eve.

## Bugs

- Rimerock Burrow isn't marked as a cave, so cave-based radiant quests won't target it.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
