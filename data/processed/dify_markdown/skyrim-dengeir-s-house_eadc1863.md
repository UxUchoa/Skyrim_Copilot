Dengeir's House

**Dengeir's House** is the home of the former [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") and current [Thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/SR-interior-Dengeir%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dengeir%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dengeir%27s_House.jpg)

Dengeir at home

He lives there with his brother, [Thadgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Thadgeir "Skyrim:Thadgeir"), and his maid, [Tekla](/wiki/Skyrim:Tekla "Skyrim:Tekla"). It is a large L-shaped single-room house. There is only one interior, zone, *[Dengeir's House](#Dengeir.27s_House)*.

### Exterior

There is a small fenced garden at the front of the house with a tanning rack, wooden bench and several unusable barrels along the wall of the house. At the rear of the property the area has several tree stumps and a [thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"), most of the area at the back is used by the wood mill which is owned and run by [Bolund](/wiki/Skyrim:Bolund "Skyrim:Bolund"). There is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") and several tree trunks by the river running behind the house.

### Dengeir's House

Upon entering there are two single owned beds, where [Tekla](/wiki/Skyrim:Tekla "Skyrim:Tekla") and [Thadgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Thadgeir "Skyrim:Thadgeir") sleep, coming out from the southwest wall, with a dresser between them. On the dresser is a copy of *[Lost Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends "Skyrim:Lost Legends")*. Opposite the entrance is a fireplace, to the left is a food barrel with a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") on top. There are three loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") on the mantel and a kettle containing four [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") by the hearth. To the right of the fireplace is a long wooden table and chair with bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), two potatoes, three [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") on top. Above the table are two [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and to [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") hanging from racks as well as two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), two garlic braids and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging from the ceiling.

Further to the right on the northwest wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and three bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). In front of this is a long fancy table holding a bottle of wine and one of alto wine, three loaves of bread, a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese") and two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). To the right, long the northeast wall is a chair and wardrobe with a [sabre cat pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Pelt") on top. At the end of the room, coming out from the southeast wall is a queen sized bed with end tables either side. The left of these holds a [potion of haggling](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling "Skyrim:Potion of Haggling"), with a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") on the floor to the left of it. The end table to the right of the bed holds four loose septims. There is an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe") under the bed and an unlocked non-respawning [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing leveled items at the foot of it.

Along the southwest wall at this end of the house is a food barrel and a round wooden table and two chairs. On the table are two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). Around the corner on the southeast wall is a cupboard holding two common books, a copy of *[The Tale of Dro'Zira](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Tale_of_Dro%27Zira "Skyrim:The Tale of Dro'Zira")* and a copy of *[Flight from the Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Flight_from_the_Thalmor "Skyrim:Flight from the Thalmor")*. There are a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") and bowl of [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat") on top of the cupboard and on the floor, to the right is a flower basket are three more pine thrush eggs.

## Notes

- All dressers, cupboards, and end tables in the location are non-respawning.
- Based on many internal names, the name of the relevant key, as well as its name on the local map, it seems that this house was originally known as "Dengeir's **Hall**" instead.
- If [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") will take Falkreath during Civil War or trading hold from Imperial control during Season Unending, all items on this house are free take without penalty.
