Baral Sendu

**Baral Sendu** is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") found lying dead on his bed in [The Stumbling Sabrecat](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Stumbling_Sabrecat "Skyrim:The Stumbling Sabrecat"), a tavern within [Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dunstad "Skyrim:Fort Dunstad"). He was presumably killed by the [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") who have overrun the fort. He wears [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0005b6a1 "Skyrim:Clothes") and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0 "Skyrim:Shoes") and carries a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and up to nine gold.

His body and blood-stained bed are removed during the course of the related quest [The Battle for Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Dunstad").

## Bugs

- Baral has the `FemaleCommoner` voice type. This results in a female voice if the body is [resurrected](/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate "Skyrim:Reanimate").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.5, addresses this issue. His voice is set to the `MaleCommoner` voice type.
