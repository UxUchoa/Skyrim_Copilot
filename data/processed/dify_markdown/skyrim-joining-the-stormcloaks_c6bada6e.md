## Quick Walkthrough

1. Head to [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") and speak with [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak").
2. Speak with [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist").
3. Travel to [Serpentstone Isle](/wiki/Skyrim:Serpentstone_Isle "Skyrim:Serpentstone Isle").
4. Kill the [Ice Wraith](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith").
5. Take the oath.

### Joining

Proceed to [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") and enter the [Palace of the Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings"). If this is your first time in the palace, then Jarl [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak") and his lieutenant, [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist"), will have a long conversation concerning the war. Speak with Ulfric, who recognizes you from [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen") and will direct you to speak with his lieutenant. Galmar will ask you why you want to join, then tell you that to prove yourself worthy of joining the Stormcloaks, you must travel to the [Serpentstone Isle](/wiki/Skyrim:Serpentstone_Isle "Skyrim:Serpentstone Isle") and defeat an [Ice Wraith](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith#Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith"). If you are below level 20, he will also give you an [Elixir of Resist Cold](/wiki/Skyrim:Elixir_of_Resist_Cold "Skyrim:Elixir of Resist Cold") and three [Ice Wraith Banes](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Bane "Skyrim:Ice Wraith Bane"), both of which can be incredibly helpful on this quest if you are at a low level.

### Test Your Mettle

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-place-Serpentstone_Isle.jpg/200px-SR-place-Serpentstone_Isle.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Serpentstone_Isle.jpg)

Serpentstone Isle will be your proving grounds

Travel to the Serpentstone Isle, located far north of Windhelm. Once you're there, kill the Ice Wraith. Using a fire spell or adding the Ice Wraith Bane to your weapon will make the fight easier. Remember to use the resist frost potion before you engage if you need it. You can also find the Serpent Stone while there.

### The Victorious Return

Travel back to the Palace of Kings to speak with Galmar. When you return, Galmar and Ulfric will be discussing your next mission for the Stormcloaks. Galmar will express his surprise at seeing you return and ask you to take the Stormcloak oath:

:   *"I do swear my blood and honor to the service of Ulfric Stormcloak..."*
:   *"...Jarl of Windhelm and true High King of Skyrim."*
:   *"As Talos is my witness, may this oath bind me to death and beyond..."*
:   *"...even to my lord as to my fellow brothers and sisters in arms."*
:   *"All hail the Stormcloaks, the true sons and daughters of Skyrim!"*

Repeat the oath as Galmar says it and he will reward you with Fur Boots, a Stormcloak Cuirass, Fur Gauntlets and a Hide Helmet.

### Reward

Upon completion of the quest and repeating the oath you will be awarded the following:

- Hide Helmet
- Fur Gauntlets
- Stormcloak Cuirass
- Fur Boots

"You're a Stormcloak now, you ought to look the part. "--Galmar Stone-Fist

## Notes

- [Joining the Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Legion "Skyrim:Joining the Legion") and Joining the Stormcloaks are mutually exclusive quests: completing one automatically fails the other (if it was started or the prerequisite miscellaneous quest was started).
- [![Flag Germany.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/Flag_Germany.png/22px-Flag_Germany.png)](/wiki/File:Flag_Germany.png) The German version of the quest name is "So jung und schon bei den Sturmmänteln?", which is a take-off of the German translation of Princess Leia's famous Star Wars quote to Luke, "Aren't you a little short for a Stormtrooper?" The word "Sturmmänteln" (Stormcloak) is substituted for the original "Sturmtruppen", however.

## Bugs

- [Galmar](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist") would not complete Joining the Stormcloaks properly if [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") was an active quest.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
- Galmar refers to all players as "brother", including females, when telling them to get kitted out after joining the Stormcloaks.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
