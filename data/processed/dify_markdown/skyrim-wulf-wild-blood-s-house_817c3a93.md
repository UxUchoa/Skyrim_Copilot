Wulf Wild-Blood's House

**Wulf Wild-Blood's House** is the home of [Wulf Wild-Blood](/wiki/Skyrim:Wulf_Wild-Blood "Skyrim:Wulf Wild-Blood") in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

It is on the northwest side of the village, between [Oslaf's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf%27s_House "Skyrim:Oslaf's House") to the south and [Edla's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Edla%27s_House "Skyrim:Edla's House") to the northeast.

### Exterior

Along the east wall is a pen adjacent to Edla's house holding a [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat"), with two crates with a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk") on top against the side of the house. Behind the pen are two [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"). The entrance has a novice lock.

### Wulf Wild-Blood's House

The entrance leads into the main room of the two-room home. On your left is a long table and bench against the south wall, there are chairs at either end as well. On the table is a mead barrel, a bowl containing three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), there are two [snow fox pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Fox Pelt"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and a wooden bowl containing [hawk feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk_Feathers "Skyrim:Hawk Feathers"). Above the table is a wall shelf with animal skulls on it. There is also an open crate containing a [deer pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt "Skyrim:Deer Pelt") and a quiver of [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow") against the south wall. In the southwest corner is a cupboard with a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide"), a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk") and a wooden bowl containing a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") on the shelves. To the right against the west wall is a barrel with a mead barrel on top and a chair. To the right of these is a pair of wall shelves containing three fillets of [horker meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat "Skyrim:Horker Meat"), a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), a horker tusk and two sacks.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-interior-Wulf_Wild-Blood%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Wulf_Wild-Blood%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Wulf_Wild-Blood%27s_House.jpg)

Wulf Wild-Blood's House interior

In the northwest corner is a doorway to a storeroom and to the right of this along the north wall is a fire pit complete with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking"). In the fire is a cast iron pot containing a [horker loaf](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Loaf "Skyrim:Horker Loaf") and a portion of [clam meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat "Skyrim:Clam Meat"). Around the fire is a kettle containing a joint of [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"), a cabbage and a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). To the right of the entrance is a stack of four barrels and a sack. On one of the barrels is a basket containing two cabbages. A set of shelves are to the left containing a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat"), a fillet of horker meat, two bottles of ale and one bottle of alto wine. On the left of the shelves is a sack and between this and the fire pit is the doorway to the bedroom.

Walking into the bedroom, there is a single bed on the left against the wall with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") underneath. Beside the bed is a dresser with a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and a bowl containing a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") on top. There is also a chair in the northeast corner. Entering the storeroom, there is a narrow table against the west wall on your left with a pair of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots") underneath and a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow") on the floor. There is a chair beside it. Against the north wall is an end table with a wooden plate containing a loaf of bread on top. Against the east wall that separates this room from the bedroom is a shelf containing a horker tusk and underneath a wood pile, two pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"), and a bucket containing six large pieces of [charcoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal "Skyrim:Charcoal"), with one more piece on the floor.
