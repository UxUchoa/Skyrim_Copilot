## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Glover Mallory](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory "Skyrim:Glover Mallory") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").
2. Retrieve his [ancient Nordic pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nordic_Pickaxe "Skyrim:Ancient Nordic Pickaxe") from [Crescius Caerellius](/wiki/Skyrim:Crescius_Caerellius "Skyrim:Crescius Caerellius").
3. Return to Glover Mallory.

### That Foolish Old Man

The blacksmith of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock"), [Glover Mallory](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory "Skyrim:Glover Mallory"), has plenty of problems, both with his affiliation with the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") and his messy [family affairs](/wiki/Skyrim:Paid_in_Full#One_Last_Thing "Skyrim:Paid in Full"). Additionally, he's got a problem with an eighty year old ex-miner who borrowed his [ancient Nordic pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nordic_Pickaxe "Skyrim:Ancient Nordic Pickaxe") and forgot to give it back. Locate Glover Mallory near his smithy in the center of town and he will ask, *"You haven't seen  [Crescius Caerellius](/wiki/Skyrim:Crescius_Caerellius "Skyrim:Crescius Caerellius") have you? That foolish old man's taken my pickaxe again!"* He will then explain that it's not an ordinary pickaxe, but a rarity in [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim"). Since Glover has some shady business going on he will refuse to call the guards and will tell you, *"Um, I prefer to handle these things on my own. Look, if you see Crescius, tell him to give me that pickaxe back and I'll pay you for the trouble."* You can then ask what the pickaxe is good for and he will readily explain that it's the only tool able to crack [stalhrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim "Skyrim:Stalhrim") and that he traded that pickaxe with residents from [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

### The Damn Fool

Seek out Crescius Caerellius. If you have yet to finish [The Final Descent](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Final_Descent "Skyrim:The Final Descent") you can find him around the clock inside [Raven Rock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Mine "Skyrim:Raven Rock Mine") and you will have to initiate said quest before he will discuss the pickaxe. Otherwise, he will have returned to his daily routine and can be found either at home or in the mine. When you mention the pickaxe, he will immediately say, *"The damn fool doesn't even deserve to have it! The pickaxe was made for mining, not selling. I'll bet he stole it from the Skaal in the first place."*

Note: If you already have an Ancient Nordic Pickaxe in your inventory, you may not be able to open any dialogue with Crescius about it, preventing you from advancing the quest. If this happens, simply drop, store or sell the one in your inventory until after you have gotten beyond the above dialogue with him.

Once Crescius has enabled the dialogue about the pickaxe and responded to your inquiry as outlined above, you will have three reply options available, and the reward will differ depending on your decision:

| Option | Dialogue | | | | Result |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| *"It doesn't belong to you."* | *"Oh, very well. Here. Tell him I hope he drops the thing on his foot."* | | | | Return to Glover Mallory with the axe. |
| *"Fine, you keep it."* | *"Now you're finally talking some sense. A mining tool belongs in a mining family. Just tell him I lost it or something."* | | | | Return to Glover Mallory and lie. |
| *"Let me think about this."* | *"Good. Now let me get back to what I was doing."* | | | | Nothing. |

### The Two Rewards

If you chose the first option, return to Glover and tell him you have his pickaxe, which he will then let you keep as a reward: *"So you finally tracked down old Crescius, eh? Quite a character, isn't he. Tell you what. Since you went through all the trouble of finding it for me and all, you keep it."* He will then tell you why: *"I just wanted to remind that codger you can't just go around taking things from other people. Now that you've delivered the message, I'm satisfied. Besides, that pickaxe hasn't done me any good in years. Maybe you can put it to good use."* If you chose the second option, he will be quite bitter about the whole thing but will still hand you a leveled amount of gold: *"Blast! That careless old fool. Well, thanks for trying anyway. Here you go... for your trouble."*

## Notes

- The quest name is a play on the phrase "[ax to grind](http://wiktionary.org/wiki/ax_to_grind "wiktionary:ax to grind")".
- The Ancient Nordic Pickaxe you gain access to during this quest has a unique base ID ([xx](/wiki/Skyrim:Form_ID "Skyrim:Form ID")0206F2).
- Although Glover lets you keep the pickaxe as a reward for finishing the quest, you can choose not to return the pickaxe and keep it that way instead. It will remain in your inventory as a quest item, making it a useful, weightless pickaxe.
- If you return the pickaxe, Glover explains that he only wanted to teach Crescuis a lesson that stealing is wrong. This, however, is ironic due to his own connections with the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)").
- If you haven't triggered the quest [The Final Descent](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Final_Descent "Skyrim:The Final Descent") beforehand, talking to Crescius will start it, making it unavoidable.
- If you let Crescius keep the pickaxe, you'll need to either buy one from [Baldor Iron-Shaper](/wiki/Skyrim:Baldor_Iron-Shaper "Skyrim:Baldor Iron-Shaper") in the [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village") or find one as random treasure on Solstheim before you can mine Stalhrim.

## Bugs

- Despite its unique base ID, the Ancient Nordic Pickaxe Crescius gives you during this quest cannot be upgraded at a grindstone.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.5, addresses this issue. It can now be improved with an [iron ingot](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot").
- If you let Crescius keep the pickaxe but then pickpocket it from him later before the quest is finished, the objective to lie to Glover Mallory will be stuck in your journal forever.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
