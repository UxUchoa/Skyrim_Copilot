Froki's Shack

**Froki's Shack** is a small [shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") southeast of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead").

The [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") hunter [Froki Whetted-Blade](/wiki/Skyrim:Froki_Whetted-Blade "Skyrim:Froki Whetted-Blade") lives here with his grandson [Haming](/wiki/Skyrim:Haming "Skyrim:Haming"), a survivor of [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen"). He is a follower of the old ways and a devout worshipper of [Kyne](/wiki/Lore:Kyne "Lore:Kyne"). Froki will guide you through the [Sacred Trials of Kyne](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials") if you allow him. It consists of one area, *[Froki's Shack](#Froki.27s_Shack)*.

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance is a basket containing a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), two loose [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"), and a quiver of the same, with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") nearby. To the right of the door is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"). In the cliff below the shack, there is an [orichalcum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore Vein") with a [diamond](/wiki/Skyrim:Diamond "Skyrim:Diamond") visible and collectible on the surface of the vein. There is a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") on the ground beside the vein. A [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") and a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") grow near the [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block").

A [shrine of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Talos "Skyrim:Shrines") can be found outside the shack farther south in the mountains, surrounded by [steel cuffed boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Cuffed Boots"), an [Imperial light shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Shield "Skyrim:Imperial Light Shield"), a copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[King](/wiki/Skyrim:King "Skyrim:King")*, a copy of *[The Amulet of Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Amulet_of_Kings "Skyrim:The Amulet of Kings")*, a satchel, five loose [gold coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a [heavy Imperial helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Helmet "Skyrim:Imperial Helmet"), and an [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword"). There are two [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes on the way from the shack to the shrine.

### Froki's Shack

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-interior-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg)

The shack's interior

Upon entering, you will find a [steel battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Steel Battleaxe") to the left of the door. There is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") on your left, with a large shelving unit next to it. The shelves hold a woodcutter's axe, an [eye of sabre cat](/wiki/Skyrim:Eye_of_Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Eye of Sabre Cat"), a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), a copy of the [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") skill book *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor "Skyrim:Vernaccus and Bourlor")*, a [Nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"), and an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing"). A small table and two chairs are opposite the door, with a [luna moth wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth_Wing "Skyrim:Luna Moth Wing") and a [butterfly wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly_Wing "Skyrim:Butterfly Wing") on the table. Against the southeast wall is a single bed and an unlocked chest containing minor loot.

## Notes

- Even after you complete the trials and Froki says he counts you "as a friend," the items in the shack can't be taken without stealing.
- Though Haming lives here with his grandfather, there is only one small bed, and Froki sleeps in it.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the basket, leather strip, iron arrows, and woodcutter's axe just outside the shack as owned.

## Bugs

- On the wood chopping block outside to the north-northwest of the shack, you can find a respawning [Diadem of the Savant](/wiki/Skyrim:Diadem_of_the_Savant "Skyrim:Diadem of the Savant"). It is likely this was a mistake, as the Creation Kit specifies the diadem was meant to be a unique reward for completing [Shalidor's Maze](/wiki/Skyrim:Shalidor%27s_Maze "Skyrim:Shalidor's Maze").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. It changes this instance of the diadem into a leveled circlet with a [Fortify Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Apparel#Fortify_Archery "Skyrim:Generic Magic Apparel") effect.
- The woodcutter's axe may be partially embedded into the wood chopping block, making the Diadem of the Savant or the archery circlet hard to pick up.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.1, fixes this bug.
- The floor of the shack is higher than it should be. This is evident from the door's position on the inside, the low ceiling, and the proximity of the mounted animal heads to the chairs and table. **?**
