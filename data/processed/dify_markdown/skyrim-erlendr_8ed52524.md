**Erlendr**, a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H"), is one of the souls in the [Hall of Valor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Valor "Skyrim:Hall of Valor"). He was turned to stone by the wizard [Grimkell](/wiki/Lore:Grimkell "Lore:Grimkell"), along with [Hunroor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunroor "Skyrim:Hunroor") and [Nikulas](/wiki/Skyrim:Nikulas "Skyrim:Nikulas"), while on a quest to find [Sovngarde](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovngarde_(place) "Skyrim:Sovngarde (place)").
