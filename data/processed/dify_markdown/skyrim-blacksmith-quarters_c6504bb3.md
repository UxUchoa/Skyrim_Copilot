The Blacksmith Quarters in Windhelm

**Blacksmith Quarters** is a building on the east side of the market place in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") that belongs to [Oengul War-Anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Oengul_War-Anvil "Skyrim:Oengul War-Anvil"), the local [blacksmith](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith "Skyrim:Blacksmith").

It is home to both Oengul and his assistant [Hermir Strong-Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Hermir_Strong-Heart "Skyrim:Hermir Strong-Heart"). The house itself does not serve as a shop; merchant services are offered at the forge area outside. There is only one zone, which is the [interior](#Blacksmith_Quarters).

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/SR-place-Blacksmith_Quarters_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Blacksmith_Quarters_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Blacksmith_Quarters_02.jpg)

Oengul's forge, located outside his home in Windhelm

During the day both [Oengul War-Anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Oengul_War-Anvil "Skyrim:Oengul War-Anvil") and [Hermir Strong-Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Hermir_Strong-Heart "Skyrim:Hermir Strong-Heart") can be found working at the forge. There is a tanning rack beside the forge, with a grindstone and workbench further to the right and against a wall to the north is a smelter. [Silda the Unseen](/wiki/Skyrim:Silda_the_Unseen "Skyrim:Silda the Unseen") will often use the forge as a source of warmth at night.

### Blacksmith Quarters

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-interior-Blacksmith_Quarters.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Blacksmith_Quarters.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blacksmith_Quarters.jpg)

Hermir's bedroom in the Blacksmith Quarters

Upon entering the property there is a large open-plan area with fire pit along the north wall with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") over it and a metal bowl containing three [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato") on the floor beside it. To the left is a square wooden table with a joint of [horse meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Meat "Skyrim:Horse Meat") and [horker meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat "Skyrim:Horker Meat") on top and two baskets on the floor in front, one holds [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and the other three [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"). Behind the table is a wall shelf with two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, two bunches of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") and a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") hanging from it. Beside the table is an empty cart. On the far side of the fire pit are three more wall shelves that mostly holding clutter along with an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"). There is a chair in the northeastern corner and a long end table further along the east wall with a [blacksmith's apron](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Apron"), a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") and [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") on top. Against the south wall are several crates and an unusable [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge"). Against the west wall, on the right of the entrance when entering are a row of three weapon racks, all of which are empty. In the center of the room is a large round table holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and a tomato.

There is a flight of stairs up to the second floor against the south wall heading to the east. At the top of the stairs is a large landing with a long wooden table against the east wall with an [iron greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Greatsword "Skyrim:Iron Greatsword"), a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), a [hide shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Shield "Skyrim:Hide Shield") and a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield") on top. Against the north wall are a couple of bookcases holding sixteen common books including a non-skill version of *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor "Skyrim:Vernaccus and Bourlor")*, a bottle of alto wine and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and in the northwestern corner is a chair. against the west wall is a small square wooden table holding an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") and [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield") and a bottle of wine. By the stairwell is a small unit holding a bottle of alto wine and a leather strip.

To the right of the bookcases on the north wall is a doorway through into a bedroom containing a fireplace on the south wall with two chairs arranged in front of it. By one of the chairs is a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum"). To the right of the fireplace is a small set of shelves holding a bottle of alto wine and a bottle of wine. There is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") against the west wall with an empty set of shelves to the right of it.

Opposite the fireplace, out from the north wall, so that you can walk all the way around it, is a single unowned bed used by Hermir Strong-Heart, with a small, low table on either side and a long unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing random items at the foot of it. In the northeast corner is a wash basin with a long end table against the east wall with a copy of *[Gods and Worship](/wiki/Skyrim:Gods_and_Worship "Skyrim:Gods and Worship")* on top.

## Notes

- There is a [![Loot](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Loot") Loot [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Shadowmarks "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") to the right of the entrance door.
- With the exception of most barrels and sacks, all containers in the house are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning"), and you can safely store items in them.

## Bugs

- The house only has one bed, but two people live here so one of them would always be unable to sleep.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.7, addresses this issue. A second bed has been added. Ownership of each bed is now per NPC so they pick the same one each time. The navmesh has also been adjusted to accommodate this.
