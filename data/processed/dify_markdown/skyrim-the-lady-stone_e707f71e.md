The Lady Stone

**The Lady Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones") scattered across [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-Lady_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lady_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Lady_Stone_(active).jpg)

The Lady Stone, when activated

*Those under the sign of [The Lady](/wiki/Lore:The_Lady "Lore:The Lady") regenerate [Health](/wiki/Skyrim:Health "Skyrim:Health") and [Stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Stamina "Skyrim:Stamina") more quickly.*

Activating the Lady Stone provides the player with a 25% boost to both health and stamina regeneration rates.

## The Lady Stone

It can be found in middle of [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta"), on an island directly north of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). There are a few upright stones flanking it and many [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") and [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") on the island and around the stones. You will also find many [monarch butterfies](/wiki/Skyrim:Monarch_Butterfly "Skyrim:Monarch Butterfly") and [blue butterflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly "Skyrim:Blue Butterfly") during the day and [luna moths](/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth "Skyrim:Luna Moth") and [torchbugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug "Skyrim:Torchbug") in the evening and at night. Three [slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Slaughterfish") linger near the island which will attack if they see you. An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") can be found on the southwestern end of the island.

## Notes

- The treasure chest for [Treasure Map X](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_X "Skyrim:Treasure Map X") is located underwater just east and slightly north of the stone.
- The [loading screen](/wiki/Skyrim:Loading_Screens#Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Loading Screens") for The Lady Stone implies the stone provides a power rather than a constant effect ability, which is incorrect.
