Leigelf's House in Dawnstar

**Leigelf's House** is the residence of [Leigelf](/wiki/Skyrim:Leigelf "Skyrim:Leigelf") in the center of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"), next to the blacksmith.

## Related Quests

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

### Exterior

There are a few steps up to a porch on the front of the house, on the porch are two [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and a wooden bench at one end and several piles of firewood at the other. The house faces the bay and is novice-locked the majority of the time.

### Leigelf's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-interior-Leigelf%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Leigelf%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Leigelf%27s_House.jpg)

Leigelf's house

This is another single-room home, on your left upon entering is a set of shelves holding two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulb, three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and has several pieces of cheese on top. Above are two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). To the left is a basket on the floor containing two leeks and to the right is a food barrel. In the middle of the east wall and coming out from the wall is a large double bed with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of it. On the floor to the left of the bed are four bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). To the right of the bed is a table holding a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") and six loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold").

The fireplace is in the middle of the south wall, opposite the door and has a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front of it, a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") on the mantelpiece and a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") on the hearth. To the left is a hanging rack holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and to the left of these is a wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). To the right of the fireplace is a long wooden table still against the south wall. On the table is a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), three [sweet rolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge").

In the northwestern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table are three bottles of Nord mead, a pickaxe, five loose coins and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse").
