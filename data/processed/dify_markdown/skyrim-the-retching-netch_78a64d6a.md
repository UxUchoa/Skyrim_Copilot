**The Retching Netch** is the cornerclub in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

It is in the marketplace between [Alor House](/wiki/Skyrim:Alor_House "Skyrim:Alor House") to the northwest and [Glover Mallory's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory%27s_House "Skyrim:Glover Mallory's House") to the southwest. It is the only inn in Raven Rock, and the only one on [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim").

It is owned and operated by [Geldis Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Geldis_Sadri "Skyrim:Geldis Sadri"), who sells [food and drinks](/wiki/Skyrim:Geldis_Sadri#Standard_Merchandise "Skyrim:Geldis Sadri") and rents out beds for 10 gold. Its interior includes a [ground floor](#Ground_Floor), the [taproom](#Taproom), and the [private rooms](#Private_Rooms).

The establishment earned its name several years ago, when Geldis saw one of his patrons staggering around the docks with a bottle of [sujamma](/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma "Skyrim:Sujamma") while naked and singing loudly. A [netch](/wiki/Skyrim:Netch "Skyrim:Netch") floated over the [Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark "Skyrim:The Bulwark"), and the naked man threw the bottle at it. The bottle broke on the netch, which began to tilt, then eventually vomited.

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance are three ash yam urns and two [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plants. To the right of the entrance are four more scathecraw plants. A [Redoran guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran_Guard "Skyrim:Redoran Guard") is posted outside the entrance, which is unlocked.

#### Ground Floor

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch.jpg)

Retching Netch ground floor

The entrance leads into the upper floor of the establishment. Near the west wall of this small circular room, mercenary-for-hire [Teldryn Sero](/wiki/Skyrim:Teldryn_Sero "Skyrim:Teldryn Sero") can be found at all hours sitting close to the roaring fireplace and the [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") where the cook [Drovas Relvi](/wiki/Skyrim:Drovas_Relvi "Skyrim:Drovas Relvi") works. Along the south wall is a small round table holding a bottle of [shein](/wiki/Skyrim:Shein "Skyrim:Shein"), while Teldryn's table holds another bottle of shein and a bottle of [sujamma](/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma "Skyrim:Sujamma"). Along the north wall around Teldryn's table are four food barrels, with three more bottles of shein atop two more small round tables and an unusable barrel. With the [Staff of Sheogorath](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Sheogorath "Skyrim:Staff of Sheogorath") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, the [Fork of Horripilation](/wiki/Skyrim:Fork_of_Horripilation "Skyrim:Fork of Horripilation") and a *[Mysterious Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Mysterious_Note_(Staff_of_Sheogorath) "Skyrim:Mysterious Note (Staff of Sheogorath)")* can be found pinned to a keg in this room, surrounded by a green glow. In the middle of the room, stairs lead down to the lower floor.

#### Taproom

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch_02.jpg)

Retching Netch taproom

At the bottom of the stairs is the spacious taproom, with numerous small round tables for the regulars, as well as two short corridors to the north and west leading to doors to four private rooms reserved for paying or permanent residents. Near the foot of the stairs are two food barrels and two food urns. In the southeast corner is a food barrel standing next to a stack of barrels, six of which are food barrels. To the south behind the bar is a meat barrel, a food barrel, and a fish barrel, two of which have bottles of shein on top, flanking the entrance to a small storage alcove containing three more food barrels in a stack. In the southwest corner are two huge brewing vats, with two food barrels nearby among the two large kegs by the west corridor. In the northwest corner are two more large kegs, another food barrel, and a set of shelves holding five bottles of shein and copies of all three volumes of *[History of Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock "Skyrim:History of Raven Rock")*. To the north are two food barrels and two tables, with the table by the north corridor holding a bottle of shein. In the northeast corner is another table and a food barrel.

In the middle of the room are two tables, with one holding a bottle each of shein and sujamma, and the other holding a bottle of shein. To the south is the bar, where [Geldis Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Geldis_Sadri "Skyrim:Geldis Sadri") offers his services and [chats](/wiki/Skyrim:Geldis_Sadri#Conversations "Skyrim:Geldis Sadri") with the regulars. Two bottles of sujamma and a bottle of shein sit on the bar, while under the counter is an [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox") and a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). Next to the bar on the floor is an open box containing three bottles of shein and two bottles of sujamma.

#### Private Rooms

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Retching_Netch_03.jpg)

Retching Netch room for rent

The west corridor connects to two rooms. The room you can rent is to the south, and is filled with goodies for the paying customer, including two chests containing minor loot, two ingredient urns, a food barrel, a food urn, and a large amount of food. In the northeast corner, the food barrel and an unusable barrel have a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and two [eidar cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel") on top. To the right of these barrels and opposite the single bed is a set of shelves holding two baskets containing two bowls of [moon sugar](/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar "Skyrim:Moon Sugar"), a wooden bowl containing a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), two bottles of shein, a [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Stamina"), a [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Healing"), an [ash yam](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam"), two eidar cheese wheels, a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and a copy of *[The Reclamations](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reclamations "Skyrim:The Reclamations")*. To the right of the set of shelves is a small round table holding another ash yam, an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), and a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"). Lastly, a small table along the south wall holds three ash yams, another sliced goat cheese, and a large coin purse. The small neighboring room to the west belongs to Drovas Relvi, and features his owned single bed, an ash yam urn, an open box containing three more ash yams, and a small round table holding a bottle of sujamma.

The north corridor connects to two rooms. Both rooms are sparsely decorated, and the second room is apprentice-locked. The first room belongs to Geldis Sadri, and features his owned single bed with a chest at its foot, a small round table holding a loaf of bread, an ash yam, and a bottle of shein, and a set of shelves holding two bottles of shein and a copy of the [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(quest) "Skyrim:Deathbrand (quest)")-related tome *[Deathbrand](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(book) "Skyrim:Deathbrand (book)")*. The second room belongs to [Mogrul](/wiki/Skyrim:Mogrul_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Mogrul (Dragonborn)"), and features his owned single bed with a chest at its foot, a small round table holding a loaf of bread and a bottle of shein, and a set of shelves holding another bottle of shein and copies of both volumes of *[The Red Year](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Year "Skyrim:The Red Year")*. On the floor outside Mogrul's room is the owned bedroll used by his lackey [Slitter](/wiki/Skyrim:Slitter "Skyrim:Slitter").

## Notes

- The bed you can rent here is the only bed in Solstheim that grants the [Well Rested](/wiki/Skyrim:Well_Rested "Skyrim:Well Rested") bonus.
- Redoran guards may occasionally remark on this inn, stating: *"They serve the best Sujamma I've ever tasted over at the Retching Netch. Worth every coin."*
