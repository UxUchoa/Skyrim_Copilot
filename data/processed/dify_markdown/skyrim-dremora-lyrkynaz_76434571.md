Dremora Lyrkynaz

**Dremora Lyrkynaz** is a [Dremora](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora "Skyrim:Dremora") mage whom you must summon during the quest [The Arms of Chaos](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:The Arms of Chaos") as part of the [Arms of Chaos](/wiki/Skyrim:Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:Arms of Chaos") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). Once you have obtained the [Sovereign Band](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovereign_Band "Skyrim:Sovereign Band"), you will be directed to use it at a Daedric altar east of [Skytemple Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins "Skyrim:Skytemple Ruins") in order to summon three Guardians of the Sigil Stones. Activating the altar will summon three Daedra in short succession: [Dremora Sehtkynaz](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Sehtkynaz "Skyrim:Dremora Sehtkynaz"), Dremora Lyrkynaz, and [Dremora Sovereign](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Sovereign "Skyrim:Dremora Sovereign"). Dremora Lyrkynaz is the second Dremora to appear, and will be summoned as soon as you kill Dremora Sehtkynaz.

Dremora Lyrkynaz utilizes powerful [shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage "Skyrim:Shock Damage") magic in combat and can summon a [storm atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Storm_Atronach "Skyrim:Storm Atronach"). He carries a dagger and warhammer along with a [Green Sigil Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Sigil_Stone "Skyrim:Green Sigil Stone"). He will drop a [Daedra Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra_Heart "Skyrim:Daedra Heart") when killed.
