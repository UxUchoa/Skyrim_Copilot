Borri

**Borri**, an [elderly](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder "Skyrim:Elder") [priest](/wiki/Skyrim:Priest "Skyrim:Priest"), is one of the reclusive [Greybeards](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards") in [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar") who refuses to speak, lest a mere whisper of their voice unleashes destruction. Borri teaches you a word of the [Whirlwind Sprint](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Sprint "Skyrim:Whirlwind Sprint") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Shout "Skyrim:Shout") during [The Way of the Voice](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Way_of_the_Voice "Skyrim:The Way of the Voice"). He will react when interacted with, greeting you with a muttered "Dovahkiin" and causing the surroundings to rumble in doing so.

Borri wears a set of [Greybeard's robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Robes "Skyrim:Greybeard's Robes") with the matching [hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Hood "Skyrim:Greybeard's Hood") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Boots "Skyrim:Greybeard's Boots"). He carries a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), one sample of [ice wraith teeth](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth "Skyrim:Ice Wraith Teeth"), an [amulet of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Talos "Skyrim:Amulet of Talos"), and one sample of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries").

He will start his day at 4am, rising from bed to meditate inside facing the windows, on the mats, changing to a different window each hour. At 12:30pm, he will meditate in the courtyard, as well as in the courtyard tower, occasionally using the [Clear Skies](/wiki/Skyrim:Clear_Skies "Skyrim:Clear Skies") shout while up there. At 4:30pm he will meditate in front of the shrine in the main entry hall, and at 8pm he will meditate in the main entry hall, rotating positions in a clockwise direction every hour until retiring to his sleeping quarters at 12:30am to read; finally retiring to bed at 2am. He will then sleep for two hours and repeat his daily routine.

Borri knows the following shouts: [Disarm](/wiki/Skyrim:Disarm "Skyrim:Disarm"), [Dismay](/wiki/Skyrim:Dismay "Skyrim:Dismay"), [Elemental Fury](/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Fury "Skyrim:Elemental Fury"), [Fire Breath](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Breath "Skyrim:Fire Breath"), [Frost Breath](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Breath "Skyrim:Frost Breath"), [Ice Form](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Form "Skyrim:Ice Form"), [Unrelenting Force](/wiki/Skyrim:Unrelenting_Force "Skyrim:Unrelenting Force"), and [Whirlwind Sprint](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Sprint "Skyrim:Whirlwind Sprint").

Aside from his greeting to you, the only other word ever heard by Borri is the shout *"Bex!"* during the quest The Way of the Voice. The shout in English means *open*.
