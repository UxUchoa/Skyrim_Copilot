Alvor and Sigrid's House

**Alvor and Sigrid's House** is a medium-sized home in [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"). It is home to the local blacksmith, [Alvor](/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor "Skyrim:Alvor"), and his family.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-npc-Dorthe_2.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dorthe_2.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dorthe_2.jpg)

*"I'm bored standing around, papa. I could take over for you..."*

The building encompasses both the house and a smithing area outside and to the side of the house.

The house consists of one area only, *[Alvor and Sigrid's House](#Alvor_and_Sigrid.27s_House)*.

## Exterior

All the tools to make and temper weapons and armor are here, namely a [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"). There are five [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot") and five [steel ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot "Skyrim:Steel Ingot") under the workbench. On the table by the tanning rack are some gauntlets, boots, a helmet, a [steel shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield "Skyrim:Steel Shield"), a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"), and an [ancient Nord helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord_Helmet "Skyrim:Ancient Nord Helmet"). Around the corner from the workbench is a display rack with an [iron warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer "Skyrim:Iron Warhammer"), and an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword").

## Alvor and Sigrid's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-interior-Alvor_and_Sigrid%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Alvor_and_Sigrid%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Alvor_and_Sigrid%27s_House.jpg)

Ground floor

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-interior-Alvor_and_Sigrid%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Alvor_and_Sigrid%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Alvor_and_Sigrid%27s_House_02.jpg)

Cellar

There are two floors to the house: the first floor and the cellar. They are split into areas discernible by the furniture, with the stairs to the cellar by the far left wall. The first floor is split into two areas: the bedroom and the kitchen. The bedroom contains two beds, two chairs by a table, a wardrobe, and a chest. The double bed is Alvor and Sigrid's, and the single bed is Dorthe's. There is a [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and some [loose coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") on the table, as well as a copy of *[A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun "Skyrim:A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun")*. Next to this table is a cupboard, which has a [blacksmith's apron](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Apron") on top. At the foot of the double bed is a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing leveled items. [Stump](/wiki/Skyrim:Stump "Skyrim:Stump") can sometimes be found sleeping on the rug between the beds, after following Dorthe home. The other side of the room is a mix between a dining room and kitchen; by the fire is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"), and above the fire is a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword"). Next to the fire is an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield"), and three [dead rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). Opposite the fire is a dining table with an [Eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), three pieces of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry "Skyrim:Snowberry"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), two [chicken eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and a piece of [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"). There is a basket next to the table with two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") in it. Next to this is a cupboard containing [sliced Eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), an [Eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), an [Eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and a single [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"). Hanging from the roof near this are two [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), three [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and a [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam").

The cellar is both a social and living area for the family, and a storage room for any extra weapons and armor that Alvor has made. Moving from the stairs there is a small table with a chair, a weapons table to the right, an L-shaped "bar", shelves behind the bar, a chair by an end table, and a wardrobe and chest by the far wall. On the weapons table are three [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"), and a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow"). On the woodpile next to the table is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"). On top of the end table are two [blacksmith's potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Potion "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Potion"), while the chest has leveled items in it. There is a cupboard next to this, with an [iron battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Iron Battleaxe") on top of it. On the bar you can find [iron gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets"), an [iron cuirass](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and a pair of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots") under the bar. Behind the bar is a cupboard containing clutter. Hanging under the stairs is a [garlic braid](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), a dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), and a dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"). There is also a small table containing a piece of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread").

## Notes

- The front door has a novice-level lock. The house is locked most of the time, so it does not make a very good temporary home for the player despite Alvor's proffered hospitality. Furthermore, if you manage to get inside the house after midnight you are not welcome and may be told to leave.
- The [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Alvor%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Alvor's House") to the house is carried by Alvor, Sigrid, and Dorthe.
- If you befriend Alvor during Before the Storm, the iron and steel ingots beside the workbench will be free to take.
- The armor sitting on the table in the work area will respawn, and will also be leveled. The iron and steel ingots beside the workbench will also respawn.

## Bugs

- Dorthe's bed isn't a child's bed, allowing her to sleep in the parents' bed, causing one of them to stay up all night.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. Her bed wasn't marked as a child's bed, which forced her AI to pick the nearest one available.
