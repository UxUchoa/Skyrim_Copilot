Forgotten Vale Cave

**Forgotten Vale Cave** is a small cave in the [Forgotten Vale](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale "Skyrim:Forgotten Vale") containing [chaurus](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus "Skyrim:Chaurus") and one of the rare [shellbugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug "Skyrim:Shellbug"). It contains only one zone, *[Forgotten Vale Cave](#Forgotten_Vale_Cave)*.

### Exterior

The exterior of this cave is the [Forgotten Vale](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale "Skyrim:Forgotten Vale"). The entrance is in the first section of the vale along the east wall of the valley, and is guarded by a [Falmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer "Skyrim:Falmer"). A [gleamblossom](/wiki/Skyrim:Gleamblossom "Skyrim:Gleamblossom") plant is to the left of the entrance.

### Forgotten Vale Cave

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/SR-interior-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg)

Main chamber

The entrance leads into a passage with [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes") hanging from the ceiling. A gated pen to the north contains a [chaurus](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus "Skyrim:Chaurus"), a [chaurus hunter](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Hunter "Skyrim:Chaurus Hunter"), two unoccupied [chaurus cocoons](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Cocoon "Skyrim:Chaurus Cocoon"), seven chaurus egg sacs, and five [glowing mushrooms](/wiki/Skyrim:Glowing_Mushroom "Skyrim:Glowing Mushroom") on the walls. The path splits to the west, with the left side rising along a ledge to a rope release that opens a concealed door in the northwest wall of the main chamber, and the right side descending past a Falmer [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") to the floor of the chamber. The chamber features six [Falmer hives](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Hive "Skyrim:Falmer Hive") and five cocoons, but only one of each is occupied by a chaurus hunter.

The passage behind the concealed door on the northwest ledge of the chamber descends into another chamber containing another rope release that operates the door, a few glowing mushrooms, an unoccupied cocoon, and one of the rare [shellbugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug "Skyrim:Shellbug"). In order to obtain their [chitin](/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug_Chitin "Skyrim:Shellbug Chitin"), these creatures must be [mined](/wiki/Skyrim:Mining "Skyrim:Mining") with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"); the material cannot be obtained through killing the creatures by conventional means. Shellbugs are also not detected by the [Detect Life](/wiki/Skyrim:Detect_Life_(spell) "Skyrim:Detect Life (spell)") spell.

## Bugs

- You may be unable to mine the shellbug after it respawns. Leaving and reentering the cave will enable the shellbug, allowing you to mine it normally.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.0, fixes this bug.
