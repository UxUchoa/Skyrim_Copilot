The Ultimate Feast

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Viriya](/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya "Skyrim:Viriya") and read the [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Wanted:_A_Seafood_Feast "Skyrim:Wanted: A Seafood Feast").
2. Obtain [Steamed Mudcrab Legs](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamed_Mudcrab_Legs "Skyrim:Steamed Mudcrab Legs"), [Cooked Arctic Char](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Arctic_Char "Skyrim:Cooked Arctic Char"), and [Crab Stew](/wiki/Skyrim:Crab_Stew "Skyrim:Crab Stew").
3. Set the table in [The Winking Skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Winking_Skeever "Skyrim:The Winking Skeever") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude").
4. Wait an hour and collect your tip.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This quest can be started after [Spirits Away](/wiki/Skyrim:Spirits_Away "Skyrim:Spirits Away") has been completed by talking to [Viriya](/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya "Skyrim:Viriya"):

:   **Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?**
:   *"You're headed to the capital. It's all in this note."*

She then hands you a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Wanted:_A_Seafood_Feast "Skyrim:Wanted: A Seafood Feast") that requests [Steamed Mudcrab Legs](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamed_Mudcrab_Legs "Skyrim:Steamed Mudcrab Legs"), [Cooked Arctic Char](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Arctic_Char "Skyrim:Cooked Arctic Char") and a bowl of [Crab Stew](/wiki/Skyrim:Crab_Stew "Skyrim:Crab Stew"). This will likely involve [cooking](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking").

You should have access to plenty of [Crab Meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Crab_Meat "Skyrim:Crab Meat") and [Mudcrab Legs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Legs "Skyrim:Mudcrab Legs") after completing the [previous quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Spirits_Away "Skyrim:Spirits Away"), and the other ingredients needed for the stew are not hard to acquire. [Butter](/wiki/Skyrim:Butter "Skyrim:Butter") can be a little trickier to find, although you can simply make your own at a churn if you've [constructed](/wiki/Skyrim:Construction "Skyrim:Construction") a [kitchen](/wiki/Skyrim:Kitchen "Skyrim:Kitchen"). If you don't wish to go fishing for an [Arctic Char](/wiki/Skyrim:Arctic_Char "Skyrim:Arctic Char"), you can go diving at [Hela's Folly](/wiki/Skyrim:Hela%27s_Folly "Skyrim:Hela's Folly") instead and catch one with your hands.

Once you have obtained all three dishes, head to [The Winking Skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Winking_Skeever "Skyrim:The Winking Skeever") and set the upstairs table for [Gisli](/wiki/Skyrim:Gisli "Skyrim:Gisli"). She will sit down to eat, which will take her an hour. Once she's finished, she'll leave a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Gisli%27s_Note "Skyrim:Gisli's Note") and a [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") on the plate.
