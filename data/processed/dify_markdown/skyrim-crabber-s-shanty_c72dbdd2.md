**Crabber's Shanty** is a small run-down [shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") on the [River Hjaal](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Hjaal "Skyrim:River Hjaal").

The shack is home to a lone [fisherman](/wiki/Skyrim:Fisherman "Skyrim:Fisherman"). It contains only one zone, which is the exterior zone *[Crabber's Shanty](#Crabber.27s_Shanty)*.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty_02.jpg)

Inside the shanty

There are two doorways into the shanty and several holes in the roof and a large glassless window in the wall, making it barely better than sleeping outside. One doorway faces east and the other faces west. There are several sacks and a barrel containing [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") around the shanty; one small sack outside the south side of the shanty isn't marked as owned and therefore can be emptied freely. Outside the doorway to the east on a small piece of decking is an open crate containing two dead [mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab") that can be harvested freely. The fisherman seems to spend all of his time sitting on the edge of the decking. There is also a [bucket](/wiki/Skyrim:Bucket "Skyrim:Bucket") and an unsearchable barrel near the fisherman. There is a [clam](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam "Skyrim:Clam") in the stream near the shanty that can be harvested for [clam meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat "Skyrim:Clam Meat"), while several mudcrabs are found up and down the stream from here.

### Crabber's Shanty

Inside the shanty, there is a single owned bed in the southeastern corner. Against the west wall is a small unit holding another dead mudcrab that can be freely harvested. To the right of this is a basket containing three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). Against the north wall is a set of shelves holding three [tankards](/wiki/Skyrim:Tankard "Skyrim:Tankard"), bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), three [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), and six [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"). The tankards and bottles of ale can be freely taken, but the vegetables are owned.

## Notes

- The barrels in the house are [respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning"); however, the sacks are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the small sack, tankards, and bottles of ale as owned like everything else.
- The [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") Creation adds a [fishing rod](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Rod "Skyrim:Fishing Rod"), a pair of [fishing boots](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Fishing_Boots&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Fishing Boots (page does not exist)"), and a book.
- Version 4.3.3 marks everything added with the Fishing Creation as owned like everything else.

## Bugs

- [Fast traveling](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Travel "Skyrim:Fast Travel") to this location can sometimes cause the player to spawn high in the air, only to fall to their death.
- Crabber's Shanty is erroneously marked as being in [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.1, addresses this issue. It has been set to be part of [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch") instead.
