Skytemple Ruins

**Skytemple Ruins** is a small [outdoor Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings "Skyrim:Nordic Dwellings") north of the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)") containing a [draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr") and [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton").

## Related Quests

- **[The Arms of Chaos](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:The Arms of Chaos")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:Arms of Chaos"): Complete a mage's quest to reassemble two powerful staves.

## Walkthrough

The sparse ruins are at the top of a large glacial mound on an island off the north coast of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"). They are guarded outside by two archer skeletons and three melee skeletons. To the west is an altar overlooking the sea holding a dead skeleton and a leveled [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Poisons "Skyrim:Potions").

To the east through a novice-locked wooden door is a small burial chamber containing an apprentice-locked boss-level chest guarded by a leveled draugr slumbering in an upright sarcophagus opposite the door. Next to the chest are a copy of the [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Before the Ages of Man](/wiki/Skyrim:Before_the_Ages_of_Man "Skyrim:Before the Ages of Man")*, a leveled [regeneration potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"), and a leveled [lasting potency potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Lasting_Potency "Skyrim:Potion of Lasting Potency").
