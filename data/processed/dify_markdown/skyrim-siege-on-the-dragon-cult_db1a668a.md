Siege on the Dragon Cult

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Captain Valmir](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Valmir "Skyrim:Captain Valmir") to begin the quest.
2. Enter the ruins of [Forelhost](/wiki/Skyrim:Forelhost "Skyrim:Forelhost").
3. (Optional) Find the [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Skorm_Snow-Strider%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Skorm Snow-Strider's Journal") of Skorm Snow-Strider.
4. Make your way through the first zone into the Crypt.
5. Either pick the lock on the well or find the key, then move on to the Refectory.
6. Travel through the Refectory, find the glass claw and locate the [puzzle door](/wiki/Skyrim:Traps#Puzzle_Door "Skyrim:Traps").
7. Use the claw to enter and defeat [Rahgot](/wiki/Skyrim:Rahgot_(creature) "Skyrim:Rahgot (creature)").
8. Return to Valmir with the mask.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-place-Forelhost.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forelhost.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Forelhost.jpg)

Valmir's camp sits in the ruins of Forelhost

When you enter the courtyard of Forelhost for the first time, [Captain Valmir](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Valmir "Skyrim:Captain Valmir") will call to you. When the two of you get close enough, he'll initiate a conversation. He will claim he has been sent there by military forces in an attempt to recover a powerful artifact for the war effort. He asks for your help, since he cannot do it alone. According to him, this was the last stronghold of the Dragon Cult back in the First Era, and the commander's mask was allegedly an extremely powerful artifact. He requests that you enter the ruin and retrieve the item. He also mentions that the military general who stormed the fortress to wipe out the cult supposedly left his journal here, so finding it might provide some more information.

### Forelhost Stronghold

Shortly after entering the zone, you'll begin encountering ghosts of the cultists. They must be defeated or avoided to proceed. Work your way around to get behind the portcullis that is down, and you'll be able to enter the crypts. *[Skorm Snow-Strider's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Skorm_Snow-Strider%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Skorm Snow-Strider's Journal")* is found along this path. It recounts that when they took the fortress, the defenders had collapsed the entry and all killed themselves, leaving the ghosts to haunt the place. The well was locked and poisoned, and with the losses suffered from the poisoned water, Skorm decided not to search for the key in the crypts, simply choosing to leave the survivors sealed within.

### Forelhost Crypt

Once in the crypt, make your way to the well. If you can pick the lock, do so, and move on down; otherwise turn right instead and head through the crypt tunnels to the far end, where you'll find the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Forelhost_Well_Key "Skyrim:Forelhost Well Key"). Then head back to the well. After going down the well, follow the tunnels on to the Refectory. You will find a potion on top of a [stand](/wiki/Skyrim:Weight_Sensitive_Plate "Skyrim:Weight Sensitive Plate") with [dart](/wiki/Skyrim:Dart_Trap "Skyrim:Dart Trap") holes behind it - you can drop a wooden plate or something similar on the ground, pick up the potion, and use the plate/object to stop the darts.

### Forelhost Refectory

Once in the Refectory, move through and find the [glass dragon claw](/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Claw "Skyrim:Glass Claw"), which you'll need to open the [puzzle door](/wiki/Skyrim:Puzzle_Door "Skyrim:Puzzle Door") later. Continue on to the door. From top (outer ring) to bottom (inner ring), the solution is: **Fox—Owl—Snake**.

You'll need to defeat the dragon priest [Rahgot](/wiki/Skyrim:Rahgot_(creature) "Skyrim:Rahgot (creature)") and four leveled draugr. Afterwards, loot Rahgot's body. He'll have the [mask](/wiki/Skyrim:Rahgot_(item) "Skyrim:Rahgot (item)"), a [dragon priest staff](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Priest_Staff_(Rahgot) "Skyrim:Dragon Priest Staff (Rahgot)"), and the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Forelhost_Balcony_Key "Skyrim:Forelhost Balcony Key") to the battlements.

### Battlements

Head out onto the battlements to reach a [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall"), and learn a word for the [Storm Call](/wiki/Skyrim:Storm_Call "Skyrim:Storm Call") shout.

After visiting the word wall move near the inside of the battlements; you'll see Captain Valmir talking to an enemy soldier. The objective to return to Valmir remains, so drop down. You'll find that Valmir has now dressed in the armor of the opposing side, to convince this other soldier to do the same job he sent you to do! He's an impostor! Defeat both Valmir and the enemy soldier to conclude the quest. If you wish, loot *[Valmir's Orders](/wiki/Skyrim:Valmir%27s_Orders "Skyrim:Valmir's Orders")* to discover that he was actually an agent (strongly implied, but not explicitly stated, to be [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor")) who was sent to retrieve the mask and return to [Labyrinthian](/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian "Skyrim:Labyrinthian") with it (presumably to use the mask on the matching pedestal in [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Bromjunaar_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Bromjunaar Sanctuary")).

## Notes

- It is possible to ignore Valmir at the beginning of the quest (by ending conversation before agreeing to take the quest) and pickpocket the key to the ruins from him. You'll get an entry in your journal about his quest only when you reach the battlements and overhear his conversation with the second NPC.
- It is also possible to kill Valmir immediately after arriving at this location but before going into the ruins. You can then loot his body for the scrap of paper revealing his treachery.
- Version 1.3.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") delays the conversation between Valmir and the soldier until you're on the ground in the same area with them.

## Bugs

- If the puzzle door does not work, check [here](/wiki/Skyrim:Puzzle_Totems#Bugs "Skyrim:Puzzle Totems") for possible fixes.
- Captain Valmir may attack you on sight.
- If you pickpocket the key and proceed to get the mask, after stepping outside, the quest will initiate with the objective to kill Captain Valmir. The soldier attacks but Valmir does not; you will gain a bounty for killing him but the quest does not complete.
- If the claw is knocked off the pedestal before you pick it up, the metal gate may not lower.
  - Dragging an item back onto the pedestal and removing it again will trigger the gate.
- If you find the Refectory Library gate is raised you cannot proceed.
  - Leave the area and then return, the gates should be open.
- Multiple masks may be found on Rahgot's body.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- Just before you enter the ruin, Valmir will mistakenly refer to finding a staff instead of the mask.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=14181))
- If you Switch sides during the civil War Valmir will wear the first selected faction uniform. And may not be able to talk to the player. **?**
