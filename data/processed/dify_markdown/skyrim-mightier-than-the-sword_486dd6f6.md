## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read [Looter's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Looter%27s_Note "Skyrim:Looter's Note") in [Silver-Blood Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver-Blood_Inn "Skyrim:Silver-Blood Inn") of Markarth
2. Search the tower at [Reachwind Eyrie](/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwind_Eyrie "Skyrim:Reachwind Eyrie").
3. Pick up the leftover loot.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This quest can be started by heading to the [Silver-Blood Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver-Blood_Inn "Skyrim:Silver-Blood Inn") within [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). Go inside and find the *[Looter's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Looter%27s_Note "Skyrim:Looter's Note")* left by a patron upon the bar counter. Now head to [Reachwind Eyrie](/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwind_Eyrie "Skyrim:Reachwind Eyrie"), a Dwemer tower southeast of Markarth and northeast of [Dushnikh Yal](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnikh_Yal "Skyrim:Dushnikh Yal"). Upon arrival, three [Looters](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Looter&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Looter (page does not exist)") will begin attacking. Go inside the tower and go up the steps. At the top will be the body of dead Dark Elf named [Casival](/wiki/Skyrim:Casival "Skyrim:Casival"). Loot him to find a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v9](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v9 "Skyrim:The Crimson Dirks, v9")*. On the table and ground you will find the two pieces of [Dwarven Plate Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven#Dwarven_Plate_Armor "Skyrim:Dwarven") and *[Casival's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Casival%27s_Note "Skyrim:Casival's Note")*.

## Bugs

- The Looter's note may be clipped into the bar. **?**
  - Using a spell that has knockback seems to work. For example, Fire Storm, Elemental Burst and Fireball may work. The illusion spell Harmony also works if you do not want to kill the patrons or make them hostile to you.
- If you have already visited Reachwind Eyrie and picked up the Dwarvern Plate armor, you will not be able to complete the quest.
  - Dropping it back in Reachwind Eyrie before activating the quest prevents this.
  - If you have already activated the quest, are in Reachwind Eyrie, and the Armor is now marked as a quest item try the console command:  `drop xx027800 1`  (where xx is the load order - usually fe) then pick up the armor.
  - Re-acquiring the Dwarven Plate armor in Reachwind Eyrie, through a trade can also fix the quest.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")If you've already sold the Dwarven Plate Armor or the above suggestions do not work, you can force completion by using the console command: `SetStage ccBGSSSE061_quest 1000`
  - Once completed the Dwarven Plate Boots will no longer be marked as quest items.
- Looters and bandit around and in the Reachwind Eyrie may not spawn upon reaching the area or heading into the tower making it impossible to complete the quest. **?**
- The two quest armor pieces have strange weight behaviors. At one lb. under max weight, picking up the Plate Armor caused no apparent change in weight carried. Picking up the Boots caused the weight from the Armor to be "noticed", but despite now being 44 lbs. overweight, movement speed was unaffected. **?**
