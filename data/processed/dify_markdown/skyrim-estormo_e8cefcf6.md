Estormo

**Estormo** is a [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor") agent reporting to [Ancano](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancano "Skyrim:Ancano"). He will confront you at the end of [Labyrinthian](/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian "Skyrim:Labyrinthian") during the related quest. When approached, he will converse with you briefly. Shortly afterward he will attack regardless of your response.

Estormo wears [hooded Thalmor robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Thalmor_Robes "Skyrim:Hooded Thalmor Robes"), [Thalmor gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Gloves "Skyrim:Thalmor Gloves"), and [Thalmor boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Boots "Skyrim:Thalmor Boots"). He knows a number of spells, namely [Stoneflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Stoneflesh "Skyrim:Stoneflesh"), [Conjure Flame Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Flame_Atronach "Skyrim:Conjure Flame Atronach"), [Chain Lightning](/wiki/Skyrim:Chain_Lightning "Skyrim:Chain Lightning"), [Flame Cloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Cloak "Skyrim:Flame Cloak"), [Lightning Bolt](/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Bolt "Skyrim:Lightning Bolt"), [Fast Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Healing "Skyrim:Fast Healing"), and [Steadfast Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Steadfast_Ward "Skyrim:Steadfast Ward").

### The Staff of Magnus

He will barge through the metal doors to confront you and block the only exit, saying:

:   *"So, you made it out of there alive. Ancano was right... you are dangerous. I'm afraid I'll have to take that Staff from you now. Ancano wants it kept safe... oh, and he wants you dead. Nothing personal."*

:   :   **Get out of my way.**
    :   *"You fool, you don't stand a chance."*

:   :   **I have no quarrel with you.**
    :   *"I am afraid you do."*

After this he will become hostile.

## Bugs

- Due to his low agression, if you sheathe your weapon, he will accept your yield.
