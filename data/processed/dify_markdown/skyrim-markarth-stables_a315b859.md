The Markarth Stables

**Markarth Stables** is a [stable](/wiki/Skyrim:Stables "Skyrim:Stables") located outside [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"), opposite the entrance.

A black and white [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses") may be purchased here for 1000 [Septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") from [Cedran](/wiki/Skyrim:Cedran "Skyrim:Cedran") and a war [dog](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog "Skyrim:Dog") [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") named [Vigilance](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilance "Skyrim:Vigilance") for 500 septims from [Banning](/wiki/Skyrim:Banning "Skyrim:Banning"). They both live at the stables along with Vigilance unless you have bought him.

### Exterior

There is a row of pillars in front of the building supporting a stone porch area where the horses that are for sale are kept, there are several bales of straw for them to used for bedding. To the northwest and also behind the building to the northeast are two [Juniper Berry Trees](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") and to the southeast is a lookout tower manned by a [Markarth Guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth_Guard "Skyrim:Markarth Guard") with flights of stairs leading up to it. During the day one or both of [Cedran](/wiki/Skyrim:Cedran "Skyrim:Cedran") and [Banning](/wiki/Skyrim:Banning "Skyrim:Banning"), along with [Vigilance](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilance "Skyrim:Vigilance") will be stood leaning against or near the pillars out front. The entrance is normally novice-locked.

### Markarth Stables

Through the door is a flight of stairs down to the east. There are stone shelves on either side at the bottom with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") and a bucket containing a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") on top of the shelf on the left and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") is on top of the shelf to the right. There is a doorway in front of you leading into the single-roomed dwelling. There is a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") on a block of wood on the left and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand beside the north wall, with a leather strip on a shelf beside that. To the right, still against the north wall is a bucket containing a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and a kettle further to the right against the same wall containing two cabbages and a potato, with a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), a [carrot](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot") and two potatoes (one in a basket) on a wall shelf on the north wall.

Coming out from the same wall is a long wooden table with two chairs. On the table is a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a sheaf of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") and several pieces of cheese. To the east there is a large wall shelf above several bales of straw with a three [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and a basket containing two cabbages and a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"). To the south is a raised section with three stairs up to two owned single beds. To the right of the stairs is a round wooden table and chair, with two loaves of bread and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge") on top of the table, a food sack on top of a crate beside it on one side and a mead barrel on the floor of the raised section on the other side. There is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") on the raised section between the table and wall. One of the beds is along the east wall, while the other is along the south wall. On a wall shelf by the first bed is the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger") and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), with a Crate above the headboard with an apprentice-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") on top and a food sack and leather strip on a block of wood beside the crate and another food sack with a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat") on another crate by the foot of the bed. There is a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide") under the second bed.

## Notes

- Once you have completed a delivery for Banning you may freely take most items from inside the Stables. If you have also completed the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Taste_of_Death "Skyrim:The Taste of Death") to his satisfaction, you can even break in and use [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") to break into the strongbox and take all the gold and Banning will not complain or accuse you of trespassing or stealing.
- If either Cedran or Banning dies - either can die during a dragon attack, or Banning can die in [The Taste of Death](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Taste_of_Death "Skyrim:The Taste of Death") if the player chooses to turn against the dinner guests - then the survivor will NOT take over his mate's services: you cannot end up buying a dog from Cedran or a horse from Banning.

## Bugs

- The player horse here has an improper enable parent setting that can interfere with Civil War handling.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. The enable parent setting on the horse has been removed.
