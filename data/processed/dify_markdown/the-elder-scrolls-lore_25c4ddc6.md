# The Elder Scrolls Lore

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg/400px-MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg)](/wiki/File:MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg)

The most recent and authoritative map of Tamriel, from *[The Elder Scrolls Anthology](/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls_Anthology "General:The Elder Scrolls Anthology")*

:   *For guidelines on the Lore section, see [UESPWiki:Lore](/wiki/UESPWiki:Lore "UESPWiki:Lore").*

The events of *[The Elder Scrolls](/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls "General:The Elder Scrolls")* unfold on the vast continent of [Tamriel](/wiki/Lore:Tamriel "Lore:Tamriel"), located on the planet of [Nirn](/wiki/Lore:Nirn "Lore:Nirn") (one of the [Planes of Existence](/wiki/Lore:Planes_of_Existence "Lore:Planes of Existence")) in the realm of [Mundus](/wiki/Lore:Mundus "Lore:Mundus"). [Aldmeris](/wiki/Lore:Aldmeris_(language) "Lore:Aldmeris (language)") for "*Dawn's Beauty*", Tamriel is home to nine distinct provinces: [Black Marsh](/wiki/Lore:Black_Marsh "Lore:Black Marsh"), [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil"), [Elsweyr](/wiki/Lore:Elsweyr "Lore:Elsweyr"), [Hammerfell](/wiki/Lore:Hammerfell "Lore:Hammerfell"), [High Rock](/wiki/Lore:High_Rock "Lore:High Rock"), [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind"), [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), [Summerset Isle](/wiki/Lore:Summerset_Isle "Lore:Summerset Isle"), and [Valenwood](/wiki/Lore:Valenwood "Lore:Valenwood").
