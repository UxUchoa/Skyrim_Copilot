Windpeak Inn

**Windpeak Inn** is an inn in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"). It is the first place you'll pass coming off the road into Dawnstar. [Thoring](/wiki/Skyrim:Thoring "Skyrim:Thoring") runs the inn, rents rooms, and sells typical innkeeper merchandise ([food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") and [drinks](/wiki/Skyrim:Beverages "Skyrim:Beverages")). His daughter, [Karita](/wiki/Skyrim:Karita_(bard) "Skyrim:Karita (bard)"), is the inn's bard, and takes over renting rooms and selling food and drinks if her father dies. [Abelone](/wiki/Skyrim:Abelone "Skyrim:Abelone") and [Erandur](/wiki/Skyrim:Erandur "Skyrim:Erandur") also live here. Erandur only lives here until you start the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Waking_Nightmare "Skyrim:Waking Nightmare") and you agree to accompany him.

### Exterior

There are two sets of steps leading up to the partially covered porch; to the left of the main set are two [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes and two food barrels. To the right of the building there is a small farm plot with a small lean-to housing a chicken's nest that contains a [chicken's egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") next to a long wooden table with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") on top. Also, just beyond the fence there is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block").

### Windpeak Inn

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/SR-interior-Windpeak_Inn.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Windpeak_Inn.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Windpeak_Inn.jpg)

Windpeak inn

When you first enter the place Karita, [Fruki](/wiki/Skyrim:Fruki "Skyrim:Fruki") and [Irgnir](/wiki/Skyrim:Irgnir "Skyrim:Irgnir") are having a conversation about the nightmares occurring in Dawnstar, while Erandur tries to explain things and calm them down. You enter into the main common room with a central firepit and five long wooden benches, two against the north wall and three against the south. Four of the table hold several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and a couple of bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") or [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") or both. The end table against the south wall also has two chicken's eggs on it and the table on your right, against the north wall has a selection of cheese pieces and two [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") on it. Along the west wall are several barrels and a pile of firewood. Against this wall is normally where [Alesan](/wiki/Skyrim:Alesan "Skyrim:Alesan") sleeps on the floor.

At the eastern end of the room is a counter with several pieces of cheese, a bottle of wine and a bottle of alto wine on top. Under the counter are fourteen bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), six bottles of wine, two bottles of alto wine, a [steel warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Warhammer "Skyrim:Steel Warhammer"), three small [coin purses](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). Along the wall behind the counter are a row of vats and several barrels of foodstuffs. There are three hanging racks holding ten [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and six [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") as well as two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, a bunch of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") all hanging from the ceiling.

There are two doorways to bedrooms on the north wall and one on the south wall that is much larger. The one on the south wall is the room you are shown to if you rent a room. It contains a single bed in the southeast corner, against the east wall. To the right of this are two dressers containing [clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing"). The dresser on the left holds two bottles of alto wine, a [tankard](/wiki/Skyrim:Tankard "Skyrim:Tankard"), a lit candle, and a copy of [The Cabin in the Woods](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cabin_in_the_Woods "Skyrim:The Cabin in the Woods"). Between both dressers is a bucket holding a broom. Against the west wall are a pile of food sacks and a long unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). Next to the north wall is a chair next to a square wooden table holding a lit candle, a tankard, a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl"), and a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"). There are three [baskets](/wiki/Skyrim:Basket "Skyrim:Basket") between the chest and the chair, and one more just to the right of the square table. You can take everything from this room for free, except for the broom and bucket. On either side of the doorway to the large bedroom are two sets of shelves holding three bunches of elves ear, seven bottles of Nord mead, four loaves of bread, two bottles of alto wine and six bottles of wine. The set of shelves on the left side of the doorway also has a barrel next to it.

The left-hand bedroom on the north side consists of a single owned bed in the northwest corner, against the west wall, with a table and chair to the right of it. On the table is a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") and a copy of [Song of the Askelde Men](/wiki/Skyrim:Song_of_the_Askelde_Men "Skyrim:Song of the Askelde Men"). At the foot of the bed is an unlocked chest and between this and the doorway is a wardrobe also against the west wall containing clothes. The right-hand bedroom is quite similar except the single owned bed is in the northeast corner and along the east wall, with an unlocked chest at the foot of the bed, but with a dresser containing clothes between this and the doorway. To the left of the bed is a table and chair. On the table is a copy of [A Dream of Sovngarde](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dream_of_Sovngarde "Skyrim:A Dream of Sovngarde").

## Notes

- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide")* describes the building as follows:

:   *"Windpeak Inn is Dawnstar's only tavern and is run by Thoring and his young daughter Karita, who both live in the building. Karita serves as a bard, and their servant Abelone handles most of the menial chores, chopping wood or using the grindstone in the pen outside. Currently, the place is a meeting spot where anxious townsfolk (such as Fruki and Irgnir the miners) gather to gossip and worry about the strange nightmares that have been keeping them from their beds."*

- You can find a wooden bowl and a tankard outside one of the guestroom walls, but you'll have to clip through it to gain access.

## Bugs

- Although Thoring is supposed to provide innkeeper merchant services, he was not added to the `ServicesDawnstarWindpeakInn` faction, nor was anyone else. Therefore it is not possible to purchase food and drink at this inn.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
- NPCs standing between the bar stools will not be able to move.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.0, fixes this bug.
- After you finish "Rise in the East", [Stig Salt-Plank](/wiki/Skyrim:Stig_Salt-Plank "Skyrim:Stig Salt-Plank") and his men may permanently remain here, and only say general quotes if you talk to them. **?**
