Haknir's Shoal

**Haknir's Shoal** is a small [pirate](/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate "Skyrim:Pirate") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Camps "Skyrim:Camps") north-northwest of [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

## Related Quests

- **[Deathbrand](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(quest) "Skyrim:Deathbrand (quest)")**: Find the treasure of [Haknir Death-Brand](/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir_Death-Brand "Skyrim:Haknir Death-Brand").
- **[Pain in the Necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Pain_in_the_Necklace "Skyrim:Pain in the Necklace")**: Find [East Empire pendants](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Pendant "Skyrim:East Empire Pendant") for [Fethis Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fethis_Alor "Skyrim:Fethis Alor").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_02.jpg)

There be pirates!

The three pirates, comprising a [pirate](/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate "Skyrim:Pirate"), a [pirate mage](/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_Mage "Skyrim:Pirate Mage"), and their [pirate captain](/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_Captain "Skyrim:Pirate Captain"), will attack as soon as they detect you. Once you have taken care of them, you may loot the pirate captain for the [Deathbrand Treasure Map](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_Treasure_Map "Skyrim:Deathbrand Treasure Map"). Obtaining the map will complete the miscellaneous quest to search for the treasure of [Haknir Death-Brand](/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir_Death-Brand "Skyrim:Haknir Death-Brand") and initiate the quest [Deathbrand](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(quest) "Skyrim:Deathbrand (quest)").

A campfire is in the middle of the camp, with a wooden plate holding a [venison chop](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison_Chop "Skyrim:Venison Chop") beside the fire. There are three tents pitched around the fire. The tent nearest the small boat contains a bedroll only, and to the left of the tent is a firewood pile, a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). The middle tent contains a bedroll and a wooden plate holding a [grilled chicken breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Chicken_Breast "Skyrim:Grilled Chicken Breast") and a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), while at the back of the tent is an expert-locked [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox"). To the west of this tent is a mead barrel on a rock with a random potion, three sacks, and a random healing potion beside it. The tent nearest land contains a bedroll, a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), a pair of random light [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor"), and two food sacks. Outside this tent on the left is a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack").

The treasure

To the northwest of the campfire is an expert-locked ancient chest containing the [Deathbrand Helm](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_Helm "Skyrim:Deathbrand Helm"). There are two [orichalcum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore Vein") in the area: the first is west-northwest of the ancient chest, and the other is farther north at the tip of the isle. Scattered around the isle are nine [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Ashen Grass Pod") plants, a [Nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") plant, three [pearl oysters](/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl_Oyster "Skyrim:Pearl Oyster"), and to the northeast a [clam](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam "Skyrim:Clam") that can be harvested for [clam meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat "Skyrim:Clam Meat"). There is a small boat at the edge of the water just northeast of the fire, with a random [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"), two crates, four sacks, and a random [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace") inside.

On the shoreline opposite the shoal are more spiky grass plants, two more Nirnroot plants, and a [horker](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker "Skyrim:Horker").
