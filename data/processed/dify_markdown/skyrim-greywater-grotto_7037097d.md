Greywater Grotto

**Greywater Grotto** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") east of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") containing leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals"). It contains only one zone, *[Greywater Grotto](#Greywater_Grotto)*.

## Related Quests

- **[Animal Extermination](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Extermination_(B) "Skyrim:Animal Extermination (B)")**: Fulfill a contract given to the [Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions") to clear out a beast den. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

### Exterior

There is a leveled animal guarding the entrance, which is covered in splattered blood and gore, with a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") lying on the ground among it. There are also a number of [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes in the vicinity and an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") above the entrance, accessed by heading west then ascending back to the east.

### Greywater Grotto

The cave is a long descending corridor following a stream down to a shallow pool. The animals in the cave are leveled, and seem to be entirely random. You are as likely to encounter [wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf") as you are to encounter [snowy sabre cats](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowy_Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Snowy Sabre Cat"), [snow bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear "Skyrim:Snow Bear"), or [ice wraiths](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith"), depending on your level. The cave itself is entirely natural in appearance, and descends in a series of plateaus with earthen ramps linking them.

Upon entering, there is a dead [deer](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer "Skyrim:Deer") in front on a ledge, with a ramp down to the left and an iron ore vein beneath it. Partway down, there is a wellspring that has formed a partially frozen pool that feeds a stream flowing to the south, with a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") to the north. To the right, the ground rises, with a couple more animals found at the point before the cave continues to descend, passing a dead [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(dead) "Skyrim:Bandit (dead)") near the west wall. A short way farther south, the stream forms a waterfall, with the path becoming a ledge along the west wall. Near the bottom, the path crosses below the waterfall as a bridge over another partially frozen pool. Three more animals are found at the bottom next to an unlocked wooden [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), with a second dead bandit and a medium coin purse on the ground among more human debris. There is a [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") against the south wall of the cave. There are no plants or fungi anywhere inside the cave.

## Notes

- Whenever there are different animals present, it is possible you will only find the victors of any fights alive and the carcasses of the losers.
- In [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
