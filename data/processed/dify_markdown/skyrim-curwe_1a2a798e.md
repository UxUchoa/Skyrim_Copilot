**Curwe** is a [High Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Elf "Skyrim:High Elf") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") and the wife of [Vantus Loreius](/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius "Skyrim:Vantus Loreius"). She lives with him on [their farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Loreius_Farm "Skyrim:Loreius Farm"). If you alert the guards to Cicero's activities during [Delayed Burial](/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial "Skyrim:Delayed Burial"), then Cicero will kill Curwe and her husband.

She wakes up every morning at 7am and starts the day with a one-hour breakfast. At 8am she heads outside and starts working the land, at times sweeping the porch. At 7pm she takes a one-hour dinner before she goes to bed early at 8pm.

Curwe wears a set of green farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots"). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Loreius_Famhouse "Skyrim:Key to Loreius Famhouse") to the farmhouse, as well as a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

After betraying [Cicero](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero "Skyrim:Cicero") during [Delayed Burial](/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial "Skyrim:Delayed Burial"), the corpse in Loreius Farm uses a different form, the refid is 0007f42c, the baseid is 0007f42a.

## Dialogue

Attempting to speak to her will result in only one response:

:   *"You need something talk to my husband. Vantus Loreius. Or just Loreius, if it's all the same to you."*

## Conversations

While she doesn't talk much to strangers, she will often debate with her husband:

:   **Curwe**: *"You know, I was thinking of expanding the herb garden. Make room for some new plants I'm planning."*
:   **Vantus**: *"That should be all right. How much space were you planning?"*
:   **Curwe**: *"Oh, half the field should do."*
:   **Vantus**: *"Half the field? What could need half the field?"*
:   **Curwe**: *"I just think pollinating would be easier if I could do a lot of plants at once."*
:   **Vantus**: *"Uh... why don't we talk about this later?"*

:   **Curwe**: *"I had a look at that boneyard the other day, Vantus. The one to the Northwest."*
:   **Vantus**: *"I told you I don't like you going there. I've seen scavengers picking at the mammoths. They could be dangerous."*
