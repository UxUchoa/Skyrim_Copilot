Karthwasten Hall

**Karthwasten Hall** is a farmhouse in the southeastern part of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten"), located next to [Enmon's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon%27s_House "Skyrim:Enmon's House").

It is the home of [Ainethach](/wiki/Skyrim:Ainethach "Skyrim:Ainethach"), the owner of Karthwasten's mines. It consists of one zone, *[Karthwasten Hall](#Karthwasten_Hall)*.

## Karthwasten Hall

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall.jpg)

Ground floor

The front door opens to a bedroom area on the right and a kitchen and dining area to the left. Immediately to the right is a single bed (belonging to the [KarthwastenAinethachHouse](/wiki/Skyrim:KarthwastenAinethachHouse "Skyrim:KarthwastenAinethachHouse") faction) in the northwest corner. Farther along the west wall is a small table holding five gold coins, five [silver ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot"), and a medium-sized [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). A non-respawning wardrobe stands along the south wall nearby, next to a woven basket containing two [potions of vigor](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor "Skyrim:Potion of Vigor"). A double bed, owned by the same faction, is situated to the left, with a small non-respawning chest at its foot. To the left of the bed is the home's fireplace, with a [steel mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Steel Mace") on its mantel and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") at its base. A hanging rack with three freshly caught [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") can be found just to the left of the fireplace.

The east side of the north wall hosts the dining and food storage area, starting with a large dining table. The table is set with two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), one bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), three [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a serving of [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). On the floor next to the table is a woven basket containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), and a set of shelves which hold two more leeks, three potatoes, and a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulb, as well as some [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), and a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") sitting on top. Above the shelves are several hanging ingredients, including two garlic braids, three portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and some dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). A respawning food barrel can be found in the northeast corner.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall_02.jpg)

Basement

Heading downstairs will lead to the basement, which is relatively bare. A garlic braid and one portion each of dried frost mirriam and dried elves ear are hanging above a small round table which holds a single loaf of bread. A set of shelves on the south wall holds various kitchenware, and two respawning food barrels sit to the right. A training dummy stands near the center of the west wall with a non-respawning large sack nearby. A long work table sits against the north wall near the stairs, holding nothing except a medium-sized coin purse, and another respawning food barrel rests to the right.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Karthwasten_Hall "Skyrim:Key to Karthwasten Hall") to which is carried by [Ainethach](/wiki/Skyrim:Ainethach "Skyrim:Ainethach").
- Two respawning food barrels can be found next to the steps to the porch, which hosts a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning "Skyrim:Tanning"), six more respawning food barrels, and a table holding four bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread").
- If you [marry](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") Ainethach, you will have the option of living here.
