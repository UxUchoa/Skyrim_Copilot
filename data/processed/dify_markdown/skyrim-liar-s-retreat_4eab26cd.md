Liar's Retreat

**Liar's Retreat** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") and connected underground cellar northeast of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten") containing [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") and invading [Falmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer "Skyrim:Falmer"). It contains only one zone, *[Liar's Retreat](#Liar.27s_Retreat)*.

Apparently, until very recently this used to be the site of a bandit bar run by a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") named [Rahd](/wiki/Skyrim:Rahd "Skyrim:Rahd"), who is known as "Longhammer" due to a unique weapon he carries. Falmer have tunneled in and are attacking the last of the occupants when you arrive for the first time.

## Related Quests

- **[Liar's Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Liar%27s_Retreat_(quest) "Skyrim:Liar's Retreat (quest)")**: Retrieve [the Longhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Longhammer "Skyrim:The Longhammer") from a fallen [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rahd "Skyrim:Rahd").

### Exterior

The entrance is marked by a cairn. Just below the first cairn is a corundum ore vein. Two lit braziers are on either side of an unlocked wooden door that has been set into the rock face. There is a leveled beast that may attack you.

#### Dining Hall

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat.jpg)

Liar's Retreat interior

Upon entering, there are stairs descending to a balcony overlooking the main bar area. You'll witness a raid of Falmer finishing off the last of the bandit residents. Descend the stairs to the main bar area to find everything in an uproar. Overturned tables and chairs, the corpses of bandits, a burnt corpse in the fire to the left, and blood and gore everywhere show the ferocity of the attack. There are seven leveled Falmer, including two magic-users, in this area including one hidden in a bedroom and another in the kitchen, which can lead to a challenging fight for the unwary. The Falmer have tunneled their way from deeper nests which contain more Falmer, a [chaurus](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus "Skyrim:Chaurus") (replaced with a chaurus hunter if *[Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard")* is installed), and some [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider").

There are the bodies of six bandits including one that appears to be a bard still holding his [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") and one behind the bar that appears to be acting as a serving girl. Around the room are two [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), three [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish") steaks, two portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), and several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and pieces of cheese. Against the southeast wall is a food barrel. A [Falmer war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_War_Axe "Skyrim:Falmer War Axe") has been chopped into the counter, while under it are a random [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow"), an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), two leveled [poisons](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison"), and a leveled [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"). Behind the counter is a food barrel and along the rear wall are three sets of shelves and a cupboard. The cupboard only holds clutter, while the shelves hold two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a loaf of bread, two bottles of [skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma"), two food barrels, and two mead barrels, and hanging from one of the shelves is a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam").

#### Sleeping Quarters

From the northeast corridor you can hear a bandit that is still alive talking to himself. There are three bedrooms in this corridor; the first of these has a novice lock and has been barricaded by the bandit. He will immediately attack as soon as you open the door. The barricade is made up of many baskets and buckets. The room contains two unowned single beds, a table and chairs in the western corner, and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") against the rear wall. A cupboard in the eastern corner holds a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, a pair of random [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets "Skyrim:Gauntlets"), and a random [potion of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"). By the foot of one of the beds is a pair of [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots"), and one of the baskets contains a cabbage, a bulb of garlic, and a piece of bread. On the table are a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold").

The middle bedroom contains a Falmer, unless they joined the fight in the main bar area. It appears to be a communal room containing six beds, two of which are now unusable. The bodies of another three bandits can be found in this room, and along the rear wall are three bookcases containing seven common books, many [ruined books](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book "Skyrim:Ruined Book"), a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum"), a random [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"), and on top of the last of these is a [cow hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow_Hide "Skyrim:Cow Hide"). On the floor are another couple of common books and a random [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword") as well as plenty of blood and gore. The last bedroom has also been partially trashed by the Falmer, with a bookcase knocked over and clutter spread over the floor with a couple of common books among the carnage. The room contains an unowned single bed in the eastern corner, an adept-locked chest against the rear wall, and a long wooden table by the door with a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and three common books on top. At the foot of the bed is a pair of [fine boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots "Skyrim:Fine Boots"), and under it is a [potion of vigor](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor "Skyrim:Potion of Vigor"), which can be most easily retrieved with the use of [telekinesis](/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis "Skyrim:Telekinesis"). A stone shelf on the far side of the room holds a copy of the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Biography of the Wolf Queen](/wiki/Skyrim:Biography_of_the_Wolf_Queen "Skyrim:Biography of the Wolf Queen")*, along with a helmet and several potions.

#### Kitchen

Behind the bar in the main room, a hole in the wall leads to the kitchen, where another Falmer waits unless it had also been alerted by the fighting. The kitchen area contains a sideboard against the northwest wall with a table beside it coming out from the wall. There are four bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a random potion of healing, and a random [battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe "Skyrim:Battleaxe") on top. Against the northeast wall is a large fireplace with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") in front, while on the right-hand side of the hearth are a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") leaning against a pile of firewood and a small coin purse hidden behind an empty bucket in the corner (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

Along the southeast wall are a sideboard with two [salt piles](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") on top and a set of shelves to the right holding two [rock warbler eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), a [spider egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Egg "Skyrim:Spider Egg"), four [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), and another woodcutter's axe. In front of this on the floor is a cast iron pot containing a [slaughterfish egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg"), two carrots, and a bulb of garlic. In the southern corner is a brewing vat with a cupboard and set of shelves to the right, along the southwest wall; they hold two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and some cheese. Overhead, hanging from two beams are three bunches of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), four [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), a garlic braid, and a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), while in the middle of the room is a table with a baked potato, a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), a fillet of [salmon meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat "Skyrim:Salmon Meat"), a piece of cheese, and a [red mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") on top. The kitchen also connects to the corridor with the three bedrooms via a wooden door in the northwest wall.

#### Spider Tunnels

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_03.jpg)

Spider tunnels

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_04.jpg)

Falmer tent

As you pass through the door to the northwest, you find the hole where the Falmer broke through to mount the attack; ahead is the fire from a dropped [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch") beside the body of another dead bandit. The tunnel takes a couple turns and passes a [glowing mushroom](/wiki/Skyrim:Glowing_Mushroom "Skyrim:Glowing Mushroom") growing on the wall, before opening out into a cavern covered with spiderwebs. There is a frostbite spider by the entrance, with a Falmer off to the right that will investigate any fighting. A couple more spiders may drop from the ceiling as well. Around the corner, to the southwest, is a Falmer tent containing an apprentice-locked Falmer chest and a random potion of healing. Next to this is a narrow tunnel that twists and turns with several [chaurus egg sacs](/wiki/Skyrim:Egg_Sac "Skyrim:Egg Sac"), numerous glowing mushrooms, and several types of fungi growing in patches throughout.

At the end of the tunnel is a leveled Falmer waiting on a ledge. Beyond is a ramp heading up and to the west, with a dormant Falmer hive on the wall to the right. There are two [corundom ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") at the base of the ramp. However, mining these before clearing the cave system will alert several Falmer and a bandit to your presence. There are a glowing mushroom, a [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") fungus, a [human heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Human_Heart "Skyrim:Human Heart"), a random [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), and a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poisons "Skyrim:Poisons") scattered on the ground by the ore veins. As you climb the ramp there is a rock on the left, by a pillar, with a random potion of healing, two [skeever tails](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever_Tail "Skyrim:Skeever Tail"), a [Falmer ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Ear "Skyrim:Falmer Ear"), and another bleeding crown fungus on top. To the right is an unlocked Falmer chest with a random potion of healing beside it.

#### Chaurus Den

At the top of the ramp, the path descends again to the northwest to an area with a leveled Falmer and two pens. Each pen contains the body of a dead bandit, while the pen on the right also has two living bandits trapped inside who attempt to escape.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Liar%27s_Retreat_02.jpg)

Altar with Rahd

As the path starts to rise to the north there is another leveled Falmer standing guard with two more nearby that can easily be alerted by any fighting. There is a third corundum ore vein on the left where the path turns to the east. As you descend there is some carved stonework on the left, and a Dwemer piston is pumping above. As the path turns back to the northeast, there is an open Falmer gate leading into a large circular cave, partially fenced in with Dwemer metal work. A leveled chaurus is off to the right, and a Falmer tent with a leveled Falmer standing inside is to the left. In the center of the room, on an altar, is Rahd's body. Next to him, or slightly underneath the altar, is [the Longhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Longhammer "Skyrim:The Longhammer"), a unique [warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer "Skyrim:Warhammer") notable for its 30% faster swing speed. In the rear are two more Falmer tents. An unlocked Dwemer chest is next to the tent on the left, hidden among some vegetation. The tent on the right has a Falmer bow on the ground. On the north wall, there is a flat rock with a [Falmer shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Shield "Skyrim:Falmer Shield"), a [Falmer bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Bow "Skyrim:Falmer Bow"), two skeever tails, and three glowing mushroom samples next to the body of a dead chaurus. There is an unlocked Falmer chest to the right with three loose [chaurus eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Eggs "Skyrim:Chaurus Eggs") on the ground. Behind the Dwemer fence is an area full of chaurus egg sacs and the gory remains of a human. A ramp is also behind the fencing, leading up to a fourth Falmer tent.

There is no shortcut back to the bar, so you need to retrace your steps. When you leave the tunnels, returning to the bar area after clearing the cave, you will be confronted by some aggressive bandits that are just entering the bar. Among them is a bandit chief who remarks, *"Hey, Longhammer, set us up with a round of... what in Oblivion happened in here?"*, indicating Rahd was probably the barkeep of the establishment before the Falmer attack.

## Notes

- There is an additional corundum ore vein outside the cave to the south down the cliff.
- While the Falmer will respawn, the bandits may not. Occasionally, on return visits, bandits may be heard fighting Falmer deeper in the cave, and their corpses can be found as far in as the boss cave. Also, a live bandit may appear in the fenced-off area just before the final room, but they will only act as a generic enemy.
- Liar's Retreat and [Volkihar Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Keep "Skyrim:Volkihar Keep") are the only two places in the game where you can obtain the semi-rare [tavern clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000d191f "Skyrim:Clothing") without killing or stealing, as the outfit is not sold by any merchants.

## Bugs

- You may not be able to interact with the coin purse in the kitchen. However, if you are a crossbow user and have explosive bolts with you, firing any such bolt at the coin purse will cause it to vanish; it may often reappear (rather bizarrely) at the top of the steps leading up to the main entrance door of the dungeon. It can also appear randomly in any of the passages deeper in the dungeon.
- The Longhammer may be under the table, not on it. It also may have fallen off the table and be near the gate through which you entered the room.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- If you clear the dungeon without taking the Longhammer, it will be repopulated with Falmer, but the warhammer and Rahd's body may be missing.
- If you grab the Longhammer without killing the Falmer boss (thus not clearing the dungeon), everything in the cave will respawn after a few days, including the Longhammer. This allows you to obtain multiple copies of the weapon as long the location is not marked as cleared. **?**
