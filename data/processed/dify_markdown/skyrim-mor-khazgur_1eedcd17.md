Mor Khazgur

**Mor Khazgur** is an [Orc stronghold](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") in the far northwest of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), west of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") and north of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth").

The stronghold contains three zones: *[Larak's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Larak%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Larak's Longhouse")*, *[Mor Khazgur Cellar](/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Cellar "Skyrim:Mor Khazgur Cellar")*, and *[Mor Khazgur Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Mine "Skyrim:Mor Khazgur Mine")*.

The stronghold owns and operates an [orichalcum](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum "Skyrim:Orichalcum") mine containing seven [ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore Vein"). Notable NPCs found here include [Chief Larak](/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Larak "Skyrim:Chief Larak"), a master [trainer](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers") in the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") skill, and [Borgakh the Steel Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart "Skyrim:Borgakh the Steel Heart"), a potential [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") and potential [Blade](/wiki/Skyrim:Blades "Skyrim:Blades").

As with all Orc Strongholds, you must become [Blood-Kin](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood-Kin "Skyrim:Blood-Kin") to the [Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") before you are welcome in the stronghold and able to barter or receive other services.

## Walkthrough

There is just a single entrance to the stronghold's compound near the northern edge of the surrounding palisade. There is a small vegetable patch immediately on your right upon entering, which contains eleven [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), eleven [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and eight [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") plants. On the left is a lookout tower, while in front is a tree stump that has a [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") fungus growing from it. Behind the vegetables is an open-sided hut, beneath which is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") and a rack hanging from the ceiling holding a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid and [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant").

The path leads past the [longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Larak%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Larak's Longhouse"), passing a wooden table and chair on the right where [Chief Larak](/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Larak "Skyrim:Chief Larak") is generally found sitting. Beside this is a rack holding three whole [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"). On the other side of the entrance to the longhouse is a practice dummy where [Borgakh the Steel Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart "Skyrim:Borgakh the Steel Heart") is normally found training her skill-at-arms. Opposite the longhouse entrance is a second open-sided hut with an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") beside it and a set of shelves under it. These hold an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), two random [potions of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"), a sample of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") and a sample of [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap") fungi, two [purple](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") and a [red mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower").

Continuing past the longhouse there is a covered stack of food barrels on the right with a trapdoor leading to [Mor Khazgur Cellar](/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Cellar "Skyrim:Mor Khazgur Cellar") beside them. On the left is a second lookout tower, while ahead is a covered blacksmithing area consisting of a [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") and [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), with a set of shelves at the rear holding a random enchanted [Orcish battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Orcish Battleaxe"), a copy of the [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Armorer's Challenge](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Armorer%27s_Challenge "Skyrim:The Armorer's Challenge")*, three owned [orichalcum ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ingot "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ingot"), a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword"), an enchanted [Orcish sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Sword "Skyrim:Orcish Sword") and an [Orcish helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Helmet "Skyrim:Orcish Helmet") and [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Shield "Skyrim:Orcish Shield").

The path continues up and around, passing a second tree stump with mora tapinella fungus growing on it. Further up it passes between another lookout tower and a [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") to the top where [Mor Khazgur Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Mine "Skyrim:Mor Khazgur Mine") is located. On the right of the entrance are some more food barrels and three non-respawning orichalcum ingots.
