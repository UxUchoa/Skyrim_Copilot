Skaal Village

**Skaal Village** is a [village](/wiki/Skyrim:Towns "Skyrim:Towns") on [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim").

The village is home to the [Skaal tribe](/wiki/Lore:Skaal "Lore:Skaal"), who strive to live in harmony with nature in accordance with their worship of the [All-Maker](/wiki/Lore:All-Maker "Lore:All-Maker"). They live a simple existence in the frozen north of Solstheim, subsisting on hunting and minor agriculture, but are also familiar with the secrets of forging [stalhrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim "Skyrim:Stalhrim").

The village's eight buildings circle the central well, with a communal [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") and a meat preparation shed sheltering a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on either side of the well. All other crafting stations are found outside the houses of [Baldor Iron-Shaper](/wiki/Skyrim:Baldor_Iron-Shaper "Skyrim:Baldor Iron-Shaper"), [Deor Woodcutter](/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter "Skyrim:Deor Woodcutter"), and [Edla](/wiki/Skyrim:Edla "Skyrim:Edla"). Chief [Fanari Strong-Voice](/wiki/Skyrim:Fanari_Strong-Voice "Skyrim:Fanari Strong-Voice")'s imposing greathall and shaman [Storn Crag-Strider](/wiki/Skyrim:Storn_Crag-Strider "Skyrim:Storn Crag-Strider")'s more modest hut are at the east end of the village, while the remaining residents' homes are to the west and north.

## Notes

- Next to the village cooking pot hangs a unique [dead horker](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_(dead) "Skyrim:Horker (dead)").
- Next to the village cooking pot is an unusable bedroll. The closest places to sleep are [Haknir's Shoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir%27s_Shoal "Skyrim:Haknir's Shoal") to the north-northwest, [Bujold's Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Bujold%27s_Retreat "Skyrim:Bujold's Retreat") to the south-southeast, or [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall") to the south-southwest, depending on your progress in the quests [Retaking Thirsk](/wiki/Skyrim:Retaking_Thirsk "Skyrim:Retaking Thirsk") or [The Chief of Thirsk Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Chief_of_Thirsk_Hall "Skyrim:The Chief of Thirsk Hall").
- Skaal Village also [appears](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Skaal_Village "Bloodmoon:Skaal Village") in *[Bloodmoon](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon "Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon")*. In *Bloodmoon*, the village has a unique architectural style similar to Thirsk. However, in *Dragonborn*, Thirsk still looks visually distinct, while the buildings in Skaal Village are repurposed from [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), albeit with an added layer of snow on the roofs.
- This wiki's current Skyrim satellite map displays Skaal Village in regular its state; the previous Solstheim satellite map displayed Skaal Village the way it did at the beginning of the *Dragonborn* storyline.
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch") marks the charcoal pieces, dead salmon, and ice wolf pelt as owned by the village.

## Bugs

- The village residents will never sleep, as all beds in the village are incorrectly owned by [Crescius Caerellius](/wiki/Skyrim:Crescius_Caerellius "Skyrim:Crescius Caerellius") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- The village residents have crime detection and will call for guards if they witness a crime. However, they do not actually have guards or a jail and have no way of arresting you.
