Crystaldrift Cave

**Crystaldrift Cave** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") southwest of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") containing leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals"). It contains only one zone, *[Crystaldrift Cave](#Crystaldrift_Cave)*.

It serves as the final resting place for an insane [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Gadnor "Skyrim:Gadnor"), with a shrine to [Kynareth](/wiki/Lore:Kynareth "Lore:Kynareth") built to watch over him and bring peace to weary travelers.

### Exterior

The path to the cave is a short way past [Snow-Shod Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow-Shod_Farm "Skyrim:Snow-Shod Farm") coming from Riften. It winds its way around before reaching a fork in the path: taking the right-hand path takes you past the cave entrance. It then loops around the rock outcrop that the cave is formed from, passing a path to [Lost Tongue Overlook](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Tongue_Overlook "Skyrim:Lost Tongue Overlook") and a [dragon mound](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Mounds "Skyrim:Dragon Mounds") before completing the loop along the left-hand path. As you approach the cave, there are up to two leveled animals standing guard by a dead [deer](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer "Skyrim:Deer"). Blood is splattered on the ground around the cave entrance. Following the loop past the cave, to the south, you come to a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") on the right after a short distance.

### Crystaldrift Cave

As you enter the cave, there will be a random grouping of animals which may attack each other first. After descending an earthen ramp and entering the cave proper, there is a pile of eight portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks") that have been knocked out of a long open box in front of you. To the right, on a large rock against the northwest wall is a [shrine of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Shrine of Kynareth"), with a random [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a [plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") holding five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), and several lit candles around it. With the [Saints & Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, you can also find the journal *[Gadnor's Last Wishes](/wiki/Skyrim:Gadnor%27s_Last_Wishes "Skyrim:Gadnor's Last Wishes")* on the shrine, which gives instructions on how to obtain the artifact [Nerveshatter](/wiki/Skyrim:Nerveshatter "Skyrim:Nerveshatter"). In the western quarter of the cave is a camp consisting of a campfire, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") hanging over it and a couple of pots holding three [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and three [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"). There is a hay pile against the west wall, with a rack to the right holding two bunches of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear"). To the left of the hay pile, against the southwest wall is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot. There is also a barrel containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") with an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") on top near the fire.

On the other side of the fire next to a rock pillar is a crate with a basket holding two [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and a few more loose vegetables on top. In the center of the cave is a large flat-topped rock. Resting on top of the slab are the remains of a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") adventurer named [Gadnor](/wiki/Skyrim:Gadnor "Skyrim:Gadnor") with his unique [Staff of Charming](/wiki/Skyrim:Gadnor%27s_Staff_of_Charming "Skyrim:Gadnor's Staff of Charming") lying next to him. He carries a copy of the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mystery of Talara, v 2](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_v_2 "Skyrim:Mystery of Talara, v 2")*. Around the rock are three [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") plants, with several fungi found throughout the cave, including five [blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort"), five [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), and one [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown"). To the northeast, near a shallow pool of water, is an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"). At the rear of the cave, a short way up an earthen ramp against the south wall, is a [moonstone ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein").
