Icewater Jetty

**Icewater Jetty** is a small isolated [dock](/wiki/Skyrim:Other_Places#Docks "Skyrim:Other Places") far west of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") and northwest of [Northwatch Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Keep "Skyrim:Northwatch Keep").

The boat at the pier can be used to reach the nearby island to the northwest, where [Castle Volkihar](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Volkihar "Skyrim:Castle Volkihar") is located. Two [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Grass Pod") plants, a [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), a [Nordic barnacle cluster](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle"), and a tree stump with [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") growing on it may be found in the area. Nothing else of interest is in the vicinity.

## Notes

- You can hire a [ferryman](/wiki/Skyrim:Transport#Ferrymen "Skyrim:Transport") to take you to this location for 500 gold, from [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"), Solitude, or [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). You can also travel here using the map in *[Dead Man's Dread](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Man%27s_Dread_(place) "Skyrim:Dead Man's Dread (place)")*[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Man%27s_Dread "Skyrim:Dead Man's Dread") once this option has been unlocked.
- It is not mandatory to use the boat to access Castle Volkihar; it is possible to swim to the castle instead.
