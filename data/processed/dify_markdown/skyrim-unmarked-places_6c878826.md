This overview article describes places of interest in Skyrim that are not marked on your world map upon discovery, are not represented by [icons on your compass](/wiki/Skyrim:Maps#Icons "Skyrim:Maps"), thus cannot be [fast traveled](/wiki/Skyrim:Transport#Fast_Travel "Skyrim:Transport") to, and in most cases are not directly related to any [quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Quests "Skyrim:Quests"). Some of the places on this list do not have official names or titles, in which case unofficial names have been used to describe the places.

Each location listed in the table here is discussed in detail, with images, on the unmarked locations pages for the regions linked in the menu above.

This sortable table features ALL unmarked locations in the game. Only exterior, unmarked [shrines](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines "Skyrim:Shrines") are included, not those inside locations or at locations with map markers. The secondary location label is as listed in the [Prima Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide"), for example 8.D is A Peddler's Misfortune. Location types include Hostile (bandit/forsworn/hagraven/necromancer/etc), Creature, Camp (hunter/fisher/empty/etc), Caravan, Boat (afloat/wrecked), Shrine, House, Dragon Mound, Jail, Ruin/monument, just Loot, or Other place of interest.

## Bandit Camps and Bandit Ambushes

- FALKREATH Bandit Camp: Ilinalta Foothills ----- On the road between [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen") and [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"), just south of the [Guardian Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Guardian_Stones "Skyrim:Guardian Stones") and east of a Shrine of Talos, there is a camp inhabited by three leveled [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). It contains a chest, [Treasure Map I](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_I "Skyrim:Treasure Map I"), and a copy of the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") skill book *[Night Falls on Sentinel](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Falls_on_Sentinel "Skyrim:Night Falls on Sentinel")*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=3760&y=-65659&zoom=6.288&legacy=true)
- THE PALE 2 mixed up (Dawnstar Frost Troll Den / Shoreline Bandit Camp) ----- Between [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") and [Pale Imperial Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Imperial_Camp "Skyrim:Pale Imperial Camp"), there is a camp inhabited by three leveled bandits. It has a chest, food barrels and a copy of the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") skill book *[Death Blow of Abernanit](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Blow_of_Abernanit "Skyrim:Death Blow of Abernanit")*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=10657&y=99519&zoom=6.288&legacy=true)
- FALKREATH Khajiit Caravan Massacre ----- On the road between Helgen and [Haemar's Shame](/wiki/Skyrim:Haemar%27s_Shame "Skyrim:Haemar's Shame") is a wrecked Khajiit caravan, complete with two carts, seven dead Khajiit and two dead horses. In the furthest cart are some sacks and a chest, as well as a copy of the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill book, *[The Buying Game](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Buying_Game "Skyrim:The Buying Game")*. When you approach from Helgen, multiple bandits will ambush you, usually consisting of one or two melee fighters and two archers. If you head up the road over the hill and then turn around and come back, another three-four bandits will spawn around the wreckage and attack. Note that this ambush only spawns if you head directly there after exiting Helgen. If you went through Helgen Keep with Hadvar, you can go to Riverwood, recruit Faendal, and have him help you battle the bandits and collect the skill book for you, but only if you do not enter Alvor's house. If you went through Helgen Keep with Ralof, the bandits and caravan will disappear as soon as you enter Riverwood or another village or city. If you've already spawned the ambush previously, by the time the nearby Stormcloak camp spawns (which happens only after visiting a major city), the caravan remains will have been cleared up and all the bandits will respawn, sitting around the stump just off the road to the right. They will be non-hostile unless spoken to, or outright attacked. x=37220&y=-79480 When the [Falkreath Stormcloak Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Stormcloak_Camp "Skyrim:Falkreath Stormcloak Camp") is enabled for the first time it permanently disables an unmarked place of a Khajiit Caravan Massacre south-southwest with a skill book The Buying Game.

## Caravan Remains and Shipwrecks

- HJAALMARCH Ambushed Caravan ----- On the side of the road southeast of Dragon Bridge is a caravan loaded with crates, rugs, and a knapsack. Next to it are the corpses of a horse, a Nord man, and a Redguard woman, the latter carrying the *[Merchant's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Merchant's Journal")*. [Falmer arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Arrow "Skyrim:Falmer Arrow") stuck in the horse, other Falmer arrows around the location, and a [Falmer ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Ear "Skyrim:Falmer Ear") suggest a [Falmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer "Skyrim:Falmer") attack, possibly related to the nearby [Chillwind Depths](/wiki/Skyrim:Chillwind_Depths "Skyrim:Chillwind Depths"). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-92152&y=67427&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- FALKREATH The Silvermoon: Lake Ilinalta ----- South of [The Lady Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lady_Stone "Skyrim:The Lady Stone") is a shipwreck beneath the waters of Lake Ilinalta. The shipwreck contains a chest and a leveled weapon. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-32600&y=-59576&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- WINTERHOLD ----- Northeast of the [Wreck of the Winter War](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Winter_War "Skyrim:Wreck of the Winter War") are the sparse remains of ship. Among the oars and wood are three barrels. A short distance to the north is a birds' nest with a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=184352&y=74240&zoom=15&legacy=true)
- FALKREATH Burning Caravan: Evergreen Grove ----- East of [Knifepoint Ridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Knifepoint_Ridge "Skyrim:Knifepoint Ridge") and west of the Evergreen Woods dragon mound, there are remnants of a caravan loaded with crates, rugs, and a chest. Next to it are the corpses of a horse and two burned humanoids. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-62808&y=-70289&zoom=7.964&legacy=true)

## Creature Lairs

- FALKREATH ----- Southeast of [Evergreen Grove](/wiki/Skyrim:Evergreen_Grove "Skyrim:Evergreen Grove") is a bugling elk near a stony Nordic bust. A hostile animal often wanders into the area. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-46997&y=-70386&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- THE PALE Barnacle Boat ----- On the coast between [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") and the [Wreck Of The Brinehammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_Of_The_Brinehammer "Skyrim:Wreck Of The Brinehammer"), there is an upturned skiff inhabited by two mudcrabs and containing a skeleton hand, a knapsack, a scimitar, two pieces of mudcrab chitin, and a copy of the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") skill book *[Daughter of the Niben](/wiki/Skyrim:Daughter_of_the_Niben "Skyrim:Daughter of the Niben")*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=13463&y=108099&zoom=6.288&legacy=true)
- FALKREATH Bear Cave: Halldir's Cairn ----- One or two bears lair WNW of Halldir's Cairn under a rocky overhang. The corpses of two hunters and an elk are found on the site, and beehives can be found in the trees nearby.
- FALKREATH Sunken Barrow: Lake Ilinalta ----- Underwater in the east end of Lake Ilinalta, southwest of Anise's Cabin, there is a sunken ruin where two slaughterfish reside. The ruins also contain a leveled mace, two soul gems in a stone bowl, and an unlocked chest under the stairs.

## Houses

- HJAALMARCH ----- Northeast of [Ustengrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Ustengrav "Skyrim:Ustengrav") and north of [Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp"), there is a ruined, burning shack. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=12462&y=109512&zoom=8&legacy=true)

## Warlock Altars

- FALKREATH Sacrificial Altar: Evergreen Woods ----- Halfway between [Cracked Tusk Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Cracked_Tusk_Keep "Skyrim:Cracked Tusk Keep") and [Evergreen Grove](/wiki/Skyrim:Evergreen_Grove "Skyrim:Evergreen Grove") is a Nordic altar occupied by a [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer"). This site contains embalming tools, a sword, a burial urn, and the Conjuration skill book *[2920, Hearth Fire, v9](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Hearth_Fire,_v9 "Skyrim:2920, Hearth Fire, v9")*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-7964&y=-66405&zoom=7.512&legacy=true)
