Moss Mother Cavern

**Moss Mother Cavern** is a small, open-roofed [cavernous](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") grove lush with many kinds of flora and fungi, located due north of [Hunter's Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter%27s_Rest "Skyrim:Hunter's Rest") and northwest of [Half-Moon Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill "Skyrim:Half-Moon Mill") which is inhabited by bears and spriggans.

You may be directed here by asking the [innkeeper](/wiki/Skyrim:Valga_Vinicia "Skyrim:Valga Vinicia") at [Dead Man's Drink](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Man%27s_Drink "Skyrim:Dead Man's Drink") about rumors once you are level 16. She will inform you of a missing hunting party, which will initiate the [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest) "Skyrim:Moss Mother Cavern (quest)") to locate them.

### Exterior

As you approach the cave for the first time, [Valdr](/wiki/Skyrim:Valdr "Skyrim:Valdr"), a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit archer](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Archer "Skyrim:Bandit Archer"), beckons you to help him: *"Hello? You! Over here! Please, I need help!"* His hunting party chased a wounded [bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear") sow into the cave, and was ambushed by [spriggans](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan"). His companions [Ari](/wiki/Skyrim:Ari "Skyrim:Ari") and [Niels](/wiki/Skyrim:Niels "Skyrim:Niels") were not so lucky, and can be found dead inside. Valdr requires immediate healing, or he will die due to his injuries: *"I may have lost a drop or two of blood. Truth is, I'm not going anywhere like this. Hurts to breathe."* There are many plants and fungi near the entrance, of the same types as found inside.

After the quest is complete and some time has passed in game, two graves will appear outside, in front of the log where Valdr is first found.

### Moss Mother Cavern

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-interior-Moss_Mother_Cavern.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Moss_Mother_Cavern.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Moss_Mother_Cavern.jpg)

Moss Mother Cavern interior

As its name suggests, there is a lot of [hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss") that can be harvested from the various ledges throughout the cave system. It is also a good spot to harvest [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") fungi. Other flora and fungi present inside the cave include various colors of [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"), [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown"), [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap"), and [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot").

The cave consists of two main areas linked by a sunken path; the second chamber contains a small interior pool. Opposite the entrance, on a ledge by a rock pillar is a [spriggan](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan"), with a leveled [bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear") close by. Ari's corpse is lying in front of the hunted dead bear, next to two [bear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap"). The path continues to the east, passing beneath a fallen tree that spans between the ledges. A hollow tree stump in a corner to the northwest along this path contains some leveled loot, including a coin purse, potion, and weapon.

On the left ledge behind the pillar is an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"). Using the fallen tree to cross to the right ledge and heading back toward the exit takes you to an adept-locked chest with an empty random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") in front of it.

As you continue along the path, look for a coin purse in the foliage on the ground to your right. There is another fallen tree on the left, with Niels' corpse in front, to the left of the pool. A nearby tree hides a second spriggan. To the east, near the rear of the second chamber, you'll find the last of the spriggans. There are more bear traps in this second chamber in deep undergrowth. Along a path northeast of the pool, near a towering tree, look around to find a [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein"). At the end of the cave, on a ledge by the southern wall, a boss chest can be found, along with a random [one-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:One-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:One-Handed Weapons"), a potion, and a pair of boots. A random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield") and [two-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:Two-Handed Weapons") can be found lying at the bottom of the pool near a skeleton.
