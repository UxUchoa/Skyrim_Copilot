The Hall of the Vigilant

The **Hall of the Vigilant** is a secluded [lodge](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") south-southwest of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"), home to the [Vigilants of Stendarr](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilants_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Vigilants of Stendarr").

The [Vigilants](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilants_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Vigilants of Stendarr") are led by [Keeper Carcette](/wiki/Skyrim:Keeper_Carcette "Skyrim:Keeper Carcette"), who offers expert [training](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers") in [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration").

### Exterior

The path leading to the hall is off of the road between [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") and [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") and starts a short distance south of [Red Road Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Road_Pass "Skyrim:Red Road Pass"). At the front of the hall is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") and small stable that is big enough for a single [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse"), while the rear of the building backs up to a rock face. There is a short flight of steps leading up to a covered porch with a few barrels and crates stacked at either side of the only entrance. If [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is installed, a [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Quarried_Stone "Skyrim:Quarried Stone") can be found down the road north of the building.

### Hall of the Vigilant

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant.jpg)

Hall of the Vigilant

Entering the hall leads directly into a large common room with a long fire pit across the width of the room at the eastern end. There are two [venison chops](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison_Chop "Skyrim:Venison Chop") on the edge of the pit and hanging above the far end is a rack holding five [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). Behind the fire pit are a few barrels, one of which has a haunch of raw [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison") on top, while from the ceiling hang two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, a bunch of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). In the northeastern corner is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") with a [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Stamina") and a [potion of the defender](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Defender "Skyrim:Potion of the Defender") on top. At the western end of the room is a long wooden table with a [shrine to Stendarr](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Shrine of Stendarr") on top, along with a [wolf pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf_Pelt "Skyrim:Wolf Pelt"), a bowl of [vampire dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Dust "Skyrim:Vampire Dust"), a bowl of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"), a [daedra heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra_Heart "Skyrim:Daedra Heart"), a random, possibly enchanted [steel mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Steel Mace"), a couple of [candlesticks](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlestick "Skyrim:Candlestick"), and some plates. All of these items are marked as owned, and taking them is considered stealing. There are three pairs of wooden benches facing the shrine.

Opposite the entrance is a practice dummy with some bales of straw behind it and a long wooden table to the left. On the table are two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") in a bowl. In the southeastern corner is a couple of barrels, with a tall wardrobe containing [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") to the right and a narrow table against the south wall, to the left. In the northwestern corner is the top of a flight of stairs leading to the basement. Against the north wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of alto wine, six bottles of wine, and four loaves of bread. To the right of this is a door to a larger bedroom used by [Keeper Carcette](/wiki/Skyrim:Keeper_Carcette "Skyrim:Keeper Carcette").

It contains an unowned single bed (see [bugs](#Bugs)) coming out from the north wall with an expert-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot and small bedside tables on either side. To the right is a narrow wooden table with three bottles of alto wine, a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and an empty [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") on top and a food sack and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") underneath. Against the east wall is a dresser with a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and some cheap crockery on top. In the southeastern corner is a long wooden table with another alchemy lab built in and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a medium coin purse, three bottles of wine, a bowl of [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), four loose [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") coins and some bread on top.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_02.jpg)

The cellar

Along the south wall are two small bedrooms. The first contains a single bed in the southeast corner with an unlocked wooden chest and wardrobe at the foot of it and a round wooden table and chair to the right, with a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") and a bowl of [tomato soup](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato_Soup "Skyrim:Tomato Soup") on the table top. The second bedroom contains a single bed in the southwestern corner with an unlocked chest and dresser at the foot and a wooden table and chair to the left, with a bottle of alto wine on the table top. On the dresser is a basket containing three [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and a bowl containing a loaf of bread and a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek").

The staircase leads down to a wooden door with another flight of stairs opening into a large room containing six empty weapon racks on the west wall with an unlocked chest to the left. Against the south wall is a set of shelves holding two [ice wraith teeth](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth "Skyrim:Ice Wraith Teeth"), a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a [Nordic barnacle](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle") and four [slaughterfish eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg"), a [taproot](/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot "Skyrim:Taproot"), two bunches of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"), three [potions of cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Potion of Cure Disease"), one of [cure poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison "Skyrim:Cure Poison"), a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and a [potion of the Knight](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Knight "Skyrim:Potion of the Knight"). To the left is a barrel containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") with a [giant's toe](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant%27s_Toe "Skyrim:Giant's Toe"), a bunch of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"), and a bowl of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust") on top. In the southeastern corner is another practice dummy with bales behind it, and leaning against the east wall on top of a small unit is a tactical map of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"). In the northeastern corner is a low table and chair with a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Knights of the Nine](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Knights_of_the_Nine "Skyrim:The Knights of the Nine")*, a medium coin purse and five loose gold coins. All of these items, including the book, are also owned by the Vigilants.

#### Dawnguard

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-place-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_%28destroyed%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_%28destroyed%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_(destroyed).jpg)

The destroyed hall

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_%28destroyed%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_%28destroyed%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Vigilant_(destroyed).jpg)

The interior of the hall after being attacked

With the [Dawnguard add-on](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") installed, the Hall of the Vigilant is destroyed by [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") once you reach level 10 or start the quest [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(quest) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (quest)"). As you approach you will see blood splatters on the ground, then the body of a [death hound](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Hound "Skyrim:Death Hound"). The tanning rack and front porch will both have been destroyed, while inside will be a complete shamble, with the bodies of dead Vigilants, dead vampires and death hounds among the smoldering rubble, along with a copy of *[The Aetherium Wars](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars "Skyrim:The Aetherium Wars")* and miscellaneous clutter. There is an unlocked chest in the eastern corner, the long table where the shrine to Stendarr once stood, and a food barrel at the back of the room. Unlike when the Hall was operational, nothing will be owned by the Vigilants anymore, and everything will now be free to take. Nothing else of interest survived the fire, nor is the basement accessible any longer.

## Notes

- This location can be marked as cleared by killing all the Vigilants inside. With Dawnguard installed, this becomes impossible after level 10, due to the Hall's destruction.
  - Once killed, the residents will not respawn.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The Hall can be reverted to its original form by entering the code `"xx003531".enable` into the console.
- If you ask the Vigilants stationed here who they are, only the "Yes. Our order was founded after the Oblivion Crisis. We dedicate our lives to facing the threat of Daedra wherever they appear." line will be heard.
- If Dawnguard is installed and your character is here when level 10 is reached, the Hall may not be destroyed until you travel far enough away. Fast traveling here immediately after reaching level 10 with Dawnguard installed may also yield the same results.
- With the Hall destroyed, none of its interior cells can be accessed anymore, as the ruin exists as an exterior cell only. If you were to use the console to teleport to these cells, you will find that they remain unaltered.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the searchable barrel outside as owned by the Vigilants. This will, of course, no longer be the case once the building is destroyed.
- Even if the Hall is destroyed, the wood chopping block outside will remain unscathed and fully functional.

## Bugs

- Loading a save made in the cellar after installing *Dawnguard* will corrupt that save and/or cause several errors due to the area being broken. It will be locked with no way in or out. **?**
