[Mauhulakh](/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Mauhulakh "Skyrim:Chief Mauhulakh")'s Longhouse

**Mauhulakh's Longhouse** is the longhouse of [Chief Mauhulakh](/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Mauhulakh "Skyrim:Chief Mauhulakh"), located in the Orc stronghold of [Narzulbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Narzulbur "Skyrim:Narzulbur").

The longhouse contains two zones: *[Mauhulakh's Longhouse](#Mauhulakh.27s_Longhouse)* and *[Mauhulakh's Cellar](#Mauhulakh.27s_Cellar)*.

The longhouse is home to [Chief Mauhulakh](/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Mauhulakh "Skyrim:Chief Mauhulakh") and his aunts and children.

### Mauhulakh's Longhouse

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse.jpg)

The longhouse

It's a typical Orc longhouse with a central room for cooking and eating with wings at either end for sleeping. There is a large fireplace opposite the entrance with a three pots hanging over it, none of them usable as a cooking pot. The middle pot contains two [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"). To the left is a long wooden table that has a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat"), two bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), two [rock warbler eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), a joint of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"), two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and three [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato") in a bowl on top, while in a basket underneath are two [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"). Hanging above is another leg of goat, a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), a garlic braid, a bunch of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). To the left of the table is a set of shelves holding mostly clutter with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), six bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"). There are two dining tables in the room, one on your left as you enter is set for four and holds two portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a bottle of Nord mead and a [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") in a tankard. The second table is to the right of the fireplace and is set for two and holds a rock warbler egg, a bottle of Nord mead and also has a purple mountain flower in a tankard. In the southeast corner is a set of shelves holding two [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), three common books, a [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") and a set of clothes.

The wing to the northwest contains four single beds, two against each wall and is where [Chief Mauhulakh](/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Mauhulakh "Skyrim:Chief Mauhulakh") sleeps along with his children [Dushnamub](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnamub "Skyrim:Dushnamub") and [Urog](/wiki/Skyrim:Urog "Skyrim:Urog"). The room contains an end table, a dresser with a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat") on top, a master-locked chest and at the end a pile of sacks and barrels mostly containing food, except one barrel that contains [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and has a common book on top. The southern wing contains a double bed where [Bolar](/wiki/Skyrim:Bolar "Skyrim:Bolar") sleeps, with an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") leaning against the side of the bed. In this room, there is an end table, a dresser, an adept-locked chest, and a small set of shelves holding copies of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Knights of the Nine](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Knights_of_the_Nine "Skyrim:The Knights of the Nine")*, *[The True Nature of Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:The_True_Nature_of_Orcs "Skyrim:The True Nature of Orcs")* and *[The Code of Malacath](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Code_of_Malacath "Skyrim:The Code of Malacath")* along with a bottle each of alto wine and wine. There is also a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") leaning against these shelves. On top of the dresser, there is a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a [long taffy treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Taffy_Treat "Skyrim:Long Taffy Treat") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger").

### Mauhulakh's Cellar

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse_02.jpg)

The cellar

Despite lying below the longhouse, this area must be entered from outside. It is a small room with several sets of shelves against the walls. These hold a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide"), a [deer hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide "Skyrim:Deer Hide"), three baskets containing [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), tomatoes and red and green apples, four sheaves of wheat, twenty-three bottles of Nord mead, a kettle containing a bottle of [weak aversion to fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Aversion_to_Fire "Skyrim:Weak Aversion to Fire"), a [cow hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow_Hide "Skyrim:Cow Hide"), a copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") skill book *[Song Of Hrormir](/wiki/Skyrim:Song_Of_Hrormir "Skyrim:Song Of Hrormir")* and a chest with a leveled lock.
