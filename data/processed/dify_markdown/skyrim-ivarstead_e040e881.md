Ivarstead

**Ivarstead** is a milling [town](/wiki/Skyrim:Towns "Skyrim:Towns") in the western [Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift").

It is at the base of the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World") on the shores of [Lake Geir](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Geir "Skyrim:Lake Geir"), with one of the forks of the [Darkwater River](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_River "Skyrim:Darkwater River") powering the sawmill on its way to join the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River"). The town consists of five buildings, with [Shroud Hearth Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Shroud_Hearth_Barrow "Skyrim:Shroud Hearth Barrow") just to the east overshadowing the settlement. Some of the town's residents believe the barrow to be haunted; the innkeeper [Wilhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Wilhelm "Skyrim:Wilhelm") willingly tells you more. He runs [Vilemyr Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Vilemyr_Inn "Skyrim:Vilemyr Inn") at the southern end of the town opposite [Klimmek's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Klimmek%27s_House "Skyrim:Klimmek's House"). To the north is [Fellstar Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Fellstar_Farm "Skyrim:Fellstar Farm") opposite [Temba Wide-Arm](/wiki/Skyrim:Temba_Wide-Arm "Skyrim:Temba Wide-Arm")'s sawmill, and across the river is [Narfi](/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi "Skyrim:Narfi")'s destroyed house.

It is a town in decline, with fewer travelers visiting since the haunting of the barrow began. Some residents fear for the town's survival, as many of the townspeople have either left for [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") or want to move away. Pilgrims and travelers often pass through the town on their way up the "[7,000 steps](/wiki/Skyrim:Climb_the_Steps "Skyrim:Climb the Steps")" to [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar").

## Notes

- If *[Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")* is installed, one [clay deposit](/wiki/Skyrim:Clay "Skyrim:Clay") is southeast of [Narfi](/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi "Skyrim:Narfi")'s dilapidated house and one [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry "Skyrim:Stone Quarry") is west of it. 2 more clay deposits are on opposite sides of the bridge on the southern edge of town.
- Ivarstead is marked on your map without anyone mentioning the town once you start the quest [The Way of the Voice](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Way_of_the_Voice "Skyrim:The Way of the Voice") (from [Balgruuf the Greater](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater")).
- You can ask all residents except Narfi what they know about High Hrothgar.
- After the main quest is completed, if you look up at the Throat of the World, you may see the dragon [Odahviing](/wiki/Skyrim:Odahviing "Skyrim:Odahviing") flying around.
- Ivarstead also [appears](/wiki/Online:Ivarstead "Online:Ivarstead") in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online").
