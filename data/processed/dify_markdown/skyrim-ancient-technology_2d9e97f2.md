## Detailed Walkthrough

One of the schematics

### Find the Schematic

Speak to Sorine Jurard in [Fort Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dawnguard "Skyrim:Fort Dawnguard") and ask "*What can I do to help?*" to receive this quest. She will instruct you to travel to a random [Dwarven ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwarven Ruins") or [bandit hideout](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Bandit_Camps "Category:Skyrim-Places-Bandit Camps") (this includes locations added by [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")) and retrieve the Dwemer schematic specifying a new [crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Crossbow "Skyrim:Crossbow") design. The schematic will be located in the boss chest in the location. If it is a bandit area, the note [Dwarven Haul](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Haul "Skyrim:Dwarven Haul") will be found in the chest as well.

Once you have the schematic, return to Sorine to receive your reward of a leveled enchanted item. In addition, she will also teach you how to craft an [Enhanced Crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Enhanced_Crossbow "Skyrim:Enhanced Crossbow") at a forge (as long as you possess the [Steel Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing "Skyrim:Steel Smithing") perk).

This quest can be completed a further five times (six in total). In addition to a leveled enchanted item, each time Sorine will teach you how to craft a different item. After the [Enhanced Crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Enhanced_Crossbow "Skyrim:Enhanced Crossbow"), these will be [Exploding Steel Bolts of Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Steel_Bolt_of_Fire "Skyrim:Exploding Steel Bolt of Fire"), [Ice](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Steel_Bolt_of_Ice "Skyrim:Exploding Steel Bolt of Ice") and [Shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Steel_Bolt_of_Shock "Skyrim:Exploding Steel Bolt of Shock"). After the fifth run you will be able to craft a [Dwarven Crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Crossbow "Skyrim:Dwarven Crossbow") and [Dwarven Bolts](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Bolt "Skyrim:Dwarven Bolt") (as long as you possess the [Dwarven Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Smithing "Skyrim:Dwarven Smithing") perk), and Sorine will also begin selling [Exploding Dwarven Bolts of Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Fire "Skyrim:Exploding Dwarven Bolt of Fire"), [Ice](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Ice "Skyrim:Exploding Dwarven Bolt of Ice") and [Shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Shock "Skyrim:Exploding Dwarven Bolt of Shock"). After the final run, you will be able to craft an [Enhanced Dwarven Crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Enhanced_Dwarven_Crossbow "Skyrim:Enhanced Dwarven Crossbow"). For more details on the required components for each time see [Steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing "Skyrim:Steel Smithing") and [Dwarven Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Smithing "Skyrim:Dwarven Smithing").

## Notes

- This quest is not available if you sided with the Volkihar, or if you are in the Dawnguard ranks but are currently a vampire.
- With the [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") add-on, if the item happens to be found on Solstheim, there will be no quest marker. Traveling to Solstheim will make the marker appear in the relevant location.
- You cannot craft standard Steel Bolts unless you ask Sorine how to craft a crossbow, and you also cannot craft Dwarven Bolts until you have done the part of the quest where you find the Dwarven Crossbow Schematics and return them to her. You can craft both Dwarven and Steel elemental bolts of each element after you turn the schematics in for them.

## Bugs

- If you advance in the Dawnguard questline the quest marker may disappear off of the radiant location where the schematics are located. The quest marker will instead be back over Sorine's head and will remain there.
  - If you have the [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") add-on, head to Solstheim and activate the quest. This may resolve the issue.[*verification needed — Does this work for others? Worked for 2 others; failed for 2 other*]
- Rarely, after obtaining the first several upgrades, you will not be presented with the option to repeat the quest and retrieve further schematics. This may be caused by starting the quest [Kindred Judgment](/wiki/Skyrim:Kindred_Judgment "Skyrim:Kindred Judgment").
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") It is possible to manually add the missing recipes with the following console commands (which add all the recipes from exploding fire bolts onwards—only the ones that are actually missing are required):

:   :   ```
        Set DLC1TechXbowBoltsExplodingFireChanceNone to 0
        Set DLC1TechXbowBoltsExplodingIceChanceNone to 0
        Set DLC1TechXbowBoltsExplodingShockChanceNone to 0
        Set DLC1TechXbowDwarvenChanceNone to 0
        Set DLC1TechXbowDwarvenEnhancedChanceNone to 0
        ```

- You can get stuck with the "Help Sorine with her project to improve the crossbow" and the dialogue item "What can I do to help?" that gives the "Ancient Technology" doesn't appear on Sorine.
- Rarely, the schematic is not in the indicated location.
- Bandit hideouts that were picked to hold Dwarven items for the quest will continue to spawn them (minus the "Dwarven Haul" note) in the boss chest on subsequent trips to said areas, even when the Ancient Technology quests are over. **?**
- The schematic for the Enhanced Dwarven Crossbow may appear on the ground or floor next to the boss chest after the Ancient Technology quest is done and the area it was first picked up in respawns. It can be picked up, but is translucent when dropped and will fall through any piece of furniture it is placed on (such as a table). **?**
- This quest may run more than 6 times despite that fact that there are exactly 6 schematics to retrieve.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dawnguard Patch"), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage DLC1RH05 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest DLC1RH05`.
