Tel Mithryn

**Tel Mithryn** is a Telvanni tower [settlement](/wiki/Skyrim:Settlements "Skyrim:Settlements") east of [Fort Frostmoth](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Frostmoth "Skyrim:Fort Frostmoth") on [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim").

The settlement is home to [House Telvanni](/wiki/Lore:House_Telvanni "Lore:House Telvanni") Master Wizard [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth"). Its four mushroom structures were grown near the beginning of the [Fourth Era](/wiki/Lore:Fourth_Era "Lore:Fourth Era") out of special fungal spores from [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind"). The structures include the [main tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)"), the [apothecary](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Apothecary"), the [kitchen](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Kitchen"), and the [steward's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Steward's House"). Master Neloth has a dubious reputation on the island, but is instrumental in progressing the *[Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")* questline and offers other [unique services](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_Enchanter "Skyrim:Staff Enchanter") to the enterprising adventurer.

Each of the structures is accessible by a long organic ramp ascending to a round wooden door. A [heart stone deposit](/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone_Deposit "Skyrim:Heart Stone Deposit") and a convenient [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") are behind the kitchen, with two more deposits on the cliffs near the coast to the southeast, while a few [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") and [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") fungi and [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plants grow near the ramps. Other than the unique exterior architecture, most items of interest are inside the buildings.
