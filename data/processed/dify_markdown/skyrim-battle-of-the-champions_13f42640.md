## Quick Walkthrough

1. *(if you have not yet chosen a side in the Civil War)* Read the [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_of_Champions "Skyrim:Battle of Champions") in [The Drunken Huntsman](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Drunken_Huntsman "Skyrim:The Drunken Huntsman"), then pass a Persuasion [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") check with [Legate Adventus Caesennius](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Adventus_Caesennius "Skyrim:Legate Adventus Caesennius") *or* give [snow bear pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Bear Pelt") or [Klija's Snow Bear Pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Klija%27s_Snow_Bear_Pelts "Skyrim:Klija's Snow Bear Pelts") to [Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced](/wiki/Skyrim:Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced "Skyrim:Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced").

:   :   *or*

1. *(if you have already chosen a side in the Civil War)* Wait for a letter from the Stormcloaks or the Legion (you may need to enter The Drunken Huntsman once to trigger the courier).
2. Speak to Adventus Caesennius or Yrsarald and loot all items from the nearby chest.
3. Make your way to the battlefield east of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") and kill all enemy soldiers. Loot the Champion's body to complete the quest.

### Become a Champion

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_02.jpg)

You may have to take out the competition...

**Note: This stage of the quest is only available if you install the [Civil War Champions](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War_Champions "Skyrim:Civil War Champions") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") on a character that has not yet sided with the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") or the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks").**

Go to [The Drunken Huntsman](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Drunken_Huntsman "Skyrim:The Drunken Huntsman") in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). On the counter inside, you will find a note called *[Battle of Champions](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_of_Champions "Skyrim:Battle of Champions")*, which describes an upcoming battle between the Legion and the Stormcloaks in the ongoing [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"). Both sides intend to nominate a Champion, but nobody has been chosen as of yet.

If you wish to side with the Legion, you must journey to [Castle Dour](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour "Skyrim:Castle Dour") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") to speak with [Legate Adventus Caesennius](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Adventus_Caesennius "Skyrim:Legate Adventus Caesennius"). You can wear 3 imperial pieces(armor, boot and bracer, referring details to [Steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel "Skyrim:Steel")) to pose as an imperial soldier, if don't, you will have to pass a [Persuasion check (Speech 50)](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech#Average "Skyrim:Speech") to convince him to let you act as the Legion Champion. If your [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill is not particularly high, it would be wise to bring a [Fortify Persuasion](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Persuasion "Skyrim:Fortify Persuasion") potion with you. Once you persuade him, you will be handed the [Imperial Armory Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Armory_Key "Skyrim:Imperial Armory Key") to unlock a chest in the nearby equipment room.

If you instead wish to fight for the Stormcloaks, you must bring five [snow bear pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Bear Pelt") to [Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced](/wiki/Skyrim:Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced "Skyrim:Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced") in the [Palace of the Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). These can be taken from [snow bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear "Skyrim:Snow Bear") or purchased from hunters and general goods merchants starting at level 10, although at low levels you may have difficulty with this task. Luckily there is an alternative and more underhanded approach: the note mentions a prospective Champion named [Klija Wolf-Claw](/wiki/Skyrim:Klija_Wolf-Claw "Skyrim:Klija Wolf-Claw") who wishes to fight for the Stormcloaks. She has already gathered the pelts and can be found at the small settlement of [Kynesgrove](/wiki/Skyrim:Kynesgrove "Skyrim:Kynesgrove") just south of Windhelm. She can be found at all hours at the [Steamscorch Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine "Skyrim:Steamscorch Mine") Camp and carries an item named [Klija's Snow Bear Pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Klija%27s_Snow_Bear_Pelts "Skyrim:Klija's Snow Bear Pelts") on her person. It can be a challenge to [pickpocket](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket "Skyrim:Pickpocket") the item from her, but be warned that killing her counts as murder and will land you with a significant [bounty](/wiki/Skyrim:Crime#Bounties "Skyrim:Crime") if you are caught. Whichever way you go about obtaining enough pelts, speak with Yrsarald to receive the [Storm-Bear Chest Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Chest_Key "Skyrim:Storm-Bear Chest Key") unlocking a chest in the Palace's war room.

### Answer the Call

**Note: This stage of the quest is only available if you install the Creation on a character which has already joined the Legion or the Stormcloaks.**

If you're already a member of the Legion or the Stormcloaks, you will receive a letter by [courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Courier "Skyrim:Courier") [*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Battle_of_the_Champions#Letter_from_the_Empire_.2F_Stormcloaks_when.3F)*] from your commanding officer, advising you that a challenge has been issued by the Stormcloaks and requesting your assistance in dealing with it. If you are a member of the Legion, you will receive a *[Letter from the Empire](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_the_Empire "Skyrim:Letter from the Empire")* requesting that you speak with Legate Adventus Caesennius in Castle Dour to receive your custom forged equipment for this task. If you are a Stormcloak, you will instead receive a *[Letter from the Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_the_Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Letter from the Stormcloaks")* asking you to meet with Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced in the Palace of the Kings. Meet with your commanding officer to receive a key to a nearby chest.

### Prepare for Battle

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Battle_of_the_Champions_03.jpg)

Approach your fellow Champion

Whichever path you take, once you are named Champion you will need to unlock the relevant chest and loot every item within. The quest will not progress unless you take everything, so be sure to pick up both weapons even if you only mean to use one. The chest also contains the [Map of Battle Location](/wiki/Skyrim:Map_of_Battle_Location "Skyrim:Map of Battle Location"), which can be examined in your inventory if you wish. The quest marker will direct you to the battle location in any case, a clearing on the plains of [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold") directly west of [Shimmermist Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Shimmermist_Cave "Skyrim:Shimmermist Cave"). When you arrive, you will find five soldiers from both sides facing off against each other, with the other Champion patiently waiting for you in the center. If you side with the Legion, the Champion will be Klija Wolf-Claw if you didn't steal her pelts, but if you stole her pelts you will face a generic [Stormcloak Champion](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Champion "Skyrim:Stormcloak Champion") instead. If you side with the Stormcloaks you will face a generic [Imperial Champion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Champion "Skyrim:Imperial Champion").

Speak with the other Champion to begin the battle. You will need to slaughter all enemy combatants and loot the fallen Champion for their gear in order to complete the quest. You may have issues with [encumbrance](/wiki/Skyrim:Encumbrance "Skyrim:Encumbrance") at this stage if you intend to bring all this equipment away with you. Both sets of items are extremely powerful and make for a worthy reward. After the completion of the quest, you will receive a *Letter of Commendation* (either the [Legion version](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_of_Commendation_(Imperial_Legion) "Skyrim:Letter of Commendation (Imperial Legion)") or [Stormcloak version](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_of_Commendation_(Stormcloak) "Skyrim:Letter of Commendation (Stormcloak)")) via courier from your commanding officer to congratulate you on your victory.

## Notes

- If you install Civil War Champions on a character who has already chosen a side in the Civil War, Klija Wolf-Claw will not appear at Kynesgrove.
- If you decide to steal Klija's pelts but side with the Legion anyway, you will face a generic [Stormcloak Champion](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Champion "Skyrim:Stormcloak Champion") at the battle.
- If over-encumberance occurs from taking the gear of both factions it would be helpful to have either Arvak or the Daedric horse to summon, mount and fast-travel away.
- [Bits and Pieces](/wiki/Skyrim:Bits_and_Pieces "Skyrim:Bits and Pieces") has a snow bear pelt on display that respawns every 10 days, making it easy to obtain the powerful Storm-Bear set even at level 1.
- Whichever side you choose, surviving soldiers will make their way to the inn in the capital and remain for a short time before you receive Letter of Commendation, Imperial soldiers to [The Winking Skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Winking_Skeever "Skyrim:The Winking Skeever"), Stormcloak soldiers to [Candlehearth Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlehearth_Hall "Skyrim:Candlehearth Hall").

## Bugs

- Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced will not actually take the five snow bear pelts from you if you choose to go that route.
  - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch"), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
- If Klija Wolf-Claw appears at Kynesgrove and you side with the Legion, two versions of her will exist at once. Killing her at the battle site will not disable the version at Kynesgrove. **?**
- After the associated armor is picked up, the game may crash.
  - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch"), version 1.6.1130, fixes this bug.
- After the AE update patch, completing this quest can cause the following with great frequency: graphics issues, loading screens that fail to end, and crashes. Reloading a save before the battle portion of this quest can fix this, but completing the quest will cause it to happen again.
  - This bug may be fixed by storing the armor somewhere (i.e. a chest or display). It appears that being equipped or just in one's inventory (including companions) can cause these glitches.
  - Possible Fix (tested on PS5): Save after battle then loot gear. After first attempted fast travel from the battle site at end of battle, unending loading screen should occur. When this happens, close game and restart. Loot gear, then walk to a location in a new cell with item storage (like a home), Myrwatch is a verified working location. Store all items from this quest inside a container, then leave home and make new save. Close Skyrim and restart game. Associated bugs should stop, items should be retrievable and usable without further issue.
- After obtaining the Imperial Dragon Armor, the quest may fail to update, leaving you unable to progress.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") If this occurs, you can enter `setstage ccFFBSSE001_Quest 240` in the console to progress the quest.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage ccFFBSSE001_Quest stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest ccFFBSSE001_Quest`.
