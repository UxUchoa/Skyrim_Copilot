**Alea Quintus**, a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H"), is one of the three people that may be selected from and killed to gain entry into the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") during the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:With_Friends_Like_These... "Skyrim:With Friends Like These..."). She is the mother of six children. She does not act scared when spoken with; rather, she says that she has no time to deal with this and demands to be released. Furthermore, Alea states that she may have made enemies with the take-no-nonsense attitude she has had to adopt in order to raise her children.

She wears a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0001be1a "Skyrim:Clothes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0001be1b "Skyrim:Clothes"). She, like the other two captives, also wears an [execution hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Execution_Hood "Skyrim:Execution Hood").

## Quest-Related Dialogue

When approached for the first time:

:   *"I don't have time for this nonsense. I've got a home to keep and children to feed. Now let me out of here!"*
:   *"Cowards! Stealing a woman from her home! For shame!"*
:   *"Get these things off of me!"*
:   *"By the gods, when I get out of here you're dead! You hear me? Dead!"*
:   *"Aggghh! Re...lease me!"*

If you kill another captive and talk to Alea, she will say:

:   *"What's happening? Damn it, what are you doing?"*

Speaking to her:

:   **Who are you?**
:   *"None of your damned business who I am! If you're going to kill me, just do it already! As [Mara](/wiki/Lore:Mara "Lore:Mara") is my witness, if I didn't have this damned hood on right now I would spit right in your face..."*
:   **Would someone pay to have you killed?**
:   *"Excuse me? What kind of question is that?"*

:   :   **One last time: Would. Someone. Pay. To. Have. You. Killed? (Intimidate)**
    :   *"I'm kneeling here with my hands bound and a sack over my head, and you have the gall to ask me that? What do you think, genius?"*

:   :   **It's all right. Just tell me what I need to know. (Persuade)**
    :   *"I'm a woman living in Skyrim with six children and no husband. I don't have the time or patience to be 'nice.' Do some people look down on me? Have I made some enemies? You're damn right."*

If you kill Astrid, you can initiate conversation with Alea to free her:

:   *"You cut these bindings this minute! You hear me? You cut these bindings right now!"*
:   **(Cut the captive's bonds)**
:   *"Well it's about damn time. Capturing a woman from her home, keeping her bound like an animal. Ridiculous..."*

Talking to her after this will have her respond:

:   *"I don't know who you are, or what's going on here, but you ought to be ashamed of yourself."*

:   *"Hmm. The fearless wife and mother, and the [underhanded Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Vasha "Skyrim:Vasha"). Had to be one of them, right? Interesting choice."*
