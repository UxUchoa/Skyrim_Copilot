A Fort Commander

The **Fort Commander** is available once a side in the [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") has taken over a fort. If there is a Fort Commander's Quarters he will be found in them, if not he can be found inside the fort. Apart from his name he is a generic [Imperial soldier](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Soldier "Skyrim:Imperial Soldier") and has generic [Imperial soldier](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Soldier "Skyrim:Imperial Soldier") dialogue.
