**The Pale** is a [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in northern [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), with its capital in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"). It is initially affiliated with the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks"), but can be captured by the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") after completing [A False Front](/wiki/Skyrim:A_False_Front_(Imperial) "Skyrim:A False Front (Imperial)") and [The Battle for Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Dunstad (Imperial)"). It can also change sides as part of negotiations during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending").

The Pale is sparsely populated, with safe places for travelers few and far between. The snowy terrain that makes up most of the hold is unsuitable for farming; however, the far southern edge, where not mountainous, is more hospitable.

One of three entrances to [Blackreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackreach "Skyrim:Blackreach") is in the depths of [Raldbthar](/wiki/Skyrim:Raldbthar "Skyrim:Raldbthar"), a [Dwarven ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwarven Ruins") south of [Lake Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Yorgrim "Skyrim:Lake Yorgrim"), the largest lake in the Pale. [River Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim "Skyrim:River Yorgrim") flows from this lake.

## Flora and Fauna

[Snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") thrive in the harsh, snow-covered environment, but the sturdy [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") and [purple](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") variants of the mountain flower can also be found. Along the coast, [Nordic barnacles](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle"), [clams](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam "Skyrim:Clam"), and [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Spiky_Grass "Skyrim:Spiky Grass") dominate.

[Giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") are common here, as are the usual denizens of the snow: [sabre cats](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowy_Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Snowy Sabre Cat"), [wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf"), [ice wraiths](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith"), [trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll "Skyrim:Troll"), and [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider").

## The Pale Quests

Quests that are started in or related to the Pale (except for radiant quests begun in other holds) are listed here:

### Main Quest

- **[Elder Knowledge](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder_Knowledge "Skyrim:Elder Knowledge")**: Recover an [Elder Scroll](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder_Scroll "Skyrim:Elder Scroll") to learn an [Ancient Secret](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonrend "Skyrim:Dragonrend").
- **[Alduin's Bane](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin%27s_Bane "Skyrim:Alduin's Bane")**: Exploit the Time-Wound to learn [Dragonrend](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonrend "Skyrim:Dragonrend") and defeat [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin") at the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World").

### Imperial Legion Civil War Quests

- **[Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim")**: Reunite Skyrim under the leadership of the Empire. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[A False Front](/wiki/Skyrim:A_False_Front_(Imperial) "Skyrim:A False Front (Imperial)")**: Find and deliver some forged [documents](/wiki/Skyrim:StormCloak_Documents "Skyrim:StormCloak Documents") to the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks").
- **[The Battle for Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Dunstad (Imperial)")**: Take [Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dunstad "Skyrim:Fort Dunstad") for the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[The Jagged Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Jagged_Crown_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Jagged Crown (Imperial)")**: Find the [Jagged Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Jagged_Crown "Skyrim:Jagged Crown") for the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion").

### Stormcloaks Civil War Quests

- **[The Jagged Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Jagged_Crown_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Jagged Crown (Stormcloaks)")**: Find this [crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Jagged_Crown "Skyrim:Jagged Crown") for Ulfric Stormcloak.

### Daedric Quests

- **[Waking Nightmare](/wiki/Skyrim:Waking_Nightmare "Skyrim:Waking Nightmare")**: Relieve the people of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") from [Vaermina](/wiki/Lore:Vaermina "Lore:Vaermina")'s curse.
- **[Pieces of the Past](/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past "Skyrim:Pieces of the Past")**: Retrieve the scattered pieces of [Mehrunes' Razor](/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor "Skyrim:Mehrunes' Razor") and reforge this deadly blade.
- **[Discerning the Transmundane](/wiki/Skyrim:Discerning_the_Transmundane "Skyrim:Discerning the Transmundane")**: Uncover the secrets of the mysterious Dwemer lockbox.

### Dark Brotherhood

- **[Kill Beitild](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Beitild "Skyrim:Kill Beitild")**: Resolve a feud in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").
- **[The Cure for Madness](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cure_for_Madness "Skyrim:The Cure for Madness")**: Confront the traitor among the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood").
- **[Where You Hang Your Enemy's Head...](/wiki/Skyrim:Where_You_Hang_Your_Enemy%27s_Head... "Skyrim:Where You Hang Your Enemy's Head...")**: Restore the Dark Brotherhood's sanctuary.
- **[The Dark Brotherhood Forever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever")**: Yet another child has prayed to their [mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Mother "Skyrim:Night Mother").
- **[Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!")**: One of the biggest [contracts](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real) "Skyrim:Emperor Titus Mede II (real)") in [Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") history is yours to fulfill.
- **[The Silence Has Been Broken](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Silence_Has_Been_Broken "Skyrim:The Silence Has Been Broken")**: Meet [Amaund Motierre](/wiki/Skyrim:Amaund_Motierre "Skyrim:Amaund Motierre") inside [Volunruud](/wiki/Skyrim:Volunruud "Skyrim:Volunruud") and learn who he wants assassinated.
- **[Delayed Burial](/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial "Skyrim:Delayed Burial")**: Convince an unwilling [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius "Skyrim:Vantus Loreius") to help fix a stranded [jester's](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero "Skyrim:Cicero") broken wagon wheel.
- **[Recipe for Disaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_for_Disaster "Skyrim:Recipe for Disaster")**: Assume the identity of the famous [Gourmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Balagog_gro-Nolob "Skyrim:Balagog gro-Nolob") in an effort to assassinate the Emperor.

### Thieves Guild

- **[Blindsighted](/wiki/Skyrim:Blindsighted "Skyrim:Blindsighted")**: Stop [Mercer Frey](/wiki/Skyrim:Mercer_Frey "Skyrim:Mercer Frey").

### College of Winterhold

- **[Destruction Ritual Spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction_Ritual_Spell "Skyrim:Destruction Ritual Spell")**: Learn the most powerful [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") spells Skyrim has to offer.

### Dawnguard

- **[Awakening](/wiki/Skyrim:Awakening "Skyrim:Awakening")**[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard"): Find out what the [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Vampire_Clan "Skyrim:Volkihar Vampire Clan") are looking for.

### Side Quests

- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials")**: Participate in [Kyne](/wiki/Lore:Kyne "Lore:Kyne")'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").
- **[The Pale Lady](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale_Lady "Skyrim:The Pale Lady")**: Find out what happened in [Frostmere Crypt](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostmere_Crypt "Skyrim:Frostmere Crypt").
- **[Rise in the East](/wiki/Skyrim:Rise_in_the_East "Skyrim:Rise in the East")**: Help the [East Empire Company](/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company "Lore:East Empire Company") take care of some [pirates](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood_Horker "Skyrim:Blood Horker").
- **[Repairing the Phial](/wiki/Skyrim:Repairing_the_Phial "Skyrim:Repairing the Phial")**: Acquire the materials necessary to repair the [White Phial](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Phial_(item) "Skyrim:The White Phial (item)").
- **[A Scroll For Anska](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Scroll_For_Anska "Skyrim:A Scroll For Anska")**: Help [Anska](/wiki/Skyrim:Anska "Skyrim:Anska") trace her roots in [High Gate Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Gate_Ruins "Skyrim:High Gate Ruins").
- **[The White Phial](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Phial_(quest) "Skyrim:The White Phial (quest)")**: Retrieve a [legendary artifact](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Phial_(item) "Skyrim:The White Phial (item)") to fulfill an old [alchemist's](/wiki/Skyrim:Nurelion "Skyrim:Nurelion") wish in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").
