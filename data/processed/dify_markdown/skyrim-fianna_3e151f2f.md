Fianna

**Fianna** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") who works as a maid in [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach").

Fianna works alongside fellow servant [Gerda](/wiki/Skyrim:Gerda "Skyrim:Gerda") and they both spend the entire day sweeping the floor in the waiting area in front of the throne room. At 8am, Fianna starts sweeping the northwestern area, while Gerda sweeps the adjacent area. At 2pm they switch places and they both keep sweeping until 8pm when they head downstairs to their humble rooms in the [Jarl's quarters](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach_Jarl%27s_Quarters "Skyrim:Dragonsreach Jarl's Quarters"). The entire night is spent either wandering, relaxing or eating, despite the presence of a bed nearby (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

Fianna wears a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") along with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b "Skyrim:Boots"). She also wields an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger").

## Dialogue

Fianna takes her job very seriously and even if you become a [thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") she will remark:

:   *"Ain't nobody here high and mighty exceptin' the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater") and don't you forget it!"*
:   *"Don't you go trackin' your dirty boots on my clean floor!"*
:   *"Soldiers! They're everywhere these days. And not a one of them knows how to pick up after themselves."*

## Bugs

- Fianna was supposed to sleep from 8pm to 8am, but her sleep package mistakenly includes other activities, causing her to stay up all night.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
