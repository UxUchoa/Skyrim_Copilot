Nilheim

**Nilheim** is a small ruined [Nordic tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Towers "Skyrim:Nordic Towers") east-northeast of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") containing [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") disguised as guards.

## Related Quests

- **[Telrav's Request](/wiki/Skyrim:Telrav%27s_Request "Skyrim:Telrav's Request")**: Escort [Telrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Telrav "Skyrim:Telrav") back to his camp in Nilheim.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-place-Nilheim_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Nilheim_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Nilheim_03.jpg)

Nilheim's deceit

If you do not first speak to [Telrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Telrav "Skyrim:Telrav"), the occupants are disguised as [guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard_(NPC) "Skyrim:Guard (NPC)") and are non-hostile. You can loot everything in the tower without any of them caring.

If you do speak to Telrav and initiate his [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Telrav%27s_Request "Skyrim:Telrav's Request") to return him to his camp, all of these "friendly" guards are revealed to be [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). Killing Telrav unprovoked without initiating the quest results in the guards being hostile when you approach. After speaking to Telrav, he will lead you across the bridge and up several flights of stairs until you reach the main camp. At this point he will reveal his true colors and attack along with the bandits in the main camp. Once they are dealt with there may still be one of the imitation guards on the second floor of the tower. He will be hostile when you approach.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-place-Nilheim_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Nilheim_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Nilheim_02.jpg)

Nilheim camp

The main camp consists of a campfire with three long wooden tables and benches arranged around it. One of these has a [steel battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Steel Battleaxe"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), three loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), and a sack of food on top, the next has an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") beside it, and the third has two [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish") and two samples of [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales") on top and three food barrels beside it with a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") on one of these. To the north of the fire is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block").

More stairs rise to the southwest; at the top is a ruined wall and to the right of this is a stone bridge crossing to the tower. Inside, there is a dresser opposite the door with a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") on top. Stairs lead from here to a wooden ramp that takes you up to the next floor, where you may find a hostile imitation guard. There is a table next to the entrance with a leveled war axe, a large coin purse, an empty random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), and a leveled [potion of lasting potency](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Lasting_Potency "Skyrim:Potion of Lasting Potency") on top and several food sacks underneath. Farther into the room are three unowned bedrolls around an extinguished fire and several barrels and sacks of food. There are wooden stairs up with a wardrobe underneath.

The top of the tower has an unlocked chest as well as an awe-inspiring view of southeast [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"). On a clear day, one can see the entire [Jerall Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Jerall_Mountains "Skyrim:Jerall Mountains") that surround southeastern Skyrim, and to the north one can see farther than [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). To the west the view is blocked by the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World"), and [Northwind Summit](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwind_Summit "Skyrim:Northwind Summit") to the east.

There are two unnamed bodies, an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_(dead) "Skyrim:Imperial (dead)") and a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton_(dead) "Skyrim:Breton (dead)"), thrown among the rocks at the base of the tower to the north. Farther down the slope on the rock cliff is the body of a bandit.

## Notes

- Though the bandits are named after the standard "Bandit", they'll be stronger than the usual bandits, but won't level and be named (for example, "Bandit Marauder", "Bandit Thug").
- There is an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") a few paces to the south of the south end of the bridge to the tower. With the [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") add-on, a [clay desposit](/wiki/Skyrim:Clay "Skyrim:Clay") can be found south of there.
- A [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") can be found underneath the eastern end of the main bridge that leads to the dungeon.

## Bugs

- When the bandits are disguised as guards, they may not be hostile even if you approach as a werewolf.
- When the bandits are disguised as guards, they are only friendly toward you, but not toward your [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses") or [followers](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"). They will attack and even kill your horse if you ride it to Nilheim.
- If a radiant quest sends you here after you have cleared the tower, it will be impossible to complete.
  - Reload a previous save and obtain a different location for the quest.
  - Alternatively, enter the following commands in the console to resurrect the bandit chief of the tower and teleport him to you: `prid 72a8d`, `resurrect` and then `moveto player`.
- If you attack Telrav to make him hostile before the quest marker, but don't kill him, the guards will only watch and Telrav will not attack you.
- The items and containers may respawn despite Nilheim being classed as a "no reset zone". **?**
