Candlehearth Hall

**Candlehearth Hall** is a large [tavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Inns "Skyrim:Inns") in the center of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

A brief history from the tavern's owner, [Elda Early-Dawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Elda_Early-Dawn "Skyrim:Elda Early-Dawn"), reveals the building was originally home to a great warrior named Vundheim from the early [Fourth Era](/wiki/Lore:Fourth_Era "Lore:Fourth Era"). When he died in [4E 38](/wiki/Lore:Fourth_Era#4E_38 "Lore:Fourth Era") his son, Deroct, lit a candle above the hearth in his honor. Since that day, the candle still burns and as such the inn owes its name to this phenomenon.

It is a popular destination for many of the city's folk for beverages and the performances from the bards. [Nils](/wiki/Skyrim:Nils "Skyrim:Nils") serves as cook, but will take over running the inn if Elda dies. It consists of one area, *[Candlehearth Hall](#Candlehearth_Hall)*.

## Candlehearth Hall

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall.jpg)

The top floor with the namesake candle above the hearth

Candlehearth Hall is divided into two stories, a ground floor and a top floor. The ground floor functions as a taproom and has proprietor [Elda Early-Dawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Elda_Early-Dawn "Skyrim:Elda Early-Dawn") behind her counter, at times chatting with the regulars. The second floor is spacious and consists of one huge room with several tables where the regulars can enjoy their drinks and the [performances](/wiki/Skyrim:Bardic_Performances "Skyrim:Bardic Performances") from the bard, [Luaffyn](/wiki/Skyrim:Luaffyn "Skyrim:Luaffyn").

The top floor consists of one large room and is where most of the regulars gather during the day. Luaffyn stands in the southwestern corner, next to the ladder leading down to the kitchen. In front of her is the trademark hearth and the eternally burning candle. Facing the hearth are three chairs accompanied by tables with some assorted food, two [eidar cheese wedges](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), some [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), one of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"). On the other side of the hearth is another table with some [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and one of alto wine. Adjacent to the stairs leading down to the bar is an exit to Windhelm and a table with an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"). In the southeastern corner, [Stenvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Stenvar "Skyrim:Stenvar") sits around the clock, ready to be hired as a mercenary for 500 gold. The table in front of him has a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") and a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat"). In the adjacent corner is the table where local author and potential [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Delivery "Skyrim:Delivery")-giver [Adonato Leotelli](/wiki/Skyrim:Adonato_Leotelli "Skyrim:Adonato Leotelli") often sits when he writes his tales of heroism. His table has a slice of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), and a bottle of Nord mead.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall_04.jpg)

The ground floor

The ground floor has two entrances next to each other, both of them located adjacent to the main gate of Windhelm. Both entrances open to the sight of Elda behind her bar on the left, the guestrooms and kitchens down the hallway in front, and the stairs leading to the second floor on the right. A single bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") stands on the bar, while Elda hides several items underneath it, including a [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Healing"), an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and ten [gold coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold#Currency "Skyrim:Gold"). On the shelves behind her sit a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield"), a [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Stamina"), a [potion of resist fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Fire "Skyrim:Potion of Resist Fire"), along with several more alcoholic beverages, including four bottles of alto wine, two bottles of regular wine, and seven bottles of Nord mead.

Down the hallway are four rooms, three of which have beds while the fourth is the kitchen and has a ladder leading up to the second floor. [The Crimson Dirks, v4](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v4 "Skyrim:The Crimson Dirks, v4")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Alternative_Armors_-_Dragonscale "Skyrim:Alternative Armors - Dragonscale") can be found on a table in the hallway. The first room to the right is where [Nils](/wiki/Skyrim:Nils "Skyrim:Nils") sleeps between 2am and 8am. Along the western wall is a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") on the floor, while a small dinner table in the northwestern corner holds a bottle of Nord mead, some baked potatoes, and a honey nut treat. The cupboard in the adjacent corner holds clutter and the shelves above contain a bottle of wine, a copy of the [quest-related](/wiki/Skyrim:Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest) "Skyrim:Rebel's Cairn (quest)") book *[The Legend of Red Eagle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle "Skyrim:The Legend of Red Eagle")*. The southeastern corner has an end table with clutter and a large food sack along with it. Near the door is a set of shelves with three food sacks and the tavern's [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Candlehearth_Hall_03.jpg)

The room for rent

The first room on the left is the room you can rent for 10 gold. Just inside the door is a dresser with [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing") and a bit of food on top: a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), two [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and a [carrot](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"). In the corner next to the dresser is a set of shelves with a food sack, a potion of healing, a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"), and a [potion of resist fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Fire "Skyrim:Potion of Resist Fire"). Next up is a wardrobe with more clothes and the bed. At the foot of the bed is an end table with clutter and a bottle of wine on top.

The second room on the left is reserved for the [Stormcloak courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Courier "Skyrim:Stormcloak Courier") during the [civil war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") quest [A False Front](/wiki/Skyrim:A_False_Front_(Imperial) "Skyrim:A False Front (Imperial)"), if you side with the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"). Save for a few items, the room is almost completely empty. The door opens to a wardrobe with clothing, and, along the northern wall, more clothing in a dresser and a bottle of wine. The owned bed stands in the southeastern corner.

The kitchen is where [Nils](/wiki/Skyrim:Nils "Skyrim:Nils") cooks every day between 4pm and 10pm and consists of a huge amount of different foodstuffs and a ladder to the second floor. In the southeastern corner is a set of shelves and a cupboard, separated by two large food sacks, and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes "Skyrim:Shoes") on the floor. The shelves on the right contain three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a goat cheese wedge, a slice of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"), two salt piles, a sample of [frost salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Salts "Skyrim:Frost Salts"), a bottle of alto wine, and a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"). The cupboard contains clutter but has several items on the shelves above, including a food sack, a [chef's hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Hat "Skyrim:Chef's Hat"), a bottle of Nord mead, a bottle of wine, a chunk of [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"), a bottle of alto wine, and an apprentice-locked non-respawning [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"), which contains random items. Following the wall towards the [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"), next is a small table with more food, an apple pie, a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), two [raw rabbit legs](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg") and a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). Above the table hangs some game, specifically two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") and a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), along with two [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid") and a bouquet of [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam"). Near the fireplace, underneath the kettles sit three [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato") and, in a cast iron pot in the northwestern corner, a slice of bread. In the alcove leading to the ladder are four food sacks and a small table with even more food, a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a bottle of Nord mead, a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), four heads of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") and three carrots.

## Notes

- If Elda dies and Nils takes over, he will not stand behind the counter. You can, however, acquire his innkeeper services around the clock. See [this article](/wiki/Skyrim:Nils "Skyrim:Nils") for his schedule.
- Fighting [Rolff Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Rolff_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Rolff Stone-Fist") anywhere in the city makes low-level items free to take, but all the beds are still owned until you pay for the room.
- All cupboards, dressers, and end tables in the location are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") and safe to store items in.

## Bugs

- The candle above the fireplace will spill blood when attacked as if it were an NPC. This is actually caused by an erroneous havok material on the chimney itself; the chimney is what is being struck. The candle itself is a generic static object.
- During the [Battle for Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Windhelm "Skyrim:Battle for Windhelm") or immediately thereafter, if you enter Candlehearth Hall through the right entrance the other door will have an expert lock needed to get out. Re-entering Candlehearth Hall from the right entrance again locks the door from the inside and will still give XP. **?**
- The innkeeper's vendor chest here doesn't always stock bread despite Elda saying she sells it consistently.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.
