Mara's Eye Pond

**Mara's Eye Pond** is a small lake due east of [Gallows Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock "Skyrim:Gallows Rock").

There is an island in the center of the lake that hides a trapdoor, which is the entrance to [Mara's Eye Den](/wiki/Skyrim:Mara%27s_Eye_Den "Skyrim:Mara's Eye Den"), a [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") inhabited by leveled [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire"). Quests pointing to this location will lead you down into the cave under the lake.

[Gallows Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Hall_(place) "Skyrim:Gallows Hall (place)") is on the northern side of the pond.

### Mara's Pond

There are two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") and a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") near the shore, to the north, south, and west-southwest respectively. A [clay](/wiki/Skyrim:Clay "Skyrim:Clay") deposit([HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")) can be found by the edge of the water to the west. Several [mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab") call this home, both around the shore and on the island in the middle. Strangely there are no plants that can be harvested growing along the shoreline around the lake. To the east is a cart containing a crate (and a bottle of skooma), with a second crate on the ground next to it. On the island there is a large rotten tree stump with a [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") fungus growing on it and inside it there is the trapdoor entrance to [Mara's Eye Den](/wiki/Skyrim:Mara%27s_Eye_Den "Skyrim:Mara's Eye Den"). There is a small rowboat pulled up on the island shore, with a crate, a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sack, and an oar onboard.

## Notes

- With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") Creation, Mara's Eye Pond is a reliable source of [goldfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldfish "Skyrim:Goldfish") for [Catch of the Day](/wiki/Skyrim:Catch_of_the_Day "Skyrim:Catch of the Day") and [Bounty: Goldfish!](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Goldfish! "Skyrim:Bounty: Goldfish!") as a walk through the waters can net you between 3-6 of the flaxen fish.
- The pond gets its name from the way it looks from above. The pond is the shape of an eye and the small island in the middle, Mara's Eye, resembles a pupil.
- This location is only marked as cleared once you also clear Mara's Eye Den.
