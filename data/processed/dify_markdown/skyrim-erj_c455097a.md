Erj

**Erj** is a dead [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatMageConjurer "Skyrim:CombatMageConjurer") found in the [Nchuand-Zel Armory](/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel_Armory "Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel Armory"). His [notes](/wiki/Skyrim:Erj%27s_Notes "Skyrim:Erj's Notes") can be found next to him.

## Related Quests

- **[The Lost Expedition](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition "Skyrim:The Lost Expedition")**: Recover the journals from the expedition in [Nchuand-Zel](/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel "Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel").

## Notes

- Reanimating Erj's corpse will also reanimate Erj's Familiar, a ghost wolf.
