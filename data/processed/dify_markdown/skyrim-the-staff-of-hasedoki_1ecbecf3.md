## Quick Walkthrough

1. Locate and read the *[Smuggler's Trade Notes](/wiki/Skyrim:Smuggler%27s_Trade_Notes "Skyrim:Smuggler's Trade Notes")* at the bandit camp west of [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=22032&y=3456&zoom=17&legacy=true).
2. Obtain the *[Smuggler's Ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Smuggler%27s_Ledger "Skyrim:Smuggler's Ledger")* from [Modig Gilgar](/wiki/Skyrim:Modig_Gilgar "Skyrim:Modig Gilgar") at his besieged cart near the [Western Watchtower](/wiki/Skyrim:Western_Watchtower "Skyrim:Western Watchtower") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-4648&y=-8009).
3. Enter [Brittleshin Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Brittleshin_Pass "Skyrim:Brittleshin Pass") and take the [Staff of Hasedoki](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Hasedoki_(item) "Skyrim:Staff of Hasedoki (item)") from [Ivara of Olenveld](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivara_of_Olenveld "Skyrim:Ivara of Olenveld").

### Smuggled Goods

Travel to an [unmarked bandit camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places#Unmarked_Bandit_and_Forsworn_Camps "Skyrim:Unmarked Places") located beneath a cliff, west of [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=22032&y=3456&zoom=17&legacy=true). Once there, you must deal with the hostile [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") and locate the *[Smuggler's Trade Notes](/wiki/Skyrim:Smuggler%27s_Trade_Notes "Skyrim:Smuggler's Trade Notes")* (not to be confused with the *[Smugglers' Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Smugglers%27_Note "Skyrim:Smugglers' Note")* located nearby). Reading it will reveal that the smugglers were recently approached by an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") looking to purchase the [Staff of Hasedoki](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Hasedoki_(item) "Skyrim:Staff of Hasedoki (item)"), a legendary artifact which they had recently sold to someone else. The smugglers sold this Orc the location of the buyers, planning to intercept and ambush him. Be wary if you are looting the camp, as opening the nearby chest will cause more bandits to spawn.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Hasedoki_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Hasedoki_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Hasedoki_02.jpg)

[Bonemen](/wiki/Skyrim:Boneman "Skyrim:Boneman") fill the passages of Brittleshin Pass

The ambush site is located along the road west of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), next to the ruined house near the [Western Watchtower](/wiki/Skyrim:Western_Watchtower "Skyrim:Western Watchtower") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-4648&y=-8009). The Orc in question, [Modig Gilgar](/wiki/Skyrim:Modig_Gilgar "Skyrim:Modig Gilgar"), will be found standing over the bodies of multiple bandits. He will turn hostile if you get too close, so you are left with the option to either kill him or pickpocket him to obtain the *[Smuggler's Ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Smuggler%27s_Ledger "Skyrim:Smuggler's Ledger")*. Reading this will reveal the location of the buyers: [Ivara of Olenveld](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivara_of_Olenveld "Skyrim:Ivara of Olenveld") and [Lushak gra-Ragdam](/wiki/Skyrim:Lushak_gra-Ragdam "Skyrim:Lushak gra-Ragdam"), located in [Brittleshin Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Brittleshin_Pass "Skyrim:Brittleshin Pass"). Be sure to check Modig's cart for loot, including a copy of *[To Raise the Living](/wiki/Skyrim:To_Raise_the_Living "Skyrim:To Raise the Living")* as well as a container named Modig's Satchel which holds minor loot.

### A Path to the Soul Cairn

You must now venture to [Brittleshin Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Brittleshin_Pass "Skyrim:Brittleshin Pass"), and enter either through its north or south entrance. Taking the southern approach will result in marginally fewer enemies to face. The enemies in question are [bonemen](/wiki/Skyrim:Boneman "Skyrim:Boneman"), which appear in addition to the usual undead [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") encountered in Brittleshin Pass.

You will need to make your way to the inner sanctum, where the boss level [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer") would usually be present. In his place, you will find Lushak gra-Ragdam and Ivara of Olenveld, two necromancers who will be hostile on sight. Beware Ivara, as she wields the Staff of Hasedoki. She can repeatedly use the staff to immobilize you. Once you've defeated them both, the remaining bonemen will disappear, leaving you free to loot and exit the cave. Lushak carries copies of *[Arkay the Enemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Arkay_the_Enemy "Skyrim:Arkay the Enemy")* and *[The Black Arts On Trial](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Arts_On_Trial "Skyrim:The Black Arts On Trial")*, the latter being an [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") skill book. Ivara carries copies of *[The Light and the Dark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Light_and_the_Dark "Skyrim:The Light and the Dark")*, *[Ivara's Notes](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivara%27s_Notes "Skyrim:Ivara's Notes")*, and *[Souls, Black and White](/wiki/Skyrim:Souls,_Black_and_White "Skyrim:Souls, Black and White")*, as well as the Staff itself. Reading her notes will reveal that the two necromancers traveled all the way from the island of [Olenveld](/wiki/Lore:Olenveld "Lore:Olenveld") to open a portal to the [Soul Cairn](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Cairn "Skyrim:Soul Cairn") and offer Hasedoki's soul to earn the favor of the [Ideal Masters](/wiki/Skyrim:Ideal_Master_Crystal "Skyrim:Ideal Master Crystal"). Loot his Staff from Ivara's corpse to end this plot and complete the quest.

The Staff creates a permanent 40-point [ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Ward "Skyrim:Ward") protection while being held, and when cast it will ragdoll and forcefully knock targets backwards. (This includes you during your fight against Ivara.) If the target then dies within 60 seconds, they will be [soul trapped](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Trap_(effect) "Skyrim:Soul Trap (effect)").

## Notes

- Ivara, Lushak, and the three bonemen will spawn in Brittleshin Pass as soon as Modig Gilgar is killed or pickpocketed. Reading the ledger is optional. Brittleshin Pass is not reset by this. If you have not cleared it previously, Ivara will kill the necromancer boss, clearing the dungeon.
- Completing the quest will cause Modig Gilgar and his cart to immediately disappear.
