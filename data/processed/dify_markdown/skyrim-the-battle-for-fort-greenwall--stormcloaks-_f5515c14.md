Stormcloaks and Imperials battling in Fort Greenwall.

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Get your orders from [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist") at the [Rift Stormcloak Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Stormcloak_Camp "Skyrim:Rift Stormcloak Camp").
2. Meet the Stormcloaks south of the fort.
3. Take the fort.

## Detailed Walkthrough

You will only receive this quest if you chose to give [The Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift") to the Imperial Legion during the quest [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"). Go to the [Rift Stormcloak Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Stormcloak_Camp "Skyrim:Rift Stormcloak Camp") and talk to [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist"). Galmar will tell you to go to [Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall "Skyrim:Fort Greenwall") and meet the Stormcloaks preparing for the attack. Meet with the men south of the fort, and then attack the fort. Your journal will keep track of the percentage of enemies left. Once all the Imperials are dead, you will have to head back to [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak") for the next part in the [Liberation of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Liberation_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Liberation of Skyrim") quest.

## Notes

- This quest will take the place of [The Battle for Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Sungard (Stormcloaks)").
- Completing this quest will re-install [Laila Law-Giver](/wiki/Skyrim:Laila_Law-Giver "Skyrim:Laila Law-Giver") as [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of The Rift.
