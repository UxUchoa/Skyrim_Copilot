**The Reach** is a [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in western [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), with [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") as its capital. It is initially affiliated with the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"), but can be captured by the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") after completing [Compelling Tribute](/wiki/Skyrim:Compelling_Tribute_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Compelling Tribute (Stormcloaks)") and [The Battle for Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Sungard (Stormcloaks)"). It can also change sides as part of negotiations during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending").

The terrain is very mountainous, containing the [Druadach Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Mountains "Skyrim:Druadach Mountains"). The far eastern edge of the hold from [Broken Tower Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt "Skyrim:Broken Tower Redoubt") in the north to [Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard "Skyrim:Fort Sungard") in the south is more hilly. The mighty [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River") and several smaller tributaries originate here.

The Reach is home to the native [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"), who will attack any outsiders on sight.

## Flora and Fauna

The most characteristic flora are the [juniper berry bushes](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") which are native to the Reach. [Hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss"), more common to caves, also seems to thrive there. [Mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") and sparse populations of [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Mountain Flower") are to be found throughout the hold but in the northeast the flowers can be quite common.

## Bugs

- Manually mining ore in Cidhna Mine can make it impossible to serve your sentence here properly.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.5, fixes this bug.
- The guard at Soljund's Sinkhole will always be an Empire-affiliated Reach Hold Guard, due to `SoljundSinkholeLocation` missing keywords for changing the garrison if the Stormcloaks take the Reach.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
- The Imperial guard who patrols near Salvius Farm is called "Markarth City Guard", even though he isn't in the city.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The guard is now called "Reach Hold Guard".
- Due to the fact that [Ramati](/wiki/Skyrim:Ramati "Skyrim:Ramati"), [Mani](/wiki/Skyrim:Mani "Skyrim:Mani"), and [Sudi](/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi "Skyrim:Sudi") at [Frostflow Lighthouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostflow_Lighthouse "Skyrim:Frostflow Lighthouse") are part of the Reach crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill them, then you could theoretically receive a bounty here.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
