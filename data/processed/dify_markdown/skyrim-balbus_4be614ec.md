Balbus

**Balbus** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") who can be [encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Finding_the_Gourmet "Skyrim:World Interactions") wandering randomly around Skyrim, gathering [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") for [the Gourmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Balagog_gro-Nolob "Skyrim:Balagog gro-Nolob").

He wears some farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#00017695 "Skyrim:Clothes"), along with a pair of [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers") and a pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"). He carries a copy of *[Uncommon Taste](/wiki/Skyrim:Uncommon_Taste "Skyrim:Uncommon Taste")*, [his fork](/wiki/Skyrim:Balbus%27s_Fork "Skyrim:Balbus's Fork"), some [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), and a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"), as well as the ingredients he is supposed to give you. He wields a [leveled dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") in combat.

## Dialogue

Balbus will occasionally comment:

:   *"You know, I've heard the Gourmet is in Skyrim right now."*

When you initiate conversation with him for the first time, you may greet him, and he'll explain what he's doing:

:   *"I'm collecting ingredients to bring to the Gourmet. He'll think my tastes so refined when I offer them to him. You have heard of the Gourmet?"*

You will now have three dialogue choices, the first two are always available, but the third option is only available if you have completed [Recipe for Disaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_for_Disaster "Skyrim:Recipe for Disaster") and possess the [Gourmet's Writ of Passage](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourmet%27s_Writ_of_Passage "Skyrim:Gourmet's Writ of Passage").

:   **"I have."**
:   *"Then you know that that Gourmet*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *keeps his identity a secret. I'm hoping to discover who he is, so I can tell him how he changed my life. For all I know, the Gourmet is a woman!"*
:   **"Never heard of him."**
:   *"You've not heard of the Gourmet? Author of "[Uncommon Taste](/wiki/Skyrim:Uncommon_Taste "Skyrim:Uncommon Taste")"? He's a wizard with a spatula. Well, not an actual wizard, but his recipes are legendary."*
:   **"I am the Gourmet!" (Show Writ of Passage)**
:   *"Oh my! You? It is an honor! Your food has opened my eyes to the wonders this world can hold. I've cooked every dish in your book, and I must say your brilliance is inspiring. My technique doesn't even approach yours...but whose would?"*

The third option will cause Balbus to continue:

:   *"Allow me to give you this, the fork I used the first time I tasted one of your dishes. Oh, and these ingredients I've been collecting for you. I've risked life and limb so that you may create another masterpiece. Make sure to think of me when you use them!"*

He will give you his fork, a [daedra heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra_Heart "Skyrim:Daedra Heart"), a [spider egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Egg "Skyrim:Spider Egg"), and some [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat").

If the third option has been selected, when leaving him, he may say:

:   *"I can't believe I just met the Gourmet!"*

## Notes

- After your first encounter with him, Balbus may stay where he is for the rest of your game. As this position will normally be a typical location for [random encounters](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions "Skyrim:World Interactions"), he may be killed if you trigger another encounter at that spot.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. He will now unload properly after you leave the area.
  - PC users can prevent his death by using the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to set his [essential](/wiki/Skyrim:Essential_NPCs "Skyrim:Essential NPCs") flag, so random creatures can't kill him.
- The random encounter with Balbus may still not shut down due to the alias not registering itself to stop the scene when Balbus unloads.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. In addition to fixing this, the scene will no longer run if he has given you the Gourmet reward, or if he has been killed at some point.
- He has both of the ingredients needed for [The Cursed Tribe](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe "Skyrim:The Cursed Tribe").
