## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Tonilia](/wiki/Skyrim:Tonilia "Skyrim:Tonilia").
2. Deliver [moon sugar](/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar "Skyrim:Moon Sugar") to [Ri'saad](/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad "Skyrim:Ri'saad").
3. Return to Tonilia.

### A Word with Tonilia

Once you have completed three [reputation quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Special_Job_Quests "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") for the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), speak with [Tonilia](/wiki/Skyrim:Tonilia "Skyrim:Tonilia") in the [Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon"). Before you get a chance to say anything, she will ask you to do a favor for her. The Guild needs a reliable way to transport goods across Skyrim, and Tonilia believes the [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit "Skyrim:Khajiit") [caravans](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_Traders "Skyrim:Khajiit Traders") can be an asset in this regard - if they are willing, that is. She requests that you speak to the leader of the caravans, a Khajiit peddler named [Ri'saad](/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad "Skyrim:Ri'saad") about securing an alliance between the caravans and the Guild. In addition, you are to deliver a [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items "Skyrim:Quest Items") of [moon sugar](/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar "Skyrim:Moon Sugar") to Ri'saad as a gift.

### Finding the Caravan

To find Ri'saad, use the map to fast travel close to his caravan. The caravan travels between [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") and [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"), and stays outside each city for approximately two days before setting out again. Unless the caravan is camped for the night, it will move around. So, expect the target to move while you fast travel, and think about which direction it is traveling in and jump ahead.

### A "Sweet" Deal

After you have located the caravan, speak to Ri'saad, and tell him that you bring an offer from the Thieves Guild. This will cause the Khajiit to become thoughtful. To continue, hand him the moon sugar, which he will gratefully accept, saying that he looks forward to the partnership. Ri'saad will also comment that he and his fellow caravans will buy any stolen goods brought to them.

### Return to Tonilia

To finish the quest, return to Tonilia. She will thank you for your help and reward you with a leveled amount of gold, as detailed below. In addition, the Khajiit [Atahbah](/wiki/Skyrim:Atahbah "Skyrim:Atahbah"), [Ma'jhad](/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27jhad "Skyrim:Ma'jhad"), and [Zaynabi](/wiki/Skyrim:Zaynabi "Skyrim:Zaynabi") will now fence stolen goods for you.

## Notes

- When not on the road, the caravan can be found either across the road from [the stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables "Skyrim:Whiterun Stables") outside Whiterun, or just east of [Salvius Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Salvius_Farm "Skyrim:Salvius Farm") outside Markarth.
- The Khajiit do not become available as fences until after you have returned to Tonilia and finished the quest.
- Atahbah is a member of Ri'saad's caravan, as above. Ma'jhad's caravan travels between [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") and [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), and Zaynabi's caravan takes a route between [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") and [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").
