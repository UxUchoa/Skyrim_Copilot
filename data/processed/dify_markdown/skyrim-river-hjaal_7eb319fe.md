The **River Hjaal** is the main river that flows through the center of [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch").

This river's headwaters are located in the highlands south of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal"), along the border between Hjaalmarch and [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold"). After dropping down a series of waterfalls, the river passes through Morthal, then spreads out into a wide marshy region that eventually drains into the [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River"), which flows into the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts").

The river is inhabited by many species of fish, such as [Abecean Longfin](/wiki/Skyrim:Abecean_Longfin "Skyrim:Abecean Longfin"), [Cyrodilic Spadetail](/wiki/Skyrim:Cyrodilic_Spadetail "Skyrim:Cyrodilic Spadetail"), [Histcarp](/wiki/Skyrim:Histcarp "Skyrim:Histcarp"), [River Betty](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Betty "Skyrim:River Betty"), [Silverside Perch](/wiki/Skyrim:Silverside_Perch "Skyrim:Silverside Perch"), and [Salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(creature) "Skyrim:Salmon (creature)").
