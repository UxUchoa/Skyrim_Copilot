Badnir

**Badnir** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") trying to make a living in [Whistling Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Whistling_Mine "Skyrim:Whistling Mine"). He blindly listens to [Thorgar](/wiki/Skyrim:Thorgar "Skyrim:Thorgar"), and continues to work the mine since Thorgar really needs him.

He will sleep in one of the bed rolls at 8am for 12 hours, after which he will wander the mine, mining the few ore veins, using the cooking pot, or sitting at the table.

He wears a set of [hide armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor") and a matching pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"). He is equipped with an [iron warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer "Skyrim:Iron Warhammer") and carries some [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

When spoken to, Badnir will discuss the mine with:

:   *"It's cold in the mine, but Thorgar really needs me, so I stay."*
:   *"Thorgar says we'll find rocks soon, the kind worth gold."*
:   *"I'm the best at breaking up rocks. Thorgar says so."*
