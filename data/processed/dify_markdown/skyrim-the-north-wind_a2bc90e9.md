## The North Wind

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-place-Windhelm_Fishing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Windhelm_Fishing.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Windhelm_Fishing.jpg)

Fishing supplies

The vessel has several crates and unusable barrels on deck, along with a couple of chairs. You will find both the captain, [Kjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjar "Skyrim:Kjar"), and [Dalan Merchad](/wiki/Skyrim:Dalan_Merchad "Skyrim:Dalan Merchad") on deck at all times; however, the other member of the crew, [Voldsea Giryon](/wiki/Skyrim:Voldsea_Giryon "Skyrim:Voldsea Giryon"), will be found wandering [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), and will happily speak to you, explaining that she's glad they are laying over in Windhelm for a while, and that she likes to walk when on land to keep her legs strong. Speaking with [Dalan Merchad](/wiki/Skyrim:Dalan_Merchad "Skyrim:Dalan Merchad"), on the other hand, has him complaining and wishing they were underway. The captain will ask you to assist him with a [problem](/wiki/Skyrim:Harsh_Master "Skyrim:Harsh Master"), and will reward you with a year's salary (a leveled amount of gold) if you accept and complete the quest.
