Stonehill Bluff

**Stonehill Bluff** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") south-southwest of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

## Walkthrough

The camp is at the end of a ravine, with the easiest approach from the east. If *[Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")* is installed and [Heljarchen Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Heljarchen_Hall "Skyrim:Heljarchen Hall") has been built, a short distance uphill west of the hall is a path leading west into the ravine. A [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") guards the ravine. The path features the painted stones, [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes"), and mammoth ribs and skulls typical of giant camps. As you follow the ravine into the camp, you will find a patch of [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap") near a patch of [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), with two more white caps in the camp itself.

Two giants (including the guard in the ravine) occupy the camp, which is in a basin surrounded by large rock formations. There are two large campfires with a total of six [charred skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") cooking on fire-hardened stakes. Seven [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl") and two unlocked [chests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") are found in the camp, with one chest at the northern end of the basin and the other against the rocks to the east.
