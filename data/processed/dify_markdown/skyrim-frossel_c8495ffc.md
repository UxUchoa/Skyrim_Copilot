Frossel

**Frossel** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") on a peninsula north of [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village") containing [bristlebacks](/wiki/Skyrim:Bristleback "Skyrim:Bristleback") and [rieklings](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling "Skyrim:Riekling"). It contains only one zone, *[Frossel](#Frossel)*.

## Related Quests

- **[Pain in the Necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Pain_in_the_Necklace "Skyrim:Pain in the Necklace")**: Find [East Empire pendants](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Pendant "Skyrim:East Empire Pendant") for [Fethis Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fethis_Alor "Skyrim:Fethis Alor").

### Exterior

There are eight [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Spiky_Grass "Skyrim:Spiky Grass") plants producing [ashen grass pods](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Ashen Grass Pod"), a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") plant, and three [pearl oysters](/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl_Oyster "Skyrim:Pearl Oyster") on the shore around the entrance. There is also a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") to the right of the entrance next to a crate. Just south of the entrance are two dead [reavers](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver "Skyrim:Reaver") on the shore, among the remains of a camp with a boat, a [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein"), and a few [riekling spears](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Spear "Skyrim:Riekling Spear") scattered about.

### Frossel

Frossel begins as an ice tunnel descending to the east. On the left side of the tunnel as you descend is a crate with a [deer pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt "Skyrim:Deer Pelt") and a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") on top. Near the bottom is an ice pillar in the middle of the tunnel, with a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") on a crate beside it. When the tunnel turns to the south, look to the corner for a barrel with a piece of [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") on top. There are many [cups](/wiki/Skyrim:Cup "Skyrim:Cup"), [goblets](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"), [plates](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate"), [jugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug"), and other crockery and clutter throughout the cave.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-interior-Frossel.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Frossel.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Frossel.jpg)

Riekling camp

The tunnel opens into a chamber with four rieklings and two bristlebacks. A small fenced ledge in front of you holds a piece of [refined moonstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Refined_Moonstone "Skyrim:Refined Moonstone") and a deer pelt. The path down is to the left. On the left as you reach the cavern floor are two barrels; one is a fish barrel, and each has a sample of [hawk feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk_Feathers "Skyrim:Hawk Feathers") on top. Beneath the ledge via which you entered the chamber, among some riekling tents, crates, sacks, and barrels, there is a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat"), a pair of [fine boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots "Skyrim:Fine Boots"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), two [chicken breasts](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken_Breast "Skyrim:Chicken Breast"), a set of [small antlers](/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Antlers "Skyrim:Small Antlers"), a pair of [iron gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets"), four [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot") (see bugs), three samples of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), a riekling hut, and two barrels containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients").

To the right of the riekling hut is an unlocked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"), a sample of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"), a drum, an empty [common soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem"), and a piece of [moonstone ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore") all on what appears to be an archery target laid on a rock, with a piece of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") on the ground in front. The chamber continues to the east. To the left of a bristleback pen is an ice pillar, with two unusable barrels beside it. On these barrels is a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a plate with a rare [beehive husk](/wiki/Skyrim:Beehive_Husk "Skyrim:Beehive Husk") on top. To the right of the pen is another tent with a deer pelt, two iron ingots, a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") (see bugs), and a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek").

A tunnel exits to the east. Along the tunnel are five bottles of wine, including one inside a bucket. On the right just before a corner is another unlocked strongbox on a rock. Farther along are more crates and barrels; among these, you will find a drum, a piece of firewood, three pieces of [charcoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal "Skyrim:Charcoal"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and a [silver ingot](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-interior-Frossel_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Frossel_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Frossel_04.jpg)

Claw trap

A [tripwire](/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire "Skyrim:Tripwire") at the bottom of the tunnel triggers a hinged [claw](/wiki/Skyrim:Claw "Skyrim:Claw") trap. To the left is an unlocked chest flanked by two [riekling barrels](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Barrel "Skyrim:Riekling Barrel"), which will burst open to reveal two rieklings. A set of small antlers and a [cup](/wiki/Skyrim:Cup "Skyrim:Cup") will get scattered when the barrels burst. Behind the chest is a fish barrel, and to the right, a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") lies on top of another, empty chest. In the easternmost section of this chamber is a tent, with another riekling ready to ambush you from beneath the snow in front of the tent. Inside the tent, among unusable barrels and crates is a large coin purse and a leveled [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"). To the right of the tent, on a crate is a [quicksilver ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore") and a [chicken's egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") in a wooden bowl. To the right on another crate is a random [spell tome](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tomes "Skyrim:Spell Tomes"),[†](#intnote_1) and on the ground is an expert-locked [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox").

Opposite the two riekling barrels to the south is an empty chest to the left of a [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein"). To the right of the vein is a tent with crates and an unusable barrel inside. On the barrel is a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") that can be harvested, and on a crate is an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and three loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). Opposite the tripwire to the south is a riekling hut, with a barrel containing ingredients beside it. The tunnel continues to the west, past an empty chest. After another riekling barrel ambush next to a barrel of meat, the tunnel turns to the south. There are two rieklings in the small, narrow chamber ahead. A large coin purse is on a rock to the right. In a short alcove on the left, there's a riekling barrel in front of an unlocked chest. The unique [Imperial war horn](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_War_Horn "Skyrim:Imperial War Horn") is found here, along with a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"), and seven loose coins.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-interior-Frossel_02.png/200px-SR-interior-Frossel_02.png)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Frossel_02.png)

Strange sculpture

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-interior-Frossel_03.png/200px-SR-interior-Frossel_03.png)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Frossel_03.png)

Worshippers

At the next corner, there is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") on the ground (see bugs) and a pair of random [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets "Skyrim:Gauntlets") in an open crate. Farther along are two bowls on a crate next to a food barrel; one bowl holds four [small pearls](/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Pearl "Skyrim:Small Pearl"), and the other holds a [Nordic barnacle](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle") and a [pearl](/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl "Skyrim:Pearl"). On top of the barrel is a sample of [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales"), as well as two [slaughterfish eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg") in another bowl. The tunnel then opens to the main chamber of the cave.

In the main chamber is a curious sight: a group of seven rieklings worshipping a strange idol. A mounted riekling observes from the side. Following the ledge along the eastern wall to the right, you will come upon a riekling hut and a sample of wheat in an adjacent tent. To the left at the entrance is a ramp down to the cavern floor. Any sounds of fighting may draw two more rieklings from a passage beyond. Under the ledge in the northwest section of the chamber is a boss chest near many crates, barrels, and sacks, among which are a total of sixteen loose coins, five silver ingots, two [garnets](/wiki/Skyrim:Garnet "Skyrim:Garnet"), a [silver garnet ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Garnet_Ring "Skyrim:Silver Garnet Ring"), two [silver rings](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ring "Skyrim:Silver Ring"), four small coin purses, three [silver necklaces](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Necklace "Skyrim:Silver Necklace"), a [gold ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ring "Skyrim:Gold Ring"), two [gold necklaces](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Necklace "Skyrim:Gold Necklace"), a [corundum ingot](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ingot "Skyrim:Corundum Ingot"), a pair of fine boots, a pair of fine shoes, two sets of [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"), two fish barrels, a random enchanted [weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons"), a [fine hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Hat "Skyrim:Fine Hat"), a candlestick, two empty, open chests, a [gold ingot](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ingot "Skyrim:Gold Ingot"), a [Dibella statue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dibella_Statue "Skyrim:Dibella Statue"), and four barrels containing ingredients.

To the southeast, past a tent that contains nothing of interest, is an open pen with a mead barrel in the corner against the ledge, with three bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), a woodcutter's axe, a random shield, and a [ruby](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby "Skyrim:Ruby") lying on the ground near it. To the southwest, between the pen and a gold ore vein and on top of some barrels, there is a broken cart holding five pieces of firewood, a gourd, and a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"). Northwest of the cart is a ramp leading up to the ledge with the strange idol on top, and to the left of the ramp is another cart containing three pieces of iron ore and a leather strip. To the right of the gold ore vein is a pickaxe on a barrel, and farther to the right is a tent containing clutter. Continuing along the wall to the right, there is a barrel with a gourd and two [raw rabbit legs](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg") on top, and farther along another stack of crates with several sacks and more clutter among them.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-interior-Frossel_05.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Frossel_05.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Frossel_05.jpg)

Treasure

To the left of where the rieklings are worshipping is a flat-topped rock with several crates and barrels around it. There is a deer pelt, an empty soul gem, and a leather strip here, while on top of the rock is a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") in a basket. In the western corner of the main chamber, near the top of the ramp leading up to the strange idol, there is a [steel ingot](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot "Skyrim:Steel Ingot") on a barrel.

An icy tunnel exits the main chamber from the ledge to the north. A short way up the tunnel is a chicken's egg in a wooden bowl on the ground, with a fish barrel and a crate on your left, followed by a gold ore vein on the west wall. The path ascends to a switchback, then up to a second switchback, where a wooden block holds an iron dagger, a bulb of garlic, two samples of [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales"), and a [slaughterfish egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg"). On the west wall here is another gold ore vein. Continuing up the path, you will pass some barrels and crates with a [snow fox pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Fox Pelt") and a cabbage on top, plus three random potions in an open box. The path continues to the top, where there is a bowl containing a leek, with a piece of [corundum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore "Skyrim:Corundum Ore") and a couple cheese wheels beside it. There is an ice tunnel heading to the southeast, with a bowl on the ground containing a red apple, and farther along another goat cheese wheel and a cast iron pot with a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg") inside. The tunnel ends at a ledge above the first chamber near the exit. To the left on the ledge before you drop down is a drum and a deer pelt.

## Notes

[†](#note_1)The appearance of this spell tome is complicated. The tome is first randomly selected from all expert-level tomes, then your level in the corresponding skill is checked, and the tome will only appear if your relevant skill is 65 or above. So, unless all your magic skills are at least 65, there is a chance you will not see a tome here.

- Under the ramp leading up to the alcove overlooking the first chamber of the cave is a unique glazed urn which cannot be picked up. However, a jug with a similar appearance can be found in the middle of the chamber.
- An unused safe internally known as `DLC2FrosselSafewithLock` was once intended to appear in this cave. This unique safe would have contained a [fork](/wiki/Skyrim:Fork_(item) "Skyrim:Fork (item)"), a [knife](/wiki/Skyrim:Knife_(item) "Skyrim:Knife (item)"), [Redguard clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Clothes "Skyrim:Redguard Clothes"), a [Redguard hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Hood "Skyrim:Redguard Hood"), [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000e0dd4 "Skyrim:Boots"), and a [scimitar](/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar "Skyrim:Scimitar"). These items would have formed a reference to [Nazir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazir "Skyrim:Nazir"), the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") assassin.
- In [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
- Frossel means "frostbite" in Norwegian.
- Frossel also [appears](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Frossel "Bloodmoon:Frossel") in *[Bloodmoon](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon "Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon")*.
