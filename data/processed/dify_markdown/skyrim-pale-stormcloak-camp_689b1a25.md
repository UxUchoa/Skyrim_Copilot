Pale Stormcloak Camp

**Pale Stormcloak Camp** is a [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Camps "Skyrim:Stormcloak Camps") in [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale"), located southeast of [Bronze Water Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Bronze_Water_Cave "Skyrim:Bronze Water Cave") on the south shore of [Lake Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Yorgrim "Skyrim:Lake Yorgrim").

This camp will only be present if the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") hold [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") and the rest of the Pale, whether through martial might or via the negotiation during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"). [Stormcloak soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Soldier "Skyrim:Stormcloak Soldier") are camped here under the command of [Frorkmar Banner-Torn](/wiki/Skyrim:Frorkmar_Banner-Torn "Skyrim:Frorkmar Banner-Torn"), except during the related quest, when [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist") takes command. Like similar military camps that only exist when the hold has changed hands, it has no [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse").

## Pale Stormcloak Camp

It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. Around the campfire are three small tents containing unowned hay piles. Northwest of the campfire is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and an end table. To the left of the commander's tent is the quartermaster's tent and the workstation for the [Stormcloak quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Stormcloak Quartermaster"), consisting of an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"). A long wooden table in front of the quartermaster's tent and the tent itself hold various [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") and [steel weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") and [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor"). To the right of the commander's tent is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). North of the campfire is the command tent, containing a lit brazier, a round table, and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of their time. Northeast of the campfire is the hospital tent, which contains three hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers and a low table holding a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). To the left of the hospital tent is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs"). To the right of the hospital tent are some barrels. Northwest of the command tent on the shore of the lake is a [clay deposit](/wiki/Skyrim:Clay_Deposit "Skyrim:Clay Deposit")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") and an [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") vein, while to the east-southeast is a second iron ore vein.

Any time [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale") is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Legion "Skyrim:Joining the Legion"), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.
