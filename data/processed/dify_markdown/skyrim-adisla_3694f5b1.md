**Adisla** is an aging [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") working the land for [Belyn Hlaalu](/wiki/Skyrim:Belyn_Hlaalu "Skyrim:Belyn Hlaalu") at [his farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Hlaalu_Farm "Skyrim:Hlaalu Farm") east of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

Adisla crawls out of her bed at 6am and spends the first hour of the day wandering around the [farmhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Hlaalu_Farm "Skyrim:Hlaalu Farm"), then sits down and enjoys a one-hour breakfast. At 8am she heads outside and starts working the land. She keeps going for twelve hours until 8pm when she heads back inside for a one-hour dinner. She spends the rest of the evening performing miscellaneous tasks in the farmhouse until she finally goes to bed at midnight.

She wears a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000209a6 "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000209a5 "Skyrim:Clothing"). She carries an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") to defend herself and a copy of the [Hlaalu Farmhouse Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Hlaalu_Farmhouse_Key "Skyrim:Hlaalu Farmhouse Key"). Besides those, she also has a selection of [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists") in her inventory.

## Dialogue

Speaking to Adisla will have her provide a few comments about herself:

:   *"Starting to get too old to work all day, but hopefully Master Hlaalu will let me slow down a bit."*
:   *"Every now and then someone from the city comes by. Think they aren't used to seeing a Nord working for a Dark Elf."*
:   *"Better to work in the cold than in the heat, I say. Though some sun would be nice."*

## Conversations

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-npc-Belyn_Hlaalu_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Belyn_Hlaalu_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Belyn_Hlaalu_02.jpg)

Adisla and Belyn

Adisla will talk with her employer, Belyn, on a few occasions. One will have Adisla ask him about a wheat shipment:

:   **Adisla**: *"I was wondering if you had ordered the shipment of that frostwheat, sir."*
:   **Belyn**: *"Yes, but that fool [Endario](/wiki/Skyrim:Orthus_Endario "Skyrim:Orthus Endario") can't get it in. I'll thrash him when I get back to the city, don't you worry."*
:   **Adisla**: *"I wasn't wanting to get anyone in trouble, sir. Just wanting to get the new planting started, is all."*
:   **Belyn**: *"Yes, wouldn't want to miss the lush summer we have ahead."*

In another conversation between the two, Belyn will order Adisla to perform repairs around the farm, and criticize her resource usage:

:   **Belyn**: *"Make sure the back fence is repaired. I swear I saw some [skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever") in nibbling the leaves yesterday. "*
:   **Adisla**: *"Oh, no skeevers out here, sir. They can't stand the cold."*
:   **Belyn**: *"Maybe, but the way you burn through all that wood to keep the cabin toasty, they might mistake this place for [Elsweyr](/wiki/Lore:Elsweyr "Lore:Elsweyr")."*
:   **Adisla**: *"Sorry, sir. These old bones do like a bit of warmth in the evening. There's no need to make fun."*
:   **Belyn**: *"You just need to remember that there's a war on, and firewood isn't cheap these days."*

In another, Belyn will ask her about [Captain Lonely-Gale](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Lonely-Gale "Skyrim:Captain Lonely-Gale"):

:   **Belyn**: *"So have you ever actually spoken to Captain Lonely-Gale?"*
:   **Adisla**: *"By the gods, he's just a retired ship's captain."*
:   **Belyn**: *"Maybe to you, but he's just so... his story is..."*
:   **Adisla**: *"He's just a guard captain. And not a very good one, at that."*
