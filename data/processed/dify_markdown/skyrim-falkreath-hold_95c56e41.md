**Falkreath Hold** is a [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in southern [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), with its capital in [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). It is initially affiliated with the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"), but can be captured by the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") after completing [Rescue from Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Rescue from Fort Neugrad"). It can also change sides as part of negotiations during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending").

It is the second southernmost hold in Skyrim, after [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"). It borders [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil") in the south, across the [Jerall Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Jerall_Mountains "Skyrim:Jerall Mountains"), and [Hammerfell](/wiki/Lore:Hammerfell "Lore:Hammerfell") in the west. Other than its capital, Falkreath, the only notable town in the hold is [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen"), the first settlement in Skyrim to be attacked by a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon").

Falkreath Hold is blanketed by characteristic pine forests and described by its citizens as being covered in a fine mist that makes it seem seasonless. The mountains rising above Falkreath are well above the snow line striking a stark comparison to the green of the rest of the hold. The most distinguishing geographic feature is [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta") which covers a large portion of the central region and acts as the source for the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River").

The town has the largest cemetery in Skyrim that houses generations of the dead, due to a large number of battles that have taken place over the years. The citizens take pride in this and many of the local shops and the farm derive their names from their close proximity to so much death.

## Flora and Fauna

The pine forest of Falkreath is very rich in [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle"), [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella"), and all common varieties of [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Mountain Flower"). [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta") is home to a large population of different [fish species](/wiki/Skyrim:Fish "Skyrim:Fish") which can be found near [the Lady Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lady_Stone "Skyrim:The Lady Stone") and by the start of the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River"). The hold is one of the few places where [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") is fairly common. In the snowy eastern parts only the [snowberry bush](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") thrives.

## Falkreath Hold Quests

All quests that are started in or are related to Falkreath Hold (except for radiant quests begun in other holds) are listed here:

### Main Quest

- **[Unbound](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound "Skyrim:Unbound")**: Escape [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") custody and a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") attack.
- **[Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (quest)")**: Retrieve the [Dragonstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonstone "Skyrim:Dragonstone").

### Stormcloaks Civil War Quests

- **[Liberation of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Liberation_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Liberation of Skyrim")**: Drive the Imperials from Skyrim. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Rescue from Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Rescue from Fort Neugrad")**: Rescue [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") soldiers from the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") and capture the [fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Fort Neugrad"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

### Daedric Quests

- **[Boethiah's Calling](/wiki/Skyrim:Boethiah%27s_Calling "Skyrim:Boethiah's Calling")**: Become [Boethiah](/wiki/Lore:Boethiah "Lore:Boethiah")'s new champion.
- **[A Daedra's Best Friend](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Daedra%27s_Best_Friend "Skyrim:A Daedra's Best Friend")**: Help [Barbas](/wiki/Skyrim:Barbas "Skyrim:Barbas") reunite with his [master](/wiki/Lore:Clavicus_Vile "Lore:Clavicus Vile").
- **[Ill Met By Moonlight](/wiki/Skyrim:Ill_Met_By_Moonlight "Skyrim:Ill Met By Moonlight")**: Participate in the Daedric Prince [Hircine](/wiki/Skyrim:Hircine "Skyrim:Hircine")'s Great Hunt.
- **[Pieces of the Past](/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past "Skyrim:Pieces of the Past")**: Retrieve the scattered pieces of [Mehrunes' Razor](/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor "Skyrim:Mehrunes' Razor") and reforge this deadly blade.

### Bards College

- **[Rjorn's Drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Rjorn%27s_Drum "Skyrim:Rjorn's Drum")**: Retrieve a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items#Rjorn.27s_Drum "Skyrim:Quest Items") for [Giraud Gemane](/wiki/Skyrim:Giraud_Gemane "Skyrim:Giraud Gemane").

### Dark Brotherhood

- All the Dark Brotherhood primary quests except for [Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!"), and most of the other quests received before Hail Sithis!, are related to Falkreath Hold.

### Thieves Guild

- **[Darkness Returns](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkness_Returns "Skyrim:Darkness Returns")**: Walk the [Pilgrim's Path](/wiki/Skyrim:Twilight_Sepulcher "Skyrim:Twilight Sepulcher") and return the [Skeleton Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton_Key "Skyrim:Skeleton Key") to its rightful place.
- **[No Stone Unturned](/wiki/Skyrim:No_Stone_Unturned "Skyrim:No Stone Unturned")**: Find the [24 gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem") to restore the Crown of Barenziah.

### Companions

- **[Blood's Honor](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood%27s_Honor "Skyrim:Blood's Honor")**: Claim the head of a [Glenmoril Witch](/wiki/Skyrim:Glenmoril_Witch "Skyrim:Glenmoril Witch") for [Kodlak Whitemane](/wiki/Skyrim:Kodlak_Whitemane "Skyrim:Kodlak Whitemane").

### Side Quests including Divine Quests

- **[The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature")**: Restore the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") for [Danica Pure-Spring](/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring "Skyrim:Danica Pure-Spring").
- **[The Golden Claw](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Golden_Claw "Skyrim:The Golden Claw")**: A [shopkeeper](/wiki/Skyrim:Lucan_Valerius "Skyrim:Lucan Valerius") wants you to retrieve his [prized possession](/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Claw "Skyrim:Golden Claw") from a [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Arvel_the_Swift "Skyrim:Arvel the Swift").
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials")**: Participate in [Kyne](/wiki/Lore:Kyne "Lore:Kyne")'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").
- **[Moss Mother Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest) "Skyrim:Moss Mother Cavern (quest)")**: Help an [injured hunter](/wiki/Skyrim:Valdr "Skyrim:Valdr") avenge his fallen companions.
