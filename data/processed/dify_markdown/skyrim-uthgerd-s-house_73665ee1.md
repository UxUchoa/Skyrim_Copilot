Uthgerd's House

**Uthgerd's House** is the home of [Uthgerd the Unbroken](/wiki/Skyrim:Uthgerd_the_Unbroken "Skyrim:Uthgerd the Unbroken"). It is located in the Wind District of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), across the street from [Carlotta Valentia's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House "Skyrim:Carlotta Valentia's House").

It is owned by [Uthgerd the Unbroken](/wiki/Skyrim:Uthgerd_the_Unbroken "Skyrim:Uthgerd the Unbroken"), although she rarely sets foot in the house, spending most of her time inside [The Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare"). It consists of one zone, *[Uthgerd's House](#Uthgerd.27s_House)*. She will always be in residence during the [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Imperial) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Imperial)") and may be found there at other times.

### Exterior

It is the first house on the right as you enter the Wind District of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") from the stairs nearest the gate. There are three [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") plants around the house which is attached to [Amren's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Amren%27s_House "Skyrim:Amren's House").

### Uthgerd's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-interior-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg)

Inside Uthgerd's House

The front door opens to the living room, with a ladder leading to the bedroom upstairs. Left of the door sits a small table with a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") on top. At the foot of the ladder is a dresser with some random [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing"). Under the ladder are three [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks, two food barrels, a bowl with four [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), an end table with clutter, and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") with a bit of gold. In the northwestern corner is a dinner table that seats three, laid with a bottle of ale and a piece of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). In the adjacent corner is a cupboard with clutter, while the shelves above hold a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") and an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"). At the foot of the cupboard, scattered on the floor, are three [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"). A small table in front of the fireplace holds a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), and a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"). The alcove to the south has a small cupboard with clutter with four [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato") on top. A bookshelf holds several [common books](/wiki/Skyrim:Books "Skyrim:Books").

The ladder leads upstairs where a wardrobe contains some clutter, clothes, and a slim chance of some [jewelry](/wiki/Skyrim:Jewelry "Skyrim:Jewelry"), while an unlocked chest holds a bit of gold. A display case contains two [iron daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). The bedroom has a dresser with random clothing and the end table near the bed contains clutter. At the foot of the table sits a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#Boots "Skyrim:Clothing") and on top of the table is a bottle of alto wine and a bowl with five [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple").

## Notes

- Once you recruit Uthgerd as a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"), all items, including the bed, are free to use.
- There is an [![Empty](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/SR-icon-shadowmark-Empty.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Empty.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Empty") Empty [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Shadowmarks "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") to the left of the entrance door due to the sparseness of loot; however, the location will no longer be so empty if you are on one of [Vex's](/wiki/Skyrim:Vex "Skyrim:Vex") burglary or sweep jobs.
