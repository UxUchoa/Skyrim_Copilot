The Pawned Prawn

The **Pawned Prawn** is a general goods store in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). The store is owned and run by [Bersi Honey-Hand](/wiki/Skyrim:Bersi_Honey-Hand "Skyrim:Bersi Honey-Hand"), although his wife [Drifa](/wiki/Skyrim:Drifa "Skyrim:Drifa") will take over running the store if anything happens to Bersi. It is located next door to [Black-Briar Meadery](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Meadery "Skyrim:Black-Briar Meadery"), to the west of [The Bee and Barb](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bee_and_Barb "Skyrim:The Bee and Barb") near the center of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften").

### Pawned Prawn

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-interior-Pawned_Prawn.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Pawned_Prawn.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pawned_Prawn.jpg)

The main shop

Upon entering the shop, you will find that the room is also their main living room, where they cook and dine. There is a fireplace opposite the door on the west wall, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front of it. Against the south wall is a cupboard holding [displayed merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise), including some [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), and to the left is a round wooden table holding a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), and two chairs.

The counter is L-shaped and comes out from the north wall, and holds various displayed merchandise, including a couple [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot") and an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"). Also on the counter are three loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") and a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:An_Apology "Skyrim:An Apology") from [Wilhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Wilhelm "Skyrim:Wilhelm") in [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"), who refuses to go into [Shroud Hearth Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Shroud_Hearth_Barrow "Skyrim:Shroud Hearth Barrow"). This note is here regardless of whether you have cleared the barrow or not.

There is a set of shelves in front of the counter against the north wall that holds only display merchandise and clutter. To the right, against the west wall is a dresser with a Dwarven Urn on top that can be used as a bargaining piece when trying to complete the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Taking_Care_of_Business "Skyrim:Taking Care of Business").

Behind the counter, against the west wall is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot, with a wall shelf above holding displayed merchandise, with two pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") between the fireplace and chest. Above the counter is a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, three bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), all hanging from the ceiling.

### Cellar

There is a door to the north that leads to the residential area. Beyond the door are three [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels, with a fourth over a flight of stairs leading down to the cellar. Under the stairs is a pile of firewood and a couple straw bales, with a dresser to the left, against the west wall, holding a bottle of wine. Another food barrel is in the southwestern corner, with a set of shelves to the left, against the south wall. The shelves hold five [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a couple loaves of bread, and several pieces of cheese, with an adept-locked [safe](/wiki/Skyrim:Safe "Skyrim:Safe") to the left. The safe contains minor loot, plus several copies of a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:To_A_Concerned_Citizen "Skyrim:To A Concerned Citizen") that doesn't seem to have been used in a quest. Beside the safe is a doorway to the south that leads into [Bersi's](/wiki/Skyrim:Bersi_Honey-Hand "Skyrim:Bersi Honey-Hand") and [Drifa's](/wiki/Skyrim:Drifa "Skyrim:Drifa") bedroom. It contains a double bed coming out of the middle of the south wall, with an end table to the left which holds the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"), and a wall shelf above holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). At the foot of the bed is a dresser with an apprentice-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") on top. A chair is against the north wall, and a wardrobe containing [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") is in the northwestern corner.
