Ancient's Ascent

**Ancient's Ascent** is a mountaintop [dragon lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Lairs "Skyrim:Dragon Lairs") southwest of [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen"). Its [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") teaches part of the [Animal Allegiance](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Allegiance "Skyrim:Animal Allegiance") [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-word_wall-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg/200px-SR-word_wall-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-word_wall-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg)

Word wall

The route to the word wall is through [Bonechill Passage](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonechill_Passage "Skyrim:Bonechill Passage"), a short cave. The word wall can also be reached by using a [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses") to climb the mountains around the area or on foot by approaching from [Bloodlet Throne](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodlet_Throne "Skyrim:Bloodlet Throne"). There are three [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") plants to the right of the path after exiting Bonechill Passage. There are a couple wide flights of stairs leading up to a plateau in front of the word wall, with lit braziers at the bottom and top of each flight.

Before the [dragons' return](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising"), the word wall will be guarded by a few leveled enemies.

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon"), which will attack as soon as you approach from the cave.

Regardless of whether [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") has been completed, the boss chest is by the base of the platform holding the word wall.

## Notes

- Ancient's Ascent is one of the locations suggested by [Marise Aravel](/wiki/Skyrim:Marise_Aravel "Skyrim:Marise Aravel") as a place to find [ice wraith teeth](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth "Skyrim:Ice Wraith Teeth") for her [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Cold "Skyrim:Ice Cold").
- [Climbing](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing "Skyrim:Mountain Climbing") the rocks behind the wall provides a shortcut to [Bloodlet Throne](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodlet_Throne "Skyrim:Bloodlet Throne").
