## Quick Walkthrough

1. Enter [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)") and go up to the main floor.
2. (Optional) Talk to [Talvas Fathryon](/wiki/Skyrim:Talvas_Fathryon "Skyrim:Talvas Fathryon") as you exit Tel Mithryn.
3. Kill the [ash guardian](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Guardian "Skyrim:Ash Guardian") near the tower.
4. Return to Talvas Fathryon for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-npc-Talvas_Fathryon_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Talvas_Fathryon_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Talvas_Fathryon_02.jpg)

*"It's on your head if something goes wrong."*

When you arrive in Tel Mithryn, you'll find [Talvas Fathryon](/wiki/Skyrim:Talvas_Fathryon "Skyrim:Talvas Fathryon") and [Varona Nelas](/wiki/Skyrim:Varona_Nelas "Skyrim:Varona Nelas") in the middle of an argument. Talvas is attempting to summon an [ash guardian](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Guardian "Skyrim:Ash Guardian"), but is having no luck. Enter the [main tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)") of Tel Mithryn, go up to the main floor, then go back outside. As soon as you leave, you'll see Talvas' summoning has finally worked. He'll run to you in a panic: the ash guardian he got is hostile!

You'll have several reply options. However, even if you tell him to clean up his own mess it's no use: the quest will start and the enemy nearby will disable fast travel. Head down the stipe of the mushroom and confront the ash guardian out in front of Tel Mithryn. Be prepared for a tough fight at lower levels: this creature is level 30 and spews ash at long range that is also wide-arcing.

Once the wayward summon is defeated, you'll have to talk to Talvas again. He's fled into Neloth's tower, so go find him. *"What a relief,"* he says once you have reported in. *"I don't have any gold I can pay you with, but I can give you this staff."* He will then prove true to his word and present you with a [staff](/wiki/Skyrim:Staves "Skyrim:Staves"). In addition, you can now purchase the spell tome for Conjure Ash Guardian from him and recruit him as a follower.

## Notes

- You must go up to the main floor of Tel Mithryn to advance this quest—merely entering and exiting the tower does not work.
- You don't have to speak with Talvas when he's summoned the Ash Guardian. You can, for example, fast travel away and when you come back, you can kill the Ash Guardian without ever speaking with Talvas.
- The Ash Guardian does not leave any remains behind after its death.
- If you traded with Talvas outside, you will have to wait for him to restock his inventory before the Conjure Ash Guardian spell book becomes available.
- If the Ash Guardian hasn't yet been destroyed, you can tell Neloth "Talvas summoned an Ash Guardian outside." Neloth will reply *"What? That fool. His incompetence made this mess. Let's see how he decides to clean it up."*
- If your [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") skill is at least 65, Talvas gives you your choice of a random staff or the spell tome for Conjure Ash Guardian.
- If you have a follower with you when the quest has begun, they may kill the Ash Guardian while you and Talvas are talking to each other. You should be able to talk to him immediately after that to get your reward.
