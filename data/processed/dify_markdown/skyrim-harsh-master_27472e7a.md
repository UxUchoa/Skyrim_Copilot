## Radiant Options

This is a radiant quest which will send you to one of the following bandit camps: [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern"), [Gallows Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock "Skyrim:Gallows Rock"), [Lost Knife Hideout](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Knife_Hideout "Skyrim:Lost Knife Hideout"), [Stony Creek Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave "Skyrim:Stony Creek Cave"), [Traitor's Post](/wiki/Skyrim:Traitor%27s_Post "Skyrim:Traitor's Post"), [Uttering Hills Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Uttering_Hills_Camp "Skyrim:Uttering Hills Camp"), [Wreck of the Winter War](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Winter_War "Skyrim:Wreck of the Winter War").

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Kjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjar "Skyrim:Kjar") onboard his ship at the [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") docks.
2. Travel to the random location and kill the bandit leader.
3. Return to Kjar for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[Kjar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kjar "Skyrim:Kjar"), ship master of The North Wind currently anchored at the docks in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), will ask you to hunt down and kill a runaway crew member. The sailor had formed a group of bandits, who are currently hiding out in one of seven random locations. Travel to the location and kill the bandit leader there. Return to Kjar, who will give you a year's salary (a leveled gold reward).

## Bugs

- This quest is known to generate the boss NPC prior to actually getting it from Kjar, which would result in you killing the boss NPC and then being told to return to someone you never visited.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- If you have completed the mission and later reenter the same location once it is repopulated, it can give you the completion again, allowing you to collect a bounty for re-killing the rogue crewman.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- The bandit boss is prematurely made available to kill before Kjar actually offers the quest.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.0, fixes this bug.
- If you are sent to Uttering Hills Cave, and have already completed [Summerset Shadows](/wiki/Skyrim:Summerset_Shadows "Skyrim:Summerset Shadows") for the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), you will be unable to complete this quest, as the bandit leader is replaced by [Linwe](/wiki/Skyrim:Linwe "Skyrim:Linwe") and the former is nowhere to be seen.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You can return the cave to its previous occupants by typing `prid c5675` in the console, followed by `disable`.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You can still progress through the quest by typing `"c5688".kill` in the console.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You can progress through the quest by typing `SetStage FreeFormShipsWindhelm 15` in the console.
- Oddly, since the [Jarl's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Winterhold)") in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") is marked as being owned by the Winterhold crime faction, once you finish the quest, you will have helped someone in the hold, allowing you take things from the longhouse without it being considered theft.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19319)) Kjar is now part of the Eastmarch crime faction, and a new faction was created specifically for the residents of the longhouse in order to set ownership correctly.
