Sarethi Farm

**Sarethi Farm** is a farm east of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") that cultivates [Nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"). It contains only one zone, *[Sarethi Farm](#Sarethi_Farm)*.

After the eruption of the [Red Mountain](/wiki/Lore:Red_Mountain "Lore:Red Mountain"), the Sarethis moved to [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim") and founded this farm. Their daughter [Avrusa Sarethi](/wiki/Skyrim:Avrusa_Sarethi "Skyrim:Avrusa Sarethi") once studied as an apprentice in [Skingrad](/wiki/Lore:Skingrad "Lore:Skingrad") under [Sinderion](/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion "Skyrim:Sinderion"). He randomly showed up on the farm and set up a field research lab there for several decades. In exchange, he taught Avrusa everything he knew about Nirnroot, leading to her unique ability to cultivate the elusive plant.

### Sarethi Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm.jpg)

Farmhouse interior

You enter into a room with a cooking pot and a small table with two chairs opposite you, and a set of double doors on your left. On the table are a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). There is a dresser with two samples of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") on top, to the right of the exit with a barrel next to it. There is a large set of shelves on the west wall, with an open crate on the floor between the shelves and the barrel. There is also a staircase down to the basement with a barrel in the corner above them. In the open crate are two [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), jazbay grapes, two [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries"), two more lavender, a [red mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") and [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"). On the shelves are three gourds, three loaves of bread, two [goat cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), an eidar cheese wedge, two [goat cheese wedges](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), eight potatoes, a [green](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") and [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), two [boiled creme treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), and a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"). Hanging above the dresser are two of each [dried elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear"), [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") and [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm_02.jpg)

Bedroom

Through the double doors is a double bedroom containing two single beds against the north wall, a chest in front of the bed on the left, a barrel and wardrobe opposite, and a wall shelf on your right all as you enter. There is a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") under the bed on the right. The wall shelf holds two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sarethi_Farm_03.jpg)

Downstairs

Downstairs is an alchemy lab, a single bed, a small set of shelves containing a [solution of strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Strength "Skyrim:Solution of Strength"), a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), a [paralysis poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Paralysis_Poison "Skyrim:Paralysis Poison"), a [philter of extra magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Philter_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Philter of Extra Magicka"), a [restore health potion recipe](/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Health_Potion_Recipe "Skyrim:Restore Health Potion Recipe"), a [paralysis poison recipe](/wiki/Skyrim:Paralysis_Poison_Recipe "Skyrim:Paralysis Poison Recipe"), and an alto wine. There is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") under the stairs. Next to the alchemy lab is an open crate containing a lavender, two [deathbells](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell"), a [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") and a [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown"). There is a dresser with a copy of *[Spirit of Nirn](/wiki/Skyrim:Spirit_of_Nirn "Skyrim:Spirit of Nirn")* on top, and finally another large set of shelves containing two Nirnroot, two [swamp fungal pods](/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod "Skyrim:Swamp Fungal Pod"), a tundra cotton, an [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), a [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade"), a bleeding crown, a blue mountain flower, a [blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort"), a [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota"), a dried elves ear, two [Nordic barnacles](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle"), a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"), a [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue"), a [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), and deathbell. Above the lab is a wall shelf containing two Nord mead and two sacks.

## Notes

- Two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") are west of the farm on the far side of a pond. A third iron ore vein is to the north-northeast.
- Eight Nirnroot plants are within the fences outside, representing the densest concentration of Nirnroot in the game.
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the 3 gourds in the cart as owned by the farm.

## Bugs

- After completing [A Return To Your Roots](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Return_To_Your_Roots "Skyrim:A Return To Your Roots"), [Avrusa Sarethi](/wiki/Skyrim:Avrusa_Sarethi "Skyrim:Avrusa Sarethi") was supposed to start growing some [Crimson Nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Nirnroot "Skyrim:Crimson Nirnroot").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
- If the [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") questline is completed in favor of the Empire, an [Imperial soldier](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Soldier "Skyrim:Imperial Soldier") will appear to guard the farm, but the Stormcloak-aligned Rift guard will not be removed. This results in a fight to the death between the two.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
- The guards at the farm aren't linked to their XMarkers and thus would not patrol the area.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
- The door is locked night and day, and no key exists. If you marry [Avrusa Sarethi](/wiki/Skyrim:Avrusa_Sarethi "Skyrim:Avrusa Sarethi"), you will need to pick the lock every time you want to go into your wife's house, and the presence of the guard means that you can be arrested for it.
