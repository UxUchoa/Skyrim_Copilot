Bjornolfr

**Bjornolfr** is a dead [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") found in a hidden Dwemer room at the back of [Hrodulf](/wiki/Skyrim:Hrodulf "Skyrim:Hrodulf")'s [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Hrodulf%27s_House "Skyrim:Hrodulf's House"). When entering the house, you may hear some [reavers](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver "Skyrim:Reaver") laughing about a [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Letter (Dragonborn)") which Bjornolfr wrote to Hrodulf before his death. This letter reveals that Bjornolfr was concerned for his lover, that he wished Hrodulf would return to [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), and that he intended to come to Solstheim if that didn't happen. When you find Bjornolfr's body, he carries a [bloodstained letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodstained_Letter "Skyrim:Bloodstained Letter") from Hrodulf warning him not to come there.

Bjornolfr wears [novice robes of Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Robes_of_Conjuration "Skyrim:Novice Robes of Conjuration"), a [novice hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Hood "Skyrim:Novice Hood"), and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0006b46c "Skyrim:Boots"). His body is surrounded by a few coins and a [Nordic dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dagger "Skyrim:Nordic Dagger").
