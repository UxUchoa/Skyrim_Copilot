Niranye's House in Windhelm

**Niranye's House** is a residence in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

It is a large single-story house immediately to the east of the main gates into [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), with the rear of the house formed by part of the walls that surround the city. It has a yard between the side entrance and [Calixto's House of Curiosities](/wiki/Skyrim:Calixto%27s_House_of_Curiosities "Skyrim:Calixto's House of Curiosities"), with a barrel in the northeastern corner (see [notes](#Notes)) and a wooden bench.

## Niranye's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-interior-Niranye%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Niranye%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Niranye%27s_House.jpg)

Niranye's house

There are two entrances: a front entrance, and a side entrance which is in the yard attached to the property. The house is locked with novice-level locks on both doors whenever Niranye is not present and at night. When entering through the front door, you enter into the middle of three rooms, containing end tables on either side of the door, a dresser opposite against the south wall with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") on top, and a fancy [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") on the floor to the left. Against the east wall, to the right of a doorway, is a set of shelves holding a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"), a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute"), a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl"), and a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"). Against the west wall is a column of three wall shelves with a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow"), a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), and a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide") on them (see [bugs](#Bugs)). There is a chair in the southwestern corner, and another to the right of a doorway on the west wall.

The doorway leads into Niranye's bedroom, which contains an owned double bed coming out from the west wall, an end table in the southwestern corner, and a set of shelves against the south wall holding several sets of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"), a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), and copies of *[Lost Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends "Skyrim:Lost Legends")* and *[The Firsthold Revolt](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firsthold_Revolt "Skyrim:The Firsthold Revolt")*, with a wash basin on a stand to the right of the shelves. Against the north wall is a dresser containing more clothes, and against the east wall is a cupboard holding a bottle of alto wine and four more common books. The doorway to the east leads into the kitchen and dining room, with an entrance on the east wall and [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks all around the room. There is a wooden table with two chairs in the middle of the room, with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and a bowl containing two [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") on top. In the southeastern corner is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand, with a pile of firewood beside it. There are a couple cauldrons in the southwest corner. Against the north wall is a set of shelves holding a bottle of Nord mead, a bowl of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), a wooden bowl containing four bunches of [jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes"), and a cast iron pot containing two [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), with a basket containing a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") to the right. Against the west wall, to the right of the doorway to the middle room, is a cupboard holding a bowl of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulbs, a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), and a bowl of [frost salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Salts "Skyrim:Frost Salts"). There is a hidden panel in the south wall between the cauldrons and the cooking pot, behind which is a flight of wooden stairs leading down to the right. At the bottom is a master-locked door that opens into Niranye's basement.

## Niranye's Basement

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/SR-interior-Niranye%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Niranye%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Niranye%27s_House_02.jpg)

Niranye's basement

The basement consists of one large dimly lit room with a practice dummy in the southwestern corner, a small unit and a tall wardrobe against the west wall, a small square wooden table and two chairs, a set of shelves, and an expert-locked [safe](/wiki/Skyrim:Safe "Skyrim:Safe") against the north wall, and a couple food barrels with a crate in front and two archery dummies on either side against the east wall. Everything is empty except for the safe, which contains some average loot along with two letters from [Linwe](/wiki/Skyrim:Linwe "Skyrim:Linwe") which relate to the [reputation quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Summerset_Shadows "Skyrim:Summerset Shadows"), but aren't vital to the quest's successful completion.

## Notes

- This barrel only appears once you have completed the relevant [reputation quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Summerset_Shadows "Skyrim:Summerset Shadows"). It is marked with the [![Cache](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/SR-icon-shadowmark-Cache.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Cache.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Cache") Cache Shadowmark, and is one of three thieves' caches found in the city.
- The [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Pit_Key "Skyrim:Darkwater Pit Key") to the safe in the concealed basement can be obtained by [pickpocketing](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket "Skyrim:Pickpocket") Niranye (see [bugs](#Bugs)).
