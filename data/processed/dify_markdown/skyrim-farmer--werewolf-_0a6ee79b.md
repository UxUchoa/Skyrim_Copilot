Farmer

This **Farmer** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") who can be [randomly encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions "Skyrim:World Interactions") in the wilderness. He turns into a [werewolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf "Skyrim:Werewolf") when approached and will proceed to attack. He carries three pieces of [raw meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Raw_Meat "Skyrim:Food").
