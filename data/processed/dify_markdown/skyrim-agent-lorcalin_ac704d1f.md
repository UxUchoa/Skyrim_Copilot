Agent Lorcalin

**Agent Lorcalin** is a [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor") [wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Wizard_(class) "Skyrim:Thalmor Wizard (class)") that can be found near a [shrine of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Talos "Skyrim:Shrines") in [White River Gorge](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River"), but only after you've approached the shrine. He is carrying out his [orders](/wiki/Skyrim:Lorcalin%27s_Orders "Skyrim:Lorcalin's Orders") from [Elenwen](/wiki/Skyrim:Elenwen "Skyrim:Elenwen") to hunt down any "heretics" at the shrine. He and a [Thalmor soldier](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Soldier "Skyrim:Thalmor Soldier") will appear at the shrine once you get close to it, but will not be present before then.

He wears the standard [robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Thalmor_Robes "Skyrim:Hooded Thalmor Robes"), [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Boots "Skyrim:Thalmor Boots"), and [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Gloves "Skyrim:Thalmor Gloves") of a Thalmor wizard, and wields either a steel, elven, or glass [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"), depending on [your level](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor#Leveled_Equipment "Skyrim:Thalmor"). He also carries his orders and a random selection of [gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Gems "Skyrim:Gems"), [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"), [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), and gold.

## Bugs

- Agent Lorcalin and his escort may be seen insta-spawning on top of the shrine instead of ambushing you properly.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.8, addresses this issue. They now have added travel packages to force them to walk up from where they are initially enabled.
