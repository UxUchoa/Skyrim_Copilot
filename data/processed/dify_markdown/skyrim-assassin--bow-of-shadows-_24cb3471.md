## Related Quests

- **[In the Shadows](/wiki/Skyrim:In_the_Shadows "Skyrim:In the Shadows")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow_of_Shadows "Skyrim:Bow of Shadows"): Save the Jarl of Whiterun from an assassination plot.

## Quest-Related Events

The assassin will not appear if the Jarl is occupied, and will not appear if you haven't started the quest [Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (quest)"). The Jarl is considered occupied when the following quest stages are active:

- [Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (quest)") - Stage 10: When Jarl Balgruuf is showing you to [Farengar](/wiki/Skyrim:Farengar_Secret-Fire "Skyrim:Farengar Secret-Fire").
- [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") - Stages 10, 12, 13, 15, 20, 110, 120, 130, 150: During the scenes inside Dragonsreach at the beginning of the quest and after the [Greybeards](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards") summon you to [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar").
- [The Fallen](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Fallen "Skyrim:The Fallen") - Stages 25, 27 - When persuading the Jarl about to trap [Odahviing](/wiki/Skyrim:Odahviing "Skyrim:Odahviing") in Dragonsreach.
- At any point during [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun"), [Battle for Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Solitude "Skyrim:Battle for Solitude"), or [Battle for Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Windhelm "Skyrim:Battle for Windhelm").
