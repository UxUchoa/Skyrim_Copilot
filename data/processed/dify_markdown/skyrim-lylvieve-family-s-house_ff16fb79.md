Lylvieve Family's House

**Lylvieve Family's House** is a small farmhouse on the northwestern side of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge"), located next to [Horgeir's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Horgeir%27s_House "Skyrim:Horgeir's House").

It is home to [Azzada Lylvieve](/wiki/Skyrim:Azzada_Lylvieve "Skyrim:Azzada Lylvieve"), his wife [Michel](/wiki/Skyrim:Michel_Lylvieve "Skyrim:Michel Lylvieve"), and their children, [Julienne](/wiki/Skyrim:Julienne_Lylvieve "Skyrim:Julienne Lylvieve") and [Clinton](/wiki/Skyrim:Clinton_Lylvieve "Skyrim:Clinton Lylvieve"). It consists of one zone, *[Lylvieve Family's House](#Lylvieve_Family.27s_House)*.

### Lylvieve Family's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg)

Ground floor

There are two levels to this home, including the ground floor and the basement. Upon entering, the right side holds a bedroom area, along the east wall. The double bed (belonging to the [Lylvieve Family House Occupants](/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_Family_House_Occupants "Skyrim:Lylvieve Family House Occupants") faction) is flanked by a small table to the right and a non-respawning dresser to the left. Atop the table are five loose gold coins and a basket containing four [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"). The dresser holds a [common soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem") (filled), two [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), two [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"), a [potion of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Magicka"), and a [potion of plentiful stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Stamina"). Along the north wall is a set of shelves holding four bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and one bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") on the top shelf, along with three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), three [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), two [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), and two [goat cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") on the middle shelf. On the floor to the right of these shelves is a woven basket containing three [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). Above the shelves are some hanging ingredients, including two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, one dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), and two portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"). At the center of the room is a large dining table which holds a messy arrangement of food, including two [goat cheese wedges](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), one [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), three loaves of bread, two chicken's eggs, two bottles of alto wine, and two pieces of [salmon meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat "Skyrim:Salmon Meat"). In the southwest corner of the room, there are two respawning meat barrels.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House_02.jpg)

Basement

Proceeding down the stairs to the basement, the children's bedroom area is along the east wall, while the rest of the room is used for general storage. A child's bed owned by [Clinton](/wiki/Skyrim:Clinton_Lylvieve "Skyrim:Clinton Lylvieve") is in the northeast corner, while a single bed owned by [Julienne](/wiki/Skyrim:Julienne_Lylvieve "Skyrim:Julienne Lylvieve") is in the southeast corner. At the foot of each bed is a small non-respawning chest. Along the north wall, there is a small round table holding one goat cheese wedge, one loaf of bread, and two boiled creme treats. To the left of the table is a respawning food barrel, and between them are four freshly caught [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") hanging above. Another respawning food barrel can be found below the stairs, among various clutter.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_House_Key "Skyrim:Lylvieve House Key") to which is carried by all four members of the Lylvieve family.
- A [grain mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Milling "Skyrim:Milling") can be found behind the house, which can be used to produce [flour](/wiki/Skyrim:Sack_of_Flour "Skyrim:Sack of Flour")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") from [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") if [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is installed.
