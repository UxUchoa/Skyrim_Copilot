Drelas' Cottage

**Drelas' Cottage** is an isolated [cottage](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") at the foot of a mountain in northern [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold"), and south of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal").

It is home to [Drelas](/wiki/Skyrim:Drelas "Skyrim:Drelas"), a powerful elemental mage who will attack on sight. It consists of one zone, *[Drelas' Cottage](#Drelas.27_Cottage)*.

## Drelas' Cottage

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage.jpg)

Cooking and dining area

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage_02.jpg)

Enchanting and alchemy area

Drelas' cottage is a spacious residence sectioned off into a few areas. The ground floor consists of a fireplace and food area, a small alchemy and enchanting space, and a spacious sitting area, while the bedroom area can be found upstairs. Upon entering the front door, a large table will be to the right, holding a leveled potion, a sample of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") mushroom, a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell"), two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), a [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue"), an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing"), a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"), two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"), a sample of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"), a cluster of [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries"), a sample of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), and a serving of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"). A large floor fireplace sits in the northeast corner, and the room holds two respawning large sacks—one along the wall between the door and the table, and the other in the northwest corner.

Proceeding through the doorway to the next area of the cottage, a small alchemy and enchanting space can be found on the north side. This is where [Drelas](/wiki/Skyrim:Drelas "Skyrim:Drelas") is likely to be found during the day. On the right side of this space is a small set of shelves holding a copy of *[Galerion The Mystic](/wiki/Skyrim:Galerion_The_Mystic "Skyrim:Galerion The Mystic")* and a thistle branch on the top shelf, and two [blue dartwings](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Dartwing "Skyrim:Blue Dartwing") on the bottom shelf. An [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") is situated in the corner, with two wall shelves above—the right holding a non-respawning large sack, and the left holding a sample of [vampire dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Dust "Skyrim:Vampire Dust"). Two [spider eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Egg "Skyrim:Spider Egg") and a respawning large sack sit in a small crate to the left of the alchemy lab. At the top of the next set of shelves along the north wall is a sample of [hagraven feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Feathers "Skyrim:Hagraven Feathers"), while a leveled magicka poison and a sample of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal") can be found on the top shelf, and the bottom shelf holds a sample of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), two deathbells, and a sample of [ice wraith teeth](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth "Skyrim:Ice Wraith Teeth"). The [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter") sits in the left corner, to the left of which rests a large silver bowl containing three [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota") mushrooms and two [sabre cat teeth](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Tooth "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Tooth").

The remaining portion of the ground floor is a spacious sitting area, with the stairway leading to the bedroom upstairs. Along the railing separating the rooms on the east side, a small table is set with an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and a bowl containing two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). In the southwest corner, a desk holds another bottle of wine and a copy of *[The Firmament](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firmament "Skyrim:The Firmament")*, while a bookshelf holds a sample of vampire dust, a thistle branch, and one copy each of the following books: *[Life of Uriel Septim VII](/wiki/Skyrim:Life_of_Uriel_Septim_VII "Skyrim:Life of Uriel Septim VII")*; *[Brief History of the Empire, v4](/wiki/Skyrim:Brief_History_of_the_Empire,_v4 "Skyrim:Brief History of the Empire, v4")*; *[The Real Barenziah, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Real_Barenziah,_v3 "Skyrim:The Real Barenziah, v3")*; *[Ruins of Kemel-Ze](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Kemel-Ze "Skyrim:Ruins of Kemel-Ze")*; *[Yngol and the Sea-Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol_and_the_Sea-Ghosts "Skyrim:Yngol and the Sea-Ghosts")*; and *[Biography of Barenziah, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:Biography_of_Barenziah,_v3 "Skyrim:Biography of Barenziah, v3")*. Beneath the stairway are three respawning food barrels, one of which holds a respawning large sack, while another holds a [draught of strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Strength "Skyrim:Draught of Strength"), a leveled potion, and a sample of tundra cotton. Another respawning sack sits in front of the southernmost barrel.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Drelas%27_Cottage_03.jpg)

Upstairs bedroom area

Heading upstairs, you'll find a small non-respawning cupboard directly ahead, atop which sits a glazed bowl containing two [goat cheese wedges](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"). Against the wall to the right is a non-respawning dresser holding a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and another copy of *Biography of Barenziah, v3*. Turning the corner, there are two tall bookcases along the wall to the left, and a single bed in the main bedroom space on the right. At the foot of the bed is a respawning chest containing leveled items, and to the right of the bed is an end table holding a [potion of plentiful magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Magicka") and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). On the bookshelves are two samples of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") in a basket, a sample of tundra cotton, and a basic [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Poison "Skyrim:Potions"), as well as twelve more books: *The Real Barenziah, v3*; *[Antecedants of Dwemer Law](/wiki/Skyrim:Antecedants_of_Dwemer_Law "Skyrim:Antecedants of Dwemer Law")*; two copies of *[The Firsthold Revolt](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firsthold_Revolt "Skyrim:The Firsthold Revolt")*; two copies of *[The Cake and The Diamond](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cake_and_The_Diamond "Skyrim:The Cake and The Diamond")*; two copies of *[The Hope of the Redoran](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Hope_of_the_Redoran "Skyrim:The Hope of the Redoran")*; *[The Book of Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra "Skyrim:The Book of Daedra")*; *[Frontier, Conquest](/wiki/Skyrim:Frontier,_Conquest "Skyrim:Frontier, Conquest")*; *[Mysterious Akavir](/wiki/Skyrim:Mysterious_Akavir "Skyrim:Mysterious Akavir")*; and *[The Real Barenziah, v1](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Real_Barenziah,_v1 "Skyrim:The Real Barenziah, v1")*.

## Notes

- The door is not locked, but entering the cottage still counts as trespassing. Additionally, items in the house, other than those looted from Drelas' corpse, will remain owned by Drelas upon his death and will be marked as stolen if taken.
- The cottage is a hot spot for [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") encounters due to the nearby [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") at [Eldersblood Peak](/wiki/Skyrim:Eldersblood_Peak "Skyrim:Eldersblood Peak").
- Two [deer](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer "Skyrim:Deer") or [elk](/wiki/Skyrim:Elk "Skyrim:Elk") are likely to be found nearby.
- All cupboards, dressers, and end tables in the location are non-respawning.
- A [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Quarried_Stone "Skyrim:Quarried Stone")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") can be found just north of the cottage.
