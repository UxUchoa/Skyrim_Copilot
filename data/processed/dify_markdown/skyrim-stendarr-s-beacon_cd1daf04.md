Stendarr's Beacon

**Stendarr's Beacon** is a small [tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Towers "Skyrim:Imperial Towers") southeast of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften").

## Walkthrough

The path to the beacon branches south from the main road southeast of Riften, opposite the path toward [Black-Briar Lodge](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Lodge "Skyrim:Black-Briar Lodge"). The path passes another path leading northwest to [Broken Helm Hollow](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Helm_Hollow "Skyrim:Broken Helm Hollow"), and continues south then east before climbing north. At the top, the beacon is ahead to the left. Outside the tower, the three resident [Vigilants of Stendarr](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilant_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Vigilant of Stendarr") have set up a makeshift kitchen, complete with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") and a nearby table holding a [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel "Skyrim:Satchel"), a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), and a joint of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"), with a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") beside the table. A [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") is a few feet west of the cooking spit. Around the back of the tower is a lootable [skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton"), with a random [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") and [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet") lying next to it.

Inside the tower, the Vigilants have set up a [shrine of Stendarr](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Shrine of Stendarr") with their bedrolls to the left, one of which has a copy of the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Exodus](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Exodus "Skyrim:The Exodus")* resting on it that you may freely take. If you take the stairs to the top of the tower, you will find a large campfire and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot owned by the Vigilants; being seen to loot the chest will turn the Vigilants hostile.

## Notes

- To the southeast, a flag can be seen flying up on the ridge. The path up to the ridge is splashed with blood, and a [frost troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll") roams the area. At the base of the flagpole is an apprentice-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") and one or two other pieces of loot, with some humanoid remains nearby. The strongbox doesn't respawn. Under a nearby wolf corpse is a skill book [A Dance in Fire, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v2 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v2").
- At the foot of the next ridge to the east, before turning right onto the blood-stained path upward, there is a second silver ore vein on the right.
- A [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") is west of the tower, and two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") are east of the tower.
- If you have [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") installed, the cave entrance to [Dayspring Canyon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dayspring_Canyon "Skyrim:Dayspring Canyon") will be northeast of here.

## Bugs

- To mark this location as cleared, you must kill all three Vigilants in one go. If you leave one or more alive and leave the area for long enough for them to despawn, it will be impossible to clear the tower later, as the three Vigilants never respawn.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. The incorrectly applied clearable flag has been removed altogether.
- The fix from Unofficial Skyrim Patch 2.0.1 overlooked a major detail. Based on further examination, it appears that the flag's presence was intended as a clearable target for vampire players - several of the Vigilants here are flagged as Bosses, meaning the clearable flag should never have been removed in the first place.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.1, addresses this issue. The previously removed clearable flag has been restored.
