## Quick Walkthrough

1. After [Roggvir's](/wiki/Skyrim:Roggvir "Skyrim:Roggvir") execution, ask [Sorex Vinius](/wiki/Skyrim:Sorex_Vinius "Skyrim:Sorex Vinius") who's in charge of Solitude.
2. Deliver a bottle of [Stros M'Kai Rum](/wiki/Skyrim:Stros_M%27Kai_Rum "Skyrim:Stros M'Kai Rum") to [Falk Firebeard](/wiki/Skyrim:Falk_Firebeard "Skyrim:Falk Firebeard").

## Detailed Walkthrough

Entering Solitude by the main gate for the first time will trigger an execution, as hinted by the guard outside. The man fated to die is [Roggvir](/wiki/Skyrim:Roggvir "Skyrim:Roggvir"), a former friend of [Sorex Vinius](/wiki/Skyrim:Sorex_Vinius "Skyrim:Sorex Vinius") and a 'traitor to the Empire'. Talking to Sorex after the execution will open up discussion about the leaders of Solitude; continuing the conversation will end with him asking you to bring a delivery of [Stros M'Kai Rum](/wiki/Skyrim:Stros_M%27Kai_Rum "Skyrim:Stros M'Kai Rum") to one of those leaders, [Falk Firebeard](/wiki/Skyrim:Falk_Firebeard "Skyrim:Falk Firebeard"). Falk resides in the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace") and is nearly always found by [Elisif the Fair's](/wiki/Skyrim:Elisif_the_Fair "Skyrim:Elisif the Fair") side in the main room. Upon delivering the rum, he will thank you and reward you with a leveled amount of gold to complete the quest.

## Notes

- Upon finishing the quest, you'll have the option to marry Sorex. You'll also be able to take almost everything from the Winking Skeever for free.

## Bugs

- If you have already accepted the quest [No News is Good News](/wiki/Skyrim:No_News_is_Good_News "Skyrim:No News is Good News"), Sorex will not offer this quest.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
