Reach Imperial Camp

**Reach Imperial Camp** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Camps "Skyrim:Imperial Camps") in [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"), located south of [Bthardamz](/wiki/Skyrim:Bthardamz "Skyrim:Bthardamz") and west of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten").

This camp will only be present if the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") hold [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") and the rest of the Reach, whether through martial might or via the negotiation during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"). [Imperial Soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Soldier "Skyrim:Imperial Soldier") are camped here under the command of [Legate Emmanuel Admand](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Emmanuel_Admand "Skyrim:Legate Emmanuel Admand"), except during the related quest, when [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") takes command. Like similar Imperial camps that only exist when the hold has changed hands, it has neither a [quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Imperial Quartermaster") or any [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse").

## Reach Imperial Camp

Unlike most camps, this camp is laid out on two levels. The lower level has a campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, and an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil") beside the fire. To the north are three small tents containing unowned bed rolls, while to the east is a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). To the west, on a higher level, is the Legate's tent, containing an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. Farther west is the command tent, containing an end table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. To the southeast is the hospital tent, containing two bed rolls, both of which are occupied by wounded soldiers, and an unlocked chest containing various items to treat the sick, including potions of [cure poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison "Skyrim:Cure Poison"), potions of [cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Cure Disease"), and [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). Outside the hospital tent is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"). Between the hospital tent and the lower part of the camp is a tree stump with [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") growing on it, a bird nest containing a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), and a [juniper](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") tree. There is a wild leveled [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") nearby that will often attack the soldiers while they are patrolling around the camp.

Any time [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Joining the Stormcloaks"), you are free to sleep in the small tents.
