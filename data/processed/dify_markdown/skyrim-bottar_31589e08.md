Bottar

**Bottar** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") who can be [encountered randomly](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Dwemer_Junk_Peddlers "Skyrim:World Interactions") out in the wilds. He wears green colored [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Child's Clothes") and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8 "Skyrim:Shoes"). He is accompanied by his friend [Sond](/wiki/Skyrim:Sond_(child) "Skyrim:Sond (child)"), who will sell you a waypoint to [Deep Folk Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Deep_Folk_Crossing "Skyrim:Deep Folk Crossing") for one septim.
