Bonechill Passage

**Bonechill Passage** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") east of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") containing leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals"). It contains only one zone, *[Bonechill Passage](#Bonechill_Passage)*.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/SR-place-POIPineForest15.jpg/200px-SR-place-POIPineForest15.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-POIPineForest15.jpg)

Ruined shack

Following a dirt path south from the road between Falkreath and [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen") east of [Pinewatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Pinewatch "Skyrim:Pinewatch") will take you past an unmarked [ruined shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Falkreath#Prospector.27s_Shack:_Bonechill_Ridge "Skyrim:Unmarked Places Falkreath") that was attacked by the [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") occupying [Ancient's Ascent](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient%27s_Ascent "Skyrim:Ancient's Ascent") to the south. The path curves east around an outcrop of rock before proceeding south to a large stone square arch over the steps leading up to the cave entrance. A [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") is just west of the base of the steps.

### Bonechill Passage

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-interior-Bonechill_Passage.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bonechill_Passage.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bonechill_Passage.jpg)

Interior

Upon entering, you are in an ice tunnel heading south. To the left is a skeleton with a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") beside it and a pair of random [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor") opposite. A leveled snow animal or creature (which may be a [frost troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll"), [ice wolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf "Skyrim:Ice Wolf"), [ice wraith](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith"), or [snowy sabre cat](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowy_Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Snowy Sabre Cat")) guards the path ahead. Moving forward, to the right is a mammoth skull with a dead [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") and a leveled [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") lying beside it. Farther along and also to the right is a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") next to another skeleton and a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, MidYear, v6](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_MidYear,_v6 "Skyrim:2920, MidYear, v6")*.

The tunnel narrows and bends to the east, then opens into an unroofed section with a bridge across a chasm and a leveled snow animal or creature on the far side. To the left at the start of the bridge is a dead [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat"). Under the bridge are two lootable skeletons and a random [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"). Across the bridge, the tunnel rises quite sharply, turns twice, and leads to the exit near Ancient's Ascent.

## Notes

- Among other items described on the [dedicated page](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Falkreath#Prospector.27s_Shack:_Bonechill_Ridge "Skyrim:Unmarked Places Falkreath"), the ruined shack contains a copy of the [Pickpocket](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket "Skyrim:Pickpocket") skill book *[Purloined Shadows](/wiki/Skyrim:Purloined_Shadows "Skyrim:Purloined Shadows")*.
- In [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.

## Bugs

- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Some users have experienced a crash to desktop after entering Bonechill Passage. This crash appears to have been fixed in an early patch, though there is no mention in any patch notes.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The issue can be worked around by playing in windowed mode while inside the cave.
