When the Cat's Away

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read [M'Sharra's Diary](/wiki/Skyrim:M%27Sharra%27s_Diary "Skyrim:M'Sharra's Diary")
2. Find and read [M'Sharra's Confession](/wiki/Skyrim:M%27Sharra%27s_Confession "Skyrim:M'Sharra's Confession") in Dragonsreach Dungeon
3. Travel to [Lund's Hut](/wiki/Skyrim:Lund%27s_Hut "Skyrim:Lund's Hut")
4. Loot the Silver Armor
5. Fight the East Empire Warden

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/SR-quest-When_the_Cat%27s_Away_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-When_the_Cat%27s_Away_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-When_the_Cat%27s_Away_02.jpg)

M'Sharra's diary

To start this quest, head to the Bannered Mare in Whiterun. Find *[M'Sharra's Diary](/wiki/Skyrim:M%27Sharra%27s_Diary "Skyrim:M'Sharra's Diary")* on top of the table within the kitchen area. This will begin the adventure after reading it.

Next, visit the Dragonsreach Dungeon and look in the corner near the Prisoner's Evidence Chest to find *[M'Sharra's Confession](/wiki/Skyrim:M%27Sharra%27s_Confession "Skyrim:M'Sharra's Confession")*. This indicates that she left a full set of Silver Armor within a hut near Rorikstead.

This is actually [Lund's Hut](/wiki/Skyrim:Lund%27s_Hut "Skyrim:Lund's Hut"), the skeever-infested hut once owned by Lund, who lies deceased inside. Head there and inside find a chest that indeed contains a full set of Silver Armor along with a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v2 "Skyrim:The Crimson Dirks, v2")*. Head outside and an [Imperial Captain](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Captain "Skyrim:Imperial Captain") and two [East Empire Wardens](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Warden "Skyrim:East Empire Warden") will begin attacking. Fight them off and enjoy the new set of armor.

## Bugs

- The note "M'Sharra's Confession" may not spawn on the ground in the dungeon where it is supposed to, but under the floor.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") If the note seems to be missing, it can be reached by using the `tcl` console command to go under the floor.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Alternatively, one can use the console to advance the quest to stage 30 and bypass the need for the note.
- It is possible to Clip through the wall with a wooden Plate, Pressed against the wall, and Sprint into it until you are inside the wall, Then you must walk along the Dirt that is loaded within the negative Space, By which you can grab the note before falling and being re-positioned in the Dungeon.
