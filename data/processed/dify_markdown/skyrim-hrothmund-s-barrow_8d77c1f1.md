Hrothmund's Barrow

**Hrothmund's Barrow** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") southwest of [Benkongerike](/wiki/Skyrim:Benkongerike "Skyrim:Benkongerike").

It contains the axe once wielded by [Hrothmund](/wiki/Lore:Hrothmund_the_Red "Lore:Hrothmund the Red"). During the quest [Retaking Thirsk](/wiki/Skyrim:Retaking_Thirsk "Skyrim:Retaking Thirsk"), after you have helped a group of [Nords](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") clear [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall") of [rieklings](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling "Skyrim:Riekling"), you are sent here to witness a ritual confirming the hall's rightful leader.

### Hrothmund's Barrow

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-interior-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg)

The large flooded chamber

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-item-Hrothmund%27s_Axe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Hrothmund%27s_Axe.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-item-Hrothmund%27s_Axe.jpg)

Hrothmund's Axe

The entrance leads into a natural cave with a [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") and three patches of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") fungus. A short flight of stairs ascends to the north along the west wall. At the top of the stairs, a set of iron doors is on the northeast wall, while a concealed door on the southeast wall is opened by the pull chain to the left. Beyond the concealed door is a damp twisting tunnel ending at a second concealed door opened by the pull chain to the right. Beyond this door is a small shallowly flooded chamber containing a [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") and a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), with a [burial urn](/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn "Skyrim:Burial Urn"), a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), three loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), a [flawless garnet](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Garnet "Skyrim:Flawless Garnet"), an [amethyst](/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst "Skyrim:Amethyst"), and a [flawless emerald](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Emerald "Skyrim:Flawless Emerald") next to the chest.

Returning to the area outside the first concealed door, beyond the set of iron doors is a flight of stairs descending into a large flooded chamber. As you descend the stairs, the water will drain to reveal two paths on either side of a large stone sculpture. The paths rejoin and continue to the far end of the room, where a raised platform features a stone holding Hrothmund's Axe. Two chests flank the alcove with the stone.

## Notes

- Southwest of the entrance at an [unmarked location](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places#Reaver.27s_End "Skyrim:Unmarked Places") is an [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox").
- Northwest of the entrance are two [sapphire geodes](/wiki/Skyrim:Sapphire_Geode "Skyrim:Sapphire Geode"). Due north of the second sapphire geode above a waterfall is a chest and a random [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword").
- Southeast of the entrance are two sapphire geodes, a silver ore vein, and a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein").
- Hrothmund's Axe is not an actual weapon, and cannot be obtained by any means.
- [Hrothmund's Barrow](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Hrothmund%27s_Barrow "Bloodmoon:Hrothmund's Barrow") previously appeared in [Bloodmoon](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon "Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon").
