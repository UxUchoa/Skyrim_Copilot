Hall of the Dead

The **Hall of the Dead** is the final resting place for many of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun")'s late residents.

The building is located in the Wind District of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). It is the second building on the right from the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") tree, between the [Temple of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Temple of Kynareth") and the [House of Clan Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:House_of_Clan_Battle-Born "Skyrim:House of Clan Battle-Born"). There are two parts to the building, the *[Hall of the Dead](#Hall_of_the_Dead)*, and the *[Catacombs](#Whiterun_Catacombs)*.

The hall and catacombs are maintained by a single priest, [Andurs](/wiki/Skyrim:Andurs "Skyrim:Andurs"). If a resident of Whiterun should die, their remains will be interred in the catacombs, in a coffin or urn, along with all of the items they carried in life.

### Hall of the Dead

This first area serves as Andurs' living quarters and chapel. The middle section of the room contains six [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") plants and two benches, with the door to the catacombs at the far end. The section to the right is the chapel, and contains two benches and a [shrine of Arkay](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Arkay "Skyrim:Shrine of Arkay"). The section to the left contains two benches and three lavender plants, and the door to Andurs' bedroom.

Andurs' bedroom contains his owned bed, two wardrobes, a chest containing gold, and a chair by a table. On the table is a coin purse, a [Weak Aversion to Fire potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Aversion_to_Fire "Skyrim:Weak Aversion to Fire"), and three books. On the shelf above the table is a [Weak Poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Poison "Skyrim:Weak Poison"). To the left of the table is a bookshelf containing various books.

### Whiterun Catacombs

The Catacombs are where the bodies of the dead are interred. It is guarded by three respawning skeletons: to the right is the first, at the bottom of the stairs is the second, and in the closed room to the left of the main door is the third. Also in this room is a copy of the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Death Blow of Abernanit](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Blow_of_Abernanit "Skyrim:Death Blow of Abernanit")* and two coin purses. Down the stairs on the left, in the first bottom alcove on the left, is an [unusual gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem"). In the room behind the second skeleton is [Andurs' Amulet of Arkay](/wiki/Skyrim:Andurs%27_Arkay_Amulet "Skyrim:Andurs' Arkay Amulet"), which only appears once the quest [Andurs' Arkay Amulet](/wiki/Skyrim:Andurs%27_Arkay_Amulet "Skyrim:Andurs' Arkay Amulet") has begun and can be found on the small table to the right of the brazier.
