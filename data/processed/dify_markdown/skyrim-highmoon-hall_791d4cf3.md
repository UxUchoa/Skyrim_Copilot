### Exterior

There is a guard standing to the right of the entrance at all times. There are snowberry bushes on either side of the stairs leading up to the entrance. To the right of the entrance is a lean-to, with two chicken's nests holding chicken eggs under it. Farther to the right is a nirnroot beside a pool of water, and a few paces away is a patch of giant lichen and a blue mountain flower.

### Highmoon Hall

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall.jpg)

Highmoon Hall throne

Upon entering, you are in the main room, where the Jarl holds court. During the day, the Jarl will be sitting on their throne at the far end of the room, with their bodyguard standing beside them. On either side of the room are wooden benches, stairs leading up to two bedrooms, and a door and a doorway leading to two ground floor rooms. There is also a long fire pit in the middle of the room. There are a large number of mounted [mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab") on plaques around the building.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall_Armory.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall_Armory.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall_Armory.jpg)

Armory inside Highmoon Hall.

The doorway to the southeast leads to the military advisor's room. This room consists of six weapon racks holding an assortment of [iron weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") on the northwest wall to the left of the doorway. There are many straw bales around the room. To the right of the weapon rack is a practice dummy. In the northern corner of the room is an unowned single bed beside a pile of firewood. Against the southeast wall is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), with a wall shelf above holding two [potions of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), two [potions of extra magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Extra Magicka"), a [potion of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Enhanced Stamina"), and a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"). Against the southwest wall is a long wooden table holding a map of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") showing the progress of the civil war; the military advisor will spend a lot of their time studying this. To the right is a second unlocked chest, with a wall shelf above holding two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), two minor healing potions, and two [potions of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall_Jarl%27s_Bedroom.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall_Jarl%27s_Bedroom.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Highmoon_Hall_Jarl%27s_Bedroom.jpg)

The sleeping quarters of the jarl.

Opposite the doorway is the door leading to the Jarl's bedroom, which consists of a large unowned double bed in the center of the room on a raised platform, and three wardrobes to your left as you enter, two tall and one small. On top of the small wardrobe is a [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") and [goblets](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"). To the right, still against the wall to the southwest, is a large round wooden table and two chairs holding a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and two goblets. Against the rear wall to the northwest are two long unlocked chests. In the northern corner of the room are two barrels of [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and two sacks of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"). In the eastern corner is a small round wooden table and chairs. On the table is a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), seven loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), two bottles of wine, a bottle of alto wine, and a goblet. There is a wooden bench to the right of the door against the southeast wall.

The stairs to the southeast lead to a balcony area and a door to Idgrod the Younger's bedroom. On the balcony is a small round wooden table and chair holding three bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and at the other end is a larger round wooden table and chair holding a mead barrel, two bottles of Nord mead, and a bottle of alto wine. The bedroom consists of an unowned single bed in the eastern corner, with a small wardrobe to the left holding a bunch of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). To the left of the door is a set of shelves holding three potions of minor healing, two potions of minor magicka, two potions of enhanced stamina, a [draught of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Draught of Enhanced Stamina"), a bowl of [frost salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Salts "Skyrim:Frost Salts"), a bowl of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), a [giant's toe](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant%27s_Toe "Skyrim:Giant's Toe"), three [hagraven claws](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Claw "Skyrim:Hagraven Claw"), a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), a bunch of [jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes"), and a wooden bowl holding three [chaurus eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Eggs "Skyrim:Chaurus Eggs"). To the right of the door is another set of shelves holding a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), two common books, four bottles of wine, and two bottles of alto wine. Against the southwest wall is a dresser holding two loaves of bread. Above this is a wall shelf holding a bottle of Nord mead, a medium coin purse, and three [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"). To the left in the corner is a food barrel with five loose coins and a large coin purse. Against the southwest wall is a long unlocked chest, with another wall shelf above holding three bottles of Nord mead.

The stairs on the right lead to a matching balcony and a door to Joric's bedroom. On the balcony are several barrels of food, one of which has four bottles of Nord mead on top, with another holding a bottle of alto wine. There is a wooden bench and a small round wooden table and chairs. On the table are three more bottles of Nord mead, a bottle of wine, and a bottle of alto wine. The bedroom consists of a single unowned bed in the northern corner, as well as a set of shelves opposite holding three cheese wheels, three lockpicks, a potion of minor healing, a potion of minor magicka, two [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), three [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), and copies of *[Cats of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Cats_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Cats of Skyrim")*, *[Dwemer Inquiries Vol II](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_II "Skyrim:Dwemer Inquiries Vol II")*, *[Horker Attacks](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Attacks "Skyrim:Horker Attacks")*, *[Nords of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Nords of Skyrim")*, *[The Monomyth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Monomyth "Skyrim:The Monomyth")*, *[Walking the world, Vol XI](/wiki/Skyrim:Walking_the_World,_Vol_XI "Skyrim:Walking the World, Vol XI")*, and the [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Locked Room](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Locked_Room "Skyrim:The Locked Room")*. Against the southwest wall is a long unlocked chest, with a chair in front of it. On the southeast wall is a wall shelf holding a [long taffy treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Taffy_Treat "Skyrim:Long Taffy Treat"), two red apples, and three green apples.

## Notes

- As you approach the door to Highmoon Hall for the first time, you may witness a conversation between some of the residents of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") and [Aslfur](/wiki/Skyrim:Aslfur "Skyrim:Aslfur"), the Jarl's steward.
- Though it may seem like [Falion](/wiki/Skyrim:Falion "Skyrim:Falion") is Morthal's [court wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Court_Wizard "Skyrim:Court Wizard"), he is simply an ordinary spell vendor trying to make a living within [his shop](/wiki/Skyrim:Falion%27s_House "Skyrim:Falion's House"), and the court of Morthal does not employ a court wizard.
- Due to how densely packed the hall is, Gorm the housecarl sleeps in the war quarters where the military advisor normally sleeps. This results in Highmoon Hall being the only Jarl's longhouse to not feature the usual military cot there, but instead a proper bed for Gorm. It also results in the advisor having to sleep [elsewhere](/wiki/Skyrim:Guardhouse "Skyrim:Guardhouse"), resulting in [some](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Taurinus_Duilis#Bugs "Skyrim:Legate Taurinus Duilis") [bugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrald_Frozen-Heart#Bugs "Skyrim:Arrald Frozen-Heart").

## Bugs

- If the Stormcloaks take over Hjaalmarch, Pactur is supposed to move to the hall with Sorli and act as steward, judging from his dialogue and faction. However, due to an oversight, she will only bring Teeba-Ei to Morthal, while Pactur stays in [Stonehills](/wiki/Skyrim:Stonehills "Skyrim:Stonehills"). This is fixed by the [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") add-on.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
- Joric is assigned a bed upstairs but seems to have trouble using it. When he's up there he cannot get into bed, and stays up all night, either sitting on the chair in the same room or occasionally popping from one spot to another.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, addresses this issue. The bed was neither owned by Joric nor marked as a child's bed, which made it impossible for him to use it.
- You may not be able to wait or sleep inside the hall at night, and if you try, you will receive notifications that you cannot do so when being told to leave, even though the hall is marked in the game files as a public area and nobody is telling you to leave. This is because the sleep packages of the hall's occupants have "lock doors" and "warn before locking doors" checked when those in other Jarl longhouses do not.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=16831))
- If the Stormcloaks take over Hjaalmarch, Pactur may accuse Teeba-Ei of trespassing when they're here because Teeba-Ei was never assigned to the relevant [faction](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal_Longhouse_Faction_(Both_Sides) "Skyrim:Morthal Longhouse Faction (Both Sides)") like all the other possible occupants.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.4, fixes this bug.
