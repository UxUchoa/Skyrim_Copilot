The Battle-Born Farm

**Battle-Born Farm** is a farm just outside [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), owned by the [Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle-Born "Skyrim:Battle-Born") clan who are influential in the city. [Alfhild](/wiki/Skyrim:Alfhild_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Alfhild Battle-Born") and [Gwendolyn](/wiki/Skyrim:Gwendolyn "Skyrim:Gwendolyn") tend the farm. The farm contains an [exterior](#Exterior) and a [farmhouse](#Battle-Born_Farm).

### Exterior

The farm consists of several fields containing ten [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), five [gourds](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), and six bushels of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"). Alfhild will buy any leeks, gourds, or wheat you have. There is a [cow](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow "Skyrim:Cow") in a pen and three [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken") wandering freely. The farm has lots of equipment around the property including a [grain mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Milling "Skyrim:Milling"), which is south of the house, a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), and a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") that are either side of the cow pen. There is also a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") which is under a covered store with a table and chair next to it and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on the table. There are two [chicken nests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") next to a walled field. There is a [quicksilver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore Vein"), two [corundum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") to the north and one to the northeast, across the road from the property. There is an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") among some rocks to the south as well. Around the house are several [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and large vats, with a few flowering plants at the back of the house and along the roadside.

### Battle-Born Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-interior-Battle-Born_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Battle-Born_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Battle-Born_Farm.jpg)

Inside Battle-Born Farm

The house is a single-room property with a stack of food barrels and sacks in the southwestern corner. Against the west wall is a narrow wooden table with a bowl of [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") on top. To the right, also against the west wall, is a bookcase holding eleven common books and a bunch of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"). In the southeastern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs, with an alcove to the left, along the east wall. The alcove serves as a bedroom and contains a double bed in the southeastern corner with an end table to the left holding a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") in a bowl. In the center of the room, toward the back, is a fire pit with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") over it. In the northwestern corner of the room is a cupboard holding several pieces of crockery, two bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [leathers strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), and a rack of [dog meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat "Skyrim:Dog Meat"). To the right of this is a food barrel with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") on top. Further along the north wall are a couple of wall shelves that hold a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), crockery, and a couple of food sacks. Against the east wall is a long wooden table and chairs, with a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a bowl of [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a bowl containing three [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"). There is an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie") by the hearth and a narrow wooden table against the partition wall between the bedroom and cooking areas. On this table is a bowl containing loaves of bread and crockery. There are also four [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") and two bunches of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear") hanging from a beam that supports the roof.

## Notes

- Though you can find and activate a [grain mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Milling "Skyrim:Milling") here, it will only lead to an animation, with no actual function in the game.
- The guard outside the farm will not be replaced should the Stormcloaks take Whiterun.
- All containers in the area, other than some barrels and sacks, are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
- Even if you help Alfhild Battle-Born, everything in the farmhouse may still be considered owned.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the sacks, barrels, cow, pichfork, and bottles of wine outside the building as owned by the farm.
