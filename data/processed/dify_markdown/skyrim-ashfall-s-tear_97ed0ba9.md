Ashfall's Tear

**Ashfall's Tear** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") north of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock") containing a hidden [Tribunal Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Tribunal_Temple "Skyrim:Tribunal Temple") shrine to the goddess [Almalexia](/wiki/Lore:Almalexia "Lore:Almalexia"). It contains only one zone, *[Ashfall's Tear](#Ashfall.27s_Tear)*.

The cultists within are outcasts from [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") society, persecuted by the [New Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple "Skyrim:Temple") for their heretical beliefs. They will be hostile towards you, although it is possible to infiltrate and later join the cult during the quest [Ghosts of the Tribunal](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest) "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal (quest)").

### Exterior

The cave can be reached by following the slope east of [Raven Rock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Mine "Skyrim:Raven Rock Mine"), then turning left until you reach the mountainside. An [emerald geode](/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald_Geode "Skyrim:Emerald Geode") can be found north of the cave.

### Ashfall's Tear

Walk along the corridor, there's a door and a pull chain, you need rotate 3 pillars to the correct position and pull the chain to open the door, the opened door can be closed again after respawned. After the door is a long winding downslope to the door of the heretic temple, the door is guarded by two [Ordinators](/wiki/Skyrim:Ordinator "Skyrim:Ordinator"). There's a Shrine of Almalexia(+10 Heavy Armor, +10% One-handed weapon damage) in front of the temple door. The area behind the door can be seen as a chamber, there are some plants, ebony ore and heartstone ore in it. Walk along the chamber corridor, you will come into a large hall, within it are [Temple Priest](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_Priest "Skyrim:Temple Priest"), [Matriarch Drevlan](/wiki/Skyrim:Matriarch_Drevlan "Skyrim:Matriarch Drevlan") and 4 potential followers [Hand Ethra Mavandas](/wiki/Skyrim:Hand_Ethra_Mavandas "Skyrim:Hand Ethra Mavandas") a one-handed warrior, [Hand Kydren Indobar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hand_Kydren_Indobar "Skyrim:Hand Kydren Indobar") a one-handed warrior with [Hand Indobar's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Hand_Indobar%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Hand Indobar's Journal"), [Vesparth the Toe](/wiki/Skyrim:Vesparth_the_Toe "Skyrim:Vesparth the Toe") a double dual warrior with [Cleaver of St. Felms](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleaver_of_St._Felms "Skyrim:Cleaver of St. Felms") and [Vesparth's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Vesparth%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Vesparth's Journal"), [Watchman Sindras](/wiki/Skyrim:Watchman_Sindras "Skyrim:Watchman Sindras") an electric mage.

There's are 7 rooms in total including the large hall, the exit where you come is to the west.

- To the north is a garden, a [Caretaker Ineril](/wiki/Skyrim:Caretaker_Ineril "Skyrim:Caretaker Ineril") may be within if you have delived the translated propaganda letter to [Geldis Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Geldis_Sadri "Skyrim:Geldis Sadri") during [Her Word Against Theirs](/wiki/Skyrim:Her_Word_Against_Theirs "Skyrim:Her Word Against Theirs").
- To the east are 4 rooms.
  - East-north is a sculpture room, within are Shrine of Sotha Sil(+10 Magicka, +5% Magicka Regeneration) and Shrine of Vivec(5% better price, 5% harder to detect), [Sculptor Visthar](/wiki/Skyrim:Sculptor_Visthar "Skyrim:Sculptor Visthar") is always sculpting in it, [Sculptor Visthar's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Sculptor_Visthar%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Sculptor Visthar's Journal") is on the rack.
  - East-east is a upslope circling road to a higher room, it's where Matriarch Drevlan stays during night, there's [Matriarch Drevlan's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Matriarch_Drevlan%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Matriarch Drevlan's Journal") on the desk, west of the small room is a high platform, it looks like a speech podium, from which you can overlook the large hall.
  - East-south is a smithing and resting room with 4 beds.
  - East of smithing room is the armory room, which can only be accessed after completing all three concurrent tasks during [Ghosts of the Tribunal (quest)](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest) "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal (quest)"), inside the armory room are 6 display cases and 7 mannequins, [Fahlbtharz Forge Gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_Items#Fahlbtharz_Forge_Gem "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal Items"), unique mask [Mask of Almalexia](/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Almalexia "Skyrim:Mask of Almalexia"), unique weapons [Light of Day](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_of_Day "Skyrim:Light of Day"), [Magebane](/wiki/Skyrim:Magebane "Skyrim:Magebane") and [Skull Crusher](/wiki/Skyrim:Skull_Crusher "Skyrim:Skull Crusher") are in the display cases. There's a portcullis at the east of the armory room which need be opened by a mask riddle during [Ashen Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Heart "Skyrim:Ashen Heart"), behind the portcullis is a chest, the unique weapon [Hopesfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hopesfire "Skyrim:Hopesfire") and [Erden Relvel's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Erden_Relvel%27s_Note "Skyrim:Erden Relvel's Note") are in the chest.
- To the south is a alchemy and enchanting room with an Alchemy Lab and an Arcane Enchanter, there're also a lot of potions on 5 racks, [Priest Drureth](/wiki/Skyrim:Priest_Drureth "Skyrim:Priest Drureth") lives in it, [Priest Drureth's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Priest_Drureth%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Priest Drureth's Journal") is on the desk, [Curate Melita](/wiki/Skyrim:Curate_Melita "Skyrim:Curate Melita") also lives in it should she survives in [Careless Curation](/wiki/Skyrim:Careless_Curation "Skyrim:Careless Curation").

## Bugs

- Even after you have befriended the Temple, the pillar door may close and the first interior door may lock again, with no in-game option but to pick the lock.
- Summoning [Hilda](/wiki/Skyrim:Hilda "Skyrim:Hilda") or any of the pets added by the [Pets of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Pets_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Pets of Skyrim") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") within Ashfall's Tear will cause the cultists to become hostile and attempt to attack the creatures (but not you) relentlessly. This can cause congestion in the narrow passages, making it difficult to exit the temple.
