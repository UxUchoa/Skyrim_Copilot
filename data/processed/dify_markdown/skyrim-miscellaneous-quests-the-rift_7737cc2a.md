# Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests/The Rift

## Overview

This page lists all of the **[Miscellaneous Quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests")** for the hold of **[The Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift")**. This page only lists the miscellaneous quests that happen outside of the major towns. For the quests in towns, see [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests/Ivarstead "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests/Ivarstead"), [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests/Riften "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests/Riften"), and [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests/Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests/Shor's Stone").
