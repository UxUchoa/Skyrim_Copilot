Arcturus

**Arcturus**, an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial"), is part of the group of [Penitus Oculatus](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Agent "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Agent") agents sent to the [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary") during the quest [Death Incarnate](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Incarnate "Skyrim:Death Incarnate").

When you search the destroyed sanctuary for survivors, you will encounter [Nazir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazir "Skyrim:Nazir") battling Arcturus near the dinner table in the northern chamber. Obviously losing the fight, the weakened Nazir will call upon you for help and you will have no choice but to battle and kill Arcturus.

Arcturus wields an [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword") and a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"). He wears a complete set of [Penitus Oculatus armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Armor "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Armor") and carries a few spare coins and possibly some random items like a [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch"), an [amulet](/wiki/Skyrim:Specialty_Gear#Amulets_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Specialty Gear"), some inn [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") and/or a drink.
