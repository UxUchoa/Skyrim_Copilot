## Quick Walkthrough

1. Report for duty to [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") at the [Rift Imperial Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Imperial_Camp "Skyrim:Rift Imperial Camp").
2. Join the Legion soldiers staging for the attack.
3. Clear the fort of Stormcloak rebels.

### Report for Duty

Report for duty to [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") at the [Rift Imperial Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Imperial_Camp "Skyrim:Rift Imperial Camp"), where she will give you orders to rendezvous with a group of Imperial soldiers preparing to attack [Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall "Skyrim:Fort Greenwall") and to assist in the assault.

### Join the Soldiers Preparing the Assault

Make your way to Fort Greenwall. The fort is just north of Riften, and the soldiers are waiting just southeast of the fort. As you approach the group, they will storm the southeast entrance to the fort.

### Take the Fort

Assist your fellow soldiers in clearing the fort of Stormcloak rebels while the game updates you with the percentage of enemies remaining. The battle takes place in the exterior parts of the fort only, in the courtyard and on the walls. The entrance is blocked by wooden barricades, but these can be easily brought down by melee attacks.

Once you have killed all Stormcloak rebels, the quest will be completed.

### Report to General Tullius

Your objective then becomes to report to General Tullius at Castle Dour to receive your reward, but doing so technically occurs under the overarching [Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim") quest. After you have taken the fort, The Battle for Fort Greenwall will clear from your journal. If you didn't flee the battle, Tullius will promote you to [Tribune](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion#Ranks "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") along with a leveled sword (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).

### Achievements

One [achievement](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements") is unlocked at the end of this quest:

## Notes

- Corpses will disappear shortly after death, so you may wish to loot them as you go to avoid missing out.
- If [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") is given to the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") then this quest will not be given to you.
- Even after you are told that 0% of the defenders remain and that you have won, there may still be several Stormcloak survivors who flee northeast along the road in the direction of the mining settlement of [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"). If you decide to pursue them, be careful of who you target, especially in the dark, since you can easily kill some of the local guards of Shor's Stone by mistake and incur a bounty in the Rift.
- Once the battle begins at the fort, Rikke leaves The Rift Imperial Camp and it is instead commanded by [Legate Fasendil](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Fasendil "Skyrim:Legate Fasendil"). After the battle is finished and the quest clears in the journal, the Rift Imperial Camp will be packed up and abandoned.
- Completing this quest will exile [Laila Law-Giver](/wiki/Skyrim:Laila_Law-Giver "Skyrim:Laila Law-Giver") and her courtiers to the [Palace of the Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings"), and install [Maven Black-Briar](/wiki/Skyrim:Maven_Black-Briar "Skyrim:Maven Black-Briar") as [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of The Rift.
- After completion of this quest, like any Civil War quest under [Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim"), unmarked by in-game references, you can approach Legate Rikke for a [medium leveled gold reward](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Gold_Rewards "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"); this is not always recommended, as entering her cell sets up the next quest in the series and, if triggered improperly, causes the missing *"Reporting for duty."* dialogue option (see [this](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim#Bugs "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim") bug section). Return to Legate Rikke to receive the leveled gold reward only when you want to start the next quest in the series and skip the reward when the game doesn't prompt you to return to her. Don't approach Rikke between quests of different holds.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage CWFortSiegeFort stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest CWFortSiegeFort`.
