These **Dark Elves** are all male [farmers](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") and wear randomly determined farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") (7 variations) or a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic"), a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") (4 variations), with a 50% chance for a [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Hat "Skyrim:Hat") (3 variations). Additionally there is a slim chance they may carry a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and a small amount of gold.

They can be found in the following locations:

- [Cronvangr Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Cronvangr_Cave "Skyrim:Cronvangr Cave")
- [Faldar's Tooth](/wiki/Skyrim:Faldar%27s_Tooth "Skyrim:Faldar's Tooth")
- [Hob's Fall Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Hob%27s_Fall_Cave "Skyrim:Hob's Fall Cave")
- Northwest of [Half-Moon Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill "Skyrim:Half-Moon Mill"), east of [Moss Mother Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern "Skyrim:Moss Mother Cavern") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-48572&y=-52172&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- East of [Vahlok's Tomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Vahlok%27s_Tomb "Skyrim:Vahlok's Tomb")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")

The dead Dark Elf peddler

A dead **Dark Elf** peddler[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Netch Leather Armor") is found at a camp southeast of [Haknir's Shoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir%27s_Shoal "Skyrim:Haknir's Shoal"). [His journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Peddler%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Peddler's Journal") is found on his body, detailing how he made a deal with rieklings for netch leather and presumably died fighting off the nearby [horkers](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker "Skyrim:Horker"). He wears a [Dunmer Outfit](/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Outfit "Skyrim:Dunmer Outfit") and [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Shoes "Skyrim:Dunmer Shoes"). Additionally he carries a [Purple Mountain Flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") and a piece of [charcoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal "Skyrim:Charcoal"), as referenced in his journal to be part of dyeing the leather. He may also carry a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and a small amount of gold.
