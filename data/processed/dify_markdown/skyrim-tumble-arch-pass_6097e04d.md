Tumble Arch Pass

**Tumble Arch Pass** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") northeast of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/SR-place-Tumble_Arch_Pass_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Tumble_Arch_Pass_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Tumble_Arch_Pass_02.jpg)

Tumble Arch Pass

One [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and one [mammoth](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") occupy the camp, which features the painted stones and mammoth ribs and skulls typical of giant camps. The camp has a large campfire and a pile of firewood with a dead [deer](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer "Skyrim:Deer") beside it. A [mammoth cheese bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl") is beside the fire, with three more to the west. Southeast of the fire against some rocks is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). Above the camp on the rocks to the south is a large stone bowl containing three sprigs of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") and a [giant club](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Club "Skyrim:Giant Club") (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

## Notes

- Up the mountain southeast of the camp is a skeleton on the ground with a [shrine of Julianos](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Julianos "Skyrim:Shrines") almost covered by snow and a [quicksilver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore Vein") nearby. This area is easier to reach from [Shearpoint](/wiki/Skyrim:Shearpoint "Skyrim:Shearpoint") to the south. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=70163&y=26768)
- Version 2.0.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") replaces all [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") pieces here with the snow-covered version of the same plant.

## Bugs

- The giant club cannot be used by you, but can be picked up by a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") if you order them to do so. However, it will then be impossible to remove it from their inventory.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.1, fixes this bug.
