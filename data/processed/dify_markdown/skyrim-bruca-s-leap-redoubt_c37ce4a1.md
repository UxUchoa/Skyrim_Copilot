Bruca's Leap Redoubt

**Bruca's Leap Redoubt** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") southwest of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") containing [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"). It contains only one zone, *[Bruca's Leap Redoubt](#Bruca.27s_Leap_Redoubt)*.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/SR-place-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt_02.jpg)

The exterior campsite

Two Forsworn, one a melee fighter and the other an archer, have made camp outside the cave entrance. The campsite features a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), five bedrolls, and a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"). Also in the area are two [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot") fungi, a [chickens' nest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), and a [chicken](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken").

### Bruca's Leap Redoubt

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-interior-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.jpg)

The main chamber

The cave interior is quite small, consisting of a curving passage leading down to a chamber with wooden scaffolding. The [Forsworn Briarheart](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Briarheart "Skyrim:Forsworn Briarheart") shaman and its melee fighter bodyguard will likely be immediately alerted to your presence. However, by sneaking, you may snipe at them from the window-like opening straight down from the entrance. An [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") is on the ledge nearby. At the bottom of the passage is a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a [Dwarven arrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Arrow "Skyrim:Dwarven Arrow").

Lying on a table in the chamber is the corpse of a Forsworn fighter who was in the process of being transformed into a Briarheart, with his [heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Human_Heart "Skyrim:Human Heart") and an iron dagger placed nearby. A [bear pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Bear Pelt"), a [cave bear pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Cave_Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Cave Bear Pelt"), and two [cow hides](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow_Hide "Skyrim:Cow Hide") are scattered around, and an [ingredient barrel](/wiki/Skyrim:Barrel "Skyrim:Barrel") of uncommon quality is near the table. A bookshelf holds a copy of the [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Tragedy in Black](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Tragedy_in_Black "Skyrim:A Tragedy in Black")*, a few ingredients (including one sample each of [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries"), [sabre cat tooth](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Tooth "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Tooth"), [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat"), and [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap")), and an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") of uncommon quality. A random [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") is in a bucket nearby. The boss chest is behind the bookshelf, but beware the [bear trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") in front of it.

A few fungi grow in the cave, including one [blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort"), three Namira's rot, and one white cap.

## Bugs

- Bruca's Leap Redoubt isn't marked as a cave, so cave-based radiant quests won't target it.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- The Forsworn members here may attack their own chickens.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.1, addresses this issue. Both factions have been set to be friendly towards one another to prevent this from happening.
- The cave contains a goat hide you cannot interact with.
