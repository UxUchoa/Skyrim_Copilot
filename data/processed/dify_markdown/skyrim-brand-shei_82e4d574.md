**Brand-Shei** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") trader within [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). He was orphaned at a young age and was raised by [Argonians](/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian "Skyrim:Argonian") in [Black Marsh](/wiki/Lore:Black_Marsh "Lore:Black Marsh"). He owns a stall in Riften Grand Plaza and specializes in selling [goods](#Standard_Merchandise) from [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind"). Like other vendors of the plaza he is suspicious of [Brynjolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Brynjolf "Skyrim:Brynjolf") and his actions have earned Brand-Shei the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)")'s ire.

Brand-Shei is a permanent resident at [Haelga's Bunkhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse "Skyrim:Haelga's Bunkhouse") and sleeps there every night between midnight and 6am. He starts the day with a one-hour breakfast then heads down to his market stall at Riften Grand Plaza. He works there for a solid thirteen hours before he returns to the bunkhouse for another meal at 8pm. After eating for four hours, he heads to bed at midnight and sleeps for six hours.

He wears a set of merchant [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0004223c "Skyrim:Clothes") and matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d "Skyrim:Boots"). He carries a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") (up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") quality at level 20).

## Dialogue

When you meet him in the market he will try and sell his wares by shouting:

:   *"Rare trinkets and the finest oddities from Morrowind!"*
:   *"Gems, potions, exotic ingredients... I have them all!"*
:   *"The fairest prices and the highest quality in Skyrim!"*

When you talk to him he will continue:

:   *"Can I interest you in some fine goods from Morrowind?"*
:   *"Have anything you wish to sell? I pay fair prices for all sorts of goods."*
:   *"If there's anything you wish to purchase, just let me know."*

If you meet him outside his working hours he may greet you with:

:   *"If you're ever in the market, I deal in specialty goods from Morrowind at one of the stands."*

### A Chance Arrangement

When you meet Brynjolf in Riften, it soon becomes apparent that Brand-Shei is in bad standing with the Thieves Guild. Brynjolf has set up a scam and you're supposed to plant [Madesi](/wiki/Skyrim:Madesi "Skyrim:Madesi")'s [silver ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Madesi%27s_Silver_Ring "Skyrim:Madesi's Silver Ring") in Brand-Shei's pocket. Brynjolf will not provide much explanation about what Brand-Shei has done. When Brynjolf starts his distraction, Brand-Shei will leave his stand and express his doubts about yet another product from Brynjolf:

:   **Brand-Shei**: *"Come on, Brynjolf... what is it this time?"*
:   **Brynjolf**: *"Patience, Brand-Shei. This is a rare opportunity, and I wouldn't want you to get left out."*
:   **Madesi**: *"That's what you said about the Wisp Essence and it turned out to be crushed nirnroot mixed with water!"*
:   **Brynjolf**: *"That was a simple misunderstanding, but this item is the real thing. Lads and lasses I give you, Falmerblood Elixir!"*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"Oh come on, are you talking about the Snow Elves?"*
:   **Brynjolf**: *"The one and only."*

Brynjolf will then continue his prank while you steal the ring. Brand-Shei, on the other hand, will become quite tempted by Brynjolf's sales speech and will even ask:

:   **Brand-Shei**: *"How much does it cost?"*

While Madesi remains skeptical, Brand-Shei will decide that Falmerblood Elixir may be a good investment, and chooses between a few random lines:

:   **Brand-Shei**: *"I dunno, sounds like I should try it."*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"What if he's right? Maybe I'll buy one."*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"I could get rich and stop selling these trinkets."*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"This is just what I need right now."*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"I can't afford to get one, but can I afford not to?"*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"That Wisp Essence didn't help, but maybe this will."*

Once you plant the ring on Brand-Shei, Brynjolf will end the prank and a disappointed Brand-Shei will return to his stall with the words:

:   **Brand-Shei**: *"Damn. I knew I shouldn't have waited."*

However, Madesi will soon find out about the theft from his lockbox, and alert a guard who will confront Brand-Shei:

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/SR-npc-Brand-Shei_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Brand-Shei_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Brand-Shei_03.jpg)

*"This is insane! I didn't steal anything!"*

:   **Guard**: *"All right, Brand-Shei. Turn out your pockets, we know you have it."*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"Have what? What in blazes are you talking about?"*
:   **Guard**: *"Don't play stupid. I said turn out your pockets... now!"*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"I'm telling you, I don't... wait, what's this ring? This isn't mine!"*
:   **Guard**: *"That's right. It isn't yours. You're under arrest, Brand-Shei."*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"This is insane! I didn't steal anything! I never saw this ring before in my entire life!"*
:   **Guard**: *"We can do this one of two ways. You can walk with me up to the keep, or I can drag your lifeless body. Your choice."*
:   **Brand-Shei**: *"But... I... very well."*

The guard will then escort Brand-Shei to [Riften Jail](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Jail "Skyrim:Riften Jail") where he will be put in one of the cells, stripped of his belongings, and clad in a belted tunic:

:   *"Who did this to me?"*
:   *"This city is corrupt! You're all corrupt!"*
:   *"This is unfair! I'm innocent!"*

### Distant Memories

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-npc-Brand-Shei_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Brand-Shei_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Brand-Shei_04.jpg)

*"We're just going to make sure he sits in the prisons for a few days."*

Whether or not he is in prison, Brand-Shei has another problem, namely his past. When you point out that Brand-Shei is an odd name for a dark elf, he will agree:

:   *"I may be dark elf by birth, but I was raised Argonian. Through reasons I'm still trying to discover, I ended up orphaned then taken in by a kindly Argonian family in Black Marsh. I hope one day to find out what happened to me... how I ended up like that."*
:   **Do you have any clues about your past?**
:   *"Just one. I know when I was found by my Argonian father, I was wrapped in a blanket bearing the symbol of [House Telvanni](/wiki/Lore:House_Telvanni "Lore:House Telvanni"). It was one of the great houses in Morrowind long ago. Whether that means I was one of them or not, I'm uncertain. If you come across anything in your travels that might provide me with the answers I'm looking for, I'd be grateful."*
:   **I'll keep an eye open.**

    :   *"Thank you."*
:   **Why were you searching in Skyrim?**

    :   *"I learned that a matron who had served for House Telvanni had escaped Morrowind during the [Accession War](/wiki/Lore:Accession_War "Lore:Accession War"). Records showed her buying passage aboard a sailing vessel named 'The Pride of Tel Vos,' but that's where the trail ran cold. I spent years looking for what became of the ship, but I ended up empty-handed."*

A [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Lymdrenn_Tenvanni%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Lymdrenn Tenvanni's Journal") found in the wreckage of the ship reveals that Brand-Shei's true name is Brandyl Tenvanni. When you return from the [Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_The_Pride_of_Tel_Vos "Skyrim:Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos") with the journal, Brand-Shei will be most interested and hand you a reward:

:   **I found a book that might help your search.**
:   *"Let me see that! Yes... yes. This is it! I don't know how you found this stranger, but you've obviously succeeded where I had failed. Please, let me offer you this [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Brand-Shei%27s_Strongbox_Key "Skyrim:Brand-Shei's Strongbox Key"). It unlocks my strongbox under my stand in the marketplace. Help yourself to anything inside."*

## Notes

- There is a "Loot" [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (Thieves Guild)") on the front of his market stall.
- Both his [merchant chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Merchant_Chests "Skyrim:Containers") and his [service faction](/wiki/Skyrim:ServicesRiftenPlazaBrandish "Skyrim:ServicesRiftenPlazaBrandish"), as well as several object references in Riften, bear the name "Brandish". However, this has nothing to do with the [unrelated character of the same name](/wiki/Skyrim:Brandish "Skyrim:Brandish"). It instead refers to **Brandish Begin-Again**, an earlier (and possibly Nord) iteration of Brand-Shei's character. This name appears in the *[Prima Official Game Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide")* in Brand-Shei's place, and [unused dialogue for Brynjolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Brynjolf#Unused_Dialogue "Skyrim:Brynjolf") similarly shows Brandish being mentioned in Brand-Shei's stead.
- Brand-Shei does not have a unique facial model, he instead uses the default male Dark Elf face. He shares this quirk with [Faldrus](/wiki/Skyrim:Faldrus "Skyrim:Faldrus").
- Although he advertises that he sells "exotic ingredients", Brand-Shei does not sell any ingredients.

  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue.
