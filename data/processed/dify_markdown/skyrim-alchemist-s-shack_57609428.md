Alchemist's Shack

The **Alchemist's Shack** is a small [shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") south of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead").

## Related Quests

- **[Pets of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Pets_of_Skyrim_(quest) "Skyrim:Pets of Skyrim (quest)")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Pets_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Pets of Skyrim"): Find trained pets around Skyrim.

## Walkthrough

The shack is a dilapidated single-story building with its doors and windows missing. It can be seen from the road between [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen") and [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"). There is a wattle-fenced garden to the rear, with an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs") to the right of the rear doorway. A barrel containing random ingredients is between the shack and the lab, with two samples of [hawk feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk_Feathers "Skyrim:Hawk Feathers") and a [red mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") on top. A [blue mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), three [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell"), three [jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes"), a [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade"), two [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota"), and two [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Grass Pod") grow in the garden, and a [birds' nest](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") is in front to the west. This is a good area to find [blue butterflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly "Skyrim:Blue Butterfly") and [butterflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly "Skyrim:Butterfly") during the day and [luna moths](/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth "Skyrim:Luna Moth") at night.

With the [Pets of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Pets_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Pets of Skyrim") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creations "Skyrim:Creations") installed, the rabbit [Thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_(creature) "Skyrim:Thistle (creature)") can be found in the garden and can be tamed with carrots.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-interior-Alchemist%27s_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Alchemist%27s_Shack.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Alchemist%27s_Shack.jpg)

The shack's interior

As you enter through the front doorway you will find two shelving units; the one on your right contains a [Potion of Health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potions"), two books, and some clutter. The shelving unit in front of you contains a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a sample of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), two [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), three [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), and a [butterfly in a jar](/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly_in_a_Jar "Skyrim:Butterfly in a Jar"). The jar can only be found in this shack and respawns with the rest of the shack. Between the two shelving units is a barrel containing [uncommon ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and a basket containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"). To your left as you enter is a bed (unowned). Next to this is an end table holding an [alchemist's journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemist%27s_Journal_(shack) "Skyrim:Alchemist's Journal (shack)") and an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") containing [common ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"). This end table does not respawn, and can be useful as a storage option. To the right of the rear exit is a bucket containing a sample of [blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort") and hanging above are a [garlic braid](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") and a sample of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam").

## Notes

- The items will respawn over time, except for the end table beside the bed.
- Two [wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf") often spawn on the road shortly before the shack, as well as some [goats](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") further up the road from Helgen, who often attract the attention of the wolves. Up the mountain to the left of the shack is a trail which is full of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry "Skyrim:Snowberry") and an Iron Ore vein. Be careful up the mountain pass, as a [Snow Bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear") makes its home there, regardless of your character level. This is the only place a Snow Bear will always spawn.
