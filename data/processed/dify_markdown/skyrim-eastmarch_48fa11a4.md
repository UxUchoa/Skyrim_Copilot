**Eastmarch** is a [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in eastern [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"), with its capital in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). The hold is the home of [Jarl Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak"), leader of the [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") rebellion. It only becomes Imperial-run if the player joins the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") and completes the entire quest line, including both [the Battle for Fort Amol](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Amol "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Amol") and the [Battle for Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Windhelm "Skyrim:Battle for Windhelm"). Should this occur, the new Jarl will be [Brunwulf Free-Winter](/wiki/Skyrim:Brunwulf_Free-Winter "Skyrim:Brunwulf Free-Winter").

Eastmarch is in the northeastern corner of Skyrim. [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)") is to the northwest, [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale") and [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold") to the west, the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World") to the southwest, and [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift") to the south. The [Dunmer](/wiki/Lore:Dunmer "Lore:Dunmer") homeland of [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind") is to the east, across the [Velothi Mountain](/wiki/Skyrim:Velothi_Mountains "Skyrim:Velothi Mountains") passes. Since the [Red Year](/wiki/Lore:Red_Year "Lore:Red Year"), Dunmer have had a much bigger presence in Eastmarch, particularly in the capital, due to its proximity to one of the only [passes](/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmeth_Pass "Skyrim:Dunmeth Pass") through the Velothi.

## Topographical Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-place-Bonestrewn_Crest.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bonestrewn_Crest.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Bonestrewn_Crest.jpg)

Bonestrewn Crest rises from the sulfur pools

Eastmarch varies greatly. The central and southern parts of Eastmarch are dominated by sulfurous pools and rocky crags, as well as [Bonestrewn Crest](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonestrewn_Crest "Skyrim:Bonestrewn Crest"). The Velothi Mountains form a spine along the eastern edge of the hold, and a high ridge to the south leads up from the lowland sulfur pools to the Rift.

The [Darkwater River](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_River "Skyrim:Darkwater River") joins the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River") in the west, after which the White River merges with [River Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim "Skyrim:River Yorgrim") at the Windhelm bridge and flows out into the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts").

### Flora and Fauna

The sulfur pools are home to [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant"), [deer](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer "Skyrim:Deer"), [sabre cats](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Sabre Cat"), and other, smaller creatures, such as [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit "Skyrim:Rabbit") and [goats](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat"). Further north are snowy sabre cats, [ice wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf"), and [frost trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll "Skyrim:Troll") along the road to Morrowind.

There are a few [farms](/wiki/Skyrim:Farms "Skyrim:Farms") near Windhelm, but otherwise the only plant in the snowy parts of Eastmarch is the humble [snowberry bush](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"). In the sulfur region to the south, in the middle of the hold, the endemic plants of [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue"), [Jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes"), and [creep clusters](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster") are to be found. The pine forest and shrublands surrounding the sulfur region are home to [thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle"), [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"), and all common varieties of [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Mountain Flower"). [Mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") and to some extent [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") can also be found throughout the hold. In the last parts of the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River"), stretching from [Mixwater Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Mixwater_Mill "Skyrim:Mixwater Mill") to the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts"), [nordic barnacle](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle") is common.

### Routes and Pathways

Two main routes run north to south in Eastmarch. They skirt the sulfur pools on either side. The roads are connected to each other at the far northern and southern ends, forming a continuous loop. There are also major branches off of this road: one west of [Fort Amol](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Amol "Skyrim:Fort Amol") leading to Whiterun; one west-northwest of [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern") leading south to the Rift, and two near Windhelm. One leads to the Pale and beyond, and the other along the Sea of Ghosts past farms until you hit the impassable, invisible wall near [Refugees' Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Refugees%27_Rest "Skyrim:Refugees' Rest").

There are two pathways into the sulfur pools from the east road: one runs from south of Kynesgrove to [Witchmist Grove](/wiki/Skyrim:Witchmist_Grove "Skyrim:Witchmist Grove"), and the other starts near [Steamcrag Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamcrag_Camp "Skyrim:Steamcrag Camp") and heads west to Bonestrewn Crest. Other pathways in the hold are to landmarks such as the [dragon mound](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Mound "Skyrim:Dragon Mound") above Kynesgrove (and, by extension, [Narzulbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Narzulbur "Skyrim:Narzulbur")), or [Mistwatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Mistwatch "Skyrim:Mistwatch").

  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19319)) A new faction was created specifically for the residents of the longhouse in order to set ownership correctly.
