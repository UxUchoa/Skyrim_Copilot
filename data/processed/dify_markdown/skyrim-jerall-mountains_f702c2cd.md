The **Jerall Mountains** are a mountain range that stretches along the southern border of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim").

The mountains form the southern border of [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold") and [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"). Its harsh, cold climate has made the mountains nearly inhospitable, with few inhabitants living there and no major settlements throughout history. Some points of interest include the town of [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen"), [Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Fort Neugrad"), [Bloodlet Throne](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodlet_Throne "Skyrim:Bloodlet Throne"), the dragon shrines of [Ancient's Ascent](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient%27s_Ascent "Skyrim:Ancient's Ascent") and [Lost Tongue Overlook](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Tongue_Overlook "Skyrim:Lost Tongue Overlook"), the Dwemer ruins of [Rkund](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund "Skyrim:Ruins of Rkund"), and the large [Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Ruins "Skyrim:Nordic Ruins") of [Arcwind Point](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcwind_Point "Skyrim:Arcwind Point").

The [Goblins](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblins "Skyrim:Goblins") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") adds a subterranean mountain passage known as [Gromm's Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Gromm%27s_Pass "Skyrim:Gromm's Pass"), although access to [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil") was recently cut off by a tunnel collapse.

[The Cause](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause "Skyrim:The Cause") adds an underground [Ayleid](/wiki/Lore:Ayleid "Lore:Ayleid") ruin beneath the mountains known as [Rielle](/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle "Skyrim:Rielle"). It extends into Cyrodiil, but its southern tunnels have been blocked by rubble.
