## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read the [*For Sale*](/wiki/Skyrim:For_Sale "Skyrim:For Sale") notice at [The Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare").
2. Purchase a goat in [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead") for 200 gold and read [*Halvar's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Halvar%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Halvar's Journal").
3. Locate the tame fox south of [Half-Moon Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill "Skyrim:Half-Moon Mill") and feed it a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll").
4. Locate the rabbit at the [Alchemist's Shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemist%27s_Shack "Skyrim:Alchemist's Shack") and feed it a [carrot](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot").
5. Kill the [Vampire Naturalist](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Naturalist "Skyrim:Vampire Naturalist") in [Cronvangr Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Cronvangr_Cave "Skyrim:Cronvangr Cave") and rescue the tame spider.
6. Find the tame skeever within [the Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway") and the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever_Cage_Key "Skyrim:Skeever Cage Key") to unlock its cage.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/38/SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_05.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_05.jpg)

A trained goat is for sale in Whiterun Hold

### Goat for Sale

In [The Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare") in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), a [*For Sale*](/wiki/Skyrim:For_Sale "Skyrim:For Sale") notice can be found at the counter. It is advertising for the sale of [Hilda](/wiki/Skyrim:Hilda "Skyrim:Hilda"), a trained pack [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") in [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead").

Journey to Rorikstead and locate [Halvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Halvar "Skyrim:Halvar") in order to purchase the goat. Halvar is a farmhand who can be found working the field beside [Cowflop Farmhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Cowflop_Farmhouse "Skyrim:Cowflop Farmhouse"). He will sell Hilda for 200 gold, at which point it will act as your follower and can be given various orders such as instructions to wait at a specified [city](/wiki/Skyrim:Cities "Skyrim:Cities") or [player house](/wiki/Skyrim:Houses "Skyrim:Houses").

Upon purchasing Hilda, you will be prompted to read [*Halvar's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Halvar%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Halvar's Journal") to find out more about the goat. It can be found on Halvar's bedroll next to the field. Reading it will reveal that Halvar had come across many other tame animals in his travels, and your journal will update with objectives to track down each of them. They can be found and rescued in any order you wish.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_02.jpg)

Sweet Roll stands over the body of its former master

### A Bosmer and His Fox

In the forest south-southwest of [Half-Moon Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill "Skyrim:Half-Moon Mill"), you will come across a collared fox named [Sweet Roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll_(creature) "Skyrim:Sweet Roll (creature)") standing over the body of a [Bosmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") [thief](/wiki/Skyrim:Thief_(Pets_of_Skyrim) "Skyrim:Thief (Pets of Skyrim)"). If you are having trouble finding it, stand beside the pile of rocks (the cairn) at the north end of the bridge and walk south; cross over the stream on foot; he is located right over the hill at about a total of 37 running steps from the cairn. A detect dead spell will show one blue and two red bodies. The thief was probably killed by [wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf"), as evidenced by the scattered [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow "Skyrim:Steel Arrow") and the two wolf carcasses surrounding him. His body can be looted for some ill-gotten treasure as well as the [*Thief's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Thief%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Thief's Journal").

Reading the journal will reveal that the fox has a penchant for [sweet rolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") (thus the name). It will refuse to interact with you until you have a sweet roll in your inventory, at which point you can feed and tame it. Fortunately, the thief has five sweet rolls on his body.

### Alchemist Bunny

A tame [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit "Skyrim:Rabbit") named [Thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_(creature) "Skyrim:Thistle (creature)") can be found at the abandoned [Alchemist's Shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemist%27s_Shack "Skyrim:Alchemist's Shack") in the mountain pass south of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"). The nearby [*Notes on Tamed Rabbit*](/wiki/Skyrim:Notes_on_Tamed_Rabbit "Skyrim:Notes on Tamed Rabbit") will reveal that Thistle enjoys [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"). A trough of carrots can be found right next to the rabbit, making it a simple matter to feed and tame it.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_03.jpg)

Some vampires prefer the taste of fear, others the taste of pain

### The Taste of Pain

You will be led to the hidden [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") lair within the spider-infested [Cronvangr Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Cronvangr_Cave "Skyrim:Cronvangr Cave") in order to obtain a pet [frostbite spider](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider") named [Arachnia](/wiki/Skyrim:Arachnia "Skyrim:Arachnia"). It has been tamed by a [Vampire Naturalist](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Naturalist "Skyrim:Vampire Naturalist"), who is using it to harvest vast amounts of venom for the purpose of torturing her victims before she drinks their blood.

Kill the vampire and loot the [Spider Cage Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Cage_Key "Skyrim:Spider Cage Key") from her corpse in order to free Arachnia from its cage. You can also loot the [*Vampire Naturalist's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Naturalist%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Vampire Naturalist's Journal") for some background information on her experiments. Once the cage is opened, Arachnia is yours.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Pets_of_Skyrim_04.jpg)

Free Skritch from a grisly fate

### A Skooma-Sucker's Dinner

Within [the Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway") beneath [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), you will come across a tame [skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever") named [Skritch](/wiki/Skyrim:Skritch "Skyrim:Skritch") found locked in a cage. You can find the body of its owner just across from the entrance to [The Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon"): a failed [pickpocket](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket_(person) "Skyrim:Pickpocket (person)") who planned to catch skeevers for food but decided to train one instead for use as a distraction. Loot the [Skeever Cage Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever_Cage_Key "Skyrim:Skeever Cage Key") from his corpse and open the cage in order to rescue Skritch and claim the animal as your own. You can also read the [*Pickpocket's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Pickpocket's Journal") for optional background information.

### Pets of Skyrim

Once you've obtained the last of the 5 pets, the quest concludes. Like other Creation Club pets, each one can obey a range of orders and each also has its own distinct attribute. Arachnia can place paralysis web traps and provide [Potent Frostbite Venom](/wiki/Skyrim:Potent_Frostbite_Venom "Skyrim:Potent Frostbite Venom"); Hilda can provide [Jugs of Milk](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug_of_Milk "Skyrim:Jug of Milk"); Skritch can locate or provide [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") items; Sweet Roll can locate or provide loot; and Thistle can locate plants which can be harvested for [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"). Some of the pets can also be equipped with an [Animal Collar](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Collar "Skyrim:Animal Collar") or an [Animal Pack](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Pack "Skyrim:Animal Pack"), two items added by Pets of Skyrim; indeed, Hilda is already wearing a pack, and Sweet Roll a collar. The collar is simply aesthetic, but the pack provides a real benefit by increasing the pet's carrying capacity by 40.

Each time you get a pet a new "Teleport: <Pet's Name>" spell appears. This can be used to retrieve your lost pet if it ever gets "misplaced".

## Bugs

- The *[For Sale](/wiki/Skyrim:For_Sale "Skyrim:For Sale")* note, *[Halvar's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Halvar%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Halvar's Journal")*, the *[Notes on Tamed Rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Notes_on_Tamed_Rabbit "Skyrim:Notes on Tamed Rabbit")*, and the *[Thief's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Thief%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Thief's Journal")* all remain marked as quest items even after the quest ends, resulting in them being stuck in your inventory permanently.
  - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch"), version 1.6.318, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") This can be fixed with the console commands `player.dropitem <ID>` or `player.removeitem <ID>` where <ID> is the [ID](/wiki/Skyrim:Form_ID "Skyrim:Form ID") of the item you want to drop from your inventory.
- If you purchase the goat from Halvar in Rorikstead before reading the *[For Sale](/wiki/Skyrim:For_Sale "Skyrim:For Sale")* note, the marker directing the player to purchase the goat from Halvar may not be able to be marked as cleared.
- If you do not purchase the goat from Halvar before collecting all the other pets, the quest will still complete and the goat will be unobtainable -- although the dialogue option to buy it will remain there, it will have no effect besides making you lose 200 gold. **?**
