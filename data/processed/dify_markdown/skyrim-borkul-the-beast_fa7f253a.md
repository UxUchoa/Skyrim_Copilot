Borkul the Beast

**Borkul the Beast** is an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:GuardOrc1H "Skyrim:GuardOrc1H") who is found inside [Cidhna Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Cidhna_Mine "Skyrim:Cidhna Mine"). He is considered to be the toughest prisoner in the mine and guards the door leading to [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") leader [Madanach](/wiki/Skyrim:Madanach "Skyrim:Madanach"). During [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine "Skyrim:No One Escapes Cidhna Mine"), you need to convince him to let you pass in order to gain your freedom.

Once outside the city, he will run all the way to [Druadach Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Redoubt "Skyrim:Druadach Redoubt") where he will start his life anew as part of the Forsworn. He will now spend his time either patrolling the path by the river, or wandering around inside the cave, occasionally eating, relaxing, or sleeping. His only dialogue at this point will be about him refusing naming you as [Blood-Kin](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood-Kin "Skyrim:Blood-Kin") to the Orcs.

He is only armed with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") and wears [ragged trousers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers "Skyrim:Ragged Trousers") and a pair of [iron gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets").

### No One Escapes Cidhna Mine

When first encountered, he will not leave his spot near the door, though he is quite talkative. When you approach him he will say:

:   *"The new meat. So soft. Tender. What was it like killing your first one, huh?"*

| Option | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| **Exciting. I felt the rush of blood... and smiled.** | *"A true killer, like me. The gods put us here to fill their halls with souls. You'll fit in fine down here."* |
| **Horrible. My soul is heavy with guilt.** | *"Pah. The gods have a place for killers. You can't carry the burden? You're weak."* |
| **I'm not a murderer.** | *"Liar."* |

You will then get the option to ask him a couple of simple questions:

:   **What are you in for?**
:   *"Murder. Banditry. Assault. Theft. And lollygagging."*
:   **How long have you been here?**
:   *"Guards brought me in about 12 years ago. Was running a good group of bandits up until then. But these Forsworn. They're nothing like the men I cobbled together. They're real killers."*

This conversation will also unlock some new greeting when he sees you:

:   *"You serve your time by digging. So dig."*
:   *"Prison's not so bad. Less complaints when I kill people."*

There will be no further dialogue options until you have spoken to fellow prisoner [Uraccen](/wiki/Skyrim:Uraccen "Skyrim:Uraccen") about Madanach.

When you return to Borkul you can ask him to see Madanach:

:   **I need to see Madanach.**
:   *"You want to talk to the King in Rags? Fine. But first you got to pay the toll. How about you get me a shiv? Not that I need one, but it's nice to have in case I need to do some "shaving." Ha ha."*

After he lets you in, he will wait by the door until you prove your loyalty to the King in Rags. When you follow Madanach out to the main chamber, Borkul will stand in the crowd and listen to the speech and shout:

:   *"The Reach belongs to the Forsworn!"*

He will then run through the secret tunnel with the rest of the Forsworn and enter [Markarth Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth_Ruins "Skyrim:Markarth Ruins"), saying nothing except:

:   *"Get going."*

Once outside, he will don a full set of [Forsworn Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Armor "Skyrim:Forsworn Armor") and fight for his freedom like everyone else, battling his way through the city towards the gates.

## Notes

- Borkul is the only member of the Forsworn that isn't a Breton/Reachman.
- Borkul will always call you a liar if you tell him you're not a murderer, even if that's the truth.

## Bugs

- Borkul's sleep package is blocked by his other Cidhna Mine packages.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- During No One Escapes Cidhna Mine, it is impossible to intimidate Borkul, due to the confidence level bug.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.5, addresses this issue. His confidence value has been lowered one notch to "Brave" instead of "Foolhardy".
- When you try to brawl with Borkul, he may equip his pickaxe during the fight.
