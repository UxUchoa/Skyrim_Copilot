Sylgja's House

**Sylgja's House** is a house in the small mining settlement of  [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"). It is located behind the outdoor campfire where the townsfolk gather every night.

It is the home of local miner [Sylgja](/wiki/Skyrim:Sylgja "Skyrim:Sylgja") who lives there alone. It consists of one area only, *[Sylgja's House](#Sylgja.27s_House)*.

## Sylgja's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-interior-Sylgja%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Sylgja%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sylgja%27s_House.jpg)

Inside Sylgja's House

As is common with houses in smaller settlements, the fireplace and the [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") are located opposite to the front door. There are three gold coins sitting on the mantelpiece. The southern part of the house holds little of interest – only two barrels, one of them with a huge [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") on top. Next to the fireplace is a dinner table with some food scattered around, two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), a clove of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), three slices of [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"). Adjacent to the table stands a cupboard with clutter, with a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") and a sample of [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat") on top.

The northern corner of the house holds a set of shelves with two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and one of regular [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), as well as a few common books. A chest in the corner holds a sparse amount of items and the dresser underneath holds [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing"). Following the wall is an end table that holds clutter and has a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") on top and a food barrel. The corner also contains Sylgja's huge dinner table, which is set up for four although Sylgja always eats alone. It holds three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), two bottles of alto wine and one regular.

The double bed stands in the eastern alcove which also has a huge wardrobe with clothes, with a [sabre cat pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Pelt") on top. There is also a small table with two bottles of [nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and a chest with valuables at the foot of the bed. Two end tables hold clutter; one has four gold coins sitting on top, while the other has a [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Hat "Skyrim:Hat") and a [potion of haggling](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling "Skyrim:Potion of Haggling") on top. On the floor, Sylgja has a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes "Skyrim:Shoes") and a hidden coin purse.

## Notes

- The chest at the foot of Sylgja's bed is *not* safe for storage.
