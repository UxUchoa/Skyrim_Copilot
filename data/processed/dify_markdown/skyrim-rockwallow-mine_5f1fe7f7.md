Rockwallow Mine

**Rockwallow Mine** is a small [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron "Skyrim:Iron") [mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mines "Skyrim:Mines") in eastern [Stonehills](/wiki/Skyrim:Stonehills "Skyrim:Stonehills"). It contains only one zone, *[Rockwallow Mine](#Rockwallow_Mine)*.

It is owned by Thane [Bryling](/wiki/Skyrim:Bryling "Skyrim:Bryling") of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") and run by the married couple [Pactur](/wiki/Skyrim:Pactur "Skyrim:Pactur") and [Sorli the Builder](/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder "Skyrim:Sorli the Builder"). [Gestur Rockbreaker](/wiki/Skyrim:Gestur_Rockbreaker "Skyrim:Gestur Rockbreaker") will [buy iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Mine_Ore "Skyrim:Mine Ore") from you.

### Exterior

A [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") is outside the mine entrance, which is east of [Sorli's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli%27s_House "Skyrim:Sorli's House").

### Rockwallow Mine

The entrance is a short, ice-filled tunnel leading to a central icy cave containing two wooden ramps up to platforms each within reach of an iron ore vein: one directly in front of you (to the east) and the other on your right (in the southwest). The latter has a bedroll beside a low table holding a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Orsinium and the Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Orsinium_and_the_Orcs "Skyrim:Orsinium and the Orcs")*. In the northeast and southeast, two short tunnels lead to dead ends, each with an iron ore vein at the end. Several [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") and shovels are found throughout the mine.

## Notes

- During the day, there are as many miners as ore veins, making it difficult to mine.
