A New Debt

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Be informed of your inherited debt by [Mogrul](/wiki/Skyrim:Mogrul_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Mogrul (Dragonborn)").
2. Pay the debt or kill Mogrul.

## Detailed Walkthrough

After the completion of the quest [Reluctant Steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Reluctant_Steward "Skyrim:Reluctant Steward") you will be made aware of a debt that [Drovas Relvi](/wiki/Skyrim:Drovas_Relvi "Skyrim:Drovas Relvi") owed to a money lender by the name of [Mogrul](/wiki/Skyrim:Mogrul_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Mogrul (Dragonborn)") operating out of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock"). Now that Drovas is under the protection of [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth") and out of Mogrul's reach, Mogrul expects you to make good on Drovas' debt. To help entice you to pay the debt, Mogrul will send his [thugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mogrul%27s_Thug "Skyrim:Mogrul's Thug") after you until the debt is paid.

Mogrul will approach you while you are in Raven Rock:

:   *"It seems you've inherited a situation."*

    :   **Situation? What kind of situation?**
    :   *"Drovas Revli*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *owes me 1000 septims. At least he did until you set him up with that [Telvanni](/wiki/Lore:Telvanni "Lore:Telvanni") wizard. Now he's untouchable. The way I figure it, it's your fault he can't pay me. So I guess I'm going to have to collect it from you."*

You will then be presented with several dialogue options:

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **I'm so sorry! Here, take the money.** | *"See, that wasn't so hard, now was it. Now get outta here."* | Pay 1000 gold |
| **And if I don't pay you?** | *"Nothing for now. But watch your back. I'll be sending collectors to find you. They won't ask so nicely. One way or the other, I'll get my money."* | Nothing |
| **Or I could just kill you now.** | *"Take your best shot, fetcher! But if you draw on me, the guards will put the bounty on your head, not mine."* | Nothing |
| **Please be patient. I'll get you your money.** | *"Patience is for fools. When you are tired of looking over your shoulder, you'll pay me."* | Nothing |
| **How about you take half? (Intimidate)** | **Passed**: *"Uh... yeah. That sounds good. Half. I think that will work."* **Failed**: *"How about I don't. You don't scare me."* | **Passed**: Pay 500 gold **Failed**: Nothing |

If you decide to pay him later:

:   **About that money Drovas owes you...**
:   *"Tired of running? I knew you were weak. Now pay me."*

At this point, you will have the same dialogue options as above.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-quest-A_New_Debt_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-A_New_Debt_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_New_Debt_02.jpg)

Mogrul's Thugs want all your money!

If you don't want to pay Mogrul, your only alternative is to kill him, with the risk of the [Redoran Guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran_Guard "Skyrim:Redoran Guard") intervening to stop you. However, if you are observant of your surroundings while you are in Raven Rock, you can detect Mogrul and his bodyguard [Slitter](/wiki/Skyrim:Slitter "Skyrim:Slitter") approaching you to demand that you pay up if you haven't been informed of your debt yet. He will follow you out of town if you are patient and don't run too far ahead, allowing you to discreetly dispatch him without drawing the guards' ire.

You can also visit Drovas and inform him of Mogrul's harassment. While he will not be willing to pay the debt on his own initiative, you can persuade him to provide you with whatever money he can to help you pay off the debt. You must pass a hard [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") check to successfully persuade Drovas to give you the money. If you succeed, he will give you 250 gold, but that won't be enough to cover the debt. The rest is up to you.

Note that you need a [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill of 75 to successfully persuade him:

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **You should pay me what you can. (Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"Well... I... Oh, fine. Here. This is all I have."* **Failed**: *"I don't see why. He's your problem now."* | **Passed**: Get 250 gold **Failed**: Nothing |

## Notes

- When Mogrul first approaches you for the initial conversation, exiting the conversation without picking a dialogue option will not start the quest. Mogrul will continue to follow, and attempt to initiate the same conversation again. You can lead Mogrul out of Raven Rock this way, allowing him to be killed without witnesses. Mogrul will not follow you into a building, or if you fast travel somewhere.
- If you attack Mogrul in the [Retching Netch](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Retching_Netch "Skyrim:The Retching Netch") and [Teldryn Sero](/wiki/Skyrim:Teldryn_Sero "Skyrim:Teldryn Sero") either currently is (or has previously been) under your employ, Sero will come to your aid. If you previously finished [Unearthed](/wiki/Skyrim:Unearthed "Skyrim:Unearthed") and spared [Ralis Sedarys](/wiki/Skyrim:Ralis_Sedarys "Skyrim:Ralis Sedarys"), Ralis will come to your aid as well.
- If you don't want to pay your debt but find it impossible to assassinate Mogrul without incurring a bounty, you can use [Nightingale Subterfuge](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Subterfuge "Skyrim:Nightingale Subterfuge"). Casting it while he, among others, is in its area of effect will result in them fighting, likely leading to Mogrul's death.
- Drovas will comment after the quest is finished, depending on your decision:
  - If you pay the debt, Drovas will say *"I heard you paid my debt to Mogrul. I don't know how I can every*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *repay you."*
  - If you kill Mogrul, Drovas will say *"I heard Mogrul is dead. That is a relief!"*
- After the quest is finished, you can pickpocket Mogrul to reclaim your money, but the gold may not be present in his inventory if you wait too long.

## Bugs

- If Mogrul is killed inside the Retching Netch while you are hidden, [Slitter](/wiki/Skyrim:Slitter "Skyrim:Slitter") will search for you. If you unhide so that he finds you and then attacks you and you flee outside, he will not follow you. Subsequently, even if you immediately re-enter the Retching Netch, Slitter will not be hostile to you, and will still tell you to talk to Mogrul.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- Mogrul can still send thugs after you, even though this quest has been completed.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
