## Quick Walkthrough

1. Exit the dungeon.
2. (Optional) Search the dungeon for more information.
3. Defeat the Pit Fighter.
4. Defeat the sabrecats.
5. Defeat the Champion.
6. (Optional) Take the champion's treasures.
7. Escape the Pit.
8. Retrieve your belongings.

### The Pit Dungeon

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon.jpg)

The dungeon hallway

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon_04.jpg)

The set of iron armor

After you choose the power option during [A Dying Wish](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dying_Wish "Skyrim:A Dying Wish"), you will pass out and awaken in a dungeon only wearing ragged robes and ragged boots. Exit the cell and you can explore the hallway and other cells. One cell has a dead hunter in it, and another a dead Thalmor soldier with a full set of Elven light armor. You can also pick up a set of iron armor and a health potion on the table in the main hallway. The optional objective "Search the dungeon for more information" refers to [Prisoner's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Prisoner%27s_Note "Skyrim:Prisoner's Note"), in a bucket in one of the cells.

### The Pit

As you enter the arena, you will have to fight a [Pit Fighter](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Pit_Fighter&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Pit Fighter (page does not exist)") and a [wolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf"). Once they have been killed, the two [sabre cats](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Sabre Cat") will be released from their cages and you will have to kill them. The pit champion, [Neski Skull-Sabre](/wiki/Skyrim:Neski_Skull-Sabre "Skyrim:Neski Skull-Sabre") will then enter the arena, kill her and you can then loot her body for the [Bittercup](/wiki/Skyrim:Bittercup_Items#Bittercup "Skyrim:Bittercup Items"), [Grand Champion's Sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Grand_Champion%27s_Sword "Skyrim:Grand Champion's Sword"), and the [Grand Champion's Helm](/wiki/Skyrim:Grand_Champion%27s_Helm "Skyrim:Grand Champion's Helm"). She also carries the note [On the Bittercup](/wiki/Skyrim:On_the_Bittercup "Skyrim:On the Bittercup").

There is a large number of dead bodies laying about the pit, these are; a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton_(dead) "Skyrim:Breton (dead)"), [hunter](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter"), [reaver](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver "Skyrim:Reaver"), [master vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Master_Vampire "Skyrim:Master Vampire"), [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_(dead) "Skyrim:Khajiit (dead)"), [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf_(dead) "Skyrim:Wood Elf (dead)"), and the [corpse](/wiki/Skyrim:Corpse "Skyrim:Corpse") of an Imperial soldier. There are also two skeletons that can be looted as well.

### Watchtower Barracks

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-quest-The_Pit_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Pit_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Pit_02.jpg)

The Pit Boss

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg)

Your belongings

You will then have to pull the lever next to the door in order to enter the [Watchtower Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Watchtower_Barracks "Skyrim:Watchtower Barracks"). Climb the stairs and you will have to enter the room on the left in order to kill the [Pit Boss](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Pit_Boss&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Pit Boss (page does not exist)") and the two [Pit Bandits](/w/index.php?title=Skyrim:Pit_Bandit&action=edit&redlink=1 "Skyrim:Pit Bandit (page does not exist)"). Loot the pit boss for the [pit key](/wiki/Skyrim:Pit_Key "Skyrim:Pit Key"), which can be used to retrieve your belongings from the chest in the previous room with an iron door. There is also a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Pit_Fighter%27s_Note "Skyrim:Pit Fighter's Note") next to the chest, with a flawless diamond, amethyst and an emerald on top of it.

You can then unlock the door to Skyrim, where you will be near the [Falkreath Watchtower](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Watchtower "Skyrim:Falkreath Watchtower"). The quest will then be complete.

## Notes

- In the Pit, most attacks can be avoided by jumping on top of the hay stacks, especially while battling Neski.
- It is possible to use the [Ritual Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ritual_Stone "Skyrim:The Ritual Stone") to reanimate the dead bodies. At lower levels, this will make the fight significantly easier.
- All belongings that can't be dropped will stay in the inventory, which could prove useful.
- The upper gallery containing archers seems to be inaccessible. But after regaining your equipment you can wreak vengeance on them from below, for example with a bow or by conjuring an ally at the gallery level, or use destruction spell [Unbounded Storms](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbounded_Storms "Skyrim:Unbounded Storms").
