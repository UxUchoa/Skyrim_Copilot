Bonestrewn Crest

**Bonestrewn Crest** is a mountaintop [dragon lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Lairs "Skyrim:Dragon Lairs") south of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). Its [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") teaches part of the [Frost Breath](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Breath "Skyrim:Frost Breath") [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").

## Walkthrough

The lair is riddled with mammoth bones, hence the name "Bonestrewn". It has a blueish mist, and a number of [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") plants grow here. It can be accessed via a slope on the south side. The word wall is found at the top of the hill.

Before the [dragons' return](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising"), the word wall will be guarded by three [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton").

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon").

Regardless of whether [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") has been completed, the boss chest is north of the word wall.

## Notes

- There are two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") here; one is on the right just before reaching the top of the slope, and the other is to the right just after reaching the top.
- One [orichalcum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore Vein") can be found 140 ft west-southwest, at the bottom of the mountain.
- The dragon that spawns here does not rest on the word wall or the ground before it; instead, it constantly circles the mountain until it detects you or a follower, whereupon it will initiate combat.
- Due to the location's proximity to [Steamcrag Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamcrag_Camp "Skyrim:Steamcrag Camp") and another [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") herding two [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") in the tundra below, the dragon might choose to fight them during its battle with you.
- Bonestrewn Crest also [appears](/wiki/Online:Bonestrewn_Crest "Online:Bonestrewn Crest") in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online").
