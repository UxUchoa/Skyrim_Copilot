Alesan

**Alesan** is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") who lives in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"). He makes money by bringing food to the miners of [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine") and [Iron-Breaker Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron-Breaker_Mine "Skyrim:Iron-Breaker Mine").

He sleeps on a bedroll at the [Windpeak Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Windpeak_Inn "Skyrim:Windpeak Inn") every night between 9pm and 8am. He wakes up to go straight to work, running through the town and delivering food from the inn workers, [Karita](/wiki/Skyrim:Karita_(bard) "Skyrim:Karita (bard)") and [Thoring](/wiki/Skyrim:Thoring "Skyrim:Thoring"), to the local miners: [Gjak](/wiki/Skyrim:Gjak "Skyrim:Gjak"), [Karl](/wiki/Skyrim:Karl "Skyrim:Karl"), [Lond](/wiki/Skyrim:Lond "Skyrim:Lond"), and [Borgny](/wiki/Skyrim:Borgny "Skyrim:Borgny"). At 6pm, he switches to feeding the chickens outside the inn, and can be seen carrying a handled basket.

He wears a set of [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes#000467bf "Skyrim:Child's Clothes") and [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8 "Skyrim:Shoes") and carries two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), along with an [assortment of random food and toys](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

If the time is between 8am and 6pm, he will be busy doing his job, and will tell you such as he runs past you:

:   *"Sorry! Gotta run!"*
:   *"One side, comin' through!"*
:   *"Move it, move it!"*
:   *"I'm late! Gotta run!"*

If you approach him when it is at least 6pm, he may complain by saying:

:   *"I'm so tired..."*
:   *"Whew! I'm worn out."*

:   **What's the hurry?**

    :   *"I'm running food and supplies to the miners. Don't pay much, but they let me sleep by the fire in the inn most nights."*

:   **Where are your parents, kid?**

    :   *"They're... dead. My pa was a sailor. Took me with him on his last trip. After he got sick, they... put us off here and left without us. He... died a few days later. I've tried to make do since then, but... it's been hard. Real hard."*

You will then have a dialogue option to [adopt](/wiki/Skyrim:Child#Adoption "Skyrim:Child") him. You can only go through with the adoption if you have a [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Houses "Skyrim:Houses") with a child's bed and container.

After you end conversation with him:

:   *"Nice talkin' with you."*

## Notes

- Alesan is the only [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") in Dawnstar.

## Bugs

- If you adopt him, Alesan may sometimes not be able to receive gifts.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Hearthfire Patch"), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
- If you adopt him, Alesan can get stuck in a loop, entering and exiting the cellar repeatedly, similar to [Lydia](/wiki/Skyrim:Lydia "Skyrim:Lydia") sitting and rising from the chair in [Breezehome](/wiki/Skyrim:Breezehome "Skyrim:Breezehome"). **?**
  - Talking to Alesan and having him do something may fix this.
- Alesan's inventory can slowly become clogged up with baskets he never uses for anything due to a bad scripting setup. This means if you adopt him it could be impossible to give him gifts.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.5, addresses this issue. The script causing this has been removed and the baskets will be removed from his inventory.
