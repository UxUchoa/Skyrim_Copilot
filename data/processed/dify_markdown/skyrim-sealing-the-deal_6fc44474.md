## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Talen-Jei](/wiki/Skyrim:Talen-Jei "Skyrim:Talen-Jei") about his plans to marry.
2. Find three [flawless amethysts](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Amethyst "Skyrim:Flawless Amethyst") he wishes to place on his engagement ring.
3. Return to Talen-Jei.

### A Proper Setting

Upon speaking with [Talen-Jei](/wiki/Skyrim:Talen-Jei "Skyrim:Talen-Jei") in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), he will reveal his affection for [Keerava](/wiki/Skyrim:Keerava "Skyrim:Keerava"), the innkeeper at [The Bee and Barb](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bee_and_Barb "Skyrim:The Bee and Barb"). He wishes to marry her, but his "meager earnings" haven't allowed him to obtain the gems needed to complete the setting for a proper engagement ring. If you offer to help, he will tell you he needs three [flawless amethysts](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Amethyst "Skyrim:Flawless Amethyst"). There are nine guaranteed gems found in eight different locations. Starting at level 12, you may also buy them from merchants at a base cost of 180 gold, find them as random loot, or get them randomly while mining. When you return to Talen-Jei with the gems, he will thank you, and reward you with a random leveled potion.

## Notes

- After this quest and [Taking Care of Business](/wiki/Skyrim:Taking_Care_of_Business "Skyrim:Taking Care of Business"), whether you threatened Keerava or not, Talen-Jei's dialogue may become inconsistent, he will call you his friend in greeting, but tell you to 'just get out of here' (The Bee and Barb) when saying goodbye. This is despite his deposition with you being 1 (friend).

## Bugs

- You may be able to do this quest even if Keerava is dead.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.5, addresses this issue. It will now be unavailable if she is dead, and will now fail if she dies after it has begun.
