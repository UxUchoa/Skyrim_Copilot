Captain Hargar

**Captain Hargar** is the [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") leader of the [Blackblood Marauders](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackblood_Marauder "Skyrim:Blackblood Marauder") residing in the caved-in [Broken Oar Grotto](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Oar_Grotto "Skyrim:Broken Oar Grotto"). He wears [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor") with matching [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots"). He wields a leveled [two-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:Two-Handed Weapons") that can be up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") in quality, and carries a small amount of gold.

He is working together with [Jaree-Ra](/wiki/Skyrim:Jaree-Ra "Skyrim:Jaree-Ra") and his sister [Deeja](/wiki/Skyrim:Deeja "Skyrim:Deeja") on some big raid [scheme](/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out! "Skyrim:Lights Out!"). When you track down the former to the grotto during the related quest, Hargar will try and protect his ally. Apparently, Hargar was locked up inside of the [Castle Dour Dungeon](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour_Dungeon "Skyrim:Castle Dour Dungeon") up until recently, when he escaped. Solitude's executioner, [Ahtar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ahtar "Skyrim:Ahtar") will send you to kill him during his [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Bandit_Leader "Skyrim:Kill the Bandit Leader").

## Notes

- Hargar's [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Hargar%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Hargar's Journal") can be found on the table at the uppermost level of the wooden tower in Broken Oar Grotto, and will inform you of some hidden treasure nearby. The key to the master-locked chest can be pickpocketed from him or looted off his corpse after he's killed.
- It is advisable to complete Ahtar's quest before any other quests in Broken Oar Grotto, as killing Captain Hargar will render that quest inaccessible, thus making it difficult to ever use Ahtar as a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers").
