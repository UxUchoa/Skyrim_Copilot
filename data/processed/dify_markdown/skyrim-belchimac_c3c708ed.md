Belchimac

**Belchimac** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)") who lives and works in [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten").

He sleeps in [Karthwasten Miner's Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Karthwasten Miner's Barracks") from midnight to 8am, and works in [Sanuarach Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Sanuarach_Mine "Skyrim:Sanuarach Mine") between 8am and 8pm. He spends the rest of the evening relaxing in the barracks.

He wears a set of [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#00080699 "Skyrim:Clothing"), and is equipped with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"). He carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Karthwasten_Miners%27_Barracks "Skyrim:Key to Karthwasten Miners' Barracks") to the miner's barracks and a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

### The Heart of Dibella

When asked if he knows where a [young girl](/wiki/Skyrim:Fjotra "Skyrim:Fjotra") who lives here is:

:   **I'm looking for a young girl who lives around here.**
:   *"Yeah, we're all looking for something."*

## Dialogue

Before you reopen the mines, he'll be depressed, saying:

:   *"The good mine is closed and the [other mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Fenn%27s_Gulch_Mine "Skyrim:Fenn's Gulch Mine") has no ore. Gods have cursed me."*

He'll keep his attitude when they're open:

:   *"Sauranach*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *mine is open again. How am I supposed to handle all this work?"*

## Conversations

He can often be seen talking to other miners:

:   **[Enmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon "Skyrim:Enmon")**: *"When are those [Silver-Blood men](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver-Blood_Mercenary "Skyrim:Silver-Blood Mercenary") going to leave?"*
:   **Belchimac**: *"Never. They've shut us down forever, Enmon. The Divines have abandoned us."*

:   **Belchimac**: *"We're doomed. The mine is going to stay closed, and we'll up*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *be carted of*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *to [Cidhna Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Cidhna_Mine "Skyrim:Cidhna Mine") as slaves."*
:   **Enmon**: *"Only criminals get sent to Cidhna Mine. They're not going to send us there."*
:   **Belchimac**: *"Oh, they'll find some way to send us there. It'll be just my luck."*

:   **Belchimac**: *"Damn [Nords](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord"). Always getting in the way of us honest folk."*
:   **[Ragnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragnar "Skyrim:Ragnar")**: *"What did you say? You lazy coward, I'll cut your throat."*
:   **Belchimac**: *"Nothing, Ragnar. Please... don't hurt me."*
:   **Ragnar**: *"I... I'm sorry, Belchimac. I didn't mean that."*

:   **Ragnar**: *"You're always talking about the [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"). You aren't thinking of joining them, are you?"*
:   **Belchimac**: *"Maybe I will. What do you think about that?"*
:   **Ragnar**: *"I'm warning you. Those people are killers. You'd never last a day with them."*

:   **Belchimac**: *"I don't like bunking with [Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc"). It's not... clean."*
:   **[Lash](/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh "Skyrim:Lash gra-Dushnikh")**: *"If you want me to leave, you could challenge me to a fight. I promise to stop once you've lost an arm."*
:   **Belchimac**: *"What I meant to say was... I love having an Orc here in the barracks. They're so... pleasant."*

:   **Lash**: *"Why do your people claim the [Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") is theirs? The Orcs have been in these lands just as long."*
:   **Belchimac**: *"You wouldn't understand, Lash. Your people aren't smart enough for politics."*
:   **Lash**: *"Is that so? I'll remember that next time we're in the mine tunnels... alone."*
:   **Belchimac**: *"Did... did I say you weren't smart? I meant to say that I'm not smart, Lash. I'm just not smart enough to answer your questions."*
