Serpent's Bluff Redoubt

**Serpent's Bluff Redoubt** is a small [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Camps "Skyrim:Camps") and ruin southwest of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead"). It contains only one zone, *[Serpent's Bluff Ruins](#Serpent.27s_Bluff_Ruins)*.

The campsite is solely inhabited by Forsworn, while their [hagraven](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven "Skyrim:Hagraven") matriarch and several more Forsworn allies reside within the ruins.

#### Radiant Quests

- **[Bounty: Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Forsworn "Skyrim:Bounty: Forsworn")**: Kill a [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") leader for a bounty reward.
- **[The Ghost of Old Hroldan](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ghost_of_Old_Hroldan "Skyrim:The Ghost of Old Hroldan")**: Put an [ancient ghost](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost_of_Old_Hroldan "Skyrim:Ghost of Old Hroldan") to rest.
- **[Kill the Forsworn Leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Forsworn_Leader_(Jarl) "Skyrim:Kill the Forsworn Leader (Jarl)")**: Do a favor for [Jarl Igmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Igmund "Skyrim:Igmund") by killing a [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") leader.
- **[Lisbet's Missing Shipment](/wiki/Skyrim:Lisbet%27s_Missing_Shipment "Skyrim:Lisbet's Missing Shipment")**: Retrieve a missing Dibella Statue for a struggling store.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

### Exterior

The redoubt is atop a steep incline leading southwest from Rorikstead. The campsite is home to numerous Forsworn, including a Forsworn Briarheart. The lower part of the camp has three tents containing bedrolls around a campfire in the middle. The first tent also contains an end table with a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") on top. The third contains a small unit with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), one of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") on the shelves. To the right are two more tents, with a [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") and [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") between them. One tent contains an apprentice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") with a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") propped up against it along with a bedroll and table. The other tent has a table with two random [two-handed weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:Two-Handed Weapons") and a random [heavy helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Helmets_2 "Skyrim:Armor"). To the left is a bookcase holding two common books including a copy of *[The Legend of Red Eagle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle "Skyrim:The Legend of Red Eagle")* and a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). Beside this is a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench").

Farther uphill are two more tents separated by a pair of long wooden tables and benches. On the tables are an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") stabbed into a tactical map of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") at [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). The tent to the left is where the Briarheart sleeps, and contains a boss-level chest at the foot of a double bed with a set of shelves beside it holding a [leg of goat roast](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat_Roast "Skyrim:Leg of Goat Roast"), a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a [deer hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide "Skyrim:Deer Hide"), and a random potion of healing. On either side of the entrance are a dresser and a small unit. The dresser has an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet") and random enchanted [battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe "Skyrim:Battleaxe") on top, while the unit holds a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese") and a medium coin purse. The second tent contains only a bedroll and a wooden bench, with a flight of stairs to the right that leads to the entrance to *Serpent's Bluff Ruins*. West of the camp, near a ruined tower, is a chest.

### Serpent's Bluff Ruins

There are six enemies (including a hagraven boss) and many traps inside this small area. The entrance leads to a well-lit passage that turns to the northeast and descends a flight of stairs. At the bottom is a corridor with a wooden table on the left with a copy of *[The "Madmen" of the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_%22Madmen%22_of_the_Reach "Skyrim:The \"Madmen\" of the Reach")*, a random [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow"), a set of [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows "Skyrim:Arrows"), and a leveled [potion of resist shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Shock "Skyrim:Potion of Resist Shock"). There is a pressure plate on the floor that is easily spotted that triggers a [poison dart trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Dart_Trap "Skyrim:Dart Trap"). The corridor continues to the southeast with barred openings on the left that enable you to see into the main chamber ahead. There is a [tripwire](/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire "Skyrim:Tripwire") partway along this passage that triggers a [spear trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Spear_Trap "Skyrim:Spear Trap") from the side wall. To the right of the door at the end of the passage is a Forsworn sitting in a chair with a common book at their feet. On your right, just after the tripwire, is a dresser partially buried in rubble containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") with a random [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") on top. Past this is a second pressure plate that triggers a [spike wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Spike_Wall "Skyrim:Spike Wall"). To the right of the door is a large fireplace with a random [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace") on the ground in front of it. The door is unlocked and leads into another narrow passage with stairs leading down. At the bottom there is yet another pressure plate; this one triggers a [battering ram](/wiki/Skyrim:Battering_Ram "Skyrim:Battering Ram") ahead. At the end of the passage, you will find a dresser that is covered in blood with an iron dagger on top. To the right is a caved-in section with a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield") among the rubble. To the left is a doorway into the main chamber seen from above.

The main chamber contains two Forsworn and a hagraven. Both the hagraven and one of the Forsworn are on a raised section to the north, while the other Forsworn is wandering the lower level. In the middle of the lower level is a large stone table with a [weight-sensitive plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Weight_Sensitive_Plate "Skyrim:Weight Sensitive Plate") and items of clutter. You will need to activate the plate by dropping an item onto it, as the door only remains open as long as the plate is activated; one of the otherwise useless items on the table can be used for this. In the eastern corner is an adept-locked door to a room under the upper level. The room contains a cupboard with a large coin purse and three common books on the shelves, a [steel warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Warhammer "Skyrim:Steel Warhammer") on the floor, and a set of shelves that are partially buried in rubble holding a set of random [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor"), a small coin purse, and two common books. Finally, at the rear of the room is an apprentice-locked chest containing minor loot.

Climbing the stairs, at the top is a barrel containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and a plinth with a *[Spell Tome: Frostbite](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Frostbite "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Frostbite")*. Once you are past the wooden gate, there is a T-junction with a table opposite with a random [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") or [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison") on top. To the right is an open door with a barrel of ingredients to the right and inside the room there is a random [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword") on the floor by the southeast wall. To the left is a pile of straw with five [hagraven feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Feathers "Skyrim:Hagraven Feathers") among it. In the eastern corner, in front of the head of an elk with mammoth tusks wrapped around it, is an unlocked boss leveled chest. To the left is a cupboard with three common books, a bowl of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"), two pieces of [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), a [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") flower, two random [potions of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), and an empty random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") on the shelves. Against the northwest wall is an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanters "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanters") with a copy of the [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Twin Secrets](/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Secrets "Skyrim:Twin Secrets")* on top. Next to this is a wooden table with a [charred skeever hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide"), a wooden bowl containing two [slaughterfish eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg"), and two empty random soul gems on top. Back in the passage there is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding two bunches of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear") and a [garlic braid](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid"). Taking the left fork at the T-junction there is a pair of random [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor") on the floor near the top of a flight of stairs leading down. At the bottom is another wooden gate with a button on the right that lowers it. Beyond is a natural tunnel containing four [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap"), three [blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort"), and two [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") fungi; this tunnel leads to the exit which is southwest of the entrance.

## Notes

- To open the gate, you need to activate the plate. While looking at an item, press and hold down the action button; instead of looting the item, this will allow you to easily drag it on top of the pressure plate. You can also walk on the plate and then use the [Slow Time](/wiki/Skyrim:Slow_Time "Skyrim:Slow Time") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") to pass through the open gate.
- An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") can be found north-northwest of camp, right next to the rock underneath the camp fortifications.
- This is the only Forsworn camp not located in [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach").
- Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") assigns Serpent's Bluff Redoubt to the Reach instead.
