The Three Guardian Stones

**The Guardian Stones** is a location which marks three [Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones") located directly outside of [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen") and [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood").

They are likely the first standing stones you will encounter, since they are on the path taken while being escorted by [Ralof](/wiki/Skyrim:Ralof "Skyrim:Ralof") or [Hadvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hadvar "Skyrim:Hadvar") to Riverwood. The stones are The Mage Stone, The Thief Stone, and The Warrior Stone. If selected, they allow you to specialize in magic, stealth or combat [skills](/wiki/Skyrim:Skills "Skyrim:Skills") respectively, by gaining 20% more experience in those skills. [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") is considered a stealth skill in this context.

|  |
| --- |
| - The Mage Stone: *Those under the sign of The Mage will learn all [magic](/wiki/Skyrim:Magic_Overview "Skyrim:Magic Overview") skills 20% faster.* - The Thief Stone: *Those under the sign of The Thief will learn all [stealth](/wiki/Skyrim:Stealth "Skyrim:Stealth") skills 20% faster.* - The Warrior Stone: *Those under the sign of The Warrior will learn all [combat](/wiki/Skyrim:Combat "Skyrim:Combat") skills 20% faster.* |

## Notes

- If you activate one of the Stones while [Ralof](/wiki/Skyrim:Ralof "Skyrim:Ralof") or [Hadvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hadvar "Skyrim:Hadvar") are present during [Before the Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Before_the_Storm "Skyrim:Before the Storm"), they will comment on the Stone you activated.
- Using the [Aetherial Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherial_Crown "Skyrim:Aetherial Crown")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") makes it possible to increase the benefits of The Guardian Stones by using it with [The Lover Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lover_Stone "Skyrim:The Lover Stone"). Do this along with [Rested](/wiki/Skyrim:Rested "Skyrim:Rested") bonuses to obtain the maximum XP boost.
  - Note that you cannot receive a Rested bonus from sleeping while The Lover Stone is active. Therefore, to obtain the maximum XP boost, you must store The Lover Stone in the Crown, unequip it before sleeping, and then re-equip it to apply The Lover Stone while maintaining the Rested bonus.
- Experience gained from [training](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers") will benefit from The Guardian Stones and any other XP bonuses. Since one session of training gives exactly one level's worth of XP to a skill, the Guardian Stones will effectively increase this to 1.25 levels of XP for any associated skill. In other words, every 4 sessions of training will grant a free level of XP. Note that the training session itself will not award the bonus level--you must manually level the skill up once the XP bar is nearly full. Any "overflow" XP will be lost.
- There is an [unmarked](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places "Skyrim:Unmarked Places") [shrine of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Talos "Skyrim:Shrines") to the southwest of the Guardian Stones location. At the base of the shrine is a dead [Thalmor Justiciar](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Justiciar "Skyrim:Thalmor Justiciar") carrying a random piece of enchanted armor.
- Further west is an unmarked necromancer's site close to road that runs along south shore of [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta"), connected to the road by short track going south. The site is occupied by a leveled [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer"). Between eight pillars is an altar with lootable skeleton, [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Hearth Fire, v9](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Hearth_Fire,_v9 "Skyrim:2920, Hearth Fire, v9")*, one-handed sword, two gold coins and embalming tool. Next to the altar is a [burial urn](/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn "Skyrim:Burial Urn"), some skeletal remains and very close is a patch of [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade").
- Three leveled [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") have established a camp directly south of the Guardian Stones, just south of the road that runs along the south shore of [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=3195&y=-66333&zoom=17&legacy=true). Their belongings include a satchel containing random alchemical ingredients, a black mage robe, a [treasure map](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_I "Skyrim:Treasure Map I") and a copy of the [one-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Night Falls on Sentinel](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Falls_on_Sentinel "Skyrim:Night Falls on Sentinel")*.
- A Thief Stone also appears in the [playroom](/wiki/Skyrim:Playroom "Skyrim:Playroom") for [Skyrim VR](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim_VR "Skyrim:Skyrim VR").

## Bugs

- [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") benefits from the Thief Stone's bonus, though it is otherwise considered a combat skill (in internal game data, for the [Oghma Infinium](/wiki/Skyrim:Oghma_Infinium "Skyrim:Oghma Infinium"), and for the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College") sidequests.)
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, addresses this issue. It now benefits from the Warrior Stone's bonus.
- Returning to the Guardian Stones after waiting for the location to respawn (by waiting 10 in game days) will cause the number of discovered Standing Stones to increase by 3, allowing you to earn the "Standing Stones" achievement faster. **?**
