**Evette San's House** is the home of [Evette](/wiki/Skyrim:Evette_San "Skyrim:Evette San") and [Octieve San](/wiki/Skyrim:Octieve_San "Skyrim:Octieve San") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). ## Evette San's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-interior-Evette_San%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Evette_San%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Evette_San%27s_House.jpg)

The interior of Evette San's house

Upon entering there is a cupboard on your right holding an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet") and a couple of sacks. On your left against the northeast wall is a small set of shelves holding a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk"), a [cave bear pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Cave_Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Cave Bear Pelt"), a [red mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), and a basket holding two bunches of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). To the right is a barrel and basket containing [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"). On top of the barrel are six [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot"). Against an interior half-height wall is a small wooden table and two chairs. On the table are two portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), two [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), and a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulb. Over the table are racks holding four [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and three [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)").

Past the interior wall, against the northwest wall, is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") in a cast iron pot on the hearth. Opposite against the southeast wall is a long table with various cheese, several loaves of bread, a couple of [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), a portion of grilled leeks, a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a seared slaughterfish steak and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). Under the table is an open crate containing a sample of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") and an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing"). To the right is a second open crate containing vegetables.

To the southwest is the rest of the house, including stairs up to the top floor. On the left is a bedroom with a single bed in the southern corner with an end table to the right and a couple of sacks at the end of the bed. Against the southeast wall is a wooden bench and against the northwest wall a small set of shelves holding a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and a mead barrel.

Back out in the corridor, to the left of the staircase to the rear of the house is a storage area with two cupboards. One holds two [sweet rolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), a bottle of alto wine, an eidar cheese wedge, a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), four [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), several loaves of bread, and a haunch of [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"). The other holds a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") and an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"). Between them are several barrels of foodstuffs. There are also two sets of shelves under the stairs which hold various cheese wheels, cabbages, potatoes, a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"), loaves of bread, two sheaves of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), a bunch of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton") and a copy of *[Songs of the Return, Vol 19](/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_the_Return,_Vol_19 "Skyrim:Songs of the Return, Vol 19")*. Hanging from a rack beside a cupboard are three garlic braids.

At the top of the stairs is a wooden bench. In the southern corner is a table holding a loaf of bread, with a cupboard to the left of this holding another knapsack and iron mace. Continuing along the southeast wall is another wooden bench and a stack of barrels all containing foodstuffs. Against the northwest wall is a single bed with an end table holding a bunch of tundra cotton to the left of it. Against a railing to the northeast are two unlocked [chests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest").

## Notes

- There is a "Loot" [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (Thieves Guild)") to the right of the entrance door.
- The [key to the house](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Evette_San%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Evette San's House") is carried by [Bendt](/wiki/Skyrim:Bendt "Skyrim:Bendt"), Evette, and Octieve. Bendt, however, does not leave the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College") at any time, has no dialogue about the Sans, or have any other possible reason for carrying the key.
