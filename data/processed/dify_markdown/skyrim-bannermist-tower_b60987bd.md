Bannermist Tower

**Bannermist Tower** is a small ruined [Nordic tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Towers "Skyrim:Nordic Towers") northwest of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") containing [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit").

## Related Quests

- **[Kill the Bandit Leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Bandit_Leader_(Jarl) "Skyrim:Kill the Bandit Leader (Jarl)")**: Slay a [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") leader for [Siddgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Siddgeir "Skyrim:Siddgeir"), the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Walkthrough

One bandit patrols outside the tower. Off the path leading up to the tower, to the south, is a birds' nest containing a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), with a second nest with another egg at the western base of the tower. A vacant [beehive](/wiki/Skyrim:Beehive_Husk "Skyrim:Beehive Husk") hangs underneath the entrance ramp, while a [blue mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") and a [thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle") are by the foot of the tower to the south. The entrance ramp is at the rear of the tower, lodged between the tower and the hill.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-interior-Bannermist_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bannermist_Tower.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bannermist_Tower.jpg)

Bannermist Tower

Just before the entrance ramp, at the base of the tower, is an apprentice-locked gate to the storage area. Inside are an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") and a table beside it holding a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), two [garnets](/wiki/Skyrim:Garnet "Skyrim:Garnet"), an [amethyst](/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst "Skyrim:Amethyst"), and four loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). The stairs down end in a cave-in where there are two patches of [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool") fungus.

The first level at the top of the entrance ramp holds only sacks and barrels of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") and two bedrolls, one of which is often occupied by a sleeping bandit. On the second level of the tower, across a plank walkway, are a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), various crates, sacks, and barrels, and a small wooden table holding a copy of the [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Advances in Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Advances_in_Lockpicking "Skyrim:Advances in Lockpicking")* (see [bugs](#Bugs)). Also, there is a weapon rack holding an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") and [warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer "Skyrim:Iron Warhammer") (which may have fallen to the floor). An unlocked chest is tucked away beneath the stairs behind more barrels and sacks leading up to the top level. The top level is simply a lookout station, and usually contains a bandit, with a small table where a copy of a random [common book](/wiki/Skyrim:Books "Skyrim:Books") has been left for some light reading while enjoying the scenery, and three loose [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow") on the floor.

## Bugs

- The skill book may have fallen through the table and floor and be found near the entrance by the top of the ramp. **?**
  - If you look at the floor directly under where the book appear to be on the table, the "read" prompt should appear.
- The chests here tend to respawn every 5 hours, instead of every 10 or 30 days. For example, if you loot the chests, fast travel to the [city of Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"), and then fast travel back to Bannermist Tower, only 5 or 6 in-game hours will have passed, but you will find that the chests will have already refilled themselves by then.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.1, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=22914)) Their encounter zone setting was incorrectly set to BannermistZone, which caused them to reset so quickly. Their encounter zone has been corrected, and they now properly reset with the rest of the dungeon.
