# Skyrim:Cities

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg)

Whiterun

This page lists all the **cities** in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"). Each city (with the exception of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock") on [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim")) is located in a different [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") and is home to that hold's ruler, or [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl"). The five largest cities (the major cities) are walled, allowing their maps (or *cells* as they are called in the [Creation Kit](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit "Skyrim Mod:Creation Kit")) to be treated separately from the main map. [Carriages](/wiki/Skyrim:Carriage "Skyrim:Carriage") are available for hire outside each major city to transport the player to any other major city.

Fast traveling to a major city places you just inside its main gate, but each major city also has a keep or palace where the jarl resides, which is a separate fast-travel destination and is listed below. Additionally, Riften has a third fast-travel destination once you have joined the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), namely the entrance to the guild's [headquarters](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(place) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (place)").
