Anise's Cabin

**Anise's Cabin** is a small [shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") west-southwest of [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"). It contains only one zone, the *[Cellar](#Cellar)*.

The sole occupant is an elderly woman named [Anise](/wiki/Skyrim:Anise "Skyrim:Anise").

### Exterior

The cabin is near your point of escape from [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen"). It's also close to [Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (place)") to the north and the [Guardian Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Guardian_Stones "Skyrim:The Guardian Stones") across the river to the south. The surrounding wilderness often contains friendly hunters or hostile bandits. The latter may engage Anise in combat, in which case she will use [flame spells](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") and may [summon a familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Familiar "Skyrim:Conjure Familiar").

A [red mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") and a [thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch") grow near the cabin. To the left of the entrance is a chair and a pile of firewood, with a small [vegetable](/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Fruit.2FVegetables "Skyrim:Food") garden to the right consisting of three [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), two [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and two [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") plants.

The cabin is in a remarkable state of disrepair. To the immediate right on entering is a tall [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawn "Skyrim:Respawn") cupboard holding an empty [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem"), a sample of [hagraven feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Feathers "Skyrim:Hagraven Feathers"), a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flower, a [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") and a sprig of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"). Opposite the entryway is a small wooden chair next to a few sacks and a barrel containing [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"). To the left is a long wooden table pressed into the southern corner. On the table is a [pheasant roast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Roast "Skyrim:Pheasant Roast"), a sheaf of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and a bowl of [vegetable soup](/wiki/Skyrim:Vegetable_Soup "Skyrim:Vegetable Soup"), with a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid hanging from the ceiling above and hanging from a rack to the right is a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). On the floor beside the table is a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine").

In the eastern corner and along the southeast wall is a single bed, that is marked as owned unless Anise is dead; then it is free to use. At the foot of the bed is small unit holding a copy of the [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Song of the Alchemists](/wiki/Skyrim:Song_of_the_Alchemists "Skyrim:Song of the Alchemists")*, with an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") beside it containing [uncommon ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"). Leaning against the unit is a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow"). Under the head of the bed is a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). To the left, against the west wall is a non-respawning dresser and a partially covered novice-locked trapdoor leading to the *Cellar*. There are a pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") next to the dresser, and there are [leather bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers") as well as a single red mountain flower on top of the dresser.

### Cellar

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-interior-Anise%27s_Cabin_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Anise%27s_Cabin_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Anise%27s_Cabin_02.jpg)

Cellar

Starting at the bottom of the ladder upon entering and moving around anticlockwise is a cupboard with a [weak poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Poison "Skyrim:Weak Poison") and a [potent paralysis poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Potent_Paralysis_Poison "Skyrim:Potent Paralysis Poison") on top, with a barrel containing uncommon ingredients and a sideboard next to that. On the sideboard are a bowl of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"), a sample of hagraven feathers, a deathbell, a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), and *[Anise's Letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Anise%27s_Letter "Skyrim:Anise's Letter")* that explains Anise is actually a [witch](/wiki/Skyrim:Witch "Skyrim:Witch") who is trying to start a coven. Against the south wall is an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter") and to the left an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"). To the left of the lab, against the east wall is a small set of shelves holding another apothecary's satchel (containing rare ingredients), a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum"), and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). Finally in the eastern corner are a few crates and food sacks.

When you exit the cellar, Anise will become hostile and attack, shouting, *"Fool! None may know my secret!"* This will happen regardless of whether or not you entered the cellar undetected.

## Notes

- When Anise dies, the bed becomes unowned. However, all other items remain owned, and taking them counts as stealing. Also, entering the cellar is always trespassing, which has little effect except that when in the cellar, most followers will only say *"You're not supposed to be in here."*
- As the cellar does not respawn, all containers are [safe](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Safe_Locations "Skyrim:Containers") for storage. The cupboard and dresser on the ground floor of the cabin are also standard [safe containers](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Safe_Containers "Skyrim:Containers"), but the barrel and apothecary's satchel are not. This makes Anise's cabin a good starter home, since it is a fast travel location with an alchemy lab, an arcane enchanter, a bed, and safe storage.

## Bugs

- Several items in the cabin are owned by the `HagravenFaction`. Stealing these might result in [Moira](/wiki/Skyrim:Moira "Skyrim:Moira") or other random hagravens sending [hired thugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Steal,_Thugs_hunt_player "Skyrim:Steal, Thugs hunt player") after you, even if you have already killed both Anise and the respective hagraven.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.1, fixes this bug.
- The barrel respawns despite being in a "No Reset Zone".
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.2, fixes this bug.
