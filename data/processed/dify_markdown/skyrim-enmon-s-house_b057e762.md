Enmon's House

**Enmon's House** is a two-story residence in the southwestern part of [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten"), located between [Karthwasten Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten_Hall "Skyrim:Karthwasten Hall") and [Miner's Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Karthwasten Miner's Barracks").

It is the home of [Enmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon "Skyrim:Enmon") and his wife [Mena](/wiki/Skyrim:Mena "Skyrim:Mena"). It consists of one zone, *[Enmon's House](#Enmon.27s_House)*.

## Enmon's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House.jpg)

Ground floor

The ground floor is used as the kitchen, dining, and storage area of the home. A non-respawning dresser immediately to the right of the door holds three [potions of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Enhanced Stamina") and two [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"). A respawning food barrel sits in the northwest corner, with a well stocked set of shelves to its left which holds a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a [potion of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), and two bottles each of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on the top shelf, while the middle shelf holds three [eidar cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel") and three [goat cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), and the bottom shelf holds a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and six [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). To the left of these shelves is a non-respawning cupboard holding three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and three bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on the top shelf and one bottle each of wine and alto wine below. Sitting before the cupboard and shelves is a dining table set with three loaves of bread, two [goat cheese wedges](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), three bottles of alto wine, and two servings of [salmon meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat "Skyrim:Salmon Meat").

On the southern wall, some hanging foodstuffs can be found on the right side, including two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, some dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), and two portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"). The fireplace is situated to the left, with two bottles of Nord mead on the mantel and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") at its base. Hanging to the left above the fireplace are four freshly caught [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). A small round table near the fireplace holds a goat cheese wedge, a bottle of Nord mead, a loaf of bread, and a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"). Towards the southeast corner, beneath the stairs, a set of shelves holds three [silver ores](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore "Skyrim:Silver Ore") on the top shelf, an additional pickaxe and five [silver ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot") on the middle shelf, and a copy of the book *[Nords Arise!](/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_Arise! "Skyrim:Nords Arise!")* along with three pairs of miner [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000e3445 "Skyrim:Boots") on the bottom shelf. A respawning food barrel sits on each side of the base of the stairs.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House_02.jpg)

Upstairs

Going upstairs will lead to the bedroom area of the home. Along the south wall, a set of shelves can be found holding an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") and various kitchenware. Directly ahead, on the western wall, is a double bed (belonging to the [KarthwastenEnmonsHouseFaction](/wiki/Skyrim:KarthwastenEnmonsHouseFaction "Skyrim:KarthwastenEnmonsHouseFaction")) with a small non-respawning chest resting at its foot. A small table to the right holds five gold coins, and a non-respawning dresser to the left holds three bottles of Nord mead and a [steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Steel War Axe"). A door leads to the balcony of the house on the north wall, and a single square table with a chair is situated just to the right of it.

## Notes

- The front door and balcony door both have a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Enmon%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Enmon's House") to which is carried by both Enmon and Mena.
- The balcony, which is accessible through the upstairs area of the home, hosts a bench which holds two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine").
- During [The Heart of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Heart_of_Dibella "Skyrim:The Heart of Dibella"), the doors to Enmon's House will lock if night falls while inside it. Unlocking the door at this point is considered a crime. Waiting until morning causes the door to unlock.
