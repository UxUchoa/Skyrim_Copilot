Bjormund Wind-Strider's corpse

Bjormund Wind-Strider is a dead [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") and former [Crimson Dirk](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Dirks "Skyrim:Crimson Dirks") who is encountered during the quest [Bones for a Crow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bones_for_a_Crow "Skyrim:Bones for a Crow"). His body is found at the very top of the tower at [Arcwind Point](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcwind_Point "Skyrim:Arcwind Point"), at the bottom of the stairs which lead to the book [Withershins](/wiki/Skyrim:Withershins "Skyrim:Withershins"), after the player has battled past the enemies that occupy the tower. When he chased the same bounty as you, [Crowstooth](/wiki/Skyrim:Crowstooth "Skyrim:Crowstooth") killed him. As part of the reward for the quest (any spoils of Crowstooth's), you may take his unique armor.

Bjormund wears the [Dragonbone Mail](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonbone_Mail "Skyrim:Dragonbone Mail"), [Dragonplate Insulated Boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonplate_Insulated_Boots "Skyrim:Dragonplate Insulated Boots"), [Dragonplate Insulated Gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonplate_Insulated_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Dragonplate Insulated Gauntlets") and a [Dragonplate Insulated Helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonplate_Insulated_Helmet "Skyrim:Dragonplate Insulated Helmet"), and carries [Nord Mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), an [Amulet of Akatosh](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Akatosh "Skyrim:Amulet of Akatosh"), and a random selection of loot.
