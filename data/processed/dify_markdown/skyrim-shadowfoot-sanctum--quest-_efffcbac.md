## Quick Walkthrough

1. Purchase [Shadowfoot Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place) "Skyrim:Shadowfoot Sanctum (place)") from [Vekel the Man](/wiki/Skyrim:Vekel_the_Man "Skyrim:Vekel the Man") for 7,500 gold.
2. Visit your new property.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This short quest will start when you talk to [Vekel the Man](/wiki/Skyrim:Vekel_the_Man "Skyrim:Vekel the Man") in [The Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon"), an underground tavern in [the Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway") beneath [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), and purchase [Shadowfoot Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place) "Skyrim:Shadowfoot Sanctum (place)") for 7,500 gold.

You will be given a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Shadowfoot_Sanctum "Skyrim:Key to Shadowfoot Sanctum") to access the sanctum upon purchasing the property, located a short distance from the Flagon's entrance. It is a fully-furnished player home with a hidden entrance and lots of storage, ideal for members of the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"). It contains many crafting stations and [shrines](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines "Skyrim:Shrines") as well as space for your [spouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Spouse "Skyrim:Spouse") and [adopted children](/wiki/Skyrim:Adoption "Skyrim:Adoption"). Enter Shadowfoot Sanctum to complete the quest.
