Ernanthil

**Ernanthil** is a [High Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Elf "Skyrim:High Elf") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") who offers the player a [rogue's job](/wiki/Skyrim:Rogue%27s_Job_Offer "Skyrim:Rogue's Job Offer"), when [the cup of Fortune](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortunate_Son#The_Cup_of_Fortune "Skyrim:Fortunate Son") is chosen at the [Roadside Ruins.](/wiki/Skyrim:Roadside_Ruins "Skyrim:Roadside Ruins") All interactions with him are by dead-drop letters until he finally appears in Eslaf's room in [The Warrens](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warrens "Skyrim:The Warrens") of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") during your battle with his brother [Cuinanthil](/wiki/Skyrim:Cuinanthil "Skyrim:Cuinanthil"), the course of which he watches.

He wears typical thieves guild garb that cannot be looted. He wields a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") and carries [a journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Ernanthil%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Ernanthil's Journal"), leveled [rich items](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich_Pocket_Contents "Skyrim:Leveled Lists") and a copy of the skill book *[Thief](/wiki/Skyrim:Thief_(book) "Skyrim:Thief (book)")*.
