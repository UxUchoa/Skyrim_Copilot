## Radiant Options

This is a radiant quest which you can receive from any of the following people. The books have a fixed location the quest marker points to, but returning any copy will do. You must find the other items on your own. **Note**: Only one radiant quest of this type can be assigned at any given time. You must clear the radiant quest from your miscellaneous quests in order to be eligible to receive another of the same type.

## Quick Walkthrough

All of the items can be found in multiple places in the game. For more thorough lists of locations, see the item's page.

### Amulet of Arkay

There is a guaranteed [Amulet of Arkay](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Arkay "Skyrim:Amulet of Arkay") at [Wayward Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Wayward_Pass "Skyrim:Wayward Pass") at the [Shrine of Arkay](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Arkay "Skyrim:Shrine of Arkay"). You can also obtain one easily by starting [The Taste of Death](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Taste_of_Death "Skyrim:The Taste of Death") in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth").

**Note**: Be careful when bringing the amulet back to [Torbjorn Shatter-Shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield "Skyrim:Torbjorn Shatter-Shield"). The quest marker may indicate that you should enter his house, the door of which may be unlocked, but if you enter you may be treated as a trespasser with no option to tell Torbjorn or his wife what your errand is. Meet him on the street during the day instead.

### Black-Briar Mead

[Black-Briar Mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") can be found in large quantities or bought at the [Black-Briar Meadery](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Meadery "Skyrim:Black-Briar Meadery"), and are guaranteed in other locations as well, most closely in [Lod's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Lod%27s_House "Skyrim:Lod's House") (on the table with the [private letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Private_Letter "Skyrim:Private Letter") required by [Dengeir of Stuhn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dengeir_of_Stuhn "Skyrim:Dengeir of Stuhn")'s quest [Some Light Theft](/wiki/Skyrim:Some_Light_Theft "Skyrim:Some Light Theft")). They may also be bought from most innkeeper merchants. There is also one at the end of the cave in [Unbound](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound "Skyrim:Unbound"), right where you are told to sneak past the bear.

### Mammoth Tusk

If you are willing to steal, you can find a [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk") in several places in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"):

- [Dragonsreach Jarl's Quarters](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach") — go upstairs and into the main room then head into the room to your right; the tusk is atop the bookcases to the right of the door
- [Jorrvaskr](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorrvaskr "Skyrim:Jorrvaskr") — on the table in the left room
- [The Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare") — located on top of a bookshelf right behind the counter; jump on the rafters from the room for rent and pluck it off the shelf

If you'd prefer not to steal one and are unable or unwilling to kill a [mammoth](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth"), [Halted Stream Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Halted_Stream_Camp "Skyrim:Halted Stream Camp") just north of Whiterun contains several mammoth tusks. [Belethor](/wiki/Skyrim:Belethor "Skyrim:Belethor"), the general goods merchant, has a chance of stocking one if you're willing to pay for it. With [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire"), a free one can be found in the [Main Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Main_Hall#Main_Hall "Skyrim:Main Hall") of your home, if you build one. With [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn"), one can be found in [Severin Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Severin_Manor "Skyrim:Severin Manor"), and is free to take after you take ownership.

### The Mirror

After accepting this quest, a copy of *[The Mirror](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mirror "Skyrim:The Mirror")* can be found in [Windhelm's Palace of Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings"). This is where your quest marker will point you, but it will count as stealing unless you are [Thane of Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_Eastmarch "Skyrim:Thane of Eastmarch"). [Captain Aldis](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Aldis "Skyrim:Captain Aldis") *will* accept a stolen copy.

Copies may also be found on the bookshelf in the sleeping area of [Broken Tower Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt "Skyrim:Broken Tower Redoubt") in [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"), or in
[Forsaken Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsaken_Cave "Skyrim:Forsaken Cave") in [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale"), over a table in the first tomb's zone.

With [Hendraheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Hendraheim "Skyrim:Hendraheim"), another copy can be found on the table towards right side end of the hall of the [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Hendraheim_(place) "Skyrim:Hendraheim (place)").

### Night Falls on Sentinel

A copy of *[Night Falls on Sentinel](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Falls_on_Sentinel "Skyrim:Night Falls on Sentinel")* will spawn on a crate in [Swindler's Den](/wiki/Skyrim:Swindler%27s_Den "Skyrim:Swindler's Den") after accepting the quest. Copies may also be found in a bandit camp near [The Guardian Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Guardian_Stones "Skyrim:The Guardian Stones"), and in [Volunruud](/wiki/Skyrim:Volunruud "Skyrim:Volunruud") during the quest [Silenced Tongues](/wiki/Skyrim:Silenced_Tongues "Skyrim:Silenced Tongues").

### Song of the Alchemists

Unfortunately, *[Song of the Alchemists](/wiki/Skyrim:Song_of_the_Alchemists "Skyrim:Song of the Alchemists")* cannot be obtained without stealing in the base game. After activating this quest, a copy spawns in the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College") on the main floor, to the right from the entrance on the counter (a blue book). It is not marked as stolen, but you will still incur the penalties of stealing if you are caught taking it. The book can also be stolen from [Anise's Cabin](/wiki/Skyrim:Anise%27s_Cabin "Skyrim:Anise's Cabin") near [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood").

If you have the [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") expansion, a copy which does not require stealing can be found in [Apocrypha](/wiki/Skyrim:Apocrypha "Skyrim:Apocrypha"), inside the Black Book called *[Waking Dreams](/wiki/Skyrim:Apocrypha_(Waking_Dreams) "Skyrim:Apocrypha (Waking Dreams)")*, in Chapter III.

A copy of the book can also be found in the hidden alchemy laboratory of [Goldenhills Plantation](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenhills_Plantation "Skyrim:Goldenhills Plantation").

## Notes

- [Halted Stream Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Halted_Stream_Camp "Skyrim:Halted Stream Camp") is the location of a [bounty quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Bandit_Boss "Skyrim:Bounty: Bandit Boss"), possible location of a [Dungeon Delving](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Bandits)") quest from [Amren](/wiki/Skyrim:Amren "Skyrim:Amren"), and possible location of the [Amulet of the Moon](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_the_Moon "Skyrim:Amulet of the Moon") quest, making it easy to complete multiple quests at once if you choose to find your mammoth tusk there. It is also one of only two locations where the [Transmute](/wiki/Skyrim:Transmute "Skyrim:Transmute") spell tome may be found.
- If Hearthfire is installed, then the Jarl of Falkreath will send you a [message via courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Jarl_(Jarl%27s_Name)_of_(Jarl%27s_City) "Skyrim:Letter from Jarl (Jarl's Name) of (Jarl's City)") once you have become level 9, as long as you have not yet started the Falkreath thane quest. If Rare Gifts is in your journal when the courier is given the message, you will fail the quest and it will be removed from your journal. Reading the note the courier gives you will start the quest [Build Your Own Home](/wiki/Skyrim:Build_Your_Own_Home "Skyrim:Build Your Own Home").
- You must have the item in your inventory for the quest giver's quest pointer and dialogue to display.

## Bugs

- If you complete the Stormcloaks quest [Battle for Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Solitude "Skyrim:Battle for Solitude"), Captain Aldis will no longer be part of the city and the indication arrow for you to follow will lead to a blank floor, where Aldis previously stood.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.0, addresses this issue. This quest will fail as soon as Battle for Solitude is started.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Captain Aldis can be spawned again using the console command `player.placeatme 00041FB8`. Afterwards, you can speak with him about quests like normal.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") An alternative is to type `SetObjectiveCompleted Favor110 10 1` in the console to complete the quest and then to remove *The Mirror* from your inventory by typing `player.additem 0001AFDE -1`.
- It seems likely that Siddgeir was intended to give a leveled piece of armor as a reward, however he gives potions instead. It's also possible that this was a last-minute change.

  **Mod Notes**: The FavorRewardArmor property on TIF\_\_00090DEE is set to FavorRewardPotion.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage Favor110 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest Favor110`.
