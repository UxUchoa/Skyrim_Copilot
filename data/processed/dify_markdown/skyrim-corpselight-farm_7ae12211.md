Corpselight Farm, Falkreath

**Corpselight Farm** is a small farmstead located in [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/SR-interior-Corpselight_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Corpselight_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Corpselight_Farm.jpg)

Corpselight Farm interior

It is run by [Mathies](/wiki/Skyrim:Mathies "Skyrim:Mathies") and his wife, [Indara Caerellia](/wiki/Skyrim:Indara_Caerellia "Skyrim:Indara Caerellia"). Their daughter was recently killed when a passing farmhand named [Sinding](/wiki/Skyrim:Sinding "Skyrim:Sinding") went berserk and ripped her to shreds with his bare hands. When first visiting Falkreath her parents can be found at the burial service being held in the graveyard beyond the rear of their plot. It consists of only one interior zone, *[Corpselight Farm](#Corpselight_Farm)*.

### Corpselight Farm

This is a single-room home, with a cupboard holding a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). To the right, in the southern corner and along the southwest wall are several barrels of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"). Opposite the cupboard on the northwest wall is a long wooden table and bench. On the table are two bottles of alto wine, a bottle of wine, a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), three [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") and various pieces of cheese. Hanging above from the ceiling are four [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), four [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") and a bunch of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear").

Opposite the door in the middle of the northwest wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a bowl of [Troll Fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat") on the mantelpiece. By the hearth is a pile of firewood and [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"). To the right of the fireplace is a cupboard holding two common books, a copy of *[The Apprentice's Assistant](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Apprentice%27s_Assistant "Skyrim:The Apprentice's Assistant")* and a copy of the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Racial Phylogeny](/wiki/Skyrim:Racial_Phylogeny "Skyrim:Racial Phylogeny")*. Above the cupboard are two more bunches of frost mirriam and three more bunches of elves ear.

Coming out from the northeast wall is a large double bed with end tables either side holding a bottle of wine and one of alto wine. Next to the bed on the southeast wall is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot, with a round wooden table and two chairs to the right of the chest. On the table are a bottle of each; wine and alto wine and a loaf of bread. Right next to the entrance is a basket containing three more chicken's eggs.

## Notes

- The grain mill can be used to produce [flour](/wiki/Skyrim:Sack_of_Flour "Skyrim:Sack of Flour")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") from [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") if [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is installed.
- The Prima guide mentions the name of the farm comes from a past resident that helped develop the idea of "cultivating" the witchlights and wisps that illuminate the edges of the town graveyard.
- Corpselight Farm also appears in [Legends](/wiki/Legends:Corpselight_Farm "Legends:Corpselight Farm").
