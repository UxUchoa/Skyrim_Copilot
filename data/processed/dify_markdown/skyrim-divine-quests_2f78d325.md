# Skyrim:Divine Quests

## Overview

These **quests** involve the **[Eight Divines](/wiki/Lore:Eight_Divines "Lore:Eight Divines")**.

- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials")**: Participate in [Kyne](/wiki/Lore:Kyne "Lore:Kyne")'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").
- **[The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature")**: Restore the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") for [Danica Pure-Spring](/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring "Skyrim:Danica Pure-Spring").
- **[The Book of Love](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Love "Skyrim:The Book of Love")**: Help the [Temple of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Mara "Skyrim:Temple of Mara") spread the cause of love throughout Skyrim.
- **[The Heart of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Heart_of_Dibella "Skyrim:The Heart of Dibella")**: Find a new [Sybil](/wiki/Skyrim:Fjotra "Skyrim:Fjotra") for the [Temple of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Temple of Dibella").
