Nord Dockworker

The **East Empire Dockworkers** are employees found at the [East Empire Company Warehouse](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Warehouse "Skyrim:East Empire Company Warehouse"). The [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") dockworker wears farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000209a6 "Skyrim:Clothes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5 "Skyrim:Boots"). The [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") dockworker wears a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b "Skyrim:Boots"). Neither of the dockworkers carry anything in their inventory.

## Notes

- The dockworkers are replaced by [East Empire wardens](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Warden "Skyrim:East Empire Warden") when the quest [Scoundrel's Folly](/wiki/Skyrim:Scoundrel%27s_Folly "Skyrim:Scoundrel's Folly") is started.
- Both of the dockworkers are members of the [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") class, which determines the level of their skills.
- The Breton dockworker does not have a voice type.
- According to their respective refs, the Nord dockworker's name is **Slading** and the Breton's is **Jonne**.
- Neither of the dockworkers have any AI packages, resulting in them just standing in place unless provoked.
