Fjotra

**Fjotra** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") who is initially found in [Broken Tower Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt "Skyrim:Broken Tower Redoubt"). She has been kidnapped by a group of [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") and imprisoned in their redoubt. She is the daughter of [Enmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon "Skyrim:Enmon") and [Mena](/wiki/Skyrim:Mena "Skyrim:Mena") from [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten").

She is the new sybil of Dibella, and you will be tasked with bringing her back to the [Temple of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Temple of Dibella") in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth").

Fjotra wears red colored [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Child's Clothes") and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000e80a8 "Skyrim:Clothing").

:   *"Are you one of them? Please don't hurt me."*

## Conversations

If Enmon is with you and you go to Karthwasten with Fjotra, this conversation will occur:

:   **Fjotra**: *"Father!"*
:   **Enmon**: *"Fjotra, dear. Did they hurt you?"*
:   **Fjotra**: *"Of course not. My spirit remains strong."*
:   **Enmon**: *"My little girl. Always strong. Do you know where you have to go?"*
:   **Fjotra**: *"I'm to go to the temple in Markarth. I... I'll have to leave you and mother."*
:   **Enmon**: *"It's our honor to have you there. This man is going to take you to Markarth. Are you ready?"*
:   **Fjotra**: *"Of course."*

## Notes

- Although both her mother and father are [Bretons](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton"), Fjotra is a Nord, suggesting that she was adopted. According to the [Prima Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide") she is a Reachwoman.[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1):332
- Fjotra is the only child [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") in the game. As a child, she is invulnerable to attacks, making her useful as bait in a fight.
