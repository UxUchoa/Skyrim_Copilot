# Skyrim:Mines

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-place-Embershard_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-place-Embershard_Mine.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Embershard_Mine.jpg)

Embershard Mine

[![SR-mapicon-Mine.png](//images.uesp.net/9/90/SR-mapicon-Mine.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Mine.png) This page lists all the **mines** in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"). Mines generally contain several veins of [ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Ores_and_Ingots#Ores "Skyrim:Ores and Ingots"). Ore veins in uncleared mines, including unclearable mines such as Gloombound Mine, respawn in 10 in-game days, while ore veins in cleared mines respawn in 30 in-game days. For details on how to mine ore and lists of where various ores can be found, see [Mining](/wiki/Skyrim:Mining "Skyrim:Mining").
