Stony Creek Cave

**Stony Creek Cave** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") southeast of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") containing [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). It contains only one zone, *[Stony Creek Cave](#Stony_Creek_Cave)*.

### Exterior

The entrance to the cave is near a small pond with a jetty and rowboat to the west. With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, some [Fishing Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Supplies "Skyrim:Fishing Supplies") can be found here. Fish from [Temperate Lakes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_(activity)#Biomes "Skyrim:Fishing (activity)") can be caught here. The middle of the pond bed features several harvestable [clams](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat "Skyrim:Clam Meat"). The cave entrance is to the east of the pier; a stream accompanied by rising steam flows out of the cave into the pond. There is a stack of crates with three food sacks to the right of the entrance, and several [Nordic barnacle clusters](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle_Cluster "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle Cluster") on the rock walls on either side of the entrance. A [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") is a short distance east of the cave, with an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") slightly northwest of that.

### Stony Creek Cave

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/SR-interior-Stony_Creek_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Stony_Creek_Cave.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Stony_Creek_Cave.jpg)

Stony Creek Cave interior

The cave itself consists of the stream that fills the pond at the entrance, and has two main areas. The first thing you see upon entering is a dead [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") drooped over a beam in front of the stream. The water here contains some [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(creature) "Skyrim:Salmon (creature)") and two lootable [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton"). The path consists of some planks that extend into the water to the right-hand side of the water. After turning the first corner along the path formed by the stream, the air appears to be shimmering; this is [flammable gas](/wiki/Skyrim:Flammable_Gas "Skyrim:Flammable Gas"), and can be ignited with [flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Flames "Skyrim:Flames") or a torch (just having a fire spell equipped is enough to set it off). The gas extends almost to the final chamber and partway into the side chamber, and if ignited, will deal significant damage to any enemies caught in the blast. However, any remaining enemies will be on high alert after igniting the gas, making any further stealth approach very difficult. The tunnel is patrolled by two bandits.

The side chamber is down a tunnel to the right shortly before the main path takes a left turn. There is a bandit on lookout here on high alert; he is always sitting on the edge of the planks beside a lantern at the entrance to the side tunnel, even though a chair is available for use. At the bottom of the planks is a lootable skeleton, and behind the bandit is a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), an [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), and a [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot"). To the left before the fungi and between the lit sconces, the tunnel continues to the side chamber. Halfway through the tunnel is a tripwire which triggers a [swinging mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Swinging_Mace "Skyrim:Swinging Mace") trap hanging from the ceiling, but if you sprint through the trap, the swinging mace should not hit you.

The side chamber contains the boss bandit, who carries [Treasure Map X](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_X "Skyrim:Treasure Map X"). With the [Ruin's Edge](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruin%27s_Edge "Skyrim:Ruin's Edge") Creation installed, the boss will also be carrying the bow [Ruin's Edge](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruin%27s_Edge_(item) "Skyrim:Ruin's Edge (item)") the first time you kill him. The boss chamber also contains an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") with a bowl of [ectoplasm](/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm "Skyrim:Ectoplasm") on top. To the left of the lab is a table with an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing") and a bowl holding some [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flowers on top. To the right is a bench with an [unusual gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem"), a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poisons "Skyrim:Poisons"), a bowl of [fire salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts "Skyrim:Fire Salts"), and the [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mystery of Talara, Part 4](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_Part_4 "Skyrim:Mystery of Talara, Part 4")*. In the corner is the unlocked boss chest, which contains [Finn's Lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Finn%27s_Lute_(item) "Skyrim:Finn's Lute (item)") and other random loot. Growing around the chamber are two [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown"), one [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap"), and one Namira's rot.

Returning to the tunnel and going north, on a ledge to the right just past the end of the gas is a Namira's rot. There is one last bandit in the large chamber, mining one of the two [moonstone ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein") that are marked by lit lanterns. The chamber also contains an apprentice-locked chest near the ore vein to the left, a couple more skeletons that can be looted, and several food sacks. With the [Saints & Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers") Creation installed, a unique [Bliss Bug in a Jar](/wiki/Skyrim:Bliss_Bug_in_a_Jar "Skyrim:Bliss Bug in a Jar") is on a pair of crates immediately to the left as you enter the chamber. Finally, a waterfall falls from a ledge on the rear wall, with an exit from [Kagrenzel](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrenzel "Skyrim:Kagrenzel") in the short tunnel beyond. This exit is accessible from this direction by carefully jumping up the small rock face beneath the waterfall or by using the [Whirlwind Sprint](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Sprint "Skyrim:Whirlwind Sprint") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") when standing on a small hill to the right of the waterfall.

## Notes

- Stony Creek Cave also [appears](/wiki/Legends:Eastmarch_Camp "Legends:Eastmarch Camp") in *[Legends](/wiki/Legends:Legends "Legends:Legends")*.
- You can enter [Kagrenzel](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrenzel "Skyrim:Kagrenzel") from Stony Creek Cave by carefully jumping up the small rock face beneath the waterfall at the end of the cave or by using the [Whirlwind Sprint](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Sprint "Skyrim:Whirlwind Sprint") shout when standing on a small hill to the right of the waterfall. However, the room with the glowing orb in Kagrenzel is not normally accessible if you enter from this direction.

## Bugs

- For quests related to Kagrenzel, your map marker may point you to Stony Creek Cave. This also applies to the [Clairvoyance](/wiki/Skyrim:Clairvoyance_(spell) "Skyrim:Clairvoyance (spell)") spell.
- It is advised not to take Finn's Lute until the quest [Finn's Lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Finn%27s_Lute "Skyrim:Finn's Lute") has been started, as it will not be removed from your inventory if taken beforehand.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.5, fixes this bug.
- The chief bandit carrying the treasure map may not appear. Forcing the dungeon to respawn, either through starting one of the quests that require a boss bandit or waiting 30 days, should make the boss spawn. **?**
