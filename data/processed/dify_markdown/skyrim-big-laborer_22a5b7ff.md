Big Laborer

The **Big Laborer** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") that may appear as a target during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever") at [Katla's Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Katla%27s_Farm "Skyrim:Katla's Farm").

The Big Laborer wears farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261C0 "Skyrim:Clothing") along with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261BD "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261c1 "Skyrim:Clothing").
