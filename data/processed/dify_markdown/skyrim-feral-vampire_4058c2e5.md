Feral Vampire

The **Feral Vampire** is a [High Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Elf "Skyrim:High Elf") [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") encountered when exploring [Castle Volkihar](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Volkihar "Skyrim:Castle Volkihar") with [Serana](/wiki/Skyrim:Serana "Skyrim:Serana") during the quest [Chasing Echoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Chasing_Echoes "Skyrim:Chasing Echoes"). After entering the secret entrance to the castle, you'll find her with some [death hounds](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Hound "Skyrim:Death Hound") before you make it to the courtyard. She is instantly hostile to you whether you have sided with the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") or the vampires. She wears [ragged trousers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers "Skyrim:Ragged Trousers"), [footwraps](/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps "Skyrim:Footwraps"), and a [Ring of Minor Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Minor_Destruction "Skyrim:Ring of Minor Destruction"), and wields an [Elven Dagger of Fear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger_of_Fear "Skyrim:Elven Dagger of Fear"). She also carries a [journal fragment](/wiki/Skyrim:Journal_Fragment "Skyrim:Journal Fragment") which gives a little insight as to why she's down there.
