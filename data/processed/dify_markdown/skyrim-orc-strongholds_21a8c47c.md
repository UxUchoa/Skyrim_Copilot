## Blood-Kin

Only [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") characters have immediate access to the strongholds unchallenged. Characters of any other race must first become [Blood-Kin of the Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood-Kin_of_the_Orcs "Skyrim:Blood-Kin of the Orcs") before being welcome in the strongholds. You can enter a stronghold without being Blood-Kin, but the residents will continuously tell you that you are not welcome. If the stronghold includes [merchants](/wiki/Skyrim:Merchants "Skyrim:Merchants"), [trainers](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers"), or potential [followers](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"), their services will be unavailable until you comply.

Several quests fulfill this requirement. Members of any stronghold you approach may give you the quest [The Forgemaster's Fingers](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forgemaster%27s_Fingers "Skyrim:The Forgemaster's Fingers") if you have not become Blood-Kin already. Alternatively, completing any quest listed below will make you Blood-Kin. Upon first completing one of these quests, the grateful Orc will say, *"For your help, I'm sending word to the Orc strongholds that you're to be trusted. You should be welcome there now."*, signifying that you are now Blood-Kin. In addition, each associated Orc's [disposition](/wiki/Skyrim:Disposition "Skyrim:Disposition") towards you will increase by 1 upon completing the quests below, with two possible exceptions.

## Notes

- If you start "[Unbound](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound "Skyrim:Unbound")" as an Orc, [Hadvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hadvar "Skyrim:Hadvar") will say: *"You from one of the strongholds, Orc? How did you end up here?"*
- If you become Blood-Kin and later bring your [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") to the Orc strongholds, the residents will treat your follower as an extra ally as long as you and your follower are together.
- Another Orc who would've made you Blood-Kin, named [Makhel Abbas](/wiki/Skyrim:Makhel_Abbas "Skyrim:Makhel Abbas"), was meant to play a role in a Civil War quest, but this quest didn't make it into the final game.

## Bugs

- [Atub](/wiki/Skyrim:Atub "Skyrim:Atub") and [Garakh](/wiki/Skyrim:Garakh "Skyrim:Garakh") at [Largashbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur "Skyrim:Largashbur") were apparently intended to be merchants (apothecary and blacksmith respectively), but do not function as merchants themselves.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. They were assigned the wrong settings. Once fixed, they will be affected by [Master Trader](/wiki/Skyrim:Master_Trader "Skyrim:Master Trader"), but not [Investor](/wiki/Skyrim:Investor "Skyrim:Investor").
- Even after becoming Blood-Kin to the Orcs, you may still be warned about trespassing in the longhouses at the Orc strongholds.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.5, fixes this bug.
