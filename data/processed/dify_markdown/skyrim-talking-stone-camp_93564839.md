Talking Stone Camp

**Talking Stone Camp** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") northeast of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead").

## Related Quests

## Walkthrough

Two [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and two [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") occupy the camp and the nearby area, with one giant in the camp and the other giant herding the mammoths to the northeast. A large campfire is in the middle of the camp, with two [charred skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") on fire-hardened stakes cooking over it. Typical of giant camps, many painted stones, [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes"), and mammoth ribs and skulls are found throughout the area. North of the campfire is an empty stone bowl with a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), a random [healing potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potions"), and a random [fortify skill potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Potion_of_Alteration "Skyrim:Potions") beside it. To the left is another stone bowl holding many [soul gem fragments](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gem_Fragment "Skyrim:Soul Gem Fragment") and a random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"). Nearby to the north is a tree stump with a [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") fungus and a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). To the left of the chest is a large painted stone with several giant clubs, a few leather strips, a random [warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer "Skyrim:Warhammer"), and a [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield") displayed on it. On the other side of the display is a [mammoth cheese bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl").

South of the display are a couple of drying racks with a bunch of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear") and two bunches of [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam") hanging from them. A large mammoth cheese bowl is between the racks. East of the drying racks and past the path to the south are another two racks. Behind them is a tree, and behind that is a high ledge where two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") can be found. Farther east from the racks is another rack with a bunch of dried elves ear and another mammoth cheese bowl nearby. Beyond this is a large rock formation, with a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") on a low ledge to the northeast. Beneath the rock is a pool of water fed by a wellspring. Submerged in front of the wellspring is a random [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet"), a [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), and an apprentice-locked chest.

## Notes

- There is a high chance of the camp being attacked by [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). If any survive the initial attack, they will flee toward [Orotheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Orotheim "Skyrim:Orotheim").
- Talking Stone Camp has more insect spawn points than any other location, with twenty-two total, allowing you to collect many [butterfly wings](/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly_Wing "Skyrim:Butterfly Wing") and [blue butterfly wings](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly_Wing "Skyrim:Blue Butterfly Wing") during the day and [luna moth wings](/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth_Wing "Skyrim:Luna Moth Wing") and [torchbug thoraxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug_Thorax "Skyrim:Torchbug Thorax") at night. Twenty-three [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton") plants also grow near the camp.
- A stream flowing east from the pool leads to a waterfall above an inlet to [River Hjaal](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Hjaal "Skyrim:River Hjaal"). Just above the waterfall is another iron ore vein. The corpse of an [alchemist](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemist "Skyrim:Alchemist") lies on an island in the middle of the inlet.
- A dirt path leading west from the campfire leads to a small stream. On the other side of the stream is another iron ore vein.
