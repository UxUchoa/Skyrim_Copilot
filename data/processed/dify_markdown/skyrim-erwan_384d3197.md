Erwan

Erwan is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") hedge wizard encountered at [Traitor's Post](/wiki/Skyrim:Traitor%27s_Post "Skyrim:Traitor's Post") during the related quest. She and her bandit companions are hostile and will attack on sight.

Erwan is a former member of the [Crimson Dirks](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Dirks "Skyrim:Crimson Dirks") and appears to be quite mentally disturbed; she has killed the merchant [Gunther](/wiki/Skyrim:Gunther "Skyrim:Gunther") and now considers his corpse to be her "best friend". Gunther had been sent her way by her brother Edward as a "gift", along with a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v5](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v5 "Skyrim:The Crimson Dirks, v5")* which describes her earlier life.

She wears [scaled armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Armor") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots") and wields an [elven war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_War_Axe "Skyrim:Elven War Axe"). She also carried a [Dwarven dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Dagger "Skyrim:Dwarven Dagger"), a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow"), and eight [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"), as well as her [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Erwan%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Erwan's Journal") and a [letter from Edward](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Edward "Skyrim:Letter from Edward"). She utilizes [frost damage](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage "Skyrim:Frost Damage") spells in combat such as [Frost Cloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Cloak "Skyrim:Frost Cloak"). You will be required to kill her and read her journal as part of the related quest.
