Snow-Shod Manor in Riften

**Snow-Shod Manor** is a large manor in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), home of the Snow-Shod clan. It is located at the northeastern part of Riften, between [Aerin's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Aerin%27s_House "Skyrim:Aerin's House") and [Riftweald Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Riftweald_Manor "Skyrim:Riftweald Manor").

### Snow-Shod Manor

The entrance is kept adept-locked most of the time. Upon entering you are in a large open room with a flight of stairs up on your left, with stairs leading down to the cellar behind and several [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and sacks around the stairwell. A long end table is against the north wall, on top of which is a [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), a [purple](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"), and two [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), as well as a bunch of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). There is a chair opposite the entrance against the east wall, with a wall shelf above holding two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). To the right of the chair is a cupboard and a set of shelves in the southeast corner. These hold a bunch of lavender, a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a bottle of wine, copies of *[Rising Threat, Vol. III](/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._III "Skyrim:Rising Threat, Vol. III")*, *[Killing - Before You're Killed](/wiki/Skyrim:Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed "Skyrim:Killing - Before You're Killed")*, and *[Dwemer Inquiries Vol II](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_II "Skyrim:Dwemer Inquiries Vol II")*, an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"), and a couple sacks. Against the south wall is an end table that holds a bottle of alto wine with a chair on either side, and against the west wall another long end table holding an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") and a [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor.jpg)

Dining room

There is a doorway in the northeast corner leading through into the dining area, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand against the west wall, a cupboard containing only clutter against the north wall, and a wooden dining table and benches in the middle of the room. On the table are three [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), two portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a bottle of alto wine, a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"). Hanging from the ceiling are two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid, and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). To the west is the staircase leading down to the cellar.

At the bottom of the stairs, against the south wall is a large pile of firewood and several bales of straw, with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") leaning against the firewood. To the east is a storage area full of various crates, barrels, and sacks. To the north is another storage area with a disused table and chairs, two sets of shelves, and a cupboard containing clutter. On the shelves are various pieces of cheese, two [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a load of [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and several loaves of bread. To the right of the cupboard is a small round table holding a mead barrel.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b5/SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor_02.jpg)

Snow-Shod's bedroom

To the right at the top of the stairs is a landing and corridor, with a bedroom to the east belonging to [Asgeir Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Asgeir_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Asgeir Snow-Shod"), a double bedroom to the west shared by [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Vulwulf_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Vulwulf Snow-Shod") and his wife, [Nura Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Nura_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Nura Snow-Shod"), and at the end of the corridor, by some barrels, sacks, and a crate, another bedroom; it would have been their daughter's room, who died after joining the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Lore:Stormcloaks "Lore:Stormcloaks").

Asgeir's bedroom is L-shaped, long, and narrow, and consists of a single owned bed against the east wall, with a small low table beside it. On the table is a bottle of wine. To the right is a chair, and against the west wall is a long end table with an iron sword and [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield") on top. Opposite the door, against the east wall is a wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). Lilija's old room consists of a single owned bed against the east wall, with an end table beside it against the north wall, and a wardrobe containing clothes beside that. Against the west wall is an unlocked wooden [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot, and opposite the door is a long end table with a bunch of lavender on top.

The parents' room contains an owned double bed coming out from the south wall, with a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and two loose coins underneath, an end table to the left, and a wall shelf above it. On the end table is a copy of *[Yellow Book of Riddles](/wiki/Skyrim:Yellow_Book_of_Riddles "Skyrim:Yellow Book of Riddles")*, and on the shelf are two bottles of wine. At the foot of the bed is a long end table, and to the right of the bed is a fireplace. Along the west wall is a pile of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"), while in the northwest corner is a small square wooden table and two chairs, with a loaf of bread and a bottle of wine on top of the table. Against the north wall is a tall wardrobe containing clothes.
