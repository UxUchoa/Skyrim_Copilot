## Detailed Walkthrough

**Note: This quest was originally added to your journal upon installing the [Arcane Archer Pack](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Archer_Pack "Skyrim:Arcane Archer Pack") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition"), this quest no longer appears in your journal. The [Spell Tome](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Telekinesis_Arrows "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Telekinesis Arrows") and [Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis_Arrows_Research_Notes "Skyrim:Telekinesis Arrows Research Notes") added by the Creation can still be acquired from [the Arcanaeum](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum "Skyrim:The Arcanaeum") in the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)"), and [Telekinesis Arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis_Arrow "Skyrim:Telekinesis Arrow") can still be crafted.**

Once you've installed the Arcane Archer Pack Creation, you will receive this quest directing you to the College of Winterhold to learn more about a spell tome which has been uncovered. The tome in question, [*Spell Tome: Telekinesis Arrows*](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Telekinesis_Arrows "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Telekinesis Arrows"), is held in the Arcanaeum, requiring you to first gain access to the college in order to proceed. See the [First Lessons](/wiki/Skyrim:First_Lessons "Skyrim:First Lessons") quest walkthrough for more information on how to join the college.

Locate the spell tome on the counter in front of [Urag gro-Shub](/wiki/Skyrim:Urag_gro-Shub "Skyrim:Urag gro-Shub"). Simply picking it up will advance the quest. However, you must read the tome in order to learn the related [Telekinesis Arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis_Arrows "Skyrim:Telekinesis Arrows") lesser power, which must be used to control the arrows. Reading the nearby [*Telekinesis Arrows Research Notes*](/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis_Arrows_Research_Notes "Skyrim:Telekinesis Arrows Research Notes") will teach you how to craft [Telekinesis Arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis_Arrow "Skyrim:Telekinesis Arrow"), provides an introductory description of how the arrows function and completes the quest.
