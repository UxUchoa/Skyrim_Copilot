Daighre's House

**Daighre's House** is the home of [Daighre](/wiki/Skyrim:Daighre "Skyrim:Daighre"), next door to [Skaggi's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaggi%27s_House "Skyrim:Skaggi's House"), near the [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine").

It is the first house on the left after crossing the bridge leading to the entrance to the Left Hand mine. It contains only one interior zone, *[Daighre's House](#Daighre.27s_House)*.

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-interior-Daighre%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Daighre%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Daighre%27s_House.jpg)

Inside Daighre's house

### Exterior

It is a single story Dwemer dwelling in a small settlement, where the miners from the [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine") live. The entrance is frequently left unlocked. The [miner's barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Left Hand Miner's Barracks") are directly opposite. There is a [juniper tree](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") growing on the roof.

### Daighre's House

Inside it is a single roomed house with a large fireplace in the middle of the north wall with a pot hanging in front of it containing a [grilled chicken breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Chicken_Breast "Skyrim:Grilled Chicken Breast") and a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"). To the left of the fireplace is an unlocked non-respawning [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing leveled items, with a basket on the floor containing two [gourds](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") and [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel on either side of it. Coming out from the west wall is a long wooden table with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a grilled chicken breast and a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") in a cast iron pot on top of the table. On a ledge on the west wall is a [deer hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide "Skyrim:Deer Hide"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a loaf of bread and a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"). Against the south wall is a dresser with a bunch of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") on top and containing [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"). At the other end of the room there are two single owned beds, [Daighre](/wiki/Skyrim:Daighre "Skyrim:Daighre")'s is along the north wall and [Erith](/wiki/Skyrim:Erith "Skyrim:Erith")'s is along the east wall. There is a ledge along the east wall with a copy of *[Horker Attacks](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Attacks "Skyrim:Horker Attacks")* and a couple of food sacks on top, with a rack hanging from the ceiling above holding a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). There is a food barrel in the southeastern corner with a crate and sack to the right. On the crate is a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips").

## Notes

- Aside from most barrels and sacks, all containers in the house are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") and safe to store items in.
