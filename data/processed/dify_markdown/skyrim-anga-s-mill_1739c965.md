Anga's Mill

**Anga's Mill** is a small wood mill built along the north bank of the [River Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim "Skyrim:River Yorgrim"), west of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). It consists of two houses and a sawmill. The mill is owned by [Aeri](/wiki/Skyrim:Aeri "Skyrim:Aeri"), a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") woman who inherited the sawmill from her father who died in the Great War. A short distance along the western road is the camp where [Ennodius Papius](/wiki/Skyrim:Ennodius_Papius "Skyrim:Ennodius Papius") lives.

## Walkthrough

The mill has two houses, [Aeri's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Aeri%27s_House "Skyrim:Aeri's House") just across from the saw mill structure and [Anga's Mill Common House](/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House "Skyrim:Anga's Mill Common House") up the hill to the west.
Outside the mill, [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken") range freely. There are two [wood chopping blocks](/wiki/Skyrim:Chop_Wood "Skyrim:Chop Wood") and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"). There are five barrels containing food and [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"). You can buy [sawn logs](/wiki/Skyrim:Sawn_Log "Skyrim:Sawn Log")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") here after installing [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") by speaking to Aeri. You can also cut your own for free if she has given permission to do so.
Up the hill, two nests with [chickens' eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg")  are outside the Common House. A [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Quarried_Stone "Skyrim:Quarried Stone")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") can be found here as well.

## Notes

- If you travel west a short distance down the road from the Mill, you will come across the tent that Ennodius Papius sleeps in during his related quest.
- There is a waterfall in the [River Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim "Skyrim:River Yorgrim"), due west of Anga's Mill, with a dry alcove behind it. The alcove holds an adept-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"), a barrel containing alchemical ingredients, a lootable skeleton, two quicksilver ingots, a weapon on top of the crate, and a buried lootable chest. A third ingot is underwater a few paces downstream. Nearby there are mudcrabs, Nordic barnacle clusters and a camp. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=102440&y=30783&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Five [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") veins can be found around the area:
  - One can be found along the bank of River Yorgrim.
  - One lies across the river, opposite the mill.
  - Two are located near Ennodius' camp; one to the south and the other to the west.
  - One can be found just northwest of Aeri's house.
