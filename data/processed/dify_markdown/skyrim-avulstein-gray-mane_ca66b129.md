Avulstein Gray-Mane

**Avulstein Gray-Mane** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") hiding out in [House Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:House_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:House Gray-Mane") in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). While his brother was reported to have fallen in combat, his family believes Thorald to have been captured by the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") instead, and that the [Battle-Borns](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle-Born "Skyrim:Battle-Born") are aware of this. Fearing for his own safety, Avulstein doesn't leave the house. He's dedicated to find out what happened to his brother, and will ask for your help during the quest [Missing In Action](/wiki/Skyrim:Missing_In_Action "Skyrim:Missing In Action").

The bad blood between the clans has caused Avulstein to never show his face in public so he stays inside the house at all times, either sitting in a chair, sleeping or eating. While optional, Avulstein may leave the house during his [related quest](#Related_Quests) (see [this section](#Missing_in_Action) for full details). He wears a set of [scaled armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Armor") with a matching pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots"). He is equipped with a [Skyforge steel battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge_Steel_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Skyforge Steel Battleaxe"), and carries a [key to the clan's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_House_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Key to House Gray-Mane") as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

Once you have gained his trust during the related quest, Avulstein will also start to reveal his strong opinions, starting with Whiterun: *"Forget [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") or [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). Whiterun was the crown jewel of Skyrim before... Well, before... Never mind."* and *"This was such a glorious city. But then everything had to change. The Empire came... and my brother was lost..."* He will then move on to Skyrim and its rightful heirs: *"Ah, but Skyrim! Skyrim is supreme among all nations! Our people, our native people, are unmatched in all Tamriel!"* and *"One day the war will end and the true heirs of Skyrim will inherit this great realm."*

He may say goodbye with, *"Remember - don't trust the Imperials. No matter what honeyed lies they tell you."*, *"Best if... you didn't mention to anyone that you saw me here."*, *"Live long. Fight well."*, or *"I've said too much already."*

As part of [Clan Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Gray-Mane"), he is the son of [Eorlund](/wiki/Skyrim:Eorlund_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Eorlund Gray-Mane") and [Fralia Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Fralia_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Fralia Gray-Mane"), brother of [Olfina](/wiki/Skyrim:Olfina_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Olfina Gray-Mane") and [Thorald Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thorald_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Thorald Gray-Mane"), and nephew of [Vignar Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Vignar_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Vignar Gray-Mane"). The now rivaling families used to be best of friends, but all that changed with the [civil war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"), where Avulstein's family took the side of the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") and the other family chose to side with the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"). When asked for a reason on how the two biggest families in Whiterun could clash like that, family patriarch Vignar Gray-Mane will explain: *"Aye, we used to be fast friends, though it was long ago. We were two of the city's oldest an' most-respected clans, an' we could trace our histories all the way back to Ysgramor's companions. But then Olfrid got rich an' decided he an his kin were too good for us simple, backwards Nord folk. When the troubles began, he took the Empire's side. We haven't spoken much since then."* Because of these political beliefs, any friendship between the clans has gone sour, as [Idolaf Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:Idolaf_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Idolaf Battle-Born") will admit: *"Avulstein Gray-Mane and I were best friends as children. If I see that traitor again, I'll throw him in jail myself!"*

#### Avulstein Stays Hidden

If you chose to do the rescue mission yourself, a grateful Thorald will ask you why you would risk your life for him, triggering two different options:

**Your brother talked me into it.**
*"I've never known Avulstein to be that convincing! Where is the little scamp?"*

**Your family was concerned for you.**
*"Of course. I should've known. This was all Avulstein's idea, wasn't it? Where is he?"*

You can then follow up with:

**I told him to wait in Whiterun.**
*"A wise decision, but I fear he may not be safe there anymore. They'll go looking for me, and Whiterun will be the first place they search. I cannot stay. Not here, not in Whiterun. It likely won't be safe for Avulstein, either. Our best hope now is to fall in with the ranks of the Stormcloaks. But would you do me a favor? Tell my mother to suffer the winter's cold wind, for it bears aloft next summer's seeds. She'll know what it means."*

Avulstein will disappear from the game at this point.

#### Avulstein Joins You

If you chose to bring Avulstein along with you he will meet you at the lake east of Northwatch Keep with his friends [Geirlund](/wiki/Skyrim:Geirlund "Skyrim:Geirlund") and [Vidrald](/wiki/Skyrim:Vidrald "Skyrim:Vidrald"). When asked why he is there, Vidrald will explain his loyalty: *"I pledged to aid Avulstein and Thorald a long time ago. There's nothing that could keep me from helping them now."* When you speak to Avulstein he will say, *"We're getting Thorald back, no matter what."* He will then reveal his plan: *"We kill every Thalmor between us and Thorald, and we bring him out alive. Let's go."* He will then follow you all the way through Northwatch Keep and keep his promise, slaying all the Thalmor you encounter.

When you reach the chained Thorald, the two brothers will have a brief conversation:

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-npc-Avulstein_Gray-Mane_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Avulstein_Gray-Mane_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Avulstein_Gray-Mane_02.jpg)

*"Thorald? Thorald!"*

**Thorald**: *"Unngh... Uhhh..."*
**Avulstein**: *"Thorald? Thorald!"*
**Thorald**: *"Brother? Is... Is that you?"*
**Avulstein**: *"I'm here! I knew I'd find you!"*
**Thorald**: *"What in the world?"*
**Avulstein**: *"There's no time. We've got to get you out of here! Come on, let's go!"*

Once outside, Thorald will thank you and ask why you risked your life for him. Two options are available:

**Your brother talked me into it.**
*"He must have been very convincing."*

**Your family was concerned for you.**
*"Of course. I should've known. Avulstein was behind this, wasn't he?"*

He will then finish the quest with, *"I cannot stay. Not here, not in Whiterun. It likely won't be safe for Avulstein, either. Our best hope now is to fall in with the ranks of the Stormcloaks. But would you do me a favor? Tell my mother to suffer the winter's cold wind, for it bears aloft next summer's seeds. She'll know what it means."* When you return to Fralia, she will be grateful although a bit sad that both her sons had to go into hiding. If you ask her if she's heard from them she will conclude: *"No, nor do I expect to. Would be too dangerous for now. It's enough for me to know that they're out there somewhere, and together again."*

Once you speak to Fralia, Avulstein will disappear from the game.

## Notes

- Avulstein is in the [housecarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl") faction and has an unused package to exile him to [Viola Giordano's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Viola_Giordano%27s_House "Skyrim:Viola Giordano's House") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), indicating that he was once intended to serve as Vignar's housecarl instead of his sister [Olfina](/wiki/Skyrim:Olfina_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Olfina Gray-Mane").
