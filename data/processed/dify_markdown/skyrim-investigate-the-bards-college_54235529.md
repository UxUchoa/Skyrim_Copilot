## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to any bard in Skyrim.
2. Speak to [Viarmo](/wiki/Skyrim:Viarmo "Skyrim:Viarmo") at the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College").

## Detailed Walkthrough

Speak to any Bard you meet in Skyrim. Bards can be found wandering around the countryside or in taverns and inns. The bard will suggest that you should investigate The Bards College. Following their advice, go to  [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") and speak to [Viarmo](/wiki/Skyrim:Viarmo "Skyrim:Viarmo").

## Bugs

- Starting this quest after you have already joined the Bards College will make this quest incompletable, permanently leaving this objective in your quest journal.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You can clear the "Investigate the Bards College" quest by using the console command `SetObjectiveCompleted MS05Start 10 1`.
- Having King Olaf's verse before investigating the Bards College may make this quest incompletable, permanently leaving this objective in your quest journal.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") This can be fixed with the console command `ResetQuest MS05Start` followed by `SetObjectiveCompleted MS05Start 10 1`.
