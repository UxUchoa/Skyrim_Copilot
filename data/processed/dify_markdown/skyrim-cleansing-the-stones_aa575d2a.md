## Quick Walkthrough

1. Use the [Bend Will](/wiki/Skyrim:Bend_Will "Skyrim:Bend Will") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") to cleanse the [Beast Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Beast_Stone "Skyrim:Beast Stone").
2. Use the Bend Will shout to cleanse the [Earth Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Earth_Stone "Skyrim:Earth Stone").
3. Use the Bend Will shout to cleanse the [Sun Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Stone "Skyrim:Sun Stone").
4. Use the Bend Will shout to cleanse the [Water Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Water_Stone "Skyrim:Water Stone").

## Detailed Walkthrough

With the [Wind Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Wind_Stone "Skyrim:Wind Stone") cleansed, return to [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village") and tell [Storn Crag-Strider](/wiki/Skyrim:Storn_Crag-Strider "Skyrim:Storn Crag-Strider") the good news. He will suggest you to do the same for the remaining four stones of Solstheim. He doubts it will fully stop whatever [Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:Miraak_(person) "Skyrim:Miraak (person)") is doing, but it may slow his progress. Your map will be updated and show you the location of your targets: [Beast Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Beast_Stone "Skyrim:Beast Stone"), [Earth Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Earth_Stone "Skyrim:Earth Stone"), [Sun Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Stone "Skyrim:Sun Stone"), and [Water Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Water_Stone "Skyrim:Water Stone").

### Beast Stone

The Beast Stone is southwest of Skaal Village; this is the nearest stone you may find. Two entranced [Rieklings](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling "Skyrim:Riekling") are working there. A leveled [cultist](/wiki/Skyrim:Cultist "Skyrim:Cultist") is patrolling around the stone and monitoring the work of Miraak's poor slaves. The cultist will attack you on sight, so defeat your enemy and use the [Bend Will](/wiki/Skyrim:Bend_Will "Skyrim:Bend Will") shout to cleanse the stone. The structure around the stone will collapse and a leveled [lurker](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker") will raise from the water. The two Rieklings won't turn hostile to you. They will stay here for a short moment then return to their tribe at Thirsk Mead Hall.

### Sun Stone

Keep traveling south and you'll find the Sun Stone in the north of [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(settlement) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (settlement)"). A group of [Reavers](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver "Skyrim:Reaver") are focusing on their building. Shout at the stone and a leveled lurker will attack you. The fight will be much easier with the help of these Reavers. They won't attack you after the combat; they'll just wander around the stone and give you some generic, non-hostile responses. After you leave this area, they will be removed from the game.

### Earth Stone

Travel to [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock") and you should find the Earth Stone without much trouble. [Bralsa Drel](/wiki/Skyrim:Bralsa_Drel "Skyrim:Bralsa Drel"), [Rirns Llervu](/wiki/Skyrim:Rirns_Llervu "Skyrim:Rirns Llervu"), and three [Redoran guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran_Guard "Skyrim:Redoran Guard") have lost themselves here, doing their work mindlessly. Cleanse the stone and two lurkers will appear this time. One is near the stone, the other one is near the coastline. The guards will try their best to give you a hand. Pay close attention to the safety of Bralsa as she gives a quest later on. They will return to the city soon after regaining their freedom.

### Water Stone

Walk along the west coast of Solstheim to find the Water Stone. The shrine at this stone is being worked on by a group of sailors, and a cultist is monitoring their work. The cultist will attack you without hesitation. Defeat your enemy and do the same to the stone. A leveled lurker will rise from the water and try to kill you. In addition, a low-level [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") will fly in from the west. If you haven't already triggered the Rieklings hidden in barrels to the southeast, they'll also arrive shortly, which can make for a chaotic battle. Fortunately, you don't need to guarantee the safety of every sailor. They will return to their boat docked in the bay once they are freed. After you leave this area, they will sail back to Skyrim with their ship.

## Notes

- Before you cleanse the stones, the entranced people working there will repeat the following texts mechanically: *"Here in his shrine.(Here in his temple.) That they have forgotten. Here do we toil. That we might remember. By night we reclaim.(Here we reclaim.) What by day was stolen.(What faithless minds have stolen.) Far from ourselves. He grows ever near to us. Our eyes once were blinded. Now through him do we see. Our hands once were idle. Now through them does he speak. And when the world shall listen. And when the world shall see. And when the world remembers. That world will cease to be."*
- If you activate the stones before cleansing them, you will be in an entranced state, and [Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:Miraak "Skyrim:Miraak") will repeat the following texts mechanically: *"Here in my shrine. (Here in my temple.) That you have forgotten. Here do you toil. That you might remember. By night you reclaim. What faithless minds have stolen. Far from yourself. I grow ever near to you. Your eyes once were blinded. Now through me do you see. Your hands once were idle. Now through them do I speak. And when the world shall listen. And when the world shall see. And when the world remembers. That world will cease to be."*
- If you activate any of the stones before cleansing them, you will be put into the same state as the rest of the occupants. However, unlike everyone else, you can exert your will and break free from the trance.
- The entranced Rieklings around the Beast Stone will repeat some similar texts in their own language: *"Wafoo athaaaal. Bertiflik a goroowala. Hra woong aba. Tika twafaara filik. Wilberk tikflaaar. Yuchaaa tungar cha tiweki. Bura warag faaanig. Tiri fuklik taaar aburak."*
- More people and Rieklings would appear at Beast Stone, Sun Stone, and Earth Stone during the night.
- Before you cleanse the stones, if you talk to someone who only works on the stones at night, they will say "The Master calls." as a reference to [Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:Miraak "Skyrim:Miraak") when they head to the designated stone each night. They will use the same quotes as the entranced people until morning.
- All stones will be marked as "clear" on the map once you cleanse them.
- The [Tree Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Tree_Stone "Skyrim:Tree Stone") is the only All-Maker Stone that is not cleansed by the end of this quest.
- This quest must be completed to finish [The Gardener of Men](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gardener_of_Men "Skyrim:The Gardener of Men") and the rest of the expansion's main questline.
- Prior to completing this quest, there is a chance that you will wake up at one of the stones whenever you sleep outdoors or in a dwelling, as long as the selected stone is still tainted.
  - The first time you sleep in an appropriate location, this random selection is guaranteed to occur, and as long as the selected stone has not already been cleansed, you will awake there. Thereafter, there is a 33% chance that a stone will be randomly selected for this purpose.
  - The Water Stone is not included in the list of stones you can wake up at.
- Activating each stone after freeing them will give you the powers of [Conjure Werebear](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Werebear "Skyrim:Conjure Werebear") (Beast Stone), [Bones of the Earth](/wiki/Skyrim:Bones_of_the_Earth "Skyrim:Bones of the Earth") (Earth Stone), [Waters of Life](/wiki/Skyrim:Waters_of_Life "Skyrim:Waters of Life") (Water Stone), and [Sun Flare](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Flare "Skyrim:Sun Flare") (Sun Stone) until you use them. You can activate them to get the powers at most once per day. The powers granted by the stones work underwater.
- You can cleanse the stones before the quest has started. If you cleanse all of them before getting the Dragonborn quest, Storn will send you to find Frea at the Tree Stone, instantly completing Dragonborn and starting [The Temple of Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Temple_of_Miraak "Skyrim:The Temple of Miraak").

## Bugs

- When attempting to use the [Bend Will](/wiki/Skyrim:Bend_Will "Skyrim:Bend Will") shout on a corrupted stone, you may lose the ability to interact with it, but the event will not start, causing the quest to become stuck.
  - Travel to the Wind Stone and acquire its power to get the quest working properly. Stones you've already tried to cleanse will spawn Lurkers as soon as you near them but the shrines will not explode.
  - Alternatively, simply leaving the cell and returning to it, or saving and reloading the saved game, may resolve the problem, with the Lurker(s) appearing and the quest updating after the fight.
- As many as three lurkers may appear (rare). One or two may be a significant distance from the stone after cleansing it. (Even out in the ocean or falling off a cliff).
- If [Reluctant Steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Reluctant_Steward "Skyrim:Reluctant Steward") is finished and the Sun Stone has been cleansed, [Drovas Relvi](/wiki/Skyrim:Drovas_Relvi "Skyrim:Drovas Relvi")'s sleep package will always be active, and therefore he will be found sleeping indefinitely in [Tel Mithryn Steward's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Steward's House").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- If Reluctant Steward is finished and the Sun Stone has been cleansed, Drovas Relvi's sleep package may still have him trying to sleep at the [Retching Netch](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Retching_Netch "Skyrim:The Retching Netch"), even though he won't work there anymore.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.3, fixes this bug.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage DLC2MQ03B stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest DLC2MQ03B`.
