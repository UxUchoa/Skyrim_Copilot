Barknar

**Barknar** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") camped out in [Dayspring Canyon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dayspring_Canyon "Skyrim:Dayspring Canyon"). He came to [Fort Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dawnguard "Skyrim:Fort Dawnguard") with his wife [Alvide](/wiki/Skyrim:Alvide "Skyrim:Alvide") for protection from the [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") but the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)") won't let him in.

He will show up in Dayspring Canyon during the quest [Beyond Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Beyond_Death "Skyrim:Beyond Death") and will do very little but wander around, at times chopping some firewood or using the [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"). He never sleeps. He wears [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes") with a [cowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Cowl#00017696 "Skyrim:Cowl") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots").

## Dialogue

He will explain his situation, saying things like:

:   *"I've no love for living out of a tent, but I'll take that over being ripped apart."*
:   *"Why can't they just let us in? We won't get in the way!"*
:   *"It looks like this is as close as we're going to get being safe from the vampires."*
:   *"I hope Alvide doesn't think less of me, but I can't protect her on my own. We're safer here."*

## Notes

- Barknar does not have a unique facial model, he instead uses the default male Nord face. He shares this quirk with numerous other NPCs, including [Erikur](/wiki/Skyrim:Erikur "Skyrim:Erikur"), [Sond](/wiki/Skyrim:Sond_(bandit) "Skyrim:Sond (bandit)"), [Lynoit](/wiki/Skyrim:Lynoit "Skyrim:Lynoit"), [Briehl](/wiki/Skyrim:Briehl "Skyrim:Briehl"), and many other generic NPCs.
- Strangely, Barknar lacks any kind of schedule. He will simply idle where he was put.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dawnguard Patch"), version 1.2.3, addresses this issue. He has been given a default sandbox package.
