## Quick Walkthrough

1. You filled the Cup of Fortune.
2. Find a bed and sleep.
3. Read the note placed in your inventory while you slept.
4. choose the side and the way you do the job

### The Cup of Fortune

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-quest-Fortunate_Son.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Fortunate_Son.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Fortunate_Son.jpg)

Go to sleep

After filling the cup labeled "Fortune", you will need to find a bed and sleep. There is a convenient bedroll nearby with a quest marker. Go to sleep and after awakening, you will receive a letter [Rogue's Job Offer](/wiki/Skyrim:Rogue%27s_Job_Offer "Skyrim:Rogue's Job Offer") and a Spiked Vial in your inventory. Read the letter, you will learn of a High Elf named [Cuinanthil](/wiki/Skyrim:Cuinanthil "Skyrim:Cuinanthil") who is a rich mark begging to be robbed. Cuinanthil has just arrived in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") with his ever-present Dark Elf bodyguard [Inimoro](/wiki/Skyrim:Inimoro "Skyrim:Inimoro"). The rogue lets you know the treasure is being hidden somewhere in the Reach and lays out a plan to get to it. You find out the bodyguard has a weakness for Sujamma, a drink from his homeland. At this point two optional different objects appear, you can finish the quest in two ways.

## Notes

- The Spiked Vial uses the same model as the [Pest Poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Pest_Poison "Skyrim:Pest Poison") from the quest [Dampened Spirits](/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits "Skyrim:Dampened Spirits").
- There are two variants of the "Rogue's Instructions" found on the dead courier in the Warrens. Each variant is gender dependent.
- The copy of the Unique "Master Transmute" spell tome is found within the large chest in the hidden vault.

## Bugs

- If you side with Ernanthil and assist the guards in killing Inimoro, you may receive a [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:WIKill04RewardLetter "Skyrim:WIKill04RewardLetter") from Cuinanthil thanking you for killing Inimoro. You will get a [miscellaneous objective](/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests "Skyrim:Miscellaneous Quests") to speak to Cuinanthil for a reward, which is impossible to complete as he will be hostile towards you while still alive. **?**
  - Using the console commands `setav aggression 0` and `stopcombat` on Cuinanthil will allow you to speak with him.
