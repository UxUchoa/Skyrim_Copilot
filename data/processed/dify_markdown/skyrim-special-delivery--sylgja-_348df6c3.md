## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Sylgja](/wiki/Skyrim:Sylgja "Skyrim:Sylgja") in [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone").
2. Travel to [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing").
3. Talk to her father, [Verner](/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_Rock-Chucker "Skyrim:Verner Rock-Chucker").
4. Return to Sylgja to receive your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-quest-Special_Delivery_%28Sylgja%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Special_Delivery_%28Sylgja%29_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Special_Delivery_(Sylgja)_02.jpg)

Sylgja's parents work in the mine.

### Shor's Stone

In [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone") you will come across [Sylgja](/wiki/Skyrim:Sylgja "Skyrim:Sylgja"), who tells you that her parents live in [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing") and asks if you will deliver a satchel of letters to her father there. She would take them herself, but she recently suffered a leg injury in [the local mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Redbelly_Mine "Skyrim:Redbelly Mine"). Agree to deliver the letters for her, and she gives you [her satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Sylgja%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Sylgja's Satchel").

### Darkwater Crossing

Travel to Darkwater Crossing and find Sylgja's father, [Verner Rock-Chucker](/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_Rock-Chucker "Skyrim:Verner Rock-Chucker"), who will usually be hard at work in [Goldenrock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenrock_Mine "Skyrim:Goldenrock Mine") or resting in his house nearby. Verner is pleased with the letters and asks you to deliver a [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Verner%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Verner's Satchel") in return, and suggests that Sylgia will be happy to compensate you for your efforts.

### Back to Shor's Stone

Return to Shor's Stone and give Sylgja the satchel from her father. Thanking you for your efforts, she rewards you with a random piece of jewelry.

## Notes

- After completing this quest you will be able to marry Sylgja.
