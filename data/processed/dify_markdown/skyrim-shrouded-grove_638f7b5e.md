Shrouded Grove

**Shrouded Grove** is a grove with a small cave located in [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale").

It is located due south of [Silverdrift Lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Silverdrift_Lair "Skyrim:Silverdrift Lair") and west of [Nightgate Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightgate_Inn "Skyrim:Nightgate Inn"), and is inhabited by leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") and [spriggans](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan").

### Shrouded Grove

The grove's only identifying features are a large stone statue head and a few stone columns. There is a leveled [spriggan](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan") camouflaged against a tree trunk to the left of the cave's entrance as well as a leveled [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") in the grove nearby. There is also a [frost troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll") in the vicinity that may attack the animal.

### Shrouded Grove Cave

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-interior-Shrouded_Grove_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Shrouded_Grove_Cave.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shrouded_Grove_Cave.jpg)

Interior of Shrouded Grove

The entrance leads into a short, ice-covered tunnel that leads into the cave itself. Upon entering, you will see two animals attacking each other; the winner will generally be quite injured by the encounter, making it an easy target. Camouflaged against a tree trunk to the left is another leveled [spriggan](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan"). Once the enemies are dealt with, you are free to explore the rest of the cave. There is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") against the east wall, by the back of the cave; to either side of it lie two dead [draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Draugr "Skyrim:Containers"), and an open sarcophagus lies behind it on the left. The sarcophagus contains a third draugr and a copy of the [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Ice and Chitin](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_and_Chitin "Skyrim:Ice and Chitin")*.

## Bugs

- Shrouded Grove isn't marked as a cave, so cave-based radiant quests won't target it.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
