Merryfair Farm

**Merryfair Farm** is a poor, small farm northwest of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), owned and run by [Dravin Llanith](/wiki/Skyrim:Dravin_Llanith "Skyrim:Dravin Llanith") and his wife [Synda Llanith](/wiki/Skyrim:Synda_Llanith "Skyrim:Synda Llanith"). The farm is located just west of [Riften Stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Stables "Skyrim:Riften Stables"), on the north shore of [Lake Honrich](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Honrich "Skyrim:Lake Honrich") in [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift").

The couple make a poor living growing bushels of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and [gourds](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"). Their [cow](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow "Skyrim:Cow") provides them with a fresh supply of [milk](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug_of_Milk "Skyrim:Jug of Milk") and Dravin can often be seen milling wheat for their [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread").

Speaking to Dravin reveals that there was a recent break-in at the farm. Among the items that were stolen was [Dravin's bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Dravin%27s_Bow "Skyrim:Dravin's Bow"). He believes the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") was behind the burglary and that they stashed his bow somewhere in the [Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway") beneath [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). This gives you an opportunity to accept retrieving it as a [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow_to_the_Master "Skyrim:Bow to the Master"). Once you complete the quest you will be able to freely take most of the items from the farm.

### Merryfair Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm.jpg)

Main room

The front door is normally novice-locked. Upon entering there is a wall shelf immediately on your left holding a pile of clutter and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). There is a doorway to the right of the shelf in the west wall. There is a crate with a couple of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks against the north wall with a bottle of wine on the crate, next to a chair that is pulled up close to the fireplace. The fireplace is in the east wall and has a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front of it. To the right is a set of shelves holding a few [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and several pieces of cheese. Against the south wall is a small square wooden table and two chairs, with a bottle of wine, a cabbage, a loaf of bread and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") on the table. The doorway to the west leads to the top of stairs leading down to the cellar; there are barrels, sacks and a crate at the top of the stairs.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm_02.jpg)

Bedroom

Descending the stairs to the south brings you to a storage area with a pile of firewood and a couple of straw bales under the stairs and several crates and barrels along the north wall and in the northeastern corner. To the east is a doorway that leads into a large bedroom with a king-sized bed with an end table to the right of it and a crate in the northeastern corner. Against the east wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and a chair in the southeastern corner. There is a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") under the left side of the bed and a dresser against the west wall with copies of *[A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun "Skyrim:A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun")*, *[Wabbajack](/wiki/Skyrim:Wabbajack "Skyrim:Wabbajack")*, *[Olaf and the Dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon "Skyrim:Olaf and the Dragon")*, *[The Woodcutter's Wife](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Woodcutter%27s_Wife "Skyrim:The Woodcutter's Wife")* and *[The City of Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_City_of_Stone "Skyrim:The City of Stone")*.
