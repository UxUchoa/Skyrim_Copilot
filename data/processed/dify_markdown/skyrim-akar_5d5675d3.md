**Akar** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [werewolf](/wiki/Skyrim:EncClassWerewolf "Skyrim:EncClassWerewolf") living under [Frostmoon Crag](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostmoon_Crag "Skyrim:Frostmoon Crag"). He is a member of the Frostmoon Pack and brother to [Majni](/wiki/Skyrim:Majni "Skyrim:Majni"), the pack leader.

Akar wears [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor") with matching [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Bracers "Skyrim:Fur Bracers") and [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes"). He wields a leveled [weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons") in combat which can be up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") in quality.

## Dialogue

When you approach him, he can greet you with:

:   *"Grr..."*
:   *"What?"*
:   *"Hmph."*

If spoken to directly:

:   **Who are you?**
:   *"Akar. Majni's brother. I hunt. He talks. Go talk to him."*
:   **You hunt? What do you hunt?**
:   *"[Deer](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer "Skyrim:Deer"). [Sabers](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Sabre Cat"). [Rieks](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling "Skyrim:Riekling"). [Boar](/wiki/Skyrim:Bristleback "Skyrim:Bristleback"). [Bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear"). I hunt soon. Go bother Majni."*
