Embry in Riverwood

**Embry** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [lumberjack](/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack "Skyrim:Lumberjack") and the local drunk of [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood").

He can always be found in and around the [Sleeping Giant Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Sleeping_Giant_Inn "Skyrim:Sleeping Giant Inn") where he is allowed to drink all night long. He starts his day at 7am, taking a one-hour breakfast in the taproom, then heads outside on the porch where he spends the next nine hours loitering around, either sitting on the bench or leaning against the railing. At 5pm he heads back inside and spends the entire evening (and night, due to a [bug](#Bugs)) drinking and dancing.

Embry wears a set of lower-class [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes"), a pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"), and a pair of [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"). He carries a selection of [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"), and a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Sleeping_Giant_Inn "Skyrim:Key to Sleeping Giant Inn") to the inn. He wields an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger").

## Dialogue

When approached, he will often respond with a drunken:

:   *"You're kinda fuzzy. What's wrong with ya?"*
:   *"Outta my way, stranger."*

## Conversations

When he spends time on the porch, he will often try to persuade one of the local guards to drink with him:

**Embry**: *"My favorite drinking buddy! Let's get some mead."*
**Whiterun Guard**: *"Not now. If I don't pull night duty, I'll drink a round with you at the Sleeping Giant."*
**Embry**: *"But I'm thirsty now."*
**Whiterun Guard**: *"You're always thirsty. Just like you're always drunk. Now let me get back to my rounds."*

Also, if he meets [Sigrid](/wiki/Skyrim:Sigrid "Skyrim:Sigrid") he will make a poor attempt at an invitation:

**Embry**: *"Care to have a drink with me, sweet Sigrid?"*
**Sigrid**: *"Drunk again, Embry? Don't let [Alvor](/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor "Skyrim:Alvor") hear you talking to me that way. He'll stick your head in his forge."*
**Embry**: *"So that's a no I take it?"*

Sometimes, he may ask for some ale from Orgnar:

**Embry**: *"I need some more ale, Orgnar."*
**Orgnar**: *"You've had more than enough. Besides, you still haven't paid for the last one."*
**Embry**: *"I'll find some coin for you. I always do."*
**Orgnar**: *"Until then, I've no ale for you."*

Sometimes, he may ask for a drink from Orgnar using one of the following lines:

**Embry**:

- *"Gimme a mug of ale."*
- *"A pint of your finest mead."*
- *"Some wine, please?"*
- *"I'll have some mead."*
- *"Pour me some ale."*

Orgnar will then reply using one of the two potential lines:

- *"Coming right up."*
- *"Certainly."*

He may also approach [Hod](/wiki/Skyrim:Hod "Skyrim:Hod") to ask him for a drink:

**Embry**: *"How's about a drink, Hod."*
**Hod**: *"You'll be wantin' me to pay for them?"*
**Embry**: *"It's only right seein' as your*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *the important lumber man."*
**Hod**: *"You come do an honest day's work at my mill, and I'll pay you an honest day's wages. Then we'll drink."*
