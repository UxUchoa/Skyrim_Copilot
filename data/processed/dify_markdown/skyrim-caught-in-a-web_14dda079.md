Caught in a Web

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read [Adonato Leotelli's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Adonato_Leotelli%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Adonato Leotelli's Journal") in [Candlehearth Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlehearth_Hall "Skyrim:Candlehearth Hall").
2. Kill the [Web Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Web_Mother "Skyrim:Web Mother") in [Cronvangr Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Cronvangr_Cave "Skyrim:Cronvangr Cave").
3. Loot the bodies of [Grenwulf](/wiki/Skyrim:Grenwulf_the_Brawler "Skyrim:Grenwulf the Brawler") and [Holrik](/wiki/Skyrim:Holrik_Frost-Sword "Skyrim:Holrik Frost-Sword").

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-quest-Caught_in_a_web_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Caught_in_a_web_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Caught_in_a_web_02.jpg)

Success

To start this quest you need to read [Adonato Leotelli's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Adonato_Leotelli%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Adonato Leotelli's Journal") which can be found on a table on the upper floor of [Candlehearth Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlehearth_Hall "Skyrim:Candlehearth Hall"). You will then need to travel to [Cronvangr Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Cronvangr_Cave "Skyrim:Cronvangr Cave").

At the end of the lair, the web mother will drop down and attack you alongside some [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider"). The web mother can spit projectile spiders. The spirits of [Holrik Frost-Sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Holrik_Frost-Sword "Skyrim:Holrik Frost-Sword") and [Grenwulf the Brawler](/wiki/Skyrim:Grenwulf_the_Brawler "Skyrim:Grenwulf the Brawler") will join the fight, and once the web mother is dead, will walk to their corpses.

Investigate Holrik's body and you will find the [Ice Blade of the Monarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Blade_of_the_Monarch "Skyrim:Ice Blade of the Monarch") and [Holrik's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Holrik%27s_Note "Skyrim:Holrik's Note"). Investigate Grenwulf's body and you will find the [Fists of Randagulf](/wiki/Skyrim:Fists_of_Randagulf "Skyrim:Fists of Randagulf") and [Grenwulf's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Grenwulf%27s_Note "Skyrim:Grenwulf's Note").

## Notes

- At the bottom of the first large chamber within Cronvangr Cave there is a button that opens to a vampire lair. This is an optional quest overlap location. This is the lair location of one of the "Pets of Skyrim". Behind the gory shack on the upper ledge and in a cage is the Tamable Frostbite spider named "[Arachnia](/wiki/Skyrim:Arachnia "Skyrim:Arachnia")". It can be picked up now or later on following its associated quest.

## Bugs

- Sometimes the quest won't start even after reading Adonato Leotelli's Journal. **?**
  - Manually triggering the quest by using console command `setstage ccBGSSSE069_quest 20` will solve this issue.
- After killing the Web Mother, Grenwulf's ghost may not lead you to his corpse after Holrik has done the same.
  - Exiting the Broodlair into the main cave and returning will remedy this.
  - You can also use `resurrect 0` in the console.
- Grenwulf and Holrik can be given follower commands by holding the action key. Doing that, then waiting for them to disappear and then giving a command to either one will instantly crash the game. **?**
