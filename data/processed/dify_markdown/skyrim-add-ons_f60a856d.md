# Skyrim:Add-ons

## Overview

This is a list of the **official add-ons**, downloadable content created or officially endorsed by [Bethesda Softworks](/wiki/General:Bethesda "General:Bethesda") for *[Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim")*. Five add-ons have been released for *Skyrim*, including two free add-ons exclusive to the PC version of the game. The [Creation Club](#Creation_Club) provides additional official content created by both Bethesda and external developers for the game's [Special Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition "Skyrim:Special Edition"). The [Nintendo Switch](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch "Skyrim:Switch") version of the game features exclusive content that is unlocked in-game through the use of [amiibo](#Amiibo).

*[Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard")*, *[Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")*, and *[Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")* are included as part of the [Legendary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Legendary_Edition "Skyrim:Legendary Edition") and the Special Edition. Neither of the free add-ons, [Fall of the Space Core, Vol 1](/wiki/Skyrim:Fall_of_the_Space_Core,_Vol_1 "Skyrim:Fall of the Space Core, Vol 1") and the [High Resolution Texture Pack](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Resolution_Texture_Pack "Skyrim:High Resolution Texture Pack"), are available for the Special Edition. The latter add-on is made redundant for this release, however, as the Special Edition uses high-resolution textures in place of the original assets. The [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition") includes *Dawnguard*, *Hearthfire*, and *Dragonborn* and all [Creation Club](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") content.

## Creation Club

[![SR-misc-Creation Club Logo.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-misc-Creation_Club_Logo.png/200px-SR-misc-Creation_Club_Logo.png)](/wiki/File:SR-misc-Creation_Club_Logo.png)

**[Creation Club](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club")** was an in-game [microtransactional](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microtransaction "wikipedia:Microtransaction") store for [Skyrim Special Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition "Skyrim:Special Edition"). A [virtual currency](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_goods "wikipedia:Virtual goods") called **Creation Club Credits** was sold within it, which was used to purchase unique add-ons called [Creations](/wiki/Skyrim:Creations "Skyrim:Creations"). Each Creation released through Creation Club is official [Bethesda](/wiki/General:Bethesda "General:Bethesda") content, developed with third-party developers and contracted [mod](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Mods "Skyrim Mod:Mods") authors. As official content, Creation Club content does not disable [achievement](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements") acquisition.

The service entered beta on September 28, 2017 and officially launched on October 4, 2017. It was never available for [Skyrim VR](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim_VR "Skyrim:Skyrim VR").

All 74 Creations are included in the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition"). Special Edition and [Switch](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch "Skyrim:Switch") users also receive free access to four Creations: [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing"), [Rare Curios](/wiki/Skyrim:Rare_Curios "Skyrim:Rare Curios"), [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), and [Saints & Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers"). Several other discounted Creations were offered for free either on a permanent or temporary basis. On Switch, the Anniversary Upgrade with all the Creations is available, but not the Creation Club itself.

As of December 5, 2023, Creation Club has been retired and the Verified Creator Program is intended to be its replacement. While Creation Club Creations are considered official content, [Verified Creator Program](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Verified_Creator_Program "Skyrim Mod:Verified Creator Program") Creations are considered third party [mods](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Mods "Skyrim Mod:Mods").
