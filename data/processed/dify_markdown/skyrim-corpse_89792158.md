Corpse of a female Imperial soldier

This **corpse** of a female [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") soldier is near the entrance of [Nchuand-Zel Armory](/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel_Armory "Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel Armory") in the camp made with a bedroll and a small barrel. She wears a full set of [Imperial Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Armor "Skyrim:Imperial Light Armor"), and was likely a guard to the members of the team from [The Lost Expedition](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition "Skyrim:The Lost Expedition"). Finding the corpse does not update the quest.
