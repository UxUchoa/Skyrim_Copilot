Witchmist Grove

**Witchmist Grove** is a small shack north of [Bonestrewn Crest](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonestrewn_Crest "Skyrim:Bonestrewn Crest") and south of [Kynesgrove](/wiki/Skyrim:Kynesgrove "Skyrim:Kynesgrove").

The easiest way to reach the grove is to travel to Kynesgrove and head due south from there.

## Related Quests

- **[A Night To Remember](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Night_To_Remember "Skyrim:A Night To Remember")**: Find your lost friend, [Sam](/wiki/Skyrim:Sam_Guevenne "Skyrim:Sam Guevenne"), as you recover from a bad hangover.

## Witchmist Grove

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-place-Witchmist_Grove_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Witchmist_Grove_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Witchmist_Grove_02.jpg)

Witchmist shack interior

It is a small grove of trees with a doorless shack surrounded by sharpened stakes in the center. There are other typical hagraven markings around the area. A short distance to the north is a leveled [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals"). Prior to starting the related [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Night_To_Remember "Skyrim:A Night To Remember") a generic [witch](/wiki/Skyrim:Witch "Skyrim:Witch") or [hag](/wiki/Skyrim:Hag "Skyrim:Hag") resides here. Once you initiate the quest it is changed to a [hagraven](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven "Skyrim:Hagraven") named [Moira](/wiki/Skyrim:Moira "Skyrim:Moira"). Inside the shack is a single bed and two barrels; one contains [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and the other foodstuffs. The shack is in total disarray, with numerous human and animal bones, goat and skeever heads and antlers strewn across the floor. The whole shack is covered in blood splatters, although the bed is still usable. There is a copy of the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Response to Bero's Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Response_to_Bero%27s_Speech "Skyrim:Response to Bero's Speech")* on the floor beside the bed, and a total of seven [hagraven feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Feathers "Skyrim:Hagraven Feathers"). Outside and underneath the wooden stairs leading up to the entrance is an unlocked wooden [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot. There are several [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") flowers, [creep clusters](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster") and patches of [jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes") in the vicinity.

## Notes

- According to a [Development Map](/wiki/File:SR-map-Development_Map_02.png "File:SR-map-Development Map 02.png"), this location was at one point located in [The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"), northwest of [Old Hroldan](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Hroldan_Inn "Skyrim:Old Hroldan Inn").
- It seems to respawn automatically when initiating the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Night_To_Remember "Skyrim:A Night To Remember"), even if that is less than the stated 10 day respawn time.
- Some of the hagraven feathers may clip through the floor, ending up on the ground underneath the shack.
- This is a good place to lure [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") from the nearby camps, as you can use the surrounding fence and small doorway to the house for defense, making it difficult for them to attack.
