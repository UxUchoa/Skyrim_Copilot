**Esmond Tyne**, a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:EncClassBanditMelee "Skyrim:EncClassBanditMelee"), was a thief who stole a [formula](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Formula "Skyrim:Bonemold Formula") for improved [bonemold](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold "Skyrim:Bonemold") armor from [Glover Mallory](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory "Skyrim:Glover Mallory"). His corpse can be found near the entrance of [Castle Karstaag Caverns](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Karstaag_Caverns "Skyrim:Castle Karstaag Caverns"), presumably killed by the [rieklings](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling "Skyrim:Riekling") there. He wears a [variant of Thieves Guild armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Variant_Armor "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Variant Armor") with matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Variant_Boots "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Variant Boots") and [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Variant_Gloves "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Variant Gloves"). In addition to the bonemold formula, he also carries an enchanted [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"), some [gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Gems "Skyrim:Gems") and gold.
