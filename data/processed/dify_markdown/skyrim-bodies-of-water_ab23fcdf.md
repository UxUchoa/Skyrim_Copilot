# Skyrim:Bodies of Water

## Overview

This page lists all the named **bodies of water** (lakes, ponds, rivers, and seas) in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").

## Notes

- The [Special Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition "Skyrim:Special Edition") adds water shaders, giving each one a more realistic look.
- If you ride your [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses") into a body of water, it will navigate the water very slowly. However, if you take your horse into the water, dismount once it starts swimming and quickly get back on, you'll still be submerged halfway, but your horse will now be able to sprint across the water.

## Bugs

- Water may appear blurry when loading a saved game after creating a save underwater.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
- The water flow may fail to render properly.
  - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
