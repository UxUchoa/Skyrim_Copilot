## Quick Walkthrough

1. Encounter the [aspiring wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Student "Skyrim:Student").
2. Help him perfect his spell technique.

## Detailed Walkthrough

When entering any of the major cities, you may come across a [wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Student "Skyrim:Student") who claims to want to join the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)"). He will then ask that you teach him a few [spells](/wiki/Skyrim:Spells "Skyrim:Spells"), usually [ward spells](/wiki/Skyrim:Ward#Spells "Skyrim:Ward"):

:   *"I've finally found you! I want to join the College of Winterhold. But I need help with my wards. Can you teach me?"*
:   **Sure, I can teach you.**
:   *"Really? Great! Okay. So, first, you raise your ward and I'll throw a little spell at it. That way I can see the 'right' way to do things."*

He will cast his spell and then ask you to cast yours:

:   *"Get your ward ready."*
:   *"Great! Now I'm going to cast some spells at you. Keep your ward up so I can study it."*

As he casts a spell against your ward, usually Flames:

:   *"I think I see what I was doing wrong... All right. That should do it. I think I'm ready to try the College again. Thanks!"*

He will then reward you with a small number of [scrolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Scrolls "Skyrim:Scrolls").

## Notes

- If you have a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"), tell them to wait a short distance away. If you don't, they will attack the wizard when he attacks you.

## Bugs

- Loading through doors (into your house for example) will result in the Student disappearing from the city when you go back outside. A quest marker will be present, but is not visible on the map, leaving no way to finish or remove the quest from the log.
