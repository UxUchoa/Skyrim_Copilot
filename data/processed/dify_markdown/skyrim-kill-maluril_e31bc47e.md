## Quick Walkthrough

1. Get the contract from [Nazir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazir "Skyrim:Nazir").
2. Kill [Maluril](/wiki/Skyrim:Maluril "Skyrim:Maluril") in [Mzinchaleft](/wiki/Skyrim:Mzinchaleft "Skyrim:Mzinchaleft").
3. Report back to Nazir.

### Get the Contract

When [Nazir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazir "Skyrim:Nazir") hands you the quest, he will not only reveal the target's location to be the ancient Dwarven ruin of [Mzinchaleft](/wiki/Skyrim:Mzinchaleft "Skyrim:Mzinchaleft"), he will also provide a bit of fair warning of [Maluril](/wiki/Skyrim:Maluril "Skyrim:Maluril"): *"He's dangerous. Very dangerous. A rogue Dark Elf mage doing research into Dwarven artifacts should be both respected... and feared. Just not by you."* Leave Nazir and travel to Mzinchaleft, in the tundra southwest of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

### Mzinchaleft

The exterior is guarded by up to six leveled [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") so dispose of them and head inside. The ruin consists of three zones, but your target is in the first zone, so there will be nothing but leveled bandit enemies to face. Make your way through the tunnels and fight the opposition. After the second chamber, you will encounter an intersection and a locked door to the north. The bandit guarding the door holds the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Maluril%27s_Room_Key "Skyrim:Maluril's Room Key"), so do what you have to do to claim it. Unlock the door and enter Maluril's Room where you will encounter a surprisingly peaceful [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") obviously not looking for trouble.

You can now talk to him and choose between three different options, but even if his responses will be hostile, he will not attack you, nor will he summon any enemies to fight you. You will have no other option but to kill him in cold blood; he uses a [Staff of Firebolts](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Firebolts "Skyrim:Staff of Firebolts").

| Topic | Dialogue | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| *(Remain silent)* | *"Who are you? Why are you here? Leave this place. I have much work to conduct...."* | Ends conversation |
| *"Fascinating race, the Dwemer. Powerful... and extinct."* | *"Yes... quite. But if that power could be harnessed. Controlled. The possibilities would be... But... who exactly are you? And why are you in here? I think it best you leave Mzinchaleft. These ruins can be quite... dangerous."* | Ends conversation |
| *"The only thing you will discover in Mzinchaleft is death."* | *"Death? Oh, there's death to be found here, to be sure. But not my own!"* | Turns hostile |

Before leaving, read through the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill book *[A Dance in Fire, v7](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v7 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v7")*. Additionally, *[Maluril's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Maluril%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Maluril's Journal")* can be found on the table, providing some insight into who ordered his demise.

### Reward

Return to Nazir in the sanctuary and tell him the good news. He will be quite satisfied and will comment: *"I hope the elf didn't give you too much trouble. I once got set on fire by a sorcerer. Nasty business, that. But I digress. Here's your payment."* He then hands you the reward, a leveled amount of gold.

| Levels | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1–9 | 250 |
| 10–19 | 400 |
| 20–29 | 500 |
| 30–39 | 600 |
| 40+ | 750 |

## Notes

- With version 3.0.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), Maluril's journal will no longer be present in Mzinchaleft until this quest has begun.

## Bugs

- Maluril belonging to the Dark Brotherhood faction can cause you to get expelled from the Dark Brotherhood if he gets attacked at the wrong time.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.7, fixes this bug.
