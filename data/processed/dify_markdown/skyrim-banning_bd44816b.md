Banning

**Banning** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [ranger](/wiki/Skyrim:Ranger_(class) "Skyrim:Ranger (class)") who lives with [Cedran](/wiki/Skyrim:Cedran "Skyrim:Cedran") at the [Markarth Stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth_Stables "Skyrim:Markarth Stables") selling war dogs. A [dog](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog "Skyrim:Dog") named [Vigilance](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilance "Skyrim:Vigilance") can be bought from him for 500 gold. You can make a [delivery](/wiki/Skyrim:Delivery "Skyrim:Delivery") to [Voada](/wiki/Skyrim:Voada "Skyrim:Voada") on his behalf. He is also one of the guests of [The Taste of Death](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Taste_of_Death "Skyrim:The Taste of Death").

He wears farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes") with [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") and matching [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"). He carries an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), some [small change](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor_Pocket_Contents "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"), and the [key to the Markarth Stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Markarth_Stables "Skyrim:Key to Markarth Stables"). He wakes up at 6am to have breakfast. At 8am, he heads outside to tend to his dogs for a long twelve hours, skipping dinner due to an oversight (see [bugs](#Bugs)). He can be found inside the stables at any other time, and he goes to bed at midnight. Banning also knows the spell  [Conjure Familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Familiar "Skyrim:Conjure Familiar").

## Dialogue

He'll greet you by advertising his dogs:

:   *"Need a trained war dog? Fiercest companion money can buy."*
:   *"A war dog is good company and good protection."*
:   *"I like the loyalty of a dog. Not as fickle as a wolf."*
:   *"You have to train a good war dog from a pup. Bite your arm clean off otherwise."*

If you have completed The Taste of Death he will greet you with:

:   *"I can smell the blood. Good and sour."*

You can then ask him about the feast:

:   **You were at Namira's feast, weren't you?**
:   *"Better if we don't talk about that. As far as anyone's concerned, we all had dinner at a nice inn along the road, and nothing strange happened."*

If Cedran has passed away, Banning will take over the stables and gain the ability to sell you a horse:

:   *"I'll be handling the horses with Cedran gone. Miss that old man."*

When spoken to directly he can be asked about the city:

:   **What can you tell me about Markarth?**
:   *"Don't fall."*

You can purchase one of his dogs:

:   **I'll take that dog off your hands.**
:   *"Good. Here you are. He'll follow you to [Oblivion](/wiki/Lore:Oblivion "Lore:Oblivion") and back, if it comes to that."*

If you try to buy one when you already have a pet follower, he'll refuse your purchase:

:   *"Sorry. You already have an animal with you."*

Similarly, if you don't have enough gold:

:   *"War dogs cost gold, friend. Come back when you have some."*

### The Taste of Death

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-npc-Banning_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Banning_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Banning_02.jpg)

*"When was your first meal, Banning?"*

Banning is found sitting at the dinner table. If you talk to him, he'll greet you with:

:   *"I remember my first feast. I envy you."*
:   **Wait, I know you.**
:   *"You buy a war dog from me? There's a reason why my hounds are so eager to bite into people."*

If you've delivered his spiced beef before, he'll recognize you as well:

:   *"Ah, yes. The courier. I guess you know my secret ingredient for that spiced beef, now."*

If you've completed the feast successfully, he may subsequently greet you with:

:   *"You were given [Namira's ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Namira "Skyrim:Ring of Namira")? A great honor."*

He will also randomly engage in conversation with hostess [Eola](/wiki/Skyrim:Eola "Skyrim:Eola") and fellow Markarth citizens [Hogni Red-Arm](/wiki/Skyrim:Hogni_Red-Arm "Skyrim:Hogni Red-Arm") and [Lisbet](/wiki/Skyrim:Lisbet "Skyrim:Lisbet"):

**Eola**: *"Not too full on bread and wine, I hope?"*
**Banning**: *"I always leave room for the main course, Eola."*

**Hogni**: *"When was your first meal, Banning?"*
**Banning**: *"Do dogs count?"*
**Hogni**: *"Of course they don't count!"*
**Banning**: *"My friend Marex. We were hunting, and I thought he was an elk."*
**Hogni**: *"That must have been quite a surprise."*
**Banning**: *"Either way, I came back with meat for the table."*

**Lisbet**: *"It's so good to have everyone around the table again."*
**Banning**: *"Eola is a fine host. She really keeps the faithful together."*
**Lisbet**: *"I'm just glad we had this, now. I was almost getting ready to eat my servant and blame it on the Forsworn."*

## Unused Dialogue

According to dialogue found in the game's DialogueViews files, Banning was at one point meant to have a minor role in [The Forsworn Conspiracy](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forsworn_Conspiracy "Skyrim:The Forsworn Conspiracy"), but only if Cedran had passed away. Although the point at which the dialogue would be available is unknown, you would have been able to ask him about a possible Forsworn escape plan.

:   **I need to know if [Madanach](/wiki/Skyrim:Madanach "Skyrim:Madanach") ever came to you about getting horses.**
:   *"Hmmm... I remember Cedran was talking about a man named Madanach once. I was supposed to be scrubbing the horses, but I heard Cedran yelling about how he wasn't going to help any criminals. That's all I know. Honest."*

Also present is an earlier version of his dialogue about Markarth. His line in the final game, which appears as an ominous warning, was evidently taken from this longer, more literal warning:

:   **What can you tell me about Markarth?**
:   *"Don't fall. [Degaine](/wiki/Skyrim:Degaine "Skyrim:Degaine") the beggar once fell off the crags near the [Temple of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Temple of Dibella"). Legs never healed quite right."*

## Notes

- If Banning has met his end, Cedran may greet you with: *"Poor Banning. Twice as much work here in the stables without him."*
