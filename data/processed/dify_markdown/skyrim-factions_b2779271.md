# Skyrim:Factions

## Overview

Almost all [NPCs](/wiki/Skyrim:People "Skyrim:People") and [creatures](/wiki/Skyrim:Creatures "Skyrim:Creatures") in Skyrim belong to one or more **factions** (or **guilds**). These faction memberships control the [combat reaction](#Group_Combat_Reaction) of all NPCs and creatures, both towards you and towards one another. You can be a member of more than one faction at a time.

## Joinable Factions

There are four primary factions that you can join.

- **[Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions")** — A guild of mercenaries, dedicated to combat, based in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). They are similar to the [Fighters Guild](/wiki/Lore:Fighters_Guild "Lore:Fighters Guild") in previous games.
  - *[The Circle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Circle "Skyrim:The Circle")* — The most trusted and accomplished members of the Companions.
- **[College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)")** — A guild dedicated to the study and practice of magic based in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"). They are similar to the [Mages Guild](/wiki/Lore:Mages_Guild "Lore:Mages Guild") of previous games.
- **[Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)")** — A secret guild dedicated to professional thievery, based in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften").
  - *[Nightingales](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingales "Skyrim:Nightingales")* — A cabal of the Thieves Guild's top members that serves [Nocturnal](/wiki/Skyrim:Nocturnal "Skyrim:Nocturnal") and protects her shrines in Skyrim.
- **[Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood")** — A secret guild made up of professional assassins, the same as from previous games.

You may also take part in the [civil war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") by joining one of these two factions.

- **[Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion")** — The military power of the empire, they seek to maintain Imperial control of Skyrim from their base in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude").
- **[Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks")** — The main organization of rebels in Skyrim's civil war, they seek Skyrim's independence from the Empire. They are based in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

You can join these factions, although they only have a short quest chain.

- **[Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College")** — A guild of musicians, vocalists and story tellers based in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). They have a single quest to join and a few side quests for members.
- **[Tribunal Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Tribunal_Temple "Skyrim:Tribunal Temple")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal")** — A [Dark Elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") cult that still worships the old gods of the [Tribunal](/wiki/Lore:Tribunal "Lore:Tribunal"). You can choose to side with them while completing the related quest chain.

Two new factions become available with the [Dawnguard add-on](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard").

- **[Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)")** — An order of [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") hunters.
- **[Volkihar Vampire Clan](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Vampire_Clan "Skyrim:Volkihar Vampire Clan")** — A clan of vampires trying to blot out the sun.

## Noteworthy NPC Factions

- **[Blackblood Marauders](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackblood_Marauder "Skyrim:Blackblood Marauder")** — A group of bandits based out of [Broken Oar Grotto](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Oar_Grotto "Skyrim:Broken Oar Grotto").
- **[Blackwater Brigands](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackwater_Brigands "Skyrim:Blackwater Brigands")** — A gang of wannabe pirates found at [Orphan's Tear](/wiki/Skyrim:Orphan%27s_Tear "Skyrim:Orphan's Tear").[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Man%27s_Dread "Skyrim:Dead Man's Dread")
- **[Blades](/wiki/Skyrim:Blades "Skyrim:Blades")** — An order of warriors that once served the Emperor before being disbanded and nearly exterminated by the Thalmor.
- **[Blood Horkers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood_Horker "Skyrim:Blood Horker")** — A group of pirates based out of [Japhet's Folly](/wiki/Skyrim:Japhet%27s_Folly "Skyrim:Japhet's Folly").
- **[Crimson Dirks](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Dirks "Skyrim:Crimson Dirks")** — A former bandit crew from Cyrodiil.[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club")
- **[Cultists](/wiki/Skyrim:Cultist "Skyrim:Cultist")** — Worshippers of the fallen [Dragon Priest](/wiki/Lore:Dragon_Priest "Lore:Dragon Priest") [Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:Miraak_(person) "Skyrim:Miraak (person)"), also known as the First Dragonborn.
- **[Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn")** — [Bretons](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") native to [The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") who want to overthrow the region's Nord rulers.
- **[Frostmoon Pack](/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf#Frostmoon_Pack "Skyrim:Werewolf")** — A pack of [werewolves](/wiki/Lore:Werewolf "Lore:Werewolf") based at [Frostmoon Crag](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostmoon_Crag "Skyrim:Frostmoon Crag").
- **[Greybeards](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards")** — A monastic order dedicated to studying and preserving the Way of the Voice.
- **[Morag Tong](/wiki/Skyrim:Morag_Tong "Skyrim:Morag Tong")** — A professional guild of [assassins](/wiki/Skyrim:Morag_Tong_Assassin "Skyrim:Morag Tong Assassin") lawfully sanctioned in [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind").
- **[Penitus Oculatus](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus")** — An Imperial espionage organization responsible for protecting the Emperor; it rose to prominence after the disbandment of the Blades.
- **[Pirates](/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate "Skyrim:Pirate")** — Criminals who prowl the shores of [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim").
- **[Psijic Order](/wiki/Skyrim:Psijic_Order "Skyrim:Psijic Order")** — An ancient and mysterious monastic order from the Summerset Isles known to practice an arcane form of magic known as Mysticism.
- **[Silver Hand](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Hand "Skyrim:Silver Hand")** — Werewolf hunters that oppose the Companions.
- **[Skaal](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village_People "Skyrim:Skaal Village People")** — An offshoot tribe of [Nords](/wiki/Lore:Nord "Lore:Nord") who inhabit [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").
- **[Summerset Shadows](/wiki/Skyrim:Summerset_Shadows "Skyrim:Summerset Shadows")** — A group of thieves based in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") that steal valuables from the corpses of the dead.
- **[Synod](/wiki/Skyrim:Synod "Skyrim:Synod")** — One of two competing mage organizations in [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil") which formed when the [Mages Guild](/wiki/Lore:Mages_Guild "Lore:Mages Guild") splintered.
- **[Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor")** — The ruling faction of the [Aldmeri Dominion](/wiki/Lore:Aldmeri_Dominion "Lore:Aldmeri Dominion"), it seeks to establish elven supremacy over the race of men.
- **[Thirsk](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall_People "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall People")** — A group of former Skaal based out of [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall").
- **[Vigil of Stendarr](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Vigil of Stendarr")** — A militant holy order of [Stendarr](/wiki/Lore:Stendarr "Lore:Stendarr") adherents founded after the [Oblivion Crisis](/wiki/Lore:Oblivion_Crisis "Lore:Oblivion Crisis"), its members are dedicated to hunting [Daedra](/wiki/Lore:Daedra "Lore:Daedra").

## Group Combat Reaction

The combat reaction value together with the [aggression](/wiki/Skyrim:Aggression "Skyrim:Aggression") and [assistance](/wiki/Skyrim:Assistance "Skyrim:Assistance") values of an NPC determine how members of a faction behave in combat[*verification needed*]. See the [CK Wiki](https://ck.uesp.net/wiki/Faction) for more.
