Heimskr's House

**Heimskr's House** is a small house in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") owned by the priest [Heimskr](/wiki/Skyrim:Heimskr "Skyrim:Heimskr"). It is located beside the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") tree, between [Jorrvaskr](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorrvaskr "Skyrim:Jorrvaskr") and the [Temple of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Temple of Kynareth"). It consists of one area, *[Heimskr's House](#Heimskr.27s_House)*.

During the [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun") rubble lands in front of the house, making it impossible to access the house for the rest of the game. Heimskr will then be forced to move to a small camp near Jorrvaskr, or be thrown into jail if you sided with the Imperials.

## Heimskr's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-interior-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg)

Inside Heimskr's house

The house is small and bare-bones, although four chairs facing a homemade podium on the northwestern wall suggest that Heimskr doesn't only preach near the Gildergreen outside. Next to the chairs is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"), while a total of five [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") can be found near the front door. To the southeast is Heimskr's bedroom which contains a chest with a few gold coins and a small dinner table dressed with some [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and a [potion of vigorous magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigorous_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Vigorous Magicka"). The owned bed stands along the northeastern wall, while a dresser with [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing") has a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), and a [potent lingering poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Potent_Lingering_Poison "Skyrim:Potent Lingering Poison") on top. There are a total of four human skulls decorating his shelves and floors with no explanation given.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Heimskr%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Heimskr's House") to which is carried by Heimskr.
- There is an [![Empty](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/SR-icon-shadowmark-Empty.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Empty.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Empty") Empty [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (Thieves Guild)") to the right of the entrance door, even though the Thieves Guild [considers](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Burglary_Job "Skyrim:The Burglary Job") his house to be a wealthy house. The house will not be empty if you are sent here on one of [Vex](/wiki/Skyrim:Vex "Skyrim:Vex")'s thievery jobs.

## Bugs

- If given a Thieves Guild radiant mission with Heimskr's house as your target location after the Battle of Whiterun, it will be impossible to complete the objective due to his house being inaccessible.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
  - You may simply return to the quest giver and quit the current job without any penalties.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Another possible fix is to type `coc WhiterunHeimskrsHouse` into the console to get teleported inside Heimskr's House. To get back outside, you can simply use the door of the house.
