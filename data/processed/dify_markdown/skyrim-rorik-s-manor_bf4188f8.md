Rorik's Manor

**Rorik's Manor** is the small home of [Rorik](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorik "Skyrim:Rorik"), the head of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead") and his best friend and companion [Jouane Manette](/wiki/Skyrim:Jouane_Manette "Skyrim:Jouane Manette").

While the name suggests that it would be a grand house, Rorik's Manor is not much bigger than the other farmhouses in Rorikstead, which he owns. It is a single-story L-shaped building located at the northwestern end of the village.

### Exterior

The only items of interest are a campfire with an empty pot hanging over it and an unowned bedroll beside it. There are two [cows](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow "Skyrim:Cow") in an animal pen to the east of the house.

### Rorik's Manor

The fireplace is opposite the entrance and has a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front of it and three loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") on the mantelpiece. The western end of the house is filled with several bales of straw, barrels and a large pile of firewood. To the right of the fireplace, against the north wall is a long wooden table and a kettle containing four [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). On the table are two more potatoes, a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulb, a [chicken's egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and three [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"). Above the table are two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") and two garlic braids. To the right of these is a rack hanging from the ceiling which holds two [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant").

To the right of the table is a flower basket containing four more chicken's eggs. Further along the north wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and three of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). In the northeast corner is a dresser containing a selection of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") and two [goblets](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") on top. Beside this, coming out from the east wall is a single owned bed, where [Jouane Manette](/wiki/Skyrim:Jouane_Manette "Skyrim:Jouane Manette") sleeps. There is an end table beside the bed and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot beside that.

A counter coming out from the east wall to the right of all this with several sacks and a barrel, all containing [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") around it. On the counter are several bottles of wine, alto wine and [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") along with several pieces of cheese. Under the counter is a copy of *[Spirit of the Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:Spirit_of_the_Daedra "Skyrim:Spirit of the Daedra")* and an empty [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem"). There is a chair and [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") behind the counter and a wardrobe further to the right of that. Coming out from the south wall is a large double bed that [Rorik](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorik "Skyrim:Rorik") uses with an end table on either side. On the left-hand end table is a [potion of haggling](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling "Skyrim:Potion of Haggling") and beside it on the floor is a medium coin purse. The right-hand end table has four loose septims on top. To the right of the bed against the west wall are a couple of barrels with more bottles of wine and alto wine on them, while further right is a small round wooden table and two chairs. On this table is a couple of bottles of Nord mead.
