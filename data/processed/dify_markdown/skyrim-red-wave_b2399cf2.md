The **Red Wave** is a pirate [ship](/wiki/Skyrim:Ships "Skyrim:Ships") docked at [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). It contains only one zone, *[Red Wave](#Red_Wave)*.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-place-Red_Wave_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Red_Wave_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Red_Wave_02.jpg)

The Red Wave, with Solitude and the Blue Palace in the background

It is commanded by [Safia](/wiki/Skyrim:Safia "Skyrim:Safia"), a pirate captain targeted in a [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Safia "Skyrim:Kill Safia").

### Exterior

The Red Wave is moored at the Solitude Docks. During the quest [The Dainty Sload](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dainty_Sload "Skyrim:The Dainty Sload"), [Sabine Nytte](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabine_Nytte "Skyrim:Sabine Nytte") can be found on the deck, ready to supply you with illicit substances. With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, some [Fishing Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Supplies "Skyrim:Fishing Supplies") can be found just off the gangplank, while farther down the docks is some [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and a fishing pole on a barrel, with a bucket of harvestable [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") on the dock.

### Red Wave

The interior of the ship is messy, with [empty wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle") all over the place and many [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels around the quarters. Upon entering the ships quarters on the deck level, you will normally find all of the crew except the captain. There is a stack of food barrels in the northeast corner with a basket on top containing a common book. Against the hull to the west is a square wooden table and a couple chairs, with a cupboard behind holding two [potions of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina") and two random [stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Stamina "Skyrim:Stamina") [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"). Against the bulkhead to the south is a second table and chairs.

To the left of the second table is a flight of stairs leading down to the middle deck. At the bottom of the stairs is a cupboard holding a bunch of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"), three bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a single loose [coin](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), a set of [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"), and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"), with a small set of shelves holding four bottles of Nord mead to the left. There is a stack of food barrels farther to the left and a door to the right. Through the door is a bedroom containing a double bed in the southeastern corner, with an empty [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed and a tall wardrobe against the south bulkhead. Against the north bulkhead is a long wooden table and chair.

The corridor follows the hull of the ship to the east, with stacks of barrels of food on both sides. Ahead is a large cupboard holding a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"), an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"), and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), with a copy of the [Sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Legend of Krately House](/wiki/Skyrim:Legend_of_Krately_House "Skyrim:Legend of Krately House")* on top, and a chair beside it against the north bulkhead. At this point, the corridor doglegs to the left.

Around the corner to the west is the dining area, with a long counter against the south bulkhead full of clutter, and a pair of random boots and a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") on the floor to the right. A cupboard against the same wall holds two more potions of minor healing and two random healing potions. Against the hull to the west is a long table with two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), a [pheasant breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and pieces of cheese on top. Hanging above this table are two racks holding a total of five whole salmon, with a further three slightly to the right hanging from another rack over some food barrels. Against the north bulkhead is a long wooden table and two chairs, with two portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a raw rabbit leg, a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), three [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), two loaves of bread, and a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") on top. To the right of this against the east bulkhead is another cupboard.

Continuing down the corridor, the first room on the right contains two owned single beds and a random [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"). There is an end table between the beds with a bottle of wine on top. The last room on the right contains two owned single beds and a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"). At the end of the corridor is a sleeping area with a total of four single owned beds against the walls. A set of shelves against the south bulkhead holds two potions of minor healing and a bottle of wine on top, and in front on the floor is a potion of minor stamina.

The last doorway on the left leads into a room and corridor with a wardrobe and a barrel against the south bulkhead on the immediate left. To the right of the wardrobe is a set of shelves holding two pieces of [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") and four [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), with a stack of food barrels farther to the right with two random potions on top of one of them. On the right as you enter is a long wooden table with a chair in front, with a food barrel to the left and a counter around the corner. Against the hull to the west is a series of sets of shelves. The first holds several mead barrels, a bottle of wine, and two bottles of Nord mead. The second holds fifteen [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot"), a piece of iron ore, four [steel ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot "Skyrim:Steel Ingot"), two [silver ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot"), and a leather strip, with a pair of random boots on the floor to the right. Farther to the right, a smaller set of shelves holds a woodcutter's axe, two bottles of Nord mead, and an empty wine bottle, with a food barrel to the right with a random shield on top. The last set of shelves is filled with mead barrels, two bottles of Nord mead, and an empty wine bottle, with a sword on top. At the far end of the ship are more barrels of food on and under a large shelf at the rear, in front of which is a chair next to a crate with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") on top. Opposite the shelf is a flight of stairs leading down to the cargo deck.

The cargo deck is full of food barrels, a single empty chest, and a master-locked [safe](/wiki/Skyrim:Safe "Skyrim:Safe"), the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Wave_Safe_Key "Skyrim:Red Wave Safe Key") for which is carried by Sabine Nytte. In the safe, among the other loot is a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Balmora_Blue_Note "Skyrim:Balmora Blue Note") from someone named Dyryn. The note mentions a chest under the ship that contains something called [balmora blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Balmora_Blue "Skyrim:Balmora Blue"), which is needed for the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") [reputation quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dainty_Sload "Skyrim:The Dainty Sload") in Solitude. The chest under the ship can be unlocked using another [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabine%27s_Footlocker_Key "Skyrim:Sabine's Footlocker Key") carried by Sabine.

## Notes

- The chest under the ship doesn't respawn.
- [Dorian](/wiki/Skyrim:Dorian "Skyrim:Dorian"), [Eris](/wiki/Skyrim:Eris "Skyrim:Eris"), and [Xander](/wiki/Skyrim:Xander "Skyrim:Xander") all have the same face paint of three stripes near their eye (Xander's may be hard to see, as his is light green on dark skin). Neither Safia nor Sabine Nytte share this trait.
- [Redwave](/wiki/Oblivion:Redwave "Oblivion:Redwave") was also the name of a unique [cutlass](/wiki/Oblivion:Cutlass "Oblivion:Cutlass") in *[Oblivion](/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion "Oblivion:Oblivion")*, a [quest](/wiki/Oblivion:The_Ghost_Ship_of_Anvil "Oblivion:The Ghost Ship of Anvil") reward for recovering a family heirloom from a [ship](/wiki/Oblivion:The_Serpent%27s_Wake "Oblivion:The Serpent's Wake") haunted by the ghosts of a crew murdered by a Dark Brotherhood member. It is unknown if there is any intended connection between the two.
- Crewmembers will not say their usual dialogue to you while inside the ship, and will act as if you are trespassing, but will never turn hostile or call for the guards.[*verification needed — May be a bug - See [talk page](/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Red_Wave#NPCs_at_Location_Say_.22You_Shouldn.27t_Be_Here.22 "Skyrim talk:Red Wave")*]
- Version 1.3.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") allows 3 crew members to visit the [Winking Skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Winking_Skeever "Skyrim:The Winking Skeever"), making them available for brawl targets, and enabling several lines of unique dialogue that otherwise would have been unavailable.
