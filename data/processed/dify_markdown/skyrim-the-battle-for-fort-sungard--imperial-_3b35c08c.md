## Quick Walkthrough

1. Report for duty to [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") at the [Reach Imperial Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Reach_Imperial_Camp "Skyrim:Reach Imperial Camp").
2. Join the Legion soldiers gathering for the attack.
3. Clear the fort of Stormcloak rebels.

### Report for Duty

When speaking to [General Tullius](/wiki/Skyrim:General_Tullius "Skyrim:General Tullius") he will make a bitter comment about your decision to give up [The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending"): *"Hurry over to our military camp tucked away in the Reach. Ulfric's been enjoying those silver mines for far too long. And, soldier. You better help win me back Markarth, or die trying. It was your brilliant strategy to give it over to the rebels in the first place."* [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") can now be found at the [Reach Imperial Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Reach_Imperial_Camp "Skyrim:Reach Imperial Camp"), northeast of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). She will give you orders to rendezvous with a group of Imperial soldiers preparing to attack [Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard "Skyrim:Fort Sungard") and to assist in the assault.

### Join the Soldiers Preparing the Assault

Fort Sungard is located far east of Markarth, and the soldiers are waiting just northeast of the fort. As you approach the group, they will draw their weapons and take off on a running pace towards the fort entrance.

### Take the Fort

Assist your fellow soldiers in clearing the fort of Stormcloak rebels while the game updates you with the percentage of enemies remaining. The battle takes place in the exterior parts of the fort only, in the courtyard and on the walls.

Once you have killed all Stormcloak rebels, the quest will be completed. The [Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim") quest will update and prompt you to return to General Tullius to report your success and receive your reward.

## Achievements

One [achievement](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements") is unlocked at the end of this quest:

- [![SR-achievement-War Hero.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-achievement-War_Hero.png/30px-SR-achievement-War_Hero.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-War_Hero.png) **[War Hero](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Hero "Skyrim:War Hero")** (10 points/Bronze) — Capture [Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard "Skyrim:Fort Sungard") or [Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall "Skyrim:Fort Greenwall")

If you did not give [The Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift") to the Imperials during Season Unending, you will already have gotten this achievement from [The Battle for Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Greenwall (Imperial)").

## Notes

- Only if [The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") is given to the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") this quest will be given to you.
- Completing this quest will exile [Thongvor Silver-Blood](/wiki/Skyrim:Thongvor_Silver-Blood "Skyrim:Thongvor Silver-Blood") and his courtiers to the [Palace of the Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings"), and re-install [Igmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Igmund "Skyrim:Igmund") as [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of The Reach.
- After completion of this quest, like any Civil War quest under [Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim"), unmarked by in-game references, you can approach Legate Rikke for a [medium leveled gold reward](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Gold_Rewards "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"); this is not always recommended, as entering her cell sets up the next quest in the series and, if triggered improperly, causes the missing *"Reporting for duty."* dialogue option (see [this](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim#Bugs "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim") bug section). Return to Legate Rikke to receive the leveled gold reward only when you want to start the next quest in the series and skip the reward when the game doesn't prompt you to return to her. Don't approach Rikke between quests of different holds.
