# Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall People

## Overview

Residents are only present at this location if you complete the quest [Retaking Thirsk](/wiki/Skyrim:Retaking_Thirsk "Skyrim:Retaking Thirsk") in the Nords' favor. Before this point, they will be found at [Bujold's Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Bujold%27s_Retreat "Skyrim:Bujold's Retreat") or elsewhere as noted.
