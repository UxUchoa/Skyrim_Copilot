## Quick Walkthrough

1. Find [Selveni Nethri](/wiki/Skyrim:Selveni_Nethri "Skyrim:Selveni Nethri") trapped in webs in [Southfringe Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum "Skyrim:Southfringe Sanctum") and cut her down.
2. Help her escape the cave.

### Caught in a Web

When exploring [Southfringe Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum "Skyrim:Southfringe Sanctum") you will find a [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatMageConjurer "Skyrim:CombatMageConjurer") named [Selveni Nethri](/wiki/Skyrim:Selveni_Nethri "Skyrim:Selveni Nethri") trapped in a spider's web. Obviously she is frantic to escape and pleads with you to cut her down.

### A Cursed Necromancer

Apparently Selveni's dealings as a necromancer resulted in her daughter being taken from her and sent to the [orphanage](/wiki/Skyrim:Honorhall_Orphanage "Skyrim:Honorhall Orphanage") in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). Selveni had gone to Riften to see if she could get her daughter out, but learned her daughter had already been moved. When she returned to the coven, [Bashnag](/wiki/Skyrim:Bashnag "Skyrim:Bashnag") accused her of trying to sell him out to the [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor"). She was cursed and chased off. Because of the curse she has been weakened and can't cast a spell to save her life. She isn't able to escape the Sanctum until the way out is safe, and she is desperate to locate her missing daughter.

### Clear the Way

You have probably already faced the many resident [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider") on your way in. While it isn't necessary to kill Bashnag and all his fellow [spellswords](/wiki/Skyrim:Spellsword_(NPC) "Skyrim:Spellsword (NPC)") to ensure her safety, the quest will fail if Selveni dies before escaping. Let her know when the way out is clear. She will thank you and move along the path in the spider area to get out of the cave by herself. When she has exited the cave, the quest will be completed. There is no follow-up to this quest, as Selveni's daughter does not appear anywhere in Skyrim and Selveni herself disappears for good once she is out of your sight.

## Notes

- There are numerous internal references that show many changes to this quest. Originally, it took place in [Boulderfall Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Boulderfall_Cave "Skyrim:Boulderfall Cave") (which is also referred to as Boulderfall *Pass*), and Selveni was to be a [werewolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf "Skyrim:Werewolf"), rather than a necromancer.
  - An unused message internally known as `dunSouthfringeQuestObjective` also refers to this, it reads: *"Help Selvani*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *Nethri escape Boulderfall Pass."* The name of this particular message "**Pack Animals**", might've originally been the name of the quest (as it isn't actually named in the final game's files), and seemingly referred to Selveni's lycanthropy.

## Bugs

- She may refuse to move after being cut down.
  - Try talking to her until she moves. This might continue past any meaningful conversation, but she should eventually move.
- In many cases, Selveni can get stuck near the entrance. Some things that can be tried to unstick her are:
  - Talk to her.
  - Use your equipment, spell, or shout to get her unstuck, such as [Unrelenting Force](/wiki/Skyrim:Unrelenting_Force "Skyrim:Unrelenting Force"), a [Paralysis](/wiki/Skyrim:Paralyze_(effect) "Skyrim:Paralyze (effect)") spell, or blocks.
  - Use the wait feature for one hour.
  - Leaving and reentering the location may also work. In that case, she will disappear from the cave, and the journal will update the quest as completed. You may be forced to go back to [Southfringe Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum "Skyrim:Southfringe Sanctum") after a long time roaming other places to solve this quest with this method, though.
  - Hitting her with an arrow may also help push her towards the entrance. However, do not use a powerful bow and arrow combination or you will risk killing her.
- Selveni may even escape but the quest journal indicates that it never completed.
- This quest is set up as a Miscellaneous quest but increments the Side Quests achievement when it isn't supposed to.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.
