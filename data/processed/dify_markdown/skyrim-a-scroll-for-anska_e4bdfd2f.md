## Quick Walkthrough

1. Meet and talk to [Anska](/wiki/Skyrim:Anska "Skyrim:Anska") in [High Gate Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Gate_Ruins "Skyrim:High Gate Ruins") and agree to help her.
2. Protect Anska as you fight through the ruins.
3. Defeat [Vokun](/wiki/Skyrim:Vokun_(creature) "Skyrim:Vokun (creature)").
4. Retrieve Anska's [scroll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sealed_Scroll "Skyrim:Sealed Scroll").

### Anska

[High Gate Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Gate_Ruins "Skyrim:High Gate Ruins") is a remote [Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Ruins "Skyrim:Nordic Ruins") on the snowy tundra east of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), near the shores of the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts"). When you enter, it soon becomes clear that someone visited the ruin recently, as the residing [draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr") have already been killed. Proceed into the first room and you will meet the person responsible, a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard "Skyrim:Bandit Wizard") named [Anska](/wiki/Skyrim:Anska "Skyrim:Anska"). When approached she will introduce herself and ask if you are there to help her as she has finally located [Vokun's](/wiki/Skyrim:Vokun_(creature) "Skyrim:Vokun (creature)") Crypt. She then explains her goal, namely to locate a special scroll that could link her family blood all the way back to the legendary [Ysgramor](/wiki/Lore:Ysgramor "Lore:Ysgramor"). She will also briefly describe Vokun as *"Some old priest buried here long ago."*, hinting at her lack of research and explaining her need for help. She fears the old priest could awaken and prove too powerful a foe, so she wants you to lead her through the ruin and help her fight the opposition. Accept her request and the quest will start.

### High Gate Ruins

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-quest-A_Scroll_For_Anska_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-A_Scroll_For_Anska_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_Scroll_For_Anska_03.jpg)

*"Sometimes I just like to watch them burn."*

Anska is not your average [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") and you will have to escort her safely through the ruin; she is, however, capable of taking care of herself using a variety of leveled wizard spells, so keep an eye out and stay upfront as you proceed into the dungeon. Take the northern tunnel to a huge chamber infested with leveled draugr. Claim some minor loot from the chest in the alcove, then head upstairs and locate the iron door leading north where three more draugr will attack. Go through the room with the [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and a few useful potions. The next tunnel contains a [pressure plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Pressure_Plate "Skyrim:Pressure Plate") triggering a [spike wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Spike_Wall "Skyrim:Spike Wall"), so make sure Anska gets through unharmed. A lever puzzle awaits in the next room, so take a thorough look at the [symbols](/wiki/Skyrim:Puzzle_Totems#Puzzle_Doors "Skyrim:Puzzle Totems") above the southern ledge. From left to right, they are: **Eagle—Whale—Fox—Snake**. Look around the room for the levers, all of them on pedestals with corresponding symbols. The two first levers are upstairs while the last two are on the ground floor. When all four levers are pulled in the right order, the grate in the middle of the room will disappear, so head downstairs and enter the [catacombs](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Gate_Ruins_Catacombs "Skyrim:High Gate Ruins Catacombs") through the door.

Only one draugr will attack you in the first section of the catacombs and a slide will lead you down to another catacomb area with more draugr. A chest can be found in an alcove, but watch out for the pressure plate and the corresponding [darts](/wiki/Skyrim:Dart_Trap "Skyrim:Dart Trap"). Before long, you will reach a room with a leveled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") resting on a [weight sensitive plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Weight_Sensitive_Plate "Skyrim:Weight Sensitive Plate"), which releases two draugr from the adjacent coffins if triggered. The locked door to the right contains a chest behind it. Continue into a hallway filled with ancient carvings and numerous pressure plates on the floor, triggering darts or [spear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Spear_Trap "Skyrim:Spear Trap"), then proceed into another puzzle room. Facing the lever, two stone walls cover hidden doors, so pull the lever and watch the wall to the right disappear, then fight the boss-leveled draugr guarding another lever. Pull that lever and the door to the left will open and release yet another boss-leveled draugr. Pull the lever behind him, then head through the now-opened entrance to the east and into [Vokun's throne room](/wiki/Skyrim:Vokun%27s_Throne_Room "Skyrim:Vokun's Throne Room").

### Vokun

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/SR-quest-A_Scroll_For_Anska.jpg/200px-SR-quest-A_Scroll_For_Anska.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_Scroll_For_Anska.jpg)

Anska vs. [Vokun](/wiki/Skyrim:Vokun_(creature) "Skyrim:Vokun (creature)")

Before the throne room, a hallway offers a few potions. As soon as you open the next door, the [dragon priest](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Priest "Skyrim:Dragon Priest") Vokun will burst out of his sarcophagus and use his [staff of fireballs](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Fireballs "Skyrim:Staff of Fireballs") to attack you from a distance. Anska will keep her distance from Vokun, but will use her ranged attacks to help out. Defeat the priest and claim his unique [mask](/wiki/Skyrim:Vokun_(item) "Skyrim:Vokun (item)") and his staff from the remains. Proceed to the chamber to the east, where you will find a [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") with a word for the [Storm Call](/wiki/Skyrim:Storm_Call "Skyrim:Storm Call") [Shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts"). The nearby table holds the [sealed scroll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sealed_Scroll "Skyrim:Sealed Scroll") for Anska, so pick it up and hand it to her. She will be overwhelmed with joy and hand you the unique spell tome for learning the spell [Conjure Flaming Familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Conjure_Flaming_Familiar "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Conjure Flaming Familiar") as a reward. Additionally, a boss-leveled chest can be found on the upper level to the north, in an alcove right next to the shortcut back to the first zone of the ruin. Anska will stay behind and try to decipher the scroll indefinitely.

## Notes

- Be careful about bringing other followers with you, since Anska's use of [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") magic can cause them to attack her if she hits them by mistake, or if they hit her.
- This quest only counts towards the [Sideways](/wiki/Skyrim:Sideways "Skyrim:Sideways") [achievement](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements") if Anska survives.
- Should you choose not to hand over the scroll to Anska right away, she'll continue to follow you around, but only within close proximity to the ruins. In other words, she can't be used as a "free" follower. If you withhold the scroll from her and head outside, then later give her the scroll, she will return to the ruins and remain in Vokun's throne room indefinitely.
- It is possible to clear the dungeon without [Anska](/wiki/Skyrim:Anska "Skyrim:Anska")'s help. To do this, avoid talking to her when you enter the dungeon. Once Vokun is dead, she will run up to you and allow for the quest to finish normally.
- Anska is not considered an essential NPC, and can be killed prior to starting the quest. This prevents the start of the quest and the acquisition of the [Flaming Familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Flaming_Familiar "Skyrim:Flaming Familiar") spell.
- Area of effect spells such as [Fire Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Storm "Skyrim:Fire Storm") or [Unrelenting Force](/wiki/Skyrim:Unrelenting_Force "Skyrim:Unrelenting Force") can cause objects, including the scroll, to fly around the room, potentially becoming lost.

## Bugs

- On entering the Catacombs, Anska will comment on the soul gem well before you reach it, and before it is in your line of sight.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- Similarly, she may comment on Vokun's presence and the scroll beyond him ("There it is! On the table!"), before the door to his throne room is even open. **?**
- You may be stuck with the sealed scroll, should you pick it up after failing the quest.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- Upon killing Vokun, Anska may just stand in battle pose, not finishing the quest.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") This can be fixed by opening the console, targeting Anska, and using the `recycleactor` command. She will then vanish, re-enter the room, run toward Vokun's tomb, and you can now speak to her.
- The scroll may be left in your inventory after you complete the quest (you can remove it at that stage though, since it is no longer a quest item).
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- Leaving the reward room without taking the scroll will lead you to be locked out of the room. Even if you return to the throne room, the gates will be locked and cannot be opened anymore since the priest is already dead.
- If Anska is killed, the quest does not fail.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
