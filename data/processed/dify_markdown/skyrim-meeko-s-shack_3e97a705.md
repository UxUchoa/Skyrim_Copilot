Meeko's Shack

**Meeko's Shack** is a run-down [shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") owned by the canine follower [Meeko](/wiki/Skyrim:Meeko "Skyrim:Meeko"), who has inherited the home from his unfortunate [owner](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_(dead)#Nord1 "Skyrim:Nord (dead)").

It is located on the edge of the [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch") marshland between [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") and [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal"), a short distance from the road leading from Morthal and [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge").

## Meeko's Shack

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Nord_%28Meeko%27s_Shack%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nord_%28Meeko%27s_Shack%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nord_(Meeko%27s_Shack).jpg)

Meeko's former owner

Around the shack are several plants and flowers including three [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") plants, a [thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch") and three [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower").

The shack is still fairly structurally sound. Inside there is a small set of shelves to the left of the doorway with a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), three [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), two [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") on top. Beside this, in the northern corner is a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel with a copy of the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Dance in Fire, v6](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v6 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v6")* on top. Against the northeast wall is a set of shelves holding a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and a bowl containing three [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"). To the right of this is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid and a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit "Skyrim:Rabbit"). Against the southeast wall is an unowned single bed with the corpse of [Meeko's](/wiki/Skyrim:Meeko "Skyrim:Meeko") former [owner](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_(dead)#Nord1 "Skyrim:Nord (dead)") laying on top. It can still be used to rest. At the foot of the bed is an end table with a [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Journal_(Meeko%27s_Shack) "Skyrim:Journal (Meeko's Shack)") written by Meeko's former owner. It explains that he had succumbed to [Rockjoint](/wiki/Skyrim:Disease "Skyrim:Disease") and that Meeko "was always a loyal companion" and would "be able to take care of himself".

## Notes

- Meeko runs back to the shack if you find him on the road.
- There is an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") to the northwest.
- A [dragon mound](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Mounds "Skyrim:Dragon Mounds") can be found to the west of the shack.
- While technically owned by Meeko, none of the items inside the shack are owned and will not be marked as stolen should you take them.
- After you have discovered Meeko and his dead master, the body and bed will be disposed of automatically upon the next cell reset.
