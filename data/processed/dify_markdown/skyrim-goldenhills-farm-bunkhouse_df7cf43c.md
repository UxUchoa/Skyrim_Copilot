Goldenhills Farm Bunkhouse

**Goldenhills Farm Bunkhouse** is a small house at [Goldenhills Plantation](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenhills_Plantation "Skyrim:Goldenhills Plantation"), east of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead"). Initially the [farmhands](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmhand "Skyrim:Farmhand") and [steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Personal_Steward "Skyrim:Personal Steward") you hire will set up camp next to the stables, east of the main house. These bedrolls will be replaced by the bunkhouse as soon as you [construct](/wiki/Skyrim:Farming_Construction "Skyrim:Farming Construction") it using the [Farmer's Workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer%27s_Workbench "Skyrim:Farmer's Workbench").

It consists of one area only, *[Goldenhills Farm Bunkhouse](#Goldenhills_Farm_Bunkhouse)*.

## Goldenhills Farm Bunkhouse

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/SR-interior-Goldenhills_Farm_Bunkhouse.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Goldenhills_Farm_Bunkhouse.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Goldenhills_Farm_Bunkhouse.jpg)

Bunkhouse interior

The bunkhouse contains little of any value. A single [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") can be found in front of the fire. [Dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam"), [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear"), [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), and [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") can all be found hanging inside. The two tables hold food and farm equipment respectively. On the opposite side of the house are the three beds used by any individuals you hire to work on the farm.
