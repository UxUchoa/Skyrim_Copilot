Peak's Shade Tower

**Peak's Shade Tower** is a small ruined [Nordic tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Towers "Skyrim:Nordic Towers") east of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") containing a [spriggan](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan").

## Related Quests

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Walkthrough

Following the main road east out of Falkreath toward [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen"), the path leading up to the tower is marked by a cairn on the right side of the road. Three [red mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), one [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"), and a patch of [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") fungus on a large tree stump are found just outside the entrance.

The tower has long eroded, and the only inhabitant is a spriggan. The interior is overgrown with vegetation, including two [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), one purple mountain flower, and two [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle"). It is also a good place to find [blue butterflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly "Skyrim:Blue Butterfly") and [monarch butterflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Monarch_Butterfly "Skyrim:Monarch Butterfly") during the day, and a few [luna moths](/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth "Skyrim:Luna Moth") or [torchbugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug "Skyrim:Torchbug") at night. Hidden among the vegetation along the west wall of the tower is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest").

## Notes

- With *[Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard")* installed, the spriggan may be an Earth Mother, depending on what level you approach the ruin.
