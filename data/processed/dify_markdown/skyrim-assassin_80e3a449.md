A female Khajiit Assassin

**Assassins** of the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") are occasionally sent to track and subsequently make an attempt on your life throughout the game. The assassin's race and gender will vary between an [Argonian](/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian "Skyrim:Argonian") female, a [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") male, or a [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit "Skyrim:Khajiit") female.

The assassins appear as [random encounters](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin_Sent_to_Kill_the_Player "Skyrim:World Interactions") starting at level 5. Unlike [hired thugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Hired_Thug "Skyrim:Hired Thug"), the assassins appear for no reason and there is no way to avoid encountering them. A [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin%27s_Note "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood Assassin's Note") detailing their mission can always be found on their bodies, though it is never revealed who performed the [Black Sacrament](/wiki/Lore:Black_Sacrament "Lore:Black Sacrament") to order the assassination.

After completing either the [With Friends Like These...](/wiki/Skyrim:With_Friends_Like_These... "Skyrim:With Friends Like These...") or the [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](/wiki/Skyrim:Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood! "Skyrim:Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!") quest, you are no longer in danger of being attacked by these assassins.

## Notes

- [Shrouded Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Armor "Skyrim:Shrouded Armor") cannot be collected from slain assassins.
