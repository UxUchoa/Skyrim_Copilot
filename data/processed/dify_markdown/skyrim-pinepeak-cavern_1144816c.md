Pinepeak Cavern

**Pinepeak Cavern** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") north of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") containing [bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear"). It contains only one zone, *[Pinepeak Cavern](#Pinepeak_Cavern)*.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-place-Pinepeak_Cavern_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Pinepeak_Cavern_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Pinepeak_Cavern_02.jpg)

A warm welcome

Following the road north out of Ivarstead, cross the [Treva River](/wiki/Skyrim:Treva_River "Skyrim:Treva River") as if you were going to climb the "[7,000 steps](/wiki/Skyrim:Climb_the_Steps "Skyrim:Climb the Steps")" to High Hrothgar, and turn north at the start of the steps. The cave is on the shore opposite town. A bear guards the entrance. Two dead [hunters](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter") lie at the mouth of the cavern, and in front of the cave by a large rock is a [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root").

### Pinepeak Cavern

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-interior-Pinepeak_Cavern.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Pinepeak_Cavern.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pinepeak_Cavern.jpg)

Cavern interior

A short tunnel soon opens into the main cave, with blood splattered on the floor. A rock formation is in the middle of the chamber, and a ledge starts on your right and curves around the cave. A bear will be sleeping or roaming at the end of the ledge. There is nothing else of interest on the ledge. By the ledge below the bear is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), a leveled potion of healing, a smashed cart, a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), and a kettle containing a [diamond](/wiki/Skyrim:Diamond "Skyrim:Diamond"). To the right are the remains of an unfortunate person; among the bones is an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). Finally, to the right of the remains is a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") and a copy of the [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Heavy Armor Forging](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor_Forging "Skyrim:Heavy Armor Forging")*.

## Bugs

- You may not be able to pick up the diamond. Nudge the kettle to tilt it slightly without dislodging the diamond, allowing you to reach the gem. You can also use the [Unrelenting Force](/wiki/Skyrim:Unrelenting_Force "Skyrim:Unrelenting Force") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") or [Telekinesis](/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis_Effect "Skyrim:Telekinesis Effect") on the kettle, then take the diamond.
