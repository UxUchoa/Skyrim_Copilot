**Arnleif and Sons Trading Company** is a general goods store located by the market stalls next to the entrance, on the north side of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth").

The store is managed by [Lisbet](/wiki/Skyrim:Lisbet "Skyrim:Lisbet"), who inherited it from her husband Gunnar, son of Arnleif.

## Arnleif and Sons Trading Company

The store consists of three rooms: a large main area where the store is located and two smaller rooms with two doors on the north wall. The store has fallen on hard times due to the [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") constantly capturing shipments of goods. The store does not display any of its merchandise. Upon entering there is a short corridor into the main room, with a raised section around the walls of the room and two pillars that support the ceiling, with a counter between them with wings from the pillars towards the back of the store forming a square U-shape. [Lisbet](/wiki/Skyrim:Lisbet "Skyrim:Lisbet") will spend most of the day stood behind the counter while [Imedhnain](/wiki/Skyrim:Imedhnain "Skyrim:Imedhnain") wanders the store and sweeps up.

There is little to nothing of interest on the raised section even with a large number of shelving units. There is a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") on a shelf against the west wall and an archery target in the northwestern corner. There is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel, an expert-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") containing random items, and a [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger") on a stone ledge all between the two doors at the rear of the store, against the north wall. There are [a piece of sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese") and two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") on two different shelves against the east wall. Everything else on the raised section is clutter. There are stairs down to a sunken middle of the room halfway along each of the walls. Descending to the sunken area there are even more shelves against the raised section around the room, with a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") in a wooden bowl on a dresser to the west, a copy of the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Dance in Fire, v7](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v7 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v7")* on a crate to the north, a dresser containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") to the east, and a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") on a shelf to the south. To the west of the counter is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). On the west side of the counter is a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") with a garlic braid hanging above, while on the east side of the counter is a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") with a bunch of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") hanging above. Under the counter is a copy of *[The Code of Malacath](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Code_of_Malacath "Skyrim:The Code of Malacath")*.

The bedroom to the northwest consists of two owned single beds; one to the east and one to the west both having stone ledges as part of the walls. The ledge to the east has an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") on top, while the other ledge has a loaf of bread and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"). There is also a dresser against the north wall. The room to the northeast consists of a single owned bed against the east wall and a huge fireplace to the north with a cast iron pot on the floor beside it holding a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks") and a potato. Against the west wall is a long wooden table with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a portion of grilled leeks, a potato, a loaf of bread and a cabbage in a cast iron pot. To the right of the table is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") on a stone ledge with a copy of *[Dwarves, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarves,_v3 "Skyrim:Dwarves, v3")* leaning against it. The chest contains leveled items. To the left of the table is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand. There are also a few food sacks throughout the building.

## Notes

- Once Lisbet has been befriended by completing [Lisbet's Missing Shipment](/wiki/Skyrim:Lisbet%27s_Missing_Shipment "Skyrim:Lisbet's Missing Shipment"), the shop can be freely looted, right down to openly picking the Strongbox towards the back and taking any gold within.
- The store will stay open for business until you enter for the first time, after which it will keep normal hours.
- The [key to the store](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Arnleif_and_Sons_Trading_Company "Skyrim:Key to Arnleif and Sons Trading Company") is carried by both Lisbet and Imedhnain. If you choose to save Brother Verulus in [The Taste of Death](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Taste_of_Death "Skyrim:The Taste of Death"), the key can be looted off of Lisbet's body without consequence.
- There is a [![Loot](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Loot") Loot [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Shadowmarks "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") to the top left of the entrance door.
- If anything happens to Lisbet, her assistant, Imedhnain, will take over running the store.
- All containers in the area, with the exception of some barrels and sacks, are non-respawning.

## Bugs

- The front door of this store may be locked at all hours of the day.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.8, fixes this bug.
  - Picking the lock and entering the shop will resolve this, and the shop will lock/unlock at the normal times thereafter.
