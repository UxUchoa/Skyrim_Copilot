Acolyte Aldren

**Acolyte Aldren** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [acolyte](/wiki/Skyrim:TrainerRestorationMaster "Skyrim:TrainerRestorationMaster") of [Kynareth](/wiki/Lore:Kynareth "Lore:Kynareth"). She can send you a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_from_the_Temple_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Note from the Temple of Kynareth") during [A Dying Wish](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dying_Wish "Skyrim:A Dying Wish") requesting [Ironwood Soup](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironwood_Soup "Skyrim:Ironwood Soup") in order to heal [Rulnik Wind-Strider](/wiki/Skyrim:Rulnik_Wind-Strider "Skyrim:Rulnik Wind-Strider") from ataxia.
