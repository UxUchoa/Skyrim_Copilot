# Skyrim:Carriage Driver

## Overview

**Carriage Drivers** only appear during the quest [Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity"); one of them acts as your means of transportation from [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") to the [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy"), where the other two drivers will be parked in the courtyard. All three drivers are [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warriors](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") and will disappear from the game upon completion of the quest.

Carriage Driver

This driver appears just outside [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), specifically at [Katla's Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Katla%27s_Farm "Skyrim:Katla's Farm"), and will transport you to the party at the Thalmor Embassy after you have spoken to [Delphine](/wiki/Skyrim:Delphine "Skyrim:Delphine"). Before departure, the driver will say, *"The lady's already paid the fee. We leave when she gives the word."* When parked in the embassy courtyard, he will say, *"I'm paid to wait to take you home. Enjoy yourself."*

He wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothing") with [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261bd "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261c1 "Skyrim:Clothing") and wields an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe").

## Bugs

- According to the game files, the two drivers situated in the embassy courtyard were supposed to share the same two lines of dialogue. Due to a programming error, all dialogue options were attached to the driver near the gate, leaving the other one with nothing but generic greetings.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
