Lake Yorgrim

**Lake Yorgrim** is a lake in the eastern part of [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale"). It is the source of [River Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim "Skyrim:River Yorgrim"). Located on the banks of the lake is [Bronze Water Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Bronze_Water_Cave "Skyrim:Bronze Water Cave"). The [Nightgate Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightgate_Inn "Skyrim:Nightgate Inn") can be found to the northwest. Due east of the lake is a [dragon burial site](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Mounds "Skyrim:Dragon Mounds") belonging to [Viinturuth](/wiki/Skyrim:Viinturuth "Skyrim:Viinturuth").

The [Dwemer ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwemer Ruins") of [Irkgthand](/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand "Skyrim:Irkngthand") run underneath the lake.
