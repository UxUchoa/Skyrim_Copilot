Uttering Hills Cave

**Uttering Hills Cave** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") southwest of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") containing [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). It contains only one zone, *[Uttering Hills Cave](#Uttering_Hills_Cave)*.

## Related Quests

- **[Summerset Shadows](/wiki/Skyrim:Summerset_Shadows "Skyrim:Summerset Shadows")**: Remove a rival guild from the picture.

#### Radiant Quests

- **[Amulet of the Moon](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_the_Moon "Skyrim:Amulet of the Moon")**: Find the [Moon Amulet](/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Amulet "Skyrim:Moon Amulet") for [Kharjo](/wiki/Skyrim:Kharjo "Skyrim:Kharjo").
- **[Dungeon Delving](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Caves) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Caves)")**: Do a favor by retrieving an item from a cave.
- **[Find Shahvee's Amulet of Zenithar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Bandits)")**: Do a favor by retrieving an item from a bandit hideout.
- **[Harsh Master](/wiki/Skyrim:Harsh_Master "Skyrim:Harsh Master")**: Take care of a runaway crew member.
- **[Kill the Bandit Leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Bandit_Leader "Skyrim:Kill the Bandit Leader")**: Do a favor by slaying a bandit leader.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

### Exterior

The cave only be reached from directly north or south. The easiest approach is to travel to [Anga's Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill "Skyrim:Anga's Mill"), cross the river to the south, and climb the path. Outside the cave is a small bandit camp, with two bandits guarding the entrance: one is an archer, and the other is a melee fighter. There are two small tents, both containing unowned bedrolls, and three [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and one food sack around the tents. In front of the tents is an old campfire, with a metal pot containing an [ingredient](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") hanging over it. An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") is among the rocks to the southwest, and a number of [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes are in the vicinity. A wooden door serves as the entrance to the cave.

### Uttering Hills Cave

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/SR-interior-Uttering_Hills_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Uttering_Hills_Cave.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Uttering_Hills_Cave.jpg)

Bandits by the fire

The entrance leads into an icy tunnel that descends to the north, passing a small table with a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison") and a random [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"). The mined area turns into snowy cave tunnels and has a few corners. Just before the tunnel opens into a small chamber, there is a [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk") on the ground beside some mammoth ribs. In the chamber, the path diverges into paths to the left and right, each patrolled by a single leveled bandit. Following either tunnel leads to two leveled bandits warming themselves by a campfire with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") hanging over it. There is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") beside the fire, a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") to the southwest, and a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") to the west.

A wooden door to the north leads to a circular tower room with stairs descending. At the bottom is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and a random [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"). Hanging above the lab is a bunch of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), and two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). A leveled bandit is patrolling between the room and the next set of descending stairs, and another bandit is sitting at the far end of the passage below. To the left of the stairs is an adept-locked chest. At the far end of the passage down the stairs are three small cells that can be opened with pull chains, but these never seem to contain prisoners.

Partway along the passage is a room on the right containing a long wooden table holding a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), some [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and some cheese. Two barrels are in the southeast corner, one containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"), the other foodstuffs. To the left, against the east wall, is a set of shelves holding many more pieces of cheese. There are wooden doors on both the east and west walls. During the related quest, there is a [Summerset Shadow](/wiki/Skyrim:Summerset_Shadow "Skyrim:Summerset Shadow") banner which can be burned hanging from the ceiling by the north wall (see the [Summerset Shadows](/wiki/Skyrim:Summerset_Shadows "Skyrim:Summerset Shadows") quest page for details).

The room to the west contains up to two bandits, one awake, and one sleeping. The room also features four unowned single beds, with two each against the north and south walls. Two sets of shelves against the west wall hold several bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"), and a leveled [invisibility potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Brief_Invisibility "Skyrim:Potion of Brief Invisibility"). The room to the east contains the bandit chief, along with an unowned single bed against the east wall. Opposite the bed is a set of shelves holding six common [books](/wiki/Skyrim:Books "Skyrim:Books"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and a copy of the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mace Etiquette](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace_Etiquette "Skyrim:Mace Etiquette")*. Against the south wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with an unlocked boss chest to the right.

## Notes

- A [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") and a corundum ore vein are east of the cave, beneath a flag near an unmarked [frost troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll")'s lair.

## Bugs

- After the quest Summerset Shadows, members of the Summerset Shadows replace the bandits and follow the bandits' routines. If you have an objective to kill the bandit leader here after the quest, it will be impossible to complete it, as the bandit leader will be the corpse of [Linwe](/wiki/Skyrim:Linwe "Skyrim:Linwe"), who is already dead. Similarly, in order to mark this location as cleared, the bandit chief must be killed prior to starting the quest Summerset Shadows.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The first issue can be fixed by resurrecting Linwe with the console by targeting his corpse and typing `resurrect 1`, then killing him again. See the [quest page](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Bandit_Leader#Bugs "Skyrim:Kill the Bandit Leader") for more details on this and other possible fixes.
