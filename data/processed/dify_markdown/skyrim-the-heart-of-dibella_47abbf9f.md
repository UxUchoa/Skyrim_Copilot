## Quick Walkthrough

1. Travel to the city of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth").
2. *(Optional)* Start the [Speak to Degaine](/wiki/Skyrim:Speak_to_Degaine "Skyrim:Speak to Degaine") quest.
3. Enter the Inner Sanctum of the [Temple of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Temple of Dibella").
4. Help the priestesses of [Dibella](/wiki/Lore:Dibella "Lore:Dibella") find a new Sybil.
5. Travel to [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten") and learn the location of [Fjotra](/wiki/Skyrim:Fjotra "Skyrim:Fjotra").
   - *(Optional)* Escort [Enmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon "Skyrim:Enmon") to [Broken Tower Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt "Skyrim:Broken Tower Redoubt").
6. Rescue Fjotra from the [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn").
7. Escort Fjotra back to the Temple of Dibella.
8. Pray at Dibella's Altar in the Temple of Dibella.

### The Temple of Dibella

The temple located in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") is dedicated to [Dibella](/wiki/Lore:Dibella "Lore:Dibella"), the goddess of love, beauty, and artistry. It is maintained by several priestesses and [Hamal](/wiki/Skyrim:Hamal "Skyrim:Hamal"). Upon entering the temple, you will find the priestess [Senna](/wiki/Skyrim:Senna "Skyrim:Senna"), who will inform you that: *"The sisters are communing with Dibella. They can't be disturbed. You'll have to come back another time."* In order to access the Inner Sanctum, you can either try to pick the expert-lock, or steal the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Dibella_Inner_Sanctum "Skyrim:Key to the Dibella Inner Sanctum") from Senna. Note that if you choose to pick the lock and do not accept the deal from the Priestess, you'll have to do it twice—once to get into the Sanctum, and once to get out.

### Karthwasten

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_05.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_05.jpg)

A distraught [Enmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon "Skyrim:Enmon") working at the smelter

If you choose to help the priestesses find the new Sybil, you are directed to visit [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten") and locate the young girl's father, [Enmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon "Skyrim:Enmon"), who may be found working in the mine or at the smelter. If you speak to any of the local residents, they will all direct you to speak to him as well. If you tell him that you are looking for his daughter, he will retort: *"Damn it, man/woman, are you trying to taunt us?"* Asking him what he meant, he will explain: *"Our daughter, [Fjotra](/wiki/Skyrim:Fjotra "Skyrim:Fjotra"), was taken from here. The [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") descended and... they didn't touch anything else. Just our little girl."* Also asking where they took her, he will continue: *"It was the Forsworn from [Broken Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt "Skyrim:Broken Tower Redoubt"). Never been much trouble before. What's it to you?"* You can now respond to him in two different ways:

If you tell Enmon that you just want his daughter to be safe, he will remark: *"That's... that's good of you. If you were to save her, I would be very grateful. I don't think there's anything I could give you, though..."* However, if you tell Enmon that Fjotra is the new Sybil of Dibella, he will say: *"She... really? That's... I wouldn't have dreamed. Of course, we need her in Markarth, then. I'll come with you."* If you agree that he should travel with you, he will thank you and accompany you to Broken Tower Redoubt. Note that if you take him along with you, he is a non-essential NPC, and can die. You can also tell him that he should stay, and he will reply with urgency: *"You're... probably right. Just hurry, please. Who knows what those things are doing to her."* You are now directed to travel to the Broken Tower Redoubt and rescue Fjotra from the Forsworn holding her captive.

### Touched by the Gods

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_06.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_06.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_06.jpg)

A [Forsworn Briarheart](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Briarheart "Skyrim:Forsworn Briarheart") guarding Fjotra's cell

Making your way through the Broken Tower Redoubt, you can find Fjotra in her cell, guarded by a Forsworn Briarheart. After you have defeated the Briarheart and freed her, she will ask: *"Are you one of them? Please don't hurt me."* When you tell her that you are to bring her to the Temple in Markarth, she will exclaim: *"Then it is true what they said? I am touched by the gods?"* Her suspicions confirmed, Fjotra will then accept her title as the new Sybil of Dibella: *"I've heard stories about the wonders of the great Temple in Markarth. But I never dreamed that I would even get to see it. I am honored to be called for this duty. Please, lead on."*

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_07.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_07.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_07.jpg)

A final farewell

You must now return Fjotra to the Temple to become the new Sybil of Dibella, but you also can take a detour to Karthwasten, so Fjotra can say goodbye to her parents, Enmon and [Mena](/wiki/Skyrim:Mena "Skyrim:Mena"), who will give her a very heart-warming farewell:

**Enmon:** *"Fjotra!"*
**Fjotra:** *"Father!"*
**Enmon:** *"Fjotra, dear. Did they hurt you?"*
**Fjotra:** *"Of course not. My spirit remains strong."*
**Enmon:** *"My little girl. Always strong. Do you know where you have to go?"*
**Fjotra:** *"I'm to go to the temple in Markarth. I... I'll have to leave you and mother."*
**Enmon:** *"It's our honor to have you there. This man/woman is going to take you to Markarth. Are you ready?"*
**Fjotra:** *"Of course."*

Afterwards, Enmon will approach and thank you: *"Thank you for getting her back from the Forsworn. Dibella was smart to have sent you."* You can now return to Hamal in the Temple of Dibella in Markarth to complete the quest.

### A New Sybil

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_08.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_08.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Heart_of_Dibella_08.jpg)

A new Sybil in the Temple of Dibella

Upon arriving back at the temple, Hamal will be grateful for the safe return of Fjotra: *"You... you found her? I can't believe it. Let the girl come with me and we can begin her preparation. I will send word to her family that she is in good hands. You've truly earned the Blessing of Dibella, child. Prostrate yourself before her altar, and she will bestow her gift."* You are now directed to kneel before and then drink the blessed water, which will bestow the [Agent of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Agent_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Agent of Dibella") passive ability. This grants an additional 10% physical damage against enemies of the opposite sex, and the quest will now be marked as complete.

## Notes

- Priestesses Senna, Orla, and Anwen become [marriageable](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") upon completion of this quest.
- If you have previously entered Broken Tower Redoubt and left the door to Fjotra's cell open, it will still be open when you return to rescue her.
- During [The House of Horrors](/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Horrors "Skyrim:The House of Horrors") quest for [Molag Bal](/wiki/Skyrim:Molag_Bal "Skyrim:Molag Bal"), [Logrolf the Willful](/wiki/Skyrim:Logrolf_the_Willful "Skyrim:Logrolf the Willful") may also be held captive within Broken Tower Redoubt. If so, and if both of these quests have been started, Fjotra will be in the same prison cell as Logrolf.
- Once you have completed this quest and received the ability [Agent of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Agent_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Agent of Dibella"), you can take almost everything inside the temple, both upstairs and in the Inner Sanctum (excluding higher value potions, poisons, jewelry, soul gems, and gems).
- Originally, [Speak to Degaine](/wiki/Skyrim:Speak_to_Degaine "Skyrim:Speak to Degaine") was part of this quest. There are several stages in the quest related to it, though they're now unused.

## Bugs

- Being caught inside the Inner Sanctum before stealing the statue may cause the priestesses to turn hostile without triggering any dialogue. **?**
- The quest marker may be displayed incorrectly while at the bottom floor of Broken Tower Redoubt, instructing to go outside while inside and to go inside while outside **?**
- The conclusion scene when returning the Sybil may never play, and after you receive your reward the quest may never actually finish.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- It is possible to tell Hamal you heard there was a valuable statue even if you had already stolen it previously and given it to Degaine.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.0.8, fixes this bug.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage T01 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete.
