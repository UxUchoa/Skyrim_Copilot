Khajiit Bandit

This **Bandit** is a male [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit "Skyrim:Khajiit") found in [Redwater Den](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Den "Skyrim:Redwater Den") in one of the booths set up for enjoying [Redwater Skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma "Skyrim:Redwater Skooma"). He is lying on a bedroll coughing, surrounded by [empty skooma bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Skooma_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Skooma Bottle") and will never move from it. If you attempt to interact with him, you will receive the message "This person is busy." He wears leveled [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Light_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"), leveled [light boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Light_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"), and has a 50% chance of wearing leveled [light bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Light_Armor "Skyrim:Armor").

## Notes

- This NPC's displayed name may be different for different players. He can appear as "Bandit", "Bandit Outlaw", "Bandit Thug", "Bandit Highwayman", "Bandit Plunderer", or "Bandit Marauder".
