Harmugstahl

This **Adventurer** is alive and can be initially encountered in [Harmugstahl](/wiki/Skyrim:Harmugstahl "Skyrim:Harmugstahl") fighting an enchanted [frostbite spider](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider"). When he exits Harmugstahl he disappears from the game.

He wears a set of regular [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor"), while his gauntlets and boots are leveled [heavy armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Armor") of the best possible quality. He wields a randomly determined weapon (up to elven quality at level 22), either a [battle axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_Axe "Skyrim:Battle Axe"), a [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword"), or a [warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer "Skyrim:Warhammer"). Additionally, he carries a small amount of gold.

### Dialogue

After the battle he will sheath his weapon and say:

:   *"That was a close one!"*
:   **What was going on with that spider?**
:   *"I don't know, but this place is full of them. Every one I've encountered has been enchanted or the like. Look, I'm getting out of here. Normally, spiders make my skin crawl, but magic spiders? Blech."*

He will then head for the entrance, but will add:

:   *"Is there anything more revolting than spiders? Too many legs..."*

Folgunthur

This **Adventurer** is the first of an entire group of curious individuals who died inside [Folgunthur](/wiki/Skyrim:Folgunthur "Skyrim:Folgunthur") in an attempt to solve the [Forbidden Legend](/wiki/Skyrim:Forbidden_Legend "Skyrim:Forbidden Legend"). He is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [bandit wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard "Skyrim:Bandit Wizard") and can be found immediately after entering the ruin, where he obviously triggered the [pressure plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Traps#Pressure_Plate "Skyrim:Traps") and the corresponding [spear trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Traps#Spear_Trap "Skyrim:Traps").

He wears [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor") with leveled light boots; there is a 50% chance they will be [fur shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes") and a 50% chance it will be [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") (up to level 6), [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") (up to level 19) or [scaled boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots") (level 19+). He carries an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), with a slim chance of carrying one or two potions, a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and/or a small amount of gold.

Folgunthur

This **Adventurer**, an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)"), is found in the very first chamber of [Folgunthur](/wiki/Skyrim:Folgunthur "Skyrim:Folgunthur"), at the foot of the [puzzle door](/wiki/Skyrim:Traps#Puzzle_Door "Skyrim:Traps"). Somehow she succeeded in solving the puzzle, but lost the fight to a [draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr") when the nearby gate was released.

She wears a randomly determined set of [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Light_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"); at all levels, there is always a 50% chance she will wear [fur shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes"), and a set of [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor"), while the other 50% can differ for both shoes and armor; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") and/or [hide armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor"), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") and/or [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor"), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots") and/or [scaled armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Armor") or [scaled horn armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Horn Armor"). Additionally, she has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Bracers "Skyrim:Fur Bracers") but, at level 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers") and at level 19, it can be [scaled bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers "Skyrim:Scaled Bracers"). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of her carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12.

Her weapon is random and leveled as well (up to elven quality at level 22) and can be either a [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), a [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") or a [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"). She also carries a bit of random loot, food and drink, with a slim chance of a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and/or some gold.

He is clad in randomly determined, leveled, [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Light_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"). At all levels, there is a 50% chance he will wear [fur shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes") and a set of [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor"), while the other 50% can differ; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") and/or [hide armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor"), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") and/or [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor"), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots") and/or [scaled armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Armor") or [scaled horn armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Horn Armor"). He carries a copy of the book *[Lost Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends "Skyrim:Lost Legends")*, two leveled [healing potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Healing") and some gold.

Reachwater Rock

This **Adventurer** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") who died in the first chamber of [Reachwater Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwater_Rock "Skyrim:Reachwater Rock") while trying to figure out the mystery of the [Forbidden Legend](/wiki/Skyrim:Forbidden_Legend "Skyrim:Forbidden Legend").

He wears leveled [heavy armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"); up to level 6 he will wear [iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor") or [banded iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Banded Iron Armor") and [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots"). Deep Folk Crossing

This **Adventurer** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjurer_(class) "Skyrim:Conjurer (class)") lying outside [Deep Folk Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Deep_Folk_Crossing "Skyrim:Deep Folk Crossing"), lying half in the river.

She wears [hooded blue robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Blue_Robes "Skyrim:Hooded Blue Robes") with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12 "Skyrim:Boots"). She carries a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") (up to glass quality at level 18) and a chance of some random wizard loot, like [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), spell tomes, [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") and/or ingredients. She always carries a small amount of gold. Despite her condition, she knows a few spells as well, specifically [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward"), [Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Flames "Skyrim:Flames"), [Oakflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Oakflesh "Skyrim:Oakflesh") and [Conjure Familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Familiar "Skyrim:Conjure Familiar").

If you have claimed the 'Glowing Chrystal Shard' from the podium south of Deep Folk Crossing, without having read the book *[The Aetherium Wars](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars "Skyrim:The Aetherium Wars")*, she will also carry a copy of said book that will send you to [Arkngthamz](/wiki/Skyrim:Arkngthamz "Skyrim:Arkngthamz") for the quest [Lost to the Ages](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages "Skyrim:Lost to the Ages").

Arkngathmz

This **Adventurer** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") who can be found inside [Arkngthamz](/wiki/Skyrim:Arkngthamz "Skyrim:Arkngthamz"), next to an arch in the middle of a room, with a skeleton and a few Dwarven arrows nearby.

He wears randomly determined, leveled, [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Light_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"). At all levels, there is a 50% chance he will wear [fur shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes") and a set of [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor"), while the other 50% can differ; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") and/or [hide armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor"), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") and/or [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor"), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots") and/or [scaled armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Armor") or [scaled horn armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Horn Armor"). Additionally, he has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Bracers "Skyrim:Fur Bracers") but, at level 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers") and at level 19, it can be [scaled bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers "Skyrim:Scaled Bracers").

He carries a leveled [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow") and [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrow "Skyrim:Arrow") (up to elven quality at level 20) and an additional 22 [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"). He also carries a bit of random loot, food and drink and/or jewelry. Most importantly, he carries a *[Scrap of Paper](/wiki/Skyrim:Scrap_of_Paper "Skyrim:Scrap of Paper")* with an important clue on how to solve the [tonal lock](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages#The_Tonal_Lock "Skyrim:Lost to the Ages") that killed him.

The dead Adventurer

This dead **Adventurer**, a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:EncClassBanditMelee "Skyrim:EncClassBanditMelee"), is found in [Gyldenhul Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Gyldenhul_Barrow "Skyrim:Gyldenhul Barrow").

He carries a copy of the book *[Deathbrand](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(book) "Skyrim:Deathbrand (book)")*, as well as a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Torn_Note_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Torn Note (Dragonborn)") which reveals that he came to the ruin in search of [treasure](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(quest)#A_Cursed_Trove "Skyrim:Deathbrand (quest)"). He couldn't find anything, but noticed a draft in the room which led him to believe there may be a secret passage. He locked himself in the ruin to continue searching while waiting for bandits outside to clear the area.

He wears [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor") with matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") and [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers"). He also carries a small amount of gold.
