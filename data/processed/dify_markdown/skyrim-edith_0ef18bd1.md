Edith

**Edith**, a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)"), is an employee of [Leigelf](/wiki/Skyrim:Leigelf "Skyrim:Leigelf") and works in the depths of [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine") in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

She is incredibly dedicated to her mining duties and can be found hacking away 24 hours a day, without ever eating or sleeping.

She wears a set of [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"), and is equipped with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"). She carries a selection of [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

When approached she can say:

:   *"Here to mine?"*
:   *"[Quicksilver ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore") is rare. Worth digging."*
