**Fruki** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)") who works in the [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine") in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

She carries her [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), her [house key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Fruki%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Fruki's House"), and a random selection of [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). She wears a set of [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") with [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0009cca1 "Skyrim:Boots").

She wakes up every morning at 7am and prepares breakfast using her [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"). At 8am she walks the short distance to the neighboring Quicksilver Mine where she spends twelve hours in the dimly lit tunnels, tirelessly mining ore for her boss [Leigelf](/wiki/Skyrim:Leigelf "Skyrim:Leigelf"). At 8pm, she goes to the [Windpeak Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Windpeak_Inn "Skyrim:Windpeak Inn") for a bit of socializing with the townsfolk before her bedtime at midnight. The only exception to this routine is when you first enter Dawnstar; she will then be inside Windpeak Inn, discussing the strange [nightmares](/wiki/Skyrim:Waking_Nightmare "Skyrim:Waking Nightmare") with [Erandur](/wiki/Skyrim:Erandur "Skyrim:Erandur"), [Irgnir](/wiki/Skyrim:Irgnir "Skyrim:Irgnir"), and [Thoring](/wiki/Skyrim:Thoring "Skyrim:Thoring").

## Dialogue

Before you have completed [Waking Nightmare](/wiki/Skyrim:Waking_Nightmare "Skyrim:Waking Nightmare"), Fruki will say:

:   *"[Lady Mara](/wiki/Lore:Mara "Lore:Mara") will save us from these nightmares. You'll see."*

Like most of the miners in town, Fruki almost exclusively talks about her work. She might also mention the feud between her boss and his ex-wife [Beitild](/wiki/Skyrim:Beitild "Skyrim:Beitild"):

:   *"Used to be twice as many miners working here before all this trouble started."*

:   *"I work in the mine, just like everyone else."*

### Conversations

When you first enter Dawnstar, you'll find her engaged in a conversation in the Windpeak Inn.

:   **Irgnir:** *"It's a curse! It has to be! I've got to get out of this town."*
:   **Fruki:** *"Irgnir, get a hold of yourself. They're just dreams. Please tell her, Erandur."*
:   **Erandur:** *"Listen to your friend, Fruki. They are just dreams, my dear. I assure you that it is quite normal."*
:   **Irgnir:** *"It's the same dream over and over again. You think that's normal? It's evil I tell you!"*
:   **Fruki:** *"Erandur, she has a point. You keep telling us no harm will follow these dreams, but they must be an omen."*
:   **Thoring:** *"Give him a chance to speak. He's trying to help us."*
:   **Erandur:** *"Everyone, please. I'm doing what I can to end these nightmares. In the meantime all I ask is you remain strong and put your trust in Lady Mara."*
:   **Irgnir:** *"I... I will. Thank you..."*

Even though they work in competing mines, Fruki can often be seen talking to Irgnir at the Windpeak Inn.

:   **Irgnir:** *"You should quit working in the Eastern Mine. Leigelf's no good to work for."*
:   **Fruki:** *"Leigelf is a good man, and I'm not running out on him now."*
:   **Irgnir:** *"Oh, got a bit of an interest in your boss do you?"*
:   **Fruki:** *"No, not that it's any of your business."*

:   **Irgnir:** *"Tough day at the mines. I swear the ore is getting harder to dig."*
:   **Fruki:** *"You're just getting older."*
:   **Irgnir:** *"That's why I come here to talk to you. You're all heart."*
