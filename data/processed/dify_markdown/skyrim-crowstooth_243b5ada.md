Crowstooth

**Crowstooth** is a [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") who is encountered during the quest [Bones for a Crow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bones_for_a_Crow "Skyrim:Bones for a Crow").

Crowstooth wears a set of [Forsworn Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Armor "Skyrim:Forsworn Armor"). She has a [Forsworn Bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Bow "Skyrim:Forsworn Bow"), a unique [Forsworn Sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Sword "Skyrim:Forsworn Sword") and the unique Forsworn axe [Tyrant's Bane](/wiki/Skyrim:Tyrant%27s_Bane "Skyrim:Tyrant's Bane"), and carries a number of [Forsworn Arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Arrow "Skyrim:Forsworn Arrow"), in addition to a random selection of loot. She is accompanied by [Alvasorr the Rat](/wiki/Skyrim:Alvasorr_the_Rat "Skyrim:Alvasorr the Rat"), who will back her up in the final fight of the quest.

She and Alvasorr appear at the northern tower in [Arcwind Point](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcwind_Point "Skyrim:Arcwind Point") after the player has killed her second-in-command [Linelle](/wiki/Skyrim:Linelle "Skyrim:Linelle") and followed the hints from [Linelle's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Linelle%27s_Note "Skyrim:Linelle's Note"). One of the other bounty hunters who went after Crowstooth, [Bjormund Wind-Strider](/wiki/Skyrim:Bjormund_Wind-Strider "Skyrim:Bjormund Wind-Strider"), lies dead at the top of the tower and can be looted for his unique Dragonplate Armor.

Be prepared for a difficult battle before you even get to Crowstooth; high-level [Draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr") will be present at Arcwind Point and are difficult to avoid as you take the path north to reach the tower. If [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") is complete and [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") is also installed, a leveled [Dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") appears, harassing the Draugr who will be [Shouting](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") at it when you arrive.
