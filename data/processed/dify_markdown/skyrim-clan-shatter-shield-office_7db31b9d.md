**Clan Shatter-Shield Office** is a small office in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") that is home to Clan Shatter-Shield's shipping operations.

The office is located on Windhelm's docks, between the [Argonian Assemblage](/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian_Assemblage "Skyrim:Argonian Assemblage") and the [East Empire Company regional office](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_(place) "Skyrim:East Empire Company (place)"). It contains one interior zone: *[Clan Shatter-Shield Office](#Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office)*.

## Clan Shatter-Shield Office

The building is a busy shipping hub run by the Shatter-Shields' employee, [Suvaris Atheron](/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron "Skyrim:Suvaris Atheron"). There is a long wooden table and three chairs holding a copy of *[Thief of Virtue](/wiki/Skyrim:Thief_of_Virtue "Skyrim:Thief of Virtue")* right in front of the entrance, with several empty carts to the east and west. The middle section is full of crates, and the rear section has a small wooden table and chair to the west, with the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"), a level-locked non-respawning [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") containing random items, a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), and an important clue required for the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Rise_in_the_East "Skyrim:Rise in the East") - [Suvaris's logbook](/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron%27s_Logbook "Skyrim:Suvaris Atheron's Logbook") - on the table. An unlocked respawning empty [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") is to the east, and to the north is a set of shelves directly opposite the entrance holding copies of *[Of Fjori and Holgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir "Skyrim:Of Fjori and Holgeir")* (see [notes](#Notes)), *[Beggar Prince](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar_Prince "Skyrim:Beggar Prince")*, *[Antecedants of Dwemer Law](/wiki/Skyrim:Antecedants_of_Dwemer_Law "Skyrim:Antecedants of Dwemer Law")*, *[Report: Disaster at Ionith](/wiki/Skyrim:Report:_Disaster_at_Ionith "Skyrim:Report: Disaster at Ionith")*, and *[The Wolf Queen, v5](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wolf_Queen,_v5 "Skyrim:The Wolf Queen, v5")*, as well as a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), with a large pile of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks on and in front of the shelves.

## Notes

- The book *Of Fjori and Holgeir* relates to the quest [Ansilvund](/wiki/Skyrim:Ansilvund_(quest) "Skyrim:Ansilvund (quest)").
- None of the sacks in the office are [respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning"), so they are safe to store items in.
