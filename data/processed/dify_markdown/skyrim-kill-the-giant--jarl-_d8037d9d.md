Kill a giant for the Jarl of Dawnstar.

## Radiant Options

This is a radiant quest which is received from [Skald](/wiki/Skyrim:Skald "Skyrim:Skald"), if he is currently the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"). The giant camp is randomly determined from the following list: [Blizzard Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Blizzard_Rest "Skyrim:Blizzard Rest"), [Red Road Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Road_Pass "Skyrim:Red Road Pass"), [Tumble Arch Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Tumble_Arch_Pass "Skyrim:Tumble Arch Pass").

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak with [Skald](/wiki/Skyrim:Skald "Skyrim:Skald"), the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").
2. Kill the [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") at the given camp.
3. Return to retrieve the bounty.

## Detailed Walkthrough

The [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"), [Skald](/wiki/Skyrim:Skald "Skyrim:Skald"), will ask you to deal with a [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") to send a message to all the other giants in the hold, for although they are not a problem now, the jarl believes they will be in the future. The giant can be found at one of three camps (above) and can be taken down by any means necessary.

### Reward

Once the giant is dead, return to Skald for your reward of gold:

| Levels | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 22-29 | 1000 |
| 30-39 | 1250 |
| 40+ | 1500 |

Completing this quest is required to start the [Thane of the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane_of_the_Pale "Skyrim:Thane of the Pale") quest and [Purchase land in Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Build_Your_Own_Home "Skyrim:Build Your Own Home")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") if Skald is still Jarl.

## Notes

- The quest has a minimum level of 22. If you are not level 22 yet, then you cannot receive this quest from Skald. If Brina is jarl (or Skald has been removed from power and then reinstated), this quest is not required to become Thane.
- This quest requires a giant to kill. If you have cleared all of the giants from all of the giant camps, then you cannot receive this quest from Skald. To solve this issue, wait or sleep indoors for 30 days and all giant camps will have been respawned.
- This quest cannot be started if you have a bounty in The Pale.
- The dialogue for starting this quest might not appear. A probable reason is you might have another [Favors](/wiki/Skyrim:Favors "Skyrim:Favors") quest running that tasks you to kill a certain enemy. This prevents this quest from starting.
  - Another possible fix is to fast travel to a non-cleared giant camp in The Pale and back to Dawnstar, which may make the dialogue option available.

## Bugs

- Sometimes the quest objective will not be removed from the journal.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
