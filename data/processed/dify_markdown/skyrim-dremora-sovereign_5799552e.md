Dremora Sovereign

**Dremora Sovereign** is a [Dremora](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora "Skyrim:Dremora") warrior whom you must summon during the quest [The Arms of Chaos](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:The Arms of Chaos") as part of the [Arms of Chaos](/wiki/Skyrim:Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:Arms of Chaos") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). Once you have obtained the [Sovereign Band](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovereign_Band "Skyrim:Sovereign Band"), you will be directed to use it at a Daedric altar east of [Skytemple Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins "Skyrim:Skytemple Ruins") in order to summon three Guardians of the Sigil Stones. Activating the altar will summon three Daedra in short succession: [Dremora Sehtkynaz](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Sehtkynaz "Skyrim:Dremora Sehtkynaz"), [Dremora Lyrkynaz](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Lyrkynaz "Skyrim:Dremora Lyrkynaz"), and Dremora Sovereign. Dremora Sovereign is the third Dremora to appear, and will be summoned as soon as you kill Dremora Lyrkynaz.

Dremora Sovereign utilizes powerful [fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage "Skyrim:Fire Damage") magic in combat and can summon a [flame atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Atronach "Skyrim:Flame Atronach"). He carries a [Daedric Sword of the Inferno](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Sword_of_the_Inferno "Skyrim:Daedric Sword of the Inferno") along with an [Orange Sigil Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Sigil_Stone "Skyrim:Orange Sigil Stone"). He will drop a [Daedra Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra_Heart "Skyrim:Daedra Heart") when killed.
