The source of the White River

**White River** is the longest river in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim_(place) "Skyrim:Skyrim (place)"), stretching from the south of the province to the northeast corner.

The source of the White River is [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta"), north of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). It flows northeast to join the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts") past [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). Along the way, it passes through [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood") and near [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). [Darkwater River](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_River "Skyrim:Darkwater River") and [River Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim "Skyrim:River Yorgrim") are the two main tributaries of the White River.
