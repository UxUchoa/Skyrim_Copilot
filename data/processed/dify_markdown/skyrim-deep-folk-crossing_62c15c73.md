Deep Folk Crossing

**Deep Folk Crossing** is an ancient [Dwarven](/wiki/Lore:Dwemer "Lore:Dwemer") bridge over a weir with structures at either end in the northern part of [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach").

A Dwemer convector, used for a [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction)#Optional_Quests "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)") quest, can be found just across the bridge. With the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") add-on installed, an [Aetherium Shard](/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherium_Shard "Skyrim:Aetherium Shard") can also be found at the location.

You can be given the location of this bridge by the children [Sond](/wiki/Skyrim:Sond_(child) "Skyrim:Sond (child)") and [Bottar](/wiki/Skyrim:Bottar "Skyrim:Bottar") in a [random encounter](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Dwemer_Junk_Peddlers "Skyrim:World Interactions").

## Deep Folk Crossing

Across the bridge on the northward path, you will see a stone plinth. On this stone plinth is an [Aetherium Shard](/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherium_Shard "Skyrim:Aetherium Shard")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") and a Dwemer Convector along with a leveled helmet and sword, which are always a [Dwarven Helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Helmet "Skyrim:Dwarven Helmet") and [Dwarven Sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Sword "Skyrim:Dwarven Sword"), respectively, from level 12.

Approaching the bridge from the south, halfway across on the right is a platform with stone steps up facing due east. Looking from here at the southern bank, you will see a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") directly in front of you. Following the river downstream, you will find an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"), approximately level where the path turns towards the east and [Druadach Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Redoubt "Skyrim:Druadach Redoubt"); a few meters farther downstream is a second nirnroot. There is nothing of interest upstream except the spring which is the source of the river.

## Notes

- [Katria's journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Katria%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Katria's Journal")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") reveals that a Dwemer city originally called Bthar-zel (meaning "allied city") is located at this site. Katria is correct, with the actual ruins of Bthar-zel being directly beneath it and an entrance to it at the end of the bridge. However, by the time of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"), the city is inaccessible.
- A short distance downstream, between the first and second waterfall below the bridge on the southern bank, lies a dead Adventurer wearing the rare [Hooded Blue Robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Blue_Robes "Skyrim:Hooded Blue Robes") and carrying a copy of [The Aetherium Wars](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars "Skyrim:The Aetherium Wars").
