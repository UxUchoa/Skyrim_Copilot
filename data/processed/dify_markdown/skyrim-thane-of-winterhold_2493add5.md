Become Thane of Winterhold

## Radiant Options

This is a radiant quest that you can receive from either [Korir](/wiki/Skyrim:Korir "Skyrim:Korir") or [Kraldar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kraldar "Skyrim:Kraldar"), whichever is the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)"). The jarl lives at the [Jarl's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Winterhold)") in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold").

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Befriend the Jarl of Winterhold.
2. Assist three people of Winterhold.
3. Return to the jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This is an umbrella quest for several other quests that must be completed to become Thane of Winterhold. In order to receive this quest you must first aid the current jarl. If the jarl is Korir, then you must usually [Retrieve the Helm of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Jarl_-_Caves) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Jarl - Caves)") for him first; however, if he was ousted from Winterhold during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") and reinstated following [The Battle for Fort Kastav](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Kastav "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Kastav"), that is sufficient to begin this quest. If the jarl is Kraldar, whether he was installed via [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") or [Rescue from Fort Kastav](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav "Skyrim:Rescue from Fort Kastav"), then the completion of that quest is sufficient to fulfill the prerequisite.

Once you have completed the prerequisite, speak with the jarl again and he will ask you to [assist the people of the hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People "Skyrim:Making Friends and Influencing People"). You must assist three people in the hold, usually by completing [favor quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Favors "Skyrim:Favors") for them. Note that many of the quest givers are non-essential NPCs, so their deaths would result in a loss of potential quests to complete. If they die after you assist them, they still count towards your Thane status. You can also befriend merchants if you have the [Investor](/wiki/Skyrim:Investor "Skyrim:Investor") perk by investing in their stores.

- **[The Black Star](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Star "Skyrim:The Black Star")**: Help find and repair [Azura's Star](/wiki/Skyrim:Azura%27s_Star "Skyrim:Azura's Star").

### College Quests

Completing any quest which increases the disposition of the mages at the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)") will also count towards this objective, including:

- **[Arniel's Endeavor](/wiki/Skyrim:Arniel%27s_Endeavor "Skyrim:Arniel's Endeavor")**: Uncover an ancient Dwemer secret. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Brelyna's Practice](/wiki/Skyrim:Brelyna%27s_Practice "Skyrim:Brelyna's Practice")**: Help [Brelyna Maryon](/wiki/Skyrim:Brelyna_Maryon "Skyrim:Brelyna Maryon") by allowing her to practice her spells on you.
- **[Fetch me that Book!](/wiki/Skyrim:Fetch_me_that_Book! "Skyrim:Fetch me that Book!")**: Fetch a book for [Urag gro-Shub](/wiki/Skyrim:Urag_gro-Shub "Skyrim:Urag gro-Shub"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant")) (second time only)
- **[First Lessons](/wiki/Skyrim:First_Lessons "Skyrim:First Lessons")**: Join the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)").
- **[J'zargo's Experiment](/wiki/Skyrim:J%27zargo%27s_Experiment "Skyrim:J'zargo's Experiment")**: Help [a student](/wiki/Skyrim:J%27zargo "Skyrim:J'zargo") test out a fiery new spell.
- **[Onmund's Request](/wiki/Skyrim:Onmund%27s_Request "Skyrim:Onmund's Request")**: Help [Onmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Onmund "Skyrim:Onmund") negotiate the return of a family amulet from [Enthir](/wiki/Skyrim:Enthir "Skyrim:Enthir"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Out of Balance](/wiki/Skyrim:Out_of_Balance "Skyrim:Out of Balance")**: Purify the focus points for magical energy around the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)").
- **[Restocking Soul Gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Restocking_Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Restocking Soul Gems")**: Help [Sergius Turrianus](/wiki/Skyrim:Sergius_Turrianus "Skyrim:Sergius Turrianus") to fill his stock with [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems").

[Enchanting Pick-Up](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting_Pick-Up "Skyrim:Enchanting Pick-Up") and [Shalidor's Insights](/wiki/Skyrim:Shalidor%27s_Insights "Skyrim:Shalidor's Insights") do not count as they do not affect anyone's disposition.

### Reward

After helping three inhabitants, the journal tells you to return to the jarl. The reward, the [Blade of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_Winterhold "Skyrim:Blade of Winterhold"), is a random, leveled, enchanted sword. There is a 50% chance that it will be a sword (one-handed), and a 50% chance it will be a greatsword (two-handed).

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.

- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage Favor257 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete.
