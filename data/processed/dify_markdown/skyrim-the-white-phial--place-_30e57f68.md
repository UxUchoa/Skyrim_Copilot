**The White Phial** is the alchemical shop located on the north side of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm")'s market district.

The owner is [Nurelion](/wiki/Skyrim:Nurelion "Skyrim:Nurelion"), who named the shop after the legendary artifact he's spent many years searching for. When Nurelion is not available, the shop duties are handled by his assistant, [Quintus Navale](/wiki/Skyrim:Quintus_Navale "Skyrim:Quintus Navale").

## The White Phial

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-interior-The_White_Phial.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_White_Phial.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_White_Phial.jpg)

The interior

When entering the shop the counter is in front of you down the length of the room with an end piece to the right and stairs up to the residence against the east wall. There is a square table and two chairs to the left, against the south wall, while in the southeastern corner is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"). There are [displayed merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise) on the counter, under the counter, on the square table to the left, on the two sets of shelves and another square table behind the counter, against the north wall. There are additionally several sacks and barrels containing [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") throughout the shop and residence.

In addition to these items there is a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a [mammoth cheese bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl") on the counter, a copy of the [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Black Arts On Trial](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Arts_On_Trial "Skyrim:The Black Arts On Trial")*, an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") under the counter, three [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot") on the table against the north wall, with an empty [lesser soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Lesser Soul Gem") on a stand beside it and a [tomato](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato") on one of the shelves. To the west is a doorway into a storeroom, which is also used by [Quintus Navale](/wiki/Skyrim:Quintus_Navale "Skyrim:Quintus Navale") as a bedroom. It is filled with crates, sacks, barrels, a cart filled with floor rugs and in the middle of the room a single bed with an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield") by the foot of it and a basket containing a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") and two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") by the head.

At the top of the stairs is a dining and kitchen area with a long wooden table and benches in the center of the room, with a haunch of raw [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") and a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") on the table and an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie") under it. The floorboards on this level have several holes in them. In the southeastern corner is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand with two racks hanging from a beam to the left, holding two [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). In an alcove on the south wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") among loads of clutter, with a chair to the right of it. Further along the south wall is an end table with a master-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") on top and a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") to the right. There is a doorway on the west wall, with another set of shelves to the left of it holding a gourd and more clutter.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-interior-The_White_Phial_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_White_Phial_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_White_Phial_02.jpg)

Nurelion's bedroom

The doorway leads into [Nurelion](/wiki/Skyrim:Nurelion "Skyrim:Nurelion")'s bedroom, which contains an owned double bed coming out from the west wall, with an end table to the left, against the south wall with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on top and a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") beside it. While to the right of the bed, against the north wall is a dresser with the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"), a bottle of Nord mead and a copy of *[Harvesting Frostbite Spider Venom](/wiki/Skyrim:Harvesting_Frostbite_Spider_Venom "Skyrim:Harvesting Frostbite Spider Venom")* all on top. To the right of the dresser, in the middle of the north wall, is an unlit fireplace with a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") to the left, a cauldron to the right and a chair in front of it. Finally in the southeastern corner is a round wooden table and chair.
