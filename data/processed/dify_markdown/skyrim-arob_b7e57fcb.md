**Arob** is an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:GuardOrc1H "Skyrim:GuardOrc1H") and the hunts-wife of [Chief Burguk](/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Burguk "Skyrim:Chief Burguk") in [Dushnikh Yal](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnikh_Yal "Skyrim:Dushnikh Yal"), the Orc stronghold southeast of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth").

While Burguk's favored wife [Shel](/wiki/Skyrim:Shel "Skyrim:Shel") gets to be near the chief around the clock, Arob and forge-wife [Gharol](/wiki/Skyrim:Gharol "Skyrim:Gharol") practically never see their husband and spend all their time doing their duties in the hold. Arob and her son [Nagrub](/wiki/Skyrim:Nagrub "Skyrim:Nagrub") share the same responsibilities, patrolling the stronghold day and night, without ever eating or sleeping, at times scouting the surrounding wilderness for prey.

She wears a set of leveled armor, excluding the helmet; at level 1 she wears [iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor"), at level 6 she wears [steel armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Armor"), at level 18 she wears [steel plate armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Plate Armor") and at level 25 she wears [orcish armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Armor "Skyrim:Orcish Armor"). She never sleeps but carries a spare set of [ragged trousers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers "Skyrim:Ragged Trousers") along with the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Key to Burguk's Longhouse") to [Burguk's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Burguk%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Burguk's Longhouse"). She wields leveled weapons as well; at level 1 she uses a [steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Steel War Axe") and a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow"); at level 6 she uses an [orcish war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_War_Axe "Skyrim:Orcish War Axe") and an [orcish bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Bow "Skyrim:Orcish Bow"). She carries at least twenty leveled arrows and a selection of [middle-class food and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

When approached she will briefly introduce herself and her opinion on Chief Burguk:

:   *"I am the hunts-wife of this stronghold."*
:   *"Burguk is a fine chief. He brings strength and wealth to the stronghold."*

She may also recommend a few service providers in the hold:

:   *"[Murbul](/wiki/Skyrim:Murbul "Skyrim:Murbul") knows many poisons and cures if you need them."*
:   *"You should see Gharol if you're looking for a blade or armor."*

## Conversations

During the long days and nights, Arob and Nagrub will occasionally share their happiness:

**Nagrub**: *"Ah, the air is good today. I welcome the smell of blood from all the kills I will make."*
**Arob**: *"Patience, my son. The hunter days*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *not welcome the blood of his prey, he waits for it."*

**Arob**: *"The air has the smell of beasts. The hunt is good today."*
**Nagrub**: *"Yes, mother. I'm eager to prove my skills today."*
