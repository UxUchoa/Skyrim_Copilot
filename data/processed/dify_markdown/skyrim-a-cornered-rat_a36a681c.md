## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Delphine](/wiki/Skyrim:Delphine "Skyrim:Delphine") in [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood").
2. Talk to [Brynjolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Brynjolf "Skyrim:Brynjolf") in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") or talk to [Keerava](/wiki/Skyrim:Keerava "Skyrim:Keerava") in [The Bee and Barb](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bee_and_Barb "Skyrim:The Bee and Barb").
3. Search the [Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway") for [Esbern](/wiki/Skyrim:Esbern "Skyrim:Esbern")'s hideout.
4. Find Esbern in the Ratway Warrens.
5. Talk to Esbern.
6. Bring Esbern back to [Delphine](/wiki/Skyrim:Delphine "Skyrim:Delphine") at [Sleeping Giant Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Sleeping_Giant_Inn "Skyrim:Sleeping Giant Inn") in [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood").

### Finding the Rat

After infiltrating the [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy"), return to [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood") and inform [Delphine](/wiki/Skyrim:Delphine "Skyrim:Delphine") of what you learned from the [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor") records. Delphine is surprised to know her old colleague and fellow [Blade](/wiki/Skyrim:Blades "Skyrim:Blades"), [Esbern](/wiki/Skyrim:Esbern "Skyrim:Esbern"), is reportedly still alive. She tasks you with finding the archivist from his hiding place in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). Due to the clandestine nature of the Blades, Delphine gives you a secret phrase to deliver to Esbern to secure his trust when you locate him.
Depending on your standing with the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), the journal will direct you to talk to [Brynjolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Brynjolf "Skyrim:Brynjolf"), or search the [Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway") for Esbern.
If you are directed to find Brynjolf, you may speak to him, [Dirge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dirge "Skyrim:Dirge"), or [Vekel the Man](/wiki/Skyrim:Vekel_the_Man "Skyrim:Vekel the Man") inside [the Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon"). All three have an option to [persuade](/wiki/Skyrim:Persuade "Skyrim:Persuade") or bribe them for information on Esbern's location.

If you have already joined the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), the Ratway is accessible and you can locate Esbern directly. Speaking with [Keerava](/wiki/Skyrim:Keerava "Skyrim:Keerava"), the Riften innkeeper, also advances the quest.

### The Ratway

The Ratway and the Ratway Vaults beneath Riften are inhabited by a number of [lowlifes](/wiki/Skyrim:Lowlife "Skyrim:Lowlife"), [vagrants](/wiki/Skyrim:Vagrant "Skyrim:Vagrant"), and three or four Thalmor warriors searching for Esbern. When you reach the door to Esbern's room in the Ratway Warrens, he is cautious of Thalmor agents and initially refuses you entry. You may persuade him by saying Delphine sent you, you can tell him the password Delphine gave you: *"Where were you on the 30th of Frostfall?"*, or you may explain that you are the [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dovahkiin "Skyrim:Dovahkiin").

### The Dragon's Return

Once Esbern lets you in, he shares his dire predictions about the world: [Alduin the World-Eater](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin")'s return has apocalyptic implications, and Esbern's warnings of this threat have been ignored. He makes it clear that there was no hope until you arrived and that he believed the world was going to end. He agrees to follow you to Delphine's base in Riverwood.

Before leaving, Esbern will quickly search his room for any sensitive information the Thalmor might find. He will also gather some supplies from the chest at the foot of his bed and then be ready to leave. The quest ends here, and the next quest, [Alduin's Wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin%27s_Wall "Skyrim:Alduin's Wall"), begins.

## Notes

- If any follower is hired before or during the quest, they will not be forced to return to their original location when [Esbern](/wiki/Skyrim:Esbern "Skyrim:Esbern") joins as a second follower.
- If he survives [Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity"), [Gissur](/wiki/Skyrim:Gissur "Skyrim:Gissur") will be seated at a table in [the Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon") when you get Esbern's whereabouts from [Vekel the Man](/wiki/Skyrim:Vekel_the_Man "Skyrim:Vekel the Man"). After you end conversation with Vekel, Gissur will get up from the table and leave the Flagon. If you are a member of the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), Vekel will suddenly say *"That one, right there, walking out the door! He was one of them asking about that old man you were looking for."* If confronted, Gissur will only rebuff your attempts at conversation, but the [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Gissur%27s_Note "Skyrim:Gissur's Note") he carries reveals his true intentions. He can be attacked and killed with no repercussions, and other Thieves Guild members will join the fight on your side if you are a guild member, which may make your escape with Esbern easier. If you choose to follow Gissur to his assigned contact instead of killing him, he will head into the [Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway"). He meets up with two [Thalmor soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Soldier "Skyrim:Thalmor Soldier") and a [Thalmor wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Wizard "Skyrim:Thalmor Wizard") and informs them about you:

:   :   **Gissur**: *"He's/She's in the Flagon right now. Get ready."*
    :   **Thalmor Wizard**: *"You're sure?"*
    :   **Gissur**: *"Of course I'm sure! He/She was asking about Esbern."*
    :   **Thalmor Wizard**: *"Doesn't matter. We'll kill them either way."*

:   At this point they'll all make their way back to the Flagon and accost Vekel (or [Dirge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dirge "Skyrim:Dirge"), if Vekel's dead), demanding to know where you are:

:   :   **Thalmor Wizard**: *"Where is he? The one who was asking about the fugitive hiding in the Ratway?"*
    :   **Vekel**: *"You're in the wrong place to be asking those kind of questions, pal."*
    :   (The Thalmor soldiers draw their weapons.)
    :   **Thalmor Wizard**: *"No. Not yet."*
    :   (The Thalmor soldiers sheathe their weapons.)
    :   **Vekel**: *"You'd better clear out before someone gets hurt."*
    :   **Thalmor Wizard**: *"Your lack of cooperation has been noted. We'll be back if we require further information. You won't get a second chance."*
    :   **Vekel**: *"The answer ain't gonna be any different the next time. Arrogant elven bastard."*

:   The Thalmor will then leave the Ragged Flagon and go back into the Ratway to wait for you, while Gissur will return to his seat in the Flagon. They will be hostile on sight, so you will only hear the above conversations if you remain hidden throughout.

- If [A Chance Arrangement](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Chance_Arrangement "Skyrim:A Chance Arrangement") has been started, Brynjolf cannot be talked to.
- It is possible to proceed directly to Esbern without returning to Delphine first. Esbern will unlock his door if you tell him you are Dragonborn.

## Bugs

- The quest may fail to start after completing [Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity"), and even trying manually start or end it with the console will not work.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") You may have to skip forward to the start of the next quest, [Alduin's Wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin%27s_Wall "Skyrim:Alduin's Wall"), by using the console and typing in `setstage MQ203 5`, which will allow you to continue.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Use console command `setstage MQ202 (stage #)` to force Esbern to open the door and talk again. Stage 160 makes Esbern open the door, if bugged. Stage 170 makes Esbern talk again, if bugged. Another easier way to avoid bugs is to type in `CompleteQuest MQ202`, which would work for any quest value.
- If following Gissur, the Thalmor may never return to the Ragged Flagon or they may be hostile when you get there, causing the second dialogue to be skipped.
- If you have already become a member of the Thieves Guild, Gissur will attempt to get to his Thalmor contact through the Ragged Flagon - Cistern, through the Crypt Secret Entrance (will not get stuck unless you actively follow him through this roundabout path), and back into the Ratway via the river entrance, resulting in an extremely delayed arrival to his contact. The Thalmor and Gissur will then follow the same path in reverse; all the way through the Crypt, Cistern, and back into the Flagon.

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
