Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!

## Quick Walkthrough

1. While in the [Abandoned Shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Abandoned_Shack "Skyrim:Abandoned Shack") during the quest [With Friends Like These...](/wiki/Skyrim:With_Friends_Like_These... "Skyrim:With Friends Like These..."), kill [Astrid](/wiki/Skyrim:Astrid "Skyrim:Astrid") instead of a hostage.
2. Travel to any city and talk to a [guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Guards "Skyrim:Guards").
3. Speak to [Commander Maro](/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro "Skyrim:Commander Maro") in [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") southwest of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude").
4. Travel to the [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary") and kill all [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") members inside.
5. Return to Commander Maro and collect your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Complete [Innocence Lost](/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost "Skyrim:Innocence Lost") by killing [Grelod the Kind](/wiki/Skyrim:Grelod_the_Kind "Skyrim:Grelod the Kind"). When you are kidnapped by the Brotherhood and brought to the [Abandoned Shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Abandoned_Shack "Skyrim:Abandoned Shack"), kill [Astrid](/wiki/Skyrim:Astrid "Skyrim:Astrid") instead of a hostage and this quest will begin. Astrid has a rather lengthy animation when she jumps off the bookshelf, during which she is vulnerable and cannot retaliate.

After Astrid's death, travel to any city and speak to a guard. Inform them that you killed the Dark Brotherhood's leader, and you will be directed to speak to [Commander Maro](/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro "Skyrim:Commander Maro") in [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") southwest of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). He can be found in the [Penitus Oculatus Outpost](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Outpost").

After speaking to Commander Maro, you will be given the passphrase ("Silence, my brother") to their hideout and directed to kill everyone inside. He says that you should "have the honor" of clearing out the place, meaning you'll be going in alone. Even your [followers](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") do not enter the sanctuary with you. However, spectral entities such as [Arniel's spirit](/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arniel%27s_Shade "Skyrim:Summon Arniel's Shade") and the [ghostly Dragon Priest](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Dragon_Priest "Skyrim:Conjure Dragon Priest") (summoned by the [Konahrik mask](/wiki/Skyrim:Konahrik "Skyrim:Konahrik")) will enter with you, and they'll assist you in the assault. The task should be relatively simple, as this quest will have them spread out between each room (as opposed to them being gathered and chatting when you first enter as an actual member). [Babette](/wiki/Skyrim:Babette "Skyrim:Babette"), [Cicero](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero "Skyrim:Cicero"), and the [Night Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Mother "Skyrim:Night Mother") won't be in the sanctuary during the attack. You don't need to kill the [frostbite spider](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider") [Lis](/wiki/Skyrim:Lis "Skyrim:Lis"), but it will become hostile if it sees you.

Loot what you please. You have access to an [Iron Battleaxe of Chills](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe_of_Chills "Skyrim:Iron Battleaxe of Chills") on [Arnbjorn](/wiki/Skyrim:Arnbjorn "Skyrim:Arnbjorn"), one of the twenty-four "unusual gems" or Stones of Barenziah needed for the quest [No Stone Unturned](/wiki/Skyrim:No_Stone_Unturned "Skyrim:No Stone Unturned"), the Alteration skill book [Sithis](/wiki/Skyrim:Sithis "Skyrim:Sithis"), many poisons lying around, four different soul gems, part of the [Marked for Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Marked_for_Death "Skyrim:Marked for Death") shout, and the Dark Brotherhood armor.

### Rewards and consequences

Return to Commander Maro in Dragon Bridge, and he will be overjoyed and reward you with 3,000 gold, regardless of your level. He will also make sure that the [Emperor](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real) "Skyrim:Emperor Titus Mede II (real)") himself hears of your accomplishments. The quest also yields a good amount of valuable loot that you'd normally only be able to obtain by joining the Dark Brotherhood. All parts of the Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary near Falkreath will remain accessible and undamaged, as opposed to finishing the Dark Brotherhood questline, where a good chunk of it is destroyed.

Destroying the Dark Brotherhood makes a number of rewards unavailable, namely [Shadowmere](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmere "Skyrim:Shadowmere"), a minimum of 20,000 gold, the [Summon Spectral Assassin](/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Spectral_Assassin "Skyrim:Summon Spectral Assassin") power, access to the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Dawnstar Sanctuary"), the ability to marry [Muiri](/wiki/Skyrim:Muiri "Skyrim:Muiri"), the [never-dying](/wiki/Skyrim:Essential_NPCs "Skyrim:Essential NPCs") [Dark Brotherhood Initiate](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Initiate "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood Initiate") followers, the Master trainers for [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") (Babette) and [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") (Nazir), [Ancient Shrouded Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Shrouded_Armor "Skyrim:Ancient Shrouded Armor"), [Windshear](/wiki/Skyrim:Windshear "Skyrim:Windshear"), and [Cicero](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero "Skyrim:Cicero")'s outfit ([clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Cicero's Clothes"), [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero%27s_Boots "Skyrim:Cicero's Boots"), [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero%27s_Gloves "Skyrim:Cicero's Gloves"), [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero%27s_Hat "Skyrim:Cicero's Hat")) and offer of being a follower. (See below for workarounds on marrying Muiri and access to Ancient Shrouded Armor.)

## Notes

- If you have the [Misdirection](/wiki/Skyrim:Misdirection "Skyrim:Misdirection") perk, you can rob Astrid of her gear before you attack her, making her extremely easy to kill.
- The key required to exit the Abandoned Shack will not appear in Astrid's inventory if you attempt to pick her pocket, but will only appear in her inventory if you kill her.
- If you cast [Calm](/wiki/Skyrim:Calm "Skyrim:Calm") on a member of the Dark Brotherhood, they will offer the same dialogue as if you had joined the Dark Brotherhood, with the exception of Nazir, who will not give contracts at all.
- After you choose to kill Astrid, any or all of the three captives can be untied and released after her death.
- Don't store anything in the Abandoned Shack, as the building resets every 10 days.
- Either joining the Dark Brotherhood or destroying it will prevent further [assassination attempts against you](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin_Sent_to_Kill_the_Player "Skyrim:World Interactions").
- After completing this quest, guards will occasionally comment on your accomplishment. See the [Guard Dialogue](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard_Dialogue#Dialogue_added_by_Quests "Skyrim:Guard Dialogue") page for specifics.
- Even if you finish this quest, [Aventus Aretino](/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino "Skyrim:Aventus Aretino") will still view you as his savior for getting rid of [Grelod the Kind](/wiki/Skyrim:Grelod_the_Kind "Skyrim:Grelod the Kind").
- Even if you finish this quest, you can still marry Muiri if [Bothela](/wiki/Skyrim:Bothela "Skyrim:Bothela") is killed and you invest with Muiri after she takes over [The Hag's Cure](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Hag%27s_Cure "Skyrim:The Hag's Cure"). However, killing Bothela yourself will lower Muiri's disposition.
- Even if you finish this quest, you can still gain access to the Ancient Shrouded Armor set in [Hag's End](/wiki/Skyrim:Hag%27s_End "Skyrim:Hag's End") by resurrecting the Assassin with [Serana](/wiki/Skyrim:Serana "Skyrim:Serana") or via the [Ritual Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ritual_Stone "Skyrim:The Ritual Stone"), or by clipping through the stone door.
- Ironically, killing Astrid during "With Friends Like These..." causes her to say "Well done..." right before this quest begins.

## Bugs

- The quest may not trigger upon Astrid's death. **?**
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Typing `setstage dbdestroy 10` in the console resolves this problem, and starts this quest.
- You still might not be able to gain entrance to the Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary after speaking with Commander Maro. **?**
- Completing this quest may prevent the [Liberation of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Liberation_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Liberation of Skyrim") quest from progressing beyond the [Battle for Fort Snowhawk](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Snowhawk (Stormcloaks)"), with [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak") repeatedly stating that they cannot move on Solitude while [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici")'s [wedding](/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Until_Death "Skyrim:Bound Until Death") is in progress. **?**
- If you finish this quest, [Maluril](/wiki/Skyrim:Maluril "Skyrim:Maluril")'s [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Maluril%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Maluril's Journal") may still be present in [Mzinchaleft](/wiki/Skyrim:Mzinchaleft "Skyrim:Mzinchaleft"), even though he'll never be there himself.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.3, addresses this issue. His journal will no longer be present if he isn't there.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage DBDestroy stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest DBDestroy`.
