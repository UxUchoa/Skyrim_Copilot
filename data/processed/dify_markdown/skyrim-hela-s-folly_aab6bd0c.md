Hela's Folly, shipwreck and campsite

**Hela's Folly** is a [shipwreck](/wiki/Skyrim:Shipwrecks "Skyrim:Shipwrecks") northeast of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"). The fast travel point is just to the south of the bow of the wreck. The camp is a few paces to the west-southwest.

[Salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(creature) "Skyrim:Salmon (creature)") and [Arctic Chars](/wiki/Skyrim:Arctic_Char "Skyrim:Arctic Char")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") can be found swimming in the waters here.

## Hela's Folly

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly_02.jpg)

Deekus' campsite

There is a makeshift camp next to the wreck which is the home of an [Argonian](/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian "Skyrim:Argonian") named [Deekus](/wiki/Skyrim:Deekus "Skyrim:Deekus"). Everything in the camp is marked as owned and is counted as stealing if taken, even after you kill him. The camp consists of a lean-to, beneath which are a bedroll, a [Dibella statue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dibella_Statue "Skyrim:Dibella Statue"), and an open crate containing two [amethysts](/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst "Skyrim:Amethyst"), a [sapphire](/wiki/Skyrim:Sapphire_(item) "Skyrim:Sapphire (item)"), and a filled [lesser soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Lesser Soul Gem"). To the right of the lean-to is a novice-locked chest "protected" by two [bear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap")—one in front and one behind. There are three crates between the chest and a campfire, which has three [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") [cooking](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") over it. To the north is another crate with a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a loose [coin](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), an amethyst, and an [emerald](/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald "Skyrim:Emerald") on top. There are two barrels by the crate, and on top of one of them are a [soul gem fragment](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gem_Fragment "Skyrim:Soul Gem Fragment") and a sapphire. On the shore, to the east of the wreck is a [clam](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam "Skyrim:Clam") that can be harvested for [clam meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat "Skyrim:Clam Meat").

Another chest can be found underwater in the northwest part of the ship. There are also several gems underwater, almost directly underneath the chest. This includes one [ruby](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby "Skyrim:Ruby") and one sapphire on the lower deck of the submerged half, between some crates near a plant.

## Notes

- If you are sent here to retrieve the [Helm of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Helm_of_Winterhold "Skyrim:Helm of Winterhold") for [Jarl Korir](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl_Korir "Skyrim:Jarl Korir"), it will be in the chest owned by Deekus, and thus considered theft. Korir will still accept it to complete the quest, however.
