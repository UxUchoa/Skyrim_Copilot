Frozen Fish

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Receive a Fishing Work Letter from a [courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Courier "Skyrim:Courier").
2. Travel to [Riften Fishery](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Fishery "Skyrim:Riften Fishery") and speak with [Swims-In-Deep-Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water "Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water").
3. Read the list of arctic fish.
4. Obtain a [Cod](/wiki/Skyrim:Cod "Skyrim:Cod").
5. Obtain an [Arctic Grayling](/wiki/Skyrim:Arctic_Grayling "Skyrim:Arctic Grayling").
6. Obtain an [Arctic Char](/wiki/Skyrim:Arctic_Char "Skyrim:Arctic Char").
7. Obtain an [Angler Larvae](/wiki/Skyrim:Angler_Larvae "Skyrim:Angler Larvae").
8. (Optional) Read [Fishing Mastery, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Mastery,_v3 "Skyrim:Fishing Mastery, v3").

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-quest-Frozen_Fish_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Frozen_Fish_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Frozen_Fish_02.jpg)

Fish tank after completion of the quest

To begin this quest, speak to Swims-In-Deep-Water in the Riften Fishery:

:   **I got your letter. I'm ready for the next fishing challenge.**
:   *"Of course. It's all in this note."*
:   **Alright. I'll be back with the fish.**

He then hands you a [list of arctic fish](/wiki/Skyrim:List_of_Arctic_Fish "Skyrim:List of Arctic Fish"), which tells you that you need to catch:

- [Angler Larvae](/wiki/Skyrim:Angler_Larvae "Skyrim:Angler Larvae") - You will need to use the Alik'ri rod. Multiple are guaranteed to spawn in [Pilgrim's Trench](/wiki/Skyrim:Pilgrim%27s_Trench "Skyrim:Pilgrim's Trench") on the ocean floor.
- [Arctic Char](/wiki/Skyrim:Arctic_Char "Skyrim:Arctic Char") - Use the Argonian rod at an Arctic spot. Can also be found at [Hela's Folly](/wiki/Skyrim:Hela%27s_Folly "Skyrim:Hela's Folly"), [Orphan's Tear](/wiki/Skyrim:Orphan%27s_Tear "Skyrim:Orphan's Tear") (within the sunken deck) and Pilgrim's Trench.
- [Arctic Grayling](/wiki/Skyrim:Arctic_Grayling "Skyrim:Arctic Grayling") - Use the Argonian rod at an Arctic spot.
- [Cod](/wiki/Skyrim:Cod "Skyrim:Cod") - Use the Argonian rod at an Arctic spot. Can also be found outside [Anga's Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill "Skyrim:Anga's Mill") and outside [Bronze Water Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Bronze_Water_Cave "Skyrim:Bronze Water Cave").

You can also optionally pickup a copy of [Fishing Mastery, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Mastery,_v3 "Skyrim:Fishing Mastery, v3") from the shelf in the backroom.

Once you have the fish required return to Swims:

:   **Here are the fish you asked for.**
:   *"It has done a good thing. It will take this gift as a token of gratitude."*

He will then reward you with 300 gold.

## Notes

- [Crustacean Extermination](/wiki/Skyrim:Crustacean_Extermination "Skyrim:Crustacean Extermination") is the prerequisite of this task, as it will reward you [Alik'ri Fishing Rod](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Items#Alik.27ri_Fishing_Rod "Skyrim:Fishing Items").
  - It is not required to wait for the Courier to deliver the letter. Upon completion of Crustacean Extermination, you can head straight to Swims-In-Deep-Water and immediately request the next quest. If the Courier was already headed your way, this will make him behave as if he already delivered the letter to you.

## Bugs

- This quest may attempt to remove a courier note from his container even if it isn't present because the quests were done in a different order.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.4, fixes this bug.
