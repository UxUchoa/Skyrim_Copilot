Daenella

Daenella is a dead [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") who was a witch of the [Beldama Wyrd](/wiki/Lore:Beldama_Wyrd "Lore:Beldama Wyrd"). Her body is found at [Karthspire Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthspire_Camp "Skyrim:Karthspire Camp") during [Crypt of the Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Crypt_of_the_Heart "Skyrim:Crypt of the Heart"), on the rock where normally the dead [Giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") lies.

She wears [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes") with matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"). A random selection of gold based on your level can be found on her body, and possibly a [gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Leveled_Gems "Skyrim:Leveled Lists") and/or an [ingredient](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"). Additionally, she knows the spell [Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing "Skyrim:Healing").

Daenella

Daenella appears as a ghost after you've cleansed [Simon Rodayne's Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Simon_Rodayne%27s_Heart "Skyrim:Simon Rodayne's Heart") at his grave, together with the ghost of [Simon Rodayne](/wiki/Skyrim:Simon_Rodayne "Skyrim:Simon Rodayne"), to show their thanks for reuniting them.

Other than her ghostly appearance and the lack of leveled loot on her, she is identical to the version above.

## Notes

- Her body cannot be [reanimated](/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate "Skyrim:Reanimate").

## Bugs

- Daenella's body may be found before the start of the related quest. A sacrificed [Giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") will be lying on top of her corpse.
