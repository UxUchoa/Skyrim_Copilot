Eriana

**Eriana**, a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)"), is one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto "Skyrim:Bloated Man's Grotto") hunting [Sinding](/wiki/Skyrim:Sinding "Skyrim:Sinding"). If you choose to side with Sinding, you will have to kill her.

Eriana wears a set of [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor") along with [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers") and a matching pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"). For close ranged combat, she relies on a leveled [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") that can be up to [Elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") in quality and a leveled [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield") that can be up to [steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel "Skyrim:Steel") in quality. For long ranged combat, she relies on a leveled [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow") and twenty [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows "Skyrim:Arrows"), both of which can be up to Elven in quality.

## Notes

- Eriana is one of two NPCs in the game, with the other being [Sabine Nytte](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabine_Nytte "Skyrim:Sabine Nytte"), who has a race-specific voice type (in her case this is "FemaleNord") that doesn't match their race.
