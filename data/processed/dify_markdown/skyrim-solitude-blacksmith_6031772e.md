Solitude Blacksmith

**Solitude Blacksmith** is a smithy in the western side of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude").

It is worked by [Beirand](/wiki/Skyrim:Beirand "Skyrim:Beirand") and [Heimvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Heimvar "Skyrim:Heimvar"). The interior is quite large, and there is a decent number of things that can be stolen. [Heimvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Heimvar "Skyrim:Heimvar") can usually be found at a grindstone downstairs.

### Exterior

The blacksmiths are found within the grounds of [Castle Dour](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour "Skyrim:Castle Dour"), just outside the walls. It is found at the top of a ramp leading up from the market area. From the top of the ramp turn to the right. On the end of the wall separating the ramp from the blacksmiths is a [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (Thieves Guild)") [![Loot](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Loot") Loot. To the right of the entrance is a cart containing several [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"). Further along is the [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") where Beirand can usually be found working. At the rear, against the wall is a wooden table with several items of Beirand's stock that will disappear when purchased. In addition to the forge there is a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") and [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench") as well as several barrels of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") all around the forge area.

### Solitude Blacksmith

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg)

Heimvar working on the grindstone

Upon entering you are in a wide room with a central pillar and a door to the residence opposite. On the right is a firepit, with a stone wall around it. There are three [silver ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot") on the top of the wall and a grindstone beside it. Above the firepit is a rack holding two [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). On your right as you enter, against the south wall is a barrel containing foodstuffs and eight [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot") on top. Beside this is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, five potatoes, a cabbage and a couple of loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). In an alcove to the east are two large piles of firewood and a dresser containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes").

In the northeastern corner are two small units holding a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), two [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). On the north wall are two wall shelves holding fifteen iron ingots, two silver ingots and four [steel ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot "Skyrim:Steel Ingot"). To the left of these is the door to the residence and to the left of that is a small unit containing clothes with a rack holding two [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids. Further along the wall is a barrel of foodstuffs and a set of shelves holding four leather strips and two pieces of [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") and in the northwestern corner a stack of more barrels containing food.

Through the door into the residence on the left is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, a cabbage, five potatoes and a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"). On the right is a table and chairs set for two, with two [cups](/wiki/Skyrim:Cup "Skyrim:Cup"), two portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), two [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), some bread, an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and a bulb of garlic. Above the table are three wall shelves holding two [goat cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), several cups and a bunch of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"). Directly opposite the door against the northeast wall is an alcove containing several barrels of food, a table holding two leather strips, three iron ingots and a cabbage, with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") underneath and a bale of straw behind. Beside the table is basket containing two cabbages and five potatoes.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith_02.jpg)

Heimvar's room

To the east is a door into Heimvar's bedroom, which contains a tall wardrobe on the right as you enter, against the north wall a narrow table holding an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") containing a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flower and bunch of tundra cotton. Above this is a wall shelf holding three common books. Against the east wall is an empty set of small shelves. There is an unowned single bed in the southeastern corner with a wall shelf above on the east wall holding two leather strips. There is an open crate at the foot of the bed containing a bunch of tundra cotton and to the right of the bed is a table holding a cup.

The stairs up to the top floor are to the west, with a barrel of food on the left of them. At the top of the stairs against the north wall is a small cupboard with a [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") on top. Opposite the top of the stairs is a door into Beirand's bedroom. To the right of this is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand, with a narrow table to the right of this, holding a bowl of bread, a plate holding a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") and an iron dagger. Above these is a wall shelf holding one iron and one steel ingot. Against the south wall is a bookcase holding a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), nine common books, a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute") and a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum"). In front of this is a low table and chair holding a common book, a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") and a [plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") of bread and cheese.

Beirand's bedroom consists of a single unowned bed in the southeastern corner, a wardrobe on your left as you enter the room, a small unit against the east wall holding a level-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") and a plate of bread and cheese. In front of this is a low table and chair holding the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"), with a wooden bench against the north wall. Further along the wall is a narrow table holding an iron dagger and bowl containing a bunch of tundra cotton. Above this is a wall shelf holding a bowl of [void salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Void_Salts "Skyrim:Void Salts") and a copy of the [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Refugees](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Refugees "Skyrim:The Refugees")*. Against the west wall is a tall set of shelves holding two [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales"), a bowl of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), two bottles of [frostbite venom](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Venom "Skyrim:Frostbite Venom"), a deathbell flower, a bowl of void salts and a basket containing two bunches of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender").

## Notes

- In [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online"), [Radiant Raiment](/wiki/Online:Radiant_Raiment "Online:Radiant Raiment") was located in this building.
- With the [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") add-on, this shop is an excellent place to stock up on iron for [housebuilding](/wiki/Skyrim:Construction "Skyrim:Construction") purposes.

## Bugs

- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")The Blacksmith sign when ran and jumped into may make you suddenly end up in a large body of water with nothing around you. When checked on the map it tilts the map upwards as if you were in the sky. Has also occurred on Playstation 3 and can be fast traveled away from.
- [Heimvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Heimvar "Skyrim:Heimvar") is officially the backup blacksmith for Solitude Blacksmith. However, Heimvar is not set up properly, meaning that if [Beirand](/wiki/Skyrim:Beirand "Skyrim:Beirand") dies there is no longer anyone who will provide blacksmith merchant services in Solitude.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- Solitude Blacksmith isn't correctly flagged as a store or dwelling, and is also missing the TGBusiness keyword for Thieves Guild radiant quests even though it has all the required parts in place.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
