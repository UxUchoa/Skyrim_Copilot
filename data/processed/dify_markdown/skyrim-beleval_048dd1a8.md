Beleval

**Beleval** is a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") and a member of the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)"), who can be found in [Fort Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dawnguard "Skyrim:Fort Dawnguard"). Like most other recruits, she is available as a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"), and with [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") installed, she is available as your [personal steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Personal_Steward "Skyrim:Personal Steward").

She arrives at Fort Dawnguard when you reach [stage 60](/wiki/Skyrim:Prophet_(Dawnguard)#Quest_Stages "Skyrim:Prophet (Dawnguard)") of the quest [Prophet](/wiki/Skyrim:Prophet_(Dawnguard) "Skyrim:Prophet (Dawnguard)"). She will then join the growing team of recruits hanging out in the western area and will spend her time wandering, relaxing, eating or using the various tools in the area. On every full hour, she may decide to train her [crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Crossbow "Skyrim:Crossbow") skills on the dummies in the northern cave for three hours. She sleeps on one of the cots between 8pm and 4am. During the [Kindred Judgment](/wiki/Skyrim:Kindred_Judgment "Skyrim:Kindred Judgment") quest, she will be in the crowd when [Isran](/wiki/Skyrim:Isran "Skyrim:Isran") holds his [speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Isran#Kindred_Judgement "Skyrim:Isran"), although she will not participate in the attack on [Volkihar Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Keep "Skyrim:Volkihar Keep").

She is clad in a set of Dawnguard heavy equipment. This includes the brown [cuirass](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Dawnguard Heavy Armor"), as well as matching pairs of [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Heavy_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Dawnguard Heavy Gauntlets") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Heavy_Boots "Skyrim:Dawnguard Heavy Boots"). She is equipped with a [Dawnguard war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_War_Axe "Skyrim:Dawnguard War Axe") and a [crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Crossbow "Skyrim:Crossbow") supplemented with fifteen [steel bolts](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Bolt "Skyrim:Steel Bolt").

## Dialogue

If you are an elf, she will say:

:   *"Not a lot of our kind here, I see. Suppose I shouldn't be surprised. Still, those of us here have as much of an obligation to protect Skyrim as any other, wouldn't you agree?"*

If you are not an elf, she will instead say:

:   *"Seems that I'm the only of my kind here. Suppose I shouldn't be surprised. Still, it's my duty to help protect Skyrim as much as any Nord's, don't you think?"*

You can also ask her why she joined the Dawnguard:

:   **Why did you join the Dawnguard?**
:   *"[Isran](/wiki/Skyrim:Isran "Skyrim:Isran") is the only one trying to do something about these vampires. I don't much like him, but I can't allow the people of Skyrim to be preyed upon by those monsters."*

## Notes

- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch 3.0.1 changes it so that Beleval participates in the attack on Castle Volkihar.
- Beleval is the only female Bosmer [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") in the game.
- Although her default gear is governed by the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") skill, Beleval is actually more proficient in [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor").

## Bugs

- Due to Beleval using the `FemaleSultry` voicetype, and said voicetype not generally being supported for follower dialogue, many of her follower lines will be completely silent, as they have no corresponding voice file attached.
  - This problem is shared by [Eola](/wiki/Skyrim:Eola "Skyrim:Eola"), another follower making use of this particular voicetype.
- Beleval may not regenerate after entering bleedout because she inherited stats from the bandit templates where the bleedout override checkbox was enabled.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.0.8, fixes this bug.
