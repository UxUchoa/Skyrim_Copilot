Erith

**Erith**, a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Children "Skyrim:Children"), is the daughter of [Daighre](/wiki/Skyrim:Daighre "Skyrim:Daighre"). She can be found in [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine"), just outside [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"), and is usually in the company of her faithful companion [Torom](/wiki/Skyrim:Torom "Skyrim:Torom").

She wears [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Child's Clothes") and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000e80a8 "Skyrim:Clothing"). She carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Daighre%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Daighre's House") to [Daighre's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Daighre%27s_House "Skyrim:Daighre's House"), a selection of [food and toys](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"), and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue

When approached she can say:

:   *"Why is everyone so upset? If there's [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"), the Jarl will take care of them, right?*"
:   *"Hope [Pavo](/wiki/Skyrim:Pavo_Attius "Skyrim:Pavo Attius") will be able to go back to his mine soon. Everyone needs a home."*
:   *"What'cha doing in Left Hand? Do you dig up rocks too, like my mom?"*
:   *"Why do they call it Left Hand Mine? Can the miners use their right hands too?"*
:   *"Have you met [Torom](/wiki/Skyrim:Torom "Skyrim:Torom")? He's the best dog in the Reach."*

## Conversations

:   **Daighre**: *"[Skaggi](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaggi_Scar-Face "Skyrim:Skaggi Scar-Face") tells me that you were bothering him today. Can't you and Torom just play by yourselves?"*
:   **Erith**: *"But mom, it's so boring with just me and Torom. I was just asking Skaggi some questions."*
:   **Daighre**: *"You leave him alone. I don't want to hear Skaggi telling me you've been bothering him again!"*
:   **Erith**: *"Yes, ma'am."*

:   **Erith**: *"Mama, can we talk. You know, about papa?"*
:   **Daighre**: *"Of course, sweetie. What'd you like to know?"*
:   **Erith**: *"I don't know. What'd he look like?"*
:   **Daighre**: *"He had your soft face, and that silly grin you get when you've done something wrong. That's his, too."*

## Notes

- Should you choose to adopt Erith, you will see Torom in your house every so often since his AI will have him seek out Erith, regardless of her location.

## Bugs

- Erith's bed isn't a child's bed, so she stays up all night.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.0.8, fixes this bug.
