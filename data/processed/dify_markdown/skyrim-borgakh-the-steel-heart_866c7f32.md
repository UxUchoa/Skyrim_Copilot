Borgakh the Steel Heart

**Borgakh the Steel Heart** is an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:GuardOrc1H "Skyrim:GuardOrc1H") and potential [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") found in [Mor Khazgur](/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur "Skyrim:Mor Khazgur"). During the day, she can be found using the training dummy in front of [Larak's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Larak%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Larak's Longhouse"), and at night, sleeping inside. Although she is proud of Orc traditions and willing to abide by them, she seems uneasy about what her future may hold.

She wears [heavy armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Armor_2 "Skyrim:Armor"), including [heavy boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots_2 "Skyrim:Armor") and [heavy gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Gauntlets_2 "Skyrim:Armor"), and she keeps a set of [ragged trousers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers "Skyrim:Ragged Trousers") for sleeping. She carries a [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") and [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Shields_2 "Skyrim:Armor"), a [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow") with 20 [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrow "Skyrim:Arrow"), a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Larak%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Key to Larak's Longhouse") to the longhouse, and a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). Her armor and weapons are leveled, and can be up to [Orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish "Skyrim:Orcish") in quality.

## Dialogue

Her greetings:

:   *"I envy you, outlander. You can choose to leave if you wish."*
:   *"Soon, I will be of age. The taste is bitter."*
:   *"Perhaps I'll join the Legion one day, as my father had done."*

When spoken to:

:   **What do you do for the stronghold?**
:   *"I am [Larak](/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Larak "Skyrim:Chief Larak")'s daughter. Soon, I'll be of age and will be sent to marry an Orc chief in some distant stronghold. Until then, I train. My new home will need strength, and my new sisters will know I'm not weak when I arrive."*

:   **You don't know who you'll marry?**
:   *"No. A chief's daughter marries who her chief wishes. It ensures the stronghold receives a fair trade in goods for my absence. I used to look forward to the day when I was of age, to see new places and people, but now it feels like a cage."*

:   **You want to leave the stronghold?**
:   *"I couldn't. It would shame my chief, and mother, and foremothers."*

If you are successful, she becomes a potential [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"), as well as a candidate for [marriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage"), and can be recruited into the [Blades](/wiki/Skyrim:Blades "Skyrim:Blades").

## Unused Dialogue

The game's DialogueViews files include an early iteration of her response to you persuading her. Said line notably includes the term **Mauloch**, instead of Malacath. Both names are in use by the Daedric Prince, but Malacath is much more prominently used:

:   *"You're right. Mauloch teaches us to meet our fate. Perhaps this is mine."*

## Notes

- Once she is following you, you can simply take back the gold you gave her for the dowry if you are in need of it.

## Bugs

- When married, she lacks most of the store dialogue, leaving it unvoiced.
- If you marry Borgakh and [adopt](/wiki/Skyrim:Adoption "Skyrim:Adoption") a [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire"), she cannot be asked how the children are doing as she has no recorded dialogue for it.
- Borgakh's confidence level is so high, you cannot intimidate her no matter how high your [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill is.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- Her class, `GuardOrc1H` incorrectly levels her Magicka instead of Stamina.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 2.3.1, addresses this issue. Changing it to level her and other Orc Warriors' Stamina rather than Magicka.
