Eydis

**Eydis** is a dead [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:EncClassBanditMelee "Skyrim:EncClassBanditMelee") who can be found in [Fahlbtharz](/wiki/Skyrim:Fahlbtharz "Skyrim:Fahlbtharz"). She wears a [Skaal coat](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Coat "Skyrim:Skaal Coat") with matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Boots "Skyrim:Skaal Boots") and [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Hat "Skyrim:Skaal Hat"). She carries an enchanted [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe") and her [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Eydis%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Eydis's Journal"), and may also carry a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and a small amount of gold. Reading her journal will reveal that she came to Fahlbtharz with [Ulyn](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulyn "Skyrim:Ulyn") to help him search for the [Visage of Mzund](/wiki/Skyrim:Visage_of_Mzund "Skyrim:Visage of Mzund"), an enchanted Dwarven helmet. She and Ulyn were forced to defend themselves against [Dwarven spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Spider "Skyrim:Dwarven Spider"), and [Ulyn's journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulyn%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Ulyn's Journal") indicates that he went further into the ruin while she stayed back to hold them off—to her obvious misfortune.
