## Quick Walkthrough

**Note**: This quest no longer appears in your journal following the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition") update.

1. Read a [notice of sale](/wiki/Skyrim:Notice_of_Sale_-_Dwarven_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Notice of Sale - Dwarven Mudcrab").
2. Buy a mudcrab from [Calcelmo](/wiki/Skyrim:Calcelmo "Skyrim:Calcelmo").

**Note**: You can also steal [notice of sale](/wiki/Skyrim:Notice_of_Sale_-_Dwarven_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Notice of Sale - Dwarven Mudcrab") from Calcemo to acquire ownership and the associated spell. This works on unpatched versions, such as the Nintendo Switch.

## Detailed Walkthrough

**Note: This quest was originally added to your journal upon installing the [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Dwarven Armored Mudcrab") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition"), this quest no longer appears in your journal. The mudcrab can still be purchased from [Calcelmo](/wiki/Skyrim:Calcelmo "Skyrim:Calcelmo").**

Once you've installed the Dwarven Armored Mudcrab Creation, you will receive this quest and a [notice of sale](/wiki/Skyrim:Notice_of_Sale_-_Dwarven_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Notice of Sale - Dwarven Mudcrab"), which directs you to talk to Calcelmo in [Understone Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep "Skyrim:Understone Keep").

Travel to Understone Keep in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") and meet Calcelmo in the ruins section of the keep. From him, you'll be able to purchase the [ownership deed](/wiki/Skyrim:Ownership_Deed_-_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Ownership Deed - Mudcrab"). Once you've done so, you'll find the [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab_(creature) "Skyrim:Dwarven Armored Mudcrab (creature)") beside the arcane enchanter. You'll also receive a [spell tome](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Teleport_Pet_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Teleport Pet Mudcrab") that teaches you a spell to [teleport the mudcrab](/wiki/Skyrim:Teleport_Pet:_Dwarven_Mudcrab "Skyrim:Teleport Pet: Dwarven Mudcrab") to you.

The mudcrab serves as an animal follower, with the added ability to carry items and allow you to send it to specific locations.
