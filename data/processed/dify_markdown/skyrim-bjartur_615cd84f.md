Bjartur, in the Castle Dour Dungeon

**Bjartur** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [prisoner](/wiki/Skyrim:Prisoner_(class) "Skyrim:Prisoner (class)") found in [Castle Dour Dungeon](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour_Dungeon "Skyrim:Castle Dour Dungeon") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), apparently jailed for being a staunch supporter of the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks").

She wears a [roughspun tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic "Skyrim:Roughspun Tunic") and carries a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic").

Without anything to do inside her jail cell, her daily routine involves standing in one place, at times conversing with the patrolling jailor and headsman [Ahtar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ahtar "Skyrim:Ahtar") through the bars. She sleeps from midnight to 8am.

## Dialogue

While you are in the dungeon during her waking hours, you were meant to hear her loudly exclaim the following lines, which can't play due to a [bug](#Bugs):

:   *"You won't get anything out of me, you filthy sons o' horkers."*
:   *"I stand with the Stormcloaks. You Imperial dogs will never break my will. Never!"*

If you attempt to talk to her through the bars she will defiantly say:

:   *"You here for my next interrogation? I got nothing but time..."*
:   *"Leave me be, Imperial. I haven't got anything to say to you."*
:   *"Don't think I don't know what's coming."*
:   *"It's only a matter of time before Ahtar's blade finds my neck..."*

## Unused Dialogue

Because it is impossible to engage in conversation with her, these two farewells will never be heard:

:   *"Tell these Horkers to set me free."*
:   *"Leave me be. I'm no harm here."*

## Bugs

- Her two idle yelling lines have a condition attached that they only play while on the package `SolitudeBlacksmithWorkDay8x14`. However, seeing as Bjartur doesn't use said package at any point (as she doesn't work at the [Solitude Blacksmith](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Blacksmith "Skyrim:Solitude Blacksmith") and has no ties to it at all), they go unused.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

## Notes

- Bjartur will never get released, not even if the Stormcloaks take over Solitude.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.2, addresses this issue. She is disabled from the game in case of a Stormcloak victory.
- For some reason, she is one of only a handful of people that have a relationship set up with [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke"). They are marked as rivals.
