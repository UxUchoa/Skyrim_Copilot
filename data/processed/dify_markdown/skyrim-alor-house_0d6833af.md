Alor House

**Alor House** is the home of [Fethis Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fethis_Alor "Skyrim:Fethis Alor") and his daughter [Dreyla Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Dreyla_Alor "Skyrim:Dreyla Alor") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

It is in the marketplace between the path to [Raven Rock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Mine "Skyrim:Raven Rock Mine") to the west and [The Retching Netch](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Retching_Netch "Skyrim:The Retching Netch") to the southeast.

### Exterior

In front of the entrance is where Fethis Alor generally sells his wares, with a narrow table holding five bottles of [sujamma](/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma "Skyrim:Sujamma") nearby. To the left of the entrance is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), and an open box containing two more bottles of sujamma. To the right of the entrance are two ash yam urns, a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"), another woodcutter's axe, and two [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plants. The entrance has an apprentice-level lock.

### Alor House

The entrance leads into the upper floor of the house. Along the south wall are three straw bales, an ingredient barrel, a fish barrel, two meat barrels, two food barrels, one of which has a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on top, an open box containing a copy of *[Words of the Wind](/wiki/Skyrim:Words_of_the_Wind "Skyrim:Words of the Wind")*, and a table holding two bottles of sujamma, a bottle of [shein](/wiki/Skyrim:Shein "Skyrim:Shein"), two bottles of alto wine, three bottles of wine, and a *[note](/wiki/Skyrim:Dearest_Dinya "Skyrim:Dearest Dinya")* to Dreyla. Near the west wall is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand in front of the fire, with two chairs nearby. Along the north wall are three food barrels, a meat barrel, three unusable barrels, three crates, a sideboard holding a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and copies of all seven volumes of *[The Poison Song](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song "Skyrim:The Poison Song")*, five sacks, an ash yam urn, and two loose pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") on a wood pile. In the middle of the room, stairs lead down to the lower floor, with three straw bales, two ash yam urns, and a bench around the sides of the stairs.

At the bottom of the stairs, to the left of a doorway are two unusable barrels. Beyond the doorway is an area with rooms beyond doors to the southeast and northeast and a crafting area ahead in an alcove to the west. In the main area is a tanning rack, three sacks, a meat barrel, many unusable barrels, one of which has a food urn on top, and many crates. In the alcove to the west are four crates, a table holding a bottle of sujamma, a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), an open box containing seven [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot"), and a chair.

The room to the southeast is Fethis Alor's bedroom. Along the north wall is an owned double bed with a chest at its foot, and a sideboard holding a bowl of [cabbage potato soup](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage_Potato_Soup "Skyrim:Cabbage Potato Soup"), a [potion of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Stamina"), a bottle of sujamma, and copies of *[The Red Year, Vol. I](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Year,_Vol._I "Skyrim:The Red Year, Vol. I")* and the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Hallgerd's Tale](/wiki/Skyrim:Hallgerd%27s_Tale "Skyrim:Hallgerd's Tale")*. Near the east wall are three unusable barrels, two chairs, and a table holding three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), an [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox"), a [steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Steel War Axe"), and a copy of *[The Axe Man](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Axe_Man "Skyrim:The Axe Man")*. Along the south wall is a fish barrel, a set of shelves holding two empty [petty soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem") and a large coin purse, with three letters to Fethis Alor titled *[first letter from EEC](/wiki/Skyrim:First_Letter_from_EEC "Skyrim:First Letter from EEC")*, *[second letter from EEC](/wiki/Skyrim:Second_Letter_from_EEC "Skyrim:Second Letter from EEC")*, and *[third letter from EEC](/wiki/Skyrim:Third_Letter_from_EEC "Skyrim:Third Letter from EEC")* on the floor in front of the shelves, and two ingredient urns.

The room to the northeast is Dreyla Alor's bedroom. Along the north wall are two unusable barrels and a bench. Near the east wall is a meat barrel, an owned single bed, a table holding a copy of *[Bone, Part I](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone,_Part_I "Skyrim:Bone, Part I")* and a bottle of shein, and a chair. Along the south wall are two meat barrels, a set of shelves holding a chest, copies of *[The House of Troubles](/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Troubles "Skyrim:The House of Troubles")* and all three volumes of *[History of Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock "Skyrim:History of Raven Rock")*, three [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"), three [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), and two [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), and a sack.
