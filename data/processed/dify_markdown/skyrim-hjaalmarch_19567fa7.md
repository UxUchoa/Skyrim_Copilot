The salt marshes of Hjaalmarch

**Hjaalmarch** is a [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in northern [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), with its capital in [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal"). It is initially affiliated with the [Empire](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"), though the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Idgrod_Ravencrone "Skyrim:Idgrod Ravencrone") laments the war and reportedly co-operates with the Empire only as far as their goals align. It can, however be captured by the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") after completing [A False Front](/wiki/Skyrim:A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:A False Front (Stormcloaks)") and [The Battle for Fort Snowhawk](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Snowhawk (Stormcloaks)").

Despite its position just east of the capital of Skyrim, [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), Hjaalmarch is remote and isolated. The enormous, festering swamp dominating the northern part of the hold could be a factor, as could be the tall, snowy mountains in the south, and the ancient, cursed city of [Labyrinthian](/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian "Skyrim:Labyrinthian") blocking a possible trade route to [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold").

Hjaalmarch is one of the poorest holds in Skyrim, second only to [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)"), and is sustained by the Morthal lumber mill and the mining town of [Stonehills](/wiki/Skyrim:Stonehills "Skyrim:Stonehills"). [Jarl Idgrod Ravencrone](/wiki/Skyrim:Idgrod_Ravencrone "Skyrim:Idgrod Ravencrone") takes a hands-off approach to leadership; her people are unhappy with her governance and several will complain openly.

## Topographical Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-flora-Swamp_Fungal_Pod.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Swamp_Fungal_Pod.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-flora-Swamp_Fungal_Pod.jpg)

Swamp fungal pods are endemic to Hjaalmarch

Hjaalmarch is well known for the salt marshes which dominate the north and central areas of the hold, stretching from [Fort Snowhawk](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Snowhawk "Skyrim:Fort Snowhawk") in the west to [Ustengrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Ustengrav "Skyrim:Ustengrav") in the east, formed by the River Hjaal breaking into many tiny streams. Much of the rest of the hold more resembles neighboring holds, for example, in the south and east of the hold, the terrain is more resemblant of [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale") along the border with that hold - snowy pine forest and plains broken up by crags around [the Stormcloak camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp") and Ustengrav. The southern half is mountainous terrain above the snow line, although in the west, the mountains give way to the Hjaal river valley, where the climate is more resemblant of neighboring [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold"). Beyond Fort Snowhawk, in the hold's western extremities lie pine forests and meadows, much like those found just across the [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River") in [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar").

### Flora and Fauna

The swamp boasts the highest numbers of wild [deathbell plants](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") in Skyrim, as well as being the only place to find [swamp fungal pods](/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod "Skyrim:Swamp Fungal Pod") and [giant lichen](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Lichen "Skyrim:Giant Lichen"). These ingredients all boast powerful poison effects for [alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy"), making Hjaalmarch an ideal place to visit for assassins wishing to brew deadly poisons. Other plants include [canis roots](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella"), [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Spiky_Grass "Skyrim:Spiky Grass") and a few scattered [nightshade plants](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade"). The snowy areas have few usable plants besides the humble [snowberry bush](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry "Skyrim:Snowberry"), but there are some [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") and [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") to be found, with the blue variant especially abundant around Morthal.

As for fauna, all manner of beasts roam the swamp; even [chaurus](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus "Skyrim:Chaurus") might ambush you at night, but more commonly [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider") and [trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll "Skyrim:Troll") lurk between the trees. Of course, [mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab") are always in abundance, from the swamp to the banks of the River Hjaal. Your typical predators, [bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear") and [sabre cats](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Sabre Cat"), and their prey, [deer](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer "Skyrim:Deer") and [goats](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat"), roam the south, but there are also more trolls to watch out for. Passive creatures include large amounts of [fish](/wiki/Skyrim:Fish "Skyrim:Fish") in the shallow waters with [dragonflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonfly "Skyrim:Dragonfly") circling above.

### Routes and Pathways

A single paved road runs from the west near [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge"), past [Fort Snowhawk](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Snowhawk "Skyrim:Fort Snowhawk") (where [the residents](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer") may attack you even if you stay on the path) and [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal"), curving north around the [Stonehills](/wiki/Skyrim:Stonehills "Skyrim:Stonehills") area before entering the Pale.

The most notable pathway starts southwest of Stonehills and ends in [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold") between [Hamvir's Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Hamvir%27s_Rest "Skyrim:Hamvir's Rest") and [Silent Moons Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Silent_Moons_Camp "Skyrim:Silent Moons Camp"), passing through the ruins of Labyrinthian along the way. Other paths run from the road east of Fort Snowhawk south to [Dead Men's Respite](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Men%27s_Respite "Skyrim:Dead Men's Respite"), and north from Morthal to [Movarth's Lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Movarth%27s_Lair "Skyrim:Movarth's Lair"). Beyond that the swamp is pathless, however, so beware of getting lost.

## Hjaalmarch Quests

All quests that are started in or are related to Hjaalmarch (except for radiant quests begun in other holds) are listed here:

### Main Quest

- **[The Horn of Jurgen Windcaller](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Horn_of_Jurgen_Windcaller "Skyrim:The Horn of Jurgen Windcaller")**: Retrieve a [Greybeard](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards") artifact from [Ustengrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Ustengrav "Skyrim:Ustengrav").

### Stormcloaks Civil War Quests

- **[Liberation of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Liberation_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Liberation of Skyrim")**: Drive the Imperials from Skyrim. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[A False Front](/wiki/Skyrim:A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:A False Front (Stormcloaks)")**: Find and deliver some forged documents to the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion").
- **[The Battle for Fort Snowhawk](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Snowhawk (Stormcloaks)")**: Take [this fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Snowhawk "Skyrim:Fort Snowhawk") for the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

### Daedric Quests

- **[Pieces of the Past](/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past "Skyrim:Pieces of the Past")**: Retrieve the scattered pieces of [Mehrunes' Razor](/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor "Skyrim:Mehrunes' Razor") and reforge this deadly blade.

### Dark Brotherhood

- **[Kill Lurbuk](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Lurbuk "Skyrim:Kill Lurbuk")**: Silence a [terrible bard](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurbuk "Skyrim:Lurbuk"), permanently.
- **[With Friends Like These...](/wiki/Skyrim:With_Friends_Like_These... "Skyrim:With Friends Like These...")**: Repay your debt to [the Family](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood"), and show you are willing to kill on command.
- **[Kill Maluril](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Maluril "Skyrim:Kill Maluril")**: Kill [Maluril](/wiki/Skyrim:Maluril "Skyrim:Maluril") in [Mzinchaleft](/wiki/Skyrim:Mzinchaleft "Skyrim:Mzinchaleft").

### Bards College

- **[Tending the Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Tending_the_Flames "Skyrim:Tending the Flames")**: Unveil the truth about a deceitful [King](/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_One-Eye "Skyrim:Olaf One-Eye") and become a member of the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College").

### Side Quests

- **[Forbidden Legend](/wiki/Skyrim:Forbidden_Legend "Skyrim:Forbidden Legend")**: Investigate the forgotten [Gauldur Legend](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends "Skyrim:Lost Legends").
- **[Laid to Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Laid_to_Rest "Skyrim:Laid to Rest")**: Investigate a tragic house fire, and discover an insidious plot against the town.
- **[Rising at Dawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_at_Dawn "Skyrim:Rising at Dawn")**: Track down [Falion](/wiki/Skyrim:Falion "Skyrim:Falion") to rid yourself of [vampirism](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampirism "Skyrim:Vampirism").

### Creation Club Quests

- **[Let Sleeping Wolves Lie](/wiki/Skyrim:Let_Sleeping_Wolves_Lie "Skyrim:Let Sleeping Wolves Lie")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Wolf "Skyrim:Bone Wolf"): Stop a necromancer from raising undead wolf minions.
- **[Myrwatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Myrwatch_(quest) "Skyrim:Myrwatch (quest)")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Myrwatch "Skyrim:Myrwatch"): Locate and enter a [mysterious tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Myrwatch_(place) "Skyrim:Myrwatch (place)").
