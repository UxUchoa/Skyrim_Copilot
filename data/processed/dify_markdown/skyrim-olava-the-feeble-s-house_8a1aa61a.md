**Olava the Feeble's House** is a small house in the southern part of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). It is located right behind [Breezehome](/wiki/Skyrim:Breezehome "Skyrim:Breezehome").

It is the residence of elderly seer [Olava the Feeble](/wiki/Skyrim:Olava_the_Feeble "Skyrim:Olava the Feeble") and consists of one area only, *[Olava the Feeble's House](#Olava_the_Feeble.27s_House)*.

## Olava the Feeble's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-interior-Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House.jpg)

Olava's House

The house is among the smallest houses in Whiterun and contains very little of value. The front door opens to a huge open fire with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") and some hanging [Dried Elves Ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear"). The table on the left holds a [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") while the single shelf on the wall holds two [soul gem fragments](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gem_Fragment "Skyrim:Soul Gem Fragment") and the end table contains a few pieces of gold.

To the right of the front door is Olava's owned bed and a cupboard with clutter. On the shelves above sits some food and, a coin purse, a book *[The Holds of Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:The_Holds_of_Skyrim "Lore:The Holds of Skyrim")*, and a sack with food or [Salt](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"). The small alcove to the west has a chest with some gold and pelts, an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"). On the adjacent shelves, above the two food barrels, sits a sample of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"), a [potion of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Enhanced Stamina"), a [potion of brief invisibility](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Brief_Invisibility "Skyrim:Potion of Brief Invisibility") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine").
