Addict

The **Addict** is an [elderly](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder "Skyrim:Elder") [citizen](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)") found dead in [Redwater Den](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Den "Skyrim:Redwater Den") in one of the booths set up for enjoying [Redwater skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma "Skyrim:Redwater Skooma"). She wears a [roughspun tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic "Skyrim:Roughspun Tunic") and [footwraps](/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps "Skyrim:Footwraps").
