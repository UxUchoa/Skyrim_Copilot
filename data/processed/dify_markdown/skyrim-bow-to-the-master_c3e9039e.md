## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Dravin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dravin_Llanith "Skyrim:Dravin Llanith") at [Merryfair Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Merryfair_Farm "Skyrim:Merryfair Farm").
2. Find [Dravin's Bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items#Dravin.27s_Bow "Skyrim:Quest Items") in [The Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway").
3. Return to Dravin.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[Dravin Llanith](/wiki/Skyrim:Dravin_Llanith "Skyrim:Dravin Llanith") is a farmer who lives at [Merryfair Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Merryfair_Farm "Skyrim:Merryfair Farm"). Talk to him and he will tell you about his stolen family heirloom, a bow. A few days ago, some thieves from Riften broke into his house and stole it. He is quite sure those [lowlifes](/wiki/Skyrim:Lowlife "Skyrim:Lowlife") took the bow with them back to [The Ratway](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway "Skyrim:The Ratway"). If you can head into the Ratway and get his bow back, he would pay you what he can.

The Ratway is somewhere under Riften, these sewers and tunnels are inhabited by some [skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever") and thugs. Pay attention to the traps at the same time. In order to retrieve the bow, you have to get into The Ratway Vaults. The only way to get to the vaults is through [The Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon") — the door is to the right of the bar. It's not necessary to join the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") to get access to the entrance. However, it would be a good choice to do this quest during [Taking Care of Business](/wiki/Skyrim:Taking_Care_of_Business "Skyrim:Taking Care of Business"). You can find the bow in a chest in a room near the stairs, guarded by a Vagrant and a Lowlife.

Return to Dravin after you get his bow. He will be very excited and reward you with five leveled gems. *"I don't know how you got it back, and I don't want to know. Frankly, I don't even care... I'm just so glad to see it again."*

## Notes

- Several side quests require a certain type of gem(s) so it may be worth making a save point just before talking to him. Loading a previous save will change the gems rewarded thus allowing you to choose, as long as you meet the level requirement for gems.

## Bugs

- If you enchant (and possibly also rename, confirmation needed) the bow, it may lose its quest item tag, and thus you can drop it normally. **?**
  - This might only apply to Special Edition.
- If you enchant and rename the bow, its name may revert back to "Dravin's Bow." The enchantment remains unaffected. **?**
  - This might only apply to Special Edition.
- If you find Dravin's Bow before being assigned the quest, you may be unable to return it to him even if he tells you about his stolen bow after you have found it.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.1, addresses this issue. You may need to save and reload to get the fix to work—using "Continue" from the main menu may not work.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") If this occurs, you can use the console command `setstage FreeformMerryfairFarm 30` to advance the quest to the correct stage, allowing you to complete it.
