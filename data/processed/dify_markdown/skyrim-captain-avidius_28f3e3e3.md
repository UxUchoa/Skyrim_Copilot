Captain Avidius

**Captain Avidius** is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [Penitus Oculatus](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_(class) "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus (class)") who is found on [The Katariah](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Katariah "Skyrim:The Katariah") during the quest [Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!"). He wears [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0006ff45 "Skyrim:Clothes") with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d "Skyrim:Boots") and is armed with an [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword"). He carries the [Katariah Master Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Katariah_Master_Key "Skyrim:Katariah Master Key"), along with two leveled potions of [restore health](/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Health "Skyrim:Restore Health") and at least 25 gold.
