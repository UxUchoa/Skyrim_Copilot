**Solitude Sewers** is a sewer system located in western Solitude, overrun with [Sheogorath](/wiki/Skyrim:Sheogorath "Skyrim:Sheogorath")'s influence. The sewers are being encroached upon by a [root tunnel](/wiki/Lore:Root "Lore:Root") from the [Shivering Isles](/wiki/Lore:Shivering_Isles "Lore:Shivering Isles"). The sewers themselves are inaccessible until starting [Restoring Order](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoring_Order "Skyrim:Restoring Order"). It is inhabited by [Elytra](/wiki/Skyrim:Elytra_Nymph "Skyrim:Elytra Nymph") and [Corrupted Spriggans](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan") resembling [Gnarls](/wiki/Lore:Gnarl "Lore:Gnarl").

Be sure to loot the many [hollowed stumps](/wiki/Skyrim:Hollowed_Stump "Skyrim:Hollowed Stump") throughout the dungeon, as they usually contain [amber](/wiki/Skyrim:Amber "Skyrim:Amber"), a rare material needed to craft amber arms and armor. Right before reaching [Thoron](/wiki/Skyrim:Thoron "Skyrim:Thoron")'s lair, you will discover a bunch of [Golden](/wiki/Skyrim:Golden "Skyrim:Golden") and [Dark](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark "Skyrim:Dark") equipment scattered around. A [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_Strange_Arms_and_Armor "Skyrim:Note on Strange Arms and Armor") on the barrel next to the battlefield will reveal that this was the site of a battle between rival [Golden Saints](/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Saint "Skyrim:Golden Saint") and [Dark Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Seducer "Skyrim:Dark Seducer"), and that the [Saint](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_Bandit "Skyrim:Saints Bandit") and [Seducer bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Seducers_Bandit "Skyrim:Seducers Bandit") took their equipment from this site. Further along is Thoron's lair, where you will find that he is conducting a ritual with the [Sword of Jyggalag](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword_of_Jyggalag "Skyrim:Sword of Jyggalag"). He is a tough opponent, but after slaying him, his [journals](/wiki/Skyrim:Thoron%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Thoron's Journal") reveal that madness had already taken root underneath Solitude, as these root tunnels were already here when Thoron took residence. He only deepened the breach between [Tamriel](/wiki/Lore:Tamriel "Lore:Tamriel") and the Shivering Isles by using the Sword in his attempt to return to the Isles.

After killing Thoron, be sure to loot the nearby table of the rare [madness ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Madness_Ore "Skyrim:Madness Ore") which is needed for another [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Nerveshatter_(quest) "Skyrim:Nerveshatter (quest)") and the unique [Sheogorath-Shaped Amber](/wiki/Skyrim:Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber "Skyrim:Sheogorath-Shaped Amber"). Additionally, be sure to read the *[Note on Amber and Madness Ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_Amber_and_Madness_Ore "Skyrim:Note on Amber and Madness Ore")* to obtain a [miscellaneous quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Amber_and_Madness_Smithing "Skyrim:Amber and Madness Smithing") to unlock the secrets of forging madness and amber gear. There's also an Alteration skill book *[Reality & Other Falsehoods](/wiki/Skyrim:Reality_%26_Other_Falsehoods "Skyrim:Reality & Other Falsehoods")* next to Thoron's cot.

## Notes

- The dungeon ambient track "Wind from the Depths" from the *[Oblivion](/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion "Oblivion:Oblivion")* [soundtrack](/wiki/Oblivion:Music "Oblivion:Music") plays while in this dungeon.
- The root tunnel dungeon tile set this dungeon uses was added to the base game and is fully available as a modder's resource.[[1]](#cite_note-SaintsandSeducers-1)

## Bugs

- [![Nintendo Switch Logo.svg](//images.uesp.net/5/5d/Nintendo_Switch_Logo.svg)](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch "Skyrim:Switch") The "Wind from the Depths" track does not play in the Solitude Sewers. **?**
- The plants here are unaffected by the [Green Thumb](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Thumb "Skyrim:Green Thumb") perk.
  - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch"), version 1.6.1130, fixes this bug.
- The Note on Strange Arms and Armor and the Note on Amber and Madness Ore both respawn when the dungeon itself does.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.3, fixes this bug.
