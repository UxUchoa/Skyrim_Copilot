Daighre

**Daighre** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)") living in [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine"), just outside [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). She is the mother of [Erith](/wiki/Skyrim:Erith "Skyrim:Erith").

Daighre wears a set of [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"). Like most miners, she is equipped with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Daighre%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Daighre's House") to [her house](/wiki/Skyrim:Daighre%27s_House "Skyrim:Daighre's House"), as well as a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Related Quests

- **[Kolskeggr Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine_(quest) "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine (quest)")**: Clear the [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") from [Kolskeggr Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine").

### Kolskeggr Mine

When the [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") attack her friends she will feel very sorry for them and during the quest will remark:

:   *"Poor [Pavo](/wiki/Skyrim:Pavo_Attius "Skyrim:Pavo Attius") and [Gat](/wiki/Skyrim:Gat_gro-Shargakh "Skyrim:Gat gro-Shargakh"). To have lived through a Forsworn attack."*
:   *"Another day, another settlement gets attacked. No place in the [Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach") is safe from the Forsworn."*

## Dialogue

When approached she can say:

:   *"I'm the hardest worker in Left Hand. Better than any man."*
:   *"Most women would rather die than dig, but I have a daughter to raise."*
:   *"My Erith has her father's eyes. She's all I have left of him."*

## Conversations

She may randomly have conversations with her daughter or her boss, [Skaggi Scar-Face](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaggi_Scar-Face "Skyrim:Skaggi Scar-Face"):

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-npc-Skaggi_Scar-Face_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Skaggi_Scar-Face_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Skaggi_Scar-Face_03.jpg)

*"I'm sorry my daughter gives you so much trouble."*

:   **Daighre**: *"Skaggi tells me that you were bothering him today. Can't you and [Torom](/wiki/Skyrim:Torom "Skyrim:Torom") just play by yourselves?"*
:   **Erith**: *"But mom, it's so boring with just me and Torom. I was just asking Skaggi some questions."*
:   **Daighre**: *"You leave him alone. I don't want to hear Skaggi telling me you've been bothering him again!"*
:   **Erith**: *"Yes, ma'am."*

:   **Erith**: *"Mama, can we talk. You know, about papa?"*
:   **Daighre**: *"Of course, sweetie. What'd you like to know?"*
:   **Erith**: *"I don't know. What'd he look like?"*
:   **Daighre**: *"He had your soft face, and that silly grin you get when you've done something wrong. That's his, too."*

:   **Skaggi**: *"You work hard, Daighre. Glad you have stayed with us."*
:   **Daighre**: *"I'm grateful, Skaggi. I'm sorry my daughter gives you so much trouble."*

:   **Daighre**: *"Do you think the Forsworn will come here? I mean, if they attacked [Kolskeggr](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine")...."*
:   **Skaggi**: *"Let's just hope that doesn't happen."*
