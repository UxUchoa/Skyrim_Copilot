**The Lord Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones") scattered across Skyrim.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-place-The_Lord_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Lord_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Lord_Stone_(active).jpg)

The Lord Stone, when activated

Activating The Lord Stone will add 50 points to your [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor") rating and 25% [magic resistance](/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Magic "Skyrim:Resist Magic"). This makes it easier to reach the [armor rating](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor_Rating "Skyrim:Armor Rating") and [magic resistance](/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Magic "Skyrim:Resist Magic") caps. Its effect is generic and useful for characters of all levels and play styles, providing decent all-around protection. [The Atronach Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Atronach_Stone "Skyrim:The Atronach Stone") is more effective at mitigating magic, but comes with a drawback and offers no physical protection.

## The Lord Stone

The Lord Stone is located on top of a stone base with the remains of a large arch on either side of it. It can be reached by following the path that leads past the [Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Mehrunes_Dagon "Skyrim:Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon"). The path starts to the west of the [Hall of the Vigilant](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Vigilant "Skyrim:Hall of the Vigilant"). There are several piles of rocks marking the route; some have the remnants of banners hanging from them and flapping in the wind. You'll pass [Dimhollow Crypt](/wiki/Skyrim:Dimhollow_Crypt "Skyrim:Dimhollow Crypt")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") along the path; once you reach a long flight of stone steps cut into the mountain leading up to the Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon, turn to face northeast. You'll see the Lord Stone a short distance up a hill.

The Lord Stone is home to a group of three [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"), at least one of whom is an archer. There are three unowned bed rolls arranged around a small campfire in front of the standing stone. There is also a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") at the southwest corner of the base of the structure upon which the stone is set. The path near the shrine is home to a leveled animal, which can be an [ice wolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf "Skyrim:Ice Wolf"), [ice wraith](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith"), [snow bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear "Skyrim:Snow Bear") or [frost troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll"), depending on your level when on the mountain pass.

## Notes

- A [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") with The Lord Stone and [Agent of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Agent_of_Mara "Skyrim:Agent of Mara") power will have a permanent 65% resistance to all magic. This can be achieved straight out of [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound "Skyrim:Unbound") at level 1 with almost no combat involved.
- Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") assigns The Lord Stone to [The Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale") instead.
