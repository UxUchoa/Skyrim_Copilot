**Alchemists** are dead [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [conjurers](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjurer_(class) "Skyrim:Conjurer (class)") found throughout Skyrim. They all wear [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") and [Black Mage Robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes"). They all carry a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"), a rare [ingredient](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"), and a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"). There is one in a cell in [Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall "Skyrim:Fort Greenwall"), one in a hanging cage outside of [Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dunstad "Skyrim:Fort Dunstad"), one at the giant camp [Cradlecrush Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Cradlecrush_Rock "Skyrim:Cradlecrush Rock"), one on a rock in a river near [Talking Stone Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Talking_Stone_Camp "Skyrim:Talking Stone Camp"), and one on a sunken altar at [Evergreen Grove](/wiki/Skyrim:Evergreen_Grove "Skyrim:Evergreen Grove"). They can be a good source of ingredients in the beginning of the game.

The dead female alchemist in Clearpine Pond

This **Alchemist** is a [High Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Elf "Skyrim:High Elf") [conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjurer_(class) "Skyrim:Conjurer (class)") found at [Clearpine Pond](/wiki/Skyrim:Clearpine_Pond "Skyrim:Clearpine Pond") up against a fallen tree. She wears [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12 "Skyrim:Boots"). She carries a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"), a [rare ingredient](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"), a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"), and a copy of the [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[De Rerum Dirennis](/wiki/Skyrim:De_Rerum_Dirennis "Skyrim:De Rerum Dirennis")*. Beside her is an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") containing one common, uncommon, and rare ingredients each, with a 25% chance of containing one random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), [of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), or [of stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"). There are also two [taproots](/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot "Skyrim:Taproot") and a [blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort") found beside her, along with an empty [flower basket](/wiki/Skyrim:Flower_Basket "Skyrim:Flower Basket").

## Notes

- One more alchemist (see BaseID 00099805 above) is found in [Volkihar Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Keep "Skyrim:Volkihar Keep") after [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") is installed, on a table in [Ronthil's](/wiki/Skyrim:Ronthil "Skyrim:Ronthil") quarters.
- Strangely, both types of Alchemists know the spells [Conjure Familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Familiar "Skyrim:Conjure Familiar"), [Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Flames "Skyrim:Flames"), [Oakflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Oakflesh "Skyrim:Oakflesh"), and [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward"), even though they are dead and cannot use them.

Three **alchemists** can be found dead in Solstheim.

|  |
| --- |
| - One is found inside the [Altar of Thrond Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Altar_of_Thrond "Skyrim:Altar of Thrond"). - The second is found at the [Wreck of the Strident Squall](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Strident_Squall "Skyrim:Wreck of the Strident Squall") on the lowest level, the stairs to which are partially concealed underwater. - The third is lying facedown on an [unmarked island](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places#Giant_Nirnroot_Island "Skyrim:Unmarked Places") with giant [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") plants in the far northeast part of the game map (north of [Frossel](/wiki/Skyrim:Frossel "Skyrim:Frossel")). |

All three alchemists wear leveled, enchanted robes; from level 1-7, it will be [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes"). From level 8 and up, the enchantments will match your level, and will fortify either [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction"), [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration"), or [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration"). From level 10, there is also a 25% chance the robes will have a [regenerate magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Regenerate_Magicka "Skyrim:Regenerate Magicka") enchantment. Lastly, all of them wear the same type of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12 "Skyrim:Boots").

They all carry a sample of nirnroot, some random, rare [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"), and a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") (up to glass quality at level 18).
