Bleakwind Basin

**Bleakwind Basin** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") west of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").

## Related Quests

- **[Bounty: Giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Giant "Skyrim:Bounty: Giant")**: Kill a problematic [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") for a bounty reward. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-place-Bleakwind_Basin_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bleakwind_Basin_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Bleakwind_Basin_03.jpg)

View of the camp

Two [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and three [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") occupy the camp, which is centered around a large campfire with two [charred skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") cooking on stakes. An expert-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") is southwest of the fire, with two [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl") and a dead [sabre cat](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Sabre Cat") beside it. Two more mammoth cheese bowls can be found to the north, with two patches of [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") and a tree stump with a patch of [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") growing nearby. To the northeast is a small pool with a [Nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") plant growing on the bank. Unlike most other giant camps, there are no [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes") and very few painted stones.

## Notes

- Bleakwind Basin is in the origin cell (coordinates 0,0) in the [Tamriel](/wiki/Lore:Tamriel "Lore:Tamriel") worldspace grid. Certain loading errors may therefore cause you to be dropped into this cell.

## Bugs

- Fast travelling here can cause the mammoths to spawn high above the ground, then plummet to their deaths.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
