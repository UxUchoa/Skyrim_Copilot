Birna's Oddments

**Birna's Oddments** is a general goods store in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold").

It is a two-story building, located between the [Jarl's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Winterhold)") and [Kraldar's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Kraldar%27s_House "Skyrim:Kraldar's House") and is home to [Birna](/wiki/Skyrim:Birna "Skyrim:Birna") and [Ranmir](/wiki/Skyrim:Ranmir "Skyrim:Ranmir"). It consists of only one zone, which is the [interior](#Birna.27s_Oddments) of the building.

## Birna's Oddments

There is a row of pillars supporting a balcony at the front of the building and stairs up to it at the rear, to the right of a chicken coop containing a single [nest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"). The main entrance leads directly into the shop, with the counter on your right, in front of the north wall. [Birna](/wiki/Skyrim:Birna "Skyrim:Birna") spends her day standing behind the counter. The first time you interact with her she will offer to sell you the [coral dragon claw](/wiki/Skyrim:Coral_Dragon_Claw "Skyrim:Coral Dragon Claw") for 50 septims. There are several barrels and sacks of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") around the building, but no displayed merchandise in the shop. Once you have completed the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol%27s_Barrow "Skyrim:Yngol's Barrow"), or improved Birna's brother [Ranmir](/wiki/Skyrim:Ranmir "Skyrim:Ranmir")'s disposition, you will be able to take all low-value items freely from the shop and residence upstairs.

Opposite the entrance is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk") on the mantelpiece. To the right of the fireplace is an unlocked display cabinet containing two each of [petty](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem"), [lesser](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Lesser Soul Gem"), and [common](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem") [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"). Above this are three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") hanging from a rack attached to the ceiling, two [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid"), two bunches of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear") and a bunch of [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam"). On the counter is a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), four loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), while under the counter is a copy of *[Dunmer of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Dunmer of Skyrim")*, another small coin purse, a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger").

Against the north wall is a cupboard holding two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), three bottles of wine, and eight bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). To the right is a set of shelves holding two [potions of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a [potion of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"), a [potion of resist fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Fire "Skyrim:Potion of Resist Fire"), two [flutes](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), six [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and several cheese wheels. Against the east wall is a narrow wooden table with an [Orcish dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger "Skyrim:Orcish Dagger") on top. To the left of the fireplace is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding four [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), with a set of shelves to the left of that, holding an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") and a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum"). Against the south wall is a flight of stairs up to the top floor, with a couple of crates and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") under the stairs.

Upstairs, against the west wall, is a long wooden table and bench, with three bottles of alto wine, two fillets of [salmon meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat "Skyrim:Salmon Meat"), a [chicken's egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), two bulbs of garlic, a couple of loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and several wedges of cheese on the table, and a loaf of bread on the bench. To the right is a small round wooden table and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing leveled items, with two more bottles of alto wine and five loose septims on top of the table. Coming out from the middle of the north wall is a large double bed with a dresser on either side, both containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a copy of the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mystery of Talara, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_v3 "Skyrim:Mystery of Talara, v3")* on top. At the foot of the bed is another chest also containing leveled items. Above the stairs, against the east wall, is a set of shelves, but they only hold clutter, with a door out onto the balcony to the left.

## Notes

- Unlike other stores in the game, Birna's Oddments has no exterior sign.
- With the exception of some barrels and sacks, all containers in the area are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") and safe to store items in.
- The display case lid collides with the rabbits above it when opened (resulting in humorous motion on the part of the rabbits).
