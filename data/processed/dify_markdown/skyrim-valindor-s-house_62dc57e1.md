Valindor's House

**Valindor's House** is a house which is owned by [Valindor](/wiki/Skyrim:Valindor "Skyrim:Valindor").

It is located on the lower levels of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), alongside the canal which flows through the city. It is next door to [Marise Aravel's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Marise_Aravel%27s_House "Skyrim:Marise Aravel's House"), and below the [Temple of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Mara "Skyrim:Temple of Mara").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/SR-interior-Valindor%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Valindor%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Valindor%27s_House.jpg)

Valindor's house

This is a single-room dwelling, with a partial wall dividing it into separate areas. The main living space consists of a fireplace against the east wall, two chairs pulled up in front, a small table with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on it between them, and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on the hearth. Along the north wall, a set of shelves holds two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), several [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), several pieces of cheese, and a bottle of [Alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). Against the west wall is a dresser, with a copy of the book *[Of Crossed Daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Crossed_Daggers "Skyrim:Of Crossed Daggers")* on top. A dining table, a bench, some barrels and some sacks of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") stand beside the partial wall; hanging above are two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and two bunches of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). The table is lain with a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), an [Eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), a bottle of Alto wine, and a bottle of Nord mead.

Beyond the partition is a sparse bedroom, consisting of an owned, single bed; on an end table beside it lies a copy of the book *[Horker Attacks](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Attacks "Skyrim:Horker Attacks").* To the right is a wardrobe, storing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"); opposite this, along the south wall, is an unlocked wooden [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot.

## Notes

- There is a [![Loot](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Loot") Loot [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Shadowmarks "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") on the left doorstep.
- Once you have joined the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), a barrel that is carved with the [![Cache](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/SR-icon-shadowmark-Cache.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Cache.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Cache") Cache shadowmark appears opposite the entrance, on the walkway that travels alongside the canal.
- If you infect Valindor with vampirism during the [New Allegiances](/wiki/Skyrim:New_Allegiances "Skyrim:New Allegiances")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") quest, a coffin will appear in his house[*verification needed — location?*] and can be used to sleep and get the vampire rested bonus. Valindor does not seem to use it however, and will still sleep in his old bed.
- During [New Allegiances](/wiki/Skyrim:New_Allegiances "Skyrim:New Allegiances")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") quest, the door to Valindor's House will lock if night falls while inside it. Unlocking the door at this point is considered a crime. Just wait until 04:00 AM for the door to unlock itself.
