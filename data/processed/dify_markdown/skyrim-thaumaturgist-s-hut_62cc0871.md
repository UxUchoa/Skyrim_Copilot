Thaumaturgist's Hut

The **Thaumaturgist's Hut** is an [alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") shop in [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal").

The shop is located across from [Highmoon Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Highmoon_Hall "Skyrim:Highmoon Hall"), where its proprietor [Lami](/wiki/Skyrim:Lami "Skyrim:Lami") runs the shop during normal business hours (8am - 8pm). There are two floors, each with their own entrance. However, the doors are never locked, possibly because it has the only [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") available in Morthal. If you are on good terms with the owner, all the lower value [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") found here are free to take and [respawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") every few days. No one lives or sleeps here and there are no beds in the building.

### Exterior

There are two [swamp fungal pods](/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod "Skyrim:Swamp Fungal Pod") near the building, one near the front door and the other around the back next to the stairs leading up to a balcony. The balcony winds around the building and at the front is the second entrance.

### Thaumaturgist's Hut

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg)

First floor

The main entrance is on the ground floor and enters into the shop. Opposite you is a fireplace, on the mantlepiece are several [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") sold by [Lami](/wiki/Skyrim:Lami "Skyrim:Lami"). To the right of this, coming out from the east wall is the counter. On the counter is a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and under the counter are three small [coin purses](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). Behind the counter against the south wall are a cupboard and set of shelves holding more potions and [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") sold by Lami and four bottles of wine, two bottles of alto wine and a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead").

Between the cupboard and shelves is a barrel of ingredients and a pile of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks. To the right of the door is a dresser holding more ingredients sold by Lami, a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). Next to the dresser is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). To the left of the fireplace is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding four [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). Under this is a barrel of food and to the left of the barrel is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"). On top of the lab are more ingredient and potions you can buy from Lami and a [weak frenzy poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Frenzy_Poison "Skyrim:Weak Frenzy Poison"). There are stairs against the north wall leading to the top floor. Under the stairs is a pile of firewood and several barrels, on top of these are even more ingredients sold by Lami.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut_02.jpg)

Second floor

At the top of the stairs, against the east wall is a long wooden table and bench holding two bottles of alto wine, several loaves of bread, several wedges of cheese, two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") that you can buy from Lami and two pieces of [salmon meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat "Skyrim:Salmon Meat") on a wooden plate. To the right, still against the same wall is a set of shelves holding an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"). Against the south wall is an unlocked chest with dressers on either side holding four common books, a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") containing three bottles of [skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma") that are sold by Lami, two bottles of alto wine and five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). There is a door to the outside in the middle of the west wall and a square wooden table to the right. To the right of the table, above the stairs are two food barrels.

## Notes

- Since Lami doesn't live here, the opening hours are approximate. She doesn't leave her [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House "Skyrim:Jorgen and Lami's House") until around 8am and it may take her an hour to walk to the shop.
- A copy of the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Response to Bero's Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Response_to_Bero%27s_Speech "Skyrim:Response to Bero's Speech")* can be found on top of a shelf on the bottom floor, next to a troll skull.
