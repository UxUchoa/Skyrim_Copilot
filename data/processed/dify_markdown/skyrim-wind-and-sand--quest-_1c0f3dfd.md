## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth") about assisting him in his research.
2. Go to a radiant location and retrieve *[Wind and Sand](/wiki/Skyrim:Wind_and_Sand_(book) "Skyrim:Wind and Sand (book)")*.
3. Return to Neloth for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

After completing [Experimental Subject](/wiki/Skyrim:Experimental_Subject "Skyrim:Experimental Subject"), speak to [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth") again about assisting in his research. This time, he will tell you that he is attempting to adapt Redguard magic to the island's ash. He has learned the location of a book through a source of his that would prove invaluable to his spell research. The book is *[Wind and Sand](/wiki/Skyrim:Wind_and_Sand_(book) "Skyrim:Wind and Sand (book)")* and is located in a radiant location: a Dragon lair, dragon priest lair, draugr crypt, Falmer hive, Forsworn camp, hagraven nest, vampire lair, or warlock lair.

Once you have the book, return to Neloth who will excitedly declare that he can create some sort of wind spell with its contents. You will receive 250 gold, and the spell tome [Whirlwind Cloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Cloak "Skyrim:Whirlwind Cloak") will be made available for purchase the next time that [Talvas Fathryon](/wiki/Skyrim:Talvas_Fathryon "Skyrim:Talvas Fathryon") restocks.
