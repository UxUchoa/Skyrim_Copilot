Fellstar Farm

**Fellstar Farm** is a farm in [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"), located across the road from the lumber mill and south of the bridge to [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar").

It is the home of [Jofthor](/wiki/Skyrim:Jofthor "Skyrim:Jofthor"), [Boti](/wiki/Skyrim:Boti "Skyrim:Boti"), and their daughter [Fastred](/wiki/Skyrim:Fastred "Skyrim:Fastred"). It consists of one zone, *[Fellstar Farm](#Fellstar_Farm).*

### Fellstar Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-interior-Fellstar_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Fellstar_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Fellstar_Farm.jpg)

Kitchen and dining area

Upon entering, you'll find that this small home is laid out with the bedroom area to the right and the kitchen and dining area to the left. To the left of the door, there are three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, and one portion each of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging along the north wall. A respawning food barrel sits in the northeast corner, next to a non-respawning cupboard holding various dishes on its shelves. Another respawning food barrel is in the southeast corner, next to a dining table on the south wall. The table holds a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), one bottle each of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and a bucket containing three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). This table also holds [Wylandriah](/wiki/Skyrim:Wylandriah "Skyrim:Wylandriah")'s satchel, which contains [Wylandriah's spoon](/wiki/Skyrim:Wylandriah%27s_Spoon "Skyrim:Wylandriah's Spoon") for the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunt_and_Gather "Skyrim:Hunt and Gather"). Beneath the table, a [chicken's egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") and another pine thrush egg lie on the floor. Above the table are some more hanging ingredients, including another portion of dried frost mirriam, two portions of dried elves ear, and three garlic braids. Next to these are two hanging racks, one of which holds three freshly caught [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") while the other holds three [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). On the right side of the table, a respawning meat barrel sits next to a set of shelves holding books, alcohol, and dishes. This includes four bottles each of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and alto wine, and a copy of the books *[A Dream of Sovngarde](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dream_of_Sovngarde "Skyrim:A Dream of Sovngarde")*, *[A Minor Maze](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Minor_Maze "Skyrim:A Minor Maze")*, *[The Cabin in the Woods](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cabin_in_the_Woods "Skyrim:The Cabin in the Woods")*, and *[Rising Threat, Vol. IV](/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._IV "Skyrim:Rising Threat, Vol. IV")*. To the right of these shelves is the fireplace, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") at the center and a kettle holding two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") on the right.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-Fellstar_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Fellstar_Farm_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Fellstar_Farm_02.jpg)

Bedroom area

The west side of the house is where you can find the three beds belonging to the residing family. The southwest corner holds [Jofthor](/wiki/Skyrim:Jofthor "Skyrim:Jofthor")'s bed, with a non-respawning chest at the foot of the bed. Next to this bed is a non-respawning end table holding one bottle of Nord mead. Along the north wall, [Boti](/wiki/Skyrim:Boti "Skyrim:Boti")'s bed is in the corner and [Fastred](/wiki/Skyrim:Fastred "Skyrim:Fastred")'s bed is closest to the door. Between these beds and the front door is a small table holding one loaf and one piece of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), as well as a bottle of alto wine.

## Notes

- The front door has an apprentice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Fellstar_Farm_Key "Skyrim:Fellstar Farm Key") to which is carried by Boti, Fastred, and Jofthor.
- The front door is unlocked from about 11:50am to 12:30pm by Fastred and again from about 4:50pm to 11pm by Boti.
- A [grain mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Milling "Skyrim:Milling") can be found here, which can be used to produce [flour](/wiki/Skyrim:Sack_of_Flour "Skyrim:Sack of Flour")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") from [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") if [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is installed.
- Two respawning food barrels sit outside the house, to the right side of the wood pile.
- A [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge") can be found in front of the chickens' nests.
