The Lover Stone

**The Lover Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones") scattered across [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim").

It is situated to the east of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"), and just north of [Kolskeggr Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine"). Approach from the west, as the east side is a cliff.

It makes all [skills](/wiki/Skyrim:Skills "Skyrim:Skills") improve 15% faster. If you have The Lover Stone active, you cannot gain [resting bonuses](/wiki/Skyrim:Sleeping "Skyrim:Sleeping") in the original version of the game. This makes it a useful choice for users with [lycanthropy](/wiki/Skyrim:Lycanthropy "Skyrim:Lycanthropy"), since [werewolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf "Skyrim:Werewolf") cannot gain any benefit from sleeping to begin with.

## The Lover Stone

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-place-The_Lover_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Lover_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Lover_Stone_(active).jpg)

The Lover Stone, when activated

There is a leveled [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") nearby that you will encounter as you approach the stone and several [juniper](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") trees in the area. It overlooks the road that passes [Kolskeggr Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine") on the edge of a cliff face next to a stream that tumbles as a waterfall to join the [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River") below. Either side of the stone are several upright stones in a semi-circle.

## Notes

- The [Aetherial Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherial_Crown "Skyrim:Aetherial Crown")([DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard")) makes it possible to obtain both resting bonuses and the combined benefits of [The Guardian Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Guardian_Stones "Skyrim:The Guardian Stones") with this one.
  - If you combine the Aetherial Crown with The Guardian Stones and this one, you'll benefit with the blessing of this Stone and the blessing of the Guardian Stone you combine it with. You'll only be able to maximize the benefits of one of the Guardian Stones, though.
