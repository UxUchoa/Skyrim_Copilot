Romlyn Dreth's House

**Romlyn Dreth's House** is a dwelling in the lower part of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), by the canal that runs through the city.

It is found next to the stairs leading down to the canal from in front of [Honorhall Orphanage](/wiki/Skyrim:Honorhall_Orphanage "Skyrim:Honorhall Orphanage").

### Romlyn Dreth's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-interior-Romlyn_Dreth%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Romlyn_Dreth%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Romlyn_Dreth%27s_House.jpg)

Romlyn Dreth at home

Just to the left of the entrance are two [fish barrels](/wiki/Skyrim:Barrel "Skyrim:Barrel") that you are free to take from. It is a single room dwelling, like all the homes all the canal-side with a partial wall separating the living and sleeping quarters. Upon entering you are in the living area that consists of a fireplace in the middle of the west wall, with a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid, a bunch of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and two bunches of [frost mirraim](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging above. Located in the northwest corner is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") and a piece of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"). Situated in front of the fireplace are three chairs and a round wooden table holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"). There is a dresser holding [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") against the partial east wall and hanging above it are three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), two garlic braids and a bunch of elves ear. There are a set of shelves and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") against the south wall. The shelves are holding a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), several [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a couple of loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and several pieces of cheese. Barrels and sacks containing [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") are situated all around this room as well.

The sleeping area consists of a single owned bed in the northwest corner, with a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") under it and on the west wall a wall shelf over the bed. On the shelf is a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). Further along the west wall by the foot of the bed is an end table holding a wooden bowl containing [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and to the right of the bed against the south wall is a wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes").
