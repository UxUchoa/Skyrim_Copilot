Boulderfall Cave

**Boulderfall Cave** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") northwest of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") containing [necromancers](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer"). It contains only one zone, *[Boulderfall Cave](#Boulderfall_Cave)*.

### Exterior

The cave is fairly remote in the wilderness. There is a small game trail that runs north-southeast near the cave, and a path leads from this to the entrance that is marked with several piles of rocks. Above the entrance is a flag post with a flag fluttering in the breeze. By the base is a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and two random [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"); these may have been knocked off and be found on the ground beneath the flag. A few paces to the northwest of the flag, nestled among the rocks is a novice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") with a corpse beside it. There is a leveled, hostile necromancer near the entrance that acts as the apprentice for the master within. He may be found sitting on the rock by the flag post above the entrance, and may raise the corpse to help with the fight upon detecting you.

### Boulderfall Cave

The entrance leads into a tunnel descending to the southwest with two barrels of [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") on the left where it opens onto some wooden scaffolding at the entrance to a small chamber. Be careful, as there are three sets of [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes") lining the passageway to navigate, and avoiding setting one of these off can be quite a challenge. Any noise will alert the necromancer that is toiling away at an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") in the chamber. The necromancer will be of the highest level possible for your level, and will immediately attack if alerted to your presence. Once past the bone chimes, there is a ramp that descends to the cave floor. Two patches of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") fungus can be found growing in the chamber.

There is a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") on the northeast wall, to the right of the ramp. Against the north wall is a wooden table with three whole [slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Slaughterfish") and five [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish") steaks on top and barrels on either side, one of which contains [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"), and the other ingredients. To the left are a stack of three open crates holding [empty wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle"), with more on the ground in front. Beside these are two baskets, one containing [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), and the other [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). More barrels are to the left, with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on one of them and a pile of firewood and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") beside them. Behind the firewood is a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") on the ground, which presumably has fallen from one of the five racks hanging from the ceiling above. These hold a further three pheasants and seven [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). Finally, against this wall is a novice-trapped chest.

At the rear of the chamber on a low platform is an alchemy lab with a bucket containing five bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") beside it. Along the wall to the right is a set of shelves with three baskets around; one of these holds potatoes, another many bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), and the third three [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"). The shelves hold three bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), a bottle of wine, a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), three bottles of Nord mead, a copy of the [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim "Skyrim:Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim")*, a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison"), two [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"). To the left of the platform is another table with three more bottles of Nord mead on top and an open crate containing two more cabbages underneath.

## Notes

- Several internal game data references suggest Boulderfall Cave was originally the site of the quest [The Savior of Selveni Nethri](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Savior_of_Selveni_Nethri "Skyrim:The Savior of Selveni Nethri"), which was ultimately moved to [Southfringe Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum "Skyrim:Southfringe Sanctum") in the final game.
- Some internal game data refers to the location as "Boulderfall Pass".
- Boulderfall Cave also appears in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online") as [Boulderfall Pass](/wiki/Online:Boulderfall_Pass "Online:Boulderfall Pass").
