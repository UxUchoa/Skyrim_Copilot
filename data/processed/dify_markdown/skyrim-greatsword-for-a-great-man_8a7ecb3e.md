### A Warmaiden's Gift

[Adrianne Avenicci](/wiki/Skyrim:Adrianne_Avenicci "Skyrim:Adrianne Avenicci") is a [blacksmith](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith "Skyrim:Blacksmith") who owns the smithy and weapons shop, [Warmaiden's](/wiki/Skyrim:Warmaiden%27s "Skyrim:Warmaiden's"), in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). When asked if she works her forge all day, Adrianne will admit that she does, and that she recently forged a greatsword for [Jarl Balgruuf the Greater](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater") in an attempt to impress him. However, she can't seem to find an opportunity to deliver it. She would like for the sword to be delivered to her father, [Proventus Avenicci](/wiki/Skyrim:Proventus_Avenicci "Skyrim:Proventus Avenicci"), the [steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Steward "Skyrim:Steward") of [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach") in Whiterun.

### Delivering the Sword

Proventus is generally found in the main chamber of Dragonsreach, beside the throne. Deliver the sword to him, telling him that it is from his daughter. He will remark, *"From Adrianne? Ah, this must be that weapon for the Jarl. Poor girl, so eager to prove herself. I'll present it to Balgruuf when his mood is... agreeable."* He will then reward you with twenty gold.

## Notes

- If you sided with the Stormcloaks in the Civil War and have completed [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)"), Proventus will be in the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace")'s basement along with Balgruuf and Irileth. The quest is otherwise the same as if you had joined the Imperials.
- As a quest item, the sword has zero weight. It can be tempered, but not enchanted, making it a potentially useful item to hold onto early in the game.

## Bugs

- If you place Balgruuf's Greatsword on a weapon rack or plaque, the quest can still be completed by talking to Proventus, and the sword will disappear from the rack/plaque. However, the slot the sword occupied can never be used again.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- Proventus Avenicci equips the greatsword that was intended to be for Balgruuf.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. It will now simply be taken from you instead and placed in a display case in Balgruuf's quarters as was implied.
