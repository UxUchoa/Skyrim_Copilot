This page lists all the main faction **guild halls** in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").

### Bards College

:   **[Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College")** — A musical school in Solitude. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Bards+College)

### Blades

:   [![SR-mapicon-Landmark.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-mapicon-Landmark.png/10px-SR-mapicon-Landmark.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Sky Haven Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Sky_Haven_Temple "Skyrim:Sky Haven Temple")** — A large mountaintop fort that served as the major Blades temple and outpost in Skyrim, now long abandoned and in Forsworn territory. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Sky+Haven+Temple)

### College of Winterhold

:   [![SR-mapicon-College of Winterhold.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/SR-mapicon-College_of_Winterhold.png/15px-SR-mapicon-College_of_Winterhold.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-College_of_Winterhold.png) **[College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)")** — A school of the arcane arts. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=College+of+Winterhold)

### Companions

:   **[Jorrvaskr](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorrvaskr "Skyrim:Jorrvaskr")** — The mead hall headquarters of the Companions. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Jorrvaskr)
:   **[The Underforge](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Underforge "Skyrim:The Underforge")** — An ancient chamber hidden underneath the Skyforge used as a ritual chamber by the Circle of the Companions. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=The+Underforge)

### Dark Brotherhood

:   [![SR-mapicon-Settlement.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-mapicon-Settlement.png/15px-SR-mapicon-Settlement.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Settlement.png) **[Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary")** — An active Dark Brotherhood sanctuary west of Falkreath. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dark+Brotherhood+Sanctuary)
:   [![SR-mapicon-Settlement.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-mapicon-Settlement.png/15px-SR-mapicon-Settlement.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Settlement.png) **[Dawnstar Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Dawnstar Sanctuary")** — A disused Dark Brotherhood sanctuary on the shore of the Sea of Ghosts near Dawnstar. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dawnstar+Sanctuary)

### Dawnguard DG

:   [![SR-mapicon-Fort.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-mapicon-Fort.png/12px-SR-mapicon-Fort.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Fort Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dawnguard "Skyrim:Fort Dawnguard")**[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") — A large fort southeast of Riften which serves as the Dawnguard's base of operations. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Fort+Dawnguard+%28DG%29)

### Thieves Guild

:   [![SR-mapicon-Landmark.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-mapicon-Landmark.png/10px-SR-mapicon-Landmark.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(place) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (place)")** — The headquarters of the Thieves Guild located with the sewers beneath Riften, known as The Ratway. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Thieves+Guild)
:   **[The Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon")** — A tavern that is part of the Thieves Guild's headquarters. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=The+Ratway)
:   [![SR-mapicon-Cave.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-mapicon-Cave.png/19px-SR-mapicon-Cave.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Nightingale Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Hall "Skyrim:Nightingale Hall")** — The home of the Nightingales between Riften and the Shadow Stone. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Nightingale+Hall)

### Volkihar Vampire Clan DG

:   [![SR-mapicon-Fort.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-mapicon-Fort.png/12px-SR-mapicon-Fort.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Volkihar Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Keep "Skyrim:Volkihar Keep")**[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") — A large fortress on an island far northwest of Solitude connected to Castle Volkihar which serves as the Volkihar vampire clan's home. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Castle+Volkihar+%28DG%29)
