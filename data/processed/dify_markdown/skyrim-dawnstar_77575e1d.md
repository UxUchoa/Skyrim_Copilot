Dawnstar

**Dawnstar** is a city on the northern coast of Skyrim, and the capital of [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale"). It is initially aligned with the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") under [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") [Skald](/wiki/Skyrim:Skald "Skyrim:Skald"). The city is a relatively small fishing and mining settlement with few services. The residents will report having [horrible nightmares](/wiki/Skyrim:Waking_Nightmare "Skyrim:Waking Nightmare"), which some initially blame on a witch's curse.

For more information on Dawnstar, see the [lore article](/wiki/Lore:Dawnstar "Lore:Dawnstar").

## Notes

- With *[Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")* installed, you can purchase [a plot of land](/wiki/Skyrim:Heljarchen_Hall "Skyrim:Heljarchen Hall") in the Pale to build a house on. Also, two [stone quarries](/wiki/Skyrim:Quarried_Stone "Skyrim:Quarried Stone") will be present.
- [Dawnstar Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Dawnstar Sanctuary") is a [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Houses "Skyrim:Houses") granted upon completion of the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") quest [Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!")
- [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") adds a permanently disabled map marker for Dawnstar Docks.
- [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine") is just south of Fruki's House, in the northwest corner of town.
- [Iron-Breaker Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron-Breaker_Mine "Skyrim:Iron-Breaker Mine") is just east of [The White Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Hall "Skyrim:The White Hall"), at the east end of town.
- A blacksmith's forge and hide tanner are outside Rustleif's house, near the water.
- A tent is northeast of town, past the hill on which the lighthouse is situated. Inside, someone has set up a lovers' retreat, complete with two unowned bedrolls, a few articles of clothing, wine, [flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), and an [amulet of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Mara "Skyrim:Amulet of Mara").
- Dawnstar also appears in *[Arena](/wiki/Arena:Dawnstar "Arena:Dawnstar")* and *[Dawnstar](/wiki/Dawnstar:Places "Dawnstar:Places")*.
