Dorian in the Red Wave

**Dorian** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [thief](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatThief "Skyrim:CombatThief") and a pirate aboard the [Red Wave](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Wave "Skyrim:Red Wave"), under the command of [Safia](/wiki/Skyrim:Safia "Skyrim:Safia").

Dorian wears a set of [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor"), including the [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots"). He carries a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic"), a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"), and an additional small amount of gold. He carries a leveled war axe, bow, and twelve arrows, all of which can be up to [Daedric](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric "Skyrim:Daedric") in quality.

## Related Quests

- **[Kill Safia](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Safia "Skyrim:Kill Safia")**: Kill a [pirate captain](/wiki/Skyrim:Safia "Skyrim:Safia") on her own [ship](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Wave "Skyrim:Red Wave").

- This NPC is one of many potential quest givers or quest targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_NPCs "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Dialogue

Should you approach him he can say:

:   *"Forget wine, women and wealth - give me a calm sea and a good book, and I'm happy."*
:   *"I hear [Adonato Leotelli](/wiki/Skyrim:Adonato_Leotelli "Skyrim:Adonato Leotelli")'s staying up in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). I've read a few of his works. Be good to meet him someday."*
:   *"Don't misunderstand me, friend. I like my quiet, and I like to read. But you trifle with my ship, my crew, or my captain? I will kill you."*
:   *"You've not seen the night sky until you've seen it on the open sea."*

He will also comment on his captain, Safia, as long as she is still alive:

:   *"I've only been a part of the Red Wave's crew for a few weeks, but I've already learned to stay out of Captain Safia's way."*

## Bugs

- While Dorian is technically a leveled NPC, you would need to be level 251 or higher to have any of his stats increase. Since this is highly unlikely, he is effectively unleveled, with Health fixed at 250 and Stamina fixed at 210.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.0.6, addresses this issue. His level is set to PCx1. The fix also includes removing his essential status.
- Dorian does not exit the interior of the ship. This becomes problematic when a radiant quest chooses him as the target, as, once you have entered the ship, all he says is that you aren't supposed to be there.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, addresses this issue. Red Wave crew members can now show up at the Winking Skeever.
  - You can start a fight with him then run outside. Once outside, and away from the entrance, lower your weapons and surrender. Talk to him before he has a chance to go below decks.
    - Having a follower can complicate your efforts to draw him out. The follower will continue to engage in combat and will prevent a surrender.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Using the command `AF525.MoveTo Player` to place him near you.
