# Skyrim:Fire Mage

## Overview

**Fire Mages** are enemy spellcasters who specialize in the [Fire Damage](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage "Skyrim:Fire Damage") branch of [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") magic. They are typically found in a few caves, keeps, and ruins across [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim") known as [warlock lairs](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock_Lairs "Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock Lairs"). Like other elemental mages ([ice mages](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Mage "Skyrim:Ice Mage") and [storm mages](/wiki/Skyrim:Storm_Mage "Skyrim:Storm Mage")), they belong to the [Fire/Frost/Shock Mage](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire/Frost/Shock_Mage "Skyrim:Fire/Frost/Shock Mage") [NPC](/wiki/Skyrim:NPCs "Skyrim:NPCs") class.

The type of fire mage you encounter depends on your level. You will continue to meet low-level fire mages even at high levels, but boss-level fire mages will always be at the highest possible level.

### Fire Mages

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-npc-Fire_Mage.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fire_Mage.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fire_Mage.jpg)

A Novice Fire Mage attacks with a blast of flame

Fire mages rely on their devastating fire-based spells in combat, and carry a leveled dagger in case of close-quarters fighting ([iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), [steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), [orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger "Skyrim:Orcish Dagger"), [dwarven](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Dagger "Skyrim:Dwarven Dagger"), [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger "Skyrim:Elven Dagger"), [glass](/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Dagger "Skyrim:Glass Dagger"), or an enchanted dagger of any quality from iron to [Daedric](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric "Skyrim:Daedric")). They also have a 10% chance to carry a leveled [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") or Destruction staff.

They wear a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000c5d12 "Skyrim:Clothing") along with [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes"), [hooded black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Hooded Black Mage Robes"), robes of [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Alteration "Skyrim:Fortify Alteration"), robes of [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Destruction "Skyrim:Fortify Destruction"), robes of [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Restoration "Skyrim:Fortify Restoration"), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Magicka_Rate "Skyrim:Fortify Magicka Rate").

In addition to the gear listed above, fire mages have a small chance to carry [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), [poisons](/wiki/Skyrim:Poisons "Skyrim:Poisons"), [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tomes "Skyrim:Spell Tomes"), [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](/wiki/Skyrim:Recipes "Skyrim:Recipes"), and a small amount of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). They also have [atronach forge recipes](/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach_Forge_Recipe "Skyrim:Atronach Forge Recipe") in their inventories, but their chance of appearing in-game is set at 0%.

On death, fire mages have a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls:

- **Alteration:** [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis "Skyrim:Scroll of Mass Paralysis")
- **Conjuration:** [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach "Skyrim:Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach"), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie "Skyrim:Scroll of Dread Zombie")
- **Destruction:**[Scroll of Blizzard](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Blizzard "Skyrim:Scroll of Blizzard"), [Scroll of Fire Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fire_Storm "Skyrim:Scroll of Fire Storm")
- **Illusion:** [Scroll of Call to Arms](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms "Skyrim:Scroll of Call to Arms"), [Scroll of Harmony](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Harmony "Skyrim:Scroll of Harmony"), [Scroll of Hysteria](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Hysteria "Skyrim:Scroll of Hysteria"), [Scroll of Mayhem](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mayhem "Skyrim:Scroll of Mayhem")
- **Restoration:** [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead "Skyrim:Scroll of Bane of the Undead"), [Scroll of Guardian Circle](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle "Skyrim:Scroll of Guardian Circle")

Pyromancers and Arch Pyromancers have the same gear as lower-level fire mages, but have a 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Fireballs](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Fireballs "Skyrim:Staff of Fireballs") and do not have the leveled lists for atronach forge recipes.

All members of the Warlock faction share a set of generic dialogue which they will use when in combat:

*"You cannot even conceive of the power I wield..."*

*"Fire, frost, or lightning - you will suffer at my hands!"*

*"Don't you see? I am master of the arcane!"*

*"Behold my power!"*

*"The elements are mine to command!"*

## Notes

- All the generic fire mages that are Dark Elves are listed twice in the leveled lists, which means fire mages are slightly more likely to be Dark Elves than one of the other races.
