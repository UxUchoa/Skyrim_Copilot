Beggar

This Beggar is an [elder](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder "Skyrim:Elder") found at the [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") docks as part of the related quest. Unlike most [beggars](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggars "Skyrim:Beggars"), he will not give you a [blessing in return for 1 gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_all_Beggars_Have "Skyrim:Quest all Beggars Have").
