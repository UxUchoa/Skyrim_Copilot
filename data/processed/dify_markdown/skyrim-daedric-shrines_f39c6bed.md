## Notes

- There are also small obelisks known as "Daedric shrines" that confer a blessing on activation similar to the blessings provided by the shrines to the Nine Divines. These smaller Daedric shrines for [Azura](/wiki/Lore:Azura "Lore:Azura"), [Boethiah](/wiki/Lore:Boethiah "Lore:Boethiah"), and [Mephala](/wiki/Lore:Mephala "Lore:Mephala") are found in the [Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple "Skyrim:Temple") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock") on [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim").
