Orphan Rock

**Orphan Rock** is a wooded valley with a central rock plateau northeast of [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen") containing [witches](/wiki/Skyrim:Witch "Skyrim:Witch") or [hags](/wiki/Skyrim:Hag "Skyrim:Hag") led by a [hagraven](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven "Skyrim:Hagraven").

This location shows no sign of habitation and the rock plateau is not easily accessible until you are sent to retrieve [Nettlebane](/wiki/Skyrim:Nettlebane "Skyrim:Nettlebane") during the quest [The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature").

## Related Quests

- **[The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature")**: Restore the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") for [Danica Pure-Spring](/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring "Skyrim:Danica Pure-Spring").

## Walkthrough

The path leading up to the rock runs north from the road between Helgen and [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"). Caution is advised when exploring the area, as four [elemental rune](/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Rune "Skyrim:Elemental Rune") traps, two [bear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap"), and two sets of sharpened stakes similar to those at the bottom of a [spike pit](/wiki/Skyrim:Spike_Pit "Skyrim:Spike Pit") trap are in the vicinity. A [dead bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(dead) "Skyrim:Bandit (dead)") is near one of the sets of sharpened stakes. Many [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") and a few patches of [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") fungus grow nearby. As you approach from the south, an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") is behind a dead tree stump to the west. Farther north is a fallen tree next to a spike with wolf heads on it. The western end of the tree is hollow, with a lootable skeleton next to a [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a random [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet") inside.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-place-Orphan_Rock_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Orphan_Rock_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Orphan_Rock_03.jpg)

The first tent

Two witches or hags are on the valley floor, and another two rest near the tent by the path at the top of the slope. All four will attack on sight using low-level [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") spells and [daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"). The large central rock is accessed by crossing a fallen tree trunk over a small chasm near the tent. The plateau is guarded by a single hagraven wielding more powerful fire spells. It is possible to fight the first pair of witches or hags without alarming the pair on the upper level and the hagraven.

On the near side of the fallen tree, a tent to the right contains two unowned bedrolls, an adept-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Fruit.2FVegetables "Skyrim:Food") sack. To the right of the tent are several barrels and sacks, most of which contain food, though one barrel contains [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and has a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") on top. In front of these is a campfire and a wooden table holding a skeleton that can be looted for a few [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-place-Orphan_Rock_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Orphan_Rock_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Orphan_Rock_02.jpg)

The hagraven

The most notable loot is Nettlebane, found on the body of the hagraven. A chest and an unowned hay pile are in the hagraven's tent on the central rock. To the left of the tent is an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanters "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanters") holding a random filled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flower, a [hagraven claw](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Claw "Skyrim:Hagraven Claw"), and a sample of [hagraven feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Feathers "Skyrim:Hagraven Feathers"). In front of the enchanter is a large bowl on a stand with the body of a dead [spriggan](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan") lying on top; this can be harvested for a [taproot](/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot "Skyrim:Taproot"). A [silverside perch](/wiki/Skyrim:Silverside_Perch "Skyrim:Silverside Perch") and a [river betty](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Betty "Skyrim:River Betty") lie on a rock among some blood splatters in front of the tent.

## Bugs

- Southeast of the hagraven's tent, past the dead spriggan, are two rubies suspended in the air over the peak of a rock.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.5, addresses this issue. The rubies have been placed on the arcane enchanter.
