Whiterun Military Camp

**Whiterun Military Camp** is a [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Camps "Skyrim:Stormcloak Camps") set up east of [Pelagia Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagia_Farm "Skyrim:Pelagia Farm") for the [attack](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)") on [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").

The camp only appears during the [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun"). The camp itself will appear regardless of the side you've chosen in the war, but a map marker will only be added during the Stormcloak version of the quest, in which case it immediately appears as discovered, allowing you to fast travel to the camp straight away.

## Whiterun Military Camp

As soon as you arrive during the quest, the camp is in an uproar, with three catapults each manned by a Stormcloak soldier firing flaming missiles at Whiterun. The camp features a total of seven small tents, which contain no bed rolls or hay piles. In the middle of the camp is the command tent, containing a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side, with a [Stormcloak soldier](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Soldier "Skyrim:Stormcloak Soldier") studying the map. A few steps southeast of the command tent is a [corundum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore "Skyrim:Corundum Ore") vein. To the west of the main camp, [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist") is giving a motivational speech to a group of ten Stormcloak soldiers.

## Bugs

- After the Battle for Whiterun, the camp's marker will be removed from the world map, but the camp itself won't be disbanded. It will instead remain even if the Empire wins the battle, along with a single, non-hostile Stormcloak soldier inside the main tent.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.10, fixes this bug.
- [![On Xbox](//images.uesp.net/3/33/Xbox.svg)](/wiki/Skyrim:Xbox "Skyrim:Xbox") Even after you complete the quest for the Stormcloaks, the Whiterun and Dragonsreach map markers will still be gone, and the Whiterun Military Camp marker will remain on the map. **?**
  - Entering Whiterun fixes this.
