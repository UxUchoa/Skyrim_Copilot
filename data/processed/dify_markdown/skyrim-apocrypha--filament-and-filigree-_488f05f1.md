The Filament and Filigree Black Book in Kolbjorn Barrow

**Filament and Filigree** is an area of [Hermaeus Mora](/wiki/Skyrim:Hermaeus_Mora "Skyrim:Hermaeus Mora")'s [plane of Oblivion](/wiki/Lore:Planes_of_Oblivion "Lore:Planes of Oblivion"), [Apocrypha](/wiki/Skyrim:Apocrypha "Skyrim:Apocrypha"). There is only one zone in the area, *[Filament and Filigree](#Filament_and_Filigree)*.

The [Black Book](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book:_Filament_and_Filigree_(book) "Skyrim:Black Book: Filament and Filigree (book)") needed to access Filament and Filigree is found inside [Kolbjorn Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolbjorn_Barrow "Skyrim:Kolbjorn Barrow"). You must complete the quest [Unearthed](/wiki/Skyrim:Unearthed "Skyrim:Unearthed") in order to retrieve the book.

The realm is inhabited by Daedra called [Seekers](/wiki/Skyrim:Seeker "Skyrim:Seeker") and [Lurkers](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker"). Tentacles constantly breach the acidic dark waters and sometimes whip at you, causing a significant amount of damage if they manage to hit you. The acidic waters with the tentacles are also corrosive and cause damage if stepped in.

## Filament and Filigree

Filament and Filigree opens on a dim platform facing a dark corridor. The [darkness](/wiki/Skyrim:Traps#Darkness "Skyrim:Traps") here is dangerous, literally; entering it will inflict continuous heavy damage which can not be resisted by any means, including the [Become Ethereal](/wiki/Skyrim:Become_Ethereal "Skyrim:Become Ethereal") shout. Floating [Wisp-Lanterns](/wiki/Skyrim:Wisp-Lantern "Skyrim:Wisp-Lantern") drift above the platforms, providing safe havens of light to walk in. Other forms of light will also work to prevent damage (e.g. torches, [Candlelight](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlelight "Skyrim:Candlelight"), [Magelight](/wiki/Skyrim:Magelight "Skyrim:Magelight")), so long as they keep away the darkness.

Ahead and to the right is a table and a font of magicka. On the table are two random soul gems and a copy of the [Sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Three Thieves](/wiki/Skyrim:Three_Thieves "Skyrim:Three Thieves")*. Hidden behind the left statue at the bottom of the stairs to the left is a pod. At the top of the stairs is a closed gate. The scrye to open the gate is to the right, and to the left is a pod and a [scroll of invisibility](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Invisibility "Skyrim:Scroll of Invisibility"). An eye floats along to the right of the long platform ahead, but does not provide full protection from the darkness. Along the platform are two seekers and a lurker, and a lurker rises from the pool at the top of the stairs at the other end. At the top of stairs at the end of the platform is the black book *Filament and Filigree*, a vessel to the left, and a table to the right with copies of the [Spell Tome: Healing Hands](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Healing_Hands "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Healing Hands") and the [Spell Tome: Conjure Seeker](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Conjure_Seeker "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Conjure Seeker") (only if your [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") skill is at least level 40), a [scroll of mass paralysis](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis "Skyrim:Scroll of Mass Paralysis"), and three soul gems (one [black](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Black Soul Gem") and two random). To exit this part of Apocrypha, use the non-circular part of the black book.

The three [Powers](/wiki/Skyrim:Powers "Skyrim:Powers") offered by this book are:

- [Secret of Arcana](/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Arcana "Skyrim:Secret of Arcana") — Spells cost no magicka for 30 seconds.
- [Secret of Protection](/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Protection "Skyrim:Secret of Protection") — You take half damage for 30 seconds.
- [Secret of Strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Strength "Skyrim:Secret of Strength") — Power attacks cost no stamina for 30 seconds.
