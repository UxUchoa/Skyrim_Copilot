# Skyrim:Places

## Overview

There is a large variety of **places** in *[Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim")*; for reference, see the key of the [icons used to mark places on maps](/wiki/Skyrim:Maps#Icons "Skyrim:Maps"). This page only lists some of the most interesting locations in the game. Other places are listed on the following dedicated pages:

- **[All-Maker Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones")** — Standing stones found in the wilderness of [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")
- **[Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Barracks "Skyrim:Barracks")** — Guard barracks throughout Skyrim
- **[Bodies of Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Bodies_of_Water "Skyrim:Bodies of Water")** — Lakes, rivers, and seas
- **[Castles](/wiki/Skyrim:Castles "Skyrim:Castles")** — Castles and Jarl longhouses
- **[Cemeteries](/wiki/Skyrim:Cemeteries "Skyrim:Cemeteries")** — Where the dead of Skyrim are buried
- **[Dragon Mounds](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Mounds "Skyrim:Dragon Mounds")** — Sites where Dragons were buried
- **[Guild Halls](/wiki/Skyrim:Guild_Halls "Skyrim:Guild Halls")** — The various headquarters for the guilds of Skyrim
- **[Holds](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds")** — The holds (political regions) that make up Skyrim
- **[Houses](/wiki/Skyrim:Houses "Skyrim:Houses")** — Properties you can purchase as a home across Skyrim
- **[Inns](/wiki/Skyrim:Inns "Skyrim:Inns")** — Inns, taverns, and places where you can rent a room
- **[Jails](/wiki/Skyrim:Jails "Skyrim:Jails")** — The jails found throughout Skyrim
- **[Shrines](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines "Skyrim:Shrines")** — Shrines to the Nine Divines
- **[Stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Stables "Skyrim:Stables")** — Stables, where you can buy [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses")
- **[Standing Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Standing_Stones "Skyrim:Standing Stones")** — Magical stones which grant special abilities or powers
- **[Stores](/wiki/Skyrim:Stores "Skyrim:Stores")** — Merchant stores
- **[Temples](/wiki/Skyrim:Temples "Skyrim:Temples")** — The various temples of the [Nine Divines](/wiki/Lore:Nine_Divines "Lore:Nine Divines")
- **[Word Walls](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall")** — Locations where word walls can be found
- **[Worldspaces](/wiki/Skyrim:Worldspaces "Skyrim:Worldspaces")** — Exterior areas that are technically separate from the main world of Skyrim
- **[Other Places](/wiki/Skyrim:Other_Places "Skyrim:Other Places")** — Other places that do not fit into another category
- **[Unmarked Places](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places "Skyrim:Unmarked Places")** — Places that do not appear on the map

Places added by [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard"), [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn"), [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire"), and [Creation Club](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") are annotated with "DG", "DB", "HF", and "CC" superscripts, respectively.

**Note:** The [Skyrim-Places category](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places "Category:Skyrim-Places") provides an index of all places in the game.

## Cities

Each city (with the exception of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock") on [Solstheim](/wiki/Lore:Solstheim "Lore:Solstheim")) is located in a different [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") and is home to that hold's ruler, or [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl"). The five largest cities (the major cities) are walled, allowing their maps (or *cells* as they are called in the [Creation Kit](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit "Skyrim Mod:Creation Kit")) to be treated separately from the main map. [Carriages](/wiki/Skyrim:Carriage "Skyrim:Carriage") are available for hire outside each major city to transport the player to any other major city.

Fast traveling to a major city places you just inside its main gate, but each major city also has a keep or palace where the jarl resides, which is a separate fast-travel destination and is listed below. Additionally, Riften has a third fast-travel destination once you have joined the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)"), namely the entrance to the guild's [headquarters](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(place) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (place)").
