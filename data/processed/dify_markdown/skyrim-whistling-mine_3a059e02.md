Whistling Mine

**Whistling Mine** is a small [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron "Skyrim:Iron") [mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Mines "Skyrim:Mines") and [settlement](/wiki/Skyrim:Settlements "Skyrim:Settlements") southeast of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"). It contains only one zone, *[Whistling Mine](#Whistling_Mine)*.

The mine is the only consistently inhabited settlement in the [hold of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)") besides the city of the same name. All of the miners originally began digging the mine thinking it would make them rich. [Angvid](/wiki/Skyrim:Angvid "Skyrim:Angvid") and [Badnir](/wiki/Skyrim:Badnir "Skyrim:Badnir") seem to have lost their morale, while [Gunding](/wiki/Skyrim:Gunding "Skyrim:Gunding") and [Thorgar](/wiki/Skyrim:Thorgar "Skyrim:Thorgar") still claim the mine will eventually be successful, possibly through the discovery of [silver](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver "Skyrim:Silver") or [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") ore. Thorgar, the mine's owner, will [buy iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Mine_Ore "Skyrim:Mine Ore") from you.

### Exterior

The mine lies along the southern road between Winterhold and [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), at the base of the mountains. A [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") is just outside and to the right of the entrance, with three pieces of owned [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") on a crate beside it. Two sacks can be found next to the crate. A [Winterhold guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_Guard "Skyrim:Winterhold Guard") patrols the road in front of the mine, and there is an [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") encounter just north on the road. A path rising to the west starts just south of the mine and leads to [Mount Anthor](/wiki/Skyrim:Mount_Anthor "Skyrim:Mount Anthor") and the [Shrine of Azura](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura "Skyrim:Shrine of Azura"). A [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Quarried_Stone "Skyrim:Quarried Stone")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") can be found down the road north of the mine.

### Whistling Mine

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/SR-interior-Whistling_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Whistling_Mine.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Whistling_Mine.jpg)

Mine interior

It is one of the smallest mines in Skyrim, containing only two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"). Upon entering the mine, the first chamber serves as the camp for the four miners. In the center of the chamber is a campfire with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand beside it. Four bedrolls are east of the campfire, and a table with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") and chairs is to the north. To the west of the table are a couple of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels, one with a copy of the [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Heavy Armor Forging](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor_Forging "Skyrim:Heavy Armor Forging")*, while a coin purse rests on a hay bale nearby. There are several other food sacks and barrels around the chamber. To the west is an ice tunnel that leads to a second chamber with a wooden ramp up to the two iron ore veins on the north wall.

## Notes

- The Winterhold guard who patrols the road near Whistling Mine is the only one who can be seen with the [Winterhold Guard's Shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_Guard%27s_Shield "Skyrim:Winterhold Guard's Shield"), as the guards in the city of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") don't carry it due to a [bug](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold#Notes "Skyrim:Winterhold").

## Bugs

- When using USSEP, the Winterhold guard who patrols the road near Whistling Mine may have two Winterhold Guard's Shields, holding one and having another one in his/her inventory.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.7, addresses this issue. The inventory copy has been removed. This was missed during the USKP 1.2.4 fix which took place back in 2012.
