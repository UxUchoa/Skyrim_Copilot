Sadri's Used Wares

**Sadri's Used Wares** is a general shop in the Gray Quarter of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

It is owned by [Revyn Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Revyn_Sadri "Skyrim:Revyn Sadri"), a [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") trader and common level [trainer](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers") for [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech"). It consists of one area only, *[Sadri's Used Wares](#Sadri.27s_Used_Wares)*.

**NOTE**: Like all other stores, Sadri's Used Wares has a number of fixed wares on display scattered around the main trading room. These can be bought from the proprietor and will disappear from the room when purchased. For a list of these special items, see [this section](#Displayed_Merchandise).

## Sadri's Used Wares

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-interior-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg)

Inside Sadri's Used Wares

Without the displayed merchandise the main trading room becomes quite empty and consists mostly of valuable kitchen utensils. The huge counter has a copy of *[Ancestors and the Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestors_and_the_Dunmer "Skyrim:Ancestors and the Dunmer")* on top and, hidden behind the counter, a copy of the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill book *[2920, Second Seed, v5](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Second_Seed,_v5 "Skyrim:2920, Second Seed, v5")*. A few weapons are hung on the southeastern wall, including an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"), a [steel greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Greatsword "Skyrim:Steel Greatsword"), an [iron battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Iron Battleaxe"), and an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), along with three shields, an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield"), a [hide shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Shield "Skyrim:Hide Shield"), and a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield"). Through the doorway to the south is a storage room with a wardrobe with random [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing"), an end table with clutter, and the store's [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") on a set of shelves.

The doorway to the north leads to Sadri's private quarters where he sleeps every night between midnight and 8am. The southwestern corner contains a small table with a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), a slice of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and some [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"). Along the western wall is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot"). The adjacent corner has two food sacks and some shelves with several items, including an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a sample of [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota"), a sample of [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat"), three [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and the [Business Ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"). The owned bed stands along the northern wall, with a handy bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") next to the pillow. The northeastern corner has a wardrobe with some assorted clothes. A dresser along the eastern wall contains clothes and has another bottle of wine and a bottle of Nord mead on top.

## Bugs

- If [Revyn Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Revyn_Sadri "Skyrim:Revyn Sadri") dies, [Aval Atheron](/wiki/Skyrim:Aval_Atheron "Skyrim:Aval Atheron") technically becomes owner of Sadri's Used Wares. However, he never takes over running the store: he never enters the store, and even if he were to enter the store, he does not own the store's merchant chest.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. [Idesa Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Idesa_Sadri "Skyrim:Idesa Sadri") has been selected to fill the backup alias and will take over when Revyn dies.
