Tel Mithryn Steward's House

**Tel Mithryn Steward's House** is the home of the steward [Varona Nelas](/wiki/Skyrim:Varona_Nelas "Skyrim:Varona Nelas") or [Drovas Relvi](/wiki/Skyrim:Drovas_Relvi "Skyrim:Drovas Relvi") in [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(settlement) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (settlement)").

It is one of the four mushroom structures in the settlement, between [Tel Mithryn's tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)") to the east and [Tel Mithryn Kitchen](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Kitchen") to the northwest.

## Tel Mithryn Steward's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg)

Tel Mithryn Steward's House interior

The entrance leads into a small circular room comprising the entire interior. To the north is a crate, a narrow table, an unowned single bed, a sideboard holding a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a copy of *[Deathbrand](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(book) "Skyrim:Deathbrand (book)")*, and another bottle of wine and an [empty wine bottle](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle") on the floor in front of the sideboard. To the east are two crates, a set of shelves holding a [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk"), a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk"), six empty wine bottles, a [swamp fungal pod](/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod "Skyrim:Swamp Fungal Pod"), a bowl of [powdered mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Powdered_Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Powdered Mammoth Tusk"), and a sample of [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), a narrow table holding another empty wine bottle, and another set of shelves holding a crate, five more empty wine bottles, and seven common books. To the south are two chairs, a small table holding a bottle of wine, and a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). In the middle of the room are two chairs, two empty wine bottles, and a bottle of wine around a lit brazier.

## Notes

- Although [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth") tells you he will reserve a bed and a chest for your use after completing the quest [Old Friends](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Friends "Skyrim:Old Friends"), there will still be no chest clearly reserved for your use, and the bed in the steward's house will remain the only unowned bed or bedroll in the settlement. However, the unowned bed here will often be occupied by the current steward.

## Bugs

- If [Reluctant Steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Reluctant_Steward "Skyrim:Reluctant Steward") is finished and the [Sun Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Stone "Skyrim:Sun Stone") has been cleansed, Drovas's sleep package will always be active, and therefore he will be found sleeping indefinitely here.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
