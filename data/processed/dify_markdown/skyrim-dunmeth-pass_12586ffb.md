**Dunmeth Pass** is a mountain [pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Border_Gates "Skyrim:Border Gates") and one of the few traversable roads through the [Velothi Mountains](/wiki/Lore:Velothi_Mountains "Lore:Velothi Mountains") between northwest [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind") and northeast [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim").

On the Morrowind side, the pass is near the city of [Blacklight](/wiki/Lore:Blacklight "Lore:Blacklight"), and in Skyrim, it emerges near the city of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). It historically served as a significant avenue of trade for Windhelm, although traffic has likely diminished since the [Red Year](/wiki/Lore:Red_Year "Lore:Red Year").

At the conclusion of [Find the Thalmor Assassin](/wiki/Skyrim:Find_the_Thalmor_Assassin "Skyrim:Find the Thalmor Assassin"), [Malborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Malborn "Skyrim:Malborn") exits [New Gnisis Cornerclub](/wiki/Skyrim:New_Gnisis_Cornerclub "Skyrim:New Gnisis Cornerclub"), and runs all the way to the Morrowind border at [Refugees' Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Refugees%27_Rest "Skyrim:Refugees' Rest"), where he will disappear from the game.
