Calder

**Calder** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") from [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

Once you have purchased the home in Windhelm, [Hjerim](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjerim "Skyrim:Hjerim"), and attained the rank of [thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") of Windhelm, Calder is appointed your [housecarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl") and will automatically appear in Hjerim.

Calder wears a set of [steel armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Armor"), a pair of [steel Nordic gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Nordic Gauntlets"), and a pair of [steel cuffed boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Cuffed Boots"). He is equipped with a [steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Steel War Axe") and a [steel shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield "Skyrim:Steel Shield").

He will wait in the home until he is ordered to [follow](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") you. If he is dismissed he will return to Hjerim and resume waiting.

He is also a potential candidate for [marriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage").
