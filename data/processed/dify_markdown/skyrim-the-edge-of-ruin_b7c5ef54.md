## Quick Walkthrough

1. Travel to [Stony Creek Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave "Skyrim:Stony Creek Cave").
2. Take [Ruin's Edge](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruin%27s_Edge_(item) "Skyrim:Ruin's Edge (item)").

## Detailed Walkthrough

This is an unmarked quest to recover [Ruin's Edge](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruin%27s_Edge_(item) "Skyrim:Ruin's Edge (item)"), a fabled bow. The [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") boss in [Stony Creek Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave "Skyrim:Stony Creek Cave") is in possession of the bow.

Defeat the bandit and claim it as your own to finish the quest. You'll also find many types of cheese on the bandit: an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), and [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"). You might want to bring a pickaxe to mine the ore here as well.

## Notes

- Ruin's Edge originally [appeared](/wiki/Shivering:Ruin%27s_Edge "Shivering:Ruin's Edge") in *[Shivering Isles](/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles "Shivering:Shivering Isles")*. It was an enchanted [Dark Seducer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Seducer "Skyrim:Dark Seducer") bow given to you as a quest reward by [Lady Syl](/wiki/Lore:Syl "Lore:Syl"), Duchess of [Dementia](/wiki/Lore:Dementia_(place) "Lore:Dementia (place)").
- This quest would originally be added to your journal upon installing the [Ruin's Edge](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruin%27s_Edge "Skyrim:Ruin's Edge") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). This was changed with the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition") update, allowing you to discover the bow through natural gameplay.
- The leveled list that adds cheese to the bandit boss is called `ccBGSSSE004_LItemCheeseForEveryone`, referring to one of [Sheogorath](/wiki/Skyrim:Sheogorath "Skyrim:Sheogorath")'s favorite lines.
- With the [Saints & Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, a unique [Bliss Bug in a Jar](/wiki/Skyrim:Bliss_Bug_in_a_Jar "Skyrim:Bliss Bug in a Jar") can be found in Stony Creek Cave. This appears regardless of whether or not you have the Ruin's Edge Creation installed.
