# Skyrim:Creatures

## Overview

This page provides a list of all **creature** types in *[Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim")*.

:   **[Animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals")** – Animals comprise various mundane creatures that have no magical or weapon-wielding abilities.

:   **[Ash Creatures](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Creatures "Skyrim:Ash Creatures")** – Horrific monsters associated with [Red Mountain](/wiki/Lore:Red_Mountain "Lore:Red Mountain"). Note that [Ash Zombies](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Zombie "Skyrim:Ash Zombie")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal") are considered [NPCs](/wiki/Skyrim:NPCs "Skyrim:NPCs"), not creatures.

    :   :   [Ash Guardian](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Guardian "Skyrim:Ash Guardian")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") • [Ash Spawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Spawn "Skyrim:Ash Spawn")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")

:   **[Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra "Skyrim:Daedra")** – Daedra are creatures from the planes of Oblivion. Note that [Golden Saints](/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Saint "Skyrim:Golden Saint")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers"), [Dark Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Seducer "Skyrim:Dark Seducer")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers") and [Dremora](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora "Skyrim:Dremora") are [NPCs](/wiki/Skyrim:NPCs "Skyrim:NPCs"), not creatures, and therefore are not listed here.

    :   :   [Flame Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Atronach "Skyrim:Flame Atronach") • [Frost Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Atronach "Skyrim:Frost Atronach") • [Lurker](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") • [Seeker](/wiki/Skyrim:Seeker "Skyrim:Seeker")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") • [Storm Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Storm_Atronach "Skyrim:Storm Atronach")

:   **[Dwarven Automatons](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Automatons "Skyrim:Dwarven Automatons")** – Dwarven Automatons are ancient, robot-like machines encountered in [Dwarven Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwarven Ruins").

    :   :   [Dwarven Ballista](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Ballista "Skyrim:Dwarven Ballista")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") • [Dwarven Centurion](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Centurion "Skyrim:Dwarven Centurion") • [Dwarven Sphere](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Sphere "Skyrim:Dwarven Sphere") • [Dwarven Spider](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Spider "Skyrim:Dwarven Spider")
