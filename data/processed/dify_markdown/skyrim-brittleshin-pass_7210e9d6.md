Brittleshin Pass

**Brittleshin Pass** is a small [passage](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") through the mountains between [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold") and [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold") containing a [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer") and [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton"). It contains only one zone, *[Brittleshin Pass](#Brittleshin_Pass)*.

Although the interior of the ruin is named "Brittleshin Pass", the map markers for the two entrances are named "North Brittleshin Pass" [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=North+Brittleshin+Pass&zoom=14&legacy=true) and "South Brittleshin Pass" [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=South+Brittleshin+Pass&zoom=14&legacy=true), counting as two separate locations for the "Locations Discovered" statistic. The northern entrance is southwest of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), and the southern entrance is west of [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood").

### Exterior: North Brittleshin Pass

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-place-North_Brittleshin_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-North_Brittleshin_Pass.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-North_Brittleshin_Pass.jpg)

North Brittleshin Pass

The path to the northern entrance leads south from the road between [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") and [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") east of [Bloated Man's Grotto](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto "Skyrim:Bloated Man's Grotto"). Just past a rock cairn on your right is a large fallen tree; inside the hollow trunk is an apprentice-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"). Pass some mammoth bones and a pile of rocks on the right with a flag attached, a rock on the left with three [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton") plants nearby, and a mammoth ribcage on the right with a [blue mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"). A second blue mountain flower grows on the other side of the entrance.

### Exterior: South Brittleshin Pass

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-place-South_Brittleshin_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-South_Brittleshin_Pass.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-South_Brittleshin_Pass.jpg)

South Brittleshin Pass

The path to the southern entrance leads north from a jetty on the shore of [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta") east of [Ilinalta's Deep](/wiki/Skyrim:Ilinalta%27s_Deep "Skyrim:Ilinalta's Deep"). Shortly before the entrance is a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush on the right of the path. Opposite the entrance is a stack of rocks with a flag attached.

### Brittleshin Pass

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-interior-Brittleshin_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Brittleshin_Pass.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brittleshin_Pass.jpg)

Garnet eyes

From the southern entrance, you enter a narrow tunnel full of vegetation leading down to the north. The lit brazier ahead marks the transition into a ruined room with rubble on one side. A [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") fungus is behind the fern on the right as you enter the room. Opposite is a double iron door with a [hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss") growing over it and a second growing from a ledge with a lit brazier to the left. There are sideboards on either side of the door, but the one to the left is broken.

Through the iron doors is a large room on multiple levels, you are on a ledge with two rock pillars opposite you, with a stone face carved high on the wall with [flawless garnets](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Garnet "Skyrim:Flawless Garnet") for eyes that can be dislodged with a ranged shot or the Flames spell. On your right is a lit brazier and past this is the path down. Be aware there are two skeletons at the bottom. Following the path down you pass an open sarcophagus on your right and a second fly amanita on your left and another by the first pillar. There is yet another fly amanita to the right at a dead end with [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") on both side walls. The path continues between the ledge you've just descended from and the pillars. Behind the second pillar are a flight of stairs up to an area with a fly amanita fungus and an apprentice-trapped chest that activates a [swinging mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Swinging_Mace "Skyrim:Swinging Mace") trap. To the northwest is a tunnel exiting this room, there is a fly amanita fungus on each side of the tunnel mouth.

The tunnel heads down a flight of stairs to another lit brazier at the bottom. In this tunnel overhead, there is a very hard to reach [web sac](/wiki/Skyrim:Web_Sac "Skyrim:Web Sac"). You can hear another skeleton and the tunnel turns to the southwest and opens into another room. You can see a leveled necromancer in front of you up a flight of stairs, facing away from you. The skeleton is just out of sight to the left. Taking out the necromancer with a [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow") will draw the skeleton out into view.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-interior-Brittleshin_Pass_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Brittleshin_Pass_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brittleshin_Pass_02.jpg)

At the altar

In the room, on your left is a stone table. On the table is a wooden bowl containing a [human heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Human_Heart "Skyrim:Human Heart"). There is blood all over the table and floor of this room. To the right are a couple of nearly completely buried sideboards. To the south is the flight of stairs leading up to where the necromancer stood. At the top, on your left against the south wall is an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter"). In front of you is an altar with a skeleton that can be looted, a random vigorous potion, a random filled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), a random enchanted [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") and copies of *[Ancestors and the Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestors_and_the_Dunmer "Skyrim:Ancestors and the Dunmer")*, *[The Book of Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra "Skyrim:The Book of Daedra")*, *[The Arcturian Heresy](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcturian_Heresy "Skyrim:The Arcturian Heresy")* and a copy of the [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Warrior's Charge](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warrior%27s_Charge "Skyrim:The Warrior's Charge")*. There are lit braziers at either end of the altar. On your right against the north wall is an upright opened sarcophagus. At the bottom of the stairs to the left is a fly amanita fungus.

On your right as you enter this room are two cages, the first is adept-locked and empty, the second has two [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow") on the floor and the door is open. Against the west wall is a slightly raised area with a hay pile on the left and various crates and a barrel to the right. On top of the barrel is a quiver of iron arrows, an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), and two [weak lingering poisons](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Lingering_Poison "Skyrim:Weak Lingering Poison"). Between these and the cages is an exit to the northwest. There is a lit brazier on your left and behind this is an unlocked boss chest (if you have been sent here to retrieve an item this is where it will be).

To the northwest is a stone bridge to a pillar with a wooden ramp spiraling down around it. At the bottom of the spiraling ramp is a botched up straight ramp to the cave floor. At the top of the straight ramp is the top of a hanging moss plant. There is another at the bottom on the other side of a lit brazier. There are two skeletons patrolling in this area and also quite a bit of vegetation and several pillars. To the northeast is a [spellcaster trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Spellcaster_Trap "Skyrim:Spellcaster Trap") that uses a filled [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem") and casts [Icy Spear](/wiki/Skyrim:Icy_Spear "Skyrim:Icy Spear") even at low levels which makes it extremely dangerous to injured or weak characters. It is in a clear area with five pillars around it. There are stairs leading up to the northeast behind it. At the top of the stairs is the tunnel to the northern entrance.

## Notes

- The marker for North Brittleshin Pass can be placed on your map by overhearing a [Whiterun guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Guard "Skyrim:Whiterun Guard") mention it in their random dialogue. However, they claim Brittleshin Pass is "southeast of here", when it is in fact southwest of Whiterun.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.7, addresses this issue. An audio splice has been done to correct this error.
- Though the pass has entrances in two different holds (Whiterun and Falkreath), it is considered to be within Falkreath Hold for radiant quests.
- Two [corundum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") are outside the south entrance: one is to the west by the lake, and the other is to the east near the mountain. An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") is outside the north entrance.

## Bugs

- NPCs and creatures are unable to properly navigate throughout the dungeon due to a poor navmesh.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.1, fixes this bug.
