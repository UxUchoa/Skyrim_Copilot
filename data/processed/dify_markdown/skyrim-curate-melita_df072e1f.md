Curate Melita

**Curate Melita** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") Curate of the Tribunal Temple. She can be rescued during [Careless Curation](/wiki/Skyrim:Careless_Curation "Skyrim:Careless Curation"), and will relocate herself to [Ashfall's Tear](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashfall%27s_Tear "Skyrim:Ashfall's Tear") as an Apothecary vendor for the [Tribunal Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Tribunal_Temple "Skyrim:Tribunal Temple"). Alternatively, you can choose to kill her.

## Related Quests

- **[Careless Curation](/wiki/Skyrim:Careless_Curation "Skyrim:Careless Curation")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal "Skyrim:Ghosts of the Tribunal"): Find the missing Curate to retrieve the [Mask of Sotha Sil](/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Sotha_Sil "Skyrim:Mask of Sotha Sil").

## Notes

- She's supposed to be a [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") trainer and does have a conversation about that, but you can never receive [training](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers") from her, even with a low Restoration skill.
