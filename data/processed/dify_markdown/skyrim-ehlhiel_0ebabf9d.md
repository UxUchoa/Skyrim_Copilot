Ehlhiel

**Ehlhiel** is a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") who may be encountered during the quest [Double-Edged](/wiki/Skyrim:Double-Edged "Skyrim:Double-Edged") if you decide to help [Zaharia](/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia "Skyrim:Zaharia") kill him.

Ehlhiel wears a set of [monk robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Monk_Robes "Skyrim:Monk Robes") along with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000bacd7 "Skyrim:Boots"). He has a leveled one-handed weapon and a [Potion of Plentiful Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Healing"). He knows some leveled spells: one or two frost spells, one or two healing spells, a [ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Ward_(spells) "Skyrim:Ward (spells)") spell, an [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor_(spells) "Skyrim:Armor (spells)") spell, and one or two [reanimation](/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate_(spells) "Skyrim:Reanimate (spells)") spells, and may know a [turn undead](/wiki/Skyrim:Turn_Undead_(spells) "Skyrim:Turn Undead (spells)") spell.
