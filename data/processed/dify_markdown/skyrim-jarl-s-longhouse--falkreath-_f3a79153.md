Jarl's Longhouse in Falkreath

**Jarl's Longhouse** is the seat of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath")'s government and home to its [jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl"). It is in the center of Falkreath, opposite [Grave Concoctions](/wiki/Skyrim:Grave_Concoctions "Skyrim:Grave Concoctions") to the east and opposite [Falkreath Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Barracks "Skyrim:Falkreath Barracks") to the north.

### Jarl's Longhouse (Falkreath)

Entry is made directly into the main room where the Jarl holds court. There is a long fire pit in the center of the room. Against the northwest and southeast walls are flights of stairs leading up to balconies and small bedrooms on the upper floor. Under the stairs there is a door leading to the Jarl's bedroom, to the southeast and a doorway to the northwest to the military advisor's room. The Jarl's throne is at the far end of the room on a raised platform.

The Jarl's bedroom consists of a king sized bed on a raised platform in the center of the room. Against the northeast wall is a long [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot, two tall and a small wardrobe all containing [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") and footwear. There are a couple of [goblets](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") and a [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") on the small wardrobe. Against the southeast wall are two locked display cases, the one on the left is expert-locked and contains a copy of the rare book *[The Night Mother's Truth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Night_Mother%27s_Truth "Skyrim:The Night Mother's Truth")*, a copy of *[Ghosts in the Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghosts_in_the_Storm "Skyrim:Ghosts in the Storm")*, and copy of the [Pickpocket](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket "Skyrim:Pickpocket") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Guide to Better Thieving](/wiki/Skyrim:Guide_to_Better_Thieving "Skyrim:Guide to Better Thieving")*, while the one on the right is adept-locked and contains an amulet (see [notes](#Notes)), and a [gold necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Necklace "Skyrim:Gold Necklace"). In the southern corner are several sacks and a barrel containing [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") and a barrel containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"). To the right of these, along the southwest wall are three more wardrobes containing fine clothes. In the western corner is a round wooden table and two chairs holding two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and seven loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). Finally along the northwest wall is a wooden bench.

In the military advisor's room, the center of the room is taken over by a long wooden table with a large map of Skyrim showing the current state of the civil war. The military advisor spends all his waking hours poring over the map. On the southeast wall is a row of six weapon racks holding a variety of [iron weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons"), while in the southern corner there are a food barrel with a [potion of plentiful stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Stamina") and a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") on top. To the right, along the southwest wall are several straw bales with two practice dummies in front. In the western corner and continuing along the northwest wall there are more barrels. Along the northeast wall is a long wooden table, an unlocked chest containing minor loot and an owned cot, used by the advisor. On the table are four [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") healing of differing strengths, a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"), an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield") and a copy of the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Death Blow of Abernanit](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Blow_of_Abernanit "Skyrim:Death Blow of Abernanit")*. Above the cot is a wall shelf holding a couple of loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat") and seven bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead").

At the top of the stairs to the southeast is a balcony consisting of a round wooden table and two chairs against the southwest wall and a wooden bench opposite. The door to [Nenya](/wiki/Skyrim:Nenya "Skyrim:Nenya")'s bedroom is to the southwest. There is a long unlocked chest on the left as you enter, with a dresser to the northeast. On the dresser are two [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), a boiled creme treat and a couple of pieces of cheese. In the southern corner is an owned single bed, with a set of shelves next to it in the western corner holding three [green](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") and two [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), three bottles of Nord mead, two bottles of alto wine and a [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") with a leveled lock. There are several sacks of food either side of these shelves.

Opposite, to the northwest is another balcony, which consists of an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter") built-in to a round wooden table in the northern corner and against the northeast wall is another round wooden table holding a bottle of alto wine, one of wine, two carrots and an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"). The door to the northwest leads to [Helvard](/wiki/Skyrim:Helvard "Skyrim:Helvard")'s bedroom. It consists of an owned single bed in the western corner with a small wardrobe holding a copy of *[The Wispmother](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wispmother "Skyrim:The Wispmother")* and a [steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Steel War Axe") to the left. To the left when entering, on the southeast wall are two wall shelves holding four bottles of Nord mead, a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), an empty [lesser soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Lesser Soul Gem"), an empty [common soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem") and a copy of *[The Legend of Red Eagle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle "Skyrim:The Legend of Red Eagle")* which will initiate the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest) "Skyrim:Rebel's Cairn (quest)") when read for the first time. Along the northwest wall is a long unlocked chest containing minor loot, with a food barrel in the corner to the right. Against the northeast wall is a dresser with a large coin purse and six loose septims. On the other side of the door is a set of shelves holding four bottles of wine, two bottles of alto wine two pieces of cheese and two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato").

## Notes

- The [Ancient Nord Amulet](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord_Amulet "Skyrim:Ancient Nord Amulet") found in an adept-locked display case in the Jarl's bedchambers, is the only of its kind in the game.

## Bugs

- If you receive the *Letter from the Jarl of Falkreath*[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") before becoming Thane, Siddgeir may automatically treat you as friendly, and allow you to take low value things from the longhouse without it being considered theft. **?**
- Speaking to the Jarl of Falkreath and then reading the courier's letter will cause the "Speak to the Jarl of Falkreath" objective to become permanently stuck in your journal.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Hearthfire Patch"), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
- Opening the display cases in the Jarl's quarters can cause them to clip through the wall decorations hanging over them.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.1, fixes this bug.
