Alvasorr the Rat

**Alvasorr the Rat** is a [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit "Skyrim:Khajiit") priest of Namira, who is found in [Arcwind Point](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcwind_Point "Skyrim:Arcwind Point").

Alvasorr wears a set of [Falmer Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Armor "Skyrim:Falmer Armor") and the [Dismal Visage](/wiki/Skyrim:Dismal_Visage "Skyrim:Dismal Visage"). He has a [Falmer Bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Bow "Skyrim:Falmer Bow") and the unique Falmer axe [Namira's Itch](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Itch "Skyrim:Namira's Itch"), and carries up to 10 [Falmer Arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Arrow "Skyrim:Falmer Arrow"). He knows a level-dependent elemental attack spell, a healing spell, a ward spell and an armor spell.
