Beautiful Barbarian

The **Beautiful Barbarian** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") and a possible assassination target during the recurring quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever"). If chosen as the target, she will appear in the center of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"), just outside the [Vilemyr Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Vilemyr_Inn "Skyrim:Vilemyr Inn").

She wears a full set of [Stormcloak Officer Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Officer_Armor "Skyrim:Stormcloak Officer Armor") and wields a [steel greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Greatsword "Skyrim:Steel Greatsword").

## Related Quests

### The Dark Brotherhood Forever

Not knowing of her fate, she will ironically say to the player:

:   *"I'm dying for a rest. I swear, I could lie down right now, and just sleep forever."*

## Notes

- According to game data, she was originally meant to be found in the now nonexistent village of [Granite Hill](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard#Notes "Skyrim:Fort Sungard"), rather than Ivarstead.

## Bugs

- Sometimes the barbarian will appear with nothing equipped, despite having armor in her inventory.
