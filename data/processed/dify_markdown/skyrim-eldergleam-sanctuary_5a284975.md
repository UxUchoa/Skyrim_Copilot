Eldergleam Sanctuary

**Eldergleam Sanctuary** is a small [underground grove](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") and worship site of the followers of [Kynareth](/wiki/Lore:Kynareth "Lore:Kynareth") south-southwest of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). It contains only one zone, *[Eldergleam Sanctuary](#Eldergleam_Sanctuary)*.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-interior-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg)

Eldergleam Sanctuary interior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg/200px-SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg)

The Eldergleam tree

The sanctuary is renowned for its remarkable beauty and the giant tree [Eldergleam](/wiki/Lore:Eldergleam "Lore:Eldergleam"), which is said to move its roots for [one weapon only](/wiki/Skyrim:Nettlebane "Skyrim:Nettlebane"). The naturally-lit cave also abounds with plants and insects that can be harvested for [alchemical ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"). With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, [spadefish](/wiki/Skyrim:Spadefish "Skyrim:Spadefish") can be found swimming in the deeper water.

## Related Quests

- **[The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature")**: Restore the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") for [Danica Pure-Spring](/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring "Skyrim:Danica Pure-Spring").

### Exterior

The sanctuary is north of [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing") among the sulphurous pools and hot springs of [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch").

### Eldergleam Sanctuary

[Asta](/wiki/Skyrim:Asta "Skyrim:Asta") and [Sond](/wiki/Skyrim:Sond_(bandit) "Skyrim:Sond (bandit)") worship inside. Just before encountering Asta, you will find a chest and a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") next to a tree atop the rocks near the northern wall. If you want to mine the corundum, do so before going up to Eldergleam, as [Maurice Jondrelle](/wiki/Skyrim:Maurice_Jondrelle "Skyrim:Maurice Jondrelle") will permanently occupy the vein once his part in the quest is complete. You can also mine it after by attacking the ore with the pickaxe equipped. A copy of the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mystery of Talara, v 2](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_v_2 "Skyrim:Mystery of Talara, v 2")* is leaning on the rock next to Asta's knapsack.

A chest protected by Eldergleam's roots is in the back along the western wall. Hit the roots with Nettlebane to access the chest, which contains a random weapon or piece of armor and other loot.

The cave contains the following harvestable ingredients: four [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), two [creep clusters](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster"), six [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue"), twenty [hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss"), three [Jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes"), and two [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella").

## Notes

- A second corundum ore vein is outside, west of the entrance. Go past the mammoth skeleton and look for it on the other side of the nearby geyser.
- A hunter's camp is outside, southwest of the entrance. At the camp, three "naked" hunters appear to be relaxing in the volcanic hot waters of the shallow pond. Among their possessions is a copy of the [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") skill book *[Cherim's Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Cherim%27s_Heart "Skyrim:Cherim's Heart")*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=112691&y=-24574&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- A copy of the [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") skill book *[A Tragedy in Black](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Tragedy_in_Black "Skyrim:A Tragedy in Black")* and various soul gems are outside, east of the entrance on a crate atop a hill. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=124040&y=-22819&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Asta's knapsack and all of the items surrounding it are marked as owned by her. Everything else can be taken for free.
