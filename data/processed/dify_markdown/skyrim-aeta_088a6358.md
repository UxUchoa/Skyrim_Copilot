Aeta

**Aeta**, a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child"), is the daughter of [Oslaf](/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf "Skyrim:Oslaf") and [Finna](/wiki/Skyrim:Finna "Skyrim:Finna"). She lives in her family's [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf%27s_House "Skyrim:Oslaf's House") in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village"). She is also one of the few villagers, along with Finna, [Nikulas](/wiki/Skyrim:Nikulas_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Nikulas (Dragonborn)"), [Frea](/wiki/Skyrim:Frea "Skyrim:Frea"), and [Storn](/wiki/Skyrim:Storn_Crag-Strider "Skyrim:Storn Crag-Strider"), not under Miraak's control after you exit the Temple of Miraak.

She wears a unique set of dark brown [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Child's Clothes (Dragonborn)") with an apparent dusting of snow on the shoulders, and a pair of unique [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes_(xx03c81a) "Skyrim:Shoes (xx03c81a)"). She carries the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf%27s_House_Key "Skyrim:Oslaf's House Key") to her house and a selection of [food and toys](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). She is much more like her mother, as she is friendly towards strangers, unlike her father.

## Dialogue

When spoken to she can say:

:   *"I want to be a great hunter like [Wulf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wulf_Wild-Blood "Skyrim:Wulf Wild-Blood"). I practice stalking prey on the rabbits and the foxes in the woods."*
:   *"Mother worries when I walk too far from the village. She says a dragon might snatch me up, but I'm not afraid."*
:   *"The elders talk of great cities much bigger than our village, but why would anyone need so many buildings?"*

### Conversations

Aeta may be seen talking to her father about her first hunt:

:   **Aeta**: *"Papa, when can I go on my first hunt?"*
:   **Oslaf**: *"I've told you before, little cub, you're not old enough yet."*
:   **Aeta**: *"I just want to see. I promise not to get in the way of the hunters, and I'm not afraid of the beasts in the forest."*
:   **Oslaf**: *"I have given my answer, little cub. I know you are brave and have the heart of a great she-wolf, but all things have a time and place."*
:   **Aeta**: *"Yes, papa."*

## Quest-Related Events

If spoken to during the [Gardener of Men](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gardener_of_Men "Skyrim:The Gardener of Men") while Storn is being sacrificed:

:   *"What's happening to Storn?"*

Once Storn is dead:

:   *"What does it mean? What was that? What happened to him?"*
:   *"Leave me alone."*

She will approach the body to pay her respects.

:   *"It can't be... please, All-Maker, send him back to us..."*

## Notes

- Aeta will be sent to [Honorhall Orphanage](/wiki/Skyrim:Honorhall_Orphanage "Skyrim:Honorhall Orphanage") and can be [adopted](/wiki/Skyrim:Adoption "Skyrim:Adoption") if both her parents are killed.[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")
  - This is not possible until the completion of [At The Summit of Apocrypha](/wiki/Skyrim:At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha "Skyrim:At the Summit of Apocrypha"), as her [father](/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf "Skyrim:Oslaf") is marked essential until then.
    - If you adopt Aeta, you won't be able to move her back to Solstheim. This includes [Severin Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Severin_Manor "Skyrim:Severin Manor") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").
- Her dialogue for seeing a dead body (*"Oh no! By the gods! This can't be happening!"*) is the same as other children, even though the Skaal believe in a single god.
- Aeta is the only child that can be found on Solstheim.

## Bugs

- Once at Honorhall, Aeta may be unadoptable, repeating *"I must get back to the village"* instead of adoption dialogue, despite the player having approval from [Constance](/wiki/Skyrim:Constance_Michel "Skyrim:Constance Michel"). Speaking to Aeta at all before speaking to Constance may trigger this, causing the dialogue to persist even after getting permission.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
  - Reloading the save at the entrance to the orphanage and speaking to Constance first should fix this.
- Sometimes, after being adopted, she cannot be interacted with like a normal child, instead repeating *"I must get back to the village."* She will still refer to you as "Mama/Papa" in passing and will initiate gift/game dialogue, but cannot be activated manually.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
