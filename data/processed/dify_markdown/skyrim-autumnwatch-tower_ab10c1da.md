Autumnwatch Tower

**Autumnwatch Tower** is a [dragon lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Lairs "Skyrim:Dragon Lairs") south of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"). Its [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") teaches part of the [Marked for Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Marked_for_Death "Skyrim:Marked for Death") [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").

## Walkthrough

The location comprises a pair of abandoned towers. A dirt path leads to the southern tower, and a stone path branching to the north leads to the northern tower. There is a [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") at the base of the southern tower. Stairs lead up toward the northern tower, past an old extinguished campfire, with a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sack and various items of clutter nearby. There is a non-respawning patch of [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool") on your right, and a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") just to the right outside the entrance to the northern tower.

Inside the northern tower, the bottom floor contains another tanning rack by the entrance, with a set of shelves to the right and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in the middle of the floor. The shelves hold two [deer hides](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide "Skyrim:Deer Hide"), two [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), and a leveled [potion of Fortify Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Potion "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Potion"). Climbing the spiral stone stairs leads directly to the top floor, where three unowned bedrolls can be found. There is also an [enchanted](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Apparel "Skyrim:Generic Magic Apparel") pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") and a [hide helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Helmet "Skyrim:Hide Helmet") on a crate beside a straw bale. Finally, a copy of the [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Jornibret's Last Dance](/wiki/Skyrim:Jornibret%27s_Last_Dance "Skyrim:Jornibret's Last Dance")* and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") are on the small crate by a chair.

Inside the southern tower, the bottom floor contains two imp stool fungi, several food sacks and barrels, a loose piece of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"), and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") on a wooden block. The second floor holds a bedroll, a random rare [book](/wiki/Skyrim:Books "Skyrim:Books"), two loose pieces of firewood, a medium coin purse, and a wooden bowl holding a [chicken egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") beside a bale of straw; on the other side of the bale is a food barrel with an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") on top. The top floor contains a [skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") that can be looted for a copy of *[Physicalities of Werewolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Physicalities_of_Werewolves "Skyrim:Physicalities of Werewolves")* beside a crate with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on top and the unlocked boss [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). In an open crate beside the skeleton are two bowls of [vampire dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Dust "Skyrim:Vampire Dust"), and in another open crate on the other side of the chest are two samples of [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales"). There is a stone bridge crossing the gap to the word wall.

Before the [dragons' return](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising"), the towers will be occupied by a group of [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). There are two bandits at the bottom of the northern tower, a bandit outside the southern tower, and a bandit chief at the top.

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon"). The dragon will likely detect you before you ascend the towers, and can sometimes detect the [undead](/wiki/Skyrim:Undead "Skyrim:Undead") of [Arcwind Point](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcwind_Point "Skyrim:Arcwind Point") and will fly off to deal with them.

## Notes

- The tower can be reached from Arcwind Point via a wall of dark, sloped rock on the road to Arcwind directly south of the tower. Descending from this point lets you literally get the drop on the Autumnwatch dragon.
- If you fall off the stone bridge to the word wall, there is a small point in the rocks where you may get trapped and be unable to escape. Luckily you can still fast travel from here.
- The dragon at the tower may be killed by undead south of the cliff overlooking the location.
- A [quicksilver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore Vein") is 210 feet southwest of the tower.
