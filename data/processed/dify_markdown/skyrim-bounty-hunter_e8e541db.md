# Skyrim:Bounty Hunter

## Overview

Three **Bounty Hunters** can be [randomly encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Bounty_hunters_vs_Player "Skyrim:World Interactions") in the wilderness after they track you down to kill you by [order of the jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty_(player) "Skyrim:Bounty (player)") from a hold where you have accumulated a [bounty](/wiki/Skyrim:Crime#Bounties "Skyrim:Crime") of 1000 gold or higher. Their inventories are fully leveled.

All three Bounty Hunters will attack you at once. There is no way to pay them, as they will try to kill you without hesitation.
