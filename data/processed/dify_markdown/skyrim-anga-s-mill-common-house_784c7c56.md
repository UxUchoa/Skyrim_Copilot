The Common House

**Anga's Mill Common House** is a home in the small settlement of [Anga's Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill "Skyrim:Anga's Mill"), located next to [Aeri's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Aeri%27s_House "Skyrim:Aeri's House").

It is the home of the two unhappy and ever-nagging sawmill workers, [Kodrir](/wiki/Skyrim:Kodrir "Skyrim:Kodrir") and [Leifur](/wiki/Skyrim:Leifur "Skyrim:Leifur"). It consists of one area, *[Anga's Mill Common House](#Anga.27s_Mill_Common_House)*.

## Anga's Mill Common House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-interior-Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.jpg)

Interior view

The front door opens to the sight of the fireplace, which holds three gold coins on the mantel and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") at its base, where Leifur often cooks in the evening. In the southwestern area sit three respawning food barrels along with a well-dressed dinner table with two loaves and one piece of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), some [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and one bottle of regular [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). Atop one of the barrels is a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"). Across from the cooking pot stands a non-respawning cupboard with clutter, with some [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat") and a [pile of salt](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") on top. At the foot of the cupboard is a basket containing three [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), and a respawning food barrel.

Next to the fireplace is a kettle containing four [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and another dinner table with plenty of food: three slices of [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), two potatoes, a cluster of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), and three loaves of bread. Above the table hangs more food, some [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam"), two samples of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear"), and two [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid"). There are also some game underneath the ceiling, two [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). On the same wall stands a set of shelves with two bottles of wine and one of alto wine, and the [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest) "Skyrim:Rebel's Cairn (quest)")-related book *[The Legend of Red Eagle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle "Skyrim:The Legend of Red Eagle")*. Next up are a non-respawning dresser with clothes and a small non-respawning chest with very minor loot. The dresser stands behind one of the three owned beds in the sleeping area. Next to the first bed, a non-respawning end table contains clutter and has a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") on top.

Behind the next owned bed sits a respawning food barrel and a non-respawning wardrobe with a [sabre cat pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Pelt") on top. Opposite the wardrobe is a small table with two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). In the eastern corner are another non-respawning end table with clutter, a hidden medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b "Skyrim:Boots"), a [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000330b3 "Skyrim:Hat"), and a [potion of haggling](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling "Skyrim:Potion of Haggling"). The respawning chest at the foot of the bed contains minor loot, same as the non-respawning end table in the opposite corner. Two respawning food barrels are nearby. The end table has four gold coins on top, as well as a copy of the [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Biography of the Wolf Queen](/wiki/Skyrim:Biography_of_the_Wolf_Queen "Skyrim:Biography of the Wolf Queen")*.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House "Skyrim:Key to Anga's Mill Common House") to which is carried by both Kodrir and Leifur.
- There are three respawning food barrels near the door, two to the south and one to the north. A [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") rests atop a crate on the north side.
- Leifur and Kodrir never sleep; they just return here and idle around all night, be it eating or just standing about.
