## Quick Walkthrough

**Note**: This quest does not appear in your journal.

1. Encounter the regretful [ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Subjugated_Ghost "Skyrim:Subjugated Ghost") outside [Rannveig's Fast](/wiki/Skyrim:Rannveig%27s_Fast "Skyrim:Rannveig's Fast").
2. Fall into the [trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Trap_Door "Skyrim:Trap Door") of a [sadistic warlock](/wiki/Skyrim:Sild_the_Warlock "Skyrim:Sild the Warlock") **or** (optional) sneak behind the warlock for an ambush attack.
3. Claim the treasure and learn a word for the [Kyne's Peace](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Peace "Skyrim:Kyne's Peace") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").

### The Ghosts of Rannveig's Fast

The [Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Ruins "Skyrim:Nordic Ruins") called [Rannveig's Fast](/wiki/Skyrim:Rannveig%27s_Fast "Skyrim:Rannveig's Fast") is located in the wilderness south and slightly to the west of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal"), not far from the shores of [River Hjaal](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Hjaal "Skyrim:River Hjaal"). No road will take you there, so either follow the river or take a shortcut through [Cold Rock Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Cold_Rock_Pass "Skyrim:Cold Rock Pass") to reach the ruin. The exterior is quite large and guarded by some rather unusual [Subjugated Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Subjugated_Ghost "Skyrim:Subjugated Ghost") and a single [Ghost Adventurer](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost_Adventurer "Skyrim:Ghost Adventurer"), all of whom will apologize for attacking you. You can either choose to put them out of their misery or simply enter the ruin, as they will not follow you inside.

### The Setup

The door opens to a huge chamber with the sky visible above and the floor overgrown with ferns. Proceed north and you will meet another subjugated ghost, who is unwilling to attack you but forced to by unknown powers. The next huge chamber has another regretful ghost wandering around. There are several paths to choose: the locked southern door leads to a small chamber with a minor loot chest, while the northern door will offer an alternate route to discover the secrets of the ruin. The most tempting option will obviously be the chiming [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") to the east, with a huge glowing chest in front.

### The Truth About Sild

After battling Sild, make sure to claim the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Rannveigs_Fast_Key "Skyrim:Rannveigs Fast Key") if you haven't picked it up already. Notice the dead body of [Sild's assistant](/wiki/Skyrim:Sild%27s_Assistant "Skyrim:Sild's Assistant") near the cage, then examine the chamber for answers. The rows of cages contain more dead bandits, a dead [dog](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog "Skyrim:Dog"), a dead [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf_(dead) "Skyrim:Wood Elf (dead)"), and a dead [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton_(dead) "Skyrim:Breton (dead)"). Most importantly, examine the table next to the [Alchemy Lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") for *[Sild's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Sild%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Sild's Journal")* to learn the truth of Sild's sadistic activities. Lastly, unlock the boss-leveled chest for your reward, and check the table for an [Unusual Gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem") and the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") skill book *[Horror of Castle Xyr](/wiki/Skyrim:Horror_of_Castle_Xyr "Skyrim:Horror of Castle Xyr")*. When all is said and done, head east and, when you reach the wooden door, open it and head south. In the last room, a door leads outside to a small ledge east of the main entrance.

## Notes

- Learning the word of Kyne's Peace can be done after or before confronting Sild. The trap door is before the word wall, but you can either walk on the edge of the floor or jump over the trap door making it possible to run past the door without falling down.

## Bugs

- The quest is never shut down when Sild the Warlock dies.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage dunRannveigQST stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete.
