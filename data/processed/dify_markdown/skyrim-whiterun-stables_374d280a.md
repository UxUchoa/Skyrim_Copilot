Whiterun Stables

**Whiterun Stables** is a [stable](/wiki/Skyrim:Stables "Skyrim:Stables") just outside the gates of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").

Whiterun Stables is owned by [Lillith Maiden-Loom](/wiki/Skyrim:Lillith_Maiden-Loom "Skyrim:Lillith Maiden-Loom") and operated by [Skulvar Sable-Hilt](/wiki/Skyrim:Skulvar_Sable-Hilt "Skyrim:Skulvar Sable-Hilt"), who also lives here with his son [Jervar](/wiki/Skyrim:Jervar "Skyrim:Jervar"). You can buy a [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse") from Skulvar for 1000 gold.

## Related Quests

- **[Horse Whisperer](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Whisperer "Skyrim:Horse Whisperer")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Wild_Horses "Skyrim:Wild Horses"): Purchase a map that lets you find wild horses.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Exterior

The stables are to the right of the residence and consist of four stalls, two of which have gates on the front and always contain at least one [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse"). There are many food barrels in and around the stable and residence and a couple of [red mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") along the fence line. It is possible for both [Skulvar Sable-Hilt](/wiki/Skyrim:Skulvar_Sable-Hilt "Skyrim:Skulvar Sable-Hilt") and his son [Jervar](/wiki/Skyrim:Jervar "Skyrim:Jervar") to die (see [bugs](#Bugs)). A carriage waits nearby driven by [Bjorlam](/wiki/Skyrim:Bjorlam "Skyrim:Bjorlam"), while beside this, once you have completed [The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature") quest you will find [Ardwen](/wiki/Skyrim:Ardwen "Skyrim:Ardwen") and her [dog](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog "Skyrim:Dog").

## Whiterun Stables

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-interior-Whiterun_Stables.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Whiterun_Stables.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Whiterun_Stables.jpg)

Inside the Whiterun Stables residence

The entrance to the stable residence is normally novice-locked, which can be opened using a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Whiterun_Stables "Skyrim:Key to Whiterun Stables") carried by [Skulvar Sable-Hilt](/wiki/Skyrim:Skulvar_Sable-Hilt "Skyrim:Skulvar Sable-Hilt"), [Lillith Maiden-Loom](/wiki/Skyrim:Lillith_Maiden-Loom "Skyrim:Lillith Maiden-Loom") and [Jervar](/wiki/Skyrim:Jervar "Skyrim:Jervar"). Entering leads into a large room with a firepit opposite and a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") to the right. There are two doorways off of this room, one to the right of the firepit in the northern corner and the other in the western corner of the room. On your right when entering is a round wooden table and two chairs. On the table is a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a [long taffy treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Taffy_Treat "Skyrim:Long Taffy Treat"). To the left is a cupboard with a bowl containing a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), a couple of pieces of cheese on the shelves and a bottle of wine and a food sack on top. Against the southwest wall is a long wooden table and bench with a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a bottle of wine, a cabbage, a bowl containing [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") and a plate of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and cheese on top of the table. Hanging from the rafter above are four [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids and three whole [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)").

To the right of the table is the first doorway into a bedroom containing an owned bed in the southern corner with an end table to the left and a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") on the floor at the foot of the bed. On the end table is the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"), while in front, on the floor are a pair of [fine boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots "Skyrim:Fine Boots"). Against the northwest wall is a dresser containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") with a bottle of wine, a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") on top. To the right of the firepit is a pair of wall shelves holding mainly clutter, but with a haunch of raw [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison") on a plate. By these is the second doorway into another bedroom used by Lillith. There is a single bed in the western corner with an end table to the left and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed. On the end table is a bowl containing four bottles of Nord mead and the chest contains only minor loot. Against the northwest wall is a small set of shelves with a display case on top containing a random [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"), beside this is a set of clothes and on the lower shelf is a level-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"). Against the northeast wall is a long end table with a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a bottle of Nord mead, an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat") and a common book on top.

## Notes

- A [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") is located north of the stables. Go to the main gate, look left, go up the stairs and follow the wall.
- Whiterun Stables is the only stable in the game which only has one horse; all others have a saddled one and a bareback one.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the barrels outside the building as owned by the stables.

## Bugs

- There is a common occurrence of a bug here, when [Louis Letrush](/wiki/Skyrim:Louis_Letrush "Skyrim:Louis Letrush") becomes duplicated multiple times and/or becomes embedded in the ground just outside Whiterun after finishing [his quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Promises_to_Keep "Skyrim:Promises to Keep").
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.8, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") His duplicates can be dealt with using the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console"): click on the Letrush duplicate and type `kill` followed by `disable`.
- There aren't enough beds for all of the people who live here.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- If [Ardwen](/wiki/Skyrim:Ardwen "Skyrim:Ardwen") and her dog reach the stables after you decide to repair the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") in [The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature"), they'll stay here 24 hours a day.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- Lilith Maiden-Loom can tell you to visit the stables to see the horses even if you're already here.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
