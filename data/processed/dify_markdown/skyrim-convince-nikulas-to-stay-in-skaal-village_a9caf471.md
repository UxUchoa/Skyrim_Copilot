## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Edla](/wiki/Skyrim:Edla "Skyrim:Edla") about her son.
2. Convince [Nikulas](/wiki/Skyrim:Nikulas_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Nikulas (Dragonborn)") to stay in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").
3. Return to Edla.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Return to [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village") after the quest [The Fate of the Skaal](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Fate_of_the_Skaal "Skyrim:The Fate of the Skaal") and you can find [Edla](/wiki/Skyrim:Edla "Skyrim:Edla") wandering around the village. She may tell you *"My son's heart is restless. He wants to see the world beyond our lands, and nothing I say will change his mind."* If you ask her about her son, she will provide you with more detailed information. His name is [Nikulas](/wiki/Skyrim:Nikulas_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Nikulas (Dragonborn)"), and he is a good lad, a strong and loyal young man. However, Edla wishes her son wasn't so eager to leave the village. If you are willing to talk to him, she will be quite happy. *"You would do that? I know he would listen to you. You've been out there in the wide world. You know its perils."* She hopes you can convince him.

You can find Nikulas sitting on a bench outside [their house](/wiki/Skyrim:Edla%27s_House "Skyrim:Edla's House"). You may talk to him about his mother and his dream. He will admit *"That's right. I want to go out into the world and earn great glory. I want to hunt the deadliest creatures and claim vast riches."* He is weary of the life in Skaal Village and he wants to do something with his life other than farm, fish, and hunt deer. You may choose various ways to convince him to stay at home or remind him to think about his mother before making the final decision.

At last, he will see that in his eagerness for adventure, he has forgotten his duties as a son and as a Skaal. He will thank you for your wisdom.

Return to Edla and tell her that Nikulas has agreed to stay. *"May the All-Maker bless you with many sons and daughters, outsider. Once again you have shown that you are a true friend of the Skaal."* She will reward you with some herbs that are unique to the land, including five [Ashen Grass Pod](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Ashen Grass Pod"), five [Emperor Parasol Moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor_Parasol_Moss "Skyrim:Emperor Parasol Moss"), and five [Scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw"). She still thinks the reward can hardly be equal to the joy in her heart.

## Bugs

- Nikulas may not receive the quest-relevant dialogue options. Disabling and re-enabling him via the console resolves this issue. **?**
- Finishing this quest may not raise Edla's disposition towards you.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
