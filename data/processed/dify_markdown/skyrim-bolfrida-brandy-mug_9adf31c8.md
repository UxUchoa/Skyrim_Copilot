**Bolfrida Brandy-Mug** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") who lives and works at [Brandy-Mug Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Brandy-Mug_Farm "Skyrim:Brandy-Mug Farm"), a short distance east of [Windhelm Stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm_Stables "Skyrim:Windhelm Stables"). She lives with a [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") and two [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken"). She grows [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") and [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") in the field beside her small cottage and will [pay you](/wiki/Skyrim:Gather_Wheat "Skyrim:Gather Wheat") five gold for every piece of wheat you bring to her.

She gets up early at 6am and eats a one-hour breakfast. She then goes out at 7am to her farm, and works for eight hours. When she is done at the farm for the day, at 3pm, she heads back to her house. She then hangs out in her house for a couple of hours, eating a two-hour dinner at 5pm, and going to bed at 11pm.

She wears some farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000209a6 "Skyrim:Clothes"), a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5 "Skyrim:Boots"), and a [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000209aa "Skyrim:Hat"). She wields an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Brandy-Mug_Farmhouse_Key "Skyrim:Brandy-Mug Farmhouse Key") to her farmhouse and some [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

Bolfrida is friendly to visitors. She also seems to love her job and keeps a positive attitude. She'll greet you with one of the following lines:

:   *"Welcome, welcome, welcome! Don't see many strangers out here. Come to get your hands dirty on a real farm?"*
:   *"Nothing tastes better than food you've grown yourself. Something magic about dirt and sunshine making food."*
:   *"The weather's harsh, but I like to think my sunny personality is what helps the grain grow."*

### Conversations

She can be seen conversing with her one employee, [Faryl Atheron](/wiki/Skyrim:Faryl_Atheron "Skyrim:Faryl Atheron"), about the farm.

:   **Faryl:** *"I don't think we're going to get those potatoes to come in."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Well of course, not with an attitude like that!"*
:   **Faryl:** *"The ground is practically frozen. We can't dig down far enough."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Come on, we just need a little elbow grease."*

They'll talk about what it would be like to try growing crops in [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind").

:   **Bolfrida:** *"I'll bet this is better than trying to grow something in Morrowind, though, isn't it?"*
:   **Faryl:** *"Firstly, I've never even seen Morrowind. Secondly, in volcanic soil the plants practically grow themselves."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Oh, my, I hadn't thought of that."*
:   **Faryl:** *"There are places in the world that aren't wrapped in ice, you know."*

They'll also talk about growing crops in permafrost.

:   **Bolfrida:** *"Now, I've been reading about the best ways to grow corn in permafrost..."*
:   **Faryl:** *"I keep telling you, without a warming enchantment, it will never grow past your ankles."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Right, but if you plow the soil with [fire salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts "Skyrim:Fire Salts")..."*
:   **Faryl:** *"Then you've salted it and nothing will grow at all. Genius."*
:   **Bolfrida:** *"Oh dear, I hadn't thought of that."*
