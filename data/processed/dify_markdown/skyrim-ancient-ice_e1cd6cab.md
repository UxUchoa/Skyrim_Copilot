## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read *[Skjol's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Skjol%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Skjol's Journal")* at the camp southwest of [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village") near the lake by [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall").
2. Journey to the [Horker Iceberg](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Solstheim#Horker_Iceberg "Skyrim:Unmarked Places Solstheim") northeast of [Northshore Landing](/wiki/Skyrim:Northshore_Landing "Skyrim:Northshore Landing") and kill [Skjol the Tongueless](/wiki/Skyrim:Skjol_the_Tongueless "Skyrim:Skjol the Tongueless").
3. Claim the [Stalhrim Fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Stalhrim Fur Armor") for yourself.

### Cold Blooded Murder

Travel to a camp south of [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village"), just across from [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall") on the island of [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim"). There you will come upon the body of a dead [Skaal villager](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Villager "Skyrim:Skaal Villager"). The murderer's name is [Skjol the Tongueless](/wiki/Skyrim:Skjol_the_Tongueless "Skyrim:Skjol the Tongueless"), as revealed by the [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Skjol%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Skjol's Journal") left at the scene of the crime. Skjol does not appear to be too bright, and killed the villager after learning the location of a [stalhrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim "Skyrim:Stalhrim") deposit north of [Castle Karstaag](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Karstaag "Skyrim:Castle Karstaag").

The deposit in question is on an [unnamed island](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Solstheim#Horker_Iceberg "Skyrim:Unmarked Places Solstheim") just northeast of [Northshore Landing](/wiki/Skyrim:Northshore_Landing "Skyrim:Northshore Landing") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=28333&y=99959&zoom=16&legacy=true). Usually home to angry [horkers](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker "Skyrim:Horker"), you will instead find it to be occupied by a much angrier Skjol wielding an enchanted battleaxe. He will attack on sight, so put him down and claim the hoard for yourself: a rare deposit of [stalhrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim "Skyrim:Stalhrim") and a full set of [Stalhrim Fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Stalhrim Fur Armor") lying in a sarcophagus. Take the set to complete the quest; you will now begin to see Stalhrim Fur items showing up as random loot in the world.

## Notes

- The location is not on the map. To find the camp, travel north from Thirsk Mead Hall, along the lake.
- Stalhrim Fur armor uses the same model as [Stalhrim armor](/wiki/Blades:Stalhrim "Blades:Stalhrim") in *[Blades](/wiki/Blades:Blades "Blades:Blades")*.
- The island where Skjol makes his last stand can be difficult to climb onto from the water. If you use [Ahzidal's Boots of Waterwalking](/wiki/Skyrim:Ahzidal%27s_Boots_of_Waterwalking "Skyrim:Ahzidal's Boots of Waterwalking"), found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolbjorn_Barrow "Skyrim:Kolbjorn Barrow") elsewhere on Solstheim, you can attack Skjol with ranged attacks while avoiding his battleaxe, or jump from the water's surface onto the shore and avoid being attacked while in the water as you struggle to climb out.
- Stalhrim Fur Armor will appear in leveled lists once the quest has been completed, sometimes being worn by [Reaver Lords](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver_Lord "Skyrim:Reaver Lord").
