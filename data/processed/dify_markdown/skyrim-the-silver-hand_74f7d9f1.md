## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Skjor](/wiki/Skyrim:Skjor "Skyrim:Skjor").
2. Meet with Skjor at night.
3. Enter the [Underforge](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Underforge "Skyrim:The Underforge").
4. Participate in the blood ritual.
5. Talk to [Aela the Huntress](/wiki/Skyrim:Aela_the_Huntress "Skyrim:Aela the Huntress").
6. Kill the [werewolf hunters](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Hand "Skyrim:Silver Hand").

### A Secret Meeting

After completing another set of radiant quests, the usual job providers will once again point you towards [Skjor](/wiki/Skyrim:Skjor "Skyrim:Skjor"). Aela will say:
*"Still eager to please. This is good. Skjor may be right about you. He has something special planned for you. Better talk to him."*, while Farkas will say: *"Skjor was looking for you before. Talk to him before doing anything else."*

Skjor turns out to be less talkative than usual and will request a meeting after dark near the Underforge, a secret crypt underneath the smithy of [Eorlund Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Eorlund_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Eorlund Gray-Mane"). At night, Skjor will wait for you near the secret entrance, determined to make you a [stronger member](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Circle "Skyrim:The Circle") of the Companions. He will then open the secret entrance and encourage you to follow.

Inside the Underforge, you will encounter [Aela the Huntress](/wiki/Skyrim:Aela_the_Huntress "Skyrim:Aela the Huntress") in her lycanthropic form, standing next to an empty fountain. Skjor will introduce her as your 'forebear', and will inform you that this is to be kept secret from Harbinger [Kodlak Whitemane](/wiki/Skyrim:Kodlak_Whitemane "Skyrim:Kodlak Whitemane"), and will then ask if you are prepared. When you are ready, Skjor will cut open Aela's wrist, filling the fountain with her blood. Activate the fountain and receive the [Beast Form](/wiki/Skyrim:Beast_Form "Skyrim:Beast Form") power, which causes you to black out.

### Rampage

You wake up outside the Underforge, now as a [werewolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Lycanthropy "Skyrim:Lycanthropy"), where you will find all of Whiterun's residents are now hostile to you. If you head into the streets, citizens will flee and guards will attack you, leaving you no choice but to avoid them or kill them. Due to your beastly appearance, you will not gain a bounty for assaulting anyone. After a certain amount of time, you will black out once again.

The easiest way to avoid unwanted casualties is to simply wait a while after exiting the Underforge, as this causes your werewolf bloodlust to wear off, and the next scene to continue.

Another option is to go back into the Underforge as soon as you've transformed, and use the secret exit that leads you out of the city.

You can also simply avoid any attackers if you do not wish to kill any people in the city, you'll black out regardless of whether you killed anyone or not.

### The Silver Hand

This time, you wake up in the middle of nowhere, with a concerned Aela looking over you. Your gear has been unequipped, but you haven't lost anything; simply re-equip to prepare for battle. Aela will congratulate you on surviving the initiation and inform you that the [Silver Hand](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Hand "Skyrim:Silver Hand"), a group of werewolf hunters, have inhabited [Gallows Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock "Skyrim:Gallows Rock") and will not hide the fact that they should be slaughtered right away. She also informs you that Skjor already entered the fort. From this point, Aela will act as your follower, dismissing your current follower if you have one. Head west towards the fort. A number of Silver Hand members will guard the exterior area, so dispose of them and enter the fort.

### Gallows Rock

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-quest-The_Silver_Hand_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Silver_Hand_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Silver_Hand_03.jpg)

Aela fighting Krev the Skinner

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-quest-The_Silver_Hand2.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Silver_Hand2.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Silver_Hand2.jpg)

*"The bastards... somehow they managed to kill Skjor."*

The first room is a sealed-off area, so pull the chain next to the severed werewolf head and proceed forward. The fort is buzzing with enemies and werewolves in cages, both living and dead, all seemingly exposed to torture by their captors. Be on the lookout for the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") skill book *[The Importance of Where](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Importance_of_Where "Skyrim:The Importance of Where")* in the huge southern chamber, the [Smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") skill book *[The Last Scabbard of Akrash](/wiki/Skyrim:Last_Scabbard_of_Akrash "Skyrim:Last Scabbard of Akrash")* near the workbench, as well as several locked and unlocked chests. Aela will comment on the first dead werewolf you find, which is found next to a set of rare [Torture Tools](/wiki/Skyrim:Torture_Tools "Skyrim:Torture Tools").

When you reach the northwestern chamber, Aela will rightfully warn you about Silver Hand leader [Krev the Skinner](/wiki/Skyrim:Krev_the_Skinner "Skyrim:Krev the Skinner"), so be prepared. When the chamber is cleared, a worried Aela will run up onto the podium and discover the dead body of [Skjor](/wiki/Skyrim:Skjor "Skyrim:Skjor"), obviously killed due to his lycanthropy. Once you speak with her, the quest will end and you will have to find your own way out, through the door to the northeast that was previously barred. Three new [radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions#Radiant_Quests "Skyrim:Companions") are now available back in [Jorrvaskr](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorrvaskr "Skyrim:Jorrvaskr") and you will have to complete two of them (for Aela) before you can access the next major quest. However, if a certain amount of time has passed, you may only have to do one radiant quest to begin [Blood's Honor](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood%27s_Honor "Skyrim:Blood's Honor").[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:The_Silver_Hand#Added_note)*]

One [achievement](/wiki/Skyrim:Achievements "Skyrim:Achievements") is unlocked once you speak with [Aela](/wiki/Skyrim:Aela_the_Huntress "Skyrim:Aela the Huntress") after surviving the initiation:

## Notes

- You don't need to wait until nightfall to meet Skjor. If you leave the Companions' compound, enter another building, and exit the building you entered, he will make his way to the entrance to the Underforge by the time you return, and the quest will continue like normal.
- If you sleep before meeting with Skjor and Aela, you will retain the effects of being Rested, Well Rested or Lover's Comfort until time expires. Your beast's blood will prevent you from getting them again unless you cure your lycanthropy after the Companions questline is completed, so do not sleep in any of the many beds in the Silver Hand base.
- If you have [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") installed, this is your first opportunity to start acquiring werewolf perks. If you're in Whiterun, you can kill and feed on guards and citizens for werewolf experience. Guards killed will respawn eventually, but citizens will stay dead.
- Once you enter the Underforge, you can talk to Aela in her beast form. If you have the *Special Edition*, Nintendo Switch version, or *Anniversary Edition*, you can also engage in conversation with Aela in her beast form (you'll hear her voice as well).
- Unless you have the [Ring of Hircine](/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Hircine "Skyrim:Ring of Hircine"), transforming is not immediately possible during the Gallows Rock section. This is because Beast Form is considered a greater power, thus there is a one day cooldown before you can transform again. Simply waiting 24 hours will refresh this and allow you to use your new gift to fight the Silver Hand.
- Version 2.1.0 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") turns Gallows Rock into a no-reset zone, and makes it unclearable during this quest.
- After being killed in this quest, Krev the Skinner will eventually respawn with the rest of the dungeon.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.1, addresses this issue. A generic boss will spawn instead.

## Bugs

- At the end of running around on your rampage through Whiterun, the screen will fade and load the next sequence in the quest. After it finishes loading, the game automatically closes for no reason.
  - Simply restarting the game and trying again should fix this.
- Sometimes, you can transform again after the Rampage stage, but before the Gallows Rock stage, enabling you to fight the Silver Hand in Beast Form and ignoring the 24 hour cooldown.
- Sometimes, you won't go on the rampage through Whiterun and instead skip straight to the part where you are with Aela, although the 24-hour cooldown period is active. **?**
- Skjor always says "Not now. The Underforge, tonight." even if it is nighttime due to either/or dialogue conditions.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3, addresses this issue. Both dialogue conditions have been merged together.
