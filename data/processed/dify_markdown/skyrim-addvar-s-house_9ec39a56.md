Addvar's House

**Addvar's House** is a small house in the residential district of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), next to [Evette San's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Evette_San%27s_House "Skyrim:Evette San's House").

It is the home of town fishmonger [Addvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Addvar "Skyrim:Addvar"), his wife [Greta](/wiki/Skyrim:Greta "Skyrim:Greta"), and their daughter [Svari](/wiki/Skyrim:Svari "Skyrim:Svari").

It contains only one zone, *[Addvar's House](#Walkthrough)*.

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-interior-Addvar%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Addvar%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Addvar%27s_House.jpg)

Addvar's House interior

This house is a comfortably lit wooden building. To the left of the rug by the entrance is a basket holding a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), along with a small crate containing a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing"), a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), and a [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), as well as a nearby bucket holding a whole [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). Hanging above the half-wall to the left are three rings, which hold a total of six [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and one [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)").

In the central area, there is a table with two chairs, above which are three hanging [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids. On the table are two tankards, a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), garlic, an [Eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and two identical meals on wooden plates, which consist of four [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks") and [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"). In front of the fireplace is a [cooking](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") pot and a cast iron pot holding a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"). Mounted above the fireplace is a goat head.

To the left is a small area set aside for food preparation. Beneath an elk head mounted on the wall is a table with four tankards, a barrel of mead, a horn candle, two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), [beef stew](/wiki/Skyrim:Beef_Stew "Skyrim:Beef Stew"), a [pheasant breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), two and a half loaves of bread, two [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), four grilled leeks, a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), seared slaughterfish, and a number of cheese wheels and wedges.

To the right are two rooms. The one next to the basket of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton") contains a bed belonging to Svari. In this room are two respawning food barrels, a basket, a bucket, a non-respawning wardrobe, and a non-respawning end table with [Alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a horn candle on it. The next room is the pantry, with seven [goat cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), seven [Eidar cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), six potatoes, three cabbages, and two [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"). On top of one shelving unit is a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"), and on the other is a copy of the book *[Uncommon Taste](/wiki/Skyrim:Uncommon_Taste "Skyrim:Uncommon Taste")*. There are also three respawning food barrels stacked behind the shelving units, and a few clutter items. Next to this room, under the stairs, is a non-respawning chest containing a small amount of gold, with a high chance for an [animal part](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Parts "Skyrim:Animal Parts") or other miscellaneous item to appear, and a very small chance of a torch appearing.

The upper level has a number of small items, along with a few pieces of furniture to sit on. On a long crate there rests a non-respawning large sack and a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). The nearby chest (non-respawning) only has loot if you have [Imperial Luck](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Luck "Skyrim:Imperial Luck"), [Prowler's Profit](/wiki/Skyrim:Prowler%27s_Profit "Skyrim:Prowler's Profit"), or [Golden Touch](/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Touch_(perk) "Skyrim:Golden Touch (perk)"). On the adjacent cupboard (non-respawning) are a gourd, a gold piece, [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000cee80 "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"), [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5 "Skyrim:Boots"), a [troll skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull "Skyrim:Troll Skull"), three pieces of garlic, some tundra cotton, a clothes iron, and a human skull. On a thin table near this cupboard are an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet"), a roll of paper, a tankard, and a horn candle. In a corner is a double bed belonging to Addvar. Near this bed are a non-respawning end table and a table with a chair next to it. On the table are two tankards, some bread, a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and a melted candle.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Addvar%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Addvar's House") to which is carried by Greta and Svari.
- There is a "Loot" [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (Thieves Guild)") to the bottom left of the entrance door.

## Bugs

- The double bed is only owned by Addvar, which results in Greta being unable to use it, and therefore she isn't able to sleep at all.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.4, fixes this bug.
