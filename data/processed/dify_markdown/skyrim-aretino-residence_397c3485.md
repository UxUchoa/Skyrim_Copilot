The Aretino Residence in Windhelm

The **Aretino Residence** is a house in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") owned by the Aretino family. After the death of Naalia Aretino, her son [Aventus](/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino "Skyrim:Aventus Aretino") was sent to [Honorhall Orphanage](/wiki/Skyrim:Honorhall_Orphanage "Skyrim:Honorhall Orphanage") until his sixteenth birthday. However, Aventus ran away from the orphanage and returned home to perform the [Black Sacrament](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother "Skyrim:A Kiss, Sweet Mother").

The house is found on the eastern side of Windhelm, above the Gray Quarter. The house is easy to spot, since its building has a curious shape, spanning over the road. It consists of one area, *[Aretino Residence](#Aretino_Residence)*.

## Aretino Residence

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_02.jpg)

Inside Aretino Residence

The front door opens to a set of stairs leading up, and you will hear [Aventus Aretino](/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino "Skyrim:Aventus Aretino") chanting somewhere in the house. The first room is dimly lit and contains a non-respawning end table with some random books and a knocked-over bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on the floor. Along the southern wall sits a non-respawning dresser, with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a non-respawning food sack on top.

The neighboring room is lit up by numerous candles used by Aventus to perform the ritual. Next to the door is a respawning knapsack and a set of shelves with some [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"), a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), a [potion of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), and a small bowl with [frost salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Salts "Skyrim:Frost Salts"). Behind the shelves is a copy of the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Hypothetical Treachery](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Hypothetical_Treachery "Skyrim:A Hypothetical Treachery")*. Along the eastern wall is Aventus' owned bed and another non-respawning dresser with a bottle of alto wine on top. In the adjacent corner is a small dinner table with some food, a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat"), a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), and a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). The unlit fireplace holds two pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") and a non-respawning pot. On the floor is a devastating [letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_the_Steward "Skyrim:Letter from the Steward") written by [Jorleif](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorleif "Skyrim:Jorleif"), the personal steward of [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_03.jpg)

The alcove

In the southern alcove sits Aventus, chanting the Black Sacrement over and over, frustrated that the [Night Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Mother "Skyrim:Night Mother") doesn't hear his plea. For the ritual he has set up a number of candles, along with a chunk of [human flesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Human_Flesh "Skyrim:Human Flesh") and a [human heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Human_Heart "Skyrim:Human Heart"). The obligatory tome *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother "Skyrim:A Kiss, Sweet Mother")* lies next to him, with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and a sample of [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") on top. Talking to Aretino will trigger the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") quest [Innocence Lost](/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost "Skyrim:Innocence Lost").

## Notes

- When you first approach the house, you will overhear a conversation between [Idesa Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Idesa_Sadri "Skyrim:Idesa Sadri") and [Grimvar Cruel-Sea](/wiki/Skyrim:Grimvar_Cruel-Sea "Skyrim:Grimvar Cruel-Sea") about Aventus Aretino.
- There is a "Danger" shadowmark on the bottom of the arch in front of the house.
- As the house is scripted to reset, it provides a supply of both human hearts and flesh.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue. It removes the ritual items but the house still respawns.
