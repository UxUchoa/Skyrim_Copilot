## Walkthrough

If you murder an NPC who has a relative, the relative may subsequently send three [hired thugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Hired_Thug "Skyrim:Hired Thug") to hunt you down and teach you a lesson (i.e. kill you in vengeance). The thugs will be carrying a [contract](/wiki/Skyrim:Contract_(Murder) "Skyrim:Contract (Murder)") which reveals the name of the relative who hired the thugs.

## Notes

- You may also be attacked if you steal from someone (see [Steal, Thugs hunt player](/wiki/Skyrim:Steal,_Thugs_hunt_player "Skyrim:Steal, Thugs hunt player")).
- You can attack the thugs at a distance via magic or archery while they are non-hostile (before they get a chance to initiate dialogue with you) individually to make the fight easier; the ones not being attacked will remain non-hostile as they continue to approach, so you can thin their numbers before they actually get to talk to you.

## Bugs

- If you kill someone then kill any of their relatives then you may find that one of the hired thugs sent after you has a contract note from someone you have already killed.
- The thugs may be hired by [children](/wiki/Skyrim:Children "Skyrim:Children").

## Quest Stages

- All of the quest stages are empty: 0, 1, 255.

It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest WIKill06`.
