## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak").
2. Receive Ulfric's War Axe.
3. Go to [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") and give the axe to Jarl [Balgruuf the Greater](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater").
4. Wait for Balgruuf's reply.
5. Return to Ulfric.

### A Gift for a Jarl

[Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak") has decided that it's time to make [Whiterun's](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") Jarl [Balgruuf the Greater](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater") pick a side. Ulfric hopes to avoid bloodshed in Whiterun, so he'll give you an axe (*Ulfric's War Axe*) and tell you to deliver it to Balgruuf. He has no further message for you to take, as he says that the axe is a gesture between two warriors who understand each other and that Balgruuf will know its meaning.

### Making Up His Mind

When you speak to Jarl Balgruuf, tell him you have a message from Ulfric. Balgruuf will take the axe, but whether or not the quest proceeds depends on if you've already finished [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") (and its prerequisite quests [Before the Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Before_the_Storm "Skyrim:Before the Storm") and [Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (quest)")). If you've already done Dragon Rising then the quest will continue normally; otherwise Balgruuf will hold on to the axe and postpone making a decision about it until Dragon Rising is complete.

With the time having come to give Ulfric an answer, Balgruuf will discuss the matter with his steward [Proventus Avenicci](/wiki/Skyrim:Proventus_Avenicci "Skyrim:Proventus Avenicci") and his housecarl [Irileth](/wiki/Skyrim:Irileth "Skyrim:Irileth"). Balgruuf will state that he's not entirely happy with Imperial policy, but ultimately he'll decide to side with the Empire. He'll give the axe back to you and begin drafting a letter to negotiate with the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") about garrisoning some of their troops in Whiterun.

### The Die is Cast

Go back to Windhelm and return the axe to Ulfric. He'll be disappointed in Balgruuf's decision, but [Galmar](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist") will welcome it; he's ready for battle and eager to launch the assault. After a short conversation, Ulfric will issue orders that initiate the next quest: [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)").

## Notes

- If you are on [The World-Eater's Eyrie](/wiki/Skyrim:The_World-Eater%27s_Eyrie "Skyrim:The World-Eater's Eyrie") quest and [Odahviing](/wiki/Skyrim:Odahviing "Skyrim:Odahviing") is still in Dragonsreach, you will not be able to deliver the axe to Jarl Balgruuf.
- If you have not completed [Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (quest)") and [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising"), you will not be able to deliver the axe to Jarl Balgruuf.
- Completing this quest places Whiterun under siege and makes the city in its normal state unavailable to you until the completion of [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)").

## Bugs

- Progressing through Message to Whiterun while Season Unending is still open will block progression for both quests.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
