The Ragged Flagon - Cistern

**The Ragged Flagon - Cistern** is the [Thieves Guild's](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") headquarters in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). It is connected to [the Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon"), but also has its own secret entrance in Riften's cemetery. This secret entrance is located beneath a tomb that dominates the small graveyard to the east of Riften's [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Riften) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Riften)"). The secret entrance can be accessed once you [join the Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Taking_Care_of_Business "Skyrim:Taking Care of Business"). To open the entrance, press a small button inside the diamond symbol in the center of the tomb. The tomb should slide back to reveal a stone staircase. Descend it and open the sewer covering to access the Cistern.

The Ragged Flagon - Cistern is inaccessible by either entrance until you have joined the Thieves Guild.

### The Ragged Flagon - Cistern

You will initially be taken to the Cistern by [Brynjolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Brynjolf "Skyrim:Brynjolf"). After you meet him in [the Ragged Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ragged_Flagon "Skyrim:The Ragged Flagon"), he will lead you to a storage cabinet with a false back. Behind the cabinet is a passage with a small bedroom on the right. The bedroom contains two owned beds with an end table between them, two cupboards, a round table with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and an unlocked chest. All items in the Ragged Flagon are flagged as owned, but are easy pickings. The passage behind the cabinet ends at a door that opens into the main headquarters for the Guild, the Cistern.

When you first enter the Cistern, [Mercer Frey](/wiki/Skyrim:Mercer_Frey "Skyrim:Mercer Frey") will be awaiting you in the center of the room, with five other thieves present round the room: [Cynric Endell](/wiki/Skyrim:Cynric_Endell "Skyrim:Cynric Endell"), [Niruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Niruin "Skyrim:Niruin"), [Rune](/wiki/Skyrim:Rune "Skyrim:Rune"), [Thrynn](/wiki/Skyrim:Thrynn "Skyrim:Thrynn"), and [Vipir the Fleet](/wiki/Skyrim:Vipir_the_Fleet "Skyrim:Vipir the Fleet"). Other thieves return or arrive as you complete specific quests. [Sapphire](/wiki/Skyrim:Sapphire_(person) "Skyrim:Sapphire (person)") returns once you have completed the [Stabilized](/wiki/Skyrim:Stabilized "Skyrim:Stabilized") quest for [Shadr](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadr "Skyrim:Shadr"). [Etienne Rarnis](/wiki/Skyrim:Etienne_Rarnis "Skyrim:Etienne Rarnis") moves here once released from the [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy") during [Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity"). Finally, two new thieves, [Garthar](/wiki/Skyrim:Garthar "Skyrim:Garthar") and [Ravyn Imyan](/wiki/Skyrim:Ravyn_Imyan "Skyrim:Ravyn Imyan"), join the guild once you have started to return the Guild to its former glory.

The Cistern consists of a large circular chamber with two storm drains emptying from the ceiling high overhead, with waterfalls cascading into a pool under a cross-shaped stone walkway. There are wooden suspension bridges linking each quadrant around the outside. All containers, excluding barrels and the chests within the training room used for practicing lockpicking, are safe for storage. There is a pair of cupboards containing clutter flanking the tunnel leading into the room from the Ragged Flagon. Nine single owned beds, in addition to your bed, come out from the outer walls; seven of the ten beds have unlocked guild chests at the foot of them, all containing minor loot. There are several end tables beside the beds around the room. The first bed on the right when entering from the Ragged Flagon is assigned to you. There are additional pairs of [thieves guild gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Gloves "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Gloves") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Boots "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Boots") on the floor on either side of your bed. Around the room are several bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), wine, and [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), along with a few [common books](/wiki/Skyrim:Books "Skyrim:Books") and three copies of *[Shadowmarks](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(book) "Skyrim:Shadowmarks (book)")*, which explains the meaning of the different [symbols](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Shadowmarks "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") scattered across Skyrim. Several [notes](/wiki/Skyrim:Notes "Skyrim:Notes") can be found around the room, including ones titled *[East Empire Connection](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Connection "Skyrim:East Empire Connection")*, *[Eyes Open](/wiki/Skyrim:Eyes_Open "Skyrim:Eyes Open")*, and *[No Word Yet](/wiki/Skyrim:No_Word_Yet "Skyrim:No Word Yet")*, all of which give insights into the Guild and/or some of its members.

Opposite the entrance, in an alcove to the southeast, is a pair of double metal doors guarding the Guild's treasure room which require two keys, with a table and a barrel to the left and another cupboard containing clutter to the right. There is a desk farther to the left of the alcove, where Mercer Frey is normally found. On the desk is the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger") and two [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"). Behind the desk are two sets of shelves; as you acquire certain unique items as part of [The Litany of Larceny](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Litany_of_Larceny "Skyrim:The Litany of Larceny") quest and sell them to [Delvin Mallory](/wiki/Skyrim:Delvin_Mallory "Skyrim:Delvin Mallory"), they will appear on the shelves or desk. Also, as you complete more side jobs for [Vex](/wiki/Skyrim:Vex "Skyrim:Vex") and Delvin, certain other trophies will begin to fill the shelves. Between the sets of shelves is a blank bust, which is used to display [Barenziahs Crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Barenziahs_Crown "Skyrim:Barenziahs Crown") once you collect all twenty-four of the [unusual gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem") and recover the crown as part of the [No Stone Unturned](/wiki/Skyrim:No_Stone_Unturned "Skyrim:No Stone Unturned") quest. To the right of the alcove is a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") and a barrel in front of a row of four weapon racks, which hold an [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") and a [steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") and an iron and a steel [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword"). To the right of the rack are three archery dummies, where Cynric, Thrynn, and Ravyn Imyan (after he joins) spend hours each day honing their [archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") skills.

In the alcove to the southwest is a ladder leading to the concealed cemetery entrance to Riften. To the left of the ladder is a long wooden table with a bottle of wine, a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") on top, and to the right is a cupboard containing clutter. In front of the cupboard is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand, beside another cupboard holding two [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), four [tomatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), a bottle of alto wine, and several pieces of cheese. On the opposite side of the Cistern to the northeast is a passage with a long wooden table on the right laid with a bottle of alto wine, a [horker loaf](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Loaf "Skyrim:Horker Loaf"), two pieces of [cooked beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Beef "Skyrim:Cooked Beef"), two loaves of bread, a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"). Beside the table is a cupboard holding two [boiled creme treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), a bottle of alto wine, and some bread and cheese. To the left of the passage is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"). The passage takes two turns before entering a training room - see below for details.

During the Thieves Guild quest [The Pursuit](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pursuit "Skyrim:The Pursuit"), when you enter the Cistern from the Ragged Flagon, Vex, Delvin, and Brynjolf will be standing by the entrance with their weapons drawn. By the alternative entrance will be Vipir the Fleet and Sapphire, by the passage leading to the training room will be Niriun and Cynric Endell with Rune a short distance behind them, and Thrynn will be in the training room. After both you and [Karliah](/wiki/Skyrim:Karliah "Skyrim:Karliah") explain yourselves, Brynjolf asks Delvin to unlock the treasure room. Once both Delvin and Brynjolf have unlocked the door, you are all able to enter, and find Mercer has emptied all seven treasure chests within. There are a couple small wooden tables in the room with a selection of steel weapons on top, and a food barrel in the corner. The locked vault door remains unlocked, unless you stay away from the Cistern long enough for it to reset, which will reset the doors to locked.

#### Upgrades

As you complete the Thieves Guild reputation quests for Delvin Mallory, the beds are upgraded, while several screens and upgrades around the Cistern are added:

- Completing the first reputation quest adds two new Thieves Guild banners high on the walls on either side of the treasury room. The table to the southwest is laid with two bottles of alto wine, a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [pheasant roast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Roast "Skyrim:Pheasant Roast"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), two potatoes, a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish") steak, and several more loaves of bread and pieces of cheese. Hanging above the table from racks are four [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), four [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), and two whole [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"). A shield plaque is installed above the weapon racks to the right of the treasury room, with a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield") and crossed [iron greatswords](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Greatsword "Skyrim:Iron Greatsword").
- Completing the second reputation quest adds two extra cupboards on either side of the ladder leading to the concealed entrance from the graveyard. Rugs are added throughout the zone, and two more banners are hung from the walls above the passage to the training room.
- Completing the third reputation quest makes a new thief, Garthar, join the Guild. Two more banners are hung from the walls above the concealed entrance.
- Completing the fourth and final reputation quest makes a new thief, Ravyn Imyan, join the Guild. Two final banners are added high above the entrance to the Ragged Flagon.
- After you complete the quest [Darkness Returns](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkness_Returns "Skyrim:Darkness Returns"), a shrine to Nocturnal is installed in the water between the entrance to the Ragged Flagon and the training room. When activated, you will be granted the [Blessing of Nocturnal](/wiki/Skyrim:Blessings "Skyrim:Blessings"), a 10-point boost to your [Sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak") skill, and will be cured of any [diseases](/wiki/Skyrim:Diseases "Skyrim:Diseases") you may have contracted.
- After completing the Thieves Guild questline, a long guild tribute chest appears before the desk. You can unlock this chest with a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Tribute_Chest_Key "Skyrim:Tribute Chest Key") given to you by Brynjolf and claim the items left as tribute for you.
- Upon completing 125 side quests for Vex and/or Delvin, a [safe](/wiki/Skyrim:Safe "Skyrim:Safe") is also added.

### Training Room

The training room contains several archery targets (both at ground level and in alcoves high on the walls), practice dummies, many more [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels, and five different locked chests, each with a different leveled lock from novice through master. There is a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Training_Chests "Skyrim:Training Chests") on the ground in front of the chests, which explains they are for practice use and they reset after a short amount of time. There is also an unlocked guild chest, two cupboards, a table, and an end table against the walls. On these are an [iron greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Greatsword "Skyrim:Iron Greatsword"), a [steel greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Greatsword "Skyrim:Steel Greatsword"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a complete set of thieves guild armor including the [hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Hood "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Hood"), gloves, boots, and [cuirass](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Armor "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Armor"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and a copy of the [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Advances in Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Advances_in_Lockpicking "Skyrim:Advances in Lockpicking")*.

## Bugs

- If any of the guild members see you picking the locks in the training room, they will interrupt you by commenting on it as if you weren't meant to be picking them.
- One of the practice chests in the Cistern doesn't respawn like the others.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. It was replaced with a respawning version of the same chest.
- There is no navmesh up to the base of the Nocturnal shrine that gets built in the Cistern.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
- The Cistern has no proper room bounds which can cause issues with the lighting in the cell.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
- The lighting may occasionally flicker when you're here.
  - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch"), version 1.6.342, fixes this bug.
