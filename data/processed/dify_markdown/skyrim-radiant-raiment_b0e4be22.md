Radiant Raiment

**Radiant Raiment** is a [clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing") store run by [Taarie](/wiki/Skyrim:Taarie "Skyrim:Taarie") and her sister [Endarie](/wiki/Skyrim:Endarie "Skyrim:Endarie"), situated on the right as you enter [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") through the main gate. It sells various clothing of all types and expenses.

It is situated next door to [Bits and Pieces](/wiki/Skyrim:Bits_and_Pieces "Skyrim:Bits and Pieces"), opposite [The Winking Skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Winking_Skeever "Skyrim:The Winking Skeever").

## Radiant Raiment

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/SR-interior-Radiant_Raiment.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Radiant_Raiment.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Radiant_Raiment.jpg)

The main shop area

The entrance is adept-locked out of hours, both [Endarie](/wiki/Skyrim:Endarie "Skyrim:Endarie") and [Taarie](/wiki/Skyrim:Taarie "Skyrim:Taarie") carry copies of the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Radiant_Raiments "Skyrim:Key to Radiant Raiments"). When entering there is a wooden bench against the east wall and opposite against the west wall is a small wardrobe containing [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") and having a [snow fox pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Fox Pelt") on top. The ceiling is vaulted and has a central pillar supporting it. As the room opens up there is a narrow table with a basket containing two bunches of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") on it, against the east wall, with another wooden bench to the right of the table. The counter is in front of the south wall and extends two thirds of the way across the shop. Against the west wall, around the corner from the entrance are double doors leading to the residence, with a small round table to the left of it, this has a plate of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and cheese on top and another wooden bench to the left of that with a [hide helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Helmet "Skyrim:Hide Helmet") on a ledge above it. In front of the counter is an open crate containing two bunches of [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton"). On the counter, the cupboard against the south wall behind it and two small sets of shelves in the southwest corner are several pieces of [clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), [fine clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"), [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") and a [circlet](/wiki/Skyrim:Circlet "Skyrim:Circlet") that are [displayed merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise) and a [silver and sapphire circlet](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_and_Sapphire_Circlet "Skyrim:Silver and Sapphire Circlet") that isn't. On a ledge above the counter is an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet"). Behind the counter to the east is an alcove with two sets of shelves and two pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") in a basket, the smaller set of shelves holds a [sabre cat pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Pelt"), a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide") and a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), while the large set has a master-locked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"), various pieces of displayed merchandise, two [jade and sapphire circlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Jade_and_Sapphire_Circlet "Skyrim:Jade and Sapphire Circlet") and a goat hide. Under the counter is an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and woodcutter's axe.

Through the double doors is a large dining and cooking area, with a wooden bench along the south wall with a small table at the end of it. Opposite the doors is a low table and chair with a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), a common book and a [plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") filled with loaves of bread, an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and a [tomato](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato") on the table. Against the north wall is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand with a small cupboard holding a [platter](/wiki/Skyrim:Platter "Skyrim:Platter") filled with a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), two [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), a [pheasant breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast") and a couple of pieces of cheese to the left and to the right is a long wooden table and bench laid for two, with two portions of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), two [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a loaf of bread, a couple of wheels of cheese, a [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration") and a bunch of tundra cotton. There are wall shelves above the table holding crockery. Against the west wall is a narrow table with a bowl of bread and a plate holding a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), with an iron dagger. Above this is a wall shelf holding a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). To the south is a stairwell with a flight leading up to the top floor and one down to the cellar.

At the top of the stairs is a T-shaped landing with three doors, one to the west, one to the north and one to the east. On the landing, either side of the door to the north is on one side, a narrow table with a basket containing a bunch of lavender and on the other, a small cupboard with a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") and [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") on top. The north wall to the left of the east door has two bookcases against it, these hold fourteen common books, a [solution of strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Strength "Skyrim:Solution of Strength") and a [potion of extreme stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extreme_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Extreme Stamina"), while opposite against the south wall is a wooden bench with a small table holding another common book beside it.

The door to the west opens into a bedroom that contains a single bed in the northwest corner with a small table beside it, a wardrobe against the east wall containing fine clothes and small wardrobe against the south wall with shelves above it. There is a bottle of alto wine, a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") of bread, a plate holding a sliced eidar cheese and an iron dagger. The door to the north opens into another bedroom, this one has a partition wall coming out from the north wall. The bed is on the west side of this wall with a small table beside the bed and an open crate at the foot of it containing a bunch of lavender. There is also a tall wardrobe against the west wall with a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") beside it. The other half of the room has a tall wardrobe against the partition wall, and unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") against the north wall, a cupboard against the east wall with a jug and goblet on top, with a large round wooden table to the right of it, on this table is the [business ledger](/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger "Skyrim:Business Ledger"), a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch") and a plate of [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"). There is also a small table against the south wall with a bowl containing a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") on it.

The door to the east opens into the largest bedroom with a large double bed coming out from the northeast corner with a small table on the right holding a bottle of alto wine. There are several wall shelves above the bed holding fine clothes and a small coin purse. In the northwest corner is a tall wardrobe, with a small set of shelves to the left, against the west wall, these hold two bottles of [frostbite venom](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Venom "Skyrim:Frostbite Venom"), two samples of [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales"), a bowl of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flower and a [potion of haggling](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling "Skyrim:Potion of Haggling"). There is a washbasin to the left of the shelves. In the southeast corner is a small wardrobe with a large coin purse and a copy of *[Fall of the Snow Prince](/wiki/Skyrim:Fall_of_the_Snow_Prince "Skyrim:Fall of the Snow Prince")* on top and a basket beside it containing two bunches of lavender. In front of this is a low table and chair, with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a plate of bread and cheese and a goblet on the table. Against the south wall is a narrow table with a bowl of bread and a plate holding a sliced eidar cheese on top.

Descending the stair to the cellar, it is heavily vaulted with a cupboard against the west wall holding loaves of bread, [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and several pieces of cheese, to the right of this is a rack with a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid, two bunches of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging from it. Against the north wall is a square wooden table with a couple of empty wine bottles and a common book on it, while in an alcove to the east is a long wooden table with a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"), a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a bunch of elves ear and a deathbell flower on top, a stack of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels beside it and an unlocked chest underneath.

## Notes

- This store has the largest inventory of enchanted jewelry in [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), making it a useful stop if you are searching for a particular enchantment to add to your repertoire.
- There is a [![Loot](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png/32px-SR-icon-shadowmark-Loot.png)](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild) "Loot") Loot [shadowmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction)#Shadowmarks "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") to the bottom left of the entrance door.
- In [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online"), a store bearing the same name was housed where the [blacksmith shop](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Blacksmith "Skyrim:Solitude Blacksmith") currently is. The current building housed a [home furnishings store](/wiki/Online:Furnishers "Online:Furnishers") called [Dragon's Hearth](/wiki/Online:Dragon%27s_Hearth "Online:Dragon's Hearth").
- [Gisli](/wiki/Skyrim:Gisli "Skyrim:Gisli") can sometimes be found behind the counter of the shop, though she doesn't sell anything.
- Either of the two sisters might run up to you in the store after entering, acting like you are in the private quarters, even though you aren't. All dialogue options are still available even though they might follow you around.

## Bugs

- Sometimes [Endarie](/wiki/Skyrim:Endarie "Skyrim:Endarie") will accuse you of trespassing in the Radiant Raiment between 8am and 8pm even though the door to the shop is open. This seems related to disposition towards [ServicesSolitudeRadiantRaiments](/wiki/Skyrim:ServicesSolitudeRadiantRaiments "Skyrim:ServicesSolitudeRadiantRaiments") or [SolitudeRadiantRaimentsFaction](/wiki/Skyrim:SolitudeRadiantRaimentsFaction "Skyrim:SolitudeRadiantRaimentsFaction").
  - Doing a [favor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fit_for_a_Jarl "Skyrim:Fit for a Jarl") for [Taarie](/wiki/Skyrim:Taarie "Skyrim:Taarie") or wearing [Radiant Raiment fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant_Raiment_Fine_Clothes "Skyrim:Radiant Raiment Fine Clothes") (or other fine clothes) and exiting and re-entering may help.
- You can't interact with either of the goat hides in the shop.
- Endarie's merchant chest appears to sometimes hit an item cap, causing some items not to be sold, such as [Amulets of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Specialty_Gear#Amulets_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Specialty Gear"). This can become particularly noticeable when additions such as [Lord's Mail](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail "Skyrim:Lord's Mail")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") or [Nordic Jewelry](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Jewelry "Skyrim:Nordic Jewelry")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") add items to the beginning of her inventory (internally), thus forcing additional items off the end of it.
