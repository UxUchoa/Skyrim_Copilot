Doorman

The **Doorman** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") found inside [Redwater Den](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Den "Skyrim:Redwater Den"), a drug den for [Redwater skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma "Skyrim:Redwater Skooma"). He wears [studded armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor "Skyrim:Studded Armor"), a pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"), a [scaled helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Helmet "Skyrim:Scaled Helmet") and a pair of leveled [heavy gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Gauntlets_2 "Skyrim:Armor"). He also carries [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a leveled [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace").

## Dialogue

When first approached:

:   *"Hold it. If you're going downstairs, you keep your weapons to yourself or we'll be getting better acquainted. Head on in and behave yourself."*

If you talk to him after that, he says:

:   *"Go on downstairs. You're bothering me."*

## Notes

- If still alive, he will be renamed to "[Vampire's Thrall](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Thrall "Skyrim:Vampire's Thrall")" when you leave.
