## The Sea Squall

The boat is docked at the end of a jetty opposite the entrance to [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine"). There are several crates and barrels on deck, along with three chairs that the captain and both sailors seem to spend most of their time sitting on.

## Notes

- Dawnstar guards can be seen traversing between the ship and the mine entrance.
