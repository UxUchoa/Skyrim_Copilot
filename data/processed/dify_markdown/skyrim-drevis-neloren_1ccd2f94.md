Drevis Neloren in the Arcanaeum

**Drevis Neloren** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [sorcerer](/wiki/Skyrim:TrainerIllusionMaster "Skyrim:TrainerIllusionMaster") and an instructor at the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)"). He is the master [trainer](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers") for [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion"), able to train you to level 90. After you officially become part of the College, he will ask you to don some [magical gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystic_Tuning_Gloves "Skyrim:Mystic Tuning Gloves") for a [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Out_of_Balance "Skyrim:Out of Balance") where you have to cleanse various magicka focus points around the college. He will also give a [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion_Ritual_Spell "Skyrim:Illusion Ritual Spell") for the master Illusion spells, and will sell many [Illusion spells](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion_Spells "Skyrim:Illusion Spells").

Drevis wears [master robes of illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Master_Robes_of_Illusion "Skyrim:Master Robes of Illusion") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0006b46c "Skyrim:Clothing"), and carries an unenchanted [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger").

He can be found during most days in his room on the lower floor of the [Hall of Countenance](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Countenance "Skyrim:Hall of Countenance"), waking at 7am to eat, drink, and perform alchemy for most of the day with the exception of between 10am to 3pm, during which time he will be found in the [Arcanaeum](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum "Skyrim:The Arcanaeum"). He will then go to sleep at 11pm.

## Related Quests

- **[Illusion Ritual Spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion_Ritual_Spell "Skyrim:Illusion Ritual Spell")**: Find some books about [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") magic to further your Illusion skills.
- **[Out of Balance](/wiki/Skyrim:Out_of_Balance "Skyrim:Out of Balance")**: Purify the focus points for magical energy around the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)").

## Dialogue

Upon first meeting Drevis, he will be surprised that you are talking to him, and say:

:   *"You... You can see me?"*

:   :   **I... No, I can't see you. Not at all.**
    :   *"But you can hear me, obviously. Something still isn't quite right. At least I'm making progress."*

:   :   **Of course I can.**
    :   *"Confound it all. I was quite sure I was invisible, or at least transparent."*

Either leads to:

:   **Which are the best Illusion spells?**
:   *"Oh my, that's quite a difficult question. Never underestimate the ability to influence others. Whether they're friends or foes, they're almost all susceptible to Illusion spells. They can turn the tide of a battle in a moment's notice. There are also spells that help in avoiding conflict altogether. Invisibility is tricky to master, but exceptionally useful."*

Upon finishing a conversation with Drevis:

:   *"If only I could be free of the mundane, day-to-day activities life so often requires."*
:   *"Illusion really is the least appreciated of the schools of magic."*
:   *"You know, they say knowledge is its own reward."*

### Conversations

While inside the Arcanaeum, he will have conversations with other College members, such as [Urag gro-Shub](/wiki/Skyrim:Urag_gro-Shub "Skyrim:Urag gro-Shub"):

**Urag:** *"Are you looking for any specific volumes, Drevis?"*
**Drevis:** *"Hmm? Oh, no. No, not really. I... Actually, I've forgotten what I was looking for."*
**Urag:** *"Ah. Well, then. Let me know if you need anything."*
**Drevis:** *"What's that? Oh yes, yes. Thank you, I will."*

He will also react negatively to [Colette Marence](/wiki/Skyrim:Colette_Marence "Skyrim:Colette Marence") if she approaches him:

**Colette:** *"How are you, Drevis?"*
**Drevis:** *"Ugh. What do you want?"*
**Colette:** *"Why should I want anything? Why can't I simply be sociable with my colleagues?"*
**Drevis:** *"I'm busy right now, Colette."*
**Colette:** *"Well, then. Fine. Have a nice day."*

## Lecture: The Doomstones

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-npc-Drevis_Neloren_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Drevis_Neloren_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Drevis_Neloren_03.jpg)

Drevis holding a lecture

Drevis will hold lectures in the [Hall of the Elements](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Elements "Skyrim:Hall of the Elements") occasionally, in which he will be heard saying the following:

*"The existence of so-called 'Doomstones' throughout Skyrim has been repeatedly verified. The meaning of these stones has not. The prevailing opinion of Skyrim natives is that the stones are indeed magical in nature. While there is no direct evidence of this, it does seem likely. References to similar stones appear in lore throughout the various Tamrielic cultures. None, however, exactly match the markings or distribution of Skyrim's stones. At present, there is no confirmation of any of the various theories surrounding the nature of these stones. Their relative positions do not indicate that any individual stone is part of a larger, unobserved pattern. Also, their placement throughout Skyrim does not correspond to any known magical phenomenon. The age of the stones themselves has yet to be officially determined. It has been widely assumed that they were placed during the Merethic Era. Writings from that period, including those of Ysgramor himself, do not mention the stones and thus this idea cannot be verified. Nonetheless, many are drawn to these stones based on the local stories describing them as a source of significant power. The College will continue to research these intriguing objects, and of course any findings will be relayed with all possible haste."*

### Main Quest

During the quest [Containment](/wiki/Skyrim:Containment "Skyrim:Containment"), when Drevis learns that there are [magic anomalies](/wiki/Skyrim:Magic_Anomaly "Skyrim:Magic Anomaly") attacking Winterhold, he will say to himself: *"I hope this isn't something I did."* After the quest, he will occasionally say in passing: *"Have you seen Mirabelle around? I know she's dead, but I thought... Well, I thought she might stop in and say hello anyway."*

After you defeat Ancano and the Eye of Magnus is removed from the College, Drevis can be heard saying: *"The Eye of Magnus. Here, but lost. Slipped through our grasp. Oh, what we might have learned."* as well as: *"I wonder what we could've done with the Eye, had it been put to better use?"*

Once you have become the Arch-Mage of the College, Drevis will occasionally say: *"We have survived difficult times, and are better mages for it. We owe you that, Arch-Mage. Thank you."*

### Out of Balance

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-npc-Drevis_Neloren_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Drevis_Neloren_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Drevis_Neloren_02.jpg)

*"I'm busy right now, Colette."*

After becoming officially inducted into the College of Winterhold, you can ask Drevis if there is anything [he needs help with](/wiki/Skyrim:Out_of_Balance "Skyrim:Out of Balance"):

:   **Is there any College business I can assist with?**
:   *"There may be a matter in which your... skills... could be useful. The various points of focus for magic energies around the College have become polluted, and need cleansing."*
:   **Why do these "energies" need to be cleansed?**
:   *"As you may be aware, the College has gone to great lengths to purify and enhance the natural energies of the world. This both enhances our connection to the realm of magic, and eliminates possible interference from outside sources. These energies, on occasion, become... polluted. Effort must be made to see that they are kept pure, clean. Do you wish to aid in this cleansing?"*

:   :   **All right, I'll help.**
    :   *"Very well. These specially enchanted gloves will allow you to delve directly into the focal points for these energies. Go to each focal point and remove whatever impurities you may find there. Return the gloves to me when you have finished. Please exercise caution. This is not a procedure without possible... consequences."*

:   :   **Maybe some other time.**
    :   *"I see."*

If you agree to help, Drevis will hand you a pair of [mystic tuning gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystic_Tuning_Gloves "Skyrim:Mystic Tuning Gloves").

Returning to him without finishing the task and asking for clarification:

:   **What did you need me to do?**
:   *"Use the gloves to clear the focal points. Return to me when you have finished. I thought I made this rather clear."*

Successfully cleansing all of the focal points and returning to Drevis to report that the focal points have been cleansed:

:   **Your focal points have been, uh, cleansed.**
:   *"Yes, they have. I can feel the difference already. But of course, I'm far more in tune with these sorts of things than most. Thank you. I appreciate your assistance."*

### The Black Star

During the quest [The Black Star](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Star "Skyrim:The Black Star"), you can ask Drevis if he knows anything about the study of stars. He will say:

:   **Is there an elven mage here that studies stars?**
:   *"I know plenty of spells that produce light, but stars? Hmm. There was that problem a few years ago, but that couldn't be related."*

:   :   **Something tells me that it is.**
    :   *"You should go into town and talk to an elf named Nelacar. He was involved in the whole mess."*

:   :   **What is it? What happened?**
    :   *"There was some bother about a group of mages being exiled. I can't remember what the details were. I think one of them came back. Nelacar. Stays at the Frozen Hearth, but I don't know why. He's not welcome in the College."*

### Master Illusionist

Once you reach level 100 (see [bugs](#Bugs)) in [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion"), you can ask Drevis if there is anything [more to be learned](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion_Ritual_Spell "Skyrim:Illusion Ritual Spell") about Illusion magic:

:   **What else is there to be learned about Illusion Magic?**
:   *"You have gone beyond my teachings, which is quite a feat. I did not think it possible. But there is always more to learn. I believe you are ready for the next step."*
:   **What's the next step?**
:   *"Knowledge of truly powerful Illusion spells is here at the College, but it is hidden from those who might misuse it. I shall teach you a spell to discern things hidden from others. With it, you will be able to acquire the texts you need. Bring all four to me, and I shall use them to inscribe the spells for you."*

Upon acquiring all of the books and telling Drevis that you have them all:

:   **Here are the texts you requested.**
:   *"Yes, very good. Here, then, is the first of the Illusion spells you sought."*
