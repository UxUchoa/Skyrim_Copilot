# Skyrim:Barracks

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-place-Dawnstar_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dawnstar_Barracks.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Dawnstar_Barracks.jpg)

Dawnstar Barracks

This page lists all the **guard barracks** in [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim"). Each [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds"), with the exception of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)"), has its own barracks.

:   **[Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch")** — A hold in eastern Skyrim, with its capital in Windhelm.

    :   [![SR-mapicon-Palace.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-mapicon-Palace.png/13px-SR-mapicon-Palace.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Palace.png) **[Palace of the Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings")** — A large castle at the far end of the Valunstrad district in Windhelm. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Palace+of+the+Kings)
:   **[Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold")** — A hold in southern Skyrim, with its capital in Falkreath.

    :   **[Falkreath Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Barracks "Skyrim:Falkreath Barracks")** — The living quarters for the Falkreath guards and also contains Falkreath Hold's jail. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Falkreath+Barracks)
:   **[Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar")** — A relatively small hold in northwest Skyrim, containing the provincial capital Solitude.

    :   **[Castle Dour](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour "Skyrim:Castle Dour")** — A large fortress in Solitude which acts as the headquarters of the Imperial Legion and residence of the Emperor in Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Castle+Dour)
:   **[Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch")** — A hold in northern Skyrim, with its capital in Morthal.

    :   **[Guardhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Guardhouse "Skyrim:Guardhouse")** — The Hjaalmarch barracks on the south side of Morthal. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Guardhouse)
:   **[Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim")**[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") — A large island in the Sea of Ghosts northeast of Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Solstheim)

    :   **[The Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark "Skyrim:The Bulwark")**[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") — An imposing stone wall created to protect Raven Rock from ash storms, with the city's garrison and jail both built directly into the wall. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=The+Bulwark)
:   **[The Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale")** — A hold in northern Skyrim, with its capital in Dawnstar.

    :   **[Dawnstar Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar_Barracks "Skyrim:Dawnstar Barracks")** — The living quarters for the Dawnstar guards. It also houses Dawnstar's jail. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dawnstar+Barracks)
:   **[The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach")** — A hold in western Skyrim, with Markarth as its capital.

    :   **[Guard Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard_Tower "Skyrim:Guard Tower")** — A Dwemer tower in the center of Markarth. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Guard+Tower)
:   **[The Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift")** — A hold in southeast Skyrim, with its capital in Riften.

    :   [![SR-mapicon-Castle 2.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-mapicon-Castle_2.png/15px-SR-mapicon-Castle_2.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Castle_2.png) **[Mistveil Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Mistveil_Keep "Skyrim:Mistveil Keep")** — A castle located at the southern end of Riften. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mistveil+Keep)
:   **[Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold")** — A hold in central Skyrim, with its capital in Whiterun.

    :   **[Guard Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard_Barracks "Skyrim:Guard Barracks")** — A building in Whiterun where guards live when not on duty. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Guard+Barracks)
    :   **[Guard Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard_Barracks_(Gate) "Skyrim:Guard Barracks (Gate)")** — A building near the main gate in Whiterun. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Guard+Barracks)
