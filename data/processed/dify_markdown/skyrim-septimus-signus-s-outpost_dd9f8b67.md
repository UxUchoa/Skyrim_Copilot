Septimus Signus's Outpost

**Septimus Signus's Outpost** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") north of the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)") inhabited by [Septimus Signus](/wiki/Skyrim:Septimus_Signus "Skyrim:Septimus Signus"). It contains only one zone, *[Septimus Signus's Outpost](#Septimus_Signus.27s_Outpost)*.

Septimus moved a mysterious Dwemer lockbox to this remote location for study. He is an expert on the lore of the [Elder Scrolls](/wiki/Lore:Elder_Scrolls "Lore:Elder Scrolls"), but his knowledge and interaction with them has mostly destroyed his mind. He claims that "Inside is the heart. The heart of a god! The heart of you. And me." However, the lockbox actually contains the book *[Oghma Infinium](/wiki/Skyrim:Oghma_Infinium "Skyrim:Oghma Infinium")*.

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost_02.png/200px-SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost_02.png)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost_02.png)

Outpost at dusk

The cave is on a small remote icy island off the north coast of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"). It may be reached by walking a short distance north of the College of Winterhold then continuing north over drift ice and the island featuring [Skytemple Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins "Skyrim:Skytemple Ruins"). The outpost is accessed through a trapdoor on the south side of the small icy island, next to a moored rowboat.

### Septimus Signus's Outpost

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-quest-Discerning_the_Transmundane.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Discerning_the_Transmundane.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Discerning_the_Transmundane.jpg)

Dwemer lockbox

The trapdoor opens into a narrow descending ice tunnel that ends on a ledge above the main chamber; following the ledge leads to an earthen ramp that circles the chamber counterclockwise. At the bottom is the Dwemer lockbox. Opposite the lockbox is a small square wooden table holding two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet"), and a [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug"), with a second jug on the ground. A nearby cupboard holds three bowls of [fire salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts "Skyrim:Fire Salts"), a filled [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem"), a filled [common soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem"), copies of *[Antecedants of Dwemer Law](/wiki/Skyrim:Antecedants_of_Dwemer_Law "Skyrim:Antecedants of Dwemer Law")*, *[Boethiah's Proving](/wiki/Skyrim:Boethiah%27s_Proving "Skyrim:Boethiah's Proving")*, *[Chimarvamidium](/wiki/Skyrim:Chimarvamidium "Skyrim:Chimarvamidium")*, *[Dwemer History and Culture](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_History_and_Culture "Skyrim:Dwemer History and Culture")*, *[Dwemer Inquiries Vol I](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_I "Skyrim:Dwemer Inquiries Vol I")*, *[II](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_II "Skyrim:Dwemer Inquiries Vol II")*, and *[III](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_III "Skyrim:Dwemer Inquiries Vol III")*, *[Effects of the Elder Scrolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Effects_of_the_Elder_Scrolls "Skyrim:Effects of the Elder Scrolls")*, and *[Pension of the Ancestor Moth](/wiki/Skyrim:Pension_of_the_Ancestor_Moth "Skyrim:Pension of the Ancestor Moth")*, and two copies of *[Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruminations_on_the_Elder_Scrolls "Skyrim:Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls")*, which is written by Septimus himself.

## Notes

- *Boethiah's Proving* will not appear until you reach level 30.
- [The Chill](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Chill "Skyrim:The Chill"), [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold")'s jail, is on an island northeast of the outpost.
- In [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
