Cronvangr Cave

**Cronvangr Cave** is a medium-sized [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") southwest of [Kynesgrove](/wiki/Skyrim:Kynesgrove "Skyrim:Kynesgrove") containing [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") and [frostbite spiders](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider"). It contains two zones: *[Cronvangr Cave](#Cronvangr_Cave)* and *[Cronvangr Broodlair](#Cronvangr_Broodlair)*.

### Exterior

The cave entrance is in the northwestern area of the sulfur pools characteristic of central [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch"), almost due east of [Mixwater Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Mixwater_Mill "Skyrim:Mixwater Mill"). The exterior hints at what can be found inside: it is covered in webs, and one [egg sac](/wiki/Skyrim:Egg_Sac "Skyrim:Egg Sac") is in front of the entrance. A dead [mammoth](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") is to the left. There are also several plants that can be harvested for ingredients in the surrounding area.

### Cronvangr Cave

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave_03.jpg)

The first chamber in the cave

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave.jpg)

Secret vampire area

The first room contains six frostbite spiders, an egg sac, and an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"). A small button on the northwest wall will open a rock door to a cavern, which contains a wooden building and several vampires on a raised area at the back of the cavern. Beware a [flame spout trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Spout_Trap "Skyrim:Flame Spout Trap") in the passage on the way to the vampires. The vampires are generally all found sleeping in the building; however, some of them may be awake, so be careful if you don't see them. On the lower level, there's a circle of candles around a dead body and two long tables with benches, one of which holds a single loose [coin](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). Two ramps up to the raised area and building are against the cave wall.

The inside of the building is a mess, strewn with viscera and gore. There are three single beds (normally all occupied by three sleeping vampires), a central table with a random filled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") and a copy of the [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Locked Room](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Locked_Room "Skyrim:The Locked Room")* on top, a second table with an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe") and a [strong box](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") on top, several chairs, barrels, and sacks, and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") with a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison") next to it at the foot of one of the beds. There are several corpses nearby and in the cavern itself, which the vampires may raise to help them fight. Six poisons and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") are on two sets of shelves, among [ruined books](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book "Skyrim:Ruined Book") and other clutter. One more random potion can be found next to the barrels to the left of the shelves, on one of which is an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Apothecary.27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Containers"). A second chest, this one adept-locked, can be found behind the building near an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs") and [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanters "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanters"). A [sapphire](/wiki/Skyrim:Sapphire_(item) "Skyrim:Sapphire (item)") can be found on top of a barrel next to the second chest. There is also a long wooden table and bench behind the building with two loose coins on it.

The rest of the zone can be reached from another tunnel in the first room. It consists of a sparsely lit cobwebbed pathway winding down into a cavern with a pool of water at the bottom, with two egg sacs and a [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Parts#Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Animal Parts") along the path. There are several small frostbite spiders along the path, and it is possible for one or more of the spiders to fall off the ledges and into the water below. The spiders will often become trapped in the water, as the pathway out is fairly narrow and the spiders don't navigate it properly. This makes killing any spiders that fall much more difficult without the use of archery or long-range spells. An unlocked chest and a floating skeleton can be found in the water. A small pathway to the east leads to the second zone, *Cronvangr Broodlair*.

### Cronvangr Broodlair

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Cronvangr_Cave_02.jpg)

Broodlair with a giant frostbite spider

The zone begins on a twisting path with several webs you need to break through and several small frostbite spiders along it. A small chest can be found in front of you where the tunnel turns to the right; it is hidden by two layers of spider webbing behind an unlootable desiccated corpse, and can be easily overlooked. The path continues to the broodlair proper, where a giant frostbite spider will drop from the ceiling to join many other, smaller spiders. A boss-level chest can be found in the back of this room, with a large egg sac to the right and a [moonstone ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein") to the left.

There's a shortcut back to the first zone up the slope to the north, with an egg sac on your left shortly before you re-enter the first zone. The shortcut will place you on a ledge above the passageway just before the first room of *Cronvangr Cave*.

## Notes

- Two ore veins are outside near the cave entrance. A [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") is 60 feet west-southwest of the entrance, straight ahead when exiting the cave [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=117664&y=-5824&zoom=16&legacy=true). A [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") is 150 feet northeast of the entrance, on the ridge near the trees [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=121792&y=-2768&zoom=16&legacy=true).
- A pair of leveled [necromancers](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer") have taken to practicing their craft on a large stone altar due east of the cave entrance. On the altar is a leveled poison, a potion, and a leveled soul gem. Scattered about the pools of hot water surrounding the altar are a few lootable [draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr") corpses, a pair of [burial urns](/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn "Skyrim:Burial Urn"), and the partial skeleton of a novice [conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjurer "Skyrim:Conjurer"). A chest can also be found in the hot water east of the cave entrance, in the same general area as the altar.
- Internally, the location is referred to as **Cronvangr Hall**, presumably a name remaining from earlier in the game's development. This name also appears in the location data, causing it to display on the local map. The cave was once intended to house a unique faction of vampires, the Cronvangr Clan, under the leadership of Vlkoslak. You would have awoken here at some point at the start of a set of [unused vampire-related quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Unfinished_Quests "Skyrim:Unfinished Quests").
