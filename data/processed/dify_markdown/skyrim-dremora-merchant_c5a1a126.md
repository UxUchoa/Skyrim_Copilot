The Dremora Merchant

The **Dremora Merchant** is a [Dremora](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora "Skyrim:Dremora") that can be summoned by the [Black Market](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Market "Skyrim:Black Market") power, one of the reward options from the quest [Black Book: Untold Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book:_Untold_Legends_(quest) "Skyrim:Black Book: Untold Legends (quest)"). When summoned, he approaches you and goes into the sell screen. He wears [black robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Robes_(xx0376da) "Skyrim:Black Robes (xx0376da)") and a pair of [Boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots_(xx0376d9) "Skyrim:Boots (xx0376d9)").

The Dremora merchant has a more pleasant personality than many of his kind—he speaks with a refined voice more akin to that of a [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") than the low-pitched growl common to his race, and seems to delight in providing his services to you, unlike the [Dremora Butler](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Butler "Skyrim:Dremora Butler").

## Dialogue

He will greet you by saying one of the following:

:   *"Ah, my favorite customer."*

:   *"You have many things I wish to buy."*

:   *"So soon? You must be hungry for a deal."*

:   *"Ah, a fresh mortal who seeks to bargain with me."*

:   *"So, we meet again."*

When you offer to sell your items to him, he will reply with one of the following:

:   *"I'm sure we can strike a bargain."*

:   *"Haggling with you can be so...entertaining."*

:   *"Let's make a deal."*

:   *"I can hardly wait."*

Once you have finished bartering with him, he will part ways by making one of the following remarks:

:   **Dremora Merchant:** *"Until you summon me again."*
:   **Dremora Merchant:** *"I do so love dealing with mortals."*
:   **Dremora Merchant:** *"Ah, sweet [Oblivion](/wiki/Lore:Oblivion "Lore:Oblivion"). Soon I shall return."*

## Notes

- His inventory is leveled, and includes weapons, armor and arrows. At higher levels, he is a reliable source of [Daedric items](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric "Skyrim:Daedric").
  - The Dremora Merchant is also a possible source for [Dragonplate armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Items#Dragonplate_Armor "Skyrim:Dragon Items").
  - The Dremora Merchant sometimes can sell Daedric and Dragon items even before they start appearing in the world, making him one of only a few locations to do that. Another such example is [Glover Mallory's Thief Cache](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory "Skyrim:Glover Mallory"). [*verification needed — At which level do they become available?*]
- While he doesn't have a large selection, his gold count is always 2000, making him a good choice if you need to sell some gear immediately.
- When selling items to the Dremora Merchant, you'll receive 9% less gold than from other merchants. If you don't have rank 2 or higher of the [Haggling perk](/wiki/Skyrim:Haggling "Skyrim:Haggling"), the Dremora Merchant will also charge 25% more for purchased items.
  - He won't accept stolen items unless you have the [Fence](/wiki/Skyrim:Fence "Skyrim:Fence") perk.
  - He is not affected by any other [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") perks.
- The quest [Buy Dwarven artifact](/wiki/Skyrim:Buy_Dwarven_artifact "Skyrim:Buy Dwarven artifact") can be triggered when buying Dwarven equipment from him.
