Attendant

The **Attendant** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [bandit wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:EncClassBanditWizard "Skyrim:EncClassBanditWizard") found in [Redwater Den](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Den "Skyrim:Redwater Den"), where he works the [Redwater Skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma "Skyrim:Redwater Skooma") booths. He carries Redwater Skooma and a [Redwater Den Backrooms Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Den_Backrooms_Key "Skyrim:Redwater Den Backrooms Key").

If you go through the quest [The Bloodstone Chalice](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bloodstone_Chalice "Skyrim:The Bloodstone Chalice"), he will appear as a [Vampire's Thrall](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Thrall "Skyrim:Vampire's Thrall") on your way out of the area, though he will go about his duties as normal.
