Bloodlet Throne

**Bloodlet Throne** is a small [fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Military_Forts "Skyrim:Military Forts") southwest of [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen") containing [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire"), [wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf"), [gargoyles](/wiki/Skyrim:Gargoyle "Skyrim:Gargoyle"), and [death hounds](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Hound "Skyrim:Death Hound"). It contains only one zone, *[Bloodlet Throne](#Bloodlet_Throne)*.

### Exterior

The fort is difficult to find, as the path leading to it has crumbled and is largely hidden. Follow the road east out of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"); you will see a marker for a short path leading south to [Peak's Shade Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Peak%27s_Shade_Tower "Skyrim:Peak's Shade Tower"). Continue east on the main road, and shortly after you pass underneath the [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit")-ridden overhanging bridge, you will see another marker next to a branching path to your right. Follow the path south to a stream flowing from a waterfall. Be mindful as you pass the stream; a small bandit campsite is to the east, and the bandits have a habit of patrolling the path. Turn right past the stream and continue as the path winds up the mountain, with markers along the way. Near [Ancestor Glade](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestor_Glade "Skyrim:Ancestor Glade"), the path appears to end; backtrack a few steps until you can continue south. The path will become clear once more and ultimately lead you east to Bloodlet Throne.

The approach is generally from the northwest along the path described above. The fort comprises a single-story square structure with a ruined tower on top. You can access the top of the fort from the outside, as the snow has piled up at the rear to enable you to walk up. Inside the ruins of the tower is an unlocked chest and a small square wooden table holding a quiver of [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow") and a random enchanted [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow"). Many [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes grow around the fort. In front is the entrance, with blood splattered on the ground outside. To the right are three unusable barrels, and next to them is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") and an empty cart.

### Bloodlet Throne

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_02.jpg)

A warm welcome

Upon entering, you are in an empty room with a doorway in front of you. In front of the doorway is an easily missed [pressure plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Pressure_Plate "Skyrim:Pressure Plate") that activates [flame spouts](/wiki/Skyrim:Flamethrower_Statues "Skyrim:Flamethrower Statues") on either side of the doorway. Through the doorway is a corridor with crates and barrels strewn everywhere. You can hear someone speaking ahead. The corridor turns to the north, and there are bars on your left. Through the bars, you can see a vampire and [vampire's thrall](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Thrall "Skyrim:Vampire's Thrall") talking. Keep quiet to avoid detection, or use a bow to snipe them from here. Beside the bars is a dresser, but nothing interesting. Just past the barred section, the corridor turns to the west, and stairs descend into the room with the two vampires. From the top of the stairs, you can see two [gargoyle statues](/wiki/Skyrim:Gargoyle_Statue "Skyrim:Gargoyle Statue"), one on either side of a large archway, as well as two lit braziers. There are three [oil lanterns](/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Lanterns "Skyrim:Oil Lanterns") hanging from the ceiling above [oil slicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Slick "Skyrim:Oil Slick") on the floor that can be used to kill the guards. There is a wooden door to the south, but it is barred from the other side.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_03.jpg)

Guard goyles

As you approach the gargoyles, the one on the left will burst to life. Past the arch is an area with shackles on each of the three walls and human bones littering the floor. There is also a wooden bench against the west wall. Under the bars is a corridor heading east to an open wooden door and stairs heading down; you can hear someone speaking ahead. At the bottom of the stairs, the corridor turns to the north and enters a large room containing a vampire on the right. On your left there is a wooden block with a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips") and [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") on top. Against the west wall is a dresser, on top of which is a [candlestick](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlestick "Skyrim:Candlestick") and a copy of *[The Wolf Queen, v5](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wolf_Queen,_v5 "Skyrim:The Wolf Queen, v5")*. On a raised area to the right is an end table and a bookcase containing a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell"), a [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") sample, a [potion of plentiful healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Healing"), a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"), a [potion of plentiful magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Magicka"), a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), two bowls of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"), a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), a [tomato](/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato "Skyrim:Tomato"), a [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") fungus, and two sacks.

On your right as you enter is a bench against the east wall, and to the left on a matching raised area is another end table with a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") on top, and also a second bookcase containing copies of *[A Dance in Fire, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v3 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v3")*, *[The Song of Pelinal, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v2 "Skyrim:The Song of Pelinal, v2")*, *[The Bear of Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bear_of_Markarth "Skyrim:The Bear of Markarth")*, *[Yngol and the Sea-Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol_and_the_Sea-Ghosts "Skyrim:Yngol and the Sea-Ghosts")*, *[Spirit of Nirn](/wiki/Skyrim:Spirit_of_Nirn "Skyrim:Spirit of Nirn")*, *[Antecedants of Dwemer Law](/wiki/Skyrim:Antecedants_of_Dwemer_Law "Skyrim:Antecedants of Dwemer Law")*, an [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool") fungus, and two sacks. In the center of the room is a small round wooden table and two chairs; on the table are two wooden plates containing bloody human hands. There is a snowy tunnel heading north from this room and then bearing west.

The tunnel is lit by two braziers on stands. At the far end is a cave with a wooden ramp in front of you leading up, and to the right of this is an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"). At the top of the ramp are two openings leading into a huge cavern with a wooden structure and ledges around the room. There are several vampires and vampire's thralls in this cavern, so caution and [sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak") ranged kills are advised.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne_04.jpg)

Beware!

On a ledge to the left above you is a vampire's thrall. There is a ramp down to the cavern floor on your left, followed by a smaller ramp leading slightly back up to the main floor level. There is a stack of barrels on your right, three of which can be looted for ingredients. Among the barrels is an open crate containing some skeletal hands and feet, as well as a ring. Nearby, a lit brazier illuminates a stack of crates and a sack. To the left of the barrels is a shallow pool of water with a [skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") floating in it that can be looted.

A vampire is wandering the cavern floor, with a second sitting at a long wooden table with two chairs to your right. The table is filled with the gory remains of a meal, and there are blood splatters all around the table - messy eaters! To the right of the table is a set of shelves containing copies of *[The Wolf Queen, v4](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wolf_Queen,_v4 "Skyrim:The Wolf Queen, v4"), The Bear of Markarth, and [Uncommon Taste](/wiki/Skyrim:Uncommon_Taste "Skyrim:Uncommon Taste")*. It also contains an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), a cast iron pot with a [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") inside, a [draught of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Health "Skyrim:Draught of Health"), a [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), a [draught of extra magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Extra_Magicka "Skyrim:Draught of Extra Magicka"), two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a sample of snowberries, and a sample of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). There are more blood splatters on the ground.

There is another wooden ramp leading up in the western corner of the cavern. At the top of the ramp is a small cage. The cage door is novice-locked, and contains the body of a [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit "Skyrim:Khajiit") (unless they were raised during the fight), a deathbell, and blood splatters over the floor of the cage. To the right is a lit brazier with a crate to the right of that. On the crate is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), with an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter") beside it. On the right is another ramp up and a cart full of human bones. Torches light the way as you climb the ramp. At the top is a ledge with crates and clutter, and also a path to the north with a gargoyle statue to the right. To the right of the gargoyle that will burst to life when you approach are a couple barrels, one of which has a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide") on top. You are at the same height as the first ledge seen with the vampire's thrall; the ledge is connected to another via a wooden bridge. The second ledge is above the cage to the west. Any noisy fighting will alert more vampires from here.

The path enters an icy tunnel and heads to the west-northwest, and is lit by a succession of braziers on stands. It opens into a collection of small areas. The first is on your right, and contains an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") in the middle with two sets of shelves around it. The first set of shelves contains copies of *[The Book of Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra "Skyrim:The Book of Daedra"), Antecedants of Dwemer Law, [Ancestors and the Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestors_and_the_Dunmer "Skyrim:Ancestors and the Dunmer")*, a [potion of vigorous healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigorous_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Vigorous Healing"), a random potion, a mead barrel, and a sack. The second set of shelves contains a random potion and copies of *[The Oblivion Crisis](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Oblivion_Crisis "Skyrim:The Oblivion Crisis"), [Dunmer of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Dunmer of Skyrim"), [Song of the Askelde Men](/wiki/Skyrim:Song_of_the_Askelde_Men "Skyrim:Song of the Askelde Men"), [The Real Barenziah, v4](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Real_Barenziah,_v4 "Skyrim:The Real Barenziah, v4"), [Argonian Account, Book 3](/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian_Account,_Book_3 "Skyrim:Argonian Account, Book 3")*, and another mead barrel.

There is a long end table farther along with the base of a shrine on top. Passing this, in front of you is an icy tunnel to the southwest with an opening in front to the west, where you can see round cages hanging from the ceiling, a square cage containing a wolf, and to the north, a gated opening with an adept lock.

Through the adept-locked gate is a small room with crates, sacks, barrels, and a small square wooden table holding a random enchanted [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"), a filled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), a medium coin purse, a random health potion, two random [poisons](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison"), a [scroll of Blizzard](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Blizzard "Skyrim:Scroll of Blizzard"), a [scroll of fury](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fury "Skyrim:Scroll of Fury"), and a copy of the [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Incident at Necrom](/wiki/Skyrim:Incident_at_Necrom "Skyrim:Incident at Necrom")*. One of the barrels contains ingredients, and both have sacks on top; there is an unlocked chest to the left of the barrels. There is a second adept-locked gate to the west.

This opens into a bedroom area with two vampires sleeping, one in a double bed against the west wall, and the other in one of two single beds against the north wall. There is a set of shelves on your right as you enter containing copies of *Antecedants of Dwemer Law*, *Spirit of Nirn*, *[Biography of Barenziah, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:Biography_of_Barenziah,_v2 "Skyrim:Biography of Barenziah, v2")*, *[The Wolf Queen, v5](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wolf_Queen,_v5 "Skyrim:The Wolf Queen, v5")*, and *[Gods and Worship](/wiki/Skyrim:Gods_and_Worship "Skyrim:Gods and Worship")*, as well as a filled random soul gem, a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), and a leather strip. To the left are the single beds with a long end table between them. On the end table is a potion of minor stamina, a potion of plentiful healing, a [scroll of calm](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Calm "Skyrim:Scroll of Calm"), a [scroll of fear](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fear "Skyrim:Scroll of Fear"), a medium coin purse, and an iron dagger that might have fallen on the floor. At the end of the double bed is an unlocked chest, and behind the head of the bed is a set of shelves containing copies of *[The Cake and The Diamond](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cake_and_The_Diamond "Skyrim:The Cake and The Diamond")* and *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor "Skyrim:Vernaccus and Bourlor")*, as well as a sample of snowberries and one of lavender.

The south end of the room contains the cages seen previously. There are three small cages hanging from the ceiling, one of which has the gory remains of a human, and the square cage, which contains a wolf. This cage is apprentice-locked.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bloodlet_Throne.jpg)

Let the games begin

The icy tunnel that heads to the southwest is lit by a string of braziers on stands and leads to an area with a waterfall down the wall to the south, two rock pillars supporting the ceiling, and the path turning to the northeast. At this turning is a ledge that was one of the two ledges joined by a wooden bridge above the first huge cavern. Crossing the bridge, there is a basket containing three pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") and a bucket containing an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"). The path turns to the south and descends to raised spears blocking access to an arena-styled area; the chain to lower the spears is on your right.

The arena area has two further blocked areas with death hounds trapped behind them. There are also some dead bandits that have obviously been made to fight and lost. Opposite and above where you enter is a throne with the [master vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Master_Vampire "Skyrim:Master Vampire") sitting where they can oversee the fights below. There is a second vampire sitting to the master vampire's right on a bench. To your right is another set of raised spears blocking access to an earthen ramp leading up to the master vampire; the chain to lower the spears is to the right. If you are seen before lowering these spears, the master vampire will lower the two sets of spears holding in the death hounds, releasing them to attack you. The lever for those spears is beside the master vampire's chair. Around the chair and benches are many more human bones. There is a third plain wooden bench on the east side of the ledge overlooking the arena area.

An icy tunnel heads away from this area to the southeast. Where it turns to the northeast, there is a [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein"). Where the ice ends is the master-level chest, and to the right is a corridor that leads to the barred door found in the first room for a shortcut out.

## Notes

- A [quicksilver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore Vein") is outside south of the entrance, with a second quicksilver ore vein farther to the southeast.
- A [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodlet_Cage_Key "Skyrim:Bloodlet Cage Key") to the cage doors here exists, but cannot be obtained.
