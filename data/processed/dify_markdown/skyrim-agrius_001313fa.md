Agrius

**Agrius** is a dead [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") found in the first part of [Halldir's Cairn](/wiki/Skyrim:Halldir%27s_Cairn "Skyrim:Halldir's Cairn"). His dead body is found along with those of his friends, [Vidgrod](/wiki/Skyrim:Vidgrod "Skyrim:Vidgrod") and [Raen](/wiki/Skyrim:Raen "Skyrim:Raen"), next to the magical cairn. According to [his journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Agrius%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Agrius's Journal"), he and his friends were in a bandit gang but were kicked out and took refuge in Halldir's Cairn. He was the last of them to be possessed by [Halldir](/wiki/Skyrim:Halldir "Skyrim:Halldir").

He wears a randomly determined set of [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Light_Armor "Skyrim:Armor"); at all levels, there is always a 50% chance he will wear [fur shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes "Skyrim:Fur Shoes") and a set of [fur armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor "Skyrim:Fur Armor"), while the other 50% can differ; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") and/or [hide armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor"), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") and/or [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor"), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots "Skyrim:Scaled Boots") and/or [scaled armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Armor") or [scaled horn armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor "Skyrim:Scaled Horn Armor"). Additionally, he has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Bracers "Skyrim:Fur Bracers") but, at level 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers "Skyrim:Leather Bracers") and at level 19, it can be [scaled bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers "Skyrim:Scaled Bracers"). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of him carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12.

His weapon of choice is random and leveled as well (up to elven quality at level 22) and can be either a [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), a [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") or a [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"). He also carries a bit of random loot, food and drink, with a slim chance of a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and/or some gold.

Agrius' body, along with his two friends, is consumed by Halldir to make his three elemental copies during your fight with him.

## Bugs

- Unlike the bodies of Vidgrod and Raen, Agrius' body is not removed after Halldir consumes their souls to summon his elemental copies.
