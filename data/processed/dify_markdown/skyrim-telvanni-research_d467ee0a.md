## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth") in [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)").
2. Collect an ash sample from the remains of an [ash spawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Spawn "Skyrim:Ash Spawn").
3. Return to Neloth for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

After making your way to [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)"), find and speak to [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth") and ask if you can help out. He will give you an extractor, and ask you to obtain an ash sample from an [ash spawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Spawn "Skyrim:Ash Spawn") as he is working on a spell to summon them. He will inform you that the ash sample can only be extracted from a dead ash spawn, but remains confident in your ability to "bring it about."

Find and kill any ash spawn. The wall outside [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock") should have plenty of ash spawn if you haven't already completed [March of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead "Skyrim:March of the Dead"). Note that you can still extract the sample from the remains of an ash spawn killed prior to starting the quest, so if you have recently completed March of the Dead, you'll likely still be able to find their remains outside of the city walls.

Searching the corpse of an ash spawn should bring up the option to use the extractor. After using it, return to Neloth for you reward. The spell tome [Conjure Ash Spawn](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Ash_Spawn "Skyrim:Conjure Ash Spawn") should be available for sale from [Talvas Fathryon](/wiki/Skyrim:Talvas_Fathryon "Skyrim:Talvas Fathryon") a day or so after completion of the quest.
