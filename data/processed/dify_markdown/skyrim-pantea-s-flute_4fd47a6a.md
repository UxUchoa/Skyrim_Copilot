## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Pantea](/wiki/Skyrim:Pantea_Ateia "Skyrim:Pantea Ateia") at the Bards College.
2. Travel to [Hob's Fall Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Hob%27s_Fall_Cave "Skyrim:Hob's Fall Cave") and find her missing [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Pantea%27s_Flute_(item) "Skyrim:Pantea's Flute (item)").
3. Return to Pantea for your reward.

### Talking to Pantea

When the Fire Festival marks the end of the [Tending the Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Tending_the_Flames "Skyrim:Tending the Flames") quest, talking to Pantea will bring up the topic of a flute: *"That damn fool! I should ring*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *her neck!"* When asked to elaborate she will explain about a foolish student, [Larina](/wiki/Skyrim:Larina "Skyrim:Larina"), who stole [Pantea's flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Pantea%27s_Flute_(item) "Skyrim:Pantea's Flute (item)") and sold it to a [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer"), who believed in Larina's story about the flute being able to make the dead dance. Pantea is understandably upset as the flute has been passed down through seventeen generations, so she asks you to retrieve it for her – right away.

### Hob's Fall Cave

A map marker will now point you towards [Hob's Fall Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Hob%27s_Fall_Cave "Skyrim:Hob's Fall Cave"), a necromancer den northeast of [Frostflow Lighthouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostflow_Lighthouse "Skyrim:Frostflow Lighthouse") in the [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)") region. While the cave itself seems straightforward, it has quite a few secrets worth exploring, so enter and take a look around. The ledge overlooking the first room is where you will return after a successful trek, so locate the icy path to the left and make your way through the tunnels and fight the hostile necromancers guarding the desk with a [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") and a treasure chest. Proceed forward until you reach a suspension bridge. Look over the edge and notice the necromancer camp deep down below, with an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and several bedrolls. Use the ledges to jump down for a surprise attack, then loot the camp for a rare [unusual gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem") and a copy of the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") skill book *[The Exodus](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Exodus "Skyrim:The Exodus")*.

### Finding the Flute

Head back up, using the path to get back on track, and then fight the necromancers guarding the next room. Examine the [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter") for a copy of the [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") skill book *[Enchanter's Primer](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Primer "Skyrim:Enchanter's Primer")*, then activate the pull chain and either dodge or destroy the [spellcaster traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Spellcaster_Trap "Skyrim:Spellcaster Trap"). The final room is guarded by three necromancers and contains the chest with the flute, a [black soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Black Soul Gem"), and a locked cage containing a dead body. After taking the flute, jump down from the ledge and exit the cave the way you came in.

Back in the Bards College, Pantea will be overwhelmed with joy and will teach you a few tricks she learned while playing for the Winterhold wizards, specifically a one-point boost to all your [Magic](/wiki/Skyrim:Magic_Overview "Skyrim:Magic Overview") skills, including [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration"), [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration"), [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction"), [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting"), [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion"), and [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration").

## Notes

- [Isabelle Rolaine](/wiki/Skyrim:Isabelle_Rolaine "Skyrim:Isabelle Rolaine")'s corpse is found at the entrance of the cave if you have started the quest [Drowned Sorrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Drowned_Sorrows "Skyrim:Drowned Sorrows").
- Hob's Fall Cave might be selected as the location for the [Helm of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Helm_of_Winterhold "Skyrim:Helm of Winterhold") from the quest [Dungeon Delving](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Jarl_-_Caves) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Jarl - Caves)"). If this is the case, then it will be in the same chest as the flute.

## Bugs

- The flute can be found before the quest is given, meaning the marker to turn the quest in never appears, nor will Pantea even offer the quest.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") PC players can use this console command to advance the quest: `setstage bardscollegeflute 40`
- After you turn in the quest, the flute will remain in your inventory, still marked as a quest item.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.9.26.0.8, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") PC players can remove the quest item using the following console code: `Player.removeitem 000DABA7 1`
- It's possible that you can turn in the flute to Pantea but the quest won't complete.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
