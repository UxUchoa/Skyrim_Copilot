# Skyrim:Conjurer

## Overview

**Conjurers**  are [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") specialists who prefer to summon [Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra "Skyrim:Daedra") in combat. They are typically found in a few caves, keeps, and ruins across [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim") known as [warlock lairs](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock_Lairs "Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock Lairs"). These mages belong to the [Conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatMageConjurer "Skyrim:CombatMageConjurer") [NPC](/wiki/Skyrim:NPCs "Skyrim:NPCs") class.

The type of conjurer you encounter depends on your level. You will continue to meet low-level conjurers even at high levels, but boss-level conjurers will always be at the highest possible level.

### Conjurers

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-npc-Conjurer_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Conjurer_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Conjurer_02.jpg)

A Novice Conjurer blasting a gout of fire with a ward ready in his other hand

Conjurers use Destruction magic and summon allies to fight for them in combat, and they carry a leveled dagger in case of close-quarters fighting ([iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), [steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), [orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger "Skyrim:Orcish Dagger"), [dwarven](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Dagger "Skyrim:Dwarven Dagger"), [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger "Skyrim:Elven Dagger"), [glass](/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Dagger "Skyrim:Glass Dagger"), or an enchanted dagger of any quality from iron to [Daedric](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric "Skyrim:Daedric")). They also have a 10% chance to carry a leveled [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") or Destruction staff.

They wear a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12 "Skyrim:Boots") along with [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes"), [hooded black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Hooded Black Mage Robes"), robes of [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Alteration "Skyrim:Fortify Alteration"), robes of [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Destruction "Skyrim:Fortify Destruction"), robes of [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Restoration "Skyrim:Fortify Restoration"), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Magicka_Rate "Skyrim:Fortify Magicka Rate").

In addition to the gear listed above, conjurers have a small chance to carry [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), [poisons](/wiki/Skyrim:Poisons "Skyrim:Poisons"), [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tomes "Skyrim:Spell Tomes"), [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](/wiki/Skyrim:Recipes "Skyrim:Recipes"), and a small amount of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). They also have [atronach forge recipes](/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach_Forge_Recipe "Skyrim:Atronach Forge Recipe") in their inventories, but their chance of appearing in-game is set at 0%.

On death, conjurers have a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls:

- **Alteration:** [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis "Skyrim:Scroll of Mass Paralysis")
- **Conjuration:** [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach "Skyrim:Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach"), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie "Skyrim:Scroll of Dread Zombie")
- **Destruction:**[Scroll of Blizzard](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Blizzard "Skyrim:Scroll of Blizzard"), [Scroll of Fire Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fire_Storm "Skyrim:Scroll of Fire Storm")
- **Illusion:** [Scroll of Call to Arms](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms "Skyrim:Scroll of Call to Arms"), [Scroll of Harmony](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Harmony "Skyrim:Scroll of Harmony"), [Scroll of Hysteria](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Hysteria "Skyrim:Scroll of Hysteria"), [Scroll of Mayhem](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mayhem "Skyrim:Scroll of Mayhem")
- **Restoration:** [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead "Skyrim:Scroll of Bane of the Undead"), [Scroll of Guardian Circle](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle "Skyrim:Scroll of Guardian Circle")

Master Conjurers and Arch Conjurers have the same gear as lower-level conjurers, but have a 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Chain Lightning](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Chain_Lightning "Skyrim:Staff of Chain Lightning"), [Staff of Fireballs](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Fireballs "Skyrim:Staff of Fireballs"), or a [Staff of Ice Storms](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Ice_Storms "Skyrim:Staff of Ice Storms") and do not have the leveled lists for atronach forge recipes.

## Dialogue

All members of the Warlock faction share a set of generic dialogue which they will use when in combat:

*"You cannot even conceive of the power I wield..."*

*"Fire, frost, or lightning - you will suffer at my hands!"*

*"Don't you see? I am master of the arcane!"*

*"Behold my power!"*

*"The elements are mine to command!"*

## Bugs

- Most conjurers will summon their atronachs before attacking you with Destruction magic, but conjurers with the Conjure Storm Atronach spell will not summon Storm Atronachs. This is likely due to an issue with their combat style script.
