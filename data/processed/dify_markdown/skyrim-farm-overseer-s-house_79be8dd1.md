The **Farm Overseer's House** is a small [Dwarven building](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwarven Ruins") in [Blackreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackreach "Skyrim:Blackreach"). It is in the southwestern part of Blackreach, west of the bridge to the [Tower of Mzark](/wiki/Skyrim:Tower_of_Mzark "Skyrim:Tower of Mzark"). The building is devoid of any [creatures](/wiki/Skyrim:Creatures "Skyrim:Creatures") or [automatons](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Automatons "Skyrim:Dwarven Automatons").

### Exterior

Fifteen mushroom patches of various types are growing outside the house, as listed below. There is a [geode vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Geode_Vein "Skyrim:Geode Vein") just behind the northern corner of the building, and a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") next to the mushroom patches on the ground.

### Farm Overseer's House

The house is a typical deserted Dwemer dwelling. It contains a stone double bed against the west wall, a novice-locked wall [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") to the left of the bed, and an adept-locked chest on the floor, also to the left of the bed. To the right of the bed is a set of shelves holding a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poisons "Skyrim:Poisons") and some random Dwemer metal items. Against the north wall is a table and chairs, with two [skeever tails](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever_Tail "Skyrim:Skeever Tail"), a [glowing mushroom](/wiki/Skyrim:Glowing_Mushroom "Skyrim:Glowing Mushroom") sample, and a [Falmer bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Bow "Skyrim:Falmer Bow") resting on the table. A quiver of [Falmer arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Arrow "Skyrim:Falmer Arrow") is on the floor in front of the table. There is a wall shelf behind the table on the north wall holding a random healing [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") and a random magicka potion. There are a further two glowing mushrooms growing on the north wall, and another on the west wall. The east wall has a fireplace in the center with an unlit fire. A set of shelves to the right of the fireplace holds various Dwemer metal items. Additional metal items are scattered around the room.
