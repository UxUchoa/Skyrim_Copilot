# Skyrim:Argonian (NPC)

## Overview

These Argonians are both female [citizens](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)") who wear randomly determined farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") (7 variants) or a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and random [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") (4 variants). They also have a 50% chance of wearing a random hat (3 variants). They have a 25% chance of carrying a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and/or a small amount of gold.

A dead male Argonian

This Argonian is a male [citizen](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)") and wears randomly determined farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") (7 variants) or a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and random [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") (4 variants). He also has a 50% chance of wearing a random [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Hat "Skyrim:Hat") (3 variants). He has a 25% chance of carrying a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and/or a small amount of gold. He always carries *[Guide to Better Thieving](/wiki/Skyrim:Guide_to_Better_Thieving "Skyrim:Guide to Better Thieving")*.
