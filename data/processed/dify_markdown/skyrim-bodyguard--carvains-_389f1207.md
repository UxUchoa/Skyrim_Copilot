The Carvains' bodyguard

This **Bodyguard** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [soldier](/wiki/Skyrim:Soldier_(class) "Skyrim:Soldier (class)") who can be found guarding [Plautis](/wiki/Skyrim:Plautis_Carvain "Skyrim:Plautis Carvain") and [Salonia](/wiki/Skyrim:Salonia_Carvain "Skyrim:Salonia Carvain") Carvain during a [random encounter](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#On_the_way_to_the_Wedding "Skyrim:World Interactions") in the wilderness. They have traveled from [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil") to Skyrim in order to participate in the wedding between [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici") and [Asgeir Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Asgeir_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Asgeir Snow-Shod"), but never make it there. According to Plautis the second time you meet him, he is ultimately eaten by a dragon. The bodyguard wears either [Imperial light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Armor "Skyrim:Imperial Light Armor") or [studded Imperial armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Imperial_Armor "Skyrim:Studded Imperial Armor"), along with [Imperial light boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Boots "Skyrim:Imperial Light Boots") and matching [bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Bracers "Skyrim:Imperial Light Bracers"), and [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Helmet "Skyrim:Imperial Light Helmet"). He carries a matching [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Shield "Skyrim:Imperial Light Shield"), [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword"), and a [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch").

## Notes

- Despite seemingly being an Imperial soldier, Stormcloaks will not attack him on sight. This is due to him not belonging to the normal Civil War-related factions which would normally provoke members of the other side to attack him.
