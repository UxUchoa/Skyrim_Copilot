High Gate Ruins

**High Gate Ruins** is a medium-sized [Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Ruins "Skyrim:Nordic Ruins") west of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") containing [draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr") and the [dragon priest](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Priest "Skyrim:Dragon Priest") [Vokun](/wiki/Skyrim:Vokun_(creature) "Skyrim:Vokun (creature)"). It contains three zones: *[High Gate Ruins](#High_Gate_Ruins)*, *[High Gate Ruins Catacombs](#High_Gate_Ruins_Catacombs)*, and *[Vokun's Throne Room](#Vokun.27s_Throne_Room)*.

Its [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") teaches part of the [Storm Call](/wiki/Skyrim:Storm_Call "Skyrim:Storm Call") [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").

### Exterior

A [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush and a small rock formation are on the left side of an arch with two lit braziers just before the entrance. The statues of two birds' faces can be found just above the braziers, and the statue of a man's head is at the top of the arch.

### High Gate Ruins

The ruins are occupied by draugr. There are numerous [urns](/wiki/Skyrim:Urn "Skyrim:Urn"), [burial urns](/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn "Skyrim:Burial Urn"), and [dead draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Draugr "Skyrim:Containers") that can be looted throughout. Speaking with [Anska](/wiki/Skyrim:Anska "Skyrim:Anska") near the entrance initiates the quest [A Scroll For Anska](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Scroll_For_Anska "Skyrim:A Scroll For Anska"). In the large room after the room where Anska is first found, there are three draugr, one on the ground level and two on the second level. There is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") toward the far wall, and some shelves against the east wall holding a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a few [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks. On the walkway above the chest is an altar and a bookshelf where a random filled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), a random one-handed [weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons"), and four random [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") may be found. Following the walkway around to the west brings you to a copy of the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Hypothetical Treachery](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Hypothetical_Treachery "Skyrim:A Hypothetical Treachery")* near a lootable [skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton"). An unlocked chest can be found by following the walkway all the way around to the east.

At the rear of the walkway is an iron door with a passage behind it descending several flights of stairs that are trapped with two [spear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Spear_Trap "Skyrim:Spear Trap"). At the bottom is a room guarded by three leveled draugr. There are many large and small shelving units holding a random weapon, two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), five random potions, two bowls of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal"), a bowl of [ectoplasm](/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm "Skyrim:Ectoplasm"), two bowls of [fire salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts "Skyrim:Fire Salts"), a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg"), a [luna moth wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth_Wing "Skyrim:Luna Moth Wing"), a bowl of [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat"), a large coin purse, and two [apothecary's satchels](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"). There is also an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanters "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanters") at the rear of the room to the left of the passage onward.

A passage leads past a [pressure plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Pressure_Plate "Skyrim:Pressure Plate") that triggers a [spike wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Spike_Wall "Skyrim:Spike Wall") trap and an upright sarcophagus containing a leveled draugr to another two-tier room containing four levers. If Anska is with you, she will comment on the room being some sort of puzzle. True enough, set into the floor is a wooden spiral stairway under a trapdoor which is opened by pulling the levers in the correct order. The solution is carved into the rock high up on the walls of the room: **Eagle—Whale—Fox—Snake**. The solution is displayed on both the south and north walls of the room, although on the north wall, the third symbol is missing. At the bottom of the spiral stairway is a small catacombs, with a sideboard on the left holding a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), and an iron door ahead leading to *High Gate Ruins Catacombs*.

### High Gate Ruins Catacombs

From the entrance into this area, a corridor leads deeper into the catacombs past an upright dormant draugr who awakens at your presence, before you descend a spiral stairway. At the bottom, the passage passes into a chamber with many alcoves on either side. There is a leveled draugr on the right before the passage turns. Pass some barrels to two more draugr at the next corner. Ahead is a [pressure plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Pressure_Plate "Skyrim:Pressure Plate") that triggers a [dart trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Dart_Trap "Skyrim:Dart Trap") from in front, and another draugr standing in an alcove to the left. The next area has a sideboard with a random [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword"), a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health"), a random [potion of regeneration](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration "Skyrim:Potion of Regeneration"), and a third random potion on top. Next is a leveled draugr in an alcove to the left, opposite a chest with a pressure plate in front that triggers a spear trap from behind the chest; eight loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") are also scattered on the ground in front of the chest. Around the corner are iron doors that open into a room with two upright sarcophagi and a plinth with a random soul gem atop a [weight sensitive plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Weight_Sensitive_Plate "Skyrim:Weight Sensitive Plate"). Removing the soul gem will cause the sarcophagi to burst open, revealing two leveled draugr. There is an adept-locked door in the southeastern corner, behind which you can find a set of shelves holding an unlocked chest and a one-handed weapon. In the southwestern corner is a passage heading south, passing a sideboard with another couple random potions, then widening similar to the passages that normally approach puzzle doors. There are many pressure plates along this section: the first five trigger spear traps thrusting up from the floor, and the last three trigger dart traps. At the far end is a set of double doors.

Through these doors is another room similar to the room with the soul gem, but this one contains a lever in the center and two doorways in the corners blocked by large stones. Pulling the lever opens the right-hand door and releases a leveled draugr. In the alcove behind that draugr is a second lever that operates the other door. After dealing with a second leveled draugr, check their alcove for a third lever that opens the iron gate to the east, which leads to a very short passage. To the right along this passage is a set of shelves holding six random potions, a random two-handed weapon, a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"), and a random [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet"). An iron door to the east at the end of the passage leads to *Vokun's Throne Room*.

### Vokun's Throne Room

A short corridor leads to the set of double doors opening to the throne room proper. Along the corridor is a large stone bowl containing three leveled potions, one each of healing, magicka, and stamina. As you approach the double doors, they will automatically open to reveal Vokun rising from his sarcophagus. When dispatched, Vokun drops his eponymous [mask](/wiki/Skyrim:Vokun_(item) "Skyrim:Vokun (item)") and a [Staff of Fireballs](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Fireballs "Skyrim:Staff of Fireballs").

On the far side of the throne room is a treasure chamber containing two unlocked chests (one of which contains good loot), several urns and burial urns, and a word wall which teaches a word for the Storm Call shout. The [scroll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sealed_Scroll "Skyrim:Sealed Scroll") for the quest A Scroll For Anska will be sitting on the stone table in front of the word wall.

A shortcut back to the exit is found on the upper level of the treasure chamber, to the left of the room as you enter from the throne room. A passageway leads via a chain-operated gate to a balcony overlooking the room in which Anska was originally found. The balcony has two sets of shelves holding a [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade"), a burial urn, a food sack, and a bowl of bone meal.

## Notes

- Two [orichalcum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore Vein") are 240 feet south-southeast of the ruins, on the hills behind the ruin.
- If *[Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")* is installed, the ruins are a very short distance northeast of [Windstad Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Windstad_Manor "Skyrim:Windstad Manor").
- When the location respawns, no boss enemy spawns in Vokun's room to replace him, so the doors to the treasure chamber will not open, as Vokun's death initially triggers their opening. To work around this, see the [Bugs](#Bugs) section below.
- Version 2.0.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") replaces the [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush outside with the snow-covered version of the same plant.

## Bugs

- If a radiant quest ([Azra's Staffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Azra%27s_Staffs "Skyrim:Azra's Staffs"), [Fetch me that Book!](/wiki/Skyrim:Fetch_me_that_Book! "Skyrim:Fetch me that Book!"), or [Shalidor's Insights](/wiki/Skyrim:Shalidor%27s_Insights "Skyrim:Shalidor's Insights")) has been accepted and directs you to High Gate Ruins, the staff or book will be in a chest in the treasure chamber behind *Vokun's Throne Room*. However, the chamber will be inaccessible if you have already visited High Gate Ruins and killed Vokun.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") This can be resolved by using [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") commands, either to resurrect Vokun and kill him again (select the body, not his ashes), or to simply pass through one of the closed gates (use `tcl` to turn clipping on and off).
  - *Vokun's Throne Room* may be accessed by using a follower to open the gate blocking you from the treasure chamber exit above the first room. This is accomplished by bringing a follower after clearing out the entire dungeon (it is not yet confirmed whether clearing the dungeon first is necessary), climbing up the pile of rubble in the first room beside Anska, and using the [Whirlwind Sprint](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Sprint "Skyrim:Whirlwind Sprint") shout or climbing the rubble at the left corner of the balcony to access the walkway leading to the exit from the treasure chamber. The follower will run through the dungeon, and eventually, in approximately 1-3 minutes, appear on the other side of the gate from you. The follower can then be instructed to pull the chain, thereby opening the gate and permitting you into *Vokun's Throne Room*.
    - Since the 1.4 patch, using the [wait](/wiki/Skyrim:Sleeping#Waiting "Skyrim:Sleeping") command while standing against the gate no longer works.
  - Another fight-fire-with-fire solution is to "kettle step" through the wall. It's a glitch where you set a kettle on the floor, mouth down and bottom up, then use Whirlwind Sprint over the top of the upturned kettle. If done right, you will go through the door.
  - You can clip through the gate by using Whirlwind Sprint while holding a bowl up to the gate in the throne room. Doing so will cause you to clip through the gate.
- The fix from Unofficial Skyrim Patch 1.2 to open the gates if you returned after Vokun was dead failed to account for arriving in the location if you were there to kill Vokun.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug.
- When you first enter *High Gate Ruins Catacombs*, Anska will state, *"That gem looks suspicious to me."* This should be triggered when you enter the room with the soul gem on a pressure plate later in the zone.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- When revisiting the ruins, the four puzzle levers used to open the trapdoor spring back, so the trapdoor will not open.
- In the puzzle room, pulling only the Eagle, Whale, and Snake levers (in that order) also opens the floor grating.
