Severin Manor

**Severin Manor** is initially the home of [Mirri Severin](/wiki/Skyrim:Mirri_Severin "Skyrim:Mirri Severin"), [Tilisu Severin](/wiki/Skyrim:Tilisu_Severin "Skyrim:Tilisu Severin"), and [Vendil Severin](/wiki/Skyrim:Vendil_Severin "Skyrim:Vendil Severin") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock"), which can be obtained as player housing by completing the quest [Served Cold](/wiki/Skyrim:Served_Cold "Skyrim:Served Cold").

It is northwest of the [Abandoned Building](/wiki/Skyrim:Abandoned_Building "Skyrim:Abandoned Building"), being the last building along the path to the [Earth Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Earth_Stone "Skyrim:Earth Stone") to the west.

## Related Quests

- **[Served Cold](/wiki/Skyrim:Served_Cold "Skyrim:Served Cold")**: Save [Councilor Morvayn](/wiki/Skyrim:Lleril_Morvayn "Skyrim:Lleril Morvayn") from an assassination plot.

### Exterior

In front of the entrance are three [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plants and a [trama root](/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root "Skyrim:Trama Root"). To the left of the entrance is a lit brazier, two ash yam urns, and another trama root. The entrance is unlocked.

### Severin Manor

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-interior-Severin_Manor.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Severin_Manor.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Severin_Manor.jpg)

Entrance room

The entrance leads into the upper floor of the manor. Along the south wall are five straw bales, an open box containing five [ash yams](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam"), a bench, and a wood pile. Near the west wall is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand in front of the fire, with a chair nearby. Along the north wall is a small table, a food barrel with four [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") on top, a table holding a variety of food and drink, three fish barrels, one of which has three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") on top, and two chairs and a small round table holding a bottle of [sujamma](/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma "Skyrim:Sujamma"), a bottle of [shein](/wiki/Skyrim:Shein "Skyrim:Shein"), and copies of *[The House of Troubles](/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Troubles "Skyrim:The House of Troubles")* and *[The Five Far Stars](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Five_Far_Stars "Skyrim:The Five Far Stars")*. The laden table holds a bottle of sujamma, a bottle of shein, three bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two loaves of bread, three ash yams, and an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"). In the middle of the room, stairs lead down to the lower floor, with two food urns on the south side of the stairs.

At the bottom of the stairs is an area with rooms beyond doors to the south and west, [alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") and [enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") areas in alcoves to the south and north of the west door, respectively, and a [smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing") area down a short corridor north of the south door. In the corridor to the smithing area is a bench, an ingredient urn, and a food urn. In the smithing area itself, in the southwest corner is a row of three empty weapon racks, a meat barrel, an unusable barrel, and three practice dummies in front of four straw bales. In the northwest corner is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), and three unusable barrels, one of which has three bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on top, and another of which has another bottle of Nord mead on top. In the northeast corner is a large [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge"). In the southeast corner is a [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") including a nearby coal pile, an open box containing seven [steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot "Skyrim:Steel Ingot") and seven [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot"), and another row of three empty weapon racks.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-interior-Severin_Manor_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Severin_Manor_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Severin_Manor_02.jpg)

Mirri's bedroom

The room to the south is initially Mirri Severin's bedroom. In the entry alcove near the door is a bench. In the bedroom itself, in the northwest corner is another bench. In the northeast corner are two sacks, a sideboard holding three [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), two [boiled creme treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), two large [coin purses](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), and a small coin purse, two ingredient barrels, and another sideboard holding a [troll skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull "Skyrim:Troll Skull") and copies of both volumes of *[The Red Year](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Year "Skyrim:The Red Year")* and *[Words of the Wind](/wiki/Skyrim:Words_of_the_Wind "Skyrim:Words of the Wind")*. In the southeast corner is a sideboard holding three bottles of sujamma, two bowls of [cabbage potato soup](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage_Potato_Soup "Skyrim:Cabbage Potato Soup"), and two copies of *[History of Raven Rock, Vol. I](/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._I "Skyrim:History of Raven Rock, Vol. I")*, an initially owned single bed, and a chest. In the southwest corner is a circular ash pile garden plot growing seven ash yams and two scathecraw plants, with two baskets containing a total of three ash yams on the edge of the plot.

In the area in front of the door to the west are two sacks and four unusable barrels, one of which has a copy of *[The Axe Man](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Axe_Man "Skyrim:The Axe Man")* on top. In the alchemy area are five ingredient barrels, two baskets, two sets of shelves, two open boxes, and an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs") in the center with two bowls of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal") on top. One barrel has an [enchanter's philter](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Philter "Skyrim:Enchanter's Philter") and a [solution of enhanced stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Solution of Enhanced Stamina") on top, another barrel has two samples of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bowl of bone meal on top, a third barrel has a pot of [ash hopper jelly](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Hopper_Jelly "Skyrim:Ash Hopper Jelly") and two samples of [Felsaad tern feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Felsaad_Tern_Feathers "Skyrim:Felsaad Tern Feathers") on top, and a fourth barrel has a sample of Felsaad tern feathers on top. The basket to the east contains sixteen [chaurus eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Eggs "Skyrim:Chaurus Eggs"), and the basket to the west contains two samples of trama root. The set of shelves to the east holds two bowls of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), six bowls of [fire salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts "Skyrim:Fire Salts"), four bowls of [frost salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Salts "Skyrim:Frost Salts"), an [ashen grass pod](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Ashen Grass Pod"), two bowls of bone meal, and three samples of [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"). The set of shelves to the west holds a bowl of frost salts, a [potent poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Potent_Poison "Skyrim:Potent Poison"), a bottle of [matze](/wiki/Skyrim:Matze "Skyrim:Matze"), a [draught of light feet](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Light_Feet "Skyrim:Draught of Light Feet"), two [taproots](/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot "Skyrim:Taproot"), a sample of [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), a pot of [spawn ash](/wiki/Skyrim:Spawn_Ash "Skyrim:Spawn Ash"), a piece of [burnt spriggan wood](/wiki/Skyrim:Burnt_Spriggan_Wood "Skyrim:Burnt Spriggan Wood"), three samples of trama root, two [potions of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a [potion of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), a [potion of cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Potion of Cure Disease"), and three sacks. The open box in front of this set of shelves contains four [mammoth tusks](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk"). The open box to the right of the set of shelves contains a [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") flower, a sample of [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita"), a sample of [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot"), and six chaurus eggs.

In the enchanting area are six empty weapon plaques and three empty shield plaques on the wall, five baskets, two of which contain a total of three empty [lesser soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Lesser Soul Gem") and eighteen empty [common soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem"), a small round table holding an empty [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem"), two empty lesser soul gems, and two empty common soul gems, and an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanters "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanters") in the center.

Master bedroom

The room to the west is initially Tilisu and Vendil Severin's bedroom. In the area near the door are four armor mannequins, each flanked by two empty weapon racks. To the west opposite the door is an initially owned double bed with a chest at its foot and a shield plaque on the wall above the bed. In the southeast corner is a food barrel with two bottles of sujamma on top, a fish barrel, two chairs, and a table holding a row of lit candles, four ash yams, three bottles of matze, a bottle of sujamma, three bottles of alto wine, a bottle of wine, a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). In the southwest corner is a chest, the expert-locked [Severin family safe](/wiki/Skyrim:Safe "Skyrim:Safe"), an empty set of shelves with a row of lit candles on top, and another chest. In the northwest corner is a sack, two food barrels, a chair and a small round table holding two lit candles, two bottles of matze, and a troll skull, a set of shelves with a row of lit candles on top holding a potion of minor healing, a potion of cure disease, two [potions of plentiful healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Healing"), a chest, and three empty common soul gems, and another chair and another small round table holding four lit candles and a bottle of matze. In the northeast corner are three display cases, with the first containing a [steel war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe "Skyrim:Steel War Axe"), the second containing two [steel daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), an [amethyst](/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst "Skyrim:Amethyst"), a [garnet](/wiki/Skyrim:Garnet "Skyrim:Garnet"), and an [emerald](/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald "Skyrim:Emerald"), and the third containing only six [skulls](/wiki/Skyrim:Skull "Skyrim:Skull").

## Notes

- Looting the manor during the related quest is unnecessary, as you'll obtain the building and its contents for free as your final quest reward.
- Storage in the manor is [safe](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Safe_Locations "Skyrim:Containers") even prior to gaining ownership.
- This is the only house you can own on Solstheim.
- Aside from [Creation Club](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") content, this is the only house you can obtain for free.
- [Spouses](/wiki/Skyrim:Spouse "Skyrim:Spouse"), [stewards](/wiki/Skyrim:Personal_Steward "Skyrim:Personal Steward") and [children](/wiki/Skyrim:Adoption "Skyrim:Adoption") cannot be moved into Severin Manor, due to Solstheim being a separate area from Skyrim proper and adoption being part of a [separate add-on](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire").
- There are no [housecarls](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl") available here.

## Bugs

- Several buildings in Raven Rock, including Severin Manor, were incorrectly assigned the [music](/wiki/Skyrim:Music "Skyrim:Music") heard in [Apocrypha](/wiki/Skyrim:Apocrypha "Skyrim:Apocrypha") rather than the default Solstheim music heard in other houses.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.1, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=11986))
- Even after gaining ownership of the manor, you may be told that "You cannot sleep while being asked to leave" when you try to use the beds. This will usually happen if you 1) killed Mirri and Tilisu at night, and 2) had followers while doing so. It can be fixed on the PC by 1) resurrecting Mirri and Tilisu through the console, 2) waiting for 24 hours outside the manor, then 3) killing them again through the console. [*verification needed — Are all conditions and the fix tested?*]
- After you gain ownership of the manor, the number of houses you own in the Stats menu may not change despite fact you now own it. **?**
- The beds may not give the [Well Rested](/wiki/Skyrim:Well_Rested "Skyrim:Well Rested") bonus when used.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.1, fixes this bug.
- Two skulls in a display case here cannot be picked up, making them impossible to remove to use the case for something else.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- Even with Unofficial Dragonborn Patch 1.0.1, the main bed may still deny the [Well Rested](/wiki/Skyrim:Well_Rested "Skyrim:Well Rested") and [Lover's Comfort](/wiki/Skyrim:Lover%27s_Comfort "Skyrim:Lover's Comfort") bonuses.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 2.0.5, addresses this issue. The main bed was never assigned as owned by the PlayerBedOwnership faction.
- The smelter may not be visible, although it can still be used. To fix this, simply exit the manor and reenter, and the smelter should then be visible and function properly.
- Books may disappear when placed on the shelves in the master bedroom.
- Armor and clothing may disappear when placed on the mannequins in the master bedroom after you then leave the manor and return. **?**
- Followers may disappear if told to wait in the manor. **?**
- The mannequins may spawn any items placed on them infinitely, even after the item has been removed.
- Items placed on the mannequin may lose any improvements, as well as any enchantments that you have applied. **?**
- [![Nintendo Switch Logo.svg](//images.uesp.net/5/5d/Nintendo_Switch_Logo.svg)](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch "Skyrim:Switch") Due to the quest [March of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead "Skyrim:March of the Dead") being removed by the Anniversary Edition on [Switch](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch "Skyrim:Switch"), the house was not available if you had not already unlocked it.
  - This was fixed by the Official Skyrim [Switch Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch_Patch "Skyrim:Switch Patch"), Version 1.1.392.392534.8.
- The exterior building may disappear, leaving only the front door.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The building can be reenabled on PC using the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") commands `prid xx01DF3A` and `enable`.
