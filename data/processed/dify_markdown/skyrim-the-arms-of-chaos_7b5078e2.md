## Quick Walkthrough

1. Go to [Skytemple Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins "Skyrim:Skytemple Ruins") and take *[Hyenril's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Hyenril%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Hyenril's Journal")*, the [Arm of the Moon](/wiki/Skyrim:Arm_of_the_Moon "Skyrim:Arm of the Moon"), the [Arm of the Sun](/wiki/Skyrim:Arm_of_the_Sun "Skyrim:Arm of the Sun"), and the [Warlock's Mark](/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock%27s_Mark "Skyrim:Warlock's Mark").
2. Go to the [East Empire Company offices](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_(place) "Skyrim:East Empire Company (place)") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") and read *[Loss of the Sea Stallion](/wiki/Skyrim:Loss_of_the_Sea_Stallion "Skyrim:Loss of the Sea Stallion")*.
3. Locate the [Sovereign Band](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovereign_Band "Skyrim:Sovereign Band") within a coffin at the bottom of [Pilgrim's Trench](/wiki/Skyrim:Pilgrim%27s_Trench "Skyrim:Pilgrim's Trench").
4. Find the summoning circle east of Skytemple Ruins to summon and defeat three [Dremora](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora "Skyrim:Dremora").
5. Craft the three items using a [Forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") and [Staff Enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_Enchanter "Skyrim:Staff Enchanter").

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos.jpg)

A young mage has perished, her work uncompleted

### A Dead Mage

This quest will automatically begin upon reading *[Hyenril's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Hyenril%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Hyenril's Journal")* at the [Skytemple Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins "Skyrim:Skytemple Ruins") north of the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)"). The ruins are usually swarming with undead [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton"), although these shouldn't pose much of a threat. You must locate the corpse of [Hyenril](/wiki/Skyrim:Hyenril "Skyrim:Hyenril"), a dead mage found within one of the ruins. On the table in front of her, you will find *[Hyenril's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Hyenril%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Hyenril's Journal")* along with two broken staves: the [Arm of the Moon](/wiki/Skyrim:Arm_of_the_Moon "Skyrim:Arm of the Moon") and the [Arm of the Sun](/wiki/Skyrim:Arm_of_the_Sun "Skyrim:Arm of the Sun"). Hidden beneath the table is the [Warlock's Mark](/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock%27s_Mark "Skyrim:Warlock's Mark"), a mundane necklace.

Reading the journal will identify these items as the property of Hyenril's former master, Ellane, who spent long years attempting to restore the two staves, which were originally created in imitation of the legendary [Staff of Chaos](/wiki/Lore:Staff_of_Chaos "Lore:Staff of Chaos") but were destroyed by their original creator. It will also reveal that Sigil Stones will be needed to restore the three items and unlock their true power. Take all three items to progress the quest; the Warlock's Mark is small and really can be difficult to see, so you may need to rely on your HUD pointer.

### Sunken Treasure

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos_02.jpg)

Locate the master's watery grave

In order to obtain the required Sigil Stones, you will need to track down Ellane's ring of summoning. Unfortunately, Ellane was still wearing this ring when Hyenril shipped her body back home for burial aboard a vessel called the *Sea Stallion*. Your first stop is the [East Empire Company offices](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_(place) "Skyrim:East Empire Company (place)") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), where you will find a letter entitled *[Loss of the Sea Stallion](/wiki/Skyrim:Loss_of_the_Sea_Stallion "Skyrim:Loss of the Sea Stallion")* on a table next to a potion of waterbreathing. The ship apparently sank to the bottom of [Pilgrim's Trench](/wiki/Skyrim:Pilgrim%27s_Trench "Skyrim:Pilgrim's Trench") off the coast of Skyrim before it delivered its cargo.

Head to Pilgrim's Trench and prepare for a deep dive to find Ellane's coffin, which can be found atop a rocky outcropping on the sea floor. Inside you will find a copy of *[Final Lesson](/wiki/Skyrim:Final_Lesson "Skyrim:Final Lesson")*, *[Master Ellane's Last Words](/wiki/Skyrim:Master_Ellane%27s_Last_Words "Skyrim:Master Ellane's Last Words")*, and the [Sovereign Band](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovereign_Band "Skyrim:Sovereign Band"), an enchanted ring which fortifies your [magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Magicka "Skyrim:Magicka").

### The Guardians of the Sigil Stones

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Arms_of_Chaos_03.jpg)

The Daedric altar

With the ring in hand, you will be directed to a stone Daedric summoning circle on the same island as Skytemple Ruins. You must descend from the peak where the ruins are located and go to the eastern shore of the island to find it. Approach the center of the altar and you will be given the option to summon Daedra. Once you activate it, three [Dremora](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora "Skyrim:Dremora") will appear in quick succession, with a new one being summoned each time you defeat the last:

- [Dremora Sehtkynaz](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Sehtkynaz "Skyrim:Dremora Sehtkynaz"), an ice mage who can summon a [Frost Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Atronach "Skyrim:Frost Atronach"), guardian of the [Blue Sigil Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Sigil_Stone "Skyrim:Blue Sigil Stone");
- [Dremora Lyrkynaz](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Lyrkynaz "Skyrim:Dremora Lyrkynaz"), a shock mage who can summon a [Storm Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Storm_Atronach "Skyrim:Storm Atronach"), guardian of the [Green Sigil Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Sigil_Stone "Skyrim:Green Sigil Stone"); and
- [Dremora Sovereign](/wiki/Skyrim:Dremora_Sovereign "Skyrim:Dremora Sovereign"), a fire-wielding warrior who can summon a [Flame Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Atronach "Skyrim:Flame Atronach"), guardian of the [Orange Sigil Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Sigil_Stone "Skyrim:Orange Sigil Stone").

Survive the encounter, then loot the bodies of the Dremora to obtain the three Sigil Stones needed to restore your items' enchantments. They also each have [Daedra Hearts](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra_Heart "Skyrim:Daedra Heart") on them. The Warlock's Mark can be reforged at any [forge or anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge"). The Arm of the Moon and Arm of the Sun require access to a [Staff Enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_Enchanter "Skyrim:Staff Enchanter"); in the base game, the only one in existence is located behind a locked door in [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)") and can only be accessed after you complete the quest [Reluctant Steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Reluctant_Steward "Skyrim:Reluctant Steward"). You can avoid this prerequisite if you have access to a Staff Enchanter elsewhere, such as the one added by the [Myrwatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Myrwatch "Skyrim:Myrwatch") or [Bloodchill Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodchill_Manor "Skyrim:Bloodchill Manor") Creations.

Once you have crafted the enchanted versions of all three items, the quest will complete and you will have in your possession several powerful artifacts.

## Notes

- It is possible to skip the visit to Windhelm entirely if you have already looted the coffin in Pilgrim's Trench. You will immediately be directed to summon the Dremora.
- The Dremora Sovereign wields a [Daedric Sword of the Inferno](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Sword_of_the_Inferno "Skyrim:Daedric Sword of the Inferno") at all levels, a powerful piece of equipment if you can best all three Dremora at a low level.

## Bugs

- Crafting the staffs using the Myrwatch Staff Enchanter is most beneficial, as it is bugged and grants 20 times more [Enchanting](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanting "Skyrim:Enchanting") experience than it is supposed to. This is due to the Staff Enchanter in Tel Mithryn having a hidden perk (`DLC2StaffEnchantingMult`) that reduces the experience gained by 95%; the Staff Enchanter in Myrwatch has a different Editor ID and thus is unaffected by the perk. As a result, crafting the Arms of Chaos at Myrwatch will result in you possibly jumping more than 20 Enchanting levels.
  - This bug was fixed by an [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition") update to this Creation.
- When you are crafting the Arm of the Moon the message that appears says the Arm of the Sun has been completed, and vice versa. **?**
- The Warlock's Mark can also be glitched in the middle of the table and there seems to be no way of dislodging it.
  - You can reach The Warlock's Mark by standing on the back of the table in the alcove and looking at the place it's marker is. This does not always work.
  - You can disable the table in the console. Do a quick save. Within the console click on the table. It should show something like `fexxx83d`. Type `disable`. Exit the console and pick up the necklace. Re-enter the console and type `enable` (with the same `fexxx83d` still showing) and the table will reappear. You will have the necklace and the quest will update.
  - Cast the spell 'Raise Zombie' on Hyenril's corpse and lead them out of the ruins until they turn to ash. Fast Travel to a major hold like Whiterun and wait 30 days in game. Return to the ruins and the amulet should be on the ground under the table.
- The [Arm of the Moon](/wiki/Skyrim:Arm_of_the_Moon "Skyrim:Arm of the Moon"), the [Arm of the Sun](/wiki/Skyrim:Arm_of_the_Sun "Skyrim:Arm of the Sun"), and the [Warlock's Mark](/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock%27s_Mark "Skyrim:Warlock's Mark") can still have weight while considered quest items.
