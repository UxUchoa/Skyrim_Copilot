Brutius

**Brutius** is an old man hosting a fishing contest. He will invite you to join him, but this is all a ruse.

His spells and perks depend on his level, see [this page](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit#Mages "Skyrim:Bandit") for details.

## Related Quests

- **[No Contest](/wiki/Skyrim:No_Contest "Skyrim:No Contest")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing"): Take part in a fishing contest.

### No Contest

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-quest-No_Contest.jpg/200px-SR-quest-No_Contest.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Contest.jpg)

Brutius reveals the ruse

When you first approach him:

:   **I'm here for the fishing contest. Are you ready to begin?**
:   *"Yeah."*
:   **You're Brutius? I expected someone younger. You're just a feeble old man.**
:   *"I don't have to take that from you!"*
:   **Shall we make a wager, then?**
:   *"How much?"*

:   :   **100 gold.**
    :   **500 gold.**
    :   **1000 gold!**

:   **Where shall we hold the contest?**
:   *"Here's where you need to go."*

When you meet at the specified location, he will attack you with his fellow accomplices.

A trigger box is not configured to be player-only activated, passing NPCs and animals may accidentally advance quest stages, so Brutius's travel packages stop working. **?**
