The Earth Stone

The **Earth Stone** is an [All-Maker Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones") southwest of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

Once [cleansed](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones"), this stone gives you the power to [ignore](/wiki/Skyrim:Bones_of_the_Earth "Skyrim:Bones of the Earth") 80% of all physical damage for 30 seconds. This power is essentially identical to the master-level [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") spell [Dragonhide](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonhide "Skyrim:Dragonhide"), though without the casting delay, high magicka cost, or skill requirement. Once you use this power, it disappears from the Powers menu, and you must return to the Earth Stone to obtain it again.

## Related Quests

- **[Cleansing the Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones")**: Use the [Bend Will](/wiki/Skyrim:Bend_Will "Skyrim:Bend Will") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") to cleanse each of the remaining [All-Maker Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-place-Earth_Stone_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Earth_Stone_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Earth_Stone_02.jpg)

The cleansed Earth Stone

The stone shows the usual signs of construction, with wooden scaffolding on the southeast side, and [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), [shovels](/wiki/Skyrim:Shovel "Skyrim:Shovel"), hammers, and carts in the vicinity. Several [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") and [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plants grow around the stone. A path leads northeast to Raven Rock, which is nearby.

Before you cleanse the stone, if [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn_(quest) "Skyrim:Dragonborn (quest)") has not been completed, [Neloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth "Skyrim:Neloth") will speak to you as you approach. During the day, [Bralsa Drel](/wiki/Skyrim:Bralsa_Drel "Skyrim:Bralsa Drel"), [Rirns Llervu](/wiki/Skyrim:Rirns_Llervu "Skyrim:Rirns Llervu"), and three [Redoran guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran_Guard "Skyrim:Redoran Guard") will be working on and bowing to the stone. During the night, [Cindiri Arano](/wiki/Skyrim:Cindiri_Arano "Skyrim:Cindiri Arano"), [Fethis Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fethis_Alor "Skyrim:Fethis Alor"), [Garyn Ienth](/wiki/Skyrim:Garyn_Ienth "Skyrim:Garyn Ienth"), [Glover Mallory](/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory "Skyrim:Glover Mallory"), and [Milore Ienth](/wiki/Skyrim:Milore_Ienth "Skyrim:Milore Ienth") will also be found at the stone. When you cleanse the stone, a leveled [lurker](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker") will rise from the water around the base of the stone and attack you and the workers, followed by a second leveled lurker approaching from the coast to the west; the Redoran guards will aid in the fight. After the lurkers are defeated, the workers will express their thanks and return to Raven Rock.

## Notes

- The Earth Stone also appears in *[Bloodmoon](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Earth_Stone "Bloodmoon:Earth Stone")*, when it was northwest of Raven Rock, not southwest.
