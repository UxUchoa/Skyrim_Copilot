Bodyguard

The **Bodyguards** are two [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [citizens](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)") who sit on either side of [Pelagius the Suspicious](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_the_Suspicious "Skyrim:Pelagius the Suspicious") during the quest [The Mind of Madness](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness "Skyrim:The Mind of Madness"), observing the arena fight between two [atronachs](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra "Skyrim:Daedra"). They wear [Dwarven armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Armor "Skyrim:Dwarven Armor") with matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Boots "Skyrim:Dwarven Boots"), [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Dwarven Gauntlets") and a [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Helmet "Skyrim:Dwarven Helmet"). When you hit one of them with the [Wabbajack](/wiki/Skyrim:Wabbajack "Skyrim:Wabbajack"), they will both turn into [wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf") and kill Pelagius.
