**Silent Ruin** is a small [Dwarven ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Ruins "Skyrim:Dwarven Ruins") in [Blackreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackreach "Skyrim:Blackreach"). It is north of the Dwarven city. The building is devoid of any [creatures](/wiki/Skyrim:Creatures "Skyrim:Creatures") or [automatons](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Automatons "Skyrim:Dwarven Automatons"); however, you do need to keep an eye out for traps.

### Exterior

Outside the building against the northeast corner is a large brass pipe with a [crimson nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Nirnroot "Skyrim:Crimson Nirnroot") growing beside it. A path north of the building leads down to a [Falmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer "Skyrim:Falmer") camp with two tents and two Falmer. Four [dead salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") hang from a rack next to the tents, and there is a [geode vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Geode_Vein "Skyrim:Geode Vein") just northeast of the two tents.

### Silent Ruin

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-interior-Silent_Ruin.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Silent_Ruin.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Silent_Ruin.jpg)

Interior view

The entrance is on the southwest side of the building, and is unlocked. It leads into a single room, with a short flight of stairs leading down to the main floor. Two huge stone pillars to the northeast and southwest support a very high roof. Opposite the entrance is a throne on a slightly raised platform. On the seat of the throne is a copy of the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(book) "Skyrim:Warrior (book)")*; however, care is needed, as there is a [pressure plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Pressure_Plate "Skyrim:Pressure Plate") in front of the throne that triggers a [spinning blade trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Spinning_Blades "Skyrim:Spinning Blades"). To the right of the throne is a crimson nirnroot growing in a large metal urn. Finally, in the northwest corner, there is a set of Dwemer shelves against the west wall holding various small Dwemer items, and to the right, against the north wall, is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest").

## Bugs

- Followers will not follow you into the building, and instead will run north, possibly attempting to leave Blackreach to find you.
