# Skyrim:Skyrim (place)

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg/200px-MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg)](/wiki/File:MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg)

Skyrim

**Skyrim** is the northernmost province of [Tamriel](/wiki/Lore:Tamriel "Lore:Tamriel") and the main [worldspace](/wiki/Skyrim:Worldspaces "Skyrim:Worldspaces") in the game of *[Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim")*. It is a cold, mountainous region, and the home of the [Nords](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord"). The province is divided into nine [holds](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds"): [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch"), [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold"), [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar"), [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"), [The Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale"), [The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"), [The Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"), [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold"), and [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)").

For more information, see the [main lore article](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim").
