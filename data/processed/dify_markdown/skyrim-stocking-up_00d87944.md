## Quick Walkthrough

1. Supply specific fish to random innkeepers

## Detailed Walkthrough

This is a repetitive quest. [Swims-In-Deep-Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water "Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water") will ask you to supply certain types of fish to randomly chosen innkeepers ([Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim") being a possible destination). The quest can be completed four times.
