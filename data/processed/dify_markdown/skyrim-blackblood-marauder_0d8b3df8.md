# Skyrim:Blackblood Marauder

## Overview

The **Blackblood Marauders** are a group of bandits living in [Broken Oar Grotto](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Oar_Grotto "Skyrim:Broken Oar Grotto") who also appear at [the Wreck of the Icerunner](/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Icerunner "Skyrim:Wreck of the Icerunner") during [Lights Out!](/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out! "Skyrim:Lights Out!"). They are led by [Jaree-Ra](/wiki/Skyrim:Jaree-Ra "Skyrim:Jaree-Ra"), his sister [Deeja](/wiki/Skyrim:Deeja "Skyrim:Deeja"), and [Captain Hargar](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Hargar "Skyrim:Captain Hargar"). During the related quest, their leader, Jaree-Ra, arranged for the light in the [Solitude Lighthouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Lighthouse "Skyrim:Solitude Lighthouse") to be extinguished by you, causing The Icerunner to run aground near the west shore. They then boarded the ship under command by Deeja, massacred the crew, and stole all the cargo, which they shipped to their base of operations at Broken Oar Grotto to keep the loot safe. They can be easily recognized by their signature black tattoos, mimicking the blood vessels, on the sides of their necks and faces.

Blackblood Marauder (Bandit Wizard)

These two Marauders are both [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit wizards](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard "Skyrim:Bandit Wizard"), differing only in their appearance. They wear a leveled set of [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes"), which will be enchanted to either fortify [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Alteration "Skyrim:Fortify Alteration"), [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Destruction "Skyrim:Fortify Destruction"), or  [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Restoration "Skyrim:Fortify Restoration") or to [regenerate magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Regenerate_Magicka "Skyrim:Regenerate Magicka"). They are armed with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and may also carry a small amount of gold. Each can cast a leveled set of spells from the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration"), [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction"), and [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") schools, including one [armor spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor_(spells) "Skyrim:Armor (spells)"), one [ward spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Ward "Skyrim:Ward"), one [healing spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Health "Skyrim:Restore Health") and one elemental damage spell.

Blackblood Marauder (Bandit Archer)

This Marauder is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit archer](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Archer "Skyrim:Bandit Archer"). He wears [leather armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Armor "Skyrim:Leather Armor") with matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots") and defends himself with a leveled [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow") with twenty leveled [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows "Skyrim:Arrows"), both of which may be up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") in quality. The bow is also supplemented by a leveled [one-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:One-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:One-Handed Weapons"), which also may be up to elven in quality. Additionally, he may carry a few [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") or a small amount of gold.
