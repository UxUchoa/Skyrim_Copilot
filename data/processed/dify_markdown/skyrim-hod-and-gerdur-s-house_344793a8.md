Hod and Gerdur's House

**Hod and Gerdur's House** is a residence in the southern part of [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"), located behind the [Riverwood Trader](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood_Trader "Skyrim:Riverwood Trader") and across from [Faendal's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Faendal%27s_House "Skyrim:Faendal's House").

It is the home of local lumber mill owners [Gerdur](/wiki/Skyrim:Gerdur "Skyrim:Gerdur") and [Hod](/wiki/Skyrim:Hod "Skyrim:Hod"), and their son [Frodnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Frodnar "Skyrim:Frodnar"). It consists of one zone, *[Hod and Gerdur's House](#Hod_and_Gerdur.27s_House)*.

## Hod and Gerdur's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House_02.jpg)

Cooking and dining area

Hod and Gerdur's house is laid out in an L-shape, with the front door entering to an open cooking and dining area along one side of the "L", and a bar and the bedroom areas situated on the other. None of the areas are separated by walls or doors. To the left of the door as you come in is a dining table set with two loaves and one piece of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), some [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). North of the table, a respawning food barrel sits in the corner. A [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") sits atop another respawning food barrel to the left of the fireplace, which is directly across from the front door. The fireplace mantel holds three gold coins, while a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") sits at its base. A kettle containing four [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") rests on the floor to the right, next to a large table holding two more potatoes, a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulb, three servings of [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), some [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), three loaves of bread, three [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"). Above the table are some hanging ingredients, including dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), two garlic braids, and two portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"). Next to these hanging ingredients are two hanging racks, one of which holds two freshly caught [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") while the other holds two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). Two bottles of alto wine and three bottles of wine can be found on the bottom of a set of shelves to the right. A non-respawning cupboard across from the food table holds various kitchenware on its shelves, along with a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") and one sample of [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat") at the top.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.jpg)

Bar and bedroom area

The first bedroom area of the home starts with a single bed in the southwest corner, resting between a non-respawning dresser on one side and a non-respawning end table on the other. A respawning chest sits atop the dresser, along with a copy of *[Olaf and the Dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon "Skyrim:Olaf and the Dragon")*, and a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") rests on the end table. The next area of the home includes the bar, atop which sit one bottle each of wine and alto wine, four bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), two [wedges of goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), and some sliced goat cheese. Behind the bar is a respawning food barrel, as well as an empty [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem") on the bottom shelf. Across from the bar is a small round table with two bottles of Nord mead. The final bedroom area can be found past the bar, in the northwest part of the home. A double bed sits at the middle of the wall, flanked by a non-respawning end table on either side and with a non-respawning chest at the foot of the bed. The left end table holds a farm [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000330b3 "Skyrim:Hat"), a [potion of haggling](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling "Skyrim:Potion of Haggling"), and a copy of *[Of Fjori and Holgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir "Skyrim:Of Fjori and Holgeir")*, while the right one holds a basket and four gold coins. A pair of farm [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b "Skyrim:Boots") sits on the floor in the left corner, in front of a medium-sized [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). An [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe") is stashed beneath the bed on the left side. Along the left wall is a non-respawning wardrobe, atop which lie a [sabre cat pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Pelt") and a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic"), and along the right wall is a single respawning food barrel.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice-level lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Gerdur%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Gerdur's House") to which is carried by Frodnar, Gerdur, and Hod. You will obtain a copy of the key if you followed [Ralof](/wiki/Skyrim:Ralof "Skyrim:Ralof") during [Unbound](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound "Skyrim:Unbound").

## Bugs

- Stump may be stuck inside the house, with no way to get him out. **?**
- Frodnar's bed isn't a child's bed, causing him to either sleep in his parents' bed or stay up all night.
