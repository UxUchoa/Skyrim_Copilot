## Walkthrough

The shrine is south of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") on the summit of a tall mountain. The path leading to the shrine is found not far south of [Whistling Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Whistling_Mine "Skyrim:Whistling Mine"), and rises sharply to the west. To reach it, travel southeast along the path from Winterhold in the direction of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). If the weather is clear, or if you use the [Clear Skies](/wiki/Skyrim:Clear_Skies "Skyrim:Clear Skies") [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts"), it is possible to see a long way in all directions from the shrine.

The shrine sits atop a stone fort-like structure that was supposedly built by [Dunmer](/wiki/Lore:Dunmer "Lore:Dunmer"), who revere Azura. There is an altar in front of the massive statue; [Aranea Ienith](/wiki/Skyrim:Aranea_Ienith "Skyrim:Aranea Ienith") spends all her time worshipping before the altar when not [following](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") you. To the right of the altar on a level below is a small camp, which consists of a small tent containing an unowned bedroll and a collection of crates and sacks containing food.

## Notes

- [Faldrus](/wiki/Skyrim:Faldrus "Skyrim:Faldrus") can be seen in a [random encounter](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions "Skyrim:World Interactions") on a pilgrimage to the shrine. You can ask him where the shrine is, and he'll mark its location on your map. He will join Aranea Ienith in front of the statue if you follow him there and he survives his pilgrimage to the shrine. Otherwise, he will disappear from the game after you leave him.
- Some innkeepers, such as [Orgnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Orgnar "Skyrim:Orgnar"), will also mark the shrine's location on your map if you ask them for rumors.
