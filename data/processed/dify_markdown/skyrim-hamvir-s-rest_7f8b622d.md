Hamvirs Rest

**Hamvir's Rest** is a small outdoor graveyard and [Nordic ruin](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings "Skyrim:Nordic Dwellings") northwest of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") containing [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") and a [draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr").

## Walkthrough

The graveyard is just north of [Dustman's Cairn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dustman%27s_Cairn "Skyrim:Dustman's Cairn"). Three skeletons patrol the area, and a draugr sleeps in the upright sarcophagus. A master-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") is beside the sarcophagus, with a random enchanted [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet") behind the grates to its left. Eight [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") plants, two [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"), and two [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") plants grow among the tombstones and in the immediate area.

This is the final destination of the [Headless Horseman](/wiki/Skyrim:Headless_Horseman "Skyrim:Headless Horseman") on his nightly travels; once he reaches this area, he will disappear regardless of the time of day.

## Notes

- An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") is west-southwest of the ruin.
