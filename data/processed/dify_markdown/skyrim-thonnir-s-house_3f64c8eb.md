Thonnir's House

**Thonnir's House** is the residence of [Thonnir](/wiki/Skyrim:Thonnir "Skyrim:Thonnir") and his son [Virkmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Virkmund "Skyrim:Virkmund") in [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal").

They live in this simple, one-room house at the center of Morthal.

### Exterior

The house is to the north of the [Thaumaturgist's Hut](/wiki/Skyrim:Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut "Skyrim:Thaumaturgist's Hut"). There are two [deathbell](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell "Skyrim:Deathbell") bushes around the building. Two flights of stairs lead up to the decking at the front, which has a wooden bench against the building and a pile of firewood beside it. To the right are a couple of small wooden boat that have been dragged out of the water and overturned for storing. At the end of the jetty that runs in front of the house a small wooden boat has been moored.

### Thonnir's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-interior-Thonnir%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Thonnir%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thonnir%27s_House.jpg)

Thonnir's home

On your left when entering is a long wooden table holding two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), with a fish barrel to the left and a basket containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"). To the right of this is a set of shelves holding two more leeks, four [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), several pieces of cheese on top and hidden under basket on the bottom shelf is a copy of the [Pickpocket](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket "Skyrim:Pickpocket") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Aevar Stone-Singer](/wiki/Skyrim:Aevar_Stone-Singer "Skyrim:Aevar Stone-Singer")*. Above the shelves are two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, two bunches of [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and two bunches of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") all hanging from the ceiling. To the left of the shelves, hanging from the ceiling is a rack holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). In the southwestern corner is a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel and next to this against the west wall is a tall wardrobe and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot.

In the northwestern corner is a round wooden table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead"). Coming out from the north wall is an owned double bed, where Thonnir sleeps. There is a large fireplace opposite the door in the middle of the north wall. On the mantlepiece there is a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword") and in front of the hearth a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand. To the right of the fireplace, hidden behind a basket is a unique item identified as [Michaela's Flagon](/wiki/Skyrim:Michaela%27s_Flagon "Skyrim:Michaela's Flagon").

In the northeastern corner, against the east wall is a single owned bed where Virkmund sleeps. Next to the bed is a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") and a pair of [drums](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") are at the foot of the bed. In the southeastern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs holding a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") and three bottles of Nord mead.
