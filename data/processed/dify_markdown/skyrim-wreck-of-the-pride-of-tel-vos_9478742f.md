The Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos

The **Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos** is a [shipwreck](/wiki/Skyrim:Shipwrecks "Skyrim:Shipwrecks") on a small island east of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold").

It is home to a small band of [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") who have established a camp around the wreck.

## Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos

On the western side of the wreck is a small camp, with two [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") standing guard. There is a central campfire, with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it. To the west of the campfire are two lean-tos, with an unowned bedroll under each of them. To the right is a novice-locked chest, and to the left a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"). On the other side of the campfire is a rack holding five [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"). On the eastern side of the wreck is a small island, with a second campfire with some crates and barrels nearby.

The ship itself is split in two, with a flight of stairs on the eastern side of the large piece leading up to the cabin, where a bandit chief lives. There are several barrels and crates around the room, along with an unlocked chest with a [bear trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") set directly in front of it and an unowned bedroll on the floor to the right. Against the wall is a table, with a random [magicka potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka") and several [empty wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle") around it. There are stairs leading down to the partially flooded mid-deck, with several crates and a [Nordic barnacle](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle") cluster on the floor. The lowest deck can only be reached from outside where the ship's hull has separated. Here you can find a second Nordic barnacle cluster, and at the waterline you can find a waterlogged chest containing the quest item *[Lymdrenn Tenvanni's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Lymdrenn_Tenvanni%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Lymdrenn Tenvanni's Journal")*. There is nothing of interest on the southern part of the ship. A search of both sides of the island that the camps are on will locate some [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod "Skyrim:Grass Pod"), two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"), one [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein"), and one [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein").

## Notes

- During a random conversation with a [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") guard, the wreck is referred to as "The Eye of Talos". The map marker is still added correctly, however.
- The ship is named after [Tel Vos](/wiki/Lore:Tel_Vos "Lore:Tel Vos"), a [Telvanni](/wiki/Lore:House_Telvanni "Lore:House Telvanni") settlement in [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind").
- There is an island to the southeast of the ship that has two spiky grass, one mountain flower, a mammoth rib cage, and leveled animals. Also on this island is a log, and underneath this log's southern end is a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Chimarvamidium](/wiki/Skyrim:Chimarvamidium "Skyrim:Chimarvamidium")* and a leveled piece of enchanted armor.
