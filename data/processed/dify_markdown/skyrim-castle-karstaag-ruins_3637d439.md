Castle Karstaag Ruins

**Castle Karstaag Ruins** is an icy clearing outside [Castle Karstaag Caverns](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Karstaag_Caverns "Skyrim:Castle Karstaag Caverns").

It was once a large frozen keep, but time has ravaged the structure since the slaying of its master, the frost giant [Karstaag](/wiki/Skyrim:Karstaag "Skyrim:Karstaag"). It is now overrun by [rieklings](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling "Skyrim:Riekling").

## Castle Karstaag Ruins

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-interior-Castle_Karstaag_Ruins.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Castle_Karstaag_Ruins.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Castle_Karstaag_Ruins.jpg)

Rieklings

The usual path to the ruins is through Castle Karstaag Caverns. Upon exiting the cavern, a leveled [mounted riekling](/wiki/Skyrim:Mounted_Riekling "Skyrim:Mounted Riekling") is in front of you, and to your left on the north side of the clearing are two more rieklings near the throne holding the skeleton of Karstaag. To the south is a master-locked door leading to the rest of the island, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Karstaag_Key "Skyrim:Castle Karstaag Key") to which is carried by the mounted riekling. If you have already found [Karstaag's skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Karstaag%27s_Skull "Skyrim:Karstaag's Skull"), you can activate the throne, which will return the skull to the skeleton and summon Karstaag to rise as a ghostly frost giant.
