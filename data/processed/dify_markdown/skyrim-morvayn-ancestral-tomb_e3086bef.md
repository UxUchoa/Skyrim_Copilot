Morvayn Ancestral Tomb

The **Morvayn Ancestral Tomb** is a [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") ancestral tomb in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

It is northwest of the flight of stairs leading up to the [Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple "Skyrim:Temple"), opposite the entrance to [Morvayn Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Morvayn_Manor "Skyrim:Morvayn Manor").

It is where members of the Morvayn family of [House Redoran](/wiki/Lore:House_Redoran "Lore:House Redoran") are interred if they die on [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim"). Past members of the family include [Remas](/wiki/Morrowind:Remas_Morvayn "Morrowind:Remas Morvayn") and his wife [Brara Morvayn](/wiki/Morrowind:Mistress_Brara_Morvayn "Morrowind:Mistress Brara Morvayn"), House Redoran Councilors of Vvardenfell and the parents of [Lleril Morvayn](/wiki/Skyrim:Lleril_Morvayn "Skyrim:Lleril Morvayn"), the current Councilor of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock") and the formal ruler of Solstheim, as well as [Varel Morvayn](/wiki/Oblivion:Varel_Morvayn "Oblivion:Varel Morvayn"), a smith resident in [Anvil](/wiki/Oblivion:Anvil "Oblivion:Anvil").

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance is a [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plant and three food urns. To the right of the entrance are three more scathecraw plants, two more food urns, and a lit brazier. The entrance has an expert-level lock.

### Morvayn Ancestral Tomb

The entrance from outside leads into a corridor lined with four lit braziers which proceeds north to the tomb room. In the room, to the east is an alcove with a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Tomb_Key "Skyrim:Raven Rock Tomb Key")-locked door to the [Temple Ancestral Tomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_Ancestral_Tomb "Skyrim:Temple Ancestral Tomb"). The key can be obtained as part of the quest [Clean Sweep](/wiki/Skyrim:Clean_Sweep "Skyrim:Clean Sweep"). The room features a total of four ash piles littered with bones, with the central pile surrounded by steps and lit candles. Also in the room are nine ash yam urns, two benches, ten unusable barrels, and four more lit braziers around the central pile.
