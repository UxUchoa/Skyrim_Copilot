## Ayleid Ruins

:   **[Rielle](/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle "Skyrim:Rielle")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause "Skyrim:The Cause") — A medium-sized Ayleid ruin located beneath the Jerall Mountains, just east of the Pale Pass. It straddles the border between the Rift in Skyrim and County Bruma in Cyrodiil. In Skyrim, it is accessed from the north by way of Varlais Cavern, a cave entrance located to the south of Arcwind Point. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Rielle+%28CC%29)
