# Skyrim:Achievements

## Overview

**Achievements** (specifically **[Xbox 360](/wiki/Skyrim:Xbox_360 "Skyrim:Xbox 360") Achievements**, **Steam Achievements**, and **[PlayStation 3](/wiki/Skyrim:PlayStation_3 "Skyrim:PlayStation 3") Trophies**) are awards given to the player for accomplishing various milestones in the game. Fifty achievements and fifty-one trophies are available in the basic game. [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") adds five achievements and [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") and [Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") add ten achievements each.

On the Xbox 360, a total of one thousand Gamer Points are available in the base game, or 1,550 points with all add-ons installed; the "Points" column in the following table provides the Gamer Points associated with each specific achievement. On the PC, Steam achievements stand on their own and don't include points. In addition, use of [console commands](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") and [mods](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Mods "Skyrim Mod:Mods") on the PC version does not prevent the awarding of achievements. On the PS3, the Trophies are sorted into types: Bronze, Silver and Gold. Earning every trophy from the base game will unlock [a Platinum trophy](#Platinum_Trophy).

The [Special Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition "Skyrim:Special Edition") has a separate achievement list for Steam, Xbox One, and PS4, meaning they can be earned twice by playing both versions of the game. Note that the use of mods (including [Verified Creations](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Verified_Creations "Skyrim Mod:Verified Creations")) disables Special Edition achievement progress for both PC and console. Since the Special Edition comes with all three major add-ons, the achievement list contains all of the below add-on-related achievements by default (i.e. 75 achievements or 76 trophies). The use of [Creation Club Creations](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") does not disable achievements as they are official content, however the Creation Club content does not add any additional achievements.

Similar to the Special Edition, [Skyrim VR](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim_VR "Skyrim:Skyrim VR") also has a separate achievement list for Steam and PS4. The use of mods disables achievement progress on PC.

More detailed information on how to unlock each achievement can be obtained by clicking on the achievement's name.

## Missable Achievements

Most of the achievements can be obtained regardless of your previous game choices. However, a handful of achievements can be missed:

- [![SR-achievement-Oblivion Walker.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-achievement-Oblivion_Walker.png/30px-SR-achievement-Oblivion_Walker.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Oblivion_Walker.png) **[Oblivion Walker](/wiki/Skyrim:Oblivion_Walker "Skyrim:Oblivion Walker")** (30 pts/Silver) — Collect 15 Daedric [Artifacts](/wiki/Skyrim:Artifacts "Skyrim:Artifacts")
  - There are a total of sixteen available Daedric Artifacts that qualify for this achievement, five of which can be missed depending upon your choices during each associated [Daedric Quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Quests "Skyrim:Daedric Quests"). See [Oblivion Walker](/wiki/Skyrim:Oblivion_Walker "Skyrim:Oblivion Walker") for more details.
- [![SR-achievement-One with the Shadows.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/SR-achievement-One_with_the_Shadows.png/30px-SR-achievement-One_with_the_Shadows.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-One_with_the_Shadows.png) **[One with the Shadows](/wiki/Skyrim:One_with_the_Shadows "Skyrim:One with the Shadows")** (30 pts/Silver) — Returned the Thieves Guild to its former glory
  - Can be missed if Erikur dies before completing The Dainty Sload. After starting Bound Until Death, he is no longer essential and can be killed.
- [![SR-achievement-With Friends Like These....png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-achievement-With_Friends_Like_These....png/30px-SR-achievement-With_Friends_Like_These....png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-With_Friends_Like_These....png) **[With Friends Like These...](/wiki/Skyrim:With_Friends_Like_These...#Achievements "Skyrim:With Friends Like These...")** (10 pts/Bronze) — Join the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood")
- [![SR-achievement-Bound Until Death.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-achievement-Bound_Until_Death.png/30px-SR-achievement-Bound_Until_Death.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Bound_Until_Death.png) **[Bound Until Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Until_Death#Achievements "Skyrim:Bound Until Death")** (10 pts/Bronze) — Complete Bound Until Death
- [![SR-achievement-Hail Sithis!.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-achievement-Hail_Sithis%21.png/30px-SR-achievement-Hail_Sithis%21.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Hail_Sithis!.png) **[Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis!#Achievements "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!")** (30 pts/Silver) — Complete Hail Sithis!
  - All three Dark Brotherhood achievements become unavailable if you choose to [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](/wiki/Skyrim:Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood! "Skyrim:Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!")
- [![SR-achievement-War Hero.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-achievement-War_Hero.png/30px-SR-achievement-War_Hero.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-War_Hero.png) **[War Hero](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Hero "Skyrim:War Hero")** (10 pts/Bronze) — Capture [Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard "Skyrim:Fort Sungard") or [Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall "Skyrim:Fort Greenwall")
  - The associated quest ([The Battle for Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Sungard (Stormcloaks)") or [The Battle for Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Greenwall (Imperial)")) can be skipped depending upon the outcome of [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending").
- [![SR-achievement-Auriel's Bow.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-achievement-Auriel%27s_Bow.png/30px-SR-achievement-Auriel%27s_Bow.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Auriel%27s_Bow.png) **[Auriel's Bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Auriel%27s_Bow_(achievement) "Skyrim:Auriel's Bow (achievement)")** (20 pts/Bronze) — Use the special power of [Auriel's Bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Auriel%27s_Bow "Skyrim:Auriel's Bow")
  - [Auriel's Bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Auriel%27s_Bow "Skyrim:Auriel's Bow") is not marked as a quest item after completing [Kindred Judgement](/wiki/Skyrim:Kindred_Judgement "Skyrim:Kindred Judgement"), so this achievement can be missed if the bow is lost or despawns.
- [![SR-achievement-Werewolf Mastered.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-achievement-Werewolf_Mastered.png/30px-SR-achievement-Werewolf_Mastered.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Werewolf_Mastered.png) **[Werewolf Mastered](/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf_Mastered "Skyrim:Werewolf Mastered")** (20 pts/Bronze) — Acquire 11 [werewolf perks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lycanthropy#Skill_Perks "Skyrim:Lycanthropy")
  - After curing lycanthropy twice (either using a [Glenmoril Witch Head](/wiki/Skyrim:Glenmoril_Witch_Head "Skyrim:Glenmoril Witch Head") or by becoming a [Vampire Lord](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Lord "Skyrim:Vampire Lord")), the player will never be able to become a werewolf or acquire werewolf perks again.
- [![SR-achievement-Stalhrim Crafter.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-achievement-Stalhrim_Crafter.png/30px-SR-achievement-Stalhrim_Crafter.png)](/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Stalhrim_Crafter.png) **[Stalhrim Crafter](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Crafter "Skyrim:Stalhrim Crafter")** (20 pts/Silver) — Craft an item out of [Stalhrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim "Skyrim:Stalhrim")
  - The associated quest can be missed if [Deor Woodcutter](/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter "Skyrim:Deor Woodcutter") or [Fanari Strong-Voice](/wiki/Skyrim:Fanari_Strong-Voice "Skyrim:Fanari Strong-Voice") dies before it starts.

## Bugs

- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")[![On PlayStation](//images.uesp.net/6/64/Playstation.svg)](/wiki/Skyrim:PlayStation "Skyrim:PlayStation") Achievements can occasionally be missed by Steam and PS4, even though they have been completed in-game.
  - To fix this, reload an earlier save and re-complete the achievement.
  - Making sure that the "Enable the Steam Overlay while in-game" is enabled (from the Steam client's properties) before starting the game prevents this issue.
