Hlaalu Farm

**Hlaalu Farm** is a small farm east of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

This farm is owned by [Belyn Hlaalu](/wiki/Skyrim:Belyn_Hlaalu "Skyrim:Belyn Hlaalu") who lives in Windhelm, but is worked by [Adisla](/wiki/Skyrim:Adisla "Skyrim:Adisla"), a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") woman who lives in the single-room farmhouse on the property.

### Exterior

As you approach the property there are several [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") and a [snowberry bush](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"). Only six bushels of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") are grown, however the farm also supports two [goats](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") and three [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken"). Behind the house, there is a chicken coop, with a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"), a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone").

### Hlaalu Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-Hlaalu_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hlaalu_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hlaalu_Farm.jpg)

The interior of Hlaalu Farm

Upon entering the farmhouse, there is a cupboard against the northwest wall holding a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a [potion of true shot](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_True_Shot "Skyrim:Potion of True Shot"), an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), three [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), three bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), and two [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl"). There is a flower basket containing four [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") on the floor to the left of the cupboard, with a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel and basket containing a selection of [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") to the right. Against the northeast wall is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot beside a pile of firewood, and hanging above from the ceiling are two garlic braids, a bunch of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). Opposite the cupboard, against the southeast wall are a long wooden table and bench. On the table are two chicken's eggs, two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and several pieces of cheese. Hanging from a rack above the table are four [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), and two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)").

To the right of the table, opposite the entrance is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a bowl of [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat") and three [iron daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") on the mantelpiece. To the right on a small pile of firewood is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), and to the right of that is another cupboard holding mainly clutter along with copies of *[The Windhelm Letters](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Windhelm_Letters "Skyrim:The Windhelm Letters")*, *[Walking the World, Vol XI](/wiki/Skyrim:Walking_the_World,_Vol_XI "Skyrim:Walking the World, Vol XI")* ,*[The Holds of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Holds_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:The Holds of Skyrim")*, *[Of Crossed Daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Crossed_Daggers "Skyrim:Of Crossed Daggers")*, and *[The City of Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_City_of_Stone "Skyrim:The City of Stone")*. Hanging above this cupboard are three more bunches of dried elves ear and two more bunches of dried frost mirriam. Coming out from the middle of the southwest wall is an unowned single bed with an end table to the left which holds a bottle of alto wine and a bottle of wine. To the right of the bed is a dresser containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a [steel mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Steel Mace") on top. To the right of the dresser against the northeast wall are another unlocked chest and a round wooden table and two chairs, with a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, and a loaf of bread on top.
