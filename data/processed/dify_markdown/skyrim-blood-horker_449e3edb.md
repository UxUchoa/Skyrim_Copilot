# Skyrim:Blood Horker

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-place-Japhet%27s_Folly.jpg/200px-SR-place-Japhet%27s_Folly.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Japhet%27s_Folly.jpg)

[Japhet's Folly](/wiki/Skyrim:Japhet%27s_Folly "Skyrim:Japhet's Folly"), the secret fortress of the Blood Horkers

The **Blood Horkers** are a notorious group of pirates led by a powerful battlemage, [Haldyn](/wiki/Skyrim:Haldyn "Skyrim:Haldyn"), who prowl the waters of the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts"), raiding alongside the northern coastline of [Tamriel](/wiki/Lore:Tamriel "Lore:Tamriel"). The [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") has given the pirates the opportunity to increase their attacks without fear of serious retaliation from either the [Empire](/wiki/Lore:Empire "Lore:Empire") or [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). In fact, the only ships which sail the Sea of Ghosts unscathed are those owned by Clan Shatter-Shield, a *"very powerful family in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm")"*, due to an [agreement](/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron%27s_Logbook "Skyrim:Suvaris Atheron's Logbook") forged with Shatter-Shield gold between Blood Horker captain [Stig Salt-Plank](/wiki/Skyrim:Stig_Salt-Plank "Skyrim:Stig Salt-Plank") and [Suvaris Atheron](/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron "Skyrim:Suvaris Atheron"), the employee of the clan's patriarch, [Torbjorn Shatter-Shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield "Skyrim:Torbjorn Shatter-Shield"), and the overseer of their shipping operations. Through this agreement, the Shatter-Shields hold a monopoly on foreign shipping, as their competitors are dealt with by the pirates. Recently, the Blood Horkers have even been [interfering](/wiki/Skyrim:Rise_in_the_East "Skyrim:Rise in the East") in the operations of the powerful [East Empire Company](/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company "Lore:East Empire Company").

The Blood Horkers *"draw council"* in their island fortress, [Japhet's Folly](/wiki/Skyrim:Japhet%27s_Folly "Skyrim:Japhet's Folly"), the location of which is kept secret through discretion and Haldyn's magic, which he uses to create fog to hide the island.

The first five entries on this page are encountered in Japhet's Folly while the last two are part of the random encounter.
