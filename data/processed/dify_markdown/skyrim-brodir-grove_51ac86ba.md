Brodir Grove

**Brodir Grove** is a small circle of stones northeast of [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock") containing [reavers](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver "Skyrim:Reaver").

## Related Quests

- **[Kill the Bandit Leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Bandit_Leader "Skyrim:Kill the Bandit Leader")**: Do a favor by slaying a bandit leader. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[Pain in the Necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Pain_in_the_Necklace "Skyrim:Pain in the Necklace")**: Find [East Empire pendants](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Pendant "Skyrim:East Empire Pendant") for [Fethis Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fethis_Alor "Skyrim:Fethis Alor").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-place-Brodir_Grove_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Brodir_Grove_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Brodir_Grove_02.jpg)

The center of the camp

Three of the reavers will confront you as you approach; the unique greatsword [Stormfang](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormfang "Skyrim:Stormfang") is in the hands of a [reaver lord](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver_Lord "Skyrim:Reaver Lord"). The grove is centered around five large standing stones, with the reaver camp within the perimeter of the stones. There are ropes strung between four of the stones, with [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes") hanging from the ropes. To the right of the stone to the south is a lean-to shelter with a bedroll underneath. Two large crates and two [trama root](/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root "Skyrim:Trama Root") plants are behind the lean-to, and a [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plant is beside it. To the left of the lean-to are three barrels, three crates, and four sacks stacked together. Following the perimeter counterclockwise, there are more crates and barrels stacked between the first and second stones, with bone chimes between the stones, and stacks of even more barrels and crates around the second stone, with an adept-locked chest beside the stone. Continuing around, another stack of barrels and crates and more bone chimes are between the second and third stones, with two [bear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") on the ground between the stack and the third stone.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-place-Brodir_Grove_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Brodir_Grove_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Brodir_Grove_03.jpg)

The bone chimes at the grove

A post by the third stone has two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids and two [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") hanging from it. There are two crates, two barrels, and a pile of firewood around the third stone as well, with an expert-locked [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox") on one of the crates. More bone chimes hang between the third and fourth stones, and another crate and two barrels are by the fourth stone. In front of the fourth stone is an unlocked chest, and to the left of the stone is a [heart stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone "Skyrim:Heart Stone") deposit. No rope or bone chimes are between the fourth and fifth stones. There are two scathecraw plants near the fifth stone, and just outside the perimeter are two more heart stone deposits. By the fifth stone is yet another crate with three sacks stacked on top, a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and an unlocked boss chest with another bear trap in front of it. There is also no rope or bone chimes between the fifth and first stones; only a couple [ash yam](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam") plants are found here. On the back of the fifth stone is a set of three weapon racks, one of which holds a leveled battle axe. In the center of the camp is a large split log acting as a bench, with a firepit in front with a spit and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") hanging over it.

## Notes

- The name "Brodir Grove" probably comes from the Old Norse and Icelandic word *bróðir*, meaning *brother*. The letter *ð* is often anglicized as *d*.
- Two [gold ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") and two [emerald geodes](/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald_Geode "Skyrim:Emerald Geode") can be found west of the camp.
- Brodir Grove also [appears](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Brodir_Grove "Bloodmoon:Brodir Grove") in *[Bloodmoon](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon "Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon")*.

## Bugs

- Since the reaver lord respawns, you can obtain Stormfang multiple times.
