The Butcher

The **Butcher** is the [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") leader of [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern").

He wears a full set of heavy armor, excluding the helmet, which can be up to [steel plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Plate Armor") ([Nordic](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic "Skyrim:Nordic") if the [Dragonborn add-on](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") is installed) in quality. He also carries a shield, which can be up to [ebony](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony "Skyrim:Ebony") in quality, but doesn't use it unless you disarm him. The Butcher wields a two handed weapon which can be up to ebony quality, and carries a leveled amount of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold").
