Giant's Tooth

**Giant's Tooth** is a small island in the [Sea of Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_of_Ghosts "Skyrim:Sea of Ghosts"). It can only be reached by speaking to the [ferryman](/wiki/Skyrim:Ferryman "Skyrim:Ferryman") [Harlaug](/wiki/Skyrim:Harlaug "Skyrim:Harlaug") in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") during the quest [A Dying Wish](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dying_Wish "Skyrim:A Dying Wish").

The island consists of a small [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") and the entrance to [Iron Tusk Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Tusk_Cave "Skyrim:Iron Tusk Cave"). You will be sent here to retrieve an [Ironwood Fruit](/wiki/Skyrim:Ironwood_Fruit "Skyrim:Ironwood Fruit") during the related quest, although these six rare trees only grow in the conditions inside the cave.

The camp is populated by two [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and contains two dead [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth"). [Snow foxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fox "Skyrim:Fox") will also spawn around the camp and inside the cave. Upon approaching the Ironwood Fruit trees inside the cave, three [spriggans](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan") will awaken and attack you. Live [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit "Skyrim:Rabbit") can be found on the island's shoreline. When you exit the cave after retrieving the Ironwood Fruit, three [ice wraiths](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith") will appear and attack you. A *[Fisherman's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Fisherman%27s_Note "Skyrim:Fisherman's Note")* can be found on a chopping block in the camp.

## Notes

- Although this location has a [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") map marker, it cannot be fast traveled to from mainland [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim_(place) "Skyrim:Skyrim (place)").
- It is not possible to swim around the island or climb over the mountains to the other side. Using TCL shows that the backside of the island is unfinished in design.
- [Nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") can be found on the outskirts of the location by the western shoreline and inside the cave.
- An [Iron Ore](/wiki/Online:Iron_Ore "Online:Iron Ore") vein can be found on the north side of the cave entrance.
- Two [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") can be found in the raft right next to Harlaug's.
