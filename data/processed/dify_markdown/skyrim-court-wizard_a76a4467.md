# Skyrim:Court Wizard

## Overview

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-npc-Calcelmo.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Calcelmo.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Calcelmo.jpg)

Calcelmo, author and Court Wizard in Markarth

**Court Wizards** are the [jarls'](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") personal mages who research and study magic. They can usually be found in the jarl's [palace or longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Castles "Skyrim:Castles"). They sell a variety of [spells](/wiki/Skyrim:Spells "Skyrim:Spells") from all of the schools of magic, and other items that are of use to mages, including [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") and [scrolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Scrolls "Skyrim:Scrolls"). Their selection of available spells and magical items are bested only by the mages at the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)").

In court politics they are the equals of [housecarls](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl"), except on magical matters, but less important than [stewards](/wiki/Skyrim:Steward "Skyrim:Steward"). They are usually left alone by their jarl due to the [Nords](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord")' suspicion and distrust of all things magical. They are also the only members of court that do not have to relocate if their [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") changes sides in the [civil war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War").

## Notes

- The jarls of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"), [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") and [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") do not employ court wizards. The absence of a wizard in Winterhold is likely due to the close proximity of the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)") (and the locals' disdain for magic), but the reasoning for the other two is unclear.
- [Aicantar](/wiki/Skyrim:Aicantar "Skyrim:Aicantar"), [Melaran](/wiki/Skyrim:Melaran "Skyrim:Melaran"), and [Dravynea the Stoneweaver](/wiki/Skyrim:Dravynea_the_Stoneweaver "Skyrim:Dravynea the Stoneweaver") all belong to the court wizard faction. They are all intended as replacements for the incumbent court wizards, but Calcelmo and Wuunferth are essential, preventing Aicantar and Dravynea from being needed. Melaran is supposed to replace Sybille in the event of her death, and he will confirm this in conversation, but he will never adopt her routine or sell anything.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2, addresses this issue. Melaran will replace Stentor as intended.

## Bugs
