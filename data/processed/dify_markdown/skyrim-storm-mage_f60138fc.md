# Skyrim:Storm Mage

## Overview

**Storm Mages** are enemy spellcasters who specialize in the [Shock Damage](/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage "Skyrim:Shock Damage") branch of [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") magic. They are typically found in a few caves, keeps, and ruins across [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim") known as [warlock lairs](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock_Lairs "Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock Lairs"). Like other elemental mages ([fire mages](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Mage "Skyrim:Fire Mage") and [ice mages](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Mage "Skyrim:Ice Mage")), they belong to the [Fire/Frost/Shock Mage](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire/Frost/Shock_Mage "Skyrim:Fire/Frost/Shock Mage") [NPC](/wiki/Skyrim:NPCs "Skyrim:NPCs") class.

The type of storm mage you encounter depends on your level. You will continue to meet low-level storm mages even at high levels, but boss-level storm mages will always be at the highest possible level.

### Storm Mages

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/SR-npc-Storm_Mage_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Storm_Mage_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Storm_Mage_02.jpg)

A powerful Arch Electromancer fires a bolt of lightning

Storm mages rely on powerful shock-based spells in combat, and carry a leveled dagger in case of close-quarters fighting ([iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), [steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), [orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger "Skyrim:Orcish Dagger"), [dwarven](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Dagger "Skyrim:Dwarven Dagger"), [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger "Skyrim:Elven Dagger"), [glass](/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Dagger "Skyrim:Glass Dagger"), or an enchanted dagger of any quality from iron to [Daedric](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric "Skyrim:Daedric")). They also have a 10% chance to carry a leveled [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") or Destruction staff.

They wear a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000c5d12 "Skyrim:Clothing") along with [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes"), [hooded black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Hooded Black Mage Robes"), robes of [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Alteration "Skyrim:Fortify Alteration"), robes of [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Destruction "Skyrim:Fortify Destruction"), robes of [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Restoration "Skyrim:Fortify Restoration"), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Magicka_Rate "Skyrim:Fortify Magicka Rate").

In addition to the gear listed above, storm mages have a small chance to carry [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), [poisons](/wiki/Skyrim:Poisons "Skyrim:Poisons"), [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tomes "Skyrim:Spell Tomes"), [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](/wiki/Skyrim:Recipes "Skyrim:Recipes"), and a small amount of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). They also have [atronach forge recipes](/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach_Forge_Recipe "Skyrim:Atronach Forge Recipe") in their inventories, but their chance of appearing in the mages' inventories in-game is set at 0%.

On death, storm mages have a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls:

- **Alteration:** [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis "Skyrim:Scroll of Mass Paralysis")
- **Conjuration:** [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach "Skyrim:Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach"), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie "Skyrim:Scroll of Dread Zombie")
- **Destruction:**[Scroll of Blizzard](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Blizzard "Skyrim:Scroll of Blizzard"), [Scroll of Fire Storm](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fire_Storm "Skyrim:Scroll of Fire Storm")
- **Illusion:** [Scroll of Call to Arms](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms "Skyrim:Scroll of Call to Arms"), [Scroll of Harmony](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Harmony "Skyrim:Scroll of Harmony"), [Scroll of Hysteria](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Hysteria "Skyrim:Scroll of Hysteria"), [Scroll of Mayhem](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mayhem "Skyrim:Scroll of Mayhem")
- **Restoration:** [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead "Skyrim:Scroll of Bane of the Undead"), [Scroll of Guardian Circle](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle "Skyrim:Scroll of Guardian Circle")

The lowest level storm mages (Novice Storm Mages) do not have any miscellaneous items; they are equipped only with the boots, robes, and daggers listed above.

Electromancers and Arch Electromancers have the same gear as lower-level storm mages, but have a 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Chain Lightning](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Chain_Lightning "Skyrim:Staff of Chain Lightning") and do not have the leveled lists for atronach forge recipes.

## Dialogue

All members of the Warlock faction share a set of generic dialogue which they will use when in combat:

*"You cannot even conceive of the power I wield..."*

*"Fire, frost, or lightning - you will suffer at my hands!"*

*"Don't you see? I am master of the arcane!"*

*"Behold my power!"*

*"The elements are mine to command!"*

## Notes

- All the generic shock mages that are High Elves are listed twice in the leveled lists, which means shock mages are slightly more likely to be High Elves than one of the other races.
