The Sun Stone

The **Sun Stone** is an [All-Maker Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones") north-northwest of [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(settlement) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (settlement)").

Once [cleansed](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones"), this stone gives you the power to cause a [Sun Flare](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Flare "Skyrim:Sun Flare"), a fiery explosion that deals up to 100 points of fire damage around you, with greater damage done within this range to closer enemies. Once you use this power, it disappears from the Powers menu, and you must return to the Sun Stone to obtain it again.

## Related Quests

- **[Cleansing the Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones")**: Use the [Bend Will](/wiki/Skyrim:Bend_Will "Skyrim:Bend Will") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") to cleanse each of the remaining [All-Maker Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:All-Maker_Stones "Skyrim:All-Maker Stones").

## Walkthrough

The stone shows the usual signs of construction, with wooden scaffolding on the west side, and [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), [shovels](/wiki/Skyrim:Shovel "Skyrim:Shovel"), hammers, and carts in the vicinity. Several [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") plants and [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") fungi grow around the stone. A path leads south to Tel Mithryn.

Before you cleanse the stone, during the day, six [unique reavers](/wiki/Skyrim:Reaver#Temple_and_Pillar_Workers "Skyrim:Reaver") will be working on and bowing to the stone. During the night, [Drovas Relvi](/wiki/Skyrim:Drovas_Relvi "Skyrim:Drovas Relvi"), [Elynea Mothren](/wiki/Skyrim:Elynea_Mothren "Skyrim:Elynea Mothren"), and [Ulves Romoran](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulves_Romoran "Skyrim:Ulves Romoran") will also be found at the stone. When you cleanse the stone, a leveled [lurker](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker") will rise from the water around the base of the stone and attack you and the workers. After the lurker is defeated, the reavers will remain peaceful and be removed from the game once you leave the area, and the other workers will express their thanks and return to Tel Mithryn.

## Bugs

- If [Reluctant Steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Reluctant_Steward "Skyrim:Reluctant Steward") is finished and this Stone has been cleansed, [Drovas Relvi](/wiki/Skyrim:Drovas_Relvi "Skyrim:Drovas Relvi")'s sleep package will always be active, and therefore he will be found sleeping indefinitely in [Tel Mithryn Steward's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn Steward's House").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- If Reluctant Steward is finished and this Stone has been cleansed, Drovas Relvi's sleep package may still have him trying to sleep at the [Retching Netch](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Retching_Netch "Skyrim:The Retching Netch"), even though he won't work there anymore.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.3, fixes this bug.
