Riverside Shack

**Riverside Shack** is a small shack east of [Gallows Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock "Skyrim:Gallows Rock") and west-southwest of [Kynesgrove](/wiki/Skyrim:Kynesgrove "Skyrim:Kynesgrove"), along the eastern bank of the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River").

It is a derelict house, with holes in the roof and walls, and no front door or windows. It is home to a leveled [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals").

### Exterior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-place-Riverside_Shack_Fishing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riverside_Shack_Fishing.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverside_Shack_Fishing.jpg)

Fishing supplies

There is a small [vegetable](/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Vegetables "Skyrim:Food") garden to the right of the shack containing six [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and a hoe. Heading downstream from the shack are a couple of [Creep Cluster](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster") plants and several [Dragon's Tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") flowers. With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") creation, some [Fishing Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Supplies "Skyrim:Fishing Supplies") can be found outside, along with a fishing rod, an empty bucket and a knocked over chair.

### Riverside Shack

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/SR-place-Riverside_Shack_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riverside_Shack_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverside_Shack_02.jpg)

Riverside Shack

When first discovered, it becomes very clear what happened to the inhabitants, as a leveled hostile [animal](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") will jump out and attack you. Subsequent visits will normally find the animal outside the shack.

It is more a sort of lean-to, having only a front and side walls, and a roof built up against a rock face that is used as the rear wall. Half of the front wall is an opening with three [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") hanging from the eaves. At the rear, against the rock face is a campfire with a cooking spit with two metal pots hanging over it. These contain two pieces of [mudcrab chitin](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Chitin "Skyrim:Mudcrab Chitin") and a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"). By the fire is the gory remains of the owner, who at the time of the attack was apparently reading the book *[Pirate King of the Abecean](/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_King_of_the_Abecean "Skyrim:Pirate King of the Abecean")*, which you can find on the floor next to an overturned chair and a [fine hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Hat "Skyrim:Fine Hat"). To the right, also against the rock face, is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"). Against the southeast side wall is a tall wardrobe and a single unowned bed, with a pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots") under the foot of the bed. At the other end of the shack, against the northeast side wall is an apprentice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") which contains [Treasure Map III](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_III "Skyrim:Treasure Map III") (the map doesn't respawn when the chest does). There is a small set of shelves to the left holding some common [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"). Finally against the northwest wall, by the opening is a long wooden table with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a [fishing map for Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Map_-_Eastmarch "Skyrim:Fishing Map - Eastmarch")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing"), a copy of the [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Rislav The Righteous](/wiki/Skyrim:Rislav_The_Righteous "Skyrim:Rislav The Righteous")* and four common books on top and a couple of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") underneath.
