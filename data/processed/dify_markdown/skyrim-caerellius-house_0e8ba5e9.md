Caerellius House

**Caerellius House** is the home of [Crescius Caerellius](/wiki/Skyrim:Crescius_Caerellius "Skyrim:Crescius Caerellius") and [Aphia Velothi](/wiki/Skyrim:Aphia_Velothi "Skyrim:Aphia Velothi") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").

It is on the waterfront between [the Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bulwark "Skyrim:The Bulwark") to the southeast and [Morvayn Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Morvayn_Manor "Skyrim:Morvayn Manor") to the northwest, opposite the entrance to the [Ulen Ancestral Tomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb "Skyrim:Ulen Ancestral Tomb").

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance are three unusable barrels and an empty cart. To the right of the entrance are three crates, four unusable barrels, and three [scathecraw](/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw "Skyrim:Scathecraw") plants. Along the back of the building on the waterfront is an ash yam urn among more crates. The entrance has an apprentice-level lock.

### Caerellius House

The entrance leads into a cooking and living area on the lower floor of the house. Along the south wall is a table holding four [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), four [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"), two [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), three bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), two [goat cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"), two [eidar cheese wedges](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), and a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), with copies of *[History of Raven Rock, Vol. I](/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._I "Skyrim:History of Raven Rock, Vol. I")*, *[Vol. II](/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._II "Skyrim:History of Raven Rock, Vol. II")*, and *[Vol. III](/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._III "Skyrim:History of Raven Rock, Vol. III")* on the wall ledge behind the table. In the southwest corner is a chair and two meat barrels, one of which has a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on top. Along the west wall are three [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") hanging from a rack, a chair, four bottles of Nord mead on the wall ledge, three [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid hanging from the ceiling, and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") on a stand over a fire. In the northwest corner is an [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox") on the wall ledge. Along the north wall is a copy of *[The Reclamations](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reclamations "Skyrim:The Reclamations")* on the wall ledge and a doorway to the next room. In the northeast corner is a meat barrel and copies of *[Confessions of a Dunmer Skooma Eater](/wiki/Skyrim:Confessions_of_a_Dunmer_Skooma_Eater "Skyrim:Confessions of a Dunmer Skooma Eater")* and *[The House of Troubles](/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Troubles "Skyrim:The House of Troubles")* on the wall ledge.

The next room is circular, and features the stairs leading up to the upper floor. Along the curved west wall are three sacks, two meat barrels, and a set of shelves holding a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), four bottles of Nord mead, two bottles of wine, two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a sample of [Felsaad tern feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Felsaad_Tern_Feathers "Skyrim:Felsaad Tern Feathers"), and a cast iron pot containing three red apples. Along the north wall beneath the stairs are two chairs and a small round table holding two loaves of bread, and a wood pile.

The small circular room on the upper floor is the sleeping area. To the northwest at the top of the stairs is a sack and an end table holding two filled [lesser soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Lesser Soul Gem"), a large and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), and three loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). To the southwest is an owned double bed. To the southeast is a chest, a sack, and a set of shelves holding a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Healing"), and copies of *[Dunmer of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Dunmer of Skyrim")*, *[Pirate King of the Abecean](/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_King_of_the_Abecean "Skyrim:Pirate King of the Abecean")*, and *[The True Noble's Code](/wiki/Skyrim:The_True_Noble%27s_Code "Skyrim:The True Noble's Code")*.
