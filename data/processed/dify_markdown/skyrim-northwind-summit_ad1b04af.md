Northwind Summit

**Northwind Summit** is a mountaintop [dragon lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Lairs "Skyrim:Dragon Lairs") northwest of [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone"). Its [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") teaches part of the [Aura Whisper](/wiki/Skyrim:Aura_Whisper "Skyrim:Aura Whisper") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4b/SR-word_wall-Northwind_Summit.jpg/200px-SR-word_wall-Northwind_Summit.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-word_wall-Northwind_Summit.jpg)

Word wall

The lair is a short distance northwest of Shor's Stone, north-northwest of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), and far south of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), on the highest point in the Rift. To access the summit, you can travel through [Northwind Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwind_Mine "Skyrim:Northwind Mine"), on the northern side of the mountain, or climb to it, starting at Shor's Stone and heading just a little ways south down the path until you see a slightly gentler mountain slope to the right. The stairs to Northwind Mine are at the northeast end of the summit. The summit is an abandoned mammoth hunter's camp with three dilapidated structures and mammoth skeletons.

Before the [dragons' return](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising"), the word wall will be guarded by three [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton").

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon").

Regardless of whether [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") has been completed, the word wall is against the mountain, with the boss chest just in front. A [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter") is to the west, between two of the structures, and an adept-locked chest is inside the farthest structure to the west, just on the precipice of the summit.

## Notes

- A [moonstone ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein") is above the Northwind Mine entrance.

## Bugs

- If the dragon crash lands during your battle, the scripted terrain alterations may make it impossible to enter or leave the northwestern cabin (the one containing the locked chest), meaning that if you are inside, you will be unable to get out.
  - If you can find a way to kill the dragon while trapped in the cabin (for example, by using a follower or summoned creature), you will be able to exit via fast travel after leaving combat.
