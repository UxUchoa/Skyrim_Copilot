Skyrim is dotted with **shrines** dedicated to the [Divines](/wiki/Lore:Divines "Lore:Divines") (including [Talos](/wiki/Lore:Tiber_Septim "Lore:Tiber Septim")), which can be activated to receive a [blessing](/wiki/Skyrim:Blessings "Skyrim:Blessings") that will cure all [diseases](/wiki/Skyrim:Disease "Skyrim:Disease") and grant a blessing from the specific deity while removing any existing blessing.

Shrines are often accompanied by a large statue, although the shrines themselves are obelisks or sculptures small enough to fit on tabletops.

They are found in the temples within various cities, inside some dungeons, and in [unmarked locations](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places#Shrines "Skyrim:Unmarked Places") throughout the wilderness. [Daedric Shrines](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Shrines "Skyrim:Daedric Shrines") are listed separately. There is a shrine to each of the [Aedra](/wiki/Lore:Aedra "Lore:Aedra") in the [Temple of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Temple of the Divines") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") (except a shrine of Talos, which later becomes available if Solitude is taken by [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") forces).

## Shrine of Akatosh

[![SR-icon-construction-Shrine of Akatosh.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-icon-construction-Shrine_of_Akatosh.png/200px-SR-icon-construction-Shrine_of_Akatosh.png)](/wiki/File:SR-icon-construction-Shrine_of_Akatosh.png)

A shrine of Akatosh grants:

[Blessing of Akatosh](/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Akatosh "Skyrim:Blessing of Akatosh"): *Magicka regenerates 10% faster.*

- [Regenerate Magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Regenerate_Magicka "Skyrim:Regenerate Magicka"), 10 pts for 8 hours
- [Cure Disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Cure Disease")

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=View+all+on+map&search=Shrine+of+Akatosh)

### Rorikstead

[![SR-place-Shrine of Akatosh Rorikstead.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Rorikstead.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Rorikstead.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Rorikstead.jpg)

This shrine is atop the hill directly north of [Frostfruit Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostfruit_Inn "Skyrim:Frostfruit Inn") in [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead").

**Offerings**:

- [Blue Dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Dartwing "Skyrim:Blue Dartwing")
- [Dragon Scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Scales "Skyrim:Dragon Scales")
- *[Kolb & the Dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolb_%26_the_Dragon "Skyrim:Kolb & the Dragon")*

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-84223&y=15343)

### Twilight Valley

[![SR-place-Shrine of Akatosh Twilight Valley.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Twilight_Valley.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Twilight_Valley.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Twilight_Valley.jpg)

This shrine is placed inside of a tower atop a hill northeast of [Twilight Sepulcher](/wiki/Skyrim:Twilight_Sepulcher "Skyrim:Twilight Sepulcher") in [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") Hold.

**Offerings**:

- *[Olaf and the Dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon "Skyrim:Olaf and the Dragon")*
- [Potion of Enhanced Stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Enhanced Stamina")
- Two [Dragon's Tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") samples

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-66608&y=-77872)

### Steamcrag Hillock

[![SR-place-Shrine of Akatosh Steamcrag Hillock.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Steamcrag_Hillock.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Steamcrag_Hillock.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Steamcrag_Hillock.jpg)

This shrine is on a hill northeast of the [Atronach Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Atronach_Stone "Skyrim:The Atronach Stone") and south and slightly east of [Steamcrag Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Steamcrag_Camp "Skyrim:Steamcrag Camp"). The shrine is in the middle of some stone pillars, with several bones and entire skeletons scattered across the area. [Gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") and [corundum](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum "Skyrim:Corundum") ore veins are nearby.

**Offerings**:

- *[Breathing Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Breathing_Water "Skyrim:Breathing Water")*
- [Iron War Axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe")
- [Skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Skull "Skyrim:Skull")
- [Troll Skull](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull "Skyrim:Troll Skull")

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=147317&y=-22145)

### Fort Sungard Shrine

This shrine is within the [Fort Sungard Shrine](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard_Shrine "Skyrim:Fort Sungard Shrine") at [Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard "Skyrim:Fort Sungard"), southeast of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead"). Only present if the fort is occupied by the Legion. By default, it is a shrine of Kynareth, and is a shrine of Talos if occupied by Stormcloaks.

### Falkreath Hall of the Dead

[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Falkreath) 02.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath)_02.jpg)

The [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Falkreath)") has a total of three Shrines of Arkay, appropriate for Falkreath's focus on the grave. Two are placed in front of the Hall of the Dead, and the final one is inside the Hall. None of the shrines have any offerings.

### Markarth Hall of the Dead

[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Markarth).jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).jpg)

This shrine is in a small rest area inside the catacombs of the [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Markarth)"). No offerings are placed next to the shrine.

### Riften Hall of the Dead

[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Riften) 02.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Riften%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Riften%29_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Riften)_02.jpg)

The [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") Arkay shrine is placed in the [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Riften) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Riften)"), beneath the [Temple of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Mara "Skyrim:Temple of Mara"). The shrine itself is placed next to the body preparation area.

**Offerings**:

- [Gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold")
- Two [Blue Mountain Flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower")
- Two [Purple Mountain Flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower")

### Solitude Hall of the Dead

[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Solitude).jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude).jpg)

### Whiterun Hall of the Dead

[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Whiterun) 04.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Whiterun%29_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Whiterun%29_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Whiterun)_04.jpg)

### Windhelm Hall of the Dead

[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead Windhelm.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_Windhelm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_Windhelm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_Windhelm.jpg)

[Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm")'s [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Windhelm) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Windhelm)") keeps a shrine of Arkay in the heart of its crypt. A [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl"), [candlestick](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlestick "Skyrim:Candlestick"), and [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") have been placed next to the shrine.

### Druadach Redoubt

[Druadach Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Redoubt "Skyrim:Druadach Redoubt") has a shrine to Arkay on a ledge by the ramps, halfway along the northeastern wall. Two lit candles are on either side of the shrine. The shrine can only be activated by clipping out of bounds.

## Shrine of Auriel DG

- [Fortify Marksman](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Marksman "Skyrim:Fortify Marksman"), 10 pts for 12 hours
- [Cure Disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Cure Disease")

### Darkfall Cave

[![SR-interior-Darkfall Cave Auriel Shrine.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/SR-interior-Darkfall_Cave_Auriel_Shrine.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Darkfall_Cave_Auriel_Shrine.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Darkfall_Cave_Auriel_Shrine.jpg)

### Inner Sanctum

[![SR-place-Inner Sanctum Auriel Shrine.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/SR-place-Inner_Sanctum_Auriel_Shrine.jpg/200px-SR-place-Inner_Sanctum_Auriel_Shrine.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Inner_Sanctum_Auriel_Shrine.jpg)

### Stendarr's Beacon

[![SR-place-Stendarr's Beacon 02.jpg](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-place-Stendarr%27s_Beacon_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Stendarr%27s_Beacon_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Stendarr%27s_Beacon_02.jpg)

[Stendarr's Beacon](/wiki/Skyrim:Stendarr%27s_Beacon "Skyrim:Stendarr's Beacon") is an active site of worship held by the [Vigil of Stendarr](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Vigil of Stendarr"). The Vigil maintain spartan quarters within the tower, and keep their shrine of Stendarr in a sleeping area. The shrine is placed upon a stone altar with lit candles and sconces flanking it.

### Hall of the Vigilant

A *[Letter to Keeper Carcette](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_to_Keeper_Carcette "Skyrim:Letter to Keeper Carcette")* has been left here. This shrine will disappear after the quest [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(quest) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (quest)") is started, as the Hall will be destroyed.

The [Temple of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Talos "Skyrim:Temple of Talos") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") keeps its own shrine of Talos, in front of multiple pews for religious services. The active priests of this temple leave no offerings at the shrine itself.

A [steel battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Steel Battleaxe") and a copy of *[The Importance of Where](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Importance_of_Where "Skyrim:The Importance of Where")* is here.

## Notes

- The description of the Blessing of Mara is misleading; the actual effect is that Restoration spells cost 10% less magicka to cast.
- When [Creation Club](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club")'s [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode") is active, shrines outside of the player's home will cost gold to remove diseases.
