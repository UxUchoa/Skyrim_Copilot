Miner's House

The **Miner's House** is a single-room cottage next to [Soljund's Sinkhole](/wiki/Skyrim:Soljund%27s_Sinkhole "Skyrim:Soljund's Sinkhole").

It is the home of [Tuthul](/wiki/Skyrim:Tuthul "Skyrim:Tuthul") and [Perth](/wiki/Skyrim:Perth "Skyrim:Perth"), the miners at Soljund's Sinkhole.

## Miner's House

The entrance is novice-locked most of the time. Upon entering there is a cupboard against the south wall holding a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a [potion of true shot](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_True_Shot "Skyrim:Potion of True Shot"), an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"), four loose [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow") and two [sabre cat pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Pelt"), with a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel to the right. Against the west wall is a long wooden table with two [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on top. To the right, in the northwest corner is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), with two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, a bunch of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging from the ceiling above. While against the north wall is another long wooden table with two bottles of alto wine, a bottle of wine, a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), a [pheasant breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"), a few loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and several pieces of cheese. Hanging from racks above the table are four [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), four [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), and two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"). There is a fireplace opposite the entrance with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") leaning against a pile of firewood and an unlocked chest to the right of the fireplace. There are two single owned beds coming out from the east wall and an end table between them with a bottle of alto wine and a bottle of wine on top. At the foot of the right-hand bed, against the south wall is a small round wooden table and a chair, with two bottles of alto wine and a loaf of bread on top of the table.
