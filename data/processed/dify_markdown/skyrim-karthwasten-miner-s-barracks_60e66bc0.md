Karthwasten Miner's Barracks

**Karthwasten Miner's Barracks** is the collective housing area for local miners in [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten"), located between [Enmon's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon%27s_House "Skyrim:Enmon's House") and [Fenn's Gulch Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Fenn%27s_Gulch_Mine "Skyrim:Fenn's Gulch Mine") in the northwestern part of town.

Local miners [Belchimac](/wiki/Skyrim:Belchimac "Skyrim:Belchimac"), [Lash gra-Dushnikh](/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh "Skyrim:Lash gra-Dushnikh"), and [Ragnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragnar "Skyrim:Ragnar") reside here. It consists of one zone, *[Miner's Barracks](#Miner.27s_Barracks).*

## Miner's Barracks

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/SR-interior-Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg)

Miner's Barracks interior

This housing area is well stocked and set up for the local miners, with all the required supplies and amenities in the small space. On the south side of the building is a single bed along the east wall, owned by [Lash gra-Dushnikh](/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh "Skyrim:Lash gra-Dushnikh"). A respawning food barrel sits in the southeast corner, next to a non-respawning cupboard holding various kitchenwares. The west wall hosts a dining table which holds two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and a bucket containing three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). Above the table are some hanging ingredients, including some dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), two portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids. Next to the hanging ingredients is a hanging rack with three freshly caught [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and three [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). A respawning food barrel sits on the floor to the right, next to a set of shelves holding one bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and two bottles of alto wine on top, a copy of the book *[Songs of the Return, Vol 19](/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_the_Return,_Vol_19 "Skyrim:Songs of the Return, Vol 19")* on the middle shelf, and two bottles of alto wine and three bottles of wine on the bottom shelf.

The fireplace is situated farther to the right on the western wall, with a copy of *[Olaf and the Dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon "Skyrim:Olaf and the Dragon")* sitting on the right side of its base. A small table in front of the fireplace holds one bottle each of wine and alto wine, along with one loaf and one piece of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). On the north side of the room, a non-respawning end table sits at the center, between [Ragnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragnar "Skyrim:Ragnar")'s single bed on the left, and [Belchimac](/wiki/Skyrim:Belchimac "Skyrim:Belchimac")'s single bed on the right. At the foot of each bed is a small non-respawning chest, and the end table holds a single bottle of Nord mead. Finally, on the eastern wall, just north of the front door, a respawning food barrel sits next to a non-respawning dresser, which holds three bottles of Nord mead and two [pickaxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"). Above the dresser are three hanging garlic braids, some dried elves ear, and some dried frost mirriam.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Karthwasten_Miners%27_Barracks "Skyrim:Key to Karthwasten Miners' Barracks") to which is carried by Belchimac, Lash gra-Dushnikh, and Ragnar.
- A [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Quarried_Stone "Skyrim:Quarried Stone")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") can be found behind the building, and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") can be found on the front porch, with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") lying nearby.
