Brood Cavern

**Brood Cavern** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") southwest of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") containing leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals"). It contains only one zone, *[Brood Cavern](#Brood_Cavern)*.

### Exterior

A stream flows out of the cave, forming a waterfall over a small cliff in front of the entrance. A [dead Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_(dead) "Skyrim:Khajiit (dead)") can be found at the base of the waterfall, with a troll skull and a humanoid skull nearby. An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") is just east of the entrance.

### Brood Cavern

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-place-Brood_Cavern_Fishing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Brood_Cavern_Fishing.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Brood_Cavern_Fishing.jpg)

Fishing supplies

The partially flooded cavern features two waterfalls flowing down the cave walls, with a stream leading back out the entrance. Many scenic plants grow in the cavern, but only three [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") fungus and a few [hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss") are harvestable. The cavern contains three leveled animals or other creatures, which may include [cave bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Cave_Bear "Skyrim:Cave Bear") or [trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll "Skyrim:Troll"). As you enter, on the left is the corpse of a female [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [hunter](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter") near a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), with a random [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison"), a random [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow"), and some random [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows "Skyrim:Arrows") next to them. A copy of the [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Black Arrow, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Arrow,_v2 "Skyrim:The Black Arrow, v2")* can be found on the corpse. A dead [deer](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer "Skyrim:Deer") is on one of the islands of dry land. A novice-locked chest is in one of the short side tunnels off the main cavern, behind the waterfall on the right wall. A [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") is just to the left of the waterfall at the rear of the room, and an iron ore vein is on the south wall.

With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, some [Fishing Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Supplies "Skyrim:Fishing Supplies") can be found here; fish from [Underground](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_(activity)#Biomes "Skyrim:Fishing (activity)") waters can be found here.

## Notes

- If you return to the cave after initially clearing it, the dead hunter, poison, bow, and arrows may not respawn. Technically the dead hunter does respawn, if enter console command `prid 2ffec` then enter `inv`, you will notice her inventory updates as usual, but the body doesn't show.
- According to the [Creation Kit](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit "Skyrim Mod:Creation Kit"), the dead hunter is named **Urwa**.
