Bodil

**Bodil** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)") who works under [Beitild](/wiki/Skyrim:Beitild "Skyrim:Beitild") at [Iron-Breaker Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron-Breaker_Mine "Skyrim:Iron-Breaker Mine") in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

Like her colleague, [Gjak](/wiki/Skyrim:Gjak "Skyrim:Gjak"), she never surfaces from the mine and spends her entire life working, relaxing, or eating.

She wears a pair of [ragged trousers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers "Skyrim:Ragged Trousers") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#00080699 "Skyrim:Clothing"). She wields a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") and carries [lower-class loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

When approached she can say:

:   *"Here to dig. That's all."*
:   *"Dawnstar's got its problems, but as long as the ore is good, I'll stay."*

## Notes

- Bodil actually owns a bedroll located in the mine. However, due to her not having any kind of sleep package set up, she will never been seen using it.
