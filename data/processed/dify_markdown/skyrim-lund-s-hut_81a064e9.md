Lund's Hut

**Lund's Hut** is a small [shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") northwest of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead") containing [skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever").

## Related Quests

- **[When the Cat's Away](/wiki/Skyrim:When_the_Cat%27s_Away "Skyrim:When the Cat's Away")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Alternative_Armors_-_Silver "Skyrim:Alternative Armors - Silver"): Find a stash of [silver](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver "Skyrim:Silver") armor.

### Exterior

To reach the building, take the road northwest out of Rorikstead and follow it until you come to a dirt path on the left leading to the shack. It is a single-level, fairly rundown building with a fenced-off porch area at the front. There are four skeevers using a couple moldy straw bales for bedding outside the building. Northwest of the shack a few paces is a cairn with a lit candle and a [silver ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ring "Skyrim:Silver Ring") (which may have fallen to the ground) on the edge and a basket containing three [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") on the ground. To the left of the building is a tree with two [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") by the trunk. There are several [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle") and more blue mountain flowers lining the path leading from the road to the shack.

### Lund's Hut

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/SR-interior-Lund%27s_Hut.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Lund%27s_Hut.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lund%27s_Hut.jpg)

A skeever infestation

The building is unlocked and contains two more skeevers. The corpse of the original owner, [Lund](/wiki/Skyrim:Lund "Skyrim:Lund"), is in a single unusable bed in the eastern corner of this one-room structure. The place is a mess: [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), furniture, and [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") are scattered around the room. There is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot at the foot of his bed (in Anniversary Edition this chest requires a key and cannot be picked; the key is obtained by completing the quest [When the Cat's Away](/wiki/Skyrim:When_the_Cat%27s_Away "Skyrim:When the Cat's Away")), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") in a cast iron pot under the bed, and a [blue dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Dartwing "Skyrim:Blue Dartwing") right in the corner between the bed and the wall, under the basket or caught in the spider web above the basket. Beside the bed, against the southeast wall is an end table with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") on top. Along the rest of the southeast wall are a sack and barrel of foodstuffs, with a barrel of [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") behind the first. In front of these is a low table with a mead barrel and two bottles of [Honningbrew mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Mead "Skyrim:Honningbrew Mead") on top. To the right of the barrels, against the southwest wall is a long wooden table, under which are two [pheasant breasts](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"). On the table are a bottle of [poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Poison "Skyrim:Poison"), a bottle of alto wine, and several red apples.

To the right of the table are a wooden bench and a couple of chairs lying on their backs and various items of footwear and clothes underneath. To the right is a fireplace that is still lit, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") and spit in front and a mounted skeever head above. On the mantelpiece is a [blue butterfly wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly_Wing "Skyrim:Blue Butterfly Wing"), and in front of the hearth are three wooden bowls; two contain pheasant breasts and the other contains a [chicken breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken_Breast "Skyrim:Chicken Breast"). In the western corner is another long wooden table and bench with a patch of [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool") fungus growing beside it, and in the corner behind it are two more blue dartwings.

In the northern corner is an open crate containing a [butterfly wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly_Wing "Skyrim:Butterfly Wing"), a sample of [giant lichen](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Lichen "Skyrim:Giant Lichen"), a joint of [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef"), and various items of clothing, with a second butterfly wing between the crate and the wall. Along the northeast wall is a set of shelves with three common books, with more on the floor in front. Between the shelves and the entrance is another wooden bench, with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks") on top and a wall shelf above holding a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"). Hanging from the ceiling above are a [garlic braid](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid") and a bunch of [dried frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Dried Frost Mirriam").
