Coldhearted Gravedigger

The **Coldhearted Gravedigger** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Warrior_(class) "Skyrim:Warrior (class)") who may appear in the [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Windhelm) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Windhelm)") in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever "Skyrim:The Dark Brotherhood Forever"). She is one of ten possible targets.

The Gravedigger wears farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes") along with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots") and a pair of [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1 "Skyrim:Gloves"). She wields an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") in combat.
