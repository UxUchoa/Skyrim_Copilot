Bolgeir Bearclaw

**Bolgeir Bearclaw** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") and [housecarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl") to [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") [Elisif the Fair](/wiki/Skyrim:Elisif_the_Fair "Skyrim:Elisif the Fair") of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), never leaving her side when she holds court in the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace").

As housecarl for the newly appointed Jarl Elisif, Bolgeir spends the majority of his life looking after her. He sleeps in the northeastern room on the ground floor every night from 2am until 6am, when Jarl Elisif enters the throne room. He then spends the entire day by her side and only allows himself a quick one-hour meal at 6pm. When the court ends at 10pm, Bolgeir takes the opportunity to relax and just wanders around near the throne room until he heads back downstairs and goes to bed.

He carries three weapons, all crafted in [Orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish "Skyrim:Orcish") quality, including a [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger "Skyrim:Orcish Dagger") and two [swords](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Sword "Skyrim:Orcish Sword"). He wears a set of [steel plate armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Plate Armor") with matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Boots "Skyrim:Steel Plate Boots") and [gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Plate Gauntlets"). He only carries a spare [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic").

## Dialogue

When approached he can say:

:   *"Please address all questions to the Steward, [Falk Firebeard](/wiki/Skyrim:Falk_Firebeard "Skyrim:Falk Firebeard")."*
:   *"My duty is to keep the Jarl safe. Not play nursemaid to you sycophants."*
:   *"Keep your weapon out of your hands, and we'll get along just fine."*
:   *"The Jarl has advised me not to talk about which court attendants I'd like to punch in the face. More's the pity."*

## Conversations

Even if he prefers to keep quiet, there are times when Bolgeir has to protest Jarl Elisif's rushed decisions:

:   **Elisif**: *"Steward, I have an idea that might help to bolster the morale of our people in this difficult time. I think we shall have a grand parade, and let [General Tullius](/wiki/Skyrim:General_Tullius "Skyrim:General Tullius") march his troops from the Blue Palace to [Castle Dour](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour "Skyrim:Castle Dour") in all their finery."*
:   **Falk**: *"What a... fine idea, my Jarl. However, I would suggest we wait until the [war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") has ended. After all, the General needs his soldiers out in the field, fighting battles against the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks")."*
:   **Bolgeir**: *"I must protest, my Jarl. A parade would make you an easy target for a Stormcloak assassin. You're safer here in the palace."*
:   **Elisif**: *"Oh, very well. I can see there's no changing your stubborn Nord minds. But when this war is won, I will have my parade!"*
:   **Falk**: *"Yes, my Jarl."*

:   **Bolgeir**: *"We lost another five guardsmen, my Jarl. If General Tullius continues to conscript our men and send them off to battle, we'll have none left to protect the city."*
:   **Elisif**: *"Yes, I know. The general acted with my blessing. It is vital that we bring [Ulfric](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak") to justice, but that cannot happen until his army of Stormcloaks is defeated utterly."*
:   **Bolgeir**: *"Told you that, did he? I'm sworn to protect you, my Jarl, but I'm the last line of defense. The first line is the walls around this city and the men who patrol them. Men who, I might add, we're precious short on."*
:   **Elisif**: *"I'm aware of that, Bolgeir, and I appreciate your dedication. However, if we lose the war, the guardsmen of Solitude will make little difference in the end."*
:   **Bolgeir**: *"No disrespect toward the general, but sometimes I wonder if he's got your best interests at heart. In any case he won't be turnin' me into no Imperial soldier. I know my place, and it's at your side."*
:   **Elisif**: *"It comforts me to know that, Bolgeir."*

## Notes

- Unusually in the game, when he speaks, it says "Bearclaw" in the subtitles, not "Bolgeir". In almost every other instance the speaker is denoted by either their first or full name.
- You will receive a [*Letter from Bolgeir Bearclaw*](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Bolgeir_Bearclaw "Skyrim:Letter from Bolgeir Bearclaw") as part of the [Bone Wolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Wolf "Skyrim:Bone Wolf") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"), which will begin the quest [Let Sleeping Wolves Lie](/wiki/Skyrim:Let_Sleeping_Wolves_Lie "Skyrim:Let Sleeping Wolves Lie").
