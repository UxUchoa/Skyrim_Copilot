Dragon Bridge Lumber Camp

**Dragon Bridge Lumber Camp** is a sawmill and lumber camp in the southwestern part of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge").

It consists of one zone, *[Dragon Bridge Lumber Camp](#Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp).*

### Dragon Bridge Lumber Camp

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp.jpg)

Ground floor

The building consists of a ground floor and a basement. On the ground floor, a double bed owned by [Lodvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Lodvar "Skyrim:Lodvar") can be found on the east wall, flanked by a non-respawning unlocked chest containing leveled items on the left and a small table on the right. The table holds two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and five loose gold coins. Another bottle of alto wine lies on its side near the foot of the bed. A set of shelves on the north wall holds a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and three bottles of alto wine. A respawning food barrel sits in the southwest corner, and at the center of the room is a large dining table holding two [goat cheese wedges](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), one [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a bottle of alto wine, a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), and two pieces of [salmon meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat "Skyrim:Salmon Meat"). Beneath the table are two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp_02.jpg)

Basement

In the basement, a respawning food barrel can be found beneath the stairs, along with another on the north wall nearby. A small table holds a goat cheese wedge, a loaf of bread, a pine thrush egg, and a bottle of wine. Between the barrel and table, a hanging rack above holds four freshly caught [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). Across from the table, on the south wall, four non-respawning sacks lie on the floor. Next to the sacks is a non-respawning dresser with a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") and an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") sitting on top, a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") on the floor to the right, and a large respawning sack to the left. Another respawning food barrel can be found in the southeast corner, next to a set of shelves holding [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") on the top shelf, a [common soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem"), five gold coins, and a [saw](/wiki/Skyrim:Saw "Skyrim:Saw") on the middle shelf, and two bottles each of wine and alto wine on the bottom shelf. To the left of these shelves is a non-respawning cupboard which holds copies of the books *[The Holds of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Holds_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:The Holds of Skyrim")*, *[Of Crossed Daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Crossed_Daggers "Skyrim:Of Crossed Daggers")*, *[Herbane's Bestiary: Ice Wraiths](/wiki/Skyrim:Herbane%27s_Bestiary:_Ice_Wraiths "Skyrim:Herbane's Bestiary: Ice Wraiths")*, and *[Alduin is Real](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin_is_Real "Skyrim:Alduin is Real")*, along with two loaves of bread, a bottle of wine, and a bottle of alto wine. A single bed, also owned by Lodvar, is situated with the head along the north wall. At the foot of the bed is a pair of miner [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"), and a small non-respawning unlocked chest containing leveled items sits to the left side. Above the bed are some hanging ingredients, including two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, one dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), and two portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), as well as a hanging rack holding three freshly caught [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)").

## Notes

- The front door has an apprentice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Lumber_Camp_Key "Skyrim:Lumber Camp Key") to which is carried by [Horgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Horgeir "Skyrim:Horgeir").
- [Sawn logs](/wiki/Skyrim:Sawn_Log "Skyrim:Sawn Log")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") cannot be purchased at the lumber mill here.
- There is a non-respawning pot located at the fire near the wood chopping block.
- All cupboards, end tables, and dressers in the area are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
