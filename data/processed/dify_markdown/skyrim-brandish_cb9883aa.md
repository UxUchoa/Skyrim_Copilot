Brandish

**Brandish** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [bandit wizard](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard "Skyrim:Bandit Wizard") who resides in the library of [Fort Neugrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Neugrad "Skyrim:Fort Neugrad") while it is occupied by [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). Because of his involvement outlined in the [Bandit Chief's journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit%27s_Journal_(Fort_Neugrad) "Skyrim:Bandit's Journal (Fort Neugrad)"), he is in the Fort Neugrad library most of the time. Otherwise, he roams outside near the wagon.

He wears a set of [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12 "Skyrim:Boots"), and carries a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), a leveled [health potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), and a leveled amount of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold").

## Notes

- A lot of internal filenames refer to a "Brandish" in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"), but these files have nothing to do with this NPC. The actual NPC that these files refer to is "Brandish Begin-Again", which was an earlier iteration/name for Riften marketplace vendor [Brand-Shei](/wiki/Skyrim:Brand-Shei "Skyrim:Brand-Shei"). For example, his [merchant chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Merchant_Chests "Skyrim:Containers") and [service faction](/wiki/Skyrim:ServicesRiftenPlazaBrandish "Skyrim:ServicesRiftenPlazaBrandish"), as well as several object references in Riften, are named after "Brandish" but assigned to, and used by, Brand-Shei.
- If you lockpick the door to the Library, but do not kill him after it, he may wander out.
