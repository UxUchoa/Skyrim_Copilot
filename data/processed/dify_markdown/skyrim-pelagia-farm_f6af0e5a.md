Pelagia Farm

**Pelagia Farm** is a large farm south of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun") owned by [Severio Pelagia](/wiki/Skyrim:Severio_Pelagia "Skyrim:Severio Pelagia").

It is a sprawling farm, with a small single-story building maintained by [Nimriel](/wiki/Skyrim:Nimriel "Skyrim:Nimriel") and [Gloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Gloth "Skyrim:Gloth"). They live at the farm, while Severio lives in his [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Severio_Pelagia%27s_House "Skyrim:Severio Pelagia's House") in Whiterun.

When approaching Pelagia Farm for the first time, you will come across [Aela the Huntress](/wiki/Skyrim:Aela_the_Huntress "Skyrim:Aela the Huntress"), [Farkas](/wiki/Skyrim:Farkas "Skyrim:Farkas"), and [Ria](/wiki/Skyrim:Ria "Skyrim:Ria") battling a [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant"). The group of [Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions") will always win the fight.

### Exterior

There are at least three fields of [vegetables](/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Fruit.2FVegetables "Skyrim:Food") with either stone walls or fences segregating them. During the day, [Severio Pelagia](/wiki/Skyrim:Severio_Pelagia "Skyrim:Severio Pelagia") is in the fields with his workers. He will gladly buy any [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") or [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") you hold. There is a [grain mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Milling "Skyrim:Milling") to the east of the house and several other outbuildings and storage sheds around the property. To the west of the house is a pen for a [cow](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow "Skyrim:Cow") and a few paces in front of the entrance are a couple of [chickens' nests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg").

### Pelagia Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-interior-Pelagia_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Pelagia_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pelagia_Farm.jpg)

Farmstead interior

Upon entering there is a long wooden table and bench on your left against the west wall, with an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), a few [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), three [boiled creme treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") containing several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), and an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") on the table, with a chair at either end and a wall shelf above holding crockery. On your right as you enter, against the east wall are a stack of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels and sacks next to a bookcase holding fourteen common books, a copy of *[Songs of Skyrim: Revised](/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_Skyrim:_Revised "Skyrim:Songs of Skyrim: Revised")* and a bowl containing a cabbage. Next to this is a doorway to a bedroom. In the northwest corner of the room is a cupboard holding a cabbage, a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge") and a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), among piles of crockery. There is a chair next to the cupboard with a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and a couple of [plates](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") on the floor and two more wall shelves above holding clutter. There is a second doorway on the east wall with a large fire pit between the two of them with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it.

The first bedroom contains a single bed against the north wall and a dresser beside it with a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") in a bowl, and a copy of *[Rising Threat, Vol. IV](/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._IV "Skyrim:Rising Threat, Vol. IV")* on top. There is a chair in the southeastern corner. The second bedroom contains a single bed against the south wall, with an end table beside it, holding a bottle of ale, a [silver ingot](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot"), and copies of *[Yngol and the Sea-Ghosts](/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol_and_the_Sea-Ghosts "Skyrim:Yngol and the Sea-Ghosts")* and *[The Book of Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra "Skyrim:The Book of Daedra")*. A narrow table against the north wall holds a bottle of ale, a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") on a plate, two common books, and a [draught of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Health "Skyrim:Draught of Health"). Underneath it is a pair of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots").

## Notes

- Though you can find and activate a [grain mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Milling "Skyrim:Milling") here, it will only lead to an animation, with no actual function in the game even with *[Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire")* installed.
- Even if you help Severio Pelagia, everything in the farmhouse may still be considered owned, and entering may still count as trespassing.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the barrels outside the building as owned by the farm.
