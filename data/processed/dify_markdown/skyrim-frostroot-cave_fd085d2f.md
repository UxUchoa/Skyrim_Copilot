Frostroot Cave

**Frostroot Cave** is a cave in [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch"), north of [Kagrenzel](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrenzel "Skyrim:Kagrenzel"). It contains the entrance to [Nchuanthumz](/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuanthumz "Skyrim:Nchuanthumz"), a Dwarven player home.

## Related Quests

- **[The Sanctuary and the Manufactory](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Sanctuary_and_the_Manufactory "Skyrim:The Sanctuary and the Manufactory")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuanthumz:_Dwarven_Home "Skyrim:Nchuanthumz: Dwarven Home"): Repair a Dwarven ruin and make yourself at home.

## Exterior

The entrance to the cave is located just uphill of and behind [Kagrenzel](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrenzel "Skyrim:Kagrenzel"), near the entrance to the latter. A [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") is located to the left of the cave entrance, and a [moonstone ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein") to the right.

## Interior

[(?)](/wiki/Category:Pages_Missing_Data "Category:Pages Missing Data")
A snow-covered passageway leads into the icy cavern. Three bandits are present, trying to plunder the cave for its Dwemer artifacts. You will have to fight your way through them to reach Nchuanthumz.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-interior-Frostroot_Cave_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Frostroot_Cave_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Frostroot_Cave_02.jpg)

Dead-end path with chest

A dead-end path branches off to the right about halfway down the main passage. At the end of that, on the left, is a Dwarven Chest. Continuing down the main passage, three of Artifacts' low-level hired bandits loiter about. They will attack if they detect you. There is another Dwarven chest on the right as you turn the final corner before the double doors of Nchuanthumz itself come into view. Finally, a half-buried barrel lies just left of the doorway.

## Notes

- A [courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Courier "Skyrim:Courier") can appear within Frostroot Cave when exiting from Nchuanthumz.

## Bugs

- Frostroot Cave is labeled as a Quick Test Cell from inside Nchuanthumz on the local map.
- Sometimes when entering Frostroot Cave, a dragon will spawn, start flying through the floor/walls and become hostile to the player.
- [![Nintendo Switch Logo.svg](//images.uesp.net/5/5d/Nintendo_Switch_Logo.svg)](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch "Skyrim:Switch") When exiting Nchuanthumz into Frostroot Cave, the door is labeled Quick Test Cell.
- [![Nintendo Switch Logo.svg](//images.uesp.net/5/5d/Nintendo_Switch_Logo.svg)](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch "Skyrim:Switch") The door from Frostroot Cave into Nchuanthumz will have two doors to open. The first with the generic "open door" and the second prompting entrance to Nchuanthumz.
