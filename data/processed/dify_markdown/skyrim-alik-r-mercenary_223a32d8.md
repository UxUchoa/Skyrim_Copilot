The yellow-hooded Alik'r Mercenary

An Alik'r Mercenary is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") mercenary who is encountered at a camp southeast of [Fort Greymoor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greymoor "Skyrim:Fort Greymoor") during the quest [Gift of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Gift_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Gift of Kynareth"). He is a member of a band of the Alik'r following [Majid the Mad](/wiki/Skyrim:Majid_the_Mad "Skyrim:Majid the Mad") that stole the [Lord's Mail](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail_(item) "Skyrim:Lord's Mail (item)"). He is hostile and will attack on sight.

The Mercenary wears a set of yellow [Redguard Clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Clothes "Skyrim:Redguard Clothes") and a unique yellow [Redguard Hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Hood "Skyrim:Redguard Hood"), as well as a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"). He wields a bow and arrows, carries a random amount of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), and has a 25% chance to carry a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"). He must be killed to safely access the chest containing the Lord's Mail.

The green-hooded Alik'r Mercenary

An Alik'r Mercenary is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") mercenary who is encountered at a camp southeast of [Fort Greymoor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greymoor "Skyrim:Fort Greymoor") during the quest [Gift of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Gift_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Gift of Kynareth"). He is a member of a band of the Alik'r following [Majid the Mad](/wiki/Skyrim:Majid_the_Mad "Skyrim:Majid the Mad") that stole the [Lord's Mail](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail_(item) "Skyrim:Lord's Mail (item)"). He is hostile and will attack on sight.

The Mercenary wears a set of green [Redguard Clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Clothes "Skyrim:Redguard Clothes") and a unique green [Redguard Hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Hood "Skyrim:Redguard Hood"), as well as a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"). He uses the spells [Steadfast Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Steadfast_Ward "Skyrim:Steadfast Ward"), [Fast Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Healing "Skyrim:Fast Healing") and either [Firebolt](/wiki/Skyrim:Firebolt "Skyrim:Firebolt"), [Ice Spike](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Spike "Skyrim:Ice Spike") or [Lightning Bolt](/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Bolt "Skyrim:Lightning Bolt"), carries a random amount of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), and has a 25% chance to carry a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"). He must be killed to safely access the chest containing the Lord's Mail.

The mercenaries and Majid can be a tough fight, so prepare accordingly.
