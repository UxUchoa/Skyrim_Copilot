Bleakcoast Cave

**Bleakcoast Cave** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") east-southeast of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") containing [frost trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll"). It contains only one zone, *[Bleakcoast Cave](#Bleakcoast_Cave)*.

## Related Quests

- **[Animal Extermination](/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Extermination_(B) "Skyrim:Animal Extermination (B)")**: Fulfill a contract given to the [Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions") to clear out a beast den. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

### Exterior

The exterior of the cave more than explains the name with its dreary landscape and wood debris. An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") can be found to the east of the entrance on the shoreline, and a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") grows nearby to the south. Skeletal remains are strewn about the outside of the cave, evidence of a large carnivore inside.

### Bleakcoast Cave

The cave's circular main chamber holds two frost trolls and the grisly remains of the trolls' meals, but nothing of interest. A third frost troll is in the smaller chamber to the west. Here also are the corpses of a [novice conjurer](/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Conjurer "Skyrim:Novice Conjurer") and a [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") with a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") on the ground beside the bandit. On the novice's corpse you'll find a copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Battle of Sancre Tor](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_of_Sancre_Tor "Skyrim:Battle of Sancre Tor")*. Next to him is an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") covered in blood.
Another ancillary chamber is to the north. A [skeletal torso is suspended, upside down](/wiki/Skyrim:Easter_Eggs#Star_Wars "Skyrim:Easter Eggs") from the ceiling with a random, enchanted [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword") lying underneath . A fourth frost troll is in the chamber ahead along with a dead [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit "Skyrim:Khajiit") next to a medium coin purse.

## Notes

- South of the cave entrance, a lonely hunter's camp can be seen across the water. The camp contains two small tents, each containing an unowned bedroll; the left-hand tent also contains a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"). There is a large coin purse on the back of a covered cart and a wooden table with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), some [horker meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat "Skyrim:Horker Meat") and [tusks](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk"), and a copy of the [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") skill book *[Rislav The Righteous](/wiki/Skyrim:Rislav_The_Righteous "Skyrim:Rislav The Righteous")* on and around it. There is an unowned [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse") and dead horker by the campfire.
- From the small camp, head down to the water, follow the coast to the east, and just as it passes a huge broken ice floe on your left, you should find the remains of the camp's two hunters surrounded by three or four horkers. One of the hunters is carrying [treasure map VIII](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_VIII "Skyrim:Treasure Map VIII"), which directs you to [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge"). By now you will be southeast of Bleakcoast Cave.
- Another iron ore vein can be found on the northwest end of the island.
- In [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
