Agrane Peryval

Agrane Peryval is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") merchant who can be found at his campsite north of the [Windward Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Windward_Ruins "Skyrim:Windward Ruins"), just outside [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"). You will be directed to him as part of the related quest, although you do not need to purchase anything from him.

He wears a set of [Saturalia clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Clothes "Skyrim:Saturalia Clothes"), which consists of boots, gloves, clothes, and a hat, and wields an [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") mace. He also carries a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") and a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). Additionally, he knows the spell [Fast Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Healing "Skyrim:Fast Healing").
