**Brina's House** is a small house in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"), located next to [Leigelf's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Leigelf%27s_House "Skyrim:Leigelf's House"), opposite [The Mortar and Pestle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mortar_and_Pestle "Skyrim:The Mortar and Pestle").

It is the home of [Brina Merilis](/wiki/Skyrim:Brina_Merilis "Skyrim:Brina Merilis") and her guard, [Horik Halfhand](/wiki/Skyrim:Horik_Halfhand "Skyrim:Horik Halfhand"). It consists of one area only: *[Brina's House](#Brina.27s_House)*.

This house will be permanently abandoned if [The Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale") is given to the [Empire](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") in [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") and subsequently retaken by the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks").

### Exterior

The house faces the bay, with a decking out front. There is a pile of firewood on the left side of the decking, and a wooden bench on the right. The house is protected by a novice lock when neither of its inhabitants are home; [Horik Halfhand](/wiki/Skyrim:Horik_Halfhand "Skyrim:Horik Halfhand") acts as [Brina Merilis's](/wiki/Skyrim:Brina_Merilis "Skyrim:Brina Merilis") [housecarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl") or bodyguard, and as such travels around the town with her at all times.

### Brina's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-interior-Brina%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Brina%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brina%27s_House.jpg)

Brina's home

The house is a typical one-room dwelling. On the left as you enter, there is a chair and [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel in the corner. Next to them, against the east wall, is a cupboard holding four bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). There is another collection of barrels in the southeastern corner. Against the south wall, there is a long wooden table with two bottles of alto wine and a single bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on top. Hanging above the table are three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, two bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), and a rack holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and three [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). Further along on the right is a food barrel and a set of shelves holding four more bottles of alto wine and four bottles of wine. To the right of this, still on the south wall, lies a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") and a kettle containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") placed on the hearth. To the right of the fireplace is a pile of firewood and a basket containing a copy of the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Importance of Where](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Importance_of_Where "Skyrim:The Importance of Where")*.

Coming out of the west wall are two owned single beds, with an end table holding a bottle of Nord mead and an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of each bed, both of which contain leveled items. Against the north wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a [steel mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Steel Mace") placed on top. To the right of the wardrobe, next to the door, is a small round wooden table and two chairs. On the table is a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine and two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread").

## Notes

- With the exception of most barrels and sacks, all containers in the house are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") and safe to store items in.
