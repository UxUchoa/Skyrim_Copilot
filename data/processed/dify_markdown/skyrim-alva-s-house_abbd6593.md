Alva's House

**Alva's House** is a small house located in [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") that is home to [Alva](/wiki/Skyrim:Alva "Skyrim:Alva") and [Hroggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hroggar "Skyrim:Hroggar").

## Related Quests

- **[Laid to Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Laid_to_Rest "Skyrim:Laid to Rest")**: Investigate a tragic house fire, and discover an insidious plot against the town.

### Exterior

It is the next house along the jetty from the [Guardhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Guardhouse "Skyrim:Guardhouse"). There are two flights of stairs leading up to the decking at the front of the house, which has a wooden bench and two [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels on it. The door is normally adept-locked.

### Alva's House

On your left when entering is a food barrel beside a long wooden table which holds two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a sample of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"), two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). Above the table, hanging from the ceiling are two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, three bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). To the right of the table is a basket containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and hanging from a rack above are three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). In the northern corner are two barrels, one of which is a fish barrel. There is a large double bed against the northeast wall and a table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") is to the right of it. At the foot of the double bed is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") which contains leveled items. Next to this against the southeast wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"), with a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") leaning against it on the right.

Opposite the door to the cellar is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a collection of common books and a copy of *[2920, Sun's Dusk, v11](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Sun%27s_Dusk,_v11 "Skyrim:2920, Sun's Dusk, v11")* on the mantlepiece. To the left of the fireplace is a flight of stairs down to the cellar. This is the only house in [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") to have a cellar. To the right of the fire are several barrels and sacks of food. Against the southwest wall is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table is a copy of *[Immortal Blood](/wiki/Skyrim:Immortal_Blood "Skyrim:Immortal Blood")*, five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"). In the western corner and along the southeastern wall is a single bed with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed, which contains leveled items.

### Alva's Cellar

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-interior-Alva%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Alva%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Alva%27s_House.jpg)

Alva's Cellar

Upon entering the cellar you will see an open coffin on a raised section in the middle of the room, during the day it will have [Alva](/wiki/Skyrim:Alva "Skyrim:Alva") slumbering inside, as well as *[Alva's journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Alva%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Alva's Journal")* (see [notes](#Notes)). Around the room against the northeast wall are several straw bales. In the eastern corner are a couple of barrels and a round wooden table holding two [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") flowers. Against the southeast wall are several crates and barrels, some of which contain foodstuffs.

## Notes

- If you haven't started the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Laid_to_Rest "Skyrim:Laid to Rest") *[Alva's journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Alva%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Alva's Journal")* will be missing from the coffin. Once the quest progresses to the point of needing proof for the Jarl, then Alva's Journal will appear in the coffin, even if you have already killed Alva.

## Bugs

- On the right side of Alva's cellar, after going through the door, there is a hole where the barrels are. A bucket will repeatedly fall and respawn.
