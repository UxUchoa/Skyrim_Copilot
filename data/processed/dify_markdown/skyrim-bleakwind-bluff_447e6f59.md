Bleakwind Bluff

**Bleakwind Bluff** is a small ruined [Nordic tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Towers "Skyrim:Nordic Towers") inhabited by [hagravens](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven "Skyrim:Hagraven") and [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"), located northwest of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead") on an outcrop of rock.

It is off a path that links [Old Hroldan Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Hroldan_Inn "Skyrim:Old Hroldan Inn") with Rorikstead.

## Bleakwind Bluff

The path leading from the main route is marked by a pile of rocks on the north side of the route. This path winds around a large rock before doubling back on itself and climbing the first of several flights of stairs that lead up to the tower, while still circling the outcrop. As you circle the first [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") will normally be found at the top of a long flight of stairs with sharpened stakes along the left of the stairs. Beyond this there are two more short flights up to a campfire in front of the tower, where you will encounter a Forsworn magic-user who prefers frost magic, a [Briarheart](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Briarheart "Skyrim:Forsworn Briarheart"), and a [hagraven](/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven "Skyrim:Hagraven").

A couple paces south of the campfire is a doorway into the tower with a couple [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes") hanging down making a quiet entrance difficult. Inside there are the normal Forsworn and hagraven symbols and decorations, with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") against the east wall and a copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[King](/wiki/Skyrim:King "Skyrim:King")* on the ground by a pile of rocks against the north wall. Behind the rocks is a stretched pelt between two sticks, with a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid hanging from the left and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") hanging from the right. Opposite this, under a strange totem made of different bones and antlers and under another stretched pelt from some kind of animal, is a bowl on a stand filled with three human skulls, with a [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), and a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") among them. To the left of the bowl, against the wall, is a dead [skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever"). It is possible to rock climb around the outside of the tower, to the rear, where there is a [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") bush.
