**Four Shields Tavern** is an inn in the village of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") in [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar"). It is the only building on the eastern side of the road through Dragon Bridge.

[Faida](/wiki/Skyrim:Faida "Skyrim:Faida") is the proprietor, and [Julienne Lylvieve](/wiki/Skyrim:Julienne_Lylvieve "Skyrim:Julienne Lylvieve") works there while learning the innkeeper trade, hoping to eventually establish her own inn.

## Four Shields Tavern

Like all inns in [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") the entrance leads directly into the common room, which has a fire pit down the middle of the room. There are five long wooden tables and benches against the side walls with a selection of bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") as well as a few loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") on all the tables. The left-hand table against the north wall also has a flower basket containing two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg") and a [chicken's egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), while the table in the southwestern corner also has several pieces of cheese and two [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") on it. There are several [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels around the room, including one that is a fish barrel.

The counter is in front of the west wall and has a bottle of alto wine and several pieces of cheese on top. Underneath can be found a [steel warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Warhammer "Skyrim:Steel Warhammer"), three small [coin purses](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), two bottles of alto wine, six bottles of wine, fourteen bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), five loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") and a copy of *[Of Fjori and Holgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir "Skyrim:Of Fjori and Holgeir")*. Along the west wall are five large vats with three racks hanging from the ceiling holding ten [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and six [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), with two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, a bunch of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") also hanging from the ceiling. There are three doorways off of this room, one to the north and two to the south. There is a set of shelves on either side of the doorway to the north holding two bottles of alto wine, six bottles of wine, seven bottles of Nord mead, four loaves of bread and three bunches of elves ear.

The bedroom to the north is the largest and can be rented for 10 septims per day. It contains a single bed in the northwestern corner, with two dressers along the north wall both containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and having a bottle of alto wine, a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), three bottles of Nord mead, a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute") and a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") on top of them. There is a large coin purse to the right of these, in the northeast corner with a few food sacks in a pile on the floor in front. Against the east wall is an unlocked long [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), while against the south wall is a square wooden table and chair with a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") and [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") on the table.

The left-hand bedroom to the south has an owned single bed in the southeastern corner, an unlocked chest at the foot of the bed, a round wooden table with a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") on top and a tall wardrobe against the east wall with copies of *[The Oblivion Crisis](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Oblivion_Crisis "Skyrim:The Oblivion Crisis")* and *[The "Madmen" of the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_%22Madmen%22_of_the_Reach "Skyrim:The \"Madmen\" of the Reach")* on top. The other bedroom has an owned single bed in the southwestern corner, a chest at the foot of the bed, a round wooden table and chair next to the bed and a dresser against the west wall containing more clothes.
