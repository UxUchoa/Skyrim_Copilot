**Firir** is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)") found dead in [Chillwind Depths](/wiki/Skyrim:Chillwind_Depths "Skyrim:Chillwind Depths") after chasing a lead on a brutal assault on a merchant caravan. He wears only a [roughspun tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic "Skyrim:Roughspun Tunic") with no shoes, and carries at least 25 gold.

## Notes

- A [torn note](/wiki/Skyrim:Torn_Note "Skyrim:Torn Note") is found on his body in the cave, apologizing for his death to his love, "Eydis". It is unclear if this is the [Eydis](/wiki/Skyrim:Eydis "Skyrim:Eydis") at [Old Hroldan Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Hroldan_Inn "Skyrim:Old Hroldan Inn"), as her "missing" husband is claimed to be a soldier and not named at any point. Additionally, bringing the note to Eydis has no effect.

## Bugs

- Firir has the `FemaleCommoner` voice type. This results in a female voice if the body is [resurrected](/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate "Skyrim:Reanimate").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
