## Notes

- You can set up your own [![SR-mapicon-Camp.png](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-mapicon-Camp.png/13px-SR-mapicon-Camp.png)](/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Camp.png)**[campsite](/wiki/Skyrim:Campsite "Skyrim:Campsite")** with the [Camping](/wiki/Skyrim:Camping "Skyrim:Camping") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed. The campsite adds a fast travel marker to your map, although you can only have one campsite set up at a time.
