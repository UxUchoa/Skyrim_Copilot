**Faida** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [food vendor](/wiki/Skyrim:Food_Vendor "Skyrim:Food Vendor") and the innkeeper of the [Four Shields Tavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Shields_Tavern "Skyrim:Four Shields Tavern") in the small village of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge"). She stays behind the inn's counter all day. During the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:A False Front (Stormcloaks)"), she will direct you towards the [Imperial Courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Courier "Skyrim:Imperial Courier").

Faida wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothes") along with some [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1 "Skyrim:Gloves") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots"). She wields an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and carries some [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Related Quests

- **[A False Front](/wiki/Skyrim:A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:A False Front (Stormcloaks)")**: Find and deliver some forged documents to the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion").
- **[Breaching Security](/wiki/Skyrim:Breaching_Security "Skyrim:Breaching Security")**: Kill and frame the [son](/wiki/Skyrim:Gaius_Maro "Skyrim:Gaius Maro") of a high-ranking [Imperial guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro "Skyrim:Commander Maro").

## Quest-Related Events

At the start of Breaching Security, you will find Faida outside the [Penitus Oculatus Outpost](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Outpost") alongside her lover [Gaius Maro](/wiki/Skyrim:Gaius_Maro "Skyrim:Gaius Maro") and his [father](/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro "Skyrim:Commander Maro"). Before starting his security inspections, Gaius shares a heartfelt goodbye with his father, and will then have a short moment with Faida:

:   **Faida**: *"Well, this is it, then. Look after yourself, Gaius. You're doing your duty, and I'm proud of you. But you'd better come back to me. You hear?"*
:   **Gaius**: *"Oh, Faida. I may travel alone, but you know I carry you always in my heart. I'll see you soon."*

After this, Gaius departs and Faida will head back to work at the inn.

## Conversations

[Julienne Lylvieve](/wiki/Skyrim:Julienne_Lylvieve "Skyrim:Julienne Lylvieve") is currently learning about the innkeeper trade from Faida. They can be found having the following conversations:

:   **Julienne**: *"Miss Faida, may I ask you a question?"*
:   **Faida**: *"Of course, Julienne. What's on your mind?"*
:   **Julienne**: *"What do you do when a customer can't afford to pay for a meal or a drink?"*
:   **Faida**: *"Since my customers are mostly local, I just knock on their front doors the next day."*

:   **Faida**: *"Mind you don't forget to sweep up, Julienne. The floor's looking a bit dusty."*
:   **Julienne**: *"I won't forget. I'll do it after I get the dishes cleaned."*
:   **Faida**: *"You've been working hard lately. You'll make a fine innkeeper someday."*
:   **Julienne**: *"If the [Nine](/wiki/Lore:Nine_Divines "Lore:Nine Divines") are willing!"*

## Rumors

Back in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), [Minette Vinius](/wiki/Skyrim:Minette_Vinius "Skyrim:Minette Vinius") may mention her in response to her father saying that running a bar isn't a lady's job:

:   *"What about 'Auntie Faida' in Dragon Bridge? She runs a bar, and you said she's one of your favorite girls!"*

## Notes

- According to relationship data as seen in the [Creation Kit](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit "Skyrim Mod:Creation Kit"), she's the cousin of [Corpulus Vinius](/wiki/Skyrim:Corpulus_Vinius "Skyrim:Corpulus Vinius"). Minette Vinius also refers to this fact in a scene.
- It seems that Faida was once meant to be a part of a love triangle with Julienne and removed Dragon Bridge resident, [Tasius Tragus](/wiki/Skyrim:Tasius_Tragus "Skyrim:Tasius Tragus"). Tasius and Faida are marked as lovers in the Creation Kit, with Julienne being marked as a secret lover.
  - Tasius' role was seemingly given to a man named Voltus Agallon, who in turn got replaced by both [Commander Maro](/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro "Skyrim:Commander Maro") and [Gaius Maro](/wiki/Skyrim:Gaius_Maro "Skyrim:Gaius Maro"). Like Tasius, Gaius is also shown to be in a relationship with Faida, although they aren't marked as such in the files.
