Eirid

**Eirid** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") living in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"). Her parents are [Haran](/wiki/Skyrim:Haran "Skyrim:Haran") and [Dagur](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagur "Skyrim:Dagur"), and they run [The Frozen Hearth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth "Skyrim:The Frozen Hearth") inn. She wanders the inn from 8am to noon, goes outside to play till 6pm then returns to the inn and sleeps in the basement at night.

Eirid wears red colored [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Child's Clothes") and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000e80a8 "Skyrim:Clothing"). She carries a selection of [food and toys](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child "Skyrim:Leveled Lists") and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue

:   *"There's not many people left in Winterhold, so I only have Assur to play with, and sometimes he's mean."*
:   *"He always wants to play 'Hunt the Elf', and he makes me be the elf."*
:   *"Assur says his pa says the College made lots of bad things happen, but the mages I've seen look nice."*
:   *"The mages don't let us go in the College. They said it's not safe in there."*
:   *"Assur and I are playing a game. Don't tell him where I am!"*

Should the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") take over Winterhold, Assur and [his](/wiki/Skyrim:Korir "Skyrim:Korir") [family](/wiki/Skyrim:Thaena "Skyrim:Thaena") will be exiled. Eirid can be found commenting on this:

:   *"Assur had to move away because of the Empire. But we get to stay."*

Should you talk to Eirid during [Containment](/wiki/Skyrim:Containment "Skyrim:Containment"), where Winterhold is disrupted by [Magic Anomalies](/wiki/Skyrim:Magic_Anomaly "Skyrim:Magic Anomaly"), she will be scared:

:   *"Did you make something bad happen?"*

## Conversations

Her parents can be heard discussing putting an end to Assur and Eirid playing 'Hunt the Elf':

:   **Haran**: *"I understand Eirid's been playing 'Hunt the Elf' again."*
:   **Dagur**: *"It's just children playing, Haran. I wouldn't fret over it."*
:   **Haran**: *"I'm not 'fretting.' I don't want Eirid playing those sorts of games!"*
:   **Dagur**: *"All right, all right. I'll speak to her."*

## Notes

- The "Hunt the Elf" scene between Eirid and Assur does not work due to invalid condition checks.
