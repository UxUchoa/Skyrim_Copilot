Red Eagle Redoubt

**Red Eagle Redoubt** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") and two exterior camps east of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") containing [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn"). It contains only one zone, *[Red Eagle Ascent](#Red_Eagle_Ascent)*.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-interior-Red_Eagle_Ascent.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Red_Eagle_Ascent.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Red_Eagle_Ascent.jpg)

Looking down over the altar area in Red Eagle Ascent

The map marker is near the highest point of the small mountain range southwest of [Sundered Towers](/wiki/Skyrim:Sundered_Towers "Skyrim:Sundered Towers"). The surrounding area is populated by a large number of Forsworn of increasing skill as you approach the peak, which itself is home to several [elk](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer#Elk "Skyrim:Deer").

### Exterior: Lower Camp

Two leveled Forsworn guard the approach from the northwest. One of them is next to a campfire, with an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") and a rack for drying hides nearby. The second Forsworn is on top of a rock with a small wooden table and chair and wooden steps up to it. The entrance to Red Eagle Ascent is to the left of this raised area.

### Red Eagle Ascent

This cave is a typical Forsworn encampment with the normal sharpened stakes and goat head decorations as well as vegetation throughout. Upon entering, there is a tunnel that twists and turns, until you pass a tree stump with a patch of [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") growing from it, after which the tunnel opens up. There is an elk head off to the left with a lit lantern in front of it, with a [deer hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide "Skyrim:Deer Hide") nearby. Just after this, on your right is an iron ore vein behind some of the sharpened stakes, next to a tree with a couple taproot ornaments. Across the path by this is a [tripwire](/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire "Skyrim:Tripwire") that drops an [oil lantern](/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Lanterns "Skyrim:Oil Lanterns") on an [oil slick trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Slicks "Skyrim:Oil Slicks"). Setting this off will alert the two Forsworn that are guarding this part of the cave and can be used to your advantage in dealing with them. Behind the previously mentioned tree is a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") on the ground. Near where the two Forsworn are standing is a lit brazier with a random healing potion next to it, while an adept-locked chest is behind some roots on the opposite side of a tunnel entrance heading east.

Through the tunnel there are [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") and several [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap") fungi before a flight of stone steps up to the next cavern. There is a [bear trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") just after entering this cavern, hidden among some of the vegetation. Three more Forsworn make this room their home; the first is at the top of another flight of stone stairs, and the other two are at the rear of the room. Before reaching the stairs, you pass another white cap patch on your right, and just before the stairs, a [burial urn](/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn "Skyrim:Burial Urn") is on your left.

At the top of the flight of stone stairs is a [pressure plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Pressure_Plate "Skyrim:Pressure Plate") that activates a [dart trap](/wiki/Skyrim:Dart_Trap "Skyrim:Dart Trap") fired from your right across the path in front of you, and on your left is a handle that can be used by the Forsworn to activate a [falling boulders](/wiki/Skyrim:Falling_Boulders "Skyrim:Falling Boulders") trap below. Off on your left behind a tall pillar is a lit brazier with a random [battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe "Skyrim:Battleaxe") and [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet") lying beside it. Following the path up over a few wide steps, you reach an area with a blood-covered altar holding a leveled [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow"), a random potion of magicka, and a decapitated skeever. Also on this plateau are a chair and a flight of wooden stairs with an unlocked chest and random stamina potion underneath.

The stairs lead up to a corridor that branches off to your right to the exit, and ahead to a blocked corridor with a fly amanita, two [bleeding crowns](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown"), a [blisterwort](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort"), and a medium coin purse. Taking the branch to the right and up a few more steps, you reach an unlocked iron door that leads outside to the upper camp of the redoubt.

### Exterior: Upper Camp

Upon exiting, you are at the bottom of several flights of stairs up. There is a campfire with a pile of firewood behind. Each flight of stairs has Forsworn guarding them, each increasing in skill as you go higher. Before climbing, if you travel around to the left of the first flight of stairs, underneath a fallen stone tower you will find a large coin purse.

At the top of the first flight of stairs there is a Forsworn either wandering or sitting on a wooden bench. Taking this one out can alert the Forsworn at the top of the next flight. Before climbing again, at the top of the first flight, turn right and double back to find a novice-locked chest. Climb the third flight and start the next flight; now there are three Forsworn, two at the top of the fourth flight, and one above you to your right on higher ground. It is possible to take out the first two without the third finding you if your Sneak skill is high enough. The one above you is always an archer.

Ahead are more steps passing under three arches. Growing from the second of these is a [hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss") plant. As you pass under the third, there is a small Forsworn camp on your left with two open tents and two long wooden tables and benches between them. There is a Forsworn sitting at the tables and a second sitting in a chair in the farthest tent. Too much noise taking these out can alert a Forsworn wizard from a tent farther behind on the next plateau up. The first tent contains a single hay pile and a barrel, while the second tent contains the chair the Forsworn was sitting on.

Back on the path, you pass a stone pillar and a lit brazier on a stand on your left. Here the camp splits, with a single tent with the wizard on your left and the main part of the camp to your right. Inside the tent with the wizard are an unowned single bed, an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"), and a food barrel with a leveled [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger") and a copy of the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Reality & Other Falsehoods](/wiki/Skyrim:Reality_%26_Other_Falsehoods "Skyrim:Reality & Other Falsehoods")* on top and random boots beside it.

The main camp consists of four tents around a campfire with a [blacksmith forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge") beside the nearest tent. Between the nearest tent and the forge is an adept-locked chest. Three Forsworn are guarding this area: one sleeping in the nearest tent, one wandering or sitting on a wooden bench, and the third behind on a raised area. On top of the forge anvil is a random [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"). Each of the tents contains a hay pile, with the first also containing a wooden bench, the next two also containing chairs, and the last also containing a dresser. There are also a wooden table with a mead barrel and sack on top and several other sacks and wooden benches around the camp. To the right of the last tent is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). If you are here seeking the priest of Boethiah for the quest [The House of Horrors](/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Horrors "Skyrim:The House of Horrors"), he will be found kneeling in the tent next to the table with the mead barrel.

The path continues heading east toward Sundered Towers and the boss of this redoubt along with one other Forsworn and another raised area reached via stone stairs. Before that, traveling south down the side of one of the tents, you'll reach a melee practice area including two dummies and a wooden table holding a random [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword"). On your left before reaching the stairs up to the boss is an archery practice area including two archery targets and a wooden table holding a random bow.

Climbing the stone stairs, you'll encounter the last Forsworn before the boss, and pass a wooden flight of stairs up to Sundered Towers, with a tree stump with a mora tapinella growth on the left of these stairs. Ahead, the Forsworn boss will be sitting on a throne, or if alerted, searching for you near an altar. The boss will have [Red Eagle's Fury](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle%27s_Fury "Skyrim:Red Eagle's Fury"). Past the throne and altar are a few steps down to another tent containing an unowned single bed, a boss leveled chest, a chair and wooden table holding a mead barrel, a copy of *[The Legend of Red Eagle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle "Skyrim:The Legend of Red Eagle")*, a leveled stamina potion, a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"), and a lit lantern. This chest will hold any item you have been sent to Red Eagle Redoubt to find.

## Notes

- One [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") is 120 feet east of the entrance, slightly uphill.

## Bugs

- Looking at the world map when inside Red Eagle Ascent places your marker north-northwest of Markarth.
- If you have previously killed the leader of Red Eagle Redoubt and get the task to kill him again, you must reanimate the body and kill him a second time. You may get this task while trying to purchase a house in Markarth from the Jarl.
- The book *[Red Eagle's Rite](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle%27s_Rite "Skyrim:Red Eagle's Rite")* on the altar might clip beneath the surface, making it unreachable without the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
  - It is sometimes possible to move the book out of the altar by using an explosive spell such as [Fireball](/wiki/Skyrim:Fireball "Skyrim:Fireball").
