The Aspiring Mage

The **Aspiring Mage** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") who is [randomly encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#College_Application_Denied "Skyrim:World Interactions") in the wilderness. He can be seen attempting to [reanimate the corpse](/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate_Corpse "Skyrim:Reanimate Corpse") of a woodland creature. He had wanted to follow in his grandfather's footsteps and become a mage, but his application to join the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)") was denied. It turns out he is only able to cast magic because of his grandfather's [leveled staff of reanimation](/wiki/Skyrim:Staves#Reanimate "Skyrim:Staves"). You may persuade him to give you the staff, intimidate him, or just pay for it.

The Aspiring Mage wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0006c1d9 "Skyrim:Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5 "Skyrim:Boots"). Apart from his staff, he also carries a leveled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems").

## Dialogue

As he tries to reanimate the creature, he will intone:

:   *"Rise up and live again!"*
:   *"I grant you a new life!"*
:   *"Come on, get up!"*
:   *"This should work. I've done it before."*
:   *"Gah! This staff is useless... I'm useless. The whole world is useless!"*

You can approach him to ask what the problem is:

:   **What's the problem?**
:   *"Oh... hello there. Didn't notice you come up. I was focusing my magicka. Or at least, I thought I was. I don't really know what I'm doing."*

You can reply in two ways:

:   **Yes. It's painfully obvious you don't know what you're doing.**

    :   *"You don't have to tell me that."*

:   **That's quite a staff you have there.**

    :   *"I used to think so, too."*

Either way, he will continue by explaining:

:   *"See, my grandfather was a wizard. My father wouldn't talk about him much. But I found some of his things in the attic. Including this staff. After that I thought I was turning into a wizard too, because dead things near me would come to life again for while. I even went to the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)"). But they just laughed at me. The magic is in the staff, not in me they said."*

At this point, you can try to acquire the staff from him.

| Player Choice | Response |
| --- | --- |
| **Give the staff to me before someone gets hurt.**  *(Persuade)* | **Passed:** *"Okay, okay. You've talked me into it."* **Failed:** *"Nah. I don't think so."* |
| **Hand over the staff, or else.**  *(Intimidate)* | **Passed:** *"No need to get rough!"* **Failed:** *"I don't have to take that from you!"* |
| **Seems like coin would be worth more to you.**   *(<BribeCost> gold)* | **Passed:** *"Gold solves most problems, doesn't it?"* |

A successful attempt will have him say:

:   *"In fact. Keep it. I must have used up the magic in the staff or something. It's just a dumb stick of wood now."*
