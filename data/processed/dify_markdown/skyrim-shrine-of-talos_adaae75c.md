Shrine of Talos in Markarth

The **Shrine of Talos** is a small shrine in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") dedicated to the worship of the god [Talos](/wiki/Lore:Tiber_Septim "Lore:Tiber Septim").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-interior-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg)

Inside the Shrine of Talos

It is a small, one-room location that is usually empty. The statue of the forsaken deity is the well-lit center-piece of the otherwise gloomy interior. A [shrine of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Talos "Skyrim:Shrines") can be found directly in front of the statue; activating it gives you the [Blessing of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Talos "Skyrim:Blessing of Talos").
