## Quick Walkthrough

1. Find [Gharol](/wiki/Skyrim:Gharol "Skyrim:Gharol") at [Dushnikh Yal](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnikh_Yal "Skyrim:Dushnikh Yal").
2. Deliver the sword to [Lash gra-Dushnikh](/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh "Skyrim:Lash gra-Dushnikh") at [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten").

### A Mother's Pride

Gharol is a [blacksmith](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith "Skyrim:Blacksmith") living in [Dushnikh Yal](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnikh_Yal "Skyrim:Dushnikh Yal"), the [Orc stronghold](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") southeast of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). If you're permitted to enter the stronghold, you may talk to her about her weapon business. She will tell you that while she occasionally trades with the outside world, Orcs infuse their weapons with their private emotions: *"An Orc blade is a very personal thing. Anger, regret, pride. All these things are put into our steel."* In the conversation you will discover that Gharol's daughter, [Lash](/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh "Skyrim:Lash gra-Dushnikh"), chose to leave the stronghold, breaking with [Orc custom](/wiki/Lore:Orc "Lore:Orc") and bringing shame and grief to her mother. Gharol tells you that she has forged a sword to channel her feelings about her daughter's betrayal, and to send her daughter a message. Then she will ask you to honor her with a task: bring the sword to her daughter in [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten"); Lash will know what it means.

### Message Received

Travel to Karthwasten and deliver the sword to Lash. She will reply, *"So that's it, then. 'Don't come back.' Thank you. Here. You honored my mother's task and should be rewarded."* The forsaken daughter will reward you with leveled gold and ask you to leave her alone.
