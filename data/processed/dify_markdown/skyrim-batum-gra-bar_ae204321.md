Batum gra-Bar

**Batum gra-Bar** is a dead [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)"), and one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto "Skyrim:Bloated Man's Grotto") hunting [Sinding](/wiki/Skyrim:Sinding "Skyrim:Sinding"). When you arrive, she is dead at the first campfire, presumably from wounds sustained fighting Sinding. She only spawns during the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Ill_Met_By_Moonlight "Skyrim:Ill Met By Moonlight").

She wears [hide armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor"), [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), a [hide helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Helmet "Skyrim:Hide Helmet") and [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"). She wields a leveled [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") and a leveled [bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bow "Skyrim:Bow") with leveled [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows "Skyrim:Arrows"), all of which may be up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") in quality at level 22. She also carries at least 25 gold.
