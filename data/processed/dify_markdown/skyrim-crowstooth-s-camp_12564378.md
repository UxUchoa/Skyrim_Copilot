Crowstooth's Camp

**Crowstooth's Camp** is a small [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") encampment southwest of [Darklight Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower "Skyrim:Darklight Tower"), and west of the [Ruins of Rkund](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund "Skyrim:Ruins of Rkund").

It is a bare bones affair. Tents have been set up, but the grass has not been cleared; there is no storage, crafting station, resting place, or furniture. It is occupied by [Crowstooth's](/wiki/Skyrim:Crowstooth "Skyrim:Crowstooth") forces, who were separated from her prior to the events of [Bones for a Crow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bones_for_a_Crow "Skyrim:Bones for a Crow").

## Walkthrough

The camp is most easily reached by traveling by way of [Darklight Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower "Skyrim:Darklight Tower") from the ground. The exit of the tower opens out onto the same continuous mountainside area as both the camp and the [Ruins of Rkund](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund "Skyrim:Ruins of Rkund"). From here, there is a path to left leading to Rkund and to the right ending at the camp.

On the southeast side of the camp is a fallen tree. Next to the tree is a copy of the [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking") skill book *[Proper Lock Design and Construction](/wiki/Skyrim:Proper_Lock_Design "Skyrim:Proper Lock Design")*.

Once these forces are defeated, Crowstooth's second-in-command, [Linelle](/wiki/Skyrim:Linelle "Skyrim:Linelle"), must be looted for [her note](/wiki/Skyrim:Linelle%27s_Note "Skyrim:Linelle's Note"), which has information on where Crowstooth has gone.

## Notes

- The enemies here, including Linelle the Hagraven, will respawn after the quest is completed. Linelle's note does not respawn.
- The lockpicking skill book, *Proper Lock Design and Construction*, is the same one as mentioned at [Darklight Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower "Skyrim:Darklight Tower"). The camp makes it easier to locate, but won't be present until you start the related quest.
