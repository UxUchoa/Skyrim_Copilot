Atheron Residence

**Atheron Residence** is the home of the Atheron family in the lower Gray Quarter of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). It is located across the street from [New Gnisis Cornerclub](/wiki/Skyrim:New_Gnisis_Cornerclub "Skyrim:New Gnisis Cornerclub").

The house is inhabited by street vendor [Aval](/wiki/Skyrim:Aval_Atheron "Skyrim:Aval Atheron"), farm worker [Faryl](/wiki/Skyrim:Faryl_Atheron "Skyrim:Faryl Atheron") and their sister, [Suvaris](/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron "Skyrim:Suvaris Atheron"). It consists of one area, *[Atheron Residence](#Atheron_Residence)*.

## Atheron Residence

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-interior-Atheron_Residence_05.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Atheron_Residence_05.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Atheron_Residence_05.jpg)

The Ground Floor

Like all other houses in the Gray Quarter, the Atheron Residence is in poor shape. The front door opens to the sight of a sparsely decorated dining area with a ladder leading upstairs. Three food sacks sit next to the door, with an additional six sacks on the shelves near the two fireplaces. The eastern corner has a bit of food scattered around, some [dog meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat "Skyrim:Dog Meat"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), and a head of [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"). The dinner table in the center holds some cheap food, a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), a slice of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), a [pile of salt](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a sample of [troll fat](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat "Skyrim:Troll Fat"), and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). The most valuable items can be found in a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Knapsack "Skyrim:Containers") underneath the ladder; this contains leveled items. Note that this knapsack respawns, and is not safe to store items in. An entrance in the western corner leads to a room with a few tables and a single bed, owned by [Suvaris Atheron](/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron "Skyrim:Suvaris Atheron"). Along the northern wall are two sets of shelves with a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina") and two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), along with some common books. Next to the bed is a dresser with [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing "Skyrim:Clothing") with a sample of [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota"), another potion of minor stamina, and some [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks") on top. The small table nearby only has a bottle of alto wine and some bread on display. Lastly, in the southeastern corner stands a wardrobe with clothes.

Second Floor

When you head up the ladder you will find a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") near a table with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") in a bowl. Nearby is a food sack and a dresser with clothes. The entrance in the northwestern corner leads to [Aval Atheron](/wiki/Skyrim:Aval_Atheron "Skyrim:Aval Atheron")'s room, with a single bed against the western wall. The northern wall has a wardrobe and a dresser, both with clothes. The small table has a bottle of alto wine and a piece of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). Back in the room with the ladder is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") and three food sacks. The table near the eastern wall holds a [boiled creme treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat") and a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple"). The shelves near the entrance to the second room hold little of interest, with a tipped-over food sack on the ground nearby. The room is where [Faryl Atheron](/wiki/Skyrim:Faryl_Atheron "Skyrim:Faryl Atheron") sleeps, with a handy bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") on the ground next to the bed and the wardrobe with clothes. At the foot of the bed is an end table with clutter, with a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina") on top. Following the wall is another dresser, with a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a copy of *[The Ransom of Zarek](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ransom_of_Zarek "Skyrim:The Ransom of Zarek")* on top, followed by a chest with random items, a food barrel and a food sack. The small table has an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and a slice of bread.

## Notes

- All containers in the area are non-respawning, with the exception of some barrels, sacks, and the knapsack.
