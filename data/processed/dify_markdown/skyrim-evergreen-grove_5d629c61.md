Evergreen Grove

**Evergreen Grove** is a grove of trees concealing a small pond fed by a rocky stream, just southwest of [Half-Moon Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill "Skyrim:Half-Moon Mill").

It can be found downstream from the mill in the western part of [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold").

### Evergreen Grove

There are several ancient [Nordic](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") features, including large partially submerged upright stones and a stone altar. The body of an [alchemist](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemist "Skyrim:Alchemist") lies on the altar, along with a copy of the [alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mannimarco, King of Worms](/wiki/Skyrim:Mannimarco,_King_of_Worms "Skyrim:Mannimarco, King of Worms")*.

A couple of [spriggans](/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan "Skyrim:Spriggan") are found around the murky pond. There are several [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle") and a single [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot") as well. Insects flock to this site including numerous [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Dartwing "Skyrim:Blue Dartwing") and [orange dartwings](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing") that skitter across the surface of the water. At night [luna moths](/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth "Skyrim:Luna Moth") and [torchbugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug "Skyrim:Torchbug") can be found. It can be hard to see them, but small fish such as [Cyrodilic spadetail](/wiki/Skyrim:Cyrodilic_Spadetail "Skyrim:Cyrodilic Spadetail") and [Abacean longfins](/wiki/Skyrim:Abacean_Longfin "Skyrim:Abacean Longfin") inhabit the shallow waters. With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") creation, this pool is the only open world source of the rare fish [Lyretail Anthias](/wiki/Skyrim:Lyretail_Anthias "Skyrim:Lyretail Anthias"), and [Spadefish](/wiki/Skyrim:Spadefish "Skyrim:Spadefish") can also be found here.

If you follow the stream that feeds the pond upstream toward the [Half-Moon Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill "Skyrim:Half-Moon Mill"), take care as there are several [mudcrabs](/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab "Skyrim:Mudcrab") inhabiting the banks. You will find a muddy pool with three more nirnroots growing beside it. This is an unmarked location referred to as the Alchemist's Camp. It is complete with an old, extinguished campfire with a metal pot hanging over it containing an [ingredient](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"). Beside the fire are a wooden block with a copy of the alchemy skill book *[De Rerum Dirennis](/wiki/Skyrim:De_Rerum_Dirennis "Skyrim:De Rerum Dirennis")* on top, and a small tent complete with bedroll. To the left of the tent are a crate and two [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sacks, with an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel") and the [alchemist's journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemist%27s_Journal_(camp) "Skyrim:Alchemist's Journal (camp)") on top. There is also another nirnroot shortly upstream from the camp. The journal refers to where the alchemist has camped as a "mushroom grove" and you'll find several patches of [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") around the trees behind his tent.

## Notes

- Use of shouts and magic can send the body and book from the altar flying into the pond or along the shore, making them difficult to find.
- It's possible to find the body and book on shore on your first entry without using any magic or shouts.
- This is a good place to harvest Nirnroot because you can find five of them at the location itself or in the nearby vicinity. You can find one sample on the alchemist's body, too.
- The water flowing into the pond is also flowing into Lake Ilinalta. There is a spot near the Alchemist's Camp where you can see it flowing in both directions.
