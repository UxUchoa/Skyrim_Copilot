Clearspring Tarn

**Clearspring Tarn** is a small mountain pool on the cliff overlooking [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch"), due east of [Snapleg Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Snapleg_Cave "Skyrim:Snapleg Cave") and west of [Shor's Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone "Skyrim:Shor's Stone").

## Clearspring Tarn

Beyond its aesthetic beauty, the tarn is mostly a landmark for the location of the [Clearspring Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Clearspring_Cave "Skyrim:Clearspring Cave") that is hidden beneath it. An apprentice-locked wooden [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") can be found in the water along with a surprising variety of small fish that can be caught for use in [alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy"). [Goldfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldfish "Skyrim:Goldfish") can also be found in the water.

You may encounter a pair of [hunters](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter") that are busy pursuing their quarry as there are a large number of elk in the region.

There is a small cave to the west of the tarn with a path heading northeast past the tarn, after a short way the path splits with the right fork leading back under the tarn to the entrance to Clearspring Cave and the left fork heading in the direction of [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing").

To achieve "Cleared" status, kill the Troll in Clearspring Cave below.
