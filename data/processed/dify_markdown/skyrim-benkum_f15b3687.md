Benkum

**Benkum** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") found at the [Water Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Water_Stone "Skyrim:Water Stone") in Solstheim.

Initially from [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), Benkum, along with fellow sailors [Liesl](/wiki/Skyrim:Liesl "Skyrim:Liesl"), [Hjalfar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjalfar "Skyrim:Hjalfar"), and [Palevius Lex](/wiki/Skyrim:Palevius_Lex "Skyrim:Palevius Lex"), decided to cast anchor along the western coast of Solstheim and immediately fell prey to [Miraak](/wiki/Skyrim:Miraak_(person) "Skyrim:Miraak (person)")'s spell. He works around the clock at the stone, oblivious to the world around him. Once the stone has been [cleansed](/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_the_Stones "Skyrim:Cleansing the Stones"), the entire crew will return to their boat on the coast. When you leave the area, they will all disappear from the game.

He wears [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes"), [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"), and [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1 "Skyrim:Gloves"), and he carries a [steel sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword "Skyrim:Steel Sword").

## Dialogue

:   *"We're already late. Not sure why we decided to take such an extended shore time."*
:   *"When the tide goes, we'll go with it."*
:   *"It's a long way back to Windhelm from here."*
:   *"Hope our shipments are still good. How long has it been?"*
:   *"Best be shoving off now."*

## Notes

- There's an unused NPC in the base game named [Benkum](/wiki/Skyrim:Unused_NPCs#Benkum "Skyrim:Unused NPCs") who was intended to manage the wagers for the removed arena in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

## Bugs

- Benkum and the other sailors are sometimes not removed along with their ship, and are left stranded by the beach down west from the Water Stone.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.2, fixes this bug.
