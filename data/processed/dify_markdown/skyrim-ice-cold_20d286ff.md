## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Marise Aravel](/wiki/Skyrim:Marise_Aravel "Skyrim:Marise Aravel").
2. Find five [ice wraith teeth](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth "Skyrim:Ice Wraith Teeth").
3. Return to Marise.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Wander around [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") and you can find [Marise Aravel](/wiki/Skyrim:Marise_Aravel "Skyrim:Marise Aravel") and her food store at the market. She will introduce her food to you with pride: *"My food is the best in all of Skyrim; always as fresh as the day it was collected."* If you ask her for the reason to this, she will tell you she picked up a trick from a butcher in [Elinhir](/wiki/Lore:Elinhir "Lore:Elinhir"), a small town over the border in [Hammerfell](/wiki/Lore:Hammerfell "Lore:Hammerfell"). Grinding up [ice wraith teeth](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth "Skyrim:Ice Wraith Teeth") and using it as a preservative is the key to keeping everything fresh. Then she will request you to bring her five of them to replenish her supply. You may ask her for some ideas where to find some ice wraiths. She will remind you of some potential danger and suggest you to travel to [Ancient's Ascent](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient%27s_Ascent "Skyrim:Ancient's Ascent").

You may buy ice wraith teeth from [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") shops (most have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples). Five samples can be found in the [Hall of Attainment](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Attainment "Skyrim:Hall of Attainment") or [Jorrvaskr Living Quarters](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorrvaskr_Living_Quarters "Skyrim:Jorrvaskr Living Quarters") and four in [the White Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Hall "Skyrim:The White Hall"). If you prefer to hunt some ice wraiths, you can easily find them in the wilderness of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"). You can also find them in the following locations:

- [Ancient's Ascent](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient%27s_Ascent "Skyrim:Ancient's Ascent"), recommended location, actually in [Bonechill Passage](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonechill_Passage "Skyrim:Bonechill Passage")
- [Forsaken Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsaken_Cave "Skyrim:Forsaken Cave"), the previous part of the first zone
- [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World"), the path to [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar")
- [Tolvald's Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Cave "Skyrim:Tolvald's Cave"), the previous part of the first zone
- [Weynon Stones](/wiki/Skyrim:Weynon_Stones "Skyrim:Weynon Stones"), two ice wraiths will ambush you there

Once you have them, return to Marise. She will be extremely happy: *"Perfect, just perfect! These should help keep my stock fresh for quite a while."* She will reward you with an apprentice-level [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") [spell tome](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tomes "Skyrim:Spell Tomes") and five [pheasant breasts](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast").

## Bugs

- After completing this quest once, you may receive the first quest objective in your journal again. The counter does not increase upon adding ice wraith teeth to your inventory, and the objective is stuck in your quest log permanently.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.
  - Collect 10 or 15 Ice Wraith Teeth instead so that once you turn in 5, the counter maxes out at 5 again (thus successfully completing the quest).
