## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to Moth gro-Bagol.
2. Return with a [Daedra heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra_Heart "Skyrim:Daedra Heart").

## Detailed Walkthrough

Moth gro-Bagol has been ordered to make a special weapon for the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). To do so he needs to dip it in the blood of a [Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra "Skyrim:Daedra"). All he needs is a heart though, and would appreciate it if you could bring him one.

He will give you a random base piece of armor in return.

## Notes

- This quest raises his [disposition](/wiki/Skyrim:Disposition "Skyrim:Disposition") towards you, allowing you to [marry](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") him.
