Alva

**Alva** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") sent by [Movarth Piquine](/wiki/Skyrim:Movarth_Piquine "Skyrim:Movarth Piquine") to [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") to transform some of the townsfolk into vampires while using the rest as cattle.

She is good friends with [Lami](/wiki/Skyrim:Lami "Skyrim:Lami") (the proprietor of the [Thaumaturgist's Hut](/wiki/Skyrim:Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut "Skyrim:Thaumaturgist's Hut")), and is the lover of [Hroggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hroggar "Skyrim:Hroggar"), who is also her thrall (and therefore may not willingly love her).

Normally, she will spend her day at either the [Moorside Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Moorside_Inn "Skyrim:Moorside Inn") or the lumber mill. This changes once you talk to [Thonnir](/wiki/Skyrim:Thonnir "Skyrim:Thonnir") during [Laid To Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Laid_to_Rest "Skyrim:Laid to Rest"), as she will begin sleeping in a coffin in the basement of her house during the day (from 6am to 10pm) while Hroggar guards the house. At that point, Alva will leave her house only at night and wander around, most likely looking for people to turn.

She wears a set of [tavern clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Tavern_Clothes "Skyrim:Tavern Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000d1921 "Skyrim:Clothing"), and is equipped with a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"). She carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Alva%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Alva's House") to [her house](/wiki/Skyrim:Alva%27s_House "Skyrim:Alva's House"), at least one leveled [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") with a small chance of up to four, and a significant amount of leveled gold, ranging from 50 to 250 gold.

She knows the spells [Vampiric Drain](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampiric_Drain "Skyrim:Vampiric Drain") and [Reanimate Corpse](/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate_Corpse "Skyrim:Reanimate Corpse"), which she will use when in combat.

During the quest [Laid to Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Laid_to_Rest "Skyrim:Laid to Rest"), she is normally killed by you if she is in the house when you break in to get [her journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Alva%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Alva's Journal"). If she is not in the house, it is not necessary to kill her to complete the quest. If she is not killed she will be found in [Movarth's Lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Movarth%27s_Lair "Skyrim:Movarth's Lair") later on.

Some other town members in Morthal will mention Hroggar's love for her and say they suspect he killed his wife and child to be with her, as he got together with her almost right after his house burned down.

## Related Quests

- **[Laid to Rest](/wiki/Skyrim:Laid_to_Rest "Skyrim:Laid to Rest")**: Investigate a tragic house fire, and discover an insidious plot against the town.

## Dialogue

If you have not yet discovered her secret, Alva will be quite flirtatious towards you if you are male, saying:

:   *"Well, aren't you the handsome one. You and I should spend some time together."*

If you are female, she will instead remark:

:   *"Well, aren't you the pretty one. I bet all the men chase after you."*

However, if you have [vampirism](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampirism "Skyrim:Vampirism"), she will drop all pretense and try to warn you off with

:   *"I know you for what you are. This town's blood is ours. Leave it! Now we must return to our roles lest the sheep suspect the wolves."*

She may also make ominous comments about the town's fate, saying:

:   *"The people here work so hard, and they never allow themselves a moment of fun. It's such a pity. It won't last forever though."*
:   *"These people are like cattle. All they do is work, sleep and eat."*
:   *"Gathering plants, picking mushrooms, brewing potions. It all gets so dreary after a while. This town needs something exciting to happen."*

## Conversations

She can be seen having this conversation with Hroggar in her house if you sneak inside:

:   **Hroggar**: *"How are you feeling?"*
:   **Alva**: *"I feel wonderful, just like every night."*
:   **Hroggar**: *"Do you need anything? Are you hungry?"*
:   **Alva**: *"Oh Hroggar, you know I'm always hungry. Don't worry, I'll eat when I need to."*
:   **Alva**: *"Oh, look! We have company!"*

When at the Moorside Inn, Alva will often try to seduce locals, likely with dubious intentions. [Benor](/wiki/Skyrim:Benor "Skyrim:Benor") is one of these unlucky few:

:   **Alva**: *"I'm bored, Benor. Nobody wants to have any fun with me."*
:   **Benor**: *"If you leave that kinslayer Hroggar, I would be happy to keep your company."*
:   **Alva**: *"Hroggar doesn't have to know if we sit together. Or drink together. I doubt he would even care."*
:   **Benor**: *"You tempt me mightily, Alva. But I won't cuckhold*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *another man, even on*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *murderer like Hroggar."*

Benor's friend and resident housecarl [Gorm](/wiki/Skyrim:Gorm "Skyrim:Gorm") will get a similar offer:

:   **Alva**: *"Would you like to buy a lady a drink?"*
:   **Gorm**: *"What would Hroggar say if he heard I was drinking with you?"*
:   **Alva**: *"Forget about Hroggar. He's always so tired lately. I need someone with a little fire in their blood."*
:   **Gorm**: *"After all you've put that man through. You should be more loyal to him."*

She will even try to seduce the innkeeper, [Jonna](/wiki/Skyrim:Jonna "Skyrim:Jonna"):

:   **Alva**: *"Why, good evening Jonna!"*
:   **Jonna**: *"What can I getcha, Alva?"*
:   **Alva**: *"What can't you get me, sweetie?"*
:   **Jonna**: *"Umm... Did you want somethin' to eat?"*
:   **Alva**: *"Eat? No, I don't think I'm... hungry. Not now, at least."*
:   **Jonna**: *"Right. Okay, tell me if you change yer mind."*

## Notes

- If Alva is not killed before raiding [Movarth's Lair](/wiki/Skyrim:Movarth%27s_Lair "Skyrim:Movarth's Lair"), she will be found there, fighting against other vampires, and will not be hostile toward you. Attacking her there will be considered a crime.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.6, addresses this issue. She is no longer hostile towards the other vampires and is hostile towards you instead.
- After the end of Laid to Rest, Alva is supposed to be removed from the Morthal crime faction, but isn't.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.8, addresses this issue. The script call was doing it wrong.
- If she is not killed in Movarth's Lair at the end of Laid to Rest, she will be found back in Morthal a few days later. Attacking her is considered a crime at this point.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.9, addresses this issue. She will no longer return to Morthal once she goes to Movarth's Lair.
- Both [Lami](/wiki/Skyrim:Lami "Skyrim:Lami") and [Hroggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hroggar "Skyrim:Hroggar") have a high disposition towards Alva. If she is killed, their disposition towards the player will be reduced to -2.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. Their disposition towards Alva will be reset to 0, but only after the player picks up the journal in the basement.
- Alva is one of the few NPCs to recognize you as a vampire regardless of the stage of vampirism.
- She is not actually a vampire by race, and her race is instead that of a normal Nord. This means that her appearance will not change with [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") installed. However, she will still use the Vampiric Drain spell, and the [Sun Damage](/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Damage "Skyrim:Sun Damage") and [Turn Undead](/wiki/Skyrim:Turn_Undead_(effect) "Skyrim:Turn Undead (effect)") effects do work on her.
- If you have already killed [Laelette](/wiki/Skyrim:Laelette_the_Vampire "Skyrim:Laelette the Vampire"), Alva will comment *"I hear you put Laelette out of her misery."*
