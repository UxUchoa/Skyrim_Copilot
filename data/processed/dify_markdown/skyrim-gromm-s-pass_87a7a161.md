Gromm's Pass

**Gromm's Pass** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") located in [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"), south of [Forelhost](/wiki/Skyrim:Forelhost "Skyrim:Forelhost"). Until a recent collapse, it served as an underground pass through the [Jerall Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Jerall_Mountains "Skyrim:Jerall Mountains") to [Cyrodiil](/wiki/Lore:Cyrodiil "Lore:Cyrodiil").

It consists of one zone: [Gromm's Pass](#Gromm.27s_Pass).

## Related Quests

- **[Blue in the Face](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_in_the_Face "Skyrim:Blue in the Face")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblins "Skyrim:Goblins"): Investigate rumors of the Blue God's return.

### Exterior

The cave entrance is marked by a lit brazier among the bones and blood. A [bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear") spawns right where the fast travel marker is, so be prepared for an ambush if you aren't approaching on foot. An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") can be found to the right of the entrance.

### Gromm's Pass

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg)

Gromm's Pass interior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass_02.jpg)

Gogh next to the throne

Not far from the entrance are two goblins guarding, on ground are seven dead skeletons. Head north and see the dead [Avanessa Calladius](/wiki/Skyrim:Avanessa_Calladius "Skyrim:Avanessa Calladius"), nearby are six dead skeletons. Head west there's another dead skeleton on ground, and a goblin guarding the north west corridor. Head west and find a large room with a pit at center, in the pit [Gogh](/wiki/Skyrim:Gogh "Skyrim:Gogh") is fighting against three goblins. West of the pit are two kneeled goblins worshipping a leader named [Blue God](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_God "Skyrim:Blue God"), the Blue God carries a unique staff [Goblin Totem Staff](/wiki/Skyrim:Staves#Goblin_Totem_Staff "Skyrim:Staves")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblins "Skyrim:Goblins"). There're several dead corpses in the large room and in the pit. Head west is a small room containing a boss chest. Head north and walk into a small room stocked plenty of [Steel-Blue Entolomas](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel-Blue_Entoloma "Skyrim:Steel-Blue Entoloma"). North of the large room mentioned before is a corridor, north of the corridor is a small mining room, a goblin is face away to the player and mining. South of the corridor leads to a way blocked by spears and a chain, pull the chain to lower the spears and reach to pit in the large room mentioned before, [Gogh](/wiki/Skyrim:Gogh "Skyrim:Gogh") is in the pit and can be talked with to release him as a fighting type pet.

## Notes

- This cave is the only place where [Steel-Blue Entoloma](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel-Blue_Entoloma "Skyrim:Steel-Blue Entoloma") grow naturally.
- If [Gogh](/wiki/Skyrim:Gogh "Skyrim:Gogh") leaves your service as a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"), he will return to the throne room here.
- A Nord named [Gromm](/wiki/Oblivion:Gromm "Oblivion:Gromm") appears in *[Oblivion](/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion "Oblivion:Oblivion")*.
