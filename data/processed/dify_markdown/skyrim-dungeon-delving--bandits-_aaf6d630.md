## Detailed Walkthrough

It is a standard fetch-and-return quest. The item will be found in the boss chest in the target dungeon, and is treated as a [quest item](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items "Skyrim:Quest Items").

### Amren's Family Sword

When you encounter Amren, he may be arguing with his wife [Saffir](/wiki/Skyrim:Saffir "Skyrim:Saffir"). If asked about the argument, Amren will tell you about his father's sword:

:   *My Saffir doesn't like that I've been spending so much time looking for my father's old sword. He fed his entire family with the gold he made using that weapon. I'm not about to let it gather dust in some thief's trophy room.*

Volunteering to retrieve the sword for him triggers the start of the quest.

When you return the sword, Amren gives you a one point bonus in both [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") and [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed"), commenting, "*Here, my father had a favorite sword and shield technique. Let me teach it to you, in honor of his memory.*"

### Shahvee's Amulet of Zenithar

If you talk to Shahvee you may have the option to ask her, "Your people have bad luck?" In response she will tell you:

:   *We have not faired*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *well outside our native [Black Marsh](/wiki/Lore:Black_Marsh "Lore:Black Marsh"), but we're determined to make the best of things. I began praying to [Zenithar](/wiki/Lore:Zenithar "Lore:Zenithar"), the god of wealth, to bring us some fortune, but thieves made off with my sacred amulet almost as soon as I bought it.*

Volunteering to retrieve the amulet triggers the start of the quest.

When you return the amulet, Shahvee gives you a one point bonus in both [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") and [Lockpicking](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpicking "Skyrim:Lockpicking"), commenting, "*Before I came into Zenithar's calling, I confess I was a thief. But I learned a few things in my time that might help you survive. Let me show you.*"

## Notes

- Because this is a favor quest which affects the quest giver's disposition, it will count towards the "Help the People" part of the [thane](/wiki/Skyrim:Thane "Skyrim:Thane") quest for the quest giver's hold.
- It is impossible to do both quests at the same time, e.g., if you do the quest for Amren, you are unable to do it for Shahvee until you have completed the quest for Amren.
- Shahvee's Amulet can spawn in Uttering Hills Camp, a location that also has a chance for Annekke Crag-Jumper or Brunwulf Free-Winter's personal quests. If they do, none of these quests can be taken at the same time. Waiting 30 days outside their map cells will allow one of the other quests to be taken.

## Bugs

- You may be unable to receive this quest from Shahvee, even if none of the other quests are active.
