Deor Woodcutter's House

**Deor Woodcutter's House** is the home of [Deor Woodcutter](/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter "Skyrim:Deor Woodcutter") and [Yrsa](/wiki/Skyrim:Yrsa "Skyrim:Yrsa") in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

It is on the south side of the village, between [Oslaf's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf%27s_House "Skyrim:Oslaf's House") to the northwest and [Baldor Iron-Shaper's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Baldor_Iron-Shaper%27s_House "Skyrim:Baldor Iron-Shaper's House") to the east.

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). Along the east wall is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), two pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"), a wood pile, and a [blue mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"). Along the west wall are two more wood piles. The entrance has a novice lock.

### Deor Woodcutter's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-interior-Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House.jpg)

Deor Woodcutter's House interior

The entrance leads into a large main room with an end table on your left with a [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") and [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") on top. here are many sacksof food throughout. Against the south wall is a wood pile with six pieces of firewood and a woodcutter's axe leaning against the pile. Above is a wall shelf holding a sprig of snowberries. In the southwest corner is a doorway to a double bedroom. In the middle of the room is a small square table and two chairs. On the table is a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") and [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"). Against the west wall is a crate, a barrel with a mead barrel on top and above it all is a wall shelf holding an [ice wolf pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf_Pelt "Skyrim:Ice Wolf Pelt"). To the right of the entrance are three barrels, with a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") on top of one of them. To the left is a set of shelves containing a copy of *[Deathbrand](/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_(book) "Skyrim:Deathbrand (book)")*. Around the corner is a fire pit with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") and a cast iron pot containing a cabbage beside it. Above the cooking pot is a hanging rack containing three whole [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") and a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braid. To the west along the north wall the room continues alongside the bedroom with a narrow table against the north wall with a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and a copy of *[The Woodcutter's Wife](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Woodcutter%27s_Wife "Skyrim:The Woodcutter's Wife")* on top. At the rear against the west wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of ale, a cast iron pot containing three pieces of [clam meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat "Skyrim:Clam Meat"), an open crate containing two loaves of bread, and two [eidar cheese wheels](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel").

Entering the bedroom there is a cupboard on your left with a [deer hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide "Skyrim:Deer Hide"), a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and copy of *[Final Lesson](/wiki/Skyrim:Final_Lesson "Skyrim:Final Lesson")*. Against the west wall is a dresser with a bottle and [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and a wooden bowl with a piece of clam meat inside. Coming out from the north wall is a double bed with an end table on either side. On the one on the left is a loaf of bread and bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), while on the one on the right is a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge").
