Confidence

**Confidence** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [citizen](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)") who is an aspect of [Pelagius the Mad's](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_the_Mad "Skyrim:Pelagius the Mad") [mind](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_a_Madman "Skyrim:The Mind of a Madman"). During the related quest, the much larger [Anger](/wiki/Skyrim:Anger "Skyrim:Anger") will be beating up Confidence, and you will be tasked with evening the odds with the help of the [Wabbajack](/wiki/Skyrim:Wabbajack "Skyrim:Wabbajack").

Confidence wears the common bartender [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0005b6a1 "Skyrim:Clothes") and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0 "Skyrim:Shoes").

## Related Quests

- **[The Mind of Madness](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness "Skyrim:The Mind of Madness")**: Gain access to and solve the mystery of the [Pelagius Wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing "Skyrim:Pelagius Wing").

## Notes

- Unlike most of the other NPCs in the Mind of Madness, he can be pickpocketed, but only after Anger is defeated.
