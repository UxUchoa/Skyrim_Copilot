**Dulug** is an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:GuardOrc1H "Skyrim:GuardOrc1H") living in [Dushnikh Yal](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnikh_Yal "Skyrim:Dushnikh Yal"), the [Orc stronghold](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") southeast of [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). She works in [Dushnikh Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Dushnikh_Mine "Skyrim:Dushnikh Mine"), mining [orichalcum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore") along with [Ghak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghak "Skyrim:Ghak"), [Mahk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mahk "Skyrim:Mahk"), and [Oglub](/wiki/Skyrim:Oglub "Skyrim:Oglub").

Dulug spends her entire life inside the mine and never sleeps. She divides her time between hacking away on the ore veins, relaxing, or eating.

She wears simple clothing, a pair of [ragged trousers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers "Skyrim:Ragged Trousers") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"). She carries her [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), a leveled [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe"), and the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Key to Burguk's Longhouse") to [Burguk's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Burguk%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Burguk's Longhouse"), along with a selection of [middle-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").
