Treva River

**Treva River** is a river in the center of [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift").

Geographically, the Treva River should flow from [Lake Honrich](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Honrich "Skyrim:Lake Honrich") past [Treva's Watch](/wiki/Skyrim:Treva%27s_Watch "Skyrim:Treva's Watch") and into [Lake Geir](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Geir "Skyrim:Lake Geir") (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).
