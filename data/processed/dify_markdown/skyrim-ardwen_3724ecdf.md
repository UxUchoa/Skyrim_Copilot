Ardwen on her pilgrimage

**Ardwen** is a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") pilgrim to the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") that can be [randomly encountered](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Pilgrim_visiting_the_tree_in_Whiterun "Skyrim:World Interactions") with her [dog](/wiki/Skyrim:Dog "Skyrim:Dog") after [The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature") is complete, if you restored the Gildergreen with sap. She does not appear if you completed the quest by bringing a sapling back. She will continue traveling from where you encounter her until she reaches [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").

She is a respectable [elemental mage](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatMageElemental "Skyrim:CombatMageElemental"), able to cast a leveled selection of spells from the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction"), [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration"), and [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") schools. Her outfit consists of [blue mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Blue Mage Robes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0006b46c "Skyrim:Boots").

## Related Quests

- **[The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature")**: Restore the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") for [Danica Pure-Spring](/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring "Skyrim:Danica Pure-Spring").

## Dialogue

If spoken to, she will tell you the reason for her journey:

:   *"We are traveling to [the Shrine of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Temple of Kynareth") in Whiterun. I hear the Gildergreen is more beautiful than ever since it's been restored. They say that eating the fruit it bears will add years to your life."*

## Notes

- If you follow her, she will eventually end up standing in front of the Gildergreen in Whiterun and will begin praying there.
- After completing her related quest, she will often just end up standing near the [Whiterun stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables "Skyrim:Whiterun Stables") 24 hours a day. She can be engaged in conversation there, but will only say *"Hello"*, with no further dialogue available.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. Her random encounter quest will now terminate if she has arrived and you change locations to anywhere other than Whiterun.
