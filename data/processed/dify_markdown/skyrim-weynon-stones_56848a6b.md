The Weynon Stones

The **Weynon Stones** are a [landmark](/wiki/Skyrim:Landmarks "Skyrim:Landmarks") consisting of a circle of stones. It is located between [Korvanjund](/wiki/Skyrim:Korvanjund "Skyrim:Korvanjund") and [Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dunstad "Skyrim:Fort Dunstad"), just south of the path.

It is home to a pair of [ice wraiths](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith") who will attack as you approach. This can be a challenging battle at low levels or if you don't have any resistance to frost magic.

## Walkthrough

As you enter the circle of stones, the two ice wraiths will appear and attack. There is an empty altar up a flight of stone stairs with a large stone head above it. Beside the altar is a [burial urn](/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn#Urn "Skyrim:Burial Urn"). Behind the large stone head is a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") with a novice-level lock containing random [loot](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). Opposite this is a [shrine of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Talos "Skyrim:Shrines"). On the shrine are two [steel ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot "Skyrim:Steel Ingot"), a pair of [iron gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets"), and two pieces of a [broken steel](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Steel_Battle_Axe_Head "Skyrim:Broken Steel Battle Axe Head") [battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Steel_Battle_Axe_Handle "Skyrim:Broken Steel Battle Axe Handle"). The two pieces cannot be picked up. In the surrounding area there are several [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes.
