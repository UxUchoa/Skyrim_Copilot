Oslaf's House

**Oslaf's House** is the home of [Oslaf](/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf "Skyrim:Oslaf"), [Finna](/wiki/Skyrim:Finna "Skyrim:Finna"), and their daughter [Aeta](/wiki/Skyrim:Aeta "Skyrim:Aeta") in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

It is on the southwest side of the village, between [Deor Woodcutter's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House "Skyrim:Deor Woodcutter's House") to the southeast and [Wulf Wild-Blood's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Wulf_Wild-Blood%27s_House "Skyrim:Wulf Wild-Blood's House") to the north.

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance is an empty cart. Along the east wall is the entrance to a pen holding two [goats](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat"). Behind the house is a small vegetable plot containing two [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), five [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), four [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and six [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), with two [blue mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower") in and near the goat pen. To the right of the entrance is a crate and two fish barrels. The entrance has a novice lock.

### Oslaf's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House.jpg)

Home cooking

The entrance leads into a small room with a fire pit in the center and a rack holding a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"), two [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") samples, and two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids. Beside the fire pit is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") on a stand. On your right against the east wall is a sideboard with a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") on top. In the northeast corner is a small round table and two chairs. On the north wall are four shelves containing a loaf of bread, three [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a leek and a cast iron pot containing a cabbage. In the northwest corner is a set of shelves containing a [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"), a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge"), a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"), three bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and two sacks. Against the west wall on a ledge are two more sacks. Three small steps lead up to the south into the main part of the house.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House_02.jpg)

Cozy living

In the next room there is a wall shelf on your left with a goat cheese wheel, a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") and two [ash yams](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam"). On your right is a sack and in the northwest corner another small round table and three chairs. On the table is a bowl of [beef stew](/wiki/Skyrim:Beef_Stew "Skyrim:Beef Stew") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"). Above the table on the north wall are two wall shelves containing three potatoes, a bottle of wine and a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat"). In the southwest corner is a cupboard and on the shelves are two potatoes, a cabbage and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and on top of the cupboard is a sack. There is a narrow table against the south wall with two sacks underneath. Leading up to the upstairs is a flight of stairs against the east wall, with a doorway underneath, this leads to Aeta's bedroom that has a small square table and two chairs in front of you, with a [Horker loaf](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Loaf "Skyrim:Horker Loaf") on a plate on the table and against the north wall a single bed with an end table has a copy of *[Words of the Wind](/wiki/Skyrim:Words_of_the_Wind "Skyrim:Words of the Wind")* and a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") on top.

Climbing the stairs, there is an end table in the southeast corner with a low table and two chairs to the left. On the table is a copy of *[Kolb & the Dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolb_%26_the_Dragon "Skyrim:Kolb & the Dragon")*. To your left is a doorway into Oslaf and Finna's bedroom. As you enter there is a wardrobe on your left against the south wall with a chair next to it. In the northwest corner is an end table with a wooden bowl containing a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") on top. A double bed is to the right of it coming out from the north wall. Beside the bed against the east wall is an open crate with three pairs of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") in. Finally in the southeast corner is a small round table with a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") on top.
