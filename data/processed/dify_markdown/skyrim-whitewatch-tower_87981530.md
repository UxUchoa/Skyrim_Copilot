Whitewatch Tower

**Whitewatch Tower** is a small [tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Towers "Skyrim:Imperial Towers") north-northeast of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").

## Walkthrough

Depending on the faction that currently controls Whiterun, the tower is occupied by either [Whiterun guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Guard "Skyrim:Whiterun Guard") or [Stormcloak soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Soldier "Skyrim:Stormcloak Soldier"). The structure comprises a largely intact eastern watchtower and a ruined western tower connected by an archway. The tower is flanked with yellow Whiterun banners if the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") has control, or blue Stormcloak banners if the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") have control. [Lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") and [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), [purple](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"), and [red mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") grow near the entrance. Passing under the arch into the courtyard, a [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry "Skyrim:Stone Quarry")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is on the south side of the eastern tower near a stone wall. Some meager provisions for the guards are near the arch, while a novice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") among the crates holds more substantial treasure. More lavender grows in the courtyard. A weapon rack to the south holds two [Imperial swords](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword") and a leveled [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") if the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") occupy the tower, or an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"), an [iron battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Iron Battleaxe"), and a leveled [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace") if the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") are in control. Wooden ramps lead up to the eastern tower, which features nothing of interest. However, jumping onto the ledge on the west side of the tower behind the guard's chair and crossing over the archway leads you to the ruined western tower, where an apprentice-locked chest is buried in the rubble.

## Notes

- [Bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") may attack the tower as you approach.
- Caution is advised when unlocking the chests around the tower; if the guards see you, you will receive a bounty.
- If you unlock the apprentice-level chest buried in the rubble, you may not get a bounty.
- If the leveled sword next to the Imperial swords is an iron or steel sword, you can take it without penalty.
- A leveled strongbox is to the northwest between some stone blocks on the top of a hill.
- An [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") and a [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") are 250 feet south of the tower at the base of the [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach") hill.
- A [gold ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") is 400 feet south of the tower up the hill. Just east of the vein is a novice-locked chest.
- An [unmarked bandit camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Whiterun#Smuggler.27s_Den:_Whiterun "Skyrim:Unmarked Places Whiterun") is southwest of the tower under a cliff at the base of the Dragonsreach hill. It is occupied by leveled bandits and features a wide variety of loot, including alchemical ingredients, weapons, potions, and the [Pickpocket](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickpocket "Skyrim:Pickpocket") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Aevar Stone-Singer](/wiki/Skyrim:Aevar_Stone-Singer "Skyrim:Aevar Stone-Singer")*. The [Smugglers' Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Smugglers%27_Note "Skyrim:Smugglers' Note") can also be found under a table in the camp. A [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") and a [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse") can be found here as well, although the horse must be stolen to be ridden.

## Bugs

- The Imperial swords and the leveled sword may be hovering in the air in front of their respective weapon racks. **?**
- The guards stationed here use the line "Be glad you're here in the city, and not out in Riverwood. Those poor sods don't even have a wall." This line indicates the tower is in the city of Whiterun itself, when it clearly isn't.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.4, addresses this issue. The "Be glad you're here in the city, and not out in Riverwood. Those poor sods don't even have a wall." line has been removed from the guards stationed at the tower.
