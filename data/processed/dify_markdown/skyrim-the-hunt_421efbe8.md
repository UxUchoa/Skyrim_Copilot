## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Fura Bloodmouth](/wiki/Skyrim:Fura_Bloodmouth "Skyrim:Fura Bloodmouth") or [Garan Marethi](/wiki/Skyrim:Garan_Marethi "Skyrim:Garan Marethi").
2. Kill a traveling target ([Traveling Bard](/wiki/Skyrim:Traveling_Bard "Skyrim:Traveling Bard"), [Traveling Merchant](/wiki/Skyrim:Traveling_Merchant "Skyrim:Traveling Merchant"), or [Traveling Pilgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Traveling_Pilgrim "Skyrim:Traveling Pilgrim")).
3. (*Optional*) Make it a discreet kill.
4. Return to the quest giver.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[Fura Bloodmouth](/wiki/Skyrim:Fura_Bloodmouth "Skyrim:Fura Bloodmouth") or [Garan Marethi](/wiki/Skyrim:Garan_Marethi "Skyrim:Garan Marethi") will send you to find and kill the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)") spy masquerading as either a [Traveling Bard](/wiki/Skyrim:Traveling_Bard "Skyrim:Traveling Bard"), [Traveling Merchant](/wiki/Skyrim:Traveling_Merchant "Skyrim:Traveling Merchant") or [Traveling Pilgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Traveling_Pilgrim "Skyrim:Traveling Pilgrim"). Do so discreetly. With a high enough speech skill (75) it is possible to [persuade](/wiki/Skyrim:Persuade "Skyrim:Persuade") them to follow you by telling them you'll take them to the 'monster' they're seeking, or intimidate them into coming with you. This makes it easier to kill them discreetly if you take them to a location where no one will see you.

## Notes

- On rare occasions, the target may spawn inside [Honeyside](/wiki/Skyrim:Honeyside "Skyrim:Honeyside"), which prevents you from reaching the target and completing the quest unless you own (or subsequently purchase) the house.
