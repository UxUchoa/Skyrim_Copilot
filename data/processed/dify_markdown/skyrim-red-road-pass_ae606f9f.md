Red Road Pass

**Red Road Pass** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") south-southwest of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

## Walkthrough

One [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and one [mammoth](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") occupy the camp. When you approach the camp for the first time, three randomly leveled [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") are attacking the giant and mammoth. A large campfire in the center of the camp features three [charred skeevers](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") cooking on stakes. A large stone bowl and a set of steps are south of the fire; a [mammoth cheese bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl") and three large leather pouches filled with mammoth cheese can be found at the top of the steps. An unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") is in a hollowed-out tree to the west, with a patch of [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot") growing next to the chest. The camp is decorated with the painted stones, [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes"), and mammoth skulls typical of giant camps. Several [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes also grow in the area.
