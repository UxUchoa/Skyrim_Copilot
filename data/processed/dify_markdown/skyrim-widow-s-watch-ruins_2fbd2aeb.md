Widow's Watch Ruins

**Widow's Watch Ruins** is a small ruined [tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Towers "Skyrim:Imperial Towers") west-northwest of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") containing a [hag](/wiki/Skyrim:Hag "Skyrim:Hag").

## Walkthrough

This is a typical tower that has tilted due to a landslide, making traversal slightly difficult. Once you have defeated the lone hag, you are free to explore. Keep climbing the structure to the top, then loop around counterclockwise until you reach a higher doorway. Through the doorway, ascend the wooden stairs and cross the wooden plank to reach an expert-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") partially hidden behind a table, next to which is a copy of the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, Rain's Hand, v4](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Rain%27s_Hand,_v4 "Skyrim:2920, Rain's Hand, v4")* behind a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"). There is nothing else of interest here.

## Notes

- Sometimes the book and shield can be dislodged and found elsewhere around the tower and surrounding area.
- Widow's Watch Ruins also appears in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online") as [Sword's Point Watchtower](/wiki/Online:Sword%27s_Point_Watchtower "Online:Sword's Point Watchtower").
- Version 2.0.7 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") replaces the Imperial tower map icon for Widow's Watch Ruins with the one for Nordic towers.
