# Skyrim:Books

## Overview

Hundreds of **books** can be found in Skyrim. Most books provide backstory or cover [lore](/wiki/Lore:Main_Page "Lore:Main Page") material. However, there are three special types of books:

- [Skill Books](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books"): opening the book for the first time gives you a one point increase in a specific [skill](/wiki/Skyrim:Skills "Skyrim:Skills")
- [Spell Tomes](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tomes "Skyrim:Spell Tomes"): opening the book for the first time teaches you a [spell](/wiki/Skyrim:Spells "Skyrim:Spells")
- [Quest Books](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Quest_Books "Category:Skyrim-Quest Books"): books that are needed for or related to [quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Quests "Skyrim:Quests")

These special books are each listed on their own individual page.

Other types of reading material found in the game include [scrolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Scrolls "Skyrim:Scrolls") and [notes and letters](/wiki/Skyrim:Notes "Skyrim:Notes"). For books and notes added by [Creation Club](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"), see [this page](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club_Books "Skyrim:Creation Club Books"). A list of books broken down by subject can be found [here](/wiki/Skyrim:Books_by_Subject "Skyrim:Books by Subject").

## Book Types

Standard books in the game are all placed in one of five leveled lists, as provided on each individual book page.

The primary use of the book type is for books found in randomly-generated loot, including [containers](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers "Skyrim:Containers") and some enemies (in particular, [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") and [Warlocks](/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock "Skyrim:Warlock")). These random books are level-dependent, as specified in the following table. The selection is also weighted to make List 0 books the most common books even at high levels. For example, at level 15 you have a 4 in 9 chance of finding List 0 books, a 3 in 9 chance of finding List 1 books, and a 2 in 9 chance of finding List 2 books.

In addition, the books stocked by [Urag gro-Shub](/wiki/Skyrim:Urag_gro-Shub "Skyrim:Urag gro-Shub") are determined by the book type. His inventory is not level-dependent; even List 4 books can be purchased from him at level 1. However, he only stocks a fixed number of books of each type, meaning that the chances of finding any specific List 4 book are very small.

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [List 0](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Books-List_0 "Category:Skyrim-Books-List 0") | 1 | 4 | 4 | Stocks 10 |
| [List 1](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Books-List_1 "Category:Skyrim-Books-List 1") | 6 | 3 | 7 | Stocks 5 |
| [List 2](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Books-List_2 "Category:Skyrim-Books-List 2") | 12 | 2 | 9 | Stocks 3 |
| [List 3](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Books-List_3 "Category:Skyrim-Books-List 3") | 18 | 2 | 12 | Stocks 1 |
| [List 4](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Books-List_4 "Category:Skyrim-Books-List 4") | 18 | 1 | 12 | Stocks 1 |

Most books sitting out on bookshelves or lying around are randomly-generated. They are usually generated from a list named LItemBookClutter, which represents a combination of lists 0-2 with equal chances, but without *[Boethiah's Proving](/wiki/Skyrim:Boethiah%27s_Proving "Skyrim:Boethiah's Proving")* from list 2. [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") adds *[The Aetherium Wars](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars "Skyrim:The Aetherium Wars")* to it.

## Dragonborn Book Types

Standard books new with Dragonborn are placed into one of four lists ([1](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Dragonborn-Books-List_1 "Category:Skyrim-Dragonborn-Books-List 1"), [2](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Dragonborn-Books-List_2 "Category:Skyrim-Dragonborn-Books-List 2"), [3](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Dragonborn-Books-List_3 "Category:Skyrim-Dragonborn-Books-List 3"), [4](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Dragonborn-Books-List_4 "Category:Skyrim-Dragonborn-Books-List 4")). Most random books found in the open are either from one of those lists, or a combination of those lists and a list from the basegame with the same number. Randomly generated treasure uses an evenly weighted combination of all four lists and lists 2-4 from Skyrim. The books sold by [Fethis Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fethis_Alor "Skyrim:Fethis Alor") are drawn from Dragonborn lists 1-4.
