Irgnir's House

**Irgnir's House** is the house of [Irgnir](/wiki/Skyrim:Irgnir "Skyrim:Irgnir"), located in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

### Exterior

It is next to [Iron-Breaker Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron-Breaker_Mine "Skyrim:Iron-Breaker Mine"), at the end of the path through town, past [The White Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Hall "Skyrim:The White Hall"). There is a good view of the bay from the partially covered porch in front of the house, between [Brina's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Brina%27s_House "Skyrim:Brina's House") and [The Mortar and Pestle](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mortar_and_Pestle "Skyrim:The Mortar and Pestle"). To the left of the main steps up to the porch is a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush. To the left of the building an area has been fenced in, however apart from a pile of firewood there is nothing in this area.

### Irgnir's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-interior-Irgnir%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Irgnir%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Irgnir%27s_House.jpg)

Five skeever plaques

Another single room dwelling. On your left, against the southwest wall is a long wooden table holding a bottle of each, [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and two [boiled creme treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"). On the floor to the left is a basket containing three cabbages and another basket to the right contains two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") and four [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"). To the right of this is a set of shelves holding two leeks, several loaves of bread, two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulbs, three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), two [gourds](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") and several cheese wheels. Above there are two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam").

In the western corner and along the northwest wall are three barrels, two of which containing [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"). Further to the right against the same wall is a round wooden table and two chairs. On the table are six loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"). Opposite the door on the northeast wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front, two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on the mantelpiece and three pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") on the hearth. To the left is a pile of firewood with five [skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever "Skyrim:Skeever") heads mounted on plaques above. To the left of these is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)").

To the right of the fireplace is a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"). Further to the right against the northeast wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") and a chair beside it. Coming out from the southeast wall is a large double bed with two bottle of alto wine on the floor to the left of it, a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") at the foot of the bed and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and chair to the right. There is an unlocked wooden [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") against the southwest wall, with two more pickaxe to the right, next to the door.

## Notes

- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide")* describes the building as follows:

:   *"[Karl](/wiki/Skyrim:Karl "Skyrim:Karl") and Irgnir, the workers of Iron-Breaker Mine, live here in this modest dwelling. They have finally saved up enough gold to purchase from [Beitild](/wiki/Skyrim:Beitild "Skyrim:Beitild"). They sometimes board with [Gjak](/wiki/Skyrim:Gjak "Skyrim:Gjak") and [Bodil](/wiki/Skyrim:Bodil "Skyrim:Bodil"), who take the other shifts to keep the mine constantly productive."*
