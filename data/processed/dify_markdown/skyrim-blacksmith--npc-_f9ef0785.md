Blacksmith

The **Blacksmith** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [blacksmith](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith "Skyrim:Blacksmith") who can be found working the forge within [Ashfall's Tear](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashfall%27s_Tear "Skyrim:Ashfall's Tear") if you choose to side with the [Tribunal Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Tribunal_Temple "Skyrim:Tribunal Temple") during the quest [Her Word Against Theirs](/wiki/Skyrim:Her_Word_Against_Theirs "Skyrim:Her Word Against Theirs"). Before this quest, the forge will be dilapidated, but it will be repaired if you help recruit more members for the cult.

He offers a selection of blacksmithing goods, including several pieces of [Indoril armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Indoril_Armor "Skyrim:Indoril Armor").
