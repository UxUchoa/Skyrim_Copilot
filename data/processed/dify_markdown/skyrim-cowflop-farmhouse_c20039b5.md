Cowflop Farmhouse

**Cowflop Farmhouse** is a small farmhouse located in [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead"). It is home to [Ennis](/wiki/Skyrim:Ennis "Skyrim:Ennis") and [Reldith](/wiki/Skyrim:Reldith "Skyrim:Reldith"), who are both [farmers](/wiki/Skyrim:Farmer_(class) "Skyrim:Farmer (class)"). It is at the eastern end of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead"), along the road that passes through the village. It is next door to [Frostfruit Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostfruit_Inn "Skyrim:Frostfruit Inn"). It contains only one zone, *[Cowflop Farmhouse](#Cowflop_Farmhouse)*.

### Exterior

There are wooden steps leading up to the entrance from the road. There are two [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") and a [purple mountain flower](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") found beside the building. It has a large, picturesque pasture to the east and consists of a [grain mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Milling "Skyrim:Milling"), twelve bushels of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), a chicken coop containing two nests, two [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken"), fifteen [cabbages](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and eighteen [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") plants. During the quest [A Night To Remember](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Night_To_Remember "Skyrim:A Night To Remember"), they will also own a beloved [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") named [Gleda](/wiki/Skyrim:Gleda_the_Goat "Skyrim:Gleda the Goat").

### Cowflop Farmhouse

The front door is novice-locked the majority of the time. It is a typical farm building consisting of a simple one-room home, that is fully stocked. The house may look modest, but it does have some worthwhile loot hidden away.

Upon entering there is a cupboard on the left against the southwest wall holding various pieces of cheese, a cabbage, five [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), five [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulbs, a [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and a [potion of True Shot](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_True_Shot "Skyrim:Potion of True Shot"). In the western corner is a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel and a basket containing three cabbages. Next to these along the northwest wall is a long wooden table. On the table is a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two [gourds](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), two [apple pies](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow") and two [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"). To the right of the table is an unlocked respawning wooden [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing leveled items, and hanging from the ceiling above are two garlic braids, a bunch of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") and a bunch of [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear").

Against the northeast wall is another long wooden table and bench. It holds several pieces of cheese, two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a [seared slaughterfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish "Skyrim:Seared Slaughterfish"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a [raw rabbit leg](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg "Skyrim:Raw Rabbit Leg"), a [pheasant breast](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), two bottles of alto wine and a bottle of wine. Hanging from racks above are four [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"), four [pheasant](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"), and two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)"). In the middle of the northeast wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") in front, and by the hearth is a pile of firewood and [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"). To the right of the fireplace is a cupboard holding more bottles of wine and alto wine, with more bunches of elves ear and frost mirriam hanging from the ceiling above.

Coming out from the middle of the southeast wall is a large double bed with an end table at either side. On these is a couple of bottles of wine and a filled [common soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Common Soul Gem"). To the right, against the southwest wall is an unlocked non-respawning chest containing leveled items and small round wooden table with two chairs. On this table is a loaf of bread, a bottle of alto wine, and a bottle of wine.

## Notes

- The grain mill can be used to produce [flour](/wiki/Skyrim:Sack_of_Flour "Skyrim:Sack of Flour")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") from wheat if [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is installed.
- All cupboards and end tables in the location are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") and safe to store items in.
