Engar

**Engar** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") and a [carriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Transport#Carriage "Skyrim:Transport") driver who can be hired at [Windstad Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Windstad_Manor "Skyrim:Windstad Manor").

He wields an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"), and wears farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0006C1D8 "Skyrim:Clothes") along with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f "Skyrim:Boots").
