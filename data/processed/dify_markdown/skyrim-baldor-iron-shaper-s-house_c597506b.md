Baldor Iron-Shaper's House

**Baldor Iron-Shaper's House** is the home of [Baldor Iron-Shaper](/wiki/Skyrim:Baldor_Iron-Shaper "Skyrim:Baldor Iron-Shaper") in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

It is on the south side of the village, between [Deor Woodcutter's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House "Skyrim:Deor Woodcutter's House") to the west and the [Shaman's Hut](/wiki/Skyrim:Shaman%27s_Hut "Skyrim:Shaman's Hut") to the east.

### Exterior

The building has two entrances, both on the north side, with one to the east and one to the west. To the east is a complete blacksmithing area, complete with a [forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge "Skyrim:Forge"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), a [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelting "Skyrim:Smelting"), a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning "Skyrim:Tanning"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), with an open box on a crate between the tanning rack and workbench containing two pieces of [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") and a [leather strip](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). Between the two entrances is another tanning rack, a fish barrel, a crate with a [snow fox pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Fox Pelt") on top, and another workbench. To the right of the west entrance is a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush. The east entrance is unlocked, and the west entrance has a novice lock.

### Baldor Iron-Shaper's House

The entrance leads into a large room with a large mezzanine above. On your left is a small round table and two chair. On the table is a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and two loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). Against the south wall is the flight of stairs to the top floor and under the stairs is a barrel and four sacks. In the southwest corner of the room is a small square table and two chairs, a bottle of wine, a bowl of [horker stew](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Stew "Skyrim:Horker Stew") and a plate with a [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese") are on the table. Above the table is a large hanging rack with three [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids hanging from it. There is a wall shelf by the table on the west wall with a shield plaque holding a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield") and two crossed [iron greatswords](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Greatsword "Skyrim:Iron Greatsword"). To the right of this are two wall shelves with a sack on and a wood pile and [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") underneath. Beside this is a bucket containing with quivers of [steel arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow "Skyrim:Steel Arrow") and then a fireplace. There is a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") in front of the fireplace and a shelf above with a bottle of wine on it. There is a stool to the right of the fire. There is a crate with an long open crate to the right inside the crate are six pieces of [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore"), three pieces of [corundum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore "Skyrim:Corundum Ore") and a piece of [malachite ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Malachite_Ore "Skyrim:Malachite Ore"). Above these is a wall shelf containing a [blacksmith's potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Potion "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Potion"), a bottle of wine and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine") and beside the crate is a small open crate containing nine [iron ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot "Skyrim:Iron Ingot"). The room continues into a small bedroom area along the west wall. In the small bedroom area is a single bed along the northern wall with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots") and a copy of *[The Lusty Argonian Maid, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid,_v2 "Skyrim:The Lusty Argonian Maid, v2")*.

To the right of the entrance is a cupboard, on it's shelves are two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk") and [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel"). To the left is a fish barrel with a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") on top and a sack on the floor beside it. Around the corner against the east wall is a pair of weapon racks separated by an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"). The weapon rack on the right has a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow") and the one on the left has an [iron warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer "Skyrim:Iron Warhammer"). Next to this is an open crate with five iron and four [steel ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot "Skyrim:Steel Ingot"). Around the corner again is a small round table and chair with a bottle of ale on the table. To the left of this is a second entrance. To the left of this entrance is a dresser with a [fox](/wiki/Skyrim:Fox_Pelt "Skyrim:Fox Pelt") and [deer pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt "Skyrim:Deer Pelt") on top and in the northeast corner is a bucket with three pieces of large [charcoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal "Skyrim:Charcoal"). Against the north wall is a second flight of stairs up to the top floor. Under these stairs are two barrels-one of which has four [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") on, a crate, four sacks and a dresser. There is a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") on top of the dresser. Between this and the small bedroom area is a weapon rack holding a [steel battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Steel Battleaxe").

Climbing the stairs by the south wall there is a barrel and sack at the top. Following the floor to the north you pass a narrow table on your right with a copy of *[Forge, Hammer and Anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Forge,_Hammer_and_Anvil "Skyrim:Forge, Hammer and Anvil")* and an eidar cheese wedge. Opposite this is an open crate with two iron ore pieces and three [iron daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). Next to this are two crates with a leather strip and [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword") on top. On the right is the main mezzanine area with a crate with an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield") and [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet") and a leather strip on. To the left of this is an open crate with a leather strip and two more large pieces of charcoal. Against the west wall is a low table with a display case on containing two iron daggers with a chair to the right of it. At the end by the stairs up from downstairs is a cupboard, on the shelves is a [blacksmith's apron](/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron "Skyrim:Blacksmith's Apron"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes "Skyrim:Shoes"), a steel ingot and two iron.
