**Gilfre's House** is the home owned by [Gilfre](/wiki/Skyrim:Gilfre "Skyrim:Gilfre") at [Mixwater Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Mixwater_Mill "Skyrim:Mixwater Mill").

Located southeast of [Gallows Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock "Skyrim:Gallows Rock") and southwest of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), [Gilfre](/wiki/Skyrim:Gilfre "Skyrim:Gilfre") has lived here alone since her five workers left to fight in the civil war.

## Related Quests

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

### Exterior

It is on the banks of the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River"), which is used to power the mill via a water wheel. The house, a [worker's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Worker%27s_House "Skyrim:Worker's House") and the sawmill itself are all part of [Mixwater Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Mixwater_Mill "Skyrim:Mixwater Mill"). Everything on the deck in front of the house is owned and taking anything is considered stealing, though there is a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") that you can take for free. There is a rack holding four [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") and a crate with a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") sack on top and the aforementioned woodcutter's axe leaning against it. There is a chicken coop with three [nests](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") for the four [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken") that wander the site between this and the worker's house. Also there are several [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") and [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") and at least one [snowberry bush](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") around the house and site. On the bank by the sawmill is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). The front door is novice-locked most of the day and all night.

### Gilfre's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-interior-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg)

Gilfre's bedroom

It is a single-roomed dwelling containing a double bed coming out from the east wall, with a set of shelves to the left, against the north wall. These hold six common books, two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") and several [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), with several pieces of cheese and a plate containing two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg") on top. Hanging from the ceiling above the shelves are two garlic braids, three bunches of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). To the left of the shelves is a flower basket containing three [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") and to the right is a food barrel. There is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed and to the right, against the south wall is a round wooden table with two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") on top, while next to the table is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). To the right of this is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)").

Opposite the entrance is a fireplace, on the south wall, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") hanging in front and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes "Skyrim:Shoes") by the hearth. There are eight loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") on the mantelpiece. To the right of the fireplace, also against the south wall is a long wooden table and bench, with two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a sprig of snowberries, a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a potato, a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread") and a [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") on top of the table, with a [potion of vigor](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor "Skyrim:Potion of Vigor") on the floor underneath. In the northeastern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs. On this table is a copy of the [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Marksmanship Lesson](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Marksmanship_Lesson "Skyrim:The Marksmanship Lesson")*, a random [healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing "Skyrim:Healing") [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), a random [stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Stamina "Skyrim:Stamina") potion and five loose septims.

## Notes

- You may choose to live here if you [marry](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") Gilfe. Like at [Volkihar Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Keep "Skyrim:Volkihar Keep") and [Fort Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dawnguard "Skyrim:Fort Dawnguard") after completing [Bloodline](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodline "Skyrim:Bloodline") quest, everything on the deck in front of the house except for the woodcutter's axe will still be owned, though, and must be stolen.

## Bugs

- If you marry Gilfre, sleeping in the bed in her house only confers a Rested bonus, not Lover's Comfort or Well-Rested. **?**
- If you move here after marrying Gilfre, she may still ask you to leave.
