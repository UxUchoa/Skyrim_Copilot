## Quick Walkthrough

1. Travel to [Myrwatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Myrwatch_(place) "Skyrim:Myrwatch (place)") and take [*Hans's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Hans%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Hans's Journal") from [Hans](/wiki/Skyrim:Hans "Skyrim:Hans").
2. Read the journal and cast a [fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage "Skyrim:Fire Damage") spell at the seal.
3. Enter the tower to complete the quest.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This quest automatically begins upon approaching [Myrwatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Myrwatch_(place) "Skyrim:Myrwatch (place)"), an undiscovered tower marked on your map. The tower is just east of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal") and is guarded by a lone leveled [Chaurus](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus "Skyrim:Chaurus"). You will need to attempt to open the locked front entrance in order to update the quest.

The body of [Hans](/wiki/Skyrim:Hans "Skyrim:Hans") can be found nearby. Loot his body and read [*Hans's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Hans%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Hans's Journal") to learn how to gain entrance to the tower. The journal provides a hint in this regard:

:   *"When the eye is doused in flames, the hair*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *will light the way."*

You will need to cast a [fire damage](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage "Skyrim:Fire Damage") spell at the eye-shaped seal on the ground in front of the entrance. Helpfully, Hans can also be looted for a copy of [Spell Tome: Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Flames "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Flames") in case you have no fire damage spells. Once you cast the spell, the [ghostly apparition](/wiki/Skyrim:Elianora "Skyrim:Elianora") of a rabbit will appear (or more than likely a hare, per the hint in the journal). The apparition will proceed to hop over to the nearby magical brazier and will cause it to alight with a tower of blue energy. You will then receive a message stating that *"The tower door was unlocked by mysterious forces."*

Head inside the tower to complete the quest and investigate your new [player home](/wiki/Skyrim:Houses "Skyrim:Houses").

## Notes

- This quest would originally be added to your journal upon installing the [Myrwatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Myrwatch "Skyrim:Myrwatch") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). This was changed with the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition") update, allowing you to discover the location through natural gameplay.
  - Myrwatch will still be marked on your map as an undiscovered location from the start of the game, a legacy of the original quest design.
