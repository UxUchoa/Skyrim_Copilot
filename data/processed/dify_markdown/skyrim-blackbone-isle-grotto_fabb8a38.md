**Blackbone Isle Grotto** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") connected to the sea, situated on [Blackbone Isle](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackbone_Isle "Skyrim:Blackbone Isle"). The cave is initially filled with [undead](/wiki/Skyrim:Undead "Skyrim:Undead"). It houses a haunted ship named *[Dead Man's Dread](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Man%27s_Dread_(place) "Skyrim:Dead Man's Dread (place)")*, which can be repurposed as a player home after completing the related quest.

## Related Quests

- **[The Restless](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Restless "Skyrim:The Restless")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Man%27s_Dread "Skyrim:Dead Man's Dread"): Search for an ancient pirate ship.

### Exterior

The entrance to this cave can be found directly next to the boat connecting Blackbone Isle to [Orphan's Tear](/wiki/Skyrim:Orphan%27s_Tear "Skyrim:Orphan's Tear"). For more information, see the [Blackbone Isle article](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackbone_Isle "Skyrim:Blackbone Isle").

### Blackbone Isle Grotto

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg)

Blackbone Isle Grotto

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto_02.jpg)

The skull

Blackbone Isle Grotto is a flooded cavern containing *Dead Man's Dread*, a famed ship which became trapped underground roughly six hundred years ago. Two undead enemies block your path when you first approach the ship, although they do not respawn. A giant skull has been hewn into the stone wall of the cavern. In the mouth of giant skull, behind and to the right of the fire is a treasure chest. To reach it you can use [Whirlwind Sprint](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Sprint "Skyrim:Whirlwind Sprint") from the ship's bowsprit and aim for the mouth.
