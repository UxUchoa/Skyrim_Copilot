**Ennoc** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)") who lives in the [Miner's Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Left Hand Miner's Barracks") for the [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine") in which he works.

Ennoc will spend all of his time mining, not ever breaking from his duties to eat or sleep. He wears a set of [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"), and he carries a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Key to Left Hand Miner's Barracks") to the Barracks, and some [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

If you speak to Ennoc in Left Hand Mine, he will stay focused on his work, saying:

:   *"I'm just here to mine."*
:   *"Got ore to dig."*
