Beldama Witch

**Beldama Witches** are hostile witches who guard [Beldama Ward Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Beldama_Ward_Mother "Skyrim:Beldama Ward Mother"), appear at [Karthspire Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthspire_Camp "Skyrim:Karthspire Camp") during [Crypt of the Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Crypt_of_the_Heart "Skyrim:Crypt of the Heart"), in front of the rock where the dead [Daenella](/wiki/Skyrim:Daenella "Skyrim:Daenella") lies.

They wear [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes") with matching [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"). One of them carries *[Beldama Witch's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Beldama_Witch%27s_Note "Skyrim:Beldama Witch's Note")*. Their appearance may differ more than two types shown in the picture.
