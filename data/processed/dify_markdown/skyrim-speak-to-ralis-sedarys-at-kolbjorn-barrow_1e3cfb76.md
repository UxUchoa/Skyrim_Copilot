## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to the innkeeper of [The Retching Netch](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Retching_Netch "Skyrim:The Retching Netch") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").
2. Talk to [Ralis Sedarys](/wiki/Skyrim:Ralis_Sedarys "Skyrim:Ralis Sedarys") at [Kolbjorn Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolbjorn_Barrow "Skyrim:Kolbjorn Barrow").

## Detailed Walkthrough

You can ask [Geldis Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Geldis_Sadri "Skyrim:Geldis Sadri") in [The Retching Netch](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Retching_Netch "Skyrim:The Retching Netch") for any recent rumors. He may tell you that [Ralis](/wiki/Skyrim:Ralis_Sedarys "Skyrim:Ralis Sedarys") is looking to excavate [Kolbjorn Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolbjorn_Barrow "Skyrim:Kolbjorn Barrow").

Travel to Kolbjorn Barrow, where you will find Ralis. After speaking with him you will begin the next quest, "[Unearthed](/wiki/Skyrim:Unearthed "Skyrim:Unearthed")".
