Traitor's Post

**Traitor's Post** is a crumbling [shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks "Skyrim:Shacks") and [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") outpost east of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), and just north of the [Sacellum of Boethiah](/wiki/Skyrim:Sacellum_of_Boethiah "Skyrim:Sacellum of Boethiah").

It is easily found by taking the road northeast from Windhelm towards the border with Morrowind. Once past the three farms on the right, it is the next building on the left, shortly after the road turns to the east.

## Traitor's Post

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_02.jpg)

Traitor's Post

A small band of [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") have made it their home and attack any passing travelers. There are numerous [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes and various colors of [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower") along the road and around the shack, and also several [bear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") hidden among the vegetation around the building. The shack has no windows or doors, large holes in the ceiling and walls and no stairs leading up to the top floor. A hundred paces farther along the path is the path that leads up to the [Sacellum of Boethiah](/wiki/Skyrim:Sacellum_of_Boethiah "Skyrim:Sacellum of Boethiah"), where the followers of [Boethiah](/wiki/Lore:Boethiah "Lore:Boethiah") worship.

The front of the shack has a typical decking area that is damaged like every other part of the building. To the right are a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") and a pile of firewood. Inside are four bandits including a boss-leveled chief. There is a fireplace in the middle of the northeast wall, with a long wooden table to the right against the same wall. On the table are a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat"), a [carrot](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") in a cast iron pot, and a random [dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger "Skyrim:Dagger"). In the southern corner is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") beside a stack of crates. In the center of the shack are two long wooden tables set end-to-end with three wooden benches. On the tables are a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), two [mammoth steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Steak "Skyrim:Mammoth Steak"), and a portion of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_03.jpg)

Where to go to reach the top level

There is a wardrobe against the wall towards the front of the building, while to the right, behind a partition wall, is a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") on the floor. Opposite the wardrobe against the northwest wall is a cupboard with a random [light armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") [cuirass](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor") and a copy of the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[A Dance in Fire, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v2 "Skyrim:A Dance in Fire, v2")* on a shelf. At the northwestern end of the room, against the southwest wall, is a long wooden table with a random empty [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") and a random [stamina potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina") on top. To the right, in the western corner, is a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"). In the northern corner are two hay piles for sleeping and a crate with a food sack on top.

To the northeast is a bedroom area used by the chief containing an unowned double bed in the corner with a dresser at the foot of the bed holding a couple of pieces of cheese. Beside this is a chair. In the southern corner of this area is a novice-locked chest with a random pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor") beside it along with another food sack. There are several barrels around the room; some contain food, and others [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_04.jpg/200px-SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Traitor%27s_Post_04.jpg)

Top floor

It is possible to access the top floor via some rocks helpfully piled up against the rear of the shack. Once you are on the main roof, you need to jump to the wing over the chief's bedroom area in order to climb to the peak of the roof. From there, you can jump back to the main roof at a far higher level, enabling you to drop in through a hole in the roof over the top floor at the southeastern end of the building. The top floor has two unowned single beds, one on each side. On the floor near the foot of the bed against the southwest wall is a random [healing potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), while by the foot of the other bed is a random [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet") on a crate with a random [weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons") leaning against it. The floor is missing at the northwestern end of the room, with a master-locked boss-level chest and a chair by the edge of the floor. The hole enables you to drop down once you've finished upstairs.

## Notes

- A chest with a novice-level lock on the main floor inside the house contains [Treasure Map VII](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_VII "Skyrim:Treasure Map VII").

## Bugs

- NPCs may only able to be use the double bed on one side due to its placed position against the wall.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.1, addresses this issue. The double bed is now a singular bed.
- The bandit leader here isn't named "Bandit Chief" like bandit bosses with the same template at similar locations. Instead, he is assigned a random leveled bandit name.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.2, fixes this bug.
