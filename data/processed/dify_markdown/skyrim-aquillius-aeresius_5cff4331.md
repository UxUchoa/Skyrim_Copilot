Aquillius Aeresius

**Aquillius Aeresius** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [citizen](/wiki/Skyrim:Citizen_(class) "Skyrim:Citizen (class)") living in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") with [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici").

At 8am, he heads down to the [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") docks and wanders around there. He goes to sleep at midnight inside of Vittoria's house.

Aquillius wears a set of [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#00086991 "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") and [fine boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#00086993 "Skyrim:Fine Clothes"). He carries a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") and the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Vittoria_Vici%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Vittoria Vici's House") to [Vittoria Vici's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici%27s_House "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici's House").

## Dialogue

He shares these two dialogue lines with Vittoria Vici. If talked to, he will say either:

:   *"Good business for the [East Empire Company](/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company "Lore:East Empire Company") means all the more gold for the [Empire](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion")."*
:   *"I don't have time for anyone too lazy or cowardly to make their own way in this world."*

## Notes

- It's unclear what exactly Aquillius' purpose is. It's possible he was intended to take over as merchant for the East Empire Company in case Vittoria died, but this role is actually given to the [East Empire Dockmaster](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Dockmaster "Skyrim:East Empire Dockmaster") internally. His factions imply that he might have also functioned as a backup for Vittoria's presence at the [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor") party during [Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity"), but besides being in all the same factions as Vittoria (which were in all likelihood just copied), there is no evidence he was to be involved in the quest. He has no alias for it, no coding mentions him, and he doesn't have equivalent lines for Vittoria's specific dialogue during the quest. Generic rumors for the guests are recorded in his voice type, which no other guest uses, but these might have also been accidentally recorded for [Malborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Malborn "Skyrim:Malborn"), as he does have the same voice type.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.2, raises Aquillius' level from 1 to 4, and removes his -49 health modifier.
- Aquillius has almost the exact same schedule as Vittoria, the only difference being his sandbox package `SolitudeDocksWander8x12`, which Vittoria doesn't possess. This results in him spending almost the entire day following her around.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.2, removes Aquillius' counter AI package from his base actor so he doesn't crowd Vittoria while she works. It's been added to the EastEmpire alias in the `DialogueSolitude` quest so that he'll resume when Vittoria is killed.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.2, adds merchant factions to Aquillius' faction list, in line with changes made to Vittoria.

## Bugs

- Due to having no cleanup script attached, Aquillius' body will remain in the world forever, in case he dies.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.0, addresses this issue. He has been given a cleanup script, and his body will now be present inside of a coffin inside the Hall of the Dead. This coffin was previously unused.
