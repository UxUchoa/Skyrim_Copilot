The dead assassin

The **Dark Brotherhood Assassin** is a long-dead [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") found behind a sliding wall in a hidden alcove revealed by pulling a handle behind the throne, in the throne room of [Hag's End](/wiki/Skyrim:Hag%27s_End "Skyrim:Hag's End"). [Olava the Feeble](/wiki/Skyrim:Olava_the_Feeble "Skyrim:Olava the Feeble") will have a vision of his final resting place if you earn a bonus during the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") quest [Breaching Security](/wiki/Skyrim:Breaching_Security "Skyrim:Breaching Security"). He is clad in a complete set of unique [Ancient Shrouded Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Shrouded_Armor "Skyrim:Ancient Shrouded Armor").

## Related Quests

- **[Locate the Assassin of Old](/wiki/Skyrim:Locate_the_Assassin_of_Old "Skyrim:Locate the Assassin of Old")**: Find the Dark Brotherhood Assassin.

## Notes

- Despite being supposedly long-dead, the Assassin's remains show no signs of decay.
- It is possible to obtain the Ancient Shrouded Armor without [Olava's Token](/wiki/Skyrim:Olava%27s_Token "Skyrim:Olava's Token"). Take [Serana](/wiki/Skyrim:Serana "Skyrim:Serana") with you into Hag's End and during the fight with the first set of witches, she will resurrect the body of the Dark Brotherhood Assassin, who will join you once you exit the cell. When he turns to ash, loot the ash pile for the entire set of Ancient Shrouded Armor.
- It is also possible to obtain the Ancient Shrouded Armor by using [The Ritual Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ritual_Stone "Skyrim:The Ritual Stone") power. Activating the power near the body will raise it; exiting the cell within two minutes (for example, exiting to the Deepwood Redoubt balcony) will cause the body to follow you outside. Once the power wears off, you can loot the body for the armor.
- Version 2.0.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") makes the Assassin's corpse immune to reanimation.

## Bugs

- Clearing out Hag's End and then starting Locate the Assassin of Old may cause the Assassin's corpse to disappear.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
