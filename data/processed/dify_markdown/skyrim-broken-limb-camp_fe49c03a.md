Broken Limb Camp

**Broken Limb Camp** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") north-northwest of [Darkwater Crossing](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Crossing "Skyrim:Darkwater Crossing").

## Related Quests

- **[Bounty: Giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Giant "Skyrim:Bounty: Giant")**: Kill a problematic [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") for a bounty reward. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/SR-place-Broken_Limb_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Broken_Limb_Camp_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Broken_Limb_Camp_02.jpg)

A giant at the camp

The camp is on the edge of the hot springs in [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch") near the bank of the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River"). Two [giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") occupy the camp, which is surrounded by the typical painted stones, [bone chimes](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes"), and bones. The central campfire has been extinguished, with a [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") plant growing among the burnt remnants. An unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") and two [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl") are south of the campfire. More dragon's tongue, [jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes"), [creep cluster](/wiki/Skyrim:Creep_Cluster "Skyrim:Creep Cluster") plants, and a birds' nest are in the vicinity.
