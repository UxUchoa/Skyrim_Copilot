## Quick Walkthrough

1. Talk to [Temba Wide-Arm](/wiki/Skyrim:Temba_Wide-Arm "Skyrim:Temba Wide-Arm").
2. Acquire ten [bear pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Bear Pelt").
3. Return to Temba Wide-Arm.

### Damn Bears

[Temba Wide-Arm](/wiki/Skyrim:Temba_Wide-Arm "Skyrim:Temba Wide-Arm") really does not like [bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear"). She is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") woman who has sunk all of her money into a mill in [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead"). Even in passing you will hear her complaining to anyone within earshot how the bears are running her business into the ground. If you stop to chat she will explain that bears are ruining the trees by scratching up the trunks, rendering them useless as lumber. She is sorely in need of help with the bear problem and offers payment if you bring her ten bear pelts, thus thinning out the bear population.

### Filling the Order

Temba will accept any combination of [bear pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Bear Pelt"), [cave bear pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Cave_Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Cave Bear Pelt"), or [snow bear pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Bear Pelt"); it doesn't matter to her as long as they are ursidae.

There are two bear dens in the vicinity of Ivarstead: [Honeystrand Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Honeystrand_Cave "Skyrim:Honeystrand Cave") to the south past the river, and [Pinepeak Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Pinepeak_Cavern "Skyrim:Pinepeak Cavern") to the north near the steps leading up to [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar"). There are also a number of bear pelts in [Broken Oar Grotto](/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Oar_Grotto "Skyrim:Broken Oar Grotto"), a cave north of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). [Giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") frequently carry bear pelts, and you can also buy bear pelts from general goods [merchants](/wiki/Skyrim:Merchants "Skyrim:Merchants"), [Khajiit caravans](/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant#Khajiit_Traders "Skyrim:Merchant"), and [hunters](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter "Skyrim:Hunter") in the wild.

Once you bring the ten pelts to her she will be surprised that you came through for her and will reward you with a random leveled enchanted war axe.

## Bugs

- If you are carrying more than ten bear pelts, she will take them all, and it seems you cannot pickpocket them back.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.
- You can get the quest reward and keep the pelts by putting the bear pelts in a container immediately after you tell her that you got the pelts. Be warned however, that doing this will cause the quest to be stuck in your journal indefinitely.
