Winterhold Imperial Camp

**Winterhold Imperial Camp** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Camps "Skyrim:Imperial Camps") in [Winterhold Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)"), located southeast of [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") and northwest of [Frostflow Lighthouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostflow_Lighthouse "Skyrim:Frostflow Lighthouse").

The camp is normally commanded by [Legate Sevan Telendas](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Sevan_Telendas "Skyrim:Legate Sevan Telendas"), except during the related quest, at which point [Legate Rikke](/wiki/Skyrim:Legate_Rikke "Skyrim:Legate Rikke") will assume command.

## Winterhold Imperial Camp

It is a typical Imperial camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. To the south, in a row are the Legate's tent, command tent, and hospital tent. The Legate's tent contains an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. The command tent contains an end table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. The hospital tent contains six bed rolls, two of them occupied by wounded soldiers. Outside is an [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab"), a low table with a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and two random [potions of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), and an unlocked chest containing various items to treat the sick, including potions of [cure poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison "Skyrim:Cure Poison"), potions of [cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Cure Disease"), and [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips"). Beside the tent are some barrels. West of the campfire are two rows of three small tents containing unowned bed rolls. Northwest of the fire is where the [Imperial quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Imperial Quartermaster") works, in front of his tent. His workstation consists of an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench") , and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), with various [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") and [steel weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") and [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor") on display on a table in front and inside his tent. North of the fire are two picketed [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse "Skyrim:Horse"). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. There is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") near the fire, and a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bush near the small tents.

Any time [Winterhold Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)") is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Joining the Stormcloaks"), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.

## Notes

- Three ore veins are near the camp: a [moonstone ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore") vein southwest of the camp, and two [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore") veins northwest of the camp.
