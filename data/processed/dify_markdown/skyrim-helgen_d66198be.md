**Helgen** is a [town](/wiki/Skyrim:Towns "Skyrim:Towns") in eastern [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-place-Helgen.jpg/200px-SR-place-Helgen.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Helgen.jpg)

Helgen after the dragon attack

It is the location where you begin the game, as you are transported there to be executed. Largely run by the [Imperial military](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"), the town features several fortified towers and buildings. Shortly after your arrival, the settlement comes under attack by the ancient [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin"), and you are forced to escape through [Helgen Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen_Keep "Skyrim:Helgen Keep").

If you return to the town after visiting [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"), the ruins will be inhabited by a group of [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit").

## Notes

- During the introductory sequence before the initial quest [Unbound](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound "Skyrim:Unbound"), [Ralof](/wiki/Skyrim:Ralof "Skyrim:Ralof") remembers [Vilod](/wiki/Skyrim:Vilod "Skyrim:Vilod"), who used to make [mead with juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Mead_with_Juniper_Berry "Skyrim:Mead with Juniper Berry"). If you later return to Helgen, bottles of this mead may be found on the second floor of the partly destroyed inn you jumped into during Unbound.
- Two additional residents are present during the introductory sequence; these unnamed NPCs are referenced only as `MQ101GenericCivilianNordMale` and `MQ101GenericCivilianNordFemale` in the game data. They're seen carrying firewood and a bucket inside the gate when the carriage enters town, but are never seen again.
- Helgen doesn't count as a discovered location, and therefore won't count toward the [Explorer](/wiki/Skyrim:Explorer "Skyrim:Explorer") achievement.
- Helgen is the default destination for bad AI targets, meaning that bugged NPCs may end up wandering nearby.
- Approximately halfway down the road to [Pinewatch](/wiki/Skyrim:Pinewatch "Skyrim:Pinewatch"), a small group of bandits has set up camp under an overhanging ledge. The bandits guard a novice-locked chest and a copy of the [Light Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor "Skyrim:Light Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Refugees](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Refugees "Skyrim:The Refugees")*.
- The locked gate, bandits, barrels, chest, potions, mead bottles, Cast Iron Pot, Wooden Bowl, Kettle, Basket, Wooden Ladle and Tankard respawn; burnt corpses, the wardrobe, and the end table don't respawn.
- The double bed at the inn is considered “owned”; however, if you choose to escape Helgen with Hadvar, you will be allowed to sleep in the bed after becoming friends with his uncle Alvor.
- This wiki's current Skyrim satellite map displays Helgen in its original state; the previous one displayed Helgen in its destroyed state.

## Bugs

- The mead with juniper berries isn't properly enabled during your arrival and will only appear upon your later return to Helgen.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.
- Vilod and Matlara are left alive after the dragon attack and can be seen wandering Helgen after its destruction.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- When [Valdimar](/wiki/Skyrim:Valdimar "Skyrim:Valdimar") is assigned as a Housecarl for [Windstad Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Windstad_Manor "Skyrim:Windstad Manor"), he may be found here instead.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Hearthfire Patch"), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Bandits, containers, and items don't have their encounter zone set and will respawn despite being in a "no reset" zone.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.0.8, fixes this bug.
