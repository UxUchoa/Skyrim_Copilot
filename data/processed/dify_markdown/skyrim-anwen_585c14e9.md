Anwen

**Anwen** is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [priestess](/wiki/Skyrim:Priest "Skyrim:Priest") of [Dibella](/wiki/Lore:Dibella "Lore:Dibella"). She can be found in the inner sanctum of the [Temple of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Temple of Dibella").

She becomes available for [marriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") after [The Heart of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Heart_of_Dibella "Skyrim:The Heart of Dibella").

She wears [hooded monk robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Monk_Robes "Skyrim:Hooded Monk Robes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000bacd7 "Skyrim:Clothing"), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"). She carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Key to the Temple of Dibella") to the temple of Dibella, a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Dibella_Inner_Sanctum "Skyrim:Key to the Dibella Inner Sanctum") to the inner sanctum, and a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). She also knows a few spells, specifically [conjure familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Familiar "Skyrim:Conjure Familiar"), [flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Flames "Skyrim:Flames"), and the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") spells [heal other](/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Other "Skyrim:Heal Other") and [fast healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Healing "Skyrim:Fast Healing").

## Bugs

- When married, Anwen may remain at the [Temple of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Temple of Dibella") even when you ask to move to another place. Because there are no beds in the temple, it is impossible to get the [Lover's Comfort](/wiki/Skyrim:Lover%27s_Comfort "Skyrim:Lover's Comfort") bonus.
  - If [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is installed, adopt a child, and move her to your house, this may fix it.[*verification needed*]
