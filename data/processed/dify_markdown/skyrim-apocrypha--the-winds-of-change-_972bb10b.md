The Winds of Change Black Book in Raven Rock Mine

**The Winds of Change** is an area of [Hermaeus Mora](/wiki/Skyrim:Hermaeus_Mora "Skyrim:Hermaeus Mora")'s [plane of Oblivion](/wiki/Lore:Planes_of_Oblivion "Lore:Planes of Oblivion"), [Apocrypha](/wiki/Skyrim:Apocrypha "Skyrim:Apocrypha"). There is only one zone in the area, *[The Winds of Change](#The_Winds_of_Change)*.

The [Black Book](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book:_The_Winds_of_Change_(book) "Skyrim:Black Book: The Winds of Change (book)") needed to access The Winds of Change is found inside [Raven Rock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Mine "Skyrim:Raven Rock Mine"). You must progress in the quest [The Final Descent](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Final_Descent "Skyrim:The Final Descent") in order to retrieve the book.

The realm is inhabited by Daedra called [Seekers](/wiki/Skyrim:Seeker "Skyrim:Seeker") and [Lurkers](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker"). Tentacles constantly breach the acidic dark waters and sometimes whip at you, causing a significant amount of damage if they manage to hit you. The acidic waters with the tentacles are also corrosive and cause damage if stepped in.

## The Winds of Change

The Winds of Change opens on a dim platform, with a bridge over the murky water ahead. Beyond the bridge and up two sets of stairs is a room with five pedestals guarded by a seeker. Starting with the pedestal nearest the stairs and moving clockwise, the pedestals hold a random book and a random soul gem on the first, nothing of interest on the second, two random books on the third, a random book and a copy of the [Spell Tome: Conjure Seeker](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Conjure_Seeker "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Conjure Seeker") (only if your [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") skill is at least level 40) on the fourth, and a random soul gem on the fifth.

To the east and northeast, two fonts of stamina flank a scrye that opens the nearby gate. Beyond the gate is a table to the left with two soul gems (one random and one leveled), followed by a font of magicka and a pod to the left. Southwest of the pod is a table with a leveled soul gem, a font of stamina, and a scrye that opens another nearby gate. Beyond the gate is a corridor guarded by a seeker. Along the corridor are two fonts of magicka, with another scrye that opens the gate at the end of the corridor.

Beyond the gate is a large room with a central pool from which a lurker rises to attack. East of the pool is a font of magicka next to a ramp that ascends to a small platform with a scrye and two pedestals flanking a table. The pedestal on the left holds two [scrolls of frenzy](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Frenzy "Skyrim:Scroll of Frenzy") and a [black soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Black Soul Gem"), the pedestal on the right holds two [scrolls of fear](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fear "Skyrim:Scroll of Fear"), and the table holds two black soul gems and a copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Words and Philosophy](/wiki/Skyrim:Words_and_Philosophy "Skyrim:Words and Philosophy")*. The scrye opens the gate on the other side of the room. West of the pool is a font of magicka and another scrye, which extends the stairs up to the gate. Beyond the gate is a vessel and the black book *The Winds of Change*. To exit this part of Apocrypha, use the non-circular part of the black book.

The three [Abilities](/wiki/Skyrim:Abilities "Skyrim:Abilities") offered by this book are:

- [Companion's Insight](/wiki/Skyrim:Companion%27s_Insight "Skyrim:Companion's Insight") — Your attacks, shouts, and destruction spells do no damage to your followers when in combat.
- [Lover's Insight](/wiki/Skyrim:Lover%27s_Insight "Skyrim:Lover's Insight") — Do 10% more damage and get 10% better prices from people of the opposite sex.
- [Scholar's Insight](/wiki/Skyrim:Scholar%27s_Insight "Skyrim:Scholar's Insight") — Reading Skill Books gives you an extra Skill Point.
