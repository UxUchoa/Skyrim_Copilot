Become Thane of the Reach

## Radiant Options

This is a radiant quest which you can receive from [Igmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Igmund "Skyrim:Igmund") or [Thongvor Silver-Blood](/wiki/Skyrim:Thongvor_Silver-Blood "Skyrim:Thongvor Silver-Blood"), whichever is Jarl of the Reach. The jarl lives in [Understone Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep "Skyrim:Understone Keep") in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth"). The jarl can be found in multiple places - arguing with [Calcelmo](/wiki/Skyrim:Calcelmo "Skyrim:Calcelmo"), roaming the halls, sleeping in his bedroom or sitting on the throne.

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Befriend the Jarl of the Reach.
2. Assist five people of the Reach.
3. Purchase [Vlindrel Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Vlindrel_Hall "Skyrim:Vlindrel Hall").
4. Return to the jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This is an umbrella quest for several other quests that must be completed if you wish to become Thane of the Reach. The Jarl requires you buy the house in town as well as performing five favors for the townsfolk. Once this is achieved you can become Thane.

In order to receive this quest you must first aid the current Jarl. If the Jarl is Igmund, then you must usually [kill a Forsworn leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Forsworn_Leader_(Jarl) "Skyrim:Kill the Forsworn Leader (Jarl)") and [Find Hrolfdir's Shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeon_Delving_(Jarl_-_Hagravens) "Skyrim:Dungeon Delving (Jarl - Hagravens)"); however, if he was ousted from [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") and reinstated following [The Battle for Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Sungard (Imperial)"), that is sufficient to begin this quest. If the Jarl is Thongvor, whether he was installed via [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") or [The Battle for Fort Sungard](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Sungard (Stormcloaks)"), then the completion of that quest is sufficient to fulfill the prerequisite.

Once you have completed the prerequisite, speak with the Jarl again and he will ask you to [assist the people of the hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People "Skyrim:Making Friends and Influencing People"). You must assist five people in the hold, usually by completing [favor quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Favors "Skyrim:Favors") for them. Note that many of the quest givers are non-essential NPCs, so their deaths would result in a loss of potential quests to complete. If they die after you assist them, they still count towards your Thane status. You can also befriend merchants if you have the [Investor](/wiki/Skyrim:Investor "Skyrim:Investor") perk by investing in their stores.

Note that there are several favor quests involving members of Orc strongholds in the Reach; assisting Orcs living in strongholds does not count towards your Thane quest, but Orcs in Markarth do count.

You may consider completing [The Heart of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Heart_of_Dibella "Skyrim:The Heart of Dibella") as successfully completing it befriends all four of the priestesses of Dibella, which is almost enough to qualify for thaneship alone. It also requires you to visit [Karthwasten](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten "Skyrim:Karthwasten"); resolving the [Sanuarach mine dispute](/wiki/Skyrim:Sanuarach_Mine_(quest) "Skyrim:Sanuarach Mine (quest)") there (for either side) will be enough to complete this requirement.

You must also purchase [Vlindrel Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Vlindrel_Hall "Skyrim:Vlindrel Hall"), which costs 8,000 gold. Simply purchasing it is enough, there is no requirement to decorate, although it becomes more useful if decorated.

### Reward

After helping five inhabitants and purchasing [Vlindrel Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Vlindrel_Hall "Skyrim:Vlindrel Hall"), the journal tells you to return to the Jarl. The reward, the [Blade of the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_the_Reach "Skyrim:Blade of the Reach"), is a random, leveled, enchanted axe. There is a 50% chance that it will be a war axe (one-handed), and a 50% chance it will be a battleaxe (two-handed). You will also receive a housecarl named [Argis the Bulwark](/wiki/Skyrim:Argis_the_Bulwark "Skyrim:Argis the Bulwark"), who will be waiting for you at Vlindrel Hall.

## Bugs

- The misnamed [Blade of the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_the_Reach "Skyrim:Blade of the Reach") is an axe (not a blade).
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
- The weapon may not place properly on weapon racks or plaques. Even on racks it will place horizontally, with the blade part of the axe not showing.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- The dialogue option to start the thane quest may not appear. This happens if you have too many unfinished miscellaneous favor quests in your quest log. Do a few of these quests and the thane dialogue will appear.
