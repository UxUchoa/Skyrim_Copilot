Ash Zombie

**Ash Zombies** are [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") victims who have been transformed into disfigured [ash creatures](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Creatures "Skyrim:Ash Creatures") by [Erden Relvel](/wiki/Skyrim:Erden_Relvel "Skyrim:Erden Relvel") using the power of the [Mask of Dagoth Ur](/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Dagoth_Ur "Skyrim:Mask of Dagoth Ur"). As part of this transformation, Erden removes the soft tissue of the upper face down to the bone, as Dagoth Ur did. However, Erden goes a step further and the zombies' lips are ritualistically stitched closed. The zombies have no dialogue as a result.

Ash Zombies are relatively weak enemies, as they only attack with their fists. They can be encountered attacking [Ashfall's Tear](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashfall%27s_Tear "Skyrim:Ashfall's Tear") and guarding Erden Relvel at the [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(settlement) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (settlement)") graveyard, and a group of them can also attack you randomly as a [world interaction](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions "Skyrim:World Interactions").
