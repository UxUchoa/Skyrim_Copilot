**Brelas** is a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") [pawnbroker](/wiki/Skyrim:Pawnbroker "Skyrim:Pawnbroker") met during [Elenwen](/wiki/Skyrim:Elenwen "Skyrim:Elenwen")'s party at the [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy") during [Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity"). If you use [Erikur](/wiki/Skyrim:Erikur "Skyrim:Erikur") as a distraction option, he will attempt to seduce Brelas. Upon her rejection of his advances, he becomes angry and has her sent to the dungeons where [Etienne Rarnis](/wiki/Skyrim:Etienne_Rarnis "Skyrim:Etienne Rarnis") is also being held. You will have the option to free her.

Whether or not you freed her from the Thalmor Embassy Dungeon, she will join [Malborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Malborn "Skyrim:Malborn") in Windhelm (see [Bugs](#Bugs)) during [Find the Thalmor Assassin](/wiki/Skyrim:Find_the_Thalmor_Assassin "Skyrim:Find the Thalmor Assassin") and also accompany him to [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind").

If Brelas was not involved in the distraction, she will not be sent to the dungeons, and will thus remain in the entrance hall of the Thalmor Embassy, where the party took place.

### Diplomatic Immunity

During the party Erikur can be seen flirting with Brelas:

:   **Erikur**: *"There's a likely-looking filly. Even if she is an elf. You there! Serving girl! What's your name, dear?"*
:   **Brelas**: *"Uh, Brelas, sir. Did you need a drink? Something to eat?"*
:   **Erikur**: *"No, no, that's not what I'm interested in right now. I just wanted to get a better look at you. I like what I see, my dear. And believe me, I don't say that to everyone. I'm very discriminating when it comes to the female form."*
:   **Brelas**: *"Um, thank you. Sir. Was there anything else I can do for you?"*
:   **Erikur**: *"Oh... not at the moment. Maybe later. Don't go far."*
:   **Brelas**: *"Yes, sir."*

If Erikur is spoken to he can ask you to talk to Brelas for him:

:   **That fellow over there asked me to talk to you...**
:   *"Ugh. Erikur, right? He was talking to me earlier. I could tell what he was after. I hate working these parties. Some of the guests are nice, but there's always a few like Erikur. Please tell him to leave me alone. Politely. I'm sure you'll have better luck getting through to him than I would."*

She can also give you a drink:

:   *"I hope you're enjoying yourself, sir./ma'am"*
:   **I'd like a drink.**
:   *"Here you go, sir. Colovian brandy."*

After talking to Erikur he will walk up to Brelas and proposition her again. Their conversation will depend on what you told him earlier:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Lie** | **Truth** |
| **Erikur**: *"Hello, my dear. I got your message. Where shall we go?"*  **Brelas**: *"What? I'm sorry, sir, but you must have misunderstood. I'm not permitted to leave the party."*  **Erikur**: *"Oh, I don't think so. I think I understand perfectly. Don't worry, my dear, I will make any necessary excuses to your employer."* | **Erikur**: *"So you think you can toy with me, is that it? No, my dear. I have my heart set on you, and I always get what I want."*  **Brelas**: *"I'm sorry, sir. I didn't mean to give you the wrong impression earlier. I meant no disrespect."*  **Erikur**: *"Don't worry, I'll let you make it up to me. Now, where can we go for a little privacy, hmm?"* |

Either way, it will end disastrously, creating a distraction in the party:

:   **Erikur**: *"I demand that you have this wench removed from my presence at once!"*

- During [Find the Thalmor Assassin](/wiki/Skyrim:Find_the_Thalmor_Assassin "Skyrim:Find the Thalmor Assassin"), she will not gain any new dialogue, nor will Malborn's dialogue change to reflect the fact she is there with him.
