Bradyn

**Bradyn** is a dead [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") miner found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolbjorn_Barrow "Skyrim:Kolbjorn Barrow") during the quest [Unearthed](/wiki/Skyrim:Unearthed "Skyrim:Unearthed"). He wears [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes"), [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots "Skyrim:Boots"), and [gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1 "Skyrim:Gloves"). He carries a copy of the book *[Ahzidal's Descent](/wiki/Skyrim:Ahzidal%27s_Descent "Skyrim:Ahzidal's Descent")*, a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and other minor items and gold.
