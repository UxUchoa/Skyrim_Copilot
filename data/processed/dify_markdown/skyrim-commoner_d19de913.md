**Commoners** are [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warriors](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") found at [Sanguine](/wiki/Skyrim:Sanguine "Skyrim:Sanguine")'s banquet in [Misty Grove](/wiki/Skyrim:Misty_Grove "Skyrim:Misty Grove"). They have no unique dialogue and spend all their time feasting.

There are four variants of each Commoner, making space for two of each variant at the table. They all carry a [tankard](/wiki/Skyrim:Tankard "Skyrim:Tankard") and a slice of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), although their appearance and clothes differ:

**Commoner #1** wears a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") with a pair of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0001be1b "Skyrim:Clothing") and a [hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000209aa "Skyrim:Clothing").

**Commoner #2** wears a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000209a6 "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000209a5 "Skyrim:Clothing").

**Commoner #3** wears a [cowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#00017696 "Skyrim:Clothing"), a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothing"), and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261bd "Skyrim:Clothing").

**Commoner #4** wears a set of [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0003452e "Skyrim:Clothing") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0003452f "Skyrim:Clothing").
