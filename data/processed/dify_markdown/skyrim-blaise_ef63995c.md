Blaise

**Blaise** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") who works and lives at [Katla's Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Katla%27s_Farm "Skyrim:Katla's Farm") near [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). He sleeps outside on a hay pile in the stables.

## Dialogue

When you first approach him, he may tell you:

:   *"You probably want someone else. I'm just the stable boy."*
:   *"I take care of the horses. And the goats. And the chickens. And the garden."*
:   *"Talk to [Geimund](/wiki/Skyrim:Geimund "Skyrim:Geimund") if you want a horse, or [Katla](/wiki/Skyrim:Katla "Skyrim:Katla") if you're looking for work. I can't help you."*

If spoken to directly he can be asked questions about himself:

:   **How do you like the farm?**
:   *"I hate it. Everyone feels sorry for me, so they don't treat me too bad, but..."*
:   **Sorry for what?** or **What was your story again?** (if you asked about him before)
:   *"Both my parents were in the Legion. There was... an ambush. Katla said she could feed me if I could make myself useful. I take care of the animals, run errands, that kind of thing. I guess it could be worse. But... I'm sick of sleeping outside with the horses. I want a real home, real parents. Not... this."*

You will then have a dialogue option to [adopt](/wiki/Skyrim:Child#Adoption "Skyrim:Child") him. You can only go through with the adoption if you have a [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Houses "Skyrim:Houses") with a child's bed and container.
