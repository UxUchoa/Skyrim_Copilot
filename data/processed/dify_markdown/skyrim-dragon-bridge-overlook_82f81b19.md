Dragon Bridge Overlook

**Dragon Bridge Overlook** is a small [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Camps "Skyrim:Camps") south of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge").

#### Radiant Quests

- **[Bounty: Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Forsworn "Skyrim:Bounty: Forsworn")**: Kill a [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") leader for a bounty reward.
- **[The Ghost of Old Hroldan](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ghost_of_Old_Hroldan "Skyrim:The Ghost of Old Hroldan")**: Put an [ancient ghost](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost_of_Old_Hroldan "Skyrim:Ghost of Old Hroldan") to rest.
- **[Kill the Forsworn Leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Forsworn_Leader_(Jarl) "Skyrim:Kill the Forsworn Leader (Jarl)")**: Do a favor for [Jarl Igmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Igmund "Skyrim:Igmund") by killing a [Forsworn](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn "Skyrim:Forsworn") leader.
- **[Lisbet's Missing Shipment](/wiki/Skyrim:Lisbet%27s_Missing_Shipment "Skyrim:Lisbet's Missing Shipment")**: Retrieve a missing Dibella Statue for a struggling store.

## Walkthrough

The camp, which is on a cliff overlooking Dragon Bridge, can be accessed by following the [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River") upstream (southwest) from Dragon Bridge and then descending upon the camp, or by starting from [Bruca's Leap Redoubt](/wiki/Skyrim:Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt "Skyrim:Bruca's Leap Redoubt") and heading east. It consists of two Forsworn tents, one on either side of a campfire. Sharpened stakes and stretched hides surround the camp, which features [blue](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Blue Mountain Flower"), [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower"), and [purple mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Purple Mountain Flower"), [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle"), and two patches of [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") fungus on a huge tree stump. The camp is home to two Forsworn, one of whom is typically found inside one of the tents, while the other is on the cliff edge overlooking the town, with a quiver of [Forsworn arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Arrow "Skyrim:Forsworn Arrow") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") on top of a piece of wooden boarding at their feet. The first tent contains an unowned hay pile, a pair of [Forsworn gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Forsworn Gauntlets"), a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), and two [deer pelts](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt "Skyrim:Deer Pelt"). The second tent contains a novice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") as well as a long wooden table holding a tactical map of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), a [Forsworn axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Axe "Skyrim:Forsworn Axe"), an iron dagger, a random [potion of magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Magicka"), two bottles of ale, and a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat").
