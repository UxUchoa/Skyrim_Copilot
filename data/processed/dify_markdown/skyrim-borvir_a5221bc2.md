**Borvir** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") mage found dead at [Journeyman's Nook](/wiki/Skyrim:Journeyman%27s_Nook "Skyrim:Journeyman's Nook"), killed by a [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"). [Borvir's dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Borvir%27s_Dagger "Skyrim:Borvir's Dagger") can be found right next to his body, suggesting he tried to defend himself. He has been killed by three arrows piercing his back. If you carefully search the room, you can find a copy of the [Alchemy](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy "Skyrim:Alchemy") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim "Skyrim:Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim")* on a crate nearby as well as [Treasure Map II](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_II "Skyrim:Treasure Map II") inside the knapsack next to the door.

Borvir only has his clothes on him, a set of [blue mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Blue Mage Robes"). His [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0006b46c "Skyrim:Boots") can be found next to a fire close to his corpse.

He and his twin brother [Rundi](/wiki/Skyrim:Rundi "Skyrim:Rundi") are mentioned by [Phinis Gestor](/wiki/Skyrim:Phinis_Gestor "Skyrim:Phinis Gestor") as two of the missing apprentices from the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(faction) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (faction)"): *"And then there's Borvir and Rundi. Twins, though they rarely agreed on anything. Those two boys were obsessed with mead. Convinced they could concoct something to compete with Honningbrew. Something about using frost magic to chill the mead for a certain period of time. I felt it was a waste, but the Arch-Mage let them proceed."*.

## Notes

- Borvir was to be included in two [unfinished quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Unfinished_Quests "Skyrim:Unfinished Quests"). In the first, you are tasked with finding him and the three other missing apprentices. In the quest, you bring [Borvir's Dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Borvir%27s_Dagger "Skyrim:Borvir's Dagger") back to Phinis Gestor, who will say, *"Yes, I recognize it. Well, I suppose I needn't wonder where he is anymore."* In the second, he and his twin brother would open up a popular meadery in [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"), and you would be tasked with bringing their mead to either [Candlehearth Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlehearth_Hall "Skyrim:Candlehearth Hall"), [The Bannered Mare](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare "Skyrim:The Bannered Mare") or [The Frozen Hearth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth "Skyrim:The Frozen Hearth").
- Similar to Rundi, Borvir still has a complete AI schedule present. Unlike Rundi, however, Borvir is also still part of the related Frost River Farm faction.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 3.0.6 of [The Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch") removes Borvir from the Frost River Farm Faction as a precaution. This update of the patch removes many crime factions that were leftover on corpse NPCs, as resurrecting and then re-killing them could net you bounties in holds that were completely removed from your current location. However, the faction in question is not a standard crime faction. Due to Borvir not being a part of any, crimes committed against him, even if registered, would technically not result in you getting a bounty.
