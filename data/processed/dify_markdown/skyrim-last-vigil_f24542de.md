Last Vigil

**Last Vigil** is a small [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Camps "Skyrim:Camps") northeast of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften").

Upon reaching level 80, you will be approached by the [Ebony Warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Warrior "Skyrim:Ebony Warrior"), who will challenge you to meet him at this location to battle him in a duel.

## Related Quests

- **[The Ebony Warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ebony_Warrior "Skyrim:The Ebony Warrior")**: Locate and defeat the [Ebony Warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Warrior "Skyrim:Ebony Warrior").

## Walkthrough

The camp is high in the snowy [Velothi Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Velothi_Mountains "Skyrim:Velothi Mountains"). Several [ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Ores_and_Ingots "Skyrim:Ores and Ingots") are found along the path, which starts east of [Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall "Skyrim:Fort Greenwall").

The only distinguishable features of the camp are a collapsed tent and some skeletal remains. The camp contains a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), a [moonstone ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Moonstone Ore Vein"), a [quicksilver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore Vein"), and a copy of the [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Art of War Magic](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Art_of_War_Magic "Skyrim:The Art of War Magic")*. Before starting the quest [The Ebony Warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ebony_Warrior "Skyrim:The Ebony Warrior"), the camp will be occupied by a single leveled [creature](/wiki/Skyrim:Creatures "Skyrim:Creatures"), which could be a [snow bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear "Skyrim:Snow Bear") or a [frost troll](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll") at higher levels. After starting the quest, the camp will be occupied by the Ebony Warrior, with a dead frost troll nearby.

## Notes

- Along with the [Northern Maiden](/wiki/Skyrim:Northern_Maiden "Skyrim:Northern Maiden"), this is one of only two locations added by *[Dragonborn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn")* within [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim_(place) "Skyrim:Skyrim (place)"), and the only discoverable one.
- On the local map, the location is called *Ebony Warrior's Camp*.

- The camp does not have its parent region set to [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift").
