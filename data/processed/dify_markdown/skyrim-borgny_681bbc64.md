Borgny mining in Quicksilver Mine in Dawnstar

Borgny is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)"), and one of the many people who work in [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine") in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

He works inside the mine around the clock and never even sees the light of day. Occasionally, he sits down on one of the available chairs and rests. He never pauses to eat or sleep.

He wears a set of [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"), and is equipped with a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"). He carries a selection of [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

Borgny will only ever speak about work if spoken to.

:   *"Plenty of iron to dig."*

:   *"Mining's an honest trade. No real tricks to it."*
