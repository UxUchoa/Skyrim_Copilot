## Detailed Walkthrough

Shortly after completing [Restoring Order](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoring_Order "Skyrim:Restoring Order"), you will be approached by a courier and given the note *[A Request to Meet](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Request_to_Meet "Skyrim:A Request to Meet")*. Travel to the specified location - the coast under Solitude's Great Arch - and you'll find a Golden Saint named [Staada](/wiki/Skyrim:Staada "Skyrim:Staada"). Speaking with her, you can give her the [Sheogorath-Shaped Amber](/wiki/Skyrim:Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber "Skyrim:Sheogorath-Shaped Amber"), the [Sword of Jyggalag](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword_of_Jyggalag "Skyrim:Sword of Jyggalag"), or neither.

If you choose to give her either the amber or the sword, she will give you her helmet as a reward, then disappear. Keeping both items will turn her hostile, but you can use the amber to learn a permanent summon spell for her (see [notes](#Notes) section). Keeping both items will also forfeit acquiring Staada's Helmet but you will still have both the Sword of Jyggalag and the Sheogorath-Shaped Amber.

## Notes

- If you kept the Sheogorath-Shaped Amber it can be used at the [Atronach Forge](/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach_Forge "Skyrim:Atronach Forge"). Warning, the Sheogorath-Shaped Amber will be destroyed upon choosing one of these options. These combinations do not require the use of a [Sigil Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Sigil_Stone "Skyrim:Sigil Stone"). The Staada summoned at the Forge using the Emerald will be hostile.
  - Sheogorath-Shaped Amber + [Emerald](/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald "Skyrim:Emerald") will resummon [Staada](/wiki/Skyrim:Staada "Skyrim:Staada") for another battle.
  - Sheogorath-Shaped Amber + [Ruined Book](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book "Skyrim:Ruined Book") + [Bliss Bug Thorax](/wiki/Skyrim:Bliss_Bug_Thorax "Skyrim:Bliss Bug Thorax") + [Sabre Cat Pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt "Skyrim:Sabre Cat Pelt") will give you a spell tome to summon Staada.
  - Sheogorath-Shaped Amber + Roll Of Paper + Charcoal will give you a scroll to summon Staada only once.

## Bugs

- It is possible to obtain infinite copies of Staada's Helmet. After choosing to give Staada the Sword of Jyggalag, you can exit the conversation and drop the sword before it is removed from your inventory. You will still receive the helmet and can pick up the sword again to repeat the glitch.
  - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch"), version 1.6.318, fixes this bug.
