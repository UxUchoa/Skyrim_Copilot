Pinefrost Tower

**Pinefrost Tower** is a small ruined [tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Towers "Skyrim:Imperial Towers") northwest of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge") containing leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals").

## Walkthrough

The fallen tower is atop [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar")'s western mountains a short distance south of [Northwatch Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Keep "Skyrim:Northwatch Keep"). Two leveled snow animals or creatures (which may include [frost trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll "Skyrim:Frost Troll"), [ice wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf "Skyrim:Ice Wolf"), [ice wraiths](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith "Skyrim:Ice Wraith"), or [snow bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear "Skyrim:Snow Bear")) guard the tower. There is a lit campfire with a pot hanging over it; inside the pot is a bowl of [venison stew](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison_Stew "Skyrim:Venison Stew"). Behind the fire are three pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"). Across the ramp near the lit campfire is a short flight of stairs. At the end of the stairs is an apprentice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing [leveled loot](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). In front of the chest is a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a random one-handed [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword").
