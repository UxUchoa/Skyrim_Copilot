# Skyrim:Corsair

## Overview

**Corsairs** are hostile NPCs unique to the [Dainty Sload](/wiki/Skyrim:Dainty_Sload "Skyrim:Dainty Sload"), a pirate ship docked near [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). They are under the command of the elusive Captain Volf, who is never encountered in-game.

The corsair patrolling the decks

These two **Corsairs** are male [Nords](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") on the Dainty Sload. They each carry a [scimitar](/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar "Skyrim:Scimitar") and a small amount of gold. They wear [ragged trousers](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers "Skyrim:Ragged Trousers"), [Redguard boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Boots "Skyrim:Redguard Boots"), and [fur bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Bracers "Skyrim:Fur Bracers"). One sits at a table in the downstairs kitchen-like room with the corsair in the section above. The other is at the top, guarding the entrance to the interior of the ship.
