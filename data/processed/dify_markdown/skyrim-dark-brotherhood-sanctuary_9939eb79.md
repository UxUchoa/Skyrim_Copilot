Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary

The **Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary** is an active [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") sanctuary west of [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath"). It contains only one zone, *[Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](#Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary)*.

Its [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") teaches part of the [Marked for Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Marked_for_Death "Skyrim:Marked for Death") [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").
The door is hidden beneath the road just west of Falkreath. The entrance will only be added to your map once you have completed the quest [With Friends Like These](/wiki/Skyrim:With_Friends_Like_These "Skyrim:With Friends Like These"). Prior to this, the door will be present, but the location will not appear on your map, and the correct answer to the question required to open the door will be unavailable until you are taught it by [Astrid](/wiki/Skyrim:Astrid "Skyrim:Astrid") during "[With Friends Like These...](/wiki/Skyrim:With_Friends_Like_These... "Skyrim:With Friends Like These...")" or by [Commander Maro](/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro "Skyrim:Commander Maro") during "[Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](/wiki/Skyrim:Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood! "Skyrim:Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!")".

### Exterior

There is a black pool to the right of the door from which [Shadowmere](/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmere "Skyrim:Shadowmere") emerges during [The Cure for Madness](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cure_for_Madness "Skyrim:The Cure for Madness"). There are also several [nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") plants around the area. To the northwest are two [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"). Once accepted into the Dark Brotherhood, travel to Falkreath and approach the door. It will ask you "What is the music of life?" Answer with the password Astrid or Commander Maro taught you, "Silence, my brother". You will be welcomed to your new "home" and the door will open. Any incorrect answers result in the door stating "You are not worthy". You are able to keep answering without further penalty.

### Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_02.jpg)

Astrid's office

Upon entering there are Dark Brotherhood banners on either side of the passage, which leads down a short flight of stairs, around a corner into the first room. The first time you arrive, you will find Astrid leaning against the wall at the top of another flight of stairs to the north, with a doorway to the west.

There is a long stone table with a tactical map of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") on top. Anytime you enter, the map will be marked with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") stabbed into it showing the location of the current main Dark Brotherhood quest. There is also a copy of *[The Night Mother's Truth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Night_Mother%27s_Truth "Skyrim:The Night Mother's Truth")* on the table. Behind the table is a throne and small cupboard holding clutter. On the right, against the wall are a couple of sets of shelves and a cupboard holding some [ruined books](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book "Skyrim:Ruined Book"), a copy of *[Lost Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends "Skyrim:Lost Legends")*, one empty and one filled [petty soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Petty Soul Gem"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a copy of the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Sithis](/wiki/Skyrim:Sithis "Skyrim:Sithis")*, a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), and a full set of shrouded clothing including [shrouded robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Robes "Skyrim:Shrouded Robes"), a [shrouded hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Hood "Skyrim:Shrouded Hood"), [shrouded hand wraps](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Hand_Wraps "Skyrim:Shrouded Hand Wraps"), a [shrouded cowl maskless](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Cowl_Maskless "Skyrim:Shrouded Cowl Maskless"), and [shrouded shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Shoes "Skyrim:Shrouded Shoes"). Only the cowl is counted as [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor"), the rest are clothes. There are also some crates, a barrel, and a couple of sacks of food.

The room to the west is Astrid and [Arnbjorn](/wiki/Skyrim:Arnbjorn "Skyrim:Arnbjorn")'s bedroom, although the double bed on the south wall is unowned. Behind the head of the bed is a set of shelves holding more ruined books, a leveled [helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet "Skyrim:Helmet"), and a bottle of alto wine. To the left of the bed is a dresser with an [unusual gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem") on top, required as part of the quest [No Stone Unturned](/wiki/Skyrim:No_Stone_Unturned "Skyrim:No Stone Unturned"). There is a large primitive fireplace against the west wall with a large pile of firewood to the left. There is an end table to the right of the bed and two chairs in front of the fire. A wardrobe is against the north wall concealing access to a hidden room (see [notes](#Notes)). To the right of the wardrobe is a small table and chairs, with another bottle of alto wine on top.

Main area

Descending the stairs to the north leads to a large chamber where you will meet the rest of the "family", who are gathered together talking when you enter. There is a word wall to the north, where you learn a word for the Marked for Death shout. To the right is a wide flight of stairs leading up to a passage and to the right of this is a pool of water fed by a waterfall with a large circular stained glass window above which depicts [Sithis](/wiki/Lore:Sithis "Lore:Sithis") as a skeleton. There are many different fungi around the room and throughout the sanctuary as listed below. There are many Dark Brotherhood banners hanging from the walls of the chamber. To the right of the pool is a second passage. To the west is a large fireplace with an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"), [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") in front along with a long wooden table holding an [iron battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe "Skyrim:Iron Battleaxe"), an [iron greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Greatsword "Skyrim:Iron Greatsword"), an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet"), and an [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield"). This is where you will normally find Arnbjorn working. To the right of this are several archery targets and practice dummies and where you will normally find [Veezara](/wiki/Skyrim:Veezara "Skyrim:Veezara") either sitting on the ground or practicing with the dummies.

Lis' pit

In an alcove to the north is an [arcane enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter"). [Festus Krex](/wiki/Skyrim:Festus_Krex "Skyrim:Festus Krex") and [Gabriella](/wiki/Skyrim:Gabriella "Skyrim:Gabriella") can also both normally be found in this area. Dining room

To the right is another counter with shelves above it holding two [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), a tomato, and a couple of loaves of bread. To the northeast is a flight of wooden stairs leading up into sleeping quarters. Under these are several crates, a barrel, and a sack of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"). To the left is a cupboard with two sacks and a bottle of alto wine on the shelves. Hanging above is a bunch of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear") and two [garlic braids](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic_Braid "Skyrim:Garlic Braid"). ### Changes during related quests

Once you have completed at least one of your initial contracts to kill [Narfi](/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi "Skyrim:Narfi"), [Beitild](/wiki/Skyrim:Beitild "Skyrim:Beitild"), or [Ennodius Papius](/wiki/Skyrim:Ennodius_Papius "Skyrim:Ennodius Papius"), the next time you enter the sanctuary you will find a commotion in the main area, with all the family including Astrid gathered around a new person named [Cicero](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero "Skyrim:Cicero"). You may have seen this person before on the road near Loreius Farm with a broken wagon wheel in need of help. There is a [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial "Skyrim:Delayed Burial") to convince Loreius to assist Cicero with the broken wheel that can be undertaken prior to meeting him in the sanctuary. Cicero is an Imperial assassin and Keeper of the Dark Brotherhood. As Keeper, he is responsible for caring for the [Night Mother](/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Mother "Skyrim:Night Mother")'s body and casket. He's come to Skyrim with the Night Mother in a large crate, seeking protection for them, at the last functioning sanctuary in the empire, after the last sanctuary in Cyrodiil fell. Most of the family seem unhappy about his arrival, with the exception of Festus Krex who is overjoyed and believes this marks the beginning of the return to the old ways.

The next time you visit the sanctuary you will find the Night Mother's casket has been moved and is standing upright in the small room behind the stained glass window. Cicero takes the strange shaped room at the back of the sanctuary next to Babette's as his sleeping quarters.

Following the completion of the Dark Brotherhood quest [Breaching Security](/wiki/Skyrim:Breaching_Security "Skyrim:Breaching Security"), a total of five journals written by Cicero can be found in his quarters. They detail his life from his origins in Bruma, all the way through to recent events, including mentioning another disused sanctuary in Dawnstar, where he originally went when arriving in Skyrim. In the final volume it talks of the existence of another sanctuary in Dawnstar and provides the password granting access.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_03.jpg)

Astrid and Arnbjorn's room

Upon returning to the sanctuary at the end of the quest [To Kill an Empire](/wiki/Skyrim:To_Kill_an_Empire "Skyrim:To Kill an Empire"), you will find several Penitus Oculatus agents on the road leading to the sanctuary along with horses and carriages loaded with firewood and barrels of oil, with more agents and barrels stacked near the entrance. There are oil slicks on and around the barrels, which can be ignited with any flames. Festus Krex can be found dead to the right of the entrance, pinned to a tree by many iron arrows. Upon entering the sanctuary, all is in pandemonium with fires raging throughout and agents slaughtering any of your brethren they find. They are led by [Arcturus](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcturus "Skyrim:Arcturus"). As you move through the sanctuary the fires continue to grow, herding you ever deeper. There are bodies of many dead agents, showing how valiantly each of your family fought, but it is obvious by the sheer number of agents it's overwhelming. In the first room, where you would normally find Astrid, is the body of Veezara, along with two agents discussing who betrayed the family. In the main chamber is Arnbjorn in werewolf form, but still wearing his shrouded cuirass; his death is scripted and unavoidable. Next Gabriella's remains can be found in the room containing the arcane enchanter. She's wearing her shrouded robes, shoes, and hand wraps. In the pit are the remains of Lis; unusually you can harvest two vials of frostbite venom from her, as if she were a giant frostbite spider. In the sleeping quarters you will find [Nazir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nazir "Skyrim:Nazir") battling with Arcturus. Once Arcturus is defeated you and Nazir need to find an escape. The Night Mother tells you to embrace her.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_09.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_09.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary_09.jpg)

The secret room where you find Astrid performing the Black Sacrament

After a cutscene, you will hear Babette cajoling Nazir to dig out the Night Mother's casket more quickly. Once opened, you will find it next to the pool in the main chamber. The Night Mother instructs you to speak to Astrid. You will find her in a secret chamber off her and Arnbjorn's bedroom. The wardrobe has been pulled aside to reveal the chamber and Astrid laid out barely alive on the floor, with the [Blade of Woe](/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_Woe "Skyrim:Blade of Woe"), an iron dagger, and a nightshade flower beside her. A cave-in has blocked the passage at the top of the stairs leading to the sleeping quarters and the doorway that leads to the crafting stations and Lis' pit. There are numerous burnt corpses found in the main chamber and first room that can be looted for a few septims or a sample of bone meal. There is nothing else of interest. It is obvious that the sanctuary is finished and that you will need to move the Dark Brotherhood to new premises.

## Notes

- When you first enter the sanctuary after speaking with the black door, the game will not autosave.
- [Followers](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") cannot enter the sanctuary, and will stand outside waiting while you are inside. However, spectral entities such as [Arniel's ghost](/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arniel%27s_Shade "Skyrim:Summon Arniel's Shade"), will follow you in.
- Sleeping in any of the beds here will normally provide the [Rested](/wiki/Skyrim:Rested "Skyrim:Rested") bonus. However, if your spouse is currently following you (and waiting outside), you will instead receive the [Lover's Comfort](/wiki/Skyrim:Lover%27s_Comfort "Skyrim:Lover's Comfort") bonus.
- If you approach the door to the sanctuary before starting "[With Friends Like These...](/wiki/Skyrim:With_Friends_Like_These... "Skyrim:With Friends Like These...")", no map marker will be seen.
- If you complete the quest "[Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](/wiki/Skyrim:Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood! "Skyrim:Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!")" instead of the Dark Brotherhood questline, all areas of the sanctuary will remain accessible and undamaged.
- Cicero's journals appear in his room during the quest [Whispers in the Dark](/wiki/Skyrim:Whispers_in_the_Dark "Skyrim:Whispers in the Dark").
- All cupboards, dressers, and end tables in the sanctuary are non-respawning and safe to store items in.

## Bugs

- After the sanctuary is destroyed, entering it may cause the game to freeze.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.9.26.0.8, fixes this bug.
- After you finish "[To Kill an Empire](/wiki/Skyrim:To_Kill_an_Empire "Skyrim:To Kill an Empire")", the items stored in the containers may disappear, and [Penitus Oculatus](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus") agents may continue to spawn outside. **?**
- If you have *[Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard")* installed, the Word Wall here may also teach you a word for the [Drain Vitality](/wiki/Skyrim:Drain_Vitality "Skyrim:Drain Vitality") shout. **?**
- If you use [Dead Thrall](/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Thrall "Skyrim:Dead Thrall") when entering the sanctuary, the thralls will disappear, but will remain on your active effects list. **?**
- The books on the bookshelf may permanently disappear after activating it. **?**
  - Reload a previous save, and try again.
- If you return to this sanctuary after the Dark Brotherhood moves into the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar_Sanctuary "Skyrim:Dawnstar Sanctuary"), the loading screen will still say "The Sanctuary in Skyrim's Pine Forest is the Dark Brotherhood's last remaining refuge in all of Tamriel." **?**
