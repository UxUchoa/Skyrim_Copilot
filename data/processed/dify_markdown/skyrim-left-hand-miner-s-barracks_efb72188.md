Miner's Barracks

**Left Hand Miner's Barracks** is the collective housing area for several miners who work in the [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine").

It is the home of three of the miners from [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine"), [Adeber](/wiki/Skyrim:Adeber "Skyrim:Adeber"), [Ennoc](/wiki/Skyrim:Ennoc "Skyrim:Ennoc") and [Willem](/wiki/Skyrim:Willem "Skyrim:Willem"). It consists of one area only, *[Miner's Barracks](#Miner.27s_Barracks)*

Before the quest [Kolskeggr Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolskeggr_Mine_(quest) "Skyrim:Kolskeggr Mine (quest)") is completed, two refugees will also live in the barracks, namely [Pavo Attius](/wiki/Skyrim:Pavo_Attius "Skyrim:Pavo Attius") and [Gat gro-Shargakh](/wiki/Skyrim:Gat_gro-Shargakh "Skyrim:Gat gro-Shargakh").

## Miner's Barracks

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-interior-Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg)

The Barracks Interior

The barracks are obviously only used for sleeping and are pretty much empty, save for a table with three bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and a basket with a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") and some [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese"). There are two bedrolls on the floor and, along the western wall, a bed with a belongings chest with minor loot and a bucket with three samples of [iron ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore "Skyrim:Iron Ore"). All items are free to take and the beds are free to sleep in.
