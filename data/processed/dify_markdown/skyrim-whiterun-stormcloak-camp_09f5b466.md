Whiterun Stormcloak Camp

**Whiterun Stormcloak Camp** is a [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Camps "Skyrim:Stormcloak Camps") in [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold"), located south of the [Ritual Stone](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ritual_Stone "Skyrim:The Ritual Stone") and east of [White River Watch](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River_Watch "Skyrim:White River Watch").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-place-Whiterun_Stormcloak_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Whiterun_Stormcloak_Camp_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_Stormcloak_Camp_02.jpg)

Whiterun Stormcloak Camp

The camp is commanded by [Hjornskar Head-Smasher](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjornskar_Head-Smasher "Skyrim:Hjornskar Head-Smasher"), and is never commanded by [Galmar Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Galmar_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist"), as he commands the [Whiterun Military Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Military_Camp "Skyrim:Whiterun Military Camp") during the related quest. Unlike most Stormcloak camps, it has no [Stormcloak quartermaster](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster "Skyrim:Stormcloak Quartermaster").

## Whiterun Stormcloak Camp

It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. To the northwest and southeast of the campfire are three small tents containing unowned hay piles. To the southwest of the campfire is the command tent, containing a lit brazier and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of his time. To the right of the command tent are some barrels. To the left of the command tent is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"), and an end table. To the east is the hospital tent, which contains three hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers. To the left of the hospital tent is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs") and a low table holding a random [potion of health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health "Skyrim:Potion of Health") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). Behind the hospital tent are two picketed [horses](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses"). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. North of the campfire is a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). Beyond the block beside a square stone arch is a workstation, consisting of an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a [workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench"). A long wooden table and a crate hold various [iron](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons "Skyrim:Iron Weapons") and [steel weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons "Skyrim:Steel Weapons") and [armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor "Skyrim:Armor"). West of the workstation among some rubble is a locked chest.

Any time [Whiterun Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Hold "Skyrim:Whiterun Hold") is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present; a hunter's camp will be here instead. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Joining_the_Legion "Skyrim:Joining the Legion"), you are free to sleep in the small tents.

## Bugs

- The camp may never be disabled after [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)") is finished for the Stormcloaks.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.0.8, fixes this bug.
