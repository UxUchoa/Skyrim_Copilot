Mythic Dawn Camp

**Mythic Dawn Camp** is an expedition camp for the Mythic Dawn to reopen the ruins of [Rielle](/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle "Skyrim:Rielle"), added by [The Cause](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause "Skyrim:The Cause") creation. The camp is located on the southern slopes of the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World"), west of [Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") and north of the [Alchemist's Shack](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemist%27s_Shack "Skyrim:Alchemist's Shack").

It can be reached by traveling from the Alchemist's Shack or [Honeystrand Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Honeystrand_Cave "Skyrim:Honeystrand Cave"), by taking a mountain path that connects the two. The path leads uphill to a large flat snow-covered area. Before the camp appears, the path and plateau are nothing more than an alternate route past the bear at Honeystrand Cave, and when traveling from the southwestern end you will encounter a leveled [Bear](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear "Skyrim:Bear"), which you must kill or calm to proceed.

Several Mythic Dawn cultists and their tents appear here once stage 60 of the [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause_(quest) "Skyrim:The Cause (quest)") is reached, and they must be defeated in order to reach the tent and find the *[Excavation Leader's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Excavation_Leader%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Excavation Leader's Journal")*; the journal explains that an excavation mission is being carried out in the mountains to the south, and updates your map with the marker for [Varlais Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Varlais_Cavern "Skyrim:Varlais Cavern") if you have not already discovered it. The quest will then direct the Dragonborn to the entrance of the Ayleid Ruin of [Rielle](/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle "Skyrim:Rielle") that the excavation team has been digging to from the Skyrim side.
