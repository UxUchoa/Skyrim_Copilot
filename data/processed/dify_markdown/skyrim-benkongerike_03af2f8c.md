Benkongerike

**Benkongerike** is a medium-sized [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") southeast of [Saering's Watch](/wiki/Skyrim:Saering%27s_Watch "Skyrim:Saering's Watch") containing [bristlebacks](/wiki/Skyrim:Bristleback "Skyrim:Bristleback") and [rieklings](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling "Skyrim:Riekling"). It contains two zones: *[Benkongerike](#Benkongerike)* and *[Benkongerike Great Hall](#Benkongerike_Great_Hall)*.

Its [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") teaches part of the [Cyclone](/wiki/Skyrim:Cyclone "Skyrim:Cyclone") [dragon shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").

### Exterior

From the Headwaters of Harstrad, follow the path to the northwest, and when you reach a junction, turn left to the southwest. You eventually pass under an arch consisting of two columns with a large slab on top. On your right is a statue with unusable barrels and crates. There is a food barrel to the right of the entrance, and a novice-locked chest to the left.

### Benkongerike

As soon as you enter, you will be in a snowy tunnel descending to the northwest. As soon as the passage levels out, there is a narrow tunnel on your right that twists and turns and eventually leads to a novice-locked chest and two [burial urns](/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn "Skyrim:Burial Urn"). There is also a hidden door next to the chest that will act as a shortcut back from the end of this cave system. At the end of the initial tunnel is a riekling tent with a [deer pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt "Skyrim:Deer Pelt") inside and a piece of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") to the left. A tunnel on the left leads into a large cavern.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/SR-interior-Benkongerike.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Benkongerike.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Benkongerike.jpg)

First cavern

In the cavern, you can see three large pillars (all leaning over), a nearby riekling camp consisting of two riekling huts and a riekling tent, and various crates, barrels, and sacks all around the room below you. Two more riekling tents can be seen in the distance. There are three rieklings running about that you can see. The path down is on your right, and follows the wall around the cavern to place you beside a riekling hut to your right. A tent to your left contains a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"). To the right of the tent is the second hut, and beside this hut is a bottle of alto wine, a [pine thrush egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), two bulbs of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), and a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), all beside and on top of a crate. In the middle of this area among more crates is a large [urn](/wiki/Skyrim:Urn "Skyrim:Urn").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-interior-Benkongerike_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Benkongerike_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Benkongerike_02.jpg)

Bristleback

On your right as you move through the cavern heading south, past the first riekling hut are two large urns and two burial urns lined up in a row against the rocks. Just past these urns is another hut to the right. Behind this hut are some large mammoth ribcages, and to the left are two more large urns by a fence behind one of the pillars. In front of the same pillar is yet another tent, with an archery target inside, and a large urn and a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd") to the right. To the left (west) is a tall lookout platform with a riekling on guard and a bristleback wandering around the bottom, next to another tent to the left. This tent has a burial urn inside, a large urn to the left, and an open crate containing two samples of [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool") and a sample of [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot") in front. Beside the fence and pillar to the right of the platform is another tent. Inside this tent is a sample of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat"), three [steel ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot "Skyrim:Steel Ingot") hidden under a kettle, and a sack. To the left of the tent is a barrel with a pair of weightless [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes "Skyrim:Shoes") on top, with a [bowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl "Skyrim:Bowl") and a [goblet](/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet "Skyrim:Goblet") on a nearby crate (see [bugs](#Bugs)). Returning to the area near the entrance of this first cavern, to the left along the southeast wall is a statue with an unlit brazier in front holding an [iron helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet "Skyrim:Iron Helmet"). To the right is a tent with the usual crates, barrels, and sacks piled on either side and a [drum](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") in front. To the right are two barrels and a crate, with an empty random soul gem and a [silver necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Necklace "Skyrim:Silver Necklace") on top. Between these items and the tunnel onward are three large urns and two burial urns. The tunnel onward leading south is to the right.

Following the tunnel south, it becomes an icy passage. At the end is a food barrel with a piece of large [charcoal](/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal "Skyrim:Charcoal") on top, and three samples of wheat, a bowl, and two more burial urns on the ground beside it. The tunnel turns to the west, passing a rock on the right with a flute on top, then a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack") and two large urns on your left beside a large rock. The tunnel then turns to the north and becomes a ledge above another cavern, where there are three rieklings. In front of the ledge is a platform with a ramp down to the cavern floor. On your right on the ledge is an alcove holding two large urns, an unlocked chest with a deer pelt to the left of it, and a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"). At the end of the ledge in the northeastern corner is a riekling hut, beside which is a large urn, a deer pelt, a sample of [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota"), some [hawk feathers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk_Feathers "Skyrim:Hawk Feathers"), a sample of [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap"), and an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"). A wooden plate to the left of the hut holds some slaughterfish scales and a large piece of charcoal. Farther west is another tent containing two wooden plates holding a bulb of garlic, a filled random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), and a [flawless sapphire](/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Sapphire "Skyrim:Flawless Sapphire").

Underneath the ramp down is an unlocked [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox") and a cast iron pot containing a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"). The cavern continues to the west. Against the southern wall is a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") beside a chair. To the west is a large rock with a bucket containing eight loose coins and a rock warbler egg. Beneath this rock is an iron helmet. In the southwestern corner is a tent with an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") in a kettle, with an urn beside the tent. To the right along the wall next to a large rock with an animal skull displayed on top are two food sacks, with two bottles of alto wine among them. The cavern turns to the northwest, with a tent to the right with a fire in front of it. Around the fire are two [ash yams](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam"), two sheaves of wheat, and two [rabbit haunches](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_Haunch "Skyrim:Rabbit Haunch"). To the left of the tent, beside the next tunnel is a drum and a large carved stone head.

An ice tunnel continues to the northwest and descends to another tent. Just before reaching the tent, a [riekling barrel](/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Barrel "Skyrim:Riekling Barrel") on the right will burst open, revealing a riekling. At the end of this tunnel, a riekling is hiding in a bush to the left of the tent, which contains two pieces of firewood. To the left is a woodcutter's axe in a kettle, and to the right, on a rock under some clutter, is a [snow fox pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox_Pelt "Skyrim:Snow Fox Pelt"). The tunnel turns to the northeast and continues to descend. Take care, as there is a [tripwire](/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire "Skyrim:Tripwire") ahead that triggers a [claw](/wiki/Skyrim:Claw "Skyrim:Claw") trap, hinged on the left. Just past the tripwire, an alcove on the left has a tent with a riekling barrel in front. Inside the tent is a pair of [iron boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots"), and two urns flank the tent. Past this alcove is a second tripwire that triggers another claw trap, hinged on the right. By the claw on the right is a riekling hut, and to the left of the hut is a stone bowl containing a [Dibella statue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dibella_Statue "Skyrim:Dibella Statue"). Farther to the left is a large rock with a knapsack, a [potion of cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Potion of Cure Disease"), an ash yam in a cast iron pot, and a salt pile. The tunnel ends to the northeast with an urn and some riekling spears on a crate, and a riekling hidden in the snow. There is another tunnel heading to the northwest, with a tripwire at the start of the tunnel triggering another claw trap, hinged from above. The tunnel turns back to the northeast and opens into another cavern.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/SR-interior-Benkongerike_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Benkongerike_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Benkongerike_04.jpg)

Bridges

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-interior-Benkongerike_05.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Benkongerike_05.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Benkongerike_05.jpg)

Flooded cavern

You will enter this cavern on a ledge, with bridges ahead joining several platforms, ledges, and columns, and a ramp down to the cavern floor below on the left. On the right is a bucket containing a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), a [chef's hat](/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Hat "Skyrim:Chef's Hat"), and a [chef's tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Tunic "Skyrim:Chef's Tunic"). Below, the cavern is partially flooded. On the ledges, quite a few rieklings are running about. Crossing the first bridge to the first platform, there are three bottles of alto wine, two bottles of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), and a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge") in a bucket. Two rieklings are on this platform. Across a second bridge heading southeast to the next platform, there is a [leg of goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat "Skyrim:Leg of Goat") and an unlocked chest hanging off the right side, with a drum to your left. Two more rieklings are in this area. Continuing to the southeast across another short bridge to a well-lit area, the next bridge is to the north. There are then two more bridges, the second of which bears to the northeast, where another riekling stands. The next bridge is to the southeast; it leads to the far side of the cavern and a ledge with a doorway to the east. There is an ash yam on a crate to the left by the bridge, and to the right of the doorway are five pieces of [clam meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat "Skyrim:Clam Meat") and two [pearls](/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl "Skyrim:Pearl"), most of which are in a bucket. From this ledge, there is also another bridge to the south to another ledge against the south wall, where another riekling stands and a ramp leads down to two [silver ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") and a burial urn.

The best place to drop into the water is from the second silver ore vein, as a novice-locked chest sits beneath it. There are two more silver ore veins slightly west of the chest, behind a rock. The only other item of interest on the lower level is a random enchanted [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword"), which is found between the ramp at the beginning of the cavern and the first platform. The sword is beside an unlootable skeleton at the narrowest part. This provides the easiest access to the ramp back up.

In the tunnel to the east, the architecture changes to that of a [Nordic barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Ruins "Skyrim:Nordic Ruins"). At the end of this short corridor is an unlit brazier on a stone plinth containing a pair of random boots. A corridor to the north leads into a room with an iron door opposite. There are two goblets, a bowl, and a burial urn in the corridor. The room contains six barrels at the other end, three of which are actually riekling barrels which rieklings will burst from to ambush you. This will scatter a number of items around the room. There are two gourds, a rock warbler egg, three random potions, and two common books; some of these items may remain on a set of shelves in the room. The iron door leads to *Benkongerike Great Hall*.

### Benkongerike Great Hall

Through the door is another room, with a broken sideboard on your left and a piece of [quicksilver ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore "Skyrim:Quicksilver Ore") in front. Farther along the left wall is a bookcase containing some riekling spears and four [random](/wiki/Skyrim:LItemBookClutter "Skyrim:LItemBookClutter") books. On the right side of the room is a riekling tent containing thirteen copies of *[The Lusty Argonian Maid, v1](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid,_v1 "Skyrim:The Lusty Argonian Maid, v1")* and six copies of *[v2](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid,_v2 "Skyrim:The Lusty Argonian Maid, v2")*, with a [Telekinesis](/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis "Skyrim:Telekinesis") [spell tome](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tomes "Skyrim:Spell Tomes") hidden under them all. At the far end of the room are three large urns (two on the left, one on the right) and a doorway to an ascending wooden spiral staircase in the northern wall.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-interior-Benkongerike_07.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Benkongerike_07.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Benkongerike_07.jpg)

Riekling infestation

Climbing the staircase, there are still plenty of signs of rieklings, with painted symbols on the walls. At the top, the corridor heads north again. On the left, you pass some bars through which you can see a chest, two pedestals, and two urns. There is an open iron door on your right heading east. Opposite is an expert-locked iron door and some more bars farther along on the left. Through the locked door, the chest in front of you is unlocked, with the two urns on the far left. The left-hand pedestal holds a copy of the [Benkongerike Key](/wiki/Skyrim:Benkongerike_Key "Skyrim:Benkongerike Key"), and the other pedestal holds a copy of *[Dragon Language: Myth no More](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Language:_Myth_no_More "Skyrim:Dragon Language: Myth no More")*. Following the corridor to the east, there are two burial urns where it turns to the south. The room ahead opens into another large room on a balcony overlooking the floor below. There are bridges around this room joining platforms on top of columns. There are three rieklings and a [mounted riekling](/wiki/Skyrim:Mounted_Riekling "Skyrim:Mounted Riekling") in this room. On your left is an urn, and to the right are the wooden steps down.

At the bottom of the steps, ahead are two barrels in the room's northeast corner (see [bugs](#Bugs)). Under the steps is a [rock warbler egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg") and a [chicken's egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg") on the ground. To the left is an open sarcophagus with a dead [draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Draugr "Skyrim:Containers") inside and a bulb of garlic on the northwestern edge of the sarcophagus, as well as a cast iron pot containing a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute") and [potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"). Along the wall to the left is another sarcophagus, which has a [stalhrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim "Skyrim:Stalhrim") deposit covering another dead draugr. The first pillar nearest the bottom of the stairs has a bucket with two pieces of [corundum ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore "Skyrim:Corundum Ore") inside. The pillar to the right has a crate and a barrel beside it with a [torchbug thorax](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug_Thorax "Skyrim:Torchbug Thorax"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and an [orange dartwing](/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing "Skyrim:Orange Dartwing") on the crate, and a bowl of [bone meal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal "Skyrim:Bone Meal") in a metal bowl on the barrel.

Against the east wall is a crate, with a medium coin purse on top and a sack beside it. To the right is a collapsed pillar, beside which are three [corundum ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ingot "Skyrim:Corundum Ingot"), several pieces of cheese, a pair of [leather boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots "Skyrim:Leather Boots"), and on top of a barrel a [gold sapphire ring](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Sapphire_Ring "Skyrim:Gold Sapphire Ring") that is easily missed. To the west, past another pillar, are wooden steps leading up to the walkways above. Under the steps are two unusable barrels, a crate, two sacks, a pair of iron boots, and a practice dummy.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-interior-Benkongerike_08.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Benkongerike_08.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Benkongerike_08.jpg)

Rune totems

The steps lead to a balcony with a riekling hut to the right. Beside the hut are two barrels and two crates, among which are a [chaurus egg](/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Eggs "Skyrim:Chaurus Eggs"), an imp stool sample in a bucket, an apothecary's satchel, and two [charred skeever hides](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide"). Opposite is an urn, and farther along is a stone walkway over to the first of the pillars and onward to the second pillar. On your left is a platform built on the side of the pillar, with a piece of [honeycomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Honeycomb "Skyrim:Honeycomb") on a [plate](/wiki/Skyrim:Plate "Skyrim:Plate") on the ground. Another walkway continues to another balcony on the other side of the room. There is another hut on this side, next to which is an open box containing two iron ingots and a flute, with a crate and two barrels beside it. On one barrel is an expert-locked [East Empire Company strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox "Skyrim:East Empire Company Strongbox"), and on the other, which is a fish barrel, is a potato on a plate. Also on this balcony is a sack next to a crate, as well as three garlic bulbs in a bucket, a [leek](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), and a [silver ingot](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot"), all by two barrels and a crate. There is also a large piece of stone carved with the symbol of a whale, as generally used for [rune totem](/wiki/Skyrim:Rune_Totems "Skyrim:Rune Totems") puzzles.

On the southern side of the room on ground level, among some rune totems, are some crates with two goblets, a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), and a bowl with [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") inside. There is also a gate blocking a doorway in the middle of the southern wall. The symbols needed are above the rune totems, except for the second, which is on the eastern balcony. The handle to operate the gate is on a raised section between two pillars, with lit braziers lighting the area. Once you've lined up the correct symbols (from left to right: **Eagle—Whale—Whale—Snake**), pull the handle to open the gate. Incorrect guesses seem to have no negative repercussions. You can see the word wall through the gate. The wall teaches a word for the Cyclone shout. In front of the wall is an unlocked boss chest.

On the east side of the room with the wall is a cave-in, leaving a ramp up to an opening in the east wall. This leads to a tunnel that twists and turns and eventually opens into a circular room with the *[Black Book: Untold Legends](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book:_Untold_Legends_(book) "Skyrim:Black Book: Untold Legends (book)")*. There is an iron door to the south leading back to the first zone. Through this door is a small room with two large urns on your left, and a short tunnel ahead leading to a hidden door. The chain to open the door is to the right of the door, and the passage beyond leads you back to the first chest with two burial urns near the exit.

## Notes

- The name *Benkongerike* is a Norwegian compound word, comprising *ben* meaning *bone/leg* and *kongerike* meaning *kingdom*. It can thus be translated as *Bone Kingdom* or *Kingdom of Bones*.
- In [Survival Mode](/wiki/Skyrim:Survival_Mode "Skyrim:Survival Mode"), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
- Benkongerike also [appears](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Benkongerike "Bloodmoon:Benkongerike") in *[Bloodmoon](/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon "Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon")*.

However, when you approach it, you don't get the option to "mine deposit"; you only get the "search draugr" option, as if the deposit was not there. **?**
