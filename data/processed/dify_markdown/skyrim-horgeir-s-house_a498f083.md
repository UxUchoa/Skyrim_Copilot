Horgeir's House

**Horgeir's House** is a residence in the northwestern part of [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge"), located next to [Lylvieve Family's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_Family%27s_House "Skyrim:Lylvieve Family's House") and across from [Four Shields Tavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Shields_Tavern "Skyrim:Four Shields Tavern").

It is the home of [Horgeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Horgeir "Skyrim:Horgeir"), his wife [Olda](/wiki/Skyrim:Olda "Skyrim:Olda"), and his brother [Lodvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Lodvar "Skyrim:Lodvar"). It consists of one zone, *[Horgeir's House](#Horgeir.27s_House)*.

## Horgeir's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House.jpg)

Fireplace and single bed area

The front door to this small home opens to a view of the fireplace at the center, with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") at its base and an [Imperial sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword "Skyrim:Imperial Sword") on the mantel. On the floor to the right of the fireplace is a woven basket containing four [red apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and five [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), while four pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") sit in a pile to the left.

On the east side of the home is a small bedroom area, with a single bed in the northeast corner. A [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute") can be found propped against the wall next to the head and two [drums](/wiki/Skyrim:Drum "Skyrim:Drum") sit at the foot of the bed. Beneath the bed is a copy of the book *[Spirit of Nirn](/wiki/Skyrim:Spirit_of_Nirn "Skyrim:Spirit of Nirn")*. In the southeast corner, a small table holds a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), two [boiled creme treats](/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat "Skyrim:Boiled Creme Treat"), three bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), and a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"). On the opposite side of the home, another bedroom area can be found in the northwest corner, with a double bed along the north wall. Next to the bed, a small table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar Mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") sits in the corner. A non-respawning wardrobe is situated next to the table, with a small non-respawning chest to its left.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House_02.jpg)

Dining area and double bed area

The south wall on the west side of the home is host to a cozy dining area. A respawning food barrel rests next to the front door with some [sliced goat cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Goat Cheese") on top, next to a large dining table with a pleasant array of food: an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") bulb, three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), one [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), two bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), a serving of [baked potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes"), a sweet roll, an [apple pie](/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie "Skyrim:Apple Pie"), a serving of [grilled leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks "Skyrim:Grilled Leeks"), and two bottles of Nord mead. To the right of the table sits a woven basket containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), with a hanging rack above it holding three freshly caught [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). A set of shelves next to the basket holds an additional two leeks, a garlic bulb, and three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), along with an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), some [sliced eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), and a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") on top. Above the shelves are several hanging ingredients, including two garlic braids, three portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and one dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). A respawning meat barrel and respawning food barrel sit in the corner next to the shelves.

## Notes

- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Horgeir%27s_House_Key "Skyrim:Horgeir's House Key") to which is carried by Horgeir, Lodvar, and Olda.
