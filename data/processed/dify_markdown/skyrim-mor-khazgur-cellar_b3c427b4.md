**Mor Khazgur Cellar** is a cellar located in the [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") stronghold of [Mor Khazgur](/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur "Skyrim:Mor Khazgur") behind the [Mor Khazgur Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Larak%27s_Longhouse "Skyrim:Larak's Longhouse").

## Mor Khazgur Cellar

Upon entering via the novice-locked trapdoor, you look down a sloping passage that appears as a natural cave rather than a normal cellar. On the left-hand side of this passage are three [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") mushrooms. The passage opens into a room with a central pillar. To the left is a wicker fence enclosing a mushroom patch of two [blisterworts](/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort "Skyrim:Blisterwort"), four [bleeding crowns](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown"), two [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot"), and two more fly amanitas. Next is an iron drying rack from which hang two braids of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") and one [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear"). To the right of this are three food barrels and three covered baskets. To the right again is another iron drying rack from which hang a dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam") and another dried elves ear. Next is an adept-locked jail door to a small room with a single shelving unit. The top shelf holds a [potion of the healer](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Healer "Skyrim:Potion of the Healer"), a [potion of minor magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Magicka"), and a random skill potion. The second shelf has a [minor healing potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"), a leveled [strength potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Strength "Skyrim:Potion of Strength"), and a random leveled skill potion. To the right of the jail door are some [hanging moss](/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss "Skyrim:Hanging Moss") and a chest containing some minor loot. Next on the east wall is another shelving unit with three bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on the top shelf and three bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on the bottom.
