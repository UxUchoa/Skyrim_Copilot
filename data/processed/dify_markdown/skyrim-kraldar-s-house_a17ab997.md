Kraldar's House

**Kraldar's House** is located in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"), the last building on the left before the bridge to the [College](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-interior-Kraldar%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Kraldar%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Kraldar%27s_House.jpg)

Interior of Kraldar's House

It is the home of [Kraldar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kraldar "Skyrim:Kraldar") and his manservant, [Thonjolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Thonjolf "Skyrim:Thonjolf").

## Kraldar's House

To the left of the entrance is a single owned bed used by [Thonjolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Thonjolf "Skyrim:Thonjolf") with a couple of barrels to the right in the corner. On these are two [sweet rolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") and a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat"). Against the south wall is a cupboard, while against the west wall is a long wooden table with a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a bucket containing [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and a basket containing [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and [green apples](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple") on top. Hanging above from the ceiling is a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), two bunches of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and two [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids. To the right is a rack holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and three [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant"). Under this, to the right of the table is a bucket containing [carrots](/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot "Skyrim:Carrot") and a [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrel.

To the right of these is a set of shelves with four bottles of alto wine, four bottles of wine, and a basket containing a copy of the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Breathing Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Breathing_Water "Skyrim:Breathing Water")*. To the right of this is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and a kettle containing two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek") on the hearth to the right. There is a round wooden table and chair with a bottle of alto wine and a sweet roll in front of the hearth and on the far side of the fireplace, still against the west wall is an expert-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest"). Coming out from the north wall is a double bed used by [Kraldar](/wiki/Skyrim:Kraldar "Skyrim:Kraldar") with an end table with a [jug](/wiki/Skyrim:Jug "Skyrim:Jug") and bottle of Nord mead on top. Against the east wall is a wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes"). To the right is a barrel and a dresser with four bulbs of garlic, two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), a bottle of [Black-Briar Mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead"), and three bunches of elves ear on top. Hanging above are three garlic braids, a bunch of dried elves ear, and a bunch of dried frost mirriam.

If the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") obtain Winterhold in the [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"), Kraldar becomes the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl"), moves out of this house and into the [Jarl's Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Winterhold)"). Thonjolf, now Kraldar's [housecarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Housecarl "Skyrim:Housecarl"), will still return at night to sleep in the single bed.

## Notes

- The cell name of the house is actually `WinterholdKorirshouse` internally. All scenes that [Korir](/wiki/Skyrim:Korir "Skyrim:Korir") has with his family inside the [jarl's longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Winterhold)") in the final game are also referred to as "Winterhold Korir's House Scenes". The location data refers to "Korri's House" instead, although this is in all likelihood just a typo.
- Completing any quest that results in Kraldar taking over as jarl, such as [Rescue from Fort Kastav](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav "Skyrim:Rescue from Fort Kastav") for the Imperial Legion or reporting to [General Tullius](/wiki/Skyrim:General_Tullius "Skyrim:General Tullius") about taking Fort Kastav during the [Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim") quest, boosts your disposition with him, which in turn makes low value items freely available, as well as the use of both beds.
