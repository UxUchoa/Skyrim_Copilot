Azarain

**Azarain** is a [Vigilant of Stendarr](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Vigil of Stendarr") who has come to [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") to investigate disappearances of his fellow Vigilants in the region. He was staying at the [Windpeak Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Windpeak_Inn "Skyrim:Windpeak Inn") but during the related quest you will find his corpse at the [Windward Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Windward_Ruins "Skyrim:Windward Ruins") outside of town, having been murdered by a Daedric cult.

You will need to read his [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Azarain%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Azarain's Journal") to progress the quest, which can be found on his body in addition to a full set of [Vigil Veteran armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_Veteran_Armor "Skyrim:Vigil Veteran Armor"), a random leveled [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace") up to [Daedric](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric "Skyrim:Daedric") quality, two random leveled [potions of restore health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potions"), a [potion of cure disease](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease "Skyrim:Potion of Cure Disease"), and one book or recipe (*[Physicalities of Werewolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Physicalities_of_Werewolves "Skyrim:Physicalities of Werewolves")*, *[The Book of Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra "Skyrim:The Book of Daedra")*, *[Varieties of Daedra](/wiki/Skyrim:Varieties_of_Daedra "Skyrim:Varieties of Daedra")*, *[Immortal Blood](/wiki/Skyrim:Immortal_Blood "Skyrim:Immortal Blood")*, *[Cure Disease Potion Recipe](/wiki/Skyrim:Recipes#000f5cb8 "Skyrim:Recipes")*, or *[Resist Poison Potion Recipe](/wiki/Skyrim:Recipes#000f5cc5 "Skyrim:Recipes")*). A lit [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch") can also be found next to his body.

If [reanimated](/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate "Skyrim:Reanimate"), he can cast three spells: [Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing "Skyrim:Healing"), [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward"), and [Oakflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Oakflesh "Skyrim:Oakflesh").
