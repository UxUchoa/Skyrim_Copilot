## Detailed Walkthrough

**Note: This quest was originally added to your journal upon installing the [Saturalia Holiday Pack](/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Holiday_Pack "Skyrim:Saturalia Holiday Pack") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition"), this quest no longer appears in your journal. Nevertheless, the rewards can still be bought from [Agrane Peryval](/wiki/Skyrim:Agrane_Peryval "Skyrim:Agrane Peryval").**

This short quest will be added to your journal upon installing the [Saturalia Holiday Pack](/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Holiday_Pack "Skyrim:Saturalia Holiday Pack") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). You will be directed to locate a merchant who has set up shop near [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") in advance of [Saturalia](/wiki/Lore:Saturalia "Lore:Saturalia"), a festival which takes place on the 25th of [Evening Star](/wiki/Lore:Evening_Star "Lore:Evening Star"). Note that the in-game time of year is irrelevant for the purposes of this quest.

The merchant's camp can be found just outside Dawnstar, north of the [Windward Ruins](/wiki/Skyrim:Windward_Ruins "Skyrim:Windward Ruins"). It is located close to a [World Interaction](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions "Skyrim:World Interactions") spawn point which may prove problematic. The merchant is [Agrane Peryval](/wiki/Skyrim:Agrane_Peryval "Skyrim:Agrane Peryval"), who can be found at the camp along with a tame [reindeer](/wiki/Skyrim:Reindeer "Skyrim:Reindeer"). Simply speak to Agrane to finish the quest; you are under no obligation to buy anything from him.

If you do choose to make a purchase, his stock includes several unique items: three sets of festive clothing ([Saturalia Boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Boots "Skyrim:Saturalia Boots"), [Saturalia Clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Clothes "Skyrim:Saturalia Clothes"), [Saturalia Gloves](/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Gloves "Skyrim:Saturalia Gloves")) and a [bill of sale](/wiki/Skyrim:Reindeer_Bill_of_Sale "Skyrim:Reindeer Bill of Sale") for the nearby reindeer. Once purchased, the reindeer can be used as a mount, and will return to this camp if you switch to another mount.
