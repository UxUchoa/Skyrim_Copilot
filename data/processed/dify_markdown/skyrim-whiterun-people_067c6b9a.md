Before the completion of any related quests, there are 73 people in Whiterun (74 with the [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") add-on).

\* Only appears after [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising").
\*\* Only appears after the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") quest [Battle for Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)").
\*\*\* Only appears after the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") quest [Message to Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Message_to_Whiterun_(Imperial) "Skyrim:Message to Whiterun (Imperial)").
\*\*\*\* Owns [Chillfurrow Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Chillfurrow_Farm "Skyrim:Chillfurrow Farm") but sleeps here.
† This person only sells goods at the market.

†† Backup innkeeper for [Hulda](/wiki/Skyrim:Hulda "Skyrim:Hulda"); also backup merchant for [Belethor](/wiki/Skyrim:Belethor "Skyrim:Belethor").
