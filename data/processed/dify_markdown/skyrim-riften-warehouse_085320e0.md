Riften Warehouse

**Riften Warehouse** is a warehouse on Riften's docks, that is home to Riften's skooma dealer until you complete the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid "Skyrim:The Raid").

Located on the docks outside of Riften's walls, the warehouse is the base of operations of Riften's skooma dealer whom the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") cannot get rid of due to corrupt [guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Guard "Skyrim:Riften Guard"). However, the player can take up a quest to get rid of them and discover where the skooma is coming from.

## Related Quests

- **[The Raid](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid "Skyrim:The Raid")**: Help the [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften") to uncover and stop the illegal [skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma") trade in town.

### Riften Warehouse

The warehouse is inaccessible before the quest: [The Raid](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid "Skyrim:The Raid"), as the door is locked and requires a key. Upon entering you'll be spotted and will have to deal with the skooma dealer and his bodyguard before you can explore further.

The whole warehouse appears disused and dusty with clutter scattered around the floors. There are many crates and sacks of [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") in the first room as well as a couple of barrels containing [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") and sets of shelves that have been knocked over. A doorway in the northeast corner leads to an office area with a long wooden table and chair, an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a couple of [ruined books](/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book "Skyrim:Ruined Book"), a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") and a wooden plate containing three loose [septims](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold").

In the main room, there is a bottle of Nord mead and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on the set of shelves, with a second bottle of wine on the ground beside it. To the left of the shelves, against the south wall, a shelf holds more sacks. Next to the eastern wall is a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), while in the northeastern corner next to a couple of food barrels is a flight of stairs leading down to the cellar. The cellar is similar to the room above with crates, barrels, and sacks everywhere. A pair of locked double doors to the east require a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Sarthis%27s_Key "Skyrim:Sarthis's Key"), which can be retrieved from the body of [Sarthis Idren](/wiki/Skyrim:Sarthis_Idren "Skyrim:Sarthis Idren").

Behind the doors is a small room that seems to be the base of operations for Sarthis, the skooma dealer. A set of shelves opposite the doors against the east wall holds five bowls of [moon sugar](/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar "Skyrim:Moon Sugar") and six bottles of [skooma](/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma "Skyrim:Skooma"). At the north end of the room is a long wooden table and chair, a bottle of wine, another bowl of moon sugar, two more bottles of skooma and a [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel "Skyrim:Satchel") containing a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Shipment%27s_Ready "Skyrim:Shipment's Ready") needed for the relevant [quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid "Skyrim:The Raid"). Another set of shelves lean back against the wall, holding two pieces of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood") and, on top of the unit, a [mammoth tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk"). Around the cellar are bottles of both Nord mead and [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine").

## Notes

- It is possible to interact with Sarthis's satchel *through* the stone wall in the basement.
- All the moon sugar and skooma respawn.
- Anything you take from here other than what is on the corpses of the 2 thugs will count as theft.
- According to [Wujeeta](/wiki/Skyrim:Wujeeta "Skyrim:Wujeeta"), the warehouse has been tightly locked up ever since the [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") began.

## Bugs
