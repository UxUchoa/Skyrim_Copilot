Orotheim

**Orotheim** is a small [cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Caves "Skyrim:Caves") that is home to bandits intent on poaching [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth"). There is only one zone to the cave, *[Orotheim](#Orotheim)*.

The cave is northeast of [Rorikstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorikstead "Skyrim:Rorikstead"), past [Talking Stone Camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Talking_Stone_Camp "Skyrim:Talking Stone Camp"), on the cliffs above [Crabber's Shanty](/wiki/Skyrim:Crabber%27s_Shanty "Skyrim:Crabber's Shanty").

#### Radiant Quests

- **[Amulet of the Moon](/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_the_Moon "Skyrim:Amulet of the Moon")**: Find the [Moon Amulet](/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Amulet "Skyrim:Moon Amulet") for [Kharjo](/wiki/Skyrim:Kharjo "Skyrim:Kharjo").
- **[Bandit Attack](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Attack "Skyrim:Bandit Attack")**[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire"): Rescue your beloved from kidnappers.
- **[Retrieval](/wiki/Skyrim:Retrieval "Skyrim:Retrieval")**: Journey to a random location to retrieve a fragment of [Wuuthrad](/wiki/Skyrim:Wuuthrad "Skyrim:Wuuthrad").
- **[Stealing Plans](/wiki/Skyrim:Stealing_Plans "Skyrim:Stealing Plans")**: Search a Silver Hand hideout for information on the location of their headquarters.
- **[Striking the Heart](/wiki/Skyrim:Striking_the_Heart "Skyrim:Striking the Heart")**: Assassinate a leader of the Silver Hand.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

### Exterior

There is a rough path running northwest from the [River Hjaal](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Hjaal "Skyrim:River Hjaal"), that curls around the edge of the mountain to the entrance. There are a number of [bear traps](/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap "Skyrim:Bear Trap") in the undergrowth around the cave and grisly stakes with corpses speared through their stomachs either side of the entrance. There is an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") among the rocks above the entrance to the cave.

### Orotheim

The cave is split into three rough rooms. Just behind where you enter there is a [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender") plant, then along the walls of the tunnel there are several types of fungi: three [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") fungi, a [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown") fungus, an [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), and a [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot"), as well as another lavender. The first room is inhabited by two [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit"), one on the ground level, and another on the wooden platform above, usually using the smithing equipment. Around the edges of the room are four imp stool, two fly amanita, a bleeding crown and a Namira's rot fungi. There is a [cooking spit](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit "Skyrim:Cooking Spit") above the campfire, and a [mammoth snout](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Snout "Skyrim:Mammoth Snout") and two [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl") are on the table. There is also a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe") in the middle of the room.

On the platform there is a table with some [empty wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle") and a random [potion of healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing"). To the right are some boxes and sacks of [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"), on top of which is a copy of the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Mace Etiquette](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace_Etiquette "Skyrim:Mace Etiquette")*. Past that there are an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), and a weapon rack with an [iron war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe "Skyrim:Iron War Axe"), an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"), and a random [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword"). There is a ledge to the right of the grindstone where two imp stools and two bleeding crowns are growing. Along the short tunnel to the next room there are two imp stool fungi, two fly amanita fungi, and a lavender plant. On the ledge high up on the right of the tunnel, another fly amanita, and a [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap") are growing.

The tunnel opens on a wooden platform where a bandit alternates between leaning on a table to the left, and using a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"). Two bandits are located in the lower part of the room, one of whom is the bandit leader. The bandit leader carries a [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit%27s_Journal_(Orotheim) "Skyrim:Bandit's Journal (Orotheim)") describing the reason behind setting up camp in the cave. Around the campfire are four hay piles. By the wooden platform is a table with two chairs and a [satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel "Skyrim:Satchel"), a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"), and a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), and behind that, under the platform, are a random [soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems"), a random pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor"), and an apprentice-locked chest. Around the room are a lot more patches of fungi; there are five imp stool, four bleeding crown, three fly amanita, two blisterwort, and a Namira's rot.

Through the tunnel to the southwest, where a rather pointless [bone chime](/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Chimes "Skyrim:Bone Chimes") hangs, is the treasure room. To the right is a table with a leveled weapon, an iron war axe, a satchel, a coin purse, a random potion, and several bottles of wine and ale. Straight ahead is an unlocked chest. Opposite the chest is a stack of crates with a random pair of boots among them. To the south is a torch, and to the right of that is a pull chain that lowers a portion of the wall above the very first room. Through the treasure room and the ledge above the first room are more [ingredients](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients"): three imp stool, two fly amanita, and two Namira's rot.

## Notes

- While Orotheim does not have a large amount of a single fungus or plant, it does have a large amount of alchemical ingredients in total, especially considering its relative size. It includes 17 imp stool, 13 fly amanita, 8 bleeding crown, 5 Namira's rot, 3 lavender, 2 blisterwort, and 1 white cap.
- Due west, below the second waterfall on the river, is a skeleton lying next to a novice-locked chest. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-70488&y=37584&zoom=17&legacy=true)
