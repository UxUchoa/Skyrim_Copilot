## Detailed Walkthrough

**Note: This quest was originally added to your journal upon installing the [Arcane Accessories](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Accessories "Skyrim:Arcane Accessories") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club"). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition"), this quest no longer appears in your journal. Nevertheless, the rewards can still be obtained by finding the lost library yourself.**

The quest involves travelling to [Hob's Fall Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Hob%27s_Fall_Cave "Skyrim:Hob's Fall Cave") in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)") to locate a long-lost archive of spell tomes. The cave is filled with [warlock](/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock "Skyrim:Warlock") and [skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") enemies. The quest marker will lead you to an "Ancient Tome Chest" in a reading area found roughly halfway through the dungeon. For more precise directions, see the [dungeon walkthrough](/wiki/Skyrim:Hob%27s_Fall_Cave#Hob.27s_Fall_Cave "Skyrim:Hob's Fall Cave"). Open the chest to complete the quest.

The chest contains all 16 [new spell tomes](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Accessories_Items#Spell_Tomes "Skyrim:Arcane Accessories Items") added by the Creation. Reading these will teach you the following spells:

:   |  |  |
    | --- | --- |
    | - *[Unbounded Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbounded_Flames "Skyrim:Unbounded Flames")* - *[Unbounded Freezing](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbounded_Freezing "Skyrim:Unbounded Freezing")* - *[Unbounded Storms](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbounded_Storms "Skyrim:Unbounded Storms")* - *[Paralysis Rune](/wiki/Skyrim:Paralysis_Rune "Skyrim:Paralysis Rune")* - *[Pride of Hirstaang](/wiki/Skyrim:Pride_of_Hirstaang "Skyrim:Pride of Hirstaang")* - *[Elemental Flare](/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Flare "Skyrim:Elemental Flare")* - *[Elemental Bolt](/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Bolt "Skyrim:Elemental Bolt")* - *[Elemental Burst](/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Burst "Skyrim:Elemental Burst")* | - *[Elemental Blast](/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Blast "Skyrim:Elemental Blast")* - *[Orum's Aquatic Escape](/wiki/Skyrim:Orum%27s_Aquatic_Escape "Skyrim:Orum's Aquatic Escape")* - *[Fenrik's Welcome](/wiki/Skyrim:Fenrik%27s_Welcome "Skyrim:Fenrik's Welcome")* - *[Mara's Wrath](/wiki/Skyrim:Mara%27s_Wrath "Skyrim:Mara's Wrath")* - *[Choking Grasp](/wiki/Skyrim:Choking_Grasp "Skyrim:Choking Grasp")* - *[Strangulation](/wiki/Skyrim:Strangulation "Skyrim:Strangulation")* - *[Hangman's Noose](/wiki/Skyrim:Hangman%27s_Noose "Skyrim:Hangman's Noose")* - *[Touch of Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Touch_of_Death "Skyrim:Touch of Death")* |

The spells obtained from these tomes are very powerful, as they use far less [magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Magicka "Skyrim:Magicka") than anything available in the base game with the same effect. If gameplay balance is a concern, you should note that you are not required to read or even take the tomes in order to complete the quest.
