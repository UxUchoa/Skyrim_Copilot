## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Fethis Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fethis_Alor "Skyrim:Fethis Alor") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock").
2. Find an [East Empire pendant](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Pendant "Skyrim:East Empire Pendant").
3. Bring the pendant to Fethis.

### East Empire Pendants

[Fethis Alor](/wiki/Skyrim:Fethis_Alor "Skyrim:Fethis Alor") runs a general goods outfit from [his house](/wiki/Skyrim:Alor_House "Skyrim:Alor House") in [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock"). You'll find that he is somewhat bitter about living and working in [Solstheim](/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim "Skyrim:Solstheim"), should you speak with him. One thing that does seem to spark his interest is the [East Empire pendants](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Pendant "Skyrim:East Empire Pendant"). The pendants were once used as employee incentives, but are no longer being produced and, as a result, are now considered a valuable collector's item. Fethis lets you know that, if you find any of these East Empire pendants in your travels throughout Solstheim, he will be glad to buy them from you for 500 gold per pendant. The pendants are located in East Empire Company strongboxes, which are locked with an expert lock and contain other valuable loot as well.

### East Empire Company Strongbox Locations

There are 33 total locations of East Empire Company strongboxes in Solstheim, giving a profit of 16,500 gold if you can find them all. Most of the strongboxes are at locations marked on your map, but for those which are in [unmarked locations](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places#Dragonborn "Skyrim:Unmarked Places"), the entry for the "Location" column is the location from which it is easiest to find the strongbox. Certain strongboxes are marked as owned, which means taking from them is considered stealing, except in cases where you have raised the owner's [disposition](/wiki/Skyrim:Disposition "Skyrim:Disposition") high enough.

## Notes

- The quest will not be completed or removed from the journal when the 33 pendants are turned in. It can only be removed from the journal by killing Fethis Alor or using the console. According to a note in the game data, this was done intentionally because the strongboxes are meant to respawn (however, they do not).
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Dragonborn Patch"), version 1.0.4, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=12856)) It sets the strongboxes to respawn.
- To complete the quest using [console commands](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") `showqueststage dlc2rrfavor05` and some combination of `sets setstage (quest)` for the missing sequences of 20, 30, and 250; or `setobjectivecompleted dlc2rrfavor05 250 1`. The quest disappears from the log at quest stage 250.
