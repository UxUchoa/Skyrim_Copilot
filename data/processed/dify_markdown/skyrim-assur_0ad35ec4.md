Assur

**Assur** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") who lives in [the jarl's longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold) "Skyrim:Jarl's Longhouse (Winterhold)") in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") with his parents [Jarl](/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl "Skyrim:Jarl") [Korir](/wiki/Skyrim:Korir "Skyrim:Korir") and [Thaena](/wiki/Skyrim:Thaena "Skyrim:Thaena"). He is Korir's heir and will become the next jarl of Winterhold after Korir, unless Korir is deposed. His only playmate is [Eirid](/wiki/Skyrim:Eirid "Skyrim:Eirid"), with whom he plays "Hunt the Elf".

Assur wakes up at 9am for a one-hour breakfast, then wanders the longhouse. At 12pm, he goes outside to play with Eirid, stopping at 6pm for a two-hour dinner at [the Frozen Hearth](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth "Skyrim:The Frozen Hearth"). He stays home for one hour until 9pm when he goes to bed. If the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") takes over Winterhold, however, he and his parents will be exiled to [Brunwulf Free-Winter's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House "Skyrim:Brunwulf Free-Winter's House"), where they will stay all day.

Assur wears red colored [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Child's Clothes") and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000e80a8 "Skyrim:Clothing"). He carries a selection of [food and toys](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child "Skyrim:Leveled Lists") and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue

When near him, he will say things like:

:   *"My pa says elves are bad and not to trust 'em."*
:   *"Did you know that most of Winterhold fell into the sea? I heard it's because of that [College](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)")."*
:   *"Someday I'm gonna grow up and be the Jarl."*
:   *"I heard the College teaches you how to kill people with magic. Is that true?"*

If you are of a human race, he will tell you:

:   *"We don't get many people like you visiting Winterhold. Usually it's elves."*

If you are not, he will instead ask:

:   *"How come you're in Winterhold? My pa says people like you are the reason no one lives here anymore."*

In exile, he will only say:

:   *"Pa says the Empire made us leave, but the Empire just does whatever the elves tell them to."*

Sometimes, Assur will come up to you and say:

:   *"Eirid and I are playing Hunt the Elf. It's my job to find the Elf, and kill it dead. Have you seen it?"*

You have five dialogue options to reply with:

| Topic | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| **She's hiding over near the Inn.** | *"Thanks!"* |
| **She's hiding at the north end of town.** | *"Thanks!"* |
| **She's hiding on the east side of town.** | *"All right!"* |
| **I know where she is, but I'm not telling you.** | *"Fine, Elf-Lover. I'll find her myself."* |
| **I don't know where she is.** | *"I'll find that Elf. I always do."* |

Whichever your reply is, he will run off to try and find Eirid.

## Conversations

:   **Assur:** *"Eirid says the mages at the College are nice people, but you said they're bad."*
:   **Korir:** *"Eirid's a little girl who doesn't understand that they're responsible for destroying our home."*
:   **Assur:** *"Why did they do that? Why would they be so evil?"*
:   **Korir:** *"Because they don't care about you or I, cub. They think they're better than all of us, and that we can just be thrown away."*
:   **Assur:** *"I hate them."*
:   **Korir:** *"That's my boy."*

## Rumors

If you approach Eirid, you will find that she does not think the same as Assur about the College:

:   *"Assur says his pa says the College made lots of bad things happen, but the mages I've seen look nice."*

She'll also tell you how Assur acts when they play:

:   *"There's not many people left in Winterhold, so I only have Assur to play with, and sometimes he's mean."*, explaining why she thinks so:
:   *"He always wants to play 'Hunt the Elf', and he makes me be the elf."*
