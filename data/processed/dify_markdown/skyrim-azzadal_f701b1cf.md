Azzadal

**Azzadal** is the [ghost](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost "Skyrim:Ghost") of a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [bard](/wiki/Skyrim:Bard "Skyrim:Bard") who can be met after jumping from [Bard's Leap Summit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bard%27s_Leap_Summit "Skyrim:Bard's Leap Summit"). If the jump is successfully completed, Azzadal will appear and say:

:   *"Been a long time since someone took the leap. Longer still since any survived it. I once performed the entirety of the Poetic Edda from atop Bard's Leap before trying my luck. Well, you can see how that turned out."*

After his dialogue, he will play a song and you will receive two points to your [speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") skill.

He wears full [Hammerfell garb](/wiki/Skyrim:Hammerfell_Garb "Skyrim:Hammerfell Garb"), including an [Alik'r hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Alik%27r_Hood "Skyrim:Alik'r Hood") and a pair of [Redguard boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Boots "Skyrim:Redguard Boots"). He carries a [scimitar](/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar "Skyrim:Scimitar").

## Notes

- In [Skyrim - The Adventure Game](/wiki/SkyrimTAG:Skyrim_-_The_Adventure_Game "SkyrimTAG:Skyrim - The Adventure Game")'s card numbered 184 titled "The Reachmen", Azzadal is referenced as being the bard of [Madanach](/wiki/Skyrim:Madanach "Skyrim:Madanach") during his rule of [The Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"). Madanach agrees to help the [Sheathed Blades](/wiki/Lore:Sheathed_Blades "Lore:Sheathed Blades") in return for helping Azzadal complete his poetic leap from Bard's Leap Summit.
