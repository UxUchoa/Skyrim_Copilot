An East Empire Mercenary

**East Empire Mercenaries** are the four [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Soldier_(class) "Skyrim:Soldier (class)") found on the ship you take to [Japhet's Folly](/wiki/Skyrim:Japhet%27s_Folly "Skyrim:Japhet's Folly") during the quest [Rise in the East](/wiki/Skyrim:Rise_in_the_East "Skyrim:Rise in the East"). After you have helped [Orthus Endario](/wiki/Skyrim:Orthus_Endario "Skyrim:Orthus Endario") by finding information on pirate attacks which have affected the [East Empire Company](/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company "Lore:East Empire Company")'s business, you'll be approached by [Adelaisa Vendicci](/wiki/Skyrim:Adelaisa_Vendicci "Skyrim:Adelaisa Vendicci"), the captain of the East Empire mercenaries. She will request that you accompany the crew to Japhet's Folly to help defeat the pirates.
