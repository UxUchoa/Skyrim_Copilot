[^a](#note_dieToD)These people can die as part of the [Taste of Death](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Taste_of_Death "Skyrim:The Taste of Death") quest.

[^b](#note_dieFC)These people can die as part of the [The Forsworn Conspiracy](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forsworn_Conspiracy "Skyrim:The Forsworn Conspiracy") quest.

[^c](#note_dieNOECM)These people can die as part of the [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine "Skyrim:No One Escapes Cidhna Mine") quest.

[^d](#note_dieEnter)These people can die when you first enter the city.

[^e](#note_dieHoH)Vigilant Tyranus can die as part of the [House of Horrors](/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Horrors "Skyrim:The House of Horrors") quest.

[^f](#note_Banning)Banning is not a potential follower, but will sell the canine follower Vigilance.

[^g](#note_Fjotra)Fjotra only appears after the quest [The Heart of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Heart_of_Dibella "Skyrim:The Heart of Dibella").
