## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Hillevi Cruel-Sea](/wiki/Skyrim:Hillevi_Cruel-Sea "Skyrim:Hillevi Cruel-Sea").
2. Deliver a bottle of [Nightshade Extract](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade_Extact "Skyrim:Nightshade Extact") to [Wuunferth the Unliving](/wiki/Skyrim:Wuunferth_the_Unliving "Skyrim:Wuunferth the Unliving").

## Detailed Walkthrough

[Hillevi Cruel-Sea](/wiki/Skyrim:Hillevi_Cruel-Sea "Skyrim:Hillevi Cruel-Sea") has a market stall in [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), selling produce. When asked whether she grows the crops all by herself she will mention that her family owns [Hollyfrost Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Hollyfrost_Farm "Skyrim:Hollyfrost Farm") outside the city, and, on top of that, also receive payment from [Wuunferth the Unliving](/wiki/Skyrim:Wuunferth_the_Unliving "Skyrim:Wuunferth the Unliving") for every portion of [Nightshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") they plant. She will ask you to deliver a bottle of [Nightshade Extract](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade_Extact "Skyrim:Nightshade Extact") to the court wizard.

Wuunferth can be found in the upstairs area of the [Palace of the Kings](/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings "Skyrim:Palace of the Kings") during the day and wanders around in the throne room at night. Hand over the extract and he will thank you with a leveled amount of gold. He mentions that the extract is very poisonous, but he does not reveal its purpose.
