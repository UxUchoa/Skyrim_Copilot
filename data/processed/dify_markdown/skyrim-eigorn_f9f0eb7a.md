Eigorn

Eigorn is a dead [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") who can be found at [Four Skull Lookout](/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Skull_Lookout "Skyrim:Four Skull Lookout") as part of the [Divine Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Divine_Crusader "Skyrim:Divine Crusader") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club").

He wears the [Fastened Shield of the Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Fastened_Shield_of_the_Crusader "Skyrim:Fastened Shield of the Crusader"), [Reforged Boots of the Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Boots_of_the_Crusader "Skyrim:Reforged Boots of the Crusader"), [Reforged Cuirass of the Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Cuirass_of_the_Crusader "Skyrim:Reforged Cuirass of the Crusader"), [Reforged Gauntlets of the Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader "Skyrim:Reforged Gauntlets of the Crusader"), and [Reforged Helm of the Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Helm_of_the_Crusader "Skyrim:Reforged Helm of the Crusader"). He also carries the [Sword of the Crusader](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword_of_the_Crusader "Skyrim:Sword of the Crusader").
