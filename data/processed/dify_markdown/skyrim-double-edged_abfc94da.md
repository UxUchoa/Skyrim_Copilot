## Quick Walkthrough

1. Go to the [Cliffside Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Cliffside_Retreat "Skyrim:Cliffside Retreat") and read [Zaharia's note](/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia%27s_Note "Skyrim:Zaharia's Note").
2. (Optional) Disrupt Zaharia's plan and remove the [Note to Ehlhiel](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_to_Ehlhiel "Skyrim:Note to Ehlhiel") in [Temple of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Temple of the Divines").
3. Go to [Lost Echo Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Echo_Cave "Skyrim:Lost Echo Cave") and defeat the person there.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/SR-quest-Double-Edged03.png/200px-SR-quest-Double-Edged03.png)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Double-Edged03.png)

The note sitting on the dresser

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg)

Zaharia waiting for Ehlhiel

This quest starts at [Cliffside Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Cliffside_Retreat "Skyrim:Cliffside Retreat"), with a barrel that has [Zaharia's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia%27s_Note "Skyrim:Zaharia's Note") on top of it alongside [Guard's Dossier: Ehlhiel](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard%27s_Dossier:_Ehlhiel "Skyrim:Guard's Dossier: Ehlhiel"). Read the note to start the quest, you can either help Zaharia, a [Crimson Dirk](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Dirks "Skyrim:Crimson Dirks"), and kill the traitor Ehlhiel, or you can help save Ehlhiel's life.
If you decide to save Ehlhiel, head to the [Temple of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Temple of the Divines") in Solitude, read and take the [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_to_Ehlhiel "Skyrim:Note to Ehlhiel") on the dresser upstairs on the right.

Either way go to [Lost Echo Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Echo_Cave "Skyrim:Lost Echo Cave"), where you will have to defeat [Zaharia](/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia "Skyrim:Zaharia") if you took the note from the Temple of the Divines or [Ehlhiel](/wiki/Skyrim:Ehlhiel "Skyrim:Ehlhiel") if you didn't. Afterwards loot your reward from Zaharia.

## Notes

- Although the quest entries read that a Thalmor double agent betrayed a member of the Blades, *[Guard's Dossier: Ehlhiel](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard%27s_Dossier:_Ehlhiel "Skyrim:Guard's Dossier: Ehlhiel")* and *[Zaharia's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia%27s_Note "Skyrim:Zaharia's Note")* reads that Ehlhiel betrayed the [Crimson Dirks](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Dirks "Skyrim:Crimson Dirks") for the city guard captains and the [East Empire Company](/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company "Skyrim:East Empire Company"). This suggests that the storyline of the quest was changed at some point, but the quest objectives were not updated to match.
