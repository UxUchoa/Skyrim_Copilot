## Walkthrough

One [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and up to two [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth") occupy the camp. An [official warning](/wiki/Skyrim:Official_warning "Skyrim:Official warning") posted on the nearby signpost grants the giant permission to camp there and warns citizens not to interfere with him. In the center of the camp is a large campfire with two [horse haunches](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Haunch "Skyrim:Horse Haunch") and a [charred skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Meat "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Meat") cooking beside it. A [giant club](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Club "Skyrim:Giant Club") is near the campfire (see [bugs](#Bugs)). Southwest of the fire is a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide") and an [ice wolf pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf_Pelt "Skyrim:Ice Wolf Pelt"), while to the north is a [dead Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_(dead) "Skyrim:Khajiit (dead)"). To the northwest near a large rock is an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") and two [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl"); taking the cheese bowls counts as theft. Two haunches of raw [horse meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Meat "Skyrim:Horse Meat") and an unowned mammoth cheese bowl sit on a rock to the right. On the rocks above is a fire with a dead [wolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf "Skyrim:Wolf") and a dead [horse](/wiki/Skyrim:Horses "Skyrim:Horses") cooking on top. The [wolf's pelt](/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf_Pelt "Skyrim:Wolf Pelt") can be looted from its body, while the [horse's hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Hide "Skyrim:Horse Hide") lies beside the fire. [Jazbay grapes](/wiki/Skyrim:Jazbay_Grapes "Skyrim:Jazbay Grapes") and [dragon's tongue](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue "Skyrim:Dragon's Tongue") grow in the vicinity.

To the southeast, near the road and the signpost, is a destroyed carriage with another dead Khajiit, a dead horse, various common books strewn about, an adept-locked chest, a barrel containing ingredients, several food sacks, and a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"). The dead Khajiit near the campfire may have come from this caravan to attempt to barter with the giant.

## Notes

- Due to the camp's proximity to the dragon lair at [Bonestrewn Crest](/wiki/Skyrim:Bonestrewn_Crest "Skyrim:Bonestrewn Crest"), you may attract the attention of the [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") while dealing with the giant and mammoths. This may be either helpful or harmful depending on whether the parties attack you or each other.
- South-southeast of the camp, atop a large rocky hill, is a [shrine of Akatosh](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Akatosh "Skyrim:Shrines") on a stone bier. The three leveled [skeletons](/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton "Skyrim:Skeleton") near the surrounding pillars will rise and attack when you approach the bier. A leveled war axe and a copy of the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Breathing Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Breathing_Water "Skyrim:Breathing Water")* are next to the shrine.

## Bugs

- The mammoth cheese containers here are erroneously marked as owned by the giants.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
- The giant club cannot be used by you, but can be picked up by a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") if you order them to do so. However, it will then be impossible to remove it from their inventory.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.1, fixes this bug.
