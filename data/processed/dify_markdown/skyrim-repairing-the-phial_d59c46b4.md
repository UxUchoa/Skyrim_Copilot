## Quick Walkthrough

**Note**: This quest can only be started after gaining full access to the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World") during the quest of the [same name](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:The Throat of the World").

1. Talk to [Quintus Navale](/wiki/Skyrim:Quintus_Navale "Skyrim:Quintus Navale") about repairing the White Phial.
2. Find the items required.
3. Return to Quintus Navale.

### One Last Attempt

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-npc-Nurelion_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nurelion_04.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nurelion_04.jpg)

[Nurelion](/wiki/Skyrim:Nurelion "Skyrim:Nurelion") on his deathbed

A few days after the [failed attempt](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Phial_(quest) "Skyrim:The White Phial (quest)") to help the alchemist [Nurelion](/wiki/Skyrim:Nurelion "Skyrim:Nurelion") realize his life's ambition (so long as you have unlocked the top of the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World") by completing the [similarly named quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:The Throat of the World")), a [courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Courier "Skyrim:Courier") will seek you out with a [letter from Quintus Navale](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Quintus_Navale "Skyrim:Letter from Quintus Navale") (Nurelion's assistant) who has grave news, and seeks your help.

Head to Windhelm and locate [The White Phial](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Phial_(place) "Skyrim:The White Phial (place)") in the market district. In there, Nurelion will still be in his bed, coughing and slowly dying. Quintus, however, will be more than ready to explain what is needed to repair the Phial: *"I have some thoughts. There are three crucial elements. Some may be easier to find than others. On the top of the Throat of the World is a patch of Unmelting Snow. No heat can touch it. Then we need the tusk of a mammoth, ground to a fine powder as only the giants know how. The final step is tricky. It requires the briar heart from a Forsworn of the Reach. If you can bring me these materials, the Phial can again be made whole."*

Although he claims not to be as skilled as his master, Quintus will provide quite a bit of information on each of the items to explain why they are needed for his plan to repair the Phial:

Repairing the Phial

*"Master, look. It's the Phial."*

The rather unremarkable patch of Unmelting Snow can be found at the [Throat of The World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World"). Mammoth Tusk Powder is in a large vat beside the giants at [Stonehill Bluff](/wiki/Skyrim:Stonehill_Bluff "Skyrim:Stonehill Bluff"), and it is possible to pick up the powder without aggravating the giants. Note that the Mammoth Tusk Powder is different from the alchemy ingredient [Powdered Mammoth Tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Powdered_Mammoth_Tusk "Skyrim:Powdered Mammoth Tusk"). A Briar Heart can be acquired from a dead [Forsworn Briarheart](/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Briarheart "Skyrim:Forsworn Briarheart") and can be obtained from multiple Forsworn camps; one will be marked on your map.

### Repairing the Phial

When you return to Quintus with the three items, he will be relieved: *"Thank the gods. I'll get to work on the Phial straight away."* He will then leave his spot behind the counter and get to work at the [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") in the corner. Once he is finished repairing the Phial, he will head upstairs, eager to show his master what he has accomplished:

**Quintus**: *"Master, look. It's the Phial."*
**Nurelion**: *"What? How?"*
**Quintus**: *"It doesn't matter. Look, it's refilling with your tonic as we speak."*
**Nurelion**: *"Marvelous."*

Nurelion will then die peacefully and Quintus will turn around and express his gratitude: *"He is gone. But thanks to you, he died knowing his life's work was not in vain."* When you say you are sorry to hear that old Nurelion is dead, he will respond: *"As am I. You acted very nobly in helping my master realize his life's work. Thank you for that. As for myself, I've always been content to simply be an alchemist. I fear keeping the Phial would just remind me of Nurelion's obsession, and how it consumed him. Here, keep it. You are as much a part of its legend now as [Curalmil](/wiki/Skyrim:Curalmil "Skyrim:Curalmil") was. I hope it brings you the happiness that my master desired."*

### The Reward

You have a number of options for which kind of liquid the Phial will be able to regenerate:

- I'd like it to have the power of healing. ([restore health](/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Health "Skyrim:Restore Health") for 100 points)
- I want to resist the forces of magic. (20% [resist magic](/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Magic "Skyrim:Resist Magic") for 60 seconds)
- I want to be tougher in battle. (+20 [stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Stamina "Skyrim:Fortify Stamina") for 300 seconds)
- I want to strengthen my magical skills. (+20 [magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Magicka "Skyrim:Fortify Magicka") for 300 seconds)
- I want to deal more damage in battle. (+50% to [one-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_One-handed "Skyrim:Fortify One-handed") damage for 60 seconds)
- I want to be better hidden in the shadows. (20% [harder to detect](/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Sneak "Skyrim:Fortify Sneak") for 60 seconds)

You will be given [The White Phial](/wiki/Skyrim:The_White_Phial_(item) "Skyrim:The White Phial (item)"), which functions as a potion of the nature you chose. Drinking it moves it to the Miscellaneous section of your items menu as The White Phial (Empty). It refills after 24 hours. Attempting to use the empty phial duplicates it, but only one phial will fill after the time has passed.

## Notes

- Nurelion's dead body will disappear after some time. You can find his remains inside the coffin in the [Hall of the Dead](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Dead_(Windhelm) "Skyrim:Hall of the Dead (Windhelm)").
- After Nurelion is dead, many of the contents of his store will be free for the taking, including many useful ingredients. You can also loot his key to the shop.
- If you are on an assignment for the [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") involving The White Phial (the shop), complete it and turn it in prior to completing this quest. Nurelion's passing at the end of the quest, though not caused by you, will still trigger you to fail the job for "killing".
- It is possible to reach the peak of the Throat of the World early, by using a horse to climb the mountain; however, doing so will not let you start this quest early - you have to advance the main questline in order to get the letter from Quintus Navale.

## Bugs

- The courier may fail to deliver the note from Quintus even after you've gained access to the Throat of the World, making this quest unavailable.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")This bug is likely to occur if you have killed the Forsworn leader of Red Eagle Redoubt before (probably during [find Red Eagle's sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest) "Skyrim:Rebel's Cairn (quest)")). If so, use the following [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") commands: `prid 2c9a0` and `resurrect 1` to resurrect him. Wait a few real-world minutes then fast travel to any major city and the courier should show up. If the courier still fails to appear, try the command `setstage MS12b 10`.
- The courier bearing Quintus' letter may fail to arrive and instead loiter at [Sarethi Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Sarethi_Farm "Skyrim:Sarethi Farm"). Traveling there and speaking to him may start the quest.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")You may be able to find the courier by using the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") command `player.moveto 39fb7` to move yourself to him.
- After you have started the quest, when you pick up a briar heart, the quest may not update, and no option to turn it in will appear when talking to Quintus. This can be caused by already having a briar heart in your inventory when you start this quest.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4.15, fixes this bug.Note that this recurs in Skyrim Special Edition and appears not to be fixed by the Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch (4.1.0)
  - Keep the briar heart in your inventory, collect the other two ingredients and use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") command: `setstage MS12b 60`.
- The quest may not start if you already have unmelting snow, mammoth tusk powder, or a briar heart in your inventory when attempting to talk to Quintus.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.4.15, fixes this bug.
- If you finish the quest with more than one briar heart in your inventory, the remaining hearts will still be marked as quest items, making them impossible to drop or store.
  - The [Official Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Patch "Skyrim:Patch"), version 1.9.26.0.8, fixes this bug.
- If you already have a briar heart in your inventory before starting the quest, it will not be given to Quintus at the end of the quest; it will not be marked as a quest item immediately, but any briar hearts retrieved later on will become a quest item.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") If you don't want to install the unofficial patch, you can manually reset all of the items' *quest item* status through the console command `resetquest MS12b`, followed by `completequest MS12b`.
- Sometimes talking to Quintus will not update the quest, and trying to talk to him again will yield a result of "this person is busy". **?**
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")Use the `setstage` console command to advance the quest.
- When you return to Quintus with all three ingredients, there may be no dialogue option to turn them in, meaning you are stuck with an uncompleted quest, the three quest items, and no White Phial.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
  - This may happen because the [Mammoth Tusk Powder](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk_Powder "Skyrim:Mammoth Tusk Powder") was not marked as a quest item, allowing you to drop or store it before you brought it to Quintus. If you stored it, retrieve it and talk to Quintus again.
  - This can also happen if you've used the briar heart in a potion. Find or buy another and the quest will continue.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") If you can't retrieve it, you can manually spawn it by using the console command `player.additem 6bc10 1`.
- If you have initiated the quest to [find Red Eagle's sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest) "Skyrim:Rebel's Cairn (quest)"), the same Forsworn Briarheart might be the target of both quests. It will carry both Red Eagle's sword and a briar heart. However, even if you pick up both, it will only complete the Red Eagle quest. You will need to find a different Forsworn camp to complete the briar heart part of this quest.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. An alias on this quest was requiring him to be alive. This alias is now marked as optional since it is not necessary for that one particular Briarheart Warrior to be the target.
- If you have killed the Forsworn Briarheart leader at the designated camp prior to this quest, collecting his heart may not clear the quest objective leaving you stuck. **?**
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") The following console command will fix this issue: `setobjectivecompleted ms12b 50 1`. NOTE: "50" is the objective number NOT the quest stage!
- It is possible to receive more than one White Phial from Quintus if, after choosing what type of solution you want in the Phial, you quit the conversation and re-initiate it.
- After Quintus Navale shows him the Phial, Nurelion suddenly stands up and acts like he's dying, and you can't finish the quest.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC")You can avoid this happening by removing his essential status before Quintus shows him the Phial. Open the console and type in `SetEssential 14148 0`.

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage MS12b stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest MS12b`.
