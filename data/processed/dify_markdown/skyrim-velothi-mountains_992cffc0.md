The **Velothi Mountains** are a mountain range that stretches along the eastern border of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").

They are the mountain range separating [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind") from [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim").

The mountains form the eastern border of the Skyrim regions of [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch") and [The Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"). In the south the mountain range meets the [Jerall Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Jerall_Mountains "Skyrim:Jerall Mountains").

Some points of interest include the orcish stronghold of [Narzulbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Narzulbur "Skyrim:Narzulbur"), [Sacellum of Boethiah](/wiki/Skyrim:Sacellum_of_Boethiah "Skyrim:Sacellum of Boethiah"), [Fort Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dawnguard "Skyrim:Fort Dawnguard"), or the Dwemer ruins of [Kagrenzel](/wiki/Skyrim:Kagrenzel "Skyrim:Kagrenzel") and [Mzulft](/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft "Skyrim:Mzulft"). The [Dunmeth Pass](/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmeth_Pass "Skyrim:Dunmeth Pass") leads through the mountains and into northern Morrowind. High in the mountains lies the ancient temple of [Skuldafn](/wiki/Skyrim:Skuldafn "Skyrim:Skuldafn"), along the [Eastmarch](/wiki/Lore:Eastmarch "Lore:Eastmarch") border.
