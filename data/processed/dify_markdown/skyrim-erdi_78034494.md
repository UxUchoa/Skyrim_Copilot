Erdi

**Erdi** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") maid living in the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace") of [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). She is involved in the quest [The Mind of Madness](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness "Skyrim:The Mind of Madness"), where she provides you with the key to the [Pelagius Wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing "Skyrim:Pelagius Wing") of the palace.

Erdi wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0006c1da "Skyrim:Clothes") along with a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd "Skyrim:Boots"). She carries a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") along with a selection of [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Related Quests

- **[The Mind of Madness](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness "Skyrim:The Mind of Madness")**: Gain access to and solve the mystery of the [Pelagius Wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing "Skyrim:Pelagius Wing").

## Dialogue

When approached, she will greet you with:

:   *"Oh, hello! Just tidying up."*
:   *"So exciting when new folk come into town. I bet you've had plenty of adventures."*

If your character is female, Erdi will talk idly about her aspirations:

:   *"Maybe you need a partner? Hmm? Just picture it. Two women, the dusty road, strapping men at every tavern, a fortune in gold to be claimed..."*
:   *"Someday, I'll be a gallant heroine. You make it look so.. appealing."*

If your character is male, she will keep talking about her dream for a chivalrous hero to arrive and take her away:

:   *"Are you my gallant hero? Have you come to whisk me away, to a life of adventure and romance?"*
:   *"I made a wish in the town well that a golden knight came to take me to his castle. Oh, does it spoil the wish if I tell?"*
:   *"Someday, a gallant hero will ride up and take me away. Someday..."*

Despite this misleading dialogue, she cannot be [married](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") or become a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers").

### Conversations

In the Winking Skeever:

:   **Jawanan:** *"So, um, mind if I buy you a drink?"*
:   **Erdi:** *"Aw, that's so sweet of you! Thanks!"*

### The Mind of Madness

Telling her that you need to get into the Pelagius Wing:

:   **I need to get into the Pelagius Wing.**
:   *"Oh, I'd get in trouble for that. It's not allowed. Besides, it's scary in there!"*
:   **Falk asked me to check it out.**
:   *"Well... I suppose in that case, it's okay to let you in. But only briefly. Be careful, and come right back!"*

She will then give you the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing_Key "Skyrim:Pelagius Wing Key") to the wing.
