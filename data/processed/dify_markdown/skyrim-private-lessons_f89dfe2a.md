## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read *[Wanted: Fishing Tutor](/wiki/Skyrim:Wanted:_Fishing_Tutor "Skyrim:Wanted: Fishing Tutor")*.
2. Speak to [Lynea](/wiki/Skyrim:Lynea "Skyrim:Lynea") about private fishing lessons.
3. Meet Lynea on the docks near the Warrens.
4. Catch fish with Lynea.
5. Speak to Lynea.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/SR-quest-Private_Lessons_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Private_Lessons_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Private_Lessons_02.jpg)

The fishing spot

This quest can be started after [Tusked Terror](/wiki/Skyrim:Tusked_Terror "Skyrim:Tusked Terror") has been completed by talking to Viriya:

:   **Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?**
:   *"You're headed to Markarth. It's all in this note."*

She will then hand you a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Wanted:_Fishing_Tutor "Skyrim:Wanted: Fishing Tutor") from a woman named [Lynea](/wiki/Skyrim:Lynea "Skyrim:Lynea") who wants fishing lessons. Head to the [Silver-Blood Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver-Blood_Inn "Skyrim:Silver-Blood Inn") in Markarth and speak with Lynea:

:   **You're the one in search of a fishing instructor?**
:   *"Indeed."*
:   **I know just the spot. We can fish off the docks near the Warrens.**
:   *"If that's what you want. I'll see you there, soon."*

Go to the docks near the Warrens and speak to her:

:   *"Is there anything else you need?"*
:   **No, let's begin.**
:   *"Of course."*

Once you have caught three fish in front of Lynea, she will say:

:   *"Well now. I guess I owe you my thanks."*

    :   **Did you enjoy the lesson?**
    :   *"I couldn't be more pleased. Take this small token of my appreciation."*
    :   **As much as I enjoy fishing, I have other things to do. Pay up.**
    :   *"You could have just asked."*

She will then give you her [amulet of Dibella](/wiki/Skyrim:Lynea%27s_Amulet_of_Dibella "Skyrim:Lynea's Amulet of Dibella") as a reward.

## Bugs

- Lynea may not appear at the docks after agreeing to meet her there, and will instead stay in the Silver-Blood Inn indefinitely.
- The wanted notice remains marked as a quest item after the quest ends. **?**
