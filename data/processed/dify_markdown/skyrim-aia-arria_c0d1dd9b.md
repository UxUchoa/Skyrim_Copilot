Aia Arria

**Aia Arria** is an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial "Skyrim:Imperial") [bard](/wiki/Skyrim:Bard_(class) "Skyrim:Bard (class)") at the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). According to her teacher, [Pantea Ateia](/wiki/Skyrim:Pantea_Ateia "Skyrim:Pantea Ateia"), Aia is the only student at the College with actual talent, a fact that Aia takes great pride in.

At 4am, she will wake up and go to a class in the Bards College. She will move to a different class at 8am and again at 2pm. She will go to the market at 6pm; however, she also goes to sleep at 6pm, which is a bug in her schedule.

Aia wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothing") along with a [cowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#00017696 "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261bd "Skyrim:Clothing"). She carries her [key to the Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bards_College "Skyrim:Key to Bards College"), a [flute](/wiki/Skyrim:Flute "Skyrim:Flute"), a [lute](/wiki/Skyrim:Lute "Skyrim:Lute"), and a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"). She also wields an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and carries a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic").

## Dialogue

When approached she can potentially say:

:   *"Lady Ateia says my voice is second only to hers. But she's selling me short."*
:   *"Few can resist my charms. Even fewer are worthy of them."*
:   *"Oh, if you haven't heard Dean Pantea sing, you should."*
:   *"I can't understand why Jorn would want to join the [Imperial Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"). Soldiers only appreciate one thing, and it isn't music."*

She will exit conversation with:

:   *"We should talk again sometime."*

Unless you have completed [Tending the Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Tending_the_Flames "Skyrim:Tending the Flames"), in which case she will say:

:   *"Always glad to talk to a bard."*

## Conversations

During the lessons at the Bards College, she may be seen having random conversations with her instructors.

:   **Pantea**: *"You there. Have you performed your voice exercises today?"*
:   **Aia**: *"Always! Perfection takes dedication, after all!"*
:   **Pantea**: *"Yes, well, see that you keep at it. That is all."*

:   **[Inge Six-Fingers](/wiki/Skyrim:Inge_Six_Fingers "Skyrim:Inge Six Fingers")**: *"Your solo performance is about a week away. I expect to hear your early rendition tomorrow, understand?"*
:   **Aia**: *"At this point, I hardly see how your advice would improve it. It's flawless."*
:   **Inge**: *"I'll be the judge of that. Now, back to your practice."*

She was also supposed to converse with [Giraud Gemane](/wiki/Skyrim:Giraud_Gemane "Skyrim:Giraud Gemane"), but due to a [bug](/wiki/Skyrim:Giraud_Gemane#bugs "Skyrim:Giraud Gemane"), her line will never be heard.

:   **Giraud Gemane**: *"Pardon me, but I trust you've been working on your poem, on the classical heroes of Skyrim, yes?"*
:   **Aia**: *"Must it be written? Poetry is so dull when it isn't set to music."*
:   **Giraud**: *"I'm sure it will be fine. Just don't be late."*

She can also be found talking to her fellow students, such as [Jorn](/wiki/Skyrim:Jorn "Skyrim:Jorn"):

:   **Jorn**: *"What're you going to do after you graduate? I'm thinking of joining the Legion. I hear they're looking for skalds. Just think. Me - a war bard."*
:   **Aia**: *"Lady Ateia has already arranged for me to perform in the court of the Jarl of Falkreath."*
:   **Jorn**: *"You'd better hope the war's over by then. The Stormcloaks would eat you alive!"*
:   **Aia**: *"Hmph. Music tames the most savage beasts. Even Nords."*

Or the unpopular [Illdi](/wiki/Skyrim:Illdi "Skyrim:Illdi"):

:   **Illdi**: *"Alaf keeps making eyes at me."*
:   **Aia**: *"You should tell him to mind his own business."*
:   **Illdi**: *"Aia. You're so mean. I don't think he means anything by it."*
:   **Aia**: *"Have it your way, then."*

[Pantea Ateia](/wiki/Skyrim:Pantea_Ateia "Skyrim:Pantea Ateia") can also be heard complimenting her:

:   **Pantea**: *"Your performance has been excellent lately Aia. You still need training but one day you will make an adequate singer."*
:   **Aia**: *"Yes, Dean. Thank you."*

When shopping at the market, Aia may speak to [Addvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Addvar "Skyrim:Addvar"):

:   **Aia**: *"Salmon looks good today."*
:   **Addvar**: *"Speaking of sounding good, I've heard a couple of your classmates on the street recently."*
:   **Aia**: *"As long as they're playing instruments they're passable. You don't want to hear them sing."*

Or to fruit vendor [Jala](/wiki/Skyrim:Jala "Skyrim:Jala"):

:   **Aia**: *"There's nothing quite like a good vegetable."*
:   **Jala**: *"Vegetables are the food of the Divines."*
:   **Aia**: *"I've heard stanger*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *things,*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *are eaten over at the [Temple of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Temple of the Divines")."*

## Notes

- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 1.2.5 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") changes the starting time of her market visit from 6pm to 4pm, so she will be there until 7pm. This change was made due to the unwieldy setup of Bards College-related AI packages, and due to the fact that Aia has no dialogue for the classes at this hour, so it appears as if she wasn't intended to partake in said classes, implying she would more likely be found away from the college.

## Bugs

- Aia can't sleep due to her sleep package being listed below her default sandbox package.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug.
- Her sleep package `BardsCollegeSleepEditorLoc18x8` also interferes with her market visiting AI, as both packages are expected to start at 6pm.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. Aia has been given the package `BardsCollegeSleepEditorLoc22x8` like Jorn in its place, making her sleep from 10pm to 8am, so her sleep schedule doesn't interfere with her market visit.
- Considerable dialogue was written for Addvar and his various customers. Unfortunately, they were recorded in [Jala](/wiki/Skyrim:Jala "Skyrim:Jala")'s voice. Aia's dialogue is mixed with Jala's other dialogue. See [Addvar Bugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Addvar#Bugs "Skyrim:Addvar") for this dialogue.
