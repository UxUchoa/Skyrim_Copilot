## Quick Walkthrough

1. Read the Black Book *[Filament and Filigree](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book:_Filament_and_Filigree_(book) "Skyrim:Black Book: Filament and Filigree (book)")* in [Kolbjorn Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolbjorn_Barrow "Skyrim:Kolbjorn Barrow").
2. Make your way through [Apocrypha‎](/wiki/Skyrim:Apocrypha_(Filament_and_Filigree) "Skyrim:Apocrypha (Filament and Filigree)").
3. Learn the Black Book's hidden knowledge and choose your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/SR-place-Apocrypha-Filament_and_Filigree.jpg/200px-SR-place-Apocrypha-Filament_and_Filigree.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Apocrypha-Filament_and_Filigree.jpg)

Dangerous Darkness

You can find the Black Book *[Filament and Filigree](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Book:_Filament_and_Filigree_(book) "Skyrim:Black Book: Filament and Filigree (book)")* in [Kolbjorn Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Kolbjorn_Barrow "Skyrim:Kolbjorn Barrow"). Defeat [Ahzidal](/wiki/Skyrim:Ahzidal_(creature) "Skyrim:Ahzidal (creature)") and enter the standing sarcophagus opposite the gate you entered through. At the end of a rough tunnel you will find the Black Book in a circular chamber. Read the Book to be drawn into Apocrypha.

This area of [Hermaeus Mora](/wiki/Lore:Hermaeus_Mora "Lore:Hermaeus Mora")'s realm consists of a long, straight, dark path with several hovering lights illuminating small patches. Use [Candlelight](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlelight "Skyrim:Candlelight") or a [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch") to light your way, as the darkness will damage your health. Before proceeding, loot the [pod](/wiki/Skyrim:Containers#Pod "Skyrim:Containers") hidden behind the left-hand statue at the foot of the stairs, as well as the random [soul gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gems "Skyrim:Soul Gems") and the Sneak Skill Book 'Three Thieves' on the table to the right. Then climb the stairs, claim the contents of a second pod to the left of the doors and the [scroll of invisibility](/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Invisibility "Skyrim:Scroll of Invisibility") beside it. Activate the scrye and deal with two leveled [seekers](/wiki/Skyrim:Seeker "Skyrim:Seeker") and one leveled [lurker](/wiki/Skyrim:Lurker "Skyrim:Lurker"), avoiding the whipping tentacles in alternating pools along the path. At the far end of the path, you will climb another set of stairs to a platform, where another leveled lurker will emerge from a pool and attack. After defeating this final foe, climb the last set of stairs to find a [vessel](/wiki/Skyrim:Vessel "Skyrim:Vessel"), a few soul gems on another stone table, and the Black Book *Filament and Filigree*. Read the book to choose your reward, then read it again to return to Kolbjorn Barrow.

### Rewards

Your choice of one of three greater powers:

- [Secret of Strength](/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Strength "Skyrim:Secret of Strength") — Power attacks cost no [stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Stamina "Skyrim:Stamina") for 30 seconds.
- [Secret of Arcana](/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Arcana "Skyrim:Secret of Arcana") — Spells cost no [magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Magicka "Skyrim:Magicka") for 30 seconds.
- [Secret of Protection](/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Protection "Skyrim:Secret of Protection") — You take half damage for 30 seconds.

## Notes

- Other than using a light source, the damage caused by the darkness cannot be avoided by any means, including the [Become Ethereal](/wiki/Skyrim:Become_Ethereal "Skyrim:Become Ethereal") [Shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") and the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") command `tgm`.
- You can change the reward at any time by traveling to Solstheim and re-reading the book. This will transport you to Apocrypha near the reward location and you can select a different one.

## Bugs

- The burning sound effect that plays when you go into the darkness might not end.
  - Exiting to the main menu might solve this issue. If not, clearing the game cache should.
  - After you exit Apocrypha, load a different save then reload the auto-save that was just made and this will stop.
  - Your health might continue to deplete as if you're still in the shadows of Apocrypha.
  - Revisiting the Filament and Filigree and waiting to stop burning before leaving to Solstheim can fix this.
  - Transforming into a werewolf/vampire lord and back will cause the sound to stop.
  - Saving the game and loading that save may also fix the issue.
