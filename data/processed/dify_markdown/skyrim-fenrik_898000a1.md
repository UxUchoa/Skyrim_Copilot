Fenrik

Fenrik is an ex-[Vigilant of Stendarr](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_of_Stendarr "Skyrim:Vigil of Stendarr") who has contracted [vampirism](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampirism "Skyrim:Vampirism"). He is the leader of a Daedric cult and has made a pact with [Molag Bal](/wiki/Lore:Molag_Bal "Lore:Molag Bal") to sacrifice nine of his fellow Vigilants in return for a vampirism cure. He has constructed an altar to Molag Bal within a hidden chamber inside [Quicksilver Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Mine "Skyrim:Quicksilver Mine") in [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar").

You will be tasked with killing him and his [thralls](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Thrall "Skyrim:Vampire Thrall") during the related quest.

He wears a full set of [Vigil Corrupted armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_Corrupted_Armor "Skyrim:Vigil Corrupted Armor") and wields a random leveled [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), which can get up to [Daedric](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric "Skyrim:Daedric") quality. He also carries two random leveled [potions of restore health](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions#Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potions"), a [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch"), and his [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Fenrik%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Fenrik's Journal").

He knows three spells: [Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing "Skyrim:Healing"), [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward"), and [Oakflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Oakflesh "Skyrim:Oakflesh").
