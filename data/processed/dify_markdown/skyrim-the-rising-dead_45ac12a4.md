## Quick Walkthrough

1. Reach level 5 and read the [anonymous letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Anonymous_Letter_(Plague_of_the_Dead) "Skyrim:Anonymous Letter (Plague of the Dead)").
2. Travel to the ritual site north of [Southfringe Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum "Skyrim:Southfringe Sanctum").
3. Find and read the [*Necromancer's Journal*](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer%27s_Journal_(Plague_of_the_Dead) "Skyrim:Necromancer's Journal (Plague of the Dead)") to complete the quest.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-quest-The_Rising_Dead_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Rising_Dead_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Rising_Dead_02.jpg)

The Ritual Site

After reaching level 5, a [courier](/wiki/Skyrim:Courier "Skyrim:Courier") will approach you and deliver an [anonymous letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Anonymous_Letter_(Plague_of_the_Dead) "Skyrim:Anonymous Letter (Plague of the Dead)"). Read the letter to learn that a group of [Necromancers](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer "Skyrim:Necromancer") plan to perform an ancient, dangerous summoning ritual to conjure an army of [zombies](/wiki/Skyrim:Zombie "Skyrim:Zombie"). The ritual site is located high in the mountains southeast of [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen"), just north of [Southfringe Sanctum](/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum "Skyrim:Southfringe Sanctum") [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=31270&y=-99871&zoom=17&legacy=true).

Once you arrive at the ritual site, you will find a group of decomposing zombies standing over the bodies of the slain Necromancers. Clearly the ritual was successful, but the Necromancers lost control of their summoned undead. Defeat the zombies and approach the body of the Necromancer marked by the quest marker. He will be holding a leveled spell tome ([*Spell Tome: Conjure Zombie*](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Conjure_Zombie "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Conjure Zombie") for lower levels, or [*Spell Tome: Conjure Foul Zombie*](/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome:_Conjure_Foul_Zombie "Skyrim:Spell Tome: Conjure Foul Zombie") for levels 11+) as well as a [journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer%27s_Journal_(Plague_of_the_Dead) "Skyrim:Necromancer's Journal (Plague of the Dead)"). Read the journal to complete the quest and learn of the consequences of the ritual. You can also loot the area for two additional copies of *Spell Tome: Conjure Zombie*.

Following the conclusion of the quest, you will have a chance to encounter [zombie horde](/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Zombie_Attack "Skyrim:World Interactions") world interactions randomly in the wilderness.

## Notes

- At release, this quest had no level requirement and the anonymous letter would be automatically added to your inventory upon installation. This was changed in [Patch 1.6](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch#Version_1.6 "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch") with the release of the [Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition").
- The unmarked location "[The Headless Skeleton](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Falkreath#The_Headless_Skeleton "Skyrim:Unmarked Places Falkreath")" can also be found here, with it being next to the pine tree.
