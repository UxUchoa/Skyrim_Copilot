Karthwasten

**Karthwasten** is a mining [town](/wiki/Skyrim:Towns "Skyrim:Towns") in the northern [Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach").

[Ainethach](/wiki/Skyrim:Ainethach "Skyrim:Ainethach") is the owner of the whole town including the two surrounding mines. The mines at Karthwasten are two of the few mines in the Reach that are not owned by a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord").

When you first enter Karthwasten, you will see the townsfolk squaring off against a group of mercenaries sent by the [Silver-Blood family](/wiki/Skyrim:Thonar_Silver-Blood#Silver-Blood_Family "Skyrim:Thonar Silver-Blood") in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") to stop them from working [Sanuarach Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Sanuarach_Mine "Skyrim:Sanuarach Mine") until Ainethach agrees to sell the land to them.

The town consists of three buildings in a semicircle: [Karthwasten Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten_Hall "Skyrim:Karthwasten Hall"), where Ainethach lives, [Enmon's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon%27s_House "Skyrim:Enmon's House"), where [Enmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon "Skyrim:Enmon") lives with his wife [Mena](/wiki/Skyrim:Mena "Skyrim:Mena") and daughter [Fjotra](/wiki/Skyrim:Fjotra "Skyrim:Fjotra"), and the [miner's barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Karthwasten Miner's Barracks"), where [Belchimac](/wiki/Skyrim:Belchimac "Skyrim:Belchimac"), [Lash gra-Dushnikh](/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh "Skyrim:Lash gra-Dushnikh"), and [Ragnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragnar "Skyrim:Ragnar") live. There is a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack") in front of Karthwasten Hall and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") on the deck in front of the miner's barracks.

Sanuarach Mine is the main [silver](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver "Skyrim:Silver") mine. Its only entrance is north of Karthwasten Hall. In front of the entrance is a [smelter](/wiki/Skyrim:Smelter "Skyrim:Smelter"), three pieces of [silver ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore "Skyrim:Silver Ore") in a cart opposite, and a [Juniper](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") tree on a rock. The entrance to [Fenn's Gulch Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Fenn%27s_Gulch_Mine "Skyrim:Fenn's Gulch Mine") is at the northwestern end of town and has another smelter, two [silver ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot"), and a piece of silver ore to the left of the entrance, with another Juniper tree to the right of the entrance.

## Notes

- Northeast of Karthwasten, there is a small bloodied altar holding a [black soul gem](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Soul_Gem "Skyrim:Black Soul Gem") and a piece of bloodied cheese.
- East-northeast of Karthwasten, on a hill facing down to the river next to a circle of ancient stones, there is a [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein").
- West of Karthwasten, below the hill with the [Shrine to Peryite](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_to_Peryite "Skyrim:Shrine to Peryite"), there is an unmarked stone totem with a dead body and some loot.
- Karthwasten appears in *[Arena](/wiki/Arena:Arena "Arena:Arena")* as [Karthwasten Hall](/wiki/Arena:Karthwasten_Hall "Arena:Karthwasten Hall"), and also [appears](/wiki/Online:Karthwasten "Online:Karthwasten") in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online").
