Solitude Military Camp

**Solitude Military Camp** is a [Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") [camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Camps "Skyrim:Stormcloak Camps") set up west of [Katla's Farm](/wiki/Skyrim:Katla%27s_Farm "Skyrim:Katla's Farm") for the [attack](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Solitude "Skyrim:Battle for Solitude") on [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude").

The camp only appears during the [Battle for Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Solitude "Skyrim:Battle for Solitude") in the Stormcloaks questline.

## Solitude Military Camp

It features several small tents, which contain no bed rolls or hay piles. Several tree stumps are scattered in the area, with two being [wood chopping blocks](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block"). In the middle of the camp is a table holding iron weapons, which are free to take, as well as an [anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil") and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"). Down to the west are most of the tents, a campfire, and one of the wood chopping blocks. Up the hill is another campfire, the other wood chopping block, two small tents, and one large empty tent. To the east of the camp, two catapults are aimed at Solitude.

After the Battle for Solitude, the camp will be disbanded, and its marker will be removed from the world map.
