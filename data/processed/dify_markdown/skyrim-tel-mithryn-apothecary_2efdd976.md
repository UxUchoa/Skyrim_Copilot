Tel Mithryn Apothecary

**Tel Mithryn Apothecary** is the home of the apothecary [Elynea Mothren](/wiki/Skyrim:Elynea_Mothren "Skyrim:Elynea Mothren") in [Tel Mithryn](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(settlement) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (settlement)").

It is one of the four mushroom structures in the settlement, north of [Tel Mithryn's tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_(tower) "Skyrim:Tel Mithryn (tower)").

## Tel Mithryn Apothecary

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.jpg)

Tel Mithryn Apothecary interior

Elynea Mothren will sell apothecary supplies between 8am to 8pm. At other times, the entrance will be novice-locked.

The entrance leads into a small circular room comprising the entire interior. To the east is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Labs "Skyrim:Alchemy Labs") holding a [nirnroot](/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot "Skyrim:Nirnroot"), six samples of [scaly pholiota](/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota "Skyrim:Scaly Pholiota"), a [potion of plentiful stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Plentiful Stamina"), two [potions of ultimate magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Ultimate_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Ultimate Magicka"), a [paralysis poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Paralysis_Poison "Skyrim:Paralysis Poison"), a [draught of resist shock](/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Resist_Shock "Skyrim:Draught of Resist Shock"), a sample of [bleeding crown](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown "Skyrim:Bleeding Crown"), and a sample of [Namira's rot](/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot "Skyrim:Namira's Rot"), with two [fly amanita](/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita "Skyrim:Fly Amanita") and a [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap") growing on the floor around the table. To the south is an open box containing two samples of bleeding crown, a sample of fly amanita, a sample of [imp stool](/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool "Skyrim:Imp Stool"), a sample of Namira's rot, and a sample of white cap, a crate with a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") on top, a set of shelves with a potion of ultimate magicka on the floor in front of the shelves, a chair, and a white cap growing on the floor. The set of shelves holds a crate, a [potion of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina"), three [enchanter's philters](/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Philter "Skyrim:Enchanter's Philter"), a [weak frenzy poison](/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Frenzy_Poison "Skyrim:Weak Frenzy Poison"), three potions of plentiful stamina, a [potion of vigorous magicka](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigorous_Magicka "Skyrim:Potion of Vigorous Magicka"), three paralysis poisons, a bowl of [void salts](/wiki/Skyrim:Void_Salts "Skyrim:Void Salts"), a sample of [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root"), a bowl of [glow dust](/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust "Skyrim:Glow Dust"), a [swamp fungal pod](/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod "Skyrim:Swamp Fungal Pod"), a sample of scaly pholiota, and two draughts of resist shock. To the west is a fly amanita growing on the floor next to a stack of crates, an owned single bed, and two [empty wine bottles](/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle "Skyrim:Empty Wine Bottle") and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on the floor around the bed.

## Notes

- After completing the quest [Healing a House](/wiki/Skyrim:Healing_a_House "Skyrim:Healing a House"), all low-value items in the building become unowned and free to take.
