Aeri

**Aeri** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [lumberjack](/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack "Skyrim:Lumberjack") who lives in [Aeri's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Aeri%27s_House "Skyrim:Aeri's House") at [Anga's Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill "Skyrim:Anga's Mill"). She [pays](/wiki/Skyrim:Chop_Wood "Skyrim:Chop Wood") for chopped firewood, and will also sell [lumber](/wiki/Skyrim:Sawn_Log "Skyrim:Sawn Log")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") if [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is installed. If her [disposition](/wiki/Skyrim:Disposition "Skyrim:Disposition") is raised high enough, she becomes a potential candidate for [marriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") and will also allow you to cut your own lumber for free.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothing"), a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261bd "Skyrim:Clothing"), and a [cowl](/wiki/Skyrim:Cowl "Skyrim:Cowl"). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), and carries a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Aeri%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Aeri's House") to her house as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

She wakes up at 7am and goes outside, sits on the bench by her house and eats breakfast until 8am. She then goes to work at the mill until 7pm. At 7pm she eats dinner sitting on the same bench for one hour, then moves to her house and goes to sleep at midnight.

## Dialogue

Her potential greetings:

:   *"I run the mill, so I'm a busy woman. What is it?"*
:   *"Always something going wrong. Mill needs more lumber or the saw needs sharpening."*
:   *"A good lumberjack is quiet, hard-working, and honest."*
:   *"Here for work? Get an axe and bring me all the wood you can chop."*

She can be asked about the mill:

:   **So you're in charge of Anga's Mill?**
:   *"That I am. My father left it to me after he died in that [Great War](/wiki/Lore:Great_War "Lore:Great War") against the Elves. I don't care what's going on in the world. [Rebellion](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"). [Dragons](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") awakening. The mill is enough trouble for me to worry about."*

:   *"If you're one of the Jarl's men, you'll get your lumber. Anga's Mill pays its debts."*
:   **"One of the Jarl's men"?**
:   *"Sorry, I thought [Skald the Elder](/wiki/Skyrim:Skald "Skyrim:Skald") sent you. We've got an order to fill, and he's not known for patience. Look, if you're heading towards [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar") anytime soon, can you give the Jarl this note? Let him know he'll get his lumber."*

    :   *"Thanks."*

:   :   **I don't have time.**
