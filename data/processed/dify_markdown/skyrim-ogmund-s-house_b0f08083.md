Ogmund's House

**Ogmund's House** is a small home in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth").

It is the home of local bard and [Speech](/wiki/Skyrim:Speech "Skyrim:Speech") trainer [Ogmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Ogmund "Skyrim:Ogmund"), who usually hangs out at the [Silver-Blood Inn](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver-Blood_Inn "Skyrim:Silver-Blood Inn"). It is located next door to [Endon's house](/wiki/Skyrim:Endon%27s_House "Skyrim:Endon's House") and directly above [Arnleif and Sons Trading Company](/wiki/Skyrim:Arnleif_and_Sons_Trading_Company "Skyrim:Arnleif and Sons Trading Company").

It consists of one area, *[Ogmund's House](#Ogmund.27s_House)*.

## Ogmund's House

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-interior-Ogmund%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ogmund%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ogmund%27s_House.jpg)

The interior of the house

Upon entering you will see this is one open room with a dining/kitchen area to the west, a raised storage in front and another raised area for sleeping to the east.

Immediately on your left as you enter is a stone shelf containing a [thistle branch](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch "Skyrim:Thistle Branch"), a [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry "Skyrim:Snowberry") sample, a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a cup and a plate. To the right is a barrel with four [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") samples on the floor around it. Further along the wall next to the dining table is a copy of the book *[Fall from Glory](/wiki/Skyrim:Fall_from_Glory "Skyrim:Fall from Glory")*, a [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") sample and an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace"). On the dining table is a lute, a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") and one of [Alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a goblet, two bowls, two cups and various foodstuffs. Behind the dining table to the west is the kitchen area with more foodstuffs on stone shelves with a portion of [dried elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Dried_Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Dried Elves Ear") and a garlic braid hanging from these. There is also a rack with two rabbits and a pheasant. There is a cook pot behind the table.

As you climb the stairs to the storage area there is a stool with a lit candle and goblet, a chair and flute next to this and a barrel behind. Against the north wall is a stone sideboard containing a mead barrel, a plate, more food, an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), a drum, [iron gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Iron Gauntlets") and a chest (this will contain the object of the quest, if that has been started). An [iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Iron Shield") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots "Skyrim:Iron Boots") are on the floor to the right of the chest, next to a practice dummy.

The sleeping area contains a bed with a chest, a sack and a copy of *[Azura and the Box](/wiki/Skyrim:Azura_and_the_Box "Skyrim:Azura and the Box")* to the left. To the right of the bed is an end table with a flute on top, while a small stone table further to the right holds a copy of *[The Song of Pelinal, v3](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v3 "Skyrim:The Song of Pelinal, v3")*, one medium and one large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a goblet, a [honey nut treat](/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat "Skyrim:Honey Nut Treat") and two bottles of wine. To the left of the exit is a stone shelf containing a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile") and a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") on a plate, with a basket containing more foodstuffs on the floor beside it.
