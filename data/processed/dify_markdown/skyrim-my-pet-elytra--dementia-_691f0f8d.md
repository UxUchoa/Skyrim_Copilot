A smuggled Demented Elytra Nymph

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Locate the Seducer's Bandit Camp.
2. Free the Elytra Nymph from its cage.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This quest is part of the [Saints & Seducers](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers") quest bundle, and can be completed during the [Balance of Power](/wiki/Skyrim:Balance_of_Power "Skyrim:Balance of Power") quest.

In the Seducers Bandit Camp northwest of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"), there is a large cage with a [Demented Elytra Nymph](/wiki/Skyrim:Elytra_Nymph "Skyrim:Elytra Nymph") pet. Nearby is a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_Demented_Elytra_Nymph "Skyrim:Note on Demented Elytra Nymph"), as well as a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Seducers_Bandit%27s_Cage_Key "Skyrim:Seducers Bandit's Cage Key") inside of a chest.

The Elytra Nymph's cage will open when either the lock to its cage is picked or the key is used.

Once the Elytra Nymph is free, speak to it and invite it to come with you. The Elytra Nymph will agree to follow you, and the quest will end.

## Bugs

- At the end of the quest to get the Elytra pet for the Mania version, the dialogue to accept it as a follower is incorrectly checking for Stage 50 in the quest for the Dementia version - this stage doesn't exist, and isn't the right quest to check either. It should be checking for Stage 25 on the Mania quest.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.2.7, fixes this bug.
