## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Feran Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Feran_Sadri "Skyrim:Feran Sadri").
2. Retrieve the [Ancient Vampire remains](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Vampire_Arm "Skyrim:Ancient Vampire Arm").
3. Return to Feran Sadri.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Speak to [Feran Sadri](/wiki/Skyrim:Feran_Sadri "Skyrim:Feran Sadri") and he will tell you his theory that adding the blood of an ancient [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") to the [Bloodstone Chalice](/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodstone_Chalice "Skyrim:Bloodstone Chalice") can lengthen the amount of time its effects last. He will send you to a random location, telling you to retrieve one of five petrified [vampire parts](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Vampire_Arm "Skyrim:Ancient Vampire Arm") from a boss level chest at the location, and infusing the remains' lingering magical essence into the Bloodstone Chalice.

Report back to Feran Sadri to conclude the mission, ending with him telling you the effects of the Bloodstone Chalice will last longer now.

### Reward

Drinking from the Bloodstone Chalice grants the [Blood of the Ancients](/wiki/Skyrim:Blood_of_the_Ancients "Skyrim:Blood of the Ancients") ability which augments the [Vampiric Drain](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampiric_Drain "Skyrim:Vampiric Drain") spell when in Vampire Lord form to absorb stamina and magicka as well as health for one day by default.

| Body parts | Duration(see [Notes](#Notes)) |
| --- | --- |
| Default | 1 day |
| 1 | 3 days |
| 2 | 5 days |
| 3 | 7 days |
| 4 | 9 days (maximum) |

## Notes

- The same body part can be requested more than once.

## Bugs

- The in-game description of the 'Blood of the Ancients' spell is not directly linked to its duration. The duration of the spell is calculated based on the `DLC1VampireChaliceStopDay` script and affixed to a specific day, while the description shows the effectively meaningless magnitude of the spell. This means that the [Necromage](/wiki/Skyrim:Necromage_(perk) "Skyrim:Necromage (perk)") perk, which increases the magnitude and duration of spells cast on undead (such as your vampiric self), causes the description in your 'spells - active effects' menu to display that its duration is increased by 25% (which in turn is caused by the perk's effect on magnitude, not duration), but the spell actually lasts only as long as calculated by the Chalice's level (as indicated in the table above).
