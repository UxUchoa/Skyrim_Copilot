**Faendal** is a [Wood Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Elf "Skyrim:Wood Elf") [thief](/wiki/Skyrim:TrainerMarksmanJourneyman "Skyrim:TrainerMarksmanJourneyman") living in [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"). He is the common level [trainer](/wiki/Skyrim:Trainers "Skyrim:Trainers") for [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery"). He is involved in [a love triangle](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Lovely_Letter "Skyrim:A Lovely Letter") with [Sven](/wiki/Skyrim:Sven "Skyrim:Sven") and [Camilla Valerius](/wiki/Skyrim:Camilla_Valerius "Skyrim:Camilla Valerius"). Should you end the love triangle in his favor, he can become a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers"), and later be [recruited](/wiki/Skyrim:Rebuilding_the_Blades "Skyrim:Rebuilding the Blades") to the [Blades](/wiki/Skyrim:Blades "Skyrim:Blades"). He may also be recruited as a [personal steward](/wiki/Skyrim:Personal_Steward "Skyrim:Personal Steward") if the [Hearthfire DLC](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") is installed.

He is the local hunter who provides Riverwood's basic needs for meat and pelts; additionally, he works at the local lumber mill, under the watchful eye of his employer [Gerdur](/wiki/Skyrim:Gerdur "Skyrim:Gerdur"). His workday starts every morning at 7am and lasts for twelve hours. He will either chop wood, work at the table, or simply relax by looking at the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River") while leaning against the fence. Three times a day, at 7am, 11am, and 3pm, he will spend two hours carrying chopped wood to unload near [Alvor](/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor "Skyrim:Alvor")'s forge. When the clock strikes 7pm, he heads back home and strolls around until 10pm when he takes a late one-hour dinner before going to bed. After only five hours of sleep, he gets out of bed again at 4am and heads up the mountain for a bit of hunting before his workday starts again. The only exception to this routine occurs upon completion of [The Golden Claw](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Golden_Claw "Skyrim:The Golden Claw"), where Faendal will skip the first two hours of his workday and instead visit Camilla Valerius inside the [Riverwood Trader](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood_Trader "Skyrim:Riverwood Trader").

He wears a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes#0006c1d9 "Skyrim:Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5 "Skyrim:Boots"). He carries the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Faendal%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Faendal's House") to his house. He wields a [hunting bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow "Skyrim:Hunting Bow") with twelve [steel arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow "Skyrim:Steel Arrow") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger").

### A Lovely Letter

If you ask him what his problem is with Sven, he will reveal his secret crush on Camilla and his frustrations:

:   **What's your problem with Sven?**
:   *"He's a bard, so he says. Occasionally he finds time to do his job here at the mill. Thinks his ballads and sonnets are going to convince Camilla Valerius to marry him. As if she would say 'yes.' An intelligent, beautiful woman like her wouldn't fall for that nonsense... I hope."*

You can reply in two different ways:

:   **You 'hope'? You don't sound very sure...**
:   *"I've been thinking... maybe Camilla needs a little help seeing Sven for what he is. Could you... could you give her this letter, and say it's from Sven? I think I've matched that Nord's lack of cleverness perfectly."*

:   **You're right. When have words ever inspired feelings of love?**
:   *"I don't need your jokes right now, but maybe you have a point. Maybe Camilla needs some help seeing Sven for what he is. Could you... could you give her this letter, and say it's from Sven? I think I've matched that Nord's lack of cleverness perfectly."*

He will then hand you a [fake letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Faendal%27s_Fake_Letter_from_Sven "Skyrim:Faendal's Fake Letter from Sven") and remind you of your task:

:   *"Tell me as soon as you've given Camilla the letter."*

You can also show it to Sven, who will write another fake letter to make Faendal look racist. You can tell Faendal that Sven wanted you to deliver this letter to Camilla, saying it was from him, and he will be quite surprised:

:   *"What is that blowhard up to now? So, he thinks he can turn Camilla against me? Two can play at this. Here. Give this letter to Camilla and tell her it's from Sven. Let's see what she thinks of him after reading that."*

If you hand Camilla the fake letter from Faendal and lie to her, she will be shocked by how chauvinistic Sven apparently is. When you return to Faendal to tell him that you don't think Camilla will be spending any more time with Sven, he will be happy and hand you a small amount of gold, with his thanks:

:   **I don't think Camilla will be spending any more time with Sven.**
:   *"I appreciate your help. Please, take this. Some gold I've saved up from working at the mill."*

## Notes

- Faendal is the only male Wood Elf [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") in the game.
- When an active follower, Faendal will not attack you when you turn into a [werewolf](/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf "Skyrim:Werewolf").
- Because Faendal can become a follower, it is possible to train up to level 50 in Archery with him for no cost. Simply ask him to train you, and then trade items with him to get back the gold you spend on training.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
- Faendal's mood is set to disgusted, which is why his facial expression tends to show this.
- Faendal also [appears](/wiki/Castles:Faendal "Castles:Faendal") in [*Castles*](/wiki/Castles:Castles "Castles:Castles").

## Bugs

- Faendal is set to follow [Camilla Valerius](/wiki/Skyrim:Camilla_Valerius "Skyrim:Camilla Valerius"), so if you marry Camilla, Faendal will sometimes either be found in your house or just outside. This can also lead to him being killed at the hands of [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit") or [vampires](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire") if Camilla is picked for radiant quests such as [Rescue](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue "Skyrim:Rescue")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") or [Rescue Mission](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_Mission "Skyrim:Rescue Mission") from the [Companions](/wiki/Skyrim:Companions "Skyrim:Companions").
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- Faendal may still talk about how he works at the lumber mill in Riverwood if he's hired as a steward for one of the Hearthfire homesteads.
  - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch"), version 4.1.1, fixes this bug.
- Sometimes Archery skill and level progress will not increase when training with Faendal; however the money will be taken for all five training steps.
- Sometimes the option to train in Archery will not show up after selecting, *"Can you train me to be better at archery?"* when speaking to him.
