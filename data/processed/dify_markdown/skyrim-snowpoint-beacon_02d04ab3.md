Snowpoint Beacon

**Snowpoint Beacon** is a small [tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Towers "Skyrim:Imperial Towers") southwest of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") containing [bandits](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit "Skyrim:Bandit").

## Related Quests

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Walkthrough

The main path to the tower approaches from the north, with stone steps leading up to a doorway in the lowest of the tower's three floors. It is also possible to climb the mountain behind [Fort Fellhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Fellhammer "Skyrim:Fort Fellhammer") to reach the tower from the southwest; from this direction, you will reach the middle floor first.

There is nothing of interest on the lowest floor, which features only a spiral stone staircase leading up to the middle floor. At the top of the stairs is a large hole in the wall leading out onto a wooden platform. A bandit wielding a [one-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:One-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:One-Handed Weapons") guards this level. Inside against an internal wall is a novice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot with a mounted wolf head above it. To the left is a stack of crates with a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and two [silver ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot "Skyrim:Silver Ingot") on top. The internal staircase continues to the top floor, where a bandit chief stands looking out to the north, allowing you to attack from behind. A quiver of eighteen random [arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows "Skyrim:Arrows") rests on a table. To the right of the table is a lean-to with an unowned bedroll under it, and farther to the right are some barrels containing [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food").

## Notes

- Two [corundum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") are east of the tower on a small plateau on the side of the mountain.
