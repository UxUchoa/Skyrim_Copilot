*Dead Man's Dread*

**Dead Man's Dread** is an ancient ship located within the [Grotto](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackbone_Isle_Grotto "Skyrim:Blackbone Isle Grotto") on [Blackbone Isle](/wiki/Skyrim:Blackbone_Isle "Skyrim:Blackbone Isle"). It is initially filled with [undead](/wiki/Skyrim:Undead "Skyrim:Undead"), but can be claimed as a player home after completing the related quest.

Once you have claimed the ship as your own, a map inside can be used for instant [transport](/wiki/Skyrim:Transport#Dead_Man.27s_Dread "Skyrim:Transport") to [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), [Dawnstar](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnstar "Skyrim:Dawnstar"), and [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm"). You can also unlock the two other markers on the map to allow travel to [Castle Volkihar](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Volkihar "Skyrim:Castle Volkihar")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") (technically [Icewater Jetty](/wiki/Skyrim:Icewater_Jetty "Skyrim:Icewater Jetty")[DG](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard")) and [Raven Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock "Skyrim:Raven Rock")[DB](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonborn "Skyrim:Dragonborn") by first discovering these locations. This mode of travel is one-way.

## Interior

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Dread.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Dread.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Dread.jpg)

The captain's quarters

What you'll see first after entering the cave is a path to get onboard the ship. After getting onto the deck, at your left will be the entrance. First sight you have's a steering wheel. Both sides will take you downstairs. There's a [Shrine of Arkay](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Arkay "Skyrim:Shrine of Arkay") and in front of it, to your left will be a room with 2 beds and to your right is the child's room, where you can find a [Scimitar](/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar "Skyrim:Scimitar"). The front door will take you to a hall with several rooms and some downstairs. If you go to the hall, first room will have 4 beds and several items and books. The room spotted at the left wall is a smithing room, and there'll be an [Anvil](/wiki/Skyrim:Anvil "Skyrim:Anvil"), a [Tanning Rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"), a [Workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Workbench "Skyrim:Workbench") and a [Grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone"), also there are 4 [Scimitars](/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar "Skyrim:Scimitar"). Across the hall entrance, you can find an [Arcane Enchanter](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Enchanter "Skyrim:Arcane Enchanter"), an [Alchemy Lab](/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Lab "Skyrim:Alchemy Lab") and many soul gems and ingredients. Also there's another room inside this place with 2 more beds and another [Scimitar](/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar "Skyrim:Scimitar").

Whether you go downstairs at the main hall or jump in the hole at the center of it, you'll see a tavern place, and deeper will be a storage for the tavern. Across the tavern is the captain's quarters with a single mannequin. There's a way left between the tavern and the captains' quarters, this path takes you to another room with 2 more beds. Finally, if you go right, you'll find the last room with 2 calabooses

## Notes

- The ship's cupboards contain unique clothing items added by this [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club").
- Although Blackbone Isle has a map marker and can be fast traveled to from the mainland, you are required to take the boat back to [Orphan's Tear](/wiki/Skyrim:Orphan%27s_Tear "Skyrim:Orphan's Tear") to leave the island. This makes *Dead Man's Dread* particularly inconvenient as a player home, requiring three loading screens to reach via fast travel. The bandits at Orphan's Tear also respawn, meaning you will likely be attacked upon landing.
- Version 1.6 of the [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition_Patch "Skyrim:Special Edition Patch") adds map markers to all 5 locations you can fast travel to from inside the ship. This mode of travel remains one-way.

## Bugs

- The Ghostly Remains of the [Ghost Sailors](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost_Sailor "Skyrim:Ghost Sailor") and [Ghost Captain](/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost_Captain "Skyrim:Ghost Captain") may not despawn and will instead remain on the deck. This happens when you read the [Pirate's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate%27s_Note "Skyrim:Pirate's Note") and return to Dead Man's Dread too soon to claim it as a home.
  - Wait for ten full days before reading the Pirate's Note and coming back to the island.
- The cooking pot in the kitchen by the bar on the bottom deck is set the wrong way round; activating it results in the player being positioned inside the wall, and leaving it results in the player being pushed inside the prison room. **?**
- The weapon stand in the blacksmith's room, aside from the three Scimitars already attached to it, may not be interactable. **?**
