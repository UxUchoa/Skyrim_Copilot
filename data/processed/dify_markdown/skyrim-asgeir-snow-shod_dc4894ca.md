Asgeir Snow-Shod

**Asgeir Snow-Shod** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [warrior](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H "Skyrim:CombatWarrior1H") living in [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). He works at the [Black-Briar Meadery](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Meadery "Skyrim:Black-Briar Meadery") as [Maven Black-Briar](/wiki/Skyrim:Maven_Black-Briar "Skyrim:Maven Black-Briar")'s investor and partner. His parents are [Nura](/wiki/Skyrim:Nura_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Nura Snow-Shod") and [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](/wiki/Skyrim:Vulwulf_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Vulwulf Snow-Shod"), and his brother is [Unmid](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmid_Snow-Shod "Skyrim:Unmid Snow-Shod"). He also had a sister, Lilija, who was killed while serving as Battle Maiden for the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks").

His family has connections to the Stormcloaks and his [marriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Until_Death "Skyrim:Bound Until Death") to someone with [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") connections is seen as a step towards reconciliation. He is marked essential until he becomes the new husband of [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici") in the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") quest.

Asgeir wears a set of [fine clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#00086991 "Skyrim:Fine Clothes") and a pair of [fine boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993 "Skyrim:Fine Boots"). He is equipped with a leveled [sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Sword "Skyrim:Sword") which can be up to [elven](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven "Skyrim:Elven") quality. He carries two leveled [gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Gems "Skyrim:Gems"), a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow-Shod_House_Key "Skyrim:Snow-Shod House Key") to [his manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow-Shod_Manor "Skyrim:Snow-Shod Manor"), and a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

He will drop [his wedding band](/wiki/Skyrim:Asgeir%27s_Wedding_Band "Skyrim:Asgeir's Wedding Band") if killed. If he is not killed during the related quest, the band will remain in his inventory unequipped and can be pickpocketed from him afterwards.

### Bound Until Death

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_02.jpg)

Asgeir and Vittoria at their wedding

During this quest, Asgeir will be found in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") as the proud groom to Vittoria Vici. When you approach him, he will greet you with:

:   *"Make sure to get yourself something to drink."*
:   *"You hungry? There's plenty of food. Eat up, now."*

He may also jokingly say either:

:   *"Now now, fella. Let's not get too close to my new bride, eh?"* (If you are male)
:   *"Well aren't you a pretty thing? Should my new bride by*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *worried? He he he he."* (If you are female)

You can congratulate him and mention how it's quite a day, which he will agree:

:   **Congratulations. Quite the day, hmm?**
:   *"The best. Vittoria's a good woman. Any man should be so lucky. I know my father's not happy about me marrying an Imperial, but that's his problem."*

As Vittoria Vici lies dead, the now-widowed Asgeir can be found screaming:

:   *"My wife is dead! Dead!"*

Should he survive the wedding, he will now detest you for murdering Vittoria.

### Spread the Love

If you [give](/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love "Skyrim:Spread the Love") him a [religious missive](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara "Skyrim:The Warmth of Mara"):

:   **Here, brother. Blessings of Mara upon you.**
:   *"Not really my usual read, but I'll give it a try."*

## Dialogue

He shows that he is charitable, as he gives a coin to Riften's beggars, saying:

:   *"Of course, here. Be well."* to [Edda](/wiki/Skyrim:Edda "Skyrim:Edda") and *"Absolutely. Here you are. Be well."* to [Snilf](/wiki/Skyrim:Snilf "Skyrim:Snilf").

Asgeir will greet you with:

:   *"Sorry, I'm afraid I can't help you much right now... I'm currently in mourning."*
:   *"You've caught me at a bad time. I've lost someone dear to me."*
:   *"Yes? What is it?"*

Ending conversation with him will have him say:

:   *"It's been good talking to you."*

When spoken to directly you can ask questions:

:   **May I ask why you're in mourning?**
:   *"It was my sister, Lilija. She served as a Battle Maiden with the Stormcloaks. We recently heard she'd been killed... I... I find it difficult to talk about. Perhaps you'd be better off speaking to my parents about it. Anyway, I'm glad my work at the Black-Briar Meadery keeps me busy. Helps me keep my mind off of things."*

:   **You're involved with the meadery?**
:   *"Yes, actually. I'm partnered with Maven Black-Briar. I put up a chunk of the gold she needed to get the place off the ground. We've been staggeringly successful so far. In fact, I'm almost surprised how quickly she's generated business for us. My name may not be on the label, but some of my blood, sweat and tears are in every drop. Uh, figuratively of course."*

:   **Tell me about your parents.**
:   *"My father was a Stormcloak soldier in his younger days. Now he just walks the streets spewing his hatred and propaganda. My mother is the Priestess of [Talos](/wiki/Lore:Talos "Lore:Talos"). She tends to the shrine and teaches his ways. I'm just not so sure she's delivering the right message."*
:   **Are you at odds with them?**
:   *"Don't mistake my political beliefs as a substitute for my family values. They're still my parents, and I love them dearly.
    I just think they've let my sister's death skew their outlook on [Ulfric](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak") and what he really brings to the table with [this war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"). I'm not so certain that Ulfric's intentions are as noble as they think. I think Ulfric cares about Ulfric and the rest is a smokescreen.
    I'd... appreciate if you kept my opinion between us. The last thing I want to do is have my parents 'lose' another child."*
