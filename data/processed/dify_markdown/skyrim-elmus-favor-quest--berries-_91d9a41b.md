## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Elmus](/wiki/Skyrim:Elmus "Skyrim:Elmus") at [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall").
2. Retrieve one sample of [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries").
3. Return to Elmus.

## Detailed Walkthrough

After the Nords from [Bujold's Retreat](/wiki/Skyrim:Bujold%27s_Retreat "Skyrim:Bujold's Retreat") have retaken [Thirsk Mead Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Thirsk_Mead_Hall "Skyrim:Thirsk Mead Hall"), speak to [Elmus](/wiki/Skyrim:Elmus "Skyrim:Elmus") there to learn that he is in need of some [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries") to make some mead:

:   **How is the mead?**
:   *"We lost most of the batches we had brewing when... before those things were here. One is just ready to take its turn, though, and it's about time to add some of the flavorings. You can't find them on Solstheim, but if you find yourself back in Skyrim, I wouldn't mind you bringing some juniper berries. They add a different kind of sweetness. Tart. Makes the flavor more round."*

The largest number of juniper berries can be found in [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"), but you can also find some elsewhere, or buy them from various apothecary [merchants](/wiki/Skyrim:Merchants "Skyrim:Merchants"). Elmus will tell you that you can't find juniper berries in Solstheim, but this is not true; there's a juniper plant in the exterior area of [Nchardak](/wiki/Skyrim:Nchardak "Skyrim:Nchardak").

Once you have retrieved a sample of the ingredient, return to Elmus at Thirsk Mead Hall for your reward: ten bottles of [Ashfire Mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ashfire_Mead "Skyrim:Ashfire Mead") and a leveled amount of gold:

:   **I've got some juniper berries. (Give berries.)**
:   *"Oh, perfect. Just the right ripeness, too. Good work, friend."*
