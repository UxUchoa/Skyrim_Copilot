A generic member of the Dawnguard

**Dawnguard** are radiant members of the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_(faction) "Skyrim:Dawnguard (faction)"). They are randomly encountered in the wilderness and, if you are a member of [Harkon's](/wiki/Skyrim:Harkon "Skyrim:Harkon") [court](/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Vampire_Clan "Skyrim:Volkihar Vampire Clan"), are hostile toward you.

They attack in groups of three and can appear upon fast traveling to a location. If you are a [vampire](/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire "Skyrim:Vampire"), they will attack on sight. These assassins will continue to appear even if the Dawnguard [questline](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Quests#Main_Quests "Skyrim:Dawnguard Quests") has been completed.

Two Dawnguard appear along with Durak to start the quest "Dawnguard".

Typically, they wear either a set of [Dawnguard armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Armor "Skyrim:Dawnguard Armor") or heavy Dawnguard armor and are armed with a [crossbow](/wiki/Skyrim:Crossbow "Skyrim:Crossbow") and a [Dawnguard warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Warhammer "Skyrim:Dawnguard Warhammer") or a [Dawnguard war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_War_Axe "Skyrim:Dawnguard War Axe"). They may also carry small amounts of gold and minor random loot.
