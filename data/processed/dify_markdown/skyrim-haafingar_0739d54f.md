Haafingar, a hold in northwest Skyrim

**Haafingar** is a relatively small [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in northwest [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), containing the provincial capital [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). As the home of the provincial capital, Haafingar is the center of [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") rule in Skyrim. It only becomes Stormcloak-run if you join the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") and complete the entire questline, including both [The Battle for Fort Hraggstad](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Hraggstad "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Hraggstad") and the [Battle for Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Solitude "Skyrim:Battle for Solitude").

Haafingar is dominated by a large ridge that runs the length of the hold. Pine forests and meadows cover the southern slopes down to the [Karth River](/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River "Skyrim:Karth River"), while the north side is colder, and dominated by mountains and frozen coastline. Solitude itself sits on a massive natural archway that extends over the mouth of the Karth from the eastern spur of the ridge.

## Flora and Fauna

Haafingar's coastline is ideal for the growth of [nordic barnacles](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle") and [clams](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam "Skyrim:Clam") in the water and [canis root](/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root "Skyrim:Canis Root") and [spiky grass](/wiki/Skyrim:Spiky_Grass "Skyrim:Spiky Grass") on land. Near [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"), [fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fish "Skyrim:Fish") can be enjoyed around [Solitude Sawmill](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Sawmill "Skyrim:Solitude Sawmill") where [dragonflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonfly "Skyrim:Dragonfly") play over the water. A fair amount of [nightshade plants](/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade "Skyrim:Nightshade") grow inside the capital. [Mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella"), [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Mountain Flower"), and [thistles](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle") can be found in the lower parts of the hold while only [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") and sporadic mountain flowers can be found in the snow-covered tail of the [Druadach Mountains](/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Mountains "Skyrim:Druadach Mountains").

## Haafingar Quests

All the quests that are started in or are related to Haafingar (except for radiant quests begun in other holds) are listed here:

### Main Quest

- **[Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity")**: Infiltrate a party at the [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy").
- **[Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending")**: Enforce a ceasefire to secure [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach").

### Imperial Legion Civil War Quests

- **[Reunification of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Reunification_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Reunification of Skyrim")**: Reunite Skyrim under the leadership of the Empire. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[A False Front](/wiki/Skyrim:A_False_Front_(Imperial) "Skyrim:A False Front (Imperial)")**: Find and deliver some forged [documents](/wiki/Skyrim:StormCloak_Documents "Skyrim:StormCloak Documents") to the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks").
- **[Rescue from Fort Kastav](/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav "Skyrim:Rescue from Fort Kastav")**: Take [Fort Kastav](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Kastav "Skyrim:Fort Kastav") for the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") and rescue the prisoners within. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[The Battle for Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Dunstad (Imperial)")**: Take [Fort Dunstad](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dunstad "Skyrim:Fort Dunstad") for the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[The Battle for Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial) "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Greenwall (Imperial)")**: Take [Fort Greenwall](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall "Skyrim:Fort Greenwall") for the [Imperials](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion"). ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

### Stormcloaks Civil War Quests

- **[Liberation of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Liberation_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Liberation of Skyrim")**: Drive the Imperials from Skyrim. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))
- **[The Battle for Fort Hraggstad](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Hraggstad "Skyrim:The Battle for Fort Hraggstad")**: Prepare for the final assault on [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") by taking [this fort](/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Hraggstad "Skyrim:Fort Hraggstad") for the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks").
- **[Battle for Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Solitude "Skyrim:Battle for Solitude")**: Conquer the [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") [capital](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude") and free Skyrim from the Empire! ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

- **[The Mind of Madness](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness "Skyrim:The Mind of Madness")**: Gain access to and solve the mystery of the [Pelagius Wing](/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing "Skyrim:Pelagius Wing").

### Dark Brotherhood

- **[Bound Until Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Until_Death "Skyrim:Bound Until Death")**: Assassinate [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici") at her own wedding.
