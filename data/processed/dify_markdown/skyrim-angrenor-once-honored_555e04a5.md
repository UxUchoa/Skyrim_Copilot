**Angrenor Once-Honored** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [beggar](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggars "Skyrim:Beggars") who was a former member of the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks"). Upon raising his disposition by giving him a coin, he will become a potential candidate for [marriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage").

He can be found relaxing in [Candlehearth Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Candlehearth_Hall "Skyrim:Candlehearth Hall") from 4am to 10am. He then goes to the Stone Quarter, leaning against a wall. At 4pm he patrols Windhelm for twelve hours, then returns to Candlehearth Hall.

Unlike most beggars, he wears a set of prison clothes: a [roughspun tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic "Skyrim:Roughspun Tunic") with [footwraps](/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps "Skyrim:Footwraps"). He carries an [iron mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace "Skyrim:Iron Mace") and a selection of [lower-class items](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

### Conversations

Upon entering [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") for the first time, you will witness Angrenor and his counterpart [Rolff Stone-Fist](/wiki/Skyrim:Rolff_Stone-Fist "Skyrim:Rolff Stone-Fist") harassing a [Dunmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [woman](/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron "Skyrim:Suvaris Atheron") and accusing her of being an [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") spy, making no attempt to conceal their disdain for elves:

:   **Rolff**: *"You come here where you're not wanted, you eat our food, you pollute our city with your stink and you refuse to help the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks")."*
:   **Suvaris**: *"But we haven't taken a side because it's not our fight."*
:   **Angrenor**: *"Hey, maybe the reason these gray-skins don't help in the war is because they're Imperial spies!"*
:   **Suvaris**: *"Imperial spies? You can't be serious!"*
:   **Rolff**: *"Maybe we'll pay you a visit tonight, little spy. We got ways of finding out what you really are."*

## Unused Dialogue

He was meant to have more dialogue once he moved into the [Aretino Residence](/wiki/Skyrim:Aretino_Residence "Skyrim:Aretino Residence"), which will never be heard due to a [bug](#Bugs). He would have defended his claim on the house to you, saying:

:   *"What? Were you hoping to claim this house for yourself? Well, the Aretinos always liked me, so I think I'm family enough to take up here."*

Angrenor also would have referenced Innocence Lost, saying:

:   *"Rumor holds that the Aretino boy brought an assassin here. Sounds like nonsense to me."*

He would also have disparaged his fellow Windhelm residents, saying:

:   *"The milk drinkers in this town wouldn't last five minutes in a real battle."*

## Notes

- He is the only beggar who is able to be married in the game.

## Bugs

- After completing the quest [Innocence Lost](/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost "Skyrim:Innocence Lost") he carries around a key to the [Aretino Residence](/wiki/Skyrim:Aretino_Residence "Skyrim:Aretino Residence"). He should have moved into the house after the quest, but the scripting is incomplete.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
- Once married, Angrenor will still beg for money, even if he is living at your home.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
- If their conversation with Suvaris is interrupted by combat (e.g. by a dragon or vampire attack), Rolff and Angrenor will be stuck standing at the main gate permanently.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") This can be fixed by using the command `startscene CD91F`.
