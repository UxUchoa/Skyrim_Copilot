**Frofnir Trollsbane** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") who can be found deceased in a troll's den between [Cradle Stone Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Cradle_Stone_Tower "Skyrim:Cradle Stone Tower") and [Valthume](/wiki/Skyrim:Valthume "Skyrim:Valthume"). An easy way of finding him is to find the [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") that is to the west of Cradle Stone Tower and then rock climb north-northwesterly down to him, near two [trolls](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll "Skyrim:Troll").

He wears a full set of [iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor") and carries a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield"), a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow") supplemented by [iron arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow "Skyrim:Iron Arrow"), and the unique warhammer [Trollsbane](/wiki/Skyrim:Trollsbane "Skyrim:Trollsbane").

## Notes

- There is a dead [elk](/wiki/Skyrim:Elk "Skyrim:Elk") and a portion of [venison](/wiki/Skyrim:Venison "Skyrim:Venison") as well as numerous troll skulls, other human and animal bones scattered around him. Further to the west are several bird's nests with either [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg") or [rock warbler eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg "Skyrim:Rock Warbler Egg").
