Eris in the Red Wave

**Eris** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [witchblade](/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade "Skyrim:Witchblade") and a pirate aboard the [Red Wave](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Wave "Skyrim:Red Wave"), under the command of [Safia](/wiki/Skyrim:Safia "Skyrim:Safia").

Eris wears [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12 "Skyrim:Boots"). She carries a [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic"), a leveled piece of jewelry, a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"), and an additional small amount of gold. She wields a leveled [mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Mace "Skyrim:Mace"), which can be up to [Daedric](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric "Skyrim:Daedric") in quality and knows the following spells: [Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Flames "Skyrim:Flames"), [Fireball](/wiki/Skyrim:Fireball "Skyrim:Fireball"), [Fast Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Healing "Skyrim:Fast Healing"), and [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward").
