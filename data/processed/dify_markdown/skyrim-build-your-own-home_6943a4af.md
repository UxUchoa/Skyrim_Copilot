## Quick Walkthrough

1. Obtain permission to purchase land from the steward/jarl of [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"), [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold"), or [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale").
2. Purchase the land from the steward.
3. Visit your new property, and use the drafting table to begin building your house.
4. Use the carpenter's workbench to build the foundation of your house. Continue building your home to your liking.

## Detailed Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_15.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_15.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_15.jpg)

Laying the foundations

Once you've obtained the favor of the Jarl of [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold"), [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"), or [the Pale](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale "Skyrim:The Pale"), usually by doing quests for them, you will be given the right to purchase a piece of land in the hold. In Morthal and Falkreath, you will need to see the steward about obtaining permission; in Dawnstar, it is the jarl who grants permission. After receiving permission, you may purchase the land from the relevant steward for 5,000 gold. The plot will then be marked on your map.

Upon arriving at your new property, you will be instructed to use the [drafting table](/wiki/Skyrim:Drafting_Table "Skyrim:Drafting Table") to begin building your house. Your only option will be the [Small House Layout](/wiki/Skyrim:Small_House_Layout "Skyrim:Small House Layout"). After selecting the plans, you will be instructed to use the [carpenter's workbench](/wiki/Skyrim:Carpenter%27s_Workbench "Skyrim:Carpenter's Workbench") to build the foundation of your house and, if this is your purchased land, a misc objective to buy lumber will appear in your journal. It directs you to [Hert](/wiki/Skyrim:Hert "Skyrim:Hert") but it can be completed by speaking to any lumberjack. To start off the building you need one [sawn log](/wiki/Skyrim:Sawn_Log "Skyrim:Sawn Log") and ten [quarried stone](/wiki/Skyrim:Quarried_Stone "Skyrim:Quarried Stone") for the foundations. There will be a [stone quarry](/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry "Skyrim:Stone Quarry") somewhere on the site, so you won't need to go far to find some.

Having done this, the quest concludes. However, there are many more construction and furnishing options to choose from. See the [construction article](/wiki/Skyrim:Construction "Skyrim:Construction") for more information.

## Notes

- If you reach level 9, and have not yet started the quest, one of the jarls will send you a letter by courier. If you're friends with the jarl of Falkreath or Hjaalmarch, the steward will send you [this letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Steward_(Steward_name)_of_(hold_name) "Skyrim:Letter from Steward (Steward name) of (hold name)"); for the Pale, it will instead be [this letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Jarl_(Jarl%27s_Name) "Skyrim:Letter from Jarl (Jarl's Name)"), straight from the jarl. If you haven't made friends with any of them yet, [this letter](/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Jarl_(Jarl%27s_Name)_of_(Jarl%27s_City) "Skyrim:Letter from Jarl (Jarl's Name) of (Jarl's City)") will be sent to you from the current Jarl of Falkreath. Regardless of who sends which letter, reading it will give you the objective to speak to the jarl and advance the quest.
- If not yet friends with any jarl, and [Rare Gifts](/wiki/Skyrim:Rare_Gifts "Skyrim:Rare Gifts") is still in your journal when the courier sets out to find you, it will be failed and removed from your journal in favor of this quest.
- It is advisable to purchase the land for [Lakeview Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Lakeview_Manor "Skyrim:Lakeview Manor") in Falkreath hold before completing [Contract: Kill Helvard](/wiki/Skyrim:Contract:_Kill_Helvard "Skyrim:Contract: Kill Helvard"), as you may not be able to regain Siddgeir's favor afterwards.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") If you use the console, you can click on Siddgeir and use `setrelationshiprank player 2` to fix this.
  - Alternatively if you side with the Stormcloaks and take Falkreath, [Dengeir of Stuhn](/wiki/Skyrim:Dengeir_of_Stuhn "Skyrim:Dengeir of Stuhn") replaces Siddgeir as the Jarl, bypassing this issue.
- If Hert is dead, the misc quest doesn't fail but will have no marker and can be completed by speaking to any other lumberjack as normal.

## Bugs

- Once you are eligible to start this quest in one of the other two holds by having befriended their jarl, Siddgeir possibly may not offer Rare Gifts or [Kill the Bandit Leader](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_the_Bandit_Leader_(Jarl) "Skyrim:Kill the Bandit Leader (Jarl)"), which will make the house unavailable in Falkreath.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Once you are level 9 or above and are certain that the courier isn't coming, enter Siddgeir's Longhouse and then use the following console commands. This should start the bandit quest, allowing this quest to proceed normally afterwards.

    ```
    prid 19822
    setrelationshiprank player 2
    setstage Favor154 10
    ```
- If you've started but not completed this quest, you cannot initiate Thane quests in other holds.
- Quest becomes impossible to start either after having started the Kill Helvard quest or after becoming thane.
  - [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") After (but also possibly before) becoming thane the quest can be force started with:

    ```
    setstage BYOHHouseFalkreath 30
    ```
- This quest doesn't have the property set for cut logs which can result in the planned inventory filter failing to apply. This led to potential script overload and stack dumps.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Hearthfire Patch"), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- If you did not talk to [Farengar Secret-Fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Farengar_Secret-Fire "Skyrim:Farengar Secret-Fire") during the [Bleak Falls Barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest) "Skyrim:Bleak Falls Barrow (quest)") quest yet, you possibly may not get the option to ask the jarl for any jobs, and may not be able to tell him you got his letter. This will block you from becoming a thane and thus accessing the house until you progress the main quest. If you want to evade starting [Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising") to avoid enabling dragons in Skyrim but still want to access to Lakeview Manor, you can progress the Bleak Falls Barrow quest to the point where you need to give Farengar the tablet and stop there. This will make Siddgeir talk to you about "tasks" or the letter but won't enable dragons. **?**

Notes

- Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=LocationHold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](/wiki/Skyrim:Console "Skyrim:Console") to advance through the quest by entering `setstage BYOHHouseFalkreath
  BYOHHousePale
  BYOHHouseHjaalmarch
  BYOHHouseBuilding stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest BYOHHouseFalkreath
  BYOHHousePale
  BYOHHouseHjaalmarch
  BYOHHouseBuilding`.
