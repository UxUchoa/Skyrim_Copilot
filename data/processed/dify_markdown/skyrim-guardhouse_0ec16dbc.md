The **Guardhouse** is the [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch") barracks on the south side of [Morthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal "Skyrim:Morthal").

This barracks appears similar to other local houses. The interior is divided into two zones: the *[Guardhouse](#Guardhouse)* and *[Morthal Jail](#Morthal_Jail)*.

### Exterior

There is a covered porch in front with a wooden bench along the wall to the left of the door. The porch continues around to the left of the building. In this area there are two [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels, one with two unowned bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") on top, a target dummy, and a [tanning rack](/wiki/Skyrim:Tanning_Rack "Skyrim:Tanning Rack"). [Benor](/wiki/Skyrim:Benor "Skyrim:Benor") often spends his time loafing here. The door is often novice-locked, and you will incur a 5 gold [bounty](/wiki/Skyrim:Crime#Bounties "Skyrim:Crime") whether breaking in or out. There are two [swamp fungal pods](/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod "Skyrim:Swamp Fungal Pod") behind the building.

#### Guardhouse

This area is the barracks proper. [Morthal guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Morthal_Guard "Skyrim:Morthal Guard") are constantly coming and going from this area. There are a total of four single owned beds, two against the west wall and two against the east wall. There is a [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot between each pair of beds. Opposite the door is in the middle of the south wall is a fireplace, with eight bottles of Nord mead and a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") on the mantlepieces. In front of the hearth is a kettle containing six [pheasant breasts](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast") and a small round wooden table and two chairs. On the table are five bottles of Nord mead, a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), five loose [coins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold") and an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger").

To the left of the fireplace is a barrel with three bottles of Nord mead on top and then, a flight of stairs leading down to *Morthal jail*. On the left of these is a food barrel with four more bottles of Nord mead on top. To the right of the fireplace is another food barrel, behind this is a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, MidYear, v6](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_MidYear,_v6 "Skyrim:2920, MidYear, v6")*. Against the north wall is a long wooden table and bench. On the table are three bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of wine, two bottles of Nord mead, several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), two [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg"), a [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll") and four [salmon steaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak") on wooden plates. There is a basket of [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") to the right and a barrel with two [potions of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina") and one [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") on top. To the left of the table is a food barrel. Along the north wall and hanging above the table are four bunches of [elves ears](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids, three bunches of [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). To the right of these are two hanging racks holding four [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant").

### Morthal Jail

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-interior-Morthal_Guardhouse_Jail.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Morthal_Guardhouse_Jail.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morthal_Guardhouse_Jail.jpg)

A Morthal guard keeps close watch over the jail

Downstairs is the jail, which consists of one large adept-locked cage at the rear of the room, containing three bed rolls and a pile of food sacks. The rest of the room is partitioned with wooden walls either side of an opening. On the northern side of the wall on the left are the evidence chest and the prisoners belonging's chest next to each other. The rest of the room is used by the guards. Against the east wall are three straw bales and a chair, which the guard normally sits on. Along the south wall are several crates and food sacks. The west wall has several food barrels against it. These aren't marked as owned and therefore can be freely emptied. There is a barrel with three [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick") and three bottles of Nord mead against the north wall. In the middle of the room is a long wooden table and chair. On the table is a bottle of wine, a bottle of alto wine and the [key to Morthal jail](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Morthal_Jail "Skyrim:Key to Morthal Jail").

## Notes

- Some items found in Morthal Jail are not owned and can be taken without stealing.
