Giant's Grove

**Giant's Grove** is a grove accessed through [Fallowstone Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Fallowstone_Cave "Skyrim:Fallowstone Cave") containing a [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") whose tribe has defiled a shrine of [Malacath](/wiki/Lore:Malacath "Lore:Malacath").

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/SR-place-Shrine_of_Malacath.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Malacath.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Malacath.jpg)

Shrine of Malacath

The giants attack the [Orc stronghold](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds "Skyrim:Orc Strongholds") of [Largashbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur "Skyrim:Largashbur") due to the curse bestowed on them by the Daedric Prince. The grove is inaccessible until the quest [The Cursed Tribe](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe "Skyrim:The Cursed Tribe") has been started.

## Related Quests

- **[The Cursed Tribe](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe "Skyrim:The Cursed Tribe")**: Help lift the curse placed on the [Orcs](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") of [Largashbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur "Skyrim:Largashbur") by [Malacath](/wiki/Lore:Malacath "Lore:Malacath").

## Walkthrough

Corpses encircle a massive bonfire in front of the gory shrine of Malacath. A boss [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") and two [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions") on the ground are near the base of the shrine. Four [orichalcum ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Orichalcum Ore Vein") are also in the vicinity: two are on the ground next to the wall (with one east of the bonfire and the other to the west), the third is south of the shrine, and the fourth is directly behind the shrine. The hostile unnamed giant will attack you on sight and carries the quest-related weapon [Shagrol's Warhammer](/wiki/Skyrim:Shagrol%27s_Warhammer "Skyrim:Shagrol's Warhammer").

## Notes

- You cannot wait in this location.
- The burnt body on a pole at the campfire cannot be eaten if you activate [Beast Form](/wiki/Skyrim:Beast_Form "Skyrim:Beast Form").
- You can fast travel *to* this location, but not *from* this location. To exit, you'll have to backtrack through Fallowstone Cave. You can fast travel again once you exit the cave via the main entrance.
- This location is one of the easternmost locations in the game you can visit.
- This location cannot be independently [cleared](/wiki/Skyrim:Dungeons#Clearing "Skyrim:Dungeons"). It will only display as cleared when the boss-level creatures in Fallowstone Cave have been killed, as the game considers these two zones as one unit for this purpose. As a result, Giant's Grove itself will never be added to your count of cleared locations, and therefore does not contribute to the [Delver](/wiki/Skyrim:Delver "Skyrim:Delver") achievement.
