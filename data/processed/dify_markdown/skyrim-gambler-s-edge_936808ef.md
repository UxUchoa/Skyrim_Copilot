## Quick Walkthrough

1. Go to the [Mistveil Keep Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Mistveil_Keep#Mistveil_Keep_Barracks "Skyrim:Mistveil Keep") in Riften.
2. Find and read the *[Guard's Dossier on Antonius](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard%27s_Dossier:_Antonius "Skyrim:Guard's Dossier: Antonius")*.
3. Find Antonius in [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern").
4. Read *[Antonius' Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Antonius%27_Note "Skyrim:Antonius' Note")*.
5. Obtain the armor from the collector at [the Bee and Barb](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bee_and_Barb "Skyrim:The Bee and Barb") in Riften.

## Detailed Walkthrough

This quest can be started by heading to the Mistveil Keep Barracks in Riften. Go inside and up to the second floor. In one of the corners find a dresser with *[Guard's Dossier: Antonius](/wiki/Skyrim:Guard%27s_Dossier:_Antonius "Skyrim:Guard's Dossier: Antonius")* atop of it. Once it has been read, the quest will begin.

The next destination is [Cragslane Cavern](/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern "Skyrim:Cragslane Cavern") located just off the road between Riften and Windhelm, North of Shor's Stone and West of Ansilvund. The entrance has a Bouncer and a couple of Pit wolves in cages. Defeat them and go inside. Further in find the Pit Wolf fighting arena. Fight off the various titled foes before finding the corpse of Antonius who is already dead. Loot his body to find *[Antonius' Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Antonius%27_Note "Skyrim:Antonius' Note")* and read it. It indicates that as a gambler he incurred some debts and sold his unique armor to a collector in Riften.

Next head back to Riften and visit the Bee and Barb tavern. Once inside, look for a patron named "The Collector". There are a few options at this point which all result in the same outcome. You can attempt to persuade or intimidate the Collector into "leaving the city's protection" or purchase the armor from them. If you convince the Collector to leave Riften, you can try to kill or pickpocket them for the armor once they are outside the city.

## Notes

- The Armor Collector carries "Orders" that can be pickpocketed.
