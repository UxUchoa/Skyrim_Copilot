Cradlecrush Rock

**Cradlecrush Rock** is a [giant camp](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps "Skyrim:Giant Camps") southwest of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

## Related Quests

- **[Bounty: Giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty:_Giant "Skyrim:Bounty: Giant")**: Kill a problematic [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") for a bounty reward. ([radiant](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant "Skyrim:Radiant"))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant#Quest_Locations "Skyrim:Radiant").

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock_02.jpg)

Huge cheese

The camp is near the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River") downstream from [Valtheim Towers](/wiki/Skyrim:Valtheim_Towers "Skyrim:Valtheim Towers") and [Darkshade](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkshade "Skyrim:Darkshade"). To reach it, follow the road east from Valtheim Towers. Shortly after a hairpin bend to the right at the bottom of the hill, you will see a ford on your left with an [iron ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein") visible on the other side. Follow this path up to reach the camp.

A single [giant](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") occupies the camp, which consists of a central campfire that has been extinguished, two [mammoth cheese bowls](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl "Skyrim:Mammoth Cheese Bowl"), a stacked woodpile with a dead painted [cow](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow "Skyrim:Cow") on top which can be looted for [raw beef](/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef "Skyrim:Raw Beef") and a [cow hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Cow_Hide "Skyrim:Cow Hide"), an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") with a leveled healing potion and a leveled stamina potion next to it, and a huge pile of straw that probably serves as the giant's bed, though you will never see him sleep in it. Among the ashes of the fire are three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), three [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a [sliced Eidar cheese](/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese "Skyrim:Sliced Eidar Cheese"), and an unusually large [Eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"), the largest of these in the game. A single stake with a [charred skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide "Skyrim:Charred Skeever Hide") is cooking over the fire. A few feet west of the campfire, a [knapsack](/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack "Skyrim:Knapsack"), a leveled enchanted helm, and a ring can be found on three separate skeletons that appear to have been crushed by a huge rock.

## Notes

- 320 feet east-northeast of the camp is an [unmarked location](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places "Skyrim:Unmarked Places") containing a [shrine of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Talos "Skyrim:Shrines"), an unlocked chest, and a copy of the [Heavy Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Armor "Skyrim:Heavy Armor") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[2920, MidYear, v6](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_MidYear,_v6 "Skyrim:2920, MidYear, v6")*.
- Cradlecrush Rock [appears](/wiki/Online:Cradlecrush "Online:Cradlecrush") as a [fort](/wiki/Online:Forts "Online:Forts") in [ESO](/wiki/Online:Online "Online:Online").
