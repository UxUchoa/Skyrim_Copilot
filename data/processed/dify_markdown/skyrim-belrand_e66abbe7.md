Belrand

**Belrand** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [spellsword](/wiki/Skyrim:NPCclassBelrand "Skyrim:NPCclassBelrand") and [blade-for-hire](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") who can be found in the [Winking Skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Winking_Skeever "Skyrim:The Winking Skeever") in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). He charges a fee of 500 gold.

He wears a set of [iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Iron Armor"), a pair of [hide bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers "Skyrim:Hide Bracers"), and a pair of [hide boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"). He is equipped with an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"). He knows the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") spell [Oakflesh](/wiki/Skyrim:Oakflesh "Skyrim:Oakflesh"), the [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration") spell [Conjure Familiar](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Familiar "Skyrim:Conjure Familiar"), and the [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") spells [Fast Healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fast_Healing "Skyrim:Fast Healing") and [Lesser Ward](/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Ward "Skyrim:Lesser Ward").

Belrand can join the [Blades](/wiki/Skyrim:Blades "Skyrim:Blades"), and can also be a potential [marriage](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") partner.

## Dialogue

He will glowingly advertise his services with:

:   *"Versatility is the key to survival. Hire me, and you'll be well prepared to face threats both magical and mundane."*
:   *"I fight with both blade and spell. You might say I'm equipped to handle nearly any threat. So, shall we talk price?"*
:   *"Sure, you can hire a swordsman or a spellcaster to fight at your side, but why not get both for the price of one?"*
:   *"By blade or by spell, if you want it dead, I'll make it dead. All you need to do is pay my fee."*
:   *"The blade can be as lethal as the spell, but deadlier still is the one who wields both!"*

If you heed his endorsement and hire him, he will gleefully say, *"What dangers and wonders shall we discover in the frozen wastes? I can hardly wait to find out!"*

After being hired, Belrand will occasionally greet you with, *"You're looking hale and healthy. It's good to see you again."* or "*Didn't think I'd be seeing you again so soon."* Like other hirelings, he may offer his services free of charge after being dismissed, saying, *"Back for more? Since we had so much fun the last time, I'll join you free of charge."* If you accept, he will tell you, *"Lead the way."*

## Notes

- He wears a heavy cuirass, despite being proficient in light armor instead. In addition, despite having a high [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") skill and a perk for Destruction, he knows no Destruction spells.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch"), version 3.0.8, addresses this issue. He is now fully clad in hide armor, and has the ability to cast [Ice Spike](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Spike "Skyrim:Ice Spike").
- In the [Creation Kit](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit "Skyrim Mod:Creation Kit") data, there's an outfit named `BelrandOutfit`, which consists of a leveled piece of light armor boots, cuirass, and gauntlets of the best quality. However, it isn't in use.
