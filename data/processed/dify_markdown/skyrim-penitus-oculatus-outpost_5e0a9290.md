Penitus Oculatus Outpost

The **Penitus Oculatus Outpost** is the headquarters of the [Penitus Oculatus](/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus "Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus") order in Skyrim, located in [Dragon Bridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge "Skyrim:Dragon Bridge").

It consists of one zone, *[Penitus Oculatus Outpost](#Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost)*.

## Related Quests

- **[Breaching Security](/wiki/Skyrim:Breaching_Security "Skyrim:Breaching Security")**: Kill and frame the [son](/wiki/Skyrim:Gaius_Maro "Skyrim:Gaius Maro") of a high-ranking [Imperial guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro "Skyrim:Commander Maro").

## Penitus Oculatus Outpost

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-interior-Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.jpg)

Penitus Oculatus Outpost interior

Immediately to the left upon entering is a respawning food barrel next to a table holding four loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), four bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), four pieces of [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), two [chicken's eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg "Skyrim:Chicken's Egg"), one [sweet roll](/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll "Skyrim:Sweet Roll"), and two bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"). Above the table are several hanging ingredients, including three portions of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"), four portions of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear"), and three [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic") braids. To the right of the table is a woven basket containing eight [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato"), and a respawning meat barrel with one [potion of minor healing](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Healing") and two [potions of minor stamina](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina "Skyrim:Potion of Minor Stamina") resting atop it. Above the basket and barrel are two hanging racks, one of which holds three freshly caught [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") while the other holds two [pheasants](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant "Skyrim:Pheasant") and another rabbit.

In the northwest corner is a single bed with a small non-respawning chest at its foot. A basket containing three [pine thrush eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg "Skyrim:Pine Thrush Egg") sits on the floor to the right of the bed, along with another respawning food barrel holding three bottles of Nord mead on top. Another single bed with a small non-respawning chest at its foot is situated next to this food barrel, and a copy of the book *[The Code of Malacath](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Code_of_Malacath "Skyrim:The Code of Malacath")* is stashed beneath the bed. Next to this bed, directly across from the front door, is the fireplace, where four bottles of Nord mead and one bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine") are found on the mantel. The base of the fireplace holds a kettle containing five [pheasant breasts](/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast "Skyrim:Pheasant Breast") on the left, and a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking "Skyrim:Cooking") on the right. In front of the fireplace is a small table holding an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), five loose gold coins, a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), and three bottles of Nord mead. To the right of the fireplace is a non-respawning end table. A small non-respawning chest rests on the floor at the center of the east wall, flanked by a single bed on each side.

## Notes

- The porch of the outpost hosts five respawning food barrels, a respawning sack, a workbench, a tanning rack, and a table holding a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale") and a copy of a random common book, such as *[Palla, volume 1](/wiki/Skyrim:Palla,_volume_1 "Skyrim:Palla, volume 1")* or *[2920, Sun's Dawn, v2](/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Sun%27s_Dawn,_v2 "Skyrim:2920, Sun's Dawn, v2")*.
- All four beds here belong to the [PenitusOculatusFaction](/wiki/Skyrim:PenitusOculatusFaction "Skyrim:PenitusOculatusFaction").
- Dialogue between [Commander Maro](/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro "Skyrim:Commander Maro") and a Penitus Oculatus Agent during the quest [Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!") indicates that the outpost is supposed to be abandoned once the Emperor leaves Skyrim, though this never occurs in-game. (See [below](#Bugs) for a workaround)

## Bugs

- If the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") capture Solitude, this outpost will remain active in Dragon Bridge, which can result in Penitus Oculatus agents and [Stormcloak soldiers](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Soldier "Skyrim:Stormcloak Soldier") engaging each other in combat.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.6, addresses this issue. If [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") questline and the [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") questline for the Stormcloaks are finished, the outpost will be cleared out.
