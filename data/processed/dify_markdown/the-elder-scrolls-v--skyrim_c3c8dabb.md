***The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim***, the fifth game in the *[Elder Scrolls](/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls "General:The Elder Scrolls")* series, takes place in the province of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim") 200 years after the [Oblivion Crisis](/wiki/Lore:Oblivion_Crisis "Lore:Oblivion Crisis"). [Dragon Shouts](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts") play an integral role in the story as you discover your fate as the [Last Dragonborn](/wiki/Lore:Last_Dragonborn "Lore:Last Dragonborn"). *Skyrim* is available on PC, [Xbox 360, Xbox One](/wiki/Skyrim:Xbox "Skyrim:Xbox"), [PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4](/wiki/Skyrim:PlayStation "Skyrim:PlayStation"), and [Nintendo Switch](/wiki/Skyrim:Switch "Skyrim:Switch").

***[Skyrim Legendary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Legendary_Edition "Skyrim:Legendary Edition")*** is a bundled release of the game with the three major [add-ons](/wiki/Skyrim:Add-ons "Skyrim:Add-ons") for PC, Xbox 360, and PlayStation 3. ***[Skyrim Special Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Edition "Skyrim:Special Edition")*** is a remastered version of the game for PC, Xbox One, and PlayStation 4. It includes the three major add-ons, uses an upgraded engine with many visual improvements, and has several important technical differences from the classic version of the game. A virtual reality version, ***[Skyrim VR](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim_VR "Skyrim:Skyrim VR")***, is available on PlayStation VR and as a standalone game on PC. The Switch and VR releases are based on the Special Edition. The ***[Anniversary Edition](/wiki/Skyrim:Anniversary_Edition "Skyrim:Anniversary Edition")*** is a Special Edition bundle that includes all DLC and Creation Club content.

The ancient home of the [Nords](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord"), Skyrim, teeters at the brink of destruction. With the death of High King [Torygg](/wiki/Skyrim:High_King_Torygg "Skyrim:High King Torygg"), a [civil war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") breaks out, with brothers slaying brothers across the province's frozen tundras and rocky crags. Many, especially the rebel [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") led by [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak"), wish to secede from [the Empire](/wiki/Lore:Third_Empire "Lore:Third Empire"), which has been in decline since the Oblivion Crisis. Many loyalists, on the other hand, prefer the unity and security provided by the Empire.

The [Elder Scrolls](/wiki/Lore:Elder_Scrolls "Lore:Elder Scrolls") foretell the return of [Tamriel](/wiki/Lore:Tamriel "Lore:Tamriel")'s primeval darkness, the [Dragons](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon"), and the coming of a mortal born with the soul of a dragon, the [Dovahkiin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dovahkiin "Skyrim:Dovahkiin") or "Dragonborn"—one who will have the power to permanently defeat his greatest foe and end the dark reign of the consumer of worlds: [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin").

Your journey will be beset with peril. Just as remnants of the kingdom's shadow guardians, the [Blades](/wiki/Skyrim:Blades "Skyrim:Blades"), will offer their aid, crafty agents of the [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor"), seeking to advance their own cause, will impede your quest. Acquire knowledge and prove yourself Dragonborn, and the revered [Greybeards](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards") will grant you an audience to learn of their ancient Draconic arts.

Now great adventurer, free yourself from bondage; take up blade or spell, bow or axe; fulfill your destiny or postpone it indefinitely.
