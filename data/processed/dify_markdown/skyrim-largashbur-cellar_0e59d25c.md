**Largashbur Cellar** is a cellar located in the [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") stronghold of [Largashbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur "Skyrim:Largashbur") behind the [Largashbur Longhouse](/wiki/Skyrim:Largashbur_Longhouse "Skyrim:Largashbur Longhouse").

Down in the cellar

## Largashbur Cellar

Upon entering via the trapdoor, the chamber appears to be a natural cave rather than a normal cellar. There is a raised wooden platform to the northeast, with a mead barrel, a food barrel, a meat barrel, two chairs, and a small square wooden table holding a loaf of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"). To the southwest are three double-banked sets of shelves across the room, holding a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a small coin purse, three random [potions](/wiki/Skyrim:Potions "Skyrim:Potions"), a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), eight common books, a copy of the [Block](/wiki/Skyrim:Block "Skyrim:Block") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Battle of Red Mountain](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_of_Red_Mountain "Skyrim:Battle of Red Mountain")*, an [ingredient](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingredients "Skyrim:Ingredients") barrel, and twelve food sacks. Beyond the shelves is a counter running across the room, with a [goat cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wheel") on top, and a small coin purse, a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), two bottles of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), and six bottles of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead") underneath. Against the northwest wall is a novice-locked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing minor loot, with a round wooden table holding a [goat cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Goat Cheese Wedge") and two chairs to the right.
