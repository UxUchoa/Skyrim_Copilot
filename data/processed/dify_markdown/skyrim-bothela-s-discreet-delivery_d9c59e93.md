## Quick Walkthrough

1. Obtain the [potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Stallion%27s_Potion "Skyrim:Stallion's Potion") from [Bothela](/wiki/Skyrim:Bothela "Skyrim:Bothela").
2. Go to [Understone Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep "Skyrim:Understone Keep") (or the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace"), see below).
3. Deliver the potion to [Raerek](/wiki/Skyrim:Raerek "Skyrim:Raerek") or [Reburrus](/wiki/Skyrim:Reburrus_Quintilius "Skyrim:Reburrus Quintilius").

## Detailed Walkthrough

[Bothela](/wiki/Skyrim:Bothela "Skyrim:Bothela"), an elderly Breton alchemist, can be found in her shop, [The Hag's Cure](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Hag%27s_Cure "Skyrim:The Hag's Cure"), located in [Markarth](/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth "Skyrim:Markarth") just west of [Cidhna Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Cidhna_Mine "Skyrim:Cidhna Mine"). When asked about her shop, Bothela will say that the people of Markarth will often come to her, secretly, for cures. Bothela will go on to explain that she has [a potion](/wiki/Skyrim:Stallion%27s_Potion "Skyrim:Stallion's Potion") for the Jarl's steward, which she asks you to deliver.

*"Still, a little knowledge of plants and potions can get you by. Not that anyone likes to admit buying things from you. See? I even have a little potion for the Steward. Mind handing it over to him? Just say it should solve that problem he has."*

After accepting this task, the Stallion's Potion will be placed in your inventory. If declined, however, selecting the initiating dialogue will prompt the conversation again, giving you another opportunity to accept the quest.

The steward can be found in [Understone Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep "Skyrim:Understone Keep") (or the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace") if the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") control the Reach and the potion is for Raerek). Approaching and informing him of Bothela's delivery will prompt this line from him:

*"Oh... yes... um... thank you. Here you go. Some gold for your assistance and your, discretion."*

After speaking with him, the Stallion's Potion will be removed from your inventory and Raerek will reward you with a leveled amount of gold. Bothela does not give you a reward, unless a friendly disposition counts.

## Notes

- In most cases, Raerek will be the target of this quest. But it is possible, although highly unusual, to be directed to [Reburrus Quintilius](/wiki/Skyrim:Reburrus_Quintilius "Skyrim:Reburrus Quintilius") once he's the steward. In order to do so, however, you must have handed The Reach over to the [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloaks "Skyrim:Stormcloaks") during [Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending") without ever having entered Markarth or the immediate vicinity. If you've already entered the area around Markarth, Bothela will still refer to Raerek as the steward and expect you to deliver the potion to him. Note that [Compelling Tribute](/wiki/Skyrim:Compelling_Tribute_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Compelling Tribute (Stormcloaks)") forces you to enter Markarth to talk to Raerek, so Season Unending is the only way to get Reburrus.

## Bugs

- If spoken to during the quest, Reburrus' dialogue has no actual audio.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.6, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=15612)) A line variant for Reburrus didn't exist and has been spliced from other pre-existing lines.
- The Stallion's Potion is marked as stolen when it is in your inventory, which means if you get arrested and decide to pay off your bounty or go to jail, it will disappear from your inventory and the quest will get stuck unless you steal it back from the evidence chest in the hold's jail.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=17520))
  - For best results if you don't have the patch installed, make sure you don't have a bounty in the Reach and deliver the potion to Raerek as soon as you get it.
- Bothela still targets Raerek even if the quest is started only after the Stormcloaks take over.

  **Mod Notes**: The quest is started invisibly and aliases filled upon entering the Markarth region and the target never gets updated after that.
- You can complete the quest by giving the potion to [Reburrus](/wiki/Skyrim:Reburrus_Quintilius "Skyrim:Reburrus Quintilius"), even if he isn't the current steward and target of the quest.
- Completing this quest may allow you to freely take things from [Bryling's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Bryling%27s_House "Skyrim:Bryling's House"), [Erikur's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Erikur%27s_House "Skyrim:Erikur's House"), the [Blue Palace](/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace "Skyrim:Blue Palace"), [Temple of the Divines](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_the_Divines "Skyrim:Temple of the Divines"), and [Castle Dour Dungeon](/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour_Dungeon "Skyrim:Castle Dour Dungeon") due to improper ownership settings. **?**
