Einarth

**Einarth**, an [elderly](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder "Skyrim:Elder") [priest](/wiki/Skyrim:Priest "Skyrim:Priest"), is one of the [Greybeards](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards") living at [High Hrothgar](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar "Skyrim:High Hrothgar"). He will teach you the second [word](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_of_Power "Skyrim:Word of Power") of the Unrelenting Force shout. At the end of the quest [Alduin's Wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin%27s_Wall "Skyrim:Alduin's Wall"), Einarth can be heard lecturing [Arngeir](/wiki/Skyrim:Arngeir "Skyrim:Arngeir") when the latter refuses to help you in your quest to learn the [Dragonrend](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonrend "Skyrim:Dragonrend") shout, saying: *"Arngeir. Rok los Dovahkiin, Strundu'ul. Rok fen tinvaak Paarthurnax."*

Einarth wears a set of [Greybeard's robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Robes "Skyrim:Greybeard's Robes") with the matching [hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Hood "Skyrim:Greybeard's Hood") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Boots "Skyrim:Greybeard's Boots"). He carries a copy of the book *[Dwemer History and Culture](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_History_and_Culture "Skyrim:Dwemer History and Culture")*, a bottle of [Black-Briar Reserve](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Reserve "Skyrim:Black-Briar Reserve"), a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk"), one sample of [juniper berries](/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries "Skyrim:Juniper Berries"), and three [leather strips](/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips "Skyrim:Leather Strips").

He knows the following shouts: [Disarm](/wiki/Skyrim:Disarm "Skyrim:Disarm"), [Dismay](/wiki/Skyrim:Dismay "Skyrim:Dismay"), [Elemental Fury](/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Fury "Skyrim:Elemental Fury"), [Fire Breath](/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Breath "Skyrim:Fire Breath"), [Frost Breath](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Breath "Skyrim:Frost Breath"), [Ice Form](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Form "Skyrim:Ice Form"), [Unrelenting Force](/wiki/Skyrim:Unrelenting_Force "Skyrim:Unrelenting Force"), and [Whirlwind Sprint](/wiki/Skyrim:Whirlwind_Sprint "Skyrim:Whirlwind Sprint").
