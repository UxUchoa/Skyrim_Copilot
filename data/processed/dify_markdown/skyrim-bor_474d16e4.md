Bor

**Bor** is an [Orc](/wiki/Skyrim:Orc "Skyrim:Orc") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)") who lives and works in [Narzulbur](/wiki/Skyrim:Narzulbur "Skyrim:Narzulbur") at [Gloombound Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Gloombound_Mine "Skyrim:Gloombound Mine"). She spends her time mining the [ebony ore](/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Ore "Skyrim:Ebony Ore") there.

Like her fellow workers, Bor spends all her time inside Gloombound Mine, mining the ore inside. She may occasionally stop to relax, drink, or eat.

Bor wears a set of [ragged robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes "Skyrim:Ragged Robes") along with a matching pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Boots "Skyrim:Ragged Boots"). She wields a leveled [war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe "Skyrim:War Axe") which can be up to [Orcish](/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish "Skyrim:Orcish") in quality and carries a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe").
