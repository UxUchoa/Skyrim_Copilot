Pilgrim's Trench

**Pilgrim's Trench** is a deep underwater ravine northwest of [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") consisting of four submerged [shipwrecks](/wiki/Skyrim:Shipwrecks "Skyrim:Shipwrecks").

The trench is only marked as discovered if you dive down to the ships.

## Related Quests

- **[The Arms of Chaos](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:The Arms of Chaos")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Arms_of_Chaos "Skyrim:Arms of Chaos"): Complete a mage's quest to reassemble two powerful staves.

## Walkthrough

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/SR-place-Pilgrim%27s_Trench_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Pilgrim%27s_Trench_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-place-Pilgrim%27s_Trench_02.jpg)

The lone angler fish[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing")

The main ship (the one that isn't broken in two) has two chests containing minor loot. The first chest is just inside on the top deck beside the rest of the furniture. The second chest is on the lower deck along with five food barrels and a dresser and wardrobe both containing clothes.

There are no underwater predators at this location. [Salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(creature) "Skyrim:Salmon (creature)") swim in the waters. With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") [Creation](/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Club "Skyrim:Creation Club") installed, [arctic chars](/wiki/Skyrim:Arctic_Char "Skyrim:Arctic Char") also swim in the area, while a single [angler](/wiki/Skyrim:Angler "Skyrim:Angler") can be found beneath a rocky outcrop on the ocean floor. At least eleven [Nordic barnacles](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle "Skyrim:Nordic Barnacle") are nearby. Most are on the main ship, both inside and outside, with some on the ceilings inside.

Due to the depth, all races except [Argonians](/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian "Skyrim:Argonian") will need a [waterbreathing](/wiki/Skyrim:Waterbreathing_(effect) "Skyrim:Waterbreathing (effect)") effect to explore this location. Explorers should allow 60 seconds of waterbreathing for a quick dive and three minutes for a leisurely perusal of the site.

You may learn what happened to the ships if you [summon](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Names "Skyrim:Forgotten Names") [Velehk Sain](/wiki/Skyrim:Velehk_Sain "Skyrim:Velehk Sain") in [the Midden](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden "Skyrim:The Midden"). He offers to give you some items he obtained during his time as a pirate in [Mundus](/wiki/Lore:Mundus "Lore:Mundus") when he and his companions sank some Imperial ships. The treasure is magically hidden on an island southeast of the trench (see [notes](#Notes)).

## Notes

- South of Pilgrim's Trench is a small unmarked camp consisting of a sheltered bedroll, a campfire, and some containers. Sitting on a barrel is a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:For_Shelly "Skyrim:For Shelly") written by [Trius](/wiki/Skyrim:Trius "Skyrim:Trius"), the camp's absent founder. If you fast travel to Pilgrim's Trench, you will arrive at this camp. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=84016&y=109776&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- There are two [shrines of Talos](/wiki/Skyrim:Shrines#Shrine_of_Talos "Skyrim:Shrines") nearby, one to the southeast on an island, and the other to the south past the unmarked camp and east of [Hob's Fall Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Hob%27s_Fall_Cave "Skyrim:Hob's Fall Cave") on the mainland.
  - Behind the statue of Talos on the island are some skeletal remains with a random [battleaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe "Skyrim:Battleaxe"), an [apothecary's satchel](/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel "Skyrim:Apothecary's Satchel"), and a copy of the [One-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:One-handed "Skyrim:One-handed") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Importance of Where](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Importance_of_Where "Skyrim:The Importance of Where")*. If you released Velehk Sain after summoning him in the Midden, his hidden treasure may be found south of the shrine at the base of the rocks. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=89647&y=109961&zoom=17&legacy=true)
  - At the feet of the statue of Talos on the mainland are a random [greatsword](/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword "Skyrim:Greatsword"), a random [shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Shield "Skyrim:Shield"), a pair of random [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor"), a medium [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), a [potion of the defender](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Defender "Skyrim:Potion of the Defender"), and a copy of the [Two-handed](/wiki/Skyrim:Two-handed "Skyrim:Two-handed") skill book *[Words and Philosophy](/wiki/Skyrim:Words_and_Philosophy "Skyrim:Words and Philosophy")*.
