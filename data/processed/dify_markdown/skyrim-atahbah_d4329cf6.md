Atahbah outside of [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun")

**Atahbah** is a [Khajiit](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit "Skyrim:Khajiit") [pawnbroker](/wiki/Skyrim:Pawnbroker "Skyrim:Pawnbroker") in [Ri'saad](/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad "Skyrim:Ri'saad")'s [trade caravan](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit_Traders "Skyrim:Khajiit Traders"). She will become a [fence](/wiki/Skyrim:Fence_(merchant) "Skyrim:Fence (merchant)") for [Thieves Guild](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_(faction) "Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)") members after completing the quest [Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Caravan_Fence_Quest "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest").

Atahbah wears a set of merchant [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0004223c "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0004223d "Skyrim:Clothing"). She is equipped with a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger") and a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow"), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Related Quests

- **[Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Caravan_Fence_Quest "Skyrim:Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest")**: Deliver [moon sugar](/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar "Skyrim:Moon Sugar") to [Ri'saad](/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad "Skyrim:Ri'saad").

This is the merchandise found in Atahbah's [Merchant Chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant_Chest "Skyrim:Merchant Chest"). These items are only available if purchased from the merchant; they cannot be pickpocketed or stolen. They are restocked every two days.

## Notes

- Internally, all data refers to her as **Atahba**, which is also the name of a Khajiit previously [seen](/wiki/Oblivion:Atahba "Oblivion:Atahba") in *[Oblivion](/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion "Oblivion:Oblivion")*. The *[Prima Official Game Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide")* also refers to her as such.
- The guide also identifies her as Ri'saad's wife, although this is never stated in-game or in the files.
