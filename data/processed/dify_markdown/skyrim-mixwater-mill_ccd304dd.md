Mixwater Mill

**Mixwater Mill** is a [lumber mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Mills "Skyrim:Wood Mills") on the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River"), southwest of [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm") and southeast of [Gallows Rock](/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock "Skyrim:Gallows Rock").

[Gilfre](/wiki/Skyrim:Gilfre "Skyrim:Gilfre") has lived here alone since her five workers left to fight in the [civil war](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War").

### Mixwater Mill

Along with the sawmill itself is [Gilfre's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Gilfre%27s_House "Skyrim:Gilfre's House") and the now empty [Worker's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Worker%27s_House "Skyrim:Worker's House"). Gilfre's residence holds a copy of the [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[The Marksmanship Lesson](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Marksmanship_Lesson "Skyrim:The Marksmanship Lesson")* (for full details, see the dedicated [house page](/wiki/Skyrim:Gilfre%27s_House "Skyrim:Gilfre's House")). All items inside the worker's house can be freely taken (for full details, see the dedicated [house page](/wiki/Skyrim:Worker%27s_House "Skyrim:Worker's House")). The [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") is next to the sawmill, and the [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") is in front of the fireplace inside Gilfre's house.

The mill can serve as a useful free home if you marry Gilfre. However, the drying salmon and containers outside Gilfre's house do not become player-owned, even after marriage, and looting them will be considered stealing, though the [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") remains free to take. You can also freely sleep in the worker's house.

With [Hearthfire](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") installed, you can buy [sawn logs](/wiki/Skyrim:Sawn_Log "Skyrim:Sawn Log")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") by speaking to Gilfre. You can also cut your own logs for free if she has given you permission to do so or if you have married her. A [clay deposit](/wiki/Skyrim:Clay_Deposit "Skyrim:Clay Deposit")[HF](/wiki/Skyrim:Hearthfire "Skyrim:Hearthfire") can be found here as well.

With the [Fishing](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") creation installed, some [Fishing Supplies](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Supplies "Skyrim:Fishing Supplies") can be found by the water, along with a small box containing five [slaughterfish eggs](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Egg") and some [slaughterfish scales](/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales "Skyrim:Slaughterfish Scales").
