Evul Seloth

**Evul Seloth** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)") who lives in [Raven Rock Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Mine "Skyrim:Raven Rock Mine") after it is reopened. He wears [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"), and he carries a [steel dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), and a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

:   *"Good to know that the mine's safe again. I heard a lot of strange things happened here."*
:   *"Getting your hands dirty, outlander?"*

:   *"Nice to be making some coin again."*
:   *"Thanks to House Redoran getting these mines reopened, I can provide for my family again."*

## Notes

- He shares a surname with [Malur Seloth](/wiki/Skyrim:Malur_Seloth "Skyrim:Malur Seloth"), who lives in [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"), but there is no indication that they are related.
