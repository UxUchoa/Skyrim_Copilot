**Snow Elves** (also known as **Ancient Falmer** to distinguish them from their devolved modern counterparts) are a largely extinct race of mer that once inhabited the province of [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"). They resemble the golden-skinned [Altmer](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Elf "Skyrim:High Elf") in height and physiognomy though differ in being white-haired, pale, and have very long lifespans, as well as a strong resistance to frost. They worshipped [Auri-El](/wiki/Lore:Auri-El "Lore:Auri-El") and built the [Chantry of Auri-El](/wiki/Skyrim:Inner_Sanctum_(Vale) "Skyrim:Inner Sanctum (Vale)") and various wayshrines in the [Forgotten Vale](/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale "Skyrim:Forgotten Vale"). Following their defeat at the [Battle of the Moesring](/wiki/Lore:Battle_of_the_Moesring "Lore:Battle of the Moesring"), Snow Elves were the victims of a genocide by the Atmorans that left their numbers significantly low. Many took refuge with the [Dwemer](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer "Skyrim:Dwemer"), who in exchange for protection forced them to eat a toxic blinding fungus that turned them into the twisted and primal modern-day [Falmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer "Skyrim:Falmer"), known by surviving Snow Elves as "The Betrayed".

For more information, see the [main lore article](/wiki/Lore:Snow_Elf "Lore:Snow Elf").

## Snow Elves

There are a total of seven Snow Elves; only Gelebor is unaffected by a form of undeath, as Vyrthur is also a vampire and the prelates are all ghosts. They are all encountered in the [Dawnguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard "Skyrim:Dawnguard") [add-on](/wiki/Skyrim:Add-ons "Skyrim:Add-ons").

## Notes

- In the game files, every member is part of a newly-created race known as `SnowElfRace`. It is largely based off of the High Elf race, and even bears the name "High Elf" internally, but for all purposes all members of the race are to be considered Snow Elves.
  - All Snow Elves that can be met, are male. This appears to be a deliberate design decision, as there are no traces of female Snow Elf NPCs ever having been created. In fact, the racial traits of snow white skin and the unique albino white hair color aren't even set up to work with female NPCs, further suggesting they were never meant to exist.
- You cannot harvest [Arch-Curate Vyrthur](/wiki/Skyrim:Arch-Curate_Vyrthur "Skyrim:Arch-Curate Vyrthur")'s Snow Elf blood with the [Essence Extractor](/wiki/Skyrim:Essence_Extractor "Skyrim:Essence Extractor") for [Discerning the Trasmundane](/wiki/Skyrim:Discerning_the_Transmundane "Skyrim:Discerning the Transmundane").
- Wispmothers in Skyrim are believed to be the ghosts of Snow Elves. [The Pale Lady](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale_Lady_(creature) "Skyrim:The Pale Lady (creature)") is said to have been a foe of [Ysgramor](/wiki/Skyrim:Ysgramor "Skyrim:Ysgramor") named Aumriel.
