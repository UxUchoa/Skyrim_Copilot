Aesrael

Aesrael is a [High Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:High_Elf "Skyrim:High Elf") who is encountered at a camp west of [Sunderstone Gorge](/wiki/Skyrim:Sunderstone_Gorge "Skyrim:Sunderstone Gorge") during the quest [Once A Hunter](/wiki/Skyrim:Once_A_Hunter "Skyrim:Once A Hunter"). He grew up as an orphan and gave up his life as a notorious member of the [Crimson Dirks](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Dirks "Skyrim:Crimson Dirks") bandit gang, now living at a camp with his wolf [Dusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Dusk "Skyrim:Dusk"). He is hostile and will attack on sight.

Aesrael wears [Hide Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor "Skyrim:Hide Armor") and [Hide Boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots "Skyrim:Hide Boots"). He wields a leveled bow, carries 50 steel arrows, a random amount of [gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold "Skyrim:Gold"), [Aesrael's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Aesrael%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Aesrael's Journal") and [a key to his chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Aesrael%27s_Key "Skyrim:Aesrael's Key"), and has a 25% chance to carry a [lockpick](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"). The key is needed for the related quest to unlock the chest containing the [Elven Hunter Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Hunter_Armor "Skyrim:Elven Hunter Armor") and a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v7](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v7 "Skyrim:The Crimson Dirks, v7")*.

He is accompanied by Dusk, who will likely detect you before he does and attack.
