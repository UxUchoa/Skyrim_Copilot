Adeber

**Adeber** is a [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") [miner](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner_(class) "Skyrim:Miner (class)") who works at [Left Hand Mine](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine "Skyrim:Left Hand Mine"). He isn't really a talker because he is too busy digging out ore.

Adeber will spend all of his time working inside the mine, never taking a break to eat, sleep, or go outside. He wears a set of [miner's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697 "Skyrim:Miner's Clothes") and a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699 "Skyrim:Boots"). He carries around a [pickaxe](/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe "Skyrim:Pickaxe"), the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Key to Left Hand Miner's Barracks") to [Left Hand Miner's Barracks](/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks "Skyrim:Left Hand Miner's Barracks"), and a selection of [common items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

Attempting to speak to Adeber will have him say:

:   *"I'm one of miners. Plenty of ore to dig up around here."*
:   *"Don't have much to say to you. Got work."*
