Captain Aquilius

**Captain Aquilius** is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") who only appears during [Hard Answers](/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers "Skyrim:Hard Answers") as you leave [Calcelmo's Tower](/wiki/Skyrim:Calcelmo%27s_Tower "Skyrim:Calcelmo's Tower") after copying the tablet. If [Aicantar](/wiki/Skyrim:Aicantar "Skyrim:Aicantar") is still alive, he will instruct Aquilius to search the building.

He wears [steel armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Armor") if you are below level 18, and [steel plate armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Plate Armor") if you are above. He carries a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield") if you are below level 6 and a [steel shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield "Skyrim:Steel Shield") if you are above. In addition, he wields a leveled one-handed weapon and carries an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Calcelmo%27s_Laboratory_Key "Skyrim:Calcelmo's Laboratory Key") to [Calcelmo's Laboratory](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep#Calcelmo.27s_Laboratory "Skyrim:Understone Keep"), the [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Museum_Key "Skyrim:Dwemer Museum Key") to the [Dwemer Museum](/wiki/Skyrim:Understone_Keep#Dwemer_Museum "Skyrim:Understone Keep"), and leveled amount of gold.

### Hard Answers

When you make a rubbing of Calcelmo's Stone, Aicantar will burst into the room with his guards and Captain Aquilius:

:   **Aicantar**: *"Accidents like this just don't happen. Someone is trying to sabotage my uncle's research."*
:   **Aquilius**: *"I...all right. If there is a thief, he won't leave this tower alive. But shouldn't we inform Master Calcelmo?"*
:   **Aicantar**: *"I'll deal with my uncle. Just...go! Scour this place from top to bottom!"*
:   **Aquilius**: *"All right men, spread out and search the tower. No telling who - or what - we're dealing with here."*
