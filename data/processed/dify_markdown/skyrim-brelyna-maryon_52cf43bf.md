**Brelyna Maryon** is a [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") [mystic](/wiki/Skyrim:Mystic "Skyrim:Mystic") and a student at the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)"). She is most likely to be met during [Tolfdir](/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir "Skyrim:Tolfdir")'s [lecture](/wiki/Skyrim:First_Lessons "Skyrim:First Lessons"). Afterwards, she is found in the [Hall of Attainment](/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Attainment "Skyrim:Hall of Attainment"), sleeping from 10pm to 6am and studying there for the rest of the day.

Unlike her fellow students, [Onmund](/wiki/Skyrim:Onmund "Skyrim:Onmund") and [J'zargo](/wiki/Skyrim:J%27zargo "Skyrim:J'zargo"), Brelyna tends to cast summoning spells and wards in combat. Because of this, she may be very useful if you seek a follower who will provide defenses and distractions. She is most skilled in [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration"), [Illusion](/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion "Skyrim:Illusion"), [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration"), [Sneak](/wiki/Skyrim:Sneak "Skyrim:Sneak"), and [Restoration](/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration "Skyrim:Restoration") in that order. She does not know any Illusion spells, however, and often uses [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") spells as well as bows, despite having minimal [Archery](/wiki/Skyrim:Archery "Skyrim:Archery") and Destruction skills.

Her default clothing is an [apprentice hood](/wiki/Skyrim:Apprentice_Hood "Skyrim:Apprentice Hood"), [apprentice robes of destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Apprentice_Robes_of_Destruction "Skyrim:Apprentice Robes of Destruction"), and [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#0006b46c "Skyrim:Clothing"). She also carries a [staff of lightning bolts](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Lightning_Bolts "Skyrim:Staff of Lightning Bolts") and a copy of the book *[Gods and Worship](/wiki/Skyrim:Gods_and_Worship "Skyrim:Gods and Worship")*.

After joining the College and completing Under Saarthal, Brelyna will [request](/wiki/Skyrim:Brelyna%27s_Practice "Skyrim:Brelyna's Practice") to test spells on you. If you agree and complete this minor quest, she will be available as a [follower](/wiki/Skyrim:Followers "Skyrim:Followers") and you can [marry](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") her. She is also a potential [Blades](/wiki/Skyrim:Blades "Skyrim:Blades") [candidate](/wiki/Skyrim:Rebuilding_the_Blades "Skyrim:Rebuilding the Blades"). If you steal from the College while she is a follower, she will tolerate it at first, but eventually attack you if you do so too many times.

## Dialogue

Attempting conversation with Brelyna during the lecture will fail, as she will tell you:

:   *"I'm trying to pay attention."*

When the class is over, you may now properly talk to her, though she will already explain everything to you:

:   *"Before you even ask, yes I have an ancestry steeped in magic, and no I don't want to talk about it. Yes, I know [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold") used to be full of my kind, and no I don't care that they're all gone now. Does that cover everything?"*

    :   **We haven't really been introduced.**
    :   *"No, I suppose we haven't. Brelyna Maryon, of [House Telvanni](/wiki/Lore:House_Telvanni "Lore:House Telvanni"). First of my family to leave [Morrowind](/wiki/Lore:Morrowind "Lore:Morrowind") in a long time. Now I'm here to study [Conjuration](/wiki/Skyrim:Conjuration "Skyrim:Conjuration"). That's what's important."*

:   :   **I'm sorry, what?**
    :   *"I've just been asked enough questions. I'm here to learn, not to explain myself over and over."* and promptly apologize, *"Forgive me, I didn't mean to assume."*

Brelyna may greet you with:

:   *"You know, this is one of a very few places where you can receive a real education in the schools of magic."*
:   *"I think my parents reserved a spot for me here at the College on the day I was born."*
:   *"There's a lot of pressure for me to excel in my studies. I hope I can live up to expectations."*

After the [excavation](/wiki/Skyrim:Under_Saarthal "Skyrim:Under Saarthal") at [Saarthal](/wiki/Skyrim:Saarthal "Skyrim:Saarthal"), Brelyna may comment on the [dangers](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr") of exploring Nordic ruins:

:   *"At least something good came out of that trip to Saarthal. I didn't find anything but dust."*

If you are Arch-Mage, Brelyna will surprisingly offer you a backhanded compliment:

:   *"I still can't believe it. You, the Arch-Mage? Never would have guessed it."*

As Brelyna is from House Telvanni, known for its skill and dedication in magic, the pressure to succeed from her family results in her lack of confidence in her skills. You may ask her if she likes it here at the College, in which case she will agree:

:   **Do you like it here at the College?**
:   *"So far, if only because no one is telling me how great I ought to be. I just want to learn, I don't want to think about what's expected of me."*

If asked, Brelyna will explain that Winterhold used to be full of Dark Elves, along with the reason they left:

:   **Winterhold used to be full of dark elves?**
:   *"Didn't you know? Before the Oblivion Crisis, many elves called Winterhold their home. More visited the College from Morrowind every year. After, growing distrust of magic made life difficult for many. Some left rather than endure the growing hatred from the local Nords. Others returned home after the [Red Year](/wiki/Lore:Red_Year "Lore:Red Year"), when [Vvardenfell](/wiki/Lore:Vvardenfell "Lore:Vvardenfell") erupted and caused much destruction. Winterhold itself died in the years between then and now. What's left out there is a husk. Only the College really remains."*

## Quest-Related Events

Should you ask her, Brelyna will tell you what she knows about [Ancano](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancano "Skyrim:Ancano"), [Arch-Mage Aren](/wiki/Skyrim:Savos_Aren "Skyrim:Savos Aren")'s advisor:

:   *"I know I don't like the way he looks at me. I can't tell if he expects me to blow myself up, or to try and murder him. But he clearly doesn't trust any of us."*

After you [meet](/wiki/Skyrim:Good_Intentions "Skyrim:Good Intentions") with [Quaranir](/wiki/Skyrim:Quaranir "Skyrim:Quaranir"), a [Psijic](/wiki/Skyrim:Psijic_Order "Skyrim:Psijic Order") monk, Brelyna will remark on it:

:   *"Is it true? One of the Psijics was here in the College?"*

You may inquire about the [Augur of Dunlain](/wiki/Skyrim:Augur_of_Dunlain "Skyrim:Augur of Dunlain"), which will be replied with:

:   *"Huh? That sounds ominous. Are you getting yourself into even more trouble? Did you ask [Mirabelle](/wiki/Skyrim:Mirabelle_Ervine "Skyrim:Mirabelle Ervine")? She runs things around here, after all."*

During [Revealing the Unseen](/wiki/Skyrim:Revealing_the_Unseen "Skyrim:Revealing the Unseen"), you will discover the [location](/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian "Skyrim:Labyrinthian") of the [Staff of Magnus](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Magnus "Skyrim:Staff of Magnus"), but Brelyna will show skepticism:

:   *"So you're off to find some staff now? I don't see the point of that."*

As Ancano releases the power of the Eye of Magnus, she will exclaim:

:   *"By [Azura](/wiki/Lore:Azura "Lore:Azura"), what's happening?"*

You will then be tasked to [retrieve the Staff](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Staff_of_Magnus "Skyrim:The Staff of Magnus"), and Brelyna will still not be convinced of the Staff's importance, saying either :

:   *"You really think the Staff of Magnus is the key?"*
:   *"How long can we keep this up?"*

When you set out to [defeat](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Eye_of_Magnus "Skyrim:The Eye of Magnus") Ancano, Brelyna will understandably be shocked over current events:

:   *"I can't believe any of this is happening!"*

### Brelyna's Practice

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-quest-Brelyna%27s_Practice_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Brelyna%27s_Practice_05.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-quest-Brelyna%27s_Practice_05.jpg)

*"We're all trying to become better mages, right?"*

Back in the College, Brelyna has been working on a spell and needs someone to test it on: you. She will ask for your help:

:   *"If you have a moment, could you help me with something?"* or if you have refused to help before, she will tell you,
:   *"I still need some help, if you can spare the time."*

:   **What do you need help with?**
:   *"We're all trying to become better mages, right? Well, I need someone I can practice a few spells on. Nothing dangerous, really. Would you be willing to assist me?"*

:   :   **I don't think so.**
    :   *"Oh. Well, if you change your mind, let me know."*

:   :   **What's in it for me?**
    :   *"Well, I... I simply thought you might wish to help out one of your fellow mages. If that's not enough, I suppose... I have this amulet you could take."*

:   :   **Can't you practice on yourself?**

*"No, not that kind of spell. Believe me when I say I wouldn't be asking if it weren't necessary."*

:   :   **Okay, I'll help.**
    :   *"Oh, good. I was afraid I'd have to go ask J'zargo. Now, you just stand there..."*

As she prepares to cast the spell, she will tell you:

:   *"Please hold still, and don't move."*

She will cast a flawed spell that causes your vision to become greenish, and exclaim:

:   *"There! Now, I..."*

She will then realize what she has done:

:   *"Oh dear. That wasn't supposed to happen."*

She will ask you worriedly,

:   *"Do you... do you feel all right? You look very, umm, green."*

Brelyna will then apologize,

:   *"I am so very sorry. I went over this again and again, and I was sure it would have better results."*

:   :   **Tell me this isn't permanent.**
    :   *"It's not! It shouldn't be. You'll be back to normal in no time. And when you are, we can try this again and I'll be sure to get it right."*

:   :   **What did you do?**
    :   *"I'm not really sure. I think it's just a minor miscalculation on my part. I'm sure it will wear off soon. When it does, you'll be perfectly fine, and then we can try again. In the meantime, I'll see about figuring out what went wrong."*

After her spell wears off, you can report back.

:   **Your spell finally wore off.**
:   *"Exactly as I said it would. Now, are you ready to try again? I am confident it will work this time."*

:   :   **Okay, you can try again.**
    :   *"This will be worth it."*

:   :   **One more chance. That's it.**
    :   *"Good, good. It'll be worth it, I assure you."*

:   :   **I've had enough for now.**
    :   *"Well I really would like to make sure I've corrected what happened last time. Please give me a chance to try again."*

Brelyna will then order you:

:   *"Okay, now don't move at all."*

As she readies her spell. You will be turned into a cow, which will have her exclaim:

:   *"Oh my. That isn't right! Just wait. Just... I can fix this."*

She will cast another spell and instead transform you into a horse:

:   *"Oh no, that's not it at all. Let me try again..."*

The next time you will turn into a dog:

:   *"This really isn't turning out the way I'd hoped."*

Brelyna will obviously be embarrassed over this as she tries to assure you,:
*"I'll get it right this time, I swear."*

She will then return you to normal.

:   *"There, all better."*

When she finishes experimenting on you, Brelyna will say:

:   **So...**
:   *"Well, it all worked out in the end, didn't it?"*

:   :   **Let's never speak of this again.**
    :   *"Agreed. And thank you for your help. You're a good friend."*

:   :   **It's been a pleasure.**
    :   *"Oh, no it hasn't. Let's be honest. I have a long way to go before I'm ready for the kind of advanced magic I've been attempting. But at least I know where to start now. Thank you, you've been a great help to me."*

## Notes

- If you decide to marry Brelyna, her dialogue is the same as if she were at the College of Winterhold no matter where she is, until you finish The Eye of Magnus. She will still open a store and will give you 100 gold per day.
  - If she moves to your house, dismissing her when she is a follower will have her reply with, *"You'll be all right on your own? Then I'll see you back at the College."* after The Eye of Magnus.
