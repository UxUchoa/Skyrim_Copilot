**Dragon Cultists** are the restless spirits of members of the ancient [Dragon Cult](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Cult "Skyrim:Dragon Cult") who chose to take their own lives rather than fall to King Harald's army. They only appear to attack invaders in [Forelhost](/wiki/Skyrim:Forelhost "Skyrim:Forelhost").

Dragon Cultists make use of [ancient Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord "Skyrim:Ancient Nord") weapons and armor, and may also carry a [dragon priest dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Priest_Dagger "Skyrim:Dragon Priest Dagger"). Some Cultists are capable of using [Destruction](/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction "Skyrim:Destruction") magic.

## Related Quests

- **[Siege on the Dragon Cult](/wiki/Skyrim:Siege_on_the_Dragon_Cult "Skyrim:Siege on the Dragon Cult")**: Enter the ruins of [Forelhost](/wiki/Skyrim:Forelhost "Skyrim:Forelhost") and defeat [Rahgot](/wiki/Skyrim:Rahgot_(creature) "Skyrim:Rahgot (creature)") to recover his mask for [Captain Valmir](/wiki/Skyrim:Captain_Valmir "Skyrim:Captain Valmir").

## Notes

- Dragon Cultists make the sounds of [Draugr](/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr "Skyrim:Draugr"), even though normally ghosts don't show that behavior.
