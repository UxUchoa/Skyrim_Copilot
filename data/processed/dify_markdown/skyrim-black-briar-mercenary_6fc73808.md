Four **Black-Briar Mercenaries** are present inside the [Black-Briar Lodge](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Lodge "Skyrim:Black-Briar Lodge"). They wear [steel plate armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor "Skyrim:Steel Plate Armor"), leveled [light boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Boots "Skyrim:Armor"), and have a 50% chance of wearing leveled [light bracers](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#Gauntlets "Skyrim:Armor"). These four carry 25-125 gold, a [torch](/wiki/Skyrim:Torch "Skyrim:Torch"), a [key](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Lodge_Key "Skyrim:Black-Briar Lodge Key") to the lodge, and a leveled [weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:Weapons "Skyrim:Weapons").
