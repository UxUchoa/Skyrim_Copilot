River Yorgrim

The **River Yorgrim** is a river flowing from [Lake Yorgrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Yorgrim "Skyrim:Lake Yorgrim") to [Windhelm](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm "Skyrim:Windhelm").

[Anga's Mill](/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill "Skyrim:Anga's Mill") is located along the river. A bridge is located near the mill, and is the only crossing point on the river. It joins the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River") at Windhelm.
