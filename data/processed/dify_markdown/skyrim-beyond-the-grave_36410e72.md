## Quick Walkthrough

1. Visit Edward's Grave.
2. Read the Khajiit's Note.
3. Investigate Knifepoint Ridge.
4. Retrieve the armor.

## Detailed Walkthrough

You can begin this quest by going into the [Dragonsreach Dungeon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach#Dragonsreach_Dungeon "Skyrim:Dragonsreach") in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). On a table just within the jail area find a journal called *[Death of a Crimson Dirk](/wiki/Skyrim:Death_of_a_Crimson_Dirk "Skyrim:Death of a Crimson Dirk")*. Following the directions given in the journal, travel to [Falkreath](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath "Skyrim:Falkreath") in search of the grave of Edward. Your search can be slightly accelerated by looking for the dug up grave with a dead Khajiit nearby. Retrieve the *[Khajiit's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Khajiit%27s_Note "Skyrim:Khajiit's Note")* from his corpse to determine your next destination, [Knifepoint Ridge](/wiki/Skyrim:Knifepoint_Ridge "Skyrim:Knifepoint Ridge"). The local bandits of the exterior camp will be hostile to you, and will protect your final target for this quest, the "bounty hunter" figure wearing the [Daedric Plate Armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Plate_Armor "Skyrim:Daedric Plate Armor") mentioned in the note. Defeat him and any bandits that get in your way to retrieve the set. Read the *[Bounty Hunter's Note](/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty_Hunter%27s_Note "Skyrim:Bounty Hunter's Note")* you can find on the body for confirmation of Edward's scheme.
