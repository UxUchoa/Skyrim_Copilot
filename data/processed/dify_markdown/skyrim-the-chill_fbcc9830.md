**The Chill** is the [jail](/wiki/Skyrim:Jails "Skyrim:Jails") for [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold "Skyrim:Winterhold"), located on an island north-northeast of the [College of Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:College_of_Winterhold_(place) "Skyrim:College of Winterhold (place)") and northeast of [Septimus Signus's Outpost](/wiki/Skyrim:Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost "Skyrim:Septimus Signus's Outpost"), across the sea.

If you are arrested by a [Winterhold guard](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_Guard "Skyrim:Winterhold Guard") and sent to jail, you will be imprisoned here.
The location of The Chill [is not marked on the map](/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places "Skyrim:Unmarked Places"); consequently, it cannot be selected as a destination for fast travel. There is only one zone, *[The Chill](#The_Chill)*.

### Exterior

The Chill is located on a mineral-rich island, with the [ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Mining "Skyrim:Mining") listed below. The island is inhabited by several leveled [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") in addition to a few [horkers](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker "Skyrim:Horker"), which can include [snow bears](/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear "Skyrim:Snow Bear"), [snowy sabre cats](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowy_Sabre_Cat "Skyrim:Snowy Sabre Cat"), and [ice wolves](/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf "Skyrim:Ice Wolf"). One of the animals is normally found near the corundum ore vein to the northwest, and another is normally found near the iron ore veins to the northeast. The leveled animals and horkers may end up fighting each other, resulting in the victors being found injured near the corpses of the defeated.

- Two [gold ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Gold Ore Vein") among the rocks above The Chill (see [bugs](#Bugs)).
- One [silver ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Silver Ore Vein") to the southeast.
- Four [iron ore veins](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Iron Ore Vein"): three to the northeast, and one on the far southern tip.
- One [corundum ore vein](/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein "Skyrim:Corundum Ore Vein") to the northwest.

### The Chill

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/SR-interior-The_Chill.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Chill.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Chill.jpg)

Interior

The interior of the cave is guarded by a [frost atronach](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Atronach "Skyrim:Frost Atronach"). There are three cages with adept-level locks; each contains a bedroll. A further three small empty cages hang from the ceiling. There are lootable skeletal remains in two of the locked cages. The third cage is the one you are sent to, and has a lit lantern on the floor. There are also ten [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") barrels in the cave.

If you are sent here, you will keep all of your equipment, including stolen items, making escaping the jail the easiest in Skyrim. Upon entering The Chill, regardless of whether you were jailed or entered of your own free will, the frost atronach inside and three others outside will all spawn. Upon exiting, the three aggressive frost atronachs outside will attack you on sight.

## Notes

- A map marker for The Chill was added by the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim:USKP "Skyrim:USKP"), version 1.3.1.
- When arrested in Winterhold, if you choose to go to jail, the [guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_Guard "Skyrim:Winterhold Guard") will respond with *"Ha, enjoy the Chill."*
- If you escape from The Chill, you do not incur the 100 gold bounty that you would normally receive from escaping jails in other holds.
- All the containers in The Chill (which are only barrels) are [respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning"), and therefore unsafe to store items in.
- If entering The Chill by walking to the entrance without a bounty instead of being sent to jail, the Frost Atronachs will still be hostile.
