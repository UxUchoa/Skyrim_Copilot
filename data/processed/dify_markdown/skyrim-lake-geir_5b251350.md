Lake Geir

**Lake Geir** is a lake in the western [Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift").

[Ivarstead](/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead "Skyrim:Ivarstead") is located on the shores of Lake Geir. In the center of the lake there is an island which is home to [Geirmund's Hall](/wiki/Skyrim:Geirmund%27s_Hall "Skyrim:Geirmund's Hall"). [Catfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Catfish "Skyrim:Catfish")[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing "Skyrim:Fishing") can be found swimming in the waters here.

Geographically, the lake is fed by the [Treva River](/wiki/Skyrim:Treva_River "Skyrim:Treva River") and is drained by [Darkwater River](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_River "Skyrim:Darkwater River") (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).

## Notes

- On the eastern shore is a row boat containing two sacks beached opposite a small island with a tree stump. On the island there are two [treasure hunters](/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Hunter_(bandit) "Skyrim:Treasure Hunter (bandit)") who will attack upon seeing you. They have dug up an unlocked chest containing minor loot. You will find two [white cap](/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap "Skyrim:White Cap") and a [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") that can be harvested on the island. Further inland is their camp including a small tent, two bedrolls, an open, empty chest, four sacks and three barrels, one of which has on it a bottle of [Honningbrew mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Mead "Skyrim:Honningbrew Mead"), a small [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse"), and a [note](/wiki/Skyrim:Note_(treasure) "Skyrim:Note (treasure)") directing you to the treasure on the island. There are also three rabbits hanging beside the tent that can be harvested. There is also a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe") next to where the rabbits are hanging.
- The name "Lake Geir" is sourced from the [Skyrim Prima Game Guide](/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide "Books:The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide").
