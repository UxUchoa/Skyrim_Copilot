Fishing Legend

## Quick Walkthrough

1. Speak to [Swims-In-Deep-Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water "Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water").
2. Read the [List of Rare Fish](/wiki/Skyrim:List_of_Rare_Fish "Skyrim:List of Rare Fish").
3. Obtain an Angelfish.
4. Obtain an Angler.
5. Obtain a Lyretail Anthias.
6. Obtain a Scorpion Fish.
7. Return to Swims-In-Deep-Water.

## Detailed Walkthrough

Speak to [Swims-In-Deep-Water](/wiki/Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water "Skyrim:Swims-In-Deep-Water"):

:   **I'm ready for the next fishing challenge.**
:   *"Of course. It's all in this note."*
:   **I'll return with these rare catches soon.** / **This looks like it'll be a real challenge. I'll do my best.**
:   *"My thanks to you."*

He will then hand you a [list of rare fish](/wiki/Skyrim:List_of_Rare_Fish "Skyrim:List of Rare Fish") to catch

- [Angelfish](/wiki/Skyrim:Angelfish "Skyrim:Angelfish") - Angelfish are rare and can only be caught when fishing with an Alik'r Fishing Rod in temperate lakes. Two guaranteed Angelfish can be found in a pool within [Ancestor Glade](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestor_Glade "Skyrim:Ancestor Glade") and can be caught without a fishing rod.
- [Angler](/wiki/Skyrim:Angler "Skyrim:Angler") - Can only be caught in freezing waters while using the Argonian Fishing Rod. A lone Angler can be found at [Pilgrim's Trench](/wiki/Skyrim:Pilgrim%27s_Trench "Skyrim:Pilgrim's Trench") in a small rocky cove on the ocean floor (there are clams to the left and right of the mouth of the cove, and more on top of it), just to the west of the sunken ships, no fishing necessary. This is found in the Food section of Items.
- [Lyretail Anthias](/wiki/Skyrim:Lyretail_Anthias "Skyrim:Lyretail Anthias")- Can only be caught in temperate streams while using an Alik'r fishing rod. Three guaranteed Lyretails can be found in the waters of [Evergreen Grove](/wiki/Skyrim:Evergreen_Grove "Skyrim:Evergreen Grove").
- [Scorpion Fish](/wiki/Skyrim:Scorpion_Fish "Skyrim:Scorpion Fish") - Can only be caught in underground fishing spots while using the Argonian Fishing Rod. They can also be found in [Darkfall Cave](/wiki/Skyrim:Darkfall_Cave "Skyrim:Darkfall Cave"). This is found in the Food section of Items.

Return to Swims:

:   **It wasn't easy, but here are the fish you asked for.**
:   *"You are like my brood to me. Here. A token."*
:   **Are there any more fishing challenges you can give me?**
:   *"You've already learned all I can teach you."*

He will then reward you with the [Fang of Haynekhtnamet](/wiki/Skyrim:Fang_of_Haynekhtnamet "Skyrim:Fang of Haynekhtnamet").

## Bugs

Sometimes the fish you can pick up (not caught by fishing) mentioned above aren't there. If this happens, you can zone and zone back to get them to spawn. (For Ancestor Glade, leave the cave and zone back in. For Pilgrim's Trench, zone into nearby Ysgramor's Tomb and back out.)
