# Skyrim:Unique Items

## Overview

**Unique Items** are items available in Skyrim generally found in fixed locations, unlike most equipment in the game, which are randomly generated or generic equipment (see [Generic Magic Apparel](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Apparel "Skyrim:Generic Magic Apparel") and [Generic Magic Weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Generic_Magic_Weapons "Skyrim:Generic Magic Weapons")). The various types of items within the scope of this field are further divided into separate groupings for [unique weapons](/wiki/Skyrim:Unique_Weapons "Skyrim:Unique Weapons"), [unique armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Unique_Armor "Skyrim:Unique Armor"), [unique clothing](/wiki/Skyrim:Unique_Clothing "Skyrim:Unique Clothing"), [unique jewelry](/wiki/Skyrim:Unique_Jewelry "Skyrim:Unique Jewelry"), and a final group for all [other unique items](/wiki/Skyrim:Other_Unique_Items "Skyrim:Other Unique Items"). Some of these items can be improved through [smithing](/wiki/Skyrim:Smithing "Skyrim:Smithing"). Improving enchanted items requires the [Arcane Blacksmith](/wiki/Skyrim:Arcane_Blacksmith "Skyrim:Arcane Blacksmith") perk.

Many of these items are quest rewards, although some of the most valuable quest rewards are listed at [Leveled Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Items "Skyrim:Leveled Items") (unique items, but with level-dependent statistics) and [Artifacts](/wiki/Skyrim:Artifacts "Skyrim:Artifacts") (unusually powerful unique items with unique appearances and enchantments). For unique items that are needed to complete quests, please see the [Quest Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items "Skyrim:Quest Items") article. For other unique items in the game, see the [Other Unique Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Other_Unique_Items "Skyrim:Other Unique Items") article. Unique items that are quest-related are listed at [Quest Items](/wiki/Skyrim:Quest_Items "Skyrim:Quest Items"). For lists of other available items, see:

- [Skyrim-Armor](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Armor "Category:Skyrim-Armor"): Category listing of all the armor available in the game.
- [Skyrim-Clothing](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Clothing "Category:Skyrim-Clothing"): Category listing of all the clothing available in the game.
- [Skyrim-Jewelry](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Jewelry "Category:Skyrim-Jewelry"): Category listing of all the jewelry available in the game.
- [Skyrim-Weapons](/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Weapons "Category:Skyrim-Weapons"): Category listing of all the weapons available in the game.
