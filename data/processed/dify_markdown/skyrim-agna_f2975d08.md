**Agna** is a dead [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_(class) "Skyrim:Bandit (class)") and [Golldir](/wiki/Skyrim:Golldir "Skyrim:Golldir")'s aunt, whom you help look for if you accept the side quest he offers, [Ancestral Worship](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestral_Worship "Skyrim:Ancestral Worship"), outside [Hillgrund's Tomb](/wiki/Skyrim:Hillgrund%27s_Tomb "Skyrim:Hillgrund's Tomb"). She went into the tomb in attempt to stop the necromancer [Vals Veran](/wiki/Skyrim:Vals_Veran "Skyrim:Vals Veran") from defiling her ancestors, but met her untimely end before reaching him.

She wears a leveled [light cuirass](/wiki/Skyrim:Armor#light "Skyrim:Armor") with light boots, and may also wear light gauntlets and/or a light shield. She would have wielded a leveled [one-handed weapon](/wiki/Skyrim:One-Handed_Weapons "Skyrim:One-Handed Weapons") and carries a random selection of [beverages](/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Beverages "Skyrim:Food"), [food](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food"), [lockpicks](/wiki/Skyrim:Lockpick "Skyrim:Lockpick"), [jewelry](/wiki/Skyrim:Jewelry "Skyrim:Jewelry"), [gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Gems "Skyrim:Gems"), [ingots](/wiki/Skyrim:Ingots "Skyrim:Ingots"), tools, and gold.
