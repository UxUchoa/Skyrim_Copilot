Casival is a dead [Dark Elf](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elf "Skyrim:Dark Elf") and former [Crimson Dirk](/wiki/Skyrim:Crimson_Dirks "Skyrim:Crimson Dirks") who is encountered during the quest [Mightier than the Sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Mightier_than_the_Sword "Skyrim:Mightier than the Sword").

Casival wears a set of farmer [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothing"), including a pair of [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261bd "Skyrim:Clothing"), and carries a [Steel Dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger "Skyrim:Steel Dagger"), a random necklace, and a random selection of loot.
