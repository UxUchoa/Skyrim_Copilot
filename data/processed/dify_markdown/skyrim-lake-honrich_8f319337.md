Lake Honrich

**Lake Honrich** is a lake in the eastern [Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"), immediately southwest of [Riften](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften "Skyrim:Riften"). It is drained by the [Treva River](/wiki/Skyrim:Treva_River "Skyrim:Treva River") and was named Lake Honnith in the [First Era](/wiki/Lore:First_Era "Lore:First Era"). [Goldenglow Estate](/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenglow_Estate "Skyrim:Goldenglow Estate") occupies a cluster of islands in the middle of the lake.

Lake Honrich is a popular spot for fishermen across Skyrim, and it even has a [fishery](/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Fishery "Skyrim:Riften Fishery") found on its eastern shores in Riften.
