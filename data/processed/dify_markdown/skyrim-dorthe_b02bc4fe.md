Dorthe

**Dorthe** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child") who lives in [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"). [Sigrid](/wiki/Skyrim:Sigrid "Skyrim:Sigrid") and [Alvor](/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor "Skyrim:Alvor") are her mother and father, and she is a cousin of [Hadvar](/wiki/Skyrim:Hadvar "Skyrim:Hadvar"). She is friends with [Frodnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Frodnar "Skyrim:Frodnar"), even though their families support opposing sides in the current civil war. While keeping up appearances, the family is split in two: Her father wanted a son and obviously raises Dorthe like one, while Sigrid is overly concerned about her daughter's upbringing and exaggerates her attempts to make her act like a little girl.

Despite her mother's reservations about her boyish behavior, Dorthe can't be bothered and goes her own ways. After waking up at 6am she takes a quick one-hour breakfast, then leaves the [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor_and_Sigrid%27s_House "Skyrim:Alvor and Sigrid's House") to play with her good friend [Frodnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Frodnar "Skyrim:Frodnar") and his dog [Stump](/wiki/Skyrim:Stump "Skyrim:Stump"). At 4pm she joins her father at his forge and acts as his 'apprentice' until they both go into the house for dinner at 8pm. After listening to her mother's complaints for four hours, she finally goes to bed at midnight. The only exception to this routine occurs when you first enter Riverwood and only if you escaped from [Helgen Keep](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen_Keep "Skyrim:Helgen Keep") with her cousin Hadvar. She will then stay inside the house until you have spoken with Alvor. At the end of the discussion, she will insistently ask Hadvar: *"Hadvar, did you really see a dragon? What did it look like? Did it have big teeth?"*

She wears a set of [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Child's Clothes") and [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000e80a8 "Skyrim:Clothing") and carries a copy of the [house key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Alvor%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Alvor's House") along with a selection of [food and toys](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Conversations

Her daily life involves fooling around with local prankster [Frodnar](/wiki/Skyrim:Frodnar "Skyrim:Frodnar") although she can be heard telling him off for some really bad suggestions:

**Dorthe**: *"Wait, so... a costume? You're not serious..."*
**Frodnar**: *"Sure I am. We get some furs and tree branches, paint them white, and then tie them to Stump. Instant [Frostbite Spider](/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Spider "Skyrim:Frostbite Spider")!"*
**Dorthe**: *"Frodnar... nobody is going to believe that your dog is a... Frostbite Spider. And if they do... they'll kill him. That's not much of a prank."*
**Frodnar**: *"Oh yeah, and what would you rather do?"*
**Dorthe**: *"How about... we play tag! You're it!"*
**Frodnar**: *"Hey, no fair! Get her, Stump!"*

## Notes

- If you escaped Helgen with Hadvar, he will be flagged as a parent for Dorthe if Alvor and Sigrid are killed.

## Bugs

- Dorthe's bed isn't a child's bed, allowing her to sleep in the parents' bed, causing one of them to stay up all night.
  - ![PC Only](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg "PC Only") The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch"), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. Her bed wasn't marked as a child's bed, which forced her AI to pick the nearest one available.
