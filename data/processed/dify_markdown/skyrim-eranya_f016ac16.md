Eranya

**Eranya** is a dead [Breton](/wiki/Skyrim:Breton "Skyrim:Breton") (see bugs below) [mage](/wiki/Skyrim:CombatMageElemental "Skyrim:CombatMageElemental") who can be found at the [Sacellum of Boethiah](/wiki/Skyrim:Sacellum_of_Boethiah "Skyrim:Sacellum of Boethiah"). She wears [black mage robes](/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes "Skyrim:Black Mage Robes") and carries [Eranya's Journal](/wiki/Skyrim:Eranya%27s_Journal "Skyrim:Eranya's Journal").
