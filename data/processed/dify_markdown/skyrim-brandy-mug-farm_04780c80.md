Brandy-Mug Farm

**Brandy-Mug Farm** is a [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") farm, a short distance east of [Windhelm Stables](/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm_Stables "Skyrim:Windhelm Stables").

It is a modest single-roomed farmhouse, that is home to [Bolfrida Brandy-Mug](/wiki/Skyrim:Bolfrida_Brandy-Mug "Skyrim:Bolfrida Brandy-Mug"). It consists of two zones: the [exterior](#Exterior) and the [interior](#Brandy-Mug_Farm).

### Exterior

As you approach the farmhouse from the road there is a blue mountain flower to the right of the path to the front door. In the fenced off field beside the farmhouse [Bolfrida](/wiki/Skyrim:Bolfrida_Brandy-Mug "Skyrim:Bolfrida Brandy-Mug") cultivates eight bushels of [wheat](/wiki/Skyrim:Wheat "Skyrim:Wheat") and a large number of [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries") bushes. In the field, there is a chicken coop, along with two [chickens](/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken "Skyrim:Chicken"), a [goat](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat") as well as a [wood chopping block](/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block "Skyrim:Wood Chopping Block") to the rear of the field and a [grindstone](/wiki/Skyrim:Grindstone "Skyrim:Grindstone") by the farmhouse.

### Brandy-Mug Farm

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-interior-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg)

Brandy-Mug farm interior

Upon entering there is a set of shelves on your left, against the north wall holding two [leeks](/wiki/Skyrim:Leek "Skyrim:Leek"), a bulb of [garlic](/wiki/Skyrim:Garlic "Skyrim:Garlic"), three [potatoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Potato "Skyrim:Potato") and several pieces of cheese on top. On the floor to the left is a basket containing two more leeks and hanging from the ceiling above are two garlic braids, three bunches of dried [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Mirriam "Skyrim:Frost Mirriam"). In the northeastern corner there is a barrel containing [foodstuffs](/wiki/Skyrim:Food "Skyrim:Food") and coming out from the center of the east wall is an owned double bed with an unlocked [chest](/wiki/Skyrim:Chest "Skyrim:Chest") containing leveled items at the foot of the bed.

In the southeastern corner is a round wooden table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead "Skyrim:Black-Briar Mead") anched against the south wall a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothes "Skyrim:Clothes") with a rack hanging from the ceiling to the right holding three [rabbits](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)"). In the middle of the south wall, opposite the entrance is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Pot "Skyrim:Cooking Pot") in front and on the mantelpiece is a copy of the [Alteration](/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration "Skyrim:Alteration") [skill book](/wiki/Skyrim:Skill_Books "Skyrim:Skill Books") *[Daughter of the Niben](/wiki/Skyrim:Daughter_of_the_Niben "Skyrim:Daughter of the Niben")*. To the right of the fireplace, also against the south wall is a long wooden table and bench with a bottle of [Nord mead](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead "Skyrim:Nord Mead"), a bottle of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage"), several loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"), a potato, an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge") and a sprig of [snowberries](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries"). Under the table is a [potion of vigor](/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor "Skyrim:Potion of Vigor"). In the northwestern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs with four more sprigs of snowberries on top.

## Notes

- Other than most barrels and sacks, all containers in the farm are [non-respawning](/wiki/Skyrim:Respawning "Skyrim:Respawning") and safe to store items in.
- [![On PC](//images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game "PC") Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch "Skyrim Mod:Unofficial Skyrim Patch") marks the barrels outside the building as owned by the farm.
