**Evette San** is a [Nord](/wiki/Skyrim:Nord "Skyrim:Nord") [food vendor](/wiki/Skyrim:Food_Vendor "Skyrim:Food Vendor") who has a [house](/wiki/Skyrim:Evette_San%27s_House "Skyrim:Evette San's House") and a market stall in [Solitude](/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude "Skyrim:Solitude"). She deals in a variety of [wines](/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Beverages "Skyrim:Food") and other beverages. Her latest shipment of spiced wine has been halted by the [East Empire Company](/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company "Lore:East Empire Company") and she needs someone to convince them to release it.

Her business very much depends on the annual Festival held by the Bards College, but since [High King Torygg](/wiki/Skyrim:High_King_Torygg "Skyrim:High King Torygg")'s death at the hands of [Ulfric Stormcloak](/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfric_Stormcloak "Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak"), and with the widowed [Elisif the Fair](/wiki/Skyrim:Elisif_the_Fair "Skyrim:Elisif the Fair") declaring the Festival as 'poor taste', Evette sees no real future for her unique spiced wine.

She owns a house in Solitude, right across the street from [Proudspire Manor](/wiki/Skyrim:Proudspire_Manor "Skyrim:Proudspire Manor"), and sleeps there every night between midnight and 6am. After waking up she heads directly to the market stalls and spends fourteen hours offering her various beverages, including her trademark [Spiced Wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Spiced_Wine "Skyrim:Spiced Wine"), based on an old family recipe created by her father, infamous town drunk [Octieve San](/wiki/Skyrim:Octieve_San "Skyrim:Octieve San"). At 8pm she heads back to the house and spends the rest of the evening there, either eating, wandering or relaxing. There are two exceptions to her daily routine. When you arrive in Solitude for the first time, she will be amidst the crowd witnessing the execution of [Roggvir](/wiki/Skyrim:Roggvir "Skyrim:Roggvir"). During the Burning of King Olaf Festival, near the conclusion of the quest [Tending the Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Tending_the_Flames "Skyrim:Tending the Flames"), she will sell her goods near the entrance to the [Bards College](/wiki/Skyrim:Bards_College "Skyrim:Bards College") and will even hand you a few bottles of spiced wine, as a reward for saving the festival.

Selling her wine hasn't made her rich and her attire reflects that, a set of farm [clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261c0 "Skyrim:Clothing") and a pair of cheap [boots](/wiki/Skyrim:Clothing#000261bd "Skyrim:Clothing"). She wields an [iron dagger](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger") and carries her [house key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Evette_San%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Evette San's House"), a spare [belted tunic](/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic "Skyrim:Belted Tunic") and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor "Skyrim:Leveled Lists").

## Dialogue

When she is at her stall, she will often try and attract customers by yelling:

:   *"Spiced Wine to warm the blood!"*
:   *"Keep the cold at bay with our spiced wine!"*
:   *"Spiced wine, the taste of home and hearth!"*

When you get near her, she will try to sell her produce even harder:

:   *"Spiced wine for ya? It's a family recipe."*
:   *"Wanderer like you needs a little fire in the veins. Some spiced wine?"*
:   *"I swear, I left another bottle right here..."*
:   *"If you see my father Octieve around, stay clear. He'll only ask you for money or mead."*

When spoken to:

:   **Is your spiced wine made from a secret recipe?**
:   *"Oh, yes. A secret family recipe. The only good thing my father Octieve ever did. In fact, it's so good, no one else in Skyrim even tries to make any."*

When finishing conversation, she will remark:

:   *"Good luck out there. It seems like a tough time to be wandering Skyrim."*
:   *"Stay warm and keep sober. It's the two things I know for sure will make life better."*

When asked about her business, before starting the quest [Tending the Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Tending_the_Flames "Skyrim:Tending the Flames")

:   **How are your sales?**
:   *"They're... adequate. Honestly, I rely on the business brought in by the Burning of King Olaf, and that's not likely to happen this year."*
:   **What's the Burning of King Olaf?**
:   *"It's a great big festival put on by the bards, every year. They burn an effigy of old King Olaf. It's quite a bit of fun. The bards give out spiced wine during the festival, so they put in a big order. And then, for weeks after, demand is so high, I can barely keep up. But with the death of High King Torygg, the Festival has been declared "in poor taste." So it looks like it won't happen this year."*

After Tending the Flames is complete:

:   **How are your sales?**
:   *"Oh, very brisk. When the snow sets in, even the hardiest Nord needs some spiced wine to warm the blood."*

### Festival Dialogue

However, once you save the Festival, Evette will be overly excited and can be found near the Bards College during the festivities, offering her spiced wine as always:

:   *"Spiced Wine here. First cup is paid for by the college."*
:   *"Get some Spiced Wine. A Festival Favorite."*

Not surprisingly, she will treat you with the utmost respect and hand you a few bottles with the words:

:   *"Just one bottle per customer. Except for the man who saved the festival. Here's a couple bottles for you."*

### The Spiced Wine

In addition to her problems with the festival, Evette also faces huge problems with the [East Empire Company](/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company "Lore:East Empire Company") as they have been holding back supplies. If you have yet to start the [Dark Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") quest [Bound Until Death](/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Until_Death "Skyrim:Bound Until Death"), Evette will greet you with:

:   *"You wouldn't have a few minutes to run down to the docks would you?"*
:   **What do you need from the docks?**
:   *"I've got a shipment of Spices the East Empire Company is holding up. I need someone to convince them to release it."*

:   :   **I'm not really heading that way.**
    :   *"Ah well. I'll keep looking for someone. Thanks anyway."*

:   :   **Shouldn't be a problem.**
    :   *"Great. Get back to me when you're done and I'll give you something for your time."*

When you return from [Vittoria Vici](/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria_Vici "Skyrim:Vittoria Vici"), Evette will be pleased to see her shipment released:

:   **The East Empire Company is sending up your spices.**
:   *"Thank you so much. I swear it's a fight with them every shipment. Here's something for your troubles."*

## Conversations

When working at the market, Evette will quite often engage in conversations with the townsfolk:

:   **[Aia](/wiki/Skyrim:Aia_Arria "Skyrim:Aia Arria")**: *"You know, my father collected exotic alcohols."*
:   **Evette**: *"Really?"*
:   **Aia**: *"Rum from [Hammerfell](/wiki/Lore:Hammerfell "Lore:Hammerfell"), mulled wine from [Daggerfall](/wiki/Lore:Daggerfall "Lore:Daggerfall") and the like."* **or**
:   *"None of what he collected tasted as good as your Spiced Wine, Evette."* **or**
:   *"I'm still finding your Spiced Wine to be the best drink I've ever tasted."*
:   **Evette**: *"Thanks, Aia. That means a lot."* **or**
:   *"Glad to hear it, Aia."*

:   **Evette**: *"Spiced Wine for the [Winking Skeever](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Winking_Skeever "Skyrim:The Winking Skeever")?"*
:   **[Sorex](/wiki/Skyrim:Sorex_Vinius "Skyrim:Sorex Vinius")**: *"Actually just a bottle for me today. I'm thinking of giving it to [Vivienne](/wiki/Skyrim:Vivienne_Onis "Skyrim:Vivienne Onis")."*
:   **Evette**: *"Sure, Sorex. Just like you did the last one."*
:   **Sorex**: *"The last one was a warm up."*

:   **[Silana](/wiki/Skyrim:Silana_Petreia "Skyrim:Silana Petreia")**: *"Are your sales going well?"* **or** *"I hope the Divines have been keeping you well."*
:   **Evette**: *"Not too bad. A few of the guests from the upcoming wedding have placed orders."* **or** *"They seem to when you're around Silana."*
:   **Silana**: *"It should be quite the affair. Nice to have something happy in the midst of the war."* **or** *"Vittoria is practically glowing."*

:   **Evette**: *"A bit of warming wine, [Illdi](/wiki/Skyrim:Illdi "Skyrim:Illdi").*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *You know you can't resist."*
:   **Illdi**: *"Not fair. You're well aware I can't seem to get warm here."* **or** *"Do you know I look forward to Burning of King Olaf each year just because of the fire?"*

:   **[Rorlund](/wiki/Skyrim:Rorlund "Skyrim:Rorlund")**: *"Ah a few bottles of Spiced Wine should hit the spot."*
:   **Evette**: *"Of course. And I'm sure it's mostly for ceremonial purposes."*

:   **[Ataf](/wiki/Skyrim:Ataf "Skyrim:Ataf")**: *"I'll take a bottle of spiced wine."*
:   **Evette**: *"I haven't known you to drink before, Ataf."* **or** *"A bottle of spiced wine for ya, Ataf?"*
:   **Ataf**: *"Viarmo is trying to get Elisif to allow the Burning of King Olaf. I don't want to look like I don't know how to drink in front of the other bards."* **or** *"No thank you. I'm still feeling sick from the festival."*

:   **[Jawanan](/wiki/Skyrim:Jawanan "Skyrim:Jawanan")**: *"I think I may wait until the Burning of King Olaf to have and*[[sic](/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes "UESPWiki:Spelling")] *Spiced Wine."*

Back at home, she and her father may chat:

:   **Evette**: *"Father, could you clean up the house a little while I'm away?"*
:   **Octieve**: *"Maybe. I'll see if I feel like it."*
:   **Evette**: *"I'm on my feet all day. Can you please do this one thing for me?"*
:   **Octieve**: *"Sure, whatever."*

:   **Octieve**: *"Where've you been all day?"*
:   **Evette**: *"At the market, father."*
:   **Octieve**: *"Well, did you bring back any mead?"*
:   **Evette**: *"I barely make enough for us to eat as it is, father. You can't just drink it all away."*
:   **Octieve**: *"Humph. Some daughter you are."*
