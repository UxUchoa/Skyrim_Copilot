Whiterun Hold

**Whiterun Hold** is a [hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in central [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), with its capital in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). It is initially neutral in Skyrim's [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War"), but both sides are actively competing to gain control of Whiterun. As a result, it is the site of the first battle of the war, Battle for Whiterun ([Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Imperial) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Imperial)") or [Stormcloaks](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks) "Skyrim:Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)")). The Imperial Legion will secure the allegiance of the incumbent Jarl [Balgruuf](/wiki/Skyrim:Balgruuf_the_Greater "Skyrim:Balgruuf the Greater"), who seeks reinforcements in the face of an imminent Stormcloak siege, and Balgruuf's government, now aligned with the Empire, will accordingly remain in control of the hold should the Imperial Legion successfully defend the city; otherwise, the victorious Stormcloaks will put [Vignar Gray-Mane](/wiki/Skyrim:Vignar_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Vignar Gray-Mane") in power.

## Topographical Overview

The hold borders six of the other eight [holds](/wiki/Skyrim:Holds "Skyrim:Holds") in [Skyrim](/wiki/Lore:Skyrim "Lore:Skyrim"), all but [Winterhold](/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_(region) "Skyrim:Winterhold (region)") and [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar").

Whiterun Hold is mostly covered in tundra and bordered by two major rivers (the [White River](/wiki/Skyrim:White_River "Skyrim:White River") to the south and the [River Hjaal](/wiki/Skyrim:River_Hjaal "Skyrim:River Hjaal") to the north), which makes the land fertile and well suited to farming.

### Flora and Fauna

The tundra is home to all manner of [animals](/wiki/Skyrim:Animals "Skyrim:Animals") and [creatures](/wiki/Skyrim:Creatures "Skyrim:Creatures"), from the large ([giants](/wiki/Skyrim:Giant "Skyrim:Giant") and [mammoths](/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth "Skyrim:Mammoth")) to the small ([foxes](/wiki/Skyrim:Fox "Skyrim:Fox") and [goats](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat "Skyrim:Goat")). The White River, flowing down from [Lake Ilinalta](/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Ilinalta "Skyrim:Lake Ilinalta"), is home to a large population of [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(creature) "Skyrim:Salmon (creature)").

The tundra is rich in [lavender](/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender "Skyrim:Lavender"), [tundra cotton](/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton "Skyrim:Tundra Cotton") and [butterflies](/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly "Skyrim:Butterfly"). At night the butterflies are replaced by [torchbugs](/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug "Skyrim:Torchbug") and [luna moths](/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth "Skyrim:Luna Moth"). Another location of interest is [Riverwood](/wiki/Skyrim:Riverwood "Skyrim:Riverwood"), where the pine forest stretching up from Falkreath Hold takes by. Here are forest plants like [mora tapinella](/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella "Skyrim:Mora Tapinella") and [thistle](/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle "Skyrim:Thistle") more common than on the tundra. All types of [mountain flowers](/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Mountain Flower"), except the yellow variant, can be found throughout the hold. The frail [red](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Mountain_Flower "Skyrim:Red Mountain Flower") variant however seems to thrive best around the rivers by Whiterun. The snowy parts, like [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World"), are only home to the humble [snowberry](/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberries "Skyrim:Snowberries").

### Routes and Pathways

As the central hold in Skyrim, its main roads are in good condition, but a few of the more remote roads are not as well maintained. There are main roads heading southward to [Falkreath Hold](/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Hold "Skyrim:Falkreath Hold") through Riverwood and [Helgen](/wiki/Skyrim:Helgen "Skyrim:Helgen"), eastward into [Eastmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch "Skyrim:Eastmarch") and [the Rift](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rift "Skyrim:The Rift"), northward to [Hjaalmarch](/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch "Skyrim:Hjaalmarch"), and westward to [Haafingar](/wiki/Skyrim:Haafingar "Skyrim:Haafingar") through Rorikstead and to [the Reach](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reach "Skyrim:The Reach"). Travelers should be encouraged to see that the main roads are regularly patrolled by [Whiterun Guards](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Guard "Skyrim:Whiterun Guard").

Quests that are started in or related to Whiterun Hold (except for radiant quests begun in other holds) are listed here:

- **[Unbound](/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound "Skyrim:Unbound")**: Escape [Imperial](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") custody and a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") attack.

- **[Dragon Rising](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Rising "Skyrim:Dragon Rising")**: Investigate a [dragon](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon "Skyrim:Dragon") sighting near [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").
- **[The Way of the Voice](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Way_of_the_Voice "Skyrim:The Way of the Voice")**: Meet the [Greybeards](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards").
- **[The Horn of Jurgen Windcaller](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Horn_of_Jurgen_Windcaller "Skyrim:The Horn of Jurgen Windcaller")**: Retrieve a [Greybeard](/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeards "Skyrim:Greybeards") artifact from [Ustengrav](/wiki/Skyrim:Ustengrav "Skyrim:Ustengrav").

- **[Diplomatic Immunity](/wiki/Skyrim:Diplomatic_Immunity "Skyrim:Diplomatic Immunity")**: Infiltrate a party at the [Thalmor Embassy](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy "Skyrim:Thalmor Embassy").
- **[A Cornered Rat](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Cornered_Rat "Skyrim:A Cornered Rat")**: Locate [Esbern](/wiki/Skyrim:Esbern "Skyrim:Esbern").
- **[Alduin's Wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin%27s_Wall "Skyrim:Alduin's Wall")**: Locate the prophecy within [Sky Haven Temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Sky_Haven_Temple "Skyrim:Sky Haven Temple").
- **[The Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:The Throat of the World")**: Receive a lesson from [Paarthurnax](/wiki/Skyrim:Paarthurnax_(dragon) "Skyrim:Paarthurnax (dragon)").
- **[Elder Knowledge](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder_Knowledge "Skyrim:Elder Knowledge")**: Recover an [Elder Scroll](/wiki/Skyrim:Elder_Scroll "Skyrim:Elder Scroll") to learn an [Ancient Secret](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonrend "Skyrim:Dragonrend").
- **[Alduin's Bane](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin%27s_Bane "Skyrim:Alduin's Bane")**: Exploit the Time-Wound to learn [Dragonrend](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonrend "Skyrim:Dragonrend") and defeat [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin") at the [Throat of the World](/wiki/Skyrim:Throat_of_the_World "Skyrim:Throat of the World").
- **[The Fallen](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Fallen "Skyrim:The Fallen")**: Capture and interrogate [an ally](/wiki/Skyrim:Odahviing "Skyrim:Odahviing") of [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin").
- **[Paarthurnax](/wiki/Skyrim:Paarthurnax_(quest) "Skyrim:Paarthurnax (quest)")**: Deal with a [potential threat](/wiki/Skyrim:Paarthurnax_(dragon) "Skyrim:Paarthurnax (dragon)").
- **[Season Unending](/wiki/Skyrim:Season_Unending "Skyrim:Season Unending")**: Enforce a ceasefire to secure [Dragonsreach](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach "Skyrim:Dragonsreach").
- **[The World-Eater's Eyrie](/wiki/Skyrim:The_World-Eater%27s_Eyrie "Skyrim:The World-Eater's Eyrie")**: Battle through [Skuldafn](/wiki/Skyrim:Skuldafn "Skyrim:Skuldafn") to reach [Alduin](/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin "Skyrim:Alduin")'s portal to [Sovngarde](/wiki/Skyrim:Sovngarde_(place) "Skyrim:Sovngarde (place)").

### Daedric and Divine Quests

- **[A Night To Remember](/wiki/Skyrim:A_Night_To_Remember "Skyrim:A Night To Remember")**: Find your lost friend, [Sam](/wiki/Skyrim:Sam_Guevenne "Skyrim:Sam Guevenne"), as you recover from a bad hangover.
- **[The Blessings of Nature](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature "Skyrim:The Blessings of Nature")**: Restore the [Gildergreen](/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen "Lore:Gildergreen") for [Danica Pure-Spring](/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring "Skyrim:Danica Pure-Spring").
- **[The Book of Love](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Love "Skyrim:The Book of Love")**: Help the [Temple of Mara](/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Mara "Skyrim:Temple of Mara") spread the cause of love throughout Skyrim.
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials "Skyrim:Kyne's Sacred Trials")**: Participate in [Kyne](/wiki/Lore:Kyne "Lore:Kyne")'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Skyrim "Skyrim:Skyrim").
- **[The Whispering Door](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Whispering_Door "Skyrim:The Whispering Door")**: Investigate [Nelkir](/wiki/Skyrim:Nelkir "Skyrim:Nelkir")'s story of a Whispering Lady behind a locked door.

### Dark Brotherhood Quests

- **[Delayed Burial](/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial "Skyrim:Delayed Burial")**: Convince an unwilling [farmer](/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius "Skyrim:Vantus Loreius") to help fix a stranded [jester's](/wiki/Skyrim:Cicero "Skyrim:Cicero") broken wagon wheel.
- **[Hail Sithis!](/wiki/Skyrim:Hail_Sithis! "Skyrim:Hail Sithis!")**: One of the biggest [contracts](/wiki/Skyrim:Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real) "Skyrim:Emperor Titus Mede II (real)") in [Brotherhood](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood") history is yours to fulfill.
- **[Kill Anoriath](/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Anoriath "Skyrim:Kill Anoriath")**: Kill [Anoriath](/wiki/Skyrim:Anoriath "Skyrim:Anoriath") in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun").
- **[Take the Hidden Treasure](/wiki/Skyrim:Take_the_Hidden_Treasure "Skyrim:Take the Hidden Treasure")**: Torture the Brotherhood's prisoners for treasure.
- **[Locate the Assassin of Old](/wiki/Skyrim:Locate_the_Assassin_of_Old "Skyrim:Locate the Assassin of Old")**: Find the Dark Brotherhood [Assassin](/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin "Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood Assassin").

### Thieves Guild Quests

- **[Dampened Spirits](/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits "Skyrim:Dampened Spirits")**: Put [Maven Black-Briar's](/wiki/Skyrim:Maven_Black-Briar "Skyrim:Maven Black-Briar") competition out of business.
- **[Imitation Amnesty](/wiki/Skyrim:Imitation_Amnesty "Skyrim:Imitation Amnesty")**: Help get a convicted prisoner released from jail.
- **[No Stone Unturned](/wiki/Skyrim:No_Stone_Unturned "Skyrim:No Stone Unturned")**: Find the [24 gems](/wiki/Skyrim:Unusual_Gem "Skyrim:Unusual Gem") to restore the Crown of Barenziah.

### Side Quests

- **[Ancestral Worship](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestral_Worship "Skyrim:Ancestral Worship")**: Help [Golldir](/wiki/Skyrim:Golldir "Skyrim:Golldir") save his [aunt](/wiki/Skyrim:Agna "Skyrim:Agna") and stop a [necromancer](/wiki/Skyrim:Vals_Veran "Skyrim:Vals Veran") from defiling his [ancestral barrow](/wiki/Skyrim:Hillgrund%27s_Tomb "Skyrim:Hillgrund's Tomb").
- **[The Golden Claw](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Golden_Claw "Skyrim:The Golden Claw")**: A [shopkeeper](/wiki/Skyrim:Lucan_Valerius "Skyrim:Lucan Valerius") wants you to retrieve his [prized possession](/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Claw "Skyrim:Golden Claw") from a [bandit](/wiki/Skyrim:Arvel_the_Swift "Skyrim:Arvel the Swift").
- **[In My Time Of Need](/wiki/Skyrim:In_My_Time_Of_Need "Skyrim:In My Time Of Need")**: Find a [Redguard woman](/wiki/Skyrim:Saadia "Skyrim:Saadia") who is on the run from [her past](/wiki/Lore:Taneth "Lore:Taneth").
- **[Missing In Action](/wiki/Skyrim:Missing_In_Action "Skyrim:Missing In Action")**: Rescue a [missing Stormcloak supporter](/wiki/Skyrim:Thorald_Gray-Mane "Skyrim:Thorald Gray-Mane") from the [Thalmor](/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor "Skyrim:Thalmor").

### Creation Club Quests

- **[Balance of Power](/wiki/Skyrim:Balance_of_Power "Skyrim:Balance of Power")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers "Skyrim:Saints & Seducers"): Put a stop to two bandit gangs wielding strange otherworldly equipment.
- **[Battle of the Champions](/wiki/Skyrim:Battle_of_the_Champions "Skyrim:Battle of the Champions")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War_Champions "Skyrim:Civil War Champions"): A battle between two [Civil War](/wiki/Skyrim:Civil_War "Skyrim:Civil War") champions is about to begin.
- **[Gift of Kynareth](/wiki/Skyrim:Gift_of_Kynareth "Skyrim:Gift of Kynareth")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail "Skyrim:Lord's Mail"): Recover a legendary [Legion](/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Legion "Skyrim:Imperial Legion") [artifact](/wiki/Skyrim:Lord%27s_Mail_(item) "Skyrim:Lord's Mail (item)") and obtain the blessing of [Kynareth](/wiki/Lore:Kynareth "Lore:Kynareth").
- **[Legends Lost](/wiki/Skyrim:Legends_Lost "Skyrim:Legends Lost")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Sunder_%26_Wraithguard "Skyrim:Sunder & Wraithguard"): Track down Kagrenac's missing Tools.
- **[Pets of Skyrim](/wiki/Skyrim:Pets_of_Skyrim_(quest) "Skyrim:Pets of Skyrim (quest)")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Pets_of_Skyrim "Skyrim:Pets of Skyrim"): Find trained pets around Skyrim.
- **[The Staff of Hasedoki](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Staff_of_Hasedoki "Skyrim:The Staff of Hasedoki")**[CC](/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Hasedoki "Skyrim:Staff of Hasedoki"): Locate a legendary staff that's been smuggled into Skyrim.
