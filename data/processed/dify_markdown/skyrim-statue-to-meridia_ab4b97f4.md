The **Statue to Meridia** is the [shrine](/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Shrines "Skyrim:Daedric Shrines") to the [Daedric Prince](/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Princes "Lore:Daedric Princes") [Meridia](/wiki/Lore:Meridia "Lore:Meridia").

## Related Quests

- **[The Break of Dawn](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Break_of_Dawn "Skyrim:The Break of Dawn")**: Find [Meridia's Beacon](/wiki/Skyrim:Meridia%27s_Beacon "Skyrim:Meridia's Beacon") and use its light to cleanse her [temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Kilkreath_Ruins "Skyrim:Kilkreath Ruins") of undead.

## Walkthrough

The climb to the shrine is relatively easy. Steps are cut into the mountain, and Meridia's statue is quite visible. The stone structure supporting the statue is built in the fashion of a stepped pyramid. The door to the [corrupted remains of Meridia's temple](/wiki/Skyrim:Kilkreath_Ruins "Skyrim:Kilkreath Ruins") is built into the base of the shrine to the west, but will only be accessible after the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Break_of_Dawn "Skyrim:The Break of Dawn") is initiated. Visiting this location will start the quest if not previously begun.

A short distance up the mountain northeast of the statue is a [word wall](/wiki/Skyrim:Word_Wall "Skyrim:Word Wall") with a word for the [Elemental Fury](/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Fury "Skyrim:Elemental Fury") [shout](/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Shouts "Skyrim:Dragon Shouts").
