Braith

**Braith** is a [Redguard](/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard "Skyrim:Redguard") [child](/wiki/Skyrim:Child "Skyrim:Child"), and the daughter of [Amren](/wiki/Skyrim:Amren "Skyrim:Amren") and [Saffir](/wiki/Skyrim:Saffir "Skyrim:Saffir") living in [Whiterun](/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun "Skyrim:Whiterun"). The family is clearly dysfunctional, with Saffir buried in books all day long and Amren worrying about nothing but his lost family sword. As a result, the lonely Braith has turned into the local bully, her favorite target being [Lars Battle-Born](/wiki/Skyrim:Lars_Battle-Born "Skyrim:Lars Battle-Born"). During the [related quest](/wiki/Skyrim:Bullying_Braith "Skyrim:Bullying Braith"), you will help Lars get rid of his bully, and find out Braith's true intentions.

The family lives in [Amren's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Amren%27s_House "Skyrim:Amren's House") in the Wind District of Whiterun and Braith sleeps in her room on the second floor every night between 9pm and 9am. Immediately after waking up, Braith leaves the house and spends three hours fooling around with the other children of Whiterun, until she returns home at noon. At 3pm she exits the house again, this time for four hours of playing. The last two hours are spent inside the house, eating, relaxing, or trying to communicate with her distracted parents.

She wears [child's clothes](/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes "Skyrim:Child's Clothes") and a pair of [shoes](/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8 "Skyrim:Shoes"). She carries her [house key](/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Amren%27s_House "Skyrim:Key to Amren's House"), a selection of [food and toys](/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child "Skyrim:Leveled Lists"), and a small amount of gold.

You can see her walking around town with a chip on her shoulder, saying:

## Quest-Related Events

When you think it is time to rid Lars of the bully, seek out Braith and talk to her. She will now skip her usual greetings and instead say:

:   *"Stupid Lars Battle-Born. I fight him almost every day, but it's not much of a fight, 'cause he don't fight back."*
:   **You need to stop bullying Lars Battle-Born.**
:   *"Yeah? Why?"*

You can now choose between three options:

:   **I'm Lars' bodyguard. Mess with him and you mess with me!**
:   **He has a disease. It's very contagious.**
:   **You want to be shipped off to the [Honorhall Orphanage](/wiki/Skyrim:Honorhall_Orphanage "Skyrim:Honorhall Orphanage")?**

All three choices will lead to the same response:

:   *"Okay, okay! I'll leave him alone! I was just kidding around. Besides, if he'd only kiss me, I wouldn't have to beat him up all the time..."*
