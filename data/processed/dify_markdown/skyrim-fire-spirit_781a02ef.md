Fire Spirit

The **Fire Spirit** will be encountered in [Labyrinthian](/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian "Skyrim:Labyrinthian") during the quest [The Staff of Magnus](/wiki/Skyrim:The_Staff_of_Magnus "Skyrim:The Staff of Magnus"). It is found inside a chamber with a flaming sealed door. Approaching the door will spawn the spirit.

The Fire Spirit wears an unobtainable set of [ancient Nord armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord_Armor "Skyrim:Ancient Nord Armor") and wields two leveled [ancient Nord swords](/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord_Sword "Skyrim:Ancient Nord Sword"), which may be up to [honed](/wiki/Skyrim:Honed_Ancient_Nord_Sword "Skyrim:Honed Ancient Nord Sword") in quality. It also knows the [Firebolt](/wiki/Skyrim:Firebolt "Skyrim:Firebolt") and [Wall of Flames](/wiki/Skyrim:Wall_of_Flames "Skyrim:Wall of Flames") spells. The Fire Spirit also has a special passive ability that gives it its flaming appearance, as well as providing a 33 percent [resistance to fire](/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Fire "Skyrim:Resist Fire") at the cost of a 33 percent [weakness to frost](/wiki/Skyrim:Weakness_to_Frost "Skyrim:Weakness to Frost").
