Morwen's House

**Morwen's House** is the home of [Morwen](/wiki/Skyrim:Morwen "Skyrim:Morwen") in [Skaal Village](/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village "Skyrim:Skaal Village").

It is on the north side of the village, between [Edla's House](/wiki/Skyrim:Edla%27s_House "Skyrim:Edla's House") to the west and the [Greathall](/wiki/Skyrim:Greathall "Skyrim:Greathall") to the southeast. The player can choose to move into the home if they have [married](/wiki/Skyrim:Marriage "Skyrim:Marriage") Morwen.

### Exterior

To the left of the entrance is a bench. Along the west wall are two fish barrels and an archery target. Behind the house is a sparring dummy and a wood pile. Along the east wall is a bench often occupied by [Nikulas](/wiki/Skyrim:Nikulas_(Dragonborn) "Skyrim:Nikulas (Dragonborn)"), a crate with a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk") on top, and a fish barrel. The entrance has a novice lock.

### Morwen's House

The entrance leads into a two-room home. There is a small area on your left with a dresser against the east wall. On the dresser is a [steel horned helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Horned_Helmet "Skyrim:Steel Horned Helmet"), a pair of [steel Nordic gauntlets](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets "Skyrim:Steel Nordic Gauntlets"), and a wooden plate with a [silver jeweled necklace](/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Jeweled_Necklace "Skyrim:Silver Jeweled Necklace") on top of it. To the right in the southeast corner is a chair. There is an open crate with a [long bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow "Skyrim:Long Bow") resting in it and a basket with a quiver of [steel arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow "Skyrim:Steel Arrow") to the right. There is a shield plaque with a [banded iron shield](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield "Skyrim:Banded Iron Shield") and two crossed [iron greatswords](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Greatsword "Skyrim:Iron Greatsword") on the west wall of this small area.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House.jpg)

House-proud

To the right of the door against the east wall is a set of shelves containing [Banded iron armor](/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Armor "Skyrim:Banded Iron Armor"), a [steel mace](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace "Skyrim:Steel Mace"), a [steel plate helmet](/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Helmet "Skyrim:Steel Plate Helmet"), a large [coin purse](/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse "Skyrim:Coin Purse") and a [goat hide](/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide "Skyrim:Goat Hide"). to the left against the north wall is a narrow table with a bottle of [ale](/wiki/Skyrim:Ale "Skyrim:Ale"). To the left of this still against the north wall are two shelves, on the shelves is a [strongbox](/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox "Skyrim:Strongbox"), a bottle of [wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Wine "Skyrim:Wine"), a [green apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple "Skyrim:Green Apple"), a bottle of ale, copies of *[Beggar Prince](/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar_Prince "Skyrim:Beggar Prince")* and *[Mixed Unit Tactics](/wiki/Skyrim:Mixed_Unit_Tactics "Skyrim:Mixed Unit Tactics")*, a [horker tusk](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk "Skyrim:Horker Tusk") and two sacks. Beside the shelves, on the left is a chair, a barrel with a mead barrel on top and a cupboard with three bottles of [alto wine](/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine "Skyrim:Alto Wine"), a [gourd](/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd "Skyrim:Gourd"), a [red apple](/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple "Skyrim:Red Apple") and a wooden bowl containing an [ash yam](/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam "Skyrim:Ash Yam") on the shelves. On a ledge against the west wall are three sacks.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_02.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_02.jpg)

Very house-proud

In the center of the room is a fire pit with a [woodcutter's axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe "Skyrim:Woodcutter's Axe"), a piece of [firewood](/wiki/Skyrim:Firewood "Skyrim:Firewood"), a [salt pile](/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile "Skyrim:Salt Pile"), a cast iron pot containing a [baked potato](/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes "Skyrim:Baked Potatoes") and two [clam meats](/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat "Skyrim:Clam Meat") around it. and hanging above in front of the door are a [rabbit](/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_(dead) "Skyrim:Rabbit (dead)") and [elves ear](/wiki/Skyrim:Elves_Ear "Skyrim:Elves Ear") sample. There is a low railing between the door and the fire pit. Between the fire pit and the west wall is an open crate containing a [cabbage](/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage "Skyrim:Cabbage") and two fillets of [horker meat](/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat "Skyrim:Horker Meat"). three small steps lead to the southern part of the house.

[![](//images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_03.jpg)](/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_03.jpg)

Table for one

There is a long table against the southern wall that separates the bedroom from the rest of the house. On the table is a mead barrel, three loaves of [bread](/wiki/Skyrim:Bread "Skyrim:Bread"), an [eidar cheese wedge](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wedge"), a green apple and a plate containing a [salmon steak](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak "Skyrim:Salmon Steak"). There is a shelf on the west wall above the table with a bottle of wine on it. and hanging above the table is a rack with two [salmon](/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_(dead) "Skyrim:Salmon (dead)") and a rabbit. To the left of the table, on the floor is a bucket containing five bottles of ale. To the right of the fire pit against the east wall is a row of three weapon racks with a [Nordic bow](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Bow "Skyrim:Nordic Bow") and an [iron sword](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Sword "Skyrim:Iron Sword"). A sack is to the right of it and a bucket containing a quiver of [Nordic arrows](/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Arrow "Skyrim:Nordic Arrow") is to the left.

There is a doorway to the left of the table that leads to a small bedroom. On your left as you enter is an adept-locked display case containing a [glass war axe](/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_War_Axe "Skyrim:Glass War Axe"). There is a shelf underneath with a mead barrel and an [eidar cheese wheel](/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel "Skyrim:Eidar Cheese Wheel"). To the right are two barrels, a sack a in the corner a practice dummy. Half way along the south wall is an open crate with nine [iron daggers](/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Dagger "Skyrim:Iron Dagger"), with three more on the floor next to it. In the southwestern corner is a chair. opposite against the wall that separates the bedroom from the rest of the house is a single bed with a shelf above it containing a copy of *[Troll Slaying](/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Slaying "Skyrim:Troll Slaying")*. Finally at the end of the bed is a chest.
